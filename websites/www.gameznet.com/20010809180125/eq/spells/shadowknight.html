Shadowknight Spells

Level 9

Disease Cloud

This spell will disease your opponent and do a small amount of damage initially as well.

Invisibility To Undead

This spell renders you invisible to undead mobs.

Leering Corpse

This spell will summon a level 3-5 skeletal pet.

Life Tap

Drains Target's life and gives it to you.

Locate Corpse

This spell will point you in the direction of the nearest corpse.

Sense The Dead

This spell will point you in the direction of the nearest undead monster.

Siphon Strength

This spell will lower your targets strength and add it to yours.


Level 15

Bonewalk

This spell will summon a level 7-9 skeletal pet.

Clinging Darkness

This is a duration of time spell which causes damage over time. It also slows the targets speed down as it is somewhat of a blinding spell.

Endure Cold

This spell adds to the targets resistance to cold.

Fear

Fear will cause your opponent to turn and run away at full speed. Be warned however that he will return when the spell wears off and he may bring friends.

Life Spike

Drains target's life and gives it to you.

Numb The Dead

Numb the dead is an undead calming spell. By casting this spell on undead beings you will cause them not to attack players they would normally attack.

Shadow Step

This spell is a short mini teleportation spell that will teleport you a short distance away from your current location. It is mainly used as an escape spell.

Level 22

Convoke Shadow

This spell will summon a level 11-13 pet.

Dark Empathy

This spell will heal your target while damaging yourself

Deadeye

Deadeye grants the caster not only infravision but also the ability to see undead beings. This is important for races who do not have the benefit of infravision.

Engulfing Darkness

This damage over time spell is an upgrade to the clinging darkness spell.

Spook The Dead

This is an undead fear spell. When can upon the undead it will cause them to turn and run away. This can be a useful spell when used in conjunction with lull as you can lull one of the undead to prevent him from attacking you and spook the other one away from the area and get a chance to fight it one on one.

Vampiric Embrace

Vampiric Embrace will make your hand glow. During this period(about 2 minutes) you will randomly lifetap when in melee combat. The lifetap effect goes up every couple levels and maxes at 26 points a tap at level 50.

Ward Undead

This is a direct damage spell for use versus undead beings. This is an important spell to a shadowknight. Once you reach this level and gain the ability to ward undead you will be a powerful force versus undead monsters.

Level 30

Endure Disease

This spell will increase your resistance to disease.

Feign Death

This spell allows you to play dead. If it works the monster will assume you are dead and will leave you alone. You can lay around and regain hit points and then get up and run.

Gather Shadows

Gather shadows is an invisibility spell that can only be cast on yourself. This is very important to ogre and troll shadowknights as it will allow you to travel places you previously were cut off from.

Heat Blood

This is a damage over time spell. The importance of it is that it can be stacked with the clinging->engulfing darkness line of spells which will allow you to do a lot more damage stacking the two of them.

Life Draw

Drains target's life and gives it to you.

Restless Bones

Restless bones will summon a level 16ish pet.

Wave Of Enfeeblement

Wave of Enfeeblement is an area effect weaken spell.

Level 39

Animate Dead

This spell will summon a level 20ish pet.

Cancel Magic

This spell can be used to cancel magical effects that have been cast on you or your party members. It's great to eliminate damage over time, charm or root spells.

Expulse Undead

Expulse undead is an upgrade to the ward undead spell. It not only does more damage but is also more mana efficient.

Heart Flutter

This is a triple threat spell. It not only weakens your opponent and lowers their armor class but it also does damage over time.

Resist Cold

This spell will add to your resistance to cold.

Shadow Vortex

Shadow vortex is an armor class debuffing spell.

Shield Skin

Shield skin grants you a temporary(60 hit points or so) invulnerability. This may not sound like much(and is often only 1 hit at this level) but its same as adding 60 hit points via a buff. Also while invulerable you can not be stunned.

Shroud of Hate (V)

Imbue self with siphon attack proc.

Level 49

Breath Of The Dead

This is a water breathing spell.

Dismiss Undead

This is an upgrade to the ward->expulse undead line of spells. It is a high mana high efficiency spell which is very useful versus undead foes.

Dooming Darkness

Dooming darkness is an upgrade to the clinging darkness->engulfing darkness line of spells. It does damage over time.

Invoke Fear

Invoke fear is an advanced fear spell. It will cause the target to turn and run for a longer duration than the standard fear spell.

Life Leech

Drains target's life and gives it to you.

Shadow Sight

Shadow sight grants ultravision to the caster.

Summon Dead

Summon dead is the level 29 necromancer pet summoning spell.

Word Of Spirit

This is an area effect damage spell that centers around the caster.

Level 50

Shroud of Pain (V)

Imbue self with siphon AC.

Level 51

Siphon Life

Siphon life is a 75 hit point lifetap that takes 73 points of mana. I know what you might be thinking. Don't we have a 280 hit point lifetap already? Yes we do but this one is different.

First off it is more mana efficient than life leech. Life leech requires 1.125 points of mana for every hit point healed where siphon life requires only 0.973. Not a huge difference but it is a difference nonetheless. 

Life leech also has a two minute recast delay where you can chain cast siphon life until you are out of mana.  

On the flip side siphon life has a slightly longer casting time.

So what does this all mean? Basically life leech is still your life saver during combat. Siphon life should be used for reducing downtime or for stepping back out of combat and self healing in an emergency if life leech is already gone. 

This is not a great spell but it is still a useful one.

Level 52

Malignant Dead

Malignant dead is a new pet spell. It is a big step up from your level 49 spell. This pet is the level 39 necromancer pet spell. It can dual wield as your last pet could but has quite a few more hit points and can hit for up to 39 points of damage each hit. 

In the eyes of some this is the first shadowknight pet that can actually be considered a good pet. It dishes a pretty decent amount of damage and can take a few hits without dying. 

I personally love the level 52 pet. I keep him up at all times unless I am fetching(in which case pets tend to screw up things).

Rest the Dead

Rest the dead is an upgrade to the numb the dead spell. This is a calm spell which works against undead beings. 

This can be used in dungeons such as Sebilis(undead side), City of Mist or Karnor's Castle to isolate single undead mobs out of groups without having to resort to a feign death. 

I have found it to be quite useful when pulling in these zones. Be warned however that if this spell fails you may be jumped by the mob and any other mob near it. So don't de-memorize feign death. 

You will know this spell is succesful when it reads, "Soandso looks ambivolent."

Level 53
 
Boil Blood

This is an upgrade to the heat blood spell. It will not stack with heat blood but it will stack with the darkness and heart flutter lines. 

Boil blood will do over 500 points of damage over a very long duration.

Level 54

Banshee Aura

Banshee aura is a self only damage shield. For the duration of the spell(about 10 minutes) any being that hits you for melee damage will suffer 12 points of damage from the shield. 

12 points of damage may not soud like a lot when you are getting hit for 100 points from the mob but believe me it adds up. This is one of the most mana efficient ways of dishing damage that you have and the damage will add up on the mob quickly.

This spell requires pearl as a reagent but it will not use the pearl. You simply need to keep one pearl in your inventory to cast this spell. The reagent will never be used.

Panic the Dead

Panic the dead is an upgrade to the spook the dead spell. It is a very mana efficient fear spell with a very long duration. 

Using this spell in conjunction with dooming darkness you will be able to solo extremely effectively in zones such as Burning Wood or Trakanon's Teeth if you stick to undead.

Level 55

Bobbing Corpse

This is a slightly scaled down self only version of the necromancer's spell Dead Man Floating.  It will cause the caster to levitate, water breath and also will add to his poison resistance.

This spell is very useful for fighting mobs which might poison you such as Wyverns. It is also useful for taking shortcuts through zones(you can fly over an area quicker than you can run and you take no damage from falling). Lastly it makes it safer to explore a dungeon without worrying about falling into a pit trap.

Expel Undead

Expel undead is the last in your line of anti undead spells. This spell does up to 273 points of direct damage to undead beings. 

Shroud of Death (V)

Imbue self with siphon life.

Level 56

Spirit Tap

Spirit tap is an upgrade to your lifetap line of spells. It is a 150 hit poit lifetap for 144 mana. This is the exact same ratio as your level 51 spell with a slightly longer casting time but it allows you to tap more hit points at a time.

Life leech is still a life saver but spirit tap will make you more effective at self healing if need be.

Steelskin

Steelskin is an upgrade to the level 39 shieldskin. It can absorb a much greater amount of damage however. Somewhere in the neighborhood of 250 hit points. 

It has the same pitfalls as the original however in that it is a one time damage absorber that only works for melee damage. It also requires Jasper as a reagent which can run a bit expensive to use in every battle.

It is much more useful than the original in my opinion. I would recommend using it before large battles.

Level 57

Vampiric Curse

Vampiric curse is a DOT lifetap. It heals you for roughly 200 hit points of damage over time. Even if a monster dies you will still gain the hit points. It is by far the most efficient lifetap you will ever receive.

That having been said it also is more of a taunt than your normal lifetaps. Because the hit poits take a while to be transfered to the caster it is probably not your best bet for healing during combat especially when considering the taunt factor.

I would recommend using this spell for downtime healing.

Level 58

Cackling Bones

This is your final pet spell. It summons a level 44 necromancer pet. This pet can hit for up to 47 points of damage and can also lifetap. It is a great damage dealer and can take a decent amount of damage before dying.

Nullify Magic

Nullify magic is an upgrade to the cancel magic spell. By this level cancel magic will fail to cancel most spells without multiple casts. Nullify magic does a much better job.

Level 59

Cascading Darkness

Cascading darkness is the last in your line of darkness spells. It will stack with all DOTs other than previous darkness spells. 

This spell can do around 1150 points of damage over a long duration. It also slows as well as a snare spell. 

The downside to this spell is an extremely long casting time. This means that if you plan on using it for fear kiting you need to use it to open your fetch and you better hope it is not resisted. 

It would be wise to keep dooming darkness memorized as well for fear kiting in case cascading is resisted or in case it wears off before the mob is dead. Otherwise you will have a hard time getting the spell off again while being hit. 

Level 60

Asystole

Asystole is an upgrade to the level 39 spell heart flutter. It will stack with all DOTs other than heart flutter.

This spell does 69 points per tick over 10 ticks(one minute) for a total of 690 damage. It also debuffs(lowers) your targets strength by 40 points and its armor class by approximately 20 points. 

When used in conjunction with boil blood and cascading darkness a level 60 shadowknight can DOT for around 2300 points of damage in addition to their other abilities. 

Death Peace (V)

Improved feign death.

Drain Spirit

Drain spirit is the last in your line of lifetap spells. It lifetaps for 226 points of damage for 189 points of mana. This is a 0.836 ratio compared to the 0.973 ratio of previous lifetaps and the 1.125 ratio fo life leech. 

The weakness of the spell is it's long casting time.

This spell makes you a decent self healer if you step back or if you are not the primary tank. Life leech is still your best option as life saving spell however because of it's casting delay which is less than half as long as drain spirit.
