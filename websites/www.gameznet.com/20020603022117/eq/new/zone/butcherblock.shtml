Butcherblock Mountains

Location
The Butcherblock Mountains are located on the island of Faydwer to the east of  mainland Antonica


Zone Type
Butcherblock is predominantly a low level zone, and acts mostly as a means of acessing the three main cities of Faydwer.


Zone Borders
Butcherblock borders with Kaladim, Dangor's Cauldron (which leads to the mid level Unrest dungeon and the high level Kedge Keep dungeon) and also to Greater Faydark


Nearest Cities
That of the Dwarves; Kaladim, Of the Wood Elves; Kelethin and the home of the High Elves; Felwithe.



Inhabitants
Dwarf Guards control this outdoor zone, and are very unfriendly to evil race, diety or class players. For the lower level characters Butcher Block is a surprisingly well suited zone, with Bats and Snakes (lvl 1 to 3) as well as Giant Skeleton inhabited Chess Boards, Bandit and Orc camps (lvls 10 to 15)



Route
Butcherblock Mountains are reached from Antonica by catching the boat from East Freeport Docks, which takes you via the Ocean of Tears, stopping at Sister Island, and then zones into Butcher Block.
