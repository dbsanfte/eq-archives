Everquest Quest Guide: Kelethin Drunkard

The following quest was submitted by Bryan Turner:

The Drunkard in Kelethin is actually a goodly man by the name of Tandan Nybrite, if you hail him by his real name he gives a quest. You are to find the Nybrite Bandit Sisters (Lesser Faydark) and hunt them, returning to him their unique necklaces. I have hunted the sisters many times and they give excellent loot (bronze weapons and gems), but the only necklaces given are Pearl Necklaces.

The rest was submitted by Christopher (Chriscu of Xegony) Manescu.

"I have completed this quest, or at least up until I met the Drunkard. I hailed him, he said something to the affect that he wanted coin. I then gave him all four of the "Nybright" necklaces and he gave me a rusty halberd. I have been told that this quest goes even further in that you are supposed to go onto the Paladin's guild in Felwythe. This is where I ran into the end. When I gave the halberd to the Paladin nothing happened. So, it is still a mystery to me."
