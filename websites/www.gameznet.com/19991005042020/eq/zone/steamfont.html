Steamfont is the newbie zone of the gnomes. It connects with both Akanon and Lesser Faydark.

Steamfont is a good zone for characters level 1-10 and has a couple of monsters that can satisfy even the highest level characters in the game. Some of the typical monsters you will find here are decaying skeletons, skeletons, spiderlings, defective clockworks(a gnome contraption gone mad), elements, ebon drakes and kobolds, harpies. There are several skeleton and kobold camps throughout the zone to keep players level 4-8 happy.

The most important part of this zone however lies inside of the minotaur mines near the windmills. This is the only location on this island where a player can find minotaur axes. Also inside of these mines are a couple of upper 30ish minotaur guards and a mid 20ish necromancer by the name of Meldrath the Malevolant.

Every now and then a minotaur hero will spawn in this zone and start killing everything in it's path. This beast is around level 40 and will not only kill players but guards as well.

This zone is also home to the observer who needs a lens to construct a telescope. This lens can be found on Borxx a named evil eye in Runnyeye. This quest is used to obtain the Staff of the Observer a great 2h blunt weapon for spell casters.
