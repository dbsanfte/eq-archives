Everquest Beastiary Guide: Spectre

Spectre's are amongst the most powerful undead in the game. They can be found in the zones of Oasis, Feerot, and Rathe Mountains. Spectres range from level 33-38. These undead creatures hit very hard and very fast(double 96 point hits will drain your health bar in a hurry). Spectre's can also root their prey as well as casting a lifetap spell. They require magic weapons to be hit.

Spectre loot consists of a few platinum pieces and usually some type of scythe. The scythes vary in quality(from rusty to steel).
