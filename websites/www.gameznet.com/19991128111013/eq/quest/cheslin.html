Cheslin/Chesgard
This is an easy to do low level quest which gives some a decent reward for it's level.

Occasionally after doing other quests for Chesgard in the Temple of Thunder he will ask you to look after his son in his first few patrols. Chesgard does not have to give you this quest. You can bypass him altoghether.

His son is a guard named Cheslin who patrols in Qeynos Hills. He is quite a clutz and is destined to die on his first patrol.

You can wait for him to spawn at the guard tower near the zone to West Karana. When he spawns he will tell the other guards he is off to protect them.

To complete this quest you need to not only follow Cheslin but protect him. He is a brave young man, unfortunately he is quite a wimp. If you do not protect him he is likely to be killed by a giant rat, a wolf or a gnoll along his travels.

So follow behind him for his entire route. During the process you will notice that every so often he will say, "Did I just drop something?". An illusion card will then fall to the floor.

If you follow him for his entire route you will eventually get four different illusion cards. Return all four at once and Cheslin will give you a small reward and you will gain faction with the qeynos guard among other people. He will also give you a note to give to his father.

Return the note to Chesgard in the Temple of Thunder and you will receive faction with the paladins, clerics, guards, some experience and a decent item for this level range. Often you will receive rawhide armor which is not a bad reward.
