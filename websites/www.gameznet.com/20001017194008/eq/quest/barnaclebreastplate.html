Barnacle Breastplate
This quest was submitted by an anonymous user:

If you go to the Deepwater Knights in Erudin and talk to Weligon Steelherder he will talk about some [Deepwater Tasks].  If you follow through all the bracketed questions (And have high enough faction) he will give you a bag and ask you to fill it with the remains of the diseased shark along with the remains of its 3 young.  To get the diseased shark to spawn you must kill all the sharks that are constantly patrolling the water area by the docks.  This can be tough because these sharks are around level 17-18 and hit upwards of 36+ points of damage.   However, if you manage to kill these pests the diseased shark will spawn.  He is only level 10 or so and doesn’t hit near as hard as the healthy beasts you where slaying earlier.  Kill the diseased shark and collect the remains.  Killing the diseased shark is the key to getting the young sharks to spawn.  Once they do kill them and collect their remains as well.  These young sharks will be easier than the diseased!  Collect all the materials needed off the slain beasts and put in the bag that was provided to you by Weligon Steelherder and ‘Combine’ them.   Then proceed back to the Deepwater Knights guild and give it back to Mr. Steelherder.  In return he will reward you with the “Barnacle Breastplate”.  The stats for the “Barnacle Breastplate are as follows:  Lore Item, AC:8, SV FIRE:  +3, SV COLD:  +3, WT:  5.0, Class:  WAR CLR PAL RNG SHD BRD SHM, Race:  HUM BAR ERU ELF HIE DEF HEF.
