Ultra Spawn Strategies

Noble Dojorn Strategy One

8 Tank types, preferably with 2800-3500 HP each... no lightweights on HP. - 8 Clerics - 8 Necros - 5 mages - 5 Enchanters - 5 Bards - 2 Druids - 2 Shaman

Since melee is the only thing that will defeat the noble, it's all about keeping the clerics with as much mana as possible. The necros will act as mana batteries recharging the clerics as they can with pact. The necros will each have 1 enchanter, 1 bard, and 1 Mage in the group.

Enchanters will Clarity all before battle (or course)

Each person will have a mana rod from the mages

The bards will play their mana song for the Mage/Cleric/Enchanter/necro/Shaman groups, along with their Heal song so they can regain health at an accelerated rate.. This is most important for the Necros that are acting as mana batteris/mobile Manastones.

Tanks charge in when full buffed and the clerics heal in rotation. The easiest way to do this is the /assist on the Noble repeatedly to see who is needing the heal. When one cleric is OOM the next one takes over while he/she meds.. Each cleric will know who they come after in the heal list before hand.

Using this tactic The Noble falls.. he does have some spells to deal with but nothing that can not be dealt with... It is best to pull him inside the windmill and fight him in there to avoid falling off the island.

Note: Clerics should be using CH only, as you will run out of mana if you don't WAY too fast. Knowing when to fire off that CH is pivotal to the battle and really makes the difference with this mob. Too early, you go oom too fast.. too late and well.... you just lost a Tank. :)
