Everquest Beastiary Guide: Large Skunk

Large skunks can be found in several zones in Norrath(Butcherblock and Tox Forest). Skunks are all level 2 and are a desired target due to their stink glands which sell for 1.5 gold pieces. They are not without their nuisances however. Skunks can spray you with skunk musk(and usually do) which will lasts for about 10 minutes. During that time your charisma is drastically reduced meaning you will get poor prices for anything you sell and that you will pay more for anything that you purchase.
