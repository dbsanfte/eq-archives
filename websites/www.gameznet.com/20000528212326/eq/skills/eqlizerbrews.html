EQ'Lizer Brewing Guide
All recipes below are to be made in a "Brew Barrel." They can be found in basically every town across Norrath, but the ingredients, however, cannot. You may need to travel for those, or obtain the Foraged Ingredients from players with the ability to Forage: Rangers, Druids, and all Wood Elves.
