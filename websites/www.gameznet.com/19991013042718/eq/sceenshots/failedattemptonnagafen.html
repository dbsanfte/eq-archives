Raid On Nagafen
This collection of screenshots is a slideshow of pictures taken by several different players. The screenshots depict our attempt on Lord Nagafen(the deadly red dragon)'s life.They are a collection acquired over two different raids. The first was unsuccessful and nearly everyone lost their lives. The second one was successful after quite a few deaths.

This is a picture of the "Dragon Bridge". This is the bridge that leads to the lair of Nagafen.

These were the last group of fire giants that we had to face before getting Nagafen alone.

Nagafen's lair is right up this tunnel.

This is a peek of Nagafen in his actual Lair. Unfortunately he came running out to the doorway when we smacked the Magi. It would have been a much friendlier environment to fight in.

The battle had begun. Unfortunately when we cleared the last two giants Nagafen had run to the doorway here. He could not fit through the passage so we had to fight in this rather cramped hallway rather than his spacious lair. This caused us some headache. One might think you can simply run away and rest and recover your mana and hit points. This is not possible however as Nagafen can summon you back to his side(as many players discovered).

Here is a taste of the mass fear spell that Nagafen was casting. It would send 30 or so people running at a time. This would allow Nagafen to simply breath his lava breath(which is area effect and would reduce me(a level 45 shadowknight) by a good 2 bars at a time and claw(for 225) the remaining few players who are near him to death.

This is the best picture I have to give you an idea of the size of Nagafen. I tried to get a picture standing back a ways to emphasize his size. Unfortunately every time I attempted to I would be feared and it would go out the window.   I did not last much longer than this screenshot.

The last of my Nagafen screenshots. This is of the neck, wings and in the distance you can see his tail.

Our first attempt failed miserably. The fear spell which Nagafen would cast on us was the main culprit. Soon all of our tanks were dead. Their corpses were underneath Nagafen himself(who had summoned them there) so we could not ressurect them to continue. I died early but from what I gathered from the others there eventually we all ran out of mana. We had the beast down to about 1/3 of it's health bar but he simply summoned our spellcasters one at a time(who with no tanks had to resort to trying to meditate during battle and sending in pets) and sumarily slaughtered them. We had learned some valuable lessons for the next attack, and we would return...
