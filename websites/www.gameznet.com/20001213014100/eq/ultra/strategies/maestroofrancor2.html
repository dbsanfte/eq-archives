Ultra Spawn Strategies

Maestro of Rancor Strategy Two

You'll need to be familiar with the Buildings and associated names/numbers to understand this strategy.

Here is the map ill be referring to 

http://www.gameznet.com/eq/maps/PoH-MASTER.jpg

Using this strategy we can take down Maestro with 18 people with only a few deaths, or with 24 people and zero deaths.
 
After moving into C, Begin Clearing D, the most effective way to do this is to post a rogue as a lookout for roamers, and have a monk/SK (pref Monk) pull to the west door of C, then having someone peel off.  Clear All of Lower D, (dont worry with Upper D or Inny's floor, they wont agro if you do it right)

When you have cleared all of lower D save Maestro and his 4 guards (typically 2 golems and 2 clerics), pick a lucky druid to come harmony ALL 5 of them, and peel singles.  After you have Maestro Isolated, its time to buff up.  Buff up completely, don't bother with fake buffs, as he doesn't dispell. 

As raid leader/puller you need to be certain that all of lower D is clear, along with C, A, B, Q, R, S, T (no mobs spawn in T), or else you WILL get nasty trains.

After everyone is buffed, its time to get them into position.  The idea here is to keep casters (Wizards, Necros, Mages, Enchanters) and healers (clerics, druid, shaman)out of his AE lifetap.  Have all casters move behind the blue line on the cutout of C I made, and all the healers move behind the Green line, at NO TIME should the fight take you past the red line, if anyone pushes Maestro past the red line, shoot them.  After everyone is in position, you are ready to pull maestro.
 
Have a rogue scout to make sure roamers are clear.  Adds can be handled, but are definitely less than Ideal.  I usually pull him with a shruiken, then run my ass off.  If he catches up to you, he will stun you and kill you ;)  You should have sow/j-boots on any time when pulling, but DEFINITELY when pulling Maestro.  Pull from D following the blue lines, and come to a stop on the blue X. Your rogue needs to be watching to make sure you dont pick up any roamers, if you do, then you must decide quickly whether to mez the adds, or feign and make another attempt. 
 
Once Maestro is in the room with the melees, DONT let him hit you, as a monk you cant take his damage for too long. Simply feign before he gets to you, and let someone else reel him in.  Melees need to try to keep him centered between the lines, and casters should not be nuking till the raid leader calls for it.  Melee with him untill he is about 60% (75% if you have less than 20 people).  Use FIRE BASED nukes only, as he is resistant to most everything else. 

If he moves into the healer or nuker area, then they should IMMEDIATELY switch with the Melees, once again. the Idea is to keep them out of the AoE life tap.
 
Assuming everyone pays attention and does what they are told, then you should have a relatively easy fight, we kill him regularly with 24 people and no deaths, and have taken him with 16 people and 4 deaths before too.
