Everquest Zone Guide: Everfrost Peaks

Click Here For A Map

Everfrost peaks is a rigid icy zone which houses the barbarians. This zone borders with Halas, Black Burrow and Permafrost. This zone is great for characters of all levels.

Characters level 1-4 should hang out in the area near Halas and hunt goblin and gnoll whelps, polar bear cubs and wooly spiderlings. Characters level 5-8 can have a good time picking off the gnolls near Black Burrow. Most other characters will want to hunt wooly mammoths or orcs outside of the narrow passages near Halas. High level characters(40+) can group up and attempt to kill the Ice Giants that guard the Permafrost gate.

It is also the home of Karg Icebear(who makes lupine and polar bear armor as well as carries the gigantic zwiehander) and Tundra Jack.
