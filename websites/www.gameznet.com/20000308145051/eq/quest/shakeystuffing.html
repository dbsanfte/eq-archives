Shakey's Stuffing
This quest was user-submitted by Jagre Greye.

This quest originates from Reebo Leafsway in Rivervale. Shakey the Scarecrow has two problems, first he needs some stuffing and second he needs a new head.

1) Head to Western Karana and follow C. Miller through the fields until he drops a bail of hay.

2) Head to Feerrott and find Roror. He is usually relatively close to the
Rathe Mountains. Hail him, and then give him the Hay and 66gp. He will give you back some Cursed Hay.

3) Head to North Qeynos and visit the Temple of Life. As you head towards the temple there will some NPCs on your right. The one on the far left is the one who gives the blessed oil quest. Hail himand get the blessed oil.

4) Head to a forge and put the blessed oil and the cursed hay into it.
Combine. This has been known to fail if you have low smithing skill. If you are successful you receive Writhing Hay.

5) Head to Rivervale and give the Writhing Hay to Shakey the Scarecrow to receive either the Wee Harvester or the Belt of the River. Both these items are small races (including elves) and druid only.  Wee harvester has charges of summon food and belt charges of water
