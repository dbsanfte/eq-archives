Everquest Equipment Guide: Clay Totem

Clay totems are a decent magic item for low level clerics, druids and shamans. These items can be held in your off hand if you are using a one handed weapon. They grant you +5 hit points. Clay totems can be found off Mooto a goblin caster at one of the camps in Misty Thicket.

I personally would much rather use a shield than a totem.
