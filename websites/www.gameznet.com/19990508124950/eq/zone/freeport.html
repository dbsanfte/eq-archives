Everquest Zone Guide: Freeport

Freeport is a quite large human city. This city is spread across four different zones and is one of the most interesting in the game. First and foremost Freeport contains the port that leads to the Ocean Of Tears and Butcherblock. It is this town that connects the mainland to the dwarf/elf/gnome island. Freeport also exits to North Ro and the East Commonlands. Beware in this city because treachery is afoot everywhere. The paladins are waging their own private war against the corrupt freeport militia. Whichever side(if any) you choose will garner you hatred from the other.

Freeport makes a great newbie zone. There are many things to kill for a level 1-3 character including bats, rats, snakes, fire beetles and orc whelps.

Freeport features many different quests. Some of note include the corrupt freeport guard quest and freeport belt quest.
