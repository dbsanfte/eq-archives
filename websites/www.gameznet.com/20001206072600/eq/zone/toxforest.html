Everquest Zone Guide: Tox Forest

Click Here For A Map

The tox forest is the newbie zone of the Erudites. It borders with not only erudin but also with Kerra Island. This zone is mostly standard fare: bats, snakes, rats, skeletons, fire beetles, spiderlings and kobolds are a few of the beasties you will find here.

You will also encounter poachers who are part of a quest in Erudin.

A note on the skeleton mine: This looks like the entrance to another zone, with 2 skeletons wielding picks standing in the middle of the tunnel. You can actually talk to these two undead, but all they do is tell you to help them. They are non-aggressive, but will fight back if you attack them. Continuing on down the hallway is possible, but after a short way, you teleport backwards. The rumors I've heard say that this is supposed to lead to The Hole and the Necromancer city of Paineel, but that Zone isn't implemented yet.
