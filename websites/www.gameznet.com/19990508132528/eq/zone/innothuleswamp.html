Everquest Zone Guide: Innothule Swamp

Innothule Swamp connects the troll city of Grobb with the outside world. This zone borders with upper guk, Feerot and South Ro as well. This zone is targetted for players level 1-10. Popular targets include rats(of large and giant variety), various forms of snake(up to and including giant water mocassins), alligators(of swamp and bull variety), kobolds and froglocks of various levels.

The most popular destination in this map is by far guk. To find it you can hug a right from Grob and follow the zone ridge. If coming from Feerot or South Ro you can hug a left and follow the zone ridge. Also, there is a tower which lies west of entering from Guk.
