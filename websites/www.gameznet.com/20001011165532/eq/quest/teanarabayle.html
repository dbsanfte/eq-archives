Te'anara/Antonius Bayle Connection

This is an unsolved quest. You may discuss this quest in this ongoing thread or on our Quest Forum.

Here's the skinny:

There seems to be some sort of connection between Te'anara(the druid guildmaster in Surefall Glade) and Antonius Bayle(rule of Qeynos). The information here is in two steps. The first is a long known quest and the second is a tibdit that I stumbled accross one day on accident...

Gharin in Qeynos(he is the guy who normally is standing in front of a building near the clock in Qeynos but also patrols) has to deliver a letter to Te'anara. He personally would much rather be at the bar drinking himself in to unconsciousness. Tell him to go ahead and go to the Lion's Mane(hey you only live once).  If you feed him a few Crow's Special brews(they can be bought at Crow's in Qeynos) after he get's to the Lion's Mane he will ask you if you would deliver the letter for him.

This note basically coveres a new tax that Qeynos will be enforcing on all bear pelts. It also covers new enforcements on poachers. You are supposed to deliver this letter to Te'anara.

You may turn it in to Te'anara for faction and some gold but the quest ends there. Evil characters also have a quest to obtain this list and return it to Nomaria of the Bloodsabers. Neither side of this quest(I have both a paladin and a shadowknight) leads to anything interesting from this point... or so it was thought.

However interesting tidbit happened when I was roaming around Surefall Glade asking questions about the Bayle List to every person there a few months back(I was looking for the Fiery Avenger quest). When I spoke to Te'anara something interesting happened(she normally does not respond to anything). Here's how my chat with Te'Anara took place:

who is antonius bayle

Te'Anara: Antonius Bayle is a good man. His rule of Qeynos has been most kind to our land as well. We also provide the key elements to his long range patrol division. Our own [Ralem Christoff] serves as Field Marshall for that division.

who is Ralem Christoff

Te'Anara: Ralem Christoff is one of the first warriors to come out of Jaggedpine. He was born and raised to be a ranger. He now serves Antonius Bayle as the Field Marshall of a roving patrol. His quiet and calm exterior hide a tactical genius and a ferocious combatant.

I have searched long and hard for Ralem Christoff but was never able to locate him or to find any information regarding him. He is most likely a triggered spawn. Triggered by what though?

Seems like an intersting quest.
