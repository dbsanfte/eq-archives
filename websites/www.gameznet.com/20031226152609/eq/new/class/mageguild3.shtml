This is the Guide form the master Mage

I have played every race class combo there is but i keep going back to my Magician. The magician is one of the harder magic classes in the game but you are self sufficient and you can summon your verry own tanks they come in a variety and i will tell you about them latter but for now just be assured that you are THE class of the game. At lvl one your spell will do a measly 4 to 6 dmg but as you go along they are second only to wizards but then agian wizards dont get pets to help them out. Your main spells will be your summon spells but DON'T rely on them too much rember all summoned items will vanish after you camp out for more than 5 minutes. The main damage spells of ours are evocation and conjuration they are your bolt , rain , and direct damage spells and can be verry useful in groups. When Soloing NEVER use rain the monsters will turn agianst you and rip you apart but if you find your self with 2 monsters you cant handle leave your pet to die that is why you have him. Soloing is our main way of getting experience to gain lvls but if you do like me and solo till lvl 35 no one knows you and cant vouch for you in a group till you start and grouping with people is a great way to make friends. WORD TO THE WISE : when grouping dont try to tank let the warriors monks pallys do that you sit back let you pet melee and you hit it with spells. Getting your melee skill up is useless after lvl 16 if you melee you die that is that monsters hit too hard for you to even try and tank my life at lvl 35 is a measly 530 with FULL BUFFS and i go down FAST when trying to tank.

Your pets come in a variety i will talk about EARTH first. He has the most HP and does the 2nd least amount of damage at lvl 35 he does 20 to 34 with melee hits and no damage with his spell but he roots the monster which means it CAN'T chase you. Next is fire he hits fastest and has a damage sheild (when a monster hits him they recive damage also) His magic atack is weak but he uses it often and he has 2 types of regeneration. Next is Water this is the pet with the 2nd least amount of life and most damaging magic attack he has 1 type of regeneration and can die fast if you find yourself out gunned. The last but not least is Air he hits hardest and has a high agility his magic attack may stun a monster so he is good agians monsters that can cast spells he also cast invisabilty on himself when not fighting so if you lose sight of him he may be right there beside you.
