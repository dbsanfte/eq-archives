Burning Woods - Telin Darkforest, location positive 3000 positive 3000. Things to ask: What Forest?, What Answers?, What Evil?, Whta do you suspect?, What Change?, What about Innoruuk?, What action? - He will give you a worn note. (Telin Darkforest is a ranger and his faction is Faydark's Champions)

Greater Faydark - Find Faelin Bloodbriar, spawns at location Positive 1500, Positive 550. She has a place holder which is an orc, can't remember if it is a pawn or a centurion. Give her hte note and she gives you Faelin's Ring.

Kithicor Woods - Giz X'tin, he is kos to you, but if you turn into a wolf, you are safe. He spawns at random times from what I can tell, I got him during the daytime, so I didn't have to worry about the undead. Give him the ring and he will give you a Dark Metal Coin. As soon as you turn this in, a level 50 Tier Dal Reaver will spawn and chase you. You can kill him or just leave. You don't need antyhing else from here.

Burning Woods - Telin Darkforest, give him the Dark Metal Coin and he will give you a Worn Dark Metal Coin.

East Karana - Athele, location is negative 3600, negative 1600. Give her the Worn Dark Metal Coin. *WARNING* After giving her the metal coin, you have to complete the rest of the quest, up to killing the corruptor without stopping, becasue all the spawns after turning in the coin are triggered by turning in items. *WARNING* She will give you a Braided Grass Amulet for the coin. Find Nuien (spawns near, triggered from turning in coin). Give he the Braided Grass Amulet. He gives you the Frayed Braided Grass Amulet and triggers Sionae to spawn. Find Sionae and give her the Frayed Braided Grass Amulet and she gives it back and triggers Teola to spawn. Teola spawns on the other side of the zone and will walk to the "gathering" You can go find her or just wait till she gets to the gathering. When she gets to the gathering and has the Frayed Braided Grass Amulet, a Dark Elf Corruptor and two Reavers will spawn. They spawn on the mountain range across from the village. It is best to have a group of people up there to intercept the Corruptor. The 3 mobs will rush toward the gathering and kill everyone there. Your job is to kill the Corruptor before the Reavers get to the camp and kill everyone. You can kill all 3 mobs or just the corruptor. When he is dead, he will drop a Fleshbound Tome. If Athele is still alive, give her the Fleshbound Tome and she will give you an Earth Stained Note. If you didn't save the camp, then you will have to wait for Athele to respawn. Handing her the note will cause her to despawn.

Misty Thicket - Ella Foodcrafter. You can go looking for her, or you can go to the Wall or the enterance to Rivervale. She travels from guard to guard, giving them food. When you find her, give her the Earth Stained Note. Talk with her and she will eventually give you a Tin Bowl. Now it's time to do some foraging! If you were smart and read this guide before you started, then you will have a lot of these items already.

Greater Faydark - Forage a Ripened Heartfruit. Looks like berries and is NOT stackable. Lore Nodrop.

Innothule Swamp - Molded Speckled Mushroom - Looks like a large mushroom top, not stackable. Lore Nodrop.

Everfrost - Frozen Tundra Root - Looks like roots, not stackable, Lore Nodrop.

Misty Thicket - Sweetened Mudroots - Looks like roots, nostackable, Lore Nodrop.

Comebine these 4 items in the Tin Bowl and you will get a Hardened Mixture.

Timorous Deep - Alrik Farsight, location Negative 6000, Positive 2500. Hail him and follow the story. You will recieve a Crushed Pot. If your faction isn't high enough to do this part of the quest, you can fix it by doing the batwing quest in Felwithe.

Felwithe - Farios Elianos, he is in the first building that you come to when you zone into Felwithe B. Give him the Crushed Pot and he will give you a Grocery List. Take the list to Merchant Nora, who is in the shop of all holds. She will give you a Bag of Provisions. Go back to Farios and give him the Bag of Provisions and he will give you a Reciept.

Timorous Deep - Alrik Farsight, take the Reciept to him and he will give you an Ancient Pattern.

Surefall Glade - Merdan Fleetfoot, give him a Rose of Firiona, foraged in Firiona Vie and is droppable, he gives you a Wooden Painting.

Frontier Mountains - Human Skeleton. Location Negative 2100, Positive 2000. Give him the Wooden Painting and he despawns leaving a Silver Chained Locket on the ground.

Surefall Glade - Niera Farbreeze - give her the Silver Chained Locket and she gives you the Platinum Speckled Powder.

Kaladim - Kinlo Strongarm, found in Kaladim B, in the Everforge. Follow the story and he will ask for a Jade Reaver. Go to City of Mist and kill Black Reavers until one drops a Jade Reaver. Take it back to Kinlo and he will give you Enchanted Clay. Find any pottery wheel and combine the Ancient Pattern, Platinum Speckled Powder and the Enchanted Clay(requires no pottery skill) and you will get a Runecrested Bowl.

Misty Thicket - Ella Foodcrafter. Give her the Hardened Mixture and the Runecrested Bowl and she will give you a Softly Glowing Stone.

Karnor's Castle - You need to find Venril Sathir's Remains. It spawns every 12 hours while Venril Sathir is dead. When you find him, cast dance of the fireflies and the globe that you get from casting the spell, hand it to the skeleton to turn him into Venril Sathir's Ghost. Then, give a scroll of ressurection(purchased at any cleric guild) to turn him into Venril Sathir. Kill him and he will drop two Sickly Pulsating Stones. You only need one, so give the other to a Druid or Ranger friend or someone that helped kill him. This version of Venril Sathir is VERY simple and only requires about 6 people to kill.

Firiona Vie - Foloal Stormfrost, Give him the Softly Glowing Stone and the Sickly Pulsating Stone and he will give you a Warmly Glowing Stone.

This is only the first half of the Druid and the Ranger Epic quest. From this point, the druids and the Ranger paths split and become two differant Quests. The second half of the two quests can be found Below.
