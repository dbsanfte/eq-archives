Everquest Equipment Guide: Observer's Staff

This staff can be obtained as part of a level 30+ quest. The staff can be used by priest and mage types(wizard, magician, enchanter, necromancer, cleric, druid and shaman). This two handed staff carries a damage of 12 and a delay of 39. It also grants +5 intelligence, +5 wisdom, and +30 mana. As if that was not enough it has charges of the enchanter spell Sentinel which will warn you of others drawing near you when you are meditating.
