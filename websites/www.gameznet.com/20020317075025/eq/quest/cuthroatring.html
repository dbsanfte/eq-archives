Everquest Quest Guide: Cuthroat Insignia Ring

Seems that the Cuthroat Insignia Ring can also be used as a Quest item. The following information was sent in by Zerk, Rogue of Soulsek Ro.
"You can turn'em in for a pittance of a bounty to Occilious in South Ro (kinda hidden). This gives you faction with Soulsek Ro himself however. If ya do it enough he also hints you to a quest with Rathaman the wizard behind him. Speaking about Rathaman, he has 2 quests. One is easy, give him 30 gold after talking about traveling and spells or sometihng and he gives you a rotten illegible scroll that can be used by large races. So I am guessing its something a shaman can use. The next one up is a big one, Rathaman can create some type of magic item for you if you give him 20 gold and some "components". I haven't gotten much further but lost a few Runed totems staves and Skulls of Jhen Tra trying to figure it out."
If you ahve any more information please send it in.
