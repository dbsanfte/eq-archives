Everquest Zone Guide: West Karanas

West Karana is a large zone which borders with North Karana and Qeynos Hills.

This zone is popular for several reasons. First off there are several quest NPC's in the region. There are also various types of bear, wolf and snake. However the funnest targets in this zone are for characters levels 20-35. Hill Giants roam the countryside stepping on any who are not paying attention(doh!).

At night time you will find werewolves and the humans who hunt them. Werewolves of course are desired for their items, pelts and claws.

In an area hidden in the mountainside you will find an ogre and a cyclops camp. The cyclops camp contains froon and choon two level 30 cyclops who are a popular target of level 28-35 characters. Right next to this camp you will find an ogre camp which contains ogre shadowknights and shamans in the level 20-27 range. The shamans are sought after for the magical bone armor which they possess.
