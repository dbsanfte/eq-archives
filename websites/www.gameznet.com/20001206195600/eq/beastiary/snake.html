Everquest Beastiary Guide: Snake

Snakes are common in most zones of Norrath(the exception being the colder climates and most dungeon zones). There are many different sizes and variants of snakes. Normal snakes found in newbie zones are level 1. You will also find level 2-4 large snakes and giant varieties of snakes ranging up to level 10(including giant rattlesnakes).

Most low level snakes are non poisonous. The higher level snakes such as the darkweed snakes and giant rattlers can pack a powerful poison. If you do become poisoned finish the snake(or run) as quickly as possible and sit down to minimize the damage you will take from the poison. Remain seated until the poison runs it's course.

Lower level snakes carry snake scales and snake fangs. The scales are good because they can be stacked on top of one another and sell for 7-9cp each. The low level snake fangs are only worth 1cp but can also be stacked.

Higher level snakes carry snake skins which sell for a small piece of change but do not stack on top of one another. However they are hunted more for their giant snake fangs which make good low level piercing weapons and are a favorite among mage types.
