Dragon Tooth Choker
This quest was submitted by Slaariel. It begins in Skyshrine.

In Skyshrine, Find the room with the bickering Cook and Baker golems. In this room (assuming it's not camped) spawns a golem named Sentry Kale.

Each time sentry Kale spawns, he will give you one of three quests. So if he's not giving this quest you'll have to wait for a filthy dragon-camper to kill him before you can get the quest.

You're looking for the text: "Perhaps you wish to prove that you are a friend of the Kin".

Telling him: "I wish to become a friend of the Kin" nets you a Lore Nodrop note and these instructions:

"Good, then you will not mind assisting us with a matter of grave importance. We have received news of an alarming sort. There walks amongst us a spy of the Storm Giants, who feeds our every move to them and their accursed strategist. For the life of us, we can not deduce whom the traitor is, however we have received word from Wenglawks of Kael, an associate of ours, that he has information concerning this. For a measly sum of 100 platinum, he has offered to give us the information to rid ourselves of this traitor. If you are indeed friend to the Kin, take this note to Wenglawks and rid us of this traitor."

This speech is funny to anyone who's spent any great deal of time in Skyshrine, because there is very clearly a sentry named Sentry Rotiart in one of the rooms. I guess it's too bad they don't read Common.

Anyhow, take this note to Wenglawks in Kael. Wenglawks is completely factionless, so he won't attack you if you aren't a giant-lover. However, the giant in the room with him will hate your guts. There are several ways of turning in the note without dying including charming the giant in the room, training all the giants to zone, or just sucking it up and running to zone. Choose whichever method suits you.

Upon receiving the note and the 100 platinum, Wenglawks reveals that (GASP!) Sentry Rotiart is the spy! And he gives you a Lore, Nodrop item called a Dispelling Device (looks like a playing card). He tells you to give this to Sentry Rotiart and be prepared to fight!

Rotiart is in the pool room past the Cobalt Scars statue but just before the Real Skyshrine starts. He's alone in the room except for a green-con hungry cube that you can avoid (if you are KoS here).

Give the note to Rotiart. He despawns and in his place spawns a Krom'Dek Spy! The Spy was level 53, hitting for 149, and fairly magic resistant so have a compentent party with you when you go to do this.

Killing him yields standard giant-killing faction and a Lore Nodrop paper called 'a spy report'. Time to march back to Sentry Hale and ask the armor campers to wait outside the room.

I'm not sure if you can hand the note in to any of the Sentry Kale's that spawn (remember each time he spawns he gives one of three quests). I waited until the one who gives this quest spawned before handing it in. Handing it in gives you this:

"You have done us a great service, (your name here). I cannot believe a mere child of Zek could infiltrate our society, this whole encounter has been very frightening. Soon a time will come when we can no longer cut ourselves off from the rest of the world. Soon we will be forced to fight every day of our lives to defend our heritage and our way of life.

Hopefully we shall have allies such as yourself when that time comes. I would like you to have this small token of our gratitude. A venerable wurm allowed us to grace this piece of armor with his teeth when he passed on, wear it well."

The result is a Dragon Tooth Choker.
