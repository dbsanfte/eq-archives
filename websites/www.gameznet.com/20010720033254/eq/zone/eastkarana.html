East Karana
East Karanas is a great zone for players, lvl 15-20.  It links many high
level zones together including High Hold Pass, which is a gateway for
adventurers who travel back and forth from Qeynos to Freeport. 

Upon entering the zone from the North Karanas and a little further in, you will notice a small farm on the right.   Behind the farm, on the hill side, there is a pride of lions, this can be a real hazard if you are below lvl 15 and are trying to make the trip to Freeport.  The lions migrate from the top of the hill to the bottom and if you get attacked by the Lion Patriarch, be sure that the rest of the clan will follow suit.  Don't count on the guard and farmers at the farm to help you.

If you are heading to High Hold Pass, then after you enter the zone you want to head straight across to the pass and start climbing.  This will take you right up to High Hold Pass.  Be careful here too, occaisionally there are griffons and lions in the valley.

There are a lot of Chasm Crawlers (snakes) and Carrion Spiders that can be hunted for good exp  if you are lvl 15-17.  Beware of NPC's in this zone, they range from an escapable Gnoll NPC to the hard hitting Cyclop tandem, Droon and Broon.  If you are in a party, then you might want to concentrate on killing the Griffawns and Gnoll Reavers in this zone.  The Fawns hit hard, so a mid-high teen level party is advisable and the Reavers ususally come in mobs, so a party is advisable here too.  At night, there are a few Undead Reavers that spawn and they seem to be quite a bit harder than the regular reaver. 

Here is a bit of advise that seemed to be quite helpful, this is not a zone to be roaming and hunting.  This is more of a fetch and kill zone.  The 3 NPC cyclops seem to roam quite a bit and they are very good at dismantling a party.
