Everquest Zone Guide: North Karana

North Karana is an outdoor zone which borders with South Karana, West Karana and East Karana.

A few of the monsters you will encounter in this zone include several types of bears, wolves, and beetles in the low end range. Higher level characters will found adventure in the griffons and hill giants.

This zone also features a gypsy camp where players can buy combine weapons.
