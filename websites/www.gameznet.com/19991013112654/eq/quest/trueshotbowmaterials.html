Trueshot Bow Materials Quest
This quest takes place in Kelethin. Thanks go to Tracy for the additional information and to Hammer for revising the quest data.

This quest takes place in the Kelethin Ranger's Guild and rewards
the persistant ranger with a Treant Stave, which is the key ingredient
in fletching the legendary Trueshot Longbow. It is important to note
that the quest itself does not give you the bow outright, you will
also need to master fletching (or find someone who has) to actually
create the bow from the stave and its other components.

Keep in mind that when a fletching attempt fails, you lose the
components. Unless you like traveling on the boat a great deal,
you will want to collect several of each component while you're
at it. This will allow you multiple attempts at fletching the
bow without making the collection trip all over again. All the
components stack, with the exception of the spiderling silks.

Part One: Collection

Talk to Maesyn Trueshot and he will give you a pack to collect four
items, some treant wood from Jyle Windshot in Freeport, some dwarven
wire Trantor Everhot in Kaladim, a microservo from the clockwork
rogues in Steamfont, and a piece of regular spiderling silk.

The spiderling silk and microservo can be obtained by killing
spiderlings and clockwork rogue spiders respectively, or somtimes
simply purchased from merchants in the area or from other hunters.

The Treant wood comes from Jyle Windshot in Freeport. He will ask
you to give him a small lantern in exchange. These can be purchased
in freeport or nearly any other city and are inexpensive.

The Dwarven Wire is the most difficult item to obtain. Go to Kaladim
and look for Trantor Everhot, who makes frequent trips between
his blacksmith shoppe and the bank. He will ask you to give him
a Tumpy Tonic in exchange. (actually he will want two of them)
Tumpy Irontoe is a bartender in a pub near the Warrior's Guild.
You will need two give Tumpy two items for him to make a Tumpy Tonic:

   1 standard flask of water, can be purchased anywhere
   1 kiola nut, sold only by the female wood-elf merchant on the Isle of
     the Sisterhood of Erollisi in the Ocean of Tears. This is the
boat's
     first stop out of Freeport, or it's second stop out of
Butcherblock.

Hand Tumpy one kiola nut and one flask of water and he will give you
a tumpy tonic. Repeat to get the second tonic. Now hand Trantor the
tonics one at a time and he will give you a spool of dwarven wire.

Place one of each component in the pack that Maesyn Trueshot gave you
and hit combine. Give the combined pouch back to him to receive a
faction adjustment and the Treant bow stave, a lore item.

Part Two: Fletching

Use this bow stave along with dwarven wire, micro servo, and
planing tool in your fletching kit and you will have yourself a
Trueshot Longbow, stats 20 dam, 45 delay, 100 range, ranger only.
human, half elf, elf races only. It is a lore item. To give you
an idea of how good this bow is, a Darkwood Silk Shaped Compound
bow costing well over 300 plat [per fletch attempt] has a slightly
worse damage/delay ratio than the Trueshot Longbow.

That said, you need to be a master fletcher in order to make one.
I have 161 skill in fletching and out of 7 attempts I made 2 bows.
It also has a cool icon, not the standard bow icon.
