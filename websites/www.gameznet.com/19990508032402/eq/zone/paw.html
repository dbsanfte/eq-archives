Everquest Zone Guide: Paw


Paw is the home of the splitpaw gnolls. This zone lies dead in the middle of South Karana. If you are coming from the North Karana bridge you can head straight south and you will eventually come to a mound of hills with jagged ruins sticking out of them. In this small patch of hills you will found a hole in the ground which leads to Paw.

This zone is tuned for characters level 5-15 and contains several different low level magic items. The nobles, captain and commanders occasionally carry snakeskin armor which is stronger than leather and also has magical properties. The two Shamans carries a runed totem staff which is a favorite among spellcasters.
