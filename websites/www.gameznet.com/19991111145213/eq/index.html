Okies, today Carmine Pants are posted, Carmine of course is the new WIZ-only armor in which Verant put in along with Apothic(MAG), Insidious(ENC), and Blighted(NEC) to cut down on all the fights when people used to get Cryosilk from Fear and 4 caster-types all had to fight for it, hehe.

Also, to rectify a statement I made in yesterday's post about that new MNK-only robe is that it's not "pretty good" like i said, it actually is rather pathetic for the amount of camping time, and the degree of difficulty of the quest considering you must be at least 40+ to complete it and a Foremans Tunic is still better. So hopefully, the ends will justify the means for this quest if it's fixed.

Next on today's update would be the location of the "new" Haste belt(Swirlspine Belt) for melee-only classes which is found in Kedge Keep on a named lvl 51-52 Swirlspine Seahorse in the vicinity of Phinigel Autropos, the Lord of Kedge Keep.On that note, enjoy all.
