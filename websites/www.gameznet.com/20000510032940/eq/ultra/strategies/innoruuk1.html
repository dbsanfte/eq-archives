Ultra Spawn Strategies

Innoruuk Strategy One

We've killed Innoruuk 1 time on our server to my recollection. When we fought him he wasnt all too hard but a few things got to be done before u fight him. Make sure you clear his room of all clerics, and fight him in the archway room he roams through. If you dont and fight him on the balcony entrance to the archway his AoE gravity flux will surely toss you off into hate anywhere and will most likely agro spawned roamers below.

Also, Innoruuk has a built in Pillage Enchantment that goes off only when he hits a person, thus if he hits you, you lose most / all of your buffs. He hits hard, but If you can stick a Malosi, bring about 6 balanced groups up, make sure u debuff him well, dont go overboard buffing for the fight since he has no AoE dmg spells, just a gravity flux which isnt so bad if you get some magic resistance above 100, and he strips buffs in melee combat anyway. Just make sure u have bards, at least 1 per tank group since having that extra magic resistance is really nice since he strips away buffs like Resist Magic and re-buffing in-fight isnt something you want to be doing.

He didnt seem too hard when we killed him, he only casts Death Touch in direct melee / agro range, He typically DT's the first person to enter his chamber and agro him, and hits 1-2 more during the course of the fight (obviously depends on how long the fight lasts). Resistsances are moderate, not too bad, during our Fight Enchanters and Shamans took a few casts to land Malosi and Tashania, and after those stuck he was easily nuked by druids and wizards.

So, really keep magic resist high, also get alot of tanks bashing, interupting that AoE Gravity flux is nice since its about a 400dmg fall, and interupts anyone casting on him obviously. Bards help out tanks groups alot, nuke in melee range but not too hard since he hits 400's now he will surely wipe you out quick with that Pillage enchantment he has built in. Let tanks and clerics do most of the work, bash him down good, nuke light, and dot pretty heavily.
