Temple of Solusek Ro Magician Quests

These quests all take place at the magician NPC's in the temple of Solusek Ro. These items are all magician only.

Clay Bracelet: You need to acquire 4 runes of clay. 3 of these come from gnomish necros in Rathe mtns. The other comes from the goblin headmaster in ocean of tears.

They are 4ac weight 1.0 +5 sv magic lore NO DROP. They do NOT have any CHARGES of anything. The 3 necros in the mtns are in 2 different spots one close to the lake zone and 2 together closer to feerot zone. The necros are in buildings surrounded by undead. They are not hard to find. The goblin headmaster is by a tent on the oracle island.

Earthen Boots: You need to acquire a stone marker (glyphed guard in
mistmoore), heart of stone (stone skeleton), soiled boots (froglok Tonta
Knight in guk), and a cats eye agate.

The glyphed guards are in the graveyard in mistmore. The stone skeletons spawn in a group of 2. They have pets and cast the level 24 mage spell shock of spikes as their primary offense. This makes them very hard for their level.

Circlet of Mist: You need to acquire a robe of mist(festering hag in unrest), watery ring (aqua goblin tidal lord in dagnor's), ring of evoluoy (large alligator in cazic), and a sapphire.

Most of this is pretty strait forward kill goblin on way to unrest, kill
hag in unrest. The large alligator spawns in Cazic Thule near where the Steel Golem spawns.

Robe of the Elements: Joyce will make a Robe of the Elmements if you bring her the following: fire goblin skin (solusek), frost goblin skin (permafrost), a "Twice-Woven Cloak" (faerie guard in faydark), and a scroll of elemental armor.

The frost goblin skin is off ice goblin elite guards in permafrost. The twice woven cloak seems to drop a lot less than the twice woven silk I got 3 silks per my one cloak, there is a camp of faerie guard in lesser fay at 1000, 3500.
