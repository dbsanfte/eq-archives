Vah Shir



The Vah Shir race, is a race of cats. They make their home next to the Hollowshade Moor and are a noble, proud race. They are not considered evil when they roam near their homelands, and seem to travel with ease because of this.

The Vah Shir can be bards, beastlords, rogues, shaman, and warriors. They do not start out with the best strength or stamina for the warrior class, nor the best wisdom for the shaman. They do, however make wonderful bards and beastlords.
