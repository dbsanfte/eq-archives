Everquest Equipment Guide: Bone Bladed Claymore

The Bone Bladed Claymore is a magical two handed slashing weapon that is found on the final shadowknight in Befallen. I don't have the stats on me off hand(they changed since I last had it) but it is a very good weapon for two handed wielders in the 20-30 level range. The disadvantage is that it carries with it a negative adjustment to your agility score. It grants a damage of 17 a delay of 45 but penalizes you by -8 to your agility.
