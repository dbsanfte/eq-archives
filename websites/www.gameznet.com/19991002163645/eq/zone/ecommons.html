Everquest Zone Guide: East Commonlands

East commonlands is a waypoint that leads to Freeport, North Ro, Nektulos Forest and the West Commonlands. This zone supports players from level 4-12. It also contains two griffons which keep lower level players on their toes and give higher level characters something to hunt. Other than the aforementioned this zone contains black bears, young kodiaks, black and shadow wolves, several species of snakes(including the vicous darkweed and asps), deathfist orcs, fire beetles, skeletons, zombies, ghouls, lesser mummies, air elements and several species of great cats(including pumas and lions).

In the various huts and inns scattered throughout this zone you will find food and supplies as well as many of the items which are useful for the trade skills in the game. Evil characters should beware as there are not only several guards stationed at inns in this region but travelling guards as well.

If you head northwest and follow the mountain ridge you will come accross an orc village. This village is filled with several orcs including a shaman. This is a good place for groups of characters level 7-10 can come and hunt orcs for their belts and cash. A piece of advice is to always take the shaman out first or else he will keep healing all of the others.
