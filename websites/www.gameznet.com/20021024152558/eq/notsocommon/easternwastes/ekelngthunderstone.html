Son of Bevelious
This quest begins with Vorken in the Iceshard Manor of Kael Drakken. It was submitted by Aventine.

As a youth Vorken did some shady dealings in the Eastern Wastes. These dealings brought down the wrath of Bevelious Thunderstone who attempted to assassinate the Iceshard brothers in their sleep. They were able to fend off the assassins however and Bevelious was slain.

His son however has been making some noises about avenging his fathers death recently. Vorken wants to end these rumblings by killing his son, if only he could find him.

His son spawns in the Eastern Wastes. He was blue to a level 56 but had an obscene amount of hit points so better bring some back up. He will drop a head when killed. Turning this head in to Vorken will result in Vambraces of Avoidance.
