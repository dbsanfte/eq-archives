Everquest Spell Guide: Wizard

Level 1

Frost Bolt

The frost bolt spell is the first in the line of bolt spells. Bolt spells generally do a higher amount of damage than other lines of spells. The weakness however to this and other bolt spells is that they are unguided. You must aim where you wish to fire the bolt and hope that the monster does not move out of the way.

Minor Shielding

This is the first in the line of the wizards shielding spells. This spell adds armor class and hit points. It can be cast on yourself only.

Numbing Cold

This spell is an area effect spell which is based on the caster. That means that this spell will damage all monster that are immediately surrounding you. This can be great if you are in combat with more than one enemy.

Sense Magic

This spell will give you information on magic items.


Shock Of Frost

This is the first in the line of direct damage shock spells. Shock spells generally do not do as much damage as a bolt spell of the same level will do however shock spells are attained frequently and do not need to be aimed.

Sphere Of Light

This spell will create a glowing sphere in the casters hand. This sphere can only be used by the caster and is used commonly as a light source for human eyes.

Level 4

Fade

This spell is a great escape spell for a wizard. It causes you to randomly teleport from spot to spot in a close location. This is a good spell if a monster goes aggro on you to keep him from hitting you while your warriors taunt like mad.

Gate

The gate spell will teleport you home to your bind point.

Glimpse

This spell lets you take a brief glimpse at objects far away. Basically it gives you a 15-20 second period where your sight is zoomed in to the distance.

Ice Strike

This is an area effect rain spell. As with all other rain spells these can be effective if you have a large group of monsters as it can damage them all at a single time. Be sure to cast this on the "tank" and not on the monsters.

O'Kiels Radiation

This allows you to cast a limited fire shield on yourself or another person. For the duration of the spell every time a monster hits the radiating player it will suffer damage. This is important as it is one of the few spells that wizards have that are actually beneficial to others.

Root

This spell will hold your opponent in one place. It serves two different purposes. The first is to keep a monster from running so your party can finish it off. The second is that it prevents the monster from getting near you, this is very important if you plan on soloing at higher levels.

See Invisible

This spell allows you to see invisible beings.

Shock Of Fire

This is the second in the line of shock spells(all of which do direct damage).

Level 8

Column Of Frost

This is a cold based area effect spell which centers around your target.

Eye Of Zomm

This spell creates a moveable eye which you can control and use to scout out dangerous areas.

Fingers Of Fire

Fingers of fire is a fire based area effect spell which centers around the caster.

Firebolt

This spell is the second in the bolt line of spells. It is a high damage pell which is unguided meaning that you can easily miss if you do not line up correctly.

Lesser Shielding

This is the second in the line of shielding spells. It cannot be stacked with other spells in this line(such as minor shielding)

Shadow Step

Shadow step is an escape spell. This spell will teleport you a short distance away to avoid your attackers.

Shock Of Ice

Shock of ice is an upgrade to the shock of frost and shock of fire spells. This spell is a direct damage spell.

Sense Summoned

Casting this spell will point you in the direction of the nearest summoned monster(element).

Level 12

Bind Affinity

This spell is used to bind a player to a certain location. This will be the location where you will appear if you use the gate spell or if you die. You can only bind other players inside of a city and they must be in your group to bind them. You can bind yourself anyplace however.

Cancel Magic

This is a very useful spell which can be used to cancel a magic effect cast on you or one of your party members. This is great if someone in your party is charmed or rooted in a deadly situation.

Fire Storm

Fire storm is a fire based rain spell which can damage several monsters at once.

Frost Spiral Of Al'Kabor

This is an area effect spell which centers around the caster.

Gaze

This spell is an enhanced version of the glimpse spell. It allows you to see into the distance.

Halo Of Light

This spell summons a glowing headband which lights as well as a greater lightstone.

Resistant Skin

This spell will increase your resistances to certain effects.

Shock Of Lightning

Shock of lightning is a direct damage spell.

Level 16

Bind Sight

This spell allows you to see through the eyes of the targetted being.

Flame Shock

This is a fire based direct damage spell.

Heat Sight

I have no information on this spell though it does seem like it might be an infravision spell.

Identify

This spell is designed to give information on an item though I do not see any usefulness for it currently.

Invisibility

This will make a character invisible. Beware however that this spell can end at bad times and certain monsters can see through invisibility.

Lightning Bolt

This is an upgrade to the bolt line of spells. It is a high damage yet unguided attack.

Pillar Of Fire

Pillar of fire is the level 16 rain spell and is of course fire based.

Project Lightning

This spell is a person based area effect spell. At this point in your career however I would not suggest using these spells as they will make monsters very angry at you. Though it can be useful as a last resort if you are ganged up by multiple monsters.

Shielding

This is the third in the line of shielding spells. It not only adds to your armor class and hit points but to your magic resistance as well.

Shield Skin

This spell can only be cast on yourself. It allows you to absorb a certain amount of damage from attacks without actually being injured. It is similar to the enchanters Rune line of spells except that it can only be cast on yourself.

Level 20

Elemental Shield

This spell adds to your resistance to both cold and fire.

Enstill

Enstill is an upgrade to the root spell. It has a longer duration.

Fire Spiral Of Al'Kabor

This is a fire based area effect spell.

Force Shock

Force shock is the first of your massive damage spells. It is an upgrade to the shock line of spells and is a high damage high mana spell.

Gate to Tox, North and Fay

These spells allow you to gate to the location specified by the particular spell. For example the gate to tox will teleport you to the Tox Forest.

Sight

I have no information on this spell but I believe it is an infravision spell.

Tishan's Clash

This is a stun spell. It can be useful against casters to prevent them from getting off their spells though I personally would rather just nuke them to kingdom come.


Level 24

Cast Force

This is a magic based offensive spell.

Gates To Cazic, Nek, Ro, Commonlands, and West Karana

These are personal travel spells which will teleport you to one of the above locations

Frost Shock

This is a very mana efficient offensive spell which is cold based.

Leatherskin

Leatherskin will absorb a certain amount of damage which would otherwise hurt the caster.

Levitate

This spell allows you to levitate. It is useful for travelling over water or lava or to keep from being thrown off ledges.

Lightning Storm

This is a lightning based rain spell.

Major Shielding

Major shielding is an upgrade to the wizards shielding line of spells which begins with lesser shielding.

Level 29

Energy Storm

This is an area effect rain spell which features a good damage:mana ratio and also drains stamina.

Evacuate: North

This is the first of the wizards evacuation series of spells. These spells allow you to teleport out quickly if your party runs into trouble deep inside of a dungeon. It will teleport your entire group. The evacuate line of spells cast faster than the portal spells however there is a small chance that a party member will get left behind.

Inferno Shock

Direct damage offensive spell.

Magnify

I believe that this is an upgrade to the glimpse line of spells but I am not positive. Anyone with further information please mail me.

Portals To Fay, North and Tox

This is the non emergency version of the group teleport spells which will teleport you to one of the locations listed above. This version casts much slower than the evacuation series but have a less chance of someone getting left behind.

Shock Spiral Of Al'Kabor

This is a person based area effect spell.

Thunder Strike

Thunder strike is an offensive direct damage spell.

Level 34

Alter Plane: Hate

This spell will teleport your party into the plane of Hate.

Circle Of Force

This is a person based area effect spell.

Evacuate: Faydark

This is the faydark version of the evacuation spells.

Greater Shielding

This is the level 34 upgrade to the wizards shielding line of spells.

Ice Shock

Ice shock is a cold based direct damage spell.

Lava Storm

Lava storm is a fire based rain spell.

Nullify Magic

This is an upgrade to the cancel magic spell.

Portals To Cazic, Nektulos and Commons

These are the group travel based spells for the above locations.

Steelskin

Steelskin is an upgrade to the shieldskin->leatherskin line of spells. It absorbs damage that would otherwise hurt the caster.

Thunderclap

Thunderclap is a direct damage spell.

Level 39

Evacuate: Ro

This evacuation spell will teleport your party into the desert of Ro.

Force Spiral Of Al'Kabor

This spell is a magic based area effect spell which centers on the caster.

Immobolize

Immobolize will root your opponent in place.

Lightning Strike

Lightning strike is a direct damage offensive spell.

Portals To Commons, Ro, and West

More group travel spells.

Shifting Sight

This spell is an upgrade to the wizards sight line of spells. I have been unable to get any information on what it does.

Level 44

Alter Plane: Sky

This spell will open up a portal to the elemental plane of air.

Arch Shielding

Arch shielding is the last in the line of wizard shielding spells.

Conflaguration

Conflaguration is a high powered direct damage spell.

Diamondskin

Diamondskin is the last in the line of shieldskin->leatherskin->steelskin spells. It will absorb damage.

Elemental Armor

Elemental armor will increase your resistances to fire and cold based attacks.

Evacuate: Nek

This is the nektulos forest version of the wizards evacuation spells.

Force Strike

Force strike is a magic based direct damage spell.

Frost Storm

Frost storm is a cold based rain spell.

Level 49

Evacuate: West

This is the last in the line of wizard evacuation spells.

Ice Comet

Ice comet is a cold based direct damage spell which delivers an obscene amount of damage. This is the most powerful spell currently in the game delivering over 1100 points of damage.

Markar's Clash

Markar's clash is a stun spell which will stun your opponent for a good amount of time.

Supernova

Supernova is an area effect spell that is fire based.

Wrath Of Al'Kabor

This is an area effect spell which centers around the caster.
