Everquest Equipment Guide: Shiny Brass Shield

The shiny brass shield can be found in Crushbone. You can find information on attaining this item by reading the Screaming Mace Guide. The shiny brass shield is an excellent item for characters from level 10 all the way up to level 30+. The shield gives +10 AC which is a sizeable amount of armor in comparison to most store bought armor/shields. It also casts light similar to a lightstone which is great for human eyes. The shield is a nifty addition when fighting spellcasters as it dons a +10 bonus to your Magic Resistance.

If you are a Paladin or a Shadowknight this can be a great addition as it can be used to bash against monsters that require magical weapons to hit. Bash can also be used by warriors but they will most likely wish to use double attack(with a 2 handed weapon) or duel wield instead.

WAR,PAL,RNG,SHD,CLR,DRU,SHM,BRD,MAGIC.
