Everquest Zone Guide: Black Burrow

Black Burrow is perhaps the best known dungeon in the game for level 5-15 characters. Black Burrow is the home of the gnolls which are a part of several quests in the Qeynos area. Lower level characters usually hang out near the entrance area picking off scrawny gnolls and the like which are in the level 5-6 range. Level 12-16 players usually go deeper in and try to wage war with one of the three gnoll commanders in the zone. The gnoll commanders are level 14 and carry the much desired magical items available in this zone(onyx earrings, spiked shoulderpads and spiked collars).

Although this zone consists primarily of gnolls there are also some giant snakes in a few locations.

Black Burrow borders with both Qeynos Hills and Everfrost. To get to one another stay at the top of the zone do not go downstairs. This zone is the only way to get from Halas to the rest of the world.
