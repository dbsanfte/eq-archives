Trivia Contest

The EQ'Lizer Trivia Contest!

Each day a trivia question for the contest will be posted on EQ'Lizer. At the end of 30 days, we will post a special e-mail address where you should send your answers. The answers must be in the body of the e-mail or an attached .txt file. Other formats such as .doc files will NOT be accepted.

The answers must be submitted in the following format :

1) Answer for question #1
2) Answer for question #2
3) Answer for question #3
etc.

All entries must be received between October 1 and October 7, 2002 to qualify for the prizes.On October 8, 2002 all the questions and their answers will be posted on the EQ'Lizer web site.

The winners of the competition will receive the following prize amounts:

First Place : $ 50.00
Second Place : $ 25.00
Third Place : $ 10.00

You MUST include your name and address at the bottom of your submitted answers so we know where to send the prize money. If you do not include your name and address your entry will be disqualified, and if you had won, an alternate winner will be selected. By entering this contest, you are giving EQ'Lizer permission to post your name (not address) on the site along with the prize amount you won. If you choose to include your character name and the server you play on, we will post that on the site as well.

EQ'Lizer and its staff are not eligable for this competition. In the event of any disputes, by entering the competition you agree that the decisions of the EQ'Lizer staff are final. Due to laws in the United
States, you must be over 13 years old to participate in this competition.

Now that all the technical stuff has been said - don't forget to stop back starting on September 1, 2002 to have fun playing EQ Trivia!
