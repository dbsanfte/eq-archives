Everquest Zone Guide: Oasis Of Marr

The Oasis of Marr is a popular zone for characters level 12-40. This zone lies between North Ro and South Ro.

There are several intresting things to hunt here depending on your level range. Characters level 12-16 will probably have fun hunting the various zombies, skeletons and mummies in the region. However the most popular target for characters in this level range are the crocodiles. Beware of Lockjaw however he is a huge crocodile who levels in the mid to high twenties and he will end your career in a hurry.

Evil characters in the level 10-12 range might want to take a turn at killing the fisherman who frequent the shore region. Fisherman generally carry a rusty spear and a small bit of loot.

There is also a huge orc camp containing tons of orcs level 14-20. You will also be able to catch many of these orcs roaming the desert landscape. Beneath the waves of the lake you will also be able to find aquagoblins.

Higher level characters will also not be at a loss for targets here. There is an island in the middle of the lake that contains a large number of spectres. This will especially make clerics happy. It is advised that you do not fight the spectres on the island however as there is a roaming guard and you will not want to fight more than one at a time. I recommend you fetch them to the shore and let your best swimmer do the fetching.

If spectres aren't your gig you will be able to find a good population of sand giants in the region as well.
