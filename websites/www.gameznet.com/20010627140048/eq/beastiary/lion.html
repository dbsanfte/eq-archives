Lion
This bestiary guide was written by Cyth.

These great cats can be found in many zones, though they seem to be most common in the plains zones like the Commonlands and Karanas. They range in strength from the young lionesses (5-8th) up to the Lion Patriarch and Matriarch (14-16th).

These animals are generally hunted for their skins. These skins are used to make patchwork and better armor by tailors. The highest quality of lion skin can be used to make the excellent Lionskin gloves. They will also have a few varieties of meat.

These are aggressive beasts, finding food by hunting.  Being a social creature, they will come to the aid other lions so a small battle can quickly become a dire situation for the young adventurer.

They con perfectly for their difficulty, so most classes will have an easy time taking them on.
