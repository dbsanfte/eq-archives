Everquest Beastiary Guide: Large/Giant Spider

Large spiders can be found in a few different zones in the game including Butcherblock and the East Commonlands. They vary between level 2-4. Large spiders can carry up to four pieces of spider silk which sells for over a gold piece at some shops. They also will occasionally carry a venom sack which sells for 5gp. This makes them a favorite among loot hunters.

Hunting large spiders is not without it's danger however as they can inject a mild(S1) poison.

Giant spiders are much larger than large spiders and can range up to and into the 30s. These spiders generally have much more powerful poisons such as the S3 or S4 poison.
