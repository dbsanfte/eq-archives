Scroll of Tashania

Faction:
+Solusek Ro
Related Zones:
Neriak
Kaladin
Ak'anon
Grobb
Ogguk
Qeynos
Halas
Felwithe
Erudin
Freeport

Romar Sunto in the Temple of Solusek Ro in Lavastorm offers to give you the Tashania scroll if you can find ten coins in different cities over Norrath. You can purchase a book on where to find all of the coins for fifty gold and he will also give you a pouch to combine them in. 

The coins are called an old silver coin, and look like a small bag on the ground. 

You can find them in the following cities:

Neriak: Dive off the bridge in Neriak Commons and it is just outside the window of the Blind Fish. Next to the Wizard Rod.
Kaladim: In a very small pool by the Warrior's Guild.
Ak'anon: At the very rear of Akanon near the water wheel (on the ground).
Grobb: In the small pool near the bar when you zone in.
Oggok: Outside the warrior guild in the moat.
Qeynos: In the large pool near the Temple of Life where the Koliandl fish resides. 
Halas: In the lake in front of city. Hug the left wall when you zone in and dive in the lake, its the second ledge.
Felwithe: In the small pond near the fisherman at the back of North Felwithe.
Erudin: In the fountain outside the enbtrances to the three caster guilds in Erudin Palace.
Freeport: In the water of under the Arcane Sciences building.

Once you have the 10 coins, combine them in the coin pouch you received when you spoke to Romar in the Temple to get a Coin Of Tash. You know have to get the coin enchanted. Make sure that the NPC's con higher than indifferent. You can use illusions and faction spells for this. If they are indifferent or lower they will keep the coin and you will have to start again.

First, take the coin to Tarn Visilin in the Highkeep Library. After you hand him the coin, he will enchant it into a Glowing Coin of Tash and return it to you.

Next visit Mizr N'Mar in Neriak Library and repeat the process. He enchants it into a Gleaming Coin of Tash for you.

For the final enchantment take the coin to Raine Beteira in Erudin Library and give her the coin. 

Take the coin that you receive from Raine back to Romar in the Temple and receive the Tashania Scroll, which is NO DROP.

Note: The coins will not dissappear if you log off, neither will the pouch. You can do this quest over a period of time without a problem.

Note: The quest-giver in the Temple of Sol Ro does not tell you about getting the Coin of Tash enchanted before you give it back to him for the scroll. If you give him the Coin of Tash before it is enchanted, he will take it from you and not give you a scroll. Of course, there is no way to know this unless you read about the quest here first. Just a warning.

Reward: Scroll of Tashania
