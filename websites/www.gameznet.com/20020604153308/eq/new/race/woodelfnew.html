Wood Elf



Wood elves are the defenders of the forest. They can play as bards, druids, rangers, rogues and warriors. They make fine druids and bards and they are perhaps the best suited race in the game to play a ranger. Wood elves are not very well suited to play warriors and though they do make fine thieves they can not match up with halflings.

All wood elves have the natural ability to hide and forage.
