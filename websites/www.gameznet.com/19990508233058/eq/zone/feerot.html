Everquest Zone Guide: Feerot

Feerot connects the ogre town of Ogguk to the rest of the world. This zone also connects to Innothule Swamp and Rathe Mountains, and Cazic Thule. There is one vendor who sells here and you will want to visit her as often as possible(to save trips to Ogguk). If you are leaving town take a left at the fork and keep heading left. Before the path ends there will be one last stone house and inside of it you will find the vendor.

This zone is split into a few areas. The newbie part of the zone lies to the left of the original fork. Over here you will find rats, bats, decaying skeletons, froglock tads, minor scarabs, spiderlings and lizard man broodlings. This part of the zone is great for characters level 1-3.

If you head straight at the first fork you will find a bridge that leads to a trail to Rathe Mountains. Over this bridge is the level 4-8 area. On this side of the bridge you will find lizard man scouts, jungle spiders, skeletons and giant bats. There are several camps in this area that feature skeletons as well as zombies. There are also many lizard men camps.

The high level area of Feerot is somewhat hidden away from the rest of the zone. If you were entering from Rathe mountains you would take a right and follow the zone ridge. After the skeleton camp and before the water you will probably notice a Spectre. Near this spectre you will see a tree which is next to the zone border. This is a hidden entrance to what is known as the spectre cave. Inside of this cave you will find many spectres and a high level(40+) enchanter. She is guarding the entrance to a gate to another plane.
