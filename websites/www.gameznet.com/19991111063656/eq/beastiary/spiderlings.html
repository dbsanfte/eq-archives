Everquest Beastiary Guide: Spiderlings

Spiderlings are another quite common animal in most newbie zones of Norrath. There are several different variants(including Wooly and Jungle Spiderlings) however the differences are cosmetic only. All spiderlings range between level 1-3 in difficulty. They are both a rewarding and a dangerous monster to face at these early levels.

They are a risky fight for level 1-3 characters for a couple of reasons. First off they bite rather hard for a mob of their level. This can turn the tide of battle against you(especially if you are a casting class) rather quickly. This is compounded by the fact that they are a social animal. That means that if you are fighting one spiderling and another happens to walk in the immediate area then you will now be fighting two spiderlings. This can lead to a quick death. I advise that if you decide to target these beasts that you fight your battles from an external view rather than first person. This will allow you to see other spiderlings that may be creeping a bit too closely and take an evasive action.

However if you are willing to take the chance they can be a rewarding foe indeed for a level 1-3 character. Each spiderling can carry up to 4 pieces of spiderling silk which sells for over a silver piece each. That means you can get about 5 sp off one spiderling which isn't a bad chunk of change at that level.
