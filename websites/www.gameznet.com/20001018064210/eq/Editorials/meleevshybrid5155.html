Melee vs. Hybrids: Post 51-55
Level 51

Monk

Stone Stance

This discipline will absorb about 75% of the damage that you would normally take during combat. It only lasts for 12 seconds and has a reuse delay of 12 minutes initially. This reuse will fall to a mere 3 minutes at level 60.

Warrior

Warriors reeive no disciplines at level 51.

Rogue

Rogues receive no disciplines at level 51.

Shadowknight

Sipon Life

Siphon life is a 75 hit point lifetap that takes 73 points of mana. I know what you might be thinking. Don't they have a 280 hit point lifetap already? Yes they do but this one is different.

First off it is more mana efficient than life leech. Life leech requires 1.125 points of mana for every hit point healed where siphon life requires only 0.973. Not a huge difference but it is a difference nonetheless. 

Life leech also has a two minute recast delay where you can chain cast siphon life until you are out of mana.  

On the flip side siphon life has a slightly longer casting time.

So what does this all mean? Basically life leech is still your life saver during combat. Siphon life should be used for reducing downtime or for stepping back out of combat and self healing in an emergency if life leech is already gone. 

Paladin

Pacify

Pacify is an upgrade to the paladin's calm line of spells. This spell will reduce a monsters aggression and hopefully(if succesful) allow the paladin to pull a single mob in an area where several would normally come. 

Ranger

Ensnare

Ensnare is an upgrade to the snare spell. It is less easily resisted and has a longer duration. It requires 35 mana however which drives most rangers to keep snare memorized instead.

Level 52

Monk

Thunder Kick

This discipline will cause your next succesful flying kick to do more than the maximum damage. I'm not sure how much more it will do number wise. It seems to add somewhere near about 25-50% damage for one attack.

The base reuse delay is 7 minutes.

Warrior

Evasive

This discipline allows a warrior to take less damage in melee combat. The disadvantage is that they will also do less damage themselves. The duration is a lengthy 3 minutes compared to a 15 minute base reuse delay.

In my opinion this is an extremely useful skill. Warriors are tanks first and are the best tanks in the game. This skill allows them to be more effective at what they do best.

Rogue

Rogues receive no disciplines at level 52.

Shadowknight

Malignant Dead

Malignant dead is a new pet spell. It is a big step up from your level 49 spell. This pet is the level 39 necromancer pet spell. It can dual wield as your last pet could but has quite a few more hit points and can hit for up to 39 points of damage each hit. 

In the eyes of some this is the first shadowknight pet that can actually be considered a good pet. It dishes a pretty decent amount of damage and can take a few hits without dying. 

Rest the Dead

Rest the dead is an upgrade to the numb the dead spell. This is a calm spell which works against undead beings. 

This can be used in dungeons such as Sebilis(undead side), City of Mist or Karnor's Castle to isolate single undead mobs out of groups without having to resort to a feign death. 

Paladin

Force

Force is an upgrade to the Stun->Holy Might line of spells. It will stun your opponent which can temporarily halt attacks or fleeing mobs and interupt spellcasting. It also does up to 90 points of direct damage.

Frenzied Strength

Frenzied strength is an upgrade to the reckless strength line of spells. This spell will temporarily add 40 points of strength. Over time the strength increase will gradually decrease until it actually goes negative before returning to normal.

Ranger

Extinguish Fatigue

This spell will restore your targets stamina. It can come in handy for dual wielders during long battles. Most rangers will never use this spell however except for underwater excursions.

Firestrike

Firestrike is a fire based direct damage spell. It does up to 302 points of direct damage to your target for only 155 mana. 

Level 53

Monk

Whirlwind

At level 53 a monk will gain the discipline of whirlwind. This discipline will cause every attack against you to be riposted for 9 seconds. This wil allow you to do a bit of extra damage in combat. The problem is that the short duration ensures that it will not do that much extra damage.

It is seldom used however because of the 60 minute reuse delay.

Warrior

Charge

The charge discipline will cause all attacks to land for 14 seconds. This does not affect the damage output of these attacks but it does mean that you will land much more often than normal.

The base reuse time is 30 minutes.

Rogue

Level 53 grants rogues their first discipline of counterattack. This discipline will allow you to riposte all attacks against you for nine seconds. This can be very useful in a pinch.

The downside to this discipline is a 60 minute base reuse delay.

Shadowknight

Boil Blood

This is an upgrade to the heat blood spell. It will not stack with heat blood but it will stack with the darkness and heart flutter lines. 

Boil blood will do over 500 points of damage over a very long duration.

Paladin

Armor of Faith

Armor of faith is an upgrade to the armor->guard line of spells. It adds approximately 25 points of armor class to the targetted player. 

Ranger

Storm Strength

This is the last in the line of ranger strength buffing spells. It increases your targets strength by 35 points. Because strength adds to a players ATK rating this will cause them to hit for a bit more damage during combat. 

It is a very well liked buff.

Level 54

Monk

Voidance

This spell will allow you to avoid attacks against you for 8 seconds. It only works versus melee attacks. A common usefor this discipline is to buy time if a feign death fails and death seems certain.

You must pick and choose your useage of it very wisely however as it has a 60 minute reuse delay which will reduce by one minute per level to a minimum of 54 minutes.

Warrior

Mighty Strike

Mighty strike causes all hits during a 10 second period to be critical or crippling blows(depending on your level of health). This can be a great damage disher.

The downside is a 60 minute base reuse delay.

Rogue

Deadeye

During the 14 second duration of this discipline a rogue will land all attacks attempted. This is a great discipline if a fight is getting close and you need to dish some damage in a hurry.

It has a 30 minute reuse delay.

Shadowknight

Banshee Aura

Banshee aura is a self only damage shield. For the duration of the spell(about 10 minutes) any being that hits you for melee damage will suffer 12 points of damage from the shield. 

12 points of damage may not soud like a lot when you are getting hit for 100 points from the mob but believe me it adds up. This is one of the most mana efficient ways of dishing damage that you have and the damage will add up on the mob quickly.

Panic the Dead

Panic the dead is an upgrade to the spook the dead spell. It is a very mana efficient fear spell with a very long duration. 

Using this spell in conjunction with dooming darkness you will be able to solo extremely effectively in zones such as Burning Wood or Trakanon's Teeth if you stick to undead.

Paladin

Enstill

Enstill is an upgrade to the root spell. It can hold your target place for a longer amount of time.

Expel Undead

Expel undead is the last in the line of paladin anti undead spells. This spell does up to 273 points of direct damage to undead beings. 

Ranger

Drones of Doom

Drones of doom is a damage over time(DOT) spell. It will do 34 points of damage per tick for 10 ticks(1 minute) for a total of 340 points of damage for only 141 mana. 

It is an upgrade to the stinging swarm spell and will not stack with it. As a result it uses the conjuration spell skill which many rangers may not have kept up to par. So expect to fizzle quite a bit at first.

Skin Like Diamond

This is an upgrade to the skin like wood->rock line of spells. It adds approximately 15 points of armor class and 200 hit points to your selected target. 

It will not stack with higher level versios of the  cleric's courage or druid's skin like wood series.

Level 55

Monk

Monks gain no disciplines at level 55.

Warrior

Defensive

This discipline is somewhat of an upgrade to the evasive discipline from levle 52. In essence it does the same thing(decrease damage taken while also decreasing offensive output) bit appears to be more effective.

The base reuse is 15 minutes.

Rogue

Nimble

The nimble skill will allow you to dodge all melee attacks against you for 12 seconds. This is a great defensive skill and will allow you to tank high level mobs effectively for a short period of time.

The reuse delay on this skill is 30 minutes.

Shadowknight

Bobbing Corpse This is a slightly scaled down self only version of the necromancer's spell Dead Man Floating.  It will cause the caster to levitate, water breath and also will add to his poison resistance.

This spell is very useful for fighting mobs which might poison you such as Wyverns. It is also useful for taking shortcuts through zones(you can fly over an area quicker than you can run and you take no damage from falling). Lastly it makes it safer to explore a dungeon without worrying about falling into a pit trap.

Expel Undead

Expel undead is the last in your line of anti undead spells. This spell does up to 273 points of direct damage to undead beings. 

Paladin

Divine Aura

Divine aura grants the paladin an 18 second period of invulnerability. During this time you can not be damaged by melee or magical attacks. You may also not attack or cast spells during this time.  

This can be an invaluable way of buying time for your party to get off that gate spell or to flee. It can also be used to fetch a large number of mobs which might normally kill you until an enchanter can mesmerize them.

This spell has a long recast delay so you should only use it for emergency or sticky situations.

Divine Favor

This spell will grant a player a harmshield similar to the shadowknight or mage types shieldskin line. This will grant them protection against approximately 150 points of melee damage. The difference is that this spell can be cast on others similar to the enchanters rune line of spells. 

Ranger

Chloroplast

Chloroplast will cause your target to regenerate at 10 hit points per tick faster than normal speed for 15 minutes of time. This spell is very useful for saving your priests mana because they will not have to worry about healing party members during downtime. 

It is also useful to cast on your fetcher. This way while they are searching for mobs they will be healing. This saves them the trouble of having to wait to be healed before fetching in many cases.

Jolt

When succesful this spell will lower a monsters aggression towards the ranger. It works by placing you lower on to a mobs aggro list similar to the rogue evade skill. 

You can use this spell as an anti-taunt if you are getting beat up on a bit too much(rather than the parties primary tanks) and draining all of your cleric's mana.
