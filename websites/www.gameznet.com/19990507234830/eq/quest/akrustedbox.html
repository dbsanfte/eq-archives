Everquest Quest Guide: Rusted Blackbox

This quest takes place in Akanon. One of the Warrior's guild trainers is looking for rusted blackboxes. These can be found on Rogue Clockworks in Steamfont. You will receive money, xp, and faction adjustment. This quest is not limited to just gnome warriors, many race/class combos can complete this quest.
