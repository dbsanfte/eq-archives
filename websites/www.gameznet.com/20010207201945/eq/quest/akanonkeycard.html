Ak'Anon Keycard Quest

This quest originates in Ak'Anon. First, go talk to Manik Compolten in the Warriors' Guild. Get a keycard from him by telling him, "I am a new warrior." Give a keycard to each of four C-class clockworks scattered throughout Ak'Anon; their names should look like this: Model XIIC, Model XIC, etc. When you give each of them a keycard they will give you a black box in return as well as a good-aligned faction change increasing Gemchoppers, Merchants, and lowering with the Necro guild, Dark reflection and the Neriak necro guild, the Dead. After collecting all four black boxes return them as a set to Manik Compolten, he will give you a new blackbox to deliver to Jogl Droogbaugh out by the Steamfont Windmills(he will either be in the 3rd windmill with his pet, Charlotte, roaming, or by himself next to Watchman Mylz stuck on the little hill next to Byrola Bendil and the first windmill). Jogl will in turn give you a note to return to the Drekon Vebnebber, the Warrior guild merchant in the room next to Manik Compolten. This is where the Quest splits. He either tells you that the rumors of Red V being still alive are true and tells you to seek him out in the Mines of Malfunction. So what you do now if you get this split is go to the mines and kill Red V, he spawns by the 3 caster guildmasters in the Mines of Malfuntion in the big square room with no NPC's, an obsolete model will spawn against the middle of the wall and just stand there, that is Red V's spawn area, and he does have Placeholders. Kill Red V and get his Incomplete Blackbox(Container w/ 4 slots, appearance of Small Box). Fill this with 4 Blackbox fragments that you get by killing Obsolete Models throughout the Mines of malfunction, then when you have 4 in the box, hit combine, it will now turn into a Complete Blacbox and its appearance will change to look like a regular rusted blackbox. Now, bring this Complete Blackbox back to manik Compolten and give it to him for XP, Good-aligned faction change, some cash, and a random item, either Small Patchwork/Tattered armor, an Iony's Absorber, or a Bull Smasher.

The other split will be when Drekon Vebnebber tells you that there has been an ill-fated mistake and now two obsolete dusters are roaming the city and they must be destroyed and the evidence must be given to Sanfyrd featherhead who operates the Ak'Anon Scrap Yard for proper disposal of their remains. So now this will trigger the spawns for the two disfunctional Clockworks in Ak'Anon that you must kill, Duster X and Duster V, two rat-looking clockworks. Duster X spawns in the huge area where the Abbey of Deep Musing(Gnome Cleric and Rogue Guild) is, but down the ramp more right near "a mechanic" and the new NPC that sells Tinkering items near the water. Duster V then spawns near the palace where King Ak'Anon resides and then patrols his way past the PoD(Priest of Discord), it is best to wait for him near the PoD. Each Duster will then drop a "Scrap Metal" when killed, but it differs from regualr scrap metal because it is Lore/No Drop. By the way, the Dusters are approximately lvl's 4-6 as each time they spawn, they are a different lvl. Now go to where Sanfyrd feather head is(across the little river of water directly across from the door that leads into the Mines of Malfunction/Necro Guild. Give him both Scraps and you will receive a good-aligned Faction change, XP, some cash, and a brand new Bootstrutter's Framed Pack(10 slot Backpack w/ 10% weight reduction)!
