Everquest Quest Guide: Granin O'Gill

Thanks to Gambit for submitting this quest:

Go to Marilyn McCann in Halas. She is the one you give your guild note too for the shaman class. Once you talk to her and go through conversation. If you tell her you are a young shaman, she will tell you to go kill Granin O'Gill. He walks around in the tundra part of everfrost (near the orc shamans). He has low hps but can punch for 20 so becareful if you are low level. Take his head back to Marilyn and she gives you exp/little cash/faction.
