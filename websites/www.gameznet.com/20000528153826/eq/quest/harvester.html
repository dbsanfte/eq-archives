Harvester
This quest was submitted by Allian A'Vinn:

This quest is given by Vurgo. When you hail him and ask him about the Havester, he will tell you the first thing he needs is a Scythe from the Shadowed Men. Goto your nearest Shadowed Men camp, keep fighting until you found a Shadow Scythe, which should not be too hard to find. Return to Vurgo with the scythe, he will give you a note and tell you to go find another 3 items. A Fungus Eye from Unrest, a Shadowed Knife from Goblin Headmaster in OOT, and a Fire Opal.

The Fungus eye can be obtain by killing the Fungus man in Unrest, it is not very rare and you should be able to find it within 4 - 5 kills. The Goblin headmaster is located at the Oracle island in OOT. He is in one of the goblin camp behind the Oracle. Kill him, and get his Shadowed Knife. Lastly, return to Temple of Solusek Ro, and seek out a person named Ginni, give her 550 gold coins (Remember, must be Gold coins, 55 platium would not work), she will give you a fire opal. Return the Fungus eye, Shadowed Knife, Fire opal, plus the note he gave you before to Vurgo, and ding, here's your Havester Scythe.

The harvester is necromancer only, AC4 +5 STR/INT, -5 CHA. It looks like a weapon but can not be used for melee.
