Regent Symbol of Innoruuk
This quest was submitted by Belinda: 

After completing the Disciple of innoruuk quest, you can choose to do this one, it was just implemented not too long ago. 

The information here is presented in two versions. First the long version for those who are interested in the full story. Second is the short versio for those who would simply like to get it over with. =)

The Long Version:

You say, 'hail Ithvol K`Jasn' 

Ithvol K`Jasn says 'Hail Inyx, I sense the gift of hatred in your aura. What status do you hold within these Spires?

You say, 'I am a disciple' 

Ithvol K`Jasn says 'I have an important task fora Disciple of Hate such as yourself. It is no secret that King Naythox Thex wishes to expand the Teir'Dal Empire to the continent of Feydwer. We have a camp established in the Lesser Faydark near the stronghold of the vampire lord Mayong Mistmoore. The camp is constantly under siege by the Feir'Dal and Koada'Dal and must rely on the support of Neriak for supplies necessary to the survival of the camp. 

Are you willing to serve your King and Empire by delivering the provisions. 

You say, 'I will deliver the provisions' 

Ithvol K`Jasn says 'The provisions are crated and prepared for shipment by Dran Slug Rembor. Go to his establishment in the Foreign Quarter and tell him Ithvol sent you. Make haste young Disciple of Hate, the fate of the Empire hangs on our support of the Faydark Camp. Travel to West Foreign Quarter and talk to Dran ‘slug’ Rembor. 

You say, 'Ithvol sent me' 

Dran `slug` Rembor says 'So youre the lucky errand boy eh?! Let's hope you are more successful than the last chump that was sent. From what I hear there was something pretty important in that last crate that was sent out. It just looked like a bunch of junk to me. 

Anyhow we ran out of Steel Boning. Bring me two bonings so we can bind up the crate and be quick about it, both of our necks are on the line! These can be made with the blacksmithing skill. 

After you give him the two bonings, Dran `slug` Rembor says 'Gee thanks I cant do a whole lot without the rest! Get your act strait or we're both dead! 

Dran `slug` Rembor says

'Heres the crate of provisions. Take this to Dragoon Szorn in the Lesser Faydark immediately! 

Travel to Lesser Faydark to what used to be an abandoned camp (loc -900, 1155) and give the crate to Dragoon Szorn. 

Dragoon Szorn says 'Finally!! We have been in dire need of these supplies for quite some time Inyx! Here is the receipt for the shipment. The last shipment never made it here and there is an item of great importance stored within the lost crate. Before you return to Neriak your services are needed to retrieve our [missing shipment]. I command your obedience as is my right as a Dragoon of Naythox Thex's personal regime.

You should receive a "receipt for provisions", but the submitter was only able to get one by petitioning a gm. You say, 'What missing shipment?'

Dragoon Szorn says 'Our sources in these lands tell us that the previous courier was waylaid by a member of the Fayguard and the crate of valuables taken back to Felwithe. Our source has been working on a plan to recover the crate but we have not received word on her progress in quite some time.

Seek Ennixy and tell her [hate be my guide]. She typically works from her homeland in the steamfont mountains. Travel to Steamfont and talk to Ennixy a Gnome (1350, -850). 

You say, 'Hate be my guide' 

Ennixy Frennor says 'So you work for Szorn eh? Its about time he sent someone to check up on me! The Koada'Dal in possession of the crate is on his way here right now to cut a deal with me. I have been trying to send word to Szorn for several days to send support as I do not plan on allowing the elf to leave these mountains alive. You have arrived just in time. Kill the elf and the crate is yours! 

Somewhere near Ennixy you will find a high elf paladin with Lay Hands, kill him. Crusader swiftmoon drops Swiftmoon’s head and the missing crate.

Take the crate to Dragoon Szorn in Lesser Faydark. Dragoon Szorn says 'What is this!! The items of importance have been removed from the crate!! This is most unfortunate. However, you have done well in your service to the Teir'Dal Empire.

Take this voucher of services rendered under the command of Naythox Thex back to Ithvol along with the head of that slain Koada'Dal, the receipt for the provisions, and your Disciple Symbol of Innoruuk. Szorn gives you a voucher of service to Naythox Thex. Travel back to Neriak 3rd Gate and give the 4 items to Ithvol. 

Ithvol K`Jasn says 'Your devotion to the ideals of hatred and your service to our King Naythox Thex has proven your usefulness within these spires and within our great city.

I award you the symbol of regents Congratulations Regent Inyx! Your faction standing with PriestsofInnoruk , KingAythoxthex get better, and faction with PriestsofMarr, ClericsofTunare and PriestsofLife get worse.

The Short Version:

This is given after you complete the disciple quest.

Hail Ithvol K'Jasn and tell him 'I will deliver the provisons.' He will tell you to go the Neriak Foreign Quarter and tell Dran Slug Rembor that 'Ithvol sent me'

Travel to West Foreign Quarter and tell Dran ‘slug’ Rembor, 'Ithvol sent me.' 

Dran will ask you for 2 steel bonings. These can be made with the blacksmithing skill. After you give the bonings, he will hand you a crate and says 'Heres the crate of provisions. Take this to Dragoon Szorn in the Lesser Faydark immediately!

Travel to Lesser Faydark to the camp at (loc -900, 1155) and give the crate to Dragoon Szorn. He will hand you a receipt and ask you to find the [missing shipment]. 

If you ask about it he will tell you to 'Seek out Ennixy and tell her [hate be my guide]. She typically works from her homeland in the steamfont mountains.' Travel to Steamfont and talk to Ennixy Frennor (a gnome at 1350, -850). 

You say, 'Hate be my guide.' Ennixy will ask you to slay a paladin named Crusader Swiftmoon. He will spawn right near her. After you slay him he will drop the missing crate and his head.

Travel back to Lesser Fay and take the crate back to Dragoon Szorn and he will give you a Voucher of Services Rendered.

Travel back to Neriak 3rd Gate and give to Ithvol the Voucher, the head of Crusader Swiftmoon, the receipt for the provisions, and your Disciple Symbol of Innoruuk. Ithvol will then give you your Regent Symbol of Innoruuk.

Brief Info:

Starts in: Neriak 3rd Gate
Who: Ithvol K`Jasn (Cleric Guild)
Estimated Time: 1 day
Minimum Level: 30
Factions Raised: King Aythox Thex & Priests of Innoruk
Factions Lowered: Clerics of Tunare, Priests of Life, Priests of Marr
