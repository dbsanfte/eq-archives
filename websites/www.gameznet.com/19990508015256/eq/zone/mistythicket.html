Everquest Zone Guide: Misty Thicket

Misty Thicket is the newbie zone of the halflings. It is a great zone for players levels 1-10 with a few monsters for characters level 11-15. This zone borders with Runnyeye and Rivervale.

This zone is split into two areas.

The newbie area consist of the majority of the zone. This is everything that is outside of the great wall. Here you will find goblin whelps, bats, rats, fire beetles, snakes, black wolves, bixies and spiderlings. Beware the queen fire beetle if you are hunting the lower level ones.

Beyond the wall is targetted for players level 5-10. Behind the wall you will find many roaming young kodiaks, giant wasps, tree snakes, large and giant bats, giant rats, and shadow wolves. Roaming monsters aside there are several goblin camps and one deathfist orc camp.

If you turn left upon passing the wall and hug the zone you will come accross a goblin camp with a spellcasting(level 8-10) goblin by the name of Mooto. Mooto occasionally has clay totems which can be held by mage classes to gain a +5 mana bonus.

Also behind the wall you will find the entrance to Runnyeye.
