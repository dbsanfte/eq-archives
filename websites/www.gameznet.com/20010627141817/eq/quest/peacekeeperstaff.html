Peacekeeper Staff
The peacekeeper staff is a staff useable only by erudite clerics and paladins. The staff does 5 damage and has a delay of 28. It also grants +10 charisma when held.

This quest begins at the clerics guild in erudin. If you accept the quest you will be sent to the Tox Forest to search for an NPC. The NPC is in the first hut to the right after you leave the city gates. He will give you a staff and directions on how to continue the quest.

He will require you to give him treant chips to continue this quest. There are two ways that you can go about this. The first is to go to the karana's, find and kill a treant. Unfortunately treants are in their low 20s and are a bit beyond the level range for those to whom this staff would be useful. Luckily you can also get treant chips by giving Rogar the Tall (upstairs from the cobbler in Qeynos) an Odus pearl(this is the easiest way to do the quest). When you do so he will grind them into treant resin and hand them back to you.

You know must get the other required item to combine in with the treant resin to create the staff. Go to the vault in erudin(the bank) and talk to the banker on the far left. Tell him you are in need of some vasty deep water. He will tell you that you need a guild coin in order for him to give it to you. Now return to the clerics guild and tell them that this NPC sent you to get a guild coin. You will be given the coin. When you give it to the banker you will receive the vasty deep water in return. Combine the water and the resin in the staff and the end result will be a peacekeeper staff.
