Princess Lenya
This quest was submitted by Baldarov.

First of all you need to talk to a High Elf by the name of Tearon who is at the gate after you zone into High Keep.  You hail him and he asks if you are a citizen of High Keep.   You say "No I am not a citizen of High Keep."  He then comments on how he feels all the greed in High Keep is appalling and then asks you if you feel the same way.  You say "Yes I do find it appalling."  Then he says that High Keep is 2nd to Neriak when it comes to greed and evil.  And that how having troubles with the Dark Elves is what brought him here and that he is looking for his peoples princess.  You say "I know where the Princess is being kept."   He is amazed that you know about Princess Lenya who is the daughter to the King of the High Elves Tearis Thex.  He then asks you if you want to become a hero to the High Elves.  You say "I will become a hero to the High Elves and save Princess Lenya." 

He then gives you a bracer called Tearon's Bracer (Lore Item).  It weighs 5.0 but has no AC.  Medium can wear it...that's about it.  He says take the bracer to Princess Lenya so she will follow you and then bring her back to him and give him back the bracer when you are done with it.  You have to go to the 4th of floor of High Keep to find her.  There is a Princess Lenia in the dungeon who is a Dark Elf imposter who if you give her the bracer she repays you by casting a couple of spells on ya that make you lose control of yourself.  But there is a hidden wall the moment you get to the 4th floor marked by a picture.  Walk through the wall and find Princess Lenya. 

You hail her and she says, "Aren't you a little short to be a High Keep Guard."   You hand her the bracer...and boom you get some exp and a faction adjustment.   She disappears and then you go back to Tearon.  Give him back his bracer and you get more exp and another faction adjustment.  Then he asks you to give Princess Lenya her amulet.  You give to her and she thanks you for it.  So all this quest is faction adjustment with a little exp.  But it is possible that if you are in good with the High Elves you might get something out of it.
