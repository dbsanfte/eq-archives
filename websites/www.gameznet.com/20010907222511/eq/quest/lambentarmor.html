Lambent Armor
This quest was submitted by: Grimalkin

These quests take place in the Temple of Solusek Ro. These quests are either given by Cryssia Stardreamer or Walthin Fireweaver. Each of these quests requires one Lambent Stone. It is the only piece of any of these quests that is not "no_drop". They are dropped by giants and can usually be bought from players pretty easily.

You will need to trade this Lambent Stone with two other gems to someone in the

Temple in exchange for a Lambent Gem. If you cannot find the gem in question from a Jewelry Shop you can always buy them from Cryssia (for a pretty steep price) if you hail her and ask her about whatever gem you are looking for. MAKE SURE when you turn in the two gems and the lambent stone for the lambent gem that the two regular gems are given at the same time as you hand over the lambent stone but NOT stacked. This will make the trade window on the left appear like so:

<slot 1> Lambent Stone
<slot 2> Whatever Gem 1
<slot 3> Whatever Gem 2
<slot 4> Empty

Here is a list of the Quests and the items you have to retrieve to receive them.

Lambent Helm (Cryssia Stardreamer)

Mudwater Rune - Lizard Fanatic in Cazic Thule

Lambent Stone

NOTE: the Gypsy Lute needed for the quest is _ Not _ the Lute of the Gypsy Princess it is just called "Gypsy Lute" and is no_drop.

Lambent Stone
