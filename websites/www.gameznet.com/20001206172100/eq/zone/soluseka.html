Solusek A
Solusek A is a very interesting dungeon which can be entered from lavastorm or one of several locations in Solusek B. This is one of the more interesting, and dangerous dungeons in the game. It is well suited for characters level 20-35.

It is interesting because it houses not one, but two populations: Fire Goblins and Gnomes.

The gnomes have a mining company in the depths including a vendor who sells the tinker's bag(a weight reducing bag) for a mere 5000pp. Goblins can be found throughout the rest of the dungeon. There is also an efreeti, an imp and a drake who can occasionally be seen in the dungeons depths.

The danger comes because of the lava. If you fall in you are going to have a hard time getting your corpse back without GM assistance.

Another thing to be noted is that large races can not fit through certain areas of the dungeon without being shrunk. The annoying part is that trolls and ogres can't fit past the entrance from lavastorm. So if you are a troll or ogre who can not be shrunk you will need to enter from Solusek B(which can be especially dangerous).
