Troll



Trolls are a hideous race. Although their natural charisma is higher than that of an ogre(this must be here only for balance reasons) they are still not much to look at. They stand alone with ogre's as the most powerful warrior races. Ogre's have the strength and the stamina, but trolls have another ace up their sleeve: regeneration. This can dramatically cut down on resting time especially at mid levels. At higher levels you will probably want to ante up for a bark potion or use some other sort of regeneration item but even then your natural regeneration simply tacks on to these items properties.

Trolls are powerful and can regenerate naturally. Their spellcasting abilities are not the strongest. Trolls can be beastlords, shadowknights, shamans or warrior.
