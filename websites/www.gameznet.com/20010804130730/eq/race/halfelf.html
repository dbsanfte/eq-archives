Everquest Race Guide: Half Elf

Half elves are sort the offspring of a human and an elf. They retain the all important ability to see at night(infravision). Half elf abilities are not as high as most other demi humans but they do have the ability to be bards which is shared only with humans. Half elves can be bards, druids, rangers, rogues or warriors.

Ability wise they are a bit better than humans, they get infravision and they do not have as stiff an experience penalty as most other demi humans.  They are decent in all of their other available classes and they gain levels more rapidly than most other races.
