Battle Worn Halberd
This quest originates at the shadowknights guild in Paineel. Davorre will ask you to deliver a sealed letter to Veisha Fathomwalker who patrols the Tox Forest at night. She is a patroller so you might want to bring a tracker.

When you find her and give her the note you will find out that she is Davorre's ex-lover. She does not want to have anything to do with him and gives you a ring to return to him. Upon returning it he is so angry that he wishes you to kill her new lover, a man by the name of Phaeril Nightshire.

Phaeril is also a patroller out in Tox. Kill him and return his head to Davorre. I received no experience or faction for returning his head. What I did receive was another box to give to Veisha.

Go seek out Veisha again. Hand her this box(which probably contains Phaeril's head) and she will go berserk and shout something at you that you will not understand because the .txt file is missing(Grrr). Kill her and return her head to Davorre and you will be rewarded with the Battleworn Halberd an Erudite Shadowknight only weapon.
