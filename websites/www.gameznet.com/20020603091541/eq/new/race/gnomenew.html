Gnome



Gnomes are a funny race. Their homeland of Akanon is an exotic place indeed. There are clockwork guides and animals running all throughout town. Gnomes have a very high intelligence and infravision which makes them well suited to be pure casters, but the list doesn't stop there. Gnomes can be clerics, enchanters, magicians, necromancers, paladins, rogues, shadowknights, warriors or wizards. I would recommend playing a pure casting class.

Gnomes also are able to use the tinkering skill which allows them to make all sorts of cool trinkets including the much wanted(by human races) infravision goggles and gnome vanishing devices(usable by all and casts invisibility).
