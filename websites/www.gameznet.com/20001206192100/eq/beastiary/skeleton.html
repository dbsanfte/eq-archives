Everquest Beastiary Guide: Skeletons

Skeletons are a mainstay in most low level zones in Norrath. Regular run of the mill skeletons are a favorite of many classes between the levels of 5-8 and especially by clerics. They are the first undead monsters that a cleric can really try out his Ward Undead spell on and get good experience. Using Ward Undead a level 5 cleric can kill even level 7 skeletons in many cases(though a couple resists will make things ugly in a hurry). A level 5 or a level 6 skeleton should not be much of a problem. They are the best way to powerlevel for a level 5-6 cleric.

Skeletons however are not only a favored by other classes as well because they deliver a good amount of loot for their level. They not only carry cash(in their eye sockets presumably) but items and rusty weapons as well. The fact that each skeleton carries up to 2 bone chips on his person makes them a favorite among necromancers in this level range(who need the bone chips to summon their pets).

HIGHER LEVEL SKELETONS

Higher level skeletons come in many ways, shapes and forms. They are all undead, many of them require magical weapons to hit, and they are all generally fearless. Many of these higher level skeletons(such as dry boned or ice boned) skeletons can cast spells. For example an ice boned skeleton can cast cold related spells. Higher level skeletons generally carry very good loot including bronze platemail armor and fine steel weapons. Higher level skeletons can be found at Unrest, Mistmoore and Lower Guk.
