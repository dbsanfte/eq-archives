Everquest Beastiary Guide: Minotaur

Minotaurs are found in maze like surroundings. The two areas they frequent in the game are the mazes of Beholder and the maze like mines of Steamfont. The minotaurs in steamfont are smaller and weaker than their beholder cousins. Steamfont minotaurs range between levels 9-12. There are a couple of level 40 minotaur guards however deep inside the mines(as well as in Lower Guk). There is also a level 40 minotaur hero who will occasionally make an guest appearance killing all the the young players and taking a few guards down with him. The minotaurs of Beholder vary by title. Lesser minotaurs are level 9-13 in range, Gorge minotaurs are ~15 and chasm minotaurs can range between level 23-25. There is no minotaur hero in this zone but there are Evil Eyes which can be almost as dangerous.

Minotaurs give a good amount of treasure for their level. However the true reason they are hunted lies more in the fact that they occasionally carry minotaur axes. These axes are one of the best weapons you can get at lower levels.
