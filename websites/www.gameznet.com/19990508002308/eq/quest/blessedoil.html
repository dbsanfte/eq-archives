Everquest Quest Guide: Blessed Oil

This quest takes place at the Temple of Life(Cleric's Guild) in Qeynos. Nomloe Jugusta is in need of an eager young recruit to deliver the blessed oil to Brother Estle. Brother Estle will be near the entrance to West Karana(probably fighting with another priest) which is just a short walk away. Deliver the oil to him and you will receive a faction adjustment, some experience and a small cash compensation. You might also receive information on further quests as reported by one player.
