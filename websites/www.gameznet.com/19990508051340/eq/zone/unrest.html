Everquest Zone Guide: Unrest

Unrest is an undead dungeon in Dagnor's Cauldron. This dungeon is tuned for characters level 15-30.

Some of the monsters you can expect to find here include Jack-O-Lanterns(in the hedge maze), tormented dead(a level 12-15 version of zombies), ice boned skeletons, carrion ghouls, lurking mummies, ghouls, and werebats.

Lower level characters should conecentrate on fighting the jack-o-lanterns in the hedge mazes. They are the safest prey to fight as you can usually get them one on one. You will notice death beetles patrolling the courtyard. Death beetles are not agressive they will attack anyone who is in combat with undead however and once they begin coming it's usually best to just zone(unless they still give you experience).

Mid level characters will probably want to pull from the front of the house to the fountains. The reason you want to pull to the fountain is because wizards and clerics can cast from a safe enough distance if they stand back against the hallway and not make the monster go agressive to them.

Around level 20-24 you will want to start pulling from the left side of the house. this will allow you to pull carrion ghouls(for small ringmail) and ice boned skeletons(for human sized ringmail) and not have to worry about as many of the lower level monsters getting in the way.

Around level 25+ you will start wanting to get a group together and conquering the unique monsters of this dungeon. There is a mortuary fungus to the right side of the house down a tunnel underwater. He is not undead and ranges in the level 26-28 range. The fungus can cast a fungus drowsy spell which is rather effective.

By far the most exciting things in this dungeon take place in the basement. If you were entering from the front of the house and turned down the left hallway and went to the last doorway you could open that door. It would appear to be empty except for a bookshelf. Click on the bookshelf and you will find the entrance to the basement. In this basement lies all sorts of baddies including barbed bone skeletons(level 24-26 skeletons which whose barbed thorns will damage you when hit them), skeletal monks(level 24-26 undead monks), and werebats.

In the center of the basement is one of the two hags in Unrest. Festering hags are level 28-30 undead spellcasters who can cast some very powerful spells including charm, blood boil and engulfing darkness. The hag carries small bronze platemail armor which is much sought after.

There is also a secret passageway in the basement which leads to another room. Beware the trap in this hallway. You will come to an area where it appears to be a safe walkway on each side and lava in the center. The lava does not hurt and you can walk right through it. The walkways however are a trap that leads to a pit that you will likely die in.

Deep in this room you will find not only tentacle terrors but a dwarven spirit by the name of Garanel Rucksforth who is part of some quest i'm sure.
