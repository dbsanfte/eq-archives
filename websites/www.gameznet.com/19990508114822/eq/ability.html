Ability Guide
On this page you will find information on what each of the different abilities(Strength, Intelligence, etc) actually do in the game. What areas they affect and how to tailor your character to get the most out of your available ability scores. Select an ability from the list below:

Agility - How agile you are and your ability to make blows miss.
Charisma - How charismatic and charming, directly affects prices.
Dexterity - Your hand eye coordination, affects probability to hit.
Intelligence - This affects mana for wizard types and learning speed.
Stamina - Your constituation and how many hit points you will have.
Strength - Strength affects not only damage but chance to hit.
Wisdom - This is the primary stat of priest types and will affect mana.
