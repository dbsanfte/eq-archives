Everquest Zone Guide: Navigation Guide

Navigation Guide - Making travelling in Norrath easier and less frustrating

There is nothing easier in EQ than getting lost. Nothing more frustrating than using sense direction when it is low. Here is a very easy way to know exactly where you are going without the aggravation of 20 consecutive failed attempts at sense direction. First modify
a social that you don't  need with the following - name: loc, command: /loc. Second, add  the social as a hotkey.  This will be your new nicely priced compass and greatly facilitate navigation.

How to use the command effectively

Location provides 4 pieces of data - X,Y,Z and heading values. Discard Z as it is worthless - it measures the height of the ground
on which you stand on. What interests us is X Y and heading. Oddly enough, the X value is for the Y axis (north-south), the Y value is for the X axis (west-east). The heading value is tells you what direction you are heading in.

The heading value's range is 0 to 512, with 0 and 512 being true north.

                       north
                       0/512
west                                     east
128                                       384
                       south
                       256

- travelling west raises your Y value (X axis)
- travelling east lowers your Y value (X axis)
- travelling north raises your X value (Y axis)
- travelling south lowers your X value (Y axis)

Strategy : When u travel, hit your hotkey continuously. It keeps you updated as to what direction  you are heading as well as your current coordinates. When finding an interesting location take down the coordinates (or right before you die to find your corpse). This makes it very easy  to return to the site afterwards, no searching around anymore, just a straight path to where you want to go. Hope this helps you in your journey.
