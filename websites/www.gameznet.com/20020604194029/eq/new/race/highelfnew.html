High Elf



High elves are the snobby cousins of the rest of the elven races. They are excellent casters and they have the ability to wear both small and human sized armor. High elves can be clerics, enchanters, magicians, paladins, and wizards. They make pretty good paladins if your holy desire is to cast spells but their low strength is a definate disadvantage. High elves are all intelligence and wit and no true brawn.

They are not the best race for any one class, however they are pretty good at every class which they can play. They have a great base wisdom for the cleric class, as well as intelligence for the pure caster class.
