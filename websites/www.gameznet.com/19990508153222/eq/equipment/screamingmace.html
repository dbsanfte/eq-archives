Screaming Mace
 
MAGIC ITEM
Skill: 1H Blunt    Atk Delay: 35
DMG: 8
Effect: Screaming Mace
WT: 8.0
Class: WAR CLR PAL RNG SHD DRU MNK BRD ROG SHM
Race: All
Worn in Melee slot.

The Screaming Mace effect gives +10 STR and +22 AC for approxiametely 18 seconds. It also gives off light similar to that of a greater lightstone. The Screaming Mace can be obtained by completing the Screaming Mace Quest.
