Drolvarg Fangs Quest

 Faction:

 Related Zones:

Dreadlands


A simple quest. One must hunt Drovlogs, and collect 4 of their Canines. Take the Canines in and turn them into the Warrior trainer, the name of whom I have forgotten. You can find the Warrior guild by taking a right from the ramp into the Paladin guild and following the moat to the first building you see. Your reward is some change, minor exp, some "Elven Trail Mix" and some nice faction changes. Drolvargs can be found in a variety of zones, including Firiona Vie and The Dreadlands.
