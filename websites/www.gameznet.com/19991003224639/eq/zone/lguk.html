Lower Guk
Lower Guk(aka the Ruins of Old Guk) is one of the scariest dungeons in the game. This dungeon is well tuned for characters level 30 and up. It can be entered from one of several paths from Upper Guk.

This dungeon is popular not only due to it's large size but for the many items which can be found in it's depths some of which include Azure Armor, the Executioners Axe and the Dark Reaver.

There are two major factions of monsters in this dungeon. The first is the Froglocks of Guk. These are the same frogs which you can find upstairs yet in much higher level variants(beginning around level 28). The froglock king can be found in this zone.

The other major faction is the Undead Froglocks of Guk. These are the minions of the Froglock Ghoul Lord who can be found deep in the depths of Lower Guk. The ghouls can paralyze and there are also ghoul wizards which can make short work of an unprepared party.

Other beasts which can be found in the ruins of old Guk include Basilisks, Minotaurs and Evil Eyes.
