Everquest Zone Guide: Lower Guk

Lower guk is one of the highest level dungeons in the game. It can be entered from one of several different locations from Upper Guk. This dungeons features some of the highest level and scariest mobs in the game. It also features high level rewards. Some of the most powerful magical items in the game can be found in this dungeon so it should not be overlooked. Generally this dungeon is tuned for characters level 35-50 with a few monsters that a group of level 30-35 characters can handle.

Some of the monsters you will find down here include high level froglocks and skeletons, froglock ghouls, vampire bats, giant black widow spiders, greater minotaurs and gargoyles.
