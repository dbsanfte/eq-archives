Shaman Spells
Level 1

Burst Of Flame

Burst of flame is the first direct damage spell that a magician receives. It is a low damage spell but it will have to tide you over for the time being.

Cure Disease

This spell can be used to cure disease.

Dexterous Aura

This spell allows you to grant increased dexterity to either yourself or another player.

Endure Cold

This buffing spell allows you to increase the resistance to cold for yourself or another player.

Flash Of Light

This spell will temporarily blind your opponent.

Inner Fire

This is the first in a line of shaman buffing spells. It adds both armor and hit points.

 

Minor Healing

Minor healing is the first in the line of healing spells.

Strengthen

This buffing spell allows you to add to the strength of yourself or another player.

Level 5

Cure Poison

This spell will counteract poisons.

Drowsy

This spell will slow the rate of your opponents attacks.

 

Endure Fire

This spell adds to a players resistance to fire.

 

Feet Like Cat

Feet like Cat adds to the targets agility ability.

Fleeting Fury

Fleeting Fury adds to your targets armor class, dexterity and strength scores for a short period of time(similar to yalup).

Frost Rift

Frost rift is a direct damage offensive spell.

 

Gate

The gate spell will teleport you home to your bind point.

Scale Skin

This spell adds to the targets armor class. It requires snake scales as a component.

Sicken

This spell diseases your opponent for a short period of time.

Spirit Pouch

Spirit Pouch summons a no rent lightweight pouch.

 

Summon Water

This spell allows you to summon some water. Summoned water is no rent meaning that if you log out of the game the water will dissapear.

Level 9

Cure Blindness

This spell will cure a player of blindness.

Endure Disease

This spell adds to your resistance to disease.

Light Healing

Light healing is the second in the line of healing spells.

Sense Animals

This spell will point you in the direction of the nearest animal.

Spirit Of The Bear

Spirit of the bear adds to a players stamina score.

Spirit Of The Wolf

This will allow the player this is cast on to run at a very fast pace in outdoor areas.

Spirit Sight

Spirit Sight is a see invisibility spell.

Summon Food

This will summon some magical food that will dissapear after a player logs out.

Tainted Breath

This is a poison spell.

Level 14

Bind Affinity

This spell will change you or one of your party members bind point.

Burst Of Strength

Burst of strength is a temporary strength buffing spell.

Disempower

This spell will lower your targets strength and armor class. It also will drain their stamina.

Endure Poison

This spell adds to a players poison resistance.

Enduring Breath

Enduring breath is a water breathing spell.

Invisibility vs. Animals

This spell renders you invisible to animals.

Levitate

Levitate is a flight spell which is useful for travelling over bodies of water or lava. It is also useful to prevent a player from being knocked off of ledges.

Root

This spell will root a monster in place and prevent it from moving.

Shrink

This spell will reduce a players size. This is a godsend for large races who can not fit in certain areas.

Spirit Of The Snake

Spirit of the snake adds to a players charisma ability.

Spirit Strike

This is a direct damage offensive spell.

Turtle Skin

Turtle skin is a buffing spell which adds armor class.

Walking Sleep

Walking sleep will cauase your opponent to attack at a slower pace.

Level 19

Affliction

This disease attack will do damage over time.

Cancel Magic

Cancel magic can be used to get rid of harmful spell effects cast on yourself or your group members.

Endure Magic

This spell adds to a players resistance to magic.

Frenzy

This spell will increase a players melee combat ability.

Healing

This is the third in the line of healing spells. It heals roughly 80 points of damage.

Infectious Cloud

Infectious cloud is an area effect disease spell which does a bit of damage up front and has a very small damage over time factor.

Insidious Fever

This spell will lower your opponents resistance to disease as well as doing damage over time.

Malise

This spell is a resistance debuff which can be cast upon your enemies.

Spirit Of The Cat

This spell adds to a players agility score.

Spirit Strength

This is a more powerful version of the strength spell.

Vision

This spell will allow you to view through another players eyes.

Level 24

Cannibalize

This spell will lower your health and raise your mana.

Counteract Disease

This is a more powerful version of the cure disease spell. It works on higher level diseases.

Envenomed Breath

This is an upgrade to the tainted breath spell which does damage over time(poison).

Frost Strike

Frost strike is a direct damage offensive spell.

Invigor

This spell returns a players lost stamina.

Poison Storm

This is an area effect poison attack.

Protect

Protect is an armor class buff.

Regeneration

This spell will allow a player to regenerate at an accelerated rate.

Resist Cold

This spell adds to a players resistance to cold based attacks.

Spirit Of The Cheetah

This spell grants the player a temporary speed burst. It is even faster than spirit of the wolf but has a much shorter duration.

Spirit Of The Monkey

Spirit of the monkey adds to a players dexterity.

Spirit Of The Ox

This spell is a stamina buffing spell.

Level 29

Alluring Aura

Alluring aura will add a nice boost to a characters charisma ability.

Befriend Animal

This spell allows you to charm animals and use them as your personal pets.

Counteract Poison

Counteract poison is a more poweful version of the cure poison spell. It works on more powerful poisons and it works more often.

Greater Healing

Greater healing is the last in the line of shaman healing spells. It heals approximately 300 points of damage.

Invisibility

They can't hit what they can't see.

Listless Power

This spell is a whole scale debuff spell. It will lower your opponents ability scores.

Quickness

This spell will increase a players attack speed.

Raging Strength

This is an upgrade to spirit strength.

Resist Fire

This spell adds to a players resistance to heat based attacks.

Rising Dexterity

This is a dexterity buffing spell.

Tagar's Insects

This spell is an upgrade to the walking sleep spell. It lowers your opponents rate of attack.

Ultravision

This spell grants a player ultravision.

Level 34

Companion Spirit

This spell will summon a wolf that can be used as your personal pet.

Enstill

Enstill is an upgrade to the root spell. It has a longer duration but takes longer to cast and uses more mana.

Fury

This is an upgrade to the frenzy line of spells.

Health

This spell is a high level stamina buff.

Malisement

This is an upgrade to the malise spell. It lowers your opponents magic resistance.

Nimble

Nimble is a high level agility buff.

Resist Disease

This spell adds to a players resistance to disease.

Scourge

Scourge is a powerful damage over time spell.

Shifting Shield

This spell is an armor class buff.

Talisman Of Tnarg

This is a hit point buffing spell.

Winter's Roar

Winter's Roar is a direct damage spell. It does about 255 damage.

Level 39

Assidious Vision

This is an upgrade to the level 19 vision spell.

Blinding Luminance

This spell blinds your opponent temporarily.

Chloroplast

Chloroplast is a more powerful version of the regeneration spell.

Deftness

Deftness is a dexterity buff.

Extinguish Fatigue

This spell will return a players lost stamina.

Furious Strength

This is an upgrade to the raging strength spell.

Gale Of Poison

This is an area effect poison based DoT spell. It does about 220 damage to each target.

Glamour

This is a high level charisma buff.

Insidious Malady

Insidious malady is an upgrade to insidious fever. It is a disease resistance debuff.

Resist Poison

This spell will add to a players resistance to poison.

Togor's Insects

This spell will lower your opponents rate of attack.

Venom Of The Snake

This spell is a poison based attack which does damage over time.

Level 44

Agility

This will add to a players agility score.

Alacrity

This is the last in the line of attack speed enhancing spells.

Guardian

This is the last in the line of shaman buffing spells that begin with scaleskin->turtleskin->etc.

Guardian Spirit

This spell summons a high level wolf to be used as a pet. This is an upgrade to the companion spirit spell.

Incapicitate

I have no information on this spell. Anyone who knows what this spell does please send a mail to jcsmith@gameznet.com.

Nullify Magic

Nullify magic is a more powerful version of the cancel magic spell.

Resist Magic

This spell adds to a players resistance to magic.

Stamina

This will increase a players stamina score.

Talisman Of Altune

This spell will grant a player additional hit points.

Level 49

Abolish Disease

I'm not positive but I believe this is an area effect cure disease spell.

Charisma

This will add to a players charisma score.

Dexterity

This spell will add to a players dexterity score.

Envenomed Bolt

This is the last in the line of shaman direct damge spells. It is a poison based attack.

Frenzied Spirit

This is a buffing spell which can be cast upon your summoned pet.

Malosi

This is an upgrade to the malise line of spells. It is a magic resistance debuff.

Plague

This is an upgrade to the scourge line of spells. Its a powerful damge over time spell.

Strength

This will add to a players strength score.

Wall Of Fire

Wall of fire is a high level damage shield.
