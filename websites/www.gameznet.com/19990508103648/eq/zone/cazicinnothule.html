Everquest Zone Guide: Cazic Thule

Click Here For A Map

The temple of Cazic Thule is the home of the Lizard Men. This zone is good for players 15-35. Beware however that lizard men spawn quickly in this temple and there may not be a lot of time to rest. The dungeon is one of the most beautiful in the game however.

This temple is found in the zone of Feerot. It is the largest temple in the zone. If you are coming from the Rathe Mountains entrance you can hang a right and continue hugging the zone edge until you reach the water. Swim accross and more or less follow the temples at the zone until you reach the large one. Up the stairs is the entrance to Cazic Thule.
