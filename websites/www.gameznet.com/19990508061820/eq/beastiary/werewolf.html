Everquest Beastiary Guide: Werewolf

Werewolves are a common form of lycanthrope. They can be found in many of the forested areas of Norrath including the karanas and kithicor forest regions. They can also be found in Mistmoore. Most werewolves range around level 22 however there are some very strong variants inside the walls of mistmoore that range up to level 28. Werewolves are a creature of the night, they will seldom be found during the day.

Werewolves are highly sought for their magical items. They are also sought because their claws and pelt can be used to create lupine armor.
