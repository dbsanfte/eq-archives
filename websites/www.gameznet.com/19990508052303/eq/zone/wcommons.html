Everquest Zone Guide: West Commonlands

The west commonlands links together the East Commonlands and the Kithicor Forest. It is also the entrance point to the dungeon of Befallen.

This is a good zone for players level 10-40. On the low end of things you will find pumas, several types of giant snakes and spiders and a few orc and cutthroat camps.

Higher level characters will find the occasional werewolf, griffon, or hill giant. Evil races might want to pick on the freeport militiamen who insist on charging a toll to use the road.
