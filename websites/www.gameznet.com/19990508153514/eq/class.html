Class Guide
On this page you will find screenshots, information, and tips on how to play each of the various classes in the game. You will learn which races are the best fits for each class. You will also learn the strengths and weaknesses of each of the classes in the game. Select a class from the list below:

Choosing The Best Class For Your Playing Style

Bard - Jack of all trades, great in a group.
Cleric - The ultimate group class, healer and buffer extraordinaire.
Druid - The priests of the forest.
Enchanter - An interesting cross between an enchanter and illusionist.
Magician - Masters of conjuration with some offense to boot.
Monk - Hand to hand martial artists.
Necromancer - Masters of the dead.
Paladin - The holy warriors of Norrath.
Ranger - The guardians and keepers of the wilderness.
Rogue - Thieves and assasins.
Shadowknight - The unholy warriors of Norrath.
Shaman - Withdoctors, the priests of the primitive tribes.
Warrior - The bulk and muscle of any party.
Wizard - The masters of damage, sight and travel spells.
