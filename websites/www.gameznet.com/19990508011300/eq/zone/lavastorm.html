Everquest Zone Guide: Lavastorm

Lavastorm is a popular zone for characters level 10-20. It is extremely popular among druids and magicians who specialize in destroying summoned beasts. It is a popular zone for it's destination points as much as the monsters it contains. Lavastorm links to Najena, Solusek and Nektulos. It also contains a gypsy camp where players can buy combine weapons.

That having been said lavastorm has a plethora of monsters for characters level 10-20 to fight. In addition to the fire goblin camps you will find fire snakes, fire drakes, and both earth and fire elements. As you can probably tell from the list of monsters it is wise to bring along someone who can cast the endure fire spell.

One of the more serious dangers in lavastorm does not come from the monsters but rather from the lava in the zone. This lava is very dangerous and if you fall in you may lose your corpse forever. To make a long story short be very, very careful.
