MoneyQuest
An age old question... Can gaming make you money? Well if it comes to EverQuest...yes. When you hear the words money and EverQuest I'm sure Ebay comes to mind. I'm sure at one time or another you have visited the site to see what's there, if he's a hoax or not. Well it's not a hoax, and infact verant is against it.

Who in their right mind would pay 2500$ for an eq account you may ask yourself at times, even I do sometimes. Way I see it is that for the ammount of time/monthly fees that it would take to get all the items/levels a character has. Many find it cheeper to just buy an account rather than spend time leveling it themselves. Some are newbies just wanting a boost, others are old AD&Ders just seeking a new begining with another class, and not wanting to have to do it all over again.

Here I am talking about how it's nice, back to the subject, Can gaming make you money? Is EverQuest a job? Is Camping a Short Sword of the Ykesha the same as working for a day or 2? For some...well...yes. I don't know about all of you, infact I know about 5% of all the people that play EverQuest, but as common observence I have found that apx. 35-45 compleated transactions are done on EBay each day. Yet... if everyone is against ebay, who is buying the items...

EverQuest is a source of income to some, source of fun for others, and a source of escape for the rest. If people are able to make money thru the game, then I see little reason to not count playing EverQuest as a job. Infact I wouldn't be suprised if some people have added: EverQuest Merchant to their resume. It's not far-fetched, infact EverQuest is playing it's own role on the economy.

If you take a larger look at things, you can make some damn nice cash off playing the crack. Spend a bit of time, get a level 50, get some nice items, sell it off, doit again, u can support yourself from playing a computer game... weird huh? So let's take a larger look, you could make 25,000$ + a year from EverQuest. You could quit your job and just play the game and support yourself. Don't go telling the boss you quit just yet!

As much as the world is evolving, people aren't able to fully support themselves from just EverQuest. Verant is even trying to stop the sale of goods on EBay. So is the MoneyQuest over? Or just begining...
