Testament of Vanear
This quest starts in the cleric's guild in Erudin. Rarnan will ask you to fetch him a book from the library.

When you go to the library the book will already be checked out. After a q&a with a few of the librarians you will find out that Moodoro Findharn checked the book out last. He was then executed and the book was never returned.

You can go talk to his sister in the palace and she will provide you with further information. It appears that Moodoro was not actually executed and that he is alive and well and drinking himself into a coma at Crow's in Qeynos.

Before bothering to venture to Qeynos it is probably wise to do the Eruds Tonic quest first so you have the tonic handy when you speak to Moodoro. He is sick and needs a tonic to ease his pain(too many of Crow's Specials I suppose).

Moodoro claims he will sell you the testament for a measly 2gp. After you give him the 2gp however you will find that it is missing two pages. These pages evidentally were stolen, rendering the book useless.

Apparently the thief then sold these pages to Ran in Highpass. Ran is in the library in Highpass. When you get there however he will tell you that he was only sold page 30 and not page 34. He said that someone in a qeynos guard uniform had stolen page 30 from him.

The man who has stolen page 30 is an NPC by the name of Dyllin. He is in highpass in a hidden camp near the lake. Kill him and he should have page 30 of the testament. He is a corrupt qeynos guard it appears so you are going to lose faction with all the bad guys in qeynos. Also any bad guys in the immediate area will assist him when you are attacking, so be careful.

You will probably be stumped at this point. Noone seems to have page 34. The truth of the matter is that Moodoro himself still has the page. So return to qeynos and demand he gives you the page. He will then tell you that he will play you for the last page. Give him 4gp a play and keep doing it until you get the jester and win the game. He will then give you the other page.

Combine page 34 and 30 in the testament(place them inside it and hit the combine button) and you will be rewarded with the Testament of Vanear. Priests will probably elect to keep this item as the Wisdom and Mana come in handy. You can also return the testament to Rarnan for a minor reward.
