The third Monk Shackle quest. After completing the Shackle of Stone quest, Master Raksa will mention that you need to see Master Niska. Ask her about the Shackle of Rock, and she will tell you she needs the Fists of Talon, from legendary monk. When asked for more information, she refers you to the Court Chronicler. The Court Chronicler has a book about Talon, which describes his whole life, and at the end states that his fists were removed after his death and placed in a jar inside of Kurn's Tower. Here is the trick. Talon actually had three fists: two left ones and one right one.

The first left fist is dropped from a Sarnak Courier in the Lake of Ill Omen.

To get the right fist, you need to talk to Master Rinmark. On one of the mountains here, loc -5055 by 2792, you will find "an iksar master". He scowls at good races, and is amiable to Iksars. Hail him and he says nothing, just emotes "you can see he is aware of your presence". Asking him who Master Rinmark is gets you an emote of "squints an eye at you'(something like that). If you say "What fists of talon southpaw" he tells you he is looking for a monk to return the fist to Cabilis. Saying that you will return the fist to Cabilis gets you a no drop lore pack (4 slots).

He tells you to combine 4 things in it:

An Excavator's Claw - Found on Burynai Excavators in Field of Bones or Kurn's Tower

An Intact Brutling Chopper - Found on a Skulking Brutling in Warslik's Woods a location - Negative 2200 Positive 2400. They are fairly rare.

A Sash of an Exiled Human Monk - found on a Young Ronin in the Oasis of Marr, I've heard you can find them at any derv camp on antonica, but I found mine in the Oasis at the derv camp there. He spawns in place of the Dervish sometimes. - Fairly Rare

Sparring Gloves - Found on a Goblin Pitfighter in Warsilik Woods. He spawns in the tents located in the big goblin fort in W-Woods and is pretty rare, took me a day to get him.

 

The final left fist also starts with Master Rinmark. He mentions an Ogre you need to seek out. This Ogre is named The Great Oowoomp, and he is in the Timorous Deep, located on the Ogre island where you catch the evil boat, you are dropped off on this island if you take the shuttle boats from The Oasis of Marr docks. If you give him 5 gp (you need to sneak up to him since he is aggressive), he will summon a dancing skeleton. This is really who you need to talk to. Hail the skeleton. Ask him about Talon Southpaw. It turns out he has talon's hand. Tell him I will return the hand, and he will give you a hand with one thumb, which is a 4 slot container, and tell you to search out the other fingers in the tower of past pain and torture.

The fingers are from the fingered skeleton(He spawns near the jester's spawn point in the basement of Kurn's Tower. He has a 30 minute or so spawn time and will always spawn if you keep his area cleared. He will often drop the same finger more than once, so this make take a while. Combining these fingers in the hand container will result in the last fist needed for this quest.

Bring Master Niska all three hands and you will receive your shackle

Reward: Shack of Rock
