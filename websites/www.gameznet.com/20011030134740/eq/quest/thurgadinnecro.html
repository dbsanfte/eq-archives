Thurgadin Necromancer Quests
This quest begins with in the library in Thurgadin It was submitted by Azul.

You say, 'hail Lorekeeper Zorik'

Lorekeeper Zorik says 'I seek those who practice the art of dark magicks. I seek those who call themselves necromancers. Are you a necromancer?

You say, 'i am a necromancer'

Lorekeeper Zorik says 'Excellent. Are you sure enough of your skills to undertake my tasks? If not, get out of my sight, weakling!

You say, 'i will do your tasks'

Lorekeeper Zorik says 'I thought so. One should never back down from a challenge. Once you have completed them, I will have a cap, a robe, sleeves, wristbands, gloves, leggings and boots to reward you with.

You say, 'what about a cap'

Lorekeeper Zorik says 'For an enchanted head guard bring me a torn enchanted silk turban as well as three pieces of crushed flame opal.

You say, 'what about a robe'

Lorekeeper Zorik says 'If you wish to have a fine robe, bring unto me a torn enchanted silk robe and three pristine emeralds. Then you will receive a robe beyond your wildest dreams.

You say, 'what about sleeves'

Lorekeeper Zorik says 'Bring unto me a set of torn enchanted silk sleeves as well as three flawed topaz. With these in my possession I can create a set of enchanted sleeves the likes of which spellcasters like you dream of.

You say, 'what about leggings'

Lorekeeper Zorik says 'Three nephrite and a pair of torn enchanted silk leggings will net you a fine set of pantaloons.

You say, 'what about gloves'

Lorekeeper Zorik says 'If you seek gloves of great power, bring to me three crushed topaz and a pair of torn enchanted silk gloves. I will use the power of the gems to imbue the gloves.

You say, 'what about boots'

Lorekeeper Zorik says 'Three crushed jaundice gems combined with torn enchanted silk boots would make a most interesting set of footwear. The latent powers of the gems can be harnessed to create magical effects.

You say, 'what about wristbands'

Lorekeeper Zorik says 'An enchanted silk wristguard and three crushed onyx sapphires is all that I require to create an imbued wrist wrap of great power. Seek out these items and return to me when you have acquired them.
