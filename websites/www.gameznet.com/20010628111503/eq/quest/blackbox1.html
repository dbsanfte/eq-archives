Ak'Anon Rusted Blackbox Quest

This quest originates in Ak'Anon. Manik Compolten of the Gnome Warriors' Guild is looking for rusted blackboxes. These can be found on Rogue Clockworks in Steamfont. This quest may be completed by most class/race combos, it is the primary way to raise faction for most Ak'anon quests that require amiable or better faction to intitiate.

Quest Info
Level needed to complete: 6+
NPC's Involved: Manik Compolten, Rogue Clockworks
Zones Involved: Ak'Anon, Steamfont

Cash reward: GP, SP, CP
