Unrest
Unrest is an undead dungeon in Dagnor's Cauldron. This dungeon is tuned for characters level 15-30, with a special 35th level mob at the bottom.

Some of the monsters you can expect to find here include Jack-O-Lanterns(in the hedge maze), tormented dead(a level 12-15 version of zombies), dry boned skeletons, carrion ghouls, lurking mummies, ghouls, and werebats.

Lower level characters should concentrate on fighting the jack-o-lanterns in the hedge mazes. They are the safest prey to fight as you can usually get them one on one. You will notice death beetles patrolling the courtyard. Death beetles are not aggressive, however they will attack anyone who is in combat with undead.

Mid level characters will probably want to pull from the front of the house to the fountains. This is so wizards and clerics can cast from a safe enough distance. Also it leaves the player easy access to the zone.

At around level 20-24 players will want to start pulling from the house. Dry boned, greater dry boned and barbed boned skeletons are all known to drop small bronze platemail from time to time making them perfect targets.

On the second floor you will find carrion ghouls, many varieties of skeleton, and an occasional werebat.

The third floor is where you'll find the bar, also called the magi room. This room is inhabited by all sorts of undead uglies, including the undead barkeep, the zombie of an unrest noble, the reclusive ghoul magi, and countless other mid twenties spawns.

On the top floor of the mansion, you'll find the much sought after undead knight, which occasionally drops the bloodstained tunic, although he usually drops a bloodstained mantle, a very nice booby prize. In addition to the knight there are two other spawn spots, which yield werebats, greater darkbones, barbed bones, and hags. 

By far the most exciting things in this dungeon take place in the basement. Click on a bookshelf and you will find the entrance to the basement. In this basement there lurks all sorts of badness, including barbed bone skeletons (level 24-26 skeletons whose barbed thorns will damage you when hit them,) skeletal monks and werebats.

In the center of the basement are two of the hags in Unrest. Festering hags are level 28-30 undead spellcasters who can cast some very powerful wizard spells. The hag has been said to carry gossamer armor which is much sought after, although this is an extremely rare drop.

There is also a secret passageway in the basement which leads to another room. Beware the trap in this hallway. You will come to an area where it appears to be a safe walkway on each side and what looks like a pool of lava in the center. The lava is actually a pool of blood which you can walk right through. The walkways however, are traps that lead to pits inhabited by some nasty spell casting fungi, the Mortuary Fungus.

Deep in this room you will find not only tentacle terrors (including Dark Terrors) but a dwarven ghost by the name of Garanel Rucksforth who drops jagged bands and rarely, the dwarven workboots.
