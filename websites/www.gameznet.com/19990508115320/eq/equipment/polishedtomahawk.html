Everquest Equipment Guide: Polished Granite Tomahawk

Nice magic 1H Slashing weapon found in High Pass Hold. It has a damage of 6 with only a 26 delay, as well as occasionally casting 'Berserker Stregnth' on the wielder, giving +18 Str bonus, -10 Agility penalty, and a 50 to 150 hit point damage shield.
