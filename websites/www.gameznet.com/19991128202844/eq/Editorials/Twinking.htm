Twinking
I was in black burrow last night when I noticed a few people in steel plate. They were level 5, so I said, ah twinkies. Now normaly I have nothing against it, but something struck me as odd, they were the only 2 people in an area where there are usally 5 or so. I decided to dig a little into this. I created a new character, leveled him to level 2, and went into black burrow. Upon entering the room, I asked if I could join into the rotation. The 2 in steel said no, leave you idiot. I said, there are 5 monsters that spawn here, I'm sure you can share, one said no, we can't, and logged off. I wondered where he went. I found out soon enough, he had gone on his higher level char, and began to kill anything that spawned. I then just left the room, and they began to insult me a few times, called me a few names, nothing too big. The thing that upset me was, that if you are a twinked char, people usally know you have a higher level, but if you use your higher level as a way of saying, get out of this room it is mine, then you are ruining the whole effect of a newbie area. It doesn't matter how twinked you are, for all I care you could have a crimson robe of whatever, but you are still a newbie character, and you have to live up to being one. You are not a level 50 wizard or what ever, you are a newbie. A newbie can't use a 1100 dmg spell to get other newbies out of the area. No, they have to live with what they have. If you are twinked, you aren't supposed to just say, oh you can't kill these or I will go on my high level character and kill them all so you can't have any. You are supposed to take up and live the rules of the area, as a newbie. Not as a level 50 wizard bully, but as a level 10 newbie fighting the monsters you are supposed to be fighting. I feel that if you are going to just use your higher level to keep people out of the area, then it is pointless to even make a new character. The world is set by an eco system. If you ruin an area, the world takes damage. So if you are going to twink a character, please play the character in the sense of the character, not as the higher level.
