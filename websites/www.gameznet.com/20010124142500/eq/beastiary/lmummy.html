Mummy
Lesser Mummies are a favorite among soloing clerics and necromancers. This is becauase they fill that undead void between zombies and greater skeletons.

Mummies can be quite profitable for their level range. They carry rawhide armor and other items. The loot varies but it generally decent.

Mummies carry disease, so unless hunters have the ability to cure disease it is best to avoid them. Lesser mummies can stricken with the M1Mummy disease which lasts for a very long time and will not allow players to heal unless they sit down. This makes them unappealing to melee classes.

One of the more noteworthy features of this m.o.b. are their haunting, pain filled moans. This makes them all the more exciting for slayers of the undead, especially when soloing in the middle of night. 

HIGHER LEVEL MUMMIES

Higher level mummies can be found in several different zones accross Norrath, including Unrest, Befallen, Southern Desert of Ro, and Oasis. These mummies are identical to lower level mummies except their diseases are more powerful and they carry much better loot. There is a named mummy that is known to appear around Northern Ro and Oasis, and carries the scepter of Rahotep.
