You say, 'Hail Commander Leuz'

Commander Leuz says 'Hello Keyno , Im the new commander of the Sky ShrineMilitia. This group of Shrine residents and newly aligned outlanders aregiven various tasks to do for the benefit of the Shrine. Are you here to request a task?

You say, 'I am here to request a task.'

Commander Leuz says 'We have had recent news from one of our scouts inWestern Wastes that there have been Storm Giants wandering the coastal area.We have not heard back from this scout in some time. What we need is a volunteer to go deliver some tools so the scout can accurately communicate what is happening back to us.

You say, 'I will volunteer'

Commander Leuz says 'Very good! Take this to the scout and wait for them to return the report.

In Weastern Wastes there is an npc named Scout Charisa that stands on a hill near the Sirens grotto zone in Weaster Watelands. Give her the tool set.

Scout Charisa says' Great! I was wondering when they would send someone out to give me these. In my haste and excitement of this new assignment I forgot these. This scout business is great I must say. It sure beats setting in
Southern Ro studying the ruins. I've been following these Storm Giants for days. They've been wandering up and down the coast looking for something.

This will spawn 8 giants near the shoreline back over a hill near the SG zone cave. 7 Soldiers and 1 Kromzek Captian. Kill the captian, loot the Broken disk and return it to Scout Charisa.

She will reward you with a robe as well as a note that is to be returned to Commander Luez in Skyshrine.

Giving the robe back to Scout Charisa will result in her giving you a nice bracer.

If you return the bracer to her, she will give you a talisman. All 3 items are nice but each one is good only based on what class you are. The 3 rewards are located at the bottom of this quest.

Return to Skyshrine and hand the note to Commander Luez.

Commander Leuz says' Well this artifact will defently need study. Thank you for this report. Perhaps after we have researched this object more we will have more for you to search for.'
