Brown Leather Gloves
This quest was submitted by Catrine:

This quest is received after you complete the Rogue Mail Delivery quest in Qeynos. 

Den needs an high quality wolf skin, high quality bear skin, snake scales, and bat fur for an important client. Give these to him and you get faction, coin, xp and a note to take back to Ghil. Give the note to Ghil and you get the brown leather gloves, faction, and 5gp - 

Factions here are: 

CircleofUnseenHands worse, 
MerchantsofQeynos worse,
CorruptQeynosGuards better, 
GuardsofQeynos worse, 
KaneBayle better 

Funny thing is other than the KaneBayle faction getting better these are opposite the faction adjustments you get for completing the earlier parts of the quest.
