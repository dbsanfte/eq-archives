Kael Warrior Armor
This quest was submitted by Corbokk. It begins in past King Tormax's area in Kael Drakken. It will require a ton of faction work to gain passage here.

You say, 'hail Kragek Thunderforge'

Kragek Thunderforge says 'Greetings to you.  I seek the service of a powerful warrior.  Are you a warrior? If not then be gone.

You say, 'I am a warrior'

Kragek Thunderforge says 'Excellent.  I see that you are a seasoned veteran of many battles.  I admire your strength and ferocity.  Life is but a series of battles, is it not?

You say, 'It is a series of battles'

Kragek Thunderforge says 'We share the same view then.  I wish to test your skills in battle.  Not with me of course, for I will destroy you where you stand.  Instead I wish for you to retrieve some trinkets for me and once I have them I will reward you handsomely with a helm, a breastplate, armplates, bracers, gauntlets, greaves, or boots.

You say, 'What helm?'

Kragek Thunderforge says 'All I require are an ancient tarnished plate helmet and three crushed cora pieces.  This should be a small task for one such as you.  Go now and I shall await your return.

You say, 'What breastplate?'

Kragek Thunderforge says 'As the resolve of your discipline and strength shall endure through a battle, so shall this breastplate.   All that is required are an ancient tarnished breastplate, and three flawless diamonds.  Do this quickly so that you may return to the field of battle.

You say, 'What armplates?'

Kragek Thunderforge says 'So, a pair of armplates, is what you require?  Well, I require ancient tarnished plate vambraces, and three flawed emeralds before you may receive it.  May your deeds be spread throughout the lands!

You say, 'What bracer?'

Kragek Thunderforge says 'Bracers for the mighty?  Retrieve these components and I shall forge the item for you.  Bring me ancient tarnished plate bracers and three crushed flame emeralds.

You say, 'What gauntlets?'

Kragek Thunderforge says 'Mighty gauntlets to aid in obliterating your foes, eh?  It is no easy task but I shall require a pair of ancient tarnished plate gauntlets and three crushed topazes.  Hurry along, Ogre.

You say, 'What greaves?'

Kragek Thunderforge says 'Strength and balance are important, are they not?  I shall help give you an advantage with these greaves.  Gather three flawed sea sapphires and ancient tarnished plate greaves.

You say, 'What boots?'

Kragek Thunderforge says 'A pair of boots you shall have once you have acquired a pair of ancient tarnished plate boots and three crushed black marbles.
