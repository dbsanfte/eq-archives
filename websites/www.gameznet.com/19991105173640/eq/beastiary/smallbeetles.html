Everquest Beastiary Guide: Small Beetles

Small beetles are found throughout Norrath. Every newbie zone has just about one type of variant or another of this mob. Small beetles include the popular Fire Beetles, Klikniks/Klaknaks, Worker Scarabs etc. While the appearance of these different mobs can vary greatly they are all of identical level(level 2). These are a popular mob where ever they are located for a couple of reasons.

First off they are often the most common level 2 mob in most newbie zones. There are also spiderlings running around usually but they are vary between level 1-3 and are usually a tougher fight than their beetle counterparts.

Secondly the beetles all carry fire beetle eyes an other body parts. The eyes sell best in stores at a bit over 2 silver pieces each. More importantly however they make a great lightsource for human eyes(humans, erudites, and barbarians). While a human race can buy a torch or a lantern for relatively little money those items must be held in their off hand preventing the player from using a shield or a two handed weapon. A fire beetle eye can be stored in your inventory and still give off light so long as it is not stored within a backpack or sack. This will be the primary lightsource for most humans until they are able to attain a lightstne or a weapon or shield which casts light(such as the screaming mace).

Beetle slaying is not without it's share of danger however. Beetles are social creatures and will attack you if they see you fighting one of their kind. It is important to note that although there may be kliks or klaks in the area they are still the same type beetle as a fire beetle and though the name varies they will still assist one another. Most dangerous however is the queen klik or klaks. Once in a while a queen will spawn which is level 5-7 or so and it will wreak havoc on anyone who happens to be doing battle with any beetles. So be careful, use your external view when fighting, and if someone shouts queen it may be wise to run to the guards.
