October 4th --

There is a new weapon that holds the title of "best weapon in the game". This one was granted in a GM run quest that took place on several servers this weekend. It is Cleric and Paladin only and is entitled the Butcherblock Hammer. This fine weapon not only sports the best damage:delay ratio of any weapon currently in the game but also procs superior heal. In that same event another new weapon entitled the red bladed short sword also appeared, but this one was less impressive.

I posted screenshots of the two weapons as well as a few other items including one of the quest items from the Temple of Solusek Ro(a magician only head piece entitled the circlet of mist).
-J.C.

New/Updated Equipment: Butcherblock Hammer, Red Bladed Short Sword, Circlet of Mist, Vermiculated Tunic, Crown of Leaves.
