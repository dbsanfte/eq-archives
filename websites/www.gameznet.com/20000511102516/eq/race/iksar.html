Race Guide - Iksar
It's been a very long time since I have written any race guides. This is not so much of a race guide as a recommendation of starting stats. I have listed each Iksar playable classes starting stats and the stats which I personally recommend that you if you choose to play one. Keep in mind that everyone has their own personal preferences when creating a class. I am not saying that these are bar none the definitive starting stats for the Iksar race. These are simply the stats which I personally would start off with.

MNK STR STA AGI DEX WIS INT CHA
Start 75 75 100 95 80 75 55
Reco 95 75 100 95 80 75 55

Many monks would tell you to pump your points into agility or dexterity rather than strength. However it has become apparent that dexterity does not do much for a monk. Dexterity is primarily beneficial for procing weapons and there simply are not any for a monk. That leaves agility or strength. In my opinion strength is not only more important but is harder to raise via equipment.

NEC STR STA AGI DEX WIS INT CHA
Start 70 70 90 95 80 85 55
Reco 70 70 95 95 80 110 55

Some would tell you that dexterity helps you to regain your concentration. I however have never found that to be the case. I could be wrong but I personally would rather put those points into agility. Higher agility means you will get hit less often. Max your intelligence(25 points) and put the remaining 5 into agility.

SHD STR STA AGI DEX WIS INT CHA
Start 80 75 90 85 80 85 60
Reco 90 75 90 85 80 95 60

This is a touchy one. In my opinion shadowknights are 75% warrior and 25% necromancer. However it has become painfully apparent to many shadowknights that when we reach the higher levels are mana pool is not that large. It also appears that your base intelligence affects mana more than the intelligence which is added by items. I am split on this one. I personally would say put 10 points into both strength and intelligence. It would be hard to argue against putting all twenty points into either intelligence or strength however.

SHM STR STA AGI DEX WIS INT CHA
Start 70 75 90 85 90 75 60
Reco 70 75 95 85 115 75 60

Wisdom is of course the most important stat for a shaman. I recommend you max it out(25 points) and put the remaining 5 into either agility(to avoid taking damage) or strength. I personally feel that agility is more important but strength can help at lower levels.

WAR STR STA AGI DEX WIS INT CHA
Start 80 80 95 85 80 75 55
Reco 105 80 95 85 80 75 55

Iksar warriors only receive 25 points to distribute. I would recommend throwing all 25 into strength. Strength is bar none the most important skill for a melee type in my opinion. Dexterity is good for procs and agility helps in avoiding damage. However agility and dexterity items are easy to come by where strength items are not.
