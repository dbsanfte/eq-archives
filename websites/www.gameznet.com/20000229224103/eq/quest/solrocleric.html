Cleric Temple Quests
This quest was submitted by Jarrane Zexus:

Boots of the Reliant, for this quest, you have to fing the note of Turgan the Reliant in his burnt house in the Kithicor woods (loc: +1725, +1125) itès a 7 hours item on the floor spawn! When you'll have it, read it, it will guide you to the Ingot of the Reliant (the ingot is on a tree slump in Kithicor forest at -80, +3200) Then, deliver the "note" to Turgant himself, he lives now in Lake Rathetear, near the gnoll/undead camp (to the left) he will give you his Icon of the Reliant... bring the ingot and icon of the Reliant plus an enchanted platinum bar, and you will recieve the Boots of the Reliant!

Greaves of the Penitent, for this quest, you will have to find the Ingot of the Penitent in Upper Guk... in the room of the Scyer (the exact place qhere the ingot is, there is 2 waterfalls in this room, face the scyer spawn, in the right water fall, there it is) After getting the Ingot... got to Kerra Island, there's a little erudite's island in the lake of the Kerra, thats where Maugarim spawns, you'll have to kill her... she drops the Icon of the Penitent almost everytime, and she spawns randomly. Bring the Ingot and the Icon of the Penitent plus 1 magnetized platinum bar and you'll recieve the Greaves of the penitent!

Chestplate of the Constant, for the chestplate, you have to kill 2 goblins, one in Dagnor's Cauldron, and one in Runnyeye! Dagnor's Cauldron one is named Blige, i heard rumors that he's roaming somewhere in Dagnor... don't know where though (sorry, didn't do this quest). The goblin you have to kill in Runnyeye is named Sludge, he's in the "star-shaped" fountain near the Goblin King... Bring the Ingot and the Icon of the Constant plus 2 galvanized platinum bars and you'll recieve the Chestplate of the Constant!

Sleeves of the Fervent, ok, for this quest, you have to find Jovan in High Keeo... he's roaming around King's court, well, Hail him, he will say he haves big head atches, he needs a Tumpy Tonic (can be made by a good Brewer). Give him the Tumpy Tonic and he will give you the Icon of the Fervent... then... you have to go in OOT, on the Aviak island (loc +2000 , +4000) there's an Aviak named Gull Skytalon who's roaming on the island... i'm not sure what to do with him (Hail him, or kill him) but a friend of mine killed him twice and he dropped nothing... he will give you the Ingot of the Fervent... Bring the Ingot and the Icon of the Fervent plus 1 vulcanized platinum bar and you'll recieve the Sleeves of the Fervent!

Gloves of the Ardent, First of all, you have to go in Everfrost, go under the ice of the Frozen river (you'll need ENDURING BREATH for this one) The Ingot of the Ardent is somewhere underwater! Then, the 2nd part of the quest, you have to kill Babbinsbort in Befallen... he's a VERY rare spawn on the third floor (note: he's not on the same side of the Shadow knight that drops the BBC) Babbinsbort is a Gnome, Necromancer, kill him and you will get the Icon of the Ardent... Bring the Ingot and the Icon of the Ardent plus 1 galvanized platinum bar and you'll recieve the Gloves of the Ardent!

The following information was submitted by Dolmant Darkspirit. It explains how to get the various metal types and the bars you need for the pieces whose description is missing from our page...

Bracers = 2 bars enchanted  platinum
Shield = 2  bars vulcanized platinum
Caduceus = 2 bars magnatized platinum

To get the galvanized, magnatized, or vulcanized bars you give a bar of enchanted platinum to the following people and they will return to you a bar of the appropriate type:

Ostrum = Magnatized
Villisia = Vulcanized
Genni = Galvanized
