Kedge Keep: The high level zone that never was.
Kedge Keep is the pure underwater zone located in Dagnor's Cauldron, under the water near The Estate of Unrest. The zone is very amazing in the design, and gives good experiance for the level monsters that are there. Why is it always empty? Few reasons, one the zone has pretty much no items. Zip zero nada. The main item you will even need to get into the zone (fish bone earring) is a barbarian that spawns ever 6 hours, meaning it will take a long time before the players that need the item to get it. With zones like Nagafen's lair and The Ruins of old Guk being overflowed with people, and the number of level 50s growing with each passing day, it is urgent that this zone be itemized, combat tested, and re-released. Also to ensure that this zone would start getting players, fish bone earrings would a) need to be a quest. or b) have the npc spawn more often. It is a shame that the only underwater zone in the whole game is empty, and is the only high level zone that is empty. With the Plane of Fear pretty much unstable, and the Hate and Air planes still a dream, I think that Kedge Keep is a much needed zone. If they re-did Kedge Keep I think that people would start seeing the other zones narrowing down, and then getting to the numbers that they were ment to be. This zone will someday be enjoyed by others, but for now, it remains what it always has been, resting at the bottem of the ocean.

-Welor
