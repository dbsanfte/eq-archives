Everquest Quest Guide: High Elf Paladin Newbie Quest

The following Quest was submited by Talisan

This quest you get from the High Elf Paladin guild master in Felwithe. It is the first quest he gives to young Paladins. He asks you to go speak to a fallen paladin by the name of Tandan Nybright. He can be found in the Kelethin by the jewelry shop named a Drunk. To get him to talk to you hand him a bottle of Elven wine and then ask about his [Shocking Experience]. He will then ask you to kill off the Nybright bandits(10-13thlvl) in Lesser Faydark and return the necklesses they wear. You need one of each neckless and if you get a double it will disapear from your pack. Once you return the necklesses to the Drunk he gives you his old shield and a faction adjustment. The shield is a plain old buckler worth about 2gp.
