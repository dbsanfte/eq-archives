Everquest Zone Guide: Neriak

Neriak is the home of the dark elves. This is an absoleutely huge city that spreads accross four seperate zones. The exit lies in the foreign quarter and exits to the Nektulos Forest.

This is an important city for several reasons. First off this is the only dark elf spellcasters will be able to train their skills or buy new spells as other races will not deal with them. Secondly it is one of three cities that ogres and trolls can be bound to. Ogres and trolls can likewise train their skills here in the foreign quarter.
