05/25/2004

GameZNet and Spoilers Network Merge
 4:08 EST - J.C. Smith

For those of you who are visiting this site from the Gameznet.com URL, you probably just think this is another day. For those accessing this web site from EQSpoilers.com though, you may be wondering what happened. We have made the decision to merge EQ'Lizer and EQ Spoilers in to a single site. The Spoilers Network is now a part of the Gameznet Network. For a few days things are going to be a little herky jerky around here as we get everything working together properly and all of the searches set up. We will also be opening the site up to non-subscribers, and this transition is underway. We have not announced any of this publicly, except on this web site as the sites are not yet fully functional.

So what does this change mean to EQ'Lizer readers? It means that there will once again be an extensive quest and bestiary database at EQ'Lizer. It also means a completely new faction and trade skill database for the site. The zone guides and maps will also be receiving updates as part of the merger. There will be some new class guides which previously were only available at EQ Spoilers.

EQ Spoilers readers will also see some big changes from this change. A new comments database will be available. The items database now supports multiple mobs dropping an item and multiple zones and instances of like named items. A slew of new class, zone and mob guides will also become available. The spell database has been upgraded also. The equipment database will now allow you to search specific amounts of increases or decreases on items. For example Str >15.

This is a huge undertaking, so expect this all to take a few days to get everything back running smoothly. You will notice the new databases are available currently for searching under databases. There are still a ton of files to convert over to the new formats and such. Right now things are a little mix and matched here. We have a new site design coming soon, and until then expect to see some little inconsistencies here or there as we get everything running. You can also expect to see a lot of reorganization of data. For example the old EQ'Lizer rare spawn, ultra spawn, etc guides will be integrated in to the new bestiary, and the class guides will be integrated from one another, etc. Please bear with us, and I promise that a week from now you will all be very happy with the results.

New Equipment: Runed Flesh-Banded Leggings ~ Cranial Bludgeoner ~ Onyx Ring of Prayer ~ Rageshale of Bolstering ~ Deathcaller's Stonewind ~ Jagged Onyx Staff ~ Jointed Scale Greaves ~ Shale Shoulderpads ~ Deathwrap of Rites ~ Darkglint Blade ~ Bloodlink Chestmail ~ Aegis of Blackscales ~ Spiritstone of Life ~ Bracelet of the Fearsome ~ Gem-Inlaid Ritual Cloak ~ Ring of Brutish Beasts ~ Resonating Earring of Battle ~ Glyphed Prayer Ring ~ Sharp Irestone Fragment ~ Bloodied Ritual Trinket ~ Bauble of the Forlorn ~ Veil of Entrancement ~ Tome of Tainted Axe of Hatred ~ Stone-Worked Pauldrons ~ Glyph-Lined Vrex Cloak ~ Darkened Coldstone ~ Blazing Stone of Demise ~ Robe of Destruction.
