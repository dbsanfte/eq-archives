Everquest Zone Guide: Butcherblock Mountains

Click Here For A Map

The Butcherblock mountains are on the outskirts of the dwarven city of Kaladim. Butcherblock also leads to the zones of Dagnor's Cauldron and Greater Faydark. Butcherblock is also the home of the port that connects this island to the rest of the world. The boat arrives every 40 minutes and sets sail for Freeport.

This zone has plenty of things for a character level 1-12 to hunt. You will find skeletons, scarabs, emerald drakes, goblins, snakes, and large spiders roaming much of the countryside. There are also a few notable camps in Butcherblock.

If you were entering from the Greater Faydark zone and took a right you could follow the zone to the chessboard. At this chessboard you will find undead pawns. This is a favorite location of characters level 9-12. Occasionally an undead rook or knight etc will appear and these will keep chessboard campers on their toes.

If you are coming from the docks you will notice a valley to your right with a tombstone looking object in front of it. Further back in that direction you will come accross what is known as the graveyard. This area contains hordes of skeletons and is a favorite stomping ground of clerics. Also in this general area is a dwarven bandit camp.

There is a camp of orc soothsayers in a valley to the right of the crossroads. Soothsayers are a favorite target among low teen players.

There are two dangers to lower level characters in this zone. First is the orc runner who is part of a quest which takes place in Felwithe. The orc runner will run along the road beginning at the greater faydark border and leading to the docks. He is aggressive and is a level 9 or so beast.

Of greater danger are the two ogre's. These ogre's are very dangerous to low level characters. They are also part of a quest.
