Mantle of Souls
This quest was submitted by: Dreaded DemiseBringer

Hail Ernax the Scholar on the first floor of the library in Paineel. He wll say leave him be because he his reading about mana storage devices.

Say to him 'I wish to speak to you on a matter of the soul', and he then says he hasn't heard anyone say that in a while and is surprised that you want to follow the legacy of azraxs. He then gives you a letter and tells you to follow and write down the instructions on it and then give the letter back to him. 

Give the letter back to Ernax and you get a Soul Trap Box, a 4 slot container with a combine button.  The letter explains how to make a Soul Trap. The needed components are a bloody mantle, the heart of a druid of reknown, and a crystal of capturing. These items need to be combined in a Soul Trap Box (which he gives you.) The letter also said that the completed Soul Trap needs to be given to the soul you wish to manipulate. 

The bloody mantle comes from the dark assassin, which is a level 50 mob that randomly spawns from Faydwer to the Karanas, to Everfrost.  The druid of reknown is Alicia Peaceheart in Gfaydark, who drops a heart when killed. The crystal of capturing is made by Jalen Goldsinger the Tox forest.

Jalen says he needs a Potion of Sorrow and 1000 gp to make a crystal  for you.   The potion is obtained from Gorth Bearsoul in Halas for 500 gp.  Combine these items in the box and give it back to Ernax and you will receive a Mantle of Souls. 

Additional Information 

Information on the Dark Assassin 

Level 50 Mob. Hits for 140s, and backstabs for 450s. He will not taunt off of you so whoever casts on it is the person who better have fresh SoW and ready to run in circles around around those who are casting (heal will taunt him off of you I found out). Myself (51 Necro), another 52 Necro and a 53 Wizard dispatched this easily. He resists a lot, and has a lot of hitpoints. Best thing is to SoW a couple necro pets and get a wizzie to nuke. He dropped the bloody mantle. A decent item in itself, -5 cha and -3 to ac, adding 5 to dexterity and strenght fir shoulder slot, wearable by many classes.

Information on the Crystal

In order to get the crystal, you need to have a potion of sorrows (bought from Gorth Bearsoul) This person will KoS erudite necros from level 1 with base faction, so buying the potion yourself is not an option Best to get a barbarian to buy it and have him hand in the potion and you the other 1000gp for the crystal. Make sure you are standing closest to the Mob, and you initiate the trade.

Information on the Heart of a Druid Reknown

She runs around NW area of Gfay using SoW. She runs kinda a triangular circuit, over and over. Some loc's she ran by were 1200, 1200. and 1400, 1100. Rare spawn from what Ive found, and may have an unknown trigger.
