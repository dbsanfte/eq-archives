Btw, just a tidbit of info for people that like to listen to music while playing EQ, winamp has a new plugin called sneaky amp. It monitors keyboard strokes and allows you to change songs, volume and pretty much do anything with winamp that you can do on the face, by only typing in commands. This works with while in EQ and is totally undetectable by everquest or anyone else, so you don't have to worry about them trying to say you are cheating! =)

Also, for those guilds unfortunate enough to have never heard of Roger Wilco, I suggest that you check out http://www.rogerwilco.com and download this program. It's totally free and it allows many many people to connect in a sort of chat room and talk, with a microphone and your basic speakers so that you can communicate with anyone else that is in the channel, my guild recently started using this and it seems to work great! I've had up to 20 people in one chat session and no one says you can't do more. This also works while in the game!

Enjoy!

Foramen Magnum of Black Company, 53rd Druid, Tarew Marr
