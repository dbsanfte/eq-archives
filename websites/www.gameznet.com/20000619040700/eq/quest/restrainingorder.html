Restraining Order

An involved quest, you begin by going to the Tink N Babble in East Cabilis which is near the Temple of Terror. Go inside and upstairs. In the corner during the day is a Clerk Doval. You can hail him and find out more, but if you simply say to him that you [will deliver the restraining order] he will give you a legion order and tell you to have ten Troopers sign it... in alphabetical order. Simply select them and say [sign the restraining order]. Five of these Troopers will sign it for nothing, but the other five will require you to give them something else when you hand them the Order for them to sign it. Make SURE you get the right things or they will destroy the Order and you will have to start over.

Here is the order you need to have them sign, their (relative) locations and what they'll want, if anything, to get you to sign.

Frogzin: Lake of Ill Omen, at the front gate.

Fryp: Half hiding (glitched?) in a wall near the Warriors Guild.

Gummin: Field of Bone (within the ruins). Wants Legion Lager. (1)

Inkin: Swamp of No, at the front gate. Skipping Stone (2)

Nishnish: Swamp of No, in the Cave before the gate. Two Baraccuda Teeth. (3)

Ogmire: Lake of Ill Omen, in the Cave before the gate.

Ozlot: Warslilk Woods, front gate or cave. Two Sabretooth Kitten Teeth. (4)

Roklon: Warslilk Woods, on the path far from gate. Gobblin Scout Beeds. (5)

Selbat: Lake of Ill Omen, (within the ruins).

Taer: Field of Bone, at the gate or on the path... usually.

(1) Legion Lager, you have to make yourself. You need a Bottle, Deathbone Barley, a Giant Blood Sac and some Bloodwater. I know you can buy the Bottle and the Bloodwater, you might be able to buy the Deathbone Barley somewhere,

but I Foraged for it in the Field of Bone. The Blood Sac is on the Giant Leeches in the Swamp. You then combine them back in the little room at the Tink N Babble (or any Still) but be warned, it CAN fail so get a few extra Sac's while you're at it. (Which are used for other quests too.)

(2) Skipping Stone can be found on Frogie Skipper. They're NOT NODROP so

sometimes people will sell them around town.

(3) Baraccuda Teeth can be found in East Cabilis' waterway. If you're having problems finding the pygmy or regular Baraccuda's... if you head to sewers (under the glitched boat) they're usually in those waterways.

(4) Sabretooth Kitten Teeth can be found in the Lake of Ill Omen on Sabretooth Kittens.

(5) Also in the Lake, are the Goblin Scouts who drop their Beeds.

After you've colleced everything and gotten them all to sign in order, you can take the Order back to the Clerk and he'll give you some Exp (as do the 5 Troopers who require things to sign... at level 6, the entire quest gave me one bubble) and some money and a book from his private library. The Code of Combat. I do not yet know what it's for, but it's NODROP so it's probably Quest related.
