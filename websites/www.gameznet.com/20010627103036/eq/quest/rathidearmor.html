Rat Hide Armor Quest
There is a wood elf that I found out in the marketplace near the Lion's
Mane in Qeynos, but I do not remember his name.  He is, however, the only wood elf in the marketplace, and he moves around through out the day, commuting around to the other markets in town, and at one point he is found hanging around by the docks.
   
For 3 rat pelts and 4 gold, he makes a Rat Pelt Cape, which is .5 weight, AC:1, and +2 Save vs. Cold.

For 3 snake fangs and 3 gold, he makes a Snake Fang Necklace that is no armor, but +2 Save vs. Poison.

For 3 pieces of rat fur and 3 gold, he makes a Rat Skin Cap, that is almost identical to a cloth cap except that it only give 1 armor.

For 1 rats foot, 1 rat whisker, and 1 honey mead, he makes a Lucky Rats Foot Necklace that is no armor, -1 Charisma, and +2 hit points.
   
All of these items are wearable by all classes and races.  Each of these quests give you experience and a faction increase with CitizenQeynos.  None of them sell for more than a silver, except for the cape, which will sells for about 2 silver.  So unless you just want faction increase, these are kind of a waste of time for anyone but a newbie.
