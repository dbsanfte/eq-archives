Monday, Monday Posted Monday, November 27, 2000 by jcsmith
As Velious draws closer the excitement begins to build. It also becomes apparent that we need to explain the way that our submission process works before the madness of expansion hits us.

First and foremost if you submit something and your email is returned with an error then please ignore it. Our staff is on a mailing list. Each mail is sent out to every member of the staff. On heavy submission days some staff members may be away and not able to check their mails. Their quota may become exceeded which will result in an error being returned to you. Please do not resend the submission. Every other member of the list has already received it. What happens here is that on days where traffic is already in large amounts it becomes compounded by half of our submissions being resent over and over again.

Secondly if you submit an item or quest and it is not posted immediately don't despair. I personally have three differnet systems which I update from. Some times I will download my email to one system then I won't be back to that location for a few days. As a result it is not uncommon for submissions to go a few days before posting. Items which are already cropped and ready to go are usually posted first during times of heavy submissions as they are the easiest to get to. If a week or more goes by and your submission is still not posted then it may have been lost in the shuffle and feel free to re-submit.

Enough of that, to the update!
