Everquest Zone Guide: Lake Rathetear

Lake Rathetear connects the Rathe Mountains to South Karana. It also leads to the special zone of Arena. It is mostly a waypoint for the evil races of Southern Antonica to reach the western cities and dungeons. However there are several interesting opponents here. Beneath the waves you will not only find sharks but a level 53 sea monster which will make short work of all but the mightiest of adventurers. There are several bandit camps, an orc camp, a gnoll camp, a skeleton rune and the home of some aviaks.
