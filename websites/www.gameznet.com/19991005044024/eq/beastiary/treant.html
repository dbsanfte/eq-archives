Everquest Beastiary Guide: Treant


Treants are a species of living trees that range in the mid-20's level wise. They are sort of nature's druids. They are generally friendly to most races although they dislike evil races or those whose aim is to destroy the forest. Treants can cast a variety of druid spells which can make them a formidable adversary. Some of these spells include a thornskin spell which will cause damage to any who hit it, stinging swarm a Duration of Time spell that can kill you even after the treant is dead, and lightning.

Treants can be found in the outdoor zones of South Karana and North Karana(where the head treant hangs his hat).
