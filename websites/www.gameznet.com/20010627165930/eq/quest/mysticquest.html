Mystic Cloak Quest
This quest was submitted by Timon of Fennin Ro.

The quest begins in Najena. In one of the jail cells near Rathyl there is a high elf enchanter named Linara Parlone. If you hail her she will tell you how she was locked up and that a mob called Priest of Najena has the key but left to an estate, Unrest. The Priest of Najena looks like a Ghoul and is at least lvl 29. If you kill it you will get a No Drop key.

Give this key to Linara in Najena and she will give you a note to give to her father in Felwithe. He is near the lake. If you give him the note he will then give you a Worn Cloak, ac5, and tell you to give it to his wife in Erudin Palace to have the cloak's "powers" restored. His wife is in the Enchanter Guild section of Erudin. If you give her the cloak she will restore its power turning it into a cloak, named Mystic Cloak, which stats are Lore Item, No Drop, ac8, int+5, sv magic+10, 10 charges of Steelskin, 0.5 weight.

Class wearable by NEC, WIZ, MAG, and ENC.
Races are HUM, ERU, HIE, DEF, and GNM.
