Everquest Zone Guide: Ocean Of Tears

The Ocean of Tears is a zone which you will cross on the boat ride from Butcherblock to Freeport. This zone is not devoid of beings however there are three seperate islands where you will find alluring sirens, bucanneers and bandits, a hill giant, aquagoblins and sharks.
