Geozite Tool
This quest was user-submitted by Trana.

In the warriors guild at the very top you ask the NPC that warriors give their initial note too (Drill Master Vygan) “What is Geozite Tool“. He tells you information about it prompting you to ask “I want a Geozite Tool”.  He tells you that if you want a Geozite Tool to put two large scorpion pincers into a bag (which he supplies) and combine them, returning the bag to him afterwards. Upon completing this you are given a note to give to one of the 3 Warlords in the adjacent zones. The information he gives you when you turn in the bag with the pincers should be enough to conclude which Warlord you need to talk to.  There are 3 different quests that can be given to obtain the Geozite Tool.

1.)              Give you a note to give to the Warlord in the Field of Bone.  He gives you a box, to put four charred bones from militia skeletons which spawn approximately just north of the Entrance to Abandoned Crypt (and only at night).  Given back to him he gives the Geozite Tool.

2.)              Give you a note to give to the Warlord in the Swamp of No Hope.  You are asked to place 8 shackles from Escaped Frogloks Slaves in a container (he gives you) and return to him after combining.  The Froglok Slaves you are searching for seem to be seen the most on the first island you come to as you follow the path out of the buildings and to the right heading south.  Once you return the combined container to the Warlord he gives you the Geozite Tool.

Give you a note to give to the Warlord in Warsliks Woods. You are given a book and told 4 poems have been stolen from him and he will give you the Geozite Tool if you help him get the poems back. You are searching for a Froglok Thief that spawns in a clearing just over the first hill to the left of the buildings but before the lake. Combine the 4 poems in the book he gave you and return to him for the Geozite Tool.
