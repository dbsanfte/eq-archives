VAULT NETWORK SEARCHES FOR LOGO

As the Vault Network has continued to expand over the past year, one thing we've felt was lacking was a consistent symbol to identify sites in the Vault Network, and also to use as our official logo on our main web site, business cards, t-shirts, and the like. With E3 fast approaching, we thought it would be fun to run a contest and see if any of you talented artists out there could come up with something we could proudly use as the logo for the Vault Network.

Since this could be a complicated and time-consuming endeavor, we decided that there's got to be some sort of an incentive to get people to submit entries. Thus, I'm pleased to announce that there will be a $200 cash prize if we pick your composition to use as our logo, which would become property of the Vault Network. Of course, the Vault Network reserves the right to discard all entries if we don't find one that we feel is what we're looking for.

Some brief guidelines - The logo should contain either the words "Vault Network" or a stylized "VN". It might also have some sort of imagery to convey the feeling of what we do, which is provide coverage of RPGs and massively multiplayer virtual worlds. Elegant simplicity is best. The logo should be either square or rectangular, with no more than a 2:1 horizontal aspect. Finally, the logo should be submitted in a high-resolution Adobe .PDF format. Multiple submissions from the same author are accepted.

Submissions should be sent to vn logo@vaultnetwork.com by the end of next week.

Eric Neuman Managing Editor The Vault Network http://www.vaultnetwork.com
