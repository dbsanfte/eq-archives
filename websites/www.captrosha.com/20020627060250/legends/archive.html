July 31st  Membership Information!
Legends of the Realm is currently not accepting any new membership applications.  We have recently grown fairly large recently and need to take time to get to know the newer members before accepting any others into our guild.  If you are interested in joining Legends, the best advice I can provide is to simply group with as many Legends members as often as you are able.  This will greatly expedite the process should we once again begin accepting new members.  If you are not currently aligned with the Claws of Veeshan faction, that is something else you should consider working on before attempting to join Legends.  We are Pro-Dragon aligned and actively hunting in areas where it is imperative you be at least dubious to CoV faction.
