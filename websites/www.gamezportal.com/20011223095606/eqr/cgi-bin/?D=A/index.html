Four pages have been created for your convenience in the current directory (/usr/local/apache/sites/gamezportal.com/htdocs/eqr/cgi-bin).

Guests should be linked to eqitems_form.html to search your database

Administrators should go to eqitems_admin.html to add, modify, and remove records, import and export, modify fields, and add members.

Members with accounts can go to eqitems_members.html to add and modify only their own records.

If you choose to allow instant members (admin preferences) you can direct new members to eqitems_instantmember.html.
