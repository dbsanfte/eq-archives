Guild Charter of Draco Nobilis

We are a good guild created by a large group of friends wanting to promote fun and goodwill amongst the peoples of Norrath through fair game play, honest trade, helping one another and traveling together.

Guide Lines:

1.

Violating the "Terms of Service" agreement is not acceptable. Those who violate them will be removed from the guild.

2.

We are a very family oriented group. As such, please conduct yourself honorably at all times, taking into consideration the youths in our group. Don't use profanity or language with sexual overtones in the guild channel.
3.

The guild meets once every 2 weeks as a group to perform promotions, give awards and distribute information. Please attempt to make the meetings and we will attempt to keep them a manageable duration.

4.

When you become an officer, you become a servant of the guild and are there to serve, not to be served.

5.

If you come across someone that you think might need help ... ASK first! We do NOT kill steal and they might see your unrequested assistance as such.

6.

Recruits are bound by a 2 to 4 week probationary period. This can be extended or decreased depending on the circumstances.

7.

Recruits are limited to one guild tagged character period. After probationary period, Alts. maybe tagged.

8.

To become a hero to the Guild you must Train Zaxon back to lvl 40

9.

Unless you are sponsored by a existing guild member, please leave a post with , in-game name , lvl, class, and a brief statement on why you would like to join, and if you want about yourself.

10.

I am sorry about this next post but it seems to be needed. WE ARE NOT HERE TO POWERLEVEL YOU, EQUIP YOU, OR BECOME YOUR PERSONAL SLAVE.

This will be revised a lot.
