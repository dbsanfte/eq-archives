    ALCHEMY

    --Mimsa Starphire--High Priestess of The Tribunal of the Six Hammers Warder of Black Tower Xegony

    Alchemy is the art of making potions, available to shamans at L25. The intelligence stat may improve the rate of success. After training in Alchemy, potions can be made by placing the components in a medicine bag (available at the shaman’s guild for about 2p) and clicking the COMBINE button. Lesser (or minor) potions have a shorter duration than their regular or greater equivalents. Potion effects will not stack with each other; however, each seems to stack with regular buffs, and a few potions interact provided stats do not overlap. Drinking another identical potion will cancel the effects of the previous. Potions cannot be used while moving. Unlike the shrink spell, the shrink potion can be used outdoors. Other potions have the same limitations as the spell; i.e., SoW can only be used outdoors. Potions can be combined into a multi-dose potion by combining single-charge potions in a bearskin potion bag (made with tailoring skill, trivial at 80-90). Two spiderling silks combined in a sewing kit make a silk string; three silk strings combined in a sewing kit make a silk cord. Combine one grizzly bear skin (WK or NK; rare drop) and one silk cord in a tailoring kit to make a bearskin potion bag. The bag will only combine 5 or 10 identical potions. Trying to combine different potions or a quantity other than 5 or 10 will result in the loss of all potions in the bag. No skill is needed for combining.
