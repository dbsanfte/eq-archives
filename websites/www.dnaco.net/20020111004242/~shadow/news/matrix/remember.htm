****MATRIX SPEED REMINDER****

Just a reminder, when you go into a matrix host, don't zip along commands like nobody's business.

1) You could trigger something. MJudges can get in there and unlock your deck and get things set up without you even knowing you triggered, but if you slam off commands left and right, the mjudges
can't do that.

2) Sometimes a judge will be following you. MJudges will follow you from place to place, and then spring surprises on you when you're least expecting it. However, if you hop into one host, then into the
next, you might trigger something and miss it.

So, when you hop into a host make sure you take time to read the
description, and pose your system commands as well. Always pose. A good 10 to 15 second wait after a command can't hurt and it'll increase RP potential.
