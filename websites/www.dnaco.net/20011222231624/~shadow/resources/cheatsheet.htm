MUX Commands Cheat Sheet
  
 

Here's a quick reference for some of the commands you'll most commonly use on the MUX, and how they work. This is intended for new players who aren't familiar with the MUX setup.

IC and Roleplay Commands

What you want to do, or see on the screen

What you type

"Hi, I'm James Doe."

"Hi, I'm James Doe.

James Doe waves hello!

:waves hello!

James Doe's hair is strawberry blonde.

;'s hair is strawberry blonde.

To look at the items you own:
With database numbers:

i
invnum

To look at your character object
(warning, do NOT do this unless you know what you're looking for or have a reason to do so… it's huge and spammy.)

Ex me

To look at everyone in a room and get a short description

+scan

To look at johndoe

l johndoe

To make an ooc comment

+ooc This is an OOC comment.
(note: if you type +ooc by itself you'll wind up in the OOC room)

To move in a direction on/in a vehicle

Drive direction (i.e. west is "drive w")

To look outside your vehicle

Scan

To see who's on

+who or +3who (Note: I recommend the latter, less spammy)

To see who's where on the MUX

+where

If you need a staffer to judge something

+judge Reason you need him.

For info on a character

+finger name

For the local weather report

+weather

To enter a vehicle

enter

To leave a vehicle

Leave

To join a place (usually tables and bars in public spots)

Join #1 (or whatever the place # is)

To leave a place

Depart

To speak only to the people at your table

tt I am talking to my tablemates.

To speak another language

+speak Sperethiel=Oh, these breeders DO annoy me.

 

Help and Information Commands

What you want to do, or see on the screen

What you type

For Help files on systemwide commands

Help command

 

 
For Help files on customized commands for the SR MUX +help command
For Help files on local commands on items, like vehicles, weapons, business manager objects, etc. +lhelp command

To look at your character sheet

+sheet

To see who's where on the MUX

+where

To get a list of hangouts that have active PCs inside

+ho

To get a list of staffers who are online

+staff
To get a list of all staffers, online or not +staff/all

 

Communication Commands

What you want to do, or see on the screen

What you type

To send a private message (also called a page) to johndoe:

p johndoe=This is a private message.

To send a private message (also called a page) to johndoe, marysue and janeroe:

p johndoe marysue janeroe=This is a private message.

To use the MUX mailing system to send a mail to johndoe (hit Return at the end of each line):

@mail johndoe=topic

-Body of message.

--
To look at the list of boards on the MUX bulletin board system +bbread
To read the topics of all the messages on a specific board +bbread [board#]
To read a message on the MUX bulletin board (BB system) +bbread [board#]/[message#]
To post a message to the MUX bulletin board (BB system) +bbpost [board#]=Topic
+bb Type your text here.
+bbpost
