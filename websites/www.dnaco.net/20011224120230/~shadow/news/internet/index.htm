In trying to stay up with the times, Shadowrun MUSH has several resources available on the internet.

1) Shadowrun MUSH mailing list That's right, a mailing list for the MUSH. To subscribe follow these simple instructions:

Send email to majordomo@dnaco.net

Include in the body of the message: subscribe shadowrun

Send the message

It's that simple. To send email to the list just send email to shadowrun@dnaco.net. For a web based archive of the mailing list go to http://www.dnaco.net/cgi-bin/lwgate/SHADOWRUN.
