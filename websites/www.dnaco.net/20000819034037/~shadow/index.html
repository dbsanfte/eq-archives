Have you got what it takes, chummer?

The year is 2058, and things have changed. Technology has made humans better than human and magic has returned with a vengance. A place where the world wide computer matrix is real enough to touch back. This is a world of tall corporations with long shadows...

Shadowrun MUX is based on the FASA Role Playing Game of the same name: Where Man Meets Magic And Machine. Players can opt to play any number of different types of characters: we have Mafioso, Yakuza chunin, samurai, mages, riggers, one incredible matrix, rock stars, reporters, gangers, simsense queens and much, much more!
