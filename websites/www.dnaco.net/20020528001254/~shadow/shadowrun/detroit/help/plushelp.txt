& mail
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

-------------------------------------------------------------------------

& bbs
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
<< Displaying help on:  bbs >>
The Bulletin Board System is a way for players to communicate on a 
broadband level. +bblist for a listing of board that are available for 
you to subscribe to. +bbhelp for more help on the Bulletin Boards.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

& roll
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
<< Displaying help on:  +roll >>
Usage: +roll <# of dice>
Rolls <# of dice>. Results are displayed to you and everyone in the room.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

& proll
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
<< Displaying help on:  +proll >>
Usage: +proll <# of dice>
Rolls <# of dice>. Results are displayed privately to you.
+proll <# of dice>=*<name> - Results are displayed to you and <name>.
Used when a judge requests you to roll dice.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

& scan
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
<< Displaying help on:  +scan >>
Usage: +scan
OOC: Scans the current room returning players' short description.
IC:  The result of you glancing around the room you are in.
To set a short description for your character, type:
\&short-desc me=<A brief one or two sentance desc of your character.>
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

& places
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
<< Displaying help on:  places >>
places                    -  Scans the room for places to sit, and
                             shows who is present at that spot.
                             You will commonly find 'places' in bars,
                             clubs, restraunts, etc..

place <#>                 -  Looks at a certain place.

join at #<place number>   -  Sit down at a place.

join with <name>          -  Sit down with <name>.

depart                    -  Stand up.

tt \[;\]\[:\]<blah>           -  Table Talk or Tete et Tete.  Lets you
                             talk/pose to your table.  Those not at
                             your table cannot hear you.

tspeak <language>=<blah>  -  Same as tt, but in a different language.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

& where
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
<< Displaying help on:  +where >>
Displays where players currently are.  With the UNFINDABLE flag set,
this will only show their Region(Downtown, Novi, Southfield).  This
obviously is an OOC command. 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

& nom
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
<< Displaying help on:  +nom >>
Usage: +nom <player>
Nominates a player for karma.  You +nom a player if you feel they showed 
good role playing skills.  The player you are nominating is not notified 
that you did so.  You are only allowed 2 +noms per month, so use them 
wisely.  At the beginning of each new month the results will be tallied
up and +nom winners will be posted.  Two +noms = 1 karma point and NO 
you can't nominate yourself :P
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

& look
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
<< Displaying help on:  +look >>
Usage: +look <name>
Returns the result of you carefully examining or looking someone over.
The player is notified that you are 'staring' at them.  This command
rolls your perception against the conceilablity of the items they are 
carrying + any armor(Secure Jacket, Secure Longcoat, etc..). 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

& lhelp
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
<< Displaying help on:  +lhelp >>
This displays the 'Local Help', if any, in your area.  If you are
ever in doubt about how something works, type +lhelp to see if 
there is help on that topic.  If so, +lhelp <topic> will show you
the helpfile.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

& umd
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
<< Displaying help on:  umd >>
Universal Multi Descer.  This is an easy way for you to set and change
multiple descriptions for your character.  umd help for more info.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

& staff
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
<< Displaying help on:  +staff >>
+staff      will show the staff members currently on.
+staff/all  will show all the staff members.
+staff/last will show all the staff members and the last time they

& ooc
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
<< Displaying help on:  +ooc >>
Typing +ooc will teleport you to the OOC Room.  When you leave the 
OOC Room, either through the exit or by typing +ic, you will be 
teleported back to where you were.  

+ooc <blah> is used when you need to speak out of character. 
The format displayed is: <OOC> Daemon says, "<blah>".
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

& speak
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
<< Displaying help on:  +speak >>
Usage: +speak <language>=<blah>
Allows you to speak in <language>.  All others that can speak <language>
will understand you.  Those that cannot, will just be shown that you 
spoke in <language>.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

& finger
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
<< Displaying help on:  +finger >>
Usage: +finger <player>
Shows information about <player>, such as: alias, race, gender, position,
email address, location(if not set UNFINDABLE), last time on, etc..%r
To set your email address, type:
&email me=<address>
If you don't want your email address to be seen put a > in front of it.%r
To set your position, type:
&position me=<position>%r
To set a unique quote for yourself, type:
&quote me=<quote>%r
NOTE: ALL players must set an email address and be set
      UNFINDABLE.(default)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

& eavesdrop
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
<< Displaying help on:  +eavesdrop check >>
This commads allows you to see if any admins are observing you and the 
room you are in to watch for roleplaying.  This command is in place so 
that if you object to being monitored, you can ask the admin(s) to stop.
Please do NOT expect admins to give you karma just because you are being
monitored.  It may or may not happen.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

                      bgview
                      bgcheck

& bgdata
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
<< Displaying help on:  bgdata >>
Usage: bgdata <text>
This command lets you enter text into your background before it is 
approved.  The text will be entered in order, so start at the beginning.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

& bgview
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
<< Displaying help on:  bgview >>
bgview will display your complete background.
bgview <number> will display the text in a particular slot.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

& bgcheck
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
<< Displaying help on:  bgcheck >>
Checks the current status of your background.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

& bgedit
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
<< Displaying help on:  bgedit >>
Usage: bgedit <slot #>=<current text>//<new text>
This command allows you to edit parts of your background.  It replaces 
<current text> with <new text>.  This command will not work after your 
background has been approved.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

& bgclear
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
<< Displaying help on:  bgclear >>
bgclear will erase all of your background.
bgclear <number> will erase all background info in a certain slot.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

& doors
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
<< Displaying help on:  doors >>
Usage: +knock <door>
Allows you to knock on a door.
                                                                           
Usage: +loudknock <door>
Like +knock, the sound from +loudknock will carry to all adjoining rooms.
                                                                           
Usage: +call <door>=<message>
Your voice will carry through the door.
                                                                           
Usage: +shout <message>
Your voice carries through all adjoining doors.
                                                                    
Usage: +ring <door>
Lets you press the doorbell on a door (If one exsists).
                                                                           
NOTE: If a door is soundproof, any type of knock or shout will not be
      heard on the other side.

& clips
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
<< Displaying help on:  clips >>
                                                                         
Clipcode version 1.1
    For those familiar with Seattle's clips and boxes, the following 
    should be very familiar. Commands are:
                                                                         
moveammo <from dbref>/<to dbref>=<# rounds>
    This moves ammunition from one ammunition container to another. As of
    version 1.1, the ammunition types in these containers must match.
clearammo <dbref>
    This clears all the ammunition from a container. Use with caution.
conceal <dbref>
    This puts a container into concealment.
unconceal <dbref>
    This pulls a container from concealment.
                                                                         
<dbref>:  This parameter is a dbref. The dbref appears in the name of all
          ammunition containers, in brackets, at the end.
