posted by Orion, 6/19/99

So of you might remember this. Some of you won't. But (drum roll please.........)

Street Docs are BACK

Ok Street docs ONLY need a shop to do their work. they can not store cyberware. They can not do bioware. They can not do anything over alpha. They can do cyber surgery, they can heal dying people. Here's how they work.

Street Docs only cost you 30 percent of the normal surgery or medical fees. yes that's right, drastic invasive surgery at a bargain price of 75K!!! There is a catch.

They get a +3 to all tn's for medical stuff, bad conditions and all. Yes, you get a +3tn when rolling recovery from something they did to you, same reason. Yes, street docs can ghoul and re-install used cyber from dead guys. It's second hand, have fun.

And remember, if you read 10/3, what happens should that +3tn keep them from getting any successes on you during the installlation. Let the buyer beware, and the primitive, back alley surgeries begin! Have fun, The Big O.
