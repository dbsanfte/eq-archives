Sign Guestbook:
Please take a moment and fill in the form to the right to sign the Shadowrun Detroit guestbook. Your location and URL are optional and not needed but please fill in all of the other fields to complete the form. Comments are appreciate if they be kind or critical, but please be wise with your choice of words as this will be seen by everyone and you wouldn't want to make a fool of yourself, eh chummer!?..

And for those of you who don't know what a URL is. It's the address for a webpage. If you have a homepage, feel free to edit or fill in the URL field to the right. This will enable users to click on your name in the guestbook and connect to your site. Enjoy it and please refrain from posting any useless or unappropriate URLS as this site is viewed by many different types and ages of people.

Thanks! And for any questions or comments please send feedback to <rle@alder.nesc.epa.gov>.
