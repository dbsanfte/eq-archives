Character Types: Needed and Denied
 
 

 

This information can also be found on the MUX under the +cgneeded and +cgdenied commands!

Needed character types:

Net23 & MetaNite: One PC Metanite reporter slot is available, and PC reporter/cameramen are always in demand. For information contact Raum, Erlkonig, or Enigma.

Gangers: RipperJacks, Ancients, Neon Blades. Please see Eclipse, Savage or Ajax for information.

UG'ers: Trolls, Orks, Dwarfs

Yakuza: Entry level and upper echelon soldiers and a rigger are top priority. A non-pure blooded characters will be considered. Contact Raum or Enigma for info if interested.

Denied character types:

The following character types are prohibited for players to chargen on Shadowrun MUX Seattle:

Any Legal Law Enforcement agents

Officials working for any government body including secret organizations such as Tir Special Forces, UCAS Special Forces, CIA, FBI, ect. If in doubt, contact a RPC, ARPD, or RPD

Employees of megacorporations (except for Media types - see +cgneeded)

Insect shamans/spirits

Other paranaturals

Special metatypes such as those found in Beyond The Shadows (without advance permission from RPC: Magic)

Otaku

Deckers (There's only one mjudge, sorry)
