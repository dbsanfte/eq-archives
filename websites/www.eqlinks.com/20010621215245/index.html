Release of EverQuest Character Status 1.0.0 Full Version

EQGuardians.com is pleased to announce the 1.0.0 Full version release of EverQuest Character Status.  EverQuest Character Status (EQCS) is a first of its kind in EverQuest development. EQCS is an EverQuest utility which will allow players to list their online presence on any listing server (for now EQGuardians.com has the only authorized listing server at http://www.eqguardians.com/charstat/main.pl).  


Happy Questing!

RPGWEAR SHADOWS OF LUCLIN AND SHADOW KNIGHT DESIGNS RELEASED AT FAN FAIRE
 
Minneapolis -- RPGwear released the brand new Shadows of Luclin package art and its Shadow Knight designs, both on black T-shirts, at the exciting Minneapolis Fan Faire. Both are available for immediate purchase from www.RPGwear.com.
 
The Shadows of Luclin package art for the eagerly anticipated EverQuest expansion was provided by Sony Online Entertainment on event T-shirts to attendees of the Minneapolis Fan Faire, and is now available to everyone.
 
The Shadow Knight design features the artwork of the renowned Barry Blair, and is the second in RPGwear's series of T-shirts of EverQuest character classes.
"We plan to finish the series on an accelerated schedule," said Cory Rafiner, president, as fans wanting to honor their favorite class besieged him.
 
An officially licensed provider of EverQuest apparel and accessories, RPGwear provides premium quality products and offers a satisfaction guarantee. With employee characters in the game having earned over 300 levels in combined experience, the RPGwear staff is sensitive to what is expected by EverQuest fans.
