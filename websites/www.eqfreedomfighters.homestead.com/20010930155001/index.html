Sep. 12, 2001
Due to the tragedies of yesterdays Attack on America we will be holding no events or meetings and only making groupings and such as to ease our hearts. We pray for our mates we have no contact with as of yet and those families and children whom are suffering from this atrosity.

Please pray we hear from our family and friends.  Our mates in the armed services, we wish you well and safe in your journey in seeking justice in this day of this most horrible tragedy.

Please everyone be well and safe.
hugs, kisses, & prayers we send out to all.
