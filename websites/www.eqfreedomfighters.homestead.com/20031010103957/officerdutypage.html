Officer Duties
The Council of Officer's is the embodiment of the guild and will make an appearance at most guild events.  The Council will be an open ear to guildmates and help resolve any interguild and intraguild problems.  The Council will interpret the guild rules and have final say in judgement on infractions of those rules.  The Council performs Guildinvites and Officer Promotion as needed.

The Guild Representative is the public relations specialist for the guild.  This person meets with members of other guilds to establish a positive relationship where the guilds can enjoy each others goals and abilities.  The Guild Representative assists the Council in resolveing any interguild problems.  This person will provide advice to the Council, will be an open ear to guildmates, and help resolve intraguild problems. The Guild Representative performs guildinvites as needed.

The Guild Treasurer will maintain the guild bank, which funds all guild raids and rescue missions.  This person is in charge of taking donations and selling items to and for the guild.  This person will organize bank raids as needed.  This person will provide advice to the Guild Leader, will be an open ear to guildmates, and help resolve intraguild problems.  The Guild Treasurer performs Guildinvites as needed.

The Epic Leader helps in researching Epic Quests.  This person will set up and run any raids needed to help a guildmate obtain their Epic Weapon (once guildmate is 3/4 complete).  The Epic Leader will lead other raids as needed.  This person will provide advice to the Council.


The Raid Leader plans and implements guild sponsored raids.  The Raid Leader will post for attendance, assign groups, and lead the raid.  The Raid Leader will confer with the Guild Treasurer for funds to pay for any items and spell components required for a successful raid.  The Raid Leader will provide a write-up of the raid on the message boards detailing the successes.  This person will provide advice to the Council, will be an open ear to guildmates.


The Festival Leader organizes non-raiding events for the guild to enjoy.  This person will provide advice to the Guild Leader, will be an open ear to guildmates, and help resolve intraguild problems.  The Festival Leader performs Guildinvites as needed.

The Recruiting Council makes sure that the guild is made aware of applications posted in Welcome Hall.  Ensures that the recruiting process as outlined in the Guild Rules is followed, and that the process is completed in a timely fashion.  This person will provide advice to the Council, will be an open ear to guildmates, and help resolve intraguild problems.  The Recruiting Council performs Guildinvites as needed.

The Class Leader organizes class specific leaders and assists in class research and the planning and running of mini-raids.  This person will provide advice to the Council, will be an open ear to guildmates.
