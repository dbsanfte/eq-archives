Dark Age of Camelot

Welcome to DLC's Dark Age of Camelot Knowledge Base. Here you'll find the information that DLC has compiled about DAoC. This site is always changing, look through the Web Changes topic to see what's recently changed or browse the menu on the left.

DLC plays on the Bedevere server in Midgard, and has a small presence on Palomides in Albion. Most've the information on this site relates directly to Midgard, but we're trying to expand to all the realms.
