DLC no longer has a "Clan Leader." It was decided that the day-to-day management of the clan should be handled by more then one person. The Justicars were then formed. The Justicars help to manage and guide DLC. They also serve as mediators in disputes in the clan and with outside persons.

There are nine Justicars in the clan. They are:
