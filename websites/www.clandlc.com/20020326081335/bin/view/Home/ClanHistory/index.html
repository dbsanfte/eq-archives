Clan History

Divided Loyalties was started approximately 5 years ago in Loki's Minion's Capture the Flag for Quake 2. We've evolved through the years into several games and generations including Counterstrike, Tribes 2 (Both which we run servers for), Quake 3, Everquest, Dark Age of Camelot and just about any other game that takes our fancy. We are a multi-gaming guild, which answer's the question: What does Divided Loyalties mean? Our Loyalties are spread out among various games. In Roleplay settings, we're generally a mercenary force.
