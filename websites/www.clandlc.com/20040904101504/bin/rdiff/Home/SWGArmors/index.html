Chitin Armor's difficulty lies in the number of resources required and the time to complete a set. It requires Reinforced Fiber Panels from a Tailor with Reinforced Fibers II. The crafter must also create multiple similar armor segments per piece, meaning segments that are made from the exact same resources. This armor takes an hour to complete 1 set. Tantel Armor

Most suits can be bought from a smith for 75,000 credits. I charge much much less.

Tantel armor is very nice! It consists of only 3 pieces, a breastplate, helmet and boots. All of which can accept crafted Layers in their schematic. This armor crafts quickly, and like chitin, has a very high resource cost. It requires 1 piece of synthetic cloth (Novice Tailor), 1 piece of reinforced fiber (tailor:RFII) and 3 or so identical tantel armor segments. This is good for costs though, since I can do the factory-indentical work myself. The materials of these additional items are included in the materials cost below. This is the first armor type that takes armor layers. Layers is a skill line in the armorsmith class which allows us to create items that improve damage vs energy, kinetic, etc. Currently, I don't know any armorsmiths who can make layers, including me. Layers rank 1 gives two schematics, Kinetic Protection Layer and Energy Protection Layer.

Tantel Armor is the armor that Lando Calrissian wore in Jabba's palace.
