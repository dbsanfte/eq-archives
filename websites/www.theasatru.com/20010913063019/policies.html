Policies:

1. If a member is found to be in serious violation of aforementioned ideals in the charter, then the guild senior council reserve's the right to vote on dismissal from guild. The idea of the game is to have fun and we'll not lose out on fun of the game by dealing with any persisting problems in the guild.

2. Though we try to group and work together as much as possible there are times when a person is busy doing something else and at no time is anyone obligated to participate in a raid, provide a teleport, resurrect someone's corpse, power level someone, camp a item for another, etc.

3. Rude behavior, being extremely annoying, cybering, flaming or any other inappropriate behavior on guildsay or the guild forum page will not be allowed. Disciplinary action will include a warning, and may finally result in removal from the guild. (Reinstatements to the guild)

4. Any members removed from the guild, that wish to rejoin, must have thier membership voted on. They need to receive more then 80% of the votes to be reinstated. If a person is reinstated any future infractions will disallow them from guild membership.

5. Any member whom removes themselves without voilating our charter can rejoin without having to reapply.

6. Anytime investigations show there were no infractions of our policies and charter there will be no formal recording of the incident.

7. In summation if you are having problems with a fellow member please take it to tells, say, or group. Please try to avoid inappropriate behavior at all times. Please advise any of us officers of the problems. Please feel that we are not difficult to reach out to. We are here to help and serve you. We want you to contact us!

Loot Policy :

1. Need before greed with a class orientation of the characters on a raid always receiving priority.

2. Win one item out of rolls until all in group/class have won, epics/bp/robes are exceptions and all can roll.

3. If no one wants an item you can loot it without it counting towards the loot you recieved.

4. If a raid is being conducted for a specific person to get a specific item they get only that item and have to pass on other rolls. Ofcourse we will not let an item rot either.

5. We encourage randomness on all raids. Unless the raid is being conducted for a specific reason we will allow all to roll on an item.

6. Ninja Looting will never be tolerated under any circumstances. This doesn't mean a multi-questable item can't be looted prior to a complete wipe-out. However, if you do not have rights to loot a no-drop item it is better to let the item rot then loot inappropriately.

New Member Policy:

First all applicants must have a sponsor. A member can only sponsor one person at a time. All applicants need to apply on our site (soon to be coming to a theater near us). Once the application has been completed there will be a 3-4 day comment period on that application from all or any members. If two or more members have a concern the enforcement committee will step in and do an evaluation. More then likely, if two or more people have a concern they will not be accepted. If there are no concerns, they will be an "interim" guild member. They will have our tag, participate with us in any and all events, and get to know us. This "interim" period will last 2-4 weeks. During this time further comments will be able to be made either in support or rejecting this candidate. If the overall message is positive they will be moved by the membership committee to full status and have access to the full boards. If negative, the will be moved to the enforcement group for enforcement.

The only exception (always seems to be one eh?) is for mass recruiting. This needs to be brought to Skarp please prior to any efforts made here.

Powerleveling Policy:

We as a guild encourage all main characters to level themselves as opposed to power-leveling. We will conduct level appropriate raids and guild events to support our members and encourage them to learn how to handle the differing challenges incurred by them. We will, on an individual basis, power level and alternate character to support the needs of the guild. All request for such support should be posted for the membership committe to handle.
