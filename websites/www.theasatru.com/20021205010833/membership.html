Membership steps for Asatru - New Applicants

1) First and foremost, read and understand our policies. By submitting an application, you agree to follow them, so get to know them. They can be found here on our site under Our Policies or you can read them on our Forum at the following link: pub84.ezboard.com/ftheasatruofpovarfrm20. Also take a moment to look at Our Charter.

2) Make an EzBoard account if you don't already have one. We grant people access to the members' section by adding their ezboard account name, so if you don't have one, you can't get access. ezboard has been very good about not giving e-mail addresses out, so you don't have to worry about spam-mail. You can make an account through this link: www.ezboard.com/create_join.html

3) Register with GuildMagic. This makes it a lot easier to see who we all have in the guild, what level ranges we have, and basic logistical stuff. the link to sign up with guild magic is here: eq.guildmagic.com/create_user.cfm

4) For your application, we don't have a specific set of questions to answer, so just tell us a little bit about yourself. Why do you want to join asatru? What are you looking for in a guild? What do you hope to get out of everquest? Things like that. One line posts should try to be avoided, since they don't really give members an accurate description of yourself. Post your application on our board in the following forum: pub84.ezboard.com/ftheasatruofpovarfrm2 You will need to have a Sponsor introduce you into the guild and bring you with on some events, groups etc. so that we have an opportunity to get to know you. It will be up to your sponsor to report to us and recommend your Membership.

If you have any questions or concerns, Please go to Our Council page and email the Membership Council. They will be more than happy to assist you!
