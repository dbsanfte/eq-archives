Asatru-EQ Guild on Povar

We are a guild of friends and family gaming together on the Povar Server. Our name does have significance, though we want to be clear in stating we are not a religious organization. The word Asatru, means "faith in the Aesir", who are the gods of pre-Christian Scandinavia.

The main gods and goddesses of the Asatru, which are all considered friendly, practicable, dependable, and approachable include the following: Thor, the god of thunder and friend of the common folk. Odinn (or Allfather), the chief god, poet and wandering wizard. Tyr, the god of war and justice. Ingvi Frey, the god of peace, fertility, and nature. Freya, the goddess of love, magic, and war and quiet a few other gods and goddesses.

Modern Asatru, which is open to anyone, regardless of race, ethnic origin, or sexual orientation was organized in the 1970s, almost simultaneously in Iceland, the USA and the UK.

While devoid of rigid dogma, Asatru is by no means amoral. It is in fact founded on the Nine Noble Virtues: courage, truth, honor, loyalty, hospitality, industriousness, perseverance, self-discipline, and self-reliance. From these individuals can decided upon the appropriate course of action in any situation and honor themselves, their families, the communities and their gods by striving to do what is right. The gods organized the universe from chaotic material. The remaining chaos allows for a random factor, which helps the universe keep evolving. Not even the gods are all-powerful, so perfection is neither required nor respected.

It is this code of ethics with its humanity and perspective that lead us to this name for our guild. We hope to reflect the noble virtues in our playing. We encourage individuals to follow their own path with the understanding the perfection is neither required nor respected. If you want to know more about the religion any websearch will provide links for more information.
