How to Replace This Page   Domain Name Registrations/Transfers

The page you are viewing is called index.html. A page titled index.html, or index.htm, is the default or first page of your web site.

For example, the default page that comes up in your browser when you type in http://www.theasatru.com would be http://www.theasatru.com/index.html.

Once your web site is built, you will need to name your first page index.html and upload it to your web site. If you wish to name your home page index.htm instead, be sure to delete this sample index.html file.

 

 New domain registrations take 24-48 hours to complete. You will receive an email confirming registration.

If you are transferring this domain, please refer to the Domain Name Information section of your confirmation email. This will provide you with instructions to make sure your domain transfer is completed successfully.

Once your domain has transferred, your account with Yahoo! Website Services will be functional in 24-48 hours.
