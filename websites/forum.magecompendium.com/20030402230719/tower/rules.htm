The Magician's Tower Rules

 

 

Note : These are privately run message boards and are subject to our rules.

 

Note : If it gets to the point where enforcing these rules is taking up way to much of our time and effecting the running of the site, expect to be banned on your first offence. You have been warned.

 

 

1) No spam.


2) No personal attacks, flames or abuse even in Rants..

3) No excessive swearing out of context, except in the Rants Forum.


4) No trading of virtual or real items and information for real money.


5) No posting of scams, exploits or in game cheats that would not be allowed by SOE.

6) No pornography (Breaking this rule will result in an immediate IP ban).

7) No malicious code, either in your post or signature (Breaking this rule will result in an immediate suspension of your posting rights and a ban pending investigation of the malicious code).

 

8) Do not create posts questioning a Moderator's or Administrator's actions (This will result in your plea being ignored), take up your issues in private (If you publicly undermine the authority or publicly attack the Moderators or Administrators on my boards you will loose any chance of a fair hearing).

 

9) Advertising is not allowed.

 

10) Signature testing is frowned upon, please use the preview button.

 

11) Your avatar and your signature images combined may not exceed 40 kb in size. If they do, the larger of the two will be removed, and the user contacted to let them know what has happened. You are more than welcome to add a new one within the rules.

 

Note : Avatars may be no larger than 150x150 pixels, as anything larger will distort the layout of the forums.

Note : Repeatedly breaking this rule will result in the removal of your ability to use an avatar and a signature.

 

If you see a person breaking any of the above rules, please private message any moderator as abuse of these rules spoils the community forums for all.

First Offence :

Post edited or moved to Administration Forum for reference and review, warning E-Mailed to poster. Note placed in Administration Forum. Warnings will be deleted after 1 month.

 

In an extreme circumstance, you can have your posting privileges suspended or be banned on your first offence (For example see Rule 6).

Second Offence :

Suspension of posting rights for 7 days. Additional note placed in Administration Forum.

Third Offence :

Complete account and IP ban, you will not be welcome back to The Magician's Tower.

If for any reason someone else is using your account or has used it to break these rules, appeals will be considered providing you can get an upstanding member of the community to vouch for you.

 

If you have any suggestions for changes to these rules please feel free to E-Mail me ronaldor@magecompendium.com.
