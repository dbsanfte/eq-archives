Teppeny Rainsparrow
Ambassador of the Winds
 
History: Teppeny was the firstborn daughter of Dinehsab Silentwolf, and a mother she can barely remember. Her mother, you see, died soon after her sister Byrdie was born, and her father refused to speak of her after that.

Growing up, Teppeny always spent time around the druids and came to love the teachings of Karana, which were taught to her as she helped on the small farm. Some afternoons, she would visit the wall in Misty Thicket, and sing to the travelers for a bit of copper to pay for spells she was attempting to learn. During the time she spent singing at the wall, she met many friends, including the rogue Mibbo, and the wizard Xhiris Ce`Andar.

As time went on, Teppeny started learning more of the world around her, and was eager to explore it. She ended up seeing much of Antonica, and all of Faydwer and Odus. And when Kunark was discovered, she and Xhiris went there, as well.

One day, while returning from a trip, her sister began to tell her of a group of people called the Wind Riders. Byrdie spoke of the leader, a paladin named Sugan, and urged Teppeny to join, showing her their badge, which she now wore.

Teppeny, though, chose to stay with her current guild, the Souls of Peace, even though things had been going badly as of late. More and more of her friends from that guild were leaving, to seek more opportunities... and more people whome Teppeny barely knew, were taking their places. It came to the point where, at one gathering, there were only two faces that Teppeny recognized. The goals for this guild had also changed. Teppeny knew then, that she could not stay.

One day, she sought out Sugan, whom Byrdie had spoken of, and joined the Wind Riders alongside her sister, and Annabella (whom Teppeny had met sometime ago in Rivervale).

Though Teppeny seems, to most, like a very young girl, it may not be true at all. But whether she is 6, or 16, no one will ever be sure. But perhaps, if one were to ask enough of her past, they might just find out. Until then, she'll just keep them guessing... and she likes that just fine.

Sugan's View: Little Teppie is nothing short of a Scamp! Well.. maybe "nothing short" is the wrong phrase. I don't know how many times this one has put critters in my bed, or stuck her fingers in cake icing, or colored in Karana's holy books! But... I love her. *grins*

Faileas' View: Stay AWAY from MY library. (You know, I'd almost be afraid to capture this one, for fear of wax rainbows on the dungeon walls.)
