Wind Riders
A Brief History
 

(The Wind Riders is a good-aligned roleplaying guild on the Tunare Server, for the Online RPG EverQuest.)

We, the Wind Riders, began our mission as a sigificant line of communication within the Temple of Thunder of Qeynos. More recently, under the leadership of Sugan Imshee'Cro, we have allied ourselves with the good people of Norrath to quell the blossoming darkness, which has become a serious threat. We are a loosely bound network of brave protectors and covert spies under combined duties to Karana, Rodcet Nife, Quellios, and other Deities of Light. It is our sworn duty to purge the corrupt factions from Norrath with an all-consuming force of chaos and fury in the name of the Storm.

We have one hell of a time doing it, too.

A Wind Rider travels lightly and swiftly, cleansing the land with the grace and fury of the Storm. He seeks out darkness and infiltrates it using the power of light. Only through his wisdom and mercy shall it be allowed the chance to be redeemed and purified.
The guild mission statement (thus far) is simple.
  • Allow the beauty, chaos, and strength of the Storm to be witnessed in our actions.
  • Protect and Serve the inhabitants, especially the travellers of the land.
  • Spread the influence and philosophies of the Wind Riders to all people.
  • Abolish those who would corrupt the Light, or destroy Norrath.
