Laneni Grimfist
Disciple of Storms
 
History: Laneni was born in the Karanas to wealthy parents who were farmers and traders. Her name then was Laneni Yamura and she was youngest of 5 children. Being the only girl she was spoiled rotten. Her older brother Nishi was her favorite because he was an adventurer and told her stories form far away lands. When she was sixteen years old her parents bade her marry a boy she did not love so the families businesses could merge. Rebelling she ran away from home and found herself in Freeport. Taken in at the Ashen house she was trained as a monk and a follower of the child. After much adventure in the lands she had only heard of in stories she met a bard named Thryn and friendship blossomed into love. Thryn introduced her to Sugan of the riders and an alliance was born that neither time nor even Laneni forming her own guild could destroy. Thryn and Laneni were married and were very happy until Laneni was called away on a pilgrimage of knowledge and let Thryn have his freedom.

Now after much time She has returned a diffrent person with a new name..Grimfist. A new fire burns in her eyes and she is even more a threat to Faileas and his guild than she ever was. Faileas killed her brother Nishi and it is only her friendship to Sugan that has kept her from returning the favor in kind. Time will tell if friendship will be stronger than the blood that calls nightly to Laneni in her dreams.

Sugan's View: Such anger and passion.. Laneni can always be counted on to bring swift justice to a situation. Her discipline with Quellious is invaluable to say the least. Sometimes I can see the strain she holds herself in, just to remain calm in the face of evil. I respect Laneni very much, for her control and her friendship.

Faileas' View: Rallos Zek has no wrath like a woman scorned. Laneni's blind vengeance will do her more harm than good, but I theorize it's just best for me to stay out of the path of this tornado. She has louder bravado than her wisp of a brother did.
