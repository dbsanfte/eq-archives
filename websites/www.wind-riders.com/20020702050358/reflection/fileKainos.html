Kainos Murasame
Genesis Knight of Storms
 
History: Former apprentice to General Shizo Masamune of the Shadow Striders, Kainos Murasame has been trained in the ways of Rallos Zek. The will to battle against a worthly opponent like Shizo keeps him going. He knows no life other then war, has never loved or been loved. Only a great respect for fellow soldiers. His emotions killed so he could hide any weakness that may appear in battle. Keeps to himself and looks upon matters too seriously at times. Now a strong follower of Mithaniel Marr, he has become a expert at fighting undead. Training day and night to perfect the ancient vampire hunting techniques and studying texts and scrolls in search of magic to better aid his war.

Sugan's View: It took a long, exhaustive battle for Kainos to find the truth, but I believe that true Knights are forged from such obstacles. Kainos is proving to be one of the best.

Faileas' View: An ungrateful fool who will suffer the consequences of defying General Shizo. Unlike Envy, Shizo is one master who will never hesitate to take what he is owed in life's blood.
