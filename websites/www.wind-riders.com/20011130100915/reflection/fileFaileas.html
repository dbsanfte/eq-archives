Faileas Imshee'Cro
Human Shadowknight; Sugan's Brother
 
As I was named "Reflection" before birth, so my brother was named "Shadow". I used to think those were just my mother's nicknames until I learned the truth much later, and it makes me wonder what my parents foresaw.

Generally, Faileas looks quite a bit like me: Green eyes, Red hair. Looks can be decieving, since we're nothing alike. He leads the Shadow Striders, and has What's Her Name as an apprentice. Malgorm bears his exact features, but the farther apart he and Faileas drift, the more different they appear.

Oh, this curse business.. None of this started with Faileas, but it shall all end with him. My brother is the slave of a dark god, regardless of what the courts may decide. A God's curse was not meant for any jury to rule on. Faileas shares my name, and so, he is my responsibility. It is my duty alone that the honor of our House be restored. Should I fail, so be it, but I will not have my allies take up my vows for me while I breath. I find it hard to forgive those who raise their blade... cast judgement without knowing him as I do. After all, I love him.

- Sugan Imshee'Cro
