Membership
Steps & Rules
 
  1. Make sure you know the basic rules. Read the posted Frequently Asked Questions and the requirements below.

  2. Fill out the Membership Application as completely as you can. Applications with no Character History will be ignored. Please give us your correct email address.

  3. When you hit the submit button, the screen in that window will go white. That's normal, the application has been emailed to Sugan. You can close the window.

  4. Get in touch with Sugan or the officers online. We can't always do searches to find out when you're in the game. Names of accepted characters are emailed periodically to the officers, but you can speed the process by taking action.

  5. Once a member, get involved. Pursue what you want to become a part of. Visit the Message Board for fastest news.

Here is what you need to be a Wind Rider.
  • A Good Heart at all times.
  • A love of Roleplaying - We are a heavy RP guild. (That means you don't judge an enemy by what they "con" to you.)
  • At least level 5. Not for the level itself, but to show some dedication to your character.
  • You need to have adventured with a Wind Rider, or have one who can attest to your actions.
  • You must fill out an application on this site. Characters without a background story will not be considered for membership.
  • You need to be independant and strong, with a good reputation, able to stand as an ambassador for our name without constant assistance.
  • You need to be able to respect and have faith in those in authority - They wouldn't be leaders if they haven't already proven themselves.
If you think you can meet these qualifications, then you'll have a good chance of getting in.
Additions... Alternate characters may be invited to either Wind Riders or Shadow Striders, however they must have a story and application to the appropriate guild. Alt characters are judged as individuals in every way; for instance: you cannot be married to two characters that belong to the same player.

Here is what you need to be a GOOD Wind Rider.
  • Keep up with the Wind Riders Message Board.
  • Have a good back story. That space in the guild application is for a reason.
  • Contribute and be a part of the overall story.
  • Update your member information if you need to.
