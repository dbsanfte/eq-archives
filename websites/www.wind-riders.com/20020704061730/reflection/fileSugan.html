Sugan Imshee'Cro
Heart of the Winds
 
History: There was the legend of Imshee'Cro, a family whose vague roots extend far back into the Lost Age of Norrath... Once proud and noble, this House was cursed by the Plaguebringer. Imshee'Cro became synonymous with Bloodshed, Plague, and Death. The God of Disease, Bertoxxulous, used "his children" to build an empire in the young human race. It is whispered in fact, that House Imshee'Cro had more than a small influence on the downfall of the Combine Empire.

And then there was Sugan. With this cheery little redhead came hope; an Imshee'Cro who resisted "the hunger", as the curse was named by it's victims. She's a thoughtful paladin- innocent of malicious intent. On most days Sug is generous to a flaw. Not the intellect her brother is, she possesses great wisdom and uses her heart as a guide. Sugan is prone to chaotic mood swings; trademark of Karana, her god of Storms.

Although many of her battles are with her brother Faileas, the curse of their bloodline is her real enemy. Sug will do anything to save her brother's soul from the clutches of Bertoxxulous. That's both her quest and her weakness. Luckily, she has enough true friends to create a small army for this cause. Unluckily, so does her brother.

Sugan's View: I only pray Karana finds me worthy of his blessings.

Faileas' View: She needs to wake up and see what kind of world this really is.
