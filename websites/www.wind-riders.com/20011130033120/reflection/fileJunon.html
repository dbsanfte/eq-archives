Junon & Cratress T'Raine
Teir'Dal Shadow Knight & Voodoo Priestess
 
Nilenya T'Raine's brother and sister, neither of whom are half as sane as she is. Because of her reprieval to Light, Cratress and (to a lesser extent) Junon framed Nilenya for murdering their mother. She's since been on the run from Lodge of the Dead agents, and her path lead her to us. As for Cratress, I think the downward spirals of her Voodoo craft have taken their toll on her mind. She's used it numerous times to aid the Shadow Striders, and to my dismay seems to have an effigy of me somewhere. I remember when the girl stole some of my hair to make it. *grumbles*

Junon is only a bit less ..tenacious than Cratress. He's a Shadow Knight who can fool you with how powerful he appears to be. I found that out the hard way when I went to free Nilenya once. I think he may also be a vampire.

- Sugan Imshee'Cro
