Sirynn Windweaver
Regent of Mists
 
History: (OOC This is the story of how Siynn came into Norrath. His parents {Siren, a lovely and dainty wood elf bard; and a somewhat frail, but extremely charming human enchanter named Ledynn} met in Crushbone, fell in love during their upcoming journeys, and had their first kid after their marriage.)

Siren was trying to save the slaves that were captured by the loathsome orcs, because she was a bard always looking to help. The only bad thing was Crushbone was located near the high elven city of Felwithe and the high elves are a very snobby, and tight nit race. So she had to go it alone. She was running along killing Orc Centurions left and right.

However after she entered the great Orc castle she heard a deep growl shout, "You bards are a disgrace to these sacred grounds."

It was the Crushbone emperor himself, and on both sides he was flanked on each side by his Royal Guard, and behind him was his personal Oracle. She quickly noticed the immediate danger of her situation (seeing as she has some brains and is not as dumb as the Ogre warriors corpse that lay behind the emperor) so she quickly spins on her heels and begins to sing Selo's Accelerendo (a song to increase the speed of her feet). Unfortunately she is quickly stopped dead in her tracks by a root spell complimentary of the Emperors Oracle. So all she could do, as she was getting pounded and pierced by the fists and spears of the orcs, was yell for help. Luckily this yell for help was heard by a group of adventurers, just checking out their new surroundings, who were just outside the Orc castle. The group of adventurers ran as quick as they could to the aid of a lovely little wood elf with the angelic voice.

The leader quickly and decisively shouted out commands to the rest of his group, he wasn't the biggest or strongest one, but the group still listened to his orders (our lovely bard learned soon learned from the groups responses that the leaders name was Ledynn). Ledynn figured the first thing to do was to keep the lovely lady alive, so he had the cleric in the group cast some healing spells on her. The leader then charmed on of the Orc guards to fight along side them as his pet. Then they went to work on the orcs in the order of importance, the oracle, then emperor, then the guards. The oracle was first because he was capable fo doing the most dmg, so they had the whole group concentrate in him first. With the combined might of Ledynn and his four friends they made short work of him. By now the elven bard had regained her senses, and told the leader that she would help with the battle because she started it, and Ledynn quickly accepted her offer. So they split up evenly a tank and a caster each on the Emp and Royal guard who wasn't charmed, Siren helped out with her Anthem de Arms song (which increased the speed and strength of their arms), and her Hymn of Restoration (which took care fo the minor wounds of the group). With the oracle out, the rest were child's play for the strong and well doverse group of six.

After the fight, everyone got together and got acquainted. Siren quickly took to the charming words and personality of Ledynn. Ledynn also was astonished by the beauty of her face and voice. They quickly became a strong and well acquainted duo. When it came time for Ledynn to leave Siren's home, she said that she was coming with, and Ledynn couldn't deny that she would be welcome company on the long and lonely journey.

As they journeyed together they got really close, and they soon decided to get married. So they did. After the honeymoon little Siren wasn't so little, her stomach quickly grew and they assessed that they were soon to be parents. The birth happened on the last day in April, it was storming out, the earth was shaking with the sounds of thunder and illuminated by the strikes of lightning. On that day a lovely Half Elven baby boy was born, who sang out a beautiful and powerful scream as he was birthed. The scream shattered the windows of Siren and Ledynn's home, for this reason they knew Karana had blessed them with a baby bard. Since he was gonna be a bard he was given the first half of his mothers name and the last half of his fathers name, therefore he was donned the name of Sirynn.
