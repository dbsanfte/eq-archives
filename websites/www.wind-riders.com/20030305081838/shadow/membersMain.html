Joining the Shadow Striders
Your OOC Requirements
 
To join us in our quest to spread the Dark Disease, there are a few things required of you. Read this page thoroughly before clicking the application link at the bottom. Remember, alternate characters may apply to either Wind Riders or Shadow Striders, however they each must have an application to the appropriate guild. Alt characters are judged as individuals in every way. For instance: you cannot be married to two characters that belong to the same player (unless polygamy is an evilly RP'd trait).
  1. Go over this list carefully. Make sure you know what is expected of you. Look at the Frequently Asked Questions, then read our charter and the guidelines listed on the Wind Riders site.

  2. Fill out the Membership Application as completely as you can. Applications with no Character History will be ignored. Please give us your correct email address.

  3. Start Roleplaying a Shadow Strider initiate. If your presense is known, you'll have a better chance of a quick invitation. With hands-on involvement you can generally expect a week's wait before being invited, as Shadow Striders become familiar with you. Ask individuals if you can come RP with them, or join the "WRooc" channel and politely ask any WR or SS if they have ideas to get you involved.

  4. Once you've met a few people, get in touch with Faileas or the officers online. Only a few of us have time to search for applicants, so it will probably be up to you. Names of accepted characters are posted periodically for the officers.

Here are the requirements to be a Shadow Strider.
  • Respectful at all times, both in and out of character.
  • A love of heavy, villainous Roleplaying. (never judge an enemy or superior by what they "con" to you)
  • At least level 9.
  • A complete application, which is found at the bottom of this page.
  • Know a Shadow Strider who can say they've met and approve of you.
  • Be independant and strong with a good reputation; a responsible ambassador of your guild who doesn't need constant assistance.
  • Respect and have faith in those in authority - They've already proven themselves.
Here is how to be a competent Shadow Strider.
  • Keep up with the Shadow Striders Message Board. Make a profile in the Roster forum.
  • Have a good back story. That space in the guild application is for a reason.
  • Contribute to the big story, and lend yourself to the storylines of others.
  • Update the information in your Roster profile regularly.
