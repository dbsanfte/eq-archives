Kokiri Windrider
Inceptor of Mists
 
History: Just a simple bard, I was taken in by the bards of Kelethin after Canelon thought I was pickpocketing. But I swear I wasn't, not from him! Anyway they taught me the virtue of Quellious. Her peace of mind frees me from the anger and sadness I felt when my family was captured and taken to Mistmoore Castle. So I don't have the strength or courage that the Knights do. Well, I can write a darn good story. I couldn't believe it when I met Lord Faileas! And then the Wind Riders! They're all my greatest heroes, I have all sorts of songs about them.
Oh yes, and I'm very pleased to call Master Sirynn my mentor in the bardic ways. All my dreams are coming true...

Sugan's View: Kokiri's cute, that's a given. And she's very talented at what she does. It's her timid nature that draws her enemies though, We'll have to protect her from that.

Faileas' View: Bards: They're only historians when history suits them. I hope the warning I gave this little girl was scary enough to make her tell the truth about our War.
