Malgorm Azzothoth
Demon Wizard; Once an Imshee'Cro
 
He was once the greatest adversary I'd ever faced, a Demon with no salvation, save cold death. He was a part of me, could speak to my mind, and react to my well-being.

Malgorm is the man, Azzothoth is the Demon. The more I learned of Malgorm, the more I came to regret my temper. He lost his only love during his life. This form he exists in now... pure torture. Pure madness. From ancient texts I learned that Malgorm was the ancestor of the Imshee'Cro, a Geomancer of great power. A man of his own city. But when death took his lover, Malgorm bargained with one of the Plaguebringer's servants to bring her back. He could not pay the price. Some of the details are lost about what exactly happened, but one result is our entire cursed bloodline. Another is the man trapped by a Demon's haunting for all eternity, and those who followed him through the ages: the Disciples of Darkness.

I wonder, if Faileas knew all of this when he first summoned Malgorm back to Norrath...

- Sugan Imshee'Cro
