go here to download infantry online game

don't know how best to describe it - there are different games (zones) within the game

ctf - capture the flag, teams go against eachother - you pick character (like eq) - medic, engineer, infantry, heavy artillery, etc ... your "character" gains points with kills - you have multiple weapons, armor can buy more after getting points for winning and killing enemy

gravball - this is what i be playing though i suck at it so far - need to get good keyboard config - in this deal you play in a large arena and motor around on scooters - you try to get a "grav" ball into the opposing goal - you have weapons and can pick up goodies - you can punt the ball or pass to teammate as when you are carrying it you can't shoot ...

arena - rpg mode again where you have character and earn points killing other players - you make points for that to buy better weapons, armor, vehicles, etc ...

what's really cool about this game is that (1) all the stats on all the players are always available, (2) you can "spectate" and just watch someone else play, (3) it is a real cool blend of action/rpg elements, (4) team strategies for gravball, ctf, (5) after release, anybody running a server can create their own zone setups (can create your own eq if desired ...) and customize the game ...

bad deal is that there isn't much documentation on how the game works but there's enough to get you started - check it out - it is 12M download but no credit card is required and it is free ...
