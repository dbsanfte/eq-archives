These dolls are a collection of EverQuest characters that were modeled on my bases! Each and every one of them is a beautiful work of art, and I'm proud to display them here (with the artists' permission, of course). As with all of the completed works on my site (the bases have their own rules, which you can read in the Bases section), these dolls are NOT to be adopted, altered, mutilated, resized, recolored, redrawn, changed, or taken in ANY WAY, whole or in part.

I would be thrilled to see and display any dolls that –you- create on my bases! If you'd like me to show them to me, or for me to display them here, please Email them to me, along with a statement of your permission to display them.
