10/02

Welcome to my little corner of the web.  I've just started this site, and I'm new to how all this works, but I'm learning and I'm sure you'll have patience with me.  Several people have told me I should share my work with others, and that is precisely what I intend to do!

 

Who are you and what are you doing here?

I create custom pixellized characters for people who play EverQuest.  Some call them dolls, others call them "dollz," --  call them what you will.  I've been working with graphics programs for several years, and although I'm self-taught, I like to think I do a pretty bang-up job.  Click on my Art link and judge for yourself.  I play a ranger on the Tholuxe Paells server in the guild Acropolis.  So you see, I'm sensitive to your needs. ; )

What can you do for me?

I can make you a doll to love and squeeze and call your very own. I'm not in the business of doing screenshot art or any kind of 3D work or cartoons, but I can do what I can do, and I'm happy to do that for you. See my requests page for more information about exactly what that is.

Where, oh, where did you get your inspiration?

I owe a heap of thanks to the girls and guys of Norrath's Studio, a fantastic little EZBoard community dedicated to the pursuit of art based on EverQuest. See my links page for that and more! If you're not interested in what I have to offer, you might consider hitting some of those links to investigate someone whose artwork better suits your desires. They do some fabulous work.
