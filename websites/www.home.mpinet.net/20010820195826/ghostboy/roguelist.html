Rogue Epic Checklist
Picking up the Pieces
Find Malka Rale in the Qeynos Aqueducts at location 380, -210. Say "I will help" to get "Stanos' Pouch."
Go to North Kaladim and pickpocket Founy Jestands to get "stained parchment top"
Pick pocket the "stained parchment bottom" from Tani N'Mar in Neriak 3rd Gate.
Go to Highpass and find Stanos Herkanor. Give Stained parchment top and bottom to get Combined Parchment.
Find Eldreth in Lake Rathe and give him the Combined parchment, 100 PP and 2 bottles of milk to get Scribbled Parchment.
Find "Book of Souls." In Plane of Hate, spawns on a night stand near the Maestro.
Give Scribbled Parchment to Yendar Starpyre. Receive Tattered Parchment.
Give Tattered Parchment and Book of Souls to Yendar Starpyre to spawn Renux Herkanor. Kill her and loot Jagged Diamond Dagger and Translated Parchment.
Hand Translated Parchment to Stanos in Highpass to get a Sealed Box.
Give Sealed Box to a 'night spawn' dark elf (loc 800, 240) in Kithicor to spawn General V'Ghera. Kill the general and loot Generals Pouch and Cazic Quill if it drops.
Give Stanos in Highpass the Jagged Diamond Dagger, the "Cazic Quill," and the "General's Pouch" to receive Ragebringer.
Side Quests
Jagged Diamond Dagger
Get "Fleshripper" from the "Kobold King" in Sol B
Get "Painbringer" from the "Kobold Champion" in Sol B
Get "Mithril Two-Handed Sword" from the "Froglok King" in Lower Guk
Get "Gigantic Zwiehander" from "Karg Icebear" in Everfrost Peaks.
Give all 4 to Vilinus the Small in West Karana for the Jagged Diamond Dagger.
Cazic Dagger
Get "Robe of the Kedge" from "Phinigel Autropos" in Kedge Keep
Get "Robe of Ishva" from the "Coercer" in Kithicor Forest
Get "Shiny Metallic Robe" from the"Ghoul Arch Magi" in Lower Guk
Get "Robe of the Oracle" from the Oracle in the Ocean of Tears.
Give all 4 items to Vilinus the Small in West Karana for the Cazic Quill.
Stanos' Pouch
Get "Burning Rapier" obtained from a quest
Get "Martune Rapier" from "A Scorn Banshee" in the Plane of Hate
Get Eyerazzia from "A Scareling" in the Plane of Fear
Get "Well Balanced Rapier" from the "Shady Swashbuckler" in East Commons.
Give all 4 to Vilinus for Stanos' Pouch. Why you would do this is beyond me, just go ask Malka Rale in Qeynos Aquaducts for one for free.
