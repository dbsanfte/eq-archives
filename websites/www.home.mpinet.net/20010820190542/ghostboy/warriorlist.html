Warrior Epic Checklist
Jeweled Dragon Head Hilt
Pick up unjeweled head hilt on lake bottom in Lake Rathe.
Collect a diamond, black sapphire and jacinth.
Give the 3 gems and hilt to Wenden in the slums of East Freeport. Get Jeweled dragon head hilt.
Finely Crafted Dragon Head Hilt
Go to Timorous Deep, find the Chessboard and pick up the 'damaged dragon head hilt.
Loot "ball of Everliving golem" in Plane of Fear.
Kill Mountain Giant Patriarch in Dreadlands and loot 'a giant monacle'.
Give monacle to Mentrax Mountainbone in Frontier Mountains and get rejesium ore.
Give Ore, Ball and damaged hilt to Wenden in East Freeport and receive Finely Crafted Dragon Head Hilt.
Ancient Sword Blade
Buy a Voxtail Ale from a vendor.
Kill ice giants in Permafrost and loot a 'block of permafrost'
Attain 2 rebreathers (tinkered).
Give Ale, Block and rebreathers to Denken Strongpick in OOT on aviak island and receive Blade #1
Ancient Blade
Kill the Queen of Chardok. Loot Ancient Blade
The Red Scabbard
Talk to Kargak Redblade in east freeport. Get 'wax sealed note'.
Give note to Oknoggin in Feerot and get a 'tiny lute'.
Give Tiny Lute back to Kargak, receive a book.
Bring book to Tenal Redblade in East Karana. Get Frozen War Totem.
Give Totem and frozen goblin heart back to Tenal Redblade. Get Totem of Fiery War.
Give green dragon scale, red dragon scale and Totem to Tenal Redblade. Get The Mark of the Sword
Kill Maestro in Plane of Hate and loot his hand. Give his hand and the Mark of the Sword to Tenal, receive Note to Kargak
Kill Spiroc Lord in the Plane of Sky, and loot a Spiroc Wingblade. Return this to Kargak with the note and receive the Scabbard and combine the 2 blades and 2 hilts to get the Epic Swords.
