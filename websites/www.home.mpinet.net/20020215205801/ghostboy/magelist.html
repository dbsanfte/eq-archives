Mage Epic Checklist
Element of Fire
Travel to Skyfire and locate Jennus Lyklobar. He needs 4 items:Torch of the Elements, Blazing Wand, Power of the Orb, Burning Embers
Torch of the Elements: Go to City of Mist, kill Black Reavers until Neh`Ashiir spawns. KIll him and loot Torch.
Blazing Wand:Locate Undertow the seahorse in Kedge Keep, past Estrella's room. Kill him and loot the Wand.
Burning Embers:In Burning Woods camp the Nekezekena/Phurzicon cycle, loot Burning embers.
Power of the Orb
Locate Rykas in Lake Rathe, hail him and get a Token of Mastery. He requires 3 items to give up the Power of the Orb
Go to West Commons and find Jahsohn Ak'sot, Give Token, get Words of Magi'kot quest.
Collect the 3 pages for the word quest from (1)dread wolf in Kithicor, (2)Tentacle Terror in Unrest, (3)Frenzied or Bloodthirsty ghoul in lower guk. Give all 3 to Jahsohn and get Words of Magi'kot.
Walnan in Butcherblock wants the 4 Powers of the Elements. Bring her the Elements from:
Faerie Guard in Lesser Faydark--Earth
Blazing Elemental or Lava Elemental in Sol A.-- Fire
Any Ravener in Kaesora-- Water
A Gypsy Dancer in Mistmoore--Wind
Go to Najena and speak with Akksstaff. He wants the 4 torn pages of mastery. Find all 4 in Najena and give them to Akksstaff to get the Words of Mastery.
Give the Words of Mastery, Power of the Elements, and Words of Magi'kot to Rykas to get the Power of the Orb.
Go back to Skyfire and give the Torch of the Elements, Blazing Wand, Power of the Orb, and Burning Embers to Jennus Lyklobar to get the Element of Fire.
Element of Earth
Tiblner Milnik in Firiona Vie holds the Element of Earth quest. He needs:
Shovel of Ponz-Quested mage focus item.
Broom of Trilon-Quested mage focus item.
Staff of Elemental Mastery: Earth. Dropped from Magi P`tasa in the Plane of Hate.
Dirt of Underfoot. Drops from Slixin Klex in Burning Woods.
Bring all 4 items to Tiblner Milnik to receive Element of Earth.
Element of Water
Locate Jinalis Andir in Dagnars Cauldron. She requires 3 items.
Staff of Elemental Mastery: Water. Drops from Phinigel Autropos in Kedge Keep.
Rain of Karana. Drops from Tarbul Earthstrider in East Karana.
Tears of Erollisi. Drops from Captain Rottgrime in The Overthere.
Return all 3 items to Jinalis Andir to get the Element of Water.
Element of Wind
Kihun Solstin in the Plane of Sky gives this quest. He needs 3 items.
Crown of Elemental Mastery from the 7th island in the Plane of Sky. Anything on this island may drop the Crown
An Elemental Binder from the Hole. Fairly common.
Pegasus Feather Cloak from Quillmane in South Karana.
Give the Crown, Binder and Cloak to Kihun to get the Element of Water.
The Master
When you hand Kihun the Items for the Element of Wind, the Master of Elements spawns. Give him the 4 Elements you've collected to recieve the Summon Orb spell. This spell summons the Orb of Mastery which comes with 1 charge of Manifest Elements..and makes a giant pet.
