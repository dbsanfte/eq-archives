Monk Epic Checklist
Robe of the Lost Circle
Complete red sash quest in Freeport.
Complete purple headband quest in Qeynos.
Kill Raster of Guk and loot Idol of Zan Fi.
Kill Targin the Rock in Sol B and loot Code of the Whistling Fists.
Give Purple headband and Code of the Whistling Fists to Brother Qwinn in South Karana, receive Needle of the Void.
Give Red sash and Idol of Zan Fi to Brother Zephyl in Rathe Mountains, receive Rare robe pattern.
Combine Rare robe pattern, Needle of the Void, a patch of shadow silk (tailor made) and scroll of Jonothans Whistling Warsong in a sewing kit, receive Robe of the Lost Circle.
Robe of the Whistling Fists
Kill "an Iksar Betrayer" in Chardok and loot 'A metal pipe'.
Kill Pawduster in Karnors Castle and loot 'A metal pipe'.
Have an enchanter charm Brother Balatin in Dreadlands and turn in both metal pipes and the Robe of the Lost Circle to receive the Robe of the Whistling Fists.
The Book of Immortals
Talk to Tomekeeper Danl in Erudin library, learn of the Book of Immortals to begin the quest.
Kill Soul Devourer' , 'lava walker' , 'Black Scar', 'a wandering wurm', 'a Shadow Drake', 'Guardian of Felia' or any other named mob in Skyfire and loot the Book of Immortals.
Give Book to Tomekeeper Danl and receive Danl's reference.
Give Robe of the Whistling Fists and Danl's reference to Lheao in Timorous Deep, receive Celestial Fists Book.
The Masters
Locate Kaiaren in Trakanons Teeth and give him the Celestial Fists book, receive it back and either kill or feign Kaiaren off.
Locate new Kaiaren in Traks Teeth by the lake and give him the book. Receive Book of the Celestial Fists and info on first Master.
Challenge Eejag in Lavastorm, kill him and loot a Charred Scale.
Give Charred Scale to real Kaiaren, receive info about next Master and the scale back. (can skip this part)
Travel to the Plane of Air, give charred scale to "a presence" on Noble Dojorns island. Kill Gwan and loot Breath of Gwan.
Sneak up on Trunt in Nurga and hand him the Breath of Gwan. Kill Trunt and loot 'a head'.
Hand Trunts head to Deep in Lake Rathe, receive info about her master in Overthere and Trunts head back. (can skip this part)
Hand Trunts head to "an Astral Projection" in Overthere, receive eye of Kaiaren.
In Lake of Ill Omen hand the Eye to Astral Projection. Vorash and Deep spawn. Kill them to spawn Xenevorash.
Kill Xenevorash and loot Demon Fangs.
Give Book of the Celestial Fists and Demon Fangs to either form of Kaiaren to receive Celestial Fists.
