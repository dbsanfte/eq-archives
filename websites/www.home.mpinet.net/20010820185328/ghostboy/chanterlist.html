Enchanter Epic Checklist
The Notes
Talk to Stofo Olan in the Erudin Library to start the quest and get info.
Give Shiny Metallic Robe to Rilgor Plegnog in Ak'Anon to get a Mechanical Pen
Get 'empty ink vial' from Reania Jukle under Qeynos.
Charm the Ghoul Scribe in Lower Guk and give him the vial to get "Ink of the Dark"
In WK, give quill and parchment to Chrislin Baker to get a bandit sash.
Find Thrackin Griffen and kill him. Loot "White Paper"
Return Pen, Paper and Ink to Stofo Olan to receive "Copy of Notes"
Meet the Masters!
Give 'copy of notes' to Jeb Lumsed in Burning Woods. Get 'Jeb's Seal'.
-Modani Qu`Loni-
Find Modani Qu`Loni in Overthere. Give her Jeb's Seal, get Enchanters Bag.
In Oggok, meet Bozlum Blossom and do a short quest for her. Get Snow Blossoms.
Kill Verina Tomb in Neriak 3rd Gate to get Innoruuks Word.
Kill Vessel Drozlin in the Cabilis shaman guild to get Xolion Rod.
Kill Prince Selrach Di'zok in Chardok and loot his head.
Give Prince Selrach Di'zok's head to Joren Nobleheart in the Felwithe Mage guild. Get Kings Chalice.
Combine Chalice, Xolion Rod, Innoruuks Word and Snow Blossoms in Enchanters bag. Give to Modani. Receive 1st Piece of Staff.
-Mizzle Gepple-
Locate Mizzle Gepple in Ak'Anon and get another enchanters bag and the list of items he needs.
Kill Cazel, the named Sand giant in Oasis for 'a Spoon'.
Go to Loc: -190, -380 in Overthere and pick up a Key.
In Dalnir on level 3, pick up The Lost Scroll at Loc -20, 50.
Go to Plane of Sky and find a Book of Charm and Sacrifice at Loc:1300, 560
Combine the Spoon, Key, Lost Scroll and Book in the enchanter bag and return to Mizzle Gepple, get 2nd piece of the staff.
-Nadia Starfeast-
Speak with Nadia in Firiona Vie and receive 4 dull gems, Diamond, Sapphire, Ruby and Emerald.
Go to Kaesora and charm Spectral Librarian, give him the Dull Diamond to get an Enchanted Diamond.
Find Wraith of Jaxion in City of Mist and charm him, give him the Dull RUby to get the Enchanted Ruby.
Go to Overthere and find Impaler Tzilug at Loc: -90, 810. Give him the dull emerald to get the Enchanted Emerald.
In Skyfire charm Felia Goldenwing and give her the dull sapphire to get the Enchanted Sapphire.
Combine these 4 gems in a enchanters bag and give to Nadia for the 3rd piece of the staff.
Polzin Mrid
Give Polzin in the Hole Jeb's Seal, receive it back with some info.
Kill the Ghost of Kindle in the Hole for Essence of a Ghost
Kill Cazic Thule, which spawns A Shissar Spirit and loot Head of a Serpent.
Loot a Forsaken Revenant in Plane of Hate for Essence of a Vampire
Kill The Tangrin in Field of Bone (Loc: 3300, 2530). and loot Sand of the Mystics.
Combine in enchanters bag and give back to Polzin. Get 4th piece of staff.
Staff of the Serpent
Combine the 4 pieces of the staff in an enchanters bag and return the Bundle of Staves to Jeb and receive Staff of the Serpent
