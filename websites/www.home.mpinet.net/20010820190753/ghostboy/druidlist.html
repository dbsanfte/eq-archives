Druid Epic Checklist
The Beginning
Speak with Telin Darekforest in Burning woods and receive a note.
Bring note to Faelin Bloodbriar in Greater Faydark and receive a ring.
Take ring to Gix X'Tin in Kithicor Woods. He will give you a dark metal coin. A reaver will spawn, kill or run away.
Go to East Karana druid ring. Give the coin to Athele, receive amulet. Give amulet to Sionae, then Nuien, then Teloa. Kill the Dark Elf Corruptor who spawns and loot a tome.
Hand Tome to Athele and receive a Earthstained note.
Take Note to Elle Foodcrafter in Misty Thicket and receive Mixing bowl and more info.
The Hardened Mixture
For this step you need to forage 4 items from the Old World.
Frozen Tundra Root from Everfrost Peaks
Sweetened Mudroots from Misty Thicket
Speckled Mushrooms from Innothule Swamp
Heartfruit from Greater Faydark
Combine all 4 in the mixing bowl to get 'A hardened Mixture'.
The Runed Bowl
Go to Timorous Deep and get a crushed pot from Alrik Farsight.
Give crushed pot to Farios Elianos in South Felwithe. Get Grocery list.
Give List to Merchant Nora in North Felwithe. Receive bag of provisions.
Return provisions to Farios Elianos to get receipt for ancient bowl.
Bring receipt to Alrik Farsight in Timorous Deep to get ancient pattern.
Combine pattern, Platinum speckled powder and enchanted clay in pottery wheel to get Runed bowl.
Powder and Clay
Give Kinlo Strongarm in Kaladim a jade reaver (rom City of Mist) for the enchanted clay.
Forage a Rose of Firiona in Firiona Vie and give it to Merdan Fleetwood in Surefall Glades.
Give old wooden painting you receive to human skeleton in Frontier mountains to get a silver chained locket.
Give locket to Niera Farbreeze in Surefall glade for the Platinum speckled powder.
The Stones
Give Ella Foodcrafter the hardened mixture and the runed bowl to get a softly glowing stone.
KIll Venril Sathir and loot the gem he drops.
Give gem from VS and softly glowing stone to Foloal Stormforest in Firiona Vie. Get warm pulsating stone.
Head back to Misty Thicket and give warm stone to Ella Foodcrafter to receive Elaborate Scimitar
Spirit of Faydwer
Kill Corrupted Cyclops in OOT and loot OOT Seavine.
Kill Corrupted Brownie in Lesser Faydark and loot cracked green branch.
Kill Corrupted Seahorse in Kedge keep and loot Kedge crystal.
Give all 3 items to the Pained Unicorn in Lesser Faydark and get gleaming horn.
Give gleaming horn to Silox Azrix in Ak'Anon to get Cleansed Spirit of Faydwer.
Spirit of Antonica
Kill Corrupted Hill Giant in Rathe Mountains and loot ancient rock.
Kill Corrupted Mammoth in EF and loot Chunk of tundra.
Kill Corrupted Aqua Goblin Shaman in Lake Rathe and loot Clean Lakewater.
Give those 3 to Withered treant in NK to receive Warm Pulsing Treant Heart .
Give Warm Pulsing Treant Heart to Yeka Ias in SK and get Cleansed spirit of Antonica..
Spirit of Kunark
Kill Ulump Pujik in Swamp of No Hope and loot essense of froglok.
Kill Corrupted barracuda in Lake of Ill Omen and loot Pure lake water.
Kill Corrupted Gorilla in Emeral Jungle and loot green tree bark.
Kill Faydedar in Timorous Deep and loot Pod of Seawater.
Give these 4 items to Nekexin Virulence in Overthere for the Cleansed Spirit of Kunark.
Finale
Give 3 Cleansed Spirits and the Elaborate Scimitar to Xanuusus in North Karana for the Nature Walkers Scimitar.
