Wizard Epic Checklist
Solomens Quest
Speak to Solomen in the Temple of Ro. Receive a note for Camin Rah.
Go to Erudin and find Camin in the Vasty Deep Inn. Give him the note and hail him. He will request 1000p. Get info on Arantir Karondor.
Travel to Halas and buy a "Ro's Breath" from Dargon for about 1000p. Return to Camin and hand him the potion. Receive new potion.
Back to Halas, give the new potion back to Dargon. She will turn into Arantir Karondor. Receive note/ring to Challice.
In the Felwithe paly guild give the ring to Challice. Get ring back and some dialogue.
Head back to Halas, hand the new ring to Dargon, Arantir will spawn. Get info on the other 3 wizards. Receive note for Kandin Firepot.
The Staves
Collect these 3 items from the other wizards.
From Phinagel Atropos in Kedge Keep: Blue Crystal Staff
From Venril Sathir: Gnarled Staff
From Kandin Firepot quest: Staff of Gabstik
Kandins Quest
Go to Butcherblock and hail Kandin. Chat and receive 'spare gears'.
In the Plane of Fear there is a Broken Golem. Hand him the Gears and then deal with him. Get "green oil".
Go back to Butcherblock and give the Green Oil to Kandin. He will give a note and a Staff of Gabstik for Arantir.
Finishing Up
Make one last trip to Halas, hand the note to Dargon to spawn Arantir again. Hand him all 3 Staff's to get the Pack to give to Solomen.
Go back to Solomen in the Temple of Ro and hand him the pack. He will then give you the Staff of the Four.
