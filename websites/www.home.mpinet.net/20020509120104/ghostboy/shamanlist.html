Shaman Epic Checklist
The Setup
Kill one of these 4 NPC's to start the quest. Capt Surestout in the Ocean of Tears, Binde the Cutpurse in Rathe Mountains, Peg Leg in Butcherblock Mountains, Iksar Outcast or Manslayer in the Field of Bones. Receive Tiny Gem.
In North Freeport look for Bondl Felligan. Give him the Tiny Gem and follow him to the Jade Tiger and meet 2 Spirits. Get another Gem. .
Patience
Go to Eruds Crossing and Give Ooglyn the Gem. Follow Ooglyn into the ocean to meet Abe, do NOT take orders from them. Get another Gem.
Take this Gem to West Karana and give it to the Wandering Spirit.
Wisdom
Go to Rathe Mountains and kill Glaron the Wicked and Tabion the Goodly. Loot Envy, Woe and Marrs Promise.
Give Envy, Woe and Marrs Promise to the Spirit in WK, talk, receive Shield of Falsehood and a Gem.
Strength
Go to Emerald Jungle and find the Spirit Sentinel. Give him the Gem.
Go to Mistmoore and kill Dire Wolf. Loot Dire Pelt.
Give Dire Pelt to Spirit Sentinel, receive Black Fur Boots.
The Writings
Continue conversation with Sentinel and get Booklet.
In the City of Mist, collect the 6 Reports.
---Personal Diary Page.
---Criers Scroll.
---Merchants Letter.
---Priests Diary Page.
---Written Announcement.
---Students Log.
Combine all 6 in the Booklet and return to Sentinel Spirit.
Kill Lord Ghiosk in City of Mist. Loot the 3 books. .
Return to Emerald Jungle, this time to the Spirit Sentinel under the lake and give him the 3 books.
Cleaning up the Mess
Locate the baggie spawn in CoM and take it. This is the Icon.
Go to the Hole. Find High Scale Kirn and give him the Icon. Kill him and loot his ring.
Bring the ring to Neh`Ashiir in CoM. Kill her and loot her Diary.
Give the Diary to the Sentinel in the lake.
Travel to the Plane of Fear and kill "the Iksar Child". Loot the Tear.
Give the Tear to Lord Rak' Ashiir in CoM. Kill him. Loot Scale.
The Spear!
Give Scale to the Sentinel in the lake. Receive Spear of Fate!
