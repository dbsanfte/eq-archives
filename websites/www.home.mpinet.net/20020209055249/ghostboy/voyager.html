The Voyagers are on Xegony server in EverQuest



Voyagers Creed

  • Render aid to, help and protect other Voyagers.
  • To help those in need.
  • Help new players adapt to and achieve a basic understanding of the game.
  • To help fight the injustice and protect the good people of the server whenever possible.


*Note* Not all items on this web site apply to Everquest, The Voyagers guild spans any game we deem worthy to contain us.




. . A little something about this guild to those who may be looking for information or may be interested in joining. We are not, as some have implied an elitist guild. Some people in the guild choose to hunt with others because of long standing friendships, class compatibility and plain old enjoyment of working together. We as a guild have few rules, these will be placed here on the front page so everyone can see.
  • First, if you're looking for a guild who will twink your characters look somewhere else. We do not, as a rule give freebies to lower level people in the guild. If someone offers some left over items they have in their bank that is their choice, but frequently asking for handouts to twink your characters may result in being removed from the guild.
  • Secondly, do NOT ask the guild to help power level your characters. Again, if someone offers their time to help someone level it does not mean they should be requested to do it for anyone who asks. Most likely there are certain circumstances behind it.
  • And lastly, while not being a rule, it is certainly a fact. Getting into our guild can be a bit of a challenge. We have been around for almost 5 years now, spanning several online games and in that time many of us have become to be like a family. Allowing someone new into our family takes trust, which is why many people who dont know anyone in the guild have a hard time getting in. While not an impossible feat, for the most part we just like to make sure that anyone we allow to join us has the right attitude (and sometimes the silly sense of humor) to put up with the normal things we do.
