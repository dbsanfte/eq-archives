Cleric Epic Checklist
Orb of Frozen Water
Kill Lord Bergurgle in Lake rathe, loot "Lord Bergurgles Crown".
Give crown to Shmendrik Lavawalker, watch him die and kill the "spirit of flame". Loot "damaged goblin crown"
Give Crown to Natasha Whitewater. Receive Ornate Sea Shell (I).
Turn in "Ornate Sea Shell (I)" to Omat Vastsea in Timorous Deep and receive "Coral Statue of Tarew" in return.
Go to Temple of Sol Ro and give Coral Statue to a certain seeker who will turn into a plasmatic priest. Kill Priest and loot "blood soaked plasmatic robe."
Kill Lord Gimblox in Sol a and loot Lord Gimblox's signet ring.
Give Blood Soaked Robe to Omat in Timorous Deep to get Orb of Frozen Water and Natasha Whitewater to spawn.
Orb of Clear Water
Give 'copy of notes' to Jeb Lumsed in Burning Woods. Get 'Jeb's Seal'.
Give Lord Gimblox's ring to Natasha Whitewater and receive Ornate seashell (II)
Give Ornate Seashell to Naxot Deepwater in Burning woods. Receive Note to Natasha and then kill Ixiblat Fer. Loot Sceptre of Ixiblat Fer
Go to Chardok and kill Overking Bathezed and loot a singed scroll.
Give Scepter and scroll to Omat to get Orb of Clear Water
Orb of Vapor
Give Note to Natasha to get a Shimmering Pearl.
Give pearl to Zordak Ragefire and kill him. Then kill his true form Zordakalicus Ragefire, loot his heart.
Return heart to Omat and get Orb of Vapor.
Orb of the Triumvirate
Give all 3 orbs to Jhassad Oceanson in Timorous deep to get the Orb of the Triumvirate.
Water Sprinkler of Nem Ankh
Locate the Avatar of Water and hand it the Orb of the Triumvirate to receive the Water Sprinkler of Nem Ankh.
