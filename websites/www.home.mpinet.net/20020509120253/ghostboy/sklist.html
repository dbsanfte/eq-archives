Shadowknight Epic Checklist
Shafted!
Get a Darkforge Breastplate.
Get Darkforge Legs.
Get Darkforge Helm.
Bring the 3 pieces of armor and 900 plat to Overthere and give it to Kurron Ni.
Listen to him ramble then kill Kurron Ni and loot the Letter to Duriek.
The Cure
In Paineel give the Letter to Duriek Bloodpool.
Go to Neriak and find Smaka in Foreign Quarter. Give him 1000 plat to get cough elixir.
Return to Paineel and give the Elixir to Duriek.
The Tome
Find and kill a ratman guard in The Hole. Loot 'A Dusty Tome'
Give the Tome to Duriek. Go to next part.
The Corrupted Blade
Get a Ghoulbane from the Shin Lord in Upper Guk, or do the quest.
Get the Soul Leech from Cazic Thule.
Get the Blade of Abrogation from the Plane of Sky
Get Decrepit Sheath from Teydar in Qeynos Aquaducts.
Kill Rharzar in Rathe Mountains and loot a Drake Spine..
Loot a Decrepit hide from an ashenbone drake in Plane of Hate.
Give the Spine, Hide and a bar of enchanted Plat to Teydar for the Sheath.
Give Ghoulbane, Soul Leech, Blade of Abrogation and Sheath to Duriek. Receive Corrupted Ghoulbane.
The Ritual
Go to Tox and talk to Knarthenne Skurl.. Receive Soul Case.
In Kerra speak to Marl Kastane and get the seal of Kastane.
Go to Paineel and give the Seal to Gerot Kastane. Receive Note to Marl.
Bring note back to Marl in Kerra. Get info.
In the Hole, kill Mimics until you get a Cell Key
Give Cell Key to Caradon in the Hole. Kill Caradon and Kyrenna. Loot Blood and Heart of Kyrenna.
Give Blood to Marl and get a Dark Shroud.
Back to the Hole. Give the Dark Shroud to Ghost of Glohnor.
Kill Mummy of Glohnor and loot Head of Glohnor and Glohnor wrappings.
Give Head of Glohnor to Gerot Kastane, get Head of the Valiant.
Give Glohnor Wrappings to Marl. Get Will of Innoruuk.
Combine Heart of Kyrenna in the Soulcase to get the Soul of the Innocent.
In the City of Mist hand the Corrupted Ghoulbane, the Heart of the Innocent, the Head of the Valiant, and the Will of Innoruuk to Lhranc.
Kill Lhranc and loot Innoruuk's Curse.
