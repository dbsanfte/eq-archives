Southern Ro

Our first guild event took place in South Ro. After the GuildMeeting, we all TP'd to SRo, and were quickly transformed into a pack of Ravenous Dogs! As the 2 groups of CoS members zoned in, a comment was heard at the Druid Ruins.. "Someone call the pound!"

 

Shortly thereafter, we ran into Terrorantula! This was not to be our only meeting with him today. Once the CoS engaged him, he was no longer a threat to the denizens of SRo.

At this point, we scoured the zone, soon to find The Ancient Cyclops! He was no match for the CoS, and his death supplied Darice with a brand new pair of JourneyMans boots!
