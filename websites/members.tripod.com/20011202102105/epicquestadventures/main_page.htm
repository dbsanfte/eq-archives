Welcome to the Epic Quest Adventures.  My name is Aline Griffonfeather and I am a Wizard in the lands of Povar.  Here you will find complete walkthroughs of all the EverQuest Epic Quests.  I made this site to have a place where people could go to find Epic Quest Info.  Sure there are other sites with info specifically for their class but none where there are all of them together.

-Aline Griffonfeather
