Welcome to the home page of Equites Justitium.  We are a guild on EverQuest's Rathe server dedicated to following the path of Good and defending those in need.

 

We are an informal guild.  We do not have "departments" or "divisions."  There are no dues required.  We don't worry about rank.  We're here to have fun.  Of course we are also a new guild, so some of this might change over time as we develop a personality as desired by our members.

 

For our members, this will a place to come and keep up to date on what's happening in our guild, as well as keep in touch with each other.  For those who are not members and are here because they are curious about us, drop us an e-mail and we'll anwer any questions!
