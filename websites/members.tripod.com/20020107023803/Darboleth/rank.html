Titles/Responsibilites
 
With each and every rank, even wanre, there will be responsibilites following that rank.
 
Title/Rank Task/Responsiblity
Wanre Wanre shall travel with the members of the house and get used to the house. The Wanre should try their best to role-play and speak in dark elven, like the regular members of the house. There will be only 10 Wanre at any given time.
Velg'larn Will serve as scouts and they may do small attacks on enemies. All of the Rogues of the house will follow the path of the Velg'larn. There will be only 10 Velg'larn at any given.
Sargtlin The main fighters of the house; they will always be in the front lines of a battle. All of the Warriors of the house will follow the path of the Sargtlin. There will be only 10 Sargtin at any given time.
Malla Velg'larn These elves have showed their battle and stealth skills in the midst of combat and they have rised to the top. They shall also do the more important missions that the velg'larn would not be able to do. There will only be 3 Malla Velg'larn at any given time.
Malla Sargtlin These warriors have proven themselves in battle, and they have risen to the top. These will be the important warriors and the battle and they will be treated with great respect. There will be only 3 Malla Sargtlin at any given time.
C'rintri del Azuviir These are the Nobles of the house. They will serve as the Patrons personal advisors and they shall also serve as diplomats for the house. There will be only 3 C'rintri del Azuviir at any given time.
Qu'el'velguk This is the best assassin of the entire house. The Qu'el'velguk will teach the Velg'larn the art of combat and stealth. The Qu'el'velguk is responsible for the Velg'larn's combat and stealth abilities. The Qu'el'velguk will do the most important missions and the most secret missions, often working with the Malla Velg'larn to complete them. There will be only 2 Qu'el'velguk at any given time.
Qu'el'saruk This is the best warrior of the entire house. The Qu'el'saruk will teach the Sargtlin about combat tactics and the Qu'el'saruk is responsible for the Sargtlin's combat abilites. There will be only 2 Qu'el'saruk at any given time.
