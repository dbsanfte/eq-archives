EverQuest related links

  • Essential

    • EverQuest Portal
      The official EverQuest page at Sony with basic information about the game.
    • EverQuest at Stratics
      My daily source for information about EQ. Also hosts a bunch of subsites.
    • EQ'lizer
      The ultimate EverQuest Item Spoiler Site
    • EQ Vault
      Lotsa information. Almost as complete as Stratics.
    • AllaKhazam's Magical Realm
      Probably the best quest infos out there.

  • Class Specific Sites

    • The Rangers Glade
      Our home on the web. Ranger information through and through. Also great collection of maps.
    • EQ Druids
      Complete information about the druid class. A must read even for rangers.
    • EQ Casters Realm
      Probably the best information page for INT caster related onformation (NEC, WIZ, MAG, ENC).
    • EQ Cleric
      Class specific information about the clerics. Plus zone guides to undead realms.

  • General Information Sites

    • The Quester of Norrath
      Simply the best hand drawn maps available. A must stop-by for everyone.
    • EverQuest Trade Council
      Guides to all trades that are available in the world of Norrath.A subpage of...
    • Realms of Everquest
      A compemdium site like Stratics or EQVault.
    • EverQuest at Mystics
      German EverQuest page. Translations of english sources, but also a lot of original stuff.
    • EQ Online
      One more compendium page like Stratics or the Vault.
    • Everworld.net
      Quite surprisingly this is another 'vault' type site with tons of information.
    • EverQuest Express
      The EverQuest Express is a weekly newsmagazine featuring many articles on roleplaying and strategy.
    • Norrath Roleplay
      About 'serious' roleplaying in Norrath. Also features the Copper Compendium, a nice fanzine.
    • Xexol's Everquest Site
      'Low-budget' site but a ton of useful information about trades, skills and equipment.
