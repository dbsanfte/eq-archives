THURGADIN WIZARD ARMOR

The Wizard Armor in Thurgadin is given out by Mauren Frostbear in, of course, Thurgadin.  She can be found in the store called Frostbear's Furs.   For each quest you will need torn enchanted silk and then the piece of armor you are getting.  The silk is most abundantly dropped in Kael Drakkal off of 56 or higher level giants.  It seems that there is major dropage in The Arena part of Kael Drakkal so you might want to try this out.

The Boots

You say, 'what boots?'
Mauren Frostbeard says 'Three crushed jaundice gems combined with torn enchanted silk boots would make a most interesting set of footwear. The latent powers of the gems can be harnessed to create magical effects.

The crushed jaundice gems are random drops off of high level mobs on the bottom of Icewell Keep.  This zone is off of Thurgadin.

The Effect, O'Keils Radiation, is a 2 dmg. damage shield.

 

The Cap

You say, 'what cap'
Mauren Frostbeard says 'For an enchanted head guard bring me a torn enchanted silk turbin as well as three pieces of crushed flame opal.

The crushed flame opals are random drops off of high level mobs on the bottom of Icewell Keep.  This zone is off of Thurgadin.

 

The Robe

You say, 'what robe?'
Mauren Frostbeard says 'If you wish to have a fine robe, bring unto me a torn enchanted silk robe and three pristine emeralds. Then you will receive a robe beyond that of your wildest dreams.

The pristine emeralds are very rare gems.  There are a few places so far that have been confirmed to drop them.  One place is Siren's Grotto off of very high level mobs.  Another place is Dragon Necropolis.  The high level rats here also known as the Chetari, drop the pristine emeralds but very rarely.  It would take about a week to get three of them.  The last place I know of is Velketor's Labyrinth.  Just like other zones, the emeralds drop off of high level mobs here, usually kobolds.

 

The effect, Enticement of Flame, is a direct damage spell and the first Lure Spell for wizards.  It does 360 damage and can hit monsters that usually resist your other spells.

 

The Pantaloons

You say, 'what leggings?'
Mauren Frostbeard says 'Three nephrite and a pair of torn enchanted silk leggings will net you a fine set of pantaloons.

The three nephrites drop most commonly in Cobalt Scar off of Wyverns.  The Wyverns are easily quad-kited for they are only level 38-42.

The Effect, Elemental Armor, adds 30 resist to both your Fire and Cold Resist.

 

The Gloves

You say, 'What gloves?'
Mauren Frostbeard says ' If you seek gloves of great power, bring to me three crushed topaz and a torn enchanted silk gloves. I will use the power of the gems to imbue the gloves.

The crushed topaz have been confirmed to drop in Icewell Keep off of high level mobs on the lower levels. 

 

The Sleeves

You say, 'what sleeves'
Mauren Frostbeard says 'Bring unto me a set of torn enchanted silk sleeves as well as three flawed topaz. With these in my possession I can create a set of enchanted sleeves the likes of which spell casters like you dream of.

The flawed topaz can be found in the Armor Suits in Wakening Lands.  The creatures here are high level and the drops are medium rare.

 

The Wrist Bands

You say, 'what wristbands'
Mauren Frostbeard says 'An enchanted silk wrist guard and three crushed onyx sapphires is all that I require to create an imbued wrist wrap of great power. Seek out these items and return to me when you have acquired them.

The crushed onyx sapphires drop location are unknown to me at the moment.  If you know where they drop please send me the information.  I am guessing they are dropped in Kael Drakkal, Cobalt Scar, or Icewell Keep.

The Effect, Chill Sight, is an ultravision self-only spell.  Since Dark Elves already have ultravision, the effect is useless to them.
