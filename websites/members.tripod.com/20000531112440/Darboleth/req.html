Requirements
 
These are our standards if you wish to join.

 

 
 
1. You must be able to role-play or be willing to learn to role-play. 

2. You must be of the Dark Elven race. 

3. Only Warriors and Rogues are accepted into House Azuviir 

4. You must be able to attend most of the meetings, if you miss most of the meetings and do not contact the Patron of the house or you will be deemed inactive and be dropped from the house. 

5. You must respect other members of the house, especially those of a higher rank than you. 

6. You must be able to keep a secret...secret house information should only be kept to yourself. 

7. Have fun!
