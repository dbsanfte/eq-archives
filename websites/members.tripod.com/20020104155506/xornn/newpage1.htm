I would like to post a copy of the quest I used, too. It is unedited. I found it very helpful.

One other note: I still use the pouch needed to combine all the coins, to hold gemstones. It IS a 10-slot container, but only holds Tiny items.

THIS IS THE MASSIVE QUEST FOR THE TASHANIA SPELL (level 44 enchanter spell) DETAILS

Romar Sunto offers to give you the tashania scroll if you can find 10 coins in different cities around the world. He will sell you a book on where to find all 10 coins for 50gp and will give you a pouch to combine them in. The coins are called an old silver coin, and can be found in the following places:

(1.) Ak'anon - Near great wheel (not in water);
(2.) Kaladim - Pool by warrior's guild;
(3.) Erudin - in the pool outside the guilds in the palace;
(4.) Qeynos - Under Temple of Life UFO in the Koalindl Fish Pool;
(5.) Halas - In lake in front of city to the left of docks on a ledge;
(6.) Oggok - in lake near warrior guild at the north end;
(7.) Grobb - in pool near bar where you first enter the zone;
(8.) Neriak - Ouside blind fish window near ice comet rod;
(9.) Felwithe - at the edge of the fishing pond; and
(10.) Freeport - Under the mage guild building in the water.

Take the 10 coins and combine them in the coin pouch to get a "Coin Of Tash". Next, Take the coin to Tarn Visilin in the Highkeep Library and give him the coin. He will enchant it into a glowing coin of tash and give it back. Then take the coin to Mizr N'Mar in Neriak Library and give him the coin. He enchants it into a gleaming coin of tash. Then take the coin to Raine Beteria in Erudin Library and give her the coin. It is important to note that you must have a high faction with her or she will just keep the coin and give you nothing in return. Casting Illusion Erudite and Benevolence before giving her the coin should take care of this problem. Take the coin that she gives you and return it to Romar and receive the Tashania scroll (level 44 enchanter spell), which is no drop, so if you want this spell you have to do this quest.
