A Mystery....

by Jack of Swords.

I have been hunting around the tower in North Karana with my rogue for several levels now, and every time I passed by the two empty farms behind the tower I would hear humanoid footsteps. They concentrated in one particular spot, but there was never anything visible there. I did a thorough check of the buildings and roofs in the vicinity but never found anything, so finally I decided to bring in my wizard.
Shaidaxi is only level 8, but he has See Invisible and a radiating AE spell, Numbing cold. With another young Wyrm, Siaballa, for a witness and invis'er, I ran down to NK to check it out. Found the spot, and Siaballa told me that she could hear the footsteps too, though barely; she had her music on, while I always turn mine off. See Invis turned up nothing, so I started casting my AE.
