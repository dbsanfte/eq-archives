In the distant past, Veeshan--the Wyrm Queen--found Norrath and claimed it with a swipe of a crystalline claw.  Jealous gods followed her, eager to displace her kin... dragons.  Now it is time for the Conclave of the Wyrm to make our mark on the young cultures of this wild land.  Who you ask?  Let me tell you about the Conclave...

Sanctioned Guild:  Conclave of the Wyrm
Server: Xegony

With the rising of the Mists, we find ourselves among the denizens of Norrath.  They treat us as one of them.  Sometimes they note our odd accents or other-worldly ways, but they are easily convinced of our intentions.  We craft weapons and armor, assisted by dwarves.  We practice the new magics and wield them like a surgeon's scalpel.  We gather a hoard--coins, magics, armor, arms, and books of lore, like we once did on Cynnabaris.  Very few are aware of the game we play. 

The Conclave, after years of exile, seeks to expand their influence again, this time in Norrath.  New gods oppose us.  New peoples believe us.  A few old friends remember us and share a drink in passing.  With time, we grow and regain our former glory.  As much as things change, we are still dragons at heart.  For more history of the guild, visit the home of the Conclave.

All good things come to those who wait.



The Conclave is not currently accepting 'off the street' submissions for membership.  If you are interested in joining the Conclave of the Wyrm, then hook up with some of us, adventure with us, and be our friend, our comrade, our hunting partner, and once you are known to us,  apply to Windheavin or one of the officers, in person, and speak privately about joining.


"It was a challenge adapting to a life without magic in Tanarus.  But Norrath is much more like my home of Neverwinter and my magic has returned.  Come join us in Norrath as we explore Xegony and make a name for ourselves again."
