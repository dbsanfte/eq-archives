Wyrm.  Serpent.  Drake.   Monster.  We exist on all worlds in some form, we are a Constant in the equation of Reality.    Some people's realities include us as monsters who's appetite, greed and viciousness is only matched by it's cunning and power.   Others have us as spirits of Wisdom and Magic.  The Wyrm symbolizes Power, Wisdom and Wealth to many peoples.  We are symbols of royalty, and of savage intellegence and calculating, destructive violence.  Immortal, yet able to perish at the hands of mortal men, We are Paradox.  We live and watch the world of mortals change around us, wilst we remain, like a standing stone around which sheep graze.

We move amongst you, often unseen, undetected,  and unnoticed, for we are never what you think we should be. 

We are amongst you, Mortals. 

To Join with us, you must first submit yourself to the inspection of the master of the rookery - to enter without joining with us is fine, but you will never know power within the Conclave if you do not submit yourself to the sensitives of the Conclave - we want no enemies amongst us, and it is within our power to deny you.

The symbol of the Egg is one of birth and rebirth, new beginnings.  Touch it, Seeker, if you wish to pass unchallenged within the Aerie of the Conclave.
