2nd Edition Player's Handbook

Charm Person / Suggestion: These spells are switched in level. In other words, Suggestion is treated as a first level spell and Charm Person as a third level spell.

Cure Critical Wounds:  This spell has been moved to fourth level.

Cure Serious Wounds: This spell has been moved to third level.

Cone of Cold: This spell has a maximum damage of 15d4+15 (15th level caster).

Dispel Magic: This spell can be used to target an individual spell, effect, person, or item, or all spell effects within the Dispel Magic’s area of effect. Against a Minor Globe or other negation, the Dispel Magic has no effect unless it is directed at the individual globe spell.

Endure Cold / Heat: Magical damage of a different type (ie. Cold vs. an endure heat) does NOT end the spell in this campaign setting.

Find Familiar: This spell has been moved to the "universal" school of magic. Any mage can cast it, regardless of specialization and opposition schools. Likewise, a more powerful version exists, as found in the Familiar section.

Fireshield: This spell is not disrupted by magic resistance. Rather, the magic resistant creature is simply immune to damage from that particular engagement.

Glyphs, Wards, Firetraps, and Related Spells: These spells cannot be placed on weapons, devices or objects to be used in combat. The intent of these spells is as traps.

Polymorph Self: During a Polymorph Self spell, the caster’s items merge with him or her unless the caster specifies otherwise. Items still work even if merged if they are constantly running itmes (such as a Ring of Protection).

Poison, Slay Living, and Destruction: Reduces an opponent to zero HP minus d8, and they are dying. See also lethal poison.

Stoneskin: This spell is not used in this campaign setting.

Teleport / Wizard Mark: If the mage leaves his wizard mark at a location, he receives a +/- 1 modifier to the probability chart for teleportation, in his favor.

Transmute Rock to Mud: Victims of this spell sink as per the Priest version of the spell, not the mage version, regardless of caster.

Complete Wizard's Handbook

Chromatic Orb: This spell has been revised and can be found in Saphrael’s Grimoire.

Tome of Magic

Dimensional Folding: This spell only works between two sites which are both holy ground, are known to the caster, and both have been personally blessed by the caster. If these conditions are fulfilled, the spell works without chance of failure.

Time Pool: A Time Pool can only be used to view events in which the caster was present or which took place where the Priest casts the spell. The priest must name the specific event he or she is attempting to view and make any checks appropriate based on how difficult the viewing is, as determined by the DM.

Seven Sisters

Blood Lightning: This spell requires 1 chit to activate if used under the Revised Initiative System.

Iron Maiden: This spell is not used in this campaign setting.

Laerael’s Disrobement: A caster may only have one Laerael’s Disrobement in effect at any time. The duration is 24 hours and the spell it contains must be a personal use spell (no ranged or offensive magic).
