Twilight Company's Code of Conduct

Do unto others as you would have them do unto you.

Halfling version: Be Nice. Nice is Good.

Almost all of the following rules can be summed up in the above statements.  When in doubt, refer to the primary rule of the guild.

Conduct and Behavior

  • /shout should never be used for OOC. Ever.
  • Don't beg. Not from other guildmates, not from people outside the guild. Beggers are not welcome in the guild.
  • No OOC names should be considered - outwardly, always be in character whenever possible, and that includes your name.  For Instance: Racecar is not acceptable into this guild.  Buttlicker for a high elf is not acceptable, and maybe not for anyone else, either.
  • Intentional Killstealing is frowned upon.  Everyone has done this by Accident.  Don't do it on purpose.
  • Absolutely NO spamming, scamming, cheating, ninja looting, and other things that you would object to if done to you.  If the only way to get what you need in the group you are in is to ninja-loot -- find another group. These things make the guild look bad. Don't make the guild look bad.
  • Keep it fun at all times - and not just for you. Make sure someone knows you're teasing before you go into a 2 hour tease session. If the other person isn't laughing - it's not funny.   Make sure the other person understands that you are roleplaying if you are in character -- and that they want to roleplay too. Don't force it on non-rp'ers - but recognize that you can stay in character while dealing with them.

Membership

  • Officers must always make an email or a note somewhere in the forum of people they have guilded. Sponser, email address, a little background on them should be included.
  • No one who will not submit an email address where they can be reached is welcome in the guild.  No email address, no guildtag.
  • Signing up on Guildmagic is absolutely required.  If you cannot for some reason reach Guildmagic, then let the Scribe know immeadiatly.
