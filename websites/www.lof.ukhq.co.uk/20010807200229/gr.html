We the Legion of Fate are a group of adventurers.
We are willing to accept members of all classes and races.
Membership is restricted by level only.
New members MUST be at least of level 40 to be considered for entrance into Legion of Fate.
Real life family and close friends may be considered at lvl 15,
but must acquire approval from at least 2 officers.
Twinks of existing members may be liberally invited into the guild, but must acquire the level of 10 first.
Sometimes these new recruits are required to complete a quest to become a full legionnaire.

ALL potential new members MUST be interviewed by an officer prior to receiving an invite.
Also, an established member must sponsor the new applicant prior to entrance.
They are considered on probationary membership for a period of 1 month.
During this time, they will enjoy all rights and privileges of full membership,
however they are to be monitored by members for appropriate behavior.
They will be encouraged to group with other guild members as much as possible during that time.
All concerns involving this new applicant will be brought to the attention of an officer and the sponsor.
Membership in Legion of Fate is a privilege and may be revoked when necessary.
All items dropped during a guild event will go to the class and individual that needs the item.
If more than one member needs the item, it will be randomed by the raid leader.
All looting decisions made by the raid leader are final.
This game is a family oriented game and several member's children also play characters
that are in the Legion of Fate and thus profanity will NOT be tolerated in guild chat.
The first offense will be a warning.
If necessary, a player will be removed if they can not keep the conversation clean,
especially when it offends or is inappropriate.
UNDER NO TIME will racial or gender slurs be tolerated.
Good clean fun is of course encouraged, but please remember
that even though YOU might not find something offensive, others might.
They are entitled to asking you to stop if they find it offensive.
Please be courteous, and STOP!
Requests for members to powerlevel your character are EXPRESSLY forbidden in guild chat.
That is not to say that powerleveling issues can not be discussed in guildchat,
just that you can not ask for it for YOUR character.
It is ok to ask for items for your character in guildsay,
but keep in mind that the purpose of the Guild is for fellowship and fun.
Not for the personal outfitting of your character.
DO NOT presume that members are going to GIVE you items for free for your low level twink.
Is a large body of adventurers or military force consisting of different sizes at different periods.
The Legion of Fate always allows freedom of choice, and nothing can ever take that away from you.
We understand above all others that there is no good without evil, and no light with out dark.
Some of our members will be seen as the Agents of Balance, while the others will be the Fulcrum of Chaos.
One cannot exist with out the other.
You can change the outcome of your fate, however you must always be aware of the hand fate has dealt you.
Remember that Fate encompasses all arranged or predetermined events from your past,
spanning through time and the universe.
Is a power superior to gods and men- swaying all things irresistibly.
Fate is the sum of the laws of the universe,
the product of eternal intelligence and the blind properties of matter.
The purpose of our guild is to provide a organization or family for like minded individuals and to build a organization who’s presence is felt everywhere in Norrath.
We will have members everywhere.
We will sponsor events to bring fame, power, recognition, wealth, and comradery to our guild.
We will seek out knowledge, and embrace our fate above all other things.
Some will hate us, others will embrace us, So be it!But none will ever say we are with out Courage.
We will accept our fate, while others try to elude it.
Fear, bravery, love, and hate are just opposite sides of the coin.
We understand this above all others.
So we are able to use these concepts to our advantage.

 

All Rules can be changed at anytime with out notice.
