Joining Vitae Rising

 

This question is asked so much that we decided to put up this page, and take the opportunity to explain a bit about our group.

First of all there is no structure to VR.  We are basically a group of friends who try to get together in-game.  There are no Allegiance, Level, or Skill requirements to join.  In fact, our only real requirement is "No Jerks".  Some of our members are members of Allegiances, and some are Leaders of Allegiances...but swearing to someone is not, and never will be a requirement to join.  We have some members who are PK,  they are all mature and know how to keep VR fun apart from any PK nastiness.  We try to have regular meetings with the details normally posted on the message board by whoever comes up with an idea.

Who are we looking for?

That's easy.  If you want to have fun with a great group of people we want you.  If you have a good sense of humor we want you.  In short, we want cool people to hang out with =)

What we aren't looking for:

If you are looking to join a large Monarchy we're not for you...see above.  If you are looking for a group to twink you to greatness we're not it.  We value Self Reliance almost more than a sense of humor.  If you can't take care of yourself you can't help out your mates.

How to Join:

Easy.  Hang out on our message board and get to know everyone.  Or /tell one of our members in-game and ask (politely please -- no one is ever REQUIRED to make nice with idiots) if they'd like an extra sword for whatever misadventure they are engaged in.

How to be ignored:

  • Continuously asking if you can join.  When you understand the group you will discover that you largely select yourself for membership.  Asking over and over just annoys us.

  • Acting like a complete moron.  Now granted, we sometimes act stupid, or crazy...but it's done intelligently and in a way that doesn't harm people who don't want to get involved.

  • Pestering current members.  We want new people to join the group, but it is NOT our responsibility to make sure that YOUR needs are catered to.  People will help you if they like you and they have the time to spare.  Remember, they are players too and they are trying to use their precious game time to have fun.

  • Allegiance Pimping.  There is no faster way to be squelched and ignored.
