Tumeroks in Socks
by Pele
17 February 2000

Lumbering Oaf introduced me to hordes of Tumeroks near Fort Teth last night in an adventure full of vitae and idiocy (the former mostly his, the latter mostly mine). I will make it into a story for my three-year-old -- goes like this.

See Pele run. Run, Pele, run. Pele is lost. Help, Oaf, help. Where is Teth? Oaf sends coords. Run, Pele, run! Wasp chases Pele. Shadow chases Pele. Run, Pele, run! Pele finds Teth. Where is LS? Help, Oaf, help! Oaf sends coords. Pele finds LS.

Go see Tumeroks. Run through wasps. Run through rats. Help, Oaf, help! Oaf sends buffs. Jump in portal. Pele fights Tumeroks. Oaf sends heals. Help, Oaf, help! Pele fights reedsharks. Oaf sends heals. Pele fights golems. Oaf sends vulns. Pele fights High Tumerok. High Tumerok wastes Pele! Help, Oaf, help!

Pele sees LS. Oaf sees LS. Must have revenge!

Pele and Oaf run back. Oaf vulns drudges. Pele flames drudges. Pele makes level 20! Hooray for Pele! Hooray for Oaf!

Run through wasps. Run through rats. Help, Oaf, help! Jump in portal. Run through dungeon. Oaf picks locks. *Oaf* fights High Tumerok. Pele gets corpse.

Oaf calls portal. Oaf jumps in portal. Pele jumps in portal. Nothing happens. Oaf mistypes "double-click." Pele is confused. Portal vanishes! Pele is alone in dungeon!

High Tumerok respawns! Help, Oaf, help! Pele waits for Oaf. Oaf must run through dungeon alone!

Oaf kills Tumeroks. Oaf kills reedsharks. Oaf kills golems. Oaf runs out of amber! No more heals. No more stam-to-mana. Poor Oaf!

Oaf says, "heal." Oaf shows up on radar. Heal, Pele, heal. Oaf fights Tumerok Priest. Pele heals. Oaf makes it!

Oaf says, "Components are for wimps." Pele gives Oaf 12 ambers anyway. Heal, Oaf, heal.

Oaf makes portal. Oaf waits for Pele. Pele double-clicks on portal. Pele lands in surface room. Pele runs wrong way. Pele gets lost. Oaf says, "Come back." Pele comes back. Oaf is standing next to right portal. Pele jumps in. Oaf jumps in.

Oaf makes portal to Dereth. Pele jumps in. Oaf jumps in. Run to Crag. Jump to Hebian. Run to Mayoi. Home, Oaf, home!

Pele is tired. Very, very, tired. Pele says goodnight. Oaf says goodnight. Pele goes to bed.

3:30 A.M.: Marcus wakes up.
