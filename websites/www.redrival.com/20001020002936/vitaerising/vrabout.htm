About Vitae Rising

Formed on 13 September 99, Vitae Rising is a collection of adventurers who get together semi-regularly to explore the dungeons and other dangerous areas in the game Asheron's Call.  VR was originally to be a guild in the UO and EQ sense.  But the only system for showing this relationship in-game is the Allegiance system.  In essence Turbine has told us "Use the Allegiance system or use nothing".  I didn't particularly want people swearing allegiance to me.  All I wanted to do was bring people together to have fun.  So we therefore went the "nothing" route.  We are an extended Fellowship, which basically means a group of friends.  Members are of many professions, and there are no level or "class" requirements to join in.  Members are not required to swear Allegiance to anyone.  An oath of allegiance is a personal matter that shouldn't be taken lightly.  

If you are looking for a group to adventure with now and then, and you follow our one rule (NO JERKS), then you are more than welcome to join in.  All you have to do is  drop by the message board and let us know who you are.  Just a warning though...we die.  A lot.  In droves sometimes.  The way we see it, if an adventure doesn't end with a large body count then we weren't challenging ourselves enough =)  It's not that we're suicidal or anything...just that we seem to have more than our fair share of "adventurous" Adventurers...heh.  

The name Vitae Rising has no special significance.  It's something I came up with as a fellowship name sometime near the beginning of beta2.  I liked it so much (and it was oh, so appropriate most of the time) that I decided to use it as the name for this group when it was formed.

Come on along!  And bring some ideas with you...sometimes we just sit around and look at each other until someone comes up with a place to go =)
