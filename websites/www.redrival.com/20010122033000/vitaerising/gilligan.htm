RB's Island
by Rabbitsbane

To the tune of the Gilligan's Island theme.

This is a bit of doggerel about one of the first adventures I shared with Lurky and Oaf back in the late fall. Enjoy! -RB

Just sit right back and you’ll hear a tale, a tale of a fateful trip
That started out near Mayoi port but never boarded ship
The mage was a fifteenth-level life, Lurky surnamed Lost
Who lifted vitae every week and never counted costs.
The tank was an Oaf of great renown who lumbered to and fro,
From mines in the ice-encrusted north to dungeons here below.
The noobs were eager for a thrill, Terminus and RB
And wary of the monsters small, great heroes they would be.

(Insert storm-at-sea sound fx)

The weather started getting rough – our tiny crew was tossed
Into the arms of mosswart swarms: “Fear not!” said Lurky Lost.
“Just run straight past me, do not wait. I’ll curse them all I know
And we’ll get you to level ten before we have to go.”
Then hordes of barkers joined the fun, with shamans casting spells –
Before RB knew what they were she rang the lifestone’s bells.
“Don’t sit there, Terminus!” rang out. “Sit where?” and he was slain
A pair of spawning Lugians were now Terminus’ Bane.
The Oaf leapt up to take them on and soon their lewt was won
But then the Zofrit Zefirs came to join the monsters’ fun.
We ran like screaming schoolgirls, Terminus and I
And even Lurky left a corpse beneath the gloaming sky.
But when the midnight hour had come and gone in two time zones
We’d brought our vitae down again and cleared away our bones
So every time we meet again I hail the VR crew
(Although I never make it to a Thursday night with you)
And wish you all adventures bright and shining as a star
In Dereth’s vales and Direlands’ plains, or wherever you are.
