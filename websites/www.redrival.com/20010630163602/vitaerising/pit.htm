The Pit
by Zaketh
28 November 1999

It was another fine day in Dereth that found myself, Putty, and Dany in Stonehold. We had trekked here to adventure in the dungeon known as the pit. Little did we know that we would be cursing it as the Pit of Hell.

Our trip up while long had some minor adventures. However, after visiting Stonehold Lifestone we set upon the trail to the pit. Btw, these Lifestones are notorious gossips. Not 2 seconds after attuning putty and me are made fun of by the lifestone and how he hopes to see us a few times. I resemble…oops resent that comment.

We reached the entrance in short order and found it empty of guards. For some reason I don’t like something that isn’t defended. It either means that what’s in there isn’t worth anything or its so nasty it doesn’t need guards.

Again, I have the hackles rise on my neck and misjudge it for battle fever instead of premonition of imminent vitae. You would think I would have learned this by now…makes me wonder if my helmet really does protect my head.

Well upon entering we find there are many many monougas on many levels. Of course a welcoming committee of about 4 Rough Monougas try to give us their weapons in an aggressive display of niceness. “Oh thank <ouch> you <ouch>, be careful <ouch> you could put someone’s eye out with that <ouch>.”, that’s when Dany roles her eyes and says they are guards. Oh…sorry…then I lay into them and slaughter the whole bunch for their deceit.

The trip down the levels mainly consisted of this practice of finding a whole bunch of monougas and inserting iron into their diet. Putty and Dany are two true companions a tank really likes to have. Especially seeing as they keeps this tanks keister guarded. We hacked and slashed our way down down down the many levels. On the way many close fights were fought with many Rough, Crude, and Wild Monougas cursing our names to their many many deaths. Along the way two weak and piddly cousins of the Skel Mines Skeleton Lord got in our way and we dispatched them with ease. They call themselves lords they should be ashamed of themselves. We continued our downward trek with much decent loot gathering.

Little did we know the one female on the trek was in for a surprise near the bottom. It seems Wild Monougas had gotten satellite and DirectTV out in the sticks. They all seemed to be great soccer fans too. Because they treated Dany like a soccer ball and sent her through the goal (lifestone). I hope these guys aren’t Rockette fans too. Many would flee in fear of a line of high stepping Monougas.

Being so far down in the dungeon myself and putty continued down to the exit portal at the bottom. I did jump and head down to the portal room dodging many spells cast my way. There while narrowly dodging flung spells and many many skeletons I did spy the true master of this fell place. He was the “Revenant”. Well lucky for me I have practiced the run like a school girl maneuver extensively and was able to lure most away from the exit so I could duck back in and get out.

While waiting for the return of my intrepid companion Dany putty succumbed to Monougas in his failed attempt to return the way we came. Sigh hardly here an hour and two deaths. We are getting really good at this. So when we were gathered again we did set into the dungeon again and did thus make havoc on the way down. Along the way another group of hardy adventurers did pass us by. We did pass them and fell in beside and thus a great battle took place with many Wild Monougas who had watched too much soccer.

During this grand melee with spells, blades, and blood a flying did I find myself the soccer ball of a Wild Monouga. Man they kick hard. Made me feel like a drudge. Thus I was forced to execute a run like a school girl maneuver. But alas my mighty companion Putty fought to save me from this Pele of the Monougas. Little effect did this have on the monouga because he kicked me right to the lifestone.

Brrr….where is that draft coming from….HEY!….where’s all my armor. Thus I said while covering myself from a snickering Lifestone. I swear this one had a rotten attitude. After using choice language to stem the snickering, which failed, I retreated to town to replace my armor.

While I was flashing the lifestone Putty was able to recover his items from a corpse of his. Dany was also lucky to recover her stuff too. Thus after purchasing MUCH inflated platemail…damn sticks vendors think they can gouge adventurers because the nearest competition is miles away….the nerve. I digress though.

Upon entering the pit again I was told by my companions that they would work up while I work down. Well along the way the Revenant cast a lag spell on me and I was set upon by packs of evil monougas. HA! I escaped two of these evil traps. I was feeling good so I continued along the way…that is when the last vile trap was sprang on me. There I was running for my life when Putty tells me Dany had succumbed to a pack of Wilds and he was running for his life. Damn the irony. Again heading down with many fell creatures a following I did get cornered a final time and was giving a trip to the smart-ass lifestone.

Of course by the time we get there he is still laughing at Dany rendition of a soccer ball. When I appear he goes into unintelligible howls of laughter. The nerve of some Lifestones. Well this time I was fully clothed just missing choice items.

At this point Dany had lost much armor that was replaced by some highly inflated plate. Bet that vendor saw us coming. Greedy Aluvians! Much was done to lighten Dany’s load so she was not carrying too much. Alas putty was to soon join us in town after making it to the site of my most recent death.

For the third time we did venture into this festering pit. I must confess the Revenant runs a tighter ship than the Skel Lord out in his mine. I mean this place was nasty compared to the mine. After much fighting Putty and me did retrieve our gear.

After this we did proceed to fight its way down again with many close calls to our lives. Shortly after recovering our companion Dany’s things we were set upon by Pele monouga again. Alas before we could dispatch this soccer playing monouga Dany fell again and was to weary to continue.

Putty and me pressed on to the site of my first death. Dangit I had some sweet armor I wanted back. After some conjoling Putty and me continued on cautiously fighting and winning our way close. There we were in the hall to the room where I died. Thus we played a cautious game of bait the monouga.

Well it didn’t work. There we were set upon by three of the pains in the butt. Unfortunately, after dispatching one together we split our attention. Wouldn’t you know I get the soccer fan. Well I taught this soccer fan what’s what. Putty on the other hand was forced to execute a running schoolgirl maneuver. After dispatching soccer boy I healed and went to rescue putty. I succeeded in delaying the monouga but before Putty could realize he was safe another Monouga sent him packing. After dispatching the last Monouga I retrieved my sweet armor.

At this point putty too weary from the fight had to bow out. There I was alone in a hostile pit. What is a tank to do. Well I will tell you what I did. I ran my ass off to get out. There is where I met two nice people who had cleared the way for me with out knowing it. I love adventuring mages and archers who make it easier for me to escape. After much profuse shoe kissing and thank you’s I made my escape back to Stonehold where I got a cool drink and reflected on the long bad day I just had.
