A Day In The Life Of Lurky The Lost
by Lurky The Lost

It started last Friday, that was the date
When she decided things must change from the current rate
For her Baron was mighty, known through the realm
She had great envy of his ability to get the Olthoi helm

How does he do it? How can this be?
To have such great power, and not suck like me?
It must be the robe, the one that’s all green
The whisper came forth, in her mind she had seen

She would have this power, she would have this great might
The green robe would be hers, it would be in her sight
But how do I get this? I know not the quest
But it’s Kilk you should be talking too, he knows all the best

So Saturday the 27th I asked him with glee
“The secret of Frore will you teach me?”
“Ofcourse,” he said. “You must do just this.
Run to the second stone, and the letter you won’t miss”

So handing me the book, that he had made
Of his previous adventure to Frore and the raid
Where he had died, just after getting the lewt
And the Gelidite Lord had turned him into a newt

Yes Kilk had died with all but the robe
The Baron had it, in it he now strode
So a return trip was planned, Kilk was quite willing
Of finding phat lewt, in the frost we’d go milling

But first we must have two Lich Lord seals
While Kilk and I ate online our evening meals
The Monarch instead went out for Chinese
Oaf was the only one, who could take off with ease

He had one of these seals from a Lich Lord
But only he could kill one, and vitae WE could not afford
So after dinner and a great deal of lag
Oaf returned and tried to find this bag

But the booty did not give up without a fight
The Lich Lords had killed him off with might
So another day in the week he would return
To seek a 2nd seal, those Liches would burn!

The Lich Lords would pay, each and everyone
But now for the key of Frore, we had none
So camping monoliths 2 and 3
2 copies of each transcript we got, Kilk and me

Oaf would return during the week and would try
To kill more Lich Lords, oh man they would fry!
The date was set, on April first
For Frore how we had such a great thirst

During the week Oaf tried as he could
No seal had been given as it should
So a copy of each transcript, I had
Except for the first, and we were quite mad

During the week I went forth with Zaketh and glee
To the transcriber in Zaikhal we went, he and me
We got one complete list of portal coords
To give to the lady at the plateau with some words

The party was planned for each and everyone
Kilk, Chan, Zaketh, Lurky and the Lumbering one
But then it struck on Friday night
Kilk had something come up in real life

A postpone was put forth on April Fool’s day
Perhaps a good omen, what can you say?
So Sunday morning at eleven I turned on
The computer of which I am so fond

It reminded me this, it reminded me well
It’s Daylight Savings Time day, oh Hell!
The party was set, it was set today
“Oh god I hope they know the time change, NO WAY!”

But everyone showed, every last one
Everyone was ready to have lots of fun
Dany had joined us, and we muled with delight
We didn’t want to loose any goodies tonight

So Zaketh the mighty Sorceror indeed
Pulled up a portal to Plateau without heed
To transport there swiftly, oh yes
Running up there would have been quite a mess

So quickly we entered, and Zaketh did change
To his melee character, no longer the sage
I buffed and hit everyone for cold
I and Oaf knew the magic, all were told

“If the magic wears out, I gotta know”
“If you die inside, you get portaled pronto”
So everyone was careful, and I asked Kilk
“Do I give this transcription to the chick in the silk?”

“Yes,” was the reply from all that were there
“Are you all ready?” I asked with my hands in my hair
Oh I must have this goody, I must have the robe
I want to be known like my Monarch, throughout the globe

So in we went and we noticed with glee
Frore was empty, it was just we
All the kills and phat lewt were ours to share
Everyone got something but Oaf, which was unfair

There was not enough for everyone in the room
Dany got from Frore the great Ice Heaume
Chan got the Ice Hammer of Frore
Kilk got a great bow of war

Zaketh got the huge blue shield
And all the melees, got healing kits for the field
Then Oaf gave me a staff just like his
Oh how thankful I was at such a great wiz!

A great Monarch he is, to be so kind
Such a Monarch is such a truly rare find
And so it began the rest of the evening
“Level Chan,” was the cry, death was just beginning

For Chan was close to getting Item Enchantment
The rest of the evening was set in encampment
Of the South Dires Portal we all did travel
To get spit out by golems, and gromnies like gravel

Try as I might I simply couldn’t tank
Like the great one in green with such a high rank
So the plans did change, and Kilk did thus
He kept letting them all hit him, just like a bus

“Heal!” was the should I kept hearing
As the gromnies, all ash just kept appearing
We really needed help, we really kept trying
So unlike Oaf, all I did was keep dying

I called Oaf for help but Kilk was reminding
Oaf was playing another character, unwinding
“He’s called for help time and again.
He rarely gets to play like a normal kinsmen.”

“So leave him alone,” was the Kilk shout
He was right ofcourse, but I had my doubts
I die so easily, the green robe didn’t change
All of these creatures are way out of my range!

But alas we progressed, and sometimes we fellowed
To get Lurkys vitae down, as “LEVEL CHAN” was bellowed
Once the vitae was gone, Chan was once again alone
For me to heal he and Kilk on my own

Then later that night Pele did return
To arrive in time to watch me get burned
The vitae was returned, and Kilk got some too
Those damn gromnies attacked as we ran out of view

We accidentally left, Chan behind
His stamina had run out, and was in a bind
So returning from the stone we did
Another fellowship we had bid

Again the vitae went down
“LEVEL CHAN” shouted round
So by eleven I got my third death
I ran back from Mayoi, about out of breath

Just then I heard it, the sound so sweet
WOOSH went Chan, oh it was so neat!
He glowed and then we all said together
“Let’s get the hell out of here and into fair weather!”

So this I have learned though it really bites
The only great mage, Olthoi he smites
The green doesn’t do it, it’s the person inside
There’s only one Lumbering Oaf, that I can’t deride!
