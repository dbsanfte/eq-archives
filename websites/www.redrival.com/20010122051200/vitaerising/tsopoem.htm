Ode to the Golden Wasp
by Tso

The Golden Wasp poundeth you
For 40 points abludgeon

Oh, what the heck am I doing here
In this forsaken dungeon

Spinning Lifestone, please tell me
How do I kill that wasp

I only cast perhaps once or twice
Before my mage butt gets off'd

Even warriors have trouble
With this war spell casting fiend

There's GOT to be a way to kill this thing
A way to do the deed

With Vitae Rising even higher
I plunge to recover my stuff

Now where's my corpse, oh over there
CRAP! That wasp is tough.



In memory of the 4 fellows who died in 30 seconds due to the golden greeting at Bandit Prison.
