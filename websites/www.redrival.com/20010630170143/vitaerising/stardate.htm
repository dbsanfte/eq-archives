Stardate .01062001

Medical Officer Reporting
Planet Frostfell
Mayoi Outpost
VR Dweebs Section

 

I am concerned about the Captain’s mental and physical well being.  This concern has been building over time.  It came to an ultimate climax on our last assignment Stardate .12301999.

The VR Dweeb section was ordered to clear the woods of hostile non-sentient life forms from the outposts of Mayoi and Kara. This planet of Frostfell has recently submitted application to the Federation as you know, so we are helping the locals to settle their vast wilderness.  This will also help Starfleet Corp of Engineers to come in and mine for Dylithium Crystals.

A Starfleet Earth holiday called “New Years Eve” was due to start the following day of our cleanup.  However, Captain Hearding decided to start celebrating a tad early.  As we waited for several minutes to see if any other VR’s would volunteer he announced he was going to start drinking Vodka.

This is a powerful depressant, slowing attack response and cognitive abilities, but we didn’t think much of it. It was a simple hunt and kill and with myself healing the frequent “Boo Boos”, Lieutenants Rabbitsbane as Navigator, Lieutenant Chan Tatao as Weapons Officer, and Ensign Kensai (new to the VR establishment) acting as security chief, we all felt confident that we could handle this clean out.

Well as no other volunteers showed up, we quickly made use of the new transporter technology, code name LifeStone, and got started.  We made our way past the standard wild animals without a problem.

Even the mutant Tribbles here that the locals call Mattekars didn’t present much of a problem.  Even though their overdeveloped since of smell makes them intolerant not only of Klingons but also humans, we were able to quickly dispatch them.  I made a mental note that the war souvenirs on these are much worse since the snow began to fall.

At about 7 clicks out from the LifeStone we began to run across mutant Cro-Magnon men, the kind that were encountered when the Enterprise’s shuttle Galileo crash landed with Commander Spock. The locals here call them Lugians.  These ones don’t throw spears but big boulders. “UGH,” I heard Chan and Rabbitsbane let out cries of pain, but with my superior healing skills and long distance medikit sprays I was able to heal everyone.  I did quite well I thought.

I began noticing at this point that Captain Hearding was throwing himself at any and every creature we came across.  Even innocent rabbits! His wanton destruction made it necessary to have me cast him with armor and regenerating to keep him alive.

“Captain Hearding, please stay NEAR the group so I can regenerate my medikit please,” I shouted numerous times.

It was at this point I began to see something ominous on the horizon.  A little voice started ticking in my brain.  Vodka is Russian…. Russian sounds like…. ROMULAN!  I don’t know, maybe it was the strain of my concern for him.

As we were lead over a hill at about 10 clicks out we quickly dispatched some Horta mutants called K’naths.  They do wiggle and jiggle and have the consistency of Jell-O but they can run over anything and acidize them like their Horta cousins.  But these critters are not tame, and instead of helping us mine crystals, they spit spells like rocket launchers. I think they were Hortas that got loose and bred in the wild and decided they had enough with us using them as slave labor!

I got pretty hurt but thanks to the quick action and emergency medical training of the navigator Rabbitsbane I survived.  Then came the wild stray Armoredillos.  Imported by many Texans these guys have been sucking the same radiation tinged rivers as the Hortas.  BZZZZZ  I would hear, “AHHH,” as I took a good hit.  This is where I did the classically and perfected “Scream like a school girl” move that VR is renowned for.  Thanks to the quick actions and archery skill of Kensei and Chan, the Rabbitsbane and were saved. Whew.

Captain Hearding stumbled up the rest of the slope and leaning over them said, “Hum, tough little buggerss huh? Here have a sssip of thisss and you’lllll be much betterrrr.”  I swear I saw him pour Romulan Ale (It was blue) on the corpses!

Then true disaster struck.  Captain Hearding went tumbling down the hill and as Rabbitsbane went running after.  The rest of us ran down when she ran smack dab into the hated ZEFIR PACK!

Well I stupidly thought I could tank Kilif while the others helped.  Unknown to me at the time, there were three of the buggers and I was the only one tanking the Kilif while the other VR’s were hitting Zofrit and Jibrit his big and little brothers, respectively.

Captain Hearding, well he went stumbling around the Zefirs doing a “Bet you can’t catch me,” dance.

“ARGGGHHHH,” I screamed, then Booom Whiiirrrrrr!  I knew the sound.  The LifeStone Transporter Recall had been activated.  Well it regenerated my body quickly enough, however it left me with the normal slight weakness.  Then I realized I hadn’t set myself to the Mayoi LifeStone!  I panicked and used my Transporter Recall II, a.k.a. Portal Recall, and was back in Mayoi.

At the same time I got the messages…. “Your fellow Rabbitsbane has died, Your fellow Chan Tatao has died.”  We all let out a shout for joy.  As with our tradition it is not a true Vitae Rising event if the new LifeStone technology is NOT put to the full test.  A pause ensued then “Your fellow Hearding has died.”  Hum, a Zefir conspiracy.

I pleaded with the others to wait up, but they were off chasing the now NAKED Captain Hearding as he ran gleefully through the snow back to his body!  I panicked. “HELP HELP”  I shouted.  I felt like one of those damsels in distress in the old 1920’s movies.  I was on the railroad track calling while the train kept coming,  “Help Help, oh my Help I say.”

I asked for coordinates three times.  Rabbitsbane found another pack of Zefirs waiting for us and she took her revenge upon them while Hearding, well he ran around in circles naked.  Chan Tatao was kind enough to come to my aid. I was SO alone in the wilderness.  Then it happened again.

“Your fellow Hearding has died.” Seems he got speared in the back by a group of Mosswarts.

“Not quite the way I planned to die. Not real heroic is it?” he said.  We collected he and Rabbitsbane and began a run back to Kensei, who was the only one not to die on our little run in with the Zefirs.

Well we regrouped and picked up our belongings.  I’d like to mention now that the recall LifeStone still needs some touch up.  It leaves behind some much needed items.  It’s an inconvenience but does make it so that we don’t really like the idea of LifeStone recalling.  I just would like to have the Corp of Engineers look into that little glitch.

At this point, I began to theorize that Captain Hearding is not only a Romulan but he’s CRAZY!  He decided at this point that armor was still unnecessary.  Yes I tried to remind him he’d already died twice.  We could not get him to put on clothing, so our group ran up the hill.

VR found a PRIME spawning point of mutant Cro-Magnon men.  On the other side of the hill the K’nath Hortas were shooting rockets over our heads.  It was quite a scene.  It was then, that I remembered that I was suppose to be taking pictures for training purposes.  I missed the opportunity though when Captain Hearding ran down into the spawning point of Lugians.

I had been occupied by the pretty “Screaming Like A School Girl” dance of Rabbitsbane and was healing her. I didn’t notice Captain Hearding alone naked and down by the Lugians when “Your fellow Hearding has died”.  I looked down and there he was.  His corpse was being thrown into the fire and roasted on a spit by these creatures.  What was left was being stomped on.

Our group was a good 15 clicks out of Mayoi.  We regrouped and while waiting for the captain, Ensign Kensei announced that he was out of ammunition and needed to return to a supply depot.  He put on his super Nikes, the ones with Fleet Footed II Power Up, and he ran for much needed supplies.

Waiting waiting waiting, Hearding finally showed and we got his armor.  At this point Chan Tatao reminded us of the time and we decided to run for Kara.  We were out of time to finish the clean up, and with the Captains condition getting worse, well…we were undermanned as well.  So after Kensei ran off we decided to go ahead and run forward to meet him, big mistake.

I got hit with some sort of Temporal Displacement, the locals call it LAG.  As the naked Hearding ran forward, the noble Rabbitsbane tried to follow. When I came out of my state I saw “Your fellow Rabbitsbane, Your fellow Hearding” and then I see Chan running.  I started to kill the evil Zefir but was screaming “HELP HELP.”

I survived then harassed Chan with, “Why didn’t you help me?”

“I couldn’t,” he said putting a medikit to his head.  I saw he was hurt badly. “So that’s why you ran!” I said.  I felt bad.  I healed him up.  As Zefirs, Shreths and Mosswarts spawned near the bodies of RB and Heard, we kept the area clear.  I was panicking, hoping Kensei’s power up would get him back soon.

Luckily RB had enough command experience to power up herself and Hearding.  At this point the Captain came up with a brilliant idea. “Hum maybe I should put some clothes on.” We died laughing. He said he’d switch to water to clear his mind up. Secretly I think he got a transmission from Romulan space while I was lagging and they told him I was suspicious.  You know those Romulans, they have perfected the “Long Distance Mind Melding Technique” and I KNOW they were reading my thoughts!

While Chan and I were waiting I couldn’t help myself.  “Hey Rocky, watch me pull a rabbit out of my cowl.” Hehe

Someone came back with “Silly Rabbit, Zefirs are for kids.” I was wondering where her lucky rabbit foot was when I realized the Zefirs had it.  I’ve heard another famous warrior claim that he’s “What’s for dinner” regarding Zefirs but I KNOW they prefer rabbit meat.  Our navigator and Zephirs have a thing for each other. She must be wearing “Tenderize Me Cologne.”

Hearding got his clothes and Rabbitsbane recollected herself just when Kensei came over the mountain.  We all got powered up Nikes thanks to RB, and ran. As we stopped to get powered up again we saw a two building village and though it wasn’t near the coordinates of Kara, we ran to it in hopes of rest.

I was in the building first when I heard the distinct sound of the UNDEAD! I got the message “You evaded Lich Lord.” I screamed to the group, “LICH LICH LICH. Run, stay away.”

Captain Hearding yelled, “It’s a trap!”

We got out of there fast. I ran so far, the others had to catch up! Then RB spotted her archenemy the Zefirs.  Unfortunately she lost the battle again.  Then Chan stated he had to go and ran toward Kara.  We regrouped again and RB found her body.  As we were running to town Chan announced he too was hit with a time warp.  He was “stuck on a mountain and couldn’t get up!”

Sounded like a commercial for “Transporter Aid.  We come to your rescue. Just hit your transponder.”  You know the one.

As we were running we saw him running in place.  With a quick transporter shift, a.k.a. relog, he was back with us.  We ran through the most gorgeous part of the island at this point.  We ran past lakes, ocean ports, and up mountains with a waterfall.  RB had to lead us up the “Cliffs of Insanity.”

Then we found them.  Hearding’s archenemy, the evil Snowman pod!  We made quick work of them and as I ate my carrots we looked over the horizon.  It was a gorgeous view. We finally made it to Kara.

Then the captain proceeded to strip naked and make a snow angel!  We were all exhausted so we joined in.  Nice picture I think.  Well anyway, this is my report.  I feel it a great responsibility to have the captain take a forced leave of absence.  He tested the LifeStone technology a total of four times.  Rabbitsbane came a close three, all by Zefirs. Chan and I died only the one time.  Ensign Kensei Sansui deserves a medal. He never died!
