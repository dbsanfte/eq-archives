Lessons Not Learned
by Zaketh
28 November 1999

Well it was another day in which to adventure that Putty and me found ourselves near the Samsur triangle. We had gained much experience in our time away and I had completed my 19th circle of training and Putty his 18th. We thought it was high time to go back to the skel mines and r00l!!!!! Boy were we wrong.

After visiting the lifestone near the mine we headed out to its entrance. We decided not to slow ourselves and ran past many monsters to the area around the mine. It seemed not to have changed much since last our vitae flowed out of the place. For some reason I ignored the hackles rising on my neck and thus we pressed forward.

With our superior training the entrance was not that hard to breach. The skels did not stand a chance against us. Inside it was still dank and gloomy. Thus the adventure began on such a trivial note.

After clearing the other areas of the mine we decided to revisit the areas of which Vitae Rising had fought and perished. Thus we fought valiantly teaching Skel Warriors and Skels to fear members of Vitae Rising, especially since we are better trained. We hacked our way through mountains of skels, skel warriors, and a Skel Captain. We were a pair of bladed death scything our way through the dungeon.

Thus we found ourselves at the locked door to the Skeleton lords antechamber. Having dispatched his minions with ease we felt a sense of pride and accomplishment. Denial..its such a beautiful thing. With putty pulling out some trusty lockpicks we preceded into the belly of the beast.

Like before it was a hard fought battle of us against skel warriors. While wounded we did dispatch these vile und00ds and their leader the captain. We were happy and oblivious to the cold hand of doom coming our way. We investigated the lich room and found them waiting. We then decided to check out the downstairs first. We were set upon at the first landing and vanquished the skel warriors with ease. Little did we know those were the Lords outer door guards.

Thus we entered another long hall with more skel warriors quickly dispatched. We looked forward and saw that the hall had another side around a corner. We approached this corner and were again set upon by a few skeleton warriors. When we heard…

“WHAT YOU LET THEM IN HERE!!!!

“YOU BUFFOONS, I WILL HANDLE THIS!!!!

Well hearing that all of the sudden I had this quiver in my knees and a need to use the little adventurers room. I looked at Putty and it was like looking in a mirror. Alas that is all the time we had. THUMP THUMP THUMP THUMP THUMP….then he was among us.

EEEEEEEKKKKKKKKK…it’s the skel Lord we yelled. We tried to fight this evil und00d with all our strength. He cast spells we stood…he hacked at putty we stood…he hacked at me we stood…he cast spells at putty we still held then he cut putty to pieces I ran like a schoolgirl.

But the skel lord wanted two adventurers that day. Man I can run with the best of them. Thus I found convenient shelter from the Lords spells behind a much cracked and pitted wall. After resting I begin to poke away at the lord who was so enraged that he was not thinking of coming around the corner to turn me into pate. Thus I whittled away at him slowly. Putty had returned and retrieved his armor from his corpse and joined me in the whittling of the enraged skel lord. After much time and many unintelligible curses from the skel lord he succumbed to our blades. Thank goodness und00ds don’t have saliva glands. That lord was so pissed if he had saliva glands the wall would be thoroughly slimed with foam from his mouth.

Thus there was much rejoicing, healing, backslapping, healing, resting, healing, and did I mention healing. After our recuperation we preceded to get rid of the Lords staff of skel captains and skel warriors and loot his goodies chest. Upon exiting his inner sanctum the reserves showed up and again we fought and prevailed.

There is a saying, “Pride Goes before a Fall”…no more apt words were spoken and typical of our confidence. After dispatching the reserves we decided to tackle the liches. Well seems skel lord upgraded his minions to guard the treasure room. There were now 4 liches in all, 3 in the room and one in the hall. The hall guard must be the newbie lich. He fell to our blades too easy. Putty thus picked the worrisome lock that had given us earlier pause.

We proceeded to the end of the hall where a door separated us from 3 awfully ticked liches. Seems that was their little brother. When we got close they all started casting fearsome magic that weakened us and hurt in many ways. Sometimes we had to retreat down the hall to heal our wounds. Ever returning to the door to hurl taunts.

“YOUR MOTHER WAS A DRUDGE!”

“DEATH HAS IMPROVED YOUR STENCH I SEE!!!!”

“HEY WAS THAT A SPELL OR BAD BREATH”

Putty’s plan thus worked and we one by one drove the liches mad with rage to where they rushed the locked door to our awaiting blades. While it was not easy to dispatch them their rage was to our benefit. After the last fell I ran to a point I knew with which I could jump into the treasury. After healing from the fall we did proceed to get lots of Phat Lewt. Ahhh those were the days. We continued with the cleaning operation when we heard a faint scream. Hmmm we thought wonder what that was. Little did we know that it was not a hapless creature that wandered in but an enraged but controlled Lord being resurrected.

Thus while looting he set forth an insidious plan that upon hindsight at the end we were shivering in our boots at how easily we got out only beaten to a pulp.

While we finished looting a small group of skel warriors did set upon us again. We fought them off yet again. However, we had over stayed our welcome and the younger lich did come back and set upon us in quick order. It was a valiant fight but his fearsome spell casting might took me to Mr. Lifestone. After assuring him I am not the type to be a repeat customer I ran back to the mine. On the way putty left alone took the lich out then fell to a skel captain blade while distracted.

Forsaking caution I did enter the mine alone and set to get my nice stuff back. After fighting my way through I saw my previous body. Ahh there you are….little did I know the Brothers lich had returned and they preceded to spell me to death while retrieving my items and before I could escape their wrath.

No, no, I really mean it I am not the type Mr. Lifestone. I won’t be back.

Again I made my way to the mine to be set upon again inside the dungeon. This time I was too weak from the last trip to Mr. LS I had to run outside yet again. Putty valiantly tried to come to my rescue again but was swarmed under by more skeletons. Which was shortly joined by me when a crack squad of skeleton archers did ambush me on exit of the fell mine.

No…I really mean it…me and putty don’t lie like that. We really mean to not come back...it just happens. HEY! There is no need for insults…I am not a newbie that can’t run away…

Thus we ran back and dispatched the archers and retrieved more of my goodies. It was fortune that I carried extra armor this day for both of us had lost much. Again we entered but with more of a slow purpose then a rush to get our things. Ah the bliss of ignorance. We again proceeded down to the dungeon killing along the way. Again we faced the liches to retrieve our things and again I was sent packing. Again putty joined me after reserves were set to bring him down. While returning, Mr. LS stopped talking to me now, Putty ran to join me at the entrance but was swarmed by a crack unit of the Lords minions. I entered the dungeon yet again and set upon going down to the lich lair. However, it was unnervingly easy. Too easy you could say. It was part of the plan. I again got to my body and was fixing to retrieve stuff when Putty called from another part of the mine for help. Knowing our luck I ran to help but was too late. The crack squad had struck again and disappeared.

With this I set in for a wait waiting for putty to return before preceding. Again he did return and again the squad attacked…but I was waiting…unfortunately all I did was join putty in his express trip to the lifestone. After getting more armor for ourselves we set out again with notions of getting our stuff back. Ah…sentimental attachment to phat lewt can fog a tanks brain. Again we entered much battered and beaten and with much vitae did we proceed to enter again.

Again we were set upon but we prevailed and won our way to our bodies and did thus recover all our stuff without another visit to Mr. Lifestone. Man that lifestone has a foul mouth when he is mad.

What is the moral of this story kiddies? Let und00ds keep their stinking treasure. You think we would have learned our lesson about the place but oh no you know tanks. Denial can be nasty in groups of more than one….=).

p.s. I left with 8% vitae and Putty with 17%
