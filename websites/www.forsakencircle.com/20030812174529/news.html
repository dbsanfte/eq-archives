Forsaken Circle Headline: 4-05-03

General goes down 3 times!!
FC annoints another Officer!
AP Wire 4-05-03 FL  |  FCNN Combat Journalist Crystoph Litheblade

From FCNN Combat Journalist Crystoph Litheblade:

Last WNQN (Wednesday Night Quest Night) we pulled a long one in Kithicor (skeery) Forest. The General for the Rogue epic was pulled an killed 3 times for Bludpoint, Vuruka, and Breazie. (gratz to them btw)

All in all the evening went quite well with only a single wipe.

In other news, Zzith was brought in as FC's newest Officer. His formal title is Advisor until we're done compiling and organizing his responsibilities and then his title will change to more accurately reflect what he does.

Gratz Zzith and welcome to the insanity!
