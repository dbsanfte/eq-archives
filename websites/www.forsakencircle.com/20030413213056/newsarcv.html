From FCNN Correspondant Giluuk:

Tonight, after most of the CoM raid had to call it a night, we pulled a Black Reaver from the first room. We had 8 of us left at the time...

Group 1
Lorith
Grammies
Tokalot
Jlnolan

Group 2
Giluuk
Xiddix
Humphery
Vuruka

We managed to bring him down, and another imediately took his place. Evac was suggested, but we stayed and took down the second, only to have a third take its place. The first reaver had no possessions, and the call to evac was made as the second was being inspected. Before the casting was done (with the very last bit of mana our casters had), the Jade Reaver was in hand, and was awarded to Humphery at the zone line.

We could not in good conscience leave the third reaver near the bridge with out a single mob to keep it company. So we made our way back toward the bridge, medded to full, and proceeded to beat this most recent reaver as well. Lord Giosk took its place when it fell, and Jlnolan quickly searched the reavers remains to find a second Jade Reaver. Having what we came for, and not wanting to end the night on a bad note, we evaced to the zone and Tokalot was awarded the Jade Reaver.

And to think we nearly left without pulling one last reaver...
