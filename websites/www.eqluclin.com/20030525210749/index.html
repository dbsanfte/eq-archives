Latest "Game Update" Message
Posted by: Bicrius
We're not sure if SOE is moving away from "Patch Messages" and now calling them "Game Updates", but essentially they're the same thing. You'll also notice that on EqLive that network status, latest patch message, and scheduled downtime have all been moved from the "Support" section to new "Network Status" and "Game Updates" sections. Take a look at the "Game Updates" section. We can now see where SOE is taking us quite clearly.

At anyrate, here's the latest "Game Update":

"** Paladin AA Act of Valor **

Fixed a problem with Act of Valor. Use of this AA ability was causing zones to crash. That shouldn't happen any more."
