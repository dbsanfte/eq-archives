Defending the Roost - the tale of a GM event
Posted by: Bicrius
This was posted on the boards by Molleni Rainkeeper. It's a pretty good read. Click here to read the original post on the boards and to discuss... or read onward to read the story here:

I awoke in the morning just before dawn. I crept up to the top of the hill and watched as the sun crest over the horizon. I smiled as I felt the warmth of the sun on my face. I caressed and bathed in it's golden rays and felt at once in harmony with the nature around me. I sat down and opened my tome of spells and began to read over a distant incantation, one for changing forms. My mind immediately refreshed, began chanting the words to my lips, which spoke them aloud, a soft whisper at first, "Molleni, Molleni, Molleni," then growing to a roar as my hands changed to paws and my nose to a snout. My golden form shimmered in the sun.

Latest "Game Update" Message
Posted by: Bicrius
We're not sure if SOE is moving away from "Patch Messages" and now calling them "Game Updates", but essentially they're the same thing. You'll also notice that on EqLive that network status, latest patch message, and scheduled downtime have all been moved from the "Support" section to new "Network Status" and "Game Updates" sections. Take a look at the "Game Updates" section. We can now see where SOE is taking us quite clearly.

At anyrate, here's the latest "Game Update":

"** Paladin AA Act of Valor **

Fixed a problem with Act of Valor. Use of this AA ability was causing zones to crash. That shouldn't happen any more."
