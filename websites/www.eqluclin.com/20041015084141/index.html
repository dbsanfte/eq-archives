Busted Guild links - Aug 18, 2004
Posted by: Bicrius
After receiving a bunch of broken link submissions, I ran a link valedation process and came up with a listing of guilds who's websites either don't exist, or are broken. They are as follows:

1. Nemesys (fixed, thx Zutix)
2. Knights of Velious
3. Guardians Of The Rose
4. Chosen Soul
5. Pale Horsemen (fixed, thx Waylanderr)
6. Infinite Warriors
7. Asura (fixed, thx Dippen)
8. Five Rings (fixed, thx Dippen)

I'm looking for help from anyone who might know if either a) any of these guilds simply don't exist anymore or b) if the guild's link has changed, what it really is now. If the guild doesn't exist anymore (or their web presence no longer exists), please make sure you're logged in to EqLuclin.com and make a comment to this article letting it be known, and we'll remove the link to that guild. Also, if a guild's link has changed and you know what it SHOULD be, please give us a hand and submit a link modification request (there's an FAQ on it if you don't know how) and we'll get it fixed. Thanks! ~Bic
