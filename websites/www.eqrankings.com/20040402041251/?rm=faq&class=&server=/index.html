  1. How do I remove my profile from this list?
    In your magelo short bio, you can add the folloing code <!--nolist--> to prevent your profile from appearing on these lists.
    After you add the tag, you have to update your magelo here 1 time before it will be gone.
  2. How do I use the counter in my magelo profile?
    In your magelo short bio, add the following code: <IMG src="http://www.eqrankings.com/counter.pl">
    This will insert a 1x1 transparent gif, so you might want to put it at the bottom of your bio if you have any other stuff there.
  3. How do I find my magelo id?
    When logged in on your magelo account, go to the 'Characters' page. There you will see your profile link, which should end with 'num=number'. That number is your unique magelo id.
  4. How do I get my guild tag to show?
    First you need to have your Guild Leader register your guild. If you are the Guild Leader, choose Guilds->Add a guild from the top menu and fill out the form.
    After submiting the form you will recieve an email with your guilds login information.

    Once you have logged into the guild manangement section, You can add / delete your members to the guild database.

    Note: If you are having problems adding members to your guild, Make sure their profiles are on the correct server as the guild. If the member was recently in a previous guild, have that member run the guild tag removal form.
  5. My guild is already registered, what do I do?
    It should say who registered the guild (hopefully someone in your guild). Contact that person and ask ;)
    If your guild is registered by someone whom is not a member of your guild, have your guild leader eMail guilds@eqrankings.com and we will take care of it.

  6. How do I remove my guild tag?
    We have added a form you can use to remove your guild tag. Click here to remove your guild tag.

    You can also add <!--removeguild--> to your magelo short bio to hide your guild tag.
    Note: If you people abuse this, we will remove it. Please don't ruin something many people will enjoy.
