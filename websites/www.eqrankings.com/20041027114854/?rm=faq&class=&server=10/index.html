  1. How do I add my profile to EQRankings?
    You may submit your profile to EQRankings by submiting a search for any particular class and then clicking the link that says "Add/update your magelo profile here".

    If you are a Premium Magelo user and would like the Authentic icon to appear next to your profile, you may do so by doing the following:

    Goto Magelo.com and select 'Characters'
    Click 'Update this character' for the character you wish to submit as Authentic.
    Scroll down to 'Privacy Settings' and check the box in front of 'Keep this character synced with EQ Rankings'.
    Click the 'Update' button at the bottom of the page, and then go run Magelo Update.
    Once you have completed the previous steps, your profile will show the Authentic icon next to your name on EQRankings.

    Note: EQRankings uses a caching system. This means it may take up to 10 minutes before your profile will show the Authentic Icon.

  2. How do I remove my profile from EQRankings?
    In your magelo short bio, Add the following code: <!--remove-->
    Once you are done, re-submit your profile to EQRankings and it will be deleted from our database.

    Note: You must make sure the "Keep this character synced with EQRankings" option is not enabled on your characters options.

  3. How do I use the counter in my magelo profile?
    In your magelo short bio, add the following code: <IMG src="http://www.eqrankings.com/counter.pl">
    This will insert a 1x1 transparent gif, so you might want to put it at the bottom of your bio if you have any other stuff there.
  4. How do I find my magelo id?
    When logged in on your magelo account, go to the 'Characters' page. There you will see your profile link, which should end with 'num=number'. That number is your unique magelo id.
  5. How do I get my guild tag to show?
    If your Guild is registered here on EQRankings, you must contact your Guild Leader or Officer who is responsible for adding you to the roster. EQRankings no longer parses profiles for <!--GuildName-->. The only way your Guild tag will show is if you are added within the Guild Management tools.

    If your Guild is not registered, Talk to your Guild Leader and have him do the following... Choose Guilds > Guild Registration from the top menu and fill out the form.

    After submiting the form you will recieve an email with your guilds login information. Once you have logged into the Guild Manangement section, you will be able to Guild/Deguild members here on EQRankings.
  6. How do I remove my guild tag?
    We have added a form you can use to remove your guild tag. Click here to remove your guild tag.

    You can also add <!--removeguild--> to your magelo short bio to hide your guild tag.
    Note: If you people abuse this, we will remove it. Please don't ruin something many people will enjoy.
