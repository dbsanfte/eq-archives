     What Sacred Legions is:

Sacred Legions is an Everquest guild located on the Vazaelle server.  Our guild is comprised of people who seek to reach their goals and have fun.  As a guild, we strive to help each other out in the land of Norrath.  Whether your looking to depart on a quest or buy a new sword, we always attempt to help fellow players out.  We are a large group of friends who stand for Honor, Courage, Integrity and Friendship.  Sacred Legions accepts all classes and races, for we believe each class offers their own unique strengths.  We are a family of adventurers, tradesmen/women and casters that will stay together through it all... but most importantly, we want to have fun!

 

     Basic Conduct:

  • Show all classes and races of Norrath sincere respect:

  •       1. Do not Kill/Steel

  •         2. No Harassing of fellow players

  •         3. No Spamming chat channels

  •  

  • Obey your commanding rank. Any disagreements should be reported to Guild Leader.

  • Notify fellow guild members when leaving the guild... it can be tough updating the roster :)

  • Guild Officers are asked to recruit at least one new member per week.

  • Try to help fellow guildies when they are in need.

  • And have fun!

 

     Ranking:

General Member:  This is the core of sacred legions.  This is the reason the guild is here... to help fellow players with similar interests and goals in Everquest.  The members of this guild allow it to reach its goals and have more fun every day.  Each of the following ranks (officers, treasurer) are in place to make Everquest a more enjoyable experience for our members.

Guild Leader:  The Guild Leader is responsible for the well being of the entire guild.  The Guild Leader makes the final decisions regarding all issues and plans out the future events for the guild.  He/she is to act as a friend to all guild members.

Commanding Officer:  The Commanding Officer is the secondary leader of the guild.  He/she is responsible for the well being of the guild while the leader is not present.  The Commanding Officer also holds rank over all other guild members, except the leader.  They often plan events, organize raids and attempt to solve all problems that may arise.  It is also important they inform the guild leader of important issues.

Officer:  It is a great honor to be an Officer in Sacred Legions. There are always very few officers in the guild. The officer is responsible for recruiting new members and seeing to the guilds security.  They help organize the guild and keep our morale high.  Officers are there to assists other players and answer questions as well as solve problems that may arise.

Treasurer:  The treasurer is responsible for organizing and keeping track of guild items and finances.  They dole out money as they see fit and send in a report each week regarding guild finances.
