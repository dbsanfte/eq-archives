Description:
A cute half-elf bard, Genneth's cheerful expression belies a troubled past. When her mother died of plague, she was raised by her adopted brother's family in the Karanas. She and Crispinskyhawke were later orphaned when gnolls attacked and killed his parents. Because of this, both Crispinskyhawke and Genneth vowed revenge on all gnolls. Brother and sister are very close. You will usually see them together.Genneth's main fault is her warrior tendancies. Her bardic teachers are forever faulting her lack of advancements in the bardic arts on her too warrior-like habits. But, Genneth is sure that being a warrior-bard is better than just being a straight bard. Her brother just shakes his =head at her and tries to keep her out of trouble. Genneth has many friends who all do the same. She feels she is fortunate in her luck and  blesses Bristlebane each day for it.
