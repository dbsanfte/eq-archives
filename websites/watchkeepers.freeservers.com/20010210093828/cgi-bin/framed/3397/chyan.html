Background:

Who am I?

That is a question that I cannot readily answer. I do not know who I was born to be or who I was as a child. My mind cannot see myself at those times as if it was wiped of those precious moments by another's hand. My first memory that I have now is awakening to a friendly face in the mountains of West Karana. The only thing that I knew at that moment was that the other person awakening beside me was my brother, my twin Mian. Neither of us remembered anything before that moment but we knew and felt each other through our hearts. Nothing could ever erase what we feel between us, an extension of one another.

A ranger named Scootle had found us lying there asleep but unharmed. She had been scouting the ogre camp and the rumors of a werewolf roaming the area. I was immediately in awe of her abilities and the pride she showed in her skills. My brother was confused and scared and from that moment on and has been reclusive. With his more naturally ability towards magic I can only guess that he knew what was done to us, and that changed him forever.

Scootle took us quietly through the woods to a beautiful glade. The residents in the area knew it as Surefall Glade. Mian and I stared in wonder at the waterfall while Scootle told Hagar Sureshot of our plight. We were taken to a cottage over the lake and fed, while the elders of the glade held a council meeting. It was decided that we could stay in the glade as long as we did chores like everyone else and that we could be tested for training as a ranger or a druid .

We stayed in the glade for a season before we were both tested, Mian great ability for magic was sensed and he was offered a chance to become a mighty druid. I was a little more aggressive and wished to become a ranger like my friend Scootle. Hagar Sure shot and Larsk Juton trained Scootle and myself together and we both learned quickly, while Mian was still reclusive and was learning his craft at a slow pace, luckily the druids had great patience.

After I had learned weapon skills it was decided that I could go with Scootle on patrols. It was while scouting and hunting in West Karana that Scootle introduced me to another pupil of Larsk's. A half elf like myself, named Crispinskyhawke , he was carrying a heavy two hand sword and running slower than us due to some older leg injury. We became fast friends and we three adventured across the Karanas together. I later finished my ranger training and set to travel across the lands honing my skills and abilities.

Ah but u asked who am I. I can only say that I a m kind to strangers, and deadly to my enemies, I am faithful to my friends, I am a Ranger, but the I can say is I am Chyan.
