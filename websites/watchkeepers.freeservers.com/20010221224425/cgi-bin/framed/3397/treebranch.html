TREEBRANCH SHIFTARROW

Character Name:

Treebranch Shiftarrow

Class:

Ranger

ICQ:

NA

Background:

    Treebranch is a ranger raised in Surefall Glade who early on, learned to protect nature and also to honor his friends. He always remains within the boundaries of ranger code but knows that some of his friends cannot, and will look the other way on occasion, knowing that nature must have some imbalance for it to remain natural.

    He is of the Swiftarrow bloodline, which has a history of superior archers. Excellent with the use and crafting of the bow, he will defend both nature and his friends with his skills. Though Treebranch is a good fighter, that is hardly his whole existence. Conversing with his friends, and making new ones are always common things he does, knowing that one can truly never have too many friends.
