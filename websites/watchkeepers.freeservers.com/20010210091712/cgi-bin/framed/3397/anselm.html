Background:

    Anselm is a bit mysterious. Found in a natural cavern which drawven miners broke into he was cared for by the clerics of Brell Serilis until able to function in dwarven society.

    It was never ascertained how Anselm came to be in that cavern or for how long he had been there as he was full grown when found. No natural entrance to that cave was ever found other than a small stream that flowed from underground and disappeared again. Other than the unmentionable cave creatures Anselm had apparently lived on the only other thing found in that cave was a hatched dragon egg.

    Anselm is dedicate to proving the honor of the Underfoot by his total commitment to the well being of his companions always will to sacrifice himself for them. While in one of his many frequent trance states, Anselm was visited by the avatar of the great father of all dragons.

    He was told that was old must be undone in order for all to be made one in the new. That to do this he must dedicate himslef completely to the support of a half elf brother and sister, a ranger and a bard and they, though they did not know it, would some day be the means by which he learned the secret of his relationship with the dragons
