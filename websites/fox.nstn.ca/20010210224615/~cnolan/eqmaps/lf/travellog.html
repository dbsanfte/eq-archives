Jan/3173  I first travelled through the Lesser Faydark on route to Ak'anon, the home city of the Gnomes.   It was an uneventful trip on the way there, and I stopped in for lunch @ the Gnomish Observatory. 
The trip home was a little bit more adventurous.  I got lost, and was chased by an Orc, I was luck to make it out alive.
I had my first look at the Fae Drakes this trip as well.
 March-Jun/3173  I spend a lot of time exploring and mapping the area. 
I saw the great unicorn, Equestrielle - what a magnificent sight.
I also encounted some awful undead - skelton's and Mummies.  I was diseased with Rabies from the Mummy for a while.
I also made many visits to the Elven Outpost.  To take protection from the guards posted there, and to sell my loot to the nice Half-Elf merchant there.
I received word from the Gods that there would be a terrible storm and that all should stay in shelter if they could.  I made camp in under a quiet tree and stayed, resting for a long while.
Late Oct/3173 Something was up with the faeries.  They were particularily hostile towards me and I was forced to defend myself from 2 of the maidens.
Nov/3173 My film seems to have not developed for a # of the pictures I took on this trip.  It is very disappointing, but I'll have to try to reclaim the photos on another journey.  Safe travels to you.
