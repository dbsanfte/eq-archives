The Orcs in the Lesser Faydark are tougher than your average Orc found in the Greater Faydark.
    You usually only find them in or near their camps, they don't wander much.
    They are dressed in a Yellow tunic, but I have yet to learn what this signifies.

    Be extra careful of the orc camp near the Elven Outpost.  It has a high potential for trains, and the train usally has a Legionaire in it - this is deadly for most travels who normally feel safe in the Lesser Faydark.  If you have the misfortune of trigging, or being nearby when one of these is triggered I strongly advise you to take cover in the elven outpost.  This doesn't guarentee your safetly, as the guards are only so strong and there are only so many of them.  If you are lucky, you can duck into one of the buildings and leave the orcs outside with the Guard.  You're unlucky if an orc stinks in with you and the guards are on the other side of the door.
