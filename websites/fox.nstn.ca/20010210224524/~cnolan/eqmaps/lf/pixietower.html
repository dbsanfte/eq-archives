Oct 7/3173 I had heard rumors of this place, but had never come across it until today.  It is a large tower, guarded by Pixie Jongiers (magic users) and some tricksters.  The rumors I heard mentioned the sale of high level spells in the upper portion of the tower, but when I was there there was no one selling anything.
Watch out for the ladder up to the top, it is a long drop.
