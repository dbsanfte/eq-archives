Hockey

Hockey, the best sport ever invented. It is one of Canada's most famous and popular sports ever.

I love watching hockey but I don't think that I would ever be really good at it. It's a sport that I find better to watch because I know I wouldn't be good at playing it. It's more fun watching the people who are good at it and who know what they are doing. I think that I would probably spoil the game if I played because I like fast action filled games and I would never be able to play at that level.

My favorite team in the NHL is the Ottawa Senators. Although there are lots of people who says that the Senators don't always try and that they just aren't good, I think that they are very skilled. The Senators have been playing really well lately and they have a large amount of talent on their team. Ottawa is still a very young team and therefore they are still adapting. The good thing about Ottawa is that they are one of the only teams that still plays like a team, rather than a team with one or two players getting all the attention and getting treated better like Mario Lemieux and Jaromir Jagr on the Pittsburgh Penguins.
