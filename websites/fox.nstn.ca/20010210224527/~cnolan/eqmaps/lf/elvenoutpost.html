A group of Elves have setup camp near the toughest Orc camp in the Lesser Faydark.  There is a friendly half-elf merchant in one of the buildings.  Just be sure not to annoy her when she is busy - she can be violent!
   Be careful though if you try to run here from the orc camp and expect to be immediatly rescued.  The elves here take their time in dispensing with the orcs, and I've seen many the hero dead in the camp.
