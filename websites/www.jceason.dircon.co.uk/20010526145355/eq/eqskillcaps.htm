Below the maximum level many skills are also capped by level to ensure that level one characters can't suddenly rise to master level in things. These level caps can be broadly organised into the following groups:

  • Weapons: casters - 3x(L+1)
  • Weapons: priests - 4x(L+1)
  • Weapons: melee - 5x(L+1)
  • Offense/Defense - 5xL
  • Normal class skills - 5x(L+1)
  • Trade Skills - don't think these are capped but I've never had enough money at low level to try pushing it higher than a normal level limit to check! Fishing is an exception and is capped as a normal skill.
where 'L' is your current character level
