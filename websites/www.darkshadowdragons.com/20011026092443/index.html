Welcome new Members

10/21/01: First Guild event took off today at around 1 PM Pacific time at the Halas water crossing.  Although the turnout was not great, we had allot of fun. The race started off with Wardader and Stimpcat racing first. Wardader was the winner of the first heap and won a Ruined Totem Staff, a Bow with 10 Dam 39 Del, and some cash. The second race was between Garbus and myself. Well Garbus just plain kicked my butt. Garbus won a bow with 10 Dam 39 Del and 30 Plat.

   Afterwards we all celebrated with beer and other hard liquor, which resulted in a fight between Garbus and Wardadar. My money was on Wardadar but Garbus surprised us as it turned out to be an even fight. Congratulations goes out to the winners of the event, and thanks goes out to all that attended. Pictures are up from the event.
