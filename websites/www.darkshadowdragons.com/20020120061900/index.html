SoL Issues

By Lonestrike on Wednesday, December 5, 2001 at 10:11:50 AM PST

If you are having problems with patching, go to everlore and there is a link to download the latest everquest.exe file. Also you will notice on some ATI video cards that the gamma does not work. You must upgrade to the latest ATI drivers that work with DX8.1 to get the gamma to work. I noticed that when I got in the game it was very slow. But thats what all the controls are for in the beginning. Do you really need high rez and every texture right now. Wait till the game stablizes then change them to high.

Inside the game:
To change the face, go into the inventory and click on face. Self explained from there. Noticed that in the bazaar that you cant set up as a vendor yet. Also at the wizard spires there is a person that you get a crystal from that will allow you to port to the nexxus. The spires go off every 15 minutes. I ported from odus to nexxus then to faydwer, hehe saved an hour of running.

Just dont get frustrated, as things will fall into place and get better. Remember the old day of EQ

Lonestrikes

Making Money

By Lonestrikes on Monday, November 26, 2001 at 10:25:49 AM PST

I have been in Paineel for the past few days killing nothing but guards. Each one has a shield worth 3plat 7gold and a sword worth 2plat 5 gold. Also the guards will drop anywhere between 3gold and 2plat. So off two guards I walk to the bank with about 12 to 14 plat. I am banking about 100 plat per hour limited only by my healing time. The hardest part is if you are KOS allready to the guards, you have to work your faction up to at least dubious, once there you should be able to walk around freely. There are no faction hits for killing the guards

The Warrens

By Lonestrikes on Friday, November 9, 2001 at 12:00:57 PM PST

Since they removed the trivial loot code from the Warrens, I thought I would take a trip there. Very nice place to level and make some nice cash. Even the low level mobs drop bronze weapons. The mid level mobs drop allot of soft wicker armor, about the same ac as leather but a green look. The higher mobs drop cold iron armor, about the same stats as bronze but a black look. The level range is from about 5 through 23. Anyway I plan on spending a few days there because I could use the money. Then its off to the stonebrunt mountains. Just a note to let everyone know they could share in the wealth of this place. Hope to see you there

Lonestrikes
