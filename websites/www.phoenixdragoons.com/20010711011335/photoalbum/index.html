A WEDDING IN NORTH KARANA



Just before dawn, I happened to be travelling through North Karana to Qeynos when I noticed a very large gathering of players under the wizard teleport arches. I quickly learned that I was just in time to witness my very first wedding ceremony!

Upon hearing of this joyous event, Juzamm Holyknight made a mad run from South Karana and came in time to congratulate the bride and groom.

A scintillating display of lights, colors, and loud explosions followed the announcement of 'You may kiss the bridge'.

However, the ever-surly guards were not in the mood and foolishly attacked the celebrants. For any future guards wishing to interrupt celebrations, please note the rather dead corpses of Cordelia Minster and Guard Fredrick in these two very candid shots!

Afterwards, I broke out the alcohol and offered everyone a few shots of Honey Mead (no joke!). Unfortunately, I had drunk several shots myself and was not thinking clearly enough to take pictures of all the players lining up for their alcohol! Doh!

For our records,

On 1999-Sep-06, Avale and Zilvra were officially wed under the wizard teleport arches in North Karana.

--- Siuan Saidar
