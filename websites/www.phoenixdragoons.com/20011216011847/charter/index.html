DRAGOONS HYMN

Dragoons,

As some of you may know I served in the United States Marines. I believe it is time we had a Dragoons Hymn; Marines, Green Berets, and Navy Seals all have their own Hymns, and they are the best fighting units in the world. I believe we are the best guild in Norrath and it's time for a Hymn:

From the Halls of Befallen
To the Lair of Nagafen
We have fought in many battles
That most have never seen
First to fight for Right and Justice
And to our Hearts be True
We are Proud to claim the title of
Mighty Phoenix Dragoons

We are there to Always help those in need
From Dawn to Setting Sun
We have fought in every Climate and Place
And never shall we Run
If ever you are in need of us
We will come to your aid Soon
We are the Protectors of this Realm of ours
The Mighty Phoenix Dragoons

Here's a Toast to You and to our Guild
Which we are Proud to Serve
If there is Strife we fight for Life
And Never lose our Nerve
Have no Fear when you leave this Realm
And hear Heavens Beautiful Tune
For you will see the roads are guarded by
Mighty Phoenix Dragoons

written by Creigus Brommel
Proud Cleric of Almighty Mithaniel Marr &
Proud Member of the Mighty Phoenix Dragoons




CHARTER OF THE PHOENIX DRAGOONS

DECLARATION
By decree of the members present at the founding of the guild known as the Phoenix Dragoons, we acknowledge the governing body herewith and declare ourselves unified with all the powers and judgement of a formal assembly.


STATEMENT OF PURPOSE
The Phoenix Dragoons are a lawful group of adventurers dedicated to restoring order to the lands of Norrath. Along side the good people of Norrath, we will heal the wounds that were rent in our land by centuries of turmoil and chaos. The forces of chaos are strong and we are called upon to restore the balance through order.


BILAWS
The High Lord and the High Council shall bear the reigns of government and shall make, through representation, and enforce, through participation, laws for the betterment of the guild as a whole. The guild will consist of the High Lord, three (3) Council Members, one (1) Treasurer, five to seven (5-7) officers and the regular members of the guild.

Each member of the guild will have specific duties and privileges that will be described on the guild website in detail.

The guild will provide services to its members upon admission into the guild. The services are as follows:

  • The guild will provide each member with equipment compatible with his or her level and standing in the guild.
  • The guild will provide corpse-locating services and if the member’s corpse is not retrievable, the guild will provide for the member that which was lost. If a GM cannot help you, the guild will do its best to replace what you lost.
  • The guild will keep a treasury to pay for services to its members and to pay for the guild house when it is available. Each member is urged to contribute 5% of his/her income to the guild treasury.
  • Within the guild, every member will be able to find a compatible group of adventurers to party up with. Each party will have a leader and that leader will update one of their officers of the status and whereabouts of that party. If that party needs help or more people, we can send help or more members their way.
  • Guild events will be held monthly and e-mail of any changes or requirements of them will inform the guild members.
  • Members can request an email address that begins with their character's name followed by @phoenixdragoons.com (eg. vanion@phoenixdragoons.com).

We are Dragoons, and we hold our name in pride. Together, let us ride.
