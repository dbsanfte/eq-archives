WHAT IS EVERQUEST?
The official Everquest website is www.everquest.com

For other great links, check out the Links page.
Unless you have been living under a particularly large rock for the past 8 months, you have heard about Everquest.

Some people claim EQ is an online role-playing game (RPG). While it is certainly that, it also strongly resembles a 3D virtual chatroom -- I have made many new friends by adventuring and chatting in the Everquest world.

If you see a Phoenix Dragoon online sometime, send them a Hello!

--- Siuan Saidar (that's me posing on the left!)
