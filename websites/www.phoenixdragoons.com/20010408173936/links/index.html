LINKS

EVERQUEST INFORMATION WEBSITES
My personal favorite EQ website is eq.stratics.com.
  • Allakhazam's Magic Realm This site is constantly updated and a wealth of useful information.
  • Caster's Realm Complete reference for Enchanters including a jewelry chart and mana calculator.
  • Cazic Thule Corner Excellent design -- intended for Cazic Thule server but has information relevant to all servers.
  • EQ Atlas Excellent maps can be found on this site with well-written descriptions -- very few dungeon maps, unfortunately.
  • EQ Journal Excellent maps, complete with most Kunark zones.
  • EQ Links Comprehensive site (Zone guides, Class guides, Trade Skills, etc).
  • EQ'Lizer This site is frequently updated. It has a thorough item description section.
  • EQ Stratics Thorough reference of each class, many spells, abilities, and more.
  • EQ Vault One of the first and most constantly updated EQ sites -- also shows a realtime status of each EQ server.
  • Everlore A nice-looking EQ site.
  • Everquest Official website What more can I say?
  • Graffe's Wizard Compilation Excellent site for wizards - everything you need to know for spells, research, quests, items, wizard-related news, etc.
  • Maximum Everquest Good EQ reference site with fair prices (in platinum) for many items.
  • Mythiran Tower Research reference for magic users.
  • Outriders of Karana Maps Simply the best maps of Kunark.
  • Prexus Tribune New website dedicated to the Prexus server.
  • Quester of Norrath Excellent maps can be found on this site.
  • The Rallos Zek Chronicles Nice EQ website.
  • The Shaman's Crucible Excellent site for shamans: spells, skill guide, alchemy info, etc.

EVERQUEST GUILDS ON THE INTERNET

Prexus Server

  • 5 Rings The 5 Rings is a L31+ only guild. I love their Member's Bios page (now why did they take down such a nice site just because the guild disbanded?).
  • Amber Fellowship Amber is the enchanters Gem of Strength, so our name "Amber Fellowship" simply means the "Strength of Friendship".
  • Everquest Guilds A website dedicated to providing links to other Everquest Guildsites.
  • Fires of the Heaven Excellent site with very up-to-date information (check out their message board).
  • Flower Jihad Adventurers who are here to have fun (their Who We Are page is awarded two smiles!).
  • The Hunters Alliance Shockwave-only site the looks very similar to the Everquest Official website.
  • Keepers of the Faith Gotta love their Quotes page!.
  • Sovereign Guard The Sovereign Guard guild website for Prexus server.
  • Twilight Council Guildside for the Twilight Council.
  • Waves Of The Dark Tides Small site that Riliphaen promises me will grow =).
  • Wild Knights Amusing Blair Witch Project parody in Comics -> Kithicor By Soltan (big).
Veeshan Server
  • Guardians of Veeshan Well-designed with a cool intro animation.
