NEW RECRUITS

WELCOME NEW RECRUITS!
I am Preceptor Vanion Brightblade. Please read through and agree to abide by our Guild Charter before deciding to apply for membership to the Phoenix Dragoons (basically, we look for the company of good people where a sense of humor is an asset =)).

After you are satisfied with our mission, please e-mail me with the following information to:

recruit@phoenixdragoons.com

  • Subject: New Recruit
  • Character: Name, Race, and Level
  • References: at least 2 references from current guild members
  • History: give a brief history of yourself and how you can help further our cause

Upon acceptance to the guild, every Dragoon who hears about this in /guildsay is asked to do a:

/shout Let it be known across the land that through valiant effort and perseverence [name] has earned the honor-mark of Phoenix Dragoon! Wear your name with pride, sir [class].

Thank you for your interest in our guild. I look forward to hearing from you.
