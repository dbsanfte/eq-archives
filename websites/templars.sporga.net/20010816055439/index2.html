< Monday, August 13, 2001 >

"A true friend is someone who is there for you when he'd rather be anywhere else."

~ Len Wein ~

Hello hello hello. We got to do a little bit of big game hunting this last week, and I actually remembered to take pictures. So, here's the new illustrated week in review:

First, we got the fabled Kromriff Military Leggings for Leithafae on Tuesday. Enkidu, Polgarath, Ruabain, Lady Leithafae and yours truly were in attendance. Perhaps its just me, but does the appearance of these legs remind anyone else of Olivia Newton Johns' pants in the carnival dance scene in Grease?

On Thursday, we joined Serenity Within and the Brethren of the Mystic Dawn in the slaying of the big dumb fire elemental, Ixiblat Fer. Attendance was great, and too many Templars to mention showed up to help Tristor earn the next step in his Epic quest. On a side note, with as difficult as the magician epic is, I think ol' Ixiblat should be our epic elemental pet, that would be balanced, no? =)

We also had a bit of fun on Friday, when we had a little hunt in the City of Mist. I arrived late, so I didn't get a chance to take attendance, but we had a blast. Here's a little pic of the fire pets doing all the grunt work while the rest of us take a nap.

WTG!!!, Woohoo!!!, Grats!!! and Huzzah!!!

You have become better at being a giant lemonhead (119)!! I forgot to have my /log on this week, so I wasn't able to see everyone that dinged. But we had a couple other important graduations.

Melchiades got the pinnacle skill level of 250 in jewelcraft, and then he went on to make 2 Blue Diamond tiaras, 1 Black Sapphire necklace, and 2 Velium Fire Opal Wedding rings with no failures. The man is clearly unstoppable.

Ehvann succeeded in making the Vale Sewing kit, and can now work on making some halfee super tailored armor. We can all expect to see Ehvann hunting pretty much anything with a pelt for a while to come.

So, hit these two guys up to make your flowing black imbued flayed velium silken tunic hide earring (LORE ITEM) that you've been waiting for =)
