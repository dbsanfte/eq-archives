Welcome to the home of Dark Spiral, a Role-Play friendly, EverQuest Guild on the Cazic-Thule Server. The majority of our members are based in Europe, but we welcome members from all over the world.

You can apply for membership to Dark Spiral  by going to the Joining page. Should you wish to contact us out with the game, for further information, you will find a number of e-mail addresses on the Mailing page.
