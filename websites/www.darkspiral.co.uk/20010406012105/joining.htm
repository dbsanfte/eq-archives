To join Dark Spiral you should approach a member and tell them of your interest, let them know your race, class, level and any trade skills you have. For the first few days you are considered an initiate of the guild. The main thing that will work against you is blatant bad manners, for example kill stealing. After this time you will be a full member of Dark Spiral, if however we decide you are not suitable an officer will get in touch with you to let you know the details. 

Friends of members will be given a large degree of leniency during the above process.

On being accepted into the guild, as many members as possible will try to get to your current location and throw a little party for you, it might be a good idea to start working on your alcohol tolerance.

Anyone wishing to join the guild must give us some character details, you can do so by clicking on the link below or mailing a member of the Inner Circle from the Mailing page.

We will then update the members page to include your character details and hopefully a short biography about your character.  It would also be handy to get your RL e-mail address, this will not be published on the website, nor passed on to any mailing sites or 3rd parties but we do require this for contact purposes if necessary.

Good luck, we'll see you in game.
