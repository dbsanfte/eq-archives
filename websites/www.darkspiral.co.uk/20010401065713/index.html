Welcome to the home of Dark Spiral, an EverQuest Guild on the Cazic-Thule Server. We also welcome interest from any players who may be thinking about joining us.

If you wish to contact us, you will find a number of e-mail addresses in the Mailing page or you can approach us in game. You can also apply for membership to our guild by going to the Joining page.
