Nula

  I always knew what I was from the day my father found me practicing with one of his swords, he was so proud, he immediately made me a small practice sword and began to teach me the skills I would need as a warrior in our world. Mother wasn't quite so happy, she had hoped I would follow in her footsteps and become a magician, I never saw the appeal in all that arm waving, but then, I was always my daddies girl.

As soon as I was old enough, father took me to the warriors guild, I think when I walked out of the guild with my training tunic and sword, was the proudest moment of his life. Mother cried.

Father took me to the entrance to the outside world, gave me some food and told me not to come home until I reached my 20th season. I miss him so much, but am slowly getting there.

I met Sarolayn almost straight away, he was a handsome young Necromancer, he helped me out and introduced me to his friends, we joined "The Servants of the Void" and spent many a happy season together, we got engaged.

I went on holiday for a while, traveled around and finally came home to be reunited with my love. When I found out he had changed guilds, I immediately left The Servants and applied to Dark Spiral.
