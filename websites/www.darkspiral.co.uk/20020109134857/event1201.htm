Guild Event 1st, December 2001

Saturday, 1st December 2001

The chaotic fighting championship took place in the arena. The fighters were the Champion Agreu, but also Salpetre, Oundrea, Delusions, Valan, Bunky, Tephlonb, Rastila, Kintaria. Nohky was a referee and photograph.

The screenshots of the actual fights are not impressive at all, but the group photos are !.

Finally the champion : Salpetre.

 

 

The Fishing Competition took place the same evening, with Agreu defending his title. The final were between Agreu and Bunky. Finally Bunky won and is now our Master Baiter !
