Zothrak

Zothrak has a somewhat strange if almost unbelievable story. Born over 2 thousand years ago and found imprisoned with a Green Dragon, deep in some ancient dwarven mines, long since unused. Zulran (the green dragon) never revealed his origins or his being there to him, just calmly saying "patience, you have a higher destiny awaiting, now stop pestering me you dolt and get on with your studies!" Zothrak, having no means with which to practice many of the mundane everyday tasks such as cleaning and dishes that apprentices usually have became quite adept with his latent magical skills. Zulran tried to teach as many languages as he could but Zothrak just didn't want to know about the ancients in his early years. One day a paladin must have broken the seal on the door which imprisoned the unlikely pair. Zulran was happy with breakfast then asking Zothrak to climb onto his back he started concentrating. Instantly the pair were soaring high into the clear blue sky. Zulran caused quite a storm when he landed outside the gates to Neriak I can tell you.
