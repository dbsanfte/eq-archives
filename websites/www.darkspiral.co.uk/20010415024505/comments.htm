Welcome to Dark Spiral's recruitment page, if you are interested in joining us fill out a form, none of the fields are required, but obviously, the more information you give us, the easier it will be for us to reach a decision about you. If your application is acceptable, you will be contacted in game, where we will endeavour to spend some time with you, to see if you are Dark Spiral material. If you only wish to make a comment, not to apply, then just fill in the parts of the form that are relevant. Your Characters level will not work against you. We would prefer applicants who enjoy role-playing, but we don't mind non-role-playing in Guild Chat and groups, we want to get to know you and your character.
