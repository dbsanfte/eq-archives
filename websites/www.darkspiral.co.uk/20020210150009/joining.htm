To join Dark Spiral you should fill out an application form from the link at the bottom of the page. Please fill out the form completely, let us know your race, class, level and any trade skills you have, if you do not complete the form fully you will probably be contacted by an officer to get more information from you. 

For the first few weeks you are considered an initiate of the guild. The main thing that will work against you is blatant bad manners, for example kill stealing, d00d sp34k, excessive swearing, begging or harassing others in/out with the guild. After this time you will be a full member of Dark Spiral, if however we decide you are not suitable an officer will get in touch with you to let you know the details. Please remember that we are a Role-Play friendly guild and try to get into the swing of things.

Friends of members will be given a large degree of leniency during the above process.

Please enter a valid email address so that we can add you to our mailing list if you wish to be added, or contact you about any changes in guild policy, events etc if you don't join our mailing list.

If you are accepted into the guild, please go to the link on our members page, and enter your character details.

After you have sent the form, the guild leader or her deputy will be in touch by email to arrange to meet you in game.

Good luck.
