Event Details
 
Tournament:

 Every one should be bound in the Arena Entrance.

 Any who wishes to take part, can. Rhiannah will watch over the tournament from the throne, any attempts to attack her will result in an immediate loss for the perpetrator and a “Nasty Person” mention on Guild Magic. No use of any DoT spells is allowed, to prevent loss of Experience. If you die, collect you corpse and leave the fighting area, you are not allowed to return to the fray. The last person standing will win the title of, “Dark Spiral Champion”, which will be added to the Guild Magic site so that all can see how great they are. They will also receive 50 platinum pieces prize money.

 Anyone who is caught cheating shall be told to leave the fight and get a “Big Cheater” mention on Guild Magic.

 Rhiannah’s decision is final, though she will listen to any grievances.

 

Fishing Competition:

 All will gather at The Aviak Island with the pretty tower, in Lake Rathe, rods and bait at the ready. The aim is to collect the most fish in the time allotted. The winner will receive the title of “Master Baiter” (sorry, couldn’t resist) and a prize of 20pp. Any-one not actively participating in this event (in fact even if they are) is encouraged to work on their alcohol tolerance and/or swimming during this.

 This event will be judged by Trystn

 

Gambling:

 (I need volunteers to play test some games before this event)

 

The Scavenger Hunt:

 All will gather at the plague huts and be given a list of items to collect and return to Rhiannah. The first one back with all the items is the winner, and will be awarded with the title “Master Scavenger” and 50pp prize money. You can collect these items in anyway you wish, the more inventive, the better. *grin*. No-one except for Rhiannah, will know what these items will be until it is announced at the event, but be warned, it may involve travelling the length and breadth of Norrath, to find them.

 No porting or SoW capable characters are allowed to enter this event to prevent an unfair advantage, but you are free to enlist the help of others to get you to your destination.

 

Language Workshop:

 Everyone who attends will group up and talk at each other in their (Norrathian) native languages.

 

Role-Play Event:

 Nohky is planning this event.

 

Story Telling and Songs:

 An opportunity to express the creative side of your personality, any who wish to participate, can take the stage and tell a story or sing a song. I would like this to be done in Say mode, so that any passers-by can see how great we are. I will also keep a log of this event so that the artistes can be recognized on our website. After, the entries will be put on the website and a vote for the best story/song will be had, with a 20pp prize and title (“The Entertainer”) going to the winner.

 

Dwarf Race:

 First Dwarf to reach Erud docks, from Kaladim, is the winner and will be given the title “Great Speedy Feet” to the alt/main of their choice, and 50pp prize money.

 Anyone wishing to take part in this event should meet at the Kaldim Gates with a level one, Dwarf Warrior, where they will be met by an officer and guilded. They should then make their way to the Butcher Block Docks. The race officially starts as soon as everyone is ready and the Freeport boat arrives. Use of /anonymous or /role-play is not permitted.

 Your names MUST have DS in it, to prevent us grabbing a genuine new dwarf by mistake. When you arrive, find Nirissi or Rhiannah and tell them “That be damn fine beer they ‘ave in Odus.” You will then be guilded.

 Anyone caught asking for help with SoW or Ports will be given a “Big Cheater” award and denied the chance to win, even if they get to the destination first. If anyone asks what you are doing, tell them that you have heard of a great new beer being brewed in Erud and want to try it. You must speak in Dwarvish at all times.

 The use of emotes to get your point over in English will not be allowed, nor will begging, /ooc or /shout. /auc can only be used for buying and/or selling items, and must be done in Dwarvish. The only help you can receive is from fellow runner, or strangers (no friend please). No begging (once again).

 You can go to the E. Commons tunnel and hope you get a sow, but you can't ask "Jobaber, can you please sow me?"  If someone /shout sow at tunnel entrance poke me. You can poke him. And thank him in Dwarvish :)

 Bare in mind that a fellow racer could over hear you cheating, and may even be rewarded for turning you in. On that note, I want everyone to turn /log on, so that if you do catch someone cheating, you have the means to prove it.

 It’s not that we don’t trust most of you, but we just want to make sure that this is done as fairly as possible. The fun is in getting there on your own two feet, cheating just makes the event pointless, having already had one member recently caught lying to us, we are currently a little wary, sorry.

 In the event that two Dwarves reach the Erud docks simultaneously, they must duel, the last one standing being the winner. Unfortunately I don’t think there is an arena in Erud, so things get tricky if three or more arrive simultaneously. I suggest using the /random command to ascertain who fights who first.

 Rhiannah and Nirissi will make sure everyone is guilded, before guilding their own dwarves.

 

Prizes:

The Prize money will be awarded on the Monday at 22:00 GMT, 23:00 CET, 14:00 PST at the Plague huts in East Commons, where everyone can congratulate the winners, have a drink and chat about the weekends events. You can also then let the officers know if you enjoyed the weekend and if you would like to see more events of this nature.
