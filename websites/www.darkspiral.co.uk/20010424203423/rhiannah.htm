Rhiannah - The Lady Spiral

I am a Shaman, and spent my early years in Halas with my best friend Plyskin. One day we decided it was time to see more of the world. The journey was long and dangerous. On arriving in West Commons we were mugged, Plyskin disappeared and I was left naked and hungry. I managed to reach Freeport where I met a High Elf, Myrror, who bound me to the area. I spent time clearing creatures from the city gates, just to make enough money to acquire food, garments and a weapon. I found no real satisfaction in this, so I ventured into East Commons. There I met a Dark Elf called Naniu, a Monk, Gwynfyd and a Dwarf named Demal. We soon became friends and I no longer regretted my decision to journey. Since that time I have met many new people and formed this guild. I now strive to keep my friends in top health and always ready for battle. In my quiet times I hunts snakes for their scales, a needed component for one of my spells and do the occasional bit of tailoring and fishing.

Oh, Pliskyn has re-appeared and that thieving wretch Demal invited him to join the guild. Unfortunately, Plis and I had a huge falling out, he's going to have to get his act together if he wants to stay.
