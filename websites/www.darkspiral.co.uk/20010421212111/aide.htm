Aide - Guild Treasurer

Hi, my name's Aide and this is how I got myself mixed up with this disparate crowd.

I had just finished meeting with the Clerics guild for the first time, when I met Rhiannah, we were both fishing at the Freeport docks. We got to talking and very soon became fast friends, we made a point, from that day forth, to fish together. One day she told me about this guild she was setting up, and asked me if I would be interested in joining, of course, I said yes. Later she approached me, "I need someone I can trust to be the treasurer of Dark Spiral, you are my best friend now that Plyskin has gone, and to be honest, I wouldn't have trusted that rogue with this task, are you interested? In return I personally will pay for all your spells and any equipment you might need." I thought about it for a few days, and decided, yes. So here I am, I can usually be found in Freeport or the East Commons area, but if you can't find me, let Rhi or Sar know, and they will get me for you.
