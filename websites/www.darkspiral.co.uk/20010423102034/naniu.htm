Naniu

 Dark and evil to his core, one of the founder members of the Dark Spiral guild. Not much is known about his past but it was a hard upbringing for this young Shadow Knight. As he is a keen worshiper of Innorruk - Prince of Hate he has a difficult time in most places outside his home town of Nerriak.

Naniu has a lot of friends and few enemies, despite of his evil followings and doings. Once you get past the surface based scowl and arrogance he is quite friendly and often helps people in need when he can.

This role surprises a lot of people due to his evil nature but it just proves, no matter what, there is good in everyone. Or is there?
