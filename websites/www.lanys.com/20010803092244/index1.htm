LANYS.COM SERVER WIDE RP EVENT

Here are the basics. Keep in mind that this is first draft and dont send me hatemail, damn grammar nazis. (Peers at Atuk) If youre interested after reading, sign up in the Player Run Quests category of the forums. Be creative with your names if you want, but dont show up with a troll named Assmonkey. (Interesting Fact: I actually have an Iksar Shadowknight named Assraptor.) Anyway ....

LANYS.COM SERVER EVENT INFORMATION:

Each person must have two open character slots on their account. On Saturday, April 29th, 2001 roleplayers will create a character based out of either Erudin, Qeynos, Surefall Glade or Halas. These will all be level one characters completely naked barring the newbie sword and tunic. We will meet at 6pm EST at the Qeynos Gate, so if youre coming from Halas, you'd better get on early... the trip will surely be dangerous. Promptly, at 6pm EST the entire band will divide themselves into groups of 6. These groups may be arranged in advance via the Player Run Quests forum. Out of all the groups in attendance, one group will be randomly selected to be Quest PCs for this event. That group will then venture to the given area to recieve their instructions. Quest PC's will have special uniforms and be given such at that time.

Each group will have the opportunity to take part in many small quests while on the greater adventure that is scheduled for all groups. Incorporated into the storyline (I'll go ahead and give it away) will be the formation of a guild.

The next roleplaying day, we will all be selecting EVIL characters ( remember, I said you would need two empty slots on your account to participate), from Grobb, Neriak or the ogre village? A different quest will begin... another guild will be formed. In the weeks following, some groups will be playing their EVIL groups, and some will be playing their GOOD groups. These two guilds will be in PERMANENT GUILDWAR STATUS. Their adventures will frequently cross paths and will definately include many a war. (Im not talking about someone waiting until youve almost killed a mob and then jumping you. I mean one guild on one side of WC, one guild on the other. The battles will be real. The consequences of decisions made within the Adventure will affect the future of the world. In some cases, single individuals will be given choices that affect the entire group and their future. Make your emotes, think about your character, but if youre not an avid roleplayer and would like to be, the storyline is built to aide you. You'll just have to see what I mean when you get there.

There ARE rules. If you would like to discuss a rule, do it in the forum under Player Run Quests BEFORE OR AFTER THE EVENT, NOT DURING. The rules are simple and Im sure you can figure out why they are in place.

Rule #`1 : No twinkage, no money, no leveling the characters outside of the Adventure. If you miss a day that we hold RP day, then you will be able to level your character to catch up. Also, folks that arent here for the first event (IF THERE ARE MORE) may be able to join the next event with a naked level x character.

Rule # 2 : The things you kill produce the gear you get. Your group will decide who gets what (I cant wait to see people randoming a cloth cap). Money dropped off of mobs or given by Quest PC's are to be spent in NPC SHOPS ONLY. You are not to buy, trade, or sell to players outside of the guild.

Rule #3 : Dont Complain. After the first Event, if there is a desire for another, you will have the opportunity to aide in the development of the storyline. In fact, after the original event, the storyline will be a product of the adventureres themselves via the Player Run Quests forum.

Helpful Guideline : React to others as they are. Dont sit and wait for the quests to come to you. Adventure with your group, learn about each other, get married, have children, live an adventure. If you have no desire to develop your persona and the relationships with other roleplayers, then this is going to be extremely boring for you.


Having Said that.... If youre interested in participating, sign up in the Player Run Quests forum. I would advise creating your characters now, just so you know what name you'll be using. Do not use the forum for roleplaying. Do not start revealing the nature of your character in the forum. Its mechanical, the event is creative... the further apart you can keep these two frames of mind, the better your roleplaying experience will be.
