Hey Dej, unless you are just really keen on writing a new board, you might want to check out some of the existing free boards out there.

The one I would recommend if you are not opposed to using PHP is called Phorum, and can be found at http://phorum.org. It is an open-source project so you can customize it however you want and all that. I know personally the founder of the project -- he happens to be my cousin and also works for me here at dealnews.

As a fellow coder, I understand the desire to DIY, though, and especially if you don't want to mess around with learning or installing a new scripting language. But I just thought I'd let ya know in case you were interested.

Good luck with the project, however you choose to do it. I know it will rock in the end :)
