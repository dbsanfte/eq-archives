NEW PRODUCER LETTER

The Official EverQuest Producer Letter has been updated today.

SERVER NEWS REMINDER

Just a reminder for everyone that you can both submit and read news specific to your server in our Server News area.

To submit news, just add a character to our character list for your server and then use your name and password that you edit that character with to submit your news.

You can access server news from the top of the left menu, or by clicking here.

WARRENS ON TEST

The long awaited 'Warrens' zone has finally been released on the test server. This is a lower level dungeon on the continent of Odus accessable by both good and evil characters. While only on test at the moment, it should reach live servers relatively soon.

Submitted by Kivlan, 3:19 AM
 

NEW FIREPOWER FOR WIZZIES?

Two new spells have begun being balanced on the Test Server as possible solutions to the Wizard Classes problems with dealing damage on high level raids in Velious.

The new spells are unresistable, but are restricted to a specific monster type. The wizard class balance area has been updated with details and can be accessed here.

Wednesday February 28, 2001

COLORING CONTEST UPDATE

Our first coloring contest has now closed. In total we had well over 100 entries which will be judged over the next few days by our resident artist William J. Murray.

Due to the popularity of the contest we have decided to start holding these on a bi-monthly basis. We will also be selling packs of coloring posters in our shop for people who wish to color them at home.

You can view the entries for the first competition, and download the art for the second competiton here.

Submitted by Baelish, 12:41 AM
 

RATHE AND E'CI TO SPLIT!

Alan VanCouvering announced today that the Rathe and E'ci Servers will be split.

Here is his announcement:

Some time in the near future we will be splitting The Rathe and E'ci to a new server. We will announcing the move logs for this split when we get a firm date. I don't have a name for the new server, or a date for its arrival. But I will let you know when I do.

- Alan
No other servers have been announced at this time besides this new split.
