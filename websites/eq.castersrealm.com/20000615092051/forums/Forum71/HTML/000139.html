The new cleric spells are incredibly disappointing if no changes are made between now and the release.

In the current world of EQ, clerics possess three spells that truly set them apart from the other classes: divine aura, superior healing, and resurrection.

All three of these spells are losing some amount of exclusivity in the expansion; shamans and druids will receive superior healing, while paladins will receive superior healing, an exp rez, and DA (of which they *should* get ZERO).

The only thing we're actually getting in this expansion is a new set of hp/ac buffs. Our new hp buffs, in tandem with the insane hp totals of level 60 tanks, will make complete healing a lot more valuable than it was pre-expansion, but that is a spell we've had since 39. Beyond the hp/ac buffs, we're gettin jack squat... some new worthless fears, another dd that doesn't hit hard enough, no real melee advantages over our supposedly weaker casting counterparts... oh yeah, and another splendid PBAOE DD... yeehaw. I have high hopes for the undead uber-nuke at 60, but necros get that too (bogus... thought they were supposed to get undead dd one rank after us, and therefore they would NOT get our level 60 upgrade). The attack buffs sound interesting, but a reagant spell that lasts 6 minutes is simply too much money.

I wanted to get something NEW in the RoK, especially when our main priest counterparts (druids and shamans) are becoming primary healers. Granted, we'll have a faster, cheaper heal for less hp and a more expensive heal for more hp, but superior healing will still be the happy medium... and now everyone has it. We'll get some new group heals, but with single-mob pulls and enchanters (aka: the NORM), there will never be any call for group heals... not unless Verant suddenly decides that a group can take on more than one or two ultra-high level mobs at once.

So now that the other priest classes heal about as well as the cleric, isn't it about time that the cleric got some of those goodies we've been giving up for our now defunct, once vastly superior healing capabilities?

Btw, if one person even *tries* to bring up group complete heal, I think I'm gonna delete my cleric right now. That spell is definitely a cool spell to have, but it's a neat trick, nothing more. The casting cost as well as the taunt factor and the rarity of conditions that call for such a thing make the spell WORTHLESS.

All things being equal, I wanted a REAL trick at 60.
