Necro is fun to play .. no doubt, but the higher you go the more difficult it will be. Sure at lower level you can kill low red, but at higher level the NPC tends to make a beeline for you as soon as you cast. Your pet can so it down, but sometime they fail. At around 20 the NPC can hit for about 30+ points per hit. As a necro we cannot take many 30+ hits.
I have made it to 27 and still kicking. Just want to warn you that gaining a level a night at 20+ will be difficult, unless you play alot.
