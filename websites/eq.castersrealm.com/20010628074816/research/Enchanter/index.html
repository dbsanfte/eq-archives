ENCHANTER RESEARCH

Researching is a skill which becomes available to Enchanters from level 16 onwards.  It initially only requires 1 point to be placed in the skill to begin your training.  Research rises very quickly, generally you will get a point of skill for every attempt, or every second attempt.  Pouring all your spare points into research isn't really a good idea early on.  You may wish to save these for trade skills or some other purpose. 

Once you have trained your skill the necessary one point you then need to purchase the Tome of Endless Enchantments.  It is in this book that you will combine all of your research attempts.

Enchanters use four types of pages.  Tasarin's Grimoire, Velishoul's Tome, Sali's Writ, and Niliitm's Grimoire.  These research components can be found on various NPC's throughout Norrath.  Generally the page for the level spell you are after will be dropped on monsters whose level is between that of the spell and five levels above the spell.  So for example, the level 16 spell disempower spell components can be found on level 16 to 21 NPC's.  This is why Oasis is such an excellent spot for level 16 research components since most crocodiles and caimans fall within this range.

To combine the spell itself you will have one of three options. 

  • Either you will have the left and right half of one whole page.  You can tell which side it is by which one has the straight edge and which has the tattered edge.  An example of this would be combining the left and right sides of page 23. 
  • You may combine successive pages such as page 51 and 52.
  • Or you combine a faded page on it's own in an attempt to restore it.

Now that you know how to combine, you then have two choices.   You can either jump straight away into combining spells, or you can attempt to do the research quests first and raise your skill through these. 

Many people find it faster to simply purchase pages in bulk since they are all stackable for enchanters.  You can often pick up a number of the right components by going to a zone which has the pages you want and offering to purchase them through auction at the right price.  Keep in mind also that some research spells can be purchased from other casting class guilds.  Disempower can be obtained from the Shaman Guilds for instance.  Check the Spell List for details on these.
