Sunday May 6, 2001

FORUM CHANGES

As some will be aware, we recently changed our forums to require registration before you can post or reply.

There was a bug which was causing some people to receive verification emails which had no password in them. This should now be fixed.

Please re-register on the forums to get a new password, or email baelish@castersrealm.com if you experience further problems.

NEED MORE MOJO

An enchanter posted to the Developer's Forum today asking Alan if any more spells would be implemented for Enchanters due to them having less higher level spells than other classes. Alan replied that while they may have less higher level spells they have by far the most overall spells and thus no new spells are being implemented at the time.

PVP CHANGES COMING SOON

The new PvP changes are here! In the next patch the following changes (and perhaps more) will be activated: Sanctuary levels which create a minimum level for PvP to be participated in, a new consider system, and anti-delevelling code. These will effect all PvP servers.

THINKING CLEARLY

Absor replied to the comments of some players regarding the Robe of Flowing Thought. Absor explained that there was never a promise made that a mana regeneration item would not be created and also the fact that this item does not take away from the abilities of an Enchanter. He also stated that while this ability regenerated mana it stacks with bard and enchanter regeneration creating just as much desire for clarity and clarity II.
