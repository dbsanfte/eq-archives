NO BENEFITS FOR THE GENOCIDAL

Alan posted briefly to the Developer's Corner forums to explain to a player why there are no positive faction hits for PvP combat. Alan stated that this would be easily exploitable in any manner and just can't be added.

HELP AT HAND

A confused player asked Absor the reason why items were not clickable from within bags. Absor explained that this is not a bug, but rather the rationalization that an item must be 'at hand' in order to use it.

Click here for details.

PATCH WARNING

The EverQuest team released some very important news regarding the next patch. The next patch is scheduled for may 30th, however the warning is the fact that some corpses may dissapear during the patch. They are recoverable if a corpse is lost, however it is best to have no corpses out during the time of the patch.

Monday May 14, 2001

RING OF THE DAIN COMPLETED

Congratulations to the Triton Guild on Povar who are the first worldwide to finish the complete Coldain Ring Quest.

The final battle goes for over 4 hours, involving an epic staged fight between the Giants of Velious and their Dwarven foes.

The reward is an upgrade to the previous rings of the quest. A number of other items drop during the main event.
