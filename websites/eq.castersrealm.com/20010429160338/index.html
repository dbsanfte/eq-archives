Saturday April 28, 2001

RESIST THIS!

A player questioned Absor as to why damage shields have always been unresistable even for low level players while a high level player can be completed resisted on the same monster. Absor clarified that this is an advantage of the spell type and is not going to be changed.

Click here for details.

OOPS, WE DID IT AGAIN

Alan posted to the Developer's Forum to make clear a mistake on the recent patch message. While the patch message stated that Great Divide kodiaks were being flagged as animals, Alan stated that this was in fact not live and only on the test server. However this should go live in the next patch.

Click here for details.

Submitted by Kivlan, 1:05 AM
 

YOUR EYES DECIEVE YOU

A hybrid posted to the Developer's Forum today asking a question regarding the mana cost values of hybrid spells. This player asked that why hybrid spells, which cost more to cast, are still listed as the same mana cost as the parent class spell. Absor explained that the hybrid cost increase is done via the game code, not the spdat.

Click here for details.

Submitted by Kivlan, 1:03 AM
 

THINKING CLEARLY

An enchanter queried Absor regarding the difference between the item effect Flowing Thought and the Clarity line. Absor replied with a quote from Lawrence Poe, the 'spell guy' of EverQuest. Lawrence explained that while similiar spells, Clarity is still the more powerful. Absor also commented that the 'new Cazic Thule' from which this item is obtained is no push over.

Click here for details.

BARRING THE FORCE
