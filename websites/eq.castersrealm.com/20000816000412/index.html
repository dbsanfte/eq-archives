ACTION ON THE HOMEFRONT

Now that the server move is almost complete I thought I would give a brief site update. We currently have a large backlog in FAQ's and Strategies but we will be working through these in the next week or so to bring them up to date. We will be totally redesigning how strategies operate to make them easier to use. People continue to submit irrelevant prices which we are weeding out as we catch up on our price validations, and we also hope to have a full Rogue Poisoning section up by late tomorrow.

We will be moving the forums on to a more powerful forum server today, so there may be a brief period of downtime for these. We will also soon have irc.castersrealm.com online for player discussions, as well as a number of new site areas.

Most people should have any of their DNS issues with the site resolved by now, but if not give your ISP a little more time. This is probably the last major revision of site design we will be doing until the next expansion comes out, so if you have any ideas for additions or changes you would like on the site, please drop me an email.

Once again I would like to thank you all for your patience over the last month or so with our servers performance. I have always tried to ensure that the site is as fast as possible with the least fuss and best information online. With our new servers we should be able to deliver this.

THE RUBICITE RUMOURS

After numerours discussions with players it seems not all servers have yet had any Rubicite dropping in Najena. There appears to be different zone data on different servers and drops of the rare red armor are moving across the various worlds of Norrath. The drops seem to have finished on many servers, but are only starting on others.

The closest explanation we have so far is that it was part of a GM run quest involving an abduction of the Avatar of Fear from Cazic-Thule by King Thex and his minions. Since we have heard that the drops have been moving from server to server at different times, this may very well be true. There has yet to be an official statement from Verant, but one will no doubt emerge some time today.
