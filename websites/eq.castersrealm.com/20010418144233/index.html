Wednesday April 18, 2001

LUCLIN REVEALED

It has been revealed that the new expansion that Verant is creating is to be titled as 'Shadows of Luclin'. It turns out that Luclin is the Demi-Goddess of Shadows. Hopefully this will give some insight on what the expansion might offer!

Luclin, the Maiden of Shadows rules the Plane of Shadow, She is short of stature, about 4'9' tall; sleek and thin, with an elfin body structure. Her short hair is deep gray with long side locks down to her stomach. Her silver eyes have no pupils and her skin is solid black. She wears black and gray robes and a shadowy mist perpetually swirls around her feet. In one hand she bears a massive silver and platinum staff carved in the shape of two tendrils wrapping around one another to form two humanoid hands at the top. Floating just above the cupped palms of the hands is a small black orb whose surface has an iridescent sheen similar to that of oil.

YOU'RE OUTTA HERE!

Some new wizard spells are currently on the Test Server.  An extension of the translocate series, should these spells go live they would enable Wizards to send players one at a time to certain Wizard spires throughout the world of Norrath, without having to zone themselves.  As always with test information, these spells could change before going live, or not go live at all.

  • Translocate: Combine
  • Translocate: Fay
  • Translocate: North
  • Translocate: Tox
    •

Also on Test, the Troubador's Breastplate has had its its movement speed buff increased from a 65% to a 95% bonus, and the Wizard Spell Atol's Spectral Shackles has been made less resistable.
