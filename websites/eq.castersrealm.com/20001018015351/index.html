WE GOT MORE FOR YOU YET

We have had a busy day working on the site and made a few modifications:

  • Search News: You can search news from the left menu to find histories of patch messages, or statements by the Verant team. We have one of the most comprehensive databases of EverQuest news archives available and this feature will allow you to quickly find it. The new upgrade features a better layout and allows more results per page.
     
  • Archived News: Missed a little bit of EverQuest? Looking for something from a month or two ago? You can peruse our newly updated news archive section, also on the top of the left menu. This area is best used to quickly recap on the last week or two of news.
     
  • Player List Journals: A lot of people have found using our player list journals a great deal of fun. We have now added the ability to edit a journal entry. This will make it much easier if you make a typo or want to add to or change an entry in your characters life. You can manage your player journal in your player profile editor.
     
  • Guild Admin Update: We have updated the administration section for Guild Leaders in the player list. You can now generate lists of who is yet to accept your invitation to be listed in your guild, as well as your current membership. If you haven't yet had your leader add your guild to our database ensure they add their character and click on the guild "how to" link in the player profile editor.
     
  • Server News: We have updated our daily server news pages to now also include a quick headline section. If you would like to submit news for your server, or even become a voluntary reporter, simply add you character to the player list and start submitting news!
     
  • Server Forums: Want to discuss the latest happening on your server? You can do so by posting on our Server Forums which are accessible through the top left menu, the top right of this page and on the daily server news pages.
