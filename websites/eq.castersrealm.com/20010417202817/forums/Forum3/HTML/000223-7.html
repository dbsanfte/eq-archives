I've been playing my enchanter since May. She's interesting and by far the most fun character to be out of combat. In groups she is very effective at the myriad forms of damage prevention available to her. (Buffs, crowd control, debuffs, crowd control, dot/dd, crowd control). But in truth, the only thing she has that's unique, crowd control, exists amongst more and more other casters. If the resists I'm hearing about at higher levels are true (she's only 17th), then retirement will come.

I watched a number of enchanter nerfs come along. Some have effected me, others have not. In general, we submit to them quietly, turning to other portions of our varied spell list to manage the results. Overall they have hurt a fair amount, repeatedly narrowing our options. I revelled in the wide range of spells I get as an enchanter, until I've gradually seen how many are just not worth the trouble. It seems as I get higher and higher, my list of usable spells will continue to shrink.

The truly scary part is the continuing thread of magic resists I've seen in this long series of posts (yes, I read them all). If my role is eventually to diminish to crack and fighting speed dealer, then Meredeth will die at that point. Persoanally I'd be very interested in seeing Verant turn clerity into a self only spell. No, I don't want to deny the rest of the casting world the effects of it. But yes, I would be curious to see how many enchanters can get into groups if clarity is no longer something they bring to the party. While drastic, the results might finally force Verant to recognize that a class that's been diminished to only that is a class that might as well be removed.

The pet nerf is annoying, since generally now my pets are so fragile that I'm better off charming, while staying very very near the zone line on those rare nights I solo. But the pet nerf is more a side effect of what I see Verants overall attitude to our class as. Specifically, "Hey, enchanters are fine. We tuned them up just right a long time ago." Which, a long time ago, was true. Now, after repeated nerfs (Why fix the spell when you can just kill it? Enchanters don't complain), that is no longer the case.

I can understand Verant wanting to make Necro's more group friendly. In general, I've found them to be powerfull enough that groups have no problem accepting them. But when what is added to the Necro class in grouping comes straight out of enchanter turf, I wonder how many more changes Verant will make before realizing that their enchater population has gradually died out.

I'll never give up Meredeth. But at this point, I'm ready to sideline her while I develope a class that Verant is happy to support, such as a warrior or necro.
