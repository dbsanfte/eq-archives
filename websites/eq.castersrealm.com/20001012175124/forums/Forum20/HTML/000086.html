The swirlspine seahorses (SSS's) are completely resistant to magic and cold-based spells (though they seem to be vulnerable to fire-based spells) so don't even bother trying to root, mez, or stun them. If you get more than one just focus on one at a time and tank the others. Always helps to have plenty of clerics and you can play pseudo-cleric with Rune. The SSSs will attempt to heal each other but it seems to be a fairly weak heal so it's not hard to outdamage it.

The swirlspine guardians (SSGs) are mezzable and actually seem to have a reasonably low MR. I only got a couple resists during a battle against three of them. You can't let them break mez at all though or they'll Complete Heal each other and Phingel.

I took part in a failed raid over the weekend and in hindsight we shouldn't have killed the SSGs before going on to Phingel himself since the battle was way too long and respawns occurred. The trick seems to be to pull the SSSs, kill them, pull the SSGs and mez them, then pull Phingel and kill him, and then kill the SSGs.
