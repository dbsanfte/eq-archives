Verant can, and has, enforced any rule they've wanted whether it existed previously or not. They also choose NOT to enforce their rules when it suits them.

It is, unfortunately, one of the hazards of playing this game. From seeing others suspended for being victimized (however temporarily) and sometimes worse, I have low personal opinions of Verant's collective idea of customer support. In general, the attitude seems 'guilty until proven innocent', and of late, 'You were guilty of breaking this rule, even if we never told you it WAS a rule, shame on you.' There is no recourse, generally. There is little, if any, way to defend yourself or your actions if they decide you've crossed any one of a number of lines.

The moral of these stories is(IMO): Be ready for it, take it in stride, and try to enjoy the game anyway. Because there's nothing else YOU can do, except leave or be miserable.
