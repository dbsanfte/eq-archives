THE COMBINE EVACUATION

THE COMBINE EVACUATION

In the depths of the Timorous Ocean lies the greatest relic of the combine empire.  When the combines were seeking to expand into the Kunarkian continent they required a means to quickly bring people from their most powerful cities into the areas.  They developed the means to teleport small groups of people from one place to another.  Unfortunately, once the Iksar and Sarnaks realised their intent they did for a short time band together to drive them off the lands.  The shattered towers of the Dreadlands reflect perhaps the greatest memory of these days. 

The wielders of magic did however have an escape route planned.  Out in the depths of the ocean midway between Kunark and Antonica they placed an underwater chamber.  With the use of a special word anyone who was stranded on the islands could make their way into the chamber and return to their home city, or another city of their choice.  As Firiona led her people across the oceans, one small group on their rafts got lost.  They drifted for days until they saw four crumbling pillars emerged from the water.  After investigating they realised they had discovered the ancient emergency chamber.

Once they went inside they discovered there was no way back out!  They were forced to try the ancient magics imbued in the room and discovered them still fully functional!  Before they did this however they took their sextant co-ordinates and recorded them as -12000, 4360.

A few sidenotes:

  • No class can bind here.

  • Cabilis is the only Kunark city you can travel to, and it is a long way from the more serious actions in Kunark.

  • It is a long run to this chamber, it's no joy ride for easy transportation.

  • Whilst there are no guards in the room, you are near guards in the cities after almost every pot, and if you are KOS you will be killed.

  • Once you are inside the room there is no way out except by a pot.

  • Invisibility will not drop from using a pot.

May you find the use of the ancient chamber a boon to your life in Norrath!
