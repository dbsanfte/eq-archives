 ART COLORING COMPETITION 2!
 

 How would you like to win a sketch of your EverQuest or other RPG character by our site artist, William J. Murray?

Copy, save or print the black and white image below, color, and return the colored e-file here to enter our new coloring contest.

Print it out and color physically, or use your favorite graphics program!  As long as the final version can be e-mailed in it's up to you!  Preferred format is 600 pixels wide, in a .jpg format.

All entries must be received by Casters Realm by April 30, 2001.  Email your entry to art@castersrealm.com. 

All good entries will be posted on a Gallery page, and then judged by site artist William J. Murray to determine a winner.

The Winner will be announced May 5!
