I left Toxx and Erudin at lvl 5 when i got a frienly wizard to teleport me to Karana.

The run from Karana to Qeynos sewers, where I finally was bound, was one of the most exciting adventures I've played in the game. I knew if i died, the chances of EVER recovering my corpse were slim.

By the way - most people are really helpful - just explain what you're trying to do and they'll help. I got the following for free doing this:
Teleport to Karana and a +2int ring
Sow in North Karana
Camo in N Karana
Camo refresh in Qeynos Hills
Bind in Qeynos (after being shown the secret entrance to the tunnel)
Sow refresh in Qeynos.

After making it to Qeynos, I was bored of BB as a means to getting xp (I have a 21 Druid from Surefall), so I made another trip at lvl 7 to Freeport. Going thru Highpass as a KOS is tricky! LOL.

All this without the Gather Shadows which I only got at the necro guild in FP.

Last thoughts? The necro guild in Qeynos rocks (pentacle et al) as compared to the FP and Toxx ones! I still have to visit the Neriak one. Lvl 18 now.
