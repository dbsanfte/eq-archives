WIZARD RESEARCH

Aeons ago on the continent of Norrath, the wizard guild were all powerful masters of magic.  They could literally bend time and space to their will hopping easily around the many dimensions of Norrath.   But the wizards were arrogant and greedy.  Rivalries soon  broke out as to who was the most powerful Wizard of all, and what ensued was the most devastating war to ever take place in this once peaceful realm.  Under the guiding hand of Innoruuk the Prince of Hate sent forth his minions to take advantage of the moment.  Comets were brought down from the sky, fires rose from earth to the very pillars of the heavens.   The tier'dal were driven back into their dark forests, but not without a cost.   Many of the greatest wizards perished in the war.  As a result much knowledge was lost, and the remaining wizards who survived vowed that their guild would never again supply knowledge of such powerful spells to young wizards again.

But time has a way of unearthing secrets.   The guilds have found numerous books over the years which are held to contain the means of recreating these spells.  Unfortunately they are written in tongues so old only the greatest wizards can decipher them.  What has been discovered however is that the Lexicon, the powerful book where all spells are combined from the fundamental runes, can still work to recreate these lost spells. 

The guilds have been producing Lexicons en masse, and scribes work feverishly in the night to provide all with the unearthed books in an attempt to return the glory of the wizardry.

Once a Wizard or Wizardress reaches their sixteenth level they can be taught the fundamentals of researching these spells.  It initially only requires 1 point to be placed in the skill to begin your training.   Research rises very quickly, and generally you will get a point of skill for every attempt, or every second attempt.  Pouring all your spare points into research isn't really a good idea early on.  You may wish to save these for trade skills or some other purpose. 

Once you have trained your skill the necessary one point you then need to purchase one of the Lexicons sold by the guild.  It is in this book that you will combine all of your research attempts.

Wizards use mystical runes to recreate their spells.   These runes are the leftover remnants of artifacts lost in the great war. Runes can be found on various NPC's throughout Norrath.  Generally the rune for the level spell you are after will be dropped on monsters whose level is between that of the spell and five levels above the spell. 

To combine the spell itself you simply place the correct runes within the Lexicon and combine them.  If you succeed, the runes will meld and produce a scroll which you can then scribe into your book.

Now that you know how to combine, you then have two choices.    You can either jump straight away into combining spells, or you can attempt to do research quests first and raise your skill through these.   

Many people find it faster to simply purchase runes in bulk.   You can often pick up a number of the right components by going to a zone which has the runes you want and offering to purchase them through auction at the right price.   Keep in mind also that some research spells can be purchased from other casting class guilds.  Check the Spell List for details on these.
