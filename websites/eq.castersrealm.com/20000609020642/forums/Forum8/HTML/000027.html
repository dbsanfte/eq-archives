The spectre in Feerrot is just outside the cave to PoF, and yes you will get exp, you can lvl as far as level 47 before they start turning green. Use Venom of the snake(VoS), Panic undead and sick your pet on them. Expel undead as well. Spectres have approximately 1600HP

WARNING... Spectres have Large Agro ranges don't take an active spec near the cave kite it away from the cave...

The spectre are levels 34-37 and the location is in the southwest corner of zone. If you are standing in the south west corner in zone and start to run North, when you come to the first hill turn and head east slowly, The spector will be standing next to a tree there. Or stand on that hill and cast your locate undead spell, It should point you at him. Very good exp.


Oh yea make sure to pull spectre north because if he heaads south he will pull the others out of tha cave.
