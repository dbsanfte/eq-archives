A number of things may be occuring here. One is that even though pet delay no longer improves when giving them fast weapons, I bet the AI code that determines what a pet uses is still called. Therefore giving them a "worse" weapon will cause them to ignore it.

Also, a pet will dual wield in favor of single wielding. They will not equip two-handed weapons if they have a couple of single-handers already. (The higher level animation sword is actually 1-handed, even though it is big-assed like a 2-hander).

What likely happened though, knowing how software development works, is they just removed the code that speeds up NPCs with faster weapons, but left the code in that is called when an NPC has various weapons to choose from. Your staff was probably slower than your dagger.

You only need to give an animation one weapon, it comes with one already, and will use it in addition to the one you have.
