	<td valign='top' align='left' class='news' width='90%'><p class='boldheader'>SUMMIT FROM THE MAC PERSPECTIVE</p><font face='verdana' size='2pt'>EQ Mac have their report up on the summit with some specific details regarding their server.<P> Khama was rallying the Mac Flag during the event and it was good to see SOE invite people from every server.<P> Here's a portion of the article: <ul> Al'Kabor is not going away.
