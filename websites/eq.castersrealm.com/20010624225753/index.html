Sunday June 24, 2001

FAN FAIRE GOES ABROAD

It's been announced that the next EverQuest Fan Faire will be held in Europe! This will be the first EverQuest Fan Faire held outside of North America.

Dates and other information are still to come. We will post details as they come to hand.

Submitted by Baelish, 5:58 PM
 

ROGUE PVP CHANGES

Kendrick posted this onto The Safehouse message boards regarding Rogue PvP Changes next patch:

Hiding and Invis will take you off targetting with the next patch on Sullon Zek.  Spells cast before losing the target will resolve, so it will require some tactics to use properly.  If the person that has you targetted has See Invisible, it's easy to retarget if you are in line of sight.

In addition, on Sullon Zek, you [Rogues] will be able to steal coin (and only coin) from other teams players, with some limitations I will leave you to discover on your own.

Submitted by Baelish, 5:51 PM
 
Saturday June 23, 2001

STACKING FIXES

Lawrence Poe announced some new stacking fixes to be implemented in the next patch. After the next patch Skin Like Nature and Natureskin will no longer be overwritten by necromancer DOTs or DOT heals.

Click here for details.

Submitted by Kivlan, 1:19 AM
 

NEW PATCH DATE

Alan announced a change to the planned patch on today's boards:

On Wednesday, June 27th at 3AM PDT (10AM GMT), all servers will be coming down for a scheduled patch. The estimated downtime is 10 hours. This is a reschedule of the patch previously scheduled for Tuesday June 26th.

The EverQuest Operations Team

Submitted by Kivlan, 1:17 AM
 

BETTER FOR BARDING

A concerned bard queried Absor regarding the new change being done that will cause spell interuption when ducking and sitting. Absor explained that this will not effect bards and that they will still be able to sing and even start singing while sitting.

Click here for details.

Submitted by Kivlan, 1:14 AM
 

YOU WILL NOT EVADE ME!

A player suggested that instead of an anti-gate spell wizards recieve a spell that would prevent the summoning of players. Alan replied and explained that while this is a good idea it would never be a viable spell due to the fact that summoning prevents exploits.

Click here for details.

Submitted by Kivlan, 12:58 AM
 
Friday June 22, 2001

ESSENCE OF THE PROBLEM

Absor replied to a necromancer who posted some concerns regarding the high cost to obtain an essence emerald. Absor stated that this cost is understood and the team feels it is well balanced and will not be changed in the near future.

Click here for details.

Submitted by Kivlan, 12:50 AM
 

DUCKING TO INTERRUPT

A new change has been added to the Test server to allow players to immediatly interrupt a spell that is being cast by ducking. Alan explained that while this is merely to fix an exploit the benefits will remain if this change goes live. Please remember that this change is on the Test server and may not make it to live servers.

Click here for details.

Submitted by Kivlan, 12:48 AM
 

MONSTER ILLUSIONS STILL NO

An enchanter posed a question to Alan regarding monster illusions being available to enchanters since monster summoning is available to mages. Alan explained that these are two different things and a monster mimic illusion would not work due to faction reasons.

Click here for details.

Submitted by Kivlan, 12:45 AM
