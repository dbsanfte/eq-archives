EBAY IS GONE!

In a move which has probably been just a question of time, Sony Online Entertainment appaears to have finally made a move against the online auctioning of EverQuest items through the Ebay website.

No official statement has yet been made from either Ebay or SOE regarding the recent removal of a large number of EverQuest related auctions but it appears the days of Ebaying EQ products may have come to an end. Whilst some auctions are still running they may have just been missed by the first cut.

Update: It has now been confirmed that this was a move done by SOE. Now that they have received notification from SOE requesting restrictions on the auctions, Ebay will no longer support the EverQuest related transactions on their site.

BROWSER PAINS

Just as a recap. We are still working to try and resolve the issues with Netscape 4.xx browsers. We understand that some people not on windows based systems are restricted to this older browser and are trying our best to get our pages working efficiently for them.

Internet Explorer 4 or higher will work perfectly on the site. Netscape 6 will also work well.
