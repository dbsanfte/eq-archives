TRAVELLER BOOTS LIVE

Traveller Boots are now available for purchase in Solusek A off the tinker bag gnome vendor.

These boots are similar to journeyman boots except they take 1.5 seconds to cast the effect on the player. They are also no drop and lore.

Prices range from 5250 - 7000pp depending on your faction and charisma, making 10 stack shaman potions still very attractive unless you have money to burn.

THE WINNER IS...

The winner of our second art coloring competition has been decided. This from our judge William J. Murray:

Again, this contest had many professional-level entries. In my opinion these four entries demonstrated superb creativity, use of color, and coloring mechanics that makes them stand out from the rest. I would like to congratulate each of these entrants for making my job very difficult in choosing a single winner.

However, the image sent in by Kasane evoked such a complete sense of the ethereal, as if the figures in the art were either mythological beings or something out of a dream, that I’m going to have to declare this the winner. Though I personally prefer the 'tighter' coloring styles used in each of the other three finalist entries, the contrast between light and dark, the flowing color scheme that forces the viewer into a sense of the surreal, and the absolutely stunning 'mood' of the minotaur figure in particular demands first place recognition.

Congratulations, Kasane! Well done.

The winning entry: Kasane

Runners-Up: Uberbechin Crimsonwolf, Amanda Zoller, Nizzaren Shadowpact

The third coloring contest is now underway, and you can get your copy of the raw art here.

Also in conjunction with the announcement of today's winner we have also released a series of coloring in poster packs in the Casters Realm Shop.  The packs come with 15 posters which are ready to either be hung on the wall, or colored in.  Colored posters will also be available in the near future.
