To address the Druid list, there are several errors and omissions.

====

- DOTs changed negatively, making Kiting less efficient

Yes.

+ DoT changed positively to enhance group play

Yes, but the mob vanishing(no corpse/xp) bug still exists in certain *multi-group* situations. Happened last night in Kedge and 3 times on my last PoF trip.


+ Increased druid's tracking ability

Incorrect. Druid tracking was nerfed in May. It has been capped at skill 20 (same as a level 4 Ranger) ever since (For comparison bard tracking caps at 100).

+ Many new quests requiring a tracker to find outdoor spawn

Druids can *see* farther than they can track. How is this going to help a druid find an outdoor spawn? (Dungeon yes, outdoor no)

+ Added more Class Specific Quests

There were only two druid specific quests added since release. Solvedi scim and Sap Sheen Staff in he Rathe Mountains. If you are not familiar with the stats and items required to get these, please take a look at your nearest spoiler site. Then deny that this quest is an insult to anyone who completes it.

+ Added more Class Specific Items

Solvedi Scim and Sap Sheen Staff. See above.
The *only* other Druid-specific items added since release are in PoF/PoH.

+ Enhanced High Level (35+) Meditation

Yes.

+ Enhanced Pet/Charmed Animal Pathing

Druids don't have "pets". They have "charms". No one in their right mind would issue a "/pet follow me" command to a charm. Charms are purposely killed after each battle for the safety of all involved.

+ Enhanced Some Druid Anti-Movement Spells (c.f. Ensnare, Grasping Roots, etc.)

No change was made to grasping root line of spells. Snare was nerfed to have a higher casting time. Ensnare was nerfed to have the same speed decrease effect as snare. Nerfed, not enhanced.

+ Lowered the Casting Level of Some Druid Teleport Spells

This was a bug fix, not an enhancement. Refer to the proper eqnews.txt file that stated that Succor Ro was incorrectly tagged as 49 when it should have been 39. "some spells" is incorrect. Succor Ro was the only one affected and it was a bug fix. In fact it was not until four patches after the original patch message that it finally got in.


========

Itms left out:
- Increased mana cost for Succor spells from 150 to 300.
+ Changed level 49 Ice from Column to DD spell.
+ Harmony was fixed.
+ Treeform was given a hp regen effect.
- You can no longer move while in treeform.
+ Invisibility (camo) now gives a warning before it disappears.
+ Form of the Great Wolf was given Ultravision.
- Levitate nerf.

Brad's list is woefully short on actual nerfs, and misrepresents the truth at the very least. Many have commented on casters so I will focus on the bard.

BARDS:
+ Added stat and other bonuses to instruments

Unless the instruments from the UberMobs have been changed this has not ocurred. No one has even seen the small brass trumpet mentioned in the patch; and the mino horn is unchanged.

+ Added more Class Specific Quests

Only added quests for bards are the ones to replace Rubicite. That's it, nothing more. What other class can say this?

+ Added more Class Specific Items

Only new Bardic items are found on Planar Lords or Dragons. Come to think of it, only Bardic items are found on Planar Lords or Dragons. Oh, except that Wind Spirit Shawl that I discarded in favor of patchwork at lvl 2.

+ Corrected Bug that let monsters attack you from far distances through your pet

Charm/root bug? Bards cannot Root. No bard could ever have suffered from this bug. Not that they shouldn't fix it.

+ Added Addition Effects to Some Bard Songs

What new effects? If there are any they must be after level 21. Of course they could mean the level 22 song that did absolutely nothing until the last patch. How they failed to notice that was broken from day one is a mystery.

+ Added New Bard Songs

From day one we were told bards get one song each level. That still isn't true. The songs that got implemented were always supposed to be there. When they were implemented they were obviously untested. How else do you explain 3 DoT's that could only be cast on yourself. Thankfully they are fixed now.

+ Added Ability for Bards to Punch 'Magic Only' Creatures

WooHoo! An actual honest to goodness enhancement. We did have this ability in Beta though before it was nerfed there.

+ Increased the Power of Some Bard Songs (c.f. Chant Series, Fifil’s, etc.)

Yeah, again so we hear. No change that anyone has been able to quantify.

So what about the nerfs left out?

How about the Charm cap. We don't get Charm until lvl 28 so its not good for long.

How about the Accelerando nerf? It used to break Root but the balancing team decided that was too powerful by GZ's own admission. It even used to work indoors at one time. At least you can still cast SoW before you enter a dungeon and have it there still. Obvbiosly Brad didn't ask GZ what had been changed.

How about our DoT's? They suffered equally with everyone elses. They do less damage in PvP and do less damage against Mobs chasing us. Oh yeah, if they included them they'd also have to admit how our DoT'd did not get a longer duration like everyone elses did.

What about the 'silent nerfs' Lullaby at one time had a very consistent effect. If a Mob did not resist it nodded. When it nodded it acted as if stunned, it stopped moving, it stopped attacking, it stopped casting. Now, as of about 3-4 patches ago, 50%+ of the time it isn't resisted the Mob nods. That's it, nothing more. The mobs Nods, keeps moving, keeps attacking, keeps casting. If its not a nerf it sure is a peculiar bug.

So lets do a recap.
Plusses for Bards not refuted: 3 (best case)
Nerfs not admitted: 3 (best case)

So at best, giving some benefit of the doubt we have 3 nerfs I'd rate at 8 or higher on a 1-10 scale. And 3 enhancements I'd rate at 3 or less on the same scale. Not particularly balanced.
