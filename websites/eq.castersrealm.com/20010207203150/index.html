Sunday February 4, 2001

MONKS GET A GRANDMASTER

Congratulation to Daahoud of Veeshan who won the Monk Best of the Best final on the Test Server today.

Daahoud defeated Kimoto of The Tribunal to claim the title of Grandmaster winning the first two of the three finals. Kimoto was awarded the title of master.

The final was between two humans, with only one iksar making it past the quarter finals.

We will have the video of this and the wizard event up in a couple of weeks.
