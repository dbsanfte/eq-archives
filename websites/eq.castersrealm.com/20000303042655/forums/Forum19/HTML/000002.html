They mean mesmerization, the area affect mesmerize you get at level 16, not the standard level 4 mes. Mesmerization will have a chance to mesmerise all NPCs within range of whomever you have targeted. Note that if you're in range, you may be mesemerized as well (but not your team members, except in PvP where it works differently). The other difference is I don't believe mesmerization ever wipes that hate list - at least I've never seen it do that.

Another neat thing about mesmerization and hate lists is that the wipe is done, I believe, at the beginning of the stun. Therefore, if they are all close together when you break stun on one, the others hate you too since they are in aggro range. In effect, if you pull one sleeper out of the bunch you'll have to wipe the others if you want them to forget you.

Since you can't cast mesmerize or enthrall over mesmerization, you'll have to either case AE Mes again or mem blur them.

Generally if you are a fair distance back away from the MOB's and tanks are up there fighting you won't get jumped when area mez wears. I usually drop area mezz, dot the one the tanks are fighting, redrop area mezz every 5-10 seconds. I very very rarely have problems with getting the mobbo's broken up and on me. Another key is not to nuke the daylights out of the mob the tanks are on either. If you pull him over and split up the group then it gets real difficult to keep them all under.

I just recently got our area effect spell gravity flux as well. Thus far I have had a good success rate at dropping area mez, dot, gravity flux, and area mez again without getting anything on me (key again is put good distance between you and the mobs). Last night in Sol B I was able to work area mez, gravity flux, area mez three times on four mob's prior to any of them breaking and jumping me. At which point I color fluxed, moved and area mezzed again.

Stay away from the mob cluster and go super nuke easy and you should have no problems using this spell. Remember enchanters are more about finesse not brute force. If you want to be a brute play a wizard.
