It's alsoe quite easy, to collect dervish cutthrugh rings (sp?, also called derv rings) from various derv camps and trade them in at Ortallius in Soutern Dessert of Ro. That Orc asks for 4 rings each time, but actually 3 rings are sufficient. You gain a liitle bit experience, a rusty weapon, some small change and ... tada .... faction with temple of solusek ro goes up. Only drawback is, that your faction with shadowed men goes down. I did this quest very often and now the fire thingies in the temple con amiably to me. Next to Ortallius is an erudite which allows you to grab in his sack to get a random scroll if you give him 3 pp. Mostly you get a rotten illegible scroll, which purpose i don't know. But once in a while you get wizard spells in the level 16 or 20 range. Same faction adjustments here. Both can be found if zoning in from Oasis turn right and stick to the wall.
