PVP TEAMS TRANSFER STATUS

Gordon posted details concerning the Talon Zek / Vallon Zek character transfers. It was decided that the transfer will not be executed, and that Vallon Zek may be changed into a coin loot only server similar to Talon Zek after all.

TUNING MEETING HIGHLIGHTS

Abashi released some details on yesterdays tuning meeting including spell changes such as an extended window for XP Res, and a lower casting time for Jolt. The Hole is also expected to go live this week.

Click here for details.

Submitted by Maea, 2:29 AM
 

 

HIGHBIES BANNED FROM DRAGONS?

Gordon claimed that a change will be happening in the near future to restrict very high level characters from easily killing Nagafen and Vox. The change would be designed so as to not prevent those of mid to high level from attempting the beasts.

Click here for details.

Submitted by Maea, 2:13 AM
 

 

THE SEARCH FOR USEFUL RAIN

Abashi responded to a post on the Gameplay Forum relating to Area Effect rain spells. After a large number of posts and illustrations as to where this spell-type had problems, he asserted that the way they currently operated was consistent with the design philosophy.
