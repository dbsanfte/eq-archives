Thursday May 24, 2001

GAME CARDS COMING!

SOE announced today that you will soon be able to buy Game Time cards to pay for your monthly subscriptions to EverQuest, amongst other titles. JCB cards will also now be accepted.

This should help the younger players, or players without credit cards, get into the game much more easily.
