	<td valign='top' align='left' class='news' width='90%'><p class='boldheader'>PATCH MESSAGE</p><font face='verdana' size='2pt'><p>A big patch today!  Here are some of the major changes:</p> <ul>   <li>The revamped Plane of Mischief is now available on all Live servers. </li>   <li>The Coirnav encounter (Plane of Water) has been changed. Be much more    careful if you have been relying on stuns to deal with this encounter. </li>   <li>Completing Hard adventures will yield more points. </li>   <li>Find Feature added. This will create a mystical indication of the path to    take to find certain people. This feature works in the Bazaar and in newbie    zones and cities. In the Bazaar you just click on the merchant you seek in the    Bazaar window and click the Find Trader button.
