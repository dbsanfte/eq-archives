The Spiderwebs Of Rebirth
               
I was travelnig along dressed in the attire of the necromancers of the dark elves as normal and was advanced by people unknown to me. They told me the only way I could get by was to pay or fight. I didn't have any gold on me so I said, "I will fight." They told me that was my last mistake. I went and took off those robes I treasured as much as my own skin and prepared myself. What I had not known was of the man behind me who jumped out and impaled me on his blade. I arose from the grave and took up a blade made of pure obsidian and killed them all . Now I stand as Lord Renzokuken.
- Renzokuken
