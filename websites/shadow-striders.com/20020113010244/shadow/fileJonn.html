Jonn
Troll Shaman
 
History: Greetings fellow walkers of the dark path,

My name is Jonn, and I am a troll from the fine city of Grobb. Well, as fine as one could expect it to be. At an early age, I found myself lacking the, how should I say it, intellectual inadequecies of my brethren. I think more articulately than most trolls, which lead me to the path of the Shaman. Unfortunately it is the only magick taught in the caverns of Grobb. It was during my time within the guildhalls of Grobb that I met Jane. She, like most of my kind, found that brute strength was a very effective way of finding food. She's a warrior. She has befriended me, which I find to be very helpful in many situations. Like when I was nearly killed by a disgusting froglok. I'm a little embarrassed by the incident, but suffice it to say, I overexerted my magicks and she "bashed the little froggie."

Not very long ago, I met the human, Lord Faileas. I think I once read about him. Maybe not. But I do remember he commanded the respect of a Teir'Dal. Any human capable of that should be honored and congratulated. He offered us the chance to spread our influence. Even after Jane tried to eat him. He was very polite about the whole thing. I hope he doesn't hold it against me. I think I will take the opportunity to show the world that some trolls can speak common articulately. Ok, most can't, but I feel I must try and destroy the stereotype. Jane's just hungry.

Faileas' View: Quite impressive intellect for a troll. For anyone, really. Now how to get troll drool out of silk? Another story.

Envy's View: Oh Jonn was nice enough, but the company he keeps is terrible. If I have to explain one more time to his girlfriend that I'm not an appetizer, I swear I'll...

Sugan's View: I don't look forward to meeting this couple, if Faileas' meeting was any indication.
