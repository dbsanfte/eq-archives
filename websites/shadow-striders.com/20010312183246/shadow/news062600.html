Greetings!

As you can see, the new websites for the Wind Riders and Shadow Striders have gone live. This is the look we always intended to have, though will take a bit more updating since it's hardcode. (Old sites had a lot of scripting, more efficient for forms, but sacrificed unique design) There is still quite a bit to do, especially with the great many stories everyone has submitted. I'll update those as I can.

Please do explore. Refamiliarize yourself with SS policies, and check out the FAQ on the front page. Also, take a look at the Member Files, they have a few neat features.

Both the new membership application form, and the story submission form are ready to be used! Instead of immediately appearing on the site though, your submissions will be emailed to us. We'll then post them manually to the website.

We hope to start a multi-day roleplaying (that goes unspoken) event on Friday, June 30th sometime between 3pm and 5pm PST. This event will begin with the SS, but will encompass both guilds during the weekend. Please note, this is very important. It's appreciated that everyone focuses thier energy to it and sparks great roleplay between our two guilds.

Officers you may have noticed the login screen that appeared. This will be enabled soon, when we create the officer site on this new server. That site will look an awful lot like the old ones, and will still use the convenient scripting. In other words, it's a stripped-down, information only website for officers.

Tell us what you think of the site!
