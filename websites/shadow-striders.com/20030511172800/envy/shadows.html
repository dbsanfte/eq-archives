Shadows.

So many fools casually place the shadows and darkness in the same group, and so many fools are wrong. There is not a place that the light does not touch, and therefore no true darkness. I can agree that darkness and light are true opposites, but not shadow and light. Shadow's true other is actually Reflection. Matter - things - are the middle ground. Things both reflect light and cast a shadow; and almost always do both. As for darkness… darkness is nothingness. Darkness cannot serve my goals, for it is devoid of Hate as well as light, love, and well… me. Dark is easy to bring forth; all one must do is destroy everything, and then destroy self.

Devotion to darkness = Madness.

I recall a time once, long ago, when I was stricken with such insanity… but we'll speak of that later. As of now I prefer a much more sophisticated approach to my existence, molding matter and shadow to create in my image. True power is not in darkness, but in Shadow. As for reflection… well, that is only to be manipulated as well.
