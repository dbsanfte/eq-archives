What Curiosity can do to People
                
I started off in Norrath like anyone else. I was a simple misled boy. But one day i saw a page in a magical book. It told of a dark, evil malicious guild known as the shadow-striders. I went home and asked my parents about it. They told me to stay away from them and anything that signify's them. The next year I became apprentice to my master, LOrd Saggreas. He taught me everything of my heritage as a dark elf and the heritage of necromancy. He teaches me of everything i know and more. i practice raising the dead and kill him in an attempt to gain more. They found him with his neck gashed open and me feeding off of his soul energy. They attacked me while i was feeding and banished me to wander. I remember from my childhood of the shadow-striders and wander to find them. i go and find them hidden because a member left evidence of where to find them. I go in and i sign up and become a shadow-strider!
- Galin Kar Symar
