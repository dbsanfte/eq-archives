The Confrontation I
               
T'was one fine night in Neriak, and after several hours of hunting, Septus decided that he should forget his troubles and wash them out with some kind of beverage. As he walked towards the bar closest to him, he could not hear a thing. Now, Septus may not have been the most world wise person in the Shadow Striders, but he knew something was wrong. Normally, you would hear the sound of drunkards yelling that they had just lost a game of King's Court, or the serene conversations between lightwalkers of how they looked down to watch the sunset the previous day.

When he opened the door to this abandoned inn, he was slightly relieved to see a barmaid, thanking the Gods that he wasn't going to be alone. However, the young Tier'Dal seemed rather distant. Her eyes staring out into the horizon. Shaking his head, he walked over to a table and sat down, to examine the riches he had found, bought, and stolen. He would probably use them as gifts for the Mistress. After a while, he stood up, and realised he had been there for quite a while, as the sun was no longer wearily looking down at Neriak, it was completely dark. Bewildered, Septus sat down again, trying to understand how he could have been there that long. It just didn't make sense.

Suddenly, out of nowhere, he heard breathing. Heavy breathing. And not soon after, he could tell it was near him, for he could feel it on the back of his neck. Confused, he turned around, to see what was happening, and there it was...The head of Morticus the damned. It's face, more horrid than he ever imagined. It began to laugh at him. Septus could no longer take this, so he ran as far as he could away from the inn...To a library. He was delighted to see that there were many there, all reading what they could. He saw a book there, labeled 'Revolution'. As he took a quick glimpse at the pages, he saw that it was documenting the great and infamous bloodlines across Norrath. He showed no interest in this, until , he saw the word 'Renegade'. That page read 'Arthur Renegade married Pryde K'Nen, only to embrace a curse, where they accidentaly had two children rather than one, the forbidden act. The two children Tritus and ----- were to be born. Tritus was to be the child of light, and -----, the Dark Chosen.' Septus didn't understand why his name had been inked out, and looking away from the book, he saw that everyone there appeared to be 'frozen'. It reminded him of the time he heard Sapphon say 'When time freezes, so no one can hear you scream'. When he looked down at the book again, all the ink had dissapeared, and the same image, on every page was slowly coming into vision. It was the face. The face of Morticus. Septus closed the book tightly and dropped it onto the ground, only to see the spectre of Morticus the damned, also known as The Carnage standing before him. The spectre spoke the words 'Come child...You are mine now'. At that, Septus fainted. Upon reaching conciousness, Septus stood up and ran. He ran all the way to Nektulos forest, and waited there, for Envy T'Druis to come. Only, he couldnt push what he had just seen out of his mind...He couldn't help but think that this was only the beginning...
- Septus
