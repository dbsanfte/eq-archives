Zanshin
Iksar Shadow Knight
 
History: Hatched for one purpose his Ancestors & Lord demand... to Dominate the lands that are the Iksar's right by any means neccesary. Fear, Pain, Torture, even Bloodshed. Preferably all of the above.

Power is to be respected, but also to be earned. Owing Allegiance only to those that deserve his respect, and can prove that they deserve such a blessing. For to earn his respect, is to earn that of his ancestors and Lord. And to have the blood of untold generations of Darkness at your side. Still striving to etch his place in the world. Death will only bring his wrath in another form, or even worse... another incarnation.

"In my Being, flows my Heritage.
In my Soul, courses their Will.
By these Powers I hereby serve.
And you, to me. Are just another Kill."

-Zanshin, Claws of Destiny

Faileas' View: I have heard of this newly found continent, known as Kunark. And it seems to have revealed a previously unknown race, known as the Iksar. I have yet to meet any in person, for I have been imprisoned. But it seems that they follow Cazic-Thule, and are therefore more aligned to my way of thinking than the rest of the world. We shall see if these Iksar are worthy of the Shadows.

Envy's View: Until I learn more about them, I am wary of the Iksar.

Sugan's View: Perhaps the Iksar don't know any better. Perhaps they can still be saved.
