Tantrik
Teir'Dal Warrior
• 12/20/00
History: I was found under a table in The Maiden's Fancy by one of the...girls...it seems my mother had left me there for reasons best left unsaid for the time being.

I became the son of all the girls there. I couldn't have asked for a better childhood. They loved and cared for me like I was there true son. They taught me to read and write, taught me of the ways of the Tier'Dal, and brought in trainers that I might be trained in the warriors ways and help protect them in their...business ventures.

My life stayed this way until about my 16th birthday...that's when a few of the members of the Indigo Brotherhood came to tell me that it was time for me to serve my GoD and join them that I might put my blade in service for the glory of Innoruuk. I didn't want to go, but they didn't really give me a choice.

I did my training, it was hard but bearable and I ended up with the respect of many of the elder members of the Brotherhood. But the thing that kept me going was knowing that once my training was over I would see my mother's again...or so I thought.

I still remember the feeling that came over me when I walked into the Maiden's fancy to find out that a band of masked brigands had come in late one night and robbed and killed everyone that was there. Of all my mothers only one had survived, and she was not long for the world.

I spent the next few months caring for her until her wounds eventually took her from me.

Something snapped when she died. I realized that nothing really mattered...not gods, not people, not anything...because all things ended or died. It was actually a rather freeing revelation. I realized that no matter what I did or didn't do, it really wouldn't matter in the grand scheme of things...Which made everything so much easier.

I did later find out who my real mother and father where...It even turned out I had a brother...but that is another story...
