hat we are
We are a large group of friends that are working towards being able to do big things together. We are supportive of each other and put effort into helping each other so we grow as a guild. We are a 'nice' guild. We do not kill steal. We do not train on purpose. We do not harass. We can be trusted in trades and things such as holding gear for someone. Stealing kills or camps, scamming, ninja looting or deliberate training are all grounds for suspension from ToN.

 

ur Dream
Our dream is simple. We dream of being able to stand on the cold dead necks of every big mob in the game, and look around to see a room full of friends grinning back (alive if possible).

We are at heart a very large group of friends. Unfortunately, like in all social groupings, the group is comprised of dynamics that don’t always promote unity. This means that conflicts will arise. Some of those conflicts will arise in regards to the leadership. All our policies are publicly posted. If you don’t agree with our policies, don’t apply for membership. Don’t expect that if you join us you can change us to meet your expectations.

One thing that is necessary is that the membership believes in and supports the officer core. If you can’t do this, then this is not the guild for you. The Guild Leader and the Officers will decide the day to day efforts of the guild. Voice your opinion, discuss it but when the decision is made - pull with your guildmates and support the Officers.

 

articipation
This is simple. Participate or Level or Leave. Travelers are working hard on achieving the best that Everquest has to offer. We are going new places, seeing new things, and improving our gear and raid skills. You need to be 60, and you need to participate in guild activities. Therefore if you are a member of or hope to be a member of ToN, you will participate in activities, or level your buns to at least 60, or decide that ToN's not right for you.

 

oot
Items gained from guild raids or activities will be awarded to the membership or guest attendant at the raid via DKP or /random.

If the item is dropable and there is no interest, item will be held in the guild bank to be used by members not present at the raid or to be sold for guild funds. Items that are no drop will be destroyed or opened to the zone as the raid leader requests.


ydras
Members running multiple accounts can expect to see their hydras rewarded, but not at the same rate as main characters. You will not be required to bring multiple characters on raids, but many of our raids are strengthened by our members who hydra and their participation will be rewarded.

 

aid loot when with an allied guild
We split it approximately down the middle, they get to decide how to distribute theirs, and we distribute ours according to our loot rules.

 

aid loot with multiples and with non-guild
Typically, non-guild will be present on raids because they are interested in learning more about ToN, and getting to know us. Usually these will be players applying or considering applying to ToN as members. They are not eligible for loot that main characters, hydras, alts, or probationary members want. This means that although there is some possibility of loot for non-guild on our raids, it's limited. If you are inviting someone to a raid, ensure they are aware of this, and ensure you've cleared their presence with the raid leader.

 

egular Raids
Our Events forum lists the schedule for our regularly planned raids. Every member of ToN is required to check the forums at least weekly.

 

embership
As our requirements are constantly changing, please find our current requirements here.

New members have approximately a 1 month trial during which time they and we can find out how well they fit in. New sponsorships can be done through the forum. They will remain on the board for a full week to allow people to have the chance to say yeah or nay before they are added or dismissed. Comments should be accompanied by sound reasons.


ponsors:
Any guild member in good standing may be primary sponsor for ONE person per month. Any number of seconding. Sponsors are required to insure that the person they sponsor meets all the requirements and make sure that they learn all those little details that we take for granted. Sponsoring someone is a serious responsibility.

 

rial Period
1 month long during which you may not sponsor anyone else. This is a time to get to know the guild and see how well you fit in with us. Secondary will not be guilded in during this time, but upon achieving full member status every character is welcome. New members are required to attend at least 3 major guild raids during the trial month.

 

action
Travelers of Norrath are primarily on Giant and Dwarf faction. If you are unwilling to be KOS to CoV you will be better off in another guild. There will be no avoiding guild raids because of being on a different faction tree than the guild. Having some chars different is fine as long as main is same as us.

 

uild Tags
The use of /anon is strongly discouraged. Please use /role if you need to hide your class. Wear your ToN tag proudly.

If for some reason you feel the need to remove your guild tag for something that requires it (i.e.: GM event, helping a friend temporarily establish 10 members for a new guild, etc), you must tell an officer ahead of time, via tells or email. You will have to reapply if you choose to detag under any other circumstances, as would any other non-member wanting in, and all accumulated DKP will be lost. The Guild Leader may choose to allow you to retag without reapplication if you request it within 24 hours, but all DKP will still be lost. Do not remove your tag lightly, sleep on it if you're upset and then discuss issues with an officer before detagging over a concern.

 

orts/Res's/Corpse Summons
These are benefits of being in a guild. Do not abuse the people that can do these things as they too have characters to play. Those that can do such things, if you're available please do so, and don't be afraid to say no if you're not available. There is to be no charging guild members for these services. Understand that no guildmate "owes" you a port, rez, or summons and be courteous.

 

ear and the guild
If you have something to donate to the guild, we have a mule to hold this stuff. If you have something that you really want money for, please don't auction it in guild channel. You of course can tell the guild you've got item x you need money cause you're saving for item y. But do not put everything you have to sell up in guild channel or do it repeatedly. Keep it simple as the guild channel is not an auction channel. It would be best if we could keep it to giving in the guild, but that is not always feasible. Please don't sell stuff off that you know people need if you can help it. If you receive an item from the guild you may use it as part of a trade for an upgrade, but otherwise when you upgrade it the guild would like it back to give to someone else that needs. There is provision for awarding DKP for gear donated back to the guild. Selling raid loot? We can't stop you, but we frown upon it.


uild Channel
Keep it clean, we have some young players and we have people with all sorts of different tastes. What's funny to you may be offensive to someone else. Complaining. Whining. Bitching. Arguing. Threatening. Sniveling. Rudeness. Blatant sexual commentary towards individuals. Grossness. All other forms of socially questionable behavior are NOT permitted in open channel. If someone asks you to stop, stop. Greed, envy, jealousy are things that have no place amongst friends. If you have issues, please take them up in a constructive manner with individuals or the officers in direct tells. Make sure that you have sorted out in your head what your issues are, and that you can present them in a coherent manner. We will do our best to address them with you. When someone gets an item please be HAPPY for them, your turn will come. If you feel that something was a mistake or forgotten, please send a direct tell to the person distributing the loot (we make mistakes, it's no crime to make sure that we are aware of everything...and we'll be happy to take a minute when we can to explain a decision to you).

Personal attacks upon guildmates will not be tolerated in guildchat and will be grounds for a three-day suspension. Again, if you're asked to stop, stop. Expressing unchecked aggression publicly towards another guildmate will be grounds for an immediate 3-day suspension. The third such suspension will be your last, as you will be detagged permanently and unwelcome to reapply.

ower Leveling
This is an event that happens at the discretion of those that wish to do it. If a higher level character wishes to do some power leveling of a lower level character, the lower is welcome to accept or not as they wish. Lower level characters however do not request power leveling. Everyone has their characters to play and there's no reason why a person that has achieved a high level should be forced to help someone else to get there faster just because they can.

 

uspension
If it becomes necessary for an officer to remove your guild tag for violating any of these policies, that removal will not be permanent unless confirmed within 24 hours by a second officer or the guild leader. If removing your tag becomes necessary a second time and is confirmed by two officers, you can expect to have to reapply, and all accumulated DKP will be lost.
