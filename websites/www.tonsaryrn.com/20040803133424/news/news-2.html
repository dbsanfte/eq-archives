February 29, 2004

 

Been a bit since our last update. We continue to refine our Plane of Time Efforts. We are down to a smooth 2 day clearing, working on a 1 day. The place is still unforgiving if you have a mishap.

But all is well that ends up dead(before us that is......)

Plus it's always good to have industrial engineers in the guild, they have a tendancy to turn time into a work/motion study.

Plus we have had some luck on getting time breastplates to drop. Lots of bitching that leather drops to much until the guys saw the new Timeless Models.

Have fun, kill often!
