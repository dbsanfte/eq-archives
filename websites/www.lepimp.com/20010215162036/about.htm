About the Reach of Oboo

The Reach of Oboo was founded as a sister guild to the Bears of Oboo. An extension of their community outreach program, the Reach branched into its own living, breathing guildship to offer a home for the dark races of Norrath and to extend the geographic reach of the Oboo cause to include the Eastern half of Antonica.

Our goals are nearly identical to those of our Bear cousins -- to stand in unity against the Human and Surface Elven oppressors. Our efforts will most obviously focus on the greater Metropolitan Freeport area, and Neriak has been designated as our quarters of home.

Xixx, Nanami, and Cheyinde lead the Reach in title, but our hierarchy shall remain flat. Too long have we suffered under the heel of oppression; it would be hypocritical to institute a methodology that empowered one of our own above his or her comrades. We are superior to the rabbit breeding Humans and pale Elves, and as such let each Oboo be empowered!

Let each of us dance until we all feel better!
