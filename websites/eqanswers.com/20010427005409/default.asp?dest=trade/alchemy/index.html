Mastering Alchemy

Collecting special herbs selected from the finest regions can be found on different vendors located through Norrath. Then combining them inside a Medicine bag to create helpful potions to always carry inside one’s inventory. Alchemy is a skill only received by shamans of the 25th circle, and can be a very expensive and hard trade skill to advance in. At first glance there is not much money to be made, its more likely you’ll be putting a lot more into the initial stages of Alchemy then you are getting out, and is a way more expensive trade skill to learn then say tailoring.

The potions that are created using the Alchemy skill act as spells, they contain one charge of a certain spell that is casted on the user who carries the potion. Currently, potion spell effects cannot stack with other potions, but have no problem with stacking with other user casted spells.

Having the ability to use a spell without getting interrupted, or if you are out of mana can be a great addition to your arsenal. Especially since some of the potions a master alchemist can create, include faster movement effects and even the ability to teleport the user to different locations. Not only would that be useful among itself, but it can also get you out of some sticky situations.

Making the items

It is first recommended that you place full 20 skill points into Alchemy before you start out. This will save you a lot of money and time then gaining skill points the traditional way. It is also a worthy price to pay because in the long run Alchemy will create good profit and also be extremely useful to you and other party members.

The second thing you need to do is buy a medicine bag, which can be found on vendors, located near the shaman’s guild. After that you will need to find the herbs to combine them inside the bag to create a potion. Alchemy does not require any other materials other then the herbs and Medicine bag.

Lesser Power – 20-22 – Novice

Equipment needed
Medicine bag
Lucern
Fenugreek

This potion will increase both strength and dexterity by 5 and decrease your stamina also by 5. This is a useful potion for lower levels but not many people will buy it from you, at least it may be some use to you later on, as the stamina lost doesn’t really make a big difference compared to the Dexterity and strength increases. The two herbs Lucern and Fenugreek can be found either in Halas or Grobb, for about 4 platinum per herb.

Minor Aura of Cold – 22-27 - Junior

Equipment needed
Medicine bag
Birthwart
Allspice

This potion will increase your agility and resistance against fire by 5, in turn will decrease your stamina also by 5. Not a very worth while potion to try and sell to other players, its best to sell directly back to the merchant and try to cut your losses with it. Birthwart and Allspice can be found in either Halas or Grobb, for around 4 platinum per herb.

Minor Purity – 27-32 - Intermediate

Equipment needed
Medicine bag
Lucern
Night shade

This potion will increase your strength and poison resistance by 5, and decrease your charisma by 3. Again, not a bad potion for the lower levels, especially when fighting things that cast poison as this can be stack with other poison counteract spells. Lucern and Night shade can be found at the Grobb/Halas herb vendors for around 4 platinum per herb.

Etheria’s Antidote – 32-57 - Senior

Equipment needed
Medicine bag
Lady’s mantle
Hyssop

This potion will counteract poison, which will purge all current poison spells that are currently on the potion user. At times this can be a useful potion under the correct circumstances. I am sure there will be many times when you are poisoned, especially for another class that cannot cure themselves like a pure caster or melee types. You can sell this potion at a fair price, but it may take awhile to find the correct buyer. These herbs can be found in either Ogglok or Paineel combined make a total around 10pp.

Power – 57-72 – Advance Senior

Equipment needed
Medicine bag
Lucern
Fenugreek
Blue vervain bulb

This potion is an upgrade to the minor power, and will increase your strength and dexterity by 10, with decreasing your stamina by 10 also. Finally a potion that can be sold fairly well along the lower to medium levels which would like that extra strength boost. The side effect of the stamina doesn’t make a lot of difference in that short amount of time, and you should try and sell these to lower level fighter types. All these herbs can be located within Grobb or Halas.

Greater Power – 72-92 - Master

Equipment needed
Medicine bag
Lucern
Fenugreek
Valerian root

This potion is the final upgrade to the power line of potions, and like before will increase your strength and dexterity by 20, with decreasing your stamina by 20. The large stamina hit can effect lower level players some what, but higher levels don’t seem to mind it as the little amount of hit points they loose won’t make a difference compared to the extra damage they will dish out due to the strength. The dexterity boost is also nice for those high-level damage types who are carrying around swords that do high proc damage. This potion should most likely be easy to sell to higher level players willing to pay the cost for it. All the required herbs can be located within Grobb or Halas.

Potion of Frost – 92-130 – Advance Master

Equipment needed
Medicine bag
Heliotrope
Oakmoss

Congratulations on getting this far! You are now finally a master in the skill of Alchemy, and you can now see how hard and expensive skill it was to master. We aren’t done yet, and most of the good spells are still left to come. One of these is the potion of Frost, which will teleport the user to Everfrost, a very useful spell indeed. Not only does it save them from traveling across zones like West karana to get to Everfrost since there is no current wizard or druid portal point; but it can also act as a lifesaver. Since you have the ability to use the potion while running or in combat, this can be one of the most useful escape routes you can have in your inventory, and any melee fighter will love it. No more getting left behind to die when going on that plane of fear run, of course, now you have the problem of walking back. I’d rather spend the time walking back then trying to get the experience back. This potion should be extremely easy to sell to many high levels willing to pay the price for a free life chance. The herbs for this potion are located within Oggok or Paineel for a costly price of near 150 platinum per herb.
