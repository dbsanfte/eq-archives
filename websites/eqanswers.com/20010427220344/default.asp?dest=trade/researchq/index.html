Research Skill Quests

One of the most difficult skills to raise in EverQuest is the Research Skill. It is one of the most valuable skills in the game, as it allows you to create the spells that you could normally not buy from a vendor. However, as valuable as it may be it is still a very difficult skill to master. I'm sure we have all spent long hours gathering up our Research components just to fail. However, there is a solution - The Research Quests. By combining various items into a special research book, you can create Runes of Practice. After creating both an Azia and Beza practice rune, you can combine then and raise your research much faster and easier than you could simply creating spells. There are currently two quests available to do this. They are both based on faction of your character, good or bad. If you are having difficulty using one try the other. Often an illusion spell will help offset the problem with faction.

Good Faction: Travel to North Karana and seek out the gypsy Mrysilia--roughly located at (-650, -150) she will further help you with your training.
Evil Faction: The deep sands of Southern Ro is where you can find Smith Ty'ysa. She is roughly located at (100,500) and will further help you with your training.

Starting the Quests

The most important thing to remember when doing these quests is that it is all in the name of Research Skill. Little more will come of this, however, you will notice a much faster increase in research than if you were attempting to raise it through means of spells.

To begin, you will need to go kill wisps or buy a Greater Lighstone off a player. These go for roughly 7-10pp off players. You will probably want to go ahead and kill wisps, so that you can save the normal Lightstones for purposes explained later (although completely optional). After you have obtained the Greater Lightstone, give it Mrysila or Smith Ty'ysa for your reward, the Concordance of Research. This is required for making the practice runes in which you will use to raise your Research skill. Now, if you decided to kill those Wisps and save some Lightstones, you can hand them over to her one at a time to receive one of the two Component Guides. These will help you in game to save you the trouble of writing all the component listings down or camping out to get the next recipe. Now that you have the Concordance of Research, it's time to get to work! To create Practice Runes, you must gather various components from various places. At first it can be as easy as bone chips and bat wings. Once you gather the required components for an Azia rune, you simply put them into your Concordance of Research along with a quill and parchment and hit combine. Next, you gather the Beza rune components and add in your quill and parchment and combine this as well. Be sure that you have created the same type of Runes (Practice Azia and Practice Beza, Apprentice Azia and Apprentice Beza, and so on). The needed components are listed with the quest. After you have created the pair of runes, simply put them in your normal research book (Lexicon, Tomb of Endless Enchantments, Book of Dark Bindings, or your Elemental Grimoire) when combining the Runes. If you are successful at combining the runes, you will create one new rune which you can sell to a vendor to gain some of that money you used to create it in the first place. Remember to check below for the pair to make at the appropriate level. Attempting something too expensive and difficult to get or something already trivial will gain little to no results.

Be sure that you combine them in the Concordance of Research and ALWAYS include a Pen and Parchment with each set of components.

For detailed information on where to get each component, check the chart below. I have simply included easy to note and find listings for players that know much about the game. If you need help finding one of the components the chart below will be of use.
