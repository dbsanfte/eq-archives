Magician Research

Research allows you to create spells that cannot be bought, are too expensive to buy, or are rare and hard to find. To use research, you simply need to combine a few items in your personal research book, and if successful you will be wielding a new and powerful spell! It is suggested that you place at least one skill point into research skill and continue to gain skill with the practice rune quest instead of using expensive and hard to find real components.

The Magician uses a combination of words, items and other spells to create newer more powerful ones. They compile the various components inside a book of Elemental Grimoire, which can be found at any Magician guild. When researching you new elemental spells, you need to buy a version of a lesser level elemental from a merchant and place it inside the book along with other components.

To create the spell you must go find the required research components for that spell. The half pages are found on various monsters that can be found almost everywhere in the game. The monsters will always be around the level of the spell that its component may make, and a monster under level 16 will never drop a research component. If you would prefer, you can always auction off saying that you are looking to buy pages but be sure you know which pages you need before doing this. After gathering the correct pages you put the left and right side into your Tome of Endless Enchantments and hit combine. If your skill is right and you're lucky, you will create yourself a new spell. Otherwise, the components will be lost without a new spell thus failing. If you are failing more than you are succeeding, then you probably need to do the Research Quest to raise your skill before wasting more valuable components.

Some words have the same name, but there are different versions of them. You can tell this by the name inside the parenthesis. An example would be Words of Collection (beza). Please make sure you are using the correct version of the word else you will fail.
