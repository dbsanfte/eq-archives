Class Research

Research is the ability to create spells from simple components once thought of as useless.  By researching spells, you can both save money from buying them from vendors and create spells that you cannot buy from vendors.  Research is one of the most important caster skills in the game.  Without it, we would be missing many important spells that are necessary for many battles.  For example, many of the Magician pets cannot be bought from vendors.  Thus, we have included a detailed recipe book for each of the 4 caster types.

Wizard – By combining two magical runes, wizards can create spells of devastating power!  These runes are found on the creatures roaming Norrath. By combining two runes into a Lexicon, wizards are able to call forth spells of unspeakable power!

Magician – Magicians use powerful words along with natural items to create their spells.  From personal experience, this seems to be by far the most difficult class to use research with, as even maxing out your research for your current level yields difficult attempts at researching a spell.  However, the arcane power you summon up with these spells is greatly worth it.  Magicians combine a word and two other natural components into their Elemental Grimoire to create their powerful spells.

Enchanter – By finding the left and right side of the ancient pages torn and lost from Tasarin's Grimoire, Velishoul's Tome, Sali's Writ, and Nilitim's Grimoire, enchanters are able to reactivate their lost magic.  Enchanters long for finding the left and right side of a matching page from one of these lost and torn books for when they do, they can combine it within their Tomb of Endless Enchantments and yield its lost magic.

Necromancer – Necromancers, like magicians, focus on ancient words to create their dark magic.  However, they simply find 2 words to create the appropriate spell.  When they combine two evil words in their Book of Dark Bindings, they scribe both a sinister and deadly new spell.
