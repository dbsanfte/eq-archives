The Bonus points are extra points that you can use to add to your starting stats,
The primary stats represented by the star are what your class uses. All others apply but
not as much as the primary ones. So you get an extra amount of points to add to your
stats and stats are permenent they can be increased by magic items as in Armour or
weapons that give you a Stat bonus
