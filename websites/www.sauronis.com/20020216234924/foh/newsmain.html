December 18, 2001

A new era for FoH. There are many changes in progress for our guild and will be more in the future. I've always said this guild is capable of becoming more and going further. Kutter is taking the charge of leading FoH through this next stage. He's determined to take the guild to a whole new level of play. If you're a member of FoH, your participation is key. If your a guest to our website and looking for a new guild thats on the rise, FoH is on the move and looking for good, mature players.

So what's next? Watch the forums, get active in the guild. How? Take 10 minutes before you log on to hit our forums and discuss the vital guild issues and events. Ingame, get with a guildie. Always hook up with guildies. Find a way to get to them even if it costs you some pp for a port. Go to the events, join the pickup raids, even if your the 7th group member. FoH has a certain bond. And this bond has kept it alive for over a year now as other guilds have folded. That says alot. Are you part of that bond?
