Dwarves

The Dwarves come from Kaladim, an underground city, well planned, constructed and organised. The Dwarves are an orderly, structured people.

 

Dwarves were created by Brell Serilis. Dwarves are famed for their drinking ability and it is common to find their warriors 'stocking up' before charging into battle. They are strong and hardy, considered to be one of the wisest races on Norrath.

 

One of the qualities Dwarves are known to lack is charm, they sometimes seem grumpy and hasty; Dwarves have a certain attitude having no time for frivolous, careless people. They like to get things done and do them properly, for this reason Dwarven engineers and smiths are highly sought after.  

 

Kaladim is rumoured to be one of the richest cities of Norrath, trade is central to Dwarven society. The Dwarves export gems and brews far from their homeland, commonly to the Human city of Freeport, from the docks in Butcher Block Mountains. A ship from the docks also sails to Firiona Vie; the Dwarves have always been keen to explore, more in recent times since the discovery of their brethren on Velious.
