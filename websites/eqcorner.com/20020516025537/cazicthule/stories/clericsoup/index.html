Dwarven Cleric Soup
by Wads (Sung to the tune of “L.A. via Omaha”)


Well it was Friday night, I was playing EQ
When I decided that I would do a /who
And see if there was anyone I knew on who could help

There were 98 people playing at 10 o’clock
It’s always kinda’ crowded in Butcherblock
So I pounded on another hapless goblin whelp

I finished the whelp and headed for town
When it comes to Kaladim, I know my way around
So I thought I’d head on in to the “Staff and Spear”

I passed the guards as they were standing there
I jumped over a corpse from the Scouts of Tunare
And instead went to Tumpy’s to have myself a beer

I had one beer, then a couple more
Stumbled around and fell on the floor
Then decided to go and buy myself a shield

I got the shield and bought a fine steel mace
I’d like to try it out on Dvinn’s face
Then thought, “Too bad I don’t have double wield.”

So I headed back to Butcherblock
Kept to the right and ended up at the dock
Then cursed myself and headed back the other way

I passed guard houses and humble abodes
Took me a left at the “Crossroads”
And was finally on the path to “Greater Fay’”

It was cold and dark and really gloomy
Did I just pass an ogre chic named Zarchoomie?
She was big and mean and started to give chase

Well I stopped and hit my “A” key to fight
But somehow it just didn’t look right
I was targeted on myself, and hitting myself in the face

I targeted Zarchoomie and I was in trouble
With her last hit I was down to one bubble
And I decided to make a hasty retreat

I got to the double houses in BB
And when I got there I was happy to see
“Dimond” patrolling the area for Clan Axepeak

She killed Zarchoomie like she wasn’t even there
She’s a sweet looking dwarf but you better beware
Cause “Dimond” packs a lotta punch in her strong arms

I healed myself a little and sat down
Being out of mana and far from town
I decided to meditate inside a house-safe from harm

When I was at full snuff I went outside
Dusted myself off and swallowed my pride
And thanked Dimond for saving my skin

She said, “Np” and went away to
Help a newbie who had lost his way
And said, “Farewell, my Dwarven kin.”

I was back on track and ready to zone
When came a ring from my other line’s phone
So I set myself up as AFK

I answered the phone just to hear
My wife screaming at me, hurting my ear
“You better not me playing that game-it’s garbage day.”

So, of course, I did what any husband would
I begged on the phone, “Honey… could
I just spend another hour on the game?”

She agreed and I returned to playing
Hoping that I wouldn’t be found laying
On the side of the road almost dead and lame.

I was OK, but the sky was now dark
I started to zone into Greater Faydark
And waited for a minute or two to for the zone to load

I went to Kelethin and saw the tree city
Saw a couple of wood elves that looked real pretty
Stocked up on bandages and started to head north

I passed the orc camps and a little orc pawn
Fighting them always makes me yarn
So I ignored them and kept on venturing forth

I saw Crushbone’s entrance and a few battles
Kept walking by, heard some death rattles
And stopped a couple times to heal a newbie or two

I summoned some food and a little drink
Then stopped to take a moment to think
And decided to cast Center and Holy Armor for a buff

I passed more corpses laying on the ground
Saw a naked enchanter running around
Saying, “Watch out for D’vinn! He’s out and he is tough!”

I zoned and when I gained my sight
I saw right in front of me… in all their might
What looked like a meeting of Crushbone’s citizens.

There were cents and pawns… more than a few
A trainer, slaver, legionnaire or two
D’vinn, the Emporer, and the oracle thrown in.

They were thumping on a rogue from level 23
When he saw me he shouted, “PLEASE HEAL ME”
But I didn’t have time to target him at all.

I got jumped by trainers, maybe three
An oracle’s spell made me a little drowsy
So I decided to try and run on down the hall.

Well they gave chase and I saw the rogue die
D’vinn and the Emporer gave me the eye
And pretty soon I was leading the train of death.

I turned left and jumped and ran
Looking for the “safe spot” over yonder, and
Ran a bit-all the while holding my breath!

I almost made it to the safe spot
But when I got stunned and I knew I was caught
Getting beat up by a dozen creatures or so

My health bar was down to one thin line
I knew it was just a matter of time
The next hit would cause my life to go

D’vinn stabbed me for a quick 40 damage
But before I died I barely managed
To shout out, “Next time I do CB, it’ll be in a group”

Well I was naked, hungry, and back home
But the orcs are still there… waiting at Crushbone
Enjoying their bowls of dwarven cleric soup!
