Stats:
What does STR do?

STR makes you hit for max damage more often. Also for every STR point you can carry 1Lb of weight. So if you want to carry full Bronze then you may want to consider how much STR you start with.

What does STA do?

The higher the STA the more HPs you get per level. It also helps when duel wielding and swinging the heavier weapons due to the stamina drain these take

What does AGI do?

AGI does NOT makes you dodge and parry more often. All it really does is make you take less damage by raising your AC. It is still pretty important for races with lower HP so that you can take less damage. I personally think this is one of the more important stats after STR.

What does DEX do?

DEX helps you get more critical hits and also makes your weapon effects go off more often. It also helps with ranged weapons. This skill is important at higher levels when you start getting weapons that proc.

What does INT do?

The only thing INT does for warriors is helps them learn skills faster. Not too important unless you want to master all weapon skills early in the game.

What does CHA do?

This is only good for getting better prices at merchants. A pretty useless stat.
