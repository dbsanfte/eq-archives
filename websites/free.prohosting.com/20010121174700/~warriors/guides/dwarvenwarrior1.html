Dwarven Warrior
By Brylden

For starters let me tell you how I distributed the bonus points. I put 20 in AGI and 5 into STR. So this brought my stats to:

STR 105
STA 100
AGI 95
DEX 90
WIS 83
INT 60
CHA 45

Levels 1 - 4
For level 1 I killed decaying skeletons, bats, snakes and goblin welps. For level 2 I also started killing skunks and scarabs. That is about all I did until level 3 where I went to the crossroads and started killing more skunks and white large snakes and large spiders. During this time you should try to get patchwork armor and a rusty 1h slash and if you are lucky a rusty 2h sword. You can also turn in 2 scarab carapices for the scarab helm. At level 4 you should be able to kill more large snakes and large spiders. By the way, you should keep all spider silks because since the new tailoring patch people buy the silks for 3-4gp each.

Levels 5 - 8
At level 5 I grouped with a like level cleric and killed the skeletons at the druid stones. I pretty much did this until I made level 9. You should keep all bone fragments that you get for a quest that you can do at the Paladin guild. At this time you can do some quests. There is one real good one that I did quite a few times. It is the Bull Smasher quest in Ak'Anon. I will let you discover the specifics for the quest yourselves.

Level 9 - 16
At level 9 I got on the boat and went to Freeport. The first thing I did of course is get bound at the East Gate. Then I went to Oasis with my Cleric friend and killed caimans. You can stay here till about level 22 but I got bored and left at level 17. I have almost full bronze, a Crafted Gauntlet, 2 Langseax, and a Brutechopper.

Level 17- 21 (Present)
This is when you start being able to do many things. At 17 I went to High Hold and killed orcs and gnolls. I got a Polished Granite Tomahawk and sold it for 250pp well worth waiting for the spawn. I stayed here for a while but decided to go explore the world. The next place I went is Rathe Lake and killed the Aviak Guards outside the Arena. When I hit level 18 we went to South Karana and killed roaming aviaks until 19. At 19 we went the the Beholder Maze and killed Gorge Minos and Evil Eyes. (just on a side note you are going to have to find a decent sized group to start doing anything worth while.) At level 20 I found myself at Mistmoore and this is where I am going to stay for a couple more levels. I am currently level 21 and have Crafted Boots, Greaves, Gauntlets, and Bracer. The rest is Bronze.
