Knights of Shining Light
I would like to announce that the guild, Knights of Shining Light, are again recruiting, all levels, all races, all classes. We are on the Zeb server. We are a family oriented guild, that has much fun, grouping, and exploring Everquest. Thank you Texxan Bravesoul Leader KoSL.

HANDS OF HAVOC NOW RECRUITING
Located on the Tholuxe Paells server, Hands of Havoc is a great guild geared for all levels of play, we have members from 6-60, so please, send a tell to any of our officers, or myself, Rahlan.

Guild Recruiting
Phalanx of the Phoenix on the Xev server is now actively recruiting players level 46+. We are a "weekend" raiding guild with impromptu raids when enough people are online and a mob is up. To find out more please visit www.phop.guildportal.com. Thank you.

Remnants of Gondwana Recruiting on Stromm
Remnants of Gondwana are an Australasian prime time guild on Stromm. Currently in Elementals, we are looking to expand our player base before the final push on Time and beyond. If interested, please visit www.rog-stromm.com.

Guild Recruiting on Morden Rasp
Vanguard of the Valiant is recruiting members levels 20+. We are a family oriented guild although raiding will be a part of the experience. If you are interested contact Radagast or Sanni in game on the Morden Rasp server.

Imperial Destiny Now Recruiting
Imperial Destiny is looking for new members level 50 and up for a raiding guild on Saryrn server. If you like to have fun and play in a relaxed family environment then check us out at www.imperialdestiny.org or send Dembian a tell in game.
