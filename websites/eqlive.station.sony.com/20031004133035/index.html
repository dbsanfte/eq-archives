Using the Same Augment Twice
You cannot seal two of the same augmentation onto the same item. The duplicate augmentation will not bond. We just wanted everyone to be clear on this point when making plans to spend their adventure points.

Augmentation Restrictions - Worn Locations
It is important to remember that the restrictions on an augmentation will be applied to the item that it is bonded with. This includes the worn location. Please click through for details and a review of the augmentation restrictions.

Augmenting Deity Restricted Items
Deity restricted items do not accept augmentation items with no deity restrictions. This is a bug, and we should have it fixed when we next patch.
