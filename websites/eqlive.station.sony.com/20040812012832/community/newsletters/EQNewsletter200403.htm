EverQuest® turns 5

Five years. Seven expansions. Millions of players. And a journey that’s lasted the test of time, truly living up to the name EverQuest.

Now it’s time to celebrate!
The Las Vegas 5th Anniversary Fan Faire is coming April 1-4, 2004, and it’s shaping up to be, by far, the most spectacular one ever. More details on that below. If you’re a regular, you can’t miss it. If you’ve never been, there’s no better time to attend one- but act fast before the reservations are gone!

Speaking of all things EverQuest, Lost Dungeons of Norrath™ has been nominated for Best Multiplayer Game of 2003 by the Academy of Interactive Arts and Sciences. And our newest expansion, Gates of Discord, has launched to massive success. EverQuest is better than ever.

Champions of Norrath™, the action-adventure epic for the PlayStation®2 computer entertainment system, has received fantastic reviews and even better sales. Players everywhere are taking their Champions to victory and discovering many of the thousands of items.

-The EverQuest Newsletter Team

EQ Fan Faire Goes to Vegas!

Be a part of the greatest EverQuest fan event ever. On April 1-4, 2004, the Las Vegas Hilton will be the setting for the 5th Anniversary Fan Faire. Not only will you be partaking in the Vegas excitement, but there’ll be tons of 5th Anniversary-themed events, parties, and merchandise to browse and discover.

There are 3,000 spots available, making it potentially the largest Fan Faire ever. Reserve today, and make sure you’re there with us!

Spotlight on Guilds!
This month we take notice of Healers United on Quellious. Over 200 members strong, these healers adhere to a strict set of guidelines, pledging to help those in need. The guild’s members take time out of their busy schedules to set up buffing stations in Greater Faydark on Friday nights. They do so without asking for any compensation, all to help those who are buff challenged.
We salute you, Super Buff Makers!
www.healersunited.com
