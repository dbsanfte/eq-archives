EverQuest: Lost Dungeons of Norrath to Launch!

When Lost Dungeons of Norrath is launched on September 9th, players will be introduced to several exciting new concepts in adventuring through the world of EverQuest, including the introduction of personalized dungeons for groups up to 36 people. With 48 distinct zones, equipment augmentations and interactive objects, Lost Dungeons of Norrath is our biggest expansion to date! You can still pre-order from the Station.com Store until September 8th and save money!


Champions of Norrath™

An action game like no other. Champions of Norrath will let you experience unlimited battles, deadly missions and unparalleled character customization. Five Champions to Master - Select from five races and five classes (Female and Male versions). Huge Combat World - 45 Levels to master with more than 50 areas to explore and conquer. And so much more!

Announcing EverQuest® Online Adventures: Frontiers™

This second edition of EQOA, the first-ever MMORPG for the Playstation®2 computer entertainment system, will be released in the fourth quarter of 2003. Frontiers will introduce a new playable race, the Ogres, along with newly discovered zones.

 


Sign Up For the Lords of EverQuest™ Beta Test

Apply now for the beta testing of the Lords of EverQuest, the upcoming Real- Time Strategy game inspired by the popular EverQuest online game. Although it's designed primarily as a single-player game, up to 12 people can play via LAN or online.
