The future of EverQuest®

The 5th Anniversary Fan Faire finished with great success in Las Vegas. If you weren't able to make it, we have the whole event covered for you to check out!

Photo galleries of the people and events throughout Fan Faire are up for viewing, as well as the recaps and hot news from the many media sources that showed up and were blown away by the exclusive hands-on preview of EverQuest® II. The announcement of full voice-over in the game was only one of several exciting developments. Read more in the Fan Faire Page.

EverQuest® is undergoing a graphic upgrade that's equally exciting. Get ready to experience areas of Norrath in epic detail like you've never seen before.

We even have some news for you EverQuest® Online Adventures™ users: player-vs-player dueling has been added, as well as a brand-new set of characters and a special quest!

Get ready: the future is going to bring even more excitement than the first five.

-The EverQuest Newsletter Team

Vegas Fan Faire: record attendance

The greatest number of attendees ever came to the Las Vegas Hilton on April 1-4, 2004 to exchange tales of adventure, take part in live quests, speak to the faces behind the game, and take peeks at the future of EQ. New graphic improvements to EverQuest and a hands-on preview of EverQuest II were just two of the weekend's greatest moments. Read more about it and check out the extensive photo gallery on our Fan Faire page.

Player vs Player Dueling in EQOA!

A long-standing request of many players, player-versus-player gameplay has finally come to EverQuest Online Adventures with Dueling! Challenge a friend or foe and ante up money or a prized possession. The best fighter wins! Read all about it, and check out the dueling movie that shows this cool feature in action.

Zboard™ EverQuest Keyset

An EQ keyboard: Ideazon, designer and developer of next-generation gaming keyboards, has launched a new Zboard™ Keyset designed exclusively for EverQuest. The EverQuest® Keyset for Zboard™ will adorn your desktop with the beauty of Norrath and show everyone how much you love EverQuest. Not only that, but the specially labeled hot-keys will help veterans or newbies learn game commands previously unknown to them. It's the best new peripheral for the dedicated EQ gamer!

Station Store: 5th Anniversary Mousepad

If you missed the Fan Faire and all the stuff the Station Store had there, make up for it by ordering some EQ gear online! The EverQuest watch, EQ Jigsaw puzzle and the 5th Anniversary Mousepad are just some of the dozens of great gifts for sale.
