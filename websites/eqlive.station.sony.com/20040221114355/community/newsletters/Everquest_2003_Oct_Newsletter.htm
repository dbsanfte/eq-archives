EverQuest®: Lost Dungeons of Norrath™ launches with flying colors
Lost Dungeons of Norrath launched on September 9th, unleashing new personalized dungeons for single groups of 4-6 players of level 20 and up and 48 new distinct zones, along with raid content for high level characters. Our biggest expansion ever, it offers hours of new adventures that will take you through the holiday season. The launch has been a rousing success. Of course, what would you expect? If you're not playing LDoN, you're not experiencing the ultimate in EverQuest.

EverQuest® Online Adventures™ Auction System Now Live
Starting right now, you can buy, sell and bid on wares and treasure across the whole of Tunaria with EQOA's new Auction System feature, which allows you to bid on and purchase in-game items using Tunars, the in-game currency. To participate, you can either go find a friendly merchant; or you can select "auction" from the "community" area of the main menu when you're traveling. This amazing new feature adds a whole new dimension to EQOA.
