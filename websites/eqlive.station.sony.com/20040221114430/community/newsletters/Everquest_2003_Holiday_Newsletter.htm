Expanding the World of EverQuest®
This holiday season, Sony Online Entertainment is taking the EverQuest universe and pushing it to all new realms.

EverQuest® Online Adventures: Frontiers™ for the PlayStation®2 computer entertainment system is here! Whether you’re a new player or a returning one, this second edition of the first massively multiplayer online console role-playing game has dozens of new features to explore. If you haven’t played EQ on a console yet, it’s a great time to start.

Lords of EverQuest™ has gone gold and will be available this December for PC players everywhere. A revolutionary Real-Time Strategy (RTS) game based in the world of EverQuest, Lords offers an enthralling single-player experience through 75+ hours of gameplay and storyline with the fury of online warfare through multiplayer action on SOEgames.net. Using forces and strategies as unique as their diverse races, the mighty Lords of the Elddar Alliance, Dawn Brotherhood and Shadowrealm launched an epic war that would live forever in the legends and lore of EverQuest. Don’t forget to take advantage of a special purchase incentive – read more below (hint, it involves EQ II beta).

Next, get ready for Champions of Norrath™: Realms of EverQuest™. This incredible hack ‘n’ slash RPG for the PlayStation®2 computer entertainment system takes EverQuest into a world of cinematic adventure unparalleled in console entertainment. New Frontiers for EverQuest Online Adventures

A new level of MMORPGs has arrived: The celebrated EverQuest Online Adventures now has its second edition with the upcoming EQOA: Frontiers. A lot's changed in the massive world of Tunaria. There's a new playable race: the Ogre. A new class: the Alchemist. A new continent. Three new cities. A level 60 cap. New quests. This exciting new chapter of the continuing online console saga has a host of new features, challenges and upgrades, but best of all, it's also made to be beginner-friendly. So if you haven’t played EverQuest on a console before, now’s your time. Order it now!
Lords of EverQuest Purchase Incentive
Check out the latest incentive to play Sony Online Entertainment’s greatest new game. For current EverQuest players, buy Lords of EverQuest™, the next generation of real time strategy games, and register on SOEGames.net with an existing EQ account before January 3, 2004 to get 30 days of gameplay tacked onto the end of your current EverQuest subscription cycle. And there’s more: you’ll gain exclusive enand an invitation to a special phase of the EverQuest®II beta when it launches in 2004 (special restrictions apply). That’s three hot games tied together in one pretty cool deal.

Take the helm of one of three factions and engage in battles that require strategy, skill and cunning. Control up to 15 Lords and colossal armies as you wage war for total domination of Antonica! Play on your own across three different single-player campaigns or online via the SOEGames.net service with up to 12 players. Either way, get ready for a whole new adventure - Lords of EverQuest scheduled to hit stores Dec. 2.

Champions of Norrath™: Realms of EverQuest™
Coming in early 2004 is Champions of Norrath: Realms of EverQuest, an action game like none other on the PlayStation®2 computer entertainment system.

Experience unlimited battles, 45 beautiful locales to conquer, and cooperative gameplay with up to 3 friends online or offline, all brought to you by the creators of Baldur’s Gate™: Dark Alliance™. The ultimate gaming experience is on its way – it’s scheduled for February.

Latest EQ Update: 3D Targeting Indicator, Changeable Fonts and more!

Speaking of the classic EverQuest, among the new features of the latest EQ update are a cool 3D targeting indicator that shows the con color, and an added ability to change the UI font in the game. Plus, you’ll now only need three people to start an adventure instead of four. Good stuff!
