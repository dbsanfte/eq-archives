Hello!

By now you know that we’ve announced a new product, EverQuest®: The Legacy of Ykehsa™. Those of you that have been taking polls about the possibility of a new race were right on top of it - Frogloks. We’ve also added a story window, a feature that we will use to make new story elements of the game available to players.

The basic version is just a quick cut-and-paste of HTML code to get you running. Webmasters will also appreciate the customizability of the advanced version.

On behalf of The EverQuest Team I want to thank you for allowing us to provide entertainment for you in this new year.

What Is The Legacy of Ykesha?

EverQuest: The Legacy of Ykesha (LoY) is a new kind of addition to the world of EverQuest.

What’s the difference between the traditional “expansion” and this “extension”? Foremost, the extension will be available for download; it's the first EQ product to be available through the Internet. After its release, a relatively small number of LoY CD-ROMs will be available at retailers.

This extension focuses heavily on the addition of new features to the game. For example, LoY adds: an advanced "Looking for Group" tool, that will allow players to map the world as they travel, tools to help you better administer your guilds, and expanded bank capacity, among others.

Of course, you’ll find adventure in the extension as well: new zones, treasures and spells will continue the familiar tradition of additions to the game.

Pre-order The Legacy of Ykesha, available directly from SOE by download and online credit card purchase, at the special price of $17.99 (plus tax, if applicable) for orders received between now and February 24, 2003 (the anticipated “live” date). On the live date and thereafter, SOE's price will increase to $21.99 (price subject to change). Additionally, shortly after the live date, SOE will make a relatively small number of The Legacy of Ykesha CD-ROMs available at retail as well.

Perhaps one of the best parts about a downloadable product is there are no shipping fees!

To find out more about EverQuest: The Legacy of Ykesha, check out the official site. We’ll be adding new information on features every Tuesday and Friday leading up to the projected release date of February 24. And visit the Station Store to find out how to pre-order.

Fan Faire Heads to Las Vegas!

EverQuest Fan Faire is bound for the glittering city of Las Vegas, Nevada, March 28-30!
