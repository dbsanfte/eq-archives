News From E3 2004: EverQuest® Announcements!

The eighth expansion for EverQuest, EverQuest: Omens of War, has been announced! And that’s not all that’s going on. EverQuest II was shown at the E3 Expo in Los Angeles to tremendous acclaim, and a sequel to the EverQuest-themed console action-adventure hit Champions: Return to Arms™ was announced.

A new EQ Tutorial has been launched, making it easier than ever for new gamers to find their way in Norrath. And new features keep being added to the game, like the new and improved graphics engine.

There’s also Fan Fiction to enjoy.

Read on!

-The EverQuest Newsletter Team

Guild Summit: The Players and the Developers Meet

60 representatives from player guilds and the game press came to Sony Online Entertainment's San Diego offices on the weekend of June 5th to discuss the game and their issues with the EQ dev team. This unprecedented summit of gamers and the game's creators was a unique opportunity for all, and the results were extremely productive. Read reports from individual guilds and the press on the Guild Summit page, and check out the gallery of pictures from the event.

EverQuest: Omens of War Coming Soon!

Experience EverQuest: Omens of War and enter the twisted lands of a realm where war is persistent and death is constant. Face the fear of the Battlegrounds of Discord. Explore the shattered landscapes of the Discordant Plateau. Then take your fury to a landscape that teems with Muramites preparing to invade not just Norrath, but countless innocent worlds. Adventure solo, or in groups and raids to complete "epic quests" and earn prestigious weapons. New graphics, a level cap beyond 65, new hand-tailored Epic Weapons, Custom PC Titles and much more await you in the newest expansion.

New Graphics Engine

You may have been noticing your EverQuest experience getting prettier than ever before. That's the new graphics engine, filling new and old parts of Norrath with brand-new graphic effects and character models. Plus, there's a brand-new new graphics engine zone to explore. Ah, but how do you find this zone, you ask? Talk to an NPC in Nedaria's Landing...
