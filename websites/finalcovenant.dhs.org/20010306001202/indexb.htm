The Final Covenant bids thee welcome!

We are a good aligned role playing guild that is found within the realms of Norrath, located on the Innoruuk Server. Within these few meager pages will be found some basic information about who we are and our memberships colourful and varied histories. Please, take a moment to view them. We beg thy indulgence and please excuse the mess as we continue to grow and add more each day.




January 5th, 2001--Plane of Hate
The Final Covenant again has proven their worth. Assisted by friends from other families, such as Ashen Vendetta, The Pantheon, Justifiers, Patronas Veritas, Daughters of Innoruuk, Lost Souls of Glaznor, we moved into the Plane of Hate. Running to camp proved to be a thing of luck this night. Several were not able to make it past a little rat skulking in a corner on the wall. After a wait, and the ressurections completed, we began to let our might be felt.

This was an absolute first for many, having never before been to this place of hatred. Cragie and Nadilya learned the names of many of the denizens, learning how to be primary and secondary assists. Lifted and Ishogio, learning how to pull within the place, led by Balandrus.

Soon, we encountered the Maestro. Only ONE fatality during that battle, happening within the first 5 seconds. Krattack called out words the Maestro took great exception to and was dead before his body hit the ground.

All in all it was a very successful raid. Many people walked away with something this night as a momento
