July 16, 2001 Plane of Fear

What a night. It started out well.. buffed, ready to go. Staggered our entrance into the Plane. We ran to the west wall to prepare to fight. The break in was looking good. One scareling in the camp. Suddenly the words you hve been disconnected Flashed across my screen.. No slow link death, but an abrupt dump from the plane of fear. Fourty plus people experienced this phenomenon. Logging back in as quickly as possible, they found they could not cast spells and could not swing thier weapons. They were taking damage but the results were not showing on their hit point bars.

Ninety minutes later, after petitioning and NO answer from guides of GM's online.... the Chat GM being ineffective. We prepared to go back and attempt to recover our corpses. Only to have several people in the two groups that zoned through... go link dead. The fact that our two primary tanks could NOT manage to stay within the game severely hampered our attempt at Corpse Recovery.

Enter Eclipsing Sojourn. Thanks to their patience, we were able to regroup, and group by group make it to the west wall camp and our corpses. Grabbing weapons first.. we managed to one by one loot our corpses and break the plane.

We had an interesting time as we continued to hunt the plane of Fear. With only one person truly familiar enough with the zone to pull, the task fell to...... Zoal. Yes.... a wizard pulling in fear. He did die a few times, but no more than any other puller without the ability (or even with ) the ability to Feign Death. We tried Fear kiting. That was very successful with minimal damage to any of the tanks. Aggro after the plane had been broken was reduced to a minimum and the creatures did not last long enough to make it very far.

Thanks go to our enchanters for doing an incredible job with the crowd control.
