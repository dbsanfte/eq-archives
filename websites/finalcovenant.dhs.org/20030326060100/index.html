Plane of Hate

Kelorak`Dar

May 24th, 2002


For those of you that attended the Plane of Hate raid last night, you will know what a blast it was. Jorele... will not be losing any experience at the next Guild Meeting. He managed to get the entire first floor pulled in under two hours. Including Maestro. There was one pull I thought would undo the raid, but we perservered. (I did not realize there were that many revenants in Hate at any given time.) It was a very successful raid. A bard, Shamans and Rangers all died .. At one point all three were dead at the same time. Thus, ensuring great success.

Pathing on the second floor is very poor. After poor pathing and warping mobs caused people to be summoned and the fact that we had a very long ToV raid planned for the following morning, we decided it was time to camp out. Roaar came in to find we'd all had to leave without him. A follower of Innoruuk and a disciple decided to lend a hand in our exit. Arduatha, Korrann, Masquerade and Jorele, went back to hate to retrieve the Iksar Warrior... (who was found to be dancing.. yes.. dancing, with his tail in the Maestro's throne room. ) Jorele went to him, handed him a cloudy potion and they ran back to the port in. They immediately vacated Hate.

The goodness gets even better. As we were departing from Plane of Hate, the call went out that Kelorak`Dar was swimming in Cobalt Scar. Heading over we confronted her.

Three other spells dropped. Two Gift of Brilliance and Focus of Spirit. They will be held until the next individual attains appropriate level to use. A Black Marble went into the Guilds Gem Collection for the eventuality of someone obtaining a Breastplate and needing the gem.
