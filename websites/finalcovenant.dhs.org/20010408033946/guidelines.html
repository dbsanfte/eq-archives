Guidelines For The Final Covenant


1.Always be aware that you are carrying our banner. As such, conduct yourself in such a way as to bring pride to The Final Covenant. Your behavior reflects back upon the rest of the guild.
2.Airing of grievances with another guild member in a public forum will not be tolerated.
3.Respect and courtesy towards each other will be enforced. That means, arguments and disagreements must be held in tells or private modes of communication. This applies in our interactions both in guild and with non-guild individuals. If you can not resolve the disagreement, call in an officer to assist in the mediation. No shouts or ooc arguments across a zone will be tolerated.
4.Inflammatory posts towards another member of the Final Covenant upon The Final Covenant's Message board or any other message board will not be tolerated.
5.Refrain from public bashing of Verant/EQ. There are other venues that are appropriate for these matters.
6.Officers within the Final Covenant have a greater responsibility to uphold the guidelines that will be set in place.
7.We are here to help and assist each other. But, please, do not abuse the kindness we have towards another. If you ask for help, be sure to ask for a "need" and not a "want".
