VII. BASIC REQUIREMENTS

 

BASIC REQUIREMENTS:

  1. A. The follow basic requirements will be required by all members to acquire and maintain at all times. The basis of establishing these basic requirements is to increase our individual capabilities, and establish a higher standard of performance and readiness necessary to overcome in-game challenges.
    1. Guild resources may be used to help members acquire certain items and cover expenses, however, ultimate responsibility will be each members'.
    2. Members are responsible for replenishing expendable or lost items.
    3. Class Leaders are responsible for ensuring members within their class acquire these items and addressing matters to High Council where guild resources are needed to accomplish this.
    4. The majority of these items will be required as a basis for ending probationary period.
    5. Unless otherwise noted, the minimum level requirement to join is 55th.
  2. KEYS:
    1. Sebilis key
    2. Cobalt Scar Tooth
    3. Howling Stones key
    4. Skyshrine Key
    5. Veeshan's Peak
      1. Members are required to work towards acquiring this, not to necessarily have it, until we commence killing Trakannon in the very near future.
    6. Faction issues are not an acceptable basis for not having any of these keys. Even if one may not kill things in a particular place, you are still required to possess a key to the area.
  3. RESIST GEAR:
    1. Gold Diamond Mask
    2. Electrum Diamond Mask
    3. Two(2) Platinum or Velium Diamond Rings
    4. Two(2) Blue Diamond Earrings
    5. Shaman Greater Resist Potions (5 or 10 charges each)
      1. Greater Null (+25 vs magic)
      2. Greater Disease Resist (+25 vs disease)
      3. Greater Poison Resist (+25 vs disease)
      4. Greater Cold Resist (+25 vs cold)
      5. Greater Fire Resist (+25 vs fire)
  4. MINIMUM RESIST LEVELS:
    1. FIRE KIT: All members must acquire items that provide resists versus fire and magic such that their base, unbuffed resists are 120vsFire and 120vsMagic
    2. COLD KIT: All members must acquire items that provide resists versus cold and magic such that their base, unbuffed resists are 120vsCold and 120vsMagic
    3. POISON KIT: All members must acquire items that provide resists versus poison, disease and magic such that their base, unbuffed resists are 120vsPoison, 120vsDisease and 120vsMagic
  5. INVISIBILITY:
    1. All members must have a means to cast INVISIBILITY on themselves. Item Examples for non-casters to consider getting:
      1. Mask of Larrikan (10 charges Superior Camo)
      2. Vendor Bought Potions
      3. Circlet of Shadows
    2. All members must have a means to cast INVISIBILITY vs UNDEAD on themselves.
      Item Examples for non-casters to consider getting:
      1. Shaman made potions (10 charges)
  6. GATING:
    1. All members should have the ability to GATE out of any situation. Members who cannot normally cast any GATE-type spell should have on them at all times any two of the following items, preferrably with multiple charges and for different continents:
      1. Thurgadin Gate Potion
      2. Innothule Swamp Gate Potion
      3. Halas Gate Potion
      4. Cazic Blood Potion
      5. Field of Bone Gate Potion
    2. NOTE: Non-wizard/druid casters may wish to keep these on them as well in case they are bound in an unsafe area.
  7. NIGHT SIGHT:
    1. All members should have an alternate light source or some other means to see in dark areas. Members should also keep additional items in their bank to use in case they have to run through dark areas to recover their corpse. This generally applies to humans and erudites only, but is worth considering for other races as well in order to assist when needed.  There are too many items to list but some good examples are:
      1. Shaman made Ultravision or Infravision Potions
  8. ENDURING BREATH
    1. All members must have a means to eliminate the need to breath for themselves. Those whose spells require components may want to acquire an item as well in the event they run out of components.
      Examples:
      1. Fishbone Earring
      2. Aqualung
      3. Ancient Fishing Pole
  9. OTHER NECESSITIES:
    1. A means to accelerate with the healing of yourself and others:
      Example:
      1. Bandages
      2. Regeneration Item/Potion
    2. No less than five(5) mini-coffins, ten(10) for necromancers, for use in casting the 57th level New Corpse Summon spell.
    3. Ranged weapon with adequate ammunition
    4. Spell Components for frequently cast spells:
      Examples:
      1. Peridots
      2. Pearls
      3. fish scales
  10. CLERICS: <Galadraal>
    1. Must have the Lake Rathe portion of their epic quest completed.
    2. Will be evaluated on equipment to ensure minimum standards in wisdom and armor class.
    3. Have their minimum level requirement reduce to 49th to join.
  11. SHAMANS: <Eatter>
    1. Have no additional requirements.
  12. DRUIDS: <Lime>
    1. Must undergo a preliminary consideration period of not less than three weeks to five weeks, during which the following must be accomplished:
      1. Having grouped with the Druid Class Leader not less than five(5) times
      2. Having grouped with at least three different High Council members
      3. Having grouped with at least three(3) other IC druid primaries on no less than two occasions
    2. Must have simultaneously have the following statistic scores:
      1. Wisdom value of 190 unbuffed
      2. Armor Class value of 700 with druid buffs only
      3. No other stat value below 80, except resists
      4. Should be able to acquire any resist value equal to or greater than 65 with buffs and minimum switching of gear.
    3. Must have any two requirements to complete the Veeshan's Peak key quest
    4. Must have one portion completed for the Druid Epic Quest
    5. Must have all buyable spells up to current level
    6. Must have a Sense Heading value of 100+
    7. Must have a Tracking value of 100+
    8. Must have a Foraging value of 100+
    9. Must have a Swimming value of 175+
    10. Must be able to identify their role within each group without having being told
    11. Must be comfortable with porting people, especially prior to start of any event
    12. Must join the ICDruids e-mailing list
    13. If membership is denied at first, may not reapply until after 30 days
  13. WIZARDS: <Tulong>
    1. Have their minimum level requirement reduced to 52nd
    2. Must have all "lure" series spells up to their level
    3. Must be comfortable with porting people, especially prior to events
    4. Must have an Intelligence value of 200
    5. Must have all researchable spells, or adequate components to make such
    6. Must demonstrate an adequate familiarity with zones
  14. NECROMANCERS: <Aireth>
    1. Must demonstrate proficiency with spells under varying situations
    2. Must demonstrate ability to control pet under varying situations
    3. Must already have some of the above listed resist gear prior to joining
    4. Must have all Corpse Summon spells up to current level
    5. Must be comfortable with traveling to do CR when necessary
    6. Must have VII.B.1-4 keys items
    7. Must demonstrate some zone knowledge
  15. MAGICIANS: <Kalaan>
    1. Have their minim level requirement reduced to level 51st
    2. Must have all pet spells
    3. Must have all researchable spells, or components to make such
    4. Must demonstrate the ability to control pet under varying situations
    5. Must post a character profile at "eq.castersrealm.com" listing all normally worn equipment.
    6. Must agree to a seniority order concerning all Epic Quest matters.
    7. Must have VII.B.1-4 keys items
    8. Must have completed the Phenocryst Focus quest prior before ending probationary period
  16. ENCHANTERS: <Remelio>
    1. Have their minimum level requirement reduced to 54th
    2. Must have an Intelligence value of 190+ after personal buffs
    3. Must have a Charisma value of 190+ after personal buffs
    4. Must demonstrate proficiency with spells under varying situations
    5. Must demonstrate some zone knowledge
    6. Must agree to a seniority order for all Epic Quest matters
  17. WARRIORS: <Boffen>
    1. Must demonstrate willingness to participate in events and work well with others
    2. Must demonstrate competence and adaptability under varying situations
    3. Must not have a reputation of misconduct or participation in questionable activities
    4. Will be evaluated in quality of equipment for current level
  18. ROGUES: <Thart>
    1. Must have a MAKE and APPLY poison of 100 or greater
    2. Must have some of the resist items as listed in VII.C.1-4
    3. Must have a LOCKPICKING skill of 201 or greater
    4. Must have with them at all times a mort and pestel.
    5. Must be ready to, or already have completed, the RAGEBRINGER epic quest.
  19. RANGERS: <Ipath>
    1. Must have some of the above listed resist gear items prior to joining
    2. Will be evaluated in quality of equipment for current level
    3. Must have the Sebilis key prior to joining
    4. Must demonstrate proficiency with spells under varying situations
  20. PALADINS: <Anzeal>
    1. Must have VII.B.1-4 keys items
    2. Will be evaluated in quality of equipment for current level
    3. Must demonstrate proficiency with spells under varying situations
    4. Must show willingness to participate in events and work well with others
  21. SHADOW KNIGHTS
    1. Have no additional requirements
  22. BARDS: <Mourn>
    1. Must demonstrate proficiency with songs under varying situations
    2. Must be able to twist three(3) songs with accuracy
    3. Must be able to "crowd control" two mobs
  23. MONKS: <Rzarector>
    1. Must demonstrate excellent zone knowledge in most zones
    2. Must demonstrate some knowledge in mob pathing in a variety of zones
    3. Have their minimum level requirement increased to 56th
    4. Will be evaluated in quality of equipment with high expectations
