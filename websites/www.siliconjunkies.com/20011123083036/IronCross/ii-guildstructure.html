II. GUILD STRUCTURE

 

A. HIGH COUNCIL: 

The responsibilities of guild operations will be shared by a committee of at least three qualified guild members appointed by the founding members. This committee will act as the ruling body of the guild and is empowered to enforce, modify, and add to existing rules and requirements by process of majority vote when necessary. The appointed High Council members are as follows:

  1. Hawkwinnd
  2. Regan
  3. Soulflayer
  4. Yokatsu

B. CLASS LEADERS: 

Each class shall be represented by one guild member of the appropriate class, whose responsibilities will be to address in-class concerns to the High Council and ensure that members of their class comply with guild rules and minimum standards. The appointed Class Leaders are as follows:

  1. Cleric - Galadraal
  2. Shaman - Eatter
  3. Druid - Lime
  4. Wizard - Tulong
  5. Magician - Kalaan
  6. Necromancer - Aireth
  7. Enchanter - Remelio
  8. Warrior - Boffen
  9. Ranger - Ipath
  10. Paladin - Anzeal
  11. Shadow Knight - Desmond
  12. Rogue - Thart
  13. Bard - Mourn
  14. Monk - Rzarector

C. TREASURER: 

One or more guild members shall be appointed by the High Council to manage guild funds and supply members with necessary consumables and supplies, such as mini-coffins and certain items listed under minimum requirements. The appointed treasurer(s) are as follows:

  1. Yokatsu
  2. Eatter

D. WEBMASTER: 

One or more guild members shall be appointed by the High Council to manage all issues relevant to the Iron Cross website. The appointed webmaster(s) are as follows:

  1. Clietus
  2. Lime
  3. Ravenb
