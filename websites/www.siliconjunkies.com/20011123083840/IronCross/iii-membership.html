III. MEMBERSHIP

 

A. MINIMUM LEVEL: 

The minimum level requirement to join will be level fifty-five(55).

  1. A lower or higher level requirement may be assigned by the Class Leader if approved by the High Council.
  2. Those under consideration for membership(i.e., posted on our PUBLIC or NEW MEMBER APPROVAL web message boards) on or before March 31st shall be held to the original level fourty-five(45) requirement.

B. SIGNIFICANT OTHER CLAUSE: 

At the request of any guild member, real life only husbands, wives, live-in boyfriends/girlfriends, and parents of minors, can be exempted from any requirement and may be guild invited at any level with the approval of, and under terms set forth by, the High Council. All requests will be handled on a case by case basis. Every guild member must at all times set the proper example, regardless of age or handicap, and this should be the primary consideration before requests under this clause is requested. Those who join under this clause are not exempted from rules within this charter and are with the understanding that full membership privileges may only be granted once minimum requirements are met.

C. HOURS OF OPERATION: 

Iron Cross is most active during the late night hours of 8pm to 5am pacific time. Guild members should be willing and able to play during these times on a semi-regular basis, as most guild activities will be scheduled during this time period.

D. PROBATIONARY PERIOD: 

New invitees shall be placed on a probationary period of not less than 60 real days. During this time they will be evaluated on the following:

  1. Ability to adjust to guild rules
  2. Compliance with rules within this charter
  3. Cooperating well with others
  4. Willingness to meet and maintain minimum requirements.

Additionally, new invitees will not be eligible to receive any loot, nor request any guild event on their behalf, until such time that they pass their probationary period. Responsibility for monitoring and guiding new invitees will be the responsibility of the appropriate Class Leader.

E. CHARACTER PROFILES: 

Each member shall be required to create and maintain a character profile on "www.guildsay.com" and "eq.castersrealm.com" so that Iron Cross leadership may have a means to review and verify issues relevant to your character.

F. ALTERNATE CHARACTERS: 

Each current and new member shall be required to identify and, at their option, guild invite any alternate characters they may wish to play and use during guild functions. All guild members who play alternates are with the understanding that any alts represent the primary character, and that all activities in and out of guild events are on behalf of, reflect upon, and is for the benefit of that primary character.

  1. Only the character initially invited is considered a "primary" character. All other characters the owner of that primary plays, including those on separate accounts, is considered an alternate character.
  2. Alternates characters below the level of fifty(50) must be run "anonymous" without the guild tag showing.
  3. Guild resources may not be used on behalf of any alternate character except by direct approval from the High Council.
  4. Alternates may not compete with guild primaries in any manner during guild events, except in matters that would directly improve that alternate's primary character.
  5. All alternate characters are bound by guild rules and conduct whether guilded or not.

G. CHANGING OF THE GUARD: 

Declaring an alternate character as one's new primary character will not be allowed unless direct approval from the High Council is obtained through unanimous vote. This rule overrides the precedent established as a result of past occurrences in which this took place. As there are roles in every guild function that need to be filled, many people will be invited with the understanding that they have been chosen as among the best to fulfill that role. Deficiencies in one or more areas of any particular class does not diminish the need for that class, and in order for us to function properly, we need dedicated people with a strong online presence within the class they were invited to play.

H. OWNERSHIP AND GUILD STATUS:
  1. Characters whose ownership has changed for any reason may not be permitted to retain membership within Iron Cross, and are to be guild removed immediately upon discovering the change.
  2. Any character declared as an alt, but played on a regular basis by anyone other than the declared owner, will be deemed another person's primary character and that character will be immediately guild removed.
  3. Guild removal of any character under the above described situations may not be necessary if all persons involved are current members in good standing.
  4. All situations will be handled on a case by case basis, and special considerations can be made in any case if the matter is brought before the High Council and explained in detail. Any decisions made by the High Council in these matters are final.
  5. Characters guild removed based on change of ownership are not prohibited from applying for membership through normal inviting process.
    1. In addition to any other requirements set by the High Council and Class Leaders, characters of sold accounts who were previous Iron Cross members shall be subject to a 90 day probationary period, as opposed to the standard 60 days.
