I. CHARTER

 

Iron Cross is a guild founded on the Tarew Marr server. Originally founded by close friends who live in the San Diego, California area, Iron Cross has now grown to include members from all over the globe. Our goal is to set, and achieve, a higher standard of fairness and cooperation with the community of Tarew Marr, to further enhance the enjoyment of the game, both for ourselves and those we interact with.

Iron Cross is a high level guild for high level players., We value enjoyment of the game over everything else and take pride in our expertise within game, however, we know that it takes more than levels to be among the best. Thus we look for that unequivocal quality of character to identify those that can contribute to ensuring our goals are realized.

By rallying together within a solid structure of leadership and fairness, we become a powerful and cohesive force that will topple any challenge and ultimately achieve our personal in-game goals. Accomplishing this will not occur over night, and it must be understood that sacrifices in time and personal in-game wealth may be needed on occasion. Our members must be willing to do so, for the betterment of the guild as a whole, and in the interest of enhancing the enjoyment of the game for those we call friends.
