  Quest recipes requiring tailoring:
  • Cabilis necro quest: 2nd rank skullcap: torn tapestry and ripped tapestry off Sarnak hatchlings in the Lake of Ill Omen. Combine in a sewing kit for a "mended tapestry", lore, no drop. (trivial 23)
  • Shadow silk (necro shadow-bound armor quest, as well as the monk Robe of the Lost Circle quest) [trivial 51?]: shadow wolf pelt, silk swatch, and necromancer spell scroll "Gather Shadows"
  • Large shadow silk (necro shadow-bound armor quest): 2 shadow wolf pelts, silk swatch, and necromancer spell scroll "Gather Shadows"
  • Inky silk (necro shadow-bound armor quest) [trivial 39?]: shadow wolf pelt, silk swatch, and 2 copies of the necromancer spell scroll "Gather Shadows"
  • Cabilis monk training bag: combine 3 granite pebbles and a "prepared leech husk" in a large sewing kit or loom to get a monk training bag. (trivial at 1 or 2) (Other parts: prepared leech husk - Get a leech husk bag from Master Bain (Court of Pain), fill with 4 leech husks, combine, return to Klok Mugruk; get the granite pebbles from the Toilmaster by returning 2 broken froglok shackles for a pile of granite pebbles)
  • Patched Gnoll Fur Bundle (Halas): [trivial 21 for first combine, <=36 for the rest, bundle >31] combine four patches of gnoll fur to make a gnoll fur patch. Take four of the newly made gnoll fur patches to make a gnoll fur patch quarter. Take four gnoll fur patch quarters to make patched gnoll fur bundle (AC 4, sv 5 vs cold, wt 4.0, back slot). This cloak can be worn or traded in above the bank to the female rogue for a Fang of the Wolf, barbarian only piercing weapon.
