A.) Cobalt Scar- Wyverns and Bunthar drop misc. gems (chipped, flawed, some crushed) the ones needed for the druid quests here in Cobalt scar are the chipped onyx sapphire (for legs) and the jaundiced gems for the arms).  Wyverns also drop nephrite for wizard quest, and some other misc. (coral crescent, imperfect diamonds etc). Chances are in CS you'll run into mostly flawed gems or chipped. So if you need flawed gems come here.

B.) Velketor's Labyrinth- Velketor's has some of the more nice gem drops in Velious. Here, you will find mainly crushed gems and some of the other varieties (flawed, chipped, crushed) Here you can get the crushed lava rubies, and also many many many other CRUSHED type gems. In my travels I have yet to see the BP gems drop here in Velketor's yet (possibly off the extremely hard mobs, but none yet to my knowledge).

C.) Icewell Keep- Basically the same as above. (see B.)

D.) Dragon Necropolis- Here you will find the big time gem drops you desire. Confirmed black marble and flawless diamonds drop here off the rats and dragon skeletons. They are however a tad bit rare, and it may take you several (as many as 10) trips to see 3 drop. The mobs drop all class specific BP gems including but not limited too- Warrior, Druid, Wizard, Monk, and Rogue.

E.) Temple of Veeshan- Basically same as above. (see D.)

F.) The Wakening Lands- Suits of Sentient armor seem to have similar drop tables as the wyverns in Cobalt Scar, however, drop crushed gems more frequently than you will find in Cobalt Scar. The suits have a ton of HP, so bring a friend.

G.) Tower of Frozen Shadow- On the upper floors of the Tower of Frozen Shadow some misc. crushed, jaundiced, chipped and flawed gems have dropped. The lower floors will not drop them much (if at all) so make your way to the 4th floor at least to ensure some nice drop rates.
