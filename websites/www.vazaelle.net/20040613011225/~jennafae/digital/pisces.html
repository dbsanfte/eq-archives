May 2004
Another portrait I created as an exhibition piece for the "What Sign Are You?" Zodiac Contest Challenge I hosted at Norrath's Studio. I really had a lot of fun with this piece! I typically gravitate towards warmer color pallates, so the cool colors in this one were new and different for me. I wanted this Darkelf to have a regal Sea Goddess aura around her, and was trying to imply that her hair was made from the ocean waves. I also had never painted fish before and I'm very pleased with how they turned out! On a whim, I added the fin along her jawline below her ear during the last finishing touches.

I had thought to give her a crown of shells and pearls, perhaps some seaweed in her hair, and a trident in the background. I decided to leave these elements out, I felt they would overcrowd the simplicity of the piece. Maybe next time!
