Kissifur organized the first Halloween Howl studio challenge portrait exchange on Norrath's Studio this year. Because she was the moderator or the challenge, she of course was not participating. To thank her for all her hard work, I wrote to all of the artists in the challenge and asked them to create something special for Kissi if they had the time. Kissifurl has such a bright and magical personality, I chose to depict her redheaded elf as a genie for Halloween!

The quote is taken from one of my favorite old Ray Harryhausen films, "The 7th Voyage of Sinbad."

Another huge milemarker for me, this is only the second piece of original non-screenshot artwork NS has seen from me, next to the witchy portrait done for Lala. From start to finish, this picture was drawn in pencil, inked in pen, scanned into my computer, and then inked and colored in Photoshop entirely by my own hand.



Tools:
Paper, pencil, pen
Photoshop 7.0
Wacom Intuoso2 graphical tablet
