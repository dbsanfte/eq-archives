The Basic Portrait
The Basic Portrait consists of a torso and/or facial screenshot, encorporated into a small "signature" portrait, appropriate in size for use as a message board signature image. Backgrounds and graphic elements are generally left to the artist's discrection, but I will gladly listen to special requests. The Basic Portrait can also include youer EQ class icon (i.e. what you see in your inventory menu), a simple background, and your chosen line of text.
Basic Portrait runs 5000pp

The Basic Signature
The Basic Signature consists of a rectangular sig image, or sigline, appropriate in size and composition for use as a message board signature. Siglines generally encorporate a single player screenshot and text of choice.
Basic Signature runs 5000pp

Delux Character Portrait
A Delux Portrait can have up to 3 different character screenshots; face, profile, torso, full-body, or action full-body screenshots. Also, this portrait includes options such as specific zone location backgrounds, hand-drawn spell particles, class icon, additional weapon/item cutouts, and text of choice. Background and graphic elements in this picture are generally more complex and more time consuming. Once again, while I am glad to help acheive a certain vision, artistic freedom reigns with my work.
Deluxe Character Portrait
starts at 7000pp, priced upon request.
