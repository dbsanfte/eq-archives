8.30.04
I am finally working on a new interface for my website, as you can see! I toyed with a few other color schemes, including the bold red and orange placeholder page you might have seen the past couple of months, but ended up reverting back to a pallate a bit easier on the eyes. Out with the blues and grays of my old interface and in with the earthy greens.

Many thanks to Alison (aka Oyye) of oops I arted! for creating my new site mascot, Oscar. Autumn is coming up fast, so I'm happy to be able to show him off decked out in his Halloween wear. I love his witchy hat!

Please check back frequently as I work on opening up the other areas of my site.

Thank you for stopping by!
