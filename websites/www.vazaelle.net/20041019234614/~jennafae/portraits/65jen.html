This project was a huge challenge, due to how many new things I was trying all at once. With the dingage of level 65, I was way overdue for a self-made portrait. I did extensive repainting of Jenna's face, hair, and armor, along with an entirely hand-painted background and cloak. This was not long after I received my graphical tablet, but at this point I was starting to feel a lot more comfortable with using it and was able to do much of the repainting and detail work without the use of my mouse.

Tools:
Photoshop 7.0
Wacom Intuoso2 graphical tablet
