Hail, friend!

Allow me to introduce myself. My name is Xorrin Aarikis, Heyokah and Scaled Mystic of the mighty Iksar Empire, and this is my domain. Welcome to Vazaelle.NET, home of the Knights of the Green Mist and the Norrath Alliance Treaty Organizatoin (N.A.T.O.)!

For those of you who just happened to stumble upon our humble domain by sheer fate, allow me to elaborate&#8230;

  • Vazaelle is the best server in EverQuest&#8482;.
  • The Knights of the Green Mist is the oldest Guild on the Vazaelle Server.
  • And finally, N.A.T.O. is the largest Alliance of Guilds on the Vazaelle Server!
  • Vazaelle.NET is the unofficial official site for the Vazaelle Server.

If you have not already had a chance to do so, I would like to invite you to REGISTER with us. To do so is 100% FREE, and your information will never be given to anyone for any reason (short of a legal court order). Doing so will enable you to LOG IN and enjoy the many hidden treasures within our realm. When creating an account, please keep in mind the following two very important items:

  1. Use only the FIRST NAME of your primary EverQuest&#8482; Character as your User Name. (If you do not have one, then any unique name will do.)
  2. You must leave a valid E-Mail address. (The system will E-Mail you a random temporary password, which you will be able to change when you log on. You will not be able to log in without a valid E-Mail address. Rest assured, I HATE SPAM - You will never get it from us!)

If you are already a member, simply LOG IN and you will be able to access the special Members Only sections of the site.

Finally, if you would like additional information or require special assistance with anything relating to KGM, NATO, or Vazaelle.NET, simply drop me a line. My E-Mail address is xorrin@vazaelle.net. Safe Hunting!

HERE YE! HERE YE!

By order of Xorrin Aarikis, Grand Hierophant of the mighty Iksar Empire, leader of the noble Knights of the Green Mist, all officers and those who so desire to fill open commissions are hereby directed to make their formal requests in the KGM Forums!
___________________________

KGM will be undergoing some major changes in organization. THIS IS YOUR FAIR WARNING. If you are an officer, it is (and always has been) required of you to be active on the web site. If you are not, you will loose your commission. We have too many officers who are not doing their jobs! We have too many people who have earned the right to a commission, but lack the spots to put them in. We are going to fix this very soon!

ALL OFFICER POSITIONS, FROM TOP TO BOTTOM, ARE GOING TO BE REASSIGNED. Starting with the Knights Council, and working our way down the line. What this means for you is, if you want to be an officer, NOW is the time to let us know. (DO NOT PM YOUR REQUESTS!!!! ALL POSTS SHALL BE MADE IN THE PUBLIC KGM FORUMS FOR THE ENTIRE GUILD TO COMMENT ON. ANY PM REGARDING OFFICERS WILL BE DELETED - UNREAD!)

If you are already an officer and wish to change or resign your commission, now is the time to let it be known. If I do not hear from you, on this site, I will assume you do not have an opinion either way, and you may very well LOOSE YOUR COMMISSION. Again, do not PM me...and do not send me a /tell in game. If it is not in the forums, then you can bet I will either not remember, or misplace it. I simply have too many RL things going on with work and the other MMORPG project to remember every detail of a /tell. ;)

Site Updates: It's that time again!
As some of you know, I have taken up a position as a Lead Developer on another MMORPG. A while back, Jennafae made some awesome graphics for me to use on this site, but I simply didn't have the time to put them to use due to work.

Now, I have time, and have built a rather nice theme for the other game using them. Once finalized and the bugs ironed out, I will be posting that theme to Vazaelle.NET.

With the new theme, comes the public release of the new sites for NATO and KGM. www.vazaelle.net will transition into a server-general website. NATO and KGM will have their own, seperate sites.

I will also be updating the server software and forums to the latest stable releases. This will bring added functionality to the site, but most of the changes will be on the admin side.

NOTICE: With the updates, I will be REMOVING ALL PN-SUPPORTED PRIVATE MESSAGING! PMs will now be handled by the Forums built-in PM system. This is a much nicer system, and allows for some more advanced management and options. READ AND MOVE ALL PM'S YOU HAVE BEEN SAVING NOW. ONCE THEY ARE GONE, THEY ARE GONE FOREVER!!!!!!

I am posting this as fair warning to everyone.
