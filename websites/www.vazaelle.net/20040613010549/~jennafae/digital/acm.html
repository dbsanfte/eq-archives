April 2004
Definately an art-nouveau inspired piece. Some of my favorite artistic influences are artist Alphonse Mucha, artist and architect Frank Lloyd Wright, along with the American Bungelow arts and crafts style. I love how this piece pulls a lot of those elements together with warm and rich colors.
