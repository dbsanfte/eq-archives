10.09.04
Many of my friends and fans are aware that I've been tirelessly working as the Art Director for the past several months of a small indie project MMORPG called Aakrana. Due to irreconcilable differences with the then Project Leader, I resigned about two weeks ago. Since then, the project has returned to the able hands of the original creator of the game and is under new management. I am happy to say that I have returned to the project as a member of the development team, although not as the Art Director at this time, by choice. I will continue my work on the game's Graphical User Interface (GUI), 2D concept art and graphics, as well as redesigning the main website.

You can view my conceptual mockup of the website redesign   H E R E.   Click on the image below to visit Aakrana's new homepage, which is still under development.

10.05.04
Updated the gallery pages, as some of the links were still a bit off. Also included *NEW* artwork on the Screenshot pages, sigs and Magelo backgrounds both. I am also working on a spectacular new freehand portrait with a Halloween theme, but the unveiling will have to wait a few weeks, as it's a gift for the 2004 Halloween Howl exchange over at Norrath's Studio. Turnins aren't until the 23rd!

9.26.04
I'm lazy, it's true. It's only taken me bloody 6 months to get it done, but the majority of my website is up and running again with the new interface! There is still some work to be done and a lot of updating with new artwork, however, so don't expect to browse and see anything you've not seen before!

I'll get there, eventually!

8.30.04
I am finally working on a new interface for my website, as you can see! I toyed with a few other color schemes, including the bold red and orange placeholder page you might have seen the past couple of months, but ended up reverting back to a pallate a bit easier on the eyes. Out with the blues and grays of my old interface and in with the earthy greens.

Many thanks to Alison (aka Oyye) of oops I arted! for creating my new site mascot, Oscar. Autumn is coming up fast, so I'm happy to be able to show him off decked out in his Halloween wear. I love his witchy hat!

Please check back frequently as I work on opening up the other areas of my site.

Thank you for stopping by!
