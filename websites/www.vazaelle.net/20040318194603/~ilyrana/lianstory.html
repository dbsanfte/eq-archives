Lianess was left by her parents on the steps of the Monistary of Quellious in Freeport when she was less than a month old and was raised by the monk there. By the age of 4 she showed Great skill and promise the Monk knew she would one day be a grandmaster by the age of 14 she had progressed beyond what the monks could teach her and she had set out on her own. Living a meager life serving Quellious, one night she came across Grand master Rocsalt who took her under his wing teaching her the ways of a monk.
