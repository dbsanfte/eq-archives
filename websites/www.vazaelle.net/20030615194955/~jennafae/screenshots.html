WELCOME to Jennafae's Everquest Screenshot Archives. The purpose of this project is to provide a resource of interesting and exceptional background and scenic screenshots for the vast community of Everquest fan artists. This gallery was inspired by my peers and friends on Norrath's Studio. I am forever in debt to their undying support, creativity, inspiration, and constructive criticism on my journey as a screenshot artist. In gratitude, I dedicate this Gallery to the amazing talent that lives in the N.S. "Studio Regulars."

If you would like to contribute to the project, I am seeking screenshots of exceptional subject matter and quality. Please email your unedited screenshots to JennaEQaddict@aol.com. If you are so inclined to convert the images to jpeg format prior to mailing, please do. I also ask that if you are sending multiple images, please wrap them up in a zip file for speedy uploading and downloading. Please include the words "Screenshot Archives" in the subject heading, and your name as you would like to see it in a list of contributors, which will eventually appear on the Archives page.

Click on thumbnails to view larger images, rightclick to save images for use in your fan-art. Please do not direct link images from this page.
