As most of you all know, I'm a longtime member of the EQ fanart community of Norrath's Studio. I recently had the pleasure to participate in my first "Studio Challenge" where artists are assigned another NS member to create artwork for. It's all very secretive, as no one knows which artists everyone has been assigned until the day of the turnins. This particular art exchange was called "Halloween Howl" and we were instructed to depict our assignment's Everquest character in some sort of Halloween costume or Halloween-themed portrait.

I was thrilled when I received Lala as my "victim" but also a little nervous. I'm a huge fan of Lala's anime-style cartoon artwork (you can view Lala's showcase thread on NS here). She's extremely talented and I wanted to create something that would impress her! I decided to shed my skin as a known-screenshot artist and try something new and different for me.

This piece of artwork was a huge milemarker for me. For the first time in YEARS, I picked up a pencil and a piece of paper and began to draw something of my own. From start to finish, this picture was drawn in pencil, inked in pen, scanned into my computer, and then inked and colored in Photoshop entirely by my own hand.

Lala posted on Halloween Howl signup thread that she loved striped stockings and pointy hats. This information paired along with my extreme love of all things Witchy ... well I just couldn't resist! I present to you Lala's EQ character Aalamadara Warchild as a Witch for Halloween! Watch out, she'll put a spell on you!

You can view some of my work in progress on this portrait here.
