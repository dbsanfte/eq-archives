Hail, friend!

Allow me to introduce myself. My name is Xorrin Aarikis, Heyokah and Scaled Mystic of the mighty Iksar Empire, and this is my domain. Welcome to Vazaelle.NET, home of the Knights of the Green Mist and the Norrath Alliance Treaty Organizatoin (N.A.T.O.)!

For those of you who just happened to stumble upon our humble domain by sheer fate, allow me to elaborate&#8230;

  • Vazaelle is the best server in EverQuest&#8482;.
  • The Knights of the Green Mist is the oldest Guild on the Vazaelle Server.
  • And finally, N.A.T.O. is the largest Alliance of Guilds on the Vazaelle Server!
  • Vazaelle.NET is the unofficial official site for the Vazaelle Server.

If you have not already had a chance to do so, I would like to invite you to REGISTER with us. To do so is 100% FREE, and your information will never be given to anyone for any reason (short of a legal court order). Doing so will enable you to LOG IN and enjoy the many hidden treasures within our realm. When creating an account, please keep in mind the following two very important items:

  1. Use only the FIRST NAME of your primary EverQuest&#8482; Character as your User Name. (If you do not have one, then any unique name will do.)
  2. You must leave a valid E-Mail address. (The system will E-Mail you a random temporary password, which you will be able to change when you log on. You will not be able to log in without a valid E-Mail address. Rest assured, I HATE SPAM - You will never get it from us!)

If you are already a member, simply LOG IN and you will be able to access the special Members Only sections of the site.

Finally, if you would like additional information or require special assistance with anything relating to KGM, NATO, or Vazaelle.NET, simply drop me a line. My E-Mail address is xorrin@vazaelle.net. Safe Hunting!

N.A.T.O. News: Howling Stones (Charisis)
I don't remember who brought this up, but one of the times I was grouping with some Torture folks someone said "I wonder if anyone goes to Howling Stones anymore?" Well, I got to thinking about it and HS drops some nice stuff and is fun.
Note: The key is pretty simple to get. Got mine in about a half hour. Here is a link to the quest info: Allakhazam's

Here is a the info on Kaesora:

ZONE: Kaesora
LOCATED: Field of Bone
CLOSEST PORT: Emerald Jungle
RECOMMENDED LEVELS: 30-45+
XP BONUS: +18%

Fun zone to raid, but also quite popular for the 50+ level guilds seeking keys. Xalgoz is a pretty rare spawn...pops for us on average of once every 6 to 8 times for the placeholder, but even when he pop'd 4 spawns apart, it took a couple hours! Fortunately, he tends to drop 2 fangs at once quite often! =)

Safe hunting!
~ Xorrin

The History of the Knights of the Green Mist…

"So it is that was born a special group of Iksar bent on the freedom of their race, no longer closed minded about the enemies of their people. These Knights remember that it was a Devine Act that gave them their freedom and they look back to the time of the Green Mists for inspiration in the days ahead. So did they form the Knights of the Green Mist, drawing not only from the ranks of all the groups of the Iksar but also enlisting the aid of the softskins of like mind. The Knights operate outside of the boundaries of the rulers of Cabilis. They have one, and only one true goal: the destruction of the greatest threat ever known to all of Norrath, the Dragon menace. To this end have they sought out many of the soft skinned races and found those that too realize the threat the Dragon pose to all races and joined together to exterminate this threat…"

~ Zarkorsis Kunzar
