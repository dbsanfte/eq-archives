DOMAIN NAME REGISTRATION
What's a domain name? This is your dot.com. For example, my domain name is inkymouse.com. You can also purchase a .net or a .org. Hands down, GoDaddy.com is the cheapest and easiest on the web.

You can can search the World Wide Web database to find out if your dot.com has been taken or not, and it's only $8.95 per year to register your own domain. I purchased www.inkymouse.com for two years. For free, I also chose 'forwarding' and 'masking' so that my website visitors always see www.inkymouse.com instead of my actual URL, which is http://www.vazaelle.net/~jennafae/index.html
