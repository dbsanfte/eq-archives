Hail, friend!

Allow me to introduce myself. My name is Xorrin Aarikis, Heyokah and Scaled Mystic of the mighty Iksar Empire, and this is my domain. Welcome to Vazaelle.NET, home of the Knights of the Green Mist and the Norrath Alliance Treaty Organizatoin (N.A.T.O.)!

For those of you who just happened to stumble upon our humble domain by sheer fate, allow me to elaborate…

  • Vazaelle is the best server in EverQuest™.
  • The Knights of the Green Mist is the oldest Guild on the Vazaelle Server.
  • And finally, N.A.T.O. is the largest Alliance of Guilds on the Vazaelle Server!
  • Vazaelle.NET is the unofficial official site for the Vazaelle Server.

If you have not already had a chance to do so, I would like to invite you to REGISTER with us. To do so is 100% FREE, and your information will never be given to anyone for any reason (short of a legal court order). Doing so will enable you to LOG IN and enjoy the many hidden treasures within our realm. When creating an account, please keep in mind the following two very important items:

  1. Use only the FIRST NAME of your primary EverQuest™ Character as your User Name. (If you do not have one, then any unique name will do.)
  2. You must leave a valid E-Mail address. (The system will E-Mail you a random temporary password, which you will be able to change when you log on. You will not be able to log in without a valid E-Mail address. Rest assured, I HATE SPAM - You will never get it from us!)

If you are already a member, simply LOG IN and you will be able to access the special Members Only sections of the site.

Finally, if you would like additional information or require special assistance with anything relating to KGM, NATO, or Vazaelle.NET, simply drop me a line. My E-Mail address is xorrin@vazaelle.net. Safe Hunting!

Site Updates: NEW! Vazaelle.NET version 7.2.3
Beginning today and lasting (hopfully only) over the next 3 days, I will be implimenting the next generation of Vazaelle.NET.

This is a TOTAL OVERHAUL of the site. EVERYTHING will be new.

The software will be the same, but is a total rewrite of the currently installed version. The message boards will finally be updated to a Release version (as opposed to a pre-alpha like the current ones). I will also be upgrading PHP and MySQL on the server, as well as installing some patches to Linux.

Although I am doing this from over 30,000 miles of where the server is physically located, I really do not expect any problems to occur. I either already have, or are in the process of backing up the entire server on my local computer, just in case, and also have a backup job in at the host.

I am taking every measure to ensure that no data, especially user data, is lost. After reviewing the code and database structures of the new version of the site, I do not anticipate any data compatibility issues to arise. Same for the message boards. With luck, the site itself will be back up and running after being offline for about 20 minutes (time to upload and run install scripts). While that is happening, I will have a temporary page up.

Also with this new update, the KGM and NATO sites will no longer be found at this URL. I will post the new URLs when the site is back online, but I think you'll figure it out. ;)
