Paven wanted his portrait to be very Paladin-like. I chose to go with a "Holy White Knight" theme.... except he's not white. His armor was black and orange to start with, I recolored it with permission, which inspired him to actually change his colors in-game also. The borders are created from a celtic border font, the cross was hand painted while looking at a piece of clipart as reference. The horse's tail and main are hand paitned, as well as the reigns (why EQ horses have a bridle but no reigns I will never understand).

Photoshop 7.0
