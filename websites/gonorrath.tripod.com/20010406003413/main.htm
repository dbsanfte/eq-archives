The Guardsmen Way of Life...

1). Have fun! We're all here to have a good time- and so try to respect other members of the guild's desire to do the same. Which leads us to point 2:

2). When you have an issue with another member- try and keep your cool. There are all kinds of people on EverQuest, and we all too often get to see their worst sides. Just remember that yelling hardly ever changes someone's mind- try a bit more tact and usually the results will keep everyone happy. If there's a problem you feel you can't resolve, contact a guild leader/officer for assistance. If your problem is with a guild leader/officer, another one will be happy to mediate for you.

3). Try to keep unnecessary traffic out of guildsay. Be sociable, but if it's something that can clearly fit into a personal tell, try to keep it there. It helps avoid aggravation, and when there are a lot of guildmates online it can get kind of confusing with all the scrolling!

4). Be responsive to leadership requests for information. The guild is growing, and it makes it a lot easier when everyone can provide us with stats and contact information on the first try.

5). Help your guildmates out! If you see a Guardsmen or an Ally fighting, don't be afraid to give a hand! Guild parties are probably the best method we have of improving our avatars!

6). Be aware of what's going on in the guild. Check the message board and the Guild News section often- they'll keep you informed of guild meetings and other information that you'll need to remain an effective Guardsmen!

7). DON'T BEG OTHER MEMBERS FOR EQUIPMENT! As a guild, we should naturally be inclinced to helping out one another, whether it be providing assistance for obtaining an item, or giving guildsmate first notice of an item you have for sale or trade. Don't, however, hound other members for a piece of equipment that they are using if they have not already decided to sale/trade it. Keep in mind we're a team- giving your guildsmates "discounts" encourages them to return the favor!

8). ANY INFORMATION ON OTHER GUILDMATES IS NOT FOR SALE/TRADE! Should another guildmate give you their email address, web site address, or whatever- it is meant for you ONLY. We will not tolerate members contributing the overflow of spam that I am sure everyone is already getting.
