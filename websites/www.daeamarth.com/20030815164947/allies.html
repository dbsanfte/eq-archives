FRIENDS & ALLIES
Daeamarth is not and never was intended to be an uber guild all about leveling and collecting phat lewts, but instead more about friendships between members and having a good time. While this approach is very rewarding in that we have a really good group of people in the guild, having a smaller-type guild has it's downfalls too, and we sometimes find ourselves missing the necesary firepower within the guild for some of the more challenging things we would like to accomplish, and sometimes even have a hard time finding people within the guild for just the every day stuff.

We address this issue by forming and maintaining active friendships and alliances with other guilds similar to ours. By establishing these relationships, not only do we get to meet and adventure with all kinds of new and fun people, but through proper communication and planning, we also increase our potential for accomplishing some of the more challenging battles and quests we would like to tackle.

Below is a list of guilds that we consider to be good friends of Daeamarth. For the normal day-to-day adventuring, not only do we check our personal friends list and guild roster for people to play with, but we also look to see if we can get anything going with members of these guilds. In addition, when we are trying to plan for harder raids and quests, we try to plan and coordinate with these guilds.
