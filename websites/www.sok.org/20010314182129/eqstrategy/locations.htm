Locations Page

This page is meant to help those of us who like to go into new places, but who like to know where they are going. It's also extremely useful for those of us that travel alot and don't always remember who to get to certain locations out in the middle of nowhere (SplitPaw in the middle of Southern Plains of Karana comes to mind).

Credit goes to Dennis Cox for making the original page and starting this. All I did was copy what he had, and added some extra locations. Thank you Dennis for recording all these locations, and allowing me the privilege to make this list from yours. They've helped me out alot in my travels, as I'm sure they have others as well.

Layout: Starts with Zone known name (Northern Desert of Ro), then has the actual zone name the game knows it as next to it (nro). Then under each zone will be areas of interest, especially zone borders (marked with a (Z)), and coordinated for use with the /loc command. If you don't know how to use the /loc command, type it in game. Then walk a bit, and type it again and compare the coordinates. Walk around a bit more, and check them again. Location coordinates are, as far as I understand, unique to each zone. This means that 800, 200 can be in every zone (and very likely is). It is a ZONE coordinate, not a WORLD coordinate.

Also, there are some things missing on this list. I don't know the locations for several areas of interest (the coordinates are missing), and I'm missing some of the zone names as well. I am also likely missing zones themselves. If there is anything you want to add or correct, please email me and I'll make or add the modification or addition.

NOTE: Some of the coordinates for the zones are only ONE POSSIBLE coordinate for the zone border. Some zone borders are extremely long, instead of the tendency of the small zone border. An example of a small border would be going from Everfrost into Halas or Blackburrow. Very narrow cave like entrances - this is common. The other extreme, is a very large border. For example, going from West Karana to North Karana. It's plains, and you can enter anywhere along a very long line. Thus, for these larger zone borders (which are NOT indicated in any way on this sheet, I don't know of alot of them, mainly Commons and Karana Plains - but we can work on it if people want to email me suggestions), the locations posted are one of the many places to zone across.
