Now that you are 19th head over to Highhold pass. Hunt the orcs near Kithcor (if you can get a good group of 6 people - they spawn fast and concentrated), hunt the knolls near E. Karana (good for smaller groups of 1-3)

Solo the 3 smugglers near the Keep. Use Lament to sadden the girl smuggler (she is the tuffie), then sadden the male Hummie next to her (he is the easy guy, but tends to wake her up. Then target the dwarf (hidding behind the tree) & pull him to the edge of cave w/ bellow. Fight him near door, so the others won't jump in to help, but watch out for KS's guards who wander by.

After Dwarf, stay along right wall & walk in to where dwarf stood. (Assuming you healed up.) Now pull the male Hummie & take out the greenie. Heal up again & take out the big girl. Once you have them killed so they will respawn at different times, you can heal yourself, then practice your brass skill like crazy (standing on dwarf spawn). I also have time in the rotation to work on my pottery/cooking/etc skills by running between spawns.

If it goes bad, hit selo's run over the hill to the guards & the zone to keep. Once you get them spawning at separate times, this is a easy spot for a single bard to hold.
This is also a great spot to try out new tactics...this weapons bard is finally learning to swap weapons & instrument in realative safety.

Just watch out for LOST (clueless & other negative adjective) players, who walk into the cave as you are trying to safely heal outside & totally mess up your spawn rotation.
