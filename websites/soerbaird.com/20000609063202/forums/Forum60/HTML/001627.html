Kelador
Fellowship Veteran 		posted 12-13-1999 13:07            
Given how slow the bows are in the game anyway would you really want to use a crossbow? Realistically a well-trained, unfatigued (and strong) crossbowman could get off perhaps 3 shots (maybe 4) in a minute with a light xbow. A heavy xbow required a crank just to get the string pulled back into position and would probably take 30 seconds to a minute to reload, aim and fire. So you're looking at in game delay times between 150 and 300.

Also you would have to be stationary - no runnning whilst you reload... traditionally the xbows of the late medieval period had a 'stirrup' on the front end of the stock... this was placed on the ground and the xbowman put one foot in the stirrup and pulled back with both hands on the string until it was cocked. Try doing that while someone is beating on you

A xBow is not really a ranger weapon... its more suited to the one shot strategy favored by warriors. The extra damage inflicted by a single xbow bolt might be worthwhile, but I would sooner have the average damage from 4 arrows fired from a Trueshot than a single xbow shot.

I wish these were in the game...but not for us.

I could see a crossbow with something like a 30 Dmg 150 Dly 50 Rng. Would fire 3-8 Dmg 50 Rng quarrels. Would be usable by all classes other than Rangers (but use of all longbows would be taken away from all other classes). Warriors could still get crits with them.

I think that such a change would enhance both us and other classes. A crossbow is actually more suited to the type of pulling that most other classes use archery for anyway. Our unique longbows are more suited to our long range open area archery requirements. We could kite..others could not.

Verant would have to:
Implement a mechanism for other classes to "turn-in" longbows and obtain an equivalent crossbow.
Make crossbows and quarrels fletching items in order to protect the fletching market (such as it is). Or see the first point...other classes would have to buy our fletched longbows to obtain their crossbows and would have to buy our arrows to obtain quarrels.

Personally I think such a change would be a "win-win-win" for all concerned: Other Classes, Rangers, and Fletchers.
