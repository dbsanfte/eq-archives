I went from level 27 to level 30 in a hurry in Everfrost. Camped the icy orcs and Redwind. There's usually people there that you can group with and the xp is good. The loot however is nothing to get too excited about, an occasional FS and coins is about it. Also on the down side is that there are only 5 spawn points and the area can get crowded in a hurry. But you can always pull Wooly's while you wait.

Redwind and his 4 icy orcs spawn on a pavillion in the Northeast corner of EF - beneath the giant Smurf statue - near the Ice Giant entrance to PF.

Get to the frozen river and head north then track - Redwind spawns every few minutes so he'll show up frequently - if not the icy orcs should show up.
