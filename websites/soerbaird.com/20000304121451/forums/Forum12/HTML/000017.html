Hahn - Bringing a third song into your twist really isn't any different from doing 2 songs. I am assuming that you are starting one song #1 and then after that song starts, stopping it and then going to song #2. Then going back to song #1 before song #2 ends. To bring into a third song, does just that... instead of going back to #1 after #2 move onto #3... and then back to #1....

NOW, you may physically prevented from doing this, though.... on an efficient connection/PC, the timing of the songs is set up to allow you use 3 songs simultaneously... BUT if your connection/PC isn't up to par or you are in extreme lag zones, you may not be able to do this.

On my server, I can EASILY use 3 songs at a time, even working in a fourth song and just losing the effects of my songs for just a second before the songs effects take hold again UNLESS I am in UNREST, then I am reduced to 2 songs because of lag. On my second machine, which is not as good as my server, I usually only do 2 songs and it is a CHORE to keep three songs going at once.

Utilizing the Instrument on the Cursor method, you would have one in melee slot, one on cursor and one in inven slot at all times... just switching out what one was on the cursor and into the needed slot as appropriate for the song.

Tips for reducing lag to juggle and twist (very important, lag is death to a bard):

Turn off the 3d sky (or at least drop to single layer)
Turn off the shaded polygons in full screen mode
Turn off the particles (huge difference)

And yeah, doing 3 instruments would work the way you describe. The only problem is that the mouse isn't always that responsive and its a pain to do it. You also increase the risks of dropping your instrument in battle.

Trying to get a fourth song in is theoreticly possible, but I suspect that lag would make it impossible in combat.

Yes, that's because the Hotkey Numbers only work on 'buttons', such as your skills and spells. When you put Inventory and Melee slots in the bank, you have to move the items 'by hand', with your mouse.

I often put a Container in the Inventory slot that I bind to the Hotkey bank. This allows you to effectively keep Several items in that Hotkey slot. Usefull, if you are trying to juggle three different instruments.
This can be quite helpful when Foraging. Running with Accellerando, poke the number for Forage, actually Get Something, right-click on the container, drop the foraged item into the bag, right-click again, and never stop running, or change views. *G* That goes for Fishing too.
