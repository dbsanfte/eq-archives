Greeting fellow shamans...

I have been coming to this board since before the first disease nerf. You know what? It was a great board. You could (and still) get any question you asked answered. Heh, some new person would post the stupidest of questions...and you know what...the people of this board would not flame them.

The community of this board has always represented the Shaman class of EQ. Go to most websites and they will almost always direct you here for information or answers. The most common answer, when someone asks for information about the shaman on a message board, is to come here. Fact- there is no better place on the net.

Look at what the community has become. There are flames on the boards. More rants than ever. People aren't coming here as much for answers...that needs to change or we will end up never seeing what we want in the class we represent.

Verant seems to listen to those who shake the trees. Those that do shake the tree, may find the fruit they are looking for. Verant, Brad, GZ, they all know what the Shaman's Crucible is. If we all got to gether to represent the Crucible, everyone under one banner, we will have a better chance of being listened to and taken seriously.

What I am saying, we need to start organizing and letting Verant know what about the class needs worked on. Sure they know, but if nobody is complaining/informing then they will work on other things they feel a priority. Thats how it works. If we organized and send them proposals for well thought out ideas that a majority of the crucible will stand behind, we will be heard.

This is not a simple idea of a petition. Have you seen the petitions out there? Demands for items that would imbalance the class or move away from the basic idea of the class.


What I basically propose to everyone in the Crucible is to get behind one another and "shake the tree" to get things fixed. No demands, no whining, and no threatening to quit. We need to put our collective force together and say..."Hey Verant, we as a community feel this needs addressed. Please come to OUR site and see what we are talking about". Mung has given us a place to unite and a collective name. Not just, we the petitioners. Let's use it and get things done right.

OK, what items need addressed? Propose them one at a time. Our first target...
Alchemy- we need this fixed. It is a part of our identity and would further increase our game balance. We all know how broke it is. Currently all the other trade skills except tinkering. With that in mind, why not get them to realize that this is a priority to us.

We could tell them our ideas on how to fix it, but that would just get it thrown into the garbage with things like the trade petition I saw. We need to point things out very clearly, like so:

1. You cannot train in this skill.
2. Herbal component prices are not balanced with the effect of the spell.
3. etc...

We have a lot of great minds on this board. Let's put them together and get things done for our class. We can let Verant see that we DO know what's wrong with the class and what need to be changed. It's obvious that nobody at Verant represents us like the Rangers or the Enchanters. Let's put all of our names under one banner and try to make this a great class. Make them come to us whenever they have an idea.

C'mon people, let's work with what Mung has given us....or am I just an idealist?

<*Seven Hammers Falling*>
