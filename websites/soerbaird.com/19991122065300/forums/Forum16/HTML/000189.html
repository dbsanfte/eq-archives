SHAMANS ROCK!!!!

Shamans have a very easy start, since they melee well, and can heal themself.
Its kinda hard to move from 15 to 25 since you cant melee anymore, and youre other spells arent as powerfull as those of other spellcasters. Statbuffs are near useless until lvl 30. Except for monks maybe.

But our debuffs rock, use them all the time, SoW is one of the most usefull spell in all lvls.

Our DD is very weak, but still quite usefull since we can heal as well.

But beginning with lvl 29 we get one of the most powerfull classes in the game, quickness is great, tagars insects even better.

At 34 you get your pet, which is IMHO very usefull, I use it all the time, even in dungeons, hmm took me 10 lvls to learn it though.

As you increase in lvl your statbuffs really help and you see it, but you can exist without them, do debuffs if you like, heal if that is what you want, use DD, use DoT, you are very good in everything, except melee, but there your pet comes in.

And you get those HP-Buffs that stack with the cleric ones, real nice.

And in lvl 49 you get GODLIKE buffs, see your tanks go crazy with a 66 str boost. And BTW all tanks want that lvl 44 stamina buff.

Or dots with more than 1100 damage, you rock at lvl 49.

But there are surely some flaws in our class, I NEVER used disease spells, all groups beg me not to use my great DOTs anymore because of that stupid DOT-Bug.

Buffing takes too long, I cant understand why the casting times are so long, just to max your downtime?? Stupid IMHO.

We are one of the few classes without any special ability like groupgate, charm, clarity, resurrect etc.

Very few neat shaman equipment.


But still shamans are GREAT!!!!

Got my message???
