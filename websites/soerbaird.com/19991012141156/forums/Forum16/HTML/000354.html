Do the majority of people here think a shaman petition is really necessary? I agree that there are skills/spells that are broken and need to be fixed; but please don't invite "them" to tinker around with our class anymore.

Disease, worthwhile if they double the damage and halve the time. At level 32, my current disease spell (Affliction) does 5 points per tick at 21 ticks. Well, 21 ticks is WAY too long for any kind of fighting I have ever done in this game.

Charm, replace it with longer Damage Over Time spells to get the hard core kiters back on the band-wagon. I personally thought the poison spells were much more useful after the changes, but I never kited anything.

Anyway, the point I am trying to make is that no news is good news. I carefully scan each new patch message for the word shaman, and I am always happy when I don't find it there.
