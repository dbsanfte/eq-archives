Sean's Rain Caller Quest Guide.

To get a Rain Caller you need 4 things.

1, A Trueshot Longbow.
2, a Treant heart.
3, A bag of gold Faerie dust.
4, 3000 gp.

Treant heart: Turn in small lanterns to Jyle in West FP until he gives you a heart. You will usually get it after a few lanterns.

Bag of Faerie dust: Go to the Faerie princess in L Fay and ask her about the dust. She's on a weird despawn/respawn cycle, the placeholder is to Gearheart's left, just wait around a while and she will eventually show. She tells you her wing was stolen by a gnome and she wants it back before she will give you the dust.

The gnome who stole it is Sanfryd, in the back of Ak'Anon, outside the necro guild. When you hail him and ask him about it he says he wants it to grow back his hair, and that he has no friends, etc. Tell him that you are his friend, and I think there's a little more dialog, and he will give you a box to fill with 10 Hair tonics. A gnome in a tent sells the hair tonics outside of West FP. Buy 10 hair tonics, put them in the box in separate slots, and hit combine. Then bring the box back to him.

After you give him the box he gives you a tattered toupee, which he wants fixed before he will give the wing back. To fix it you need to go to the hair tonic seller and ask him to repair the toupee. The hair tonic seller will say he needs 3 things first. He wants 2 clumps of gorilla hair and 1 piece of hair from the golden haired mermaid in Kedge.

To get the gorilla hairs you go to Cazic and kill the gorillas in the Tae yard. They drop 1 piece of hair each time. For the mermaid's hair you need to go to Kedge. She is level 40+, but not too hard. To get to her room, from the entrance of Kedge, you go straight out, swim down the big hole to the next floor down, then go straight out again. There will be a small room with a hole going up and a hole going down. The hole going up is the one that leads to her room. She is pretty much alone, I think, except there's 1 piranha I saw there. You can easily pull her solo though with a simple arrow, snare, or whatever. She drops the piece of hair every time you kill her.

Go back to Freeport to the hair tonic seller. Hand him the 3 pieces of hair and the toupee, it should take up all 4 of his slots. He will then give you the fixed toupee. Bring it back to Ak'Anon and you get the Faerie princess' wing. Bring back the wing to the princess and you get the Faerie gold dust.

Now, take the Faerie dust, the Treant heart, a Trueshot Longbow, and 3000 gp to the enchanter guildmaster in Felwithe, Kinool Goldsinger. Go through the dialog with him if you want, he basically just tells you the 4 things you need. Hand the items in and he gives you the Rain Caller.
