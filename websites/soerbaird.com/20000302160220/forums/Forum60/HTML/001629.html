Well, I solo'd a hill giant too, but not with a bow.

I was bored and in west commons, so I decided to try to melee one of these suckers since I didn't have any arrows. I was also anxious to put my new wolf form to the test =).

Pulled a HG with snare, nuked him once for 99 with lightning, dropped immolate and swarm on him, then jumped in and started meleeing.

Before long I realized I was winning. =D

I had to back off and heal 4 or 5 hundred hp since he got a few streaks of lucky blows in, but other than that I felled him with over a bub of health left.

I probably did about 350 dmg with the spells, but other than that everything was melee. I forgot to put up my dmg sheild as well, so that would have added a couple hundred dmg had I remembered.
