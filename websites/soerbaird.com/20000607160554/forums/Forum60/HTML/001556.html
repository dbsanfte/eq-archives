Kedge is a tough place to fight because it is a completely different environment that what you are used to. Its hard to target things, it's hard to stay in melee range because you're sinking or mobs push you way back when they hit you, or your swimming skill sucks, etc etc. There is a lot to think about.

The last thing I want to think about is, "Do I need to refresh EB on myself? On my teammates? How much longer do I have? How many fish scales do I have left?"

If everyone in the party had a FB it would be soooo much less to worry about, in my opinion. I've only been to kedge a half dozen times, but someone always dies because EB runs out during a fight, or they forget about it...it really sucks. This place is tough enough without having to worry about drowning too. Groups will fight much better together when they don't have that concern about drowning nagging at them in the back of their minds.

3000pp? Sure that's a bit steep. I paid quite a bit less for mine. But pp is virtually worthless at the higher levels - none of the items you'll really want at level 45 will be purchaseable. Trade only, I'm afraid, unless you get lucky now and then and find someone who likes cash more than anything in the world. Like my enchanter buddy who got 2 ykeshas for 6000pp and 6000pp worth of jewellery. Weird.

I love my FB. Never have to worry about casting EB again, which comes to think of it, leaves me another spell slot open. Yay!
