Hopefully we can get some inputs here from people who are currently soloing , safely and successfully at each level and consistently gaining experience.

I am currently LVL27 and will post my observations of successfully soloing. I will not try to remember where I was a few levels ago and post possibly erroneous information.

Armor: Ivy Bracers, Ivy Sleeves, Ivy Tunic, Bloodstained Mantle, Barbed Legs, Black Iron Boots, Black Iron Gloves, Spiked Collar, Grotesque Mask, Black Iron Crown

Weapons: Thulian Bladed Claws, Short Sword of Morin, Trueshot Longbow, 3/150 arrows

LVL 27:
In the entrance hallway of Solusek's Eye (SolA). Mobs con blue and experience giving green. Occasionally have to wait up to 5 minutes for a mob to spawn but can pull them into hallway and kill them. Fast, easy escape to zone. Not dangerous to other players because they are all deep in the dungeon.

First area of Mistmoore. Initiate Familiars and Pledge Familiars con blue to me. Casters can be beaten easily enough by getting in close and hitting fast. I can camp on the far side of the lake and fight, heal, fight, heal, with no waiting. Hide after fights to Heal and Med. Fairly short run up the ramp to the zone when other groups pull one of those nasty trains.

I am level 27 now as well.

Another good place to solo is South Karana in the Aviak village.
Rooks con blue and easy to solo.
Harriers are blue but can get very risky at times, I recommend snaring them and shooting some arrows before going into melee.
I was able to solo yellow Avocates with a bow but it isn't worth it. The time and arrows spent are too much.

In South Karana, Rangers can solo blue Centaurs in the northern part of the zone. Just track them and make sure others not around. It's important to snare them since they all run fast.

Other places I successfuly solo'd at levels 25-26 are guards in Nektolus forest, Tentacles and other things in Najena, Aviak guards in Lake Rathetear, Minotaurs in Beholder maze, Goblins in Dagnor's Cauldron and the yard in Mistmoore.

Level 20-25 can have great exp and low risk in Oasis hunting the Deepwater Crocs and caiman. The downside is that it gets boring quickly, there is no loot from them and its awfuly crowded, but the exp is great.

Can't really remember what good places I was soloing below that.

I am currently level 38 and have been soloing in Mistmoore whenever there is nobody to group with. I soloed in the graveyard during level 37 by first killing the lone guard then using harmony to pull the 4 other guards. Watch out for any warders/custodians/forbidders. (arranged in increasing difficulty) They are all healers you should aonly kill them if you want to clear the spawn. The secret room is good experience but blue casters are VERY hard to solo. Up the stairs and to the left is the "torture room." you can camou, go passed this one and check the next room for gargoyles. There are two spawns in here where jeering (about level 30), flouting (level 32-33) and leering (34-36). You can pull and solo them in the hallway that you came from. Another place to pull from is the Courtyard, which is down the tunnel that is directly across the gate to the Graveyard. (While camoued, check to the right down here and make sure nothing is stuck against the wall). There are 4 stationary spawns here. Use harmony on 2, cast your thorns, harmony the 3rd and pull. Once again, casters are a bitch and I don't recomend touching them without a group. The ancilles in plate are ghoulish, ghastish and vampiric (once again in increasing difficulty) They all have an effect that drains all of your stamina which really blows. If there is a group down here that has already cleared out the CY, it is possible to pull one at a time from up above near the castle entrance. You'll have to figure this one out yourself.
And a last note, if a wanderer hits you then runs away, this means that a train is coming. Zone the instant this happens to save yourself and everyone else the trouble of a castle train.
