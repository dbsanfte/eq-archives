Ok - few point of clarification:

Mortificator staff is usable by all shaman races and also bards as well for some strange reason...

The staff casts mana drain - this is a normal
proc that operates off your dexterity - so buff up before fighting casters. The spell procs against all types of creatures but drains casters of 100 mana per proc. I have never had it drain a caster completely as the battles don't usually last that long - so it is effectively useless. However, it can be handy in the "mob-boss" fights for draining casters like Vox or Nagafen - I like to think that my draining will help to keep Vox from casting that complete heal...

As for the location of Mortificator, I can't confirm a specific location but logically it has to be one of the wandering creatures - probably a lizard mystic. I found him near the gypsy camp stuck in a mountain pass near the Lake Rathe zone.

Not to be too discouraging, but there are only 2 staves on Tunare so far - and he also drops a nice monk tunic - do don't get your hopes up too high when you see him. I don't remember if the staff is visible - I hit him with a dot and ran like heck while our pets duked it out. I was 43 when I soloed him - 44 would be easy or a group of high 30s would be fine. He has SOC and hits for mid 70s-80s so prepare before you begin the battle.

Hope this helps,
