I've seen on this board a Hundred times "The Ebony Blade sucks because every knows It'll get your party killed." I was bored in class today and got to thinking about Everquest and decided that is just wrong.

Now, rangers in my mind have always been master tacticians (not something game mechanic's can give you sadly) and the Ebony Blade is a sword that requires tactics. Yes you walk up to a mob hit autoattack, snare, DOT, and stand there and chances are you're gonna die.

Lets look at its stats first 7/24 is a nice ratio no matter how you put it. Yes the EBW and Ykesha have better ratios, but IMO a 3 increase in speed can't make up for a 50dmg average proc (60 max), and well the Ykesha is about ten levels harder to get. Root is a heck of a taunt, and helps save your casters lives.

The main complaint with the blade is that it breaks snares and allows runners which cause trains, and I'll agree if you just sit there and press four keys in combat (kick and taunt every now and again) It'll do just that. However, I'll assume we've got a good group here and by good I mean players not stats:

1. Most casters will Nuke a runner dead before it takes 3 steps.

2. Once a snared monster turns to run, its dead. If you have an ebony you stop attacking it let the others finish it and you turn to the next one in line.

3. Ensnare overrides Ensnaring Roots every time.

4. Cancel Magic overrides Ensnaring Roots every time.

Ebony Blades are very good weapons, but get into a fight with about 6 Dar Ghoul Knights, try taunting them all off your casters and clerics that are nailing them for 585+, and then have one of the bastards run, after your ebonies go off 15 times per fight, 160dex does that, you have to hope you can kill the runner before he goes and grabs more friends. And yes, I have been in this situation and have killed all the mobs there with my group, the point is, if it didn't cancel snare, you wouldn't even have to worry about runners. I look at it this way, I have been snaring every single mob that I have fought for 39levels, and counting on the fact that snare would do its job, with ebonies, that tactic goes out the window, so my class specific weapon has made what I have been doing for 39 levels a little more challenging. Ex Axe doesn't do that, painbringer and fleshripper don't do that, they actually taunt all the mobs onto the tank. Ebony Blades are nice if you are in the middle of the Karanas, fight all the incredibly green mobs there, and aren't worried about runners, cuz all the things in the zone at one time couldn't kill you or catch you, lol
