I wouldn't worry about a 70 Str.

I had a weakling WoodElf Bard... think 70 also.... and at one point, while wearing full bronze... I was fully maxed weight jsut with my stuff.

So started my quests for STR and weight reducing items....

There are a lot of things you can do....

My FIRST mistake was I was carrying around 8 backpacks. These weigh 3lbs each. Large sacks only weigh 1lb and you only lose 2 slots. Right now I carry around 2 large backpacks and 3 large sacks. Leaving 3 inventory slots open to my hotkeys swapping. Even then, I rarely fill up my inventory with items before I sell anyway.

Jagged Bands are GOOD items... +3 Str AND 5ac

For a while, I had Spiked Shoulder Pads +5Str but -10 to magic... I eventually replaced this with Bloodstained Shoulders +3 str.

I also FINALLY got a Blackened Iron Crown +5 Str.

I don't have any troubles with STR anymore. I am focusing on increasing my Dex and Agi now... and start collecting CHA stuff... that is important stuff to have when you decide to go on Charming Sprees

Lose the ringmail! Ringmail and Bronze are among the crappiest, heaviest armor in the game.

Buy banded. Banded has an incredible AC-to-weight ratio, and is an extremely good value when you consider its stats vs its price.

Banded bracer is AC 6 WT 2, just as an example. Dunno the stats on ringmail, but I know it's heavier than it's worth!

So rather than sacrificing AC to get STR-boost bracelets (so you'll have enough str to carry those other cruddy ringmail armor pieces), just get lighter armor that has similar AC instead, and your str problem is solved.

And it's cheap, too (banded is player-made). Depends on the server, but expect to pay roughly 1pp per AC (varies by piece).

Bards should try to travel light, and my preferred stats to focus on improving are DEX (weapons hit and proc more often), and CHA (you'll know why when you hit 27, and in the meantime it'll save you lots of money)

Not necessarily in that order, either.

High AC is a good thing too, but always always always look at the WEIGHT of each piece, not just the AC!
