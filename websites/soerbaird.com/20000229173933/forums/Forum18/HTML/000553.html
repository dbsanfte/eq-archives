Hello,

Well lets start buy looking at all the facts. Thanks to all the shaman that have been posting their kills and what they experienced.

1. Players have been in the cave when the bear spawned, so there is no anti camping.

2. Every six to eight hours the two Ice Bone Skeletons spawn. I have only heard of one report of an IBS at four hours, but that might have been the second IBS.

3. The IBSs don't always spawn together. The might be thirty minutes or an hour apart.

4. There has been two glacier bears in the cave at the same time.

5 The bear has been killed and the same player returned an hour later (after completing the helm quest) to find another glacier bear.

6. Up to three bears have been killed in less than two days.

7. One IBS was killed and thirty minutes to an hour later the bear spawned. But there was no second IBS.

Just looking at the facts alone. The IBSs are the placeholders for the Glacier Bear. They are not the trigger just the placeholder, like the Goblin Wizard is for the Goblin Chieftain and the Orc Legionnaire is for the Orc Chieftain. Looking at the facts they clearly show that the spawn is every six to eight hours and is onehundred percent random if the Glacier Bear spawns or the IBS spawns.

It is clear by the range in which the IBSs spawn that Verant wanted the Glacier Bear to be a lucky find when found. But using the information you can narrow your search and waste less time. The tough part is finding the wondering IBS, after that you can some what time it.

So there ya have it, the spawn cycle for the glacier bear. The only question still in my mind is the number of hours. Is it six to eight or four to eight hour spawn. I am not onehundred percent sure on that, but I am on the rest.

Good Luck,
