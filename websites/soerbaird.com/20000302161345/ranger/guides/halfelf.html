The Boy Scout or Girl Guide Handbook (aka newbie ranger guide) for the Half-Elves

So you want to be a ranger. Not a druid, druids don't get to use bladed weapons. They may get all the cool spells and power magic first, but -you- are special. You've got that x-factor called Tracking. This is surefall Glade, your home away from home. You'll want to learn Sense Direction, Tracking, One Handed Slash, and Taunt first. Keep that extra point for later use...

So you start out with your homemade short sword. Sure, that'll last, at least until you cut down a decaying skeleton and retrieve something better, say a rusty longsword. A note? Go find Hager Sureshot. If you can't find him maybe you're not cut out to be a ranger. Hint: Use tracking. You'll get a nice piece of armor which should last you a few levels.

Go outside, to Qeynos Hills, and you can track down rats and snakes and bats. NO. That's boring. Use your skills to find decaying skeletons and gnoll pups, when they are cut down by your awesome sharp piece of metal, the rangers like you better. Plus sometimes they have weapons, armor, or *bags* on them. And more loot than say, a rat's tooth.

Warning: I know you kids like to track down lots of skeletons but don't go overboard and go too near the ruins. You'll never know when suddenly your fighting -one- decaying skeleton and his buddy Dread Corpse taps on your shoulder and before you know it, you wake up naked next to the campfire outside Surefall Glade. You don't want to experience that until you've had a few drinks.

Which brings us to Qeynos. Say you don't like the competition near Surefall Glade. Just run (I mean run) down the road to Qeynos dont stop unless you want Gnasher to disembowel you and wipe your ranger tunic on his arse. Quickly run down and zone out, and you are in the Qeynos area. It's very crowded I know, but at least there are more creatures about faster. And your tracking skill should let you keep the prime cut of the skeletons there.

Bone chips are best not sold to shadowknights. Those kids are sick psychopaths who'd harm touch your spleen out if you weren't protected by the Divine Magic. Sell them to the vendors instead.

Fishing: When you have some coin, say 3 gold pieces, go back to our Surefall Glade and buy a rod and bait, and ask your guildmaster for a few pointers first, then go to the lake at Qeynos Hills and fish. Alternately you could fish in the surefall glade pond, but you might fall in and piranhas might rip your flesh off in seconds. Fishing in Qeynos Hills requires you to keep an eye out for enemies who would creep up on you and cackle loudly as they frighten the fish off.

Quests: I think in the Surefall Inn there's someone who needs a young scout to collect a gift from South Qeynos. Hey, it's a chance to go sightseeing.

Well that's all for now kids and remember don't touch the mangy rat you don't know where its been.

Quynn, Erollisi Marr, HalfElven Ranger
