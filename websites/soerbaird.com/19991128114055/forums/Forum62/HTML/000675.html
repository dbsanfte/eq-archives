I created my beloved Trueshot on my 16th attempt. Started at a fletching skill of 70, failed 6 times. Raised skill to 92, failed 8 times more, but raised my fletching to 95 during the attempts. Went out and got 2 more sets of microservos, went back, and did my last two attempts, finally succeeding on my last one.

It CAN be done with less than master fletching ability, but it's not easy. On the other hand, I've heard people with fletching as high as the mid 180's who have yet to succeed after dozens of tries. The Random Number Generator is a cruel master at times.

And yes, the int and wisdom stuff is good. Wisdom increases your chance of success, Intelligence increases your chance to raise your skill level.

Don't get discouraged, just get out there and keep trying.

And remember, them microservos spawn on the little Runaway Clockworks in the gnome newbie area as well. Wipe out all the runaways, then run over and wipe out the Rogues, then run back to the re-spawned runaways, rinse, repeat.

OK. After weeks and weeks of reading about Trueshot failures, I feel it's my duty to give some of you some hope. When I was ready to create my Trueshot, I got enough parts for one attempt. Guildmembers convinced me to be prepared for failure, so I spent an extra day acquiring parts for ten attempts. Here's the results: First attempt at fletching skill of 120 with NO WIS or INT enhancing items....success. Seeing it was LORE, I turned in most of the rest of the components to Maeslin for the XP (was only about 12th level at the time I think. ALL spare gold and plat had gone into Fletching). I still had some leftover parts that I put in the bank.

Last night, I hooked up with a guild brother who is a ranger in his 28th season. He had recently lost all of his equipment and is in the process of replacing it. I noticed that he didn't have a bow, so off to Kelethin we went. Enroute I got a tell from someone I had met the night before and had aided (somewhat) in acquiring Meldrath's Skull for another ranger. He was offering his help, so we detoured to Steamfont. A HUGE thanks to Kinin for the help in getting both of us Mel's Skull!!! While waiting on spawns, I gathered as many micro servos as I could, which amounted to ONE. There was nothing but Ebony Drakes around. Millions of them. After we got the skulls, we set out to hunt Thistle with the help of another 28th level druid from the guild.

Next, it was on to Kelethin with my fletching now up to 133 and still no WIS or INT enhancing items. My guild brother held my Trueshot while I chatted with Maeslin for a while. Here is what happenned: first attempt....failed. Second attempt....failed. Third attempt....failed. Fourth attempt....failed. Fifth and final attempt as I was now out of micro servos (GOOD thing I picked up that ONE while hunting Mel.)....success and a fletching increase to 134.

Moral of the story....just keep trying. Help out strangers with their quests, even if it only amounts to distracting Mel's pet, being a damage shield for them and running to recover your corpse, and it will come back to you, whether by their returning the favor, or your being unusually lucky creating Trueshots. The latter doesn't make sense, but it works.

Currently questing for a suitable surname and the plat required to purchase two Rubies....
