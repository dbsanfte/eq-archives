Just a little note. Awhile back Verant instituted a patch relating to SoW and Selo's. After that patch Selo's now does nothing for a SoW'd individual. They get no speed increase but their SoW is not cancelled. Another thing that was changed was that SoW can now be dropped by the person it is cast on...regardless of whether they cast it or not.
Knowing these two things I get a SoW from a shaman or druid I'm grouped with just for convenience but if I find I'm slowing down (due to lack of health) I'll drop the SoW in a heartbeat and start pounding on my drum, screaming Selo's at the top of my lungs for a safer get away.
