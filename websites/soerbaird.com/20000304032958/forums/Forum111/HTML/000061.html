Fellowship Veteran 		posted 11-09-1999 16:53         
Guide to Runny from a 34 ranger who loves going back now when it's empty to get blackened for guild=) Incidentally, at 34 you get the immense pleasure (if the dungeon is empty which it often is) of being able to camp everything at once hehe. I've sat by the Knight room and pulled Warlord and King when they spawn.

Anyway, Knight drops BIBS, and he pretty much always drops it. He's a cpl hour spawn time I think. He's in a room with some Dazed Gobos which believe it or not sometimes still aggro though quite green.

Warlord tends to drop random blackened, BI Crown, BI Medallion. Hit two elites sometimes drop blackened pieces.

King tends to be generous and has had as much at once as a Grotesque Mask, a random blackened piece, and a fine steel sword. He drops any of these items, also drops the girdle (+5 stamina as I recall), and BI Crown. Lately, a Warlord has been spawning here in the King room too. Don't know why. He didn't wander there, I watched him spawn and the other warlord was ALSO on track. King is surrounded by a bunch of elites, some of them casters with damage shields (cancel magic on them).

Borxx drops the lens for observer staff. He spawns IN the King Room (spawned on top of a friend of mine twice in an hour). His placeholder seems to be the wandering worker who runs through the room now and then.

Hope that helps. Incidentally, a friend got a gossamer cap of all things off one of the elites last visit.....very very odd.

A quick guide on loot from the highest level mobs that spawn in Runny

Borxx: (random spawn in the King Room level 33+)
Evil Eye Lens and usual eye stuff like evil eye stalks and optic nerves

Evil Eyes: (certain rooms and are level 30+)
They drop fractured runes for crafted quest and evil eye stalks and optic nerves

Dazed Goblin Shamans various locations level 24 to 26)
They drop bone totems, black iron totem

The Goblin King every 20 minutes or so in the King room level 24 to 26)
He drops Black Iron Crown, Black Iron Girdle, Grotesque Mask, Black Iron Medallion, Fine Steel Weapons, Black Iron Armor

Goblin Warlord every 20 minutes or so level 22 to 24)
He drops Black Iron Crown (very Rare), Black Iron Medallion, Black Iron Armor

Dazed Goblin Knight: (very random spawn time from 30 minutes to 5 hours so far for me level 24 to 26)
He drops Bronze Weapons, Black Iron Bastard Sword (always dropped)

Elite Goblin Guards: (in the King room and Warlord Room level 20 to 22)
They drop Black Iron Armor

Missing from this list is:
Slime Elemental (never killed it or conned it)
Sludge (named Gobbo never killed it or conned it)
