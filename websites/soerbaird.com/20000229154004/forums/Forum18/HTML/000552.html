Yes, he's around lvl 40 and very rare. Spawns near the entrance to Unrest.. and can sometimes be found on the island facing away from the Unrest zone.

He has the reed belt, the quest item for the Cleric BP, and the single best +wis item in the game: Kedge Totem. +18 wis, -9 str, non-lore, and can be held in either melee slot.

He's always camped too.. so if you plan on getting him you better bring a group of wizards with you.
