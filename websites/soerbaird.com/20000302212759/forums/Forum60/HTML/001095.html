The "hell levels" are 30, 35, 40 and 45. They are called such because of the seemingly inordinate amount of experience you need to advance to the next level. Getting to the next level is very, very slow. Conversely, when you die, you don't lose as much experience as you do normally. You will also find that the levels after the hell levels are different than normal, as well. It is very easy to gain experience, though you lose a bubble should you die...
