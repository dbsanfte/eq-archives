Fellowship Associate 		posted 10-14-1999 06:19         
"Invisible" and "Invisible to undead" are separate spells for most casters.

Bards don't get an "Invisible to undead" song, so skels and ghouls and all that other stuff can still see us, without having any special powers other than their unfortunate status of being not-alive.

The blinky lights aren't visible to mobs. In fact, I've found my own invisibility song to be FAR more reliable than one casted on me by a caster. (The blinky lights are a dead giveaway to other *players* though, obviously)
