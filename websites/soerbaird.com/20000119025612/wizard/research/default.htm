Finding the Practice Research combination guides

Instead of wasting valuable and hard-to-find runes getting your Research skill up to par, you can now use practice recipes to improve your research skill.

To find out what combinations of items will advance your Research skill, do one of the following:

  • If you are a good wizard, go to Mrysil the Gypsy in the Gypsy camp of North Karana. Hail her and then give either a Lightstone or a Greater Lightstone. Giving her a Lightstone will give you one of the two combination guides for Practice Research, Runes and Research Book 1 or Book 2. Giving her a Greater Lightstone will cause her to give you the Concordance of Research, which is the place you use to combine the Practice Research components in.
  • If you are an evil wizard, go to Smith Tv'ysa as the merchant camp in South Ro. Give her a Lightstone for the Runes and Research books 1 and 2, and a Greater Lightstone for the Concordance of Research.
