The entrance to paw is -3000 850 I believe (will check on that when I get a chance) At any rate if you straight off the bridge from NK you will hit paw. It's the spot with the gnolls standing around and spires surrounding it.

Paw is an awesome dungeon from 8-16. Good loot for that level and great xp. The entire dungeon can be soloed at 22 except Zixx Nenix. Incidentally, Zixx is a triggered spawn when you kill Kurrpok Splitpaw in the very bottom of the zone. If you don't kill Kurrpok, you have nothing to worry about. If you would like any more info on this zone let me know. I know it inside and out. In fact, I plan to go back soon and pay a visit to Zixx.

Any self-respecting ranger beyond the 15th season can get a bind in any city in Norath, good or evil. Camo can be cast in city zones. Every city zone I've seen has at least one area just inside the zone where the guards won't see you if you pop. First things firs, mem camo, cast, and zone out. Find a nice quiet place to mem the rest of your spells. For the relatively short time I was hunting Upper Guk, I was bound just inside Grobb. Heh, don't MOVE without casting camo on yourself, though. There's a guard JUST around the corner.
