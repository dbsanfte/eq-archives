There were 2 versions of the EBW quest around for a while. The Grimrot one (requiring the Lute of the gypsy princess and / or bones) is no longer in the game.

A word of advice about the Electrum-Bladed Wakizashi: Don't go without a caster friend. The butler and maid are relatively weak for their level, which, I think, is about 30 or so, maybe up to 35. Problem is, with all the bad pathing in Mistmoore and such, and the fact that the place is usually untouched, killing them can be hard. Here's my story:

I was talkin' to the caitiff, camping for the gloves and a shiny crested helm. (That is, talking in the 4 seconds before "You let loose a great bellow", yada yada yada) So I grabbed my level 50 druid friend, and we went a-searchin' in the castle. I sang the invis song, since it's indefinite, and we didn't know if we'd have a chance to re-cast inside the castle. We wandered around for a while, searching the castle top to bottom, learning the layout. If you're looking for the maid or butler, stick to the kitchen area. They both wander in there at one point or another. Anyways, we finally see the maid come in. We begin to fight, and it's essentially a 3-spell fight. Unfortunately, the maid was one hell of a runner, and I had a small targeting problem, and ended up hitting a blue glarer that didn't attack me for some reason with Selo's Consonant Chain. In the meantime, the druid attacked the maid, and grabbed the fang.

Now the wonders of Castle Mistmoore come into play. Apparently that blue that I hit was on the other side of the castle... bad pathing and server/client updates showed it in the same room as me and let me target it. We get hit by half the frickin castle full of deathly scryers, vampires, and all sorts of unpleasant folks. Evac time.

Anyways, after gating to Butcherblock, we take a moment and med to full again. We head back to the castle and repeat, this time waiting for Mr. Butler. He doesn't drop the fang the first time we kill him. Or the second. Or the third. Or the fourth. Finally, on the seventh killing of that guy, he drops the fang. (Might I add that due to buggy pathing, screwed up AI, and entirely weak world-building that we had to evac three times, and even got stuck in a wall once.)

And in the end, we got the final fang, and the druid handed them to the caitiff. Result, a 7/21 1H slashing electrum bladed wakizashi. The caveat: NO DROP!!!

Anyways, there's my story about trying to get one from Mistmoore. It really is quite hard, and I wouldn't recommend anyone try until they're at least 40+, and by that time there are better weapons to be had.
