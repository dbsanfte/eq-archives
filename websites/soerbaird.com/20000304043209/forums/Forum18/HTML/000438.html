WARNING -- FULL SPOILER AHEAD!

FYI -- This is biased from a non-Evil character's point of view

Coupla things:

First, you don't have to kill everything in Befallen to get Gynuk to spawn. You just need to kill his placeholder, which can be almost anything, including a rat, an ice boned skeleton, and regular skells. You'll know it's his placeholder because when it spawns, it will shout the same thing that Gynuk shouts, only it will say 'a giant rat shouts "Blah blah blah suffer as I have blah".

Gynuk spawn right at the zone entrance (inside) Befallen. If you zone in and he is not there, check the first locked door, or the doorway after that -- oftentimes there is a rat stuck in the doorway. This is the placeholder. If that is not the case, kill any ice-boned skeleton you see wandering, then return to the entrance. Wait for 18 minutes (his actual spawn time), and see if anything spawns and shouts. Then kill it, rinse and repeat. Gynuk is a rare spawn, but the platinum ring is a common drop off him. It is rumored that he also drops the other rings rarely.

You don't need to kill Vin Moltor to get the paw container. Go to the bakers shop in S. Qeynos and purchase 3 bottles of Kalish. Now, see if you can get a baker to bake a Rat Ear Pie for you, or bake one yourself. There are two recipes, but the easiest one (because it does not require a pie tin), is to combine a Giant Rat Ear, Cup of Flour, and Baking Spirits in a mixing bowl. This will yield an unbaked pie. Now, bake it and Voila! A Rat Ear Pie. Give the three bottles of Kalish (unstacked) and the pie to Vin Moltor in the sewers all at once, he will always give you the container (make sure you have a free inventory slot).

To find Vin Moltor, zone into the sewers by the Monk guild in N. Qeynos. When you emerge at the first section where you are able to get out of the water, you will usually find Vin. He runs around that area, and is very easy to find. Hail him to make him stop moving.

The Silver Ring is almost always in the pool in BB. I also think it is a pretty quick spawn, because the last time I went there, it wasn't there, but a minute later it spawned.

Preistess Mitty almost always drops the copper ring, and very rarely drops the gold. I've heard only once per real day, although a friend of mine camped her for a few hours and she dropped three gold rings (go figure). Don't camp her solo until level 23 -- she is only around 16th level or so, but if you are 22 or below, her preistesses will enter the fight along with here, and you will be swamped by a bunch of greenies. With SoW you can probably make it out alive (my cleric was not so lucky ). At level 23, you will want to pull her out of the temple to fight her, because while the other preistesses will not fight you, they will heal her, which makes it much more difficult. Also, she usually has SoW up, so make sure she is rooted.

Good luck!
