What items should a Shaman be looking for?

At your level you could easily obtain:

  • Golden Jasper earrings (Jewelcraft item, +3 WIS, 25pp each)
  • Black Iron Medallion (higher level goblins in Runnyeye, +3 WIS +3 INT, 0 to 15pp)
  • Savant's Cap (reclusive ghoul magi in Unrest, +3 INT +3 WIS, 0 to 20pp)
  • Polished Bone Bracers (evil eyes in Beholder Maze, +3 INT +3 WIS 2 AC, 50 to 75pp) ... OR
  • Golden Jade bracelets (Jewelcraft item, 4 AC, +15 mana +15 hp, 28pp)
  • Silver Jasper rings (Jewelcraft item, +2 WIS, 3pp)

    Don't neglect the +MANA items because, at your level, as they are often as good as or better than the +WIS items.

    Tudamorf any suggestions for what I can get at my level since we're on the subject

    Well off the top of my head, for level 30....

  • The Savant's Cap and Polished Bone Bracers you can get solo at level 30 pretty easily. The Savant's Cap is a short camp, the bracers are a long camp.
  • Another +6 WIS ring, obviously (~300pp).
  • Platinum Ruby Veil (+7 STR, +7 WIS) if you have the funds (~1000pp).
  • Mammoth Cloak (7 AC, +4 WIS, +something sv cold). It's from the goblin alchemist in permafrost; soloable at 30 but an extremely long and frustrating camp.
  • Gatorscale Leggings (7AC, +4 WIS, +15 hp). From the ancient crocodile in upper guk. I don't think it's soloable at 30, as the crocodile is in the low 30's, so you might need another. Also a long camp, and usually occupied. On the bright side if you get gatorscale sleeves, you can sell them for 350-500 platinum to casters, and this area is extremely safe.
  • Runed Circlet (4 AC, +4 WIS, +4 INT). From the goblin preacher in Peramfrost. Not sure how easy it would be to solo at 30, you might want another just in case. Not a very long camp either, in one evening you're likely to get at least one.
  • Bloodstained Tunic (13 AC, +4 WIS, +4 AGI). From the undead knight of Unrest in the tower. I doubt you can solo this at level 30, you'll need a small group. The undead knight is a fairly rare spawn on the third floor tower, and he comes with many friends.
  • Platinum Armband (0 AC, +3 WIS, +3 STR). From the reckless efreeti in Solusek A, you might be able to solo it at 30 but a small group would be a better idea.

    With these items you can get around 160 to 170 wisdom.

    So Im to understand that as you move higher in Lvl the more you will need wisdom items than items

    give mana/ac only boots. So should I now start getting itmes that give wisdom only?

    As you increase in level, your wisdom is converted to more and more mana. In the early going, +5 in wisdom would have no noticible impact on your total mana. Only when you reach higher levels does +Wis have an impact.

    Check out one of the many mana calculators to see the effect of +Wis at various character levels. While you are at lower levels the +Wis is wasted when your really need HP and MANA.
