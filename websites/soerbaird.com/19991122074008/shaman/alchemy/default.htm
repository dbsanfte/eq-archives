Component placement...
The success rate for creating potions is much higher if you have the material component on the topmost left slot of the medicine bag, and the herb on the right. I have tested this (at much expense) and never seem to succeed if I reverse the placement of the components. - Rumbul Wulfkin, Troll Shaman of Tunare
What to make first?
Start making Somber Origins as it's the easiest. Then, try Charming Deceit. - Alchemy Board
