I hear on many differnt posts the game will be much more enjoyalbe and a blast at XXX level. That once you get to Level XXX then a Shaman will come into their own.

I guess what I am saying is stop and smell the roses enjoy the game. It will not get better through gaining levels, it only will get better by doing fun things.

Vision on yourself does what abbatoir said, you change perspective to yourself and get something that at least resembles Ultravision.

A little thing to do while you wait for the glacial bear: I discovered the other day that Vision has tremendous range and can be cast through walls. If you sit in the everfrost tunnels, casting Sense Animal to find that bear it will indicate some animal outside (unless you're insanely lucky and the bear has spawned...). Now, sit down, mem Vision, cast Vision on the animal outside. If you're lucky you can see first person perspective of the receiving end of a mammo hunt. Or maybe a live cam on Iceberg's head...
While Vision is active, you can mem spells, meditate, cast general info spells (such as Sense Animal) and buff and cast on yourself (to work with regen and cannibalize for example), use F1 to target yourself. You can also chain-cast Vision on mobs or characters you see through your vision host's eyes.

It may not sound like a blockbuster movie, but it sure as youknowwhat beats staring at the cavern walls

btw, 19-24... yeah those are tough times. I almost quit at that time because I was simply not able to get anywhere. It was frustration only and no fun, but in the end it made 24 so much sweeter. It was a rough and bumpy ride to get to 24, a ride one does not really enjoy until it's over.
