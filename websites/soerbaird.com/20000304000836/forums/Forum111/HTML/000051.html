To anyone who doesn't already know. The Undead army has returned to Kithcor forest. These Undead monsters range from lvl 35 to 39 at least. They spawn at the game time of 8pm to 6/7am. When daylight comes if not all of them have been killed they remain spawned and will attack any traveller. The tainted weapons are no longer dropping with great rate, only about 1 in a 100. Most of the time if they have a weapon it is a Fine Steel Weapon. They have become permenantly activated in the forest. If you decide to attack these monsters, due to the fact they cast fear, suggest you have a puller pull to the zone edge. Also keep an eye for the zone border because you may need it if a train comes. If your pulling, keep clear of other groups because they will stop and attack other groups and completely ignore the puller at times.

To all rangers, if you are going to the Ranger camp in Kithcor keep your eyes open and becareful. I fought these things for 4 hours or more last night. I died once and i even encountered a Enraged dread wolf that was yellow to me at Lvl 38! There are things like revenging knight, undead cleric, skeleton corporal, dread wolf, enraged dread wolf, wandering warrior, rotting Knight,etc. I don't know all the names but that's to name a few.

So if anyone was wondering why the population of kithcor forest has increased from being an almost dead zone to close to 100 people. You now know why. Most of the players are lvl 30+ or higher who are fighting these monsters. I had people last night on ooc & shout wanting to know what was going on? Some of them were rangers so i thought i better put a warning out. Good Luck to you all.
