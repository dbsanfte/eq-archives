Welcome to the Musical world that belongs to the bard. Do not neglect the extra power our instruments add to our songs as well.

The hardest part is giving up the security the cold hard steal falsely gives us. But put your weapon in your pack and weild your Lute/Mandolin with confidence.

Test Chords of Dissonance with and without your Lute/Mandolin (It is a string based song)... let us know if you find any difference.... plus if you start weaving Hymn of Restoration in with Chant of Battle (The Hymn uses stringed instruments as well)
you may even find that you will be finishing battles with full health or very little lost in the end.

Do not throw away your weapons entirely, you will still find uses for them, but if your tests go as well as mine did, you will start to feel naked without an instrument in your hands.

If only someone had taught me this when I was your age

Being a studious bard I found this comment to be a bit out of sorts, what bard wouldn't twist songs? I think there are a couple of topics of note that are brought about by the last post...

- Some bards just havn't just havn't realized that power and ability of being able to Twist songs.

- Your computer makes a big difference.

I have 2 computers networked together. If I am playing on my Server, I can effectively twist 4 songs in battle. I usually only do 3 but I can do 4 with the loss of one effect for a breif second.

Now, if I have a friend over and we are playing together, I let her use my server and I use my secondary computer which isn't quite as good as my server. The refresh rate on my graphics on my second computer is NO WHERE close to my first computer and twisting 3 songs is a chore.... so I usually just twist 2 or just leave one on unless I have to switch songs for a reason......

So if one bard you adventure with isn't quite the caliber of another bard you have adventured with. Don't necessarily blame the individual to readily, it just may be the hardware that is preventing them from being the best they can be.
