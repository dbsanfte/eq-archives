The best way to test a songs spell effects is with a friend. Your songs change in strength as you advance through the levels.

And all songs that are instrument based are DRAMATICALLY more effective when played with that particular instrument vs. singing.

What I would suggest is ask one of your friends to allow you to initiate a duel with them and then go to their persona screen and tell you the effects as you play difference songs on them.

Make sure you do this in an out of the way area as to not make any nearby NPCs annoyed and be sure not to kill your friend on accident
