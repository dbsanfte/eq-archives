Just a bit of strategy for fellow rangers (and other classes in general) when entering the Minotaur Caves.

First of all, don't enter solo unless you are least level 12. The weakest minotaur (the slavers) will be very hard to defeat and you'll be lucky to leave the cave without facing another one. Their levels are such that even at level 15, I conned some minotaurs blue. Also, very important to make sure that the minotaur just inside the cave is not the minotaur hero. He's the kind of minotaur that will rip your arm off, then proceed to beat you with it at level 30! He travels around the area outside, and I think he ends his little tour of rampage there, just inside the cave entrance.

As you wind around the corner inside the entrance you'll see the cave split into three parts, all very close to each other. Two minotaurs make a patrol run from the middle to the left tunnel, so be wary of both those two, and the single spawn points just inside each of the three tunnels. You can quickly find yourself being attacked by 3 minos in no time.

This area is a party's favorite ambush spot. It's marked by an abandoned cart. Sending in one fast member of the party down into any of the tunnels to get the minotaurs' attention and running back to the cart is a good way of gaining exp, coins and loot. Especially for the famed minotaur axe, which btw, is one about 1 in every 10 minotaurs. It's very random as to which mino has it. I've gotten one from the very first mino inside the cave, and I've gotten one from fairly well in the cave.

Lastly, be careful about going too deep in the cave. Taking the right tunnel will lead you quickly to the middle tunnel if you follow its left wall. If you continue going deeper in the cave from the right and middle tunnels, you are ok, till you make a U-turn, then things get nasty. It's pretty much the same going just a short distance in the left tunnel.

There is a very unpleasant NPC around this area who's a spellcaster (Ed note: Meldrath the Malignant) and he's tough to even see much less attack. They part that is difficult is that the walls are very thin. The minotaurs can hit you through the walls if you are standing right next to them. Not very 'realistic' but that's the way it is. On top of this, the NPC is casting spells on you. I've been hit with a disease cloud that eats at my HP, lost 50+ hit points at a time with another spell, and sent on a rampage after other PCs with yet another spell (charm.) All this at my then level of 15. I think this nasty fellow is also guarded by two minotaur guards (Ed note: They still con red to me at level 19) and they are much bigger than the other minos I've seen, but I've only glanced at them, and not much beyond that.

Hope to someday reach a level to handle the big boys there, but I think if you play cautiously, there's good exp and loot to be found for rangers with levels 12-15.

Good luck and Good hunting,
Sy, The Vicious
