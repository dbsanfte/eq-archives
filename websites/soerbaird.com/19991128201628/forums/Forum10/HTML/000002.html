Rather than trying to increase strength (which is good to do, IF you want to hit harder in melee), I much prefer the strategy of reducing weight instead.

You were wearing BRONZE? Yuck! I recommend you lose the bronze. Banded has a *much* better AC to weight ratio, and is cheaper too! I don't remember a lot of stats, but I recall that for the bracer, banded is 6AC and only 2WT. A bronze bracer buys you one tiny additional AC point... at a cost of FIVE MORE POUNDS! *NOT* worth it, if you ask me. I'd stick with banded until you can get (or afford) some nice lightweight magical bits.

Also, here's a little tidbit you might find interesting:
- A large bag has 6 slots and weighs 1.
- A backpack has 8 slots and weighs 3.

Those 2 extra slots cost you 2 pounds! Not good. Replace 3 backpacks with 4 large bags, and you save 5 pounds. And best of all, just like banded vs bronze, large bags are cheaper too.
