They are gone on Bertox. Ran through there several times in the last 2 days, at dead of night and in daytime, no undead. Not only that, but there was no fog in the zone. Clear as a bell. I love the undead in Kithicor. Not only was it another exciting place to adventure for the 30 plus crowd, the zone was exciting, spooky, and had alot of atmosphere to it. Travel was not as bad as everyone complained, if you were cautious. I sincerely hope the undead return at some point. I was hunting in there several times a week both solo and in groups. It pulled 30 or so people out of Lower and Sol B each night, which was also good. As stated above, Kithicor infested with Undead was spooky, interesting, and definitely exciting. Got your heart going. It kept you on your toes wondering whether you would get feared while pulling and sent running through the gloom, or jumped by an Enraged Dire Wolf (those things were scary) while already fighting, or just running into a nasty while trying to traverse the zone. The zone and it's occupants certainly kept your attention. There were also some nice items to try for in here. Like I said, I hope the fiends return soon. I owe 'em a few bashings, thats for sure.
