Hey, I have just recently started this quest. I started off in Befallen to camp Glynok Moltar, and after about 3 hours of killing his placeholder, he finally spawned and I got the gold ring. He is supposed to have the Platinum ring most of the time, so it looks like i'll be camping in Befallen awhile longer.

Some tips:

I'm lvl 30, and Glynok conned high green to me. Gave me quite a beating. I got down to 2 bubbles of health and no mana, although the only buffs I had on were quickness and regen. If you're lower than lvl 30, you should try and keep some buffs on.

Glynok spawns RIGHT at the zone into Befallen, then walks down to the 3rd floor. I suggest meditating by the zone, and when something attacks you (assuming it wasn't trained on you by a newbie) that will be his place-holder, which might be a large rat, iced bone skeleton, greater skeleton, ghoul or a variety of other things. Sometimes the placeholder will say "Argh! you disturbed my rest!" but not all the time.

He or a placeholder spawns ever 16 minutes, so I suggest hitting /time after you kill him, then explore the dungeon until it's time to go back. I bound myself right where he spawns at the zone so I could gate back when it's time. I got myself barbed arms & leggings, a thex mallet, and a BBC waiting for him to spawn.

Oh, and if neither he or his placeholder is spawning, it probably means the placeholder got stuck somewhere in the dungeon. Make the trip down to the 3rd floor yourself, and kill anything that looks stuck. (usually plague rat or giant rat on the 2nd level)
