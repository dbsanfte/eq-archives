Why would you want to throw money away buying food when you could be getting it for free? You should forage every time the button goes white. Of course the skill goes up when you use it -- why would it work any different from any other skill? Just make sure you put at least one point of training into it.

Once you start getting a forage skill on the 60-80 range you'll start piling up food in your inventory because you can't eat it fast enough. You can drop it, give it away, or sell it to people for brewing/cooking.

One warning: you can forage many different types of food, all with fairly equal chances of appearing, and only one of those types is water. Which means that you will end up with a lot more food than water. So, at the lower levels even if you're starting to get enough food not to buy it anymore, you should still be buying some water for safety.
