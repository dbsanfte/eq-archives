Let's see if I can remember where the songs are:

Level 8, Kelin's Lugubrious Lament: This one's in Kelethin, I do believe. Near the back of the guild hall.

Level 9, Elemental Rhythms: Might be in Kelethin but I know for certain that it's in Qeynos.

Level 10, Anthem de Arms: Any of the cities with a Bard's Guild. (Kelethin, Qeynos, Freeport)

Level 11, Cinda's Charismatic Carillon: Kelethin.

Level 12, Brusco's Boastful Bellow: Any city with a bard's guild.

As for the full bronze and not encumbered, it's rather good, but I'd not be wearing full bronze. If you're that close to being encumbered, as it seems to be implied in your post, I'd look to replace items with other things. (Like a Thick Leather Apron at the waist, +strength earrings and rings, maybe, grotesque or snakeskin mask, a few weight-reducing pouches... you see what I mean.) Anyways, hope this helps.
