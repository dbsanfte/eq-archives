Modoc of Halas
Fellowship Veteran 		posted 11-30-1999 18:38         
Modoc has also performed the gauntlet quest, after listening carefully to his fellow Shamans.

Modoc notes that the most common mistake/bug is to use non-medium banded item. Medium banded is required, small and large do not work. Medium is called just plain "banded" in the game.

Order of item delivery seems to be unimportant, though if another player had partially completed the quest (handed in a subset of the items) one can sometimes complete the quest by handing in the remaining items (for example, if one erroneously gave a large gauntlet along with the other three correct quest items, you or someone else could come back a little later with the correct gauntlet and finish the quest with just one item. The NPC does not remember WHO handed in the items, just chronology).

There are some reports of people just plain having the quest fail for no apparant reason. This may be either pilot error, or some very small failure rate. Modoc thinks it may be pilot error, since he knows some shaman that have done various of the quests many many times with no failures once they knew the procedure. If there was a random failure, one would expect them to hit it...

Modoc does not believe faction to be involved, since the NPC look indifferent to most everyone.

Modoc feels sad for his fellow shaman who didn't get the "smart gloves"
