You'll find most of the Bull Elephants in the northern part of South Karana right near the bridge. I found them between the bridge area and as far in as the centaur camp.

If you run along the coast line or in a ways you'll more than likely run into them.

When I was hunting them I also took out most of the elephant calfs and regular elephants I saw, although I can't tell if that helped get the bulls to spawn or not.

If it starts to rain, which it probably will (did a few times when I hunted them) you may want to hire a druid or ranger to track down the bull elephants. It's only dumb luck you'll run into them in the rain on your own, and the rains tend to last a long time there.

The tooth is somewhat common. The first tooth I got I believe I got after I killed my second bull elephant. The second time I went after the tooth I got it on the first bull I took down (that time I had a druid tracking for me in the rain).

There's one person on Bertox who was attempting to get shamans to pay him to loot the bull elephant tooths from the corpses(he wasn't a shaman). Paying for the tooth is totally not worth it. Bull elephants are a relitively low level (can probably kill them solo at level 24) and the tooth seems to be a common drop. Once you get the hang of where to look in South Karana it shouldn't be a problem getting the tooth.
