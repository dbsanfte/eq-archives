Lewick
Fellowship Veteran 		posted 12-09-1999 16:49         
I used to hate guard killers. I detested their loathsome attitude about the game, and I thought it was in bad taste. Then I made level 40. After you try and get in a party in SolB, LGuk, Mistmoore, wherever for 2 or 3 hours and nobody responds to your /ooc to help a party, you start getting frustrated. I now understand why many of the guard killers became guard killers.

There are inherant problems with the game. One is the basic lack of dungeon support for the rapidly growing 40+ population in Everquest. On a typical night on The Rathe, if you do a /who all 40 50, your list is cut short. There are usually 250-300 people level 40+ on the server. There are 2 main dungeons for them: SolB and LGuk. Mistmoore can be a complete nightmare for the newly initiated Mistmoore wannabes. It is also filled with rather nasty caster types, which makes people want to leave poste haste.

So Verant decided Kedge was going to be the next option. Everyone screamed and yelled for itemization of Kedge. They finally did it (after 5 months of player yelling) and then didn't add adequate support for underwater dungeon fighting (first time you get dispelled in Kedge while fighting and have to fumble for your enduring breath button or hotkey while lost 150 HP per pulse, you'll know what I'm saying.) The fishbone earring spawn is every 6 realtime hours in Q Hills, but a melee character doesn't have a snowball's chance in Solusek of getting it. There literally are people with timers set up. 15 minutes prior to the Hadden spawn, they gate to Q Hills to kill him. I've literally seen 9 people gate in at the same time to get him. Verant, being the wise folks they are, decided the Gnome tinkering capability would be great for building an underwater breathing apparatus. Only problem is, to get to the skill in tinkering required to make the rebreather (which weighs a TON) you have to invest between 10000 and 20000 plat into tinkering. Nobody in their right mind would invest that much cash into tinkering unless they're expecting to sell the rebreathers for 2000 or 3000 plat each. And who under level 40 needs one?

Now they're changing Paw. I'm expecting it to be a Cazic-Thuleish level dungeon. They call it mid-level. So I'm figuring 20 to 40 is about right. Guess the 40+ folks have to wait for the expansion to get in uncrowded dungeons. If you've ever been Solusek B when 80+ folks are in the dungeon, you'd know that every spawn is precious. You don't get the continuous experience required to get through levels 40 and 45. Those levels are just obnoxious.

So people resort to killing guards. The spawns are plentiful and fast. There are a lot of different selections they can make, and they can pick a zone. You rarely, if ever, see two guard kiters arguing about spawn stealing, etc.

I have only killed evil guards. I've gone on excursions to Neriak, Ogguk, and Grobb. I typically kill INSIDE the zone.

A better solution to buffing them up would have been to tone them down some. Make them level 25ish, but give them stats like DVinn or Dorn. Make them hit hard, not give experience over level 35, and just be a nuisance. That would pretty much solve 90% of the guard killing issues.
