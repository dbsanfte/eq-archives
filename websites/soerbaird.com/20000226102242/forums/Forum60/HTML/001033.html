OK quick capsule review of what I think was said in the Brad / NotCrazy interview.

The expansion will be available in the shops as a new product, similar to the UO Second Age, which will replace the existing box.

At the same time an upgrade disk will be made available to all existing EQ customers for a reduced rate via mail order ("probably").

As an option the expansion may be available as a download, depending on customer response and available bandwidth (as the man said, 600Mb multiplied by 50000+ users is a LOT of bandwidth.

I'm not sure exactly how many people play EQ but I would bet at least that many now - probably more by March next year, and according to a survey on www.eqhaven.com 88% of respondents who expressed a preference would purchase the expansion in preference to any other type of cat food :-)

Read the article - there's a lot more to the interview than this.
