You only get the warning if you left click and actually try to put the item in the container. If you right click it tries to move it to your inventory automatically. This will cause it to drop if there are no avialable spots in your inventory capable of housing the item (IE. big item in little bags don't work). Once a NO DROP item drops to the ground it disappears. I think Verant should warn about this thing, but it never has. If you're doing something important just take extra precautions. Leave inventory spots open (not in bags) and left click and move it manually instead of letting the game do it for you.
