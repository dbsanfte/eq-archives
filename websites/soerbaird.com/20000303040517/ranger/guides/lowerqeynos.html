As for my suggestion possibly a list of creatures to hunt every 5 levels:

1-4 :
N. Qeynos, Qeynos Hills :

Snakes (Piece of cake)
Large & Giant Rats (large rats are okay, but giant rats are suprisingly challenging)
Gnoll Pups (good for some quick coin, watch that you don't get teamed up on by a few)
Decaying Skeletons (You can get alot of goodies from these, and some coin to boot)

5-9 :
Qeynos Hills, Qeynos Catacombs, W. Karana :

Gnolls (In front of BB, attack, kill, then rest and wait for next)
Frogloks (In the catacombs, they drop Netted Pieces good for a little more cash.)
Skeletons (Regular Skells and Putrids are fun, Dreads are okay but safer with a party)
Giant Spiders (Their biggest threat is their annoying poison)
Giant Beetles (These guys can't even poison you)
Young Lions (Annoying buggers, that like to attack while you sit)

10-14: W.Karana, Erud's Crossing, Split Paw, Black Burrow:
Bandits (It's easy to get overwhelmed by many, be with a party)
Grizzly Bears (If you don't mind killing bears)
Poachers (In cave behind waterfall in Surefall Glade, suprisingly tough)
Patrolling Gnolls (Nice to solo, in Black Burrow)
Gnoll Shaman (with party in Split Paw or Black Burrow)
Elite Gnoll Gaurdsmen (with party in Paw or BB)
Willow-wisps (found on the Island in Erud's Crossing, need magical item to inflict damage on a wisp, however. Great coin and the occasional lightstone as well.)
