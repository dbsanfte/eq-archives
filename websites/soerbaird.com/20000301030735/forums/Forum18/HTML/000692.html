Actually, a barbarian could do the quest if invis works...

a) Kill all of the dwarven npc's in Crushbone -- each of these can provide faction with the Quest npc in Ogguk.

b) Kill DVinn for his heart (easy enough)

c) Go invis into Oggok (not sure if anything along the path will see you) and whack the Dark Elf Ambassador (forget his name).

d) Go invis to the Quest mob in the warrior's guild and give him the items. If your faction is amiable, you'll get a maul.
