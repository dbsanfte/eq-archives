DDD plays continuously, similar to Chords.

Another good place to practice your horn with DDD is the Arena zone in Lake Rathe. Don't go into the actual Arena -- just enter the Arena zone. There is not a single NPC in the zone, so there is no danger of someone training a guard or something to you. Just leave it playing and go afk for a few hours.
