Cush, the spirit is good but the flesh is weak. There is nothing wrong with meleeing. You will contribute to the total damage dealt by your party by doing so, even at your level. Unfortunately you will find that the trend of decreased melee damage relative to that of tanks is going to continue. At higher levels, the amount of damage that you end up doing in melee over a given period of time is less than the amount you would do by casting a spell, and sitting down and medding for the same period of time. It is at this time that you will probably want to replace that metalic club you are holding, and replace it with a gawdy looking paw. Even better, IMO, is to get one of the +wis maces. You can still make a swing if you feel the need, but you also get the big wis boost that is nearly as much as the paw.
