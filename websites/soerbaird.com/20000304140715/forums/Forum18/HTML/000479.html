I just completed this quest.

At level 14 I can't see you doing it. The Dark Elf ambassador in Oggok is difficult to kill at level 28 (took all my mana, and my previous tries at killing him resulted in one death and two times fleeing).

I had amiable faction with the Crakneks (I had been doing their quests since I was a little oggie) so when I did turn in the hearts I got the maul right away.

I'm not sure what level D'Vinn is. He was green to me at level 28 so killed him right away. Being an Ogre I did have a little difficulty getting into the tower where he is but with levitate it can be done (still cramped).

Alone I can't see doing this quest till level 28 at the earliest.

I would work on doing the Craknek quests right now. At level 14 you can still get some experience from the lizard men (most of their quests involve them).

You'll most likely not want to ruin your faction standing with the Dark Elf's at the point either. I'm sure mine has gone to hell after completing this quest.

Truthfully, at level 28, the maul sucks. I miss quite more than I hit and when I do hit it will only be for about 10-15 points more than what I would get if I used my Midnight Mallet (think the max I hit with 155 strength was 46 damage). In a warriors hand this weapon is most likely very good but in a shamans hands it's not too good (might be better at level 29 with Quickness).

On the bright side I should be able to sell/trade this item for some good stuff.
