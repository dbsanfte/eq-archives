Ok...

I'm going to try and clear up a few things about posting on the Glade boards

First, Azgorne and I are not soup nazis *grin* When I first created the Glade, I envisioned it to be a place for people to come and share ideas, stories and the like pertainent to Rangers. It has been *wildly* successful.

That said, it has also become a VERY popular place for many classes of Norrath. The traffic (on the order of 5,000 to 10,000 page views a day) is really starting to increase. So, in order to keep things flowing in a good manner, we just need to try and stick to some basic guidelines (not rules)

Let me first tell you what each board is meant for.

General: This is the place to go to talk about your Ranger in General. A place to find out what other Rangers are up to, recent news, patch info, rumors and things that don't really fit anywhere else.

Spells: Used to discuss our Ranger spells. Where to purchase, mana used, best situations, etc.

Quests and Special items: Did you find a new quest? A Doo hicky from that mob you killed? Wonder what that newfangled bow will do? This is where you go.

Skills: This is where you go to discuss skills such as Riposte, Foraging and then on to things like fletching, bow making, drinking, brewing.

Combats and Tactics: Wanna know the best way to take an aviak, or how you can tear that necro up in a duel? Here ya go.

Race Discussion: A place to talk about the various differences with our races.

Wish List: Things you'd like to see happen with our class, our skills, our game even.

Buy/Sell/Trade: Self explanatory here folks.

Zone Information: This is used for discussing zone characteristics (like the absence of light in Kithicor or the undead invasion) MOB's in that zone, etc... a good place to talk about what to do in that area.

Tales O' the Ranger: A fun tavern-like place where we keep static stories for people to read and comment on, as well as maintaining a new dynamic thread that is very fun to participate in.

Again, I don't want to close threads and move stuff around. We are all mature enough to go to the appropriate place and post. In the long run it will cost you maybe an extra click or two, but you will gain in time saved because you won't have to dig through hundreds of other posts to look for what you want.

All that said, we will not be moving the threads unless they are waaay out of topic in the wrong thread.

So, cheer up, this is the same ole Glade, just a little busier and we all want to keep it as neat as possible

I'll refresh this thread every week or so, so the new users can see this as well.

Thanks again everyone for making this a great place and as I've always said; It'll just keep getting better, I promise! As always, let me know if you think there should be a new board made, or maybe even removed.
