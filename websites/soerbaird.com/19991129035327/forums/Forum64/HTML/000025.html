Tox Forest is right outside the City of Erudin on the Odus continent. Rather a dismal zone at the moment, though plans seem to be afoot to add extra zones adjacent to it. Very dark and murky, even with infravision; a rather cruel joke played on blind newbie Erudites perhaps.

Of minor note is an NPC Troll who upon breathing his last gives positive faction with the Frogloks of Guk. Could be interesting for an unhindered wander around Guk if you fancy.
