and me, lvl 29 shaman.

"where the hell did all your mana go?"

I finally told the ranger if all he wanted were healing spells he should find a cleric, and then I left.

Me for "wasting" mana on de-buffs.
The bard for bringing too many damned gators.

Just a thought...

Don't forget your earlier spells, some spells at each rank are specifically designed to be marginally usefull until later. So, at 29, you might get more utility out of your good old level 14 Walking Sleep-3 casts for 180 MP (and it casts faster too). You could even go with Disempower if you really want to save mana (120 MP for 3). As your mana pull increases you will eventually be able to cast 3 Togars without worrying so much, but now you might consider using the good old one if you are going to do multiple casts in one fight. Of course resists might be a problem for lower level spells, but I have seen this be an issule much less often than I expected it to be.
