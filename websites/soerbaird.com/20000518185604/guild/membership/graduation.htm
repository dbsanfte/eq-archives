Academy graduation requirements

To graduate from the Soerbaird Academy, you must achieve the following:

  1. You must reach 10th level.
  2. You must write three works in three different forms, choosing from the following forms: ode, eulogy, epic, bawdy, history.
    Note: History may be written as prose instead of verse.
  3. You must perform in at least one concert in which an Ollave is in attendance.
  4. You must have visited all of these cities: Freeport, Qeynos, Rivervale, Halas, Kaladim, Kelethin, Felwithe, Ak'Anon, High Keep.
  5. You must have performed at least 2 services as a herald.
     This will most likely include either announcing an event in a city, running items to another city, or guiding someone to another city.
  6. You must attend at least 3 non-Soerbaird events, and be able to give a brief explanation of what transpired.
     Note: 'Events' include tavern nights, weddings, wars, or any other organized gathering which occurs in Norrath.
  7. You must be able to name one tavern from each of the following cities: Freeport, Qeynos, Kelethin.
  8. You must be able to name all sixteen deities of Norrath from memory, and describe the role of at least 8 of them.
  9. Your achievements and character must be approved by members of the Council.

Once all nine requirements have been met, the Council will name you to the Filidh. At that time, you must proclaim the branch of the Filidh under which you wish to serve.
