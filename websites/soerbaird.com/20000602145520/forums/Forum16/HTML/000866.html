Ok folks, Even I took the wait and see position till the Patch came out... and well, while it wasn't horrible it didn't address any of our concerns.

There is a lot of frustration being expressed, and really quite rightly so. You are a paying customer of a company thats behaviour is not suiting you. As such you have a right to make your voice heard. Unfortunatly we have all found that expressing your dismay here or even writing directly to key persons in the company has not changed this corporations actions nor increased their respect for our opinions.

As legal action is not feasible nor realistic and there are few other methods that would perhaps be more effective available, I and proposing and will take on the burden of organizing, publicising, ect. a petition tailored to the interests of Customers of VI who invested their time and money in playing a "Shaman" in Everquest.

This has been attempted several times in the past, we are all aware. It has been sabotaged and undermined in each case. To prevent this from happening again The Compiling and drafts of this document will not be posted to this sight, though a link might. I will operate a very simple webpage containing only the current draft.

All submissions will be accepted via email. I will take all the submissions and try to make a comprehensive whole out of it. Both specific issues and fully written petitions will be accepted and appreciated, starting today. On the 15th of October new submissions will be cut off from being included in the petition. From that date only revisions and editing of the document will be made, with the exception of 3 days following any new patch affecting interested parties. On the last day of October the final draft will be presented and then unless there is a major outcry, it will be fully publicised and a feature will be added for those wishing to "sign" it to add their mark to the bottom on Nov 1.

I know this sounds kind of heavy handed but I think a timetable must be set or like so many other projects of the sort it would just drift into obscurity and apathy. I am not a proficent or motivating writer so the Writing of the Petition will come from your Submissions. If you want credit please include the name you want it credited to listed.

One last Caveat. If I get no responses or very few I will simply discard the idea as I have better things to do with my time than work on a lost cause. In about 3 days I'll post again and if there has been a significant response, post an address of where to look at the first rough draft.

Submission Guidelines:
Be sure "Shaman Petition" is in the Subject line along with a few distinguishing words about your imput. If it doesn't contain the phrase Shaman Petition my filter won't catch it and it will go to the can directly

Please avoid Pure Rants, vulgarity, and flames directed twords me.

Please do not send any sort of file, attached document, or executable as for security reasons they will all be deleted.

Please DO send the petitions and suggestions to dehle@exchange.indiana.edu

Thank you.

Welan/David.
