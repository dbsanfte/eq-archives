If I were goig to do it all over again.

1 - get a FS 2H sword (got mine on E'ci for 9p) switch back and forth. keep both 1HS, and 2HS maxed if you can

2 - get banded.

3 - get dwarven axe. This is a better axe than the mino higher dps also weights less and it's faster so you'll get more chances to up skill

4 - upgrade FS 2h to Bone Bl

5 - at 17 I'd get another dwarven axe for dual wield even better a Dragoon dirk - well actually I wouldn't get a dirk - don't like the look and use the BBC as the primary weapon and the others to keep skills up.

There are some interesting assumptions about 2h vs 1h and damage but until I can prove otherwise I'll go along with the general feeling that 2H will outperform 1H until you reach the middle levels (25+)

We rangers are born to quest, and this has funded my fletching habit, and gotten me a ton of good gear at my level. We are the only class that can track before level 20, so we can find the people or creatures that need finding under circumstances that would be maddeningly difficult otherwise (I'd prefer not to just have to 'run into' Zarchoomi or Gnasher Furgutt). There are some quests that are more profitable than others, but you can raise enough at your level to be a master Fletcher now, if you wish it. I went a-questing and amassed a lot of platinum with which I purchased a Magic Halberd of shiny Brass, which never dulls and hits hard. I had gotten some Blackened-Iron Boots as a quest reward (I did this quest several more times to see if it was a rare reward and it seems to be because I only got some bronze weaponry -- and bad ones at that), so I headed off to wisp island (Erud's Crossing) and killed a lot of wisps and collected the greater stones, selling them all to one player who bought them for his guild, I think. I used this money, and money from selling other quest items to get to master Fletcher and now I am a Trueshot Archer at 14, though my armor is kind of embarrasing. I'll fix that soon enough though.

I would suggest a spiked rather than studded collar, but I need the strength even more than the stamina.
