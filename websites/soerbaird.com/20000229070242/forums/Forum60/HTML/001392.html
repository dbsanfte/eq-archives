(Should have posted this a while ago but never got to it, heard someone mentioned something about it so here it is...and sorry about the lenght)


It began like a very delightful day in EQ. I got bored with the usual Mistmoore and Unrest bashing and decided to go and investigate on our Ivy Etched armor.

Not that I had any hopes to acctualy getting the Ivy tunic, but I set my path to Steamfont anyway. After hanging a long time near the mines scouting and learning the area, a kind fellow Ranger (Thanks Chaani !) helped me (ok,ok...she killed him, I was busy getting feared and DoT'd and trying to stay alive long enough) get the skull.

Surprised but Happy I entered Lesser Faydark and planned meeting with the Thistle by myself (level 26) and see if we can come to any agreement about his Ivy Rune.

It was VERY long and heroic (some will say pathetic *grins*) battle and when I thought I would never kill him, a younger Cleric I crossed paths with helped me to stay alive long enough to slay the little annoying brownie.

This was too good to be true and after I realized that I am soon to be a proud owner of our Ranger only tunic, I started my long journey to Kithicor.

Few guildmates warned me about something I already knew well: "Beware of the Undead army in Kithicor !"

Memorizing that to myself I entered Kithicor at about noon thinking the Undead are long resting in their graves hiding from the daylight (Daylight ? In Kithicor ? What's that ?).

A slow careful journey to the Rangers camp and 10 minutes later I hold in my shaking hands the fresh new Ivy Etched Tunic. My heart was screaming with joy (and my EXPbar with half a bubble of exp).

I heartily thanked the Ranger and turned toward West Commons. Walking and whistling merry tunes to myself, stopping from time to time to take another look at my new tunic.

I didn't get too far away from the camp when I heard heavy footsteps and dreadful sound behind me....

"A Zombie Infantry hits YOU for 55 points of damage !"

Ohhh uhhh ! I draw my SSoM and Dragoon dirk and being use to slaying Undead beings, I prepare to fight back...

Hmmm...few seconds (and a RED con) cleared to me that this foe is way beyond my powers...

I didn't lose my senses. "Not a big deal" I thought to myself "I am still close to my fellow Rangers camp, they should have no trouble dispatching this foul being".

And with that thought I ran as quick as my SoW-less feet could carry my back to Morin and company.

"I will run past the two Rangers outside straight to the mighty Morin himself" I thought to myself.

I should have seen something was very wrong when I passed running by Leaf Falldim and the other Ranger outside.
Leaf pretended like he has never seen me before and the other Ranger just looked up at the sky and whistled.

I ran just inside Morin's hut...

"A Zombie Infantry hits YOU for 43 points of damage !"

"Here it comes" I thought "Few more hits and Morin will send this creature back to it's maker"

10 long seconds passed, I waited in vain for Morin to come to my aid.

The Zombie just smiled a cruel taunting smile at me like saying, "None shall come to your help in here..."

I shouted "MORIN !!!!!!!!" just before the Zombie landed the killing blow...

LOADING, PLEASE WAIT.....

Few zones later a very angry Ranger made the journey back to his corpse. I entered the hut and confronted Morin. He wouldn't even look me in the eyes. I shook my head, silently collected my things and left , swearing never to return to that place again. From the distance, I heard Morin offering his sword to a young Ranger in return for his services against the Undead forces. I sighed with contempt and continued to walk away...


Now what is going on here ?!? Those who call themselves protectors of the woods yet wouldn't lift a finger helping a fellow Ranger ?!?! Bah ! What a joke. I am offended !

And...that Ivy Etched armor which we all think is a god send gift ?
Well, I wouldn't be surprised if all this is just a cover for some illegal business those so-called "Rangers" are doing there. Maybe some fishy deal with the Undead. Something strange is going on, I can tell !

I can imagine Morin and his friends buying second hand, heavily used Crafted Armor from retired tweanked warriors, color it green, attaching a "Genuine - Made In Kithicor" label to it and send us around the world in search for silly items. Maybe they just want the gems. Maybe they just want us camping useless things for hours away from there, keeping us REAL Rangers busy while they do their secret operation with the Undead.

I plan to get to the bottom of this. Meanwhile, I will just buy that Second-hand-used-green-crafted armor from those who are willing to deal with those scums. Don't want anything to do with them anymore.

Clear skies.


(why in a roleplaying game, guards in Faydark kill innocent Wasps that never hurt a soul and Rangers in Kithicor wouldn't attack undead is beyond me...oh well)
