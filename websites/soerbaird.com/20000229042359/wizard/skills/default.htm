Attributes (stats)
back
Attribute

Effect on Wizard

Strength Makes you hit more often for maximum damage. Weight allowance is directly tied to your strength.
Dexterity Increases the ability to hit and increases your chance to regain casting when hit or interrupted.
Agility Helps dodging and improves AC.
Charisma Helps overcome resistance when "effect" style spells such as debuffs or charms are used on a critter. Also improves faction with NPCs and helps improve prices with vendors.
Stamina Gives a few extra HP per level. Also allows you to fight longer and swim longer without getting tired.
Intelligence Determines mana store for wizards and improves the rate of advancing your skills.
Wisdom Helps resist mind affecting spells, such as fear, charm, etc.

Analysis

Abjuration 1 Resistance, AC, and quickness buff spells. Also see the spell list

Channeling 4 Increases chances not to be disrupted while casting, even when hit. Eventually you can even run while casting certain spells.
Meditate 4 Allows you to regain mana faster, and increase the speed of memorizing and scribing. You must put 1 practice point into this before it will start going up on its own.
Specialization 20

Allows you to specialize a categry of magic in type of magic which means less mana usage and faster casting spells.

You can specialize all categories to 50, but only one above.
