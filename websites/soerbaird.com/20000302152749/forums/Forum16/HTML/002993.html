I believe it is generally accepted that certain mobs do in fact have certain inherent resists. Some mobs are prone to Disease, some Poison, others Cold, some Fire. Note that this was never changed even tho Disease is essentially worthless so when up against mobs that are prone to Disease you are pretty much out of luck. These things were put into place in the beginning.

I have found consistency in the resists of mobs. Yes you will occasionally find a mob with insane resists, but for the most part you can count on certain types of magic to stick on certain types of mobs. You just need to experiment and find out which mobs are prone to which types of magic.
