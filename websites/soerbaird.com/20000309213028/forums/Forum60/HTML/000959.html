> Date: Wed, 3 Nov 1999 11:29:48 -0800
> From: "Brian Hook"
> Subject: RE: RE: Fletching
>
> Currently the two proposals I have going internally are fairly
> straightforward:
>
> - enchanted stuff from an enchanter (silver, mana, whatever) that gets used
> in fletching to make nice magical arrows
>
> - store bought components (e.g. silver arrowheads) that allow you to make
> good magical arrows
>
> These are not mutually exclusive. We want enchanters to be an optional part
> of most trade skills (yes Dennis, I DO know how you feel, and I don't
> exactly disagree with you, but right now we're too far forward into EQ to
> make drastic changes to how enchantments/trade skills work and the role of
> the enchanter), but we don't want them to have a monopoly at all times.
>
> A simple summary: we would like rangers to make magic arrows without being
> dependent on enchanters, but we want there to be an incentive to have an
> enchanter involved. This means that enchanter-assisted arrows would be
> better than non-enchanter-assisted ones, but that regular magic arrows that
> don't require an enchanter would still remain viable.
>
> Brian
