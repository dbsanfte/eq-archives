I was just reading a topic about rangers spells being too late in coming, and the replies were that a ranger isnt a spellcaster and that our spells are just to help complement our skills. Then I thought, Why do rangers need spells at all? We are woodsmen, right? Cant we do just about everything that our spells allow now, using only nature? And then I thought, we are woodsmen? Woodsmen dont usually travel in huge groups, right? Why shouldnt rangers be self sufficient?

So I have come up with some rough ideas for making the ranger the king of solo classes. First of all, these ideas would NOT allow a ranger to take on a whole dungeon just by his or her lonesome, but would allow the ranger to solo white/black conning creatures in open, natural areas. The solo ranger would not be entirely nerfed in group combat, however, they would just be medium combat support, not tankers or nukers. The natural skills of the ranger would still make him/her viable in finding mobs to kill, pulling those mobs in, etc. I just had to say that before I got into the gist of my idea.

Anyway, spells could not be entirely stripped from the ranger, as they are the easiest way of making things happen in a game like EQ. The ones a ranger would keep would be-

The thistlecoat line of spells.

Feet like cat and strength of earth.

Enduring breath and harmony.

Cancel magic and invigor.

Levitate and wolf form.

Other than these spells, we can pretty much do anything with skills. Dont complain about the loss of the skin series, stinging swarm, or snare just yet, though. First I need to explain the replacement of the healing spells. We have bind wound right? Well, as a woodsmen hybrid class with an experience penalty, we need to be masters of bind wound. The best to do this, I think, is an ability to make and use different kinds of things to use with bind wound, such as poultices. The ingredients for these items we could even find ourselves, if we all got forage. With these poultice thingies, we could heal beyond halfway (albeit slowly) and maybe at higher levels even have a chance of curing such ailments as disease and poison. We wouldnt be as good of healers as clerics or druids with this ability, as it would take a very long time to accomplish or could even be made to affect only the ranger.

Now, the replacement of the skin series is the ability to wear plate armor. Also, a small bonus to our base stamina. Plate armor, however, would result in an added restriction on our dual wield ability. All dual wield restrictions (plate armor caused or not) would be nullified if the ranger wore light armor, such as leather. This would give the ranger a choice between really good armor with lessened damage dealing ability, or very effective damage dealing ability with low armor class. The bonus to our stamina would help alleviate the loss of the hp bonus without the skin series.

Camo would be replaced by an outdoor-only, total invisibility granting version of sneak. Of course, we would still walk slowly while using the skill, but would get a message saying if it was successful.

All other abilities we could no longer use without spells would be addressed in the inclusion of better bows and arrows. They would be able to have magical effects like snare or added damage procs. Some arrows could even have dot procs like stinging swarm. Maybe they could just be enchanted arrows with the effect of a spell, not actually casting a spell themselves. Naturally, we would be able to make some these arrows by ourselves, perhaps the ability is bestowed through a quest.

That is all I can think of. Anyone who actually read this whole post have any suggeestions, comments complaints? This is just for fun really, I am not planning on sending this to verant or anybody as a petition.
