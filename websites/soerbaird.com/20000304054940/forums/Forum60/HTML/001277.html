The one place that everyone seems to overlook is Runny Eye. I'd say head to highold and hunt gnolls and orcs till about lvl 20 or 21.

I made the mistake of not realizing how good RE could be till about halfway to 22. That place has great loot and expierence. Its just hard to get a group going if you don't play during the peak hours. Hell, I play on Fennin Ro at peak hours and theres usually about 20 people max there.

So basically I would suggest hunting in High Hold Pass till about 20 or 21, then give Runny Eye a try.

Oh, another good thing about RE is the fact that the zone is usually pretty close no matter where your at. You can usually head to the Beholder zone pretty safely, or if you by the knight's spawn, theres water nearby where you can heal up. The Gobs dont usually follow, only once they did to me.

Hope this helps.
