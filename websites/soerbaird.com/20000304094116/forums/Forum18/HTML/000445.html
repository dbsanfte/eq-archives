I have come to rely on the hot key switching tactic. I keep hot keys on page 3 with 4 inventory slots and the 2 melee slots. Med to full with your choice of Wisdom enhancing items (Testament and Skull or Paw for instance). Once combat begins get that first spell off and then switch to your favorite 2hb (Staff of Observors for me).

If I am in a group where I will never med to full or am at risk of not switching quick enough (or dropping something in the switch), then I just keep the Staff equipped. There is not much difference in mana at my level(29) between Staff and Testament/skull combo.
