Roleplaying a Troll Shaman - commentary by Quon

My name is Quon.

I am a Troll Shaman that can be found most times in Grobb or the swamp outside my city. I feel the need to explain my race, as it is mis-understood my most. We are Trolls, and much like the Ogre we are big, strong and ugly. Not accepted my the elves or humans. I believe that what they don't understand, they hate. We keep to ourselves and don't travel to far regions. However, a word of warning, if you come into our lands prepare to die and be feasted upon. Even your greatest heros will fall in the swamps of Innothule.

My role in Everquest is simple. Being what I believe is the most inntellegent of our race, I will travel farther then most trying to bring our culture to others. Do not misunderstand, I will never like the other races (especially the hummies or elfies) but I will tolerate them. My goal is to take what I learn and try to attain at true connection with the spirits of our lands. Many say that we have ruined our lands, this is certainly not the case since our lands have been unchanged for many years. The hummies with thier big cities have truely infected nature.

Do not judge every Troll you see by what I have written here. The Bashers (warriors) of Grobb are fierce and will not be intimidated. They live for two things, to kill and eat. It doesn't really matter what or who it is. If you cross their paths DO NOT get their attention. The Shadow Knights of Grobb are the scariest beings in existance. No one likes them, not even the ogres who will sometimes attack them on sight. They do devious things, and use thier evil spells to harm and torture. It is said that they are the favored children of Innoruuk, the Prince of Hate. They can kill with one touch. Do not seek them out, and pray to your god that you never see one.

Quon, Troll Shaman of Rathe
