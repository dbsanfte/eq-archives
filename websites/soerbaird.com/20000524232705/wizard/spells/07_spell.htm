Cazic Gate
150
Instant

Personal gate to Cazic-Thule. Very dangerous to use; you land in the maze in Cazic-Thule.

Cazic Gate gates you to pos 253 neg 466.

The exit to the maze is at pos 260 neg 70.

From Rashana Serene of Xegony:
" If you travel as much in a westward direction as possible through the maze you can get out easy but this takes time and practice to be able to navigate. I strongly urge wizards that are below level 30 not to use this spell unless you have a party already waiting on the other side. And even at 30 and up extreem caution must be used. If you get swarmed here you are most likly going to die in a place that will be very difficult to get back to. "
Common Gate
150
Instant

Personal gate to West Commonlands.
CAUTION: Several dark elves, including 20-22 level Necromancer (Kizdean Gix) and a level 23-35 Shadow Knight (Dragoon Zytl), patrol the area of the pyramid. If you are killed on sight by dark elves, you probably don't want to hang around the pyramid for very long. Other dangers are wandering Griffon's that sometimes spot you before you can figure out what direction you are facing. Be ready for the possibility of death when you land here by yourself. -- Kozmo Arkanis of Karana
