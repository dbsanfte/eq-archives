Last night I was down in SK trying to gain back my level that I lost in a nasty mistmoore train. Well, I had L39 long enough to scribe my spells at least. Anyway, I summoned a pet, cast my normal buffs (talisman, shield, sow) on the pet and decided to try giving him a weapon. All I had on me was a FS short sword (dly 23).

Eventually, I came across a way-too-rare charger and opened with Tagar's, quickened the pet, and dropped envenomed breath on the centaur. First thing I notice is my pet is hitting for 28's, which was nice as those had been rare for me the last couple weeks. The second thing I notice, the pet is absolutely tearing up this charger. His hits were literally spamming my chat box. I don't know if it was the patch or the lower dly weapon I gave it, but he seemed MUCH faster and I don't mean just because of the quickness. I think I used maybe a bbl of mana during the fight and the charger dropped quick (no hoof, though). Before, chargers usually forced me to use at least 2-3 bbls of mana.

Think I will hold onto those FS daggers from now on and see if it was just my imagination.
