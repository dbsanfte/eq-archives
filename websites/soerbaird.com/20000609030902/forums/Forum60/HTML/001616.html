Dragan
Fellowship Associate 		posted 12-12-1999 19:41            
Light Healing costs 25 mana (right click on the damn spell yourself to find out, sheesh).
Skin like Rock costs 60 mana.

Skin like rock is only *slightly* more efficient then Light Healing (and only after you reached level 32/33). And when I say *slightly* I mean *slightly* - in the order of a 5 HP gain after 10 castings.

Skin Like Rock also has a longer recycle rate (cannpt be recast as fast as Lught Healing).

However - it still has replaced Light Healing as my main healing spell. Reasons: 1) It heals a lot more in one go (if you really need to help someone NOW) and 2) it refreshes my Skin Like Rock buff (duh). To counter the long recast time I use the following way: Cast Skin then Light Healing. After that you can recast Skin .. lather rinse repeat.

BTW... only Skin like Rock has tfhe heal ability. Skin like Steel does *not* (neither does Skin like Wood).
