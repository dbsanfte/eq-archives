Planes of Power info 7-6-2002
- Suede
EverQuest: Planes of Power
Shipping Date: 10/21/2002
Price: $29.99
Platform: PC
Overview from EBGames.com:

EverQuest: The Planes of Power is the next installment in the best-selling, critically acclaimed EverQuest series. The new expansion pack will introduce more than 425,000 current EverQuest gamers to an arching storyline that takes players on an epic adventure through the Planes of Power, home of the Gods of Norrath. Hundreds of new items, quests and encounters will keep even the most revered players challenged and enthralled.

In The Planes of Power, a portal has been unveiled and now travelers can experience a world unlike anything they have ever seen. Share in the knowledge of this extraordinary discovery as you fight to bring justice to Norrath. Veteran gamers will face new challenges in each of the 18 newly discovered planes, while new players will benefit from the Plane of Knowledge that hosts scores of new and challenging quests and open portals to Norrath making travel faster and easier than ever. Fiercely battle the minions of Rallos Zek in the Plane of War, join arms with Karana to regain control of the Plane of Storms, and overcome vile pestilence in the Plane of Disease.

-20 new planer zones that push teamwork abilities to the limit
-Hundreds of new items and monsters
-Exciting new quests and challenges await even the most experienced players
-Two planar cities that provide a hub for travel and player interaction
-A central storyline links the content from every zone into one compelling quest
-Allows characters to gain even more power

CHP in New Lands 6-12-2002
- Suede
Hail!

Well, the results from the Polls are in - thank you all for voting - and it was decided that the expansion of the CHP Guild to new games will be happening here on the site. It brings me great joy to see this development happening on the CHP homesoil - and in the weeks to come there will be several changes to the site and CHP forums to upgrade it from an EQ exclusive Guild to a Gaming Guild raising it's banner in new lands.

It has also been decided that the name of any and all guilds that we establish in the new games [ie Dungeon Siege, NeverWinters Night, and others that we may wish to play] will be CHP - the Crusaders of the High Plains

Also, to help with this initiative, I am happy to announce that both Raven and Sarouk of CHP's granddaddy guilds, the Knights of Justice [KOJ of Diablo and DII] and the Aardwolf Crusaders [ARC of UO], will be joining us here. So, please join me in welcoming Raven and Sarouk to CHP, where they will be avidly working to help develop the content and the modeling of custom worlds for the Crusaders of the High Plains to enjoy in days to come.

Long live CHP!
- Suede
