Friday October 08,1999


Betazine's FGN as test script

  Repost of the september 1998 interview

Alex provided this interview from our 'friend' Charles Flock, Charles was running the beta test over at Tanarus. In fact this was one of the most pleasant and addictive beta tests that I have ever had the pleasure to be a part of, I look forward to great things at Everquest ~ Dean

Q: You are confident that EQ will rip even a huge company like Microsofts product to shreds?

A: Yes, unequivocally. Everquest is better then I thought it would be, and my standards are very high. - Charles Flock, SISA

Q: Please state your name and occupation.

A: My name is Charles Flock and I am the test manager of the San Diego studio.

Q: What is your involvement with Everquest?

A: I will be overseeing the beta test along with Brad (the producer)

Q: How do you believe EQ (Everquest) will differentiate from the other hordes of MPOG games?

A: Well there are many reasons, a few include the fact that EQ will be true 3d polygonal figures. All races are playable and come in different sizes etc. Also the game is viewed in a virtual infinity of camera angles.

Q: How much will EQ cost approximately?

A: No comment on the pricing as of yet.

Q: Lag has always been a problem with MPOG games. How does Sony intend to keep the lag down with such a large project?

A: We will be basing EQ on our Tanarus Engine. Despite what people think, it is quite good at handling latency.

Q: Will team play be as emphasized in Everquest as it is in Tanarus?

A: Well, that will be up to the players. But we certainly encourage team play.

Q: So a team won’t be essential?

A: For particular tasks a team is essential. So in reality, you will need a team at one point or the other.

Q: Who does Sony see as their main competition?

A: None, Asherons Call doesn’t come close to our graphics. And no other game I have seen has the depth of our environment.

Q: You are confident that EQ will rip even a huge company like Microsofts product to shreds?

A: Yes, unequivocally. Everquest is better then I thought it would be, and my standards are very high.

Q: Was Tanarus more of a "Spreading of wings" a step into shallow water before the plunge into EQ?

A: Yes Tanarus was actually developed to generate some engines for Everquest.

Q: And has it helped?

A: Yes it has been very helpful, both from a service/technical point of view.

Q: Shon (a member at Sony) commented that Everquest has the potential to be the best game of the year. Do you believe this, and why?

A: In terms of RPG’s I believe EQ will be the best one ever. Our entire staff is incredibly talented and we are all die hard gamers.

Q: When is EQ estimated for release?

A: "Soon"  Note this is a Friarism, if you follow the tanarus newsgroup, you get the joke - Dean

Q: Many people are very anxious to be in the beta (Including me) what are your criteria and when will we be notified about whether we are accepted or not?

A: As we stated, experience hardware and how they do on the interview are all factors.

Q: You will be hosting interviews?

A: Yes we will, be patient.

Q: Has Sony made all there deadlines as of yet?

A: Yes, it is progressing quite smoothly.

Q: From a Gamers view point, do you consider EQ a solid game?

A: Yes I would play it even if I were not involved with it’s creation.

This is where the interview ended.  I would like to thank Charles Flock very much for his time.
