1999-02-19   -   Posted by Dean - [archives on 1999-02-26]
Hammerfell - A free online browser based RPG

Hammerfell - found this tonight, almost didnt post because I was playing :)
Well here is the scoop from the Hammerfell site:
Hammerfell currently works on Netscape 4.0 and IE 4.0. Each browser will play slightly differently, but will give you the same result. Hammerfell is entirely free. Just log in and play. I wont even ask for your e-mail address.

what is it like? Well, essentially, Hammerfell allows you to generate a character, travel around 2D, tilebased maps, equip yourself with all manner of weapons, armor, rings and such, beat the crap out of beasties, and talk to other players. And if you're feeling nasty, beat the crap out of them too. PKing is not outlawed here. It looks quite a bit like RPGs of the olden days, but with slightly nicer graphics, and of course, the fact that other people play at the same time.
