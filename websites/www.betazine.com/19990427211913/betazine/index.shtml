Beta 1.2 of Zion has been released. Zion is an Online Graphical RPG. The game is in development and the creators are open to ideas.

Theys say:
Some ideas for the future are:
1. The ability to walk with other people, and get in fights with them on your team (ex. 2 or more Zion members vs. 1 bad guy). That could be used to defeat boss enemies
2. Quests - Every RPG needs Quests.
3. A story line - Let's face it. Our game has no plot right now.
4. The ability to get in fights with other people (1 vs 1)

The latest Miniverse release is ready for launch. Whether it's you first visit, or one hundred and first, the latest Miniverse experience has something for everyone.

Multiple resolutions -- 640x480, 800x600, 1024x768
Ball Textures -- Now you can see the ball rolling with your own choice of texture
Customizable Interface -- Make your own score panel, mouse pointer and crosshair.
New Environmental Particle Animations -- Real-time snow/rain effects
Extra Demo Hole -- A snowy winter scene...with the new Real-time snow/rain effect
Overview screens -- A brief description and overall look at the course Ledges -- Ball can fall off ledges now
Score -- Game is now over if you hit 2 above par
Better hills & walls -- Now look more visually accurate
Power-Ups -- Bonus stroke on 1st hole. More power-ups still to come
Keyboard shortcuts -- Arrow keys now scroll the screen, space minimizes the scoreboard


Background Info

Miniverse is arcade-style miniature golf featuring an isometric user perspective, 3D rendered scrolling playfields, and an advanced physics engine. The planets of Miniverse have been terraformed into challenging courses (and five star resorts) to provide addictive stroke play and ruthless croquet-style death matches.
