Making Ronium and Armor of Ro.

    This is a post from our message boards.  It is being posted here so that all those who are interested in the quests for Armor of Ro can easily find it.  Special thanks goes to Sushidono for taking the time to post this to the message board in the first place.

    Aremos,
    Templar at Luminus Dominus

    The following are the 6 components necessary to make Ronium:
    - Sands of Ro
    - Mistmoore Granite
    - Soil of Underfoot
    - Melatite
    - an Enchanted bar of Platinum
    - an Enchanted bar of Platinum

    (that's no typo, I said above 6 components, so it does require 2 enchanted bars of plat)

    WHERE TO GET THE ITEMS:

    Sands of Ro (looks like a small pouch)- Drop off sand giants in and around the Oasis (South Ro, and very rarely North Ro)

    Mistmoore Granite (looks like a black pearl) - dropped off the flouting, leering, and jeering gargoyles in Mistmoore Castle ... these are no drop, meaning you gotta hunt for them yourself. outside the castle will NOT drop this, you gotta fight the ones inside ... good luck, this part is probably the hardest to obtain.

    Soil of Underfoot (looks like a small pouch) - Give 4 Fairy Dusts (unstacked to be safe ... that is use all 4 slots in the trade window to give these to her) to Priestess Ghalea in the Cleric guild in Kaladim (it's in the same zone as the bank). The soil of underfoot is no-drop, LORE ... so you can only get one of these at a time. The Fairy Dust is dropped by the Fairies in Lesser Faydark, pretty easy kills, and can be bought, since they're not no drop.

    Melatite (looks like a HQ brick of metal) - I never hunted for these while doing my Ro Armor quests ... I just purchased them (for 50pp each). But I believe they drop off the gnomish robots in Solusek A ... maybe other mobs as well, but that's all I know.

    Enchanted Platinum Bars (looks like .. hehe well go to a Jewelry merchant and find out ...) - This is the easiest part, well considering it's bought from merchants ... anyway, just go to any jewelry shop in town (the "Blue Building" in North Freeport was my main shopping place for bars of plat), and purchase 2 Bars of Platinum (stackable) ... with my Charisma of 124, the bars cost 105pp each. Next find a level 34 Enchanter or higher, and kindly ask them to enchant the two bars of platinum for you ... most high level enchanters will provide this service for free, just please be polite =). You will have to trust the enchanter and give them the two bars of plat, so they can in turn enchant them and give them back. I never got ripped off in this way, but I can understand people's worries regarding this issue ... just ask someone you think you can trust.

    Ok, now that you have all 6 components necessary for Ronium, head on over to the Temple of Solusek Ro (zoned in from Lavastorm). Work your way inside and find Lord Searfire, while he is targeted say "I need the Sol Cauldron", he will then give you a cauldron with 6 slots ... place each item mentioned above inside the Cauldron (remember not to stack the Enchanted Platinum Bars, or you're screwed outta 210pp and a whole bunch of time), then hit COMBINE ... voila, you have a bar of Ronium in your hands! It is not lore, so you can keep as many as you need in your inventory ... they look like the Platinum Bars, but are unstackable.

    MAKING THE RO ARMOR:
    Place the mold, and one bar of ronium inside any regular forge and hit combine ... there you go, a new piece of Ro Armor!

    DO NOT put anything else in the Forge ... just one bar of ronium, and one Ro mold ... try to make sure you have at least 1 skill point in it just to be safe (although failures are not possible ... but be safe and put that one skill point into smithing!)

    FACTION ISSUES:

    Faction with Solusek Ro - give Derv Rings to the named orc in South Ro - Ortallious or something like that (when you zone in from the oasis, just follow the right zone wall and you will happen upon him). Turn in 4 at a time.

    Faction with Clerics of Underfoot - Give bone chips to Gunlok Jure (at the back of the Paladin guild in kaladim) ... I forget how many at a time it is .. 4 maybe? ... hehe I'm not sure, just find out =P.

    Anyway, there you go, a pretty complete guide to making Ronium ... I cannot take credit for this work entirely, as I found it while searching websites ... I've changed the text a little pertaining to my own experiences with the metal, and due credit should be given to:
