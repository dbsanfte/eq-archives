Greetings and welcome to the Hunters of the Undead website. We are based on the Morell-Thule server.  If this is your first visit, you are probably considering membership in our guild. If so, it is our wish that you read the following and consider it carefully before making your decision.

 

When you join this guild, you are joining a group of people who have made a commitment to each other and to the world of Norrath to act with honor and integrity. We will expect that same commitment from you. Our goal is to be a hallmark of truth and honor in Norrath.  In pursuit of this, every Hunter has vowed to:

 

  • Put their own needs second to that of their guild. If another Hunter is in need, you will do whatever is in your power to lend assistance. These are your brothers and sisters. Of course, with the privilige of having the entire at your disposal, comes the responsibility to call for this help only when it is necessary...when you are truly in need. The same strength of character needed to become a Hunter and to lend assistance when another Hunter calls should guide you in deciding when to call for help yourself.

 

  • Always lend assistance to others while traveling through the vast expanses of Norrath. It takes only a moment to stop and save the life of that overzealous halfling who has been swarmed by fire beetles or to cast a buff for a struggling young warrior who is off to battle orcs in Crushbone or the Commonlands. We believe that, in life, the greatest satisfaction comes not from personal gain, but from the lifting up of others. That is what we strive for. That is what we will expect of you.

 

  • Conduct himself/herself with honor, pride, and discipline. A Hunter never kill steals or intentionally directs a train on someone. A Hunter will not engage in spamming or get involved in petty arguments over open tell channels (ooc, shout, etc). In general, a Hunter never engages in any type of behavior that would bring dishonor to themself or to our guild.  If we are to be one of the great guilds of Norrath, then we must show others the way of honor and integrity. We must lead by example.

 

Of course, the Hunters are not just about rules. We are also very much about having fun and battling the evil that still exists in our world.  We group together and chat together and make our fortunes together.  We have regular meetings and regular raids. The raids are designed to help our members reach their goals, obtain elusive quest items, and to gain experience. We are all about teamwork and having fun.

 

If this sounds like something that you would like to be a part of, then read on. Look at our charter, our member list, our message board. When you are back to Norrath, look up one of our officers and tell them that you are interested in being a Hunter of the Undead. We look forward to meeting you and welcoming you into our guild.
