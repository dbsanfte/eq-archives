    Thursday, February 15, 2001 

 

 

Hail Hunters! The council met on Friday last and discussed, among other things, the future direction of the guild. Based on these discussions, a change to the guild laws and charter was drafted and put to the council for review and comment. As no council member has dissented, I now present the new charter to the guild. You can (and should) review it, as well as the minor changes to the rank system here.  I welcome any comments or suggestions concerning these changes. There will be a message thread in the General forum for this purpose, or you can e-mail me directly.

 

Other news from the council meeting:

 

-          Priestess Ishtar has graciously accepted the position of Templar et Constructus. If you read the Ranks and Charter page link above, you know what that means. You can read more about her plans, as well as upcoming raids and events in the Events forum on the message board. She now has moderator control of that forum. Make sure you check it out as she has a little something cooled up for this weekend. (And I’m not talking about her famous rabbit stew)

 

-          Details of the Guild Dues and a new Lotto were finalized. Lord Bartikus has agreed to administer these programs and even has a young subject in Freeport who maintains a bank account for this purpose. You can read all about it here. Contact Lord Bartikus or Lady Sinnfull when you see them online. They will be happy to assist you.

 

-          The roll call is proceeding nicely, but some of you still haven’t responded. If this includes you, you can respond here. Also, if you want your picture and pertinent info listed on the Heroes page or your e-mail address listed on the E-mail page, send it to me and I will get it posted.

 

The council will be meeting again this Friday, so if there are any issues, as always, you can email them to me or post them on the message board. I always check both just before council meetings. You could also bring them up yourself at the regularly scheduled guild meetings that take place every other Saturday night. Attendance at last week’s meeting was a little weak. This was partly my fault as I did not post an announcement here until the day before the meeting. In the future, I will try to announce the time and place of meetings and other events several days in advance so you can make plans to attend. I would also ask your assistance in getting the word out when you are in the game. Just do a “/who all hunters” to see who is around and then don’t be shy about using the /guild channel to inform those who might not have checked in here about upcoming guild events.

 

Well, that’s about it for now. Much of the site has been updated, so take a minute and browse around. As always, I welcome your comments and suggestions.

 

Thanks,

Mulen

     Wednesday, January 31st

 

Web Site Update

Hail Hunters!  I am Mulen, a Hunter of the Undead and a Paladin of the 27th circle. I am going to try to keep the website updated. If you have any suggestions for anything you want to see, please let me know by e-mail (by clicking the link below) or on the message board. I check both at least daily.  I am new to this, so be patient J

We had a meeting on Monday night as announced below. About ten Hunters attended and much was discussed. One of the first priorities was to perform a “roll call” to get an accurate count of the membership of the guild. To accomplish this, our fearless leader, Jacor, has started a “Roll Call” message string on our Message board. To answer the call, simply go to the message board by following the link to the left; go to the “General” forum; then go to “a Roll call”.  Now click on the “Add Reply” button. Type in your name (you can skip over password) and  post your message to answer the roll call. That’s it! If you don’t want to post your name on the roll call, you can also send me an e-mail and I will add your name.

Once the roll call is complete, I will be updating the “Heroes” page with pictures of all the Hunters, so if we don’t have your picture, e-mail me one. You can also send me any unusual screen shots that you would like to see posted. I’ll do what I can to get them up.

Other information from the Guild meeting will be posted later after I get a chance to talk to the council members. Until then, fight on Hunters and make us proud!

-Mulen
