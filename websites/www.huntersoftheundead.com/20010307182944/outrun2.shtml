Additional Techniques

    Now Rolis has done an excellent job explaining the "strafe-run," but I have a couple of extra tricks that I think might be quite helpful. So make sure you've read everthing Rolis has said before attempting my additional methods.

    (Note: this has been written with assumption that your keyboard and mouse are in Defalt mode)

    Now one of the problems with the keyboard strafe is turning. The moment you have to turn you slow down even if its just for a split second, and that mob on your tail NEVER slows down. That turn to go around a corner may mean you take up to 5 or more hits before you can out distance it again, and if it does enough damage to you so that you slow down.....that might be it. So how do your turn and still keep you speed? Simple. Instead of using the ctrl key to strafe, use your right mouse button because while you run diagonal you can use your mouse to turn by moving it side to side (make sure you keep holding your 2 diection keys to move). Now at first the view is a little disorentating but once your used to it you'll find its a lot easier to get away from that fight gone bad. Once you are well practiced at this version of the stafe-run you'll find you can use it in any zone with a excellent results. Just remember to recognize that fight gone bad and run BEFORE your speed is slowed because of damage otherwise its all over right there.

    Take it from a cleric who solos quite a bit. Soloing clerics have fights go bad on them quite often. This method works!

    -Ishtar
