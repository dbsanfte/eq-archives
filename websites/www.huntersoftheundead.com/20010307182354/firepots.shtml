This is a guide, submitted by our friend Moknin, on the Firepot room in Timorous Deep.


    The fastest way to get to the Firepot Room is to jump off the boat when you get remotely close. It will always be a long swim no matter when you jump off the boat, just some points it is shorter. The location of the Firepot Room is Neg 12260, pos 4665 in Timorous Deep. Yes very far. Once you get close to that loc, you will see four pillars pointing towards the sky. That is what you want. Swim down to the bottom of the ocean, there will be a hole, swim through it. You drop down into this room with a bunch of fires. Each fire will teleport you to one of the major cities in Norrath. I have them all mapped out with pics of the fires. There are rumors that an ice dragon spawns there, but the guy that showed me says he has been going to that room for a year or so and never ran into one. The bad thing is you can not bind in there, actually you can't bind anywhere in Timorus Deep. Well that is the Firepot Room, enjoy!

    -Moknin
