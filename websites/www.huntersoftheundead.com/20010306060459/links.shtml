These are some general Everquest links that I visit often. They contain some of the most unique and helpful resources on the game. If you have any links you'd like here, just e-mail me and I'll put it up.
    Caster's Realm-My favorite Everquest site. This site has information on classes, spells, skills, and is updated constantly. I go here when I want to know what's happening in Everquest.

    Allakhazam's Magical Realm-Another great site, this one has a huge Quest database, and a very good item database too.

    EQ Atlas-Really good zone maps. These maps include gridlines and a legend for key locations. There's also a little guide for each zone, which includes unique items and characters in the zone as well as things to look out for. I have books full of these maps in front of me now. (How sad is that?)

    EQ Vault- Good news site, lots of information about events, news, and other general Everquest related stuff.

    Eq'lizer-A good equipment site, updated often.







This site was written for Internet Explorer 4.0 or later, in 1024x768 resolution, in 16 bit colors. While I think most of it works in Netscape, I recommend that you use Internet Explorer. I will not be updating the site with Netscape in mind, so if it looks bad in Netscape, start using IE.
