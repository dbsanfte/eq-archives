     Wednesday, June 6, 2001

Hail Hunters. Well, it's me, Mulen again running the website. Aremos did a great job, but he has moved on. The Hunters have grown a lot since last I posted here and there are a lot of exciting changes on the horizon. We are developing a new council organization that promises to be more responsive to your needs and suggestions. The Heroes Page will have a whole new look, too. Their is evil afoot in Norrath and you can read all about that on the message board. The message board has grown and is now the primary place to get the latest information on what is happening with the Hunters. This page will be updated from time to time when there are changes to the site or when I just happen to be bored and feel like waxing philisophic. OK. time for my pitch. The new structure for the Heroes page will list everyone by their primary character and then list all their ALTs in the same entry. The thumbnail pic will still link to your personal info/bio page. Any info you want posted there can be sent to me through email, ICQ, the Roll call on the message board, or in game. I will post all ALT character pictures on these pages. Rolis then me and then Aremos got frustrated trying to keep this page up to date. I'm not going to make that same mistake again. These pages will only be as up to date as you make them. If I get the info, I will post it. If I don't, well, you get the idea. Anyways, it's good to be back working on the site. I'll see you all in Norrath!

Mulen

     Tuesday, April 2, 2001

Hello Hunters.  There has been a couple of major events the past few days - First of all - on Friday, March 30, 2001 I, Aremos accepted the title of Templar et Luminus Dominus.  Thanks goes to Jacor and Mulen for their recommendations and their trust.  I am more then happy to receive suggestions and ideas from my fellow Hunters  about the web site.  Please let me know what you would like to see on the site.

In a more interesting note - Trojaner was married to Felinie on Saturday, March 31, 200.  The wedding took place out doors in Greater Feydark, under the wizards' spire.  It was a beautiful ceremony, after the rude orc pawns, that tried to crash the ceremony, were "dealt with."  A good time was had by all that attended.  Hopefully pictures of this wedding will soon be available for all to see.

That is all of the news that I have to report for now.  Please feel free to e-mail me (Aremos) with any information that you would like to see here. 

May Tunare embrace us all,

Aremos

 

     Wednesday, March 28, 2001

Hello Hunters. Well, we survived two raids into Unrest and had an enjoyable meeting in Freeport since last I wrote here. I look forward to seeing pictures of this joyous event and maybe reading a report about it in the Tales of Norrath Forum on the message board.

Hunters will begin to gather at the tollbooth in West Commonlands about a half hour early. Stop by the Events Forum on the message board for the particulars and to let us know if you will attend. That’s about it for now. Keep an eye on the message board for all the latest information. Think of it as your daily newspaper and this as your weekly newsmagazine. 

Strength and Honor,

     Wednesday, March 14, 2001

 

Sorry it’s been a while since I updated here, but you can always keep up to date on what is going on with the Hunters by going to the message board.  Anyways, it’s great to see lots of new members in the guild. As I meet everyone, I am encouraged to see that you are joining because you identify with our dedication to service and honor.  Keep up the good work. Our name and our reputation are a result of your courage and your deeds. Also, don’t be afraid to tell us about your adventures on the Tales of Norrath Forum on the message board.

There are some upcoming events I wanted to tell you about. It looks like this weekend the Hunters will be descending upon the Estate of Unrest. It is on the continent of Faydwer and is reached by crossing the desolate area called Dagnor’s Cauldron on the southernmost path in the Butcherblock Mountains. It has been overrun with scores of undead creatures and we will be going in to rid the area of this disgusting horde. There is work to be done here for all levels of Hunters. The younger adepts can work in the yard outside the estate. Those in their 20’s can work inside the house or right outside while the higher level members can work their way down to the basement and try to find the source of this pestilence that has plagued the area. Check the Events Forum on the message board to get information on date and time for the raid. Oh, and take lots of screenshots for the website.

Monday, there will be a meeting for ALL of the Hunters of the Undead at the Freeport City Hall in North Freeport. It is building number 6 on the EQ atlas map. Please try to be there so that we can get a good accounting of all of the members. I will also be taking picture for the website for those who haven’t sent one to me.

Well, that’s about it for now. Please keep an eye on the message board for any changes and updates and send me your character biographies for your personal web pages in the Heroes section. I’ll update these pages with anything you want there (pics, links, etc), so just let me know.

Thanks,

Mulen

    Thursday, February 15, 2001 

 

 

Hail Hunters! The council met on Friday last and discussed, among other things, the future direction of the guild. Based on these discussions, a change to the guild laws and charter was drafted and put to the council for review and comment. As no council member has dissented, I now present the new charter to the guild. You can (and should) review it, as well as the minor changes to the rank system here.  I welcome any comments or suggestions concerning these changes. There will be a message thread in the General forum for this purpose, or you can e-mail me directly.

 

Other news from the council meeting:

 

-          Priestess Ishtar has graciously accepted the position of Templar et Constructus. If you read the Ranks and Charter page link above, you know what that means. You can read more about her plans, as well as upcoming raids and events in the Events forum on the message board. She now has moderator control of that forum. Make sure you check it out as she has a little something cooled up for this weekend. (And I’m not talking about her famous rabbit stew)

 

-          Details of the Guild Dues and a new Lotto were finalized. Lord Bartikus has agreed to administer these programs and even has a young subject in Freeport who maintains a bank account for this purpose. You can read all about it here. Contact Lord Bartikus or Lady Sinnfull when you see them online. They will be happy to assist you.

 

-          The roll call is proceeding nicely, but some of you still haven’t responded. If this includes you, you can respond here. Also, if you want your picture and pertinent info listed on the Heroes page or your e-mail address listed on the E-mail page, send it to me and I will get it posted.

 

The council will be meeting again this Friday, so if there are any issues, as always, you can email them to me or post them on the message board. I always check both just before council meetings. You could also bring them up yourself at the regularly scheduled guild meetings that take place every other Saturday night. Attendance at last week’s meeting was a little weak. This was partly my fault as I did not post an announcement here until the day before the meeting. In the future, I will try to announce the time and place of meetings and other events several days in advance so you can make plans to attend. I would also ask your assistance in getting the word out when you are in the game. Just do a “/who all hunters” to see who is around and then don’t be shy about using the /guild channel to inform those who might not have checked in here about upcoming guild events.

 

Well, that’s about it for now. Much of the site has been updated, so take a minute and browse around. As always, I welcome your comments and suggestions.

 

Thanks,

Mulen

Web Site Update

Hail Hunters!  I am Mulen, a Hunter of the Undead and a Paladin of the 27th circle. I am going to try to keep the website updated. We had a meeting on Monday night as announced below. About ten Hunters attended and much was discussed. One of the first priorities was to perform a “roll call” to get an accurate count of the membership of the guild. To accomplish this, our fearless leader, Jacor, has started a “Roll Call” message string on our Message board. To answer the call, simply go to the message board by following the link to the left; go to the “General” forum; then go to “a Roll call”.  Now click on the “Add Reply” button. Type in your name (you can skip over password) and  post your message to answer the roll call. That’s it! If you don’t want to post your name on the roll call, you can also send me an e-mail and I will add your name.

Once the roll call is complete, I will be updating the “Heroes” page with pictures of all the Hunters, so if we don’t have your picture, e-mail me one. You can also send me any unusual screen shots that you would like to see posted. I’ll do what I can to get them up.

Other information from the Guild meeting will be posted later after I get a chance to talk to the council members. Until then, fight on Hunters and make us proud!
