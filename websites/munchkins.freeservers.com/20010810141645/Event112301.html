Raid on Befallen

Its Raid time!

When: Tuesday, January 23rd 2001 7:30 PM Eastern
Where: Befallen

Captains and Corporals please show up 30-60 minutes prior to start time.

All members please don't be late. It is recommended that all melee classes to get a bind in West Freeport prior to the raid, and if you can not get a bind before the raid; please get one the night before. Any member that is on line at the same time of raid will be expected to attend. It is also recommended that you start heading towards Befallen and camping near by a day or two prior to raid.

These rules are established to avoid any unnecessary delays and out of respect towards all members that are participating.

Thank you.
