The Munchkins of Mayhem is for the small races ONLY as the name implies (sorry no wood elves). To be eligible for membership, there are some criteria to meet:

  • You must be a Dwarf, Halfing or Gnome as our name implies. We are proud to be the smallest people in Norrath
  • You must be at least level 15 to be considered.
  • Be a mature and friendly individual. We are primarily an 18+ guild, and would like to keep it as such. Being part of Munchkins of Mayhem is a privilege, not a right.
  • Play clean and fair. No KS'ing, no ninja-looting, absolutely no begging, etc.
  • Each new member will have a probation period of 3 weeks. Within the 3 weeks period we have the right to remove you from the guild if we do not believe you are MoM material.


As a member of the Munchkins of Mayhem, there are many benefits:

  • We like to keep guild chat going, as well as frequent the message boards often and most of our members are well experienced players.
  • Most any of us will bend over backwards to help out a brother, regardless of the circumstances. Be it an issue of needing equipment to assisting somebody on a difficult quest.
  • Our guild raids and events are geared towards helping out the majority of the members planning to attend. We don't expect every member to be able to attend events, but we do demand your attendance if you are online at the time of the event. All events are scheduled weeks and sometimes months in advance, with great care and planning.
  • Our message board is a great place to leave tips & suggestions, voice any concerns & such you may have. If you are needing information about a particular item or dungeon, odds are somebody will have been there, done that. False and misleading information is moderated, and will be edited for content or deleted outright.

If you want to be one of us, post a little something about yourself, the times you generally play, current level and class, etc. We will get back to you in-game, and see if you are what we are all about.
