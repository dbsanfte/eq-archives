Quick Update. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .September 14, 2001 --  Tigole Bitties

From The Official Everquest Site:

The EverQuest Events Team announced today that the Best of the Best - Enchanters events for; Morell Thule, Brell Serilis, Saryrn, Fennin Ro, Rallos Zek, The Nameless, and the finals conducted on the Test server have been rescheduled due to September 14 being proclaimed as a National Day of Mourning. The new dates for the Best of the Best – Enchanters events to take place on the servers mentioned are listed below. For more information about official EverQuest Events, check out the Event’s Page and the Arena forums.

New Dates:

Monday, September 17th

Morell Thule 4 PM PST
Brell Serilis 4 PM PST
Saryrn 5 PM PST

Tuesday, September 18th

Fennin Ro 4 PM
Rallos Zek 4 PM
The Nameless 5 PM

Saturday, September 22nd

Finals on Test 11 AM PST
