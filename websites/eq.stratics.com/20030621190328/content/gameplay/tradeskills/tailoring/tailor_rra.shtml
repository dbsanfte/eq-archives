CRYSTALLINE SILK. This silk comes from crystal spiders.

2 crystalline silk = 1 crystalline silk swatch.

Tis silk can be worn by all races/classes. These recipes can be combined in the sewing kit or coldain tanners kit. 

cap, mask, collar, mantle, wrist = 1 silk swatch + 1 silver thread + 1 pattern

sash, sleeves, gloves, slippers = 2 silk swatches + 1 silver thread + 1 pattern

shirt, pants, cloak = 3 silk swatches + 1 silver thread + 1 pattern 

CURED SILK. This is only worn by monks. As with raw silk you use silk swatches, however you also use a brewed drink called heady kiolas. Heady kiolas take some skill to make, so look up brewing before you decide to make cured silk.

  • AC is same as woven or 1ac better in general, all weighs 0.1
  • Wearable only by monks
  • Prices vary...I've seen 15gp-2pp per AC a lot
  • Heady kiolas (combine in brew barrel) = 2 packets kiola sap + 1 flask of water + 1 bottle (the flask of water must be bought not foraged).

DARK SILK. This is used by necromancers only. The spell gather of shadows is a necromancer spell. It isn't trivial to make at 40, but it works.

To get a Shadow Silk, you combine a Silk Swatch, a Shadow Wolf Pelt, and a scroll for the necro spell Gather Shadows (they cost around 4-5pp).

dark silk = 1 silk swatch + 1 high quality shadow wolf pelt + 1 gather of shadows

large dark silk = 1 silk swatch + 2 high quality shadow wolf pelts + 1 gather of shadows

inky dark silk = 1 silk swatch + 1 high quality shadow wolf pelt + 1 gather of shadows

RAW SILK. Raw silk is made of silk swatches from large spiders. They can found in almost every zone.

  • AC is the same as woven in general, all weighs 0.4
  • Wearable by the 4 pure casters and monks
  • Generally sells for 1pp per AC (on my server)
  • Trivial at 36 

1 spider silk + 1 spider silk (combine in sewing kit) = 1 silk swatch 

    Veil, collar, belt, mantle, waistband, headband,

WUS FIGHTING ARMOR. Only worn by beastlords & monks. Again this uses heady kiolas(see above), with vial of viscous mana. Enchanters make these with their semi-new level 12 spell, Thicken Mana.

The 2 components required by the enchanter are a Pearl (the same kind used in jewelry) and a poison vial, obtainable only by pottery as far as I know. I see enchanters easily getting away with charging 10pp+ on this stuff. 

- 4ac, weighs 0.1, magical, greater lightsource 

- Prices really vary here...no idea
