Welcome all ye valiant knights of the holy order!

The Paladins who serve the mighty deities of the Norrathian world are strong, valiant, and skilled in both the ways of the sword and the magic arts. Paladins follow a code of honor laid down to them by their respective deities. A good Paladin will never break this code.

Paladins are gifted with the ability to wear heavy plate armor, making them able to withstand massive attacks from the enemy, in addition to being highly skilled in the arts of weaponry but not to the extent of the mighty warrior. Paladins make up for this small lack of offensive ability however with their ability to use spells from the clerical line. Paladins are able to heal, buff, and resurrect their fellow compatriots in times of need making them a well balanced class for either solo or group play.

If it’s a good combination of magic and steel you’re looking for, the Paladin is the right choice for you.

Wednesday May 1 2002
Paladin questions
The Paladin - 1:13 PM PDT
Absor stopped by the EQ Developers Corner to comment on some questions pertaining to Paladins. Here’s what he answers:
Hello,
First off have to say thank you for the changes to hyrbrids combat tables, 2h weap changes, Healing Wave of Prexus, etc (over last several months) that have put Paladins squarely back into a desireable category for both raids and exp grinding. Sincerely appreciated.

Was wondering if you can say anything about the status of fixing Thunder of Karana back to something useful (as the change to a 100 pt mana drain for was touted as a temp fix for the balance problems created with the original silence spell).

Also, Divine Stun was changed to a clickable AA ability with a 30 second reuse (rather than being passive being on all the time); and one of the developers/designers had come on a Paladin board and implied that this too more of a temporary change while something better could be come up with.

Any update, status, anything on either or both of these issues would be greatly appreciated by myself and all of the Paladin community.

Thanks in advance!

Pankrat
Crusader of Tarew Marr

For now I wouldn’t expect any changes to hybrid spells or abilities that aren’t directly related to pure caster spells or abilities (or bug fixes). The team is concentrating on caster balance right now. When that’s complete we’ll decide if a pass over hybrids is needed (considering that many changes to casters will likely affect hybrids as well). Fiddling with hybrids and casters at the same time is just not a wise idea.

Alan

Thanks, Alan!
