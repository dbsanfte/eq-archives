Stratics Plus is Released!
Stratics Network Announcement - 10:45 AM PDT


[ Stratics Plus Registration ]

Announcing The Official Launch of Stratics Plus!

For over 5 years Stratics has stood as a comprehensive, free service to the Massively Multiplayer community.

This will not change.

Therefore we will not, nor will we ever, make you pay for the Stratics content and services that you currently enjoy. This applies not only to our present websites, but to our coverage of future titles as well. Stratics is and always will be a free service to the Massively Multiplayer community.

So what we're doing is offering you an option - an alternative - that will prevent all pop-ups, flash ads, and obtrusive advertising from disrupting your experience at Stratics. In addition, we will be making available to our Members comprehensive databases that we've never before had the resources to put together, as well as enhanced forum features, site skinning and other new services that go above and beyond that which we currently provide... [ Our Pledge ]

Advertising vs. Subscription

Why does Stratics now offer a Subscription service if our stated goal is to remain "free"? Well, the reality of the situation is that Stratics is no longer able to support itself with Advertising revenue alone. We've been forced to seek out other options in order to stay alive. Due to this, we have launched our new Subscription service, presented as an option to those of you who would like to assist us with our growth toward a more complete, database-driven network.

The decision to move towards this type of service was not an easy one. Since our launch in 1997, we've done our best to remain 100% free. Unfortunately, the Advertising market has not recovered from the downturn it's taken over the past few years--in fact, it has gotten progressively and consistently worse.

In the past, money earned from a single banner ~ such as the one located at the top of this page ~ was more than sufficient to keep Stratics online. Over time, however, the Advertising market has steadily declined and we've been forced to run more and more intrusive forms of Advertising ~ such as the dreaded Pop-up ~ merely in order to survive.

We continue to deliver more impressions, but earn less and less.

Advertising no longer covers our hosting costs.

An alternative has become necessary.

The Stratics Plus Service

Finally, we're now able to provide you with your chance to banish into the Nether Realms every single one of those annoying Pop-up, Pop-under, JavaScript, Flash and Take-over ads that appear on Stratics. By signing up and becoming a Member of Stratics Plus, you will not only help support our network by providing us an alternative to the failing Advertising market; you'll benefit from exclusive access to a number of new features and databases available only to Members:

  • No More Invasive Advertising!
    The very first thing that you'll notice about becomnig a Stratics Plus member is the removal of all advertisements from our network!

    - The removal of all intrusive and third party agency advertisements (i.e. UGO) from our pages. This means no more pop-ups, pop-unders, takeover ads, flash ads, etc.

    - Well also be removing the standard 468x60, 120x600 and similar ads that are currently deliver by third party ad agencies.

    - The removal of ads from the forum headers as well.

    - Stratics Easter Banner Hunt! If you find any banners that have escaped our notice, just send us the offending page address and you'll be entered into a drawing to win signed memorabilia from OSI!

  • Comprehensive Database Development, Exclusive to our Members
    We are just beginning the rollout of many new comprehensive online relational databases for the wide variety of MMOG titles we cover. These databases will provide in-depth information on items, quests, spells, factions, and much, much more. This will be an ongoing development process where we continue to release new databases and expanded database features over time. So as this work progresses, the value of these databases will increase tenfold.

    To begin with we will be rolling out our EQ Items Database that has thousands of EQ Items covered. You can utilize standard or advanced search features to obtain detailed information about each item. In the coming weeks, well be releasing a new add-on to this database that further expands the feature set considerably. Please refer to our Stratics Plus development calendar for details on what is on the schedule. But just to give you an idea, the plan is to begin with EverQuest Databases and Dark Age of Camelot Databases. On the calendar youll see information relating to each component that will be released over the scheduled development period.

    This is an extremely exciting new expansion on the content we provide for our web sites and so we hope youll find them to be as useful as we expect you to. This is an open-ended series of development projects and our Plus members will find them to be quite useful.

  • Free Stratics Plus e-mail aliases for every Member! (To be added in the coming months)

  • Members-Only Status and Perks
    Becoming a Stratics Plus member not only provides access to special databases and the removal of advertisements, you also gain access to an expanding number of new features within our forums! I say expanding because the list below is only the tip of the iceberg with regard to what features we offer to our Plus members.

    By joining as a Stratics Plus member, you will have the following unique features added to your account in our forums:

    - A special icon indicating your premium status within the forums.

    - Access to post polls in your forum posts. Currently reserved for moderators and administrators only.

    - Access to special premium smiley icons to use in your posts within the forums.

    - A new search option has been added to the main forum display to allow for direct searches for posts by specific forum members. This option is still available to standard users, but the location of the search form adds a level of convenience for Stratics Plus members.

    We have many new features in development as well! Be sure to watch for more announcements as they are rolled out.

    As a Stratics Plus member you will also obtain access to some exclusive areas that tend to require file transfers. These include exclusive access to online movies, demos, and more.

  • Access to the Flashlink Network of Premium Sites
    By joining the Stratics Plus membership program you also will be joining Flashlink, a network of gaming web sites that all offer exclusive content to Flashlink members. This means youll not only benefit form obtaining special access across the Stratics Network, but a great many other web sites as well.

    To list a few:

    EQMaps
    EQ Maps is your complete EverQuest resource for items, NPC's, landmarks, quests, and maps in the vast world of Norrath. Built on a dynamic, intuitive, and easy to use navigation system, EQ Maps has easily become a fan among the elite EverQuest players.

    EQSpoilers
    EQ Spoilers is the most advanced EverQuest database in existence. Quests, Equipment, Bestiary, Zones, Spells and Faction are all 100% integrated into one comprehensive database. This unique set up allows for a level of detail not available on other websites.

    DAoCSpoilers
    DAoC Spoilers features the most advanced Dark Age of Camelot database on the internet. Quests, bestiary, equipment, class and skill guides, atlas, and maps are all integrated in to one gigantic database.

    EQLizer
    EQ'Lizer is proud to be the original and most complete EverQuest spoiler site on the internet. Boasting a completely searchable database with nearly 8,000 items, over 2,000 spells, hundreds of quests, and so much more, it is easy to see why hundreds of thousands of players visit this site every month to get "the edge" on the game.

    As more and more sites join the Flashlink network youll also have access to those sites as well. So with each new Flashlink site added, the value of your membership increases that much more! [ Stratics Plus FAQ ]
In addition, every subscriber to the Stratics Plus service will receive one month's free subscription to Time of Defiance, a great new downloadable MMORTS from Nicelycrafted Entertainment. As if that weren't enough, the first 1,000 subscribers will be entered into a drawing to win a brand new GeForce Video Card!

All of the Stratics Plus features are ongoing premium services that will grow and evolve over time. You have my pledge that we will do our very best to provide you, as a Member, with customized, on-demand features and options that will consistently expand upon our current list of membership benefits.

As a result of your subscription to our new service, the network will not only stay online, but will continue to flourish and launch new features according to what you, as a Member of Stratics Plus, would like to see added to our sites.

Now is only the beginning. With your assistance, Stratics will enter a much more stable phase and, as a result, roll out bigger and better content n' features well into the future.

I sincerely thank you for your patronage,

Ryan ~Den Dragon~ Findley

    Weird Occurance in the Puzzle
    GeneralNews - 12:20 PM PDT
    After browsing some forums yestarday, I came across this post on EQ Druids from jtoast. He pointed out that the directory in which the EverQuest.com image was stored was "esbhsppn". He then went up or down one letter in the alphabet:
    dragroom

    esbhsppn

    ftcitqqo

    Now, if you look at the first one, "dragroom", it spells Moorgard backwards. As some of you know, Moorgard runs Mobhunter.com site. So maybe this was a coincidence.

    So I decided to test this new theory on today's image. The image was located in "itjmfbc". So I did the letter change and came up with this one:
    hsileab
    And that backwards, is Baelish, who runs EQ.Castersrealm.com.

    It is hard to tell whether this has to do with the puzzle, or if they are just screwing with us.

    Update: EQ Druids already had this information before I posted. I thought I had beat everyone :-)

    Frozen Mouse At EULA Screen
    GeneralNews - 10:29 PM PDT
    One problem that has plagued more than a few players is that they are getting trapped at the EULA screen. Their mouse is frozen in the upper left hand corner and they can not use their keyboard. Suraky has found a solution to this problem:
    Go to eqclient.ini and set windowedmode=TRUE, so that all parts of EQ once again agree. When the EULA screen comes up, it may be a small window.

    Then you can Alt+Enter to get EQ mouse control and carry on into the game, or restart EQ and in my case it was functioning properly in full screen mode without having adjusted anything else.
