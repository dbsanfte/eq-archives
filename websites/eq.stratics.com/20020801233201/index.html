Gamer.tv Documentary
CommunityNews - 12:22 PM PDT
Gamer.tv sent us the following:
Do you think you have an interesting gaming life? How far will you go to play a game?

The worlds of Massively Multiplayer Gaming have become an international phenomenon and Gamer.tv is producing a series that covers the world of MMRPG and their immense popularity with gamers. But we need your help!

If you are at GenCon or live in the Chicago/Milwaulkee area and you think you are interesting enough to be in a documentary which looks at the popularity of MMRPG and the people who play them, then contact us at audition@gamer.tv, and tell us tell us how you have carved your name into gaming legend.
More about Gamer.tv:

Gamer.tv is the games television content division of TWI which specialises in creating bespoke programming for games publishers, developers and broadcasters worldwide. Having produced over 700 hours of original content, Gamer.tv is fast becoming the largest producer of games television in the world.
The flagship show Gamer.tv is a dynamic half-hour magazine show aimed at casual gamers. Game Sauce is the destination for what gamers demand most; undiluted technicolour games action with the latest preview and reviews from around the world.

Using ground-breaking technology which allows us to place virtual cameras inside the game, Lock n Load fuses online games and sports programming to create a unique television experience with live commentary never seen on any other network.

The gamer.tv website is a narrowband and broadband site offering a suite of online games including massively multiplayer role playing games, first person shooters, and flash/java games. The site also provides rich editorial features, interviews and reviews of the latest titles alongside video-on-demand, competitions and community tools.

Part of TWI who are

TWI, the television arm of Mark McCormack's IMG is the largest independent producer, packager and distributor of sports programming in the world. With production centers in New York, London, Hong Kong, Sydney, New Delhi and 20 offices worldwide, TWI annually produces and distributes more than 6000 hours of original programming to more than 200 territories and broadcasts sports television and special events to every corner of the earth.

Alternatively visit our site at www.gamer.tv

Kind regards,

Neil

Wednesday July 31 2002
Custom UI Reminder
GeneralNews - 3:21 PM PDT
If you are experiencing problems when you try to play EQ it might be because when the game was patched last night it updated some of the UI Files. But they were only updated in the default directory. So to prevent problems you need to copy the following files from default into your other UI Folders:
EQUI.xml
EQUI_ActionsWindow.xml
EQUI_Animations.xml
EQUI_BazaarSearchWnd.xml
EQUI_BazaarWnd.xml
EQUI_OptionsWindow.xml
EQUI_SelectorWnd.xml
EQUI_SkillsWindow.xml
EQUI_SocialEditWnd.xml
EQUI_TextEntryWnd.xml
EQUI_TrainWindow.xml
gemicons02.tga
SIDL.xml
window_pieces02.tga
That should fix some of the problems you are having if you have your own skin. More info can be found here. Good Luck!
 - Cosmoo

New Ranger Guide
Class Portal News - 9:12 PM PDT
The first installment of a comprehensive guide to the armor and weapon quests available to new rangers has just been posted in the Ranger Portal. If you're a wood elf working on your ranger newbie quests, or are considering starting a career with the Faydark's Champions, then go take a look at this guide.

Other sections of this guide, detailing the item recipes, statistics, and tips for hunting the materials you'll need, will be added for the other ranger races soon. Ilmarinen the ranger is even now working with Megosh Thistlethorn and the rest of the Storm Reapers of Rivervale to gather information about the halfling ranger quests. When his work there is finished, he'll depart immediately for Surefall Glade.

If you'd like to help, if you have completed some or all of these quests, you're quite welcome to email any information you might have—item statistics and resource-gathering tips would be most appreciated—to anchor@stratics.com. You will, of course, receive full credit in the finished guide.

Thanks, and good hunting to you!

The Druid Portal
The Druid - 10:24 AM PDT
Cresting yet another hill, rounding yet another tree, you find yourself erupting into a clearing in the middle of the dense forest behind the Stratics complex which you’ve been jogging through for hours. The sudden sunlight is blinding and you throw your hands up to shield your eyes while they adjust to the glare. The odd yet familiar scent of mana flows into your nostrils…

In the midst of the clearing, a stone circle emits the quiet hum of hidden power. A group of elves and humans turn their heads at your noisy entrance and, though taking careful notice of you, seem unsurprised by your presence. A nearby wolf growls quietly, silenced gently by one of the elves who scratches it behind the ears before approaching slowly. A wood-elf of slight build, his blue eyes and their piercing gaze flow over you. A wise grin crosses his face…

Greetings, traveler. Welcome to this, our haven – the Druid Portal.

Here I hope to help provide the wisdom of ages of nature. To give insight to the knowledge that spans both the elder races and the younger. That of our deities and my brothers and sisters. That of the Druids.

Please feel free to explore this Portal and add to this wisdom. Come and visit our community and share stories or your own insight!

If I can help in any way, please feel free to contact me . I serve as a guide for this realm.

Enjoy your stay!
 - SirRubi
