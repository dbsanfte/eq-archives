GeneralNews - 11:37 AM PDT

Augmentation Restrictions
GeneralNews - 12:17 PM PDT
Here's the announcement from EQLive:

Remember, when you combine an augmentation and an item, the result will have all of the restrictions of both items.
I'd like to stress this specifically when it comes to worn location.

If you have an augmentation that can only be used in the wrist location and you apply it to an item that can be worn on the wrist and neck, the resultant item will ONLY be wearable on the wrist.

Please keep that in mind when augmenting your items.

Alan
Be careful!
