EverQuest Stratics Project Manager's Letter

All you do is call me,
I'll be anything you need...
-- Peter Gabriel

It's time for a change.

After more than two years of dedicated service, Greebo has stepped down as Project Manager of EverQuest Stratics. While you, the daily reader, might not care much about this, to Stratics and the EQ community in general this is a huge loss. Greebo did more to further communication between Verant and the various fansites around the Internet than even we on his staff know, as well as being a fantastic person. He will be missed, and all of us from EQSS thank him for all the work he did for us.

But, we move on. Greebo laid fantastic framework for EQSS to grow and prosper, and myself and my staff plan to do just that. There are a lot of exciting things coming, and we plan to make you a part of that. More on that later.

After Greebo left and I took over, the first and foremost task was to fill the vacant Interactive Coordinator position. I thought a long time about this. I wanted to choose someone who had been with us for a long time, still played EQ and enjoyed it, and in general contributed to the board every time he/she said something. After a bit of *bop* thought, I was able to only *bop* *bop* come up with one name, really, that *bop* stood out above the rest as far as that was concerned. I asked, and she has graciously accepted her new position. So, please join me in welcoming Samira as your new Interactive Dog...er Coordinator.

With that out of the way, it was time for us to sit down and hash out where we wanted EQSS to go. What could we better offer you, the reader, to make your EverQuest experience better? After a lot of thought, we came up with the major areas we plan to focus on. Many of them directly involve reader/viewer participation, and all allow you to further your EverQuest experience. Already you will notice changes to the cosmetics of the site - hopefully it is now a lot easier to read and take in. Let me know. As well we already have some new content, including The House at Clue Corner, a MMOG editorial penned every week by Winter from Otherworld Express. Expect more original, fun content soon to follow.

So, what exactly is in store for this site? Read on:

BETTER NEWS. If you look at the new front page of EQSS, you'll see we've split our news into two categories: General and Community. General news is when Verant speaks up - you'll find Absor, Aradune, Abashi, and all your other Verantites posts here. It's also the place to find patch messages, downtime updates, and anything else related to the servers or the mechanics of the game itself.

Community news is where you, the reader, come in. This is a place to have your events noticed, be it weddings, an open Plane raid, a bazaar, or whatever interesting tidbits you come up with while travelling Norrath. Getting married? Let us know. Leading a first-time Planar raid? Let us know. Having a roleplaying event? We love those too. You can never have too much community, so let us know what's going on and we'll help you spread the word.

Submitting your news is the same as always - simply send your findings to our News Team and we'll get right on it.

BETTER CONTENT. A MMOG is nothing without stats, data, and information, and this will be a major focus of our Editorial team in the very near future. I won't lie to you - a lot of our information is out of date or incomplete. That will be changing, and expanding. Stat tables? You got it. Character sheets and playguides? Yep. Fan fiction and editorials? Definitley. I am a writer first and a web-junkie second, and love fan fiction. Look for a special section featuring your writings and stories soon. Further down the road we'll explore some art submissions.

Of course all this great content cannot happen without you. If you have a story, or a picture, or a guide, please submit it to our Lead Editor Onnie.

BETTER FORUMS. The Forums are one of the best parts of EQSS because it encourages communication. They are in the very capable hands of Samira now, but look for us to expand upon forum usage soon. This ties directly into our Community news - if you have an event, you can post it on our Event Calendar for that server board, and discuss and plan more events on the very board itself.

This is a big job, and we need your help to do it. I am looking for dedicated people for all our departments, but especially news. If you're dedicated and think you're a good "board troll", going around picking out what Absor and Co. have to say, or have a good eye for community news among the hundreds of relevant EQ sites out there, I want you on our team. If you're interested, either in news or any other of our departments, please drop me an email.

If it sounds like we're trying to be all-in-one, we are. My goal is to make EQSS your One Stop Shop (tm) for everything EverQuest. I know that I personally hate jumping from site to site, just to get the daily dose of information I need. My goal is to make EQSS the *only* site you will need for your EQ News, Forums, and Content. All the while having a good time - after all, isn't that why we play these games in the first place?

New look, new information, new attitude. Welcome to the beginning of the new EQSS.

Thundolfe
EQSS Project Manager
