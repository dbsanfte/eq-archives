Beastlord Quests

By: merik

Iksar - Barbarian - Troll - Ogre - Vah Shir

IKSAR:
 
1st Quest: (level 1 +)
 
When you turn in your newbie note to Prime Patriarch Vuzx will ask you to bring back 4 Leech husks.  Swamp leeches can be found in the Swamp of No Hope and in the Sewers of East Cabilis. Husks seem to be an uncommon drop, at least when I was hunting them, but they are sellable so check the Vendors,
 
Reward: Patriarch's Bracer (AC 3, HP 2)
 
 
2nd Quest: (Level 3+)
 

After you turn in the Leech Husks he will give you a harder task. Now you need to find 3 Spiderling Silks and a Scarab Carapace. You can find both in the Field of Bone. The Carapace drops off of carrion Hatchlings and emerald Scarabs. Bonecrawler Hatchlings will sometimes wander near the newbie area, but there is also a mound a little ways to the left of the Newbie area that they hang around. If you go in you will find lots of them.
 
When you give your trainer the Items, make sure the Spider silks are not stacked.
 
Reward: Patriarch's Cap (AC 4, STA 1)
 
 
Quest 3: (Level 5+)
 
After getting your helm he will tell you "you do well against pests, but now try foes that
can think" and sends you after Goblin Watchers. You can find them in Lake of Ill Omen and Warsilk Woods.  I would go after the Watchers in the Lake, Warsilk woods is very easy to get lost in and there are goblin casters kind of close to were the Watchers wander. (In the Lake there are just Watchers and Scouts.)
 
It pretty easy to find the torches, most Goblin Watchers have one and it has a visible
Graphic so you can tell by looking.
 

Reward: Patriarch's Leggings (AC 5, HP 2)
 
 
Quest 4: (Level 7+)

Your Trainer sends you out to find 2 Sarnak Broodling Fangs and 2 Goblin Hunter Javelins. They can both be found in the Lake of Ill Omen. The hunters can be found in one of the ruins near the newbie grounds or wandering around past that. The Javelin has a graphic so you can tell when they have one.
 
Sarnak broodlings are an uncommon spawn and the Fang seems to be a rare drop, but you'll be able to find them around the Statue on the hill. Killing Sabertooth kits makes them appear faster.
 
Reward: Patriarch's Claw (5/25)
 
 
Quest 5: (Level 11+)
 
When you return with the items your trainer will be impressed and send you after bigger
fish... err frogs. He tells you to bring back 4 Froglok Tuk Warrior Bucklers.  The Tuk warriors can be found in the Swamp of No hope near the broken head of an Iksar Statue. It's on the Island across from the newbie area. They seem to be an uncommon spawn and the Shields are uncommon to rare.
 
Reward: Patriarch Tunic (AC 11 +3 Str)
 
 
Barbarian:
 
Quest 1: (Level 1+)
 
When you turn in your newbie note, Hoyce Mactaegan will ask you for 1 white wolf skin.  Bring it to him and he'll give you:
 
Reward: Field Priests Bracer (AC 3 HP +2)
 
 
Quest 2: (Level 3+)
 
Now he will send you out to get 3 Silk Threads and 1 Tailored Skullcap

Reward: Field Priest Scullcap (AC 4 STA +1)
 
 
Quest 3: (Level 5+)
 
Hoyce Mactaegan now needs 4 Goblin Ice Necklaces.
 
Reward: Field Priest Kilt (AC 5 AGI +2)
 
 
Quest 4: (Level 7+)
 
Now he wants 1 Gnoll Fang, 1 Snake Fang, and 1 Hunting Bow which you can find in Blackburrow.
 
Reward: Field Priest Claw (5/24)
 
 
Quest 5: (Level 11+)
 
Now he needs a favor from you. get him a Wrath Orc Wristband and he will give you:
 
Reward: Field Priest Leather (AC 11, Str +3)
 
 
Ogre:
 
Quest 1: (Level 1+)
 
Grapok is hungry, bring him 4 Lizard meats and he will give you gud stuff!
 
Reward: Ruthless Bracer (AC 3, +2 STR)
 
 
Quest 2: (Level 3+)
 
Now he needs 4 silk threads to make a cap. Get spiderling silks from jungle spiderlings and combine them 2 at a time in a kit. The threads never fail. Remember to turn them in unstacked.
 
Reward: Ruthless Cap (AC 4, +1 STA)
 
 
Quest 3: (Level 5+)
 
Grapok needs a little dolly... for his grandkid of course! Go get him 1 Mystic doll from the Lizard mystics and he'll give you some pants!
 
Reward: Ruthless Leggings (AC 5, +2 AGI)
 
 
Quest 4: (Level 7+)
 
Now he will send you out till kill some pests. As proof, bring him 2 Giant Bat Furs & 2 Giant Bat Wings and he'll give you some claws.
 
Reward: Ruthless Claw (5/24)
 
 
Quest 5: (Level 11+)
 
Now he thinks you are ready for da big stuff! He wants 4 lizard tails. They drop off Lizard warriors and mystics.
 
Reward: Ruthless Leather (AC 11, +3 STR)
 
 
 
Troll:
 
Quest 1: (Level 1+)
 
Bring Gargh 4 tadpole skins and get:
 
Reward: Dark One's Bracer (3ac, +3 agi)
 
 
Quest 2: (Level 3+)
 
Now he wants 1 Giant Rat Ear and 1 Giant Rat Pelt
 
Reward: Dark One's Helm (5ac, +2sta)
 
 
Quest 3: (Level 5+)
 
Time for some alligator wrestling! bring him 1 Alligator tooth to get some pants.
 
Reward: Dark One's leggings (5ac +2sta)
 
 
Quest 4: (Level 7+)
 
Now he wants 1 Vial of Froglok Blood.
 
Reward: Dark One's Claws (5/24)
 
 
Quest 5: (Level 11+)
 
Now he wants you to bring him 4 lizard tails.
 
Reward: Dark One's Tunic (11ac, +3str)
 
 
 
Vah Shir:
 
Quest 1:
 
Hand Animist Poren your acrylic slate he will then hand you a 4 slot sewing kit and a pattern.  You will need to combine 3 shade silk bandages with the pattern to create a pair of gloves. To make the bandages are made from 2 shade silk threads which are made be combining 2 shadeling silks in the sewing kit. The silk threads never fail and trivial at 15. The bandages can fail and trivial at 21. The hand wraps have a higher trivial. Once you succeed at the hand wraps you will also get some scraps that you can hand back to Animist Poren for the next quest.
 
Reward: Khati Shah Handwraps (AC 2, Lore, No Drop)
 
 
Quest 2:
 
When you hand in the scraps you got from making the hand wraps, Poren will tell you how to turn them into your first set of claws. go out and kill Young Rock hoppers and combine 2 of their claws with Forged Fasteners and the hand wraps.
 
To get the forged fasteners ask Kagazz outside the armory "What are forged Fasteners" he will hand you a 4 slot container to fill with scorpion legs. once fill, hit combine and hand in to him. he will then give you the fasteners.
 
The claws can fail on the combine and the trivial is higher than 40, so you may want to stock up on supplies.
 
Reward: (5/35 HTH weapon)
 
 
Quest 3:
 
Hand Poren the claws you just made and the cloak you got from the citisenship quest. He will give you a new cloak, upgrade your claws, and give you a seal. Take the seal to Mahorn Sood, he's near the bank, and he will hand you a map. Take the map to Kery Miann. (she is on the second floor of one of the buildings there.)
 
She will give you a stone blade and tells you to make 2 leather straps for her to upgrade your claws. The leather straps are made by combining a High Quality Rock hopper hide with the knife in your sewing kit. The trivial is higher than 40 and the High quality hides are kind of rare, so you may want to practice up a little before doing this part. Once you have the 2 straps hand them and your claws to her. She will upgrade them and give you a note to take to Rittuh Purrik.
 
Rewards: Claw upgrade 1 (6/35), Cloak of the Khati Shah Recruit (+1% tailoring), Claw upgrade 2 (7/28 +1str, +1 dex)
 
 
Quest 4:
 
Give the note from Kery to Rittuh in Hollowshade moor. She will give you a 6 slot container to fill with Rockhopper tails. You can find them only in that zone. Turn in the filled bag and she will give you an exaggerated report to give back to Kery. Combine your cloak of the Kahti Shah recruit with a glowing heartstone in the box and give the results to Kery to get a new cloak. The heart  stone drops off glowing muckdiggers in Paludal cavern. If you dont see any kill muckdiggers until one shows up.
 
Reward: Cloak of the Kahti Shah Apprentice (AC 6 +1 STR, +2 STA, +5 HP, +2% tailoring, Lore, No Drop)
 
 
Quest 5:
 
When Kery gives you your new cloak she tells you to take your claws to Kaam Rahiir in the Library. when you give him your claws he will look at them then hand them back along with a bag. You will need to combine 2 Sonic wolfling molars in it and return the results to him. He will then give you a set of claw mounts and tell you to talk to Qua and Jimis. This starts the sonic wolf's bane quests.
