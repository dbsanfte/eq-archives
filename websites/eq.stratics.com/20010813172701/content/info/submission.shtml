Submission Guidelines for News Posts


We are more then happy to receive news and information on the following topics:
  • Screenshots - Submit these in JPG format to EQVision.
  • General News - Events, weddings, contests and general issues that are related directly to EverQuest and or a particular server.
  • Statistics and Strategies - If you have new information or updated information on EverQuest, a guide on how to play a particular class, race or trade skill, send it in to EQArticle.
  • Spoilers - We post these with a warning and do not post the specifics.
  • Links to Web Sites - We do not normally post links to web sites, but you are invited to submit your links to our Links Database.
  • Newsgroup and Forum Postings - From the employees of Verant and highly active threads which should be pointed to show their importance to the EverQuest community.
  • Forum related postings - If you want to let the world know how you think about changes / patches / Verant / EverQuest / etc please use our forums. We can not post such things in the news as it will only result in an emailflood to us.

- Submit News -

What we do not wish to receive:

  • Huge Pictures - Anything over 100kb or not in .jpg format.
  • Game Related Suggestions - They can be sent to the makers of EverQuest, Verant at  eqmail@verant.com.
  • Complaints / Rants / Flames - Horrible things that happen to you in game or sending out a warning to the public blacklisting someone, we choose not to post these. If you have a complaint, you should Email Verant at eqmail@verant.com.
  • Questions - All the information that we have is found on our website. If you have any questions or suggestions about EverQuest Stratics we are of course more then happy to receive them, however please do not mail us with EverQuest related questions. We did not make the game. Verant did, you can contact them this way (from the official EverQuest page):
