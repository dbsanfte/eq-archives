Ok, here is what I know on the stats -

str - This stat directly effects how much you can carry (a stone of weight for each point of str). It also effects how often you hit more max damage (max damage is weapon damage *2 +1). I am pretty sure it also adds a little to max damage potential to warrior types after they hit 20 (but I haven't been told this is verified yet)

Sta - This effects # of hit points you get. The formula if different depending on class. I don't know it yet.

Dex - This effects chance to hit. It also effects chance for caster to recover from interuption.

Agi - This effects chance to not be hit (AC rating). As a side note, most people agree that the type of armor you wear determines how hard you are hit. AC rating is just if you will be hit or not.

Wisdom - This is primary stat for priest types. Mana calc is (wis/5 +2) * level. It also helps against mind attacks (charms and such), and is used as a last check against failed skill rools.

Int - This is primary stat for mage type casters. Use same formula as above, but replace wis with int. Also effects how fast you learn skills (though I have not seen a lot of difference in this area).

Cha - This effects how NPC's react to you, as well as directly effects price of items you buy, and price for item you sell to NPC. It is also rumored that it helps with charm spells.

Thee might be more to these stats, but that is a start for you.
