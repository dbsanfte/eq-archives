Stratics Plus Includes Enhanced Forum Features
Stratics Network Announcement - 7:30 PM PDT

Did you know that as a Stratics Plus member you gain access to a wide variety of enhanced features within the forums added just for you? Your Stratics Plus account grants you access a set of fifty-seven (57) special, smiley icons to enhance your forum posts, the ability to create the forum title of your dreams at will, find out what others are really thinking about an issue by creating and posting polls within your messages, special, expanded search options, as well as access to a members-only forum to discuss your Plus membership and make suggestions directly to Stratics Staff.

Some of the new features we will be adding in the future include the ability to rate topics and discussions, a selection special forum themes, as well as the ability to subscribe to threads so that any new posts are emailed directly to you allowing you to follow that hot discussion even if you cannot get to the forum. These features are only a small part of the growing list of enhanced features offered and planned network-wide exclusively for our Plus members. To learn more about Stratics Plus, what it offers, and how to become a member, check out "About Stratics Plus."

Grief Play Research
CommunityNews - 12:08 PM PDT
Here's an announcement about some research into grief play in MMOG's, if you're interested here's the announcement:

I am undertaking Ph.D work at the Curtin University of Technology (Australia), and am researching grief play in MMORPGs. The study started in September 2002 and is projected to complete in June 2006. Part of my data collection involves interviewing both developer and player respondents about their perceptions of grief play, its effects on the game world, and how it can be better managed. If you are a developer, a grief player, or a player with an opinion about grief play, I'd be most interested in seeking your input and thoughts about the issue.

---

Contact Info:

Researcher: Chek Yang Foo
Email: foocy@cbs.curtin.edu.au
Web page with more information on the project:
http://www.chekyang.com/phd/phd-invitation-interview.htm
Feel free to discuss the issues on our forums as well!

Friday August 29 2003
Today's Patch
Game Patch - 10:42 AM PDT
August 28, 2003
------------------------------

- Two Bastion of Thunder keys and one key in the Plane of Air that were supposed to open up a location for your entire group were only taking the key holder. We have fixed that problem. Also, the Enchanted Ring of Torden will now attach to your key ring the next time you use it.

- The "base" population in the Tower of Solusek Ro now has the ability to summon. Please be aware of this the next time you enter the tower of the god of flame.

- We have corrected a pathing issue in South Karana that was causing the NPCs to all travel to the same point.

- Cloth caps will no longer drop off NPCs in Siren's Grotto.

- We have reduced the number of characters in a raid required to access the Plane of Time from 40 to 18.

- The Burrower has been modified back to its original difficulty.

- A tether has been added to Fennin Ro.
