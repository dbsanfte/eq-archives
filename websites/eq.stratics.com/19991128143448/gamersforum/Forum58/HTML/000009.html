Large Bowl:
1 Glass Shard
1 Large Block of clay
1 Large Bowl Sketch
1 Flask of Water
1 Regular Firing sheet

Mixing Bowl:
1 LARGE BOWL Sketch
1 Flask of Water
2 Large Blocks of Clay
I think these use HQ Firing sheets, but I am not sure. I didn't make more than 4 of them because they are too much of a nuisance. They are containers, so they dont fit in backpacks.
