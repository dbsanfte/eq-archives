Welcome all priests of Tunare and protectors of nature!

I hope that your journey to the depths of what nature has to offer was all that you hoped it would be. As protectors of Norrath’s boundless nature you are bound with strong responsibility. However, as a Priest of Tunare she does not leave you wanting and provides you with a powerful set of skills and spells.

You are gifted with the ability to travel and fight alone, assist a group in tremendous ways in a powerful support role, or transport groups of people across the world instantly. This makes you one of the most versatile classes in the world of Norrath and is perfect for those who like to try out a little bit of everything.

Make money from ports, use your spells to solo a group of dangerous foes or help a group out with powerful buffing spells and other useful spells to expedite the defeat of your common foe.

Take up the mighty calling sent down from Tunare herself and you will be welcome to the World of Druids, where all protectors of Tunare’s creations are welcome!

Thursday August 22 2002
State of the Druid
The Druid - 9:09 PM PDT
Gwynhyfar Foxfyre, over at The Druid's Grove, posted an editorial about "The State of the Druid" after recent balancing:
The petition drew so much public awareness that some of VI couldn't not know about its existence. In an indirect response, Absor went public to say that petitions were never really the right method of approach. In other words, just get to the point and leave the prose at home — we're busy! This public statement confirmed that the petition was known at least, and it even led to two Woody Hearn /gu comics in which one satirized the response with a bit of office fun. And let's not forget that looming ever so silently in the background was the enormous caster balancing project that the EQ Live team announced on February 27, 2002. If any time was appropriate for laying the cards on the table, this was it — our petition wasn't drafted out of the blue. In a period of increasing communication between players and the EQ Live team, the petition raised issues in an effort to get others to start thinking about ways to improve druids, which are essentially dualistic by design and hard to balance. The community succeeded in its goal, but the challenge remained because there was so much to be said about and done to caster balancing. The EQ Live team wasn't exactly responsive in the immediate term, but once progress was made with focus items, pet changes, and intelligence casters, they were able to be more communicative. And it's not over yet although direct word has been brief and to the point.
It is an interesting read. Click here for the full article.

Tuesday July 30 2002
The Druid Portal
The Druid - 10:24 AM PDT
Cresting yet another hill, rounding yet another tree, you find yourself erupting into a clearing in the middle of the dense forest behind the Stratics complex which you’ve been jogging through for hours. The sudden sunlight is blinding and you throw your hands up to shield your eyes while they adjust to the glare. The odd yet familiar scent of mana flows into your nostrils…

In the midst of the clearing, a stone circle emits the quiet hum of hidden power. A group of elves and humans turn their heads at your noisy entrance and, though taking careful notice of you, seem unsurprised by your presence. A nearby wolf growls quietly, silenced gently by one of the elves who scratches it behind the ears before approaching slowly. A wood-elf of slight build, his blue eyes and their piercing gaze flow over you. A wise grin crosses his face…

Greetings, traveler. Welcome to this, our haven – the Druid Portal.

Here I hope to help provide the wisdom of ages of nature. To give insight to the knowledge that spans both the elder races and the younger. That of our deities and my brothers and sisters. That of the Druids.

Please feel free to explore this Portal and add to this wisdom. Come and visit our community and share stories or your own insight!

If I can help in any way, please feel free to contact me . I serve as a guide for this realm.

Enjoy your stay!
