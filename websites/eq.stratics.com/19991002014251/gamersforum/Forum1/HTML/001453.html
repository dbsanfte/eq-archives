Items that increase wisdom or int (depending on what you need) gives you the mana max increase right then (you still have to med for the mana). I noticed this when I had to get my corpse. At level 31, the +22 wis I have in armor gets me from 744 mana to 880 mana. So, when I put all my armor on, I was almost a bubble low on mana.

Also, sorry I forgot to mention that the above formula was verified for true magic users, but hasn't been verified by hybrids (that I have seen). So if any hybrids can help out and verify it, please let us know.

To see the casting cost of spells, just right click on it. To see how much mana you have without the formula, pick a spell and cast it a few times. See how much mana it uses, then calc your total mana. I.E. heal takes 60 mana, so if 3 heals take 2 bubbles (I try to test it to where I will land on a bubble boundry), then each bubble is 90 mana, and you have a total of 450 mana.
