Tally Ho Mighty Warrior! To arms! To arms!

Welcome to world where Strength and Steel reign supreme. Sharpen your blades and shine your armor, for you brave warrior are called to the front lines of battle to face the enemy in toe to toe combat. Your combination of might armor, a swift and sharp steel blade and a flurry of insults your mother would blush to hear you say keep you on the front lines taking the brunt of the hits coming from the enemy. Not only is your job to battle the fiends, but it is also to protect those in your party from unfriendly aggression.

You rely on friends for magical assistance, but only you can withstand the hits that would crumple your friends while still dealing out massive amounts of damage from your mighty weapons.

So protect your friends, slay the beasts, and take note of the call to arms noble warrior, and you shall be greatly rewarded with the spoils of the days battle.
