Shar Vahl Faction

[ Discuss This Article! ]  [ Return to Faction Guide Index ]

Shar Vahl:

Factions in Shar Vahl:
Guardians of Shar Vahl (Guards,Warriors) - Shar Vahl, Shadeweavers, Hollowshade Moore.
Citizens of Shar Vahl (misc. NPCs) -  Shar Vahl, Shadeweavers, Hollowshade Moore.

Monsters:

Lodakai bandits:
Loda kai - Down
Gor Taku - Down
Guardians of  Shar Vahl - Up

Quests:

Citizenship quest:

When you hand in your guild note to your guild master he will give you an application to take to Registrar Bindarah. She can be found on the south side of town in the building across from the gate. Give her the application and she will send you out to get your acrylia slate from Mignah and tax collector's seal on some documents.

The tax collector is located in teh bank in the central area. Mignah is in a tavern in the north section of town. Hand the Tax collector the document and he will put his seal on it then give it back.

To get your slate you will need to tell Mignah " I need an acrylia slate." he will then press your hand against one he pulls from a forge and hand it to you. Give the two items back to the registrar and she will give you a note to give to the King.

The king is located in the palace. Hand him the note and he will give you another note that says you are worthy of becoming a citizen. Take the note back to the registrar and she will give you back your acrylia slate and your finished application.

Give your application and your acrylia slate back to the guildmaster and you will get your class cloak.

Factions:

Citizens of Shar Vahl - Up

Shadeling Silk:

Jala needs some Shadeling Silks. Bring her 4 of them and she will ask you to do another task.

Faction:

Guardians of Shar Vahl - Up

Stretched hides:

Broker Fahaar needs the help of a citizen, prove to him that you are and he will give you a bag to put Young RockHopper hides in. fill the bag and return it to him to get a stretched hide in return.

Faction:

Guardians of Shar Vahl - Up
