Why yu wants be da Ogre?

Ogres da bestest race in da world for lossa reesons.

Wun, dey simply FUN ta be BIGNESST. It reel fun bein bigger den everting else in da world, and yu gets see reel size of everting when yu ogre. I no knew da squishies (humans and elves) was so small till I stood nexta dem! Reely it much much different see wurld through eyes da big persun. Dey TINY squishies! An da gnomies dey even smaller an it fun to try eat dem. Dey run too fast tho.

Too, it reel easy to roleplay. It ruin da grammer a talk like dis but it reel fun make other people ‘member who dey are an what they doin. Yu gets? I try explain better. Sumtimes peoples furget dat they all elves an barbarians an stuff. Dey jes try play game an get rich. Dat not gud, it make less fun for other peoples. I gots lossa compliments tellin me dat I roleplay reel gud. Wun nice lil gnomie say I do so gud an preserve da game dat he gimme spikey collar, fud, water, an sum shinies! I say thank an I no try eat him. He laugh an gimme more shinies. See? Dat gud ‘nuff?

Twee, dere not too many ogres! Dat meens yu gots whole Oggok to self sumtimes, but usually dere jes 3 or 4 people for go bashin with, so it perfect place to start. When yu travel to other places like elf city or human city also gud place, yu main attraction. Many peoples never seen ogre before. Yu be surprised. People like hunt wit yu and make friends wit yu and help yu because yu unique, not like all da other squishies runnin round. Plus dey nice cuz yu can eat dem if yu ever wants, hehehehe.

Five, ogres get da special moves call “slam” an “bash” dat super gud versus dem pesky magics persuns. Ogres made to keep on bashin and slashin at critters and bad persuns and to stop any magics from hittin dem. Dis makes ogres reel reel gud basha!

Uh.. Sevn, dey gots da gud stat… sta…. numbers!

Do Ogres got gud stat… sta…. numbers?

Dat simple even for me and I gots low int too. Da Ogres be da tuffest all creetures on world. Dey gots reel gud strongness (strength) and reel gud tuffness (stamina), and tho they not too smart an kinda ugly wit low prettyness (charisma) dey still gets rich an gets gud skills. I kno, I gots dem. Yu can gets reel gud basha skills even tho yu no too smart cuz da ogres bash reel fast and dey do lossa bashin. If yu wan get skills fast gets a wepon wit reel low delay, it not need high dmg cuz we da strong basha an everting do lossa damage. Well, most tings. Da fastness an quickness (agility and dexterity) da ogres still ok so yu can move when da critters try miserable to bash yu. Overall da ogres gots very very gud stats better at da bashin den everywun.

Da Ogre city, Oggok

Da Oggok is reel special, it only thing we no ‘llowed to mispronounce. Becuz dem ogres past not tu smarts, not like me hehe, dey make city reel simple. Dat REELY gud. Yu give it few mins an yu memorize where bank, where all guilds, where all shopps (both dem) and where exit place – dat hardest, hehe. Furst part Oggok big wall wit lossa holes. Dat Shaman place. Yu go in right hole yu find shoe merchant, dat hard to find, I spend time lookin for gud shoe cuz I gots ugly toes. Outter place on hill an on way to out Oggok yu find box merchant, an large shield merchant, an lossa inns. If yu pure Basha or yu SK yu no want go in wall wit holes. Yu go round yu find next entrance wit three bouncer bashas. From dere it short walk to Greenblood guild (SK), an bank, an fud shop, an armer shop, which all around big place in middle which basha guild. Even if yu not basha yu want go in dere gets rusty wepons dey always have and do da quests dey gud faction wit ogres. Dat pretty much Oggok – oh ya, dere armadillos runnin round dey gud basha practice an fun ta kill too. Dey get in way small hallways.

Life as da Ogre

Ogre reel simple, tho it hard. When yu make ogre da magic box tell yu dat “This class/race/deity combination is of HIGH DIFFICULTY”. Well I say dey can go to Innoruk. Ogres not meen always, fact I reely nice ogre. But dey squishies dey RACIST. Dey kill dem ogres no matters what. SO what Rhaurol tell is dat yu do dis. When yu leave Oggok yu see roadsplit. Right is bridge, lossa hard stuff cross. Left is newbie area. Yu go left yu keep goin road, dere nother cross. Right reel gud, lossa spiders an scarabs an bats an snakes an eazy stuffs. Left baker stuffs an long road. Sumtimes yu find easy stuffs on road there but… well yu see what on other end of road. SO for dem newbies 1-3 yu wants go left, den right, and doan go too far cuz dem spiders an lizzie watchers high level dey bash yu gud. When yu gets sum friends at level 3 (yu need 3 peoples an a shaman gud as un dem) yu go scaly munchy camp. Go to bridge (dat right turn from Oggok) den make left turn an follow water at distance. Yu see fire, go up to it. Dere Scouts an dere Mystics an dere Foragers. Scouts yu wants get 3 fifes an trade for gud SK tunic (ac 6 int 1 it ok ac, but reely gud fer getting wepon skills). Mystics drop dollies dat yu trade for ok PW legs. Foragers jes give gud exp an fun ta bash. Mystics get blind spell reel scary yu want bash when you see dem sparks com from dere hands. Affer yu gets 5, dey no give greats exp even alone so yu wants try new dings. Dis up to yu for now. Yu can go to Grobb an bash dem froglok tads, or try da swamp tho that hard. Dis what I do – I get reel nice Wizzie persun who drop by to kill Oggok guards (ask reel nice now sumtimes dey let yu loot wun) an ask him if he bring me to human city. He say ok he bring me an I follow him. It not too hard yu jes follow walls an run reel fast if yu see guard. I die wun time but there lossa nice clerics round human city, know how it is KoS and wun dem ressurect me. Anyways I gets to Qeynos an I gets bind in small corner where dere no guards an I go to Blackburrow bash lossa gnolls. It not too hard, tho yu gets stuck in hallways sumtimes. After bout a level da bashin da guards think yu dubious an no try kill yu. Yay! Den yu go inside human city and from dere can do lossa quests an get faction other places. I almost indifferent wit guards now, an I try go elf city soon. Me hope if yu no gots Ogre yu try makin wun, an may yu bash all dem critters gud!
