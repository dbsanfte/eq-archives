All vendors and buyers are greatly encouraged to come to find the most items, prices, buyers and sellers anywhere else in Norrath. Other servers have done this and profited GREATLY from the dissemination of items, and now we of Xegony shall as well.

From Kren:
Announcing the marriage of Kren Lighthammer to the beautiful Zentina, both members of the renowned guild, Lords of Law on the Rallos Zek server.

The marriage will take place Sunday, October 18th in the Steamfont Mountains. Kren and Zentina, both gnome necromancers wish to return to their homeland to celebrate such an event. The ceremony will commence promptly at 7pm EST at the Dragon Bones. Free drink and grub to follow soon after.

Kren, Zentina and the Lords of Law respectfully invite the masses for this gathering. Many friends have been made along the way and all parties involved wish to share this joyous occasion with those that have made the journey worth travelling.

Kren, the founding father of the Lords of Law has dedicated his dark powers to fight for the greater good of norrath and continues to do so day in and day out. When he learned that another gnome necromancer of unspeakable beauty walked the same path as he, twas a sign from the gods that could not be overlooked. Love quickly followed bringing us to this wonderful event.

Please join us for a great time. After the ceremony we hope to have a lot of fun and all are invited. Safe travells to you all!
