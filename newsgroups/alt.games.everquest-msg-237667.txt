The EQ files are:

synthus2.sf2   11,755KB
synthusr.dls       1,393KB
synthusr.sbk         487KB
synthusr.sf2          487KB

For a Sound Blaster card. Check your AudioHQ and look for the SoundFont 
Icon or Tab.

Start with the "Options" tab

First select "Remember last directory"

Then set "SoundFont Cache" to "Static Cache and then:

Either set 20MB more than your current use or

Go to the "Configure Bank" tab and mix and match the SoundFonts using 
the Load / Remove buttons.

Either way unload and load the SoundFonts you want to keep plus the EQ 
ones. Making sure that your static cache is just bigger than your final 
selection of SoundFonts.

As I don't run many other games I keep just my EQ SoundFonts in the 
cache.

When happy exit to save the settings.

As for your AC97 sorry I'm afraid you will have to check your manuals 
for the info.
