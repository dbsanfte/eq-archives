Make a hotkey page with: Auc1, Auc2, Auc3, Auc4, etc.

Each Auc key should read:

/auc WTS item, whatever, price
/pause 10
/auc WTS item2, whatever, price
/pause 10
/auc WTS item3, whatever, price

With 10 to a page, you just hit all the buttons in order every 2 min
or so for a total of 30 possible auctions (on that page). Please don't
spam, though. 2 minutes is a good interval.
