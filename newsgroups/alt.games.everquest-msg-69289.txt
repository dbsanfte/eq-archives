The only other thing I can think of is to turn off any animated cursors,
i.e. those from "Theme" packs and Active Desktop. You may also try turning
off DirectDraw acceleration in DxDiag.

Also, under your display setting Advanced properties, you may have access to
a "troubleshooting" tab - NOT the troubleshoot button on the Dispaly
properties (this just brings up Help), but a troubleshooting tab after you
push the Advanced button. Here there is a "Hardware Acceleration" slider.
One click down from full acceleration will disable mouse cursor
acceleration, and may clear up the problem.

Good luck!
