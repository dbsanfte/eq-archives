A lock-up that 'deep' in the system (where Ctrl+Alt+Del doesn't even
work) generally indicates a low-level problem: (1) A device driver
problem; (2) A damaged OS file; (3) Hardware (possibly overheating).

the case on your computer and clean out the dust thoroughly.
