>PoV/PoS - Get through the PoJ trials. Trial of Execution is the staple one
>and the most straightforward. All you need is some decent dps a good tank
>and something to keep everyone alive (slower, extra healer, CCer whatever.
>Did it with a pally once who used group-heal to take aggro from all mobs
>every wave. no CC or slow used just let them eat DamageShield). Oh and a
>healer who can keep a tank alive through the executioner. Nothing big, lots
>of pickup groups on most servers and if you're 1 class short you can often
>ask LFGers. ps Mages rule in the exec trials :).
>
>CoD - Get in a pickup raid for Grummus (hard) or do the quest. Quest also
>gives a nice insta-click item. Not too hard once you know the spawnspots of
>the questmobs.
>
>PoI Factory - Get a pickup group for the Xenamech quest. Occasionally held
>on my server but make sure you have lots of friends to warn you :).
>
>PoNb - (Lair of Therrus Thule) - No point going there but you can try to get
>17 friends together and do the mazequest in PoN. It's fun to do but you'll
>need at least 2 or 3 people capable of mezzing those mobs (61+ mez spells).
>
>
>Ask around for tier 3 flags when you think you can try them :).
