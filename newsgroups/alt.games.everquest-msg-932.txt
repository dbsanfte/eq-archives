Daniel <daniel_h_w@yahoo.com> wrote:
> How do you do logging?  What is the command and where is the log file
> created?  Also is it added to or written over or is another created every
> time you log into EQ?

You turn on logging by typing /LOG.

There's also an option in the INI file to turn it on all the time.

The logs are created in your Everquest directory, and are named
EQLOG_XX_Charname.txt.  The XX is your server number, and it varies.  New
data is appended.
