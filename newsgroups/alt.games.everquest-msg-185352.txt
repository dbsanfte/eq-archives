Like some other Rangers have said so far, don't fill your slots with buffs.
Just swap one in when in runs out, cast it, and swap the other spell back in
again. The exceptions to this are the short duration buffs like the Shield
of Thistles damage shield and the Call of Sky weapon proc. One piece of
maybe obvious advice, don't swap your snare or root spell out to cast a
buff, you never know what might turn up when you're memming.

My standard line up is:

1. Best healing spell
2. Snare
3. Burning arrow
4. Stinging swarm
5. Camoflage
6. Shield of Thistles
7. Panic animal
8. Root

Although with my new LoY Swarm of Pain DOT, I use that in place of the Panic
animal unless I am planning on some fear-kiting. Also may use Flaming arrow
or Careless Lightning in place of Shield of Thistles when I'm grouped with
someone with a better DS or I'm solo with a mana regen buff on me.
