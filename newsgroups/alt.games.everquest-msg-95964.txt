You get gems/equips onto the hotkey pad same way as any other hotkey.
Click, hold, drag, and drop.  It's done for convenience.  As a bard it's
especially true that it's easier to type a 1,1,2,2,3,3 than it is to click
the spell gems with a mouse.  Other classes might do it so they can add a
message to the cast (%t mezzed -- do not disturb).  Equips are hotkeyed if
they are right-clickable, or if you need to swap them out a lot (bards).
