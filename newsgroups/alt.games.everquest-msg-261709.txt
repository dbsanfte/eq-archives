So, what you want is:

/cast 1
/pause 30,/cast 1
/pause 30,/cast 1
/pause 30,/cast 1
/pause 30,/cast 1

That will cast 5 times, with a delay of 3 seconds between each cast.

Obviously, if the spell has a long refresh time, up the delay time 
appropriately.
