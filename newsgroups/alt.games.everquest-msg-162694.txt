>Could someone tell this little 21 Rogue what is meant by a "trial" in PoJ?

>done in the Plane of Justice, hence the name "Trial".  Am I even close?

No. The Trials are scripted events that only allow a certain number of
players in at a time and have to be completed based on the script.

For instance, the Trial of Execution is a series of four waves of four
MObs each that have to be killed in under a minute each plus a
miniboss who quads for 700 or so. Completing the trial is the only
combat-based aspect of getting the flag to enter Plane of Valor.
