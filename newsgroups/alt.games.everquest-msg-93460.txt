That's the way the pet works.  If a groupmate grabs aggro you can repeatedly  
execute a /pet back off hot key to get them out of the attack.  Also, if 
they are SOWed, they may be able to get away before being hit again.  The 
pet will attack if it is hit again.  The pet will also attack anyone that 
has the audacity to attack you.  There may be AA skills for beastlords to 
modify this behavior.
