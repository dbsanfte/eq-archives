If you take the boat to Freeport, look for the Soulbinder.  He's an NPC on
the porch of the Freeport Inn, not too far inside of the South Gate of the
city.  It'll be in the same zone as the docks when you pull into FP (East
Freeport is the name of the zone).  Just hail him, and say, "Bind my soul."

It's a great place to be bound if you're going to be adventuring in North Ro
and Oasis.
