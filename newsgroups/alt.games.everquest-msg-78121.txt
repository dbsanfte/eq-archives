Further notes on fishing.  You can fish while meditating; gives you
something to do while waiting for the blue bar to go up.  Also, if you
are a Baker, fish is needed for several recipes, and catching your own
is cheaper than buying it (although a lot slower).  A fishing party is
handy ; have several friends catch fish while you make fish fillets or
fish rolls, then you share out the baked goods to all fishermen
involved.
