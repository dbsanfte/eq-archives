> The Coldain Prayer Shawl quest. Many a doctor has made a fair que of

> quests.  For many in the casting community, a cloak with AC INT WIS (other
> stats) and Flowing Thought is a very worthy goal;  but the cost is a loss in
> both manual dexterity and sanity points.  As far as putting training points
> into trade skills, not only is it a good idea to prime it with one (why go
> through several combines just to get up to '1') but in the instance of
> tailoring - where many of your supplies will either have to be farmed or
