> I hope you're ready for a bit of adventure :)
>
> AFAIK you can't get TB or Sicken on the moon, except
> maybe in Shadow Haven (and I'm not so sure you have
> the faction to buy them).
>
> HOWEVER
>
> All kitties get an innate sneak skill of 50, and you
> can use this, some patience and guts to get to Shadow
> Haven, and from there to the North Karana portal.
>
> I did this from hollowshade - it's easier to get to.
> First, SoW (of course).  Run out the north gate to
> hollowshade moor, turn right and stay up on the wall.
> I've never been attacked up there, so you should be
> fine.  After running a while you'll get to a spot where
> the lake comes up to the wall, there's an opening on
> the wall over the water, go in, and enter paludal caverns.
>
> Paludal is made up of tunnels and caverns with lakes.
> For the most part the tunnels hold non-aggro bugs, the
> lakes have Plerg Plarg fiends, they *WILL* aggro, but,
> they usually face the water.
>
> This is where sneak comes in, if sneak succeeds, an
> aggro monster won't aggro on you if you stay to the
> back of it.
>
> Run down the first tunnel until it opens up, to your
> right there is a single Fiend, hit sneak, con him.
> If sneak succeeded, he will con indifferent, if it
> didn't, he will still be scowls-ready to attack.  If
> the second is true, wait for sneak to recycle and
> try again until you're indifferent.
>
> Now, turn right, stay high on the wall and sneak behind
> the fiend, and his friend farther over and enter the
> first tunnel you reach.
>
> Proceed up the tunnel to the Y, turn left (not right, there
> are aggro level 15 mobs up there).  At the next lake, go
> left around the lake, again stay behind the Fiends.  Enter
> the first tunnel, go down it to the intersection.  Turn
> right.
>
> This cavern is the hard one.  I turned left around the lake,
> skip the first tunnel off, keep going.  Watch for the roamer
> on the sloping ledge above the lake.  Sneak down, stay on the
> wall above the Recondite camp, once you've made the entrance
> to the big tunnel your home free, follow it to Shadow Haven.
>
> Since you've got SoW up, if you get aggroed in that last stretch
> lake, sneak will drop - just haul butt for the zone.
>
> If you can get an invis before going in, this whole thing is
> easy.
>
> Once in Shadow haven head to the Nexus, port down to NK,
> run west across NK, WK to Qeynos hills, turn right and
> run up the wall in QH, to the BB entrance, through BB,
> and into everfrost.  The poison spells are at the vendor
> on the plains.  Ask in OOC, folks will be happy to help,
> I'm sure.
>
>
> I've done this with a 17 BL and a 11 bard, it can be done,
> you just need to be patient.
>
> Go to http://www.eqatlas.com for maps
