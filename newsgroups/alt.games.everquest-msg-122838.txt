>1- Is Luclin a "STAND ALONE" or do I have to own older Eq version to play
>it?

Luclin is an expansion. You will need to have the the base version of
EQ (or the newer Everquest:Trilogy Pack which includes the base game
and the previous expansions Ruins of Kunark and Scars of Velious)

>2- Will Luclin work good on a amd 950mhz, 256mb ram, ATI radeon 7500?

You will need at least 512mb of memory to run all of the new character
models. Your video card should be fine.

>3-Is Eq playable when behind a router/firewall?

EQ can run from behind a firewall as long as the ports it communicates
on are open. You may have to make adjustments to your firewall to get
it to work.
