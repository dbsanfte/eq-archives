The Radeon VE does not support hardware T&L. Make sure you have the
following in your eqclient.ini file:
UseTnL=FASLE

Go to the following URL and download and install the latest unofficial
ATI Radeon drivers (version 4.13.7199). Unzip them into a folder
somewhere on your PC where you will remember.

Use the following guide to remove the older drivers and install the
new ones.

http://www.rage3d.com/forum/ubbhtml/Archives/Archive-000001/HTML/20010104-13-004006.html

If you follow the above guide, you will have no problems upgrading the
drivers. It takes about 15-20 minutes. It guides you through the
complete removal of the old files and registry entries.
