> A:  The map window, and in fact all windows, can be made transparent
> by opening up the menu for the window and right clicking on its title

> enter a value of 0 to 100.  0 will make the box, but not the text,

> 50 will make it semi-transparent.  Please note that you cannot
> generally click on or target things through a transparent window.
