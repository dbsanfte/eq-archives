Sean Lowe <Abuse@attbi.com> wrote:
> Hi all, I'm hoping someone can help me a bit here.
> I just upgraded to an AMD 2100+ processor, sitting on an Asus A7V8X with
> 512mb DDR RAM and am having *serious* heat issues.....

I was having similar problems.

Look in the BIOS and see if there's a setting for "standard" or "turbo" and
then set it on "standard".  This has nothing to do with clock speed - it has
to do with memory and bus timings and such.  Also do the "load standard
defaults".

When I did this, my lockup and reboot problems went away.  You will not
notice any speed difference.

For the heat, get a couple case fans.
