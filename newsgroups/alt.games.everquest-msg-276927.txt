able to survive a couple rounds from the undead guy on the dock;
then I can feign death next to the Translocator.

I've heard it rumored that you can get the TL to work from the
water, but I've never managed it.

Once you hit level 15 and get your LDON stone so you can use the
magus ports, it opens up two other ways to reach Timorous: one is
to go via the Outpost but use a Troll illusion, which can be gotten
for free if you do much fishing in Abysmal Sea.  (Look up Kutin's
Key.)  The other is to go via Oasis, which actually isn't too hard
to reach from the South Ro LDON camp.  Now that there's an outbound
magus port available in the Guild Lobby, it's not bad at all.
