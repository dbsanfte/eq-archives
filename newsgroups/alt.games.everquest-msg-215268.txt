The old standbys for everybody:

Black Alloy Medallion (knocks 10% off the mana cost of all spells up to 
level 44)

emphasis on wisdom and mana for the shaman.  20k is more than enough to 
twink out 3 toons enough that you won't be looking for upgrades until the
