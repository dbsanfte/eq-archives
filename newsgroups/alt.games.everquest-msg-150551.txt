Well, reading the info that comes up when you zone in is a good place
to start...

Buy a trader satchel. Put the stuff you want to sell in it (no
containers). Stand under one of the canopies. Type /trader. Click each
item, set the price (pp, gp, sp, cp. Click on each coin denomination
to set). Click "set price". Once all the items are priced, click
"begin trader".
