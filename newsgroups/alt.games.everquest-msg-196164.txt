Hi,

I also have McAfee Firewall and for the last month or so have had some
trouble logging on, similar to what you describe.

Here is how I got around it:
     Disable the firewall and log on - no problem.  Get into the game and
use shift-alt-r to activate the Windows cursor.  Click the box in the upper
right corner, not the X box, but the - box on the left. Reactivate the
Firewall and click on "Everquest" in the bottom left, next to START. Click
on the "OK to access box that pops up and walla you are back in business.
Now in the future whenever there is a download that changes any *.exe file
repeat the process.  If there is no such download you will get into the game
without any trouble.

Hope this helps.
