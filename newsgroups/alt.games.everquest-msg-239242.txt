Regarding the latter, of course, you can usually type just "r" (no slash)
and it'll load up your chat with "/tell <whoever-last-sent-you-a-tell> ".

What's less known (at least, I was surprised when I ran across it) is
that if you type one or more tab characters after using "r" to start
a reply-tell, it'll change the name in the command to cycle through
any other players who sent you earlier tells.
