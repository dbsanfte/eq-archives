Assuming you can hunt with your shaman friend, you two should
be able to find spots where you can duo.   His pet won't take
aggro off you until you step back, so you can "tank" while the
shaman solos.   You can actually control your aggro perfectly
this way and just step back and heal when needed.   Have the
shaman SoW you and you pull using Stun (yes, Stun, not the
ones with damage).

It's not great XP but it's not too bad, either.  I did it the same
way except with a mage as partner.

(Hint:  Tizmaks (Great Divide) and Ulthorks/Walruses (Eastern
Wastes.))
