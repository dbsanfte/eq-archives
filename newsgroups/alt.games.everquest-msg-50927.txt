Just a correction, leather paddings consist of one low quality cat, bear or wolf
pelt and a silk *thread*. Silk thread is simply 2 spiderling silks combined to-
gether - silk cord is used for a quiver or a potion bag or something, but not for
paddings or general tailoring. Pelt quality can be reduced by combining a skinning 
knife (ask a blacksmith to make one for you) and a pelt in a sewing kit.
