The more usual way to indicate haste is
percent increase in swings per unit time.

Given a delay 20 weapon (30 swings per minute)
and a 20% haste item you end up with 36 swings
per minute.

The way to figure the new delay is 
100/(100+haste) * delay.  100% haste doubles 
your weapon speed, and halves delay.
