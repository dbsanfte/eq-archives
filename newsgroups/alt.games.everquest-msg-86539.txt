> Ok, call me noobie, but what is Proc?

  A 'proc' is an magical effect on a weapon that is activated randomly during
the course of a fight while the weapon is in use.  For example, the Ghoulbane
procs Dismiss Undead (IIRC... a ~90 point undead nuke) so occassionally the
sword will cast Dismiss Undead on the target during the course of a fight,
causing extra damage to undead creatures.
