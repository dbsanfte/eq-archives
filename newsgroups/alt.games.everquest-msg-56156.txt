On Tue, 06 Nov 2001 20:21:02 GMT, slunk <noway@jose.gov> wrote:

>Is it possible to make a spell book hotkey.  I am using the view that
>has the transparent black boxes as opposed to the limited blue view.
>I looked at the help and couldn't find any.  Any help would be
>appreciated.  Thanks for your time.

Yes, just make a social key that says "/book".

It will make you sit (if you aren't already) and open
your spellbook.

If you want to make a hotkey that opens the spellbook
and flips to a particular page at the same time,
use "/book #", where "#" is the number of the page
you want to go to.
