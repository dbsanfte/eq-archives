> One hotbar is one window in your interface. Each hotbar have 10 pages
> (Reached by the two arrows in each window or by shift+number by default
> for hotbar1)
> The keybindings for hotbar1 is being used on the currently visible page
> of hotbar1. To see another hotbar click the EQ button and find it
> there, or assign a key to Toggle Hotbar2 in options>keys>ui.
>
> Its up to your own preference what you want to do. If you mostly play
> using the keyboard  you might prefer to use one hotbar and utilize all
> of its pages. Its easy to switch pages (shift+#) and then click the
> hotbuttons on that page (#).
>
> If you prefer clicking hotbuttons with your mouse you might find it
> much more convenient to have two or more hotbars up at the same time.
> This way you can easily click your mouse on the visible buttons without
> having to switch pages.
