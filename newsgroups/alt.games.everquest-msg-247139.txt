> not sure of the actual formula to work it out, although I'm sure someone

If your int/wis is 200 or less, I believe the formula is (using wis as
an example):
	(wis/5 + 2) x Level

So at level 5 a point of wis/int adds 1 mana; at level 50 it adds 10 mana.

If your int/wis is over 200 some other formula kicks in, like you get
about 6 mana per point regardless of level?  I don't recall the details.
(Hasn't mattered to me yet; my wis isn't over 200 except when I'm
practicing tradeskills!)
