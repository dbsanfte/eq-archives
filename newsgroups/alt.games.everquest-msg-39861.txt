> For commands such as assist, you firstly must set them up as a "social" on
a
> spare Macro button.
> Macros, Spell gems, Melee slots & inventory slots can all be hot keyed by
> hovering cursor over the appropriate Macro or Melee/inventory slot, press
> and hold left mouse button until the Macro appears on your cursor, then
drop
> onto a hot key slot.
> This is the key to weapon/instrument swapping.  Your main attack Hot key
> bank will include the 3 or 4 songs you will be using, both Primary &
> secondary Melee slots & (in my case) an inventory slot containing a bag.
The
> bag holds my instruments & any weapons I wish to use.
> By right clicking on the bag hot key, the bag opens. Pick up the weapon
you
> are currently wielding from its hot key slot, drop it in the bag over the
> instrument you want, drop the instrument in your secondary melee slot.
> Use the same method to change instruments - except that you will be able
to
> pick up the new instrument first & drop it over the old instrument.
>
> It all sounds a bit complicated but it is a lot easier in practice.
