On Wed, 13 Aug 2003 15:26:57 -0500, wrat@io.com (the wharf rat) wrote:

>
>	If I have say two mana preservation items do I get the effects 
>of both?  Or is it a one focus effect per spell kind of thing?

Two of the SAME type of focus effects can't stack. However, more than
one type of focus effect can stack on the same spell. Example, a focus
that preserves mana (Any of the various named focus effects that have
the effect of preserving mana) and one that increases the damage of a
DD can stack on a DD spell, but two mana preservation effect foci
can't. In the case of multiples of the same focus type with different
% effects, the highest will take over. Confused yet? :p
