> Makes me curious: How is this bonus calculated?

(level - 25) / 3, rounded down. Or something darn close to that =). So
basically at level 28, you get a +1 damage to every hit with your main
hand, at 31 it becomes +2, etc. If you hang out with high level
warriors, you can see this when they crit; you'll see the "soAndSo
scores a critical hit! (50)" and then the line right after will say
"soAndSo slashes a_mob_01 for 55 damage"; that extra 5 is the main hand
damage bonus kicking in.
