Information at eqtraders.com indicates that there are two rolls
to see whether you get a skillup.  The first depends on your
primary stat (usually wis or int), which skill it is, and whether
the combine succeeded.  The second is (200 minus level) / 200,
with a minimum of 1/20.  So once you hit 190 your chances go to
at most 1/20, and as long as you keep your stat maxed the only
thing that should affect your skillup rate is your success rate.
(Well, that and the random number generator. :-)
