mcbragg66@yahoo.com (brian) wrote:

>Friends,
>
>Does anyone know whether focus item effects stack?
>
>Specifically, if my Cleric has Mana Preservation, Extended
>Enhancement, and Regeant Preservation focus items and casts a symbol,
>will the mana cost be reduced, the duration be increased, and have a
>chance of not using a gem? (Assuming, of course, that the spell is
>within the level range of the focus item.)

Yes, different effects work together. Multiple instances of the same
effect, however, do nothing. And if two effects do the same thing for
a spell (Mana Preservation and Affliction Efficiency, for example),
the "higher" one takes effect, ignoring the lower.
