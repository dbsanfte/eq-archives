> Never heard of cannonballing.  What is it?

Cannonballing is the use of two spells to generate more mana at the expense
of HP and is a Shaman's trademark. The first spell you need to cast is
Regen - to counteract the Cannibalise spell which you then cast at regular
intervals whenever your HP bar approaches full. The Cannibalise then takes a
chunk of your regenerating HP and converts it to a smaller amount of mana.
You then wait for Regen to top HP back up again and then repeat.

Any decent Shaman will be doing this constantly from level 29 onwards. If
you <don't> see a Shaman wincing then they are either lazy or have very good
reason...(hopefully).
