The following mod will put a % of Level indicator on your player info
window. Additionally it displays % of Mana, Stamina, and health left.

Copy the base UI File, EQUI_PlayerWindow.xml, into its own directory off of
the UIFiles directory. Then cut and paste the following into the new
EQUI_PlayerWindow.xml file.
