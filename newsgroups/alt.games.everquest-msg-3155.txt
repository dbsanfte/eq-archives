This isn't necessarily the answer to your problem, but you
can fix the resolution by editing your eqclient.ini file.
Just look for the line that says "VideoMode=x" and change
the x to a number:

0 = 640x480
1 = 800x600
2 = 1024x768
etc.

That's the "manual" way to change the resolution :^)
