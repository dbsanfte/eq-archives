Mob XP in a given zone is ZL^2, where Z is a constant for the zone,
and L is the level of the mob.  So, the ratio of XP from an L+1 mob to
that from an L mob is 1+2/L+1/L^2.  A level 41 mob compared to a level
40 mob would give 1+1/20+1/1600 times as much XP, or just a very tiny
amount over 5%.
