> So why can I gate to Gfay from the moon, but not vic-versa?

It has to do with the nature of gate travel and the relative atmospheres
of the planet and moon.  During the landing process your character must
decelerate or be hit by 10K damage on landing.  This works fine as you
slow down to land on Norrath, but on the moon with its much thinner
atmosphere you would be decelerating in an environment which your body
could not handle.  This is why the Luclin ports should continue to work
as they do now, you must be on Luclin to use them, as such a gate does
not pose the same problems in that you don't actually leave the
atmosphere of the moon during the gate.
