The problem with /note is, as I understand it, it writes a file that
you can refer to later, out of game.  What I want is a /note facility
that generates notes I can access while IN game, sort of like the
journal lets me do with NPC text.  (I haven't actually tried using
/note, so I'm basing this on the documentation.  When I want to write
myself a /note I just do it with a pencil!)

For people who have trouble remembering why they're /ignoring someone,
maybe take one page of socials and create some macros like
	/tt so_and_so is on my ignore list because blah_blah_blah.
Then if you need to explain to someone why you won't group, you can
just target the person you want to tell, and bang the social.
