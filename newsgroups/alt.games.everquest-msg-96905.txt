Ensnare cast pre-mez won't break the mez... and usually lasts longer than
the mez does.

Ensnare + Nuke is a good taunt against adds... you can then kite the add
around until your party can deal with it, or even get it far enough away to
root/dot/med the critter.

In a tight dungeon, where a fleeing critter can turn a corner and break
line-of-sight before you've recovered from a fizzled snare (especially if
the critter is a lawntrash add that /cons green to the tank... they have an
annoying habit of running early and in good enough health to make good
speed) then Ensnare early can prevent a train later... and once cast, the
druid can concentrate on the rest of the group without distraction
(important if main healer or Evac'er)
