>Feasible in terms of technical limits and the in-game mechanics: Yes.
>Any player can group with any other player if they are not more than 1.5

>all the way up to sixty. As to the xp, I'm going to assume your original
>phrasing of "suck up all the extra mana" was talking about "extra xp".
>No. There will be "wasted" xp in this scenario. The level 60 will still
>"take up" sixty percent of the xp from each kill. They just won't
>receive it. It's wasted. You, as the low level 40, will, however, get
>40% of the xp from each kill while likely taking very little risk, and
>contributing little to the kills. And since these might be mobs that are
>much higher than you could normally take on your own or with a group of
>appropriately leveled friends, the xp gain rate could still be
>impressive.
