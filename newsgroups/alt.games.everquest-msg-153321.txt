>Is there a way to turn off the pretty lights without giving up the ui?

There are now two "flavors" of spell effects -- old style and
new style.  Which spell has which effects depends on whether
the newly-designed spell effects have been moved to the "live"
servers for that spell type or not.

To turn off the old style, use "/showspelleffect off" and
"/particle off" (or the equivalent Alt-O/Display settings).

To turn off the new style, set "Opacity" to "zero" in the
Alt-O/Display screen.
