I will list the major pros/cons

Barb:
Pro: Faction, Melee-Stats, Slam
Con: Night Blind, 5% EXP penalty

Vah Shir
Pro: SNEAK, Safefall, Infravision, Faction
Con:

Troll
Pro: Regen, Melee-Stats, Slam, Infravision
Con: Faction, 20% EXP penalty

Iksar
Pro: Regen, Wisdom, Infravision, Swim, Forage
Con: Faction, 20% EXP penalty

Ogre
Pro: Infravision, Melee-Stats, Cannot be stunned from front
Con: Faction, 15% EXP penalty

Overall the Troll/Iksar would be the best choice if you are playing to
60-65 and can stomach the EXP penalty. None of the races are a "bad
choice".
