  This is an old etymological urban legend. You can get the whole story 
at http://www.snopes2.com/language/acronyms/fuck.htm , but here's the 
gist:

[Fuck] is a very old word, recorded in English since the 15th century 
(few acronyms predate the 20th century), with cognates in other Germanic 
languages. The Random House Historical Dictionary of American Slang 
(Random House, 1994, ISBN 0-394-54427-7) cites Middle Dutch fokken = "to 
thrust, copulate with"; Norwegian dialect fukka = "to copulate"; and 
Swedish dialect focka = "to strike, push, copulate" and fock = "penis". 
Although German ficken may enter the picture somehow, it is problematic 
in having e-grade, or umlaut, where all the others have o-grade or zero-
grade of the vowel.
