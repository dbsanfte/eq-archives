On my wife's PC I use the following settings:

EQ
Texture Quality = Medium
Dynamic Lights = OFF
Mip Mapping = OFF
800x600

3dfx Control Panel (D3D)
LOD = -1.50
2XFSAA

EQ is playable, but not fantastic. I do not get any stutters or
massive FPS hits. The 2XFSAA clears up texture popping and shimmering.
I can play at 1024x768, but I have to disable the FSAA.

System Specs
Athlon 900
384 PC133 SDRAM
Via Sound (built into Mother Board)
WinME
DX8.1
x3dfx 1.08 (optimized for DX8 - www.voodoofiles.com)
