> definitely a challenge, it was a lot of fun to figure out how to
> do it all by myself.  Basically, clear the tentacle terrors,
> even if it takes you a few tries and runs to zone.  Once
> they're gone, cast Invis to Undead and tiptoe into the
> pit around the Festering Hags, who won't be able to see you.
> If you've never been in the pit before, it helps to know
> the way out so you're not stumbling around until your
> ItU wears off -- climb the ladder, then turn right a little
> bit and jump up onto the platform that will be about chest-high
> at that point -- the one to your left only goes to a dead-end.
> Then either run through the ghost room and out, or hop over
> the small gap in the catwalk and go out the way you came in.
>
> You should also be able to get the Meldrath bone solo at 48/49,
> but *don't* try to kill him -- you'll likely fail.  Just buff
> up, especially with all the Magic Resistance you can manage,
> buy a SoW or click your Jboots if you have them, then run
> into his lair, grab the bone, and run like hell.
>
> The only one that would be hard to solo, period, is the
