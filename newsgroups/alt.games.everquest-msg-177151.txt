> I posted this sometime last week as to how I do my swarm kiting.  I've
> found this method to be the fastest to beat 8 mobs.  Some bards like
> to continually twist the DoT's.  I like to use charm.  Here is my
> previous post:
>
> I started swarm kiting at level 40 using my level 39 charm.  I know
> the level 39 one uses mana but it lasts longer.  Plus, I can swarm
> kite mobs higher than level 37.  The Great Divide is a good zone to
> learn how to swarm kite, right around the Nexus Spires.  Cast a DoT on
> a mob and have it chase you.  A Drakkel Dire Wolf is a good mob to
> practice with.  Now, find some other Drakkel Dire Wolves or Frost
> Giants and run past them.  Keep doing this till you have a bunch of
> mobs following you.  Whatever you do, do not switch targets.  Use the
> original Drakkel Dire Wolf you DoT'd to charm.  Charm that wolf and
> have it attack any mob in the pack.  Keep Selo's up and keep track of
> your pet's health.  Once it get's down to around a bubble of health
> break the charm, especially if you have a few Frost Giants beating on
> it.  Run around in circles to keep the pack together and DoT the
> nearly dead wolf to death.  Once that wolf has died, target another
> mob and charm that mob.  Have that mob attack the pack.  If you happen
> to charm a giant and he's beating on a wolf, slap a DoT on that wolf
> so that you get full xp even if the giant kills the wolf.  DoT the
> giant to death....rinse and repeat.  Unless you have SoW, remember to
> keep Selo's up at all times and if you do this near the Nexus Spires
> be careful not to target the Nexus Scion accidentaly and send your pet
> to beat on it.  I did this once and I expected to die.  The Nexus
> Scion didn't cast a spell on me but as soon as I got near her I was
> punched for 1,967 points of damage.  If you accidentaly send your pet
> to beat on the Scion, just zone.
>
> I would suggest trying this swarm kiting only if you have a good
> handle on regualr charm kiting.  My best swarm kite has been 3 Frost
> giants and 5 drakkel dire wolves on my tail.  I beat them all without
> getting hit once.  I'm sure there are other bards out there that have
> had better packs to swarm kite.
