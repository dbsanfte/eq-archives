> http://www.geocities.com/copelandeq/
> 
> It's a program, you give it info about your char and weapons, and it
> calculates approximate damage output. Nobody claims it to be exact,
> but in my experience it's close.
> 
> 
> The "ratio" that people keep mentioning is the damage/delay ratio of
> a weapon.
> For example, a 10/29 weapon is 10 damage with a delay of 29 tenths of
> a second (2.9 seconds). 10/2.9 is 3.49/sec, 7/2.8 is 2.5/sec. This
> isn't a very good indication though because there are more factors
> involved.
> 
> Weapons do a maximum of (dmg*2)+bonuses real damage. Bonuses amount
> to 1 up until around your mid-twenties when it gets much more
> complicated. Everybody gets increases based on offence and weapon
> skills, and melee classes get an extra primary only bonus.
> 
> At least that's how I understand it.
