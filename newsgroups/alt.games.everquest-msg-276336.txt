How do buyers find you in the first place?  Unless they're
browsing all the traders (which I find hard to imagine),
they're using the /bazaar window.  When they click on Find
Trader in that window, or (as of the latest update) when
they right-click on a trader's name to get pointed in that
direction, the selected trader becomes visible.  And since
it's the ONLY trader visible, it makes it pretty darned
easy to spot him: he'll be the only character with "Trader"
as part of his name!
