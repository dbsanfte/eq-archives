>As an old UO player, in EQ what is kiting?
>

Fighting a mob without allowing it into melee range - most commonly,
root the mob, snare it, DOT (Damage Over Time spell), and blast it.
When root breaks, keep moving away as the mob approaches. It's
possible without SOW (Spirit of Wolf. Faster movement), but much
easier with it.
