The best place to research crime statistics is:
   http://www.fbi.gov/ucr/ucr.htm

The FBI, through their Uniform Crime Reporting system, has the
most comprehensive crime statistics for the United States, including
*every* murder committed in the US.  Their figures for 1999 (data
for 2000 have not been fully tabulated yet) are:

                                  Race of offender
                         Total  White  Black Other Unknown
White Victims            3,336  2,779    452    51    54 
Black Victims            2,869    154  2,674    10    31 
Other Race Victims         181     34     17   127     3 
Unknown Race                48     17     12     1    18 

Note that the total number of murders in the above table
(6,434) is significantly less than the total number of murders
reported in 1999 (15,533).  This is because, obviously,
the murderer has not been apprehended in many murders, and
thus it is impossible to determine their race.  Less obviously,
the local police who are responsible for reporting crime
data to the FBI do not always bother to include such
information as race of offender/victim in their reports to
the FBI even when it is known to them.  The table above
only lists the murders for which the race of both the
victim and the offender have actually been reported
(even if they are reported simply as "unknown").

What's interesting, though, is that if we assume that the
above table is an accurate sampling and that the rest of
the murders for 1999 followed the same pattern, we find
that "blacks murdering whites" would total 452*(15533/6434),
or 1091 total murders for the year.  Needless to say,
that's significantly less than the "1800" that "Julia"
claims.  So one has to wonder just where in the hell she's
getting her figures.

What's even more interesting is that, as you rightly suspected,
far more whites are killed by other whites than by blacks
(2779 versus 452, or if pro-rated for total murders,
6709 versus 1091), a 6:1 ratio.

Or as the 1999 FBI Uniform Crime Report itself states:

# Data suggest that the relationship among murder
# victims and offenders most often is intraracial. Figures
# based on one victim/one offender incidents in 1999 indicate
# that 94 percent of black murder victims were slain by
# black offenders and 85 percent of white murder victims
# were slain by white offenders.

So people are overwhelmingly most often murdered
by members of their *own* race.
