EJ is indeed interesting to cross.  With both live and dead mobs invis
won't help much in crossing, so sow if your shaman comes along, or sow
pots if he doesn't are a good idea as well as checking a map so everyone
knows where you are headed.  If you get attacked and stop to fight adds
often come from behind every tree, running for CoM or nearest zone line
is often wiser at your levels.  Also consider binding just inside CoM if
you plan on being there a while, if no one else than at least your
puller.  Makes CR much faster <EG>.  Fun zone, good luck!
