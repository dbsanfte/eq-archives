> Once you've used the CD keys included with the game to register an
account,
> they are forever associated with that account and can't be used to start a
> new one. So once you've activated an account (even if you activated it and
> got tired of it that same day), the CDs are useless to anyone who doesn't
> already have an account. To already have an account they must already have
> the CDs. They might be of some limited use to someone who used to play EQ,
> lost their original CDs, and wants to reinstall the game and use their old
> login, but that's about it.
>
> Yes, the buyer could use your login and password, and thus use the account
> you registered yourself, but your details are in Verant's database as the
> account owner, not theirs. Transferring accounts is against the terms of
> the license, and VI treat such cases as if the account is stolen. The
> original owner can always get back access to the account with some phone
> calls/emails to VI, so people are a bit wary of buying accounts from other
> people. Also, they would never be able to arrange things like server
> transfers, since VI would look at the registration details and assume the
> account had been eBayed. If your buyer is stupid enough to buy a suspect
> account, they may as well go buy one on eBay full of high level
characters,
> rather than buy your CDs just to save them a couple of bucks of the
> purchase price.
