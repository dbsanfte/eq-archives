I was playing with the cease and desist line of spells for paladins last 
night and was tired of mashing too many buttons so I put everything into 
one macro button named "Combo".

/doability 1 <sense heading>
/doability 4 <bash>
/doability 5 <taunt>
/cast 6 <cease>
/cast 7 <desist>

Turned out to be quite useful for chain stunning. Now I'm thinking of 
doing

/cast 1 <color flux line>
/cast 2 
/cast 3
/cast 4

for a stun button for my enchanter. Then I only need to mash one button 
for chain stuns.
