Ok, a warning before giving you the loc, Faydedar is a lv 55 wizard dragon who
spawns around the area of the firepot room, and melee's for 125-225dmg pet hit.

Loc of firepot room is neg 12260, pos 4665, (neg277 under water), as you
approach you will see the tops of  wizard spires, in the center of these on the
sea floor is a square vertical shaft a few feet long and going straight down,
the end of this is in the roof of the firepot room. The portals are arranged 3
on each wall, be invis the first time you use one to a KoS place as some pop you
out beside guards.

These are in order left to right as facing the appropriate wall:

North wall :  Grobb(fire on floor)    Oggok(6sided grey)        Erudin(6sided
tall)

East wall :    Neriak(6sided grey)    Gfey(4sided wood)         Felwythe(6sided
rust)

South wall : Halas(brown box)       East FP(Dark with blue)  Cabilis(tall gold)

West wall :  Kaladim(white marble)  Ak'anon(lava lamp)      Rivervale(torch
stand)
