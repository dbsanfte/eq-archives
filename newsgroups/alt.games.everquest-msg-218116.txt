> Which buffs help improve skillups?  Pottery, brewing, etc?

Skillups are determined by your Int/Wis whichever is higher.  Smithing
uses Int/Wis/Str, and fletching uses Int/Wis/Dex.

If you're high enough level (45 I believe), the best buff to get
is KEI, which increases Int and Wis by 25.

If you're below 45, go for Insight or Brilliance.
