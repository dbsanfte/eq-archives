With ICS (Internet Connection Sharing) The Host PC (in your case the
PC with WIn98SE and the DSL modem) acts as the router.

You will need a network card in the Host PC and PC2. The only problem
you may run into is adding the network card to the host PC. You will
need an open PCI slot and a free IRQ.

You can attach the two PCs directly with a Cross-Over cable like I
mentioned before, or you can buy a cheap Hub (20-30$) and use regular
networking cables.

DSL-->Win98SE PC with ICS-->PC2
or
DSL-->Win98SE PC with ICS-->Hub<--PC2
