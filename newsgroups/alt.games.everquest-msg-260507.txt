I don't know how he did it, but when I've measured speed boosts
(comparing SOW, wolf form, mounts, etc.) it's been simple enough:
find a level straightaway.  Run along it while occasionally hitting
my hotkey that gives /time and /loc.  Compute distance travelled
per second.  Repeat with different speed buffs.

I suppose if you're looking for zone-specific changes, some zones
might make it hard by not having straightaways, or by having too
many KOS mobs, etc.
