The art of killing something while running away, either
with spells (usually) or bows. Usually it involves putting
a snare on the mob so it runs slowly, a speed buff on
yourself so you run fast, and then firing off nukes or
dots at the mob while keeping out of melee range.
Lots of variations of the technique depending on class,
see "quad kiting" "aggro kiting" "bow kiting" "swarm
kiting" etc
