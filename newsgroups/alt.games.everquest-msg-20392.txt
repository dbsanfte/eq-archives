> > I have a level 9 Gnome wizard and what I wanted to know was, are Area Effect or

> I bought them all, but honestly I never saw much use for them at low levels. At
> level nine, I'm guessing you still solo quite a bit. And even if you don't, you're
> hunting things on the order of orc cents and such. You're AoE spells are *not* going
> to get your butt out of a jam if you get swarmed. They'll likely just tick off even
> more mobs.  In a group scenario, when everyone is /assisting your tank fighting one
> of a number of mobs, if you throw one, then *ALL* the rest of the mobs not on the
> tank will immediately aggro on you, causing the fighters and healers no end of angst
> trying to save your ass.  The AoE's will become far more vital closer to the end
> game, or when you are past 29 and start kiting (if you are soloing, at least) but at
> the low levels, no...... I found them mostly to be a waste of money.
> 
> > Also, is it worth getting Shadow Step if you've already got Fade?
> 
> Definitely. Greater range than fade, and it was often *just* enough to get my
> endangered ass far enough away to get off a gate and escape from a tight spot. Get
> it. Keep it memmed along with gate, particularly when soloing.  And remember, those
> spells only send you *downhill* or <possibly> on perfectly flat land. Try to make
> sure you are fighting on some sort of a slope, even a gentle one, with a lot of room
> on the downhill side for maximum teleport distance. If you stand in a hole at the
> very bottom and cast it, you wont' move more than a foot or so. Try it if you don't
> believe me. Impressive lightshow, but the mob killing you won't be to put off by it.
