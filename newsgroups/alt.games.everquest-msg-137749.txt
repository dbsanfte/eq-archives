> Oh Well, it should be fun, another question: Will druid harmony work on
> critter that is attacking you if you are not attacking it?


No, Harmony is only an agro radius reducer.  If you are already
being attacked it's too late for Harmony.

Harmony is useful for pulling single mobs out of a group at a
static spawn site.  Cast it on a mob near the center of the
group, (it's an area effect spell) and then pull one from the
outer edge.  As long as the mob you pull doesn't run too near
one of the harmony'd mobs they will stay put and you can thus
fight one at a time.
