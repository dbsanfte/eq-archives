Here's a good link for help...
http://www.eqinterface.com/

Basically, in the \everquest\uifiles directory, create a new folder and name
it whatever you want.  Copy the skin files from the zip you have saved into
the new folder.  In game, use the command /loadskin "new skin" 1.  Adding
the 1 will keep your windows in the locations you are using now (leave off
the 1 if you want the new skins window layout).

Hope that helps.
