The cool thing is, as you can see, you can add rules that only apply to one 
group, or one set of groups. You can also add positive things, like:

%%%%%%%%%%%%%%

or subjects I like:

%%%%%%%%%%%%%%% EQ STUFF TO WATCH FOR %%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%

%%%%%%%%%%%%%%

versatile), but you can simply click Article -> Add To Score File for quick 
kills and stuff based on a variety of criteria
