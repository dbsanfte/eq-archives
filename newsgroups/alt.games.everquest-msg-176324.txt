Taunt works like going up to the town bully, poking him and saying, "Your
Mama."

Keep saying it, and the bully will want to kick your butt. If the bully
doesn't care (taunt fails), it kicks the butt of the next person on the
list.

Keep poking and taunting the bully, and eventually, you are the only thing
he wants to kill.

By the way, the first PC that hits a mob, successful or not, gets a special
place at the top of the list. However, a PC that continues to taunt and hit
a mob, and is closer forces the mob to deal with that PC first.

Note that an eager caster, that quickly does major DD then immedialy sits
down or runs will make a mob completely forget about what it was doing and
go directly for the caster. That's why casters should wait until enough
aggro is built up by tanks before casting. Additionally, they should wait a
few seconds before sitting down.
