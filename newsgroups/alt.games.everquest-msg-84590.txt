At skill 193, you should be able to do Gold Fire Opal (196), Gold Star Ruby
(199), Gold Fire Emerald (202), Gold Sapphire (204), and Gold Ruby (207)
before switching to Platinum Malachite (218).

The gold/plat transition is the only place I recommend staying with the
lower skill metal even though the price is higher.  Gold is easier to
enchant, and the success rate will be slightly higher.
