No, the *haste* of bard songs will not stack with enchanter haste.  The
spells will stack, and if one gives you strength and another gives you
agility, you'll get both the strength and agility, but the higher haste will
be the haste you get.  In other words, with epic going and shissar on,
you're at shissar speed, not epic+shissar speed.

Bards do get a song or two that do stack with other spell haste, but in very
miniscule amounts.
