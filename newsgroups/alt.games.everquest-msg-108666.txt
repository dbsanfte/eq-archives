> Hey there,
>
> First thing I would do, is goto eqatlas.com. Print off the Padual Caverns
> map. Its pretty easy once you make the trip a few times, but can be very
> daunting at first.
>
> Then, when looking at the map, the south end is generally good for 8-12.
> Hunt bugs and shrooms. The fungus aliens are a little higher and tougher.
> The north east end is good 12-16. Again bugs and shrooms. The aliens are
> again a little tougher. Once you hit 16 start making your way to the
middle
> connecting caves to the north. Fight Bugs, shrooms and shiknars. this
should
> be good 16-20 maybe even 21 or 22.
>
> After all that the Recondites near the Shadow Haven zone are sweet 22-26.
> There are two camps, closest to the zone and at the north end of the
shore.
> Take em in a small group and you'll be fine.
>
> General advice.
> Invis or sneak/hide is your friend. The fungus aliens (patog fiends) will
> gang up on you like mad.
>
> Announce your trains when fleeing, especially if fleeing to Hollowshade
moor
> (north zone) or Shadeweavers Thicket (south Zone) there are usually
lowbies
> near each, and it is bad to die from unannounced train.
>
> Other than that make sure to keep your skills up, P caverns has a dungeon
> bonus and a under 30 dungeon bonus. Its easy to fall behind and out level
> your fighting abilities.
>
> Good Hunting
