Depends on your level.

Mana For Priest classes (Druid, Cleric, Shaman, Rangers, Paladins):

((WIS/5)+2) x Level = Total mana

Mana For Int Casters (Necromancers, Mages, Enchanters, Wizards, Bards, Shadow Knights):

((INT/5)+2) x Level =Total mana


So basically take your level and divide it by 5 to get the number
of mana points PER wisdom/int point.

So if you have a choice between a +10 mana item and
a +2 wisdom item. The +10 mana item would be better until
you reach level 25 then the +2 wisdom item will start to add more
mana.
