> So, what you want is:
> 
> /cast 1
> /pause 30,/cast 1
> /pause 30,/cast 1
> /pause 30,/cast 1
> /pause 30,/cast 1

Close, your syntax is correct but you are making the error of assuming 
the pauses happen in a logical way; they don't.

The pause appearing on the line happens after the other command on that 
line, even though it is written before the command.

So your macro will cast 1, move to the second line, and cast 1 again 
immediately, without a pause to account for cast time and spell icon 
reset, or even time for a fizzle.

Then when you get to the end, your macro will pause for 3 seconds after 
beginning cast of the final spell, even though I suspect you didn't mean 
it to do so.
