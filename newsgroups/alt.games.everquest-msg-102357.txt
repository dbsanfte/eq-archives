Actually, it is considered a virus because it can be installed locally
on your machine and do completely harmless (yet annoying) stuff to your
system. The one that was present in the "TGOS Busted" e-mail does not
have the ability to locally attach itself to your machine. It is simply
put there as an annoyance.

For future reference, if you are ever caught in a JS.Loop just hit
Ctrl-Alt-Delete and find your newsreader in the task manager list. Click
on "End Task" and it should disappear. You will have to re-open your
news reader but you do not have to restart your computer. I've seen too
many people freak out and restart their system with the loop. Ending it
in task manager ensures that you don't corrupt any files on your system,
which can happen from hitting the reset button while Windows is still
operational.
