Only under 100 stam does it slow you down to run out of fatigue (yellow
bar). Over 100 stam does 2 things, and 2 things alone:

1: You can breath longer underwater

2: You get a fixed amount of hp per point of stam, all the way to 255.

1 is situational, 2 will help you every day of your EQ life. No other
stat is more beneficial, except for AC, which at your level should be
your primary concern.
