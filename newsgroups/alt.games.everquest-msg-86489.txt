"Tom" wrote

> I'm mostly retiring my paladin, who has a frost book and would like to MQ
> it.  What is the standard etiquette for the trade?  Do I get items first,
or
> is the MQ done first?

Someone has to trust someone else.  If he gives you the items you
want in trade before the MQ is done, he's at risk. You might decide
to take the money (or items) and run.  If you do the MQ before getting
the trade, you're at risk.  He might decide to take the item and run.
So you have to decide who's more trustworthy:  you or him....

The general way it's done is both parties go to the place where the
MQ is done, the trade is done, you turn in your item and he turns
in his.  You /cheer and leave. ;)
