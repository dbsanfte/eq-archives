>     I bound myself near the PoK portal to The Nexus. As an agnostic High
Elf
> that had done very little factioning in any of the places you need to go,
> this is how I would do a cycle:
>
> (1) Get box from Trep.
> (2) Go to Grobb first, using the PoK book. Use Illusion: Dark Elf or
> Illusion: Troll, both work fine (Ootok is Indifferent to DE's, Warmly to
> Trolls). I recommend Illusion: DE, you'll see why below. You need to cast
> the illusion twice, once when you arrive in Innothule after zoning and
once
> when you zone into Grobb. If some moron has trained Basher Sutok to the
> zone, Invis before entering, then cast your illusion by the PoD.

> (4) Run right across the newbie ground and buy the Purification Tablet
from
> Toxdil. Illusion: DE should be sufficient to buy from him. I started off
> buying from Krystin Charcoal, but she gets moved out of position (and
stops
> selling) by new players that hit "a" when dealing with her. They don't
know
> about the secret wall and Toxdil. ;-)
> (5) Gate back to PoK, go to Qeynos. Illusion: Human is good enough for
Crow
> to deal with you.
> (6) Gate back to PoK, go to The Nexus. Go to The Bazaar and get that
piece.
> (7) Gate back to PoK, go to either Shar Vahl or The Great Divide.
> Shadeweaver's Thicket is the only zone where navigating with your clipping
> zone all the way in will be difficult, otherwise you can leave it all the
> way in all the time (for your trip to The Bazaar). There's a bank in the
hut
> just before the vendor's hut in Shadeweaver's, so if you get encumbered by
> coins you can stop and bank on this run.
> (8) Gate back to PoK, go to the other of Shar Vahl or The Great Divide.

to
> make five trips, not six. I chose Shar Vahl and Thurg as my "buy two"
