Harmony is a small radius AE that reduces mob agro radius.  It is (or
was?) not resistable, but no longer works on mobs over L40.  Harmony
of Nature is a single target Lull-type spell that works on mobs up to
L65(?) but is resistable just as the Enchanter/Cleric/Paladin Lull
line spells are.  Unlike the Lull-line, Harmony and Harmony of Nature
only work outdoors.
