On Thu, 06 Feb 2003 02:28:24 GMT, "imgame" <imgame@nospamkc.rr.com> wrote:

>Hello everyone, I was wondering on haste items that carry a fixed
>percentage, are they added together or just operate with the highest
>percent? In other words do item haste stack to a cap or just limited to
>highest haste percent item and the rest are null and void?

The latter -- the haste on the "highest" item is used, the rest
are ignored.

But note that this is for worn items with an "intrinsic" item-flavor haste.
A few weapons will proc a haste *buff* which will indeed stack with
a "worn" haste item, because *spell* (buff-style) haste is a separate
thing from worn, intrinsic haste.
