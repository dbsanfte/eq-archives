> If I wanted to fix a hotkey so my Wood Elf Druid forages a lot, how
> would I go about doing it?
> 
> I don't want to bind it to a directional key or anything; I just want it
> so that my Druid forages multiple times in a row when I hit the button

Well, assuming that Foraging is in your ability slot 2, and that its 
recycle time is 30 seconds (not sure about that, you'll have to time it 
yourself...) the syntax would be:

/pause 300, /doability 2
/pause 300, /doability 2
/pause 300, /doability 2
/pause 300, /doability 2
/pause 300, /doability 2
