Michael Mc Grenaghan <belfast@yaho.com> writes:
> I have just become a summoner in the game and for some reason I cannot
> reach my pet window, I know that the command to view it is <Alt>p but
> when I press it nothing happens, someone suggested I change the
> command key and I did but even after that still nothing happened, does
> anyone know what I should do?

Look in UI_charactername_servername.ini in your EverQuest folder.
Look for PetWindow (probably).  See whether the coordinates for it
place it beyond the edge of the screen, and if so, edit the coords.

Note that there can be different coordinates (and sizes) depending
on the window size you've selected (under Options/Display/Video Modes).
