>	What's a zerg guild?  

The term originates from StarCraft, Blizzard's RTS game before
WarCraft3. The Zerg were a race that specialized in mass-production of
relatively weak units. A common tactic was to produce hordes of their
weakest units, "zerglings", early on and overwhelm the enemy with
numbers.

"Zerging" in EQ (and many other games) refers to mass suicide-rushing
to acheive a kill.
