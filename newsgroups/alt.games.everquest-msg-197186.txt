> Can you really by an armor for 2.5k by farming spiders? And a 2.5k weapon?

Go to Innothule Swamp. Find Ruala (vendor just west of the entrance to
Gukta). Buy stacks of spiderling silk at a few silver per silk. Go to
bazaar. Sell for five plat per silk. If you don't get enough from
that, mass slaughter of spiderlings will get you maybe 1.5-2 silks per
spiderling, on average, and the supply is near unlimited.

Making a few thousand platinum on spiderling silk isn't hard at all,
even at very low levels, all it takes is a bit of patience. At least
on most servers, on e.g. Firiona Vie there isn't the same market for
spiderling silk.
