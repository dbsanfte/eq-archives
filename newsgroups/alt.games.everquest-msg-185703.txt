Sensei wrote:
> ** Pathing Changes **
> 
> Today we have introduced the first stage of an ongoing project to upgrade
> the pathing system for EverQuest.
> 
> Of course this means we would like you to keep your eyes open for NPCs doing
> bad things.  NPCs will (should) move a bit differently, that is to be
> expected.  If, for example, you are used to a specific NPC taking a
> round-about route to get from one place to another and he now takes a more
> direct route - that's a good thing.
> 
> Bad things would be:
> 
> - An NPC that wants to get to you but can't
> - Pets that can't follow you through a teleporter
> - Any NPC that takes a much longer path than it should to get to a player
> (like going away from the target for a bit then heading back)
> - Any time an NPC doubles back, walking past his destination and then going
> directly back to it
> - Any significant 'warping' by NPCs
> - Any NPCs that defy gravity by walking a few feet above the ground
> 
> And any other really odd behavior by NPCs when moving.
> 
> We are aware of current issues with NPCs fleeing (either from Fear or trying
> to escape death).  For now, there is no need to report problems with NPCs
> fleeing in odd ways.  We're working on that one now.
> 
> It is VERY important that you note the location of the odd behavior, the
> name of the NPC, what it was doing (just patrolling around or chasing
> someone it was mad at) and the details of the oddity in your bug reports.
> 
> 
> Thank you for helping us with this.
> 
> - The EverQuest Development Team
>
