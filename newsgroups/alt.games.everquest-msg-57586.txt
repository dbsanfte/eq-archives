ALL tags MUST  have a closing tag when they contain data.  An empty node may
be designated with an open and close, i.e. <Username></Username>, or it
maybe designated <Username />.  The space between Username and / is optional
but I include it because some parsers require it.    In most instances,
you'll see the <Username /> method used.  Easier to read and uses less space
in large datasets.
