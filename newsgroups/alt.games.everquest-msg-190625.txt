If it keeps resetting itself to the default, try this:

Find your UI_<char>_<nn>.ini file (eg UI_Skaw_20.ini)
Locate the section:

[MapViewWnd]

And change the following lines to read:

HeightFilter=false
HeightLow=25
HeightHigh=25

This will turn off the height filter (useful for all bar multi-level
dungeons) and allow you to see more when you turn the filter on. You may
want to experiment with different values for the low and high levels; it is
vertical loc points above and below where you are standing.

You will have to do this for each character
