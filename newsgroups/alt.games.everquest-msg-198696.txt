Most of the new games using skill based character advancement allow just 
this sort of play.  Of course there is a big trade off in terms of how 
high your skills can go.  By focusing all your skills in one area you 
will naturally be much more proficient in that area than someone who 
only trained half as far in that area to allow training in other 
unrelated skills.  Key to player satisfaction with these types of 
systems is the ability to "unlearn" skills and reallocate skill 
potential, else dissatisfied folks are forced to start new characters.
