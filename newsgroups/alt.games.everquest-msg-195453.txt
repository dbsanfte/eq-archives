As charted by Mike Dorr and myself a year and a half ago, int caster
defense goes like this:

5*level from 1 through 5 (5 per level)
5+ 4*level from 6 through 10 (4 per level)
(level+5)*3 from 11 onwards (3 per level)
Cap at 145.

Offense is 4 per level up to 10 and 3 per level from 11 onwards, cap
at 140.
