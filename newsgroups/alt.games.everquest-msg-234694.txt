My shaman is only 57...but what I do for LDoN (assuming there is a main
healer ala cleric / druid).

Buff outside, starting with myself, IoS / Kragg (hp and stats).  IoS and
Kragg on melee, IoS on its own for casters.  The group stamina, group regen,
group bih li (billy). That is my standard set of buffs, I then ask if anyone
wants anything else, and will then buff that.

Med up, enter dungeon, summon pet on entering.  Pet dogdog in dual-wield
mode (hand him 2 rusty weapons), HoS on pet, then haste pet to start timer
(if no enchanter), then haste other melee.

For actual fighting, if its single, malo-x on incoming then slow, if its  a
double pull will usually lead with slow on incoming mob, then malo-x / slow
adds.

Add damage / heal as you see fit (situtations change). My spell loadout for
LDoN:

1) Canni III
2) Turgurs (75% slow)
3) Maloisna (resist debuff no recast time)
4) Bane of Nife / Disinfecting Aura / Cripple (swap slot basically)
5) Stoicism (heal over time with snare)
6) Chloroblast (direct blast heal)
7) Haste / Nuke (another kinda swap slot)
8) Root

Slots 1, 2 and 8 never change, even buffing, I learnt early on not having
root up at all times is not wise :)
