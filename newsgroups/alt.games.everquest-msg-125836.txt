Quiver haste is generally accepted to be WeightReduction/3.

The store-bought Quiver has no WR and therefore no haste.
The player-made Tailored Quiver has WR=22% == Haste 7%
The player-made Fleeting Quiver has WR=68% == Haste 20%
The Luclin Quest Jewelled Quiver has WR=35% == Haste 12%
The Thunderhoof Quiver (drops in S Karana) has WR=33% == Haste 11%

See www.eqtraders.com for recipes and www.allakhazam.com for drops & quests.

Quiver haste stacks with all other types of haste, but if you equip more
than one quiver only the one in the highest inventory slot counts.
