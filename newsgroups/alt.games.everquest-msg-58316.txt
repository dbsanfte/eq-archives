Medding while the DoTs do their job has nothing to do with mana
effeciency.  The damage/mana ratio is what matters, not how long it
takes for the spell to do the damage.  You spend X mana to do Y
damage, you have to med the same amount of time to get that mana back,
whether the spell did the Y damage all at once, or over time.

Basically, it comes down to whether you want to have short fights that
you sit between, or long fights that you sit during.
