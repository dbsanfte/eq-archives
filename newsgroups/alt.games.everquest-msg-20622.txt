> >what is this skill, what does it do?
>
>   ri-poste or <ri-post>(ri pohst')  n., v. <-post-ed, -post-ing>
>        n.
>         1.  a quick, sharp return in speech or
>                 action; counterstroke: a clever riposte.
>         2.  Fencing. a quick thrust given after
>                 parrying a lunge.
>
> Every time you get hit by a monster, the Riposte skill
> gives you a chance to make a free counter-attack swing
> in response.  This swing is over and above any melee
> swings that you might be making due to autoattack.
> Over time this can add up to a substantial amount
> of additional damage.
>
> In fact, you don't even need to be meleeing.  If your
> Riposte skill is reasonably high, you can just stand
> there with autoattack off, and watch your reflexive
> Riposte swings take down the monster that's beating on
> you.  This is fun to do with greens.
>
> Another handy thing is that if four things are beating
> on you, while you're directly meleeing one of them
> (the one you have targeted), you'll be (occasionally)
> Riposting *each* of the other ones that are hitting you.
> So by the time you kill off the one you were directly
> meleeing, you may find that your number two target
> is already down a substantial amount of health because
> of your earlier Ripostes against it.
>
> Just be aware that a monster has to be reasonably
> in front of you for Riposte to work.  If something
> hits you from behind and triggers a Riposte, you'll
> get the usual "you can not see your target" message.
>
