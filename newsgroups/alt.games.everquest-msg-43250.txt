Bob Ellis wrote:

> In addition to my earlier reply, how does one go about updating BIOS?  And,
> how does one check the BIOS on ones video card?

This video BIOS revision number is the first thing that gets displayed on your
monitor when you start your machine, even before any mobo BIOS message.

Once you have this you can go to your card or chipset manufacturers website and
lok for a newer BIOS revision. They also have informaion on how to make the BIOS
update, but it usually amounts to making a boot disk and usign that boot disk to
start your computer, then running the BIOS patcher from DOS.
