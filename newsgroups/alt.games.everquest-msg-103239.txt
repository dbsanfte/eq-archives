> > First question, where should I put the 20 points I get when I first
> > create this Halfling Ranger?
>
> I believe Halfling Rangers start with Strength at 70; put all 20 points
> into Str and bring it up to 90.
>
>
> > What attributes should I concentrate on raising once I have him up and
> > running?
>
> Get more strength.  Halflings have good Stamina, Agility and Dexterity
> so don't worry much about these for a while.  Also don't worry about
> Wisdom.  IIRC, Halfling Rangers start with Wis at 85 which is more than
> enough; my Half-Elf Ranger (lvl 33 now) only has 65 Wis and has never
> run out of mana during a fight.  Rangers are a melee class, first and
> foremost.  Extra Wis will help cut downtime, (eventually) but should
> never be made a priority.
>
>
> > Can a Halfling Ranger wear smith made Banded Armor?  If not, what is
> > the best armor he can get from a Tailor or Blacksmith?
>
> Yes, small banded armor will last you a long time.  My ranger still
> wears a few pieces.
>
>
> > I guess that should help get me started, any other suggestions (other
> > than "don't do it") would also be helpful.
>
> Try and keep 1h and 2h slash maxed for each level.  This will be tough
> because of the new 'increased experience' below level 10.  You'll do a
> lot more damage with a 2 hander even after you get Dual Wield (depending
> on your actual weapons) until you reach the 30s.  You might also want to
> do piercing, though it will be very difficult keeping all three skills
> maxed.  You'll get Kick at 5 which is also an important source of
> damage.
> Get a bow and start practicing Archery as soon as you can, it will be
> the
> slowest skill to advance.  Archery costs money because arrows cost money
> (doh!) though you can fletch your own arrows rather cheaply; but don't
> get involved with more advanced fletching for a long time, it gets very
> expensive.
>
> Have fun!  ;-)
