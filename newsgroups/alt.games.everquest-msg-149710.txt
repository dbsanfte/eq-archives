>In group play (post newbee) there are several things that indicates a pourly
>played class. What *defines* bad play per class? Here is my short list, but
>I havn't played all clases yet, so please feel free to add more / modify
>
>Bard: 
>- No Heal/mana song when group is unengaged

Heal/mana during battle is very rarely useful. I'd much rather have
haste, slow, mez/charm from my bard during a fight.

>Beastlord:
>- Unengaged pet

I've still never grouped with a BL.

>Cleric:
>- Not buffing at least MT

Letting people die in routine engagements
Overhealing casters (too big, too slow heals)
Overnuking
Running out of mana during normal engagements.

>Druid:
>- No snare on target in agro enviroment

Not DSing people who are getting hit
No regen buffs
"Did you guys want me to mem evac?"

>Enchanter:
>- Not mezzing adds
>- Not mana/haste buffing

AE mez/stun in dungeons where it can create extra aggro.

>Mage:
>- Unengaged pet

Not DSing people who are getting hit
Not distributing focus items to group casters
Failure to produce mod rods in appropriate situations

>Monk:
>- Not announcing when mend is up

Inability to split pull

>Necromancer:
>- Using fear in high agro enviroment

Medding themself to death
DoTting wrong target
Using Chill/Ignite Bones on large targets
Poor pet control

>Paladin:
>- ??

Not stunning casting MObs
Misuse (over or under) of Lay On Hands

>Ranger:
>- ?? no arrows

Trying to tank
Using high-aggro spells

>Rouge:
>- Not opposing MT

Spelling their own class name "rouge."
Getting undue aggro
Using Instill Doubt in target-rich environments

>SK:
>- ??

If they're the MT:
  Failing to use high-aggro spells
  Overusing lifetaps instead of melee

If they're not the MT:
  Using high-aggro spells

>Shaman:
>- Not slowing melee mobs

Not casting appropriate buffs
Trying to cast buffs that are overwritten by cleric buffs
Overmeleeing
Medding over high-level Cannibalize spells

>Warrior:
>- Not taunting before breaking mezz

Not sitting before breaking mezz if aggro has been built up
Letting the cleric or enchanter die before you do

>Wizzard:
>- Nuking before agro is established

Spelling your own class name with two "z"s
Waiting for tank/cleric/enchanter to die before evac'ing
