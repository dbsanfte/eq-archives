> It depends on your level.
>
> Your total mana is (((A/5)+2) * L) + M, where A is INT or WIS depending on
> your class, L is your level, and M is the total of +MANA bonuses you have
on
> your gear. Roughly speaking, 1 point of INT/WIS is worth 0.2 mana per
> level - e.g. at L20 1 WIS is worth 4 mana, at L50 1 WIS is worth 10 mana.
> Note however there is a soft cap on INT/WIS after 200, whereby each point
> over 200 is only worth a maximum of 1 mana.
>
> Your two items even out mana-wise at about L26/27 (at L26 the AC4 gives
you
> just under 25 mana). If you're under L26, go for the AC5 piece, if you're
> over that it depends how much you want the extra STR and AC vs. a few more
> mana (even at L60 the AC4 would only be 52 mana, so it's not a big deal
> anyway).
