Most towns have a potion vendor, who sells a variety of
potions (such as invisibility, water breathing, etc.)

Most also sell two potions with a healing effect:
Pale Green Potion, heals you a small amount, but for a short
while you can't move, cast, or fight at all. Better for
downtime between combat.

Bark Potion, increases your HP regen by 2 HP per tick for a
few minutes, but prevents you from moving (unless you are
charmed or feared).
