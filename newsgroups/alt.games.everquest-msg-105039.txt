> First of all, don't expect the greens to stop attacking you because you
> made a few levels. We went in there last month (three of us) and the
> lowest member of the group was 53, and everything STILL jumped us, right
> from the entry. You'll either invis, or fight your way in. I reccomend
> fighting, personally. In case it goes south, it's nice to know that at
> least part of your path to zone is clear of adds for the "RUN FOR YOUR
> FUCKING LIFE!" portion of the festivities.
>
> Work down past the lake, clearing all the green mobs, then through the
> tunnels and up the ramp to the castle entry. Once that flat area in
> front of the castle is clear, sit and med/buff up. Send in puller and
> clear the front hall and be sure to have them pull the mobs hanging in
> front, including the gargoyles who are above the hall in those raised
> alcoves. We usually go to the left, through the dining room. That root
> seems easier. It's nice to set up back in the hall near the kitchen,

> butler/maid rooms between gypsy spawns. Once the path is clear you can
> start pulling the dancers and musicians from the ballroom on out to your
> camp. Watch out for the gargoyles. They can aggro in mass and be a
