Casters Realm has a nifty mana calculator; see
http://eq.castersrealm.com/misc/manacalc/ The calculator is an asp page,
so I can't tell you the formula, but it appears to be level dependent
(ie 150 wisdom at 40th level will give more mana than 150 wisdom at 3rd
level; 200 wis is pretty much wasted at 10th level and pretty much
essential at 50th level.) I do know that, after 200 wisdom (or
intelligence for INT casters), you only get 1 additional mana point per
stat (ie if 200 WIS = X mana, then 201 WIS = X+1, 202=X+2, etc, while
199 might equal X-30.)
