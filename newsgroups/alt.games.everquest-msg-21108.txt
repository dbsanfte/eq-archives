Export it as a CSV and it becomes super easy in Perl:

---begin script---

#!/usr/bin/perl

open(CSV, "druiditems.csv");
open(OUT, ">output.html");
print OUT "<table>\n";
while (<CSV>) {
    	my(@line) = split(/,/, $_);
    	print OUT " <tr>\n";
    	foreach(@line) {
    	    	print OUT "  <td>$_</td>\n";
    	    	}
    	print OUT " </tr>\n";
    	}    	    	
print OUT "</table>\n";

---end script---

Voila, just put it in a pretty page with your navigation and whatnot and you are done.
