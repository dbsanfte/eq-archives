> As others have suggested, how about a "martial throw" (or "trip", or
> some other name) that lets them briefly knock down a mob?  (A tick or
> two where the mob is unable to attack and is an easier target as it
> stands back up.)  If it included a way to physically move the mob, e.g.
> to throw it further away from the cleric it was beating on, all the
> better.
>
> Or how about something that actually lets them leverage the Feign Death
> skill in a group setting?  I'm always worried about using FD in a group
> because the mob immediately heads after the rest of the party.  If I'm
> the tank (happens sometimes, e.g. mage+cleric+monk) it's okay, because if
> a fight goes sour I can hang on while the casters run for the zone, and
> then I FD after they're safe.  Even if there's a better DPS in the group
> so the monk doesn't have agro, the monk can sometimes take over after the
> MT dies and fight a holding action while the casters boogie.  But given
> the recent tendency toward fast fights (high-damage mobs), the monk's
> lousy mitigation makes this harder.  So how about a discipline -- and
> make it available before level 50, please! -- that gives the monk vastly
> increased mitigation (and perhaps even some resists) and also increased
> agro as long as he does NOT attack?  I.e., turn off autoattack, activate
> the discipline, and now you're using your martial arts skills to keep the
> mob busy to buy time for the rest of the group.  Maybe allow the "martial
> throw" while running this discipline, but nothing that lets you directly
> damage the mob.
>
> I could even see this discipline working against multiple mobs.  You'd
> only be raising agro vs your current target, but you'd be mitigating
> damage from all comers, just like in the kung fu movies where the hero
> takes on a zillion ninjas and none of them can connect. :-)
>
> There, a couple of ideas for things that seem to fit with the "martial
> artist" image (which is, as noted elsewhere in this thread, what the
> monk class really is), that would make the monk more useful to a group.

These are the types of abilites that would make a Monk something more than a
Warrior with FD and lousy mitigation.
