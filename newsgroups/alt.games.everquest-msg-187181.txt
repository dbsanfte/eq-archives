with 50 in hide. Vah Shir start with (I think) 50 in sneak. Other
races aren't natural sneakers or hiders.

cap is lower than the starting skill levels. So if you want to make a
rogue to sneak/hide his way past mobs but don't feel like leveling
him/her, you should go with halfling.
