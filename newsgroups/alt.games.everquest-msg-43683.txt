For cold, you want either turquoise (just vC) or peridot (AC and vC); for fire you
want either hematite (just vF) or emerald (AC and vF). A quick reference:

Silver:
    turquoise: bracelet, 2 vC
    hematite: ring, 2 vF
    peridot: ring, 2 AC, 3 vC
    emerald: ring, 2 AC, 3 vF

Electrum
    turquoise: ring, 3 vC
    hematite: necklace, 4 vF
    peridot: bracelet, 3 AC, 4 vC
    emerald: ring, 3 AC, 4 vF

Gold
    turquoise: ring, 5 vC
    hematite: necklace, 7 vF
    peridot: bracelet, 4 AC, 6 vC
    emerald: bracelet, 4 AC, 6 vF

Platinum
    turquoise: bracelet, 7 vC
    hematite: ring, 7 vF
    peridot: ring, 4 AC, 10 vC
    emerald: ring, 4 AC, 10 vF

Velium
    turquoise: bracelet, 8 vC
    hematite: ring, 9 vF
    peridot: ring, 5 AC, 11 vC
    emerald: ring, 5 AC, 11 vF

Best combination, using all slots, would be 2 velium peridot rings and 2 gold
peridot bracelets for cold (18 AC, 34 vC) or 2 velium emerald rings, 2 gold
emerald bracelets and a gold hematite necklace for fire (18 AC, 41 vF).

Fire opals do not give vF, but they are VERY nice gear, giving HP and AC. At a
guild auction a few months ago, we sold a pair of velium fire opal rings, each
with 6 AC and 65 HP, for 1000pp. Fire opals only make rings.

Aside from diamond or blue diamond jewelry, you can add in a pair of electrum
black sapphire which give 2 AC, 35 HP and 25 mana each (yeah, you don't need the
mana, but come on, 70 HP??), velium ruby veil (7 STR, 7 WIS) and, with your cold
gear, a velium sapphire necklace (7 INT, 7 STR).

While EQTraders is down, you may want to look at Castersrealm's jewelry site at
http://eq.castersrealm.com/misc/jewellery/jewel_table.asp Not as nicely put
together or as printer friendly, but it does have the info you are looking for.
