No you cannot make a social hot-key.

One thing you can do is make a "hot-Inventory" item. Hold down the
mouse button while hovering over your head slot in the inventory
screen. After a couple of seconds the slot will pop onto the cursor
just like a social button. You can then add this slot to your bank of
hot keys. Now you can right-click the item without having to open up
your inventory screen.
