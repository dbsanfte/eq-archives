> Info on Spider Silk drops, to include spiderling drops
> From an earlier Post:  Credit Lance Berg

I can add a few items to this, for spiders in the Field of Bone:

Spiderling silks drop from:
- Bonebinder hatchlings (levels 3-6 or so?)
- Bonecrawler hatchlings (levels 1-3 or so)
- Burynaibane hatchlings (?? not sure of level range, green to me at 10+)
Spider silks drop from:
- Bonebinder spiders (levels 13-16 or so?)
- Bonecrawler spiders (levels 8-11 or so)

However, FoB isn't exactly teeming with spiders. When nobody's hunting
them, there's usually 3-4 each of the bonebinders and bonecrawlers, and
about half a dozen spiderlings floating around. There's a particular
area where the spiderlings seem to congregate for some reason (a bit
north of the ruin at the edge of the desert near Cabilis. However,
numbers aren't high enough for constant hunting.
