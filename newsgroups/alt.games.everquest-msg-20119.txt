> Incase you are still having trouble, to heal yourself below level 9 you
> must rely on bandages and Lay Hands.  They are both skills, put some
> practice points into Bind Wounds and buy a stack of  bandages or find a
> friendly enchanter that can conjure them for you.
>
> Make buttons for these skills so they are easy to get to.  Lay Hands is
> an extremely powful heal but you can only use it once every EQ day.  To
> use it target yourself by clicking on your name and then click you Lay
> Hands button. To bandage yourself click on your name and click Bind
> Wounds.
>
> If you don't have these buttons yet click on Abilities and then right
> click on an empty button - this will bring up you skills and then select
> the button you want.  Now place a copy of the button on you easy access
> panel - left click and hold the button you want to copy until it
> attaches to you mouse pointer then drag it to the new spot and click.
