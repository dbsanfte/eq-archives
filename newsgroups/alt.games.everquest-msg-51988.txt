> 2) How do I get my Wood Elf Ranger to run with / first use the bow ?

If you want the bow to be visible, just unequip it and then put it back in
the Ranged slot. You will see the last weapon equipped until you enter
combat.

To use the bow use the Ranged Attack button, as described in other
responses. You cannot bind the bow to the Auto-Attack key as far as I know.
