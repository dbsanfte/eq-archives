Best place I've seen is Sol A from about mid 20's - low 40's.  Lots to kill,
great dungeon exp bonus, plus merchants and a banker right in the zone!

Best class to PL with at that level would be a druid (snare, dmg shield,
good buffs).  Second best would be a shaman (haste, great buffs, slow mobs).

Also important to take time to keep backstab up, so take a druid out to
Iceclad / Emerald Jungle/ any outdoor zone with even or yellow con animals.
Use snare and terrorize animal and backstab away.  (or do this with a necro
partner just about anywhere you want)

Hope that helps!
