> Is there a way to change resolution other than in-game through the
> options menu?  (I need to change it back from 800x640 to 640x480 and
> everytime I try the game crashes).

Hi,

There is a line in your eqclient.ini file that sets the video
resolution.

I think the line reads "VideoMode" and an equals sign followed
by a number (for example "VideoMode=2")

The number value is your resolution.  Try setting it to 0.

VideoMode=0

I think 0 is 640x480.

The eqclient.ini should be within your EverQuest directory.
