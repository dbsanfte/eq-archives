Look up Kromiff Military Leggings.  Assuming you aren't KOS
to coldain, this fairly easy  quest will yeild better legs for you.

The trick is to shortcut the quest by looting a Bottle of Kasarin
Acid off the rygorr minors all over Crystal Caverns, instead
of bothering with the Scepter of the Ancients that drops
off the queen.  You should be able to purchase a blood
wolf harness in the bazaar for around 100pp, or have your
tracker buddies keep an eye out for him whenever crossing
thru Thurgadin.  Now kill a somewhat underconned 48 giant
(he sucks up damage and hits like he's 52...) and loot your
legs.
