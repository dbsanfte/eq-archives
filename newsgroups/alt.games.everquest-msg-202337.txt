noway@jose.com (Kobe008) writes:

> What is the command to do a sense heading?

Sense Heading doesn't have an explicit command like /sit or /assist
does.

Make a hotbutton for it and then link your right/left arrow keys
to that hotbutton slot.

And as the previous poster said, if you have more than one bank of
hotbuttons, make sure there's a Sense Heading button in the same
slot in each bank.
