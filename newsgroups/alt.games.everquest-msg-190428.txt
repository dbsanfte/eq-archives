As soon as they added the ability to macro commands I started putting
sense heading macros in commonly used commands as a macro. Like a /book
key for a caster, or taunt for melee. Every char I've done that with has
ended up with maxed sense heading with no effort on my part. Having 5
lines on a macro lets you add in lots of skill practice to commonly used
keys; for instance with a druid or ranger you can always have a forage
done just before sitting to open the spell book.
