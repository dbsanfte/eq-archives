> A few corrections here.
>
> Sow potion trivials at 37.
>
> Your wisdom will not affect your success rate at all, the only thing that
> will affect your success rate is your skill level in alchemy.  Alchemy is
> capped at 5 points per level over level 24, or (Level-24)*5.  So, at level
> 25, you can have a maximum alchemy skill of 5, at 26 it can go to 10, and
so
> on up to the maximum of 180 at level 60.  There is at least one item that
> will effectively increase your alchemy score, but, don't expect to be able
> to get it, until you are around level 60 and in an uber guild.  At level
30,
> your alchemy will cap at 30, which gives you a decent success rate at
making
> the sow potions.  In order to make them regularly and sell them at a
profit,
> you will want to be level 32 or higher, at which point your alchemy score
> can be higher than the trivial level for the potions.
>
> What your wisdom will affect is how quickly you learn alchemy, the higher
> your wisdom, the more likely you are to increase your skill level in
alchemy
> by making potions that are not trivial to you.
