> Folks pointed you to the website so I'll add a few suggestions:
> 
> - You'll want to change the Alpha and Fade level of the map window.  Windows
> default to 100% when the cursor is on it (alpha) and 50% when the cursor is
> elsewhere (fade).  Change both of these values to 50 or 40 and you'll likely
> be able to see much more of the map, since most zone walls don't show up
> very well (if at all) when the map is at 100% alpha.
> 
> - Kill the Height Filter button.  Although it's sometimes handy to have on
> (complicated dungeons), outdoors it serves no purpose except to hide stuff
> higher & lower than you.  It's a big button on the right, you can't miss it.
> :)   If you'd like the game to remember your preferences, please send
> /feedback.
> 
> - If you have a scroll mouse you can easily zoom in & zoom out.  That comes
> in VERY handy when you're trying to find the shortest way across a zone.
> You can also do it without a scroll mouse (using the zoom buttons).
>

> 170) the "+" marker turns into an arrow.  It's not a huge issue but I find
> it very handy.
