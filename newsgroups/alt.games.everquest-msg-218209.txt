>> im a bit unsure of how to rate a weapon. what does the dealy attack

>> attack rating of 33 and dmg of 5, whereas there is a fine steel one
>> for sale with a merchant with an attack rating of 28 and dmg of 6.

> 
> Work with the ratio of the two. The delay is the time units between
> each swing and the damage isjust that. You can work out the damage
> rate using the ratio of the two to work out damage per second.
> 
> Things get a bit more complex when deciding which weapon to use to
> limit aggro and so on but for a beginners just divide the delay figure
> by the damage figure and whichever gives the LOWEST result is the
> better one 
> 

Use (damage*2+1)/delay to compare weapons.  If you are a class with a 
damage bonus, then examine the weapon, and use (damage*2+damage 
bonus)/delay to compare weapons if they are going in your main hand, or, 
use the first formula if they are going in your offhand.
