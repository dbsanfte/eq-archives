hehe.. after reading your post and all the replies, I had to chuckle.

Ok.. why go to the trouble of editing your ini file??

In game everytime you type /autojoin channelname, it overwrites the
ini file with whatever channel(s) you previously autojoined. If you
autojoined to several channels and you use /autojoin blahblah, it
'unautojoins' all them channels and now just autojoins blahblah.
So  if you type just /autojoin with nothing after it, overwrites the
autojoined channels but this time with  NO channels
So basically it just 'unautojoins' ;-)
