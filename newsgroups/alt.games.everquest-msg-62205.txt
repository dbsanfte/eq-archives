As for starting cities, there are only two races that have a choice are
humans and half elves. (Before you start, Erudins do not get a choice. Their
starting city depends on thier class, not their deity.) Humans have a choice
of either Freeport or Qeynos, depending on which deity they choose.
(Exception: all human druids and rangers start in Surefall Glades.) Half
Elves have the choice of Freeport, Qeynos, or Kelethin as their starting
city, depending on deity choice. (Exception: Half Elf Pallys can start in
Felwithe.)

Every other race starts in their home city. No other choice possible.
