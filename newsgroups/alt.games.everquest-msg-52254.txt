Add them to your in-game friends list.

Type: 
/friend billyjoebob 
If you wanted to add the character 'Billyjoebob' to your friends list

Then just type:
/who friend all
To get a listing of all people on your list who are currently online.
Even lists them if they are completely anon.

To get a list of who is in your friend list, type:
/friend

I think you can have a maximum of 30 people on the list at any time, and
you can also edit the appropriate file on your EQ directory while off-
line


No way to tell 'when' they log on, but you can check if they are
actually online at any time
