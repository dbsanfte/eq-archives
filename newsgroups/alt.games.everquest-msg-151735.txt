>On several occasions I heard that killing only one type of mob can reduce
>the count of that mob in the zone.  Killing only spider ling in Everfrost
>can drastically reduce new spider lings. Also if you only hunt Mammoth,
>Mammoth count goes down as well.

If you're alone in a zone where the spawns have multiple possible
types, then yes.

Presume 100 spawns with 5 equally likely chances to spawn gnolls,
spiders, skeletons, bears, or wolves.

Kill all the gnolls. Leave everything else alone. There will be about
20 of them.

Each dead gnoll has a 20% chance of spawning a new gnoll. That means
you wind up with about 4. Kill those 4, maybe get 1 back.
