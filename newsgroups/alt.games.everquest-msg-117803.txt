First of all, turn of your HTML. HTML is for the web, not Usenet. The
above is what it looks like to anyone with a non-HTML newsreader or
with HTML turned off...

Now, to answer your question, use the - key on your number pad to take
a screenshot. The shot is saved into your everquest directory as
"eq000000.bmp" (for the first shot you take, eq000001.bmp for the
next, eq000002.bmp for the next, so on). If you plan on
posting/emailing the shots somewhere, use a graphics editor to change
the format to .jpg (Wish they would/could use this format as the
default for taking screenshots. BMPs are just unreasonably large)
