---Spell Casting Mastery---:
http://forum.magecompendium.com/tower/viewtopic.php?t=3184

Spell Casting Mastery 1 = 2.5% mana savings
Spell Casting Mastery 2 = 5% mana savings
Spell Casting Mastery 3 = 10% mana savings
Main Specialization is 10%, Off Specialization is 2.5%.
It stacks linearly. So SCM3 and Main Spec is 20%, SCM3 and Off Spec is 12.5%


---Spell Casting Fury---:
http://forum.magecompendium.com/tower/viewforum.php?f=9

For mages:
Spell Casting Fury 1 = 2% chance for crit, max crit is 133% damage of normal
Spell Casting Fury 2 = 4% chance for crit, max crit is 166% damage of normal
Spell Casting Fury 3 = 7% chance for crit, max crit is 200% damage of normal

Fury of Magic 1 = 9% chance for crit, max crit is 200% damage of normal
Fury of Magic 2 = 11% chance for crit, max crit is 200% damage of normal
Fury of Magic 3 = 14% chance for crit, max crit is 200% damage of normal
