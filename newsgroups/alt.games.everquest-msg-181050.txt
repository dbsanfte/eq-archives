A *non-complete* list of pros/cons off the top of my head:

Mage Pros:
Pet, summoned items, competitive nukes.

Mage Cons:
No teleports, no crowd control, spell research, lots of spell
swapping (bring up pet, buff pet, summon items, give them to
pet, summon items for yourself, equip, etc.)

Wizard Pros:
Nuke versatility (Cold, Fire, Magic), teleports, crowd control (Root).
Also can quad kite w/Bonds of Force (if you're in the mood to solo).

Wizard Cons:
Lower DPS over long periods.

For your situation, the mage might be nice because you may need that
extra oomph of damage.  Then again, the wizard might be nice to assist
in crowd control.

Overall, I'd go with the mage.
