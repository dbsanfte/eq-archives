I use the following:

/pause 3, /stopsong
/cast 1

That will immediately stop any current song, wait 3/10s of a second,
then start the song in spell gem slot 1.  You can try moving it down
to 2/10s of a second, but the /pause 3 works for me.
Remember, you need to put the /pause X at the beginning of the line,
but it will be executed after the other command.
