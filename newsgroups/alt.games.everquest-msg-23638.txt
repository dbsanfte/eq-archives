A coffin is the necessary component to cast the Summon Corpse spell
(39 Necro). Summon corpse will summon the targeted player's corpse to
their feet from anywhere in the zone. This spell is found only on the
Ishva Mal gnoll (lvl 40 or so) in Splitpaw Lair.

The coffins can only be purchased in Neriak 3rd Gate from a merchant
in the middle of the Clerics Guild. Cost is around 150pp depending on
Cha. The coffins for the 39th level spell are non-stackable. 

Tiny Jade Inlaid Coffins for the Conjure Corpse spell (lvl 57) are
stackable.
