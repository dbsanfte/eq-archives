spells that cast faster than the limit, not set a floor for the reduction.
This makes sense given that the fastest spell that wouldn't be excluded from

adjusted to a 3 second cast time anyways without the lower limit. So, to use
examples, if you had a 2.5 second cast time spell that was level 34 it would
be reduced by your level II item but not by the level III item.
