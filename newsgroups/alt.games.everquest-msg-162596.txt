Ronny Cook wrote:

> I'm setting up a necro for soloing during those thin
> times when nobody wants a cleric to group with.


      The two best races for necro are Iksar and Gnome:
Iksar for AC and Regen (which = Clarity for necros) and
Gnome for stats and faction.  If you'll be soloing a lot,
you should probably go Iksar, since the regeneration will
greatly reduce your downtime, which is the biggest
obstacle to soloing.  The experience penalty will be
partially offset by the fact that soloing is the fastest
way to gain XP.
