> As to some strategy tips if you are going there, we had very good luck.
> A four or five hour camp yielded enough optic nerves for five warriors
> to get their crafted bracers. (Got mine already! Woo Hoo!) Also seven or
> eight PBB and a couple PBH, as well as various +5 HP rings, gems,
> jewelry, and other stuff.  Advice for going there in the mid 20's:
> 1. Don't try to solo these guys. They'll wipe the floor with you.
> 2. Have a couple fighters, and a couple casters with good nukes.
> 3. Have the fighters hit them first, *then* have the casters start
> unloading the big nukes.  Even if you can't disrupt their casting, they
> will hopefully charm one of the fighters first, allowing your nuker to
> polish them off quickly.
> 4. Cast endure magic buffs on everyone. Once we started doing that, the
> percentage of times I saw the "You resist the xxxx spell!" message
> seemed to go way up and I was beating on my guildies a lot less.
> 5. Have everybody have a crappy buff in the top buff slot. The EE's will
> often wipe out some of your buffs. Have the good ones stacked lower
> down.
> 6. Once the spawn is broken, be ready to hit them FAST and HARD when
> they pop. Otherwise they summon their pet immediately, and he hits hard
> too. (ouch)
> 7. Watch out for the big guy who spawns on the center throne. (Not King
> Xorbb, I mean the level 30 guy, Lord somethingorother).  We got him once
> during the camp. He hits and casts way harder than the others, and
> healed himself completely twice during the fight. We barely got him. He
> did drop the staff, though. Sweet.
> 8.  If you're looking for the King himself, I guess I'd have to say,
> just be very, very patient. We were there a good piece of the day and
> never saw him once. The only thing to spawn in the center was the big
> named Lord. Anybody here ever actually see the King spawn? I wanted that
> ring to give to my partner, damnit!
> 9.  If you are looking for Polished Bone stuff, be patient. I read a lot
> of complaints that this drop had been nerfed, and indeed we had many
> stretches of a dozen or more eyes with no good loot. But we wound up
> with seven PBB's, a couple PBH's, by the end of the day, so they *do*
> still drop.
> 10. You can pass idle time killing the gorge and chasm minos that wander
> through. You may get no xp, but they occasionally drop some decent loot,
> and if you dont' keep them cleaned out, they will aggro on all your
> casters while they are medding. However, we found "idle time" to be in
> short supply, as the two outside spawns seem to pop within a few minutes
> of each other.
> 11. Frustrating though it may be, keep your warriors in "walk" mode, not
> run. And don't sow them. That way, if they get charmed, you can easily
> keep out of their reach till it wears off.
