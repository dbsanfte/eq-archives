"Brian Hance" wrote

> How would I compute what the final delay on my weapons is
> with a haste item/spell/whatever on?

The formula for calculating the effective, hasted delay is:

    100/(Haste_percentage + 100) * Delay.

Haste_percentage would be the total Haste you have after
stacking issues and your Haste cap are resolved.   (It's possible
to exceed the Haste cap under certain circumstances, for
instance, or, obviously, to be limited by the Haste Cap.)
