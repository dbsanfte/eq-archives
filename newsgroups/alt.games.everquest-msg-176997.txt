>Any of the three plate tank classes (WAR/PAL/SHD) can serve that role.
>If you want to be able to rez for best xp return, you'll need a Cleric.
>If you want CHeal (not a necessity for a group, but situationally very
>nice), you'll need a Cleric.  If you go with a Cleric, a Warrior is
>probably best choice for tank (most HP, maximises value from CHeal
>when you get high enough to have/use it).  Alternatively you could
>ue a Paladin to fill both Tank and rez roles.  If you do go with the
>Paladin you probably want to have a Druid or Wizard for those nasty
>CRs when the Paladin dies.  Druid or Wizard is attractive for evac as
>well (first evac is Lesser Succor at L18, not too far away from start).
>And don't forget that having a Druid or Wizard in the group means that
>you can port out of that deep dungeon dive when you leave, instead of
>having to fight your way out.
>
>A possible crew:
>1) Shaman (given)
>2) Paladin (tank/rez, can heal to shorten downtime after hard fights)
>3) Beastlord (damage, backup tank, some healing, eventually mana regen)
>4) Druid (snare, port/evac, decent heals, DS/DD, tons of other stuff)
>
>Everybody can heal to some extent.  Paladin makes a good tank, and by
>the time his damage output is lagging noticeably behind the big damage
>dealers he's offerring enough in other areas to compensate very well.
>Beast Lord has good damage output and can tank in a pinch, eventually
>gets mana/hp regen buff that stacks with everything, not a class I know
>all thatwell, but I see them used effectively a lot in groups.  Shaman
>has best slows in game, eventually has haste, decent healing, decent
>DoTs, situationally useful nukes, more buffs than you can possibly have
>on your character at once (and that's by L14, heh), gets regen 10 levels
>sooner than Druid, limited tanking ability until high levels, very good
>class.  Druid has so much to offer:  best snares in the game, best roots
>in the game, nice selection of dots (including a line with a -100 resist
>modifier), nice selection of nukes, damage shields, EVAC, ports, ...
>
