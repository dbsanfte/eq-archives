>If I put AA points into WIS will it take me past the 255 limit? Do I have to
>do anything special to get that WIS?
>
>With my WIS gear and especially with KEI, I'm at 255 pretty much all the
>time now.

The ability cap is raised 5 points per level over 60. You can also
raise your ability maxima by 5 points at a time using specific AAs. A
level 65 with all 5 cap-raising AAs can go to 305 in any ability.

At level 60 or below, without the specific AAs, you can not raise
abilities over 255.
