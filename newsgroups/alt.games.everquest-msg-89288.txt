>How does the char present them (read give) them to the NPC? 

Pick the item up out of your inventory so that it is on your mouse
cursor, and click on the NPC (you need to be standing close). A
"trade"  window will open up with 4 slots.

If the NPC requests 4 snake fangs, you "MUST" put one fang in each of
the 4 boxes. If you do not do this, you run the risk of losing the
items you hand in.  Some quests will allow you to stack items, but
most will not. Better to be safe than sorry.
