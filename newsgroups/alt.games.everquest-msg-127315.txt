Type /disc to get a list of the disciplines that you get and how long until
you can use a discipline again.

To use a discipline, type /disc <discipline name>.

Rangers get resistant at 51; 5 minutes of +10 to all resists, with a 60
minute base refresh time.
/disc resistant

Fearless at 54; 11 seconds of immunity to ALL fear spells and effects, with
a 60 minute base refresh.
/disc fearless

Trueshot at 55; 2 minutes of up to 2x bow damage, with a 72 minute base
refresh.
/disc trueshot

Weaponshield at 60; 20 seconds of auto-parry on all attacks, with 72 minute
refresh.
/disc weaponshield
