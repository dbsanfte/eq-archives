No sense undead will not find player corpses.  It will point you to the
closest undead creature in the zone.  To sense corpses the spell needed is
Locate Corpse, available to level 1 necromancers and level 9 shadow knights.
This spell when cast will point you toward either the nearest corpse (if you
have no one targeted) or the corpse of your target.  The level 4 bard song
Lyssa's Locating Lyric works in the same maner.
