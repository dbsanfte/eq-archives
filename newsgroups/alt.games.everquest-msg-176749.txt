On Thu, 23 Jan 2003 13:28:44 -0000, "Lief" <ask.me.for@it.com> wrote:

>This may be common knowledge to most people, but although I have found many
>good websites explaining about the different tradeskills, what I havent seen
>is which stat relates to which tradeskill, ie smithing is done from strength
>but fletching from wisdom?

All tradeskills will use the higher of INT or WIS to determine how
often you get skill-ups. Fletching will use INT, WIS, or DEX. Smithing
will use INT, WIS, or STR.

The only thing that determines success or failure is your skill in the
trade versus the trivial skill level of the item you're trying to
make.
