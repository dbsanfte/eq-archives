1) You'll need the following items: Small Brick of Ytrrium Ore, Meteor Dust
(from the Grey), Shissar Blood (Ssra Temple), Shiknarichor (from Fungus
Grove Shik'nar things), Fungoid Sap (From Ancient Shrieker in The Deep).

2) *Brewing* -- Shissar Blood + Shiknarichor + Fungoid Sap + Meteor Dust =
Quicksilver Temper (Brewing 238 is trivial).

3) *Smithing* -- 2 small bricks of ytrrium ore + quicksilver temper =
ytrrium steel sheet (250 trivial).

Steps 2 and 3 are Tradeable items when completed.

4) Combine Hilt Mold + Pommel Mold + (dagger/sword/etc.. mold) + Ytrrium
Steel Sheet + Quicksilver Temper = Seru Bane Weapon. (smithing 175 trivial)
