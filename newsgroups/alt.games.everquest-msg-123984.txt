XP loss is based off of the amount of xp required to get through the
previous level. This was the effect with the old post-hell levels
taking a bigger xp hit on deaths. Level 60 is an extremely easy level
to top off xp-wise compared to the rest of the post-50 levels. This
means that the hit "bubble-wise" on xp will be bigger when you die.
It's still figured the same, it's just that a smaller amount of xp is
required to fill that bar, so the hit looks bigger.
