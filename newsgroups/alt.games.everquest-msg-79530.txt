"Jeff George" wrote

> I have never received experience for light blues. Am I getting it and
> just not getting a message or are there even some light blues that you
> won't get xp for?

You won't get XP if the amount rewarded is too small.  There
are several possible reasons this could happen:

You're low level and the amount of XP you get doing full
damage is too small;

You're a pet class being out-damaged by your pet and
your remaining share of the XP is too small;  or

You're in a group and your share of the XP is too small
on light blues.
