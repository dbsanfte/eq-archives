Depends on if you want  good Live froglok faction or not.

The swamp was a great hunting spot for my first ever toon, he got free
armour from the frogloks, The shoulders gave him a magic slam the boots a
magic kick plus the froglok blood could be turned in for an easy 1 yellow of
exp below lvl 10, the Tads provided a free 2 hand slash weapon, The mushroom
men had a leader who enabled you to gain a Plus int and dex mask, and you
could even get some FR gloves.

While Grobb never had any "newbie armour quests" like the good races, if you
spent the time having a wander and talking to the npcs you could leave the
swamp at 10 plus wearing an almost complete set of armour.

Oh and the Kobolds dropped free boxes, while the skellies and snakes dropped
scales and chips for selling to all the higher levels running into Guk.

I found the swamp to be a top class hunting ground almost on par with Halas,
if you want an example of a bad newbie ground look next door at the Ogre
zone.
