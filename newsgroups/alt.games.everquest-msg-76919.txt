As a quick note:

Partition Magic wont convert from NTFS if you have any compressed or
encrypted files...

make sure to uncompress / decrypt any files with those attributes if you
want to go back to FAT32..

PQ will give an error and quit if you try to convert NTFS when files are
still compressed or encrypted.
