Others have mentioned the (expensive) Leatherfoot Haversack
and Lemming Fur Backpack.  To those I'd add the Scavengers
Satchel (available free for a quest in Shadeweavers, holds 6

Grandmaster's Satchel (No Drop No Rent, 10 giant, 100% WR) and
one Grandmaster's Carry-All (No Drop Lore, NOT No Rent, 10 giant,
100% WR).
