I've written a utility to let you toggle Mouseware (and probably 
Intellipoint and others, with a little experimentation) off and on from a 
tray icon.  That way, you can turn Mouseware off before gaming and turn it 
back on afterwards without having to screw with the Task Manager.  This 
should help for people having issues with the wheel scroll or auxiliary 
buttons during gameplay.

The install's pretty no-frills (hello, zip file), but the app itself is 
pretty much there.  If you want to give it a shot, you can grab it at:

http://www.sonic.net/~gmealer/mousemute.zip

There's a text file that explains how to set up and use the utility.  It's 
set up for Mouseware by default.
