Another good weapon for a younger Ranger would seem to be the Wakizashi of
the Frozen Skies, which is obtained via a quest in Stonebrunt:

Wakizashi of the Frozen Skies
1HS 6/22 Eff: Spirit Strike

Spirit Strike is an 81DD cold-based spell.
It procs at 20 for 80DD with the full 81DD at 21.

Also, to do the quest, you are best equipped with Track, Forage, and Panic
Animal!

Items needed:
1 Kejeken Palm Fruit (foraged in Stonebrunt)
1 Bamboo Shoot (foraged in Stonebrunt)
2 Panda Claws (drop off Pandas in Stonebrunt)
2 Tiger Skins (drop off Tigers in Stonebrunt)
2 Asp Poison Sacs (drop off Cistern Asps in Oasis/SRo !!)

And before anyone starts, none of these items appear to be on Trivial Code,
neither is the quest itself! I have done it happily at 40. And I see low 20s
grouped in Stonebrunt hunting these mobs.
