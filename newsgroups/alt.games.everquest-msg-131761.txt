> What are the 4in1 drivers for?

When Microsoft coded the OS's (Win98/2K) many of the newer Via
chipsets used to control motherboards were not available or on the
market. So the operating systems could not take advantage of some of
the features built into the chips, because they didn't ship with the
right driver files.

The 4in1 drivers update the operating system to be fully compatible
with the chipsets and hardware built into the VIA equiped
motherboards.
