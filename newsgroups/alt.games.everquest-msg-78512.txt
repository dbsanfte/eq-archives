> Heya, Darklet. Welcome to the dark side.
> MUA HA HA.
> [gasp, sputter, choke]
> Ah, well.. ok then. Playing an evil. Yes, I too started out my eq career
> playing nothing but those goody-goody light-n-fluffy types. Until I
> started my Troll. It's a bit of a different world, and you get to see
> and play in, different places than you will with the other folks.
>
> There's two ways you can approach this thing. First, like so many people
> sadly do, you can try to play your DE Necro like a good type. While far
> from easy, and in fact quite tedious, it can be done. There are a slew
> of factions you'd have to fix that you used to take for granted, and
> none of them repair easily. You have the whole enchelada of things
> against you.... race, religion, and ... err ... occupation. As a
> necromancer of an evil race worshipping an evil god, you've got a LONG
> way to go on most factions. So why bother?
>
> The other way to be evil is to just say fuck it and BE EVIL. Embrace it!
> Revel in it! As a dark race, the lighties are there for your amusement
> and destruction. Plus, as a necro, you have a moral obligation to see
> them off into the next world at any opportunity. The beauty of it is
> that, if you don't care for some of the other evil folks, Gank them too!
> Death is your domain. Deal it out!
>
> To the real mechanics of the game... EQ was set up so that all of the
> dark races would have an area they could call home, and some close by
> areas where they could grow through their early levels in comfort. Try
> to stick with those at first. Later on you may want to adjust some
> factions for ease of shopping and banking far from home, but by the time
> you hit 29 (which won't take a necro long) you'll be off to Kunark and
> none of that will matter a bit anyway.
>
> How to get there? Well, you do your first twelve levels hanging around
> Nek forest and Lavastorm like everyone else, doing the normal rats,
> bats, bugs and bears formula. Until you can get bind affinity. After you
> can reset your bind point, the world is your oyster. At 12 go to Ocean
> of Tears. For a number of levels you can do either the Alluring Sirens
> out at the spires, or the goody goody Sisters of Errollisi (sp?) on
> Sister's Isle. Outstanding xp for a soloist and the loot on the sisters
> is pretty good also. Plenty of free weapons for your pets as well. Once
> you make 18, stay right in that zone and move to the haunted isle to
> solo the gargoyles. They took my necro to 24 in no time and racked up
> nearly a thousand plat over that period. (Gargoyle eyes sell to
> merchants for roughly 180 PP per stack.)
>
> Post 24 you have lots of options. I'm camping the annoying Miller
> siblings in Qeynos Hills, but there's plenty of other lighties out there
> to be slaughtered.
> Good luck and good hunting, young dark one.
