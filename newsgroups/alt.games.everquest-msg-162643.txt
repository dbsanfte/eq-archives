Willowisps are blue floating "lights" They are magical creatures and
they drop some coin and 3 types of lightstones.

Burned out Lightstones: Useless and lore...not worth picking up.

Lightstones: Sell to vendors for @1pp

Karana and Oasis for exp and a book that sells back to the quest giver
for 7-9pp.

between levels 8-12....
Erud's Crossing
North Karana
East Commonlands
