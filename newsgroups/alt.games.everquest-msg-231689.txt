After the last big patch I lost the ability to access my skills
window, and I know that some others did also, I did a little search at
everquest.com amd came upwith the following in the tech support area:

Look in your toon's UI file in your EQ folder and find :
UI_Charname_servername.ini.

Mine is: UI_Redna_cazic.ini

You will see an entry like this:

[SkillsWindow]
XPos1280x1024=0
YPos1280x1024=0
Width1280x1024=0
Height1280x1024=0

Change it to:
[SkillsWindow]
XPos1280x1024=52
YPos1280x1024=064
Width1280x1024=300
Height1280x1024=461

Works for me!!
