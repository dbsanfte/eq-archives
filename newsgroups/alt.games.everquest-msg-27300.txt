The monk weight limit was 14 stones.  The they adjusted it so that starting at
level 15 start being able to carry more.  So basically, until level 15, you're
stuck at a 14 stone limit.  If you exceed your limit, your Armor Class starts
to drop.  The higher your level, the faster it drops.

Class Specific Changes

Monk weight allowance has been increased. Every 15 levels monks will get an extra 1 stone weight allowance. The post 50 weight

Level 15, Limit 15
Level 30, Limit 16
Level 45, Limit 17
Level 51, Limit 18
