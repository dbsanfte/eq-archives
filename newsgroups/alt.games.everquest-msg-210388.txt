Hmmm, that also appears to be incorrect.

Haste only applies to weapon delay.  To figure a new hasted delay on a
weapon, use the following formula.

New Delay= Old delay/1.(haste%*)  *Always add your item and spell/song
haste together

So, if you are trying to figure delay on a Wurmslayer when using an
FBSS, the formula would look like:

Delay=40/1.21 which works out to 33.05.  Since the EQ engine always
rounds down, the new damage/delay for the Wurmy (with FBSS) is 25/33.
