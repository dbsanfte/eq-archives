> Bah!  No need for AE fear.  When charm breaks, toggle targets back to
> your pet, re-charm, toggle to the target, tell pet to attack.  You can
> bellow and/or DoT the target down if you like, but the more damage it
> does to your pet before it dies the better, because your pet will be
> the target you actually want to kill, and the former target will be
> your new pet that you will charm after you've killed off your old pet.
>  This is swarm kiting in essence.
> 
> NOTE:
> You do all this while running in circles around your two (or more)
> mobs using Selo's, or better yet SoW.  I highly recommend using SoW
> till you reach 49 where you get a 2.5 minute Selo's.  Using SoW
> decreases your odds of death enormously, and make charm kiting so much
> easier.
> 
> I also recommend using 3rd person view for running in circles.  Get
> used to adjusting the camera position and angle on that, and you'll be
> in a great position to swarm or AE kite.
> 
> Check out this page for lots more info on swarm kiting:
