Robin Russell wrote:
> Is there a way to look at a weapon and tell the level you need to be
to use
> it?  I would hate to sell a looted item if I could use it next time I
level.

Assuming you have the skill that is listed, take the Required and
divide by five.  If a weapon has a required skill of 150, you can use
it at level 30.  If it is 153, then you can use that weapon (or spell,
art, etc) at 30.6.
