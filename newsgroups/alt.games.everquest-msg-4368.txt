Cloth armor has very little weight.  Combined with your rising defensive
skill the resulting AC is definitely useful.  You may even want to
upgrade to raw silk pieces as you can afford them to fill in some
spots.  As a Mage you can expect to take some hits at times, even by
choice to keep your pet up in some cases, so the AC does have its uses
even if its not very often.  You'll likely add in better stuff as you
go, but for starters cloth/raw silk is a good bet.
