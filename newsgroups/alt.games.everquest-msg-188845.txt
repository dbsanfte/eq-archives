>	What pelts do I keep for this and what should I dump.  Also, what

Leather padding is made from any LQ wolf/cat/bear pelt plus 1 silk
thread

OR

From LQ hopper hide plus 1 silk (or shadeling silk) thread

Convert down your MQ pelts to LQ using the skinning knife in your
sewing kit.

Sell your HQ pelts in the bazaar for cash.

See eqtraders.com for more advice.

P.S. A good place to get lots of hopper hides is Shadeweavers Thicket,
between the SharVahl entrance and Paludal Caverns. A Saurek Hopper or
other Saureks drops a pelt 1 in 2, and it is either Damaged (useless,
discard), LQ/MQ or HQ. Ignore the needleclawed hoppers. The shadeling
silk drops of the maggot things or Lesser Shades. They are all low con
and easy kills.
