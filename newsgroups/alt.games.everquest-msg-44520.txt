> 2. I have got my archery level up to 40 but seem to have fired lots
> and lots of arrows to get this far.  What is the easiest way to
> improve this skill, and is it a skill that high level rangers use ?


Root and Snare are your friends here. Find somewhere you can safely pull a
single blue mob. Snare to slow it down, root to immobilise it, pump it full
of arrows, re-apply snare and root as necessary. Take LOTS of arrows! At
level 35 I was using 2-3 stacks of arrows per point of archery.
