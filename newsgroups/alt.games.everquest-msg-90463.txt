Thought I would toss out this thread so I could share my simple grouping
hotkeys with other "newbies" (I've only been playing for 6 months) and
hopefully learn some new important ones from the more experienced players.

Please share your favourite group messaqes so we can all learn
A bit more and improve group communications. These are from my

some examples of grouping hotkeys (keyname + text lines):

Stand out and are repeated (2 lines) so they won't be missed in all the
combat message chatter.
