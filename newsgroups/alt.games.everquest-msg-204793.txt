Once you get swimming maxed for you level, an easy way to maintain it is to
swim when you come across water while traveling.  E.g., if you are heading
to Oasis from East Freeport, instead of running through Northern Ro on land,
swim up the coast.  Most cities have water, so when heading into a city to
bank, swim through any lakes or ponds you find.  A brief swim like this,
every time you get the chance, will probably keep your skill up.
