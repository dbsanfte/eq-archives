> First, the rules:
> 
> Replace A,E,H,I,O,U,W,Y with 0
> Replace B,F,P,V with 1
> Replace C,G,J,K,Q,S,X,Z with 2
> Replace D,T with 3
> Replace L with 4
> Replace M,N with 5
> Replace R with 6
> 
> Remove all zeros EXCEPT a lead zero.
> 
> Compress all double-numbers to singles, so, for example, 533 becomes
> 53. 
> 
> Tack on enough zeros to make the number 5 digits long, or truncate to
> same. 
> 
> Those are the rules for composing a soundex number (except that the
> soundex rules keep the first character as a letter).
> 
> Legolas --> 4020402 --> 4242 --> 42420
>
