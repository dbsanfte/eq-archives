>
>Expect your pet to last 5 seconds against almost anything, if the critter is
>beating on it.
>Your pet is far below your level, and this difference gets greater the
>higher level you get.
>Your pet is NOT a tank.  Repeat- you cannot let it fight FOR you... just
>WITH you.
>Think of it as a mobile, mana free DoT.  If you need to run, it may buy you
>a few precious
>seconds, but it really won't be able to stand up to much on it's own.
>Here's what I always did (and still do in certain situations):
>Find a good spot to fight, away from wandering critters.  Summon your pet,
>and tell it
>"pet guard here".  The pet will now stand in this spot.  Find a critter,
>damage it (darkness is good to
>slow it down as it runs to you) and run back to your pet.  Once the critter
>hits you, the pet will start
>attacking.  Stand on one side of the critter and fight, let the pet have the
>other side, and keep
>the critters attention so it doesn't hit the pet too much.  The pet will
>follow the critter until it dies,
>or you tell it to back off.  Once the target is dead, the pet will wander
>back to it's spot.
