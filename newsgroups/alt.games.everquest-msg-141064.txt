Try turning your hardware acceleration off on the sound card...
This can be found under Start>Settings>Control Panel>Sounds and multimedia.
Then click on Audio, then under Sound Playback, click advanced. Then go to
the performance tab and turn hardware acceleration to "None" then ok, ok
again, then restart the computer and relaunch everquest this time turning
sound back on. That should make it bearable and turning off the hardware
acceleration does not hurt anything.
