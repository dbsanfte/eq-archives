To gain exp in a group, the member must be within 66% of the level of the 
highest person in the group.

Thus, if the high level in the group is 50, a 33 could group and still get 
XP. (50 * 0.66 = 33)

Or, for level 40 as in your example (40 * 0.66 = 26) a level 26.
