> 1. Updated my firmware at http://www.linksys.com/download. I am now

> 3. A new window will pop up. For APPLICATION NAME, type EverQuest. For
> TRIGGER PORT RANGE, type 1023 in the first box and 9999 in the second box.
> For INCOMING PORT RANGE, type 1023 in the first box and 9999 in the second
> box. Then click APPLY.
> 5. That's it! I played for 3 hours last night without a single disconnect.
