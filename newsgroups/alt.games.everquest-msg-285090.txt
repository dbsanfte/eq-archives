> As others have written, the main flaw in your calculations is that the
> damage bonus for a two-handed weapon is taken from a different table
> than that for one-handed weapons.  Lucy has one such table:
>
> http://lucy.allakhazam.com/dmgbonus.html
>
> If your one-handed damage bonus is 2, then a 2-handed weapon with delay
> 36 would have a bonus of 3.  Lucy's table goes all the way up to level
> 65, with a 1-h bonus of +13 and a 2-h bonus at delay 36 of +39.  Note
> that the damage bonus for a 2-h weapon does depend on the delay,
> whereas this is not true for 1-h weapons.
>
> Finally, the figure of merit is generally taken as:
> (2*damage+bonus)/delay
>
