How cheap?  You can get a Sr Apprentice Robe from the vendors.  You can kill 
humanoids for cloth armor (the Warrens, Blackburrow).  In the Qeynos sewers, 
there are frogloks that will drop netted (at least there were back in my 
"youth").  You can start practising tailoring for when you get silk off 
spiders (not spiderlings and use the WATER pet).  Raw silk is the best 
player-made armor for mages.  Save your money for the other various armor 
types that drop and head for EC or the Bazaar (Luclin) when you have a few 
hundred plat.
