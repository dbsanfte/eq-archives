Make a basic hotkey by typing -
/hot <name> <action>

For example

/hot Pull! /g Incoming %t - get ready!

This will put a hotkey on your cursor 

Drop that in a hotkey slot.  If you want more actions on it,
just right click it and it has 5 lines that you can put stuff
on.
