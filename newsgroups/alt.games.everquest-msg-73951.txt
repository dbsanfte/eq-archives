> Enchanter Soloing Strategies at Levels 12-15
>
> PICK THE RIGHT SPOT:
> Locate hunting camp near zone or near guard
> Try to obtain a SoW
>
> Set up spell as follows:
> Gate
> Tashan (Fear)
> Weaken
> Choke
> Color Flux
> Languid Pace
> Root
> Chaotic Feedback
>
> ** When soloing with a pet, forget Charm and Mez as available spells
>
> PET PREPARATION:
> Summon a pet (5th circle)
> Buff the pet w/ Lesser Shielding and Mist and Strengthen
> Tell pet to guard while you re-med to full mana
> Practice Sense Heading and Beg (on Pet)
>
> ATTACKING THE MOB:
> Find Mob of Blue or White CON
> Cast Tashan on Mob
> Tell Pet to Attack
> Step way back
> Cast Weaken on Mob, then Choke.
> Change Tashan to Fear in spell list
> Monitor the two NPC's health (F8) while medding
>
> If pet is dying:
>
> A) Cast repeated Color Fluxes, or repeat Languid Pace to stun it
> while Mob regains upper hand
> B) Try to Root the Mob and blast it with Chaotic Feedback
> C) Cast Fear on the Mob, tell pet to Guard, Med and let Pet heal
> (guarding caster) until Mob returns;  or
>
> If pet is still dying:
>
> A) Root it and, if its health is low, Cast Chaotic
> Feedback/repeat Choke - committing to killing it.  Or if health still
> quite good, Root it and cast Gate and cut your losses.
>
> If Root fails:
>
>      A)  Run to zone / guard like little girl in pink party dress!
>
