On Wed, 27 Mar 2002 14:31:26 GMT, "Xavier" <no@no.com> wrote:

>Up to this point in my EQ career, I have solo'd. Now that I'm starting a
>shaman, I want to know what my role is going to be past 20, 30.
>
>I know it'll depend on the situation at hand, but generally speaking, what
>will be expected of me?

Assuming you are in a group with a Chanter and Cleric

1. Slow Mobs
Nothing is more important, expecially at higher levels (unless the
mobs cannot be slowed). Let the Chanter worry about mezz, and stuns.
Root park if needed.

2. Lower Mob resists.
Enchanters have a lot to do, and debuffing resists is a good way of
reducing thier workload. 

3. Haste/Regen/Buff Melee Characters
Cast your most usefull buffs on the tanks and melees. STA, Regen and
Haste will net the most benefits.

4. Back-Up healer
