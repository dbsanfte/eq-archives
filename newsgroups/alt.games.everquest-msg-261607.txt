Yup, there's definitely something to that.  Because a level 35 can
buy gear that requires level 55 to obtain, the mobs that a level 35
fights are either trivial when equipped with level 55 stuff, or are
challenging vs level 55 gear but next to impossible if you use only
"level-appropriate" gear.

There's also some group-vs-solo involved.  A group of 35s equipped
only with stuff dropped by level 35 mobs can take on stuff at or
somewhat above their level.  But if you want to be able to solo,
you need better gear.
