I am reposting this as a reminder. I hope to see

The Povar-Tarew Artisans guild is proud to
announce its first ever Masterworks Auction to
take place July 14, 2001, at 12 noon Pacific Time,
East Commons, Xev Server (9 am Hawaiian, 1 pm
Mountain, 2 pm Central, 3 pm Eastern, 8pm
Greenwich)

Our finest artisans have worked hard to perfect
their crafts. Through hard work and perseverence,
they have created some of Everquest's most sought
after player-made goods and now, the Povar-Tarew
Artisans guild is pleased to make a sample of
these rare masterworks available to the people of
Norrath.

Below, you will find the items being put up for
bids and the rules governing this Auction. Please
review these rules if you wish to participate, as
failure to observe them may result in your bid
being disqualified. We hope you can join us.

*These items are guaranteed to be offered. Other
items may be added by the day of the auction. All
prices given in platinum pieces.*

Lot 1
Idol of Karana
Valindria Briggan Soulblade, Master Potter
Minimum bid is 350pp
Useable by all who follow Karana, this idol can be
equipped in the primary, secondary or ranged
slots. It provides +3 WIS and +2 vs Magic and is
especially useful to druids, clerics and paladins
of the Rainlord.

Lot 2
Golden Black Pearl Choker
Esqubel Highmind, Master Jeweler
Minimum bid is 60pp
A lovely neck piece that gives +7 to both DEX and
AGI. A must for any rogue or monk.

Lot 3
Koada'Dal Falchion of Tunare
Athcaldonor, Grandmaster Smith
Minimum bid is 500pp
A splendid piece of High Elf cultural smithing.
This 1HS does 7 damage with a delay of 24, which
comes to a great damage ratio of 3.428 (the lower
the ratio, the better.) Imbued for Tunare, it
grants +3 STR and +3 WIS and can be used by any
Warrior, Paladin, Ranger, Druid, Bard or Rogue who
follows the Mother of All.

Lot 4
Imbued Vale Studded Armor
Rosewhite Blossom, Master Tailor
Minimum bid is 300pp
Halfling cultural is a leather armor wearable by
any small to medium race. This set of tunic,
cloak, leggings and boots have been imbued for
followers of Karana and provide 29 AC, 11 STA and
8 WIS. A great purchase for druids.

Lot 5
Trueshot Longbow
Priian, Master Fletcher
Minimum bid is 120pp
The prize of a difficult quest and the pinnacle of
the bowmaker's art, the Trueshot longbow has a
range of 100 and does 20 damage with a delay of
45, giving it an excellent damage ratio of 2.25.
This bow is LORE and useable by Rangers only.

Lot 6
Fleeting Quiver
Rhain, Master Tailor
Minimum bid is 700pp
This quiver can hold six stacks of arrows, gives
66% weight reduction and reduces the delay of any
bow by 6 when arrows are drawn from it. Perfect
for all bow wielding players, the fleeting quiver
is a must for serious rangers.

Lot 7
Idol of Tunare
Canlar, Master Potter
Minimum bid is 350pp
Another example of the wonderful new pottery
idols. Equippable by any follower of Tunare in the
primary, secondary or ranged slots, this idol
gives +3 WIS and +2 vs both Disease and Poison.

Lot 8
Enchanted Teir'Dal Chain Tunic
Miliru, Master Smith
Minimum bid is 1000pp
An incredible piece of Dark Elf smithing, this
chain mail armor can be worn by Dark Elves and
Half Elves and gives 14 AC, 4 WIS, 4 INT and 2 vs
Magic. An excellent item for just about every
class.

Lot 9
Wu's Fighting Armor
Esqubel Highmind, Master Tailor
Minimum bid is 400pp
A full suit of Wu's armor! Wearable only by monks,
this 13 piece set offers 62 AC, +30 AGI and a
light source. All pieces are magic, allowing the
wearer to fight hand to hand and instill doubt
against magical creatures.

Lot 10
Othmir Fur Cap
Rhain, Master Tailor
Minimum bid is 1000pp
One of the rarest tailored items on Norrath. This
light weight magic hat is wearable by any class
and all races, giving 3 AC, +2 AGI, +3 DEX, +6
WIS, +4 vs Cold and +7 HP.

Lot 11
Pair of Velium Fire Opal Rings
Friedrich Psitalon, Grandmaster Jeweler
Minimum bid is 700pp
Perhaps the most sought out player made jewelry,
each of these two rings provides 6 AC and 65 HP.
Especially useful to monks, these would make an
valuable addition to any player's armor.

*Rules Governing this Auction*

1 All valid bids for a given item shall be
submitted on the auction channel in the form of
Item, Bid after bidding on the item has been
opened and before bidding on the item has been
closed. Bids shall be expressed in platinum
pieces; if no currency is mentioned it will be
assumed to be in platinum. All bids that do not
meet these critera shall be disqualified.

2 In the event of a tie, the current bid will be
the first bid to appear on the Auctioneer's chat
screen. Before closing the bidding, the Auctioneer
will announce the amount of the current bid and
the name of the person who holds it.

3 After the bidding on an item has closed, the
winner shall have 15 minutes to pay for and pick
up the purchase. After this time, the item will be
offered to the person with the second highest bid.
If neither person claim the item, the Povar-Tarew
Artisans may choose to either retain the item or
place it again for bids.

4 Purchases may be picked up by the person holding
the winning bid only. Guildmates, friends or alts
may not retrieve the item. This is to guarantee
that the item is received by the person who
purchased it. It is acceptable to have one person
pay and let the winner retrieve the item, so long
as payment and pick up occure at the same time.

5 The Auctioneer and at least one other member of
the Povar-Tarew Artisans guild shall log the
Auction in its entirety for use in any disputes
which may arise.
