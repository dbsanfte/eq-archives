Jeff George wrote:

> I have just added a second PC at home, and networked them together.
> They will share a cable modem for online activity. I want to have the
> new, better, system as my main EQ box. 

If ALL you want is to be able to play your existing account on your new
box, then just copy over your EQ folder to the new machine and you're
done.

If you want to play on both computers at the same time, then you will
need a new account key. Get PoP and use it's key to start a new account
with all expansions except LoY enabled.
