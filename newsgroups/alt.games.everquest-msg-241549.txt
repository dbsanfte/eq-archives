Renaissance english recipe...
Digby's Savoury Toasted Cheese
.5 lb cream cheese
.5 pound butter
.25 pound brie
pinch fresh ground white pepper

makes enough for 1 person to have a large serving, or 2 dainty servings

melt the butter in a heavy sauce pan, or in a fondue pot
add small pieces of cream cheese and brie without the white rind 
stirring until it all melts, stir in the pepper and let simmer together 
for a minute or two.

Serve with toast points, over green beans, asparagus, dip artichokes 
into it, over chunks of potatoes like a raclette, over egg 
noodles...heck, dig in with a spoon and listen to your arteries scream 
for mercy=)
