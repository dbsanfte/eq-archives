Here's a good Warrior equipment decision tree all the way up
to level 60:

1. If your Stamina is below 100, favor STA.
2. If 1 is met and your AC is below 1250, favor AC.
3. If 1 and 2 are met and your resists are below 100, favor
resists.
4. If 1, 2, and 3 are met, favor HPs.

(Obviously there are exceptions and special cases where
you'll want to favor a particular stat or resist for a
certain event. FR/MR on a Naggy raid, for example.)
