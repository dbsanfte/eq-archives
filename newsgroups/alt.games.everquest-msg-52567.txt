Nope.  You lose some the instant you die, and then you lose more when
you /release.  A res avoids the /release penalty.  It's easy to
overlook the first part if you aren't paying attention, or are below
level 7 or so, because that 5% I mentioned is not quite right.  It is
5% at level 10 and above, 4% at 9, 3% at 8, 2% at 7, and only 1% at 6.
Also, the first death at a given level is at 1/3 the XP loss, and the
second death is at 2/3 the XP loss.
