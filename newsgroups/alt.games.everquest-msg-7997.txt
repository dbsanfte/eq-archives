Yes AC is king for the melee classes.

> Even if that involves trading out an item that has +dex,
> +str, or +agi stats?

Oh yes. For example do not get serpentine bracers, they
are crap items. Go for AC, and consider the weight so
you do not lose your natural AC bonus for being a monk.

Make sure to get yourself 1 or 2 fighting batons as you
can afford them after you upgrade your armor. Also
do not neglect your 2hb skill either. Get a bamboo bo stick
to practice with. Next big ticket item you want to save for
is a FBSS, as Martha would say haste is a good thing!
