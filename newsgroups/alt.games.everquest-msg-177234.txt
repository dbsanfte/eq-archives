> After following what others have suggested here there are a couple of
> safe ways to raise your PP skill.
>
> The method I used is to Pick Pocket an orc pawn or cent in the
> Kelethin newbie area but you can use any low lvl (lvl 5 or so)
> "humanoid" mob.
>
> Just sneak up behind the mob and use your new hotkey to start PP'ing
> it. When the mob turns to attack you DON'T attack back, let the mob
> beat on you while you keep hitting the PP key. It's boring but it
> works on getting that skill up safely and lots more quickly than
> practiceing on mobs while fighting in groups.
>
> I didn't use this method till I was lvl 32 and got my skill from about
> 5 or 6 to above Master lvl in about 3 hr's. I'm now lvl 35 and my PP
> skill is maxed (to my current lvl) useing this method. After each new
> lvl I just went to GFay and found a pawn and practiced till I got my 5
> pt's for that lvl. Took about 30 minutes each time.
>
