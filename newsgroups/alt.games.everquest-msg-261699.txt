/cast <number>... so assume you want to cast spell 1

/cast 1
/cast 1, /pause 300
/cast 1, /pause 300
/cast 1, /pause 300
/cast 1

Theres a 2.2 second delay between casting spells (not including individual
spells recast timer).. so 300 (3 seconds) should work with most spells

If it fizzles.. it just fizzles. The script is not intelligent, it will not
react to a fizzle.. just wait 3 seconds and then go again
