Picking the Right Race

The first thing you have to pick then is your race.   Which race you pick will
determine the foundation of you character for the rest of your life in Norrath
so it is kind of important you pick one you like.  Most of the rogue races are
quite well suited to the class, but there are some differences to note.

Wood Elves, Dark Elves and Gnomes all have low strength however which is a
large hindrance to to the overall damage output of the class.  Barbarians and
Dwarves on the other hand have quite high strength which is a definite bonus.
