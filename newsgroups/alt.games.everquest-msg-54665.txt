The single best thing I ever did when I started playing "pure" casters was
memorize the following formula:

Level * ( STAT / 5 ) = Mana

Replacing "STAT" with WIS or INT as the case may be. Keep a calculator
handy so you can quickly figure out which is better, +25 Mana or +5
INT. (And you're right, the higher you go the more effective stat pluses
become)
