> 35+ players, but they'll all be in Hill Giant Valley farming for cash. The rest

> orcs, bandits, lizardmen, drakes, and a host of other things.

> http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?zone=26

> make the run through Feerot, Innothule, and three desert zones to get to
> Freeport, or through Lake Rathe, SK, NK, EK, and Highpass to get to the keep
> for a bank run.
>
