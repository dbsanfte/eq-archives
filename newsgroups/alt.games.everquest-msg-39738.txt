CJBeal <crisbeal@yahoo.com> wrote:

> Does anyone know what the thin blue line in the experience bar represents?

Its a magnified view of your current yellow bubble. If you have 2.5 blue
bubbles filled up, you'll notice you have half a yellow bubble filled.
In other words, gaining a blue bubble is the same as gaining 1/5 of a
yellow bubble - it's just easier to see on the blue bar.
