Items will not add up as he described. You only get the stat increase from
one item, any others that are stacked are ignored. Everyone would walk
around with a 2 stacks of Misty Thicket Picnics  (Str: +5 Sta: +5 Wis: +5
Int: +5 HP: +20 Mana: +30 ) if that were the case.

> Items that add a percentage to a skill usually do NOT combine.
> E.g., if you have two items that each give +5% to brewing, you
> won't get +10%.  (If the items are different, you'll get the
> larger of the two increases.)  Similarly, items that give "Haste"
> (faster attacks) don't combine, though they WILL combine with
> spells and other ways to increase attack speed.
