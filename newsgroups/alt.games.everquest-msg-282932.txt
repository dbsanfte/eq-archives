> Mobs have a base amount they hit for, and then an increment which is 
> added to that, multiplied between 1 and 20.
> 
> For example, base hit of 100, increment of 10, a mob hits for 110, 120, 
> 130, 140, 150, 160, 170, etc. up to 300.
> 
> % shielding takes that % value off the base hit only.  So, 1% shielding 
> reduces the 110 hit to 109, the 120 hit to 119, the 130 hit to 129, etc.
> 
> % shielding is therefore more effective on mobs which have very high 
> base damage (usually, raid mobs).
