> Best served up with hog jowls and greens.
>
> 10 lb. chitterlings
> 2 medium onions, quartered
> 2 stalks celery
> 2 bay leaves
> 1 tsp. salt
> 1/2 tsp. pepper
> 1/2 tsp. crushed red pepper
> vinegar to taste
>
> Wash chitterlings in water, rubbing as you would

> sauce. Yields 8 servings
