/guild commands  Sends orders to or about your guild

/guilddelete  Guild leaders may use this command to disband their guild

/guildinvite <char name>  Guild leaders and officers may use this command to
invite new members

/guildinvite <char name> o Guild leaders and officers may use this command
to make someone an officer in the guild

/guildleader <char name>  Allows leader to transfer guild leadership status
to another member

/guildpeace <opposing guild leader's name>  Allows Guild leader to decline
the challenge of a guild war or to call a truce once a war has begun

/guildremove  Removes a guild member

/gu  Guildspeak

/guildstatus  Sends a text message to fellow guild members currently online

/guildstatus <char.>  Displays a character's rank within his guild

/guildwar <opposing guild leader's name>  Allows guild leader to challenge
or accept another guild in guild war
