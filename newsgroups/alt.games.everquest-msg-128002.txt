No.  MapX basically automates the following manual process:

    1. Type /loc 2. Look at the numbers 3. On a map of the current zone, put
    a marker at the position of the /loc.

It's basically a real-time log file parser that looks for log entries that
tell your position.  It *would* be possible to make it look for coordinates
in tells and on chat channels and mark those.  That could be useful...you
"/ooc loc of spires, please?" and when someone answers, having the loc they
give marked on the map with their name would be handy.
