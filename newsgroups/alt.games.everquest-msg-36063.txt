1) Divine Light (For you Superior Healing)
2) Celestial Healing (44)

Divine Barrier)

Healing Spell
6) Stun (yes the level 5 stun spell, doesn't break mez and has the same 6
second stun as the rest of the cleric spells)
7) Divine Barrier, at 38 gate may be a better option
