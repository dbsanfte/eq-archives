Here's how it works.  Each additional member of a group adds 4% to the value
of the mob.

2 in group gives 104% exp or 52% per person
3 in group gives 108% exp or 36% per person
4 in group gives 112% exp or 28% per person
5 in group gives 116% exp or 23.33% per person
6 in group gives 120% exp or 20% per person.

Adding one member drops the value per person, but you can kill quicker.  If
2 can kill with little down time, adding 1 is not worth it.  If you have 5
and add 1, probably will be worth it, no matter how fast you are dropping
mobs.
