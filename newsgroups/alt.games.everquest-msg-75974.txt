You need bait and a fishing pole.

Equip the pole in your primary weapon slot, stand near water (face the
water) and press the button for the fishing skill.
Results are:
a) Catch nothing
b) hook non fish items (usually sandals or rusty daggers)
c) catch a fish
add to those, skill increases.

Most fish are food and all can be baked to make additional dishes.
