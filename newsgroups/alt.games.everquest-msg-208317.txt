One reason for this is if eqgame.exe is in use.
An example: if you use a Nostromo Game Pad and have a profile for EQ that
auto-loads, the Nostromo driver "opens" eqgame.exe at start-up, and
therefore the patcher cannot overwrite it. The solution is to exit the
Nostromo driver and re-run the patcher. You do this by right-clicking the
Nostromo icon in the task bar and selecting exit. After successfully
patching you then restart Nostromo from the start menu.
