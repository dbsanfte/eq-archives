Go to Merchant Tekrama in the building between the two banks.
There you can buy a 10 slot container called Trader's Satchel.
Put in your stuff for sale. Then walk to where the other traders are
standing (the little tents). There type /trade, set up the prices for
your items. When don, just click on Start Trader and go away ;)
You can see in the trader window, who has inspected your goods and who
bought something.
