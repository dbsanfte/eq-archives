There is a merchant on a platform in the middle of the lake who I've
heard will trade with evil folk.  Of course there are also a few hungry
fish in that lake, so best swim fast if headed out to him <G>. 
Seriously I've never been attacked by the fish while swimming on the
surface there, but it is a risk to be aware of, especially since the
lakes a bit deep there.  No banking nearby, but as a Troll I'd guess you
can carry a fair amount of coin.

The bind spot in FM can be found by zoning back in from LoIO and heading
out and to the left, there are some ruins high up on a hill.  Lots of
stuff there eat you fast though, and the giants there see through invis,
so be careful.
