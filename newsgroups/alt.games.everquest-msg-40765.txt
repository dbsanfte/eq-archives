Quest) figures for a Human Cleric, a character with no XP bonuses or
penalties.

Barbarians have a 1.05 penalty, and Halflings have a 0.95 bonus.  There
are no class penalties any more but Warriors & Rogues still have a
small class bonus.

Level 30 is more than twice as long as level 29.  Level 51 is about
1.5 times as long as level 45.  The really-hell levels of 54 and 59
