You have pauses on your buttons? If so, you don't need a whole line for 
a pause.  Keep in mind pauses are written first but executed second when 
written on a line.
For example:
/pause 20, /cast 1
will put a 2 tick pause after you cast
but
/cast 1, /pause 20
will just cast.
