Yes, there are research books. I am also of 16th level, but i am enchanter.
There are two kinds of books, information books, and component books. In my
class example, the first book for enchanters is 'An end of an age' or some
such name and it really tells you nothing exept to keep an eye out for works
done by long forgotten enchanters, and even lists a few names (I do not
beleive this to be a spoiler, because enchanters will still have to purchase
the book to find out some of the names listed in the book).

The other types of books open up to allow you to add spell components to
them, like the books 'The Book of Ancient Resoration' or 'Endless Tomb of
Enchantments'.

Various spell components can be found on different kinds of creatures thru
out the realm. At this time i have not found any of these components myself,
but have been in groups where people have been fortunate enough to find
them. They seem to be pages of script or something, many requiring dead
languages to read.

Some of the creatures that i've been told to seek out for the components are
Gorge Hounds, Deep Water Crocks, and Gouls. I have killed many gorge hounds,
but have yet to find anything on those, and have only fought a small amount
of gouls and no DW crocks.

Admitedly my information is small, but it is a start. Hope it helps you.
