Your skill will improve with time. I ran into this problem myself with 1H Blunt. The best solution is to follow this procedure.

1) Find a group
2) Attack a mob (monster)
3) wait until the tanks get a few hits in
4) Place yourself BEHIND the mob
5) Start swinging your fang
6) When the mob is low enough on HP's, step behind the tanks and burn as usual

The only time this doesn't work is against semi intelligent mobs who have a natural distaste for casting classes. You'll find this out when they turn around and attack you. If this happens, just step back. Getting behind the mob is important
because you get a "chance to hit" bonus, and this will raise your skill faster.

Good Luck, and by the way, this question is a little bit off topic. You may be better off posting it to eq_wizards@onelist.com (another very active mailling list)
