> What is the difference between AE affect spells and PB Damage spells?

AE = Area Effect. Damage will radiate from selected target
PB = Player Based. Damage will radiate from the caster
Rain = Damage will descend on target and surrounding area for several "ticks"
Tick = Base Norathian time unit.
Shock = Will cause direct damage to targeted mob, regardless of obstructions
Bolt = Direct Damage spell requiring a clear line of sight.
