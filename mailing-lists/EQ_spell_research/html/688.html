The Handmade backpack is created by combining a backpack patern and a High
Quality Bear SKIN, not a plet. You can find the paterns at the first inn
in north ro comming from freeport. For the skins that you need. Your
going to have to kill ALOT of bears, and I mean ALOT. I follow the ONEList
for tailors as well. Generally finding one High Quality Bear SKIN in a
full day of killing bears is doing pretty good.

At a Tailoring skill of 30, you can expect to succede about 1 in 5. At a
skill of 60 you should get success about 50% of the time.
