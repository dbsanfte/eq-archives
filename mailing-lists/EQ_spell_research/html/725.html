4: Azia and Beza (I think) What happened here is when the big Z made the
spell researches, he forgot what words he used and accidently named the
words he used for different spells the same name. Later when he realized he
screwed up (read: got hundreds of email for irate players like myself
bitching at him), he decided just to add the 'Azia' for the lower level
spell and 'beza' for the higher one so you could tell the difference. A lot
of people thought the spell in the book was wrong because they got the
message, 'these items do not combine...', even when they put the correct
words in. The problem was that they were using the word from the wrong
spell, even though they had the same name. Hence the addition of the 'azia
and 'beza' identifiers.

To answer you question after that rambling diatribe: I think that the Azia
is for the lower level spell and the Beza is for higher...if you buy the
research book, or get an updated spell list from the web, It should tell you
for sure though.
