Subject: Languages and trading research components


The following announcement, about a proposed player meeting on E'ci server
at noon Central today Sunday, to trade languages and hard-to-find spell
components, was posted by Alkrum the Atomic on http://www.eqvault.com/

I just thought it would be great if people from other servers copy Alkrum's
idea (I'm sure Alkrum doesn't mind me saying this) and get these meetings
going on their own server. A regular day and time also helps if this event
is to become an on-going, well-attended thing, just like a real-life weekly
street market. If this gets off the ground, I think it would really help
open up the market both for spell components and for ready-made researched
spells.

Datalore

===================================
Research Gathering

I'd like to invite you all to a fair of sorts on the E'ci server. It will
be held in the courtyard outside the Mage-type guilds in Freeport. The
event will begin at around Noon cst this Sunday, and will last until
everyone leaves. At around 12:15 groups will form for those that want to
raise their skill in the many languages of Norrath. I also hope to have
some vendors there selling items that a mage would be interested in.
Hopefully some of the vendors will be selling research components. Anyway,
this is going to be a very relaxed event: just a bunch of magic users
sharing their knowledge of different languages, buying hard to get items,
trading stories of their adventures across Norrath.

Hope to see you all there,
Alkrum the Atomic
