Hallo,

Just to clarify on two points be discussed here:

1. You can only research spells of your class, this comes from GZ, the arch
mage of magick in EQ, so you should accept it as the gospel in regards to
this question on research.

2. When you put one point into research at level 16 you actually get 16
points, that is why you don't see a skill increase for 2-15 because you are
already at 16. This only works on a skill you have a zero in, any skill that
has a zero gets the # of points = to your current level, so if you get a new
langague option that you have a zero skill in you will get an equal amount
of points equal to your current level, so it's best to wait to level 20 to
put your points into research/langauges you have zero skill in since you'll
get 20 points for your one points instead of 16.

I have no idea if you can research a spell for your class and sell it to
another member of your class, would be nice to know as I plan to use
research as money making skill as I know many people see research as a
headache and would gladly pay a big surcharge so as to avoid learning four
different languages and searching high and low for components.
