Uhh... settle down. I appreciate the support but its a little
aggressive even for me. Besides we're WAY off topic now anyway.

Basically, his arguement is that a group of necromancers can do
something no other group can do. My arguement is that any group should be
able to do it, or there should be other penalties. I certainly think 6
level 20 people should definitely give a level 40 mob a run for its money.
And i think if 6 wizards tried this they would probably die without tank
support (or find six warriors to stand there and die horribly). I
definitely think that 6 magicians with pet support should give a griffin as
big a run for its money as 6 necromancers, but not so easily, magicians are
less frontline type of spellcasters and a lot more support and utility type.

What would I like to see? I'd like to see unique monsters be able
to do other things. Why is the griffin bothering with our skeletons when it
should know who the REAL threat is five seconds into combat. Or maybe it
knows its outgunned and calls out to its brother across the zone who now
hauls arse across the zone to trash us OOM spellcasters?

My arguement is necromancers aren't overpowered, we take a unique
place in Norrath as being quite powerful battle spellcasters with our own
bodyguards. We also have to deal with the myriad of social problems that
being a necromancer comes with, and there are a LOT of those believe me, and
we have some serious deficiencies in other areas. You won't see the six
necromancer group do NEARLY as well when hit by a train of elf skeletons and
necro theurgists, or by a group of Aviak guards.

Don't downgrade the necromancers, fix the problems with the other
classes and make the combat AI better and general combat better for such
high level monsters.

Malark on Karana
