I think research and use of other classes spells is a good idea, as
long as there is a penalty for doing so. It would be cool if you could learn
others spells from half your level. I.E. a level 40 wizard could learn level
20 necro spells. Or if the spells were less effective. But "jack of all

advantage I have over the other mage classes. But still....

properly. But simply any-mage-can-learn-any-spell policies would be
disasterous.
