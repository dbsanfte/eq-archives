Yes, plans are in the works for this very purpose, and more. The details are
being worked on now, but a "guild" or group of sorts will be soon forming on
Fennin, then branching out from there eventually. It has a very similar
premise to the Conclave of Wizardry from the Dragonlance series of novels.
(Not very original, but a very good premise nonetheless, amazing it is not
already in existence.) If you aren't familiar with the idea, it is basically
a gathering of all mages, bound together for the persuit of knowledge and
the sake of magic. Those who join the Conclave can be of any race and mage
class, (and currently in a guild). In fact, it is essential that all are
represented. The original idea was not to go official, to just create a
group for the sharing of knowledge (i.e. languages, research components
etc.), but going "official" may be in future plans. A web site is in the
works, all we need now are interested people, willing to join and/or
contribute their knowledge to the Conclave. (The Conclave is a working
title, which may or may not stick.) If anyone is interested, please mail me
personally at bwgogol@.... Any input and new ideas are gladly
welcomed.
