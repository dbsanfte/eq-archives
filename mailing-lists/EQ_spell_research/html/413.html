Staff of Writhing


The staff of writhing is one of the various skull staff's in the game(which look similar to the staff
that the Priest of Discord is equipping). This particular staff is a 1 handed blunt weapon and can be equipped by
Necromancers, Enchanters, Wizards, Magicians and Bards. It has a damage of 9, a delay of 33 and has the effect of
chaotic feedback(which does 48 points of damage when successful).
