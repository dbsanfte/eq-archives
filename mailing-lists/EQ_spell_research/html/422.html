I tend to agree. The uniqueness of each magic classes spells adds much to
the game. Taking that away would not be a good thing.

I also agree that the ability to research other classes spells would be most
welcome. Quite honestly I originally thought that was exactly how the skill
worked. In essence, I saw it as another trade skill that would be unique to
the spell casting classes. Unfortunately that isn't really the case. It is a
trade skill only in the weakest sense. Unlike the Dwarven blacksmith who can
fashion wears for Ogre and Wood Elf alike, we are bound to limit our
knowledge to a specific realm; that of our specific class. Tis most
unfortunate.
