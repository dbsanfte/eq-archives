The Island of the Sisterhood of Erollisi has canoes, but on the other hand,
being at roughly 230, -9700, it's also pretty far from Aviak Isle. Some
other islands occasionally have boats as well, but they're also pretty far
from Aviak Isle (In fact, just about everything is far from everything in
the Ocean of Tears).

Given that a canoe is probably not much faster than a Level 22 character
with maxed out Swimming, you might be better off just stroking it there. If
you really want to use a canoe, you get in one, right-click on it (You'll
get the message, "You have the helm, captain") and use the movement keys to
navigate.
