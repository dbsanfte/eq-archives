Ok. For everyone who was having problems getting that neglected skill to increase.. I'm a level 16 wiz ..my two hand blunt is maxed, but I havent used piercing for a LONG time. I have been trying to build it up.. with no luck. Well.. last night.. I was hitting things for about 2 hours.. no luck. I had to log for about 10 min. When I came back.. my second kill.. I got a message.. "You're piercing skills have increased! (3)" I was shocked. So I fought for another 30 min or so.. got it up to about 14. Had to log for a bit. When I came back. It wouldn't move again. So, I logged out, then back in real quick. Yep. That did it, it went straight to 25 or so.

Maybe you just have to log out and back in a few times to get it to move again?
