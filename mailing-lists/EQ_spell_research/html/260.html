Subject: Happy99: what it is and how to fix it.


Sorry for such an off topic post, but I want to clear the air about this
Virus. Many of the posts so far have been flames or just plain wrong.

Happy99 is a trojan horse program that appears to be a small fireworks
display when run. If you don't download, save, and execute this program,
then your computer won't become infected. Once infected, any time you send
an email message, Happy99 creates a second email with itself as an
attachement.

Please don't flame the person who sent this, nor complain to his ISP. In
all likelyhood, he had no knowledge he was even sending this virus. The
virus sends itself. Just notify him he has a virus.

For more information on how to detect and remove this virus, see
http://www.e-marketing2000.com/happy99_virus_information.html

I don't know who hosts the above site, but the instruction are very straight
forward.
