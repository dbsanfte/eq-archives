Well, I was going to stay out of this discussion about kill stealing, but
thought I would relay a little story of what happened to me.

I had returned to Faydwer at 18th level after having not been there
for over 8 levels. I decided to go into Crushbone and check out all
the high level orcs who used to train and cause me to zone at earlier
levels (The Prophet, Warlord, Emporer Crush, D'Vinn, etc.).
As it was getting late, I ran to zone to leave, and a large group of
people there were attacking D'Vinn (large being 8 to 10 people on
him). I wanted to /con D'Vinn, so I gave a small 38 point blast to an
orc pawn in the way, and I did /con on D'Vinn, he was yellow. I then
proceded to zone, without attacking D'Vinn.

After zoning, I received multiple tells from people berating me for stealing
D'Vinn. Shouts then came in the Greater Faydark zone on how I had
been kill stealing. I tried to explain to people giving me tells that it was
not me and I even had them scroll back in the text window and look,
but at that point the damage was done. The next day I was in Unrest
and again received a shout saying I was a kill stealer. I explained to the
person that it was not me, and they finally did shout an apology.

The moral of the story is, if you are going to shout about people being
kill stealers, please witness the kill steal first hand and do not believe what
everyone tells you. I found out later that someone who was attacking
him (probably the one who actually killed him), said I was the one who
killed him and that I had looted the body. Everyone there believed him
and no one spoke up about it being wrong (someone had to have seen
who actually did it).

Just something to think about,
