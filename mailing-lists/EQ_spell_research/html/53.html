I am currently working with someone to train my language skills up.

What we have found is that it is easy to get both people up to the same level
in a language, no matter what the level is.

Then... one person will learn two steps in a language (or both one... it
seems to be a bit random, though it tends to be more common that one person
will learn two)

Then you zone, and the other person will learn two, etc... on and on. So it
is slow when both people are low on the skill... but it does happen :)
