I asked about this spell earlier and as far as i can tell it has been
removed from the game, unless it HAS been moved into the research bin. If it
has I havent come across the page number yet, tho i've still got some pages
that i cant research until i get the other half.

However, if your interested in an item that i believe was created from this
spell, it is the egg shaped pumice sold by the herbalists in all major
cities. When you look at it's stats it says Pillage and the effects of it
are to remove all enchantments, buffs, and debuffs from someone. The item
costs 15 plat but can come in very handy. I purchased one and used it in
Highpass with a group fighting the gnolls there. They tended to cast root on
ya then swarm over you. The Pillage stone would imediatly remove the root so
you could run. I saved the life of several party members with it. Although
i've never tried it, it's possible it might remove a charm effect.

It has 5 charges on it, and rumor has it that after it is used up it will
recharge on it's own after 1 week of game time, however i never found that
to be true. I didnt wait long enough i dont think to find out.
