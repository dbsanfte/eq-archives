Actually, I believe the range changes. When you are below roughly level
10, yellow is one level higher and red is at least two levels higher. I
am now level 16, and I find that yellow is one or two levels higher, and
red is three or more levels higher. Talking with higher level people, I
find that yellow is up to three or even four levels higher.

I do find credence in the argument that a particular creature type must
have the possibility of spawning in the 16+ range. At level 16 I have
found items on creatures which con blue (and thus are below 16) but
which can con even on some spawns.
