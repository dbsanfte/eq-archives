ONLY put in those items that the "recipe book" tells you to.
No parchment. No quill. Nothing else.

> Also, should I train 20 points into "research" ? or is 1 point good enough, like with meditate?

At level 16, you will most likely only be able to put one
practice point into research. Any more and you will get the
"you can't train this skill any more" message.

From what I have seen, every level after 16 allows you to
put in one more practice point into research. I recommend
that you do, since

a. Pure spellcasters don't really have anything else to put
practice points into (besides languages).
b. You really don't want to screw up research.
