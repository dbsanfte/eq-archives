i play a wood elf, the biggest advantage being that you can wear small or
medium armor. plus i like to melee, i like to play a battle bard, so i put
all of my points in to strength. with my enhancing items i am currently at
113 strength, 102 agility, and 101 dexterity. so the points will come with
items as you get them. also being a wood elf, you can wear all small bronze
or steel plate, which weighs less. as far as dexterity, i don't know if it
has to do with playing instruments, but it allows you to miss less when
meleeing (right? hehe), and yes agility allows you to get hit less. i think
the biggest factor with playing instruments is merely your skill level with
that instrument. the last stat you have to think of is charisma helping
your charm spell last longer, and some say your songs get resisted less. i
believe a wood elf starts with higher charisma than a half elf.
