Subject: Maestro's Lute effectiveness.


At level 50, a lute heals 18 points alone.
Standing (at any level) regens for a total of 1 hp per tick.
Sitting at lvl 50 (and only at level 50 I believe) regens for a total of 4 hp a tick.
So, standing while play Hymn = 19 hp a tick, and sitting = 22, not 21.
If the Maestro's lute is 25 while sitting, subtract the 4 from natural regen to get 21. Which is +3 from a
normal lute. 3/18 is about 16.666...%. So the lute increases stringed songs effects by roughly +16 - +17%.
