Level is the single most factor in resisting any spell. I cannot stress
that enough. Magic resistance will then add to that factor. This holds
true for anything. I've had my MR over 125 at the kobold noble spot and
have still been charmed around L40. At L50, I resist it everytime even
lower MR of 80 or so.

I will investigate cold resist and fire resist against dragons more
in the future. But I'll tell you this. With no buffs, and a CR of
only 39, Vox's breathe weapon when she appeared in the King Room
only did 200 dmg to me. So that completely debunks the CR theory,
and as I stated that you will resist it normally on your own via the
random number generator. I will look into it more.

I will also report that we had 54 f'ing people at this Vox raid...
wanna know how I stayed alive when all hell broke loose? I knew
this raid would fail as soon as I crashed 3 times in a row, and when
5 or 6 people went LD after each battle so I took Harmonic up on the
ledge that looks over the king room, stopped all songs, and just
waited up there until all the ice giants were killed. I told my group
to move up with me as well, because it does take 54 people to kill
a handful of terrors, spiders, and giants. As soon as the shit hit
the fan, /sit /camp /quit. And I lived. I honestly saw it coming,
and was on the buttons subconsciously.

So the point here is, if you have 200 or so L50 people on your server,
there's no reason to risk the lives of many by taking L40 people.
Plus you've got limited people to work with, obviously 12 people cannot
do Vox, but neither can 54... jesus, I'm having shivers from the lag
last night. 24-30 people is probably optimal for doing _any_ mob in
the whole game. So with limited people, you need to take the right
ones. Again, if your server is strapped for people at the time of
the raid, then you have to make do. Otherwise, planning the right
mix and level of people is key. We had the right level of people
last night, no one was invited unless they were L49 or L50. Even
had 4 bards show up. There was just too many people there.
