Could you clarify your "and" in that statement? If you sing
Clouding while anyone in your group is camoed or invised, they
are visible (not to you but to the mobs) every time your song
pulses. This is pretty easy to demonstrate - have a group
member cast invis on you while you are singing clouding.
Target a far-away mob and /con it repeatedly. Every time your
song pulses, it can see you and the /con message will change.
