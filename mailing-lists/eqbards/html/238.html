You CAN sing more than one song at a time...it's called Twisting. When u
stop a song, there is a lingering affect for abour 5 seconds, time enough to
start another song and return to the origional, if u aren't bashed or
slammed in combat. The best combo is to play your lyre and "twist" Heal and
Anthem of arms...u know it is working if both icons are up at the same time.
Just rotate quickly between the 2 songs. Work on different twising combos as
u get new songs. For example, notice how when u stop accelerondo u keep
moving fast for a few more seconds...it works and is an established Bardic
tactic....There are limits however, it is difficult to twist in battle if u
are engaged with a weapon, so the tactics is best used with your instrument.
3 songs at once is Impossible, and when u get Bellow u can only work in one
Bellow before having to switch back to the other song. Work on it to get
the cadence and u will do fine with this tactic. Garet Wynn(18th circle
Bard)....
