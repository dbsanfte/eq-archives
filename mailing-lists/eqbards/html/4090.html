My experience with the boats came when I was invised and a floated near the
little spot where the orcs are near the shore. I conned one and it said
indifferent. Not knowing that invis does that I went up to the shore to
investigate why an orc would not care about me after bashing all dem ones in
WC and MT. I hailed with no response and then remembered that NPC's don't
respond to invised ppl. As soon as I turned off invis it attacked me while I
was still in the boat. I backed off and it stopped but it still looked
aggro'd to me. I decided to see what would happen if I bellowed it from a
ways out. The bellow went through and it started hitting me even though it
was still on shore, I was in the boat, and the boat was a good ways out into
the lake. I thought I'd be in trouble if it kept hitting me all the way to
the zone line (I didn't have any equipment sides a drum and flute) but it
stopped once I turned around.

Odd place it is...
