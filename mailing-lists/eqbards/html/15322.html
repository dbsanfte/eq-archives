Crier's ZONE: work tagline caused me to think of something interesting. Do
any of you have EQ pop up in real life in unexpected ways (no not the amount
of time we spend playing it as in Harmonic's note) but other ways here are a
few examples:

I live in Atlanta, GA. I commute down GA 400 one of the main arteries into
the city. It's slow bumper to bumper traffic the whole way. Last week as I
was travelling into work I glanced over to my left. There was a bright red
car, followed by a bluish red car, followed by another bright red car. What
immediately popped into my head concerning the middle driver was, "Hmmm,
he's out of the zone." It made me grin.

Today at work they are pulling lines to place more PC's in my work area. As
they were pulling a line it ran over a piece of upright aluminum and made a
noise. My immediate thought was, "Gnoll!", because it sounded exactly like
the throaty growl that they make when standing at ease.

I program for a living and am presently taking an antibiotic because my
daughter brought home "strep throat" from school. I am finding it very hard
to concentrate at times and the thought sprang into my head, "I wish someone
would cast clarity on me."

One of the TV networks changed the time of one of their shows to a less
desirable time slot and upon hearing the bit of news thought, "Uh oh, they
nerfed it."

So tell me, am I the only one?
