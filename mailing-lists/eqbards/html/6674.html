I have just recieved a Pugius the night of Wed 15th of Sept, in Unrest on
the Brell Server, from a Lesser Blade Fiend. He spawned on the first floor.
Here is the story.

I as a 19th Human Bard, was in a party with one 20th lvl Paladin, a 23rd
level Bard named Elsin (great guy) and three other mid - low twenties
characters. It was my turn to pull, so I was using my newly aquired
invisibility, ( I find it works on most blue undead ). I stepped into the
Building from the left side patio door, and just inside I saw the Lesser
Blade Fiend. It consisded of about a dozen various weapons/shields swirling
in a tornado-like fashion. I looked at it for a bit, it /con'd high blue to
me at 19th. I diddn't look like it was pulled to this spot, it looked like
it spawned there, (it was standing where spawns normaly apprear). I stood
there in awe for a bit telling my party what I had found, they started
telling Get It.. Get It.. so I did. We killed it quickly, and when it died
all of the weapons and shields fell to the ground. As the lowest member of
the party I was getting the usual slim pickins, but no one wanted the
Pugius. It is now an excellent addition to my Dragoon Dirk. I feel like a
blender...:)
