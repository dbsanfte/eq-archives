At level 6, I had already priced my combine long sword in NK. Could not
afford it but I had the speed to go take a look and see what I needed.
Also by 8 I had been back and forth a few times. I always bought every song
about as soon as I could. What is the point of being a bard if you don't
have all of your songs?

My first good sword was a short sword I bought off Olyvia Bladefinder (sp?)
in the cottage in West Karana. I think it was 4pp? That served me well and I
still have it in my bank. Before that and even after, I used a giant snake
fang that I got in Blackburrow Paw from a giant snake who died a brave and
honorable death. Also, I managed to save the 3 or 4pp for the Mino Axe and
kept the best 1HB wep from my kills (BB gnolls prolly).

The lament song will be a good one for in about 2 more levels and then up.
It will help your groups pull singles from orc and derv camps - instead of
pulling the whole camp. Well, most of the time it will! Lament the lowest
cons and pull the higher ones. Be ready to run becuz sometimes they will
all come!

At your age you can be hunting the first and mid parts of BlackBurrow Paw.
If you have a good group, you should take them to Split Paw - a bit better
loot and less competition - just a bit further away and you don't want to
die there cuz of the long run. By 15 to 18 - you should be cleaning Split
Paw out for Snake Skin armors.
