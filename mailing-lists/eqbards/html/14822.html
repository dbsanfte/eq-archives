Sorry to burst your bubble but....

The Ghoul Lord room is a camp for a 45 to 50 level group if they have to
break the room. Lev 50 necro's, and enchanter & cleric teams excepted. In
the old days, it could even be a 2 group effort. You won't be going there in
a level 38 to 43 pick-up group. I got mine young because my guild members
who have leve 50 broke the room and we camped it. I needed help just
getting to the room.

You might want to keep both items - exe hood and bic. Use the BIC when you
need the resists over the dex (dragons, casters, etc.) Dex seems to help to
a certain degree ie: there are break points. If the +9 gets you over 100
dex then it prolly helps. but if it moves you from lo 80 to lo 90 - maybe
not as much. It depends on where you are with dex now.
