In well over 2 played days in kedge, I have never seen coralyn spawn, much
less spawn with the drum.

That being said, the tamborine is no drop, has +5 int (not super useful, but
better than no int) and is slightly better in terms of song boost than the
sharskin.

The coins are all droppable and most are fairly easy to get.

Overthere, dropped off of the scropikis scroungers I zoned in, found one in
less than 5 min, killed it solo at 39 and left. Piece of cake, although
sometimes they are as easy to get. Scropiki are not able to see through
invis, so you can walk around there all you like. The scroungers are Sks, so
keep it in mind.

Kaesora, dropped off of the failed crypt raider. People have had mixed
results getting this coin. On my server, he spawns every other spawn cycle,
so if you clear the library area once, you'll prolly see him on the respawn.
The library area can be easily done with a group of 35s and the exp in
kaesora is bar none best I've seen in the game.

Emerald Jungle, foraged. You'll need help with this one. Just spend a night
hunting the denziens there and you should walk away with a coin.

City of Mist, dropped off of the Captain of the Guard. The zone to CoM can
be done by a high 30s group without too much trouble. The CotG usually
spawns just past the bridge to the right or the left and he's fairly common.
We camped there for 3 hours and saw 3 or 4 captains.

Lake of Ill Omen, dropped off Bloodgill goblins. I was lucky enough to
purchase this coin off of a vendor for a mere 5 s, so I have no idea how
hard this one is to get. The gobs are mid 30s and hit hard, they are also a
pain because they spawn in the water, so you'll need to be in the water to
fight them.

Dalnir, dropped off of the Kly Imprecator. The Imp is an easy camp for a mid
to high 30s group, but he doesn't show that often. I recommend doing the Imp
on the second floor, as opposed to the one in the church on the third floor.
The second floor one seems to spawn much more frequently.

Draelon Stormsong
