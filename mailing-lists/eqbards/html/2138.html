There have already been a few really good replies to this, but I just
want to add and reiterate a few things.

I think this is my most powerful ability at level 23 and has been for
the past few levels. This song is also a death-trap for us if we have
uninformed or uncooperative party members. Don't assume anything about
new party members. Tell them how the song works and make sure they
understand the /assist command (telling them to assist someone is not
sufficient--if they don't know about the command). Also make sure any
spellcasters in your party that have Damage Over Time spells do NOT cast
them on the mobs that you are singing lullaby to. This subtlety might
escape them, and again result in your death or severe beating.
Everytime their spell pulses the monster will wake up, and my experience
is that it will still be more angry about being put to sleep than it
will about the damage from the spell.

Narcissus of Povar
