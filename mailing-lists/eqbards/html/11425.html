A L40ish group that knows what they are doing can hold the tower on live
side, but the tower contains the frogluk priest and the crusader. The
frogluk King is much deeper, and his room when fully spawned is
something like this line up..
Frogluk King (L49 Paladin w/LoH etc)/Frogluk Guk Knight (L48/49
Warrior)/Jin Frogluk Shaman (L48-49 Shaman) on the throne

4 Bok Frogluk Knights (L42-46 Warriors), can have Kor Frog Shamen
(L42ish shamen) in their spots

Overlooking the room can be the tactician, a L48-50 frog wizard, and yes
he will cast Gravity Flux and recently Ice Comet'd (OWWWW)

and a few others.

We've done the Frog King area with a group of 6 L49-50, the trick as
always is breaking the spawn, then keeping a steady pace of pulling. You
must have an exceptional puller. You can also successfully do this room
with 12 L40-45 people, but be wary of that tactician, because he'll drop
a L40 fast, and give you flashbacks of dying to a festering hag at L20.
:(

greater lightstone. The fire/cold saves are awesome for dragon raids
