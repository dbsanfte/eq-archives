Tzeetch - I'm not big on Macros - since they can't detect whether you missed
a not or not - and then you'll skip that song. On my hotkey I have Clarity
as 1, Most important song in 2, assist in 3, Second most important song in
4, third in 5 - my normal twist is 2,4,5. Depending on what I'm twisting -
I often will repeat 2 or 4 if I get a miss and only do 5 if 2 and 4 stick.

When I mem songs after dying I use the hotkey location - not the slot along
the top for these songs to ensure the right one goes into the right slot.

I DO use a macro on Mez with a 'say so the party knows my target - and
always put that song in it's certain spot (easy to remember a certain spot
for a single song).

Out of curiousity - you say "New Bard" and are level 51 - I'm curious - did
you take over the account from someone else - or are you being modest
calling yourself a new bard?

Slyde
