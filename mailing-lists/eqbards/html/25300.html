It can be done. The white scale is the sticking point as so many know ;p.

Spawning him is quite straightforward. The Undead Bard spawns in Trakanon's
lair after Trak is killed. I have heard varying reports on when, it's between
6 and 72 at the most, some say 18 +- 4.

Hand the Undead Bard the lute body; he hands it back unchanged, then aggros and
must be killed. Once he dies, Trakanon spawns.

Don't forget to prepare with coffin(s) in case you or others get shroomed. I
didn't, which was nice :).

I read The Concert Hall's guide, and also the one at EQDiva.
