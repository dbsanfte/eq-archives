Subject: Lullay, Charm and Pixie Strike


My comments on all three songs:

1) Lullaby: Lifesaver. Not only for me but the group.
When we have to evac, I sing this song and get
pretty much everything on me while the healer keeps
me alive and our gater gets us out. The reason this
song is so effective for me is because I'm almost
always grouped with a healer. When I nod, it's the
job of my healer to keep me alive while the rest
work on killing one mob at a time. Also, because
nod pretty much taunts everything onto me, the
casters in my group have a easy time nuking one
target at a time. I usually do this when we pull
4 or more mobs. Very effective strategy from my
personal experience. Drawback is that this doesn't
really work with mobs that are higher level than me.

2) Charm: I think it's a great song. Great way to get
stuff off casters and also great when the pull is
small. However in larger pulls, I favor Lullaby. I
don't always have this song memmed though. Depends
on where I go to fight.

3) Pixie Strike: Too bad this doesn't wipe S-List as
one of the bard webpages claimed. Another great song
However, the problem I have with using Pixie Strike
for crowd control is this, I have to keep changing
targets. And I don't know about the rest of you, but
sometimes, I find it impossible to click on the mob
I wish to target. This is esp true when all of them
are clumped together.
