Monsters can "sense" when you are scared or weak - like when you sit to heal
or med - and they will come and attack you. I used this fact quite a bit to
"re-pull" the injured wolf or lion who is running away trying to train me to
some bandits. All you have to do is sit and they will reverse course and
make a bixie-line for you and attack. I bet your wiz can attest to being
attacked by little greenies while medding.

In WC at 14, you are on the bottom of the food chain. You can solo wisps
but cant take derves. Well, there a big difference between a derv and a
wisp. Remember that the con color only shows the amount of xp you might get
from winning - not how hard the fight will be. Dervs are tougher than wisps
- even if they con the same color (which they prolly don't) If you don't
wanna risk death, don't fight the hard stuff.

Holding off and letting the mobs get good and aggro on your tanks is a good
thing!
