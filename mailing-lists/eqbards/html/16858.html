Mistmoore is a great zone and you will quickly advance from level 21 upto
27ish. Best places to fight in a group is either the the left of the lake
on the slope (looking at it from the zone), along the wall behind the lake
or to the right of the tower ( not highly recommended as people run across
here as a shortcut when running from a train).

There are 10 spawns around this entrance area and 2 gargoyles also spawn on
the side of the tower. The spawns are very fast and if you are the only
group then you will probably not need to pull from the chasm area as they
will respawn by the time you have killed them all. If you find you have
time there are another 8-10 spawns along the chasm that are no harder than
the lake spawns.

Because mistmoore is so underpopulated you may not need to avoid trains,
however if there is a castle group and they cause a train then keep invis
memmed and just invis the entire group, none of the mobs from the castle
can see invis (from previous experiences inside the castle) so you should
be safe and just let the train go back before pulling again.

Always be wary of the caster mobs inside Mistmoore they do lots of dmg,
especially avoid the negotiator, I had a bad experience at level 26 with
this mob, it charmed me and walked me all the way into the castle and when
charm wore off I was in the middle of Mistmoore castle alone. Managed to
find another group who showed me the way out and I actually survived
(before being charmed I had 1% health, on my way back to the castle I was
completly healed by a mob, hehe).

Goodluck, mistmoore is an excellent zone you just need to be careful. The
exp is amazing and you can level very quickly.

Madagan Songweaver, Bard of 42 songs (Loves Mistmoore)
