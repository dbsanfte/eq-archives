For those who use it, I've got a new updated bard song analysis
spread sheet out. You can get it at Rokynn's wonderful sight:

http://rgonzalez.net/EQSongs/

For those who don't know of it, it is song list reference that I use
when playing my bard. You type in your level, as well as enter the
statistics for any magical instruments you are using, and it will
give you numerical values of each of your songs.

It also shows known song stacking problems with other songs and
spells (which I think I use the most!)

Changes in this version are:
Fixed changes to Psalm of Warmth and Cooling. Added Instrument bonus
reference. Added level 47 song. Added Numbers for Selo's. For
levels beyond your current level, numbers are calculated for the
level that you can first play the song (so you can see what it's
going to do when you get to that level). Fixed level 30 percussion
effects (bug).

Enjoy!

- Reed Stormrwind (E'ci)
