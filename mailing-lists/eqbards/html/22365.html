NPC's have limited mana - they just have much more to start out with. You
can see it when you have long fights with healers. As they run low on mana
they switch to smaller heals. After several "... feels much better" you
begin to see "....feels better" as they run low on mana. Also, a tactic for
Lady Vox is to mana sieve her so that she does not have enough left to
complete heal when she gets low on health.

Mana drain songs have generally been regarded as poor choices for anything
except PvP because groups typically kill mobs so quickly. Maybe if you and 1
or 2 others are fighting creatures with mana it could help but it seems like
a very poor choice (both as a song to use and as a "new song" to improve the
game.)
