Yea, I still urk around here ;)

Last night we were in Ssra Temple and ran into a
really wierd thing: Curse of Rah'Vashagah (sp?)

We moved into the right hall and were pulling mobs
from the water tunnel room. Then we noticed that
something was draining our mana! And then, it would
not go away! We were lucky to be in a spot without
patrollers or respawn. But we had no mana and no way
to regen it.

Eventually, we heard from friends in another guild
(thanks Ashen Vendetta!) that if you turn off NPC
spells and use a "clickie" (jboots, telescope, armor
item with an effect) your mana bar would refresh. We
also had to camp out and come back with NPC spells off
to block the effect from coming back.

It's my impression that the curse was hitting thru
walls due to some bug not feature. Dunno - but if you
go to Ssra and deleve in deeper - NPC spells off and
perhaps re-camp to make sure you don't get cursed and
mana drained.

Maybe that's old news - I been lurkin'!~ But I hope
that helps.
