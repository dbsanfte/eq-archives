aye the Psalm series has a big advantage over the Guardian series. The Psalm
songs give resist bonus in excess of what guardian gives with a drum at the
expense of only boasting one resist. So if you know you need a certain boast
(say poison for fighting Nox) just mem the correct Psalm and weave it in.
One night a party I was in took on good old Nox, I memed Psalm of Purity &
the cleric buffed poison resist & no one in the group got poisoned. And for
those you you that do not know Nox's poison is very nasty.
