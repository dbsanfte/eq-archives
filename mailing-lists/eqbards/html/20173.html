Subject: Bards and Enchanters


Since some people think we bards are very limited when grouped with bards, I
thought I would relate what happened to me Tuesday night.

When I logged in, two of my guildmates (bard and enchanter) were grouped
together with a warrior and another bard. I was invited to join them. So
our group was 3 bards, warrior, and enchanter. All of us bards were around
level 25, so it worked out where we actually had enough songs for all of us
to twist 3 songs each.

The enchanter was giving everyone alacrity, but other than that, he was just
medding. Why? There was nothing else for him to really do. He commented
on that at one point, and my fellow bard guildmate said, "Sit, med, enjoy
the ride!" ;-)

So, in this group, it wasn't the bard who was useless, it was the enchanter.
Just thought it was funny.
