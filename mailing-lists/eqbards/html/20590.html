Dialog:

You say, 'hail Dionin Needlespin'

supplies. I often have to preserve my tailoring supplies to [mend very

You say, 'How do you mend very large items?'

Dionin Needlespin says 'I cannot use a sewing kit to mend items such as ship

but only I am allowed to use it. I can't have other breaking it. I have
heard there is an old one in an ancient and ruined library.
