can use selo's. I gain tons from going there each
time. But I have a problem. I often bank in FP.
Which means I run past guk. Ah, guk, a deathtrap for
bards. Using 70% of our songs in that dungeons will
get you trained faster than standing outside a weight
watchers meeting door when the ice cream truck rolls
by. But I hear voices. "Come to guk" "troll hide
belt in guk" "lots of items you like in guk" "yes, my
precious, the yellow light, it burns my skin". 9
times out of ten I stop in guk on my trips, with
always the same result: me, 20 frogs, and a fear run
to the zone. Why? Why can't I solo one stinking blue
in guk? Why do the frogs hate me so? I just want a
few shiny baubles and I'll be on my way. Our root
even when it works won't stop them, just slow them,
and 2 blues is an even quicker death. At 27 my melee
alone just doesn't cut it, but if I use any damage
songs, it's a parade of kermit's singing "It's not
easy beeing green" followed by a Loading, please
wait...

Please, does anyone have any info that will let me
knock off just one blue in guk? Just one, thats all I
ask.
