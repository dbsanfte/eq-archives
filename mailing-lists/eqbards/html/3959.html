I've spent so much time in Unrest I'm surprised I haven't been made an
honorary member of the estate. Well when I am there I typically twist 3 or
4 songs, my personal favorite is a 3 combo of anthem/chain/hymn/chain
repeat, you could also sub out one of the chains for a bellow. If I am
fighting hags, I like to keep psalm of warmth up because she likes cold
based area spells while in combat. If I am fighting hand/magi I will
usually keep Guardian up.
As far as group combinations, I personally like
wizard/enchanter/warrior/cleric/bard/fightertype OR
cleric/bard/fighter/fighter/fighter/fighter. Of course I have been
successful in many group combos, was even successful camping the knights
room with just a druid 2 wizards and myself.
