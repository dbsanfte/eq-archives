A hybrid is considered any class that can use slashing weapons or armor.
Druids and Shaman can. Pure Casters cannot. Ask yourself, does the Druid
or Shaman follow the same weapon/armor rules as a Pure Caster (Mage/Wizard).
Clerics are the oddball, in that they can wear armor.
