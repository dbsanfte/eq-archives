OK, some details on the running of of gnomes...

Make a first-level gnome on E'ci. Give him/her a name beginning with Gn,
preferably followed by your bard's name (e.g. Gnkitasi, Gnaloro).

Meet at noon PST this Friday (11/26/99) in Ak'Anon. We have offers of
teleports, and will try to get everyone rounded up and bound in Freeport for
the run. I understand that some people can't be there then, but we all have
scheduling problems, and some people can't make it at other times. Once
gathered in Freeport, we will run to lavastorm and hurl ourselves into the
lava pits en masse.

So far about a dozen people have responded... I'd really like to see this
bring the EQ bardic community together, as much as possible. I've posted
this information on the bard boards I frequent, and will send it into the
spoiler sites soon.

Hope to see you all there!
