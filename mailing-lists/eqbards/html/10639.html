Sometimes I switch out one of the inventory slots for another song instead -
especially if I'm in a big/good group. But I do like your point about the
Largo's Binding - I will probably start weaving it in more often, especially
when in groups where I can use the instrument attack.

However, (just FYI) here are a few reasons why I set mine up the way I
did... (the second and maybe the third one is probably only pertinent to
me):

1) I use two inventory slots to have an extra one for my drum (especially
when soloing - I have selos in the next hotkey bank).

2) I have a tendency to drop things when on the pointer (itchy trigger
finger I guess).... so I don't usually leave things on the pointer unless
I'm actually back to where I pull the creature for the brass/lute
switching-song weaving, in which case I do leave the instruments on the
pointer for ease of switching.

3) Also, I like the songs in bank 1, 2, and 3 because they are right next to
each other on the keyboard and I have small hands - easier to go from one
song to the next that way.

As for never using another weapon, there are some cases where I have fought
where it was just too dangerous to use this song combination (Aviak City in
SK comes to mind) so I had to go back to the weapon/assist the puller/buff
the party deal... But that is fun too. It's just a whole lot of fun learning
different ways to fight different things, and nice to know that some of the
things I thought I couldn't fight alone, I CAN!... woohoo!!!!
