I have a base 80 and I am a HE. I never put any effort into buffing my
strength, just the armors and items I chose happened to have some str buffs
attached an my str is 93. More than enough, way more. Now dont laugh and I
will explain myself. When do you need str? In a fight, right? If your party
doesnt have a Shaman, then your in trouble to begin with. Shamans are by far
the most necessary and versatile member of a party, next to a Bard of
course. I get a nice strength buff from him just the lil one that takes me
to 126 str, then in a fight I am playing Anthem, 136, then my PGT procs in
the fight, 156. Not too shabby. If he hits me with his 39th lvl str spell
and all these other things happen I have 173 strength, woohoo!. So basically
I put all my wearable buffs into dex/agil, also good stuff that a Shaman can
buff. I love looking at my stats during a fight though.
Dex=156
Agil=170
Str=173
Stam=125

Yeah baby! Get a Shaman.
