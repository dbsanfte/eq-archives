First off, does your warrior know to taunt the bandits off of you? If not this can
really save your skin so get him to. Also with the druid/ranger combo send the
rangers (sow'ed) to pull for the group, and have the warrior & other ranger stand
so the MOB's will have to go through tanks to get to the bards. This way the MOB
will get attacked by tanks & may forget about you. You might also see if druid can
lull a few before pull. Also make sure that non-melee'er "help" the tank take out
one of the MOB's when they aren't casting.

As for songs, I don't see if you were using inst & h2h or singing & weapons during
the battle. I would try experimenting w/ using weapons vs. insts. W/ two bards if
you are both using inst decide who will use drum & who lute. The lute should play
Hymn and Chords (start twist w/ Chord just before MOB reach party & they will "run
into" your song). The other bard w/ drum should do Anthem/Elemental (or Selo's if
a real long battle) till you get 12th, then the Anthem/Bellow twist. The bard
using the lute will be the most popular, so you may want to stop & switch
songs/inst to even out damage between bards. If not have druid save enough mana to
do 1 or 2 heals on bard.

Good luck.
