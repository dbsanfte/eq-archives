Another method of earning some cash, go to Innothule swamp, hang out near
the entrance to Guk. You will find all kinds of netted armor, at least we
did about a month ago, if you are high enough level, 12-13 go into the
entrance to Guk, inside you will find netted and woven armor.


Netted armor=1pp per piece
Woven armor=3-4 per piece
Woven gloves (rare but they are there) 6-10 pp, depends on the market, but
most monks will want this as it allows them to hit magical creatures.

A party of 6, dividing up the loot at the end of the day made 50 pp each in
about 5 days.
