Ok, this is how it's done (I've never personally done this btw).
When you teach another PC, their skill goes up to your skill + 1. So
what you do is:

1) You teach the other person, until their skill is maxed (i.e., it
becomes your skill + 1)
2) The both of you zone (to update the skill list, like, if you get
trained in a language up to excellent, you have to zone before it
shows up as excellent)
3) The other person now has your skill + 1, so he teaches you (to his
skill + 1, which is your skill + 2)
4) Zone
5) Repeat

Eventually you'll max it.
