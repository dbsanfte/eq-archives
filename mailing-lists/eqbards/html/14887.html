>The Solusek Mages and Champions are just a type of mob, they
>are not a named mob. They appear in the goblin "castle" area
>of Sol A. After you zone in and crawl through the hole in the
>wall, make a right turn, hop down the ledge. There will be a
>door in front of you - everything past it is the "castle"
>area.
>
>Best spot is the king area. After you go through the door and
>past a grate, turn right and go straight, through a secret
>wall, follow the passage left to a door. The four rooms past
>that door spawn Solusek goblins, including the king (rare
>spawn). Mages and Champions will sometimes spawn in place of
>the normal Solusek goblins. Beware there is a wandering Sol
>goblin in this hallway, and all SOl goblins can see invis.
>You will need a full group in their low to mid 30s (enchanter
>highly recommended until they fix Lullaby - this was the last
>place I got to use Lullaby before they broke it). The king
>drops a +str ring, and a so-so sceptre.
>
>Not quite as plentiful is the high shaman area. It's a dead
>end so it's relatively safe. It's past the bar area. Up to
>four Solusek goblins spawn in the two rooms that make up the
>high shaman area (vs. 3-4 in the king room, 3 in the adjacent
>room, 2 in the room after, all of whom can heal through the
>walls). You could hold this room with just 3-4 low- to
>mid-30s, although you might need more to take it. The shaman
>drops a nice ring that grants infravision (useful for blind
>humans).
>
>I've never seen a Solusek goblin spawn *in* the bar area.
>It's a nice place to camp though, as there are a ton of
>goblins nearby, each separated into different rooms (i.e. if
>you have a tough fight, you can just skip a room while you
>rest without screwing up the spawn pattern).
