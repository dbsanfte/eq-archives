Personally, you need a druid. Harmony ROCKS on DW gobs and I recommend the
Oasis. I did it at 34 with a party of 3. My brothers druid was 32 and my
wife's warrior was 33 also. Basically, the druid goes down and area effect
harmonies ALL of them. DO NOT USE LAMENT. The gobs INSIDE the castle will
agro if you pull one from the outside and you will always end up with more
then one. You can't get inside to lament them all, but Harmony effects them
all since it is area effect. The warrior (yes, these are casters so you
will need some HP to get out of the water!) shoots one with a bow and runs
out of the water to the inn. The druid then snared, I feared and we
proceeded to kill them for hours one at a time with very little downtime.
Have the druid save mana for heals though instead of burning and these
things have A LOT of HP so snare sometimes runs out near the end of the
battle. Expect to have to re-snare.
