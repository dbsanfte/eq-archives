I'm level 38 now.

Over the course of 3 hours in the executioner's room in lower guk, I had a
total of 12 snares that lasted full duration. I noticed a huge amount of all
manner of froggies (wan, zol, tal, urd, nameds) break snare whenever they
felt like it. I would say 80% or more of the froggies were blue to me.

Also, I'm not talking about resists, I've come to expect that, I'm talking
about mobs breaking the effect early. Now I will say this, I don't know if
I'm getting a message based on the attack slowdown portion or the movement
portion, but I'm definitely seeing the "Your Selo's Consonant Chain has worn
off" message well before 3 ticks have gone by. I have log records of me
getting the "Surrounded by chains of music message" followed immediately (no
lines of combat text in between) by the "worn off" message.

I was always under the impression that if I could get our snare to stick it
was pretty reliable, now I'm not so sure. I watched in horror as I got a
successful snare off, saw the worn off messages after a second or two and
then saw the froggie zooming off.
