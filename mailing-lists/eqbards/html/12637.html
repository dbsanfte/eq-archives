Hail All,

When I was in my single digit levels I found Misty Thicket to be a great
place as a Bard. There are lots of goblins to kill and you can easily solo
several camps with Selos. Remember to keep turning in the Runnyeye
Warbeads even if they don't give you xps right away. Eventually after
killing enough goblins the halflings will warm up to you and start giving
you xps for the beads. The only downsides are you have to get through
Kithicor Forest, The halfling guards as spastic about stealing your kills
and all that crouching makes your back ache.

I found Misty Thicket to be a great beginning area.
