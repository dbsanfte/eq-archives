groups, but have done a bit. And until now, I've not heard this phrase
uttered. I thought I had escaped it. But noooooooooo... the pickup group I
got last night was the WORST I've had in EQ that I can remember.

Players: Bardette, Monk, Ranger, Paladin, Shaman, Wizard (all between 43 and
45)
Scene: Solb

The wizard "Shouldn't you be playing manasong?"... me... "uh, I am"...
her..."well just play it all the time".... me ... "nope"... her... "well you
aren't a very good bard are you?"... me... "nope"

The paladin "Tani, you need to play regen now"... me ... "well this song
does both so just sit tight"... her..."well i want to regen first then get
mana" ... me.. "that's nice"...

Ok so I was a little sarcastic, but I wont even get into the pulling
techniques used by the ranger... Needless to say, I took the advice of
someone here and with a "OMG I think my roommate just dropped something bad
.. heard a crash have to go check it out".. I logged... but wowowowow...
what an experience.

Hope you all had a nice weekend and a little laugh from my story :)
