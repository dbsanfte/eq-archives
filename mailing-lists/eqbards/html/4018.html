I've noticed this phenomenum a lot in Unrest lately, and I've heard people
here talking about it before.
I've been peacefully sitting up against the wall, minutes after a battle
healing my party up and strange things happen.

Picture this: There I am resting quietly, 100 feet away from the house.
*Creaaak*, opens the door. A dry bones skeleten is standing there, takes a
look around, spots me out of a crowd of 30 camped players sitting against
the wall, and makes a b-line for me. We handily dispatched him and all got
a good laugh. 30 seconds later. *Creaaak*, opens the door. A reanimated
hand is standing there, sniffs the air, spots me again and makes a run for
me, completely ignoring everyone else. Repeat. Repeat. Repeat.

This happened four or five times before we were forced to zone. My puller
was pretty happy that his job was so easy, until a flock of GDBs came
running and we had to run. I hadn't even been into the house that night to
stir anything up. This has happened to me on several occasions. Its pretty
funny as long as its something you can handle, but a pair of hags hunting me
down for no apparent reason isn't a pretty sight.

Ah well, life as a bard :)
