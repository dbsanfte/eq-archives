Lower Guk:

Shin Knights (also the ones in Lower) - drops the gaunts - dead side near
zone or live side
Shin Ghoul Knights - shin greaves
Basalt Gargoyles - lower guk dead side near the bridge to the tower - basalt
carpace for Lambent BP

From the zone to the dead side, you first encounter live frogs, then some
undead ones. Most folks get invis to undead after any live froogies have
been killed at the zone and down the hall going to the waterway. Then they
run past any undead froogs to get in deeper to where the good toys drop. So,
its zone, hall, waterway then bedroom. At bedroom is both undead and live
stuff and 2 halls that go past the vampire bat room. Most folks wait until
the bedroom campers kill any loose live stuff then get invis to undead and
run down left hall - past bat room then down left hall but HUGGING the right
side of it to get past the Greater Ice Bones Skels. Past that is the bridge
to the tower. On left is a 3 spawn od undead froogs - casters and above
some gargs. On right is a hall to the live side and the bug rooms and more
basalt gargs. If you wait in the bedroom until the campers kill all the
loose undead stuff, you might be able to invis down the right hall and pass
the bat room and pass the bugs (deadly black widows) and get out to the
bridge to the tower. Either way - it can be real easy if the zone is
heavily camped like at a busy time - or very deadly if not over crowded.
Cross the bridge to the tower and go down the hall past a ladder then make a
left and look for the next ladder. That 2nd ladder goes up to the
assassin/supplier room. Keep you back to the wall in the assassin room - he
back stabs for huge amount.

You can get invis to undead potions to supplement your invis or go with
someone who can cast it. Or go with someone who can revive you and duel him
in the zone and have him drag your corpse to the room (and revive you
there.) Or let your high level friends do the questing - the finished
Lambent pieces are trade-able. heh.

You mention dark bones - the NK gryfeenes drop the charm needed for the
Lambent bracers. Have to do that 2 times or do it with a buddy who will
carry the extras stuffs for you.

Sylly
