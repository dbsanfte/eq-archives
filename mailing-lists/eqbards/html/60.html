Ah the lute quest, possibly one of the best quests in the game. You can
seemingly do this an inifinte number of times, as one time when I was 3
plat short of buying a needed item I just went and did this quest by
going from store to store buying up every spiderling silk possible. I've
done it atleast 30 times, so its a quest to keep in the back of your
mind. In other towns there are quests bards can do, and if you hunt
enough orcs/"give" stuff to other guildmasters they will give you
quests, though none of them are as good as the bard one.
