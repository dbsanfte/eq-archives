The Warrior room has three spawn points, plus can usually grap the 2 hall
spawns with ease. The Raider room has 3 spawn points but spawns much
tougher pickclaws. On some nights if not too many people are in the zone my
group had both room spawns and the hall spawns all to ourselves. It was fast
and furious, non-stop fighting for nearly 2 hours. The smallest group I
have help the raider room with was me, and enchantress, and a ranger, we
broke the spawn up so we had them one at a time and they spawned just as we
were done recovering from the previous fight.
