Bard Armor Recap and New Item Posted Monday, November 29, 1999 by Fyndhorn
To recap from my earlier update, Imbrued Platemail armor hasn't been found
for a while on any server. It is "supposed" to drop from Scorn Banshees,
female zombies from PoH, but it currently does not drop at all. So hopefully
by next patch, Verant will fix that up for all the Bards out there.

On to equipment, Innoruuk from PoH seems to carry a HUGE plethora of
different items, and so we have found a new item drop from him today: the
Shield of the Immaculate, yet another item for Paladins only, which brings
the total of Paladin only stuff to 1 billion (j/k =p). Check it out.

New Equipment: Shield of the Immaculate

Bards' Imbrued Platemail Armor Posted Sunday, November 28, 1999 by Fyndhorn
As rumor has it, the BRD-only armor from PoF and PoH called Imbrued
Platemail hasn't been dropping at all since the Plane of Hate was first
introduced. Basically every mob in both the PoF and PoH have been killed and
not one piece of Imbrued has been dropped on the Fennin Ro server, so if
anyone has any info on what mob currently drops this armor, please drop us a
line: EQ'lizer Staff
