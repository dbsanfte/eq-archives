Pray that the fallout from your raid is nothing like the one on Prexus. The
good feelings from the raids (Vox, Nagefen and Fear all went down in a
period of 3 days) quickly turned into animosity, arguments, cries of
elitism, and just plain bad mojo. The raids seem to have touched off a
firestorm of debate about who gets to go, who gets what loot, etc... It's
just plain bad mojo and I know a lot of players who have quite Prexus
because of it. Just check out the message boards on www.prexus.com in

(40 or so) and well respected, but we only have one level 50 and almost
everyone else is in the low 30' which means we don't have any
representatives in the current raids. Since I'm getting closer to
participating in this stuff, I'm wondering if it might not make sense to
start over on another server... On the other hand, there are a lot of
really great 30 something people on Prexus both in my guild and in other
guilds with who we have great relations (also all 30 something guilds) and
I'm really really hoping the landscape changes by the time I get to 45+...
