The 15 buff trick was fixed a long time ago. They just made it so any new
icon would overwrite the top icon in your stack if your stack was full.
This cancel magic addition had nothing to do with that, they just wanted to
make dragons harder.

This change makes bards invaluable. Here's what we did against the dragons
the last few times (think we've killed 3 since the cancel magic patch went
in). Buff with a few worthless buffs first, i.e resist disease, see invis,
etc. Continue buffing with the good stuff. Dispel your first crappy buff.
Fill this new, empty top slot with the elemental rhythems icon. Every time
you get dispelled, elemental will be the one to go, to be refreshed next
pulse. You'll never lose a buff this way. Since the dragon has just
breathed on you, its unlikely it will breath again before your next 6 second
pulse so you wont miss the resists.
Its good to actually have 2 empty slots at the top, because sometimes, on
rare occasions, the fear icon will be timed just so as to appear in the top
empty slot just after its gets debuffed and hasn't been refreshed yet. That
way you still have a free, top of stack slot for elemental to fall into.
If you're blessed with being able to twist 2 songs during a dragon fight
(lag is usually too bad for me) you could leave more slots available.
