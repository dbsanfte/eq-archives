Off the top of my head the ONE place I can think of that you will RARELY see
anything except purely magic-based attacks (no cold/fire/poison/disease) is
in Cazic Thule, where the majority of casting mobs are clerics lizards. You
will get smitten, and wrathed aplenty, and occasionally HTed by Crusaders.

While in PoH or PoF I tend to stack either elemental rhythms with guardian
rhythms or switch out elemental with purifying depending on the mob. If it's
a pure melee mob I'll just use battle songs. If lag permits I'll melee with
the rest of the group, but if we have a LOT of ppl in the zone (which is too
typical of a planes raid) I'd rather just turn my back on the group and play
resists/battle songs with a drum - and I never get complaints for it.
