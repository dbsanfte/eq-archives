Months ago I bought the Pugius Dagger to dual wield with my dragoon dirk
since the delay was so low and it works vs magic only monsters. I was a
shish-kebob machine doing 10 damage with Pugius and 12 damage with Dragoon

Silvery War Axe that fetches 400pp on the PC market has an attack delay of

is an EXCELLENT piercing weapon for Bards in their teens who decide to go
with piercing, however, one thing eludes me:

Where is this weapon found?!?

I have seen it posted on websites that it comes from a Lesser Blade Fiend in
Unrest, but I have NEVER seen a Lesser Blade Fiend in Unrest and have been
told they do not exist. I never see anyone using this weapon, nor do I see
anyone selling it...is it a limited release artifact now? Who knows the
mysterious secret of the origin of the Pugius Dagger?
