Najena was a zoo over the weekend. It looked like most of the server's
post-50 retirees had come out of retirement to own the mid 20/30 Froglok
mobs. Most spawns were double and triple camped and this little 'event'
seemed to bring out the worst in people - there was a lot of KSing, farming
and ninja-looting going on.

The place was so well camped, a level 1 newbie could have walked through the
entire zone with carefree audacity, safe in the knowledge that should
anything aggro on him/her, the aggressor would have been swiftly dispatched
by at least 6 high level players.

At one point, one of the GMs popped on to have a word with the masses... I'm
not sure what was said, because the person I asked looted Rubi gloves and
ran off before his group could roll for them.

AFAIK, the breastplate doesn't drop. I did see people with greaves, gloves
and helm. I think boots and bracers were also dropping, but I can't be
sure. People were having most luck from the named spawns in areas such as
the Bonecracker and Captain's rooms.

What did I get for my 2-3 hours down there? Two bunches of Whore's bane and
a lump of clay. :P
