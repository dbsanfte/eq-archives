grats mara. first advice is as you said, get a big buffer before you go.
planes and dragons have taken me from level 55ish down to level 50 and less
than 2 bubbs. for songs i usually play mystic shielding (very few mobs use
fire/cold based spells that i've noticed) and verses of victory (celerity
and/or chant of battle before level 50) on normal pulls. i always keep
pixie up to help the enchanters. there are a few mobs up there we can get
it to stick on, but don't use it unless asked. pixieing a mob that's messed
gets the enchanter who messed it killed fast. usually someone shouts Shada
mes such and such. we can mes Loathing Liches, Abhorrents, Revenants,
sometimes Banshees and a couple other things. don't bother on Golems or
Clerics. i'm assuming you are planning on going to hate instead of fear
when i say all this. good luck to you =)
