Nope, no secret wall - just go a little deeper past the spider room. You'll take a
few turns, then you'll see a door on your right with gargs clustered around it. 3
there, I think, that can be either basalt or clay. We want basalt, of course. :)
Was very easy to pull single gargs there using lament. Then, if you go a little
further you'l reach the bridge leading into the tower, with the 3 caster froggies
straight in front of you, and a few more gargs up in an alcove on the left. I
had trouble lamenting the caster froggies for some reason, always ended up
with all 3 at once. :) Thank goodness for pixie strike and elemental...
