Here is the story:

Late one night, early 4th of July weekend, me and couple of my fiends are in
Rivervale begging for a bind. I see Silvera Tinkerbelle who I know from a
server email list and run over to say "hi I just wanted to meet you!" (She
is lev 48 or 49 and I am impressed!) She actually talks to me and I nearly
faint! Well, we do some duck dancing and other stoopid char tricks and
generally hang out.

Then she inspects my equipment and goes: "Greetings Sylly, looks like you
could use some armor?" I say: "Well yes, if you know anyone selling banded,
I do have some plats saved up." She says come to the bank and lemme look
what I might have... Then my trade screen come up and she drops in a couple
of pieces of stuff I don't even recognize. Then she fills the trade screen
and says 'take'

At that point - I say to her "ya know I am way too low level for this..."
And she says "I will understand if you say no..." But I think - hey this is
the chance of a life time! So I take!

Then she fills the trade screen a second time and I am frantically looking
to see if I got nuff slots and destroying all my food and beetle eyes. She
says take.

And that is how the Bronze bard was born (I even told her: "I will have to
call you Silvera Twinkerbelle from now on" and she goes LOL!)

help as many as I can. Casters - you need to go to Millers by Surefall
tunnel in Qeynos Hills and get them to make you stuff - casters can wear and
