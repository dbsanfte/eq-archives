I never charge for corpse locating -- however, I will graciously accept the
thanks given me, in whatever form. In my other incarnation, on Povar, I
aided a 10th level warrior or ranger in finding his body that had lots of
money and armour on it. In the process, I died once, and needed his help
to protect me. By the time we did finally arrive at his corpse intact, he
was grateful and paid me 1 plat, 5 gold (He could afford that easily). I
gratefully took it, as I had been saving for some armour of my own. He
then asked if I could help his friend out, and I did, and made another 5
gold. It was the fun of helping them out that I enjoyed (though of course
I did go back to the leather worker and buy some pants!)

For newbies, I just help them out, remembering that I too stumbled across
the orc camp when I was trying to find my way around Greater Faydark.
