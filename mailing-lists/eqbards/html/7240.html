"Loading, Please Wait..."

Sung to the tune of Turn the Page by the master Bard and Balladeer, Bob Seger

On a high and twisty rampway, east of Qeynos Hills
You can listen to the Griffawn screechin' out his killing song
You can think about the Mucktails, or the gnoll you slew the night before

But your feet will soon be wanderin' their way across the plain
When you're playin' sixteen hours and you hardly seem to gain
And you don't feel much like campin' but your eyes can't bear the strain

Here I am, crossing the Karanas again
There I am, the puppet of Fate
Here I go, ambushed by bandits again
There I go, Loading, Please Wait

As you run into an Inn just lookin' to sell a bone
You can feel her fists upon you afore you can change your tone
and you hope she doesn't stun you, 'cause you really need to zone

Most times you can't see 'em cast, other times you can
But its still the same old result, be you a wood elf or a man
'Cause you always seem encumbered, armed with an empty hand

Here I am, crossing the Karanas again
There I am, the puppet of Fate
Here I go, ambushed by bandits again
There I go, Loading, Please Wait

Way up there in Highhold Pass, you're half a dozen zones away
And every plat you have, you offer desperately to pay
To get someone to Rez your body, else that Selo's must you play

Later in the evening, you still need to go to bed
While /shouts from kOOl DeWdS, are spinnin' round in red
You examine the trip's last forage, forgetting why you're dead

Here I am, crossing the Karanas again
There I am, the puppet of Fate
Here I go, ambushed by bandits again
There I go, Loading, Please Wait

Here I am, crossing the Karanas again
There I am, the puppet of Fate
Here I go, ambushed by bandits again
There I go, Loading, Please Wait

as sung all too often by Kitasi of E'ci
