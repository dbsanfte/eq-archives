They aren't really "hot keys". They actually give you access to that slot
in your inventory or equipment. So yes, it's not just a single button press
to switch, but it is simpler than going to the actual inventory screen.
What I have is a Weapon, Shield and 2 instruments. If I want to switch to
an instrument it's 2 clicks: pick up weapon, swap with instrument and swap
with shield (I just leave the shield on my cursor). When I want to switch
back I swap the shield with the instrument, swap the instrument with the
weapon and drop the weapon back into it's slot.

SO it's 3 mouse clicks to switch to an instrument or to switch back to a
weapon, but the up side is that I can do it while I'm running, assisting in
a fight or whenever I want without having my view obstructed with the
inventory screen. Also it's very fast once you get used to it, I can switch
either way in around a second, just click click click.

Hope that makes it clearer.
