I was also in the last beta.

The other thing is that weapons are rated compared to your ability to use
them. If you use a weapon that is better than you are then you are unable
to use it to its maximum potentional. If it is way above you, you can
actually damage the weapon by using it, probably since you would be
incapable of using it correctly.

Nice twist on anti-twinking.

Looting is different. When the MOB dies it drops stuff on the ground, you
need to select it and get it. Your loot is your loot, no one else can pick
it up. On the downside, if someone doesn't want the loot and leaves it
there, you cannot ever take it. I do beleive it is theirs until it
disappears.
