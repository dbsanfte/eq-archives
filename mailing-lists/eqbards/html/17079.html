The executioner is really pretty easy. I think there are also 3 other
frogs that spawn in the same room. So there is a possibility that there
could be 4 frog wizards, if by some miracle you there before anyone else.
Most likely, you'll be put on a list again.

If not, 2 of the spawns are near the very back of the room, and can be
succesfully lamented, leaving you to fight 2 at once, if you pull to the
front of the room. (the room only has one entrance/exit, so you'll know
which one I'm talking about).

Remember, lament all of them first, starting with the rear ones.

The exe isn't too uncommon a spawn, but I've only seen him with the sword
about 1 out of 7 or so times.

To me, he didn't seem much harder with the sword than without, but that
time I was in a very tank heavy group, and didn't have to take the beating
:)

He is going to hit pretty hard, so I hope you have a warrior, paladin or
ranger with you to take the beating.

As far as directions, I can't really describe that too well, but maybe
someone here will be a bit more helpful.
