My interpretation of these drawings has always been that the ligher colored
things were goblins from Sol A, while the red things were kobolds from Sol
B. There are some inconsistencies with that, however, such as #14 that you
mention. There are drawings in Sol A that you might want to factor into
this discussion as well. At least one of those in Sol A (Gabbie's room I
think) has a map that seems to depict a coordinated attack on Lord Nagefen
by the Goblins.

If you look at #8, I always thought that that picture depicted a failed
Nagefen attempt by the Golins. You have Goblins charging Naggy with weapons
in hand, and a slew of dead Goblins around him.

When I look at #13 from the King Room, I always thought that this depicted
the defeat of a former King at the hands of the Goblins, perhaps prior to
the Nagefen raid from #8? The gold is a crown.
