On the loc casuing missed notes, we called them fizzle spots. Seems like if
you are casting repeated fizzles or getting repeated missed notes out of
line with what you ordinarily get, it will stop if you move just a small
amount. Of course, most folks percieve this as BS. My own theory is is can't
hurt to move a bit and even if it doesn't work, it may make you feel as if
you improved your chances.
