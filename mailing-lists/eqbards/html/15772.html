Those of you new to it be warned however, one of the reasons it has been
ignored is that it is so buggy. Expect to lose a corpse in the wall a few
times (GMs expect that call and will help, often fairly quickly), or for it
not to be where you would expect it(GM won't help). Oh and the lava, don't
fall in. No, really. :o) If you do fall in near the entrance , here is the
drill:

GM's won't help you.
If you can't levitate, get someone who can, have them step off the stairs
and /corpse.
They will now find they can't get back up.
At the low point of the hall on the way to the swinging blade, at the wall
which, above the levitater, is the window shortcut, there is a slight step,
it is the only way (we found) to get back out of the lava.
Don't fall in the lava again.

We only lost one person in the lava ever, and it was because a big, rude
Barbarian literally knocked her in as they passed on the stairs.

So, to summarize,
keep your back to the wall, don't fall in the lava. :o)
