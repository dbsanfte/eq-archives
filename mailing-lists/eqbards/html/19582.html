The kaesora coin
=================
Not that hard to get. I have 3 or 4 of them.

The failed crypt raider drops the coin, and seems to drop it every time. He
spawns near the library. Getting to the library is laughably easy. You can
pull singles or doubles the whole way there just by pulling slowly and
carefully, no lulling is needed. The raider seems to be on approx a 40 min
spawn, which is about twice the regular spawn rate of the dungeon.

Scrounger coin
===============
Scrounger happened to be there one day as I was passing through. I soloed
him easily despite the 2 sarnak knights that joined in. (selo, chant, chant)

Dalnir Coin
============
PAIN IN THE BUTT to get.

The imprecator is not a common spawn. I spent 3 hours in dalnir before he
showed. We were camping the church, but the 2 times he spawned, he spawned
in the upstairs location. (2nd level) A druid was at the upstairs spot, so
we went up there and I bought the coin for 50p. (which is what I had on me)

Bloodgill Coin
===============
dunno, bought if from a merchant for 6 silver. :┬¼ )

City of Mist Coin
==================
Captain of the guard spawns near the entrance. You need to clear 1-4 mobs
before you can pull him. He drops the coin everytime. I have 3 or 4 of these
as well, and saw many others go to party members.

Emerald Forest
===============
All luck. Our druid foraged it on the first try. (whoo hoo)

Star of Odus
=============
Approx 105 p. Can be purchased in Erudin or East Cabalis.

That's all there was to it for me. Got my tamborine and am loving every
second of it.
