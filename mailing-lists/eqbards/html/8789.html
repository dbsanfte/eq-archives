Pugius 5/21

I loved this weapon, sought it for a long time and finally traded my combine
LS for it and 10pp when I was level 20ish...used it in my off-hand with the
dragoon dirk in primary for many levels and had a great time with the
roguish looks and quick attacks. Finally replaced it at level 26 or so with
a silvery war axe.

It is my opinion that this is a very nice weapon for bards who like the fast
attack and when used in combo with the dirk, the 2 point delay difference
makes the combo great for interrupting spellcasters when you are in your low
to mid twenties. It also is very non-taxing on your stamina and you can
last through long fights with this combo with very little fatigue.

So essentially I paid ~40pp for it and felt the investment well worth it, it
is a rare weapon to get a hold of and I just can't figure out why it isn't
more popular. As a matter of fact I have it tucked away in my bank vault
because I just can't seem to bring myself to pawn it off after the length I
went through to get it ;-) I keep thinking I will log in one day and
finally people will have suddenly realized they all want one and be willing
to pay me 100pp for it - HA!
