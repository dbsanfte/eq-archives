It can be real easy to get plat if you go to the right places. Try this.
Load yourself up with as much plat as you can carry. Run to cazic thule.
Offer to buy fine steel weapons for 3 plat for daggers 4 plat for weapons
make sure you check what you can sell for first of course). Watch heavily
weighted players cheer your arrival! Buy until you cant carry anymore.
Stagger back to oasis or rathe mountains to sell. Repeat over and over again
until your tired of the whole process. I never did it myself as I would
rather fight..but on my sever there are several VERY wealthy folks who did
and are doing this service..and making a killing. Some of them also bring
food and water and sell them a plat a stack. For me the fun is in the
hunt..not the merchanting :) Hope you find this helpful!
