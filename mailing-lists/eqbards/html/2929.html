Some quests are lots better than others - and there are different kinds of
quests - from very simple to more complicated to ones that seem nice but
appear to be broken. Also, it appears that you cha, class and faction play
a big part of getting the quest as well as the reward (unless reward is
merely randomized.)

Some quests once you know what they are, you can do along the way while you
kill for xp. You just stop by the bards and grab some mail to deliver to
the place you are probably going anyway. Hint: write down the name of who
gets the mail - two hours later and many dead monsters, you prolly forgot
who gets the mail and if you give to the wrong one - oh well no xp for that
quest. Warning: it is dangerous to do quests & trade skills at the same
time. A certain package of mail destined for Freeport got brewed in the
brew barrel <g> (thankful it was not my full backpack!).

bandits I think) please let me know.
