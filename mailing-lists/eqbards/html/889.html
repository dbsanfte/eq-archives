Enchanters, a Bard's best friend

Posted by Rokenn on May 19, 1999 at 07:46:59:

(ps2.pge.com - 131.89.191.251)

Why should every Bard have an Enchanter as their best friend?
Well Enchanters are the only class that can buff charisma.
Charisma is a Bard's best friend, a higher charisma makes Bard
songs much harder to resist. Example: The other day while
waiting for the boat in Butcherblock an friendly Enchantress
cast a couple of Cha buffs on me raising my natural good looks
(Cha 105) to 141. When I got off the boat in Freeport I
noticed a dark elf NPC hanging out on the rocks near the dock.
She con'd as a dangerous blue, which normally would spell
death for my at 18th level. But I decided to chance it, to my
surprise the battle went quickly with none of my Bellows being
resisted and all of them hitting for max (10) damage. With
just my base Cha of 105 I have had my bellow's resisted by
greens and have never seen a battle were even a majority of
them would do max damage. So all you enchanters out there keep
those charisma buffs handy for your team's bard. It will work
wonders.
