Woohoo! I'm not the youngest bard on the list! Heh heh. I'm currently
level 10, so you'll outlevel me soon & be giving me advice. Until then...

solo: battle chant + hymn + whistle
group: chant + hymn, or chant + hymn + jig

the only time I use the wince song is if I'm pulling in an open area. It
helps avoid 10 minutes of "your target is too far away, you can't see your
target, your target is too far away, etc". Just one pulse to get their
attention then I go back to the setlist.

When I got the level 9 MR I switched to using that in place of either jig or
hymn depending on whether we had a tank & healer or what we were fighting.

When I got the level 10 Anthem I switched to using that in place of chant
unless I was in a caster heavy group. Chant gives a dexterity buff that
finger wigglers appreciate.

Also, I'm just starting to get into situations where I keep my instruments
in hand for entire battles. These are still the exception, but when I do do
it it's affects are appreciated.

Peace & happy hunting
Evulia, bard of 10 verses, Tunare
