Sit right down and you'll hear a tale..

I had a level 9 cleric & grouped with a level 10
wizard and a level 9 wizard (this is on Veeshan
also). "So what are you hunting" they ask. "Scarabs
and Tara's would be good for us" I reply. "Ok,
I'll lead" one of them says after I buff them with
my mini-buffs. Off to the ruins Derv
camp they run. "Hey, there's only one derv in the
camp, let's get it before the others spawn!" one
of them says.. I look at said derv. Euridite Caster.
I con said derv. Blood boiling red. "Um, it's red
to me and it's a caster. I'd rather not." I say.
"Ok, who wants to pull?" they ask. "We're all going
to die" I think to myself. Before I can say anything
else, I see a fireball flying towards said derv
caster. At this point I honestly think I heard
laughter coming from the derv. *BOOM* Wizard number
one is at half health. The other wizard casts a
spell. I decide I may as well do my part since we're
here.. I cast a spell.. "Your target has resisted
your Furor spell!" Doh! Again this happens. I turn
to healing my group members who, after two more casts
by the Derv are at 1/4 health each. "RUN!" they say.
Wow, the first intelligent decision I've heard.
Divine Aura on, boots start walkin'.. Wizard one goes
off the radar. Wizard two goes off the radar. Cleric
with brain enters tunnel with full health. I waddle to
the e.commons zone to get myself out of the derv's
mind. I come back and wait at the platform meditating
what little mana a level 9 cleric gets that I'd spent
on healing them and using DA. Wizard number two comes
back to n.ro zone (see him on group list). I'm waiting..
Waiting.. Wizard number two's health starts going
down. I stand, look around.. nope. Not here. Wizard
two dies. Not a word said to the group as to where he
was to give him help.

Wizard one /tell's me.. "Hey, why did you disband?" I
reply that "I'm going to move on. Do some travelling I
think." Wizard one /tell's me.. "Aw man, we really need
you!" I don't reply. You can guess what I was thinking
though.

Anyway, that's how I get out of bad groups. If you're
not wanting to be in a group with the people because
they're a bad group, you really shouldn't worry about
what they think of you because you're not going to be
looking for them again anytime in the near future.
