I've played on Bristlebane since it was brought up and the server is
great. There are a large group of us that have leveled fairly slowly,
but enjoyed every minute of it. We roleplay quite a bit and even our
EQVault board is fun to join in on.

I also have a character on the Rodcet Nife server that I created when
it first came up. Several of my RL friends were scattered on other
servers and we decided to start fresh characters on RN so we could get
together. That server has also been quite fun, though I suppose I feel
a little more loyal to Bristlebane. I've noticed ALOT of former old
timers from Fennin Ro, etc have moved to Rodcet Nife for the very
reason you spoke of... they've done the powergame thing and they want a
change. Almost like retiring to Florida.

Anyway, I would highly recommend either board if you're looking for a
good environment. Are they Doodz-free? no, but then again none of the
boards really are.

Good luck in your choice in finding a starting point.
