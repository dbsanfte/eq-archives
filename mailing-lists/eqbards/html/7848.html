Heh heh. I'll keep it short.

If you twink via equipment that other characters have
outgrown, or happened to pick up during general play, then
it's not a problem.

If you twink via equipment that was obtained specifically to
twink/trade while other people were also trying to obtain that
same equipment at the same time, then your equipment came at
the cost of depriving those people of the opportunity to get
that equipment. This may mean nothing to you now, but when
you're level 25 and get to a nice item drop site and find a
level 50 who can't even use the item camping it, and who
refuses to share or yield, then you will understand.
