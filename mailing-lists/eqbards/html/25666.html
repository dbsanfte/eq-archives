You can sing from level 1. It requires you to buy the songs from a song
vendor.
At level 3 you have two songs which is really usefull:
1. area effect (AoE) dot
2. battle buff (Chant of battle)

Be carefull about the AOE dot getting close to guards with it running is not
a thing to do.
You should also learn about "twisting" which enables you to sing 2 or 3
songs at the same time.
It requires some practice to get down, but once learned your group members
will bow in awe for you :)
