My advice? Travel, do it, go for it. It's much safer than people make out
to be. Folks have one sour experience and they tend to aggrandize their
difficulties, aggravated by the frustration they may have felt at the time.
Not that I haven't had my share of crappy times... ;)
In general I've found traveling to be one of the more rewarding things to
do in this game, for me personally. Go for it, my reccomendation is to not
stick to zone borders. Get your sense heading up, do a nice big zig-zag
pattern across new zones, just to make sure you see a good chunk of the
landmarks and inhabitants, have your drum in hand and just... go... ;)
Go.
