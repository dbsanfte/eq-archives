Best place to hunt for good money and decent money and exp? Though most
anyone will dispute me go to the Ocean of Tears(take the boat from
Kaladim or Freeport). The aqua goblins ALWAYS have atleast 1gp on them
because of the bows they carry, and are good exp for 10-11. Then you can
move onto aviaks(going back to aqua gobs for money), at lvl 12 who will
occasionally carry a bronze weapon. At 12 you can lead the out with a
bellow and strike them from there, and if you get in trouble there are
several areas around the island where the aviaks will not run back to
get their "friends" that will resul in huge trains, and from there you
can bellow them to death or sit their gaining health with a lute. Since
almost nobody hunts in OOT there is hardly anyone to take monstys from
ya. I gained 4 pp and a buble or exp last night playing my lvl 12, and
thats including swimming back and forth when my bags get full. If you
ever need a map Jooren on the Solusek Ro(sorry only there) sells them
for 1pp a piece, which is damm good considering its very helpful if you
don't know your way around and it can't be found anywhere else to my
knowledge.
