Think back to your younger days at the orc and derv camps. The mobs will
vary in level such that when she is first trying this the higher ones will
resist and go agro when hit with the song. When you first get the song it
is not as strong as it will be in even 2 more levels.

Rather than tell her to lament them all without regard to their con nor the
order in which you apply the song, I suggest lamenting from the lower con to
the higher con. And like they say - learn about agro radius of mobs and how
to avoid pulling thru - even lamented mobs.
