My female half-elf Bard (Crier on Fennin Ro) has leveled up to 18th so much
faster than my monk did (monk's 24th) it's kind of disappointing. I don't
want the fun of being a Bard to run out so quickly!

I've started numerous characters and have had the most fun being the Bard
without question. The only character that was almost as much "raw fun" was
the Troll Shadowknight that I played up to level 5 and deleted (I was
spreading myself too thin over too many characters). I figure in two months
when my Bard is 50th and the Plane of Fear is getting old (sarcasm) I think
I'll start a Troll Shaman or something. Lotta fun.

The only stressful thing about being a Bard is dropping things through the
cracks in the Inventory slots moved to the hotkeys. I dropped a set of Bladed
Thulian Claws the other night and they're LORE. They were destroyed before
they hit the ground - 700pp flushed. That was Saturday night and I still
haven't fully recovered from it - I was genuinely enraged at the game eating
that much time and money (substatial at level 17/18, I assure you). My
stomach hurts when I think about it. If anyone on Fennin Ro feels like
selling me another set (or perhaps a Silvery War Axe), please look me up.
