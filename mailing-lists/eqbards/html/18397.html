Bind sight is a good thing to have - especially when you get to some higher
level places. I really enjoy having it in all the Planes and on Dragon
runs. Bards are usually in demand - ie not enough to go around - so you end
up sticking pretty close to the important mana casters in your group
(healers, nukers, etc.) So, the pullers get to go out and have the fun but
you can bind sight and watch through their eyes. You can also bind to
wandering mobs and "chain" from one to another: "I can see the Maestro!
He's dual wielding! woo hoo!!! Opps he's got 4 golems as guards...."

Dont be afraid or forget to use it - I think you will enjoy it.

Sylly
