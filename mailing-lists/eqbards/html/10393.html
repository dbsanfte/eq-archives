I know what everyone has said on this subject of the Jig not helping mana
and I have done my own tests. My exgirlfriend has a highelf enchanter and we
tested that rumor out. I had her meditate for 15 mins to make sure she had a
total bar of mana. had her cast chaotic feedback a major DD spell on her
self till she was out of mana. then i we timed how long it took her to
meditate. it took about 5 mins for her to get a full mana bar back at lvl 18
with her wearing 0 int and mana giving items. we did it again and this time
i sang the Jig and timed it once again this time it took about 4 mins
13seconds to get the mana bar back up. so it does help slightly. My theory
is the reason why it helps slightly is because when you are sing the Jig you
dont consume food or drink. so perhaps the programming has taken the time it
would take for it to do checks on wheather you ear and drink or not and
applied that to the meditating process. Frankly whether it helps or not. I
sing it anyways during spell caster down times. It is the song i use with
most of my Bad Poems has almost become a tradition in my guild that when i
am singing that song i am telling a bad joke or poem.
