I was in Hate last night. The Maestro was dual-wielding
Evensongs. We almost got him the first time (down to 'Mae'),
but then he started flying around attacking nukers, and
clerics who had sat down to med. The melee folks couldn't
keep in range, and he actually lifetapped back up to 'Maest'
as we died. The second time I went LD just as the fight
started, and when I got back there were only 5 tanks left
standing with him at 'Maes'. Our damage wasn't enough to
overcome his lifetap and again he was gaining hp. It was
almost 5 am at this point so we called it a night.

Quad attacks for 150-250 hurt. A couple tanks died within
3 seconds of getting him aggroed on them. Combined with his
AE lifetap and buggy pathing, he was actually harder than
Innoruuk (who is widely regarded as the most difficult
god/dragon at the moment).

I would've liked to have seen how Valoran (Rokenn's guild)
would do against him in this configuration. Unfortunately,
there was a server reboot today. Hopefully that will fix some
of the pathing and pet problems (we died to a pet train in
between the Maestro attempts).

Dual wielding Evensongs... Talk about too much of a good
