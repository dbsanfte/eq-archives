There are actually numerous "special" instruments, I will make a list from
Allakazam's list:
Normal instruments:
Flute: Wind instrument. Found on vendors.
Hand Drum: Percussion Instrument. Found on vendors.
Horn: Brass Instrument. Found on vendors.
Lute: Stringed Instrument. Found on vendors.
Mandolin: Stringed Instrument. Found on vendors.
"Special" instruments:
Brahm's Horn: Wind Instrument. No special effect. Found on named
Minotaur in Beholder Maze.
Drum of Selo's March: Percussion Instrument. [may be broken(?)]
Reported +30% effect. Found on Lord Nagafen.
Lizard's Fife: Unknown.
Lute of the Gypsy Princess: Unknown effect. Found in Mistmoore.
Minotaur Horn: Wind Instrument. Found on Minotaurs in Steamfont and
Beholder maze.
Mistmoore Battledrum: Percussion Instrument. +20% effect. Found in
Mistmoore (where?)
