Subject: Effects of AGI


FYI. Just read the following on the E'ci board at EQVault. The poster got
it off a monk website.

Galtin of E'ci

There was just a post on the monk board where someone used the log programs
to test combat against 10 DW Caimans with 93 AGI and again with 107 AGI.
While most of the numbers were within general tolerances, average Dodges per
fight went from 0.4 to 1.0, and average Blocks per fight went from 1.4 to
2.4. Also, the mob's hit/miss ratio dropped from 48% to 44%, which is not
surprising.
So I'm taking this as evidence that there IS an AGI boost to Dodge and Block
(at least for monks, but probably for everyone), and likely for Parry as
well. If anyone's skeptical, well, you can type /log as well as any of us..
