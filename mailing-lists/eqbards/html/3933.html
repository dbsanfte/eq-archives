Subject: Another note on Sirens, Pixie Strike


I played around with Sirens a bit more. Charming a creature
clears its damage list. Yeah, the one that determines who
gets the kill. The person who does the most damage between
when the creature is uncharmed and when it dies gets the kill.
This has implications both for anti-kill stealing and kill
stealing. A friend remarked that a bard was kill-stealing
from his group earlier. Now we know how.

Pixie Strike does not have a memory blur effect I'd read about
somewhere. When the mob becomes unmesmerized, it makes a
beeline straight for you, just like with Lullaby. It could be
that everyone except the bard is removed from the hate list,
but you will not be able to use this song to get an annoying
green mob to leave you alone. You still need an enchanter for
that. It is single target only, so if you need to heal up in
the middle of a one-on-one fight, weave Strike and Hymn.

Brag: I found a mob that I can (barely) solo at L27 (28 now),
gives good experience, and great loot. I made about 100pp in
just under two hours this morning soloing, half of which was
running back and forth from town overloaded with loot because
I can carry only 8 pounds before I'm encumbered. :-P
