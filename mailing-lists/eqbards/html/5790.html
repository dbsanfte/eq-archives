Subject: Soloing at 23


Someone asked about this a short time ago and as I am currently 23 I was
excited about hearing any replies, welp, there were none, and I think I know
at least part of why. These are some tough levels to solo. And what soloing
you can do can be pretty tedious.

I have been grouping lately and hoping that would just get me through the
next couple of levels, however I have soloed a bit, here are some meager
suggestions:

Roaming Aviak Rooks in South Karana. The positive side is that if you get in
to trouble (which I do regularly with the tougher blues) you can heal kite
and turn to fight again.

The odd Undead reaver still cons blue in East Karana.

Chasm minos in the Gorge are good, but they are not the common spawn the
gorge minos are.

Aviak Guards in Lake Rathe.

All these are fine I guess but as they are all just slightly tougher
versions of what I did soloing at lower levels I am finding them fairly
tedious. I love soling, it is one of my favorite things to do, but my soling
days may be waning, I need a very specific situation to have fun soloing,
challenge with risk, the ability to control the way the fight progresses,
some kind of possibilitty it could all fail and I could die, and the
opportunity to prevent that with skill. The areas where this is possible are
shrinking quickly in number.

Any other suggestions would be very very welcome.
