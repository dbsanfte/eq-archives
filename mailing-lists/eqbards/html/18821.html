Well, not too sure where you can solo well, maybe Lake of Ill Omen around
the lake has some wondering mobs that you could do. Just be careful and con
anything before you attack it. The day Kunark opened I went sight-seeing
with a small group of level 51's and they attacked a Iksar Crusader (I think
they're guards). It was blue to them and nuked them (and me of course) into
oblivion.

One thing I really want to caution you about is this: Be VERY careful using
charm when fighting in Firiona Vie. There are a lot of little Firiona
Drixies (look very innocent and pretty, like very colorful butterflies) and
pilgrims that are basically sort of like wandering guards in the zone. They
frequently help you out in fights, but you have to KS them sometimes... hehe
The problem is that if a Drixie joins in your fight and aggros on a mob
that you charm, the Drixie will then aggro on you too. You will die quickly
and messily.
