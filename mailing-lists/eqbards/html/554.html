I have never set a price for my services because I find that donations work
out very well. When I hit level 4 and got the spell someone needed help
right away. I helped him and got 3PP. That was a fortune at level 3. A
few levels later while fighting in West Karana I heard someone cry out for
help. I had to run to the zone with Croon and Froon chasing me but he got
his corpse. After we met back up I got about 29 PP worth of stuff from him.
That is BY FAR the best I have been paid. If people ask I usually tell them
I have received between 1GP and 29PP before. I usually get about 1PP
depending on the difficulty. I think I'll stick with this practice. But if
I am running to somewhere an catch up to someone going the same place I will
invite them and speed them up for no charge. But if I have to go out of my
way I probably would.
