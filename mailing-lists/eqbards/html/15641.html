The GM's will help with the bags of stuff disappearing
durning a trade. Just state to them that you traded
the bug of stuff, please specifiy to them exactly what
was in the bag. Also make sure and tell them that you
did not set the bag on the ground. It is a known bug
to the GM's that this is happening.
This happened to me the other night, and the GM
replaced everything in the bag except for one item.
This was much more than I had expected and was very
pleased that I got anything back at all.
Good luck all and remember, the GM's may surprise you
once in a while:)
