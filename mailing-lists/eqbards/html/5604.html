A Call for Advanced Player Guides
I'm pleased to announce that we will soon begin overhauling our Player Guide
section. The majority of our current guides were written during EQ's third
phase of beta testing! At this time we are seeking new advanced guides
written by players who have gained considerable experience in his or her
class.

One thought we had was to reward guilds that submitted an advanced guide by
posting prominent hyperlink to the guild's homepage on any of the content
written by the guild that was submitted and published on EQV. Of course,
submissions from individuals are still most definitely welcomed.

Advance Player Guide submissions should be sent via email as an attached
file (any popular text format would do). And of course all submissions
should be sent to aurum@....
