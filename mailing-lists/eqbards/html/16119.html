Tszaaz spent 17-20 (the pull that got him 20 was so chaotic that the ding
actually was a tad anti-climactic!) in E. Karana on Gnoll Reavers... they
are a blast as well as reasonable exp and money once you figure out how to
pull them. Might be perfect for your Ogre, er, though you can't run to the
Barbarian Guards :o)

There is a point where they come closest to the river, turn and head back
(sorry exact loc is at home) hang there and pull to the river, choose your
pull *carefully* at all costs do not pull War Wolves! They bite infrequently
and for about 3 damage when alone but will join in with any Gnoll that is
attacked and when fighting like that hit for 20 per bite. If you kill the
Gnoll they revert to being weak btw so they should be made a second
priority.

Lately (25) farther I have been attacked by Undead Reavers and Dark Reavers
(doggies) that actually give exp at 25, so be careful out there. :o)
