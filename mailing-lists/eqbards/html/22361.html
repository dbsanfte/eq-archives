To add a pause, you put:

/pause 30,

In front of the command you want the pause to come after.

For example, to play hymn which is in your 3rd spell slot and then stop the
song after the song has played your hotkey would look like:

/pause 30, /cast 3
/stopsong

The pause is counted AFTER the /cast 3 command. Note that 30 equals 3.0
seconds.

I have /pause 30 in all my hotkeys, but I have heard you can get away with
28 or even 26.
