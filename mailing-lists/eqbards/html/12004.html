>whole server with "/w all bard", with the same being true of the other
>classes "/w wizard" for instance when you're trying to remember who stole
>your kill. You can find out who in the zone is a certain level by saying
>"/w 25" to see who's 25th, or "/w 20 40" to see who is 20th through 40th.
>You can even go so far as to say something like "/w all bard 40 50" to find
>all of the bards between 40th and 50th on your server.
