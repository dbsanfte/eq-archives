Subject: Mistmoore and Dark elf camp in Lesser Faydark


We were helping some guild mates in Mistmoore yesterday, and other than the
typical problems with trains had a terrific day. Granite, cauldrons, 2
diamondine earrings, Mistmoore shield, Sacrificial dagger, Nightshade wreath, 2
Crested Spaulders, and more gypsy lutes and werewolf talons than we could use
;P.

We were headed back up to the bath to wait for Xicotl and I saw the Dhampyre on
tracking, so we headed that way. Oops, undead saw through invis, so we were at
zone regrouping and then...he was trained to zone, and folks zoned out. I
jumped in, killed him, looted, and.....Yay! a Hooded Black Cloak to call my
own! Felt good to get it myself :).

It looked to me as if the drop rates were increased in Mistmoore; maybe we were
just there a long time hehe.

After we finished we decided to wander over to that camp and try our luck. My
husband Knight (54 paladin) started pulling the Teir'Dal Elites, which were all
green to us. Good faction adjusts though, I remember Emerald Warriors was one.

Then we pulled Priestess Llandra, who is blue to us both. The two of us chased
her all over Lesser Faydark with chains/fear. The fight took at least 5
minutes. Man she has a lot of HP! I wasn't sure if she was a quest mob or
not; I looked today and she is. She dropped about 9 gold hehe. Clerics of
Tunare was one faction that improved.
