Subject: Sneaking into evil city with Sonorous Clouding


Well, it is not really a story of sneaking in but sneaking out of an evil
city. Here is the story of my first vist to Innothule Swamp:

I zoned in from South Ro and walk around sight seeing. At that time I was
lv19 so everything is green to me. I saw this froglok for the first time
and it was conned dubious to me. So I decided to kill one and see what kind
of faction hit it will cause me. Well, sure enough, after killing one, the
rest are now threatening to me. So I thought, well, I need to fix this. So
I run around a bit and look for a Troll or something to kill. Well, I found
one.. a green Troll Slayer! Yay, so I whack at her and expect her to die in
a few rounds. Boy was I wrong! She was beating the crap out of me! (Later
I found out that she is an NPC like Dorn who fight a lot harder than his
level) So anyway I was down to 2 bubbles and she had still over half
health. Time to run! But wait.. I didn't know which way is the zone to
South Ro! So I just follow the wall, and as I turn a corner... there were
these whole tribe of Trolls! I came to the entrance of Grobb! But instead
of turning back and face the Troll Slayer.. I just zone into Grobb with the
speed song.

Luckily there is no guard inside the zoneline, so I healed and ready to get
out. But there were like a whole family of Trolls out there.. even if I run
out they will still see me and I am not familiar with the swamp so I may not
be able to run to the zone before the Trolls cut me up in pieces. Well, my
only hope at that time was that the Invis song will work on them! So I
fired it up and sure enough, I walked out of Grobb unharmed! =) So that's
my story of my only visit to Grobb.
