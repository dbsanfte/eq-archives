No, it's not that. Used to be, for example, number pad 8 was assigned to
the upper right hotkey (hotkey 2). Shift+number pad 8 would switch to
hotkey bank 2.

Now, number pad 8 still hits hotkey 2, but shift+number pad 8 does nothing.
I need to hit shift+top row 2 to change to hotkey bank 2.

</whine mode on>This means no switching hotkey banks on the fly. I lack
the dexterity to hit that top row of keys easily. I mean, I don't know how
others do it, but I'm right-handed and holding the shift down with my left
pinky while trying to hit top row 1 with my mddle finger (cause none of my
other fingers are long enough) makes me feel like I'm doing hand yoga.
Plus I have to look down to do it and I don't like to take my eyes off the
screen. </whine mode off>

Granted, with 10 hotkeys per bank I may not have to switch hotkey banks on
the fly, but still, you develop habits that you revert to in tight spots.
When a Nektulos Guard approached my rogue last night and Shift+4, 1 didn't
hide me from view I nearly had a heart attack. Good thing he found a
young kodiak to chase.

I /feedbacked it. Should I have /bugged it?
