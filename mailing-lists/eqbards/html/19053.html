Subject: Thoughts on the level 49 song...


I participated on a large Chardok raid this last weekend, and had a
thought about when song could be placed at level 49. How about
something as simple as Chant of Poison?

Adding another chant would do a couple of things:

1) When multiple groups (each having a Bard) take on a large MOB,
there will be more song options available. This also will help in
groups consisting of more than one Bard.
2) When fighting a MOB with a high resist in one area, a Bard can
choose the other three chants. I.E. In SolB, I might opt for MR, CR,
and PR.


Am I way off the mark here? 1) It should be simple to implement, and
2) we already have three chants by then, so this will be giving up
more option -- rounding out the class.
