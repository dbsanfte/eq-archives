>What is the Sol A Safe Room?


A.K.A. The Fur Room, A.K.A. The Austin Powers Room, both for the lovely furs
that cover the floors. The zone to Sol B is right there for bailing. While
Sol B doesn't sound like a *safe* place to zone to, in all the time I spent
in the Austin Powers room I never ONCE zoned into trouble in SolB.
I love that room, you can pull however much you want (er, and more! :o) and
it is the perfect AE song space. I did most of the pulling, I had macros set
up for "Small Pull" "Big Pull" and "Zone" then I would settle in to play AE
songs. The Warrior had her hands full taunting off me and the cleric spent a
good chunk of mana on me, but we could really handle a LOT of pull. The big
issue is runners, you need a runner team (gotta love rogues for that).
And while I am on the subject of backstabbing...at ALL times in Sol A keep
your back to the wall. This is not simply a good idea, you have to practice
it absolutly every moment you are in battle. Those Fire Gobbies backstab
often and HARD. When someone called "ZONE" I would sing lull, wait till they
made the hallway and only then follow them, with as hard as they worked
keeping me alive, covering them on escape was the lease I could do, I only
died a once or twice in weeks of hunting there and I don't think it was ever
for being the last one.
