I would say yes. It sounds like the devils own deal (clear their agro-list
then when they wake up you are the only thing on it) but it is not so bad if
you are in a group that knows how to look out for you. You CAN control over
sized pulls, and you can help a group get started on an orc camp or the
like. To do that, muster every brave cell in your body, walk into the Orc
camp with only your lute singing lullaby. They will all swarm you and
suddenly a fair number of them (if you are lucky, all of them) will start
snoozing. If you get stunned a bit first don't panic, just keep trying to
play the song. Have your group pick ONE Orc, the strongest one, and kill it,
then move on to the next, when there is only one or two you can join in
again.

Big scary fun, like nothing else Ihave done in EQ.
