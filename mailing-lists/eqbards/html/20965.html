Subject: And another bites the dust...


It is time.

Over the past couple weeks a revelation has come to me, my time in Norrath
has drawn to a close. I'm not the most eloquent bard here on the list nor
am I the most active, but I have always felt at home here. It has been a
pleasure to read of the adventures of other bards both young and old. I've
even had the chance to meet and talk to some of you in game which has always
been awesome.

The days grow long. The nights even longer. I can no longer justify the
time expenditure. I can no longer sleep in late due to the previous night's
questing while ignoring my wife and children. It is enough.

To each of you, I say farewell. Enjoy your time in Norrath. I hope you
each obtain that which your hearts desire. For me, it is enough. To obtain
the mystical power of bard charm has completed my desire. There is nothing
left that holds a candle to my real life at this point.

It is time.
