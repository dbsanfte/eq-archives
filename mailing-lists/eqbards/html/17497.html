Go to steamfont...

Near the mino caves are some HUGE gears in the ground.
Fight the skeletons that spawn there.

They should be blue and they got me to level 14 VERY quickly.

While there may be mobs that give more exp...I rarely see anyone camping
here.
Steamfont is usually only camped by people killing minos/mel or guards.
