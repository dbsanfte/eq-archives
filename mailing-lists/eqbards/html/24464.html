Nice social, but I would like to offer one word of caution -- there
are times when /assist <main assist name> will not return the correct
target. While I have found these times to be rare, they do happen.
For this reason, I would separate the social into two.

"Pet Assist"
/pet back off
/assist <main assist name>

"Pet Attack"
/pet attack (or /pet kill)
/em Charmed pet attacking << %T >>


I always try to take the extra fraction of a second to confirm I have
the correct target. If you are fighting two different MOBs, you can
get a pretty good feel by looking at your target box; if you are
fighting more than one of the same MOB, I try to look and make sure
the correct name is flashing. Our relationship with enchanters is
often shaky anyway, the last thing I want to do is break mez on the
wrong MOB. *Grins*
