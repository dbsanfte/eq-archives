On Fri, 7 Jan 2000, John Tatsukawa wrote:

> As an addition, I've always had problems finding my guildmates for one of
> the guilds that I belong to. Someone tried convincing me that there was a
> "/who all guild" command, but that certainly doesn't work.

Using quotes does work. Example:

I am in The Revolution. Doing

/w all the revolution

will return The Erudin Arcanium, as they are alphabetically higher.
However, if I do

/w all "the revolution"

I will get what I am looking for.

(N.B. Abbreviating works, /w all "the rev" works identically.)
