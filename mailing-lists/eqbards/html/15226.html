Actually, I still find concord very useful in specific situations.
Dueling, for instance--nothing more annoying than having a caster nuke
and invis, rinse repeat. But do I want to take the chance of switching
to my flute, in case they reappear? Heck, no. So I sing concord and
they are sitting ducks.
I also found it very useful for CRs for humans--a couple of humans died
in a nasty train and by the time they returned it was pitch black and
they could not even see their corpses to loot. So group up, kick on
concord and its lovely ultravision component and you have a couple very
grateful humans.
I can think of a few other situations where the see invis *or*
ultravision could be equally useful, so I am not willing to write this
song off quite yet.
