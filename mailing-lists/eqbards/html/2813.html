Subject: Binding and Chain


I took some time to test the effect of Binding (L20) and Chain
(L23) on attack speed. It was only a low level mob (fire
beetle), so maybe results will vary with higher level mobs.
But with my level 24 bard:

No song: 5 attacks in 15.0 seconds = 3.0 sec/attack
Binding: 5 attacks in 18.4 seconds = 3.7 sec/attack
Chain: 5 attacks in 18.4 seconds = 3.7 sec/attack

I timed each 2-3 times so it should be fairly accurate. Both
Binding and Chain slow down attack speed, both apparently by
the same amount - about 23% at level 24. It could be
different for different mobs, I just gave up trying to time it
after all the spellcasters nearby kept nuking the orcs and
goblins I was trying this on. :-)
