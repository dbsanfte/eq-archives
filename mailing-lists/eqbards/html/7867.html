Patchwork is all trivial at 26. Raw silk begins triviality at 33 (gloves,
mask) and is still not all trivial at 39. Cured Silk does not begin
triviality until after at least 44 where I am now unless it is the masks,
haven't made one of those in a few points. Also the raw silk and Cured silk
will sell to vendors for about break even. I wouldn't suggest that unless
desperate though since the Cured is selling for 2-3pp per AC point currently
(at least on E'ci) and even the raw silk sells at 1pp+ per Ac pt. Boots are
the biggest sellers for me. No one seems to want a raw silk tunic, but the
Cured Silk tunic is so popular (due to giving that buff guildmaster look) I
have been selling them for 25pp with no complaints or question. The Cured
pants are also very popular because the AC to wt ratio for them is ungodly
good compared to even mesh. Pants normally have the worst AC/wt ratio of
any piece in an armor set so these are an amazing upgrade even over mesh.
