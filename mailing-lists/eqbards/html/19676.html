Here is a tip from someone on this list from long time ago -

When you move items, look at the tip of your mouse pointer NOT at the item
(its lagged a bit behind the tip of the arrorw.) Put the tip of the arrow
into the center of the box and the item wil ldrop right in. If you look at
the item and put it over a box, the pointer can easily be in the cracks or
outside the box and oppsie, your item drops - and sometimes (poofs).

Rangers seem to get the blues right about where you are. Guk and Solb -
sigh. Dogs and frogs - lots and lots.
