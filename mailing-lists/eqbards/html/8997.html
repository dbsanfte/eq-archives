Subject: Galtin's late night adventure in Unrest


Last night I was in Unrest after midnight Hawaii time. There were a total
of seven people in the zone. Fortunately, one of the players was a 36th
level cleric in my guild. She was grouped with a 33 bard. I joined with
them in the basement and our goal was to get two bracelets for the bards.

The cleric went Invis and found a GDB behind the hidden wall. She was full
mana and decided to solo a GDB that she found. She was doing OK and told us
to stay by the stairs. She killed the GDB and confirmed that it dropped a
bracelet. The other bard and I randomed for it and I won. Before I could
loot the GDB, the cleric got jumped by two werebats. She returned to the
stairs and by the time we finished them off, the cleric was out of mana. I
was unfamiliar with the layout of the basement so had to wait on her to
guide me to the GDB corpse. She medded up and cast Invis Undead on the two
of us. We got to the corpse and I clicked on it to target it. Not a
microsecond later, the corpse disappeared. The eight minute time limit
elapsed right before I right clicked on it to loot.

To add to the misfortune, we got jumped by a festering hag and another
greater dark bone. We fought them at the zone border but I was getting beat
up on so had to flee. They eventually killed them both but because I was in
Dagnor's, I missed out on my first chance to get revenge on a festering hag.
Festering hags have killed me more than any other creature in Unrest and I
was looking forward to finally killing one. The GDB we killed didn't drop a
bracelet and it was getting late so I camped without getting a bracelet.
Looking forward to trying again though:-)

Galtin of E'ci
