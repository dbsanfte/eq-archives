Personally I think bards are getting the short end of the stick
when it comes to unique items. Paladins have the Flaming Two-Handed Sword
and 4 or 5 others, Shadowknights the same, of course all the casters have
special items as well that boost mana or other things.

But we bards have no BARD-SPECIFIC or even bard-flavored items
that I know of. Wouldn't it be nice to have a special drum that gave
maybe a 10% boost to the Accelerando? Or perhaps a fabled mandolin that
could instead of merely boosting the healing song, also remove disease or
poison or any adverse effects? Heck, a weapon that acted as an instrument
would be great, as well. Bone bladed lute? Well, maybe scratch that one,
eh?
