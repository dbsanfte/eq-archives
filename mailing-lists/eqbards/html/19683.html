Other than the stupid looting decision made by Verant, one which stands
directly opposed to their idea that players should not be able to harm one
another if they aren't PvP btw, I think the rogue class is very desirable.

I've had good success grouping with rogues with my bard, my druid, my
wizard, and my shaman. Any class that can 'get the attention' of another
mob will find rogues quite valuable. Bards used to be the absolute best at
this and I remember once grouping with a rogue when I was around 15th lvl.
He'd backstab it and it would turn to face him, my song would pulse it would
face me, he'd backstab it, etc. etc.

I think rogues got the shaft in a way because Verant couldn't figure out how
to make traps work. They seem to have only poorly implemented locks as
well. Other than that rogues seem to be pretty good fighters.
