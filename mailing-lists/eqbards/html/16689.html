If you think of your direction as a compass degrees, if your
friend is at 159 and you are facing 120, the obvious way to
get you to follow your friend is to gradually increase your
heading - 125, 130, 135, 140, 145, 150, 155, 159. Likewise,
if your friend was at 94, you'd decrease your heading - 115,
110, 105, 100, 95, 94. The problem with coding it like that
is if your friend is at 359 and you're facing 5. The logic
says since 5 is less than 359, to increase the 5 until you hit
359. That makes you loop around to the right.
