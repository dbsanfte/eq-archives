This requires a little clarification, just so everyone doesn't get totally
scared
away from even trying Zek. :) You can loot any *one* item a person has on
them, not including containers or anything stored in containers, plus you can
loot all the money. However, *anyone* can loot you for a few seconds after
a player kills you - whenever I'm getting attacked by a mage much higher than
me with no chance of escape, I tell my friends to stand right by me and loot me
the second I die - That way, the killer gets nothing, I lose nothing.
