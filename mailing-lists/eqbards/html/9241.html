I went to the first level of the Highkeep basement for the first time at 24th
level. Also went back at 25th level. Due to living in Hawaii and generally
playing late at night, its usually not that crowded when I have been there. One
or two groups at most. Despite this, it seemed there was a very long downtime
in between spawns. I think there are five spawn points on the first level where
pickclaws will appear. Two in the room off to the side. One by the door
leading to that room. One in a corner further down from the doorway. Last one
on the other side of the room in a corner. There may be more but I know there
are at least five. Even though the pickclaws gave good experience, I was bored
due to all the downtime. Didn't actually time it but I would guess the spawn
rate is around 10 minutes for each spawn point. Is the spawn rate faster
further down in the basement? Are there more things to fight in the basement
than on the first level?
