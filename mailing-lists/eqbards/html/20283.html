Okay, I have a few minutes to do this ;o)~

10-19 The Karanas. More importantly, North Karana fighting wolves and
bears, then moving to EK and camping Reaver Hill.

19-22 Lake Rathe. Hunting aviaks was great experience. Favorite spot?
The Cove.

22-28 South Karana. The Hermit and I go waaaaaaaaaaaay back. Not only is
he great exp, but he can drop up to 2p 6g, and FS weapons from time to time,
*but* he is a 4 & 1/2 minutes spawn (approx.). If he was still giving me
exp, I'ld probably still be camping him ;o)~

28-32 Lake of Ill Omen. (Thank GOD for RoK!) Hang out at the gobbie caves
for the first 2 levels (no one is ever camping the back entrance), then move
over to the Sarnak fortress. There is a little plataeu not far from where
the path runs out that is *perfect* for pulling to.

33-35 Lots of places. One of the best places I've found is getting into a
giant group at the FV giant camps. This gives good exp to me still at 35,
and they are just now starting to cone white.
