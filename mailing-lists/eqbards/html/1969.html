Subject: Where is a good place for a lvl 17 to hunt?


Hail Fellow Bards,
We have found the hunting to be very good Experience wise in Lake Rathe,
hunting the aviak guards that spawn on a tower on the first island out from
the dock. The spawn is quite rapid and we can usually alternate with a
second group for the 3 on the tower and both groups have as much action as
they can handle. There are also some aviaks on the Arena island. This is a
fairly isolated spot so there may not be much competition depending on your
server, on Xegony it is quite popular. You will need a group but we have
fought them from mid teens to low 20's so far with everyone getting exp. The
entrance to Lake Rathe zone is in the corner of south karanas past Paw and
the aviak town. Also, the aviaks in Lake Rathe do not appear to give a
faction adjustment. There are many other islands in Lake Rathe to explore
also.
Good Journeys,
Brilinnia of Xegony
