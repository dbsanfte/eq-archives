No one under 40 should go against the lord, and really should all be 45 to
50. the only way 6 ppl can do it is with one tank, 2 clerics, a wizard,
another tank or bard, plus an ENCHANTER. the enchanter has to mes the 2
boks so you can concentrate on the lord. the lord is level 47 or 48, but
has about 10000hp and TWO Harm Touches about 480dmg each, and double attacks
for 112+. so if you have no enchanter, you need about 12 people. The 2
cleric rule is so one can constantly rotate their 3 or 4 stun spells on him,
while the other heals. The only way to pull singly is a level 50 monk who
knows how to pull using feign death. Only tried that once but it worked.
Otherwise, need around 12 ppl or 6 or more plus enchanter, preferrably all
level 45 to 50.
