9 is hard, but if your going to group, find a group of people who will work
as a team...... the funnest time i have ever had in the realms was with a
group, of (Gasp) Dark elves... we all worked together each person doing his
or her own job... my advice is to start forming groups your self instaed of
just joining them..... you might gewt a bunch of bad people in your groups,

takes the damage etc.. etc.. and later on When you are higher levels you
are not going to have much choice but to fight in a large group. So build up
your team now... the more you play with the same people the better you will
react in danger with each other...... GOD I love this game, hehe any way
