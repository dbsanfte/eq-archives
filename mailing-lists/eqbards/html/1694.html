You don't attack the one you lament... you lament all the ones you
want to stay behind and then attack the single one you want to
engage. When you do lament them, I strongly suggest using you do so
with your lute/mandolin in hand, since without it, the lament doesn't
last very long and you risk having the ones you lamented still chase
you.
