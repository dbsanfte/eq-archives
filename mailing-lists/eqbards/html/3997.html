If you have a dirk, whip and tomahawk, I would say ditch the whip and use the
tomahawk and the dirk, as the whip isn't a very good weapon, damage ratio wise.
The effect is nice, but it doesn't proc enough to make up for the general
weakness of the weapon.

In terms of which weapon should go in which hand, in terms of pure damage, the
dirk should go in your primary hand, and the tomahawk in your other. If you are
tanking though, reverse this, as the tomahawk will proc more often in your
primary hand, and you want that sweet magic skin.
