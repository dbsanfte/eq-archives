Lullaby IMHO defines the bard up to about level 26. Only two
classes in this game can stop a train - bards and enchanters,
and bards do it by using Lullaby (or charm or Pixie Strike,
but those are level 27 and 28). If you *ever* find yourself
fighting more than one mob at a time, this song will help.
The only caveat is that your group *must* attack only one
target at a time, and a healer is *highly* desirable because
all the monsters will really hate you for putting them to
sleep.
