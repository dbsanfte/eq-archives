Live Side Lower Guk isn't too bad, but if you want it all to yourself go to
Kedge Keep.

I went there last night with two groups. One group of 6 and one group of 5.
There were 11 people in the zone.

I must say that it was one of the most singularly fun times in EverQuest
I've ever had. Working 100% underwater necessitates some tactical changes
such as playing Jig in battle and occassionally while medding, having
Aquatic Ayre memmed, etc., but man. Serious fun. Great experience.

After weeks of my "/random 1 100" being something like '7' I finally broke a
streak and rolled a 98 when we killed the big, mean, nasty shark
Cauldronboil. Of the 7 or so non-casters that rolled for the weapon I ended
up with a Sharkjaw Cutlass (10/33 2.0). This weapon looks *so* cool as it's
curved and serrated, plus it is an improvement on my Brazen Brass Kilij
(10/35 8.5).

Crier Sharpsong
