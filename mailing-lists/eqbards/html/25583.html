Few Things to remember about Gore..

1. Get 10+ nukers 54+, Mages, Druids, Wizards, heck even necros nukes and
life taps should stack.
2. Shammy, Enchanter,Bard, Druid to lower resists to the max. (Malo, Tash,
Occlusion, Ro together should take FR down so far that a lvl 54+ could nuke
with Fire based DD)
3. At least 2 Lvl 60ish tanks to take hits and taunt while rest of the ppl
nuke.
4. Bards go in caster groups to keep them from running(fear) Bards shoudl
twist Guardian + Elemental old school style, Send in nukers when Melee (even
slowed melee) has her down to 65% or so.
