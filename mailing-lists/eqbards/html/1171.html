It all depends on where you are. If you live around Qeynos go to
Splitpaw. Great exp, not many people to steal kills(but trains suck
therefore), and good money. If you live in Rivervale, either get a group
of 4-5 people around your level(you'd prolly want higher levels) and
either hunted in Beholder(gorge minos, mudites, and chasm minotaurs
depending on group level, and how many), or in Highpass, which, though
it doesn't have great money, is a great way to gain exp. I would say it
has great money, as the Polished Granite Tomahawks have gone for as much
as 300pp on Solusek Ro, but jerk high level people tend to farm the spot
around the spawn time(its not too random). If you live in Freeport
either hunt dervish Cutthroats, head to Guk, or go into the Oasis if its
not crowded(which is not often). If you live in Faydwer, try Unrest or
Crushbone, though they both have many trains and are very crowded.
