One place where I was able to use my Vox Horn and Kelin's Lute to good
(area) effect was in the Sol-B bats-bugs. Any of the rooms/places where you
can kill bats and bugs. I had a healer (cleric) in the group and the puller
would bring as many as he could. With 3 mobs, playing lute for Chords of
Disonance, Chords of Cessation, swap to horn for Denons, rinse repeat. By
the tine the first mob was dead, the second would be nearly down to half
health. By the time the 2nd one was dead, the third one would be way below
half health. You do need a healer but the efficiency of AE DoT songs shines
- and the dragon/god instruments really help.

On other occasions, I will use the Vox horn with Berserker's to add some
dramatic STR stats when ppl in the group are begging for str buffs (rogues,
etc.)
