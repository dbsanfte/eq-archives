If you are going to go to Kedge Keep, I highly, highly recommend
Phreekitty's guide. It's a mini book of info on this zone:

http://everquest.allakhazam.com/Phreekittys_Ultimate_Guide_to_Kedge_Keep.htm

And yes, I'd also agree, bard's Ayre should be for backup use only. Jig is
a necessity. Pathing is buggy, so be sure not to charm. Don't bother with
your fear song for the same reason.

You should be able to buy fish scales. There's merchants that sell fish
scales and batwings and other spell components. I sure wish I remembered
where they were though.
