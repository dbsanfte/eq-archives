Hi folks,

I was in unrest last night and had the unforutnate luck to be slaughtered
unrest style in the house. After hitting the bank and grabbing an extra
flute, I high tailed it into unrest to recovery my possessions. After
getting my corpse and pulling it to the wall I discovered a potentially
fatal bug for bards out there.

I had my mino horn equiped and was playing the invis song (as I often do
while looting my corpse) As I retrieved my items I picked up my original
mino horn from my corpse. At that precise moment, Shaurii's happened to
pulse and...

Shaurii's Sonorus Clouding stopped and said you must have a wind instrument
equpied.

What the???

I finished recovering my stuff and decided to test this extensively. Here
are my limited conclusions.

If you have Mino Horn A equiped and playing Shaurii's, then you pick up Mino
Horn B and place it on your pointer, then your song pulses, your song will
drop citing your lack of a wind instrument.

As of yet I have not tried any of this with actual flutes yet, but I was
able to replicate this bug over and over again.

So, the moral of the story is, if you are looting while invis in a dangerous
area, be careful about picking up that wind instrument.
