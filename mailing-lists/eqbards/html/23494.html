Subject: Velious faction imbalance (was Avoiding faction hits in PoG when pulling)


On a related note since we were talking about risk/reward in Velious:

I think the entire Velious faction/loot setup is all out of balance. Seems
to me that allying with the giants is far and away the best choice. The
giants give the most quests (not counting class armor quests which all three
factions have) and you have more places to hunt since you don't have to
worry about screwing up Coldain/dragon faction. The Coldain don't appear to
give any decent quests other than the Garadain series and the Phenocryst
Focus series (BTW I really like how those quest series are set up. Wish
there were a lot more like them in the game). If you want to maintain
dragon faction, your limited in the places you can go beyond Kael Drakkal.
Dragons appear to be the worst faction to ally with. Seems like all the
best items/hardest zones in Velious will ruin dragon faction. So if you
ally with them, you are really limiting your options on places you can hunt.
Not really certain how good the quests are that you can get from the
dragons.

Would like to hear from those of you who have explored a lot of Velious to
see what you think.

Galtin of E'ci
