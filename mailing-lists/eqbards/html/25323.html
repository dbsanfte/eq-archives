This may have been covered but the reasoning behind having the faster weapon
in your main hand is because the dmg bonus for STR is applied to main hand
only. So the more times the dmg bonus gets applied to your main hand
weapon, the more dmg you will do. 14/24 vs 11/23 I think I would do the
same thing and put the mace in main hand. 1dly vs 3dmg isnt worth the odds
you wont make your duel wield roll.
