They do indeed, though they aren't easy to find. They, along with combine
spears, and a few other interesting combine weapons, can be found at the
gypsy camp in the Lavastorm Mountains. I believe I paid 53 plat for one,
and I've found it more than worth it. The camp can be a little tricky to
find,
and the fire elementals in the area seem to have a thing against bards (or
maybe just me), so travel with a trustworthy party and someone who knows
the area.
