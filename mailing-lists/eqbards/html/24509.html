<P> I remember that tower well.  I went from 21
to about 26 or 27 there -- grouped  with a
ghoulbane weilding paladin, and enchanter and a
druid.  many, many close calls at lower levels
but we called it "the sweet spot" and pulled from
there to the edge of the lake because there were fewer
wandering mobs.  -- 28 and 29 I soloed the
entrance to the goblin mines (found that lament worked
much better than I expected), alternately raiding the
sarnak castle with groups...Lake of Ill Omen was one
of the very best times on my EQ career.<BR>
