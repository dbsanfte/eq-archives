I have a very complex set of hotkeys and can't live without them. I suspect
many people would find this boring, so feel free to delete. :)

I use the hotpad keys a lot, because I drive myself with the mouse, and want to
be able to move and turn while activating songs and various other buttons.

Pad 1 - the generic pad

1 - Forage
2 - Sense direction
3 - Hymn of Restoration
4 - Bellow
5 - Selo's Accelerando
6 - Anthem de Arms

Pad 2 - the hunting pad

1 - /g Incoming!
2 - Kellin's Lugubrious Lament
3 - /g Hunting!
4 - Bellow
5 - Selo's Accelerando
6 - Lullaby

Pad 3 - the miscellaneous crap pad

1 - Forage
2 - Sneak
3 - Hide
4 - Location
5 - Sit
6 - Camp

Pad 4 - the "shout things to my group" pad

1 - /g Run! We're going to die
/shout Train coming to the zone
2 - Get up! Hurry! Get up!
3 - 6 - Other random sayings

Pad 5 - the pure combat/juggling pad

1 - Anthem
2 - Melodic Binding
3 - Hymn of Restoration
4 - Bellow
5 - Selo's Accelerando
6 - Lullaby or Consonant Chains (depending on my mood)

Pad 6 - the inventory pad

1 - Main weapon
2 - Left hand weapon
3 - 5 - Inventory slots where my weapons/instruments go
6 - Sit (since usually I switch to my lute, start healing, then sit down, then I
switch to weapons, and stand up)
