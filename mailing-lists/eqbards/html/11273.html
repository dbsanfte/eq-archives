> 1) Which room in Mistmoore is the home of the gypsy lute dropping gypsies?

The ballroom, very center of the castle. There is a secret way in
I hear. Even level 50s can have it rough in there. Equate MM Castle
to a L39-L50 area. Don't be fooled, those monsters show no mercy.

> 2) Where are Choon and Froon (I've spent the vast majority of my time
> on the East coast, so I'm not too familiar with the Karanas)?
> About what level are they? Is it possible to pull one without the
> other?
>

West Karana, near the northwest corner of the zone (near north karana)
In a mountain area on a plateau near some L25-L30 ogres (very agro).
Avoid the ogres. Choon/Froon has large agro radii as well, by the
time you see both, you probably pulled both. Lamenting one lets you
get close enough to Lament the other or pull the other. They are
both L30 on the nose.

Harmonic.
