Saw Slyde's question about this on The Concert Hall boards. I don't know
about binding anywhere but the Coldain city for melee/hybrids. It wasn't too
hard to get to although we had a grateful druid show us after we came to try
and rez his corpse (too old though).
Buckle up for female bard directions, zonewall following in leiu of any good
landmarks: You need to cross the small island where the boat docked, cross
the larger island with the big tower (undead dungeon) on the side with the
smaller flat ice flows that connect it to a larger piece of land. I go in
this general direction, slightly to the right of the shoreline towards
inland from the water's edge. Look for the Very big dragon bone and ice
bridge and cross it to Eastern wastes. Here you can follow the water's edge
up/right to the zone wall corner and follow that. There's a steep pass to
the Great Divide. Inside this zone, follow the right zonewall again to the
stream. The Coldain City entrance is there on the wall where the stream
starts.
EQ Atlas already has a map of the Eastern Wastes:
