I'm pretty sure no one else's reason will be similar to mine ;o)~

Back in NWN, my character's name (my first online game, btw) was NOR Bard
(NOR for New OutRiders, and the name was simply Bard). I came upon the name
because the way the game was set up, there were very narrow paths to go from
one place to another. If you got into a fight on that path, sometimes folks
couldn't go around, and had to wait til you got done. To keep the gathering
masses occupied, I would sing naughty little limricks *eg*, ergo the name
Bard seemed appropriate when I joined NOR and needed to pick a more
RPG-worthy name (MR.Bill1711 just didn't cut it, also there was no bard
class in any online game at that time... all 2 of them heh!)

After being in NOR for a number of years, the name stuck with me like an
alter ego, so when NWN fell, and EQ finally started Beta, the name was a
natural choice (damned name filter made me pick Barrd though :o/ ). Because
of my naughty musical proclivities (I also wrote bawdy lil tunes for our
boards between NWN and EQ), folks just naturally assumed I would be a bard.

Suprisingly enough, I was VERY hesitant to be a bard as it was a complete
unknown to me (up to this point, all the games I had played were turn-based
combat, except UO, and I really didn't understand it's skill system... just
give me the old AD&D system any day :op ). Once I learned that I was the
ony magic-using class in Norrath that didn't have to worry about mana usage,
I knew this was the class for me ;o) Ever since that first burst of Selo's,
I have been hooked.
