Definitely have a couple pumice stones handy. You not only
want to get rid of bad effects on you, but you want to debuff
her at the start. Her mana is limited, yours is not. Having
the stones to cancel any spell effects essentially removes her
spellcasting ability as a factor, while she cannot do the same
to you.

A trick a ranger friend used on me was to quaff a vial of
vampire blood. He said it put his magic resist way over 100.
I only managed to get charm/fear/chain to stick on him like 3
times out of 30+ tries. Another thing to watch out for is for
her casting camo on herself to rest and med up. Keep Concord
handy so you can see invis. It may seem like a waste of a
slot at the beginning of the fight, but later near the
endgame, if she decides to try the camo move, you have got to
be ready for it or you'll be facing all those spells again.

and he still managed to get snare to stick about 30% of the
time. The spell is very good at getting through MR. Use a
pumice stone (or you can experiment with the "fixed" L22
song).

Another thing about bows (all ranged weapons really) is that
you need LOS. If you can put hills, people, etc. between you
and her when it fires, it will not hit you and she will have
to wait the firing delay again.

Make sure you keep running around so she's not facing you if

Just cancel the thorns with a pumice stone. The shock of
seeing all her pre-buffs drop should work in your favor too.
Cancel magic will not break mesmerize, so if you can get Pixie
Strike to stick, you can debuff her while she can't move (I
suspect the pumice will cancel Pixie Strike as well, but
usually it takes off 1-3 buffs, so it's still in your favor).

Tracking does not get messed up if the target is within

If it's a straight one-on-one fight: Use the tab key to
switch between targeting her and yourself (if, say, you need
to target yourself to cancel snare). If you use F1 or click
on yourself, you will lose her as a target, and can waste
precious time retargeting her. If you use tab, it remembers
your target and just toggles between you and her.

One final thing. Rangers can't bash or stun. You only need
to worry about missed notes. :)
