Guardian plate used to drop like crazy there. But one of the patches nerfed
the drop rate, either by accident or on purpose. At least on my server
(Fennin Ro), you can go there now and camp the pool room (has like 5
guardians and 4 sentries and some drakes) for days on end and only see a
couple pieces of plate. Starting to look like imbrued drop rates :( Bleah,
getting armor for my bard is just plain hard.

As for the kinds of fights, you'll need to be around 53 to pull singles, and
even then a few will be blue. If you want an idea about toughness, think
hill giant. They hit for around 90, and have so many hps it's scary. I was
with a 55 mage who could solo one if he helped his pet with a couple of
nukes. If there were two, I had to mez or charm because they'd tear his
poor pet to shreds.

If you take a group here, a balanced group of around level 45 will have it
pretty easy.
