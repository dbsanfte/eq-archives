I've been fighting alot in SS in the CY at the left closest corner. Our
tanks pull to there. I'll twist two songs and then throw up mana song. I do
a mana check and if mana is good Ill concentrate on buffs and dots. If mana
is low ill get in melee range and sing mana song while i hack and slash. I
do the same thing in the King room in Solb and the range is fine. Cantana of
soothing doesnt seem to regen as much mana as Chorus does. Of course I have
yet to test the range on Cantana of Replenishment so I dont know bout that
song. Point is situation will dictate and so will your hunting ground. There
is a use for every song in most situations.
