Given that you're only 25th, you're a bit limited in non-party or target
songs since pickclaws are so much higher level than you... you can help this
by raising your CHA (items, shaman / enchanter buffs), also if you can get
an enchanter in your group tashani'ing will help significantly.

Chant of Battle - Might want to use this instead of Anthem, especially if
anyone's using proc'ing weapons. Some will argue the slight speedup isn't
worth the extra dex chant gives ya.

Chords & Discord - Both are extremely useful especially when stacked and one
of them is played, but in HK I'm a bit warry of using them, I'm always
afraid i'll get a guard in the radius through a ceiling or something.

Binding - The -AC will be useful especially if your melee types are well
below the goblins as well. Otherwise Chain's preferable.

Chain - This song's wonderful. Especially in HK where runner control is
needed.
