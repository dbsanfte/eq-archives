My worst leveling period was 34 to 36 for some odd reason. My cleric mate
and I set death records and lost level records til some higher level friends
of ours set us down in Garton's tower in MM and force feed us exp til we got
out of that range. We all started going to Kedge Keep about then too, no one
was there so it was really cool to have bascially your own private dungeon.

V has alluded to as having the best exp modifier in the game. It better, the
loot completely sucks: just fish aned scales. Rare drops are exactly that in
KK. I do have the sharkskin drum and maybe my luck is bad but we pulled that
spawn routinely for most of the levels we spent in KK and we didn't get it
til things were starting to go green to us there.
The brakes really kick in on leveling above 50. It really goes slow. Right
now, we going to Howling Stones when we can find folks to go. That seems to
move the exp bar better than anything else we've tried.
