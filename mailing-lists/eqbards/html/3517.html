the ability ot bind themselves at SOME point... I find it shocking that you

to find someone who is able to perform the service and the /tell them a
polite IC request for aid (I usually ask if I might have a moment of thier
time and then describe myself as newly arrived and in need of thier aid to
reattune my soul to the area) most are more than willing to help. I have, I
believe, only had one or two turndowns with this method and those were
individuals who were already part of another group and on thier way to find
something to kill. In about 95% of the cases, the caster in question has
not asked a copper for thier help and some even refused the small donations
we have been able to offer. This has worked with all the classes capable of
the service, including druids, shamans and most especially clerics.
