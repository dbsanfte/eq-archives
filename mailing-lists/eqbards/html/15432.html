Another spot that I found decent is at the live zone in Upper Guk. There
are 2 spawns just around the corner, 3 on the next island (although 1 is
always green) and 3 more in the Nokta room to the left of the island coming
from the zone line. (There are 3-4 more down the right hand tunnel but a
broken LoS causes problems with breaking the spawn.) I got through 35, 36,
and 37 solo here Lamenting them and pulling to the zone line. At 37 I could
rotate through all 8 for about half a bubble of experience I think. Spawn
time on each of em is about 16-17 minits.
