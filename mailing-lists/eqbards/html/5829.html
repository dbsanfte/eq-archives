Here is a little trick in this same vain that some of the younger
SongCasters may not know..

When you memorize a song, you have that 'You have not recovered' phase
that you can't USE the newly memeorized song until it 'pops' into view.
Start and stop any other song to cut that process short, thus making it
available immediately. Really helps when you are in a crunch and NEED that
song you just learned!
