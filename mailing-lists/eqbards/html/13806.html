I think that in the situation you mentioned last night that your level may
have played a bigger factor in you taking most of the damage than bard hate
(assuming you were the lowest level player). I have seen numerous situations
where mobs picked on the lowest level character in the group, regardless of
what the others characters are doing to it. Doesn't happen all the time but
it does happen. In the situation last night, bard hate just made the matter
worse.

Three of the classes you were with can Stun mobs. John Kim posted the other
day of the importance of the Stun spell in reducing mob damage to the party.
Were they Stunning frequently? If not then that may also help explain why
you bore the brunt of the damage.
