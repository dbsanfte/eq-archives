I really like being a wood elf for RP reasons, but here are some things I
noticed:

- Humans have better bardic stats, Half Elves come in second (This depends
some on what attributes you want to pump up in character creation)
- Wood Elves can wear small or medium armour (Humans and Half Elves wear
medium only)
- Wood Elves and Half Elves can see in the dark
- Humans tend to have better *overall* factions (this could be moot
depending on your deity choice)
- Kelethin is a great area for a bard to get started because of the spider
silk quest. Humans in particular have a hard if not impossible time running
this quest
- Wood Elves end up with better forage skill
and...
- Wood Elves don't have to duck as much
- Wood Elves get stepped on a lot

I'm sure others can come up with some good feedback too.
