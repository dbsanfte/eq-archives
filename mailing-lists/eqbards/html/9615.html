Some things, especially the dread wolves and enraged dread wolves, do NOT
always despawn at the morning. This means that often there will be several
things left in the zone that are still very aggressive and dangerous.

Conclusion: Kith is definitely safer during the day, but do not consider it
totally safe. Do not casually walk by a wolf before being sure what it is.
Those enraged dread wolves hit me for 97 and cast nasty necro spells and are
red to me at level 34.

Also, walking along the very edge of the zone is safer, but there are a few
mobs who DO wander up on the side of the zone, quite far up the side wall.
All of the ones I have seen are wanderers though, so if you see a skeleton
in front of you along the side of the wall, wait a few minutes and he'll
leave.
