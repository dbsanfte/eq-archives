Here's a story from the bad new days:

When we were exploring lower guk on a night when it was so camped you could
wander around without invis to undead, we had poked around the undead tower
some. I was standing kinda near the assasain ladder saw this dumb caster
come running across the bridge with a GIBS train in tow. Hehe, training into
the tower instead of out of it? Like I said she was dumb. So, I figured
well, what the heck, and stepped in to stop that train.

I chained a GIBS, yelled for my group, hit auto-attack and started to
screetch. All but one GIBS fled back to their cave. My group arrived and we
hacked down the gibs. Then the other 4 GIBS came back and attacked us.
Chain, fear, assist on me! Dont chase them!!! Rinse, repeat. We nailed all
of the GIBS - hehe. And were lucky that the caster frogs to the side of the
bridge did not spawn during our fight.

Try that today and someone will prolly yell at you that they were "pulling"
those GIBS to the Lord room for something to kill between spawns <g> and
accuse you of KS'ing.
