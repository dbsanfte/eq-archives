> Mez's stack kind of funny. They stack based on duration, shorter mez
> overwriting longer mezs. This is by design as they did not want
> chanters to be able to cast a fast casting short mez then be able to
> overwrite it with a longer mez. The upshot of this is that our mez
> (being shorter then the shortest chanter mez) will always overwrite
> and block chanter mez's, regardless of level. One of the points in
> the class balance threads on whine play was to allow an exception to
> the mez stacking rules, do that chanter mez would always overwrite
> bard mez. No word on if they are going to do this.
