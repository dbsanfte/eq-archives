I know what it is, not where to find it.

I picked up a utility some fellow wrote which reads a data file in the EQ
directory, and will list the title of spells, the message you receive, and
what class can use the spell. The reason I even grabbed it was because a
friend was wondering if his druid spell was removed from the game, which
apparently it was (the spell was in the data file, however no class could
use it.) In case you're wondering, winged courier, it's in the list but
has a null value as to "who may use." There are other strange songs
like this, for GMs? It's a handy utility for getting a grasp of what
those high level songs do.

Anyway, the bard song for level 30 is entitled:

Drum roll, feet begin to move faster....
Adagio's Centrifugal Fuge.
Here are the messages you would receive from it: "You begin to spin," "xxx
begins to spin," "You stop spinning." Hehe sounds like the effect when
turning and getting stunned, and a great deal of fun.

Now then where O where could it be?? All I know is that I'm only level 12
now, and by the time I reach level 30 enough people will have probably found
it that it will start being sold in the shops. The powergamers have all the
fun, don't they? Least I knew before any of them, and now you do too.
