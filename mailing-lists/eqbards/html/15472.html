Here's something else, let's see if your experience agrees with mine: you have to
target the person to use locating lyric. When a person who is in your group dies
you see the empty health bar with the sad little puddle of purple at the left. You
can still select that person as part of your group at this point. Once they zone or
you zone, however, the bar goes grey and you can't select them. So if you are
fortunate to still be alive when your group mate died (hehe like that happens often
;P), and you don't have to zone, you may be able to find corpses easier this way. A
practical expample of this being useful: Kithicor when you are fighting at the
lake, you all run for the zone, and then manage to win the fight there, but one
person died somewhere on the run.

>usually you can tell from the surroundings exactly where the
