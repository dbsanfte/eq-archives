Resonant and Melodic are almost identical. It's just easier to go to PoG and
farm Resonant than it is to quest for Melodic in Thurgadin.

Highsun armor from Skyshrine was superior to the above in every way
(example: BP has 50 AC). It had a yellow guardian armor look. A couple
patches ago it was changed to Twilight armor with a dark gray guardian armor
look. Stats are same as before, they just changed the name and color.

Troubador armor from Kael is the highest AC armor for bards (BP has 54 AC).
It had a teal guardian armor look. A couple of patches ago the tint on the
armor was changed to burgundy, but the name and stats are the same to my
knowledge.
