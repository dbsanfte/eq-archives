as jhenders said before, if you get a pull of multiple mobs that would make
your AE damage songs effective, you are pretty much toast and will have to
get out of dodge anyway. multiple pulls of high level mobs means everyone
better concentrate on getting the same mob, one at a time, especially if an
enchanter is messing the others. i haven't used AE damage songs in a long
time, as in dungeons you don't want to pull more than you have, and like i
said you want to concentrate on one mob at a time. therefore my primary
role is party buffing of MR, STR, AGI, and mana, with weapon damage on one
mob as opposed to AE song damage. i am sure L48 and L2 song with lute will
deal a serious amount of AE damage, but its just not viable in high level
dungeon scenarios.
