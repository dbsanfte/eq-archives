Not really applicable to the lord room, but little known fact
is that if a runner gets away, it will not bring back green
mobs, even if the greens would normally attack you. I use
this, combined with Chain and invis to pull blue mobs past
hoards of greens all the time. The Chain is crucial though,
because you need a head start for the invis to work.
Otherwise you'll get attacked, canceling the invis, and the
greens will aggro on you.

You need to keep tabs on how your puller is pulling and
monitor your tracking list. Frogs you fear in the king room
only get about as far as the pool room, and you should've been
pulling everything up to there to being with. If your puller
keeps you informed of where he's pulling from, you should know
before you fear what extra mobs you're likely to pick up.

Also, fear is not just handy for emergencies. It can be
useful if your group just needs a short 10 second breather for
the healers to heal multiple people who are low.
