Comments

There's a 0.5 delay biasing for weapon delays. i.e. a 19
delay weapon is really 19.5 delay. This extra 0.05 seconds
per attack round is mostly negligible, but skews some of the
% speedup figures if you use the documented weapon delay
instead of the actual.

The % speedup remains the same regardless of the delay of the
weapon you're using. i.e. Verses gave roughly a 30% speedup
whether I was using my 35 delay fists, or a 19 delay dagger.

A FBSS or BoC + any song/spell speedup gives you a 75%
speedup. This may be a bug. Best way to take advantage of
this is via the Augmentation spell, since it replenishes
stamina. I am trying to test this with a haste sword to see
if those work the same.

If you have two haste song/spell effects, the faster
overrides.

Chain does not overwrite Shiftless Deeds. I tested this both
while continuously Chaining, and after letting Chain up. The
mob continued to attack at the speed of Shiftless Deeds. I
did not test this with Tepid or Languid.
