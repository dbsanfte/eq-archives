>C: Often when I play my songs my music sounds very strange. I was
>wondering if any others have experienced this. It definately sounds as if
>my Mandolin is out of tune, which would give credence to the usefullness of
>Lute Tuners.

Lute Tuners aren't for tuning the lute in that fashion; they are, in fact,
the knobs that hold the strings that you turn to tune a lute. So, they are
a lute part, to be used in the construction of a lute, not in the
enhancement of one.
