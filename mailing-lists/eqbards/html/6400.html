Hatar is a RARE spawn in Oasis.
He's about level 23 but of course he hits
way too hard and often.

Fine Silk Turbin.
For the fashion conscious, this is a great item
for your Head slot. AC 10 Save vs Fire +10 (I
think. Elyana can confirm this) and weighs
almost nothing. Monks love this item too. And
the best thing (at least for me, a human bard)
is that it looks like cloth.
