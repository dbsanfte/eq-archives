> Put 10 in Dex, 5 in Int, and the rest in Agi

The int only helps you with learning skills. Bards level
slowly so you'll have plenty of time learn skills. I'd say
concentrate on agility (bards are a mob magnet, so it helps to
avoid being hit). Put a little into dex (supposedly helps
being knocked out of a song), or chr (helps your charm-type
songs, better prices), or str (carry more stuff) as you see
fit. Sta is generally considered a waste.

The only rationale I see for increasing your int in a non-mage
type character is if you're planning to take up an expensive
trade skill.
