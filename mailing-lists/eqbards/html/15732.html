I upgraded my system to Win 2K over the weekend. I did a clean install of
Win2K onto a brand new harddrive (it is wierd having the OS on the H drive
hah). I installed EQ and patched up (I have a cable modem so this went very
fast). Then I copied over the contents of my old EQ folder to the new one,
just telling it no when it asked to overwrite existing files. It worked
fine. Also with Win 2k and a clean install I can weave 4 songs, if my
timing is absolutely perfect :) Was weaving Chords of Dissounce, Denon's
Discord, Niv's & Hymn last night vs LDCs & Lava Beetles.
