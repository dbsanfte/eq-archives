they are indeed on the warden, who hangs out in the jail cells. quick map
of jail:
O O
--------------------------------------------
| * ---> to mushrooms and shin lord
O| |---------
| ---------- |
| y | y | y |
| | y | |
| ---------- |
O| |----------
| <--- enter
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~-----------

legend = - and | are walls and the platform in the middle of the room
~ is the water
y are frogloks
* is the warden
O are troll prisioners in cells
the warden is sometimes not there, and will be replaced by a badass
froglok knight. he's been there for over 50% of the time for me, however.
notes on the warden: he can invis himself and _fight while invis_. you
NEED someone who can cast see invis if you are going to go down there. the
warden can also backstab for 150+dmg, so make sure that everyone is facing
him at all times.
the two frogloks on the platform will usually not come if you pull the 2
frogloks who are on ground level. the only time they have come down was
when the warden attacked at the same time, so be careful.
