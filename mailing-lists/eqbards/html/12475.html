Hail Fellow Bards,

I keep a blacklist as well. It's about the only way to protect yourself and
potentially get a chance at justice. When I was around L20, we set out to
camp the BBC for the warriors in the guild. This was quite a challenge for
us as most were inexperienced at dungeon tactics and the levels involved. So
we camped most of the day and along comes a guy who trains us all dead 4
times in a row so he can take the BBC. He promptly auctions it right there
in the zone while we are waiting for help to get back to our bodies. I
remembered his name til many months and levels later, he joins a group I and
one of my warrior friends are in in L Guk. I promptly disbanded and walked,
the warrior right behind me. Frantic casters /tell us and we explain: we
will never group with this person knowingly. He has no honor and can not be
trusted. Word gets around about people and that makes it harder and harder
for them to find groups. One of our guild officers maintains a website for
just this purpose, list of bad and good people as well.
