At L23 you can do a level in 2 hours by powerleveling. If you are
bumming around with similar level people who are gaining exp with
you then I don't consider that powerleveling, and most people would
call that partying. One bubble exp per hour only applies to levels
over L35. I've been truly powerleveling people in south karana, and
I took a L23 to L24 in a little over one hour. You just need to know
what you are doing and how to do it.
