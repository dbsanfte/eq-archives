A good majority of people find this very rude to do. Lots and lots
of people have to now avoid a huge section of NK or Rathe so the bard
can keep an HG from dying for 30+ minutes while it tries to find another.
In the meantime, this HG pounds to crap out of anyone near death it
finds, another group sits around waiting for an HG to spawn to kill, while
you have yours, another group tries to kill this one that is no_where_near
the bard (not accusing you, just the ones I have seen).

Try this: In Rathe, you can make HGs spawn by killing the basilisks and
skeletons and lizards. Kill as many of these as you can until you see
TWO HG pop. Then go charm the two. Don't grab one then wait for another.
