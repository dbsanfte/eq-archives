I was in Wandering Minstrels for a while. This is the
all bard guild on Bristlebane. And in my experience
it is great at lower lvls when you are learning how to
be a bard. (that was before I discovered this list
too).

It does have disadvantages. In the 2 or 3 months I
was in TWM there was one "guild activity" and it was
for lvl 25 and up.. I was 15 at the time. I ended up
leaving because I wanted to be in a guild with the
people I ran around with. (Interestingly enough one
other person left TWM shortly after I did to join my
guild and when it disbanded we are now in a third
guild together, and we often (lvl 7 to now lvl 34)
group together and sometimes with a third bard as
well)

That being said, most ppl have alternates and an all
bard guild isnt bad as long as you have guild
alliances or friends in other places.
