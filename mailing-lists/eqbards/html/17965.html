It's near the Alligator pits as I have heard them called.

Zone in to the CY and go up the later to the right as you enter. Follow around till you see a pool.
Using invis swim down and find the opening. This is the blue pool. Go around the corner to the Green
pool. SG is back in there. Has PH's of course.

The gators range from High blue to Red. SG is 35 I think. Key to this camp is at least 2 good Tanks,
a Chanter, and a healer. Druids can charm the Gators as well.

Rarely seen a list. Longest I have waited on the Rathe is about 30 minutes or so.
