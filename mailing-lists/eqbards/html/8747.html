No big secret as to what burned out lightstones are for. There is a practice
research item that allows finger wigglers to build up their research without
wasting rare research componets. And one of the practice runes requires a
Burned out lightstone. So most likely he was handing the person with the
burned out lightstone the practice research book with all the components in
it (except for the BO lightstone). The person with the lightstone places it
in the container and hits combine(no research skill it needed for this part)
then hands the rune and container back to buyer. :)
