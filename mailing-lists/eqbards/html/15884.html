One word - HERMIE (the hermit in South Karana). You can find him at loc
neg5465 neg2718 (approx). He's inside the house within the brick wall
surrounding it. You won't be able to get inside the house to kill him but
DDD, CoD (any ae song) will pull him out. Or, you can sit by the fireplace
and say, "I will kill you" and he'll come out after you. I preferred to pull
him out with the song and back way off so he can't hit you thru the wall. He
does cast, but if you are weaving DDD, CoD with hymn, it should be no
problem. Soon he pops out and hits ya and you just keep on singing. The only
thing about him, though, is he is such good exp that he is camped a LOT.
But, keeping that in mind, you can hunt the whole zone while you wait for
hermie to get free. Actually, South Karana is a great place for experience -
you can hunt aviaks, and if you get in a good group experience is great
there as well. Oh yeah, hermie is great exp from your level (you said 22?)
until you hit 28.
