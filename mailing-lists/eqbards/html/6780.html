> Around 8:00 pm est I was told by a Cleric named Archurian how to make
> raw-silk armor. This is the first person I have heard of to make the armor
> so I give credit to him =). Here is how to do it. You will need a large
> sewing kit and a skill lvl of 20 (hehe this is real easy stuff to make)
> and some spider silk. First you take spider silk (not spiderling silk you
> need to get this silk from there full grown brothers). Now you take 2
> spider silks and put it in your large sewing kit. Hit combine you will get
> a silk swatch (this was trivial to me at skill lvl 25) now that you have
> the silk swatch you take that and put it the large sewing kit with the
> pattern of your choice. Hit combine and whalla. You get a raw-silk item =)
> easy right? Tunic/Robes and pants take 2 swatches to make all others take
> 1. The items can be worn by the pure magic classes and monks. due to there
> low wt. they are good for both groups. They have one ac over cloth so they
> are really only good for low to mid lvl. The robe that you make looks like
> a light brown (like the thurm robe). None of the garments have any magic
> stats on them. I have been charging about a plat for each garment and 2pp
> for robe and pants.. I have been selling everything I make to hehe. Due to
> the low lvl of skill needed to make the armor I don't think you can charge
> much more then that. I would encourage each of you to tell as many people
> as you can on how to make silk. I think that if we have a free flow of
> information we will quickly be able to figure out what Verant made with
> this patch =).
