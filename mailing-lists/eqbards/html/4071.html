15 is an awkward level. You can always hunt Wisps and zombies on wisp
island, (between eurdin and Qeynos) good money but minimal exp at 15. I
financed my set of banded and my dirk that way. Gnoll Reavers south of the
bridge in East Karana have good loot but the Velcro wolves make this a bit
dangerous. Take a group of three or four depending on level. On the other side
of the world you might try the Aqua goblins in Dagor's Cauldron, they have
pretty good loot, just avoid the Lords. There is always crushbone and unrest
but these areas are very crowded.
