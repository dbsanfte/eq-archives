Subject: RE: Runed Bone Fork (call for a spoiler)


Found out yesterday that it is the Squire that drops it.

So I am off all this week for vacation and I am making an attempt to get
one. I get into the zone not having a clue as to where this squire is so I
ask around and eventually someone helps me out. They lead me to the spot and
point it out by attacking the gaz knight that was in the spot. I thank them,
they leave, and I wait...

Next spawn is another gaz knight. I drop it and wait some more...

Next spawn is the squire. I drop it and get the fork! woo hoo!

Thinking this to be rather easy I decide to give my fork to my level 50
bard friend and head back down to get another one for me. This is when I
learn that the fork is a super rare drop on the squire. I spend two hours on
the spawn point without even seeing a squire. I hate it when I get super
lucky on a spawn drop and then make a decision assuming that the item is
easy to get...

Off to try and get another fork now...

Good Journey,

Sineras Silverlyre, The Divine Alliance (Cazic-Thule)
