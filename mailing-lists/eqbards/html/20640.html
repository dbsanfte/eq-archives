With my current guild I am in the Enchanter group on most plane raids now.
My usually line up is focused on keeping the 2 chanters in my group alive.
On pulls I weave VoV + Nillipus + Purifying Rhythms w/drum. This adds a
nice chunk of AC to the chanters, tends to tuant mobs off them at times, and
gives them some protection from the nastier effects on the planes. After
they have locked down the pull I usually do VoV + Clarity. As keeping the 2
chanters full of mana is more important (for the mezzing group) then
anything else. The only time I get in and melee is on spite golems, since
there is usually a huge crowd around the current target.

One additional thing I have been using lately is Song of Twilight. This has
a nice effect of pulling the mobs toward you. Great for when the silly
melee'ers have pushed the mob up on the wall.
