There are TWO factors:

1. party member level difference - this get wider as you go up in level
- under 15 - seems to max at 4 or so; at level 33 - I know for sure that it
is greater than 17.
2. mob level - mobs can be TOO RED (too high level) for you to get XP
from even though your other party members get xp.

The more stuff you can kill that has xp for you and the faster you can do it
will gain you level in the shortest period of time. You need to optimize
all of those factors:

# of ded mobs
xp of mob
amount of down time
time between kills

Sometimes a steady diet of blues will yield more xp than a sparse diet of
reds. Now a steady diet of reds and no down time...
