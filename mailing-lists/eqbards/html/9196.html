> Song #8, Keliens Lubrous Lament (sp) is a WONDERFUL addition to your
> bardsong list. If you want to wow your friends, try this:
>
> 1. Find 2-5 mobs that you want to fight, but don't want to fight all at
> once. (ie. if you attack one, they all come running)
>
> 2. 'Lament' (Make em sad) the ones you DON'T want to fight.
>
> 3. Attack one of the ones you DO want to fight, pull it back a ways (to
> get out of the aggro range of his buddies)

Actually, I would advise lamenting them all, starting from the
lowest /con mob first. Then pulling the one you want.

What this does is decrease the chance that lament will fail
causing a large portion of the camp to attack you. Lamenting
the lowest level ones first has a higher chance of succeeding.
When you lament the harder ones, if you fail then at least
easier ones will not come. If you succeed, well, then you can
pull which ever one you want.

Of course my charisma is 140 so it almost never fails. Your
mileage may vary with a lower charisma.
