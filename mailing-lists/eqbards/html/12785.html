I travel on business way too much, so I only get to play weekends and
holidays. I got a few extra days between Xmas and New Years, here's how
they went:

Wednesday - Spent a lot of time in Oasis killing specters, giants and the
occasional DW goblin. Specters were camped, giants spawn slowly, not
much exp. Spent a couple of hours with a tired and overeager guild group
in Paw, got run out of zone three times, not much exp. (Those snakes are
cool, though.) Net 1/2 bubble of 35.

Thursday - Spent 8 hours at the top of 4c pyramid in Cazic. Great group,
no deaths, last two bubbles of 35, sliver of 36.

Friday - Off.

Saturday - Spent 8 hours at the top of 4c pyramid again, with a
completely different group. Great group, no deaths, got 4 plus bubbles of
36, taking me to a sliver under 37.

Sunday - Most guild members who are on are in Planes, spent an hour
apiece in each of three dungeons, /auctioning for a group (no responses),
/oocing if rooms were taken (they all were), /telling the few people I
knew if they had space (they didn't, and wouldn't for hours). Spent four
hours running around to all the places where I thinnk I can solo
something, all camped up the wazoo. Net: 7 hours, killed three blue
things solo. Still a sliver under 37. How do I avoid another one of
these days? Or is this the price I pay for having a job which didn't
permit me to powerlevel my way to 50? Am I not patient enough? Am I
supposed to pick a dungeon and sit on my heinie for four hours until
something opens up? Is this fun?
