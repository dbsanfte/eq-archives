Big time. Currently - if you have some "extra" characters you don't want -
you'd have to sell your entire account. If there's characters on that
account you want to keep you're SOL.

Now you can simply buy a new account - transfer the char over - and sell it
off singly.

If you have a two accounts anyway - and one is a PL'er character like a
Druid - you can create it on your second account - and not even bother to
buy the third account until you have a buyer. If you do this over time (ie
don't powerlevel quickly - but over a couple of months) - you'll save
$10/month by PL'ing it on an existing account.
