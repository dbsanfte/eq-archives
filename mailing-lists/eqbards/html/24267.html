If you're doing dreadlands to solo try pullin only ragers, petrified birdied
(these i found were better) and brutes... spiders can be good too but a real
pain in the butt. Try the spider webs near FV zone as there's lotsa
widows/recluse there. As well as roaming petrified birds there with other
stuff. Sadly, I've found that in the wee hours of the morning dreadlands
and karnors have been the easiest spots to find a group/solo. Which ever was
my mood that morning. I know there are better spots but I don't like
wasting my time running around/gating in order to get to these spots and
seeing that they're camped. One of these spots being the bloodgills in lake
of ill omen. They're great xp and you can tank them and go toe to toe with
them. I finished my level 49 on them. Have about 6 ancient coins sitting in
the bank from them.
