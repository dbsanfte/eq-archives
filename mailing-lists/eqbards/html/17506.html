Subject: Don't try this at home...


A rather quiet weekend -all-in-all- except on Sunday early afternoon
cruising through North Karana, I spot Grimfeather and decide to try out the
newly improved bard DD DoT (Chant Series) songs. For the non-bards reading
this, Verant fixed 3 of our songs so that their effects are enhanced when
you use an instrument - this is the original design - just took them 11
months since release to get to it. The instrument used to enhance these
songs is percussion which is also used to help you run faster.

I got close enough to GrimFeather to effect him with a song and started in.
Chant, run away a bit, different chant, run more, rinse, repeat. Turns out
that GrimFeather was no problem at all using this technique. He damaged me
for maybe 200 in the whole fight and that is because I was sloppy and let
him get too close. And it was not an ungodly long battle either.

Heh, I my euphoria to celebrate this kill, I decided to climb the wizard
spires. Hmm, I sure seem to be climbing up here pretty darn fast. Wow,
going lots faster now that its not so steep at the top. Hmm, I better slow
down before I....... whoops! OMG I am falling! I wonder if.... LOADING,
Please Wait.

Lucky for me, a good friend, who is a cleric, was in North Karana and ressed
me back. He had a good laugh too.
