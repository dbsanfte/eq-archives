I was in a guild group last week we found and killed the Chief. I just hope

SPOILER SPACE

























SPOILER SPACE

At the back of the dungeon behind the Dining Room there is a large cave with
several side caves and awnings like in the market in Freeport. One of the
side caves contains a lot of boxes and barrels, just near the box room is a
ramp leading up to a higher room overlooking the main room.

The chief spawns here but seems fairly rare.
If you see bodyguards spawning you are in the right place. This whole area
is marked as the store room on some maps.

He spawned once for us in 4 hours, and dropped a locket of escape - 1 charge
of Bind Affinity.

Almost everything here was green to me at level 43 but there are a heck of a
lot of spawns and we found we had to keep the whole area clear, we could not
just fight in the chief room.
