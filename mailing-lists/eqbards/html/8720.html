One thing I soloed at 25th were the Crag Spiders in East Karanas. As with
most tough mobs that don't drop cash, they are a good source of experience.
I could probably make a bubble of experience by soloing about 12 or so at
level 25. They do routinely drop three or four spider silks, however, and I
understand that if you carry a sewing kit with you, you can reduce them to
silk swatches which are stackable. I have not tried this however, although
I'd like to. Supposedly two spider silks make one stackable silk swatch.
Watch out of Hill Giants on the hill where they are found (although the
giants may be hunted to extinction right now).
