Greetings!

We went to Kaesora to explore and try for the coin. Me 51, 'nother Bard 51,
third bard 50 and wizard 50. I had a map - get one. We zoned in and iirc
took the left passage - then killed some bugs - then took a right (check
map) and headed for the library on a whim. There were several bugs to kill
along the way and some spec's too. Nothing to break a sweat for our levels
although we lamented and tried to pull singles as best we could. We pulled
to clear the outside of the library then moved up closer.

On the porch, we agro'd something and as we fought we also agro's the '??
librarian' and killed 'em both. The something turned out to be the froog
that dropped the coin for the quest. Something else there dropped a book
(?? iirc) that had good int +5 or +6 and that made our wiz happy. Then we
went in the library and looked around. Perhaps killed something else -
can't remember. We did happen to go up stairs and looked around the
balcony. On the ground, we found something for one of the medallion
quests - "piece of a medallion" or something. BTW - we were the only ppl in
the zone - sad but true - and it looked to be a very cool zone for ppl who
can get exp at this level. You'd want a good party, some anti-poison
songs/spells but it looked to be good.

We went outside and started to look around. Then we got our obligatory
over-pull. About 6 things agro'd and we were swarmed by greenies. Evac was
no problem as we got what we wanted and it was time to go anyhow. Next
time, we wil be heading to Dalnir's for the Royal Crown. Then Suladan will
have all 6 coins. Kaldivar needs Kasora and Dalnir. I have Selos Drums so
its not as high a priority for me.

So, I dont know if the froog that drops the coin in Kaesora roams the whole
dungeon or he is always found in the library area - but that's where we
found him.

Sylly
