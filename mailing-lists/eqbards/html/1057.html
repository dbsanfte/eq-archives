Good advice, but if you're trying to save money for armor or
weapons you need NOW, you may regret it. I've been doing a
lot of travel lately (have actually lost one bubble in
experience for the weekend due to intermittent deaths), and

have to wait long for the boats, you should be able to make it
in just over an hour. I've just decided to start adventuring
in the area that holds my next song. I get to see more of the
world that way (elephants and centaurs and mermaids, oh my).

Also, some of the songs are just not worth the trip, and I've
deferred those until I happen to be in the area (the Charisma
and Identify songs in particular).
