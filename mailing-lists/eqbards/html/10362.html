That's wrong for Hymn. This is the best equation I could come up with.

With Lute (why sing it without?) it's 4hp +2 for every 6 levels of exp. So
at 6th level it's doing 6 hp per pulse and 12th level it's 8 hp per pulse
(sitting). The weird part is that at 24th level I started doing 13 hp per
pulse and 11 sitting down (the difference used to be just one between
sitting and standing) so I that seems to blow the formula but that's the one
I always heard.

More simply put, Hymn starts at 6 per pulse at 6th level (but you'd get two
from just sitting without the song) and it improves 2 hp per pulse every six
levels after. This is not dependent on string skill level because I hear it
keeps going up even after we've maxed string.
