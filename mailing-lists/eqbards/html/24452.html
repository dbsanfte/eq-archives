As far as I know, any mobs over level 50 have the *potential* to Summon you.
That doesn't mean to say that all mobs over 51 summon, but that's the level
the tables turn.

The first time I was summoned was in Permafrost doing Ice Giants. As an
indoor dungeon, Selo's is useless anyway, but it took me three attempts to
get away from the Ice Giant Priest (or was it the Magi?)

I'd run, he'd summon and bash me a bit. I'd run again, get as far as the
portcullis and *shwoop*, summoned again. I don't know how I managed to get
away that third time, but my heart was in my mouth.

I don't know if Summoning requires mana or if it's an innate ability the
mobs have. More experienced players will be able to tell you.
