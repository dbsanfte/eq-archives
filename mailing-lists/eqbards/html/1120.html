Look for the Bardic Voices series by Mercedes Lackey for spellsinging bards
in a fantasy medieval setting.

_Bard_, by MORGAN Llywelyn, is a historical novel about the coming of the
Irish to Ireland, with a bard as the central character.

For spellsinging more than bards, try the Spellsong Cycle series by L.E.
Modesitt.
