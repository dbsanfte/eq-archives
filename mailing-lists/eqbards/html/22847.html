Heya, Minkin...

yeah, there's a mini-dungeon in thurgadim. mobs are approximately 28-31(?),
as they con blue to yellow, and i've spotted one red there as well. it's a
maze, of sorts, the left hand side loops back on itself, but not very
complicated. There are a few dead ends, and only a few mobs down that way.
The right side leads to a pool, beyond which lie some 'mine shafts', with
drops below, a few mobs spawn down that way, but i have yet to explore it
deeply, since it's a bit tough to fight those yellows in a cramped space
solo. (loot stinks, too...bats and rats, drop newbie stuff, but the spiders
drop 'crystaline silk' or some such...magic item, stackable...sells for a
gold or so, if i remember right. Probably some new sewing item, if I had to
guess (haven't seen it listed anywhere, but i don't do tailoring alot)

I think that those tunnels may/probably lead to the area that you fall into
if you drop off the bridge on the way into Thurgadim. Not sure, though...At
31, i'll prolly go a bit deeper, since i'll have levitate, and won't drop
into some of the holes in the passageways.
