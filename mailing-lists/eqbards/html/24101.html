I think this statement might be just a little short-sighted. Maybe
you meant to say "I have never used concord once; it is useless to
me" or "I find concord to be a very limited song"?

I think many songs may require "non-standard" situations before they
become of value. Let me tell you a tale of what happened this
weekend. My guild was preparing to raid the Char nobility, and while
people were on their way, some of us started hunting the zone out.
One one pull, our monk got "zapped off the bridge" by a caster, and
said he was stuck on a ledge. I decided to use another, often
worthless, song -- Solidarity of Vision. I targeted the monk, checked
out where he was located, moved to the other MOBs in the area to see
exact what the situarion was, directed our warrior as to what MOBs
needed pulling, waited for the others to leave, then went in and used
levitate to get the monk out. There may have been other ways to
handle the situation, but I found a pretty simple solution in
a "worthless song".

Anyway, I agree that this song is of very "limited" use, but I would
rather say I have thought of no good use for it instead of "it is
useless".
