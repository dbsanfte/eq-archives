There is a sample format on the page, please try and follow it, employing
strong writing skills. Nothing wrong with biffisms (well, yes there is, I
HATE them -- ur not mad r u d00dz? k3wl.), but it is generally easier to
persuad someone if your presentation is lucid and well structured. Also,
please bear in mind that, with 2 exceptions, none of our songs are as
powerful as a comperable casted verison and I would not anticipate any new
song to deviate. As well, our songs are generally party in nature or area
of effect and fall in the broad categories of buffs, debuffs, and travel.
We have three ulility songs, perhaps thats an area of exploration. As
always, try and consider the implications of the your proposed
implimentation with respect to stacking, twisting combinations and class
balance.
