Subject: Begging again


I hit 40 begging skill over the weekend and the difference is
like night and day. Whereas with a skill of 39 I'd have to
beg 5 or 10 times for each success (targeting amiable paladin
guards in North Freeport), at 40 skill I'm succeeding more
than half the time. The success rate still stinks with the
dubious militia guards in West Freeport though.

I still get 1 cp per successful beg. I've had one paladin
give me 1 sp, but that's it. All my other successes have
yielded only 1 cp. My guildmates are complaining that I'm
giving the guild a bad reputation by begging around the bank
so much. :-)

I estimate I've successfully begged close to two hundred times
(I dumped something like 35 practice points into the skill),
and the paladins are still amiable to me. I once begged from
one paladin nonstop for half an hour, and he never got upset
or attacked me. The dubious milita has not attacked me
either. I did beg from the dubious S. Ro dark elf merchants
and they got upset and attacked me. Fortunately, it was a
drive-by beg (target them from a distance, run by at full
speed with Accelerando and a drum, and hit beg just as I pass)
so they didn't even hit me.

"How DARE you beg from me!" :-)
