Another update for you speed freaks:

Having run past GrimFeather a few days ago and made it safely to zone, I got
cocky. Last night I was killing time waiting for a friend and picked GF up
on track again. I decided I'd try to kite him (level 36, MM drums, using
Fufil's because the range is sooooo much better than Bellow) and got a nasty
surprise. Yes, I am faster than him. No, I am not fast enough to kite him
- even very shallow turns brought me into his range (which is a lot farther
than other griffs - more like a giant). He hits *at least* as hard as HGs,
and seems to double attack a lot more. Well, I ended up in a crumpled blue
heap on a hillside in NK, and learned a little humility. = ) I have rather
a lot of experience kiting (made most of my experience from 15 to 21 that
way), and I must admit that this is something toward the top of my "must do
someday" list - gotta prove to myself I can do it. On the other hand, I'm
not suicidal, and I'll wait until 40+ and maybe upgrade to Selo's Drum
first... <drool>
