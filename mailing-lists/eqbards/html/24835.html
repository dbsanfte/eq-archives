Nope, I'm afraid dual wield doesn't enable you to use a weap and an
instrument at the same time. I'm level 50 and still haven't maxed out my
hand to hand, it's pretty pathetic. I'm sure other bards have, however.

Song weaving should be done at level 2. At that point you should be weaving
Chant of Battle with Chords of Dissonance.

At level 12, in a group, you could be weaving Anthem de Arms, Chords of
Dissonance and Hymn of Restoration (depending on your situation). It's been
a long time since I was level 12, however, perhaps someone else has better
advice as far as song selection. Consider Bellow as bard's kick. Very
useful, especially if you're solo, but if you're in a group just weigh
whether the few extra points of damage is more useful than buffing your
entire party.

I'm the last person to recommend any particular stats and don't really have
a strong opinion about 'em. I would think that CHA affects your ability to
Lament creatures. CHA becomes much more important when you gain the ability
to Pixie Strike or Charm creatures. I've valued STR, but only because I'm
lugging half my house with me due to working on tradeskills of some sort,
so while not overly important to the usual bard, it's important to me. :-)
