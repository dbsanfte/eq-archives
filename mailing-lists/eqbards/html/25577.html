A level 11 bard asks for some hunting advice, and all you guys (except

Sorry, I haven't chimed in for a while, but I've been busy playing a druid
on the Firiona Vie (roleplay) server. So since I've had to play a character
from scratch with no twinked equipment, I'll be happy to give some advice.

If you want to stick to the Qeynos area, then I recommend a few places.
Blackburrow is a good place to learn about grouping in a dungeon as well as
about trains. Everfrost (which is beyond Blackburrow) is a good place as
well, if you go out towards the tundra and group with a ranger. West Karana
is better hunting for you at your level (various lions and bears) I would
think North Karana would be very challenging and it's far away from the
city, but if you're having fun there, more power to you. When you're high
enough, hunting the rabid wolves in Qeynos Hills (or if with a friend, go
for rabid grizzlies even better!) and turning in their pelts is decent exp
and good faction.

I actually prefered to hunt orcs in West Commons by Freeport. Of course, in
Firiona Vie server, there's no auction spam. Northern Ro is also an
excellent place to hunt. I actually think this is my best recommendation,
as when you're done in Northern Ro, you can head to Oasis and hunt crocs.
