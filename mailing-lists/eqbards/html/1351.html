The main reason I don't advocate increasing Str is based on some Monk
research that seems to confirm that increased weight regardless of str
adversely affects the dodge skill to a great degree. therefore wearing the
best lightest armor may help you totally avoid a blow that would otherwise
hit you for reduced damage in heavier armor.
