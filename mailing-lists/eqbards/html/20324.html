One of my friends with bind sight came up with a great way to run if
there are only two of you. He leads, I run second, and he binds sight.
So, he knows exactly how far I am behind him, and exactly how to turn so
as to not rub me off on a tree or un-friendly piece of landscape. I
never get far enough behind him that he cannot see to steer himself, too.
We did a *long* run the other day, and even though I was lagging a
little, we did not het messed up once. I have a feeling this particular
method is not for the weakhearted, though..."what cliff?" heh
