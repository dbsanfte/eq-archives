I think each item is presented to the NPC, one at a time, until
all items are accounted for. If you only give the NPC one item
it will give its, 'Oops' message. It is definitely a bug, a bug
in the NPC item parser. Each time the NPC sees the next item, it
gives it's little Oops message. At the very end, it then looks
at the collective group of items to determine correctness.

In the Pit of Doom in Halas, you can turn in 3 gnoll teeth for
a faction adjust and coin. Those items are identical, yet she
still says... Aren't you forgetting something? Each time, until
the last tooth triggers the correct amount switch.

Harmonic, doesn't believe in half measures!
