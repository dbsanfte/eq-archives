Haha... I did this for a while in Unrest along the wall between the back
door and the porch.

You can see a strange dungeon back there... though if you walk up the stairs
and look left (where you are looking at the other side of the wall you're
peeking through) it is just the normal first floor of the house. I have no
idea where this part of the dungeon is, though while peeking through the
wall you can see a bookshelf there... presumably the entrance to this place.
Though I've been through down to the basement down there, I'm still not sure
where it is... I think maybe it is the hag pit by where the tentacle terrors
are, by the dwarven ghost.

Anyway, be careful while doing this though... the hags WILL see you and
blast you through the walls. (Often, if you're camped at the back of the
house you'll see their spells burst out of the walls - they really are in
the basement though, not the first floor where you'd think.)
