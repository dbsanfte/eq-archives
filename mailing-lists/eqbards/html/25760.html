your problem will solve in some time when you get better equip. then you
will have more STR and weight reducing bags so its no problem to carry
around many parts of armor.
personally, i always carry many parts with me, and switch depending on game
situation. when i know i need AC i wear the SS helm, when i am CC and have
to mezz/charm i wear the kobold jesters crown. same with rings
(stats/resists/ac) bracer (resists/ac/stats) and boots (SS for effect,
imbrued for stats and resists).
in general, i would suggest going for stats, because you will mostly not be
beaten on. and if you get hit, the ac will not matter that much. so wear the
imbrued helm for good CHA and leave the SS helm in bank. you also have less
weight then.
