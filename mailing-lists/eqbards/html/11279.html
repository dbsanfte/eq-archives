No, not dumb at all. Casters are the last ones who should get HP buffs.
Casters should do their best to avoid taking damage. Let the tanks tank.
If the group is doing good, the monsters will never lay a hand on the
caster.

Symbol takes a reagent. The cost is 5gp up to 12pp for the higher level
buffs. Buffing casters, who shouldn't be taking damage, is a serious drain
on a Cleric's resources.

At higher levels with Complete Heal, having a Symbol on a tank is even more
effective.

The only thing dumb is saying always/never. If you are trying to break a
spawn, or going through a dangerous area, then giving all members a Symbol
is a good idea. Having a safety margin "just in case" is fine if you got
the mana and gems to spare.
