Subject: More Charismat Carrilon data


Last night I did a little experimenting with the level 11 song.

Changes have definately occurred.

Here is what I have noticed:

- Singing the song at an NPC who is less than "amiable" to you will make them
look upon you amiably.

- Singing the song at an NPC who "scowls at you ready to attack" doesn't seem to
do anything.

- When you change one NPC to Amiable, all OTHERS of that type seem to change as
well. So if you change a dwarf merchant who was apprehensive toward you, all
others in the same zone who were apprehensive seem to change as well. (This
seems like a bug).

- These changes DO affect prices, provided that the NPC was not amiable to you
in the first place. Using the song on a dwarven smith who was originally
"apprehensive" changed the price of a pair of chain leggings from 47 plat to 42
plat. Using the song on a wood elf merchant who was "amiable" to me made no
difference.

Has anyone else done any experimenting?
