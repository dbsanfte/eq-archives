I don't think all IGs are level 50. I've been on several trips to perma and
fought the IGs past the king room, and found some of them to be quite
charmable.

There is a trick to it though...

IGs (and most uber mobs I think) have some additional protection that normal
mobs don't. In order to cast spells on them (bard songs included) you need
to be within melee range. This came about a long time ago because people
were abusing geometry by pulling Vox till she got stuck. Once stuck, the
casters would stand back and nuke with little fear of retribution. Shortly
thereafter, the range to cast on mobs of that nature was reduced
drastically. On my last IG raid, our casters were experiencing 90% resists
until I alerted them to this knowledge, once they moved in closer to cast
resists dropped to less than 15%.
