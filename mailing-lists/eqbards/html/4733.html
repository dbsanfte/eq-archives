Fighting same stuff as you - with a Pallidin fiend - we got some multi-mob
pulls and I used Lullaby to put the un-invited guests to sleep while we
whacked down the reaver. Then used heal to heal up some before dealing with
the 2 or 3 extra wardog nippers. So, heal in battle is not 'useless' - it
helps.

Get a med qual bear skin and 15gp (maybe more - better take 25 or 30 gp just
to be sure) find Chanda Miller in the Qeynos Hills and tell her 'baobob sent
me' and she will talk to you about how she can make you some bear skin
'clothes' - have her make you some boots. The stats are good - very close
to banded and they are not heavy. Also - you can sell them in far away
places to casters who are looking for ac. I sold a set this weekend for
15pp - some guy was auctioning for boots and I sold him mine.
