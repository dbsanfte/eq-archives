Subject: Mistress of Scorn - Spoiler!


Hi all! Last night I went up to Hate for the first time in months
(took a break). Coincidentally, this was the first time for our
alliance since the upgrade. I had read a little about the new boss
mobs that now drop the old Innoruuk loot and noticed a Mistress of
Scorn on track. Track by the way was jam packed due to so many new
mobs. Anyway, she was one of the first boss mobs that we took down
and she dropped Dawnchaser, Imbrued Gloves, and Imbrued Boots. With
the mistress in the mix, bard drops were excellent compaired to
previous trips. On top of those three items, Imbrued helm, Martune
Rapier, and Six Note Blade all dropped. Bringing the total that I
saw up to 6 drops instead of the usual 1-3 that I would see
previously. There were also 2 scorns left on track when I camped, so
I don't know what else may have dropped. But for those of you that
haven't gotten all of your Hate gear, this upgrade makes things a lot
easier! I got 4 things that I needed and an extra set of gloves last
night! Let the Martune rot, I hate those things :p

Oh, and one more spoiler about the second floor.... we got agro'd on
the second floor a lot. The nice thing is that the mobs won't come
downstairs; bad thing is that they will HT through the floor.

Good luck hunting!
