Subject: Another possible suggestion for the GZ letter


On the EQlizer website, there is a long letter written by someone that lists
a lot of recommended changes to EQ as a whole. A few of the suggestions
were bard related. Some of the issues the guy suggested are already covered
in the letter to GZ that Elwyn has written. One suggestion wasn't and bears
consideration for inclusion.

The writer suggested making the radius of Selos, Invis song, and Underwater
breathing song zone-wide versus a short radius centered around the bard.
The reason being that it is potentially very dangerous for the bards' group
members if they move outside the radius of the song, especially the levitate
song. I don't think asking that Verant to change these songs to affect the
entire zone has much chance of ever succedding. However, asking them to
increase the radius of levitate and underwater breathing is definately
something that would make life easier on our group members and therefore
have a higher chance of getting improved.

Don't think this would be a class balance issue because it does absolutely
nothing to improve the combat/survivability capabilities of the bard.

Comments?
