Probably very little. Certainly less than whatever a Lambent stone sells
for. They are useless to anyone except bards and I dont think th emerchants
will pay very much if anything for them. You just have to find folks who
have the coins - or go kill the mobs yourself.

To complete the quest, you do need to give a Star of Odus to the banker guy.
It sells for about 150pp in Erudin. he gives you the no drop container that
you combine the coins in and return to him for the Nostrolo tambourine. I
only need to get the Royal Crown coin from Dalnirs.

Been to Kaesora 2 times - got that coin both times. The failed raider
should show up with 2 hours pretty easily. We got some other trinkets
clearing our way into & around the library: war/pal/shd 2hs, rogue dagger,
truesilver helm & bracer, int+ mask - good stuff for the level of the
dungeon. We were all too old for it (except our 40 clr who saw only a
couple greens.) Even so, you need to be careful as you can get a swarm of
greenies with a bad pull or accidental agro. We had a evac person both
times and had to do in combat evac once.
