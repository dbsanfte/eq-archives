Medium bronze is dropped in Perma by elite goblin something or others, the
spot can be pretty tough considering what lingers around the area. Ice
giant diplomat (however he is behind some doors so as long as you don't AE
you should be ok), Zaharn spawns in that spot as well and some others. What
I would suggest you do is go to SolA and take the safe room zone to SolB.
It seems that greater kobolds drop medium bronze a lot more frequently than
the gobs in perma, plus the area is usually pretty safe. A group of level
25+ or so can take out one kobold pretty easily, the higher you are the
better your chances though.
