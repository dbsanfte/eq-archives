the giants. The lizards and basilisks supposedly do too,

you make more giants spawn. The lizards and basilisks are
major green to me and run away, and the pathing bugs make them
impossible to chase. So I'd just charm a gaint and tow it
around, ordering it to kill these guys.

updates (mobs blink in and out even with 0% PL), it's very
easy to accidently attack something someone else has already
attacked. So try to be understanding when it happens.
