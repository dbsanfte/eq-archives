You can practice pick locks on any of the 3 locked doors in Befallen just
off West Commonlands. Equip your lockpicks and just click on the locked
doors over and over and over. It takes a little while to get increases, I
think it took me 2 hours total to max the skill over a few sessions.

Similarly, although it is a pretty useless skill you can max out disarm
traps by clicking on the swinging blade trap near the entrance of sola
(solusek's eye).
