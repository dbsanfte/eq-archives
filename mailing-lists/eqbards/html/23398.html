I didn't start using instruments to solo until after 18. Then you have two songs
(Chords and Disssonance?) that are AE DOT's that you can use with Hym and do
more damage for weapons and you can take on a couple of mobs at the same time.

Now at 34 I switch from instruments to weapons depending on the group make up.
Check out the Concert Hall (http://www.attcanada.net/~reaper/index.html) and EQ
Song (http://www.eqsongs.com/) for other good Bard Reference material.

Being a bard (for me and imo) has been lots of fun. I have done Shaman, Mage,
Warrior, SK, Necro and Chanter and come to my Bard each and every time.

Good luck and enjoy!
