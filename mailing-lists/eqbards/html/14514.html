You can't dual wield instruments... BUT there's a lute-looking thingy which
is not really an instrument in-game; the Lisera Lute from Qeynos (Lore item
so you can't wield two of them), which is free for anyone who bothers to ask
for it. It looks like a lute, feels like a lute, and is even called a lute,
but it doesn't affect Hymn healing rates and you can wield it in one hand
and a weapon in the other - for a brief time I ran around wielding a lute
and sword 'cause I loved the confused /tells I got.
