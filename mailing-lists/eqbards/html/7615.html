TEMPLE OF SOLUSEK RO ON TEST SERVER
The Temple of Solusek Ro has gone live on the Test Server! It has all sorts
of great quests for the various caster classes, here are the details I have
so far:

The Enchanter Tashania Quest is commenced within the Temple from an NPC who
will give you a coin bag and a book of clues. Return with the 10 Coins of
Tash combined in the bag and 50gp to claim your scroll.
Enchanters can also do a quest to obtain a Rod of Glamours.
Magicians have four quests to obtain various items. The Robe of Elements,
Earthen Boots, Circlet of Mist and Clay Bracelet.
Necromancers and Wizards also have quests for various class specific items,
but I still need details on these.
There is apparently a quest involving the Crystal of Kintaz which wipes
memories. It is beleived at this time you can use this to change
specialisations.
There is also a new quest to make Shadow Silk, for Necromancer only armour.
The temple is located opposite the Solusek Dungeons in Lavastorm, and is a
little bit tricky to enter until you figure out how, but it won't take long.

Hmmm, more cool stuff for casters just what the world of Norrath needs. Why
do we even try to play non-casters any more.
