Hee hee! We have a cleric in our guild that we always count as a 1/2
warrior + 1/2 wizard when deciding if our group is balanced. He once sent a
screenshot to the guild-list from an adventure where several people died do
to lack of healing and wondered how with two clerics that happened. The
answer presented by his screen was very telling, at level 15 he had only 7
spells memorized, 1 heal, 3 summoning, 2 DD, and gate. Needless to say we
were in shock. In the middle of a romp thru Najena, this guy has summon
food, summon water, and that Hammer spell memm'd. What a maroon. His name
appropriately is Banestrike. We have trained him to be a bit better, but
he's still not really a cleric and we never count on him as such. It
wouldn't surprise me one bit if he is specialized in evocation. He's the
only cleric I know who though Befallen was way too tough a dungeon. Of
course since he was using his generic DD's instead of his undead specific
ones (which he hadn't bought yet) I guess I shouldn't be surprised.
