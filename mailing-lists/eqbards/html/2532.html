Well rubicite is very good, but very hard to get. First, you'll have to
be in yours mid-20s. Then you'll have to travel to cazic-thule, near the
ogre home city. Once you eneter cazic you'll need to find a group and
hunt down the creatures that spawn with the armor, which is easier said
than do. The tae ew templar in the throne room has the leg greaves, a
golem(forget which one) has the hands, the avatar(which also holds the
demon claw) has the breastplate, and I forget who has the helm and the
arms. Of course, expect much camping of the mobs that have rubicite, as
the rubicite is very valuable and they usually carry something else that
is very valuable(like the demon claws for instance). On the other hand,
most high level(above 30 or so) people have rubicite now probably,
because, as I hunt in cazic, I tend to see 90% of the people in cazic
above 27 or so using it.
