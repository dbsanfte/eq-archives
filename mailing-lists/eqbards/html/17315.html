Good question. I guess that the bard's role will depend on the composition
of the rest of the team (groups.) When we (my guild) goes to do some plane
stuff, we make sure we have enough clerics, enchanters, and tanks. Our SOP
(standard operating procedure) is built around those classes. I guess some
others will vary the mix and use more clerics and tanks and skip the
enchaters or have one enchanter who is the clarity caster and trys not to
get killed. Our enchanters do crowd control and are fairly often getting
killed.

So, one big part of my role is mana bard. I'll typically get grouped with a
cleric and an enchanter and a couple tank types (and hopefully *not* the
puller.) The cleric has to spend a lot of mana keeping the enchater alive
and really does not want to have to worry about me. So in those instances
where the enchanter is in critical need of support, I will stay out of the
melee and stay close to the cleric with mana song playing.

When we go for a dragon, my group will usually be 2 or 3 tanks, 2 clerics,
me and maybe a caster if only 2 tanks. We will get right next to the
dragon. I stay by the cleric and tell the tanks to stay close to us. At
first, I weave elemental and guardian with my Mistmoore drun and my palette
set for a quick switch to Hymn & Niv's with my 7 string lute - in case the
cleric get LOM and low health or dies and the tanks need some healing.

Getting back to the planes. If you are set for a pull and know what the mob
will fight you with, you can select songs accordingly. There are some mobs
that do AE cancel magic or pilliage enchantment (they strip your buffs.)
There is another one that does AE drain stamina (excellent time to play Jig
of Vigor - no stamina means slower weapon speed and casters cant med when
they have 0 stamina.) Some mobs have poison base and others have disease
base and of course some have magic base. So, the Planar bard should know
these things and be ready to provide the appropriate resist buffs.

I think a bard in a group could really shine - especially if they are going
in with a group that does not have an enchater or tanks who dont have haste
gear. But the groups I have gone with have always been my guild and we have
both enchanters and haste gear. I need to go build some xp so I can venture
forth one again.
