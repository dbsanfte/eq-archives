Alright, are you a human bard Freeport or Qeynos? Because my recommendation
is to hunt in lesser fay and take out Nybrights. If you are near Qeynos I
believe they have some bandit camps as well. The thing that makes them so

hunt there who split them evenly with other groups, but expect a long wait
