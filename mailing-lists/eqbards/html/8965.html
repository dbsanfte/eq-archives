A minotaur will most likely /con green to any character in their 20s. You
don't need the help of 30+ characters to kill one. I soloed chasm minotaurs
in the Gorge of King Xorbb when I was 22d level. The minos in Steamfont are
even easier. If you are less than 20th level then a small group is probably
necessary. The mino horns provide no bonus to bard songs and the graphic
for them looks like a pouch so the demand for these is quite small. You
could probably go to these zones and auction for one if you really want one
that bad. If your intent is to take on the Mino Hero himself then the 30+
characters will come in handy.
