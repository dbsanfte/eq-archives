> So, how encumbered do you generally find yourself? What's your natural

I think 90 would be a bit low, you won't be hauling around too much loot
until you have a fair number of strength enhancing items and/or a weight
reducing bag. And the big problem here is hauling stuff around is a lot
more of an issue at lower levels when you need money the most and the
good strength enhancing items are out of your reach. I was still wearing
a spiked collar at L30 for the extra +3 str it gave me and my half elf
bard has a base strenth of 98. At later levels loot and money become way
less of an issue unless you are trying to train the jewelry skill, so
most people end up leaving those fine steel morning stars lying on the
ground rather than carrying them, and dumping excess copper when their
weight creeps up too much. As I usually find I'm still dumping 40-60pp
in the bank every couple of days and don't really have much I can spend
it on anyway this really isnt much of a problem. Dispite the fact that
you can carry some weight over your max encumberence without it visibly
degrading your stats, I still try to stay below encumberance just
because I find the constant messages as anthem (or now berserker's)
wears off to be too annoying to put up with.

A lot of people like the Wood Elf for their easy access to bronze plate
at lower levels (not to mention the ability to wear a Crush tunic, which
I've always been amazed fit an Orc three times the size of my Half Elf
but wouldn't fit my Half Elf) but personally I prefered the Half Elf, as
much for the stats but as for not having to start in Greater Faydark
(despite having the goofiest looking plate helm in the game - ok maybe
we tie with humans on that. :)
