No?

They convince DuckFoo that joing their guild is better for him than
staying in his old guild. DuckFoo then decides that the gains for
joining the new guild outweight the loyalty he holds to old guild.

Wheres the problem here?

Its with DuckFoo or with his old guild. Either DuckFoo is leaving
behind good friends, and trustworthy companions for the allure of more
power. Or, his old guild was not providing him with what he wanted from
a guild. In the first case I say "Shame on DuckFoo, and you deserve
whatever happens to you." In the second, I'd say its a good thing
DuckFoo left and everyone is probably better off. I don't see where the
new guild has done anything wrong.

If you aren't getting from a guild what you want, then you should
leave. All the better if its to a guild which will give you what you
want/need.

Personally, I want to be in a guild with people I like and I'm friends
with. Hopefully also with a decent amount of people my level, so I can
more esaily find groups. Other people may want other things.
