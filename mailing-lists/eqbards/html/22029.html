The "3" spawn in the Overthere is a small tower where three sarnaks spawn
with a relatively quick respawn timer. It is along the path in between the
FM and WW zones. The "6" spawn is a ruin that is directly out from the FM
zone (IIRC). There is also a "10" spawn somewhere in zone also.

All of these spawns have Sarnak Bezerkers, Knights and Enthusiasts with the
associated rare gear that comes along with them.
