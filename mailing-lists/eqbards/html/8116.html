One thing I would consider is replacing at least one of your two barbed
leather whips. I used to dual wield a dragoon dirk and barbed leather whip.
In combats that lasted more than a minute, the barbed leather whip would
cause a serious stamina drain. I would venture to guess that dual wielding
two of them is even more severe. I now dual wield thulian claws (cost me
400PP) and a dragoon dirk. In groups, very rarely does my stamina drop
below one bub anymore, even after long combats. The only time it will drop
below one bub is if I am soloing a blue that takes me forever to kill.
Obviously you can avoid the stamina drain by singing the Jig but you could
free up that song slot and memorize a different song by equipping some
weapons lighter than the barbed leather whips.

Just my opinion.
