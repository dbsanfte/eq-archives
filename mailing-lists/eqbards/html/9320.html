LGuk it's usually 2 or 3 paladins with ghoulbanes, a warrior
with an executioner's axe, and a ranger with various procing

resists, I'm not using my weapons anyway, so (on the dead
side) I usually use Chant of Battle (with drum) instead of
Celerity. Those weapons proc a lot more, usually 2-3 times a
fight each (and a couple extra procs from ghoulbane pretty
much equals the damage I could do with my weapons). Of course
if I can spare the song slot, I use both. And on the live
side I usually prefer Celerity and join in the fighting.
