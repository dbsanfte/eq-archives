A "true" random would be something like a roulette wheel (assuming
it's not fixed by the house :p) or a deck of cards. As far as I
know, there is _NO_ (double emphasis on that one :p) possible way for
a computer to generate a pure random number. But, there are many
ways to "fake" a random number, some better than others. (In my
college statistics classes we used the random number tables in the
back of the book... Open the page, point to the number.. Voila!) I
definately agree with Slyde and all those who say Verant's RNG is
outta whack a bit.
