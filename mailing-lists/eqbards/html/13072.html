Bard of 19 (almost 20) songs - Tunare

In the south east cornor of SK is a house surrounded by a stone wall.
Inside lives this hermit who is a ranger about level 20, skilled in the use
of magic. You can open the door to the wall but not to the house. But you
have bardic songs that when played will make him come out and fight you.
You will need a good group to help you kill him - especially at your age. I
don't think I ever solo'd him (but then I don't kite) - maybe after I was
25. He buffs himself with thorns and fights hard - with fine steel weapons
too. When you kill him, you will find a "broken lute" (that wieghs 10
pounds!) and a page to the song. Keep one only of each.

Now, how you are supposed to know this - I have no clue since I did it all
from spoilers but you take the flute to North Karana and give it to Cordelia
Minister - the bardette who loiters near the wizard spires and patrols to
the northwestern shops. She will give you the other page of the song and
you go back to Vahlen. Give him both pages and he will reward you with a
new song! Viola - Chant of battle! Just in case your spell book catches on
fire and you lose your existing song! Woo Hoo am I ever glad I did that
quest!

The good part is the xp for slaying the hermit - affectionately known as
Herman on our server and the FS weps he drops every 4 to 6 kills.
