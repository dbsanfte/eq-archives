You're a bard, don't solo. I found the best place for exp at level 24-27
was Mistmoore. I wizzed through these levels in a matter of days. Bards are
excellent in groups in Mistmoore as they can provide the invis song (for
train avoidance), healing and combat speed bonus's. There are plenty of
spawns in Mistmoore and in a good group you can get constant exp from
pulls. Stick to the wall on the side of the lake and you should avoid
trains. Gargoyles spawn on the bank with the tower and often they drops
Garg eyes (9pp each).

Best of luck, hope this info helps.

Madagan Songweaver, Bard that loves Mistmoore

Dampyre.
