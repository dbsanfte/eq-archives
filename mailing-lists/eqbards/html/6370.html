Just by luck I witnessed this on Veeshan. Thursday at 2025 EST Hatar
spawned behind the docks in Oasis, back near the land based pillar. The
usual screams of "SG!" rang out, but I had SoW on my troll shaman and it was
pretty close to the zone anyhow... so I ran over to see where he went. I
clicked on him just as everyone started saying "hey... a blue SG..." and saw
Hatar. I noted it and thought, wow, nice of him to spawn since we were just
talking about him and gave it little more thought.

Saturday at 0230 EST the screams rang out and, being the curious little
troll that I am, I went to investigate again. Sure enough, same place, same
SG: Hatar. FYI, he's definitely slower compared to the rest of the SGs when
running so if you happen upon him and have a decent group you might want to
give it a shot since even without sow I'm sure run-jump will beat him out in
a dash for the zone.

So there, those more inclined to numbers (there may be some other triggers
to making him spawn - a group was previously hunting SG's, but they had left
a good hour before he spawned) and such can camp him until Brad makes you
yield or die. :P
