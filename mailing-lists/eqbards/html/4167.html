The faster weapon should go in your secondary slot. Since your dual wield
skill is checked when it's delay goes off. A faster weapons mean more DW
checks, which means more chance to hit with the second weapon. That said
the difference between your two weapons is only .2 seconds, so in this case
it is something you would never notice. But if you want to maximize that is
the way to go.
