Shameless plug:

Here's my map of Sol A...
http://www.betinahershey.com/images/adam_stuff/soldunga2_1.jpg

The Goblin High Shamen drops the glowing stone band. if I recall correctly,
the map is slightly dated. The High shamen actually spawns in the room just
to the east of the #17 on the map. There should be 4 spawns in the room
marked 17 and 2 spawns in the room to the east.

Invis works fairly well here, just remember that wizards, shamen and
solussek grade goblins can all see through it. Most of the time though, you
can get there unmolested. In so far as camping, the room that is North and
East of the room where the High Sham spawns (the one with the grey corridor
leading out of it) has no spawns in it and is a good place to camp. It is
accessible through a secret wall in the hall outside of the room marked 17.
