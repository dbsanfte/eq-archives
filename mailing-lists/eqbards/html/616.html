As soon as I hit level 6, I found myself a good group. Not hard, just
shouting "Bard looking for group" usually gives me about 4 or 5 responses.
Once you have a good group of people you should be able to kill a lot of
yellows and reds in fairly quick succession, with you keeping the party
healed all the time.
Levels 1-4 were fairly easy to solo. Levels 4-5 were the hardest for me, as
I didn't yet have the healing ability, but if you stick it out through these
it gets a lot easier.
