Selling your drum and going with the lute depends on how you'd lke to
play. If you plan on staying right around Freeport for a while and do not
plan on travelling then you could probably do without the drum. If you want
to travel far distances then the drum is very nice to cut the time down (not
to mention a greater ability to outrun darn near anything). For now the lute
will only boost your heal by 2 hps a pulse and increase the damage (and
range?) of Chords.
