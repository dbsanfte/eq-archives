Unrest was really good for me from 26 thru 31. Rest of the group was same
or 1 level higher. Bard, Ench or Necro, SK, War, Clr, Druid. Around 25 we
did the Bar room (2nd floor), then 27 or so (which was 2 or 3 nights later)
we moved to the Knight room (3rd floor.) In our low 30's we did the
basement torture chamber. Around mid 30, we did the Ghost room with a
higher level druid who wolfed us. Then we moved on.

Iirc, I would use the Lullaby song for crowd control in the Bar and Knight
room when we needed it. Our ench was trading off between her Ench and her
Necro. In the house, you can attack things on the 3rd floor from the second
floor. You see the particle effects from 3rd floor fights all the time when
in the bar room. And you can target and attack thru the floor. Also, the
agro ranges over-lap. Our rule of thumb for either room was both needed to
be camped to hold the one you were in. So, we paid attention to the 'other
room' and were prepared to evac if they left or were chased out.
