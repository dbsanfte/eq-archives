I personally have not found a decent map of Lower Guk dead side that
indicates any of the rooms. One of the best sites for maps that I have found
is the following:

http://www.nx.sakura.ne.jp/~chizuya/indexe.html

Most of it is in Japanese and there are few points of interest marked on
the maps but as far as finding your way around it is damn near perfect.

I also do not know where the Assassin is but people who hunt the Supplier
also seem to hunt the Assassin at the same time so they must be relatively
close. On Maps' Guk Bottom Dead Side the Basalts are in the area with the
'A' and the Supplier is in the square room above the 'To B1'. I'd guess the
Assassin would be somewhere between the 'To B1' and 'C' maybe.

Another good spot for maps is this place:

http://www.medvecky.com/EQ/NOREQMaps.htm

This is basically a collection of different maps from different people
including many from the Japanese site above. I don't think any original maps
exist here.
