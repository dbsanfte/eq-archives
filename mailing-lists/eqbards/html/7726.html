It was my understanding that the spell form of levitate just
picked your feet up off the ground, where you go from there
is your decision. (like running up a hill and flying off, or
running up a slope and hopping onto a house.) So in that
sense you control your own altitude.

With the bard song you can actually continue to rise. (as if
someone casted levitate on your already levitating body)
There is a ceiling to which the song no longer levitates you
any higher. I believe it's between 10 - 15 pulses when you
will no longer rise up, but until then your Z coordinate
continues to increase.
