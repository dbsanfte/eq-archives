This "feature" was something I was unaware of until just a few days ago. I
think it also helps to explain the high frequency of trains in Unrest.
Apparently some of the rooms are linked to one another. Like the previous
poster said, the tower is linked to the barroom. If you are in the barroom
and no one is in the tower, the mobs in the tower will come down to the
barroom when they spawn. I believe the barroom is linked to the magi room.
I was camping the magi room last Saturday and to our surprise in walked a
noble. What was even a bigger surprise is that one of the partymembers got
a BS tunic off of him.

A word of caution when camping a room in Unrest. Using an area of effect
song like Disruptive Discord or Chords of Dissonance is not a good idea due
to the z-axis bug. When you are camping the magi room, you will see
messages all the time stating the mortuary fungus begins to cast a spell.
You will also get hit with the Mort Drowsy spell from time to time. In the
hand room, you will sometimes get the same message except this time its a
festering hag casting the spell. The mort fungus is in the basement and the
hag is in the tower. If you sing the AE songs, you will likely draw their
attention to you. Lullaby and Largos's Binding are safe AE songs that you
can sing.
