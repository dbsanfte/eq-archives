Bandits are tougher than they look. You have the perfect spell for
surviving this already, and once you try it you will be amazed how well it
works. have the group stand well away from the road and the camp. You run
up to near the camp and start lulling bandits. Lull the lowest conning ones
first. Figure you can lull about 4 or 5 before it will start to wear off on
the first ones. throw something at a higher level bandit and run back to
your group fast. The lulled bandits should stay in camp and you should get
a manageable number.

My group tried to take that camp when we were 15th. It was then all
green/blue so we pulled the whole thing. Needless to say we were all
recovering our corpses soon thereafter.
