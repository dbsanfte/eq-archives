I bought mine for 100 each. The problem, though, is that few people sell these
items. People who are high enough level to obtain them generally have enough
platinum that they don't need any more, and so look instead for items to trade.
This ends up being a big problem for lower level players, who really only have
plat to offer. I suggest that if you want one of these before you are able to
kill Bonecracker yourself, buy one at whatever price you see.

They are, btw, excellent weapons for a bard, as they are very efficient and do not
have any annoying "lockup" abilities.
