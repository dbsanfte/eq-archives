Well actually i think im lucky. I opened up my pc. My internal case fan
was unplugged and 2 out of my 3 cpu fans were dead (ack!). Turns out the 2
fans that have a common power plug on the mother board seem to have a faulty
plug. I swapped it so that 2 of the 3 fans are on the other reliable power
plug and that the third fan is on the faulty one (it goes on and off).
Since i've fixed all my fans the pc has yet to hang, and i've done a couple
of 4-5 hours sprints of gaming and everything is ok woot. No upgrading just
yet...
