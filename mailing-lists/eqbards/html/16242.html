> I only ever hunted from the Fur Room (as described in those
> emails) so I can
> only talk about that. My technique for pulling was:
>
> Go out looking playing Elemental Rhythms, carrying a lute.
>
> If a wizard was in the pull I would switch to a drum, for the
> return trip.
>
> Chain to pull, then Back to Elemental for the run back to the room.
> Elemental during a wizard pull really seemed to save me a lot
> of damage.
>
> Very, very often I would get stunned, you just have to breath
> deep and hope
> your back is to the wall and wait it out, the Cleric would be
> waiting for
> me(gotta love a really diciplined group that trusts each
> other)...that is
> where the lute came in, the instant I would get unstunned I
> would start
> running and fire up Largo's Binding or Lull. When I got back
> to the room,
> start with CoD, DDD, Binding, Anthem for the tank. or when
> using weapons
> Anthem, Elemental, Binding, Lull.
>
> Again, the main rule is never ever ever let any fire goblin
> get behind you,
> they all (er, pretty sure all) backstab huge. Keep your back
> to the wall at
> all times. You can't always help that while pulling, so make
> sure you are
> going to have a heal waiting for you.
