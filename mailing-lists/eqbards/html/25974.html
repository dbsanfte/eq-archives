Actually - in a way... There are only 3 races and 3 classes, and of
those, only 6 combos are playable right now. Of those, the Jenquai
Explorer is the most like the Bard of EQ, in that they are travellers,
capable of going where no others can go, and doing most quests (the
Tradesman are actually better than explorers at faction stuff). The JE
gets several useful skills, including Jumpstart (akin to Ressurect), Cloak
(invisibility), and Wormhole (Teleport / group teleport). Though their
combat skills suffer significantly (much like the Bards) and they do, in
fact, add little to a group, they are much better as soloists. Now that I
think about it - they're the Druids of EnB ;) There really isn't a good
bard equivalent.
