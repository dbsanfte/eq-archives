Sounds like you're charming one, sicking it on your target then never
re-targetting your pet. IE: Your charm breaks and then you re-charm the
OTHER giant. Your former pet is now VERY ticked off and will attack you
instead of the other giant.

If, instead, you do the following:

1) Charm target
2) Target enemy
3) /pet kill
4) Target Pet
5) do whatever for awhile
6) Recharm pet

The other giant should stop and fight your pet every time you re-charm.
