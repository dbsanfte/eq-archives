I've been hunting there since 38. I got a Guardian BP, Sentry Pauldrons and
a Guardian Girdle which is one of the phatest items for a bard for a waist
slot. 12 AC 2wt +5 CHA and some other stats. I'm currently working on
getting Guardian arms and legs. XP is decent but I mainly hunt here for the
armor. The songs I use while grouped are. Berserk Cresc, Furfils, Chant of
Flame. On some casting mobs I usually twist in Denon's Dissension to keep
them from casting complete heals on themselves. Also throw in some DS's.
Either Cooling or Warmth will work. Lastly of course Bewitch Brauvura is a
must for crowd control. Pixie Strike will work too, but I prefer to have the
mob work for us instead of standing there loafing about. One last thing, you
can use invis song to scout out the area, BUT, be very careful in the
Courtyard. There are some named drakes in the left corner that see invis.
I've seen those drakes take out a group of 50+'s very quickly, and the
highest con, Suez, is only yellow to me at 42. If they all agro I'd make for
the zone. Try and get your CS key first so you have another zone available
in the Courtyard. Ziglark, a named drake drops the key every time and hes
only lvl 26ish. He is guarded by two Guardians though. Also, last note for
those of you going to SS, do not engage the two sentries at the entrance.
They will be green but they have uber hitpoints and still hit hard. Whenever
I get in trouble and make for the zone I always charm one and let him take
things out while I get the hell out of there.

Arud Bastad
