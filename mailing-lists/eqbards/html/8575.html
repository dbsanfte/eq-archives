Choon and Froon live in the Western Plain of Karana. Don't know them too
well but they they are roughly equal near 30. Saw Cappenvarra charm them
and fight among themselves.

Broon - wuss clops lives in Eastern Plain of Karana - starting spawn spot in
river North of wooden bridge.

Droon - Broon's older bro - when broon is blue to you droon is yellow (I
started fighting Broon high 20's and lo 30's - a mob of mixed 20's can
prolly take broon)

Proon - papa of Broon and Droon - deeeeeeeeeeeeep red (40+) just zone or
/tell merlain come kill proon PLEASE!!! zoning

Broon hunting 101

Get a partner: tanks are good, druids are good, rangers too, never had a
caster to try it with but prolly ok but risky for caster.

Find Broon in river and pull him out (trust me). Kill him: chain or snare,
fear and bellow and melee, chords if you have a clear spot to fight. Loot
fast! Make sure you are well away from his spawn spot because Droon respawns
in river real fast and can jump u b4 u are healed up.

Let Droon go unattacked and he will vanish in a minute or two. Mean while
run to the Highhold ramp and camp at the mouth of the lower canyon. Prolly
polite to warn zone to stay clear of the lower canyon.

Every 8 minutes Broon will respawn in the lower canyon and come lumbering
out. Chain/snare, Fear, DoT, Melee, Kill, rinse, repeat.

Loot is crap - small coins and occasional rusty. Well your weps would be
rusty too if you stood in the river most of the time.

One time I did loot a Top Knot Cap (ac 3 non-visible when worn) from a clops
but for the life of me I cant remember which clops - I do think it was in EK
zone and near HH ramp.
