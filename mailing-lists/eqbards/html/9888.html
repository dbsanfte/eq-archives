I know people are (reasonably) of both extremes on the topic of spoilers.
Some prefer to discover everything for themselves, others like getting help
so they can maximize other aspects of the game.

I'd suggest supplying the information but keeping it sufficiently separated
that people can delve into it as deep as their own preferences allow.

So on a Web page, for example, I wouldn't make location visible at all at
the top level but link to it. Then other than information such as general
area, I would make details yet another link.

Web pages are such a cool way to provide information at increasing levels
of detail...
