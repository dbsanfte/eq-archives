Subject: Out of bard experience


I was completely amazed tonight!

Was fleeing upper Guk as things got a little heavy, only the smallest sliver of life left, unsure I could make it past the chasm of inches to the exit. A Froglok novice cast a passing shot at me: poison. I sighed relief as I made it out, and immeditely started singing Hymn of Restoration--but before I could sit down... I pass out unconscious!

I hovered it seemed a very long time, looking down on my crumpled form, waiting for the poison to kill me... yet I was still singing Hymn of Restoration! Who would win? The infernal poison or the bard miraculously singing a rather complex song in his sleep? A minute passed. I came to my feet! Only to swoon into unconsciousness again! It was madness! But I will say that this bard never missed a beat in his song and survived!

Roop
