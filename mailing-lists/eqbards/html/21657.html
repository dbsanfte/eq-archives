Just a wee caveat to Giant hunting at the fortress in FM:

DO NOT!!! (I can't stress this enough) DO NOT stand in line of sight with
the fortress entrance and sing Hymn.

I don't know what happened in the past couple of days, but when I stand on
the slope a little ways from the entrance and sing Hymn, we start to get
faction hits from other groups and Giants come running out to come pound on
me.

I proved it to my group yesterday. A Giant popped in the doorway. We were
our usual safe distance away from the fort. The group were all sitting
medding and healing. I started Hymn for ONE pulse, and the Tump came
bounding out.

Normally I speed in to the fort to pull. Now I don't even have to move!

Later on, however, I was with a group to the right side of the entrance,
beside the flagpole. The spot we chose was actually closer than the slope
earlier, yet I could Hymn away to my heart's content without aggroing a
single mob.
