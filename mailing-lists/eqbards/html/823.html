Subject: Grouping and XP bonus


I learned this weekend that there is a 25% XP bonus for groups of 6. I
didn't believe it until my group added a 6th person, and our xp rate
increased dramatically. The 6th person we added was AFK and just sat on a
rock the whole time. We were doing crocs and caimans in Oasis so the type of
creature didn't change. My buddy (also in the groupt) Petitioned a GM to get
the skinny on this. The GM wouldn't give out a figure, but did acknowledge
there is a bonus for total XP distributed in a party of 6.

This gives high level people incentive to get a group of 5 lower ranked
party members. That person will rake in the XP so long as they're killing
things that aren't too low for his/her level.
