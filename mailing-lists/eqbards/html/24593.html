Kael is a tough zone. As a 39 bard I've found that charasmatic Carrilion
has limited effect for me and sneak is the way to go if your faction is low
and you need something. The good quests are all in King Tormax's area and
you can't get thru there with invis song -- you may be able to sneak but one
of the "features" of Kael is that anyone dragging a body is KOS, regardless
of faction so plan for a difficult CR if things go badly.
If you are going thru Kael using sonorous clouding, be sure to con giants
at regular intervals. If any of them see thru the song, you are likely
close to the temple of Rallos Zek, a very bad place to be, even if you are
agnostic -- all bards are KOS there.
