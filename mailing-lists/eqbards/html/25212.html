As far as weaps vs insts - your weapons do make a difference (heh plus
haste) that is - what weapons you have. Also - when I'm in a group without
either an enchanter or a shaman (but WITH a snarer was very common when I
was in a small guild without either) - and I'm doing hastes and slows -
those are both singing songs - so I'd use weaps regardless. Point being -
even if you do prefer insts over weaps - there are situations where you'll
be tanking and the better weaps you have in those situations you'll be in.

As far as upgrading instruments; well I've found that 90 pct of the time I'm
using Drums and Lute. At your level you are better off trying to camp your
upgrades (7-String Lute in Hate and Naggy Drum - get your red scale before
you hit 53). The Lute is No Drop - so you can't trade for one if you
want...

So again - as I said in my prior note - camp the Velious Armor, camp the
instrument upgrade, buy weapon upgrades.

Slyde
