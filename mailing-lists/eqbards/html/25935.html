quote:

During this stage of the rebalance, we're looking at issues specific to
pure caster classes (cleric, shaman, druid, necromancer, wizard, mage,
enchanter). Bards certainly also need some improvements, and that's going
to receive attention after we finish with the casters. Bards are a very
unique and interesting class, and they differ so much from the rest of the
classes that they need to be addressed individually.
After we finish with casters and then bards, we'll take an overview look at
how everything is stacking up. The melee changes were very successful, and
we have high hopes for this upcoming round of changes as well. If we find
that any particular class or class archetype needs some attention at that
point, they'll definitely get it.
Rich
