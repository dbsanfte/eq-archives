visible pieces is good (i think looks are important hehe). i would not use
anything but magical items/jewelry in all other slots.

emerald rings instead. the silver ones are 3str/3dex. don't know what your
veil is but to make up for cha lost on rings (if you went with fire emerald)
there is a star ruby veil that is Cha/Dex. as far as stamina, it is
probably the least important stat. for the extra cash, i would save up for
a golden black pearl choker, 7dex/7agi and much better than the golden opal
amulet.
