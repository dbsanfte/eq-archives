On Mon, 21 Jun 1999, Douglass Family wrote:
>
> A combine weapon is a weapon though not really magic can hit targets that
> can only be hit by magic, they also tend to be lighter and have a shorter
> delay.

Combines have the same stats as fine steel weapons, except
they count as magical weapons.

> You can buy them at two gypsy camps in Antonica, the first is in N.
> Karana in the gypsy camp along the road by the gaurd tower, the second is
> located in the Oasis of Marr, at the Northeastern corner of the Oasis
> itself. You will pay 40+ pp for one but they are worth it, and...... You
> dont have to stick em in the bank to keep them :).

THere are four gypsy camps. Oasis has sand giants wandering
through it, Karana has griffins flying through it. There's
one at Lavastorm, which is a bit safer but nearly impossible
to find. The Lavastorm camp is also the only one with the
blunt combine weapons and the combine spear and rapier. I've
never been to the camp in Rathe Lake.

Most bards tend to get the best prices or close to it, so I
wouldn't fret so much over the cost. When you outgrow it, you
can either sell it to a player, or sell it to a merchant and
only take a 10%-15% loss. So even though a combine rapier
cost me 52.5pp, I can sell it back for 47.681pp, thus it's
like I've only paid 5pp to use it.
