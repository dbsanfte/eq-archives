After spending some time in a group of 2 at bouncers, Sol B,
and Lower Guk, I'd have to say it all really depends on how
crowded the zone is. In a group of two, we were basically
able to kill stuff non-stop (I'd pull while the paladin medded
from the heals he just cast), and the experience was
phenomenal - like a bubble every couple hours. Then another
big group would move in nearby, cut off our supply of stuff to
kill, and our exp rate plummeted. Same thing with bouncers -
pull as many as you like and the experience is pretty good (I
didn't try them until they were almost green). Another group
moves in and the exp gain drops.

So if the zone is crowded, you may want to try forming a small
group and finding a small nook or cranny that isn't hunted
much.
