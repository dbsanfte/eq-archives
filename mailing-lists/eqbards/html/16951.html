Subject: screech and trains


The big myth is that screech causes trains - it doesn't if you evac or
zone. The mobs are running away and other people only get added to the
hate lists if they attempt to attack. Once the offending parties zone the
mobs will continue to run until fear wears off and then will mosey on back
to their spawn spots.

I really can't see how using dirge to clear the path for evac is useful:
In the same 3 seconds you could either hit 4 mobs and yourself for 300-odd
damage or clear away a good 90% of the mobs for the duration of the evac.
