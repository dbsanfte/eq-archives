Don't fight the webspinners and things in the FV zone. Not worth the
trouble. The are in the 28-35 level range, see invis, cast and deal quite a
bit of melee, they assist one another and the little Firionia Bixies will
come in at the last moment and KS you like the guards do. Heck they agro on
me at 50 and if I have to fight more than 2 I have to run, but I haven't
worked out a method for fighting them because they don't give me experience.

I recommend following the river that flows down to FV between the legs of
the giant Iksar statue up into the Lake of Ill Omen. There's normally about
25-40 people in that zone on Druzzil Ro in the 15-30 range fighting all
kinds of creatures. On Druzzil you can get pickup groups here. The Swamp
of No Hope also adjoins the FV zone, but this is the newbie Iksar zone and
it's generally populated with lower level Iksars killing mosquitos and
leeches and would be much harder to get a pickup group in.
