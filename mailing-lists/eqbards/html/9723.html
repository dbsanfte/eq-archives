I woke up at 4am and figured ok 2 hours to play... went to Kith and got into

trains).

control. The party was me 35 bard, a 36 sha-person, 33ish necro, 33ish sha,
33ish ranger, 31ish war.

dread wolf while the rest of them killed a rotting knight. So, I charmed
