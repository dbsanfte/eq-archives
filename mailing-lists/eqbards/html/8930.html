Practice first on a bunch of green mobs so you get an idea of
how the pet commands work, how the timing of the song works,
how to recharm while staying out of melee range, etc.

Basically, any outdoor zone is good for this song. I first
used it in Everfrost getting mammoths to fight each other
(they are about L25, and usually drop a tusk that sells for
10pp - accounted for my first 100pp night). Tidal lords in
Dagnor's Cauldron are good too if you can find two. I hear
the aviaks are good too, but I've never fought them. Lizards
in Cazic are good too, if you can find an open section of the
courtyard.

Indoors you have to be careful about starting a green
train. Since you're usually charming stuff that's blue to
you, stuff that's green and won't attack you *will* sometimes
attack your pet. But if the immediate area is clear, you can
use the song indoors as well.
