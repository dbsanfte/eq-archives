Greetings! And Welcome!

The best thing to do is get a "regular group" - find a group that will play
the same days, hours and times - then you can learn together instead of you
having to teach the group every time. Other than that - there are many
morons out there.

How are you trying to solo? Toe to toe melee? If yes, you need to change
your tactics. Bards have low HP compared to true tanks and mobs are getting
stronger while you stay about the same (ok you get more hp each level but
the mobs get more). Use tatics where you damage them but prevent them from
damageing you. Hit and run - stay just far enough away that you can damage
them but they cant get you. Use magic songs for damage - Chords, Denon's &
Bellow. And do not be in a hurry - it takes longer and you need to learn it
out side.

You would be better off to have a same-level partner and the 2 of you kill
more mobs with less downtime. From 15 thru 25 - most other classes get
arrogant and ignore the need and value of partys.

Get a better weapon - soon you will have dual wield. The whip is ok but
there is better - Dragoon Dirk or Combine Longsword. I used a giant snake
fang and mino axe along with my combine long sword for a long time.

You lose hp when monster hits you - don't let him get close enough to hit
you - as much. Hit him, run, hit him , run. Use songs to hit him!

Good luck!
