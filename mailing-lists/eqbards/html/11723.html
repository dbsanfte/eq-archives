There is a much easier way to the music room via the main gate. I suppose
it's possible to simply clear your way there, but there would be some very
nasty fights. Try to have someone who can pull singles, either from
knowledge of the zone or high enough archery.

The area behind the music room is somewhat safe. I went in with a 50
druid who knew the area very well, so I just let him do his thing. For
everyone in guilds, see if there's someone who's experienced in
Mistmoorian pulling, and make sure they come along.

I went in a week ago to help a paladin get his Soulfire hilt. The SK that
drops it is in the jacuzzi room, which we reached via the secret room
entrance to the castle. While sitting around waiting for him to spawn, I
got HT'd. One mob came through the door. Ok we thought, no big deal.
Then another came. Then another. Then at least 20 more gargs, ancilles,
ushers, thought spoilers all piled into this teensy room. The wiz, who
hadn't cast yet, was able to get off the port to faydark before anyone
dropped.

Definitely not a place to visit on a whim.
