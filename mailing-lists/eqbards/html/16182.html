1. Ok, there are 2 ways. First: get invis to undead. Take left side
tunnel to vampire bat room. Then take tunnel on left but inside that tunnel
*hug the right wall* (greater ice bone skels will agro on you if you don't -
they see thru all invis). Past the GIBS, you get to the bridge to the
tower. Cross and go towards rear. Look for the 2nd ladder you see (off to
the one side iirc). The assasin/Supplier room is up that 2nd ladder.
Second way if the BR is camped hard and all the deadly black widows are
dead, take the right tunnel and from the vampire bat room go right thru the
bugs - longer way but no GIBS (only bugs heh). It eventually pops out on
the side of the bridge to the tower.

2. Prolly 35 to 45. The named's are tough. Mean back stabs - keep your
back to the wall at all times.

3. Go at popular times to get the feel for the area and have lower
risk. Then go back at slower times to try to get a group. I think the mask
is regarded as a class specific novelty these days - since its bard rogue
only. You may be able to get loot rights just by asking and waiting. Oh
yeah waiting. Welcome to EverCamp. 2 hour spawn time iirc?
