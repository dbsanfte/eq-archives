I was lucky, I hit 30 and took a day off work. I joined some 40th level
friends to get my Guise of the Deceiver they had been bugging me to get
since I was 25th. We made the trip from zone to bedroom to tower 4 x in 2
hours, end of level 30, and I got my mask and a Black emroidered cloak. Now
35 was a different story all together. I went on vacation and that 1st
Friday at the start I hit 35. I played 16-20 hours a day in Lower Guk
fighting all sorts of nasties and hit 36 on Wednesday night. Truly hellish.
The good news, I did the same thing for the next few days and because of the
killing pace I had set for myself during 35 I went from 36 to 39 in less
than a week. Guess what, I am in 40 now and its worse.

-kill aviaks over and over and over.. eventually get one charm, so I can

quest, hey, I have a little less than a bub to go, whohoo!

-now I realize that I'm in gfay, and need to get exp, I shudder at the

thought of going to mistmoore, but my guild meet is there tonight anyhow,

about a bub to go)



-Mistmoore: not bad, kinda camped, which is good, less trains. solo a bit,

get a little exp, but slow going. I manage to avoid a few of those

late (now 9pm) and I still have more than half a bub to go. I'm determined

to level before I log, and get out of "hell level". So I dig in. I

join a group (so I don't have to KS from them) and slowly get exp. Around

11pm a train comes thru taking out half the zone, most of the group decides

thats enough, and calls it quits. I grp with a guild member who was there,

and in my dazed state (now having been up for 20 hrs) procede to take on

every single mob that was there, even green, just in case it gave me exp.



-Midnight, finally, I get lvl 31! I scribe, and learn my new song, group

leviate (fun) and force my guild to congratulate me :) I zone, camp, then go

to bed.. wake up 4 hrs later to come to work (yawn)
