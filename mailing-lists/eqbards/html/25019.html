I have a P3 866 with a GeForce2MX running at 1280x1024 and I only see slow
downs when a lot of spell affects (multiple wizzie mana dumps) are going
off. And of course the occaisional blip in NFP during peek times.

I overclocked my 866 to 975 and only saw marginal improvement, so the better
the video card you can get the better off you will be.

The GeForce2MX is also very overclockable. Some overclock sites have seen
close to the GeForce2 GTS performance with the chip clock and memory clock
jacked up. Beware, they added heatsinks and/or fans to BOTH the GPU and
the RAM on the video card. Haven't done that yet, but am going to try it
soon. Here's the link for those interested.
http://www.chip-online.com/products_tests/products_tests_301.html

Good luck,
