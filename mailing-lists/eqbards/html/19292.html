Subject: Bristlebane bards in Overthere zone


I posted this earlier on the Concert Hall board. Not certain if this is has
been mentioned before or is common knowledge to everyone so I'll post it
here as well.

If you have a Mask of Deception and worship Bristlebane, then you can
buy/sell/bank (but not bind) in the evil outpost in the Overthere zone.
Those two combined will raise your faction to Apprehensive with the guards
and merchants (at least the ones I conned). Throw in the carillon and you
can raise your faction to Amiable:-) I tried this today and was able to
roam freely through the outpost. A big bonus to this is that now
Bristlebane bards can do the sarnak war braid quest. This is an awesome
quest. I turned in 4 war braids and got a noticeable increase in EXP (at
36th level). Sarnak war braids are frequent drops so I see myself hanging
out in the Overthere for a level or two.

Not certain is agnostics with a Mask can bank there. Also don't know if
people with bad Venril Sathir (sp?) faction can do this. If you have done a
lot of fighting in Karnor's Castle then be sure to /con from a distance
before you go in.
