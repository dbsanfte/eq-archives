Just a reminder: tomorrow (Saturday, Feb 12th, 2000) is the retirement party
for JackalX, one of the first 50th level bards and a stalwart contributor to
the bardic community. This is an opportunity to say goodbye to him, to meet
and mingle with other bards from every server, and to have some silly fun at
the same time.

Meet at the Priest of Discord in Rivervale at 12noon PST (that's 3pm EST,
8pm GMT) on the Tunare server. Bring a halfling with a name beginning with
Jx and then your bard's name (e.g. Jxaloro, Jxdemari). It's been brought to
my attention that some of these names, for who knows what reason, aren't
getting through the filter. Try adding an 'a' after Jx if needed (e.g.
Jxademari). Please don't go /anon or /roleplay, so we can find you and get
a participation count, and please don't go PvP or we can't bind you.

We're going to run to High Pass, get bound, and then run out to the gorge in
EK and leap lemminglike into the great beyond. Following our ritual mass
suicide, drinks will be served and merriment will ensue.

Hope to see you all there!

Jxaloro (Tunare)
