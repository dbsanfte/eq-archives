Once you attack a mob (or aid someone attacking him) you get out on his
"hate list." Then depending on your proximity to him - he attacks you.
Line of sight and some distance or agro range may also figure in. Couple
days ago - I got a bad pull on Froon & Choon and lost hp so I decided to
zone and heal rather than risk a death. I Selo's off to the east and soon
enough was far enough away from them that the decided to go back to their
little hill and do whatever they do up there. Since I out ran them, I could
stay in zone and heal up - as it was my intent to go back and get them
anyhow and did not need to zone nor camp to clear the agro list.

One time I was trying to find my way to Sol-A at an age where fire
elementals would still agro on me. One did and the nasty greenie burned me
down bad. I was kinda new to lavastorm and ran off in a panic not able to
find a zone. So, I just ran out of their agro range and camped to clear the
hate list. The reason to clear the list is so that if you do happen to get
back near them - you wont get attacked because they have 'forgotten' about
you by now.

Your target bar is only updated when you are within a certain distance
range. You need to be fairly close to the mob to see current condition.
Although once you target, you can run across the zone and still get a 'con'
of the mob to see if he is green, blue, or what.

Grats on your levels - Misty is good for those 8, 9, 10 - even to 12 or so.
