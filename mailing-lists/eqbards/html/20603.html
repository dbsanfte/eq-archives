Sylly is dead on with those suggestions, but one thing I want to point out
is that if you are charming in Kunark, it helps immensely to charm a mob of
a different type from the one you will be attacking. What I mean is if you
want to kill a sarnak, charm a burynai or goblin and not another sarnak.
Why? It seems that like mobs in Kunark will not automatically other like
mobs. This means that you have to order your pet to attack the target
everytime you charm your pet (or let the target hit you once after every
charm).

Another important thing about charming in Kunark is that all iksar mobs are
immune to charm. At least, I've never heard of anyone getting charm to
stick on an iksar.
