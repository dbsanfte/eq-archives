Just Imagine.... A Global solution to most major problems with Everquest =P

What if, just what if Verant in there ultimate wisdom (heh)decided to throw
a hardcap on all Servers of say 1500 peeps. Now this would most likely
require an initial investment on Verant's part for new servers to
accomodate the surplus of people that were left over, but, a hardcap solves
everyone's problems (well most) I think.

Camping becomes a non-issue due to the fact that people who want experience
would easily be able to find a new place to hunt that would yeild good
exp.It's safe to say that not everybody will be on at once.

Lag becomes a non-issue as well due to the reduction in the amount of peeps
on at any one time.

Link Dead's should be minimized for the above reason.

I'm sure there are other things that would be alleviated with this kind of
change, and the only thing I can think of that would detract from something
like this is if peeps wanted to bring in friends on their server. However,
this could be accomodated by allowing a character transfer of some sort
once a month to a server that wasn't at full capacity. Then the friends
could cooridinate what server to transfer to. It seems at least as far as
this last part is concerned, they are heading in the general direction. I
just don't see anything getting better by simply adding more servers. The
population is just going to max out (and then some) and the same problems
will come up again.

The bottom line is that regardless of what Verant says, there is no cap.
Initially it was 1500, then a few months ago it became 2000, and now who
really knows, I guess Fennin Ro was up over 3000 this weekend. There is no
cap at all. The game is great, but it could be that much better and IMHO
many of these problems would be taken care of with a hard cap on all
servers or better yet, hehe, how about just some servers if Verant is
worried about spending more money for all the servers I am proposing, how
about servers with a hard cap but with an increased monthly fee. I know I
would pay much more money a month to play Everquest on server with a cap.
Would you?
