I sing my songs like follows:

:sings, Hello darkness my old friend, (just an example)

Appears as:
Orchestro sings, Hello darkness my old friend,

I find I have to type about 90% of the songs I sing in. Sometimes I get
lucky and there is a short chorus that i can program into 4-5 hot buttons,
but it is a lot of typing and Praying that the emote (:) doesnt get freaked
by what Im typing and come back with the instructions.

This is why I would really like to see a /sing mode or a Singing checkbox on
the speech redirection menu (say/tell/group/guild/sing?). that would
automatically fill in the Orchestro sings, part so I could just type.
