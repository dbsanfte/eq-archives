Although it is possible to twist/stack songs by clicking on the song gems
along the left side of the screen it is much more effective if you bring the
gems down to the hot key banks and hit the number keys to switch the songs.
In the windowed screen just left click on one and hold and the song will
attach to the cursor just bring it down to the spot you want it on the key
bank and left click again. Note that you have 6 banks of 6 keys each,
obtained by holding down the shift key and hitting the number corresponding
to the bank that you want.
I have mine set up like this and I can 90% of the time have 3 songs up
solidly.
