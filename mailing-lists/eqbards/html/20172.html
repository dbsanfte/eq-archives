I've remapped the following:

"target pet" => "del" on keypad
"target nearest npc" = > "1" on keypad
"auto attack" = > "7" on keypad
"change view" => "+" on keypad

I essentially do the same as Tomerrlin, but I would find it hard to reach
the function keys while I'm twisting songs.
this way, my fingers stay on my "home keys" without me having to look at my
keyboard or stretch them too far.
