Subject: Chain and Binding


There was some disagreement over whether the attack slowdown
from these two stacked so I timed them again. I picked a mob
(a giant fire beetle) and let it beat on me while I timed how
long it took for it to make 10 attacks.

Normal: 30.1 sec, 30.2 sec
Chain: 38.4 sec, 37.9sec
Binding: 38.2 sec, 38.2 sec
Chain + Binding: 37.8 sec, 38.2 sec

Slowdown is about 25%, and they most definitely do not stack.
I'll test it with the enchanter's spells next time I meet one
of my enchanter friends.
