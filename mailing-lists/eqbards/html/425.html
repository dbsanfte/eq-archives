Twisting songs: playing for instance Whistling Warsong and Anthem de Arms so
that both shields are on the screen at once. Singing one, getting the
shield up, then stop singing and starting another, then going back to the
first song to keep that shield up. Some call playing three like this
juggling.

I have always managed to pull it off, even at lower levels. When you miss a
note, just immediately double click the icon you need again, should only
take a split second between each attempt until you get the graphical
pyrotechnics. Doesn't seem that I miss notes that often, though, ever. I
heard charisma had more effect on songs than dexterity. Dexterity, to me,
seems like it would have an effect on more physical things like how often
one can get in blows with a weapon.

Hm, how often is it your missing notes anyway? Perhaps should test this
with lv 1 bard with low charisma/dexterity high charisma/dexterity. The
manual says that bards need intelligence, but I don't believe this.
