I have been camping the West Common zone and Rivervale Zone. I start to see
things on tracking as early as 7pm and by 9pm the list is full. One pull at
9pm by RV zone and enough other stuff agros on our fites until the they quit
spawning around 4am to 6am. From then til 10 or noonish, you need a cleric
or pallie to cast sense undead which will see the few that are left over -
but beware sense undead will see all the way across the zone.

I went out to pull for a day time kill and managed to snag not one but two
enraged dread wolf puppys. Fortunately, I managed to run - but got a bit
lost and just ran the first zone wall I hit (from between HH and WC going
past the lake) until I found an exit (WC zone.) I didn't see anyone else
but if I had it woulda been a mess for them. I have snagged OPT's (other
player's trains) in the same manner. Running the wall, see some one go by,
wonder where is he going in such a hurry, wonder hmm where did this Zombie
trooper come from???

So, run the wall at night if you dare - but mobs path all the way to the
wall and players will train to zones - no two ways about it.
