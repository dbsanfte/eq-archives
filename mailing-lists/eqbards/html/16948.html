I have parked in Hate for over a week now, and I have had fairly good luck getting
into groups.

the night though, because gauntlets dropped and the only other bard there had them
already. That made 3 pieces for me; I already had legs and one bracer (from Fear
haha). I was told arms had dropped earlier that day, and decayed =(.

Saturday we had good groups and good pulling. I quit out only once, and we had very

example of knowing your role and making quick, sound judgements.

After we cleared roamers we go a scorn banshee pull. It dropped boots! There were
three of us; one already had boots; I lost the roll. And I sat there and thought to
myself, oh now let the clumpy random number generator work its magic (wished on John
Kim for luck haha)! A couple more pulls, then another scorn banshee, and my group
got the kill. They said "Mara go ahead and look..." Boots again! A couple more
pulls...another scorn banshee...and a bracer for the third bard there.

Last night they were full, so I didn't get to go up. The banshees dropped two sets
of arms and the weapon.

I have to say, I don't know if they changed the drop rates but I hope it keep up!

Arms and breastplate left to go for me (I will leave the scary helm wearing to Shada
