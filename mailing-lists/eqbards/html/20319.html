p.s. I have been having some problems with folks on auto-follow and my Selos
drum. With 2 PC's side-by-side, I see the follower getting "lost" and
spinning in circles quite a bit at times. So, I have taken to swapping in
my lute for long distance running with someone on auto-follow and that seems
to help. Also, I have problems with folks if I try to follow them and have
selo's song with selo's drum going as they start running. Typically, I say:
"You lead, I'll speed." They do a quick 360 degree spin to look around, see
they wanna go 90 degrees left or right of the way they are facing and take
off like a rocket. They instantly vanish from my sight thus starting a
session of: "Lost you!"; "What's your loc?"; "Did you move?"; "What's your
loc now?"; "Stop moving, damnit. Gimme your loc now." So, maybe a 'slower'
drum for group travel would be worth carrying around.
