I fell off the good race ferry and into the drink. I
did an OOC for a loc and ended up swimming (NE, I
think )to an uninhabited island -- swam west two more
islands and went thru an ogre village. THere is a 35+
ogre shaman out there with a pet that can outrun a 29
bard with normal drum and killed me rather quickly.

Returning, I found that I could stand at the end of
the evil dock and was out of aggro range of ogres. I
jumped ship just before the evil ship landed at the
Overthere and made a beeling for the FM zone to get
bound in FV.

There is no other way to get out of TD short of gate
unless you have cajones enough to swim to the
underwater TP room...

BTW, the ogres at the village in TD drop FS weapons.
