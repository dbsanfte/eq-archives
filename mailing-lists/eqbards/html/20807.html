Splitpaw - most everything is fooled by invis. Invis up and you can go
pretty much anywhere (only named mobs are likely to see you if even they
do.) But after that, be aware that Paw mobs have a large "social" radius.
Attack one and others will "know" about it. Healers will heal the one you
are fighting if it's within the social range. A single group will need to
pick their spots carefully. Learn the patrol routes and find a place or
room you can clear - then pull to that room. Also, at your level, you will
prolly not be able to pull singles without using lament and even then you
will have to pick carefully. Have your druid save mana for evac and dont be
afraid to use it.

Iirc, the kisch drops back near the old commander's room. As deep as you
can go on one side of the dungeon. And it's near where the old shaman's
line was. There were like 6 to 8 mobs in close proximity - all shamans -
and they would heal if your fights got too close. They would attack if you
were low enough relative to their level. Cant remember what they are now
but the AI is the same - just the mob levels are 15-20 levs higher.
