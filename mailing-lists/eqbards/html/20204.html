At the Gypsy camp in NK you can turn in GLS's for a Book that you can
immediately sell in the same camp for ~9.5 pp. Lightstones sell for about
1pp.

The best place I know to hunt for wisps (the source of lightstones) is the
first stop on the boat from Qeynos to Erudin. While there you can kill the
wisps and the Vampire Bats. Don't kill the beetles and (I think) spiders.
This will cause Fire Elementals to spawn, which you won't be able to handle.
Also watch out for Madmen.
