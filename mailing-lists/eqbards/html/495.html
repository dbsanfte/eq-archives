John Hosenberg wrote:
> After an unfortunate night in Steamfont with a wrong turn to Meldrath I lost
> my corpse permanently. Any ideas on where to hunt for money to replenish my
> funds, as well as get good exp?

Sorry, but running around 'naked' as you are, you really can't do
both. For money, I'd start with fishing. Easy, low risk, and SLOW!

Then the next step is to find a weapon, any weapon, and some armour,
any armour. I'd recommend finding a nice decaying skeleton with a
weapon, and a friendly tailor. I know that I sew for free given
the raw materials.

Once you are armoured and weaponed, you can start to hunt the things
you used to, but carefully.

You didn't mention what level you are, so the above advice is pretty
generic. I used this tactic when my corpse was lost, at about level
9.

Of course, friends who are relatively well off may offer to loan you
reasonable sums of money, which will help you get back to where you
were before much quicker.
