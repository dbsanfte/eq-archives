For awhile on Prexus, there were 4 DW shamans making a HUGE supply of pets
to hunt on Tower island. We spent from level 20 to about 24 just pounding
on pets with probably about 40 other people (even then there was PLENTY to
hunt). In fact for a while, my guild had two full parties of 6 camping a
spot on the island. My brother's druid was pulling for one party, dropping
the pets off, getting a quick heal if needed and running back for more. He
kept both parties of 6 OOM all night long. This Nirvana went away after
about a week and a half when the servers were reset and wizards spawned
instead of shamans.

If you can get a group of level 50's to go down there and fix the spawns, it
was a GREAT time and awesome XP, although you won't make a dime (no loot).
