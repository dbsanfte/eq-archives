My guild came to the conclusion that Kael drops are so rare that it's not
worth putting a priority into Kael for anything other than getting CoV
Faction (and of course the "Big Boys".

Get your CoV faction up - and good resist gear - then move to the Halls of
Testing in ToV and you'll be very pleased with the Quest Armor drop rates.
It's streaky so you can have some nights where you'll on see two or three
drops in a five hour raid - but you'll have other nights where you'll get 15
or 16.
