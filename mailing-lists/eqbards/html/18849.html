My equipment list, please note this is an approximation as I am not an AC
hound or an item hound. I just play to have fun and wear what I get.
Granted my guild helps me get nice things but I hardly consider myself
twinked or anything like that:

Head: Executioners Hood AC5 +4str/9dex
Neck: Gold BP Choker (+7dex/agi)
Face: Mask of Deception AC 4, +13cha (and another stat, I forget)
Ears: Opaline Earrings (AC2? +5cha)
Chest: Lambent BP: AC 20 some stats
Shoulders: Carnal Pauldrons (AC 9)
Arms: Lambent Sleeves (AC 11)
Wrists: Lambent bracer + Hero bracer (ac 9 each)
Gloves: Lambent gloves (AC 12)
Waist: FBSS (who needs AC this thing nearly doubles my dmg output when I
sing haste songs)
Legs: Lambent Leggings (AC 13)
Feet: Singing Steel Boots (which I happen to like though summon drink is a
waste imho)
Fingers: Gold Amber Rings? (+5 dex/cha)
Back: Lizard Scale Cloak (AC 8)
I also use a Saeiny's dagger (AC 10, dmg 6 dly 18 with +3 int) when fighting

I think my AC with this outfit is 750 or so. I forget I really don't pay
much attention. I swap in a TBB and some Jagged bands and a spiked collar
as well as some disease resist +AC rings if I want more AC which brings me
up to the 800 range.

Things I could use to increase AC:
Kunark haste belt: 8ac +8 str/dex/agi/stam Haste
Earrings with more AC (diamondine come to mind)
Rings with more AC (currently standard set is 0 unless I'm pretending to be
a tank)
Neck piece with AC at all (runed lava pendant?)
Upgrade my shoulders to Crested or better
Upgrade my BP to EBBP (25 AC vs 20 current)
Upgrade sleeves to Azure or better (azure is 13 AC)
Upgrade legs to Mithril (1 or 2 more AC)
Upgrade hands to Singing Steel (2 more AC)

That would probably have me (at 40th level) knocking on 900AC's door.
All of the items I listed as upgrades I could get either solo or in a group.

And I'm sure there's other AC gear that's better that I don't even know
about. I rarely search item databases for armor ;) I'm not a tank :)
