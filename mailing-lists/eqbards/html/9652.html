To get to the basement - go in front door - go left - go down hall - open
second door on left - click on bookcase and go down stairs. Spawn spot. Go
on past and soon forks and go left then down more stairs. Torture chamber -
4 spawns in this room and on walls. Go straight and up the stairs (double
stairway) and don't turn but walk thru illusionary wall. More spawn spots.
See the blood or lava pit? walk thru it - do not try to avoid it - there
are pitfalls on either side - walk thru it - oh yeah kill those hags first
:-) if u need to. then further in is the ghost room. off the ghost room is
the pit. be interesting to do a loc and see where that puts you.

Another unrest note. It is somehow possible to get trapped behind the
estate wall. A friend was in a group and one of them got stuck on the other
side of the wall - petition time and wait for hours for help.
