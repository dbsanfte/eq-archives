Sub 50 ideas:
Selo's Shooting Star (39th?)
Percussion - outdoor only
Combination of Selo's Accelerando, Shauri's Sonorous Clouding, and
Agilmente's Aria of Eagles + Ultravision. Party effect, Range: Zone wide.

A new charm song, if not full charm then atleast a charm animal song (make
this one 44th or 49th)

Denon's Sonic Disruption
Target: Single
Brass (required)
Disrupts all spell casting from target.
This could also be interesting as an AE song as well. If AE having it stop
all say/shout traffic into/out of AE (this would be interesting on the PvP
servers).


50 plus songs:

Denon's Dance of Death
AE or Single
Brass
DoT + drain severe stamina drain(at least the equiv of a full bubble) +
chance to interrupt any spell casting
Dot should be twice as strong as Selo's Chords of Cessation at the same
level.

Niv's Chrorus of Restoration
String Required
2xHymn of Restoration + Fatigue restoral + 1.5xCassindra's Chorus of
Clarity.
Fully stacks with Hymn of Restoration, Cassindra's Chorus of Clarity, Niv's
Melody of Preservation.

No name idea
Voice
Converts entire mana bar to HP healing the party. Not sure what a balanced
spread would be, maybe a mana to HP conversion of 1.5 to 2 HPs for each
point of bard mana. The bard singing the song would NOT be healed by the
song.

Denon's Sonic Reflection
Brass
Gives all party members a damage reflection shield. Causing a portion of the
damage done to them in combat (melee & non-melee) to be reflected back at
the attacker).

Lyssa's Solidarity of Form
Wind (required)
Bard assumes the form of the target creature, with associated faction
adjustment.
Duration either 60 secs or continuous, ie as long as song is being played.
If continues the effect ends as soon as the song does (no residual effect).
Targeting a new creature changes form again.
