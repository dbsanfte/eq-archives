Now, to answer your question:

Lucid Lullaby and Lugubrious Lament have two completely different uses.

Lugubrious Lament reduces the agro radius of a single targeted mob. This
makes it very useful for pulling singles from a group of mob. Lament the
extra mobs and pull the one you want.

Lucid Lullaby is an area effect Mesmerization spell. In theory it will mez
all the mobs that are around the singer. In practice, it mezes about 1/3
meanwhile, the other 2/3 immediately turn on the bard and beat the bard to a
bloody pulp.

In personal exp I used lullaby up till my mid to late 20s. I never expected
it to stop all the mobs. I used it to stop some of the mobs and make the job
easier on our healer. We had no enchanter, so I was it for crowd control.
I'm sure it earned me some additional deaths over the time I used it, but
I'm equally sure it saved me in some fights.
