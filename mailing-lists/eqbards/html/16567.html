We need more party related songs that make people want us in groups.
More now than ever, bards need to demand stronger definition via
their songs. Right now we are a watered down: warrior, cleric,
enchanter, shaman, druid. That worked when I was L20 and that
versatility was welcome. Now every group has a warrior, has a
cleric, has an enchanter, has a shaman. They don't need bards
anymore. We've got nothing to offer except superior resistances
and even then that is becoming overlooked. Someone can always
do something better than us. I understand the 'Master of None'
part of our creation, but I'm no good being a 'Jack of all Trades'
if no group wants me for inferior abilities. Sure we can get the
job done, but someone else has a better, longer lasting, more
powerful, less resistant version of it out there. In a pickup
group, yeah I get to diversify some songs, in well structured
groups, bards simply are not required. (planes,dragons,events)

We are the supposed to be the strongest driving force behind
a PC party and groups should be looking for bards with strong
abilities and not avg-joe L32 manapump. Everyone wants a cleric
because they heal extremely well. Everyone welcomes an enchanter
due to the fact the make fighting much easier. At higher levels
what does the bard really have to offer? VoV, Nivs, Clarit? They
