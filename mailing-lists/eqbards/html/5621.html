> More general: please share more get-out-of death

> bards do because of the taunt effect of many of our
> songs, especially in indoor dungeons. Screech is one
> strategy. Would pixie striking help? Lullaby?

Lullaby can be used to the same effect, but Screech is usually
better since when the effect finally wears off, the mob is
twice as far since it has been running away from you instead
of standing in one spot. Pixie Strike is single target - if
you can get Strike to stick, you're probably better off
killing the mob instead of fighting it. OTOH, if you have an
enchanter, mesmerize is good for retreats since 95% of the
time it also does a memory blur and the mob will forget all
about you if you're out of range. Of course, to cast
mesmerize, you have to stop retreating...

Hmm, I wonder if fear overrides mesmerize...

> Also regarding Psalm of Warmth, I see the 'xxx was
> burned' message much more often than the 'xxxx was hit
> by non-melee damage for 5 points' (insert exact
> wording). What is your experience on the relative

"burned" is the generic message for taking damage from this
shield. "was hit by non-melee damage" is the private message
you get saying how much damage you did to the mob when it hits
*you*. So you see the former any time the mob hits someone,
but you'll see the latter when it hits you.
