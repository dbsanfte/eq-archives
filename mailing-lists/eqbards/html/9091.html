Song effects last long enough for you to play one or two other
songs before the first effect expires.

You don't have to be stopped to start a song - you can do it
on the run.

Charm's range is much much greater than melee range.

So keep Accelerando playing. When you see a giant, charm it,
restart Accelerando, and order it to attack. When it becomes
uncharmed, just recharm it while running to stay out of melee
range.
