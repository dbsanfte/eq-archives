If you play in any mode above 640x480 (default) you
can not open bags that are in your 'hot key' banks.
The sound of the bag opening is heard, but you can't
see it. You'll also notice that if you open your
spellbook it fills up only 640x480 of the screen. If
You return to windowed mode (default) it's also only
640x480 of the screen. The same goes for hitting "I"
to bring up your inventory. In 800x600 the only way
to get to your bag items is to either go into window
mode or hit I to bring up the inventory. It's EXTREMELY
annoying to say the least.
