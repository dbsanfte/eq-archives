Subject: Yet Another Accelerando Table Update


Several interesting notes in this one.

SoW does indeed get faster as the caster levels. It's speed
is not reset if you zone - it somehow "remembers" the level of
the caster.

Walking speed is not just a direct proportion of running
speed. Neither is encumbered speed for that matter. The
faster your speed enhancement, the *greater* the enhancement
it has on walking speed and encumbered speed. e.g. Accelerando
with a drum is faster than SoW. If you are encumbered, it is
even *more* faster than SoW (but still slower than being
unencumbered, though not by much).

The encumbered speeds were lining up pretty linearly until I
did my 80 overweight test. I suspect there's a greater speed
penalty that kicks in if you are carrying more than twice your
normal encumberance.

Walking with Accelerando and a battle drum is faster than
running with SoW. :-)
