well first thing to do is hope someone is camping the tower in guk (where
the ass and supplier spawn)

go to the dead side zone to lower. go down the ramp, go right... go into the
water, go right again, you'll go through a small underwater tunnel and pop
up in the "bedroom" usually some groups 33-37 camping that. Now here's where
you have a choice... there are 2 ways to get to the tower where the bridge
is.

1. go left with invis undead past the damn greater ice bones (4-5 of em
casting on ya hurts like hell)
2. go right and hope the spider halls are camped till the bridge

so basically your best shot is IVU past the ice bones to the bridge, once at
the bridge, keep going into the tower, go up the second ladder (its around
the corner). "Usually" if someone is camping the ass the whole tower will be
frog-free. Be much safer if you get someone to guide ya there though
