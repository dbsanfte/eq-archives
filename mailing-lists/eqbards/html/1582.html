I had a hard time finding the named Centaur at first, too. He's in the
building that's most West. He's around the back of the pole, so you have to
go all the way in. He stands about 15 feet tall, or so it seems to my half
elf, a bit taller than the other Centaurs.

I played in Paw for about a week and made near 100 plat. This was fighting
Nobles, Commanders, Shaman, and Rexx or Lenrel, almost exclusively. The
SnakeSkin mask and gloves are very good sellers at 5 pp, and the Tunic will
go for 7 pp if you're willing to wait for a Druid to realize he or she needs
it. :P I have only been able to get about 20 pp for the RTS', and those
spawned VERY slow. I managed to find 2 in the week I was there. It's a
very fun dungeon, I wish I had gone over before level 14 so I could enjoy
some of the other spawn spots, than just Rexx's area or Lenrel's.
