Subject: Equipping weapon(s)/shield and instruments


Yesterday I filled out a feedback form asking for some kind of hot-key
for us bards to switch from our equipped weapon(s)/shield to an
instrument. Kinda like how you can switch from your melee weapon to your
range weapon. I dunno about anyone else, but its a real pain when you
want to sit down and rest after a battle to open up your persona, click
on the Inv button above your player image, then click on your weapon,
drop it in your inv, click on your shield (or secondary weapon), drop it
in your inv, and then get your lute (or other instrument) which may be
in a backpack or something, and put it in your secondary hand. And then
by the time you've done that, you're fully healed, so you have to do the
exact reverse.
This may sound like a tedious task, but when you're in danger or trying
to equip your drum while running for your life, it really makes a
difference. Or when you have to tell your party members to hold on while
your equip or unequip your instrument before they take off on you.
I have my mouse sensitivity all the way up, and I keep all my
instruments and weapons in my inv slots (not in backpacks) so I can do
this as quickly as possible, but its still not a very quick operation,
even if it is done smoothly.
The other alternative is to never, or hardly ever use a weapon/shield
and just use hand to hand. But thats not always wise, especially when
soloing, or with a small group who depend on your 16 or 17 dam from your
minotaur axe to keep the situation from getting ugly.
If anyone else has feels the same way about this, PLEASE fill out a
feedback form in-game by typing /feedback and requesting such an option.

Good day to you all, and may your songs of battle bring pride upon us.
