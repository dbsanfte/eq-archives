Congrats on the levels :)

Personally, I don't think you should worry about kiting too much, unless its
fear-kiting. I know lots of bards that swear by chain-kiting but I think
its a waste of time. If your brass skill is decent you won't have any
problem keeping a high blue mob feared... sure it will take some time to
kill but its actaully faster than chain-kiting. If you find you spend most
of your time with groups though, don't worry about this too much even. I
would focus more on the charm/mes method of crowd control more - its very
useful.

Gear wise, you should go for the DE mask. Mostly because Lower Guk Assasin
room is kickass exp in the 30s. The Lambent quests should be all VERY easy
for you to do yourself with a group. You just have to convince them that
going to West Karana is FUN.
