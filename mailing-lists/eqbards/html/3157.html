Come on people - we should be more creative that just coming up with two ideas!

My apologies if anyone takes this personally, but, I think "Juggling" and "Weaving" are just as bad (well maybe slightly less so) than "Twisting". I'm not a clown; I'm not a textile worker as well as not being a gymnast. I'm a musician.

The term I like: "Riffing"... It seems to me - that we are playing five or six seconds of a song - so it would basically just be a riff... Musically speaking, of course, "riffs" are associated more with instrumental music than vocal music - but I think it fits a hell of a lot better than twisting, juggling, weaving, or heaven-forbid "medlying"
