Spectres (and other mobs) agro is also partly based on a level check. The
Spec island when fully populated has at least 4 (one each cornor) and 3
(tower lower and 2 upper). At 33, given the social range, you will get
multiple spectres. Also, your hp is no where near high enough to sustain
any amount of damage from them. At level 45, it was damn hard for me to
solo spectres there. The problem being getting multiple pulls and resists.
By 47, it was loads easier to pull singles and I still used Lament alot. I
just dont see this as being a productive venture for you at 33.

On the other hand, you should be able to find loads of stuff to charm kite
in the great outdoors over in Kunark.
