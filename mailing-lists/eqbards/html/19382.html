Other than that, I finished the Jarsath Medallion Quest - one of three
needed for getting the key to Veeshan's Peak. You need to kill the ancient
jarsath in Firona Vie, the bloodgill maurader that guards the gates to the
underwater zone in Lake of Ill Omen, and find the piece in the Swamp of No
Hope. Then off to Timourus Deep and the archeologist. Give him 3 pieces and
he makes the medal. Gives it back to you because "oh my! I feel this is
cursed! here take it back!"

To get a key to Veeshan's Peak, you need the 3 medals (each requires 3 sub
pieces) and 'proof of Trakanon's demise.' So, I have to wonder, will Trak
