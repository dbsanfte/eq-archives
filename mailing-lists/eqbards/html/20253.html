The Scorpicus are between level 30 and 35 I think. And they are SK's, which
means harm touch and low level necro spells. They also give bad Venril
Sather faction, so use care in case you happen to run into the evil outpost
for groceries (which are expensive as hell, anyone else notice that???) I
assume you are interested in the scorpicus scrounger for the Nostrolo
Tambourine quest. Well, in case it helps, the other day I and a level 42
wizzie killed one. Basically I ran over to where the chasm starts (where
they live), tracked the nearest Scorpicus Scrounger, and ran in with Selos
and Elemental Rythms going. Tagged him (and another unfortunately decided
to join, although the rest left me alone) and carefully drew him out towards
the zone where my wizzie was waiting. Keep Selos going (just in case), but
go slowly or you will lose it. Also keep Elemental Rythms going to protect
you from the stuff he'll try to hit you with. We finished it off fairly
quickly.
