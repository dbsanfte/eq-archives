feel like less of a weirdo. I've played bards in AD&D for about as long as I
have been playing, only to have other people that I RPG with snigger and ask
why I would want to. EQ, like some of the other MUD's I have played over the
years, really does a good job at making the bard fun to play. It's great to
randomly group with people who say things like "You bards kick ass" or
