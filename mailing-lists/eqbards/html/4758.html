Actually call me crazy but juggling songs is one of the main reasons I enjoy
playing a bard.

Every other class I've played, I get exceptionally bored during a fight. As
you're either waiting to cast, or waiting for kick/etc to recycle just to
hit it again.

Whereas with a bard, I'm constantly doing something, not to mention making
fast decisions like switch out weapons to instruments, or should I cycle in
a different song because of some change in the combat. Even after combat,
I'm still juggling hymn & clarity to minimize down time. I'd much rather be
doing that than staring at a spell book.
