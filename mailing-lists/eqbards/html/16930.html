IMHO, Mistmoore is better with few people -- as long as you don't
have anyone incompetent trying to hunt the castle. The castle trains
are what really kill you. We head for zone if we see anyone in
steel armor, or anyone hunched over out in the courtyard. That's castle
stuff and WAY too high for us.

At 20, Anna and I are staying out in the courtyard (down the ramp from
the zone... There is a pathing problem trying to get things up to the zone
that makes that not a good place to fight. If you fight up on the hill
next to the lake, then you might manage to avoid attracting the attention
of some of the castle trains if you're VERY quiet and lucky.) Occasionally,
she will try a pull, with a full group, of a glyphed from the graveyard.
I do not know what level these are, but just one is a good fight for us,
even with groups ranging up to about 25. It may depend somewhat on
what classes are the various levels. You might want to start in the
courtyard
pulling from the graveyard, and only move to the graveyard if you're
handling that
pretty well.

I know, the last time we were in Mistmoore, the 25 druid/paladin team with
us
was anxious to move to graveyard. We chose not to because Anna and I were
not comfortable enough with survivability there.
