Lake Rathe is great until level 23 if you the boredom doesn't get to
you after a couple of levels. After Lake Rathe head to the Aviak City in
South Karana. If you don't get burned out on Aviaks, you can stay there
until level 27 or 28 easily.. longer if you just go after mainly Avocets.
Pretty good loot on the mobs in the Aviak City too.
