For anyone who doesn't know, Rokenn is right about how poisons work. Meaning, poisons do have "counters". Poisons have a counter system, meaning the weakest poison will have 1 counter, and the strongest may have 10 (I don't know what the actual numbers are), and that any given cure poison type spell will remove a certain number of those counters. I *belive* in the spdat file it lists what poisons and what cures have what effect. *Shrug*. I think the same is true for disease, but am not completely sure.
