Reed,

The damage values for the 3 chant songs don't seem to be calculated
correctly. Based on the info on the eqsongs website, at 50th level with a
Selos Drum I should be doing the following amount of damage:

Level 30: 31 base damage per tick/42 per tick with Selos Drum/125 total for
3 ticks. Your spreadsheet lists 62 damage total for 50th level with Selos
drum

Level 38: 48 base damage per tick/65 per tick with Selos drum/195 total for
3 ticks. Your spreadsheet lists 93 damage total for 50th level with Selos
drum.

Level 46: Same as 38 except your spreadsheet list 95 damage total for 50th
level with Selos drum.

I think the values that are listed take into account the 2/3 PvP penalty.
But even if you factor that in, the values don't seem to be correct.

tick while you list 75. Eqsongs lists the speed debuff as 16% while you
list 30%.
