"toon"? :-)

I'm really only comfortable answering this question as I have a lot of
experience with the gargs out here. I was able to solo gargs at 23rd level
using the all instrument attack (DDD,COD,Hymn). The tougher gargs, however,
I ended up kicking in selo's and weaving in bellow & hymn while running
circles on one side of the island (safe fall will come in real handy out
here when you get it). It's a little dangerous running circles as you can
also pick up other gargs. I've pulled other gargs by accident and I survived
by just doing the same thing, selo's, bellow, hymn (and an occasional jig as
your stamina gets real low).

You can also run around the perimeter of the island, but beware of people
fighting spectres. Also, I fight in 3rd person view so it was a pain
switching views as the island gets in the way.

So you might be able to solo gargs at level 20, but you'll most likely end
up having to spend a lot of time running and bellowing towards the end of
the fights.
