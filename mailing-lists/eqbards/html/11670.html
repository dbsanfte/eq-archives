He spawns in the jail. Go like you are going to the zone to the dead side
of lower (hugging right side the upper) Before you get to the place where
the bugs are on the rocks and you cross that catwalk, there are some forks
off to the left as you head for dead zone. (It might be way before - there
are at least 2 left forks. Explore some. Take the first left fork and then
a second left fork (hard left I think). Look for the 'jail' - a room with a
platform in the center and cells with troll prisoners in them and some
guards. And there maybe a good reason you have not seen the Warden - hehe
he spawns invis! So have see invis on. One spot between 2 cells is where
he spawns - if not him then his place holder. Kill and wait 18 min. Kill and
wait. Warden is about 1 in 5 spawn. Elf hide gloves are the common drop.
THB is the uncommon. Prolly 3 or 4 to 1.

I went there when the rest of the room was too green to agro on us. Not
sure what happens if you are mid/upper 20's. Prolly need a full party to
take and hold the room.
