Actually, that's not why the Burned Out Lightstones were implemented. The
occurance of Greater Lightstones and Lightstones on wisps is unchanged, but
instead of getting nothing (which is what used to happen) you will now get a
Burned Out Lightstone.

The reason they did this is so that ALL wisps have a lightsource - so you
can no longer tell by how much light they were giving off whether they had a
lightstone or not. (It used to be that you could run around killing ONLY the
wisps that gave off light, and would be assured of a lightstone each time.
Now you must kill em all and taken your chances.)
