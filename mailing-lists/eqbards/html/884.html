Subject: Juggling justified


This is an idea that occurred to me in the time when all I can do is
THINK about EQ. Perhaps the bards magical songs are short. Perhaps the
magic is a short spell set to music. Each tick then, is one singing of
the spell. If you sing for multiple ticks, you sing the spell multiple
times. Twisting then is singing one spell, and then another, and back.

Though a bard is an entertainer and a storyteller, is it not right to
say that the songs with magical effects are not meant for
entertainment?

I'm not saying it would sound pretty to sing one song then another, but
this explanation felt better to me than to think that I am singing two
songs at the same time.
