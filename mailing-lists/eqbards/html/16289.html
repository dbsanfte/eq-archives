I initially thought this as well, but the post that I sent out earlier
showed that Gordon was not planning on changing policy on servers like
Prexus. On Prexus if you want into the Kings room and there is another
group in there already, you can request to be added to that groups waiting
list. Whenever a member of that group leaves, the next person of the
appropriate class on the waiting list gets the call to come and join the
hunt.

Gordon seemed to me to make it clear that this was a fully acceptable way to
deal with the other group that wanted to camp the same spot and the
GMs/Guides would not force the group in there to split the spawn. If this
is truely a reality, this policy should barely touch life on Prexus
generally every single camp there is works on the "list" principle anyhow...
