All the mage classes have a line of spells starting with Shieldskin (wizards
get it at 16th). It costs a spell component. Unfortunately, it is for the
self only and will absorb up to a certain amount of damage. Later on they
get Leatherskin, and later an even better one.
Now the cool thing about Enchanters is they have a similar spell that they
CAN cast on others, and it is very effective. It also gives the same message
that your magical skin absorbs the blow.
