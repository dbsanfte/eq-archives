Ok first AC versus STATS
================================
Should we base our armor on AC or stats? I decided to go
with the Bronze but I loose a little bit of stats while weighting more.

The armor I own and can wear:
---------------------------------
Small Bronze: Helm AC 10, Bracers AC 7, Chest AC 17, Arms AC8,
Legs AC 10, Boots AC 8 and Cloak AC 8
Above = Good AC (about 75) and Looks but heavy (about 45).

-OR-

Small Banded: Helm AC 8, Bracers AC 6, Boots AC 6 Cloak AC 7
Dwarven Ringmail Tunic = AC 12 and 7 against Heat/Cold/Magic
Barbed Legplates = AC 8 and 2 AGI
Barbed Armplates = AC 7 and 2 DEX
Above = Less AC (about 54) but more stats and weight less (about 28)

Other example: Thick Leather Apron AC 8 weight 1
or Troll Hide Belt AC 5 but 5 STR 5 STA
