From your description of the area, and the level of your group
it sounds like you were in the Preacher Dias room. Which is
perfect for a party of low 20s. While the preacher drops some
nice things, you won't find a war axe or icy legs here.

That area is beyond the two gates past the flag room. In
the King Throne room, you'll find most goblins are L20-25,
the elites L25-L30, and the named goblins are L27-L33, where
King Thex'Ka IV is the highest (hits for double 70).

The elite goblins will drop the icy greaves, and the elite
honor guard will drop the silvery war axes 1h/2h.

This area requires a minimum L25 full group to tackle effectively.
And even then, it's definitely a challenge until you are
around L35.
