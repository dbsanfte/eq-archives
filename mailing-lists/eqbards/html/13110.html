The closest place I know where you could buy a flute is in Rathe Mountains
from the gypsies. I remember reading on this board that someone who had died
would just go and kill a lizardmen with their DDD/COD songs and then buy a
flute.

The other idea that John Kim mentioned a while back(and someone else more
recently), was that before you go to Cazic, load up on flutes, duel a friend
in Ogguk and have him kill you, then loot everything except the flutes, so
that you'd have a storage corpse.
