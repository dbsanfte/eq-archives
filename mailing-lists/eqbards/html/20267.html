I'm in Ytrilynth's Horde. I'm the only bard in the guild who is a primary,
but the guild bard I was grouped with last night has been playing a lot
recently (his primary is a druid), and we have a couple other lower level
bards who play every once in a while.

Three bards was a blast. We had one bard doing all instrument CoD, HoR,
DDD. Another doing Warmth, Anthem, Bellow. And I was doing Binding,
Chains, and Bellow. We weren't fighting any casters, and I was the highest
level bard in the group, so those seem to be the best twists.

Myself and the guild bard were using weapons (he has dual yaks -- twink!).
The only song I think that we could have changed was Anthem for Chant of
Battle, but we still had ALOT of fun. ;-)

We were constant pull the whole time in Lake of Ill Omen. I ended up making
a bub in about 45min or so before the group broke up because of people
needing to do homework.
