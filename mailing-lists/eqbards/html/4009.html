I used to keep the Jig memorized for stamina loss, but you're
a bard. If you start to run low on stamina and the group has
plenty of tanks, it's not out of the question for you to swap
your weapons for a lute and start weaving Hymn for the added
healing bonus while you "rest up." You may also be surprised
how effective your group can be with you using an instrument.

At L28 if I run low on stamina while soloing, I just hit the
monster I'm fighting with Pixie Strike over and over while I
rest and heal. :-)

Once upon a time, that would've been an awesome price for a
Pugius. But ever since Dvinn started dropping the Dragoon
Dirk every time due to a bug (since fixed), the bottom has
dropped out of the prices on low-to-mid end piercing weapons.

If it weren't for the Dirk, I'd put the price on a Pugius at
around 80-100pp. But with the Dirk selling for 35pp, I'd say
25pp is about right.
