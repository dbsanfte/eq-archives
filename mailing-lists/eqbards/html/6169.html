I have in fact encountered the named SG Hatar in Oasis.
I was doing some safe but pretty boring croc hunting near the docks with a
friend,
when we saw an SG stoll by. Look a named SG, and he cons yellow! Lets get
him! If i remember correctly I was lvl 18 at the time.
So we jump him and soon discovers that he is one tough yellow, hitting for
some 50 a punch, but none of the 120 point hits other SGs has scored
(without killing though, I must add :). Oh well, we sound the retreat and
bolt for the zone.
Back again in the zone we quickly gather three more ppl to join us
for that rare SG hunting expedition at this lvl.
We soon find him in the desert near N Ro and promptly charged.
Again a tough battle began with Hatar swinging left and right. After a short
while most group members are down to half health, but so is Hatar and we
feel that we are winning, when suddenly BOOM and Hatar falls dead to the
ground. Cheers break out but I think wtf no exp!
I look around and, oh yes, there he is the high lvl KS nuker standing at
safe distance.
Oh well I think, hadnt had a chance to loot anyway in the right click frenzy
thats sure to take place in this quickly assembled group.
And now I can claim that I have stood my ground against an SG and lived to
tell the tale!

If I'm lucky enough to meet him again I will certainly give it a second try.

Oh, and greetings to you all! This is my first post to this excellent list
which I have enjoyed for a while now.
