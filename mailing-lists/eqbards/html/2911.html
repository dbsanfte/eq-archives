Lavastorm. I am level 14 and it is a great soloing place. Instructions:

As you enter the Nektulos (use your drum, you very much want the speed) hug the zone to your left,
swim the piranha infested river (leaving a chunk or two of your flesh as a toll) and fly all the way
to Lavastorm. Do not gawk at the stone guardians *laughs* as a Teir'Dal they are your good friends,
as a Fier'Dal they will kill ya dead.

In lavastorm go after the Fire drakes, rock dervishes and maybe the fire imps. Even greens were
giving exp at 14 btw. Avoid the Fire Elementals, blues were kicking my butt. You might do okay with
a green. Lava Crawlers are WAY more than they appear. I generally hunt the area between the entrance
and Najena (keeping me close to a zone *laughs*).
Use Elemental rhythms constantly on Drakes, Imps and Elems. I was weaving Anthem, Elemental Rhythms
and Bellow. Though it depends on your weapon, I am finding using Bellow after Bellow (on regular
monsters, or say, the Rock Dervishes) to not be as effective as Anthem/Bellow/Bellow/Anthem because
Anthem with my Dragoon Dagger is a very very potent mix.

Anyway, have fun, oh and occasionally a Stone Guardian will wander close to the Lavastorm Entrance
in Neriak, so just prepare yourself, you may not be zoning to safety.

On the other hand, I just kept laughing that I was there at all. On Rallos Zek (where I played until
it was maliciously and negligently hozed by Verant) as a wood elf I would have been hunted down like
a dog. *laughs*, so enjoy your reletive freedom in that part of the world! :o)
