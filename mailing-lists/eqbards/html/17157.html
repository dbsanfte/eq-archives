That's great! Congrats on getting the BP so quick! I've spent countless
hours at the AOF and I "might" have seen one. :)

One other thing I forgot to mention, which you discovered (sorry for it
being late to help you : /)... The sewers are somewhat tough to pull from
when unbroken. There are typically 2 lizards in each room and several
gators in the water. The gators kind of blend in with the water making it
difficult to know how many you're pulling. You can also miss gators and go
to the next room and of course they will jump on the train on the way back.
Basically, overpulls from the sewers are a fact of life.

Maybe whoever heads there next can make use of this extra stuff. :)
