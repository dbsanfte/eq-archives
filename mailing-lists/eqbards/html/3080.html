May I HIGHLY recommend soloing the aquagoblins on the rocks just inside the Ocean of Tears from
Butcherblock (not on the spires which I suspect are a deathtrap from falling). Jump off the boat and
climb up on the rock that has the two Alluring Sirens. There are two aquagoblins on that rock and
another close by with 3 on it. Destroy any rusty weapons and fill your pack with bows (2gp each from
the High elf at BB dock if you charm him). Great exp, good loot and great fun.To get back on the
boat swim towords it when it is coming at you, angle up and you will pop up inside.

Once you hit 13 may I then suggest wisps in Erudes crossing (assuming you have a magic weapon) for
total tedium but GREAT money and exp, exploring Kithicor for fun and danger (good exp, little
money), go back to wack gnoll commanders in Blackbullow :o) for being such bullies when you were
weaker.

At 14 try Lavastorm! Woooo what a blast. I am soloing there now (hit 15 last night). As bards here
is the tip: blue Firedrakes and Stone Dervishes (maybe rock bascilisks) GOOD, blue and even kinda
green Fire Elems BAD. Blue Fire Elems will kick your butt into next tuesday. Same for Rock Crawlers.
I am guessing that Feerot might be good to solo now too.
