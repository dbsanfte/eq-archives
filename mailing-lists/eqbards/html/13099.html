DEX and CHA are checked to start a song. Each song has a difficulty%
associated with it. The higher your DEX+CHA is, the more you'll succeed at
singing. The higher your level is, the more you'll succeed also. Some songs
are harder than others, but will become trivial (still 5% or so minimum
failure rate) with some more levels.
For combat DEX only affects the procs of weapons (and works like strentgh
for ranged weapons, ie. increases the damage dealt upon a successful hit,
which we don't care much about).
CHA is used to modify the succes rate of the mesmerize / charm and memory
blur spells, and thus our songs.
So if you wanna be best at crowd control, raise CHA over DEX. If you wanna
deal more damage do the opposite and use procing weapons. Best bet is to
have 2 sets of equipment.

On the other stats now, namely STR, AGI and AC. The to hit % is ONLY skill
based (ie. depends on the your level and the MOB's, along with the
defense/offense/dodge/parry/riposte skills), with the exception of AC (which
is also factored in that calculation). So raise AC to get hit less (you also
need to get your defensive skill maxed but I guess that each and every bard
maxes them pretty fast, ahem). STR will make the attacker hit for more upon
a successfull hit and AGI will lower the damage taken by the target. These
are not worthless stats, though I think CHA / DEX and AC are 1st priority.
