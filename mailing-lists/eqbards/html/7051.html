U gots armer to twink with? What size? If not twinking, then just thinking
ahead to what size u wanna wear - Halfies are stuck with med, woodies can

I'd say STR is a major stat for bards. It sets how much u can carry (which
will matter if u wear bronze plate) and how good your hits are (dmg). You
can never have too much str in my book.

Agi & Dex are important but have diminishing returns around 100 and these
seem to be easier to boost with items than Str. These help determine how
often you hit (ie don't miss) and how good u can dodge (ie don't get hit.)
So these are important as well.

Then Cha - rumored to be good for making songs "stick" (less resists) know
to be good for buying/selling. Also easily boosted with items but figure 90
or 95 base un boosted and the the ez to buy silk evening tunic gets u +5 and
u r at 100. more items will be a bonus.

Wis & Int are least important in my book. They determine mana and ability
to perform and learn skills. You will have enough time to learn your skills
in melee combat so don't worry.
