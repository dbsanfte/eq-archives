Greetings Reed and welcome!!

Will leveling get easier? Hmmm - yes and no. Each level higher you get
takes more xp imho. Some levels are by design twice as hard as regular
levels - they are called the "Hell" levels: 30, 35, 40 and 45 (imho 50 is
the ultimate Hell as well) but the teens are real tough for bards as well.
But it can get easier if you get into groups and if you can form a team that
plays together on a regular basis - it will be easier and more fun. You
should find a guild that you like and that has players who play at your
level and at your same amount of time in the game. The last time I was
able to do "a level in a day" was back at level 24 for me. It was hard to
do even though I could always do it before if I set my mind to it and
endured long camps.

But I hate camping too! It's dull and boring for the most part.
Unfortunately, it is a pretty big part of the game as you get older. Most
bards prefer to group and most groups will want to camp. So, while it may
be possible to get to high levels without camping, I do think it would take
a lot longer and it would be a lot harder. But you should play the game the
way you are happy with it and don't do stuff that you don't enjoy.

So welcome and please feel free to tell us about your adventures and we are
happy to help answer your questions ;)
