I ran into fellow Bard Umbriel on Fennin Ro and she wanted the recipe for
the Birthday Cake that I showed her. I made my first chocolate bar at a
Baking Skill of 135 and it was trivial. It's the toughie because Brownie
Parts are less common than hen's teeth. Cake Rounds you make by getting
your Pottery Skill up. The Birthday Cake itself was not trival at 135,
although I did succeed in my attempts and made 6 cakes for a friend's
wedding. A rumored Wedding Cake recipe has never been confirmed.

It was fun questing around to make those six cakes. I spent literally hours
on them, mostly in tracking down Brownie Parts: "/auc Buying Brownie Parts
(yes, I'm serious)" Then there are people that think Brownie Parts must be
needed for some quest and want 100pp for them like a Lambent Stone. Haha..
I'm not sure they believe I'm just making cakes.

- Birthday Cake -
1 Winter Chocolate
1 Clump of Dough
1 Frosting
1 Cake Round
Combine in Oven, makes two cakes

- Clump of Dough -
1 Snake Egg
1 Milk
1 Cup of Flour
Combine in Mixing Bowl, makes one clump

- Winter Chocolate -
1 Brownie Parts
2 Frostings (separate slots)
Combine in Mixing Bowl, makes one bar
