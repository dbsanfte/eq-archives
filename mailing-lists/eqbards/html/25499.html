The mobs aren't stupider, getting assistance just isn't automatic.

You'll hear it call when it does and then look out! Mobs that you would
expect to "swarm" in EQ will come running for you from a far greater agro
range than you might expect. So while those other hobgoblins won't always
assist, when they do it will be very painful.
