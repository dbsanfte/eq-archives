Subject: Praised....


Last night my friend and I managed to get invited into the Lord group. When
we arrived the group was killing 2 boks, after they were dead 2 people left
and we replaced them. 10 minutes after joining the Lord spawned with a Yak
and we killed him. I won the roll with a 52 =P

The next time round we had 2 boks spawn and being only level 40 I was a bit
dubious as to whether Kelins Lugubrious Lament would be resisted. Our
warrior was gonna pull them both but I said wait. Told the cleric to get
ready to heal me big time, I then attempted the Lament on the right bok. It
stuck, I turned round and chained the left bok and he came solo. The group
was impressed and I got comments like wow, great pull mad, excellent work
and bards are great. I was pleased to see comments like this as I often
feel that bards are only regarded as crack dealers. It's good to let other
people see the value of bards in a group over than mana song.

Anyway, pulling the 1 bok enable us to break the spawn and made life
easier, shortly after we finished the 2nd bok another Lord with the Yak
spawned (I didn't roll). All in all a good night, got a Yak and a Burlupp
sack and am less than a bubble to level 41.
