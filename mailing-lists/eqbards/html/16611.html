At 24th I solo'd the following areas:

Rathe Mtn: Skeletons
Lakerathe: Aviak Guards on 3spawn on island
Oasis: "A orcs", DW Goblin pets, DW Croc/Caim
Najena: TT's (there are 4)
Unrest: picked off train stragglers
Bugfallen
Cazic: Lizards from maze
S Karana: Elephants, some Centaurs, some Aviaks, some Gnolls (hard when it
rains)
W. Karana: Scythe beatles, some borers still give xp. Griffenes (bellow
kiting)
Highpass (orcs still give some xp)
Kithicor (corner near HP): 3 orc spawn. gave great xp till 25/26 (boring
though and dangerous at night)
GukTop: Scryer Room and sneak down to Warden to get elf hide gloves/troll
hide belt (CAREFUL!)

Lots of other places
