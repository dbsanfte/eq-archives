Greetings and well met Brothers and Sisters of the art.
I bid thee all well. I find the talk of charging for
corpse retrieval a rather gruesome buisness. I would
remind ye my brothers and sisters that we cannot long
strike out on our own and engendering ill will by
charging for our aid, while economical for us perhaps
at lower levels will most certainly turn poor later.
Remember there are many music critics to start with,
to inspire more is folly.

I find that merely volunteering my aid often allows
a reward of often great sums. What's more as they
give of themselves many seek me out later an offer
their aid for something I need or have not.. many
a party have I found myself desired in comprised of
such companions. My advise dear fellows of the art
charge nothing.. if one treats you poorly remember
and spread the word especially to this list. In days
of yore in the world we call the real world. Bards were
of the druidic order and kept lore of all the families..
it was said better a thousand deaths then the scorn of a
bard. Let us bring such truth to Norrath as well.. be
merciful to all... and let us truely create the lore
good and bad of Norrath.

Good fortune to ye all.. May you all be worthy of song.

Adon Bard of Freeport, Povar Server, Singer of glorious
Veeshan
