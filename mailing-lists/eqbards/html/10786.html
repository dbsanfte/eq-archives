the hall to the water where you swim to the right again to get to the
underwater tunnel to the bedroom. To the left is the hall to the slime then

spiral staircase to where the necro wand drops on the floor and the
waterfall. I guess that there is a zone out from the waterfall to the upper
guk. If you swim the wrong way from the bedroom, I do believe there is an
underwater zone to the water by the ancient croc on the upper side.

Also, if you have wrecked your precious live froggie faction by killing one
or two, I am told you can get it back by hanging around the ppl fighting the
shin frogs and playing heal song (I guess you got to group unless you just
want to count on the faction bug to get your factions back.) I guess you
could bop them one time and hope the other guy kills em. Dead froggies
don't like live froggies? Hmmm.

I have been trying to work the hall trash and back room for xp. Don't think
