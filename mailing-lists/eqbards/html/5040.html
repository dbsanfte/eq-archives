I have used this song for getting down off of high places (Aviak Tower in
South Karana). The song will set you down on the ground if you teleport off
the tower.

Another was in dueling outdoors. I has dueling a Paladin once, and he was
using Flash of Light to blind me, most annoying. So when he would blind me
I would fire off MMM and then bellow him till my sight returned. On a
simliar note I have used it outdoors to get the little bit of room needed to
take off running when the battle goes bad. This works very well for a party
as the group gets dispersed in all directions. Giving everyone a better
chance of getting out alive (except for the unlucky person that gets the
critters attention).
