Being that I have a level 21 warrior, I can tell you that it isn't possible
to taunt anything off of someone else, especially if the person keeps
whacking or casting. I have my taunt maxed, and usually max it at new
levels within one or two fights. I can taunt any green or blue off of
someone in 2 taunts, any white off in 4 taunts (unless they are below 2
bubbles health), but yellows and reds can be *extremely* hard to taunt off,
in that our skill isn't high compared to whatever it is that makes mobs
"resist" the taunts.

Don't rely on a warrior to take all the damage if you are dumb and get 5
mobs on you. BTW, I taunt the entire time during a fight, and I've seen
wizards stand next to the mob and peel if off of me in one cast, then I have
to turn my attention elsewhere and waste time trying to peel while getting
hit from behind.
