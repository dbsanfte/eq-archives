Well, at level 6 your supposed to be hunting stuff in qeynos hills but
there are those two annoying druids NPCs that will KOS (Kill on sight)
anyone fighting animals. I found that area very annoying so I suggest
that you dash through BB (it's a fairly safe short run at peak time
when all the surface gnolls are camped.) and get yourself bound in
Halas. Then hunt the various Ice goblin camps and the edges of the
open area for possible blue/white critters. It's a beautiful zone and
the snow makes it easier to see in the dark.

If you feel like some travel to a very empty zone take the ship to
Eurdin, work your way through the city to the Tox forest. As you come
out the gate head straight south through the newbie zone till you hit
the bridge that crosses the river (roughly 0 100), cross and hunt the
kobald camps and poachers (their heads are a good quest item at your
level.

Just a couple of suggestion, there are many other areas. Umm actually
if you feel rally brave you can sprint to Misty Thicket near
Rivervale. I think it is the best place to level up to 10-12th in the
game.
