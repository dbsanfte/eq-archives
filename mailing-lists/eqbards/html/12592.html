Subject: RE: Twisting bug?


It used to happen many months ago and I've seen it resurface lately. This
thing nails me at least 60 times an hour. In combat I often twist 3 songs,
Warsong, DDD, Chains. If there is very little lag I tend to hit Warsong just
as it is coming down. What is happening is that the server indicates that it
is coming down. The client then indicates that it is going back up. The
server, thinking that the client is trying to cheat or something, demands
that it is coming down and drops the song effect. (okay, so that's not
exactly what it is thinking. :)
When I get this a lot I often switch to a 4 song rotation to ensure that
the first song is down for a few seconds before it has a chance of coming
back up. Sure, I am not under the influence of Warsong for about 3 seconds
but that is better than not being effected for 10 seconds.

Worst song effect to have this happen: Selo's :)
