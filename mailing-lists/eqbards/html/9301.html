Hi Evulia =)

Lament (Song 8) is used 'pre-battle' to prevent other mobs from
joining in and helping a friend. A sucessfull lament will _NOT_
add you to a mob's hate list, thus they will still be standing there
waiting for you to pull it.

Lullabye (Song 15) is used to 'sleep' mobs once they have already
attacked. Typically, as long as no one hits htem, and they are
'nodding', they are just standing there. Good crowd control. It is
an AE (Area of effect) song.

Melodic Binding (song 20) is an AE song that slows the mobs down (ie
attacks) and lowers their AC slightly. Takes the sting out of hard
hitters.

Chain (Song 23 i think) is essentially the same as ensnare (druid)
It 'slows' the mob down when it trys to run. (Similar to root, but
the wonderful thing about snare is that a snared running monster
doesnt fight back, but a 'rooted' monster DOES fight back. Same for
casters.

(Works wonderfully with 'Fear' (song 26)) (snare the monster, then
fear them, and the mob sits there and trys to run away while you beat
it in the back =)) (repeat as the monster comes unfear'd)

Charm is where you take control of a mob and force it to attack another
mob. (or sit down or whatever else pets do =)) (10 sec duration)
