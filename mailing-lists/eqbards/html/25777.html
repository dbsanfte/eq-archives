Hiyas,
I soloed in EW thru my late 40s. Charm kiting is really the way to go there. Once your pet is worn down, chant it to death. Yes it gets easier once you have chant of flame but the xp is great. fear kiting ulthorks and doing the tusk quest in CS is also an option. Coldain villages offer the best charm kiting in my opinion -- simply because you have less risk of adds. If you are on dorf faction tho you might consider the giant fort instead. Once you get chant of flame, chant kiting becomes an option. I have to agree that SoW potions give you a much easier time. By the time you get your third chant, you'll be able to kite snow griffins while running backwards (SoW potions don't really give you enough speed for this so you should either mem anti magic aria to stop griffin-SoW or chain them if you use potions for speed -- Griffs are a good money maker because there are always those trying to do ring quests who need a no drop egg from them and either can't track or get a group to help them )
