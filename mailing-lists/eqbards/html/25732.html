Hi Fohat,
Magical weapons are always dropping in price and one of the best times to buy them is during off-peak hours in the Commons. Why? Because people get tired of trying to sell stuff and want to get some sleep -- You never know what cool deals you'll find. )
Look for a dragoon dirk if you can. They go for 30-40pp and when you upgrade, it can sit in your range slot and you'll still get the MR and dex from it...I carried a dragoon dirk in my range slot until I could afford a Runed Bone Fork. If you don't have 30-40 pp then hunt bears until you get a HQ pelt and auction it for 40pp or trade for a dragoon dirk -- You might get lucky and a tailor with extra gear in the bank will give you a deal...
Find a friend who has luclin and ask him/her to buy you those songs. If you are in a guild, it should be easy -- if you are not in a guild, look for one -- guilds offer a whole new perspective on the game if you join a good one.
Warsong is a self-only buff and it is a bit better than anthem which is for grouping -- I recommend it highly if you are soloing -- but with hymm under your belt, you'll be a welcome addition to most groups. ...and bellow WILL tell you the damage you do. I used it for pulling until I hit lvl 30. Damage is unimpressive from this song but it costs no mana and is seldom resisted and is a good interupt when you fight/duel casters. you'll get a lot of use from it )
Yours in Song
