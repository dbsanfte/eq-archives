Mez's stack by length of mez, not level. A longer duration mez will not
overrite a shorter duration mez. So, SoT will always overrite an enchanter
mez and block the enchanter from remezing. The level of the bard/enchanter
has no effect.

On the brighter side I spoke to GZ at the Fan Faire this weekend and they
thinking about allowing chanters to overrite bard mez.
