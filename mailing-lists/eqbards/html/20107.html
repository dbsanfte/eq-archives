My experience with Largo's is that it is safe to sing in dungeon settings.
The radius of the AE is small enough that it will only effect the mobs you
are attacking. I have never noticed it aggroing mobs thru walls or in rooms
above/below you. I don't think the z-axis bug comes into play with this
song. I have this song memmed all the time when there is a ranger/druid or
when there is no shaman in the group.
