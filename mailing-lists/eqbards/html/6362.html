There are only three mobs that spawn in the Warlord room. Their spawn is 20
minutes Two Elite Goblin Warriors and the Warlord himself. They don't always
spawn together, they spawn 20 minutes after you kill them, so if things are
worked out right you fight one Gob roughly every 7 minutes. The Elites don't
all cast, but some do, and the Warlord almost always does. They use Area
Effect spells that give my TNT2 card horrendous lag. If your video lags under
big spell effects, you might want to consider turning them down to minimum or
off completely while in this room. I'd suggest Anthem, Guardian Rhythms and
Elemental Rhythms since you won't be tanking. I'd use the drum too, because
those higher level chars will not have any trouble with the spawn if it's
spaced out in the slightest.
