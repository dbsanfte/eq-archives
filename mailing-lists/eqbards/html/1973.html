I made the mistake of giving the screaming mace to my group's
cleric. She got so giddy about the damage she was doing with
it that she tried to melee DVinn with us. Apparently the AI
is smart enough to try to interrupt spellcasters. Every time
she tried to cast a heal spell on one of us, Dvinn would

and our rogue died, I zoned with one bubble left. She learned
her lesson though. :-)

If you haven't figured out yet, kill stealing of the higher
mobs in Crushbone is a problem. Talk to people, get friendly
with them, and be nice. Nothing is funnier than someone
falsely accusing you of kill stealing (which is another thing
kill stealers seem to like to do) and to have a half dozen
people speak up saying how nice a guy you are. I've actually
given away a dwarven axe, ringmail tunic, and bracer of battle
to random folks who've helped me. Of course, the notion of
accusing a bard of kill stealing is rather ridiculous. If a
bard can steal your kill, you probably couldn't have killed it
by yourself in the first place. :-)
