Ok, I did some testing last night and confirm John's observations. Seems
like Guardian is great for stacking with other songs but NOT the primary
anti-magic song to play.

Some results:

Guardian only: MR 66 with MM Drum 90
Elemental only: MR 66 with MM Drum 90
Stacked only: MR 85 with MM Drum 136

The other reason I had for testing was a ranger friend recently got a Lute
of the Gypsy Princess (LotGP which Lyssa Catalog Libaretto reveals as:
Mynthis Lute) in a trade and was trying to determine it's worth (maybe he
would sell it - any clue what is fair price? someone mentioned 200pp). So,
I said this new patch maybe has changed what it does and we went to test.
Sadly, it looks like it has no special powers for a 35 bard in hymn or
chords.

Chords: no instrument 6; lute 13; LotGP 13
Hymn: no instrument 13; lute 20; LotGP 20

Later, my group tried to go to Kedge Keep - get equipped for underwater
breathing and swim due East from Unrest - stay on the bottom and look for
the broken pillars - reminiscent of Najena.

We (at least most if us) zoned in and were greeted by a "stiletto piranha" -
conned red to a 36 ranger and blue to a 40 SK (presumably put right there at
he zone to eat solo artists). Chomp chomp - we killed him - long melee.
Have your sta song loaded - swimming and fiting will burn sta like crazy.
We saw some other fishies to fight and they were blue cons to me.

Our enchanter could not zone in - presumably her new files were corrupted in
the down load or some such. And a high lev guild was taking over the place
and would soon own the dungeon so we left to return another time.
