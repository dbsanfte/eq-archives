> Q: Why is there no Icon for the Bard Mana Song (Cassindra's Chorus of
> Clarity)?
>
> A: There are different types of Bard Songs. Most Bard Songs leave an
> icon on your character because the song has a duration - usually 5 to 15
> seconds. Each time the Bard sings the song she renews the duration -
> this means that a Bard who continues to sing the song can potentially
> keep the song effect going indefinitely.
>
> Some Bard songs do not have durations - you receive the effect when the
> Bard sings it, but will not get another "dose" until she sings it again.
> Cassindra's Chorus of Clarity (the mana song) is one of these songs - it
> does not have an actual duration, so you don't get an icon on your
> screen.
