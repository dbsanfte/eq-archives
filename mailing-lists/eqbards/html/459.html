I have seen several mentions of this song being 'useless', etc. Actually
several of my spellcasting friends tell me that it will cause their man to
regen a bit faster. Now it does not perform like the high level song of
course, but many of my friends swear by it. We noticed this in beta and it
was widely debated, but many have told me they see a difference. Next time
you are in a group, try it while the casters are medding and see if they can
tell a difference.
Brilinnia, a Bard on Xegony
