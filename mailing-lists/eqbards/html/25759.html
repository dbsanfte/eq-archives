I never hunted Dalnir was too high - but (on Xegony at the time at least) -
it was a heavily camped zoned - and those were a common drop - so a simple
trip to zone and a shot and I got it and was on my merry way.

Kaesoria is a rarely camped zone - so I had to put a group together. The
problem is its an ultra-random drop. Some people get theirs after clearing
the area once - others have had to spend two evenings to get the mob. You
need a good mob - and will have to fight your way down. At your level it
won't be easy - but will be fun and get you some XP (I was in my mid-upper
40's IIRC)
