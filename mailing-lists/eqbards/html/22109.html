I can't comment to much on the normal stuff, since my guild is, well, kinda uberish and these aren't scary
anymore, but some hints (mostly for Hate, since I've only been to Fear twice and they were cakewalks):

1. Singing on the pull is OK, unless you're in the pulling group. Don't join the pulling group the first
few times up there, you'll forget and kill somebody.

2. Poison resist works great against Maestro's lifetaps.

3. Sing resists against Innoruuk, turn to face the wall or it will be impossible to twist a darn thing.

4. The overhead view works very well in Hate, since the ceiling is at the right height. I get a LOT less
lagged playing the overhead view, and it's a lot easier to see which direction the MOB ran if he starts to
ping-pong.

5. The Spite Golems are way way magic resistant, don't bother singing against them.
