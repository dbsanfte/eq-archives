Subject: A few general ramblings on attaining some SAFE coin...


Recently, thanks to a disagreement with a Dark-Elf in the desert of Ro I
had to find another way to generate some cash. I wanted to happily hunt Fire
Beatles to fill my purse, an he wanting to happily paint the desert red with
my blood. Needless to say his cry to the effect of, 'You are not of a
experience to waste my time on, but I will still smight you', left me to ask
to leave, for I defiantly heard my mother calling me. never the less he
still wanted to play, but being a good boy I, while running, switched my
weapon and parrying dagger with my drum, both of which I keep in easy to
reach places, And began singing the Acceleration song. By the time I reach
the Southern Gate of Freeport I was bleeding profusely and close to a loss
of consciousness. But luckily I left my new found friend behind.

Not wanting to venture in to the desert till I was sure he had gone home,
and not wanting to go back to the over hunted Commonlands. I decided to get
my fishing pole out of the bank, and see how much I could roll the mer 3
Silver, 5 Copper I had into. In spent it all on bait @ 3 Copper per unit. I
then when to the dock, and straight into the water (I forgot I was still
playing my drum and singing the Acceleration song. After a quick swim I was
back on the docks again.

I than began to fish. Besides fish, I caught fish scales, rusted daggers,
and single tattered cloth sandals; which mad me wonder, if I fished in the
pool under the Academe of Magic would I find the stray magic ring, or in the
pool by the Hall of Truth would I get a combination sword of a stray piece
of plate? Any why, it was a profitable endeavor for the most part. I sold
the fish for just over 2 Silver each, Fish scales went for just 4 Copper
(still more than the cost of bait), and the other items sold for just over 2
silver each as well. I did lose my bait 2 times but I still had more than I
stated with approximately 1 Gold, 6 Silver for the hole trip. Making for 1
Gold, 1 Silver of pure profit, the greatest expense being 5 Silver and some
time, which I did not waste for I sang to keep my voice improving and
checking my direction to better my ability for when I really needed it.

So there where side benefits. I always try to capitalize on my down time,
like in the small bathing pool near the Theater of the Tranquil. I practice
my swimming without worry of Purina or sharks. I enter it and walk to the
back wall, when I reach it (either playing my drum or just singing a song
that will improve my abilities, I will then start to sing (and play, how I
do not know) and swim. <OOC> I then hit the auto-run key, sitting back and
watching my abilities increase.</OOC> The only cost is food.

I placed part of the fishing profits back into bait to make more coin. The
only draw back was when the pole broke costing me around 2 Gold to replace.
But this is better than the drawback of a critical failure in say begging.
For once I hit the beg button by mistake to hear a guard say "I will teach
you for your blasphemy!" You think I said 'your grandmother wears Shadow
Knight's boots!' Not politely asking for a spare copper or two. It was a
lesson I will long remember, for you can lose more than your pride by
begging... Al in All, by the end of my fishing trip I was ahead by 8 Gold
for 1 full days work. Not as Profitable as hunting Fire Beatles in the
Desert of RO but a bit safer. For it also is not completely safe, for while
fishing a Shark popped out of the water to snap at my toes, I then went to
the raised area of the pier. Knowing my luck, they probably can jump; but I
digress...

I hope those near a good sized body of water think to buy a fishing pole
and keep it stashed away for those times you need a bit of safe coin. Like
after losing all ones possessions and having to start over. for one can
stomp a few rats to buy bait, then it is off to the water side so you can
get coin for a weapon (or be luckily enough to fish one out of the sea.

- SnowLynx, Gentleman Bard of Freeport; presently looking for a long hot
bath while being followed around by every stray cat in Freeport...
