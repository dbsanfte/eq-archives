Greetings Elwyn!

In the district of E'ci, I've made several trips through the smelly swamps of
Innoluthe and to Cazic-Thule. Allow me to give a few words of advice:

On your way, stick to the right wall in Innoluthe. It's easy to miss the zone to
the Ferrott, as I've passed it up several times!

Once entering the Ferrott, have your trusty drum equipped, you'll need to sing
the Accelerando upon entering. Bouncers Hurd and Flerb like to frequent the
area, so if they see you, run back to the zone with all haste! Following the
left mountain, until you reach shore, diving in, and swimming along the base of
the temple to the left. As you surface, follow the temple wall, and you'll see
the large ramp. Up at the top are a few lizard men, but nothing to big.

Inside, I recommend playing it safe. If your group is in the low 20s and full,
I'd sing and play the lute, usually alternating between the Hymn and the
Lullaby, or twisting the Lullaby, Anthem, and the Hymn. If it's not a full
group, I'd be pulling to through the doors, as many many trains come through
there, rather easily!

Sadly, unless you have faction with the Ogres or Troll, the nearest place to be
bound is Freeport. I died once in a few hours of playing, but did rather well
after that (I stayed by the zone). If you want a security blanket, find yourself
a benevolent cleric who will resurrect you!

Watch out for those Sentinels, they rarely come lured alone!
