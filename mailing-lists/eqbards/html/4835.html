Got a chance to run a couple tests with my level 31 enchanter friend (my

Times are just for one bubble of mana....

Clarity Alone : 72 seconds
ManaSong Constant: 65 seconds

Clarity+Song : 48 seconds

Couldn't find another bard around to test if the song stacks with itself,

it has a 3 round effect, and can be juggled with two other songs w/ no drop
in regen rate.
