My bard at 35 would not even think of trying to melee solo a blue. Well,
she does think of it; and dream of it; and drool for it especially if she is
trying to get out of a hell level. But the reality is against it.

Now give her 2 blues, her stein and her drum and that is another story.

But what happens as you get older is that the mobs you face get stronger
(they hit you harder.) The con is how much xp you will get - not how hard
they can hit you nor how much hp & ac they have. You will find some really
tough blues and some really weak blues. And you will find less and less of
them alone or in easy to get to places.

In about 3 or 4 more levels, you will easily see this in Aviaks (South
Karana), Unrest (if you go, i'd wait til mid 20's), PickClaw
Goblins(HighKeep), etc.

My bard has a tough time solo melee only some greens. Give me my group and
a red mob anytime!
