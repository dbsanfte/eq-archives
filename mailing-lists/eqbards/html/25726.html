the nostrolo quest is very funny and good for your level. it is very
interesting to get through all the dungeons for the coins, and much more
entertaining than just levelling in OT ledge killing pickles.

a lot of skills and knowledge. you get to know dungeon fighting and zone
travelling, group organizing and patience ;)

i had much fun doing it, so dont hesitate. its worth the effort!
