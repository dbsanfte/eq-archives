At 04:55 PM 4/28/99 -0400, A.Rubman wrote:
>So I ask
>you all Whats a good weapon that is not imposing like a mino axe for a
>level 8 bard, or even for any level bard.

I can't say I've tried many weapons, but I understand your concern. A
minotaur axe looks good in the hand of, say, a dwarf warrior, but I agree
it looks a bit out of place next to a lute. For a short time at the
beginning of my career I carried an axe, but gave over to a bastard sword.
It is a bit heavier and slower than a long sword, but cuts a bit deeper,
and personally I find it a suitable compromise between a bard's proper
finesse and brute force.

Although its piercing style may not come so naturally and I can't say how
effective it would be overall, I can also imagine a bard looking quite
dashing with a rapier.

One could extend this discussion to armor as well. If a bard is strong
enough full plate, for example, may be within the realm of possibility but
would look, in my opinion, rather odd on a bard. Image counts when one is
always in some degree an entertainer.
