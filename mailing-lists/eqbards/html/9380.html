I have been playing Kitasi more than any other character though by far,
mainly because he is perfect for completing quests which is my main focus in
the game 50% of the time. Bards provide more opportunities for role-playing
as well which makes the game more enjoyable for me.

The main reason I play my Cleric is also due to lack of finding others,
though usually it is lack of finding other's to bind me. Kaladim during
prime time last nite, 3 people no binders :( In fact if my bard could bind

day from anyone in BB last nite as they were all in too big a hurry to get
to Kedge. I had wanted to check it out for looks while there were sure to
be plenty of people cleaning it out, but couldn't get bound so I headed over
to Greater Faydark and stumbled onto a GM event :)

That's the first time lack of a bind was ever beneficial. I had a blast
fighting off the dark elf invaders, was very surprised I could hurt them.
Was even more surprised when I actually got the attention of one and they
started beating on me. Fortunately I quickly beat feet and ran circles
around her. One big disad GM run critters have over AI critters is response
time. I could run circles around her and keep bellowing away and she
couldn't get a bead on me. good thing too cause she smacked me down 3
bubbles real quick when she did catch me in melee. Made some friends with
some folks more your level or higher who had no healers available. Even my
paltry 9 points a pulse felt good to them after sitting and getting 2. One
even rewarded me with a Lambent Stone for saving him from the Dark Elf
Cleric. All in all a fun nite despite the zone crash.
