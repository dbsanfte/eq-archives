There may or may not be a saving throw aspect to it. After
running around a lot in Mistmoore while invis, accidently
causing 3 trains, and being unable to figure out a pattern, it
finally hit me that some mobs are casting see invis on

may be what's going on in the lower guk live side as well.

enough to hide you from some undead at all levels.

What I *really* want is a combo invis/see invis song. GZ's
