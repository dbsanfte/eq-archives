of the dragons tougher to fight now. On Thursday mornings patch, Verant gave

name of Downs on Tribunal, my guild The Elitists and our Sister guild The
Vagrants attempted to kill both dragons after the patch. Well, Lady Vox fell

I was just commenting on this fact so that others know what to

could refresh them. It is seems these two are quite a bit more difficult
now.
