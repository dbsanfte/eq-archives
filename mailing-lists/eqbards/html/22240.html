Subject: Speaking of climbing


There's a nasty 'hole' in Skyfire, behind the left wall just beyond the
Veeshan statue. We assumed that high wall was the zone wall when hunting
there once, and the monk ran up it to avoid getting a second mob on a pull.
He went over the top and fell to his death. I got a lev ring from our mage
and went corpse hunting. Over the top of the wall and down again, it looks
like a canyon, but I'm not sure there's any real exit. I loc'ed the corpse
and pulled it out by finding one piece of the rockwall slanted enough to let
the combo of Selo's and the lev get me and the corpse out. We came out near
the statue. I never had the time since to go back and follow it the other
direction. Having fallen through the world a couple times, it gave me the
willies to top that steep wall both ways.
