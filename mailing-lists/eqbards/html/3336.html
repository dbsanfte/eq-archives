Okay, this was a great technical description of the Kithicor, although there are things about it you
only discover over time, the spawn is VERY quirky and particular, way more than described.

But I and many others are convinced this is not all there is to Kithicor. There is story there, very
odd and mysterious story. If you are looking for "Levelquest" this is NOT your zone. If you are
looking to gain an understanding of one of the weirdest zones in the game, go spend some time in
Kithicor. You can run through it a hundred times and not notice much, you can spend a night hunting
spawn spots and not notice much, but over time you will begin to notice little odd things. Things
which in of themselves don't seem like much, but collectivly are odd enough to make you wonder.

The behaviors of Ged and Luna??? (I forget her exact name BUT we all believe it is not accidental
that she has the moon reference in her name). We are quite sure they are involved...and when she is
parted from him for a while she *runs* back. What is the story behind that?

Ghouls that stand upright (spotted one night).

One minute Ged is standing there happily, the next moment, a werewolf...(seen by a friend)

One night I saw Luna?? run into a burned out house and a female zombie run out.

And why doesn't she speak?

There appears to be references to Kithicors past in the History of Norrath.

The undead army. It's not there all the time, but when it IS there, it's just plain weird.

*one* goblin? How often do you see a lone goblin? Not named, just a regular ol' goblin. Is he lost?

No music, no footsteps NOTHING. Just plain spooky. After some time there walking out into W
Commonlands feels like stepping into a cheery garden...The whole place, from the merchants to the
other residents to the locked buildings to the bizarre nature of the spawn...The Truth is Out There.
And you won't find it while leveling through...
