Over the weekend I travelled from RiverVale to Queynos and back again
a few times. I'm a 12th level bard and went through Runnyeye and
then the Gorge all 4 trips. With a drum in hand and your running
song going, it is a pretty quick trip all in all. In the Gorge I had
a couple things chase me, along with in the Karanas plains, but they
could never catch me. In Runnyeye there are usually only a couple of
gobs along the way and the run takes only about 20 seconds... get
your running song going prior to zoning in and its effect will last
almost long enough to get you through even quicker.
