Hey all,

There seemed to be some confusion this weekend about Medium Bronze Armor on the
list. I thought i would post up here all the locations that i know where
MEDIUM bronze drops. It is more difficult to get than small (as the unrest
baddies seem to drop it by the truckload) or large (because you get a piece of
Lg Bronze EVERY time you kill the ogre capt. in najena without fail)

The problem is that MOST bards NEED Medium (i play a Half-Elf, because i think
they look the best)

Medium Bronze, as people mentioned drops in Sol B (which would keep it away
from us until a very high level)
But, it also drops in Sol A (where noone ever goes on my server)
It drops in Permafrost (a group of low 20's can get some, while it is not a
common drop, it is possible to get it)

There might be other places...but i am not sure. Sol A and perma are tuned
(the beginning parts) for roughly the same level range as Unrest, so Medium
SHOULD BE just as easy to acquire as Small. However, the fact that Unrest (on
Prexus) has a million people in it all the time makes it safer than going into
sol a and being the only people there.

I got my Med Bronze breastplate from Perma, however i did buy the Hat (ick) and
Legs, because i am too much of a not-camper to sit in a dungeon that long.
