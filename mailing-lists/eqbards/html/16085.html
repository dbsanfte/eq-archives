AC is not a number that is even used by the game engine. It is a composit
number made up of 2 parts: Hit avoidance and Damage avoidance. Your Atk
rating is also a composit number: Chance to hit and Damage applied. If I
remember correctly when Abashi made is famous post on stats he said Agility
affects the Hit avoidance portion of AC.
