Note that the enchanter spell list refers to a lot of things as stuns
that really aren't. It says mez "stuns" the target, as well as the whirl
till you hurl line of spells. This is different from the color
shift/slant/skew line which are really stuns. i.e. you can beat on the mob
for the duration of the spell without them hitting back.

By 44 enchanters have 3 stuns that are aoe around themselves and can be
chain cast almost repeatedly for very little mana. Cast time 1-2 sec

Even at 20 however, they have a 4 and 6 sec stun with recast times of 12
seconds, so they can almost keep a mob nailed down permanently just with
them.
