The best out there would be EQ Parse. I have version 2.13 I believe. Its
probably one of the best around as it also will graph your damage output in
the form of a histogram. I think they have it available from eqfiles.com or
www.everlore.com in the downloads sections. Ahhh heres the link..

http://www.everlore.com/lorefiles/fileinfo.asp?ID=112709

For best results you need to be weilding two different types of weapons
(i.e. 1 blunt 1 slashing ) so that it can distinguish between the two. But
for overall damage output, this doesnt really matter.
