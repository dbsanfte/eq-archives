Screw ALL those places. The place to get through 30
(and a bunch more levels after that) is the entrance
of Kedge Keep. Get yourself a group of 3 players of
30 or ABOVE. Pull the lancer swordfish. You have to
be careful how your pull them. There is generally an
impaler or piercer nearby so you have to make sure you
don't attack the lancers at those times. Kind of
approach the area and let the lancer attack you.
Anyway, the experience is AWESOME! There is never
anyone there (except for the occasional raids on Phin
or whatever his name is). The loot is absolutely god
awful though. However, I guarantee you will level
very quickly. The zone is right there so you should
never die.
