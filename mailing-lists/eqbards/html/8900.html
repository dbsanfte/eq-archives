I was in Oasis a couple nights ago with a lower to mid-30s group. All I can
say is that pulling a single Gob from the lake is darn difficult (we were
trying just about everything we could think of). The DW gobs are very tough
as well. One we could handle fairly easily. Two was getting difficult.
Three would be possible, but we'd be severly damaged and/or low on mana,
etc. Four is just plain out of the question. As I stated though.. more
often than not, you'll end up pulling a small army. The lil' buggers have
some nasty, nasty spells. We were able to kill 2 or 3 during the evening
(ran from a lot) and we never saw a helm. I would speculate that the
chances of them dropping it are roughly similar to lambent stones and
griffenne charms.
