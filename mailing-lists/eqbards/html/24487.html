> Also, when using the "lull" type song, do mulitple uses of it increase the
> "anti-hatred"?

Our Lugubrious Lament only lower's the aggro range of the creature, like any other lull spell. Using a string instrument will help make the aggro range even smaller. Just remember that the aggro range is never completely eliminated. For instance, say you have 2 mobs with one in the front and one in the back. If you lament in the one in the front it's aggro range is now very small. If you pull the mob in back and it runs through the front creature's small aggro range, then they will both come.

Therefore, it would be better to either Lament the creature in back and pull the front one.
