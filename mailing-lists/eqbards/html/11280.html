Well, here's something I threw together last night:

Sometimes it's Hard to be a Bard

Sometimes it's hard to be a bard, although I have to say,
I love it though it hurts to know it's changing every day
In ways I think the gods must blink in puzzlement to see -
The many ways these changing days have been the death of me.

I've had a song, though not for long, a joy for me to play
It let me fly across the sky, but then, the other day
My levitate began to wait before it would relight
And so I fell and died, oh well... a casualty of Flight.

I have a greed for superspeed, I simply hate to wait
To break a root, there's no dispute, Accelerando's great
And then one day, to my dismay, ghoul-rooted and in need,
My precious tune did nothing boon... a casualty of Speed.

I've one refrain, a sonic chain - it slows the target down
Reducing flight, and in a fight, it's justly won renown.
A fleeing whelp returned with help; one change my fate explains -
Since he had SoW, I could not slow... a casualty of Chains.

I'm very sad to say I had the horrid luck to see
Upon the plain, through fog and rain, The Thing That Must Not Be.
To show their ire, the gods rained fire, and both of us were charred...
Apparently the gods could see the Thing but not the bard.

Sometimes it's hard to be a bard, although I have to say,
I love it though it hurts to know it's changing every day
In ways I think the gods must blink in puzzlement to see -
The many ways these changing days have been the death of me.

Aloro Silvertongue (E'ci)
I'm sure there are some other stanzas I should include, but can't think of
what other unintended/undocumented nerfs have hit us lately. Any
