Actually I think Selos root breaking effect does work in dungeons. I have
ben camping out in Befallen's basement alot lately with my guildmates,
mostly big Barb warriors who covet the BBC's and we get rooted all the time.
I have tried it each time and it seems to unstick you or your group members
before you get that pesky'you can't do that in here' message. It is hard to
tell in the heat of a battle but now they will say ROOTED and when I hit
selos we can move again.

As to jig and being a mini-mana bard, we found this in beta and I am the
darling of the casters my level for it once they figure it out.
For hotbuttons, definitely add assist. You need to get used to the whole
group concentrating on one mob at a time and assist is the best way.
Essential for higherlevel dungeon hunting IMHO. I quit trying sense heading
since it always let me down when I had to know what direction NOW. For 3 pp
you can buy a compass and put it in your inventory hotkey. It never fails.
;)
