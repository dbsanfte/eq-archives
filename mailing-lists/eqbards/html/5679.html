A few notes I have made.
1. Elemental and Purifying Rhythms do not stack, so +46 is the best MR
boost you can get. +46 still puts me over 100 so nothing to sneeze
at!

2. Have noticed no difference with the Aquatic Ayre with or without
flute.

3. Flute does affect Carillion, but only slightly. I had made a habit
of checking it and on two occassions I was able to detect a difference.
Once I went from Amiable to Amiable with song, and to Kindly with
flute. The other time I went from Indifferent to Amiable with song, but
could not get quest. With flute I was also Amiable, but obviously
higher Amiable as I was able to get quest after using the flute.
Keep in mind Amiable is about 2-4 times as large, faction points wise,
as Indifferent, Apprehensive, or Dubious. Not sure if Kindly is as big
a faction point range as Amiable, but I would guess it is the same.

4. I'll retest Chords tonite if I get the chance, but as I recall it
does 2pts + 1/6 lvls per pulse when sung and 5 + 1/6 when played just
like Hymn. My memory could be faulty on that, but it is easy enough to
test what it does at 16th as I am currently there, again.
