The Avatar is level 38 or 39. I think a group of 6 level 30's would have a
very tough time taking him out. He has a ton of hit points, does a Harm
Touch at the beginning of the battle good for at least 2 bubbles of life,
and resists chain and root bigtime. i couldn't chain him before level 40,
and we always had to fight him far from the door and have all our casters
keep nuking mana cause if he runs and makes it out, you will have a train of
gators and lizzies like you wouldn't believe. I would suggest 6 level 35's
to take him. it's been a while since i fought him, so correct me if i am
inaccurate.

Oh and the demon claw is very nice. i think closer to 300 to 400pp for it
though, especially if 500pp is everything you have.
