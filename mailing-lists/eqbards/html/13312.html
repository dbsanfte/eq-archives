Melee can power level much faster than casters... A caster will
always need mana to kill anything. A melee with enough twinked armor
and a super weapon can kill a camain with a bubble of hp. A twinked
melee with a lot of cash can pay someone to walk behind him and heal.

The best way to superpower level is to get damage shields from high levels
on you. 24 damage each time you get hit... stuff dies fast.

Also, ... usually you do not power level off a random spawn very well.
Caiman are random spawns... ickie. Better to find an NPC with a very
quick spawn rate in your level range. Stuff like the dwarves or elves
in OOT are favorites for necro's.
