When I decieded to start working on my piercing and 1h blunt at 7th level I
went out and bought the fastest (not the best) weapons of each type. I
purchased a regular dagger (for about 2 plat) and a 1h staff (for a little
of 4 plat). The reason for the fast weapons is more skill checks! I was
able to top both skills by the tail-end of 8th. If you can get a fine steel
dagger do so, they are a little faster then a regular dagger. I fought lots
of weak blues to build it up. If I found health lagging behind the npc's I
would switch to my better (1h slash) weapon to 'catch up', then switch back.
Good luck!
