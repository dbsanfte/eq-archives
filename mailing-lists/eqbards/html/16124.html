Subject: Kill-stealing bards?


A couple of friends and I were in Steamfont killing Mino Lords the other
night, trying to get me out of 31 so they could bask in the glow of my mana
song, when the strangest thing happened.

I was with a level 36 Barbarian Shaman (with her pet) who was soloing, and I
was grouped with a level 30 Druid.

We figured that I would get exp faster if I grouped with the druid instead
of the Shaman. So she would solo a mino lord and then we would kill one.
While she was soloing hers, I was chaining it to make it a little easier on
her while we awaited our turn.

One time, I decided to give her a little extra help in the form of Fulfil's
Curtailing Chant (level 30 song?) which provides the MR debuff along with a
small DOT (6 or 7 dmg per pulse).

So when the mino lord died, imagine my surprise when I got the exp for the
kill!!! She had been dotting the heck out of it as well as bashing it, had
her pet helping her, and whatever else a level 36 shaman can do.. hehe, and
yet my teensy tiny DOT somehow stole her kill! Oh, and I wasn't even
equipping a drum (it IS supposed to be a percussion song isn't it?).

So, as I see it, there are a few possible explanations.

1. She wasn't getting credit for her pet's damage.
2. The DOT on Fulfil's is much higher than suspected.
3. The druid I was with (who was supposedly afk) threw in a few zaps
4. It's a bug

Any ideas?
