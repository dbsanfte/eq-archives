Agreed, I also notice, after further reading, that they did not opt to pull
any of the processing off of the CPU and onto the board. Which means that
the GeForce is, again, going to be better because the system isn't trying to
do everything from the CPU. Slower (sub 500-550mhz systems) will notice
this more than the extremely fast systems. But I would suspect that even up
at the 700-800mhz range the fact that GeForce pulls the processing itself
will give it an advantage over the 3dFX card.

Something to remember when looking at technology: Try to stay away from
"Fan sites". Voodoo Xtreme is hardly the place to go to see if a Voodoo
card is better than a NVidia just as going to www.nvidia.com isn't exactly
the best place to find out if an NVidia card is better than a Voodoo.

Tom's Hardware guide and other **Neutral** sites are the best places to go
for that kind of analysys.
