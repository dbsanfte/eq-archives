Subject: Finding a Flute


Corpse recovery in Cazic should go fine once you get your hands on a flute.
I've spent a fair amount of time there, and Shauri's Sonorous Clouding works
wonderfully. Lizards just run past in that wierd slow-mo jogging they do,
and I've never had Shauri's drop when I was playing it - on those rare
occasions when I get a "You feel yourself starting to appear" message, the
next pulse refreshes invis, so no worries.

There's only one lizard in Cazic Thule (as far as I know from scouting
pretty much the whole place on many occasions) who can cast see invis, and
that's Radiant (who as I recall is an enchanter in the low 20's). He's
usually wandering the maze, so watch out. All the other casters (jud, just,
page, et al) are too busy casting Spirit of the Torpid Lizard, or whatever
they cast to buff themselves (they cast all the time, but it never seems to
DO anything that I can see).

Your best bet is to get someone in their 30's or higher that you know and
trust to pull you out. Your next best bet is to get a flute, get someone to
cast SoW on you, and run in invisibly. When you get to your corpse, drag it
to the zone and loot there... just in case Radiant decides to pay you a
surprise visit.

Good luck!
