Subject: Hide and sneak skill


How many of you fellow bards think our Hide and Sneak skills are
worthless? I was hoping the changes they made to those two skills in
respect to rogues would also apply to us. If you weren't aware, when a
rogue successfully hides, he now gets a message he was successful. Once
a rogue is hidden, if he Sneaks successfully he can move silently and
invisible. Very handy for their backstabs. I don't see how adding this
feature to bards would unbalance us at all. As it stands right now I
doubt many of us even use these skills. However, when it comes to a
decision on which 8 songs to memorize, if we had this ability to
Hide/Sneak then that would be a factor in memorizing a different song
instead of the Invisibility song.

Galtin of E'ci
