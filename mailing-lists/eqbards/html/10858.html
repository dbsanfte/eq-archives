The only problem I have with all this talk is making the quest armor no
drop. There are many of the quests for other classes I can do for those in
my guild. They don't do them because they can't travel as fast as I can and
they don't enjoy questing and travelling like I do. They are willing to
trade me things they camp for (Yech I hate camping) when I do get them a
piece of quest armor. They could have gotten the armor themselves in most
cases but are not interesting in all the running around. If all the quest
armor was no drop that would just be another set of quests I could do but
then have to just destroy the results. At the cost of those pieces I
couldn't afford to do that and there would suddenly be no reason to do it
either. as stated before by others unless you make everything no drop,
there's not much point in making a lot of things no drop. There's nothing I
hate more than doing a quest which I often have spent hours, or days, or
even levels, (off and on) completing, only to be rewarded with a really cool
item for another class, that I can't even give or sell to someone else
because it is no drop. I still have a Collar of Neshika in the bank from
doing the tiger quest hoping to get something to trade to a monk friend who
has an obsidian shard in the bank I could use, only to find out its no drop.
Believe me, that feeling is every bit as big a let down as seeing some twink
half your level run by with better stuff.
