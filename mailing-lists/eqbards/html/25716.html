Good quest -- easier and harder than you might think. If you have a good guild that raids a bit, look to hit City of Mist, Kaesora and get a good group together to hunt scorpikus's in the canyon in the Overthere (beware the harm touch) --
Otherwise you can buy these coins for varying amounts. I have seen the Kaesora coin auctioned for up to 400pp -- too much IMHO but it is probably the hardest to come by.
If you are going to buy the coins though -- a walrus skin drum goes for 1K on Rodcet Nife and probably similar amounts elsewhere. It is a better drum and if you have the cash on hand, takes a lot less time to get )
