A good group can be a frightening thing in Dalinars. A bad group should

My cleric's group spent 4 weeks in Dalinars. 1 5 hour session a week. 2
Non hell and 1 Hell level each. Thats a (non hell) level per 5 hour
session. Add in 6 fighting batons, 2 saps, and a few other lesser items,
and as much Fine Steel as we could carry (each week).

Dalinar's (like most dungeons) is made for groups of people you know and
trust.
