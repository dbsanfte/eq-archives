Yes you can twist mana song and heal song! I didn't know it before, but
heal song actually lasts 3 ticks too like most other songs. I think the
same goes with mana song, but it would be hard to tell. So what I do is
bring out my lute to play the heal song. As soon as it starts, stop and
play the mana song for 2 ticks, go back to heal song for one tick, mana for
2 ticks. Works really well for me.

One side effect of mana song is that the healers will heal less. Since now
they can regen mana fast, they start to nuke instead of heal. Instead of
conserving mana, they want to see who can do the most damage to the critter!
