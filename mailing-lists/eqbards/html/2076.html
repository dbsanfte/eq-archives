Do you have a slow computer? If your computer can't keep up
with the graphics, you will not move until it catches up, net
result being you run slower. At L15 I tested accelerando with
a drum vs. a L30 druid using SoW - I was slowly (very slowly)
able to catch up. At L20, I can catch up to someone with SoW
fairly quickly (I don't blow by them, but the speed difference
is obvious). Sometimes I turn around after passing them and
wave at them while running backwards. :-) I suppose the
easiest way to test this would be to group with your druid
friend, make sure she *doesn't* have SoW on, and see if you
run at the same speed.

Another thing I've noticed is that my run/walk toggle is
sometimes switched when I zone. This started a few days ago;
I'd never experienced it before then.
