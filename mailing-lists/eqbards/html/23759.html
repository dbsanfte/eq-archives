Depends on the number of MOBs. If I have to mez more than one, I use the Lute since I can twist Cantata
in that way without instrument swapping, which is a pain if you have to retarget also (doesn't apply if
you aren't 55 yet). With just one, I use whatever I would use for songs or weapons otherwise (with one,
probably two if I wasn't lazy, you can mez then use weapons on the primary, just don't hit your mez guy
when you retarget). The resists are awfully low on this Song, and it lasts longer than the normal song
cycle (don't tell Verant). I wouldn't worry about using the wind instrument with it unless you have a 4+
pull, and even then I've never bothered.
