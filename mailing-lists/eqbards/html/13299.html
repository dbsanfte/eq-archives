Yup. Although on a new server, without friends, that is
much more difficult I would think. But on a server where you already have
friends, its especially true if you are able to twink with some decent items
or even 50pp cash. Then you don't need to spend your time running and
selling and trying to acquire basic items, you also have money to buy spells
all at once rather than having to run back and forth. In my opinion they
keys to good fast leveling are:
1. Don't die
2. Don't die
3. Location (i.e. plenty of spawns, low death risk)
4. The other thing that REALLY helps is to have higher level friends
who can tele your butt around when necessary.
5. Don't die
6. Don't have fun. There is no time for rp or exploring :-)

Even at the lower levels where you don't lose exp, the
amount of time it takes to run and get your body and re-mem spells without
med could easily get you a bub or two at that level.
