On Bards and Mana:
Bards use mana for two songs: Denon's Desperate Dirge (an area effect
instant-damage) and Solon's Song of the Sirens (a charm song). Denon's
tends to use all of your mana in one shot - mana-improving items don't
appear to matter there. SSS slowly eats your mana while charming
creatures. One thing of note: Bards are completely unaffected by any
sort of mana-refreshing or buffing song or spell. In other words, Breeze,
Clarity, etc. have no effect on Bards. It's a small sacrifice for having
99 percent of our songs mana-free.
