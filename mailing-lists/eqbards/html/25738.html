HQ bearpelts are the only skins you'll get a good price on from the commons -- 2pp is prolly about right for the others...that's 40p a stack and much easier to find than HQ bearpelts for the same price ... Another thing you can do is sell deathfist belts (or crushbone belts and lego pads, etc.) These quests are quite good and there is no shortage of twinks buying them for up to 5pp each.
(I am using prices for Rodcet Nife. Your server may be a bit different).
My opinion on tradeskills is that they are designed to be a money sink and protect server economies...kind of like 100k horses -- it takes cash out of circulation P
Better gear means less downtime.

> Hi Fohat,
> Magical weapons are always dropping in price and one of the best
times to buy them is during off-peak hours in the Commons. Why?
Because people get tired of trying to sell stuff and want to get some
sleep -- You never know what cool deals you'll find. )
> Look for a dragoon dirk if you can. They go for 30-40pp and when
you upgrade, it can sit in your range slot and you'll still get the
MR and dex from it...I carried a dragoon dirk in my range slot until
I could afford a Runed Bone Fork. If you don't have 30-40 pp then
hunt bears until you get a HQ pelt and auction it for 40pp or trade
for a dragoon dirk -- You might get lucky and a tailor with extra
gear in the bank will give you a deal...
> Find a friend who has luclin and ask him/her to buy you those
songs. If you are in a guild, it should be easy -- if you are not in
a guild, look for one -- guilds offer a whole new perspective on the
game if you join a good one.
> Warsong is a self-only buff and it is a bit better than anthem
which is for grouping -- I recommend it highly if you are soloing --
but with hymm under your belt, you'll be a welcome addition to most
groups. ...and bellow WILL tell you the damage you do. I used it for
pulling until I hit lvl 30. Damage is unimpressive from this song
but it costs no mana and is seldom resisted and is a good interupt
when you fight/duel casters. you'll get a lot of use from it )
