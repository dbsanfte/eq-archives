You and 3 or 4 co level friends can go to lake rathe and if you can find the
hermits cabin way back in the cornor (between gypsy's and barbs) - kill the
aqua gob that swims in that bay until Webby Murclaw (sp?) shows up with a
nice water crystal staff for you. He is a nasty caster - you _must_ fight
him in the water or he will 32k. I suggest a ranger who can cast endure
breath, wizz and or cleric, use lullaby to keep him stunned, stuns help too
and enjoy.

Sylly

p.s. I was 30 and fought him with a 26 ranger who got burned bad and died. I
did kill him but felt real bad at the loss of my friend.
