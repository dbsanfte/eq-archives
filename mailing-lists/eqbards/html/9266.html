Subject: Laments affect on the rest of the party


I've used Lament in numerous occasions when I am the puller. Never used it
with my party nearby. Lets assume your party is trying to move from Point A
to Point B without getting into any fights. There are five mobs along the
path. As the bard, you are up front and successfully Lament each mob in
order. The bard could make it easily. What happens though when your party
tries to move past them? Will the mobs ignore the entire party or will the
mobs attack them?

I'm inclined to believe that the Lament effect is centered on the bard and
that anyone else who gets within the mob's aggro range will get attacked.

This would be a moot tactic if your party was invisible. However, against
mobs that can see thru invis it may be a viable tactic if the Lament stops
the mob from attacking the rest of the party.

Galtin of E'ci
