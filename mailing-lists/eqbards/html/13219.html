Subject: Made 45 last night and love my new song :)


Made 45 last night finally. Also over the weekend I re-discovered the joys
of an old song, Lugborious Lament :) It started in Sol B when I was the
only tank in a group at the Sol A bug line. I deciced to give Lament a try
and it worked better then I remember. Was lulling bats, beetles, LDCs, Nox,
anything that got in my way :)

Then last night after Vox (which was John's (tm) first dragon run btw) I
drummed down to lower guk to help a guildmate get a Ykesha. As we are
forming up in the Magi room the Magi makes an appearence and is quickly
dispatched droping a fine new robe for our wizard. the hall between the
Magi and the Lord was fully spawned and the nearest party that was pulling
from the area was in Frenzied. So we has to break the hall and the room. We
recon and see the Lord is up with a guk wizard, a bok and a Ykesha, but our
cleric loses invis and a wondering frog agros and we have to evac.

So we get back to the Magi and I pull out lute and start pulling the hall
frogs. We quickly clear the hall and head to the room. Now the Bok is
yellow and the guk wiz is red. So we med up, I lull the Bok then the guk.
Our cleric pulls the lord and eats the HT with an invulnerbility The Guk
stays put but the Bok comes. After a long, long battle we take out the lord
and the bok only losing one of our 2 clerics. It was great if only lower guk
could be like that every night :)
