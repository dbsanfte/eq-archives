Not taunting hardly qualifies as lowering their combat effectiveness
significantly. That's like asking a rogue not to pick pockets while
fighting. Taunt is very specialized to get creatures to attack them instead
of someone else. I've never had to have our SK back out of a fight yet.
He's usually the target for the first 15-30 seconds of a fight but they
always switch to me soon thereafter and no amount of backing out by me has
ever helped unless I do it as soon as they switch to me, which kinda defeats
the purpose. We'll see how the taunting reduction affects this and how they
decide to implement it. The first 'smart ass' comment on my guild list was
that in order to reduce bard taunt, using Verant logic, they would probably
give all bard songs a refresh time of 20 seconds. I wish I could say I was
100% certain that the idea was ridiculous, but it sent a chill down my spine
just to hear it suggested.
