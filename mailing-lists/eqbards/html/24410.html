<P> * Read the archives for this message group -- all
the best bards in the game hang their hat here for at
least a portion of their EQ career.  I couldn't
do justice to those bards by mentioning only a few of
their names.  But look especially for messages by
John Kim who posted numerous statistics that he parsed
from his logs.  Lots of other good suggestions

Think of yourself as light cavelry.  You are
quick and pack a punch but don't take a lot of damage
-- once you get bellow, you'll find you can run away
from a fight, heal and return to harry that PKer out
of the zone;-)<BR><BR>*  The higher your lvl, the
more tools you have to work with.<BR><BR>* Join a
guild.  They will offer some protection from the
PK bullies. (There are anti-PK guilds)<BR><BR>* Wide
open spaces are your friend<BR><BR>* A stunned bard is
a silent bard.<BR><BR>Yours in song,<BR>Tyunes of
