Well Xegony has some enterprising marketers, that's for sure. I was asked
twice to trade for a Selo's drum when I had just gotten into the L40's. Both
times they wanted SMR's, FBSS's, that sort of thing. I laughed and refered
them to someone who might actually consider trading those items. I had
neither, I try to avoid Lower Guk as much as possible. I have been offered
singing boots twice, first time the guy said he would take a sharkbone
warhammer plus some plat, second guy wanted 'enchanter items'. I didn't make
either trade, my personal opinion was the drop rate of these items wasn't
well enough established to determine actual value. I am in wait and see on
prices (considering I have less than 2k in the bank), may even brave the New
Blackburrow to get them myself. I don't know how common the drop is or how
tought the mobs that drop it are, so that makes it hard to determine the
value.

I used to spend time hunting someone that needed any usable equipment I
found but these days I just don't have time it seems. Our guild did try to
provide equipment once, by way of collecting donations from members and
making them available on request but it turned into a bone of contention. We
also got swampped with new members who just wanted stuff. Now it's a private
thing, although we do try to help each other out when possible.
