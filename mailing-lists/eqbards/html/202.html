You will need both the drum and the string instrument since certain songs
work best with a particular instrument (i.e. The 5th level song Accelerando
works much better when playing the drum and the 6th level song works much
better with a stringed instrument). So you will eventually have 4
instruments, but this shouldn't be a problem with a couple of backpacks.

As far as the lute vs. the mandolin, I believe the more expensive one is
lighter. That's the only difference I've heard of.

As for money, you should be taking out the orc raiders left and right and
8th. This should supply a decent income. Another thing you might consider
is heading cross country to The Lair of Splitpaw (Paw dungeon). It's
geared for levels 5 to 15 and is a great source of experience and income.

Taelin, Songweaver of Kelethin
