Subject: SHARKSKIN DRUMS


Got my SharkSkin Drums tonight. Many thanks to the Twelve Year Guild on
Cazic-Thule.

The SharkSkin Drums are found on a named Mermaid called Coralyn Kelpma. She
spawns past Estrella, just to the right of the 5 StingTooths in the Ceiling
and right outside of the Seahorse Caves. A normal Gloomwater is her
placeholder. We came upon her by accident as she spawned 20 min after we
killed her placeholder. I had never heard of her before. Now I wonder what
her rare loot is. Grin. She looks like she is equipped with a lute when she
has these.

I have tested the Drums. They are infact better than the Mistmoore Drums. I
do not have Selos drums to test against.

Here are the test results from a 47 bard singing Elemental Rythms. The
number represents the point increase for the cold/fire/magic resist.

+28 = no instrument just singing
+50 = normal hand drum
+58 = Mistmoore Battle Drums
+61 = SharkSkin Drums

Judging from this Mistmoore drums give a 16% increase over normal drums. And
SharkSkin Drums give a 22% increase over normal drums. Testing with a couple
other songs also shows a slight increase when using the SharkSkin vs using
the Mistmoore.

In Addition Selos speed seems slightly enhanced (actually easier to see a
slight slow down when going from Sharkskin down to Mistmoore). I feel very
happy running with these. A visually subjective test with Encumbrance seems
to indicate they are at least as good as Mist Drums in dealing with
Encumbrance and Selos running.

All in All great Drums and ultra-rare. I shall keep these over the Selo's
Drums, even though Selos is likely better.

The Icon looks like a lute. When equipped it looks like a lute as well. The
nice thing about this is that when running with selos I actually strum a
lute instead of a flute. Unfortunately I may mistake the icon for the Lute
of the Gyspy Princess sometimes when switching instruments as they appear
the same.

I Don't really read this onelist. Just posting for a heads up and to place
in the Archives.

Good luck getting yours. I love Kedge!
