common good. Your lucky - that's not very common (especially from what I've
seen on Xegony).

My only concern is the number of instances I've seen (and allusions on
Backstage and here) of the mentality that the bard never gets any. I've
heard (first hand) of pickup raids where after the fact it was stated bards
were ineligible for haste item - and universal acceptance of that.

Priority to Warriors for weapons is fine as long as it isn't a perpetuating
thing where Bards NEVER get a chance for a good item.

And once again - maybe it's different for you - but I'm often in groups
where there is only bards and rangers meleeing - given that a Ranger's melee
skill is pretty much equal to a Bards - I'd argue that shows the Bard and
Ranger has as much "Need" as the warrior for the good weapon. One of the
reason I'm meleeing is to keep that Enchanter from getting hit for
example...

Once again - I'm not criticizing Elfson and his guild for what clearly works
for them - just pointing out that IMHO - it's and exception not the rule.
And I've seen too many cases where bards and simply screwed out of things
they can justify a need for as much as anyone else.
