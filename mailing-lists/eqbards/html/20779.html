> Subject: Really cool corpse locating tip
>
> First you use Locating Lyric like you normally do. It'll
> point you in the direction of the corpse, but more
> importantly, it'll make the corpse your current target.
>
> Next you use Solidarity of Vision (L34) to bind sight to the
> corpse. Switch to an external camera view, rotate around, and
> usually you can tell from the surroundings exactly where the
> corpse is. VERY useful for finding corpses in dangerous
> sections of a dungeon, or if the corpse isn't where the person
> thinks he died.
