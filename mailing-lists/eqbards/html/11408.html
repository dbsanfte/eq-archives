The sequence of events for me - I fell in lava right near the entrance
(from Lavastorm). Given the number of hit points a 29 bard has - and the
lava doing damage in 56 increments - I realized I had a good amount of
time - and tried to climb out.

I didn't realize this wasn't possible - and got myself in deep (nice pun
there <g>). There is a "Lavafall" (waterfall type thingy) that you can't
climb out. Right past there is a zone to Sol-B.

My situation was that my Druid helper died because our levitations wore
off. Being the selfless type - he renewed mine before his - and
subsequently died when his fizzled. I figured I could "safely" zone to B
because I had a freshly renewed Levitate and would have plenty of time to
zone back if I had no better luck getting our of the lava in B.

When I got to B - I can't remember exactly the layout (first time I was
there anyhow) but there's like a big cavernous area - so with levitate it
was pretty easy to get to solid ground.

As far as your question - the zone from Sol-B to Sol-A is already lava -
so you'd already have to have fell into lava in Sol-B to zone through Lava
to Sol-A...
