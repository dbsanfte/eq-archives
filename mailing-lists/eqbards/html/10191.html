Subject: Need song info? Check this out.


There is a resource available that many people don't know about, but it is invaluable for answering
questions like, "Does xxx song do this? Or not?"

It's eqss.stratics.com/spells

This is actually the spell data file, parsed out for the web.

Now much of it is meaningless (to me anyway), and it doesn't answer questions about song/spell
interactions, but this is a valuable resource for understanding spells and songs.

I am not in anyway affiliated with this site, so don't ask me why things are why they are, it's just a
plug.
