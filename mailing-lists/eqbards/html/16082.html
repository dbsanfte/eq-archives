Stop worrying. SPDAT.EFF is a farcical file at best. As a matter of fact
GZ posted a bunch of false data in it _on purpose_ because they don't want
people knowing what's going in for Kunark.

The stuff going in for test is for testing. I seriously doubt half of
what's listed goes into final.

Besides. I hate that people are even trying to figure this out already.
All you guys are doing is getting really worked up over something that may
or may not happen.

Sheesh folks, it's a GAME, give me a break. Yeah I'm miffed that we're
missing 3 songs still. Sure that irks me. Do I care? Not really, it
doesn't make me any 'weaker' as a Bard. Will I be upset if they don't add
songs? Yeah, but why should I borrow trouble that doesn't even exist yet?

Getting all worked up 6 weeks before the product comes out based on
information that isn't even *real* is silly.
