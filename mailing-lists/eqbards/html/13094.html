The enchanter's AE mez may have worked initially, but we assumed that he
would be OOM a long time before we killed all the mobs. If we had gone that
route, then I and the other bard could have started playing lullaby at that
point to try and keep all the mobs off the enchanter while he medded. We
decided that the enchanter would contribute more by casting his charm, stun,
and DOT spells and let the two bards handle crowd control. For each mob we
faced, he would have his pet attack, stun once, DOT once, then med and let
the two tanks and other bard finish off the mob. Doing it this way
conserved his mana for the rest of the battle.
