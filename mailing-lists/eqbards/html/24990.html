"Robert(Bob) Smith" <rws1111@...> wrote:

> I need armor/jewelry suggestions that a 46th
> mage could acquire with out much help.

* I would suggest starting out with bronze because it's easily available in the market areas. Also, Ulrich McMannus in West Karana Barb village drops medium bronze...

* Kill dragoon Zxytl and loot yourself a dirk -- easy kill at 46, it is a good piercer and it fits in the range slot when you get a better. Always a market for resale too.

* Cloak of the icebear, AC 8 and 20 cold resist -- 75-100 pp on Rodcet Nife (compare with LS Cloak with same AC for 200pp and you'll need the resists down the road anyway) This drops off Karg and Martar Icebear -- I'd just hang out in the area, kill wooly monmouths and auction for the cloak. the tusks sell for around 10pp so you'll likely break even on the purchase.

* 5 Dex/7 Cha rings. they go for 90-150pp each.

* Crystal fiber whip -- 8/26 and about 50pp in crystal caverns (on Rodcet Nife...).

* Camp a few ancient coins for you bard. Toughest ones for me to get were in Overthere and Kaesora...the other ones are pretty cheap to just buy.

* Cured Tizmak is good armor (not the regular) -- gloves are AC 9 and some stats...tunic is AC 15 or so and very light. Uncommon drops from Tizmaks in GD and all very green to a 46. Careful tho, even greens will aggro with bad faction;-)

* Hang out in EC and watch for desparate people trying to unload stuff at off peak hours -- I saw a pair of singing steel gauntlets go for 900pp last week...in EC you just never know;-)
