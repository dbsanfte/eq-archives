If Verant think the EULA can relieve them of the burden of following Federal
law they are quite incorrect.

No agreement, verbal or written, can cause an individual to abridge their
rights granted under the law except in cases of national security or to
protect the common good (commonly known as the "Fire!" clause. Any such
agreement is null and void to the extent of which it abridges those rights.

opinions.

says that you must be allowed to say anything in any place.
