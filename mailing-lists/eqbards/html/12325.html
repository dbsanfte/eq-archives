In a long, tedious 2 day struggle inside the PoF, CT was finally
dropped on Innoruuk. Had to ignore my girlfriend for hours on
end, so now I owe her big time. ;) CT would have dropped sooner,
but we tried all this during times where most people had to sleep
or go to work the next day. Thus, during the nighttime a small
group of people held the plane clear until others would arrive
after naps/work. The combined efforts of 5 or 6 guilds paid off
around 10:00pm ET yesterday when the plane was finally cleared.
At 10:30pm ET Cazic Thule was dead.

Two days in the PoF teaches you a whole lot. More than one short
12 hour burst, imo. I logged 32 hours of the 48 it was held. I
know the zone like the back of my hand now, thanks to Solidarity
of Vision and exploration.

24 people in the Cazic wave dropped him in less than 2 mins.
While we were buffing up, 4 mobs spawned in the zone, and thus
wave2 with 24 more people came to aid with the extras after the
God fell.

GMs were present during the slaying. First ever on Innoruuk.

I got the privledge of being in CT GR1, the primary and initial
group which charged Cazic. Only one member of my group perished,
100% resisted Panic. Verses of Victory and Mystical Shielding
were played in battle. Cazic Thule hit me once for 40 dmg and
once for 171 dmg. He never touched me again, although I was
Gravity Fluxed about 3 times during the battle. I did over 500
combined dmg, including 2 yk procs.

I think I died about 6 times on the whole trip. Two of those
times, I really couldn't do anything to help it if you get my
meaning.

To be honest, we bent him over hard. He didnt stand a chance.
Since I was the only bard there for 90% of the time, and since
I did breakin, I am now carrying the Lyssa's Darkwood Piccalo.
Yeah it's useless, but it's God loot. And the only one on this
server too ;)

Since there was nothing else for a bard on the plane, a lot of
people owe me hateplane trips now ;) And I've already gotten
2 invites to go during the next week. More imbrued here I come ;)
