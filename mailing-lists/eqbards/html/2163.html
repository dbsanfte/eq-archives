Subject: Wonderful Pest Control


As I ran to Halas to practice my new song the Aquatic Ayre, I noticed the
spiders were clogging up the little nook near the final path to halas. As a
young one bound there, I found it quite annoying that the spiders didn't allow
any monsters to thrive except for them.

Equipping my trusty mandolin, I furrowed my brow, and sang and played a Mighty
Painful tune about Inoruuk, and with a chord of dissonance, the entire festering
nook of spiders perished, all at once!

Take that, Trumpy Ratslayer, see if you can kill more than a Bard with nasty
Chords!
