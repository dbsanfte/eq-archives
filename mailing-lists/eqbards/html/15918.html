We have broken Az. island a few times on Brell.

Similar to other Planes, you need a large party and knowledge of the safe
spot. Try and have at least 36. I believe the SS is on the sw edge of the
island, but not really sure. Have a monk port down first and try and get
to the SS and FD. The SS isn't really all that safe, but there are few
patrols in that area of the island. Once in safe spot, he can let you know
if there are any patrols around.

Have everybody magic buffed, sowed, invis and see invis and port down group
at a time and run to SS.

Azeracks have a DoT that will also levitate you. If you get hit with that,
make sure you don't float off the island. Otherwise, when the DoT wears
off you will find yourself in the water off FP.
