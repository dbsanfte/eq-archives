I went into that cave region under the Everfrost river that
someone else mentioned. One goblin diver at the entrance.
It's dark inside so the ultravision song is handy. It's
rather maze-like but very large so it isn't too bad. I didn't
map the place, just wanted to see what was inside. Mostly
green ice-boned skeletons. There was one red human, looked
like a necromancer. Sulan Mcboor or something like that.
Ready to attack, so I stayed away. There were several more
passages that I didn't explore. I'll try to map it some time.

If ice-bones give you good experience with little chance of
death, then it might be a good uncrowded area to play in. If
you have to run though, or the bard dies, the rest of the
group is going to be in trouble since I'm not sure you can
make the trip from the ice hole to the cave without the water
breathing song.
