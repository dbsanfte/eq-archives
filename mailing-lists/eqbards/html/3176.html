I think it's time for you to make a boat trip. A good place that I hunted from level 11-13 (Although in a group...) is North Karana. At level 13-14, you should be able to solo this area fairly well. Make sure you have a magical weapon, as Wisps are a common sight in the area. Avoid Hill Giants and Griff/awns/ons/ennes. If one of these locks on to you, you're better of running for a zone instead of the guards. I liked to hunt near the gypsy camp, as this is where I saw most of the wisps.
