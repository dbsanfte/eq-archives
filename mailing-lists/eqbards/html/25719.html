JUST Mana and straggler chaser? I think NOT! First of all - AOE slow.
Those mobs are gonna be hitting low HP casters - so slows important. AT 48
Selo's Cessation has a slow, a snare, and a DOT. Drop the DOT for better
slow and snare at 54. Denon's Discord gives some AoE Dottage. And you can
throw in the level 2 AOE Dot for yucks.

But the best thing for pulling? You can only sing it during daytime - but
really the only use of Song of Highsun (56) is for PBAOE pulling. It gates
mobs back to their spawn points and DOESN'T clear agro. The mob will come
back with as many friends as he can find between you and his spawn point.

At night - sing Song of Midnight - Selo's and Fears mobs - they'll run away
FAST and come back with friends as well...
