Blurred Map: A lady by the arena in Qeynos is concerned about a map her
apprentice had on him when he was shipwrecked while crossing over from
Erudin. It is possible that you will have to perform another quest before
she tells you this information. On the island in Erud's crossing, you can
kill zombie sailors who will occasionally drop a blurred map. Give this to
the lady.
