Before you read this just keep in mind im just curious as to why worship,
sure i understand that some stuff should be wieldable by deity specific
characters, but also keep in mind that they also should keep the balance for
the agnostic people in the world. I chose agnostic for many reasons, i find
i am more accepted in different towns because of this, not much but still it
helps. I like the idea of deity specific stuff but i *hope* that they will
not favor certain deities and have deity specific stuff for each deity as
well as unholy items for us agnostics.

On another note...

Why worship. This reminds me of an old pun advertisement i read somewhere...

honda in the 70's had an add that was something like :
follow us we're riding hondas

Ride a harley, don't follow anyone...

It says it all, i know i've gone off topic abit but i personally don't like
