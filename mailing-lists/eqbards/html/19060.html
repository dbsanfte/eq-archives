Well I saw you mention Kaesora in a previous post Sylly and I thought I
would give the list my experiences from there. My guild and I have pretty
much lived there for the last 3 weeks or so. We have went to every part of
this dungeon and killed every mob there. If you are in the high 30's to low
40's, it is an outstanding place to hunt. For a while we were the only
groups there. There have been a few more people showing up lately, but it
is still under utilized in my opinion. There are several named mobs in here
which drop some good loot for the level range. Nearly everyone in the
guild, 21 people, have gotten a dusty ransacker's pack. A couple of the
books, a nice 2h slashing weapon, warrior only piercer, and several of the
bard only piercers. So the loot is very well flowing. If you plan on going
to the church, bring two groups, if you are in the 40's, and DO NOT use
pets. We were there last week, very well balanced groups that included two
necros and a shaman using pets. Well everything went well, after I died 3
times (another long story that I shan't go into now :)) and we made our way
down to the church. We started pulling 2's and 3's, no problem with the
best enchanter on Veeshan IMHO, and we were going through them like candy.

Now the fun starts. Out of nowhere we had 7 spiders on us. Mind you there
are no spiders down near the church. We kill them and finish clearing the
church. We go in and set up camp in the basement. We have a few pops, kill
them, have a few more, kill them, get some roamers, etc. Then it happened.
A pet disappears. A spider comes, and another, and another and so forth and
so on. When we finally rest, we have killed over 35 mobs in a row. They
just kept flooding the place. Very very scary being that far in a dungeon
with that many mobs. We decide to head out and we go up into the church and
the entire, I think, dungeon runs up on top of us including the named mob
Xalgoz or something like that. Well I am the first one to die, and then
seven more guildmates die, one person went LD, and 6 more evac out.
Needless to say it wasn't pretty on the corpse recovery. Fun was had by all
though.

We went back the next day with no pets, and we had no freaky aggro. The
only things that aggroed us were intentional.

So in conclusion, if you are in the range of levels I stated above, grab
your guild/group/friends and head down there. Pick up a map though, because
you can't get out of the dungeon the way you come in. You go in and out of
the same zone line, but you can't go up where you drop down from. Very
cryptic, but it makes it that much more dangerous.
