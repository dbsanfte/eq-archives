The reason for the uproar is that the ENTIRE game up to this point has been
tested and balanced with the 'haste bug' in place. All those fast spawning
kunark dungeons we are supposed to due are now going to be much harder, as
either the groups will be killing things slower or spending more mana to
kill things at the same pace. Either way total combat time (battle +
recovery) will be longer as more mana will be spent (more mana for haste or
more mana for healing/damage spells). The PvE balance is about to get a
huge push, to the detrement of the players.
