You can make a swarmattack hotkey too

/pet attack
/pet target

that way once the pet's attacking you have it up in your window for easy
recharming or dotting

Also... I tab it into the tab spool as well, in case I get hit or targets
change unexpectedly.

Add a /pet report health key to get accurate reports on your pets health
(WAY more accurate than the laggy health bar)...

and you're off and running. If you have the fortune to have a clicky
invis item of any type (tiniest cloak of the darkest night, boots of the
mosquito, and a ring... forget the ring name) then you can let the pet get
REAL low before you break charm and keel it with dots/bellow.

It's quite an amazing way to farm XP. In higher levels we are, in the
right camp, the best XP soloers in the game... hands down.
