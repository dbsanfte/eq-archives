I visited Blackburrow today in search of Onyx earrings. A
huge train came up to the zone. I couldn't just stand and do
nothing so I hit the lullaby. It worked. On more than 4
mobs. At one point, I must've had a dozen or more gnolls
stacking away the Zzzzz's. So it would seem there is no 4 mob
limit as had been previously stated.

I didn't attack a single one. They all attacked me because
they hated my song. They only managed to take a bubble of my
health in about 5 minutes because my AC was 388. All the
lower level folks loved it. They got all the experience and
took virtually no damage.

I got a ton of faction for it. The guards and merchants in
Qeynos now view me amiably (used to be apprehensive).
