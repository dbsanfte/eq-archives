For most skills you will start with the number of points equal to your
level that you first trained in it. I assume that you put your first point
in Stringed when it became available at level 8. Exceptions to this that I
know of are the languages, which seem to always start at 1 regardless of
level, and Dual Wield, which appears to start at double your level.
