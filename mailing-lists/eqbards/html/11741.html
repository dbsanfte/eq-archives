When you cross the bridge and enter the tower - you almost immediately see a
ladder going up. That is the 'supplier room' (iirc) and both the supplier
and assassin spawn in there (keep your back to the wall - the froggies have
a mean back stab.)

When you go from the bedroom to the tower, you need to have invis to unded
but even so - the GDBS (bad skellys) will agro on you if you don't hug the
wall on the right hand side as you go by them. You cant merely waltz by
thinking your invis to unded will hold them at bay. I have heard that 35ish
bards can lament them and pull singles. Or if you have 2 groups moving into
the tower and the bedroom is camped - you can just kill all them skellies
and be done with it. The reason you don't go around the other way with
invis to unded is that there is live stuff there that could see and agro you
as well as unded stuff.
