SING A SONG OF JOY, NOVEMBER 1, 2000
Source: Everquest General Discussion Forums.

I have some information about changes we will be making to a few bard songs
with the next patch.

-Songs with a damage absorbtion component will have that component removed.

-Pixie Strike will have its secondary resist reduced.

-Song of Twilight will be singable at any time of the day. Its secondary
resist will be removed completely. It will have a longer duration. And it
will require an instrument.

-The single target haste spells will be self-only.

We will also be revising the bardic epic weapon.

This should all go live with the next patch, which for now we are
tentatively scheduling for late next week.

- Alan
