When I first started playing EQ, I was having the same problem with
my computer consistently locking up on me. I have a Diamond Viper V550 RIVA
TNT card and was using the most current Diamond drivers for the card. I
installed the Nvidia Detonator drivers at the recommendation of Verant Tech
Support (thats right, they actually responded to my email). I have had no
problems with lockups since I installed the Detonator drivers. If you have
a RIVA TNT card you may want to look into that as a solution to your
problem.
