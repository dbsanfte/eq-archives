Subject: My colors


Using EQClient Generator V2 (from stratics I think) I created my fav color
scheme. While I can't post pics (without attaching them) I thought I would
quickly explain my thinking.

Geting colors you can read in all places and windowed or overlay is *hard*
some colors it means low saturation, some it means high. These, whether you
can stand them or not, are at a minimum readable in all zones supplied in
EQClient.
Some clors I chose for personal priority (I don't fundamentally care as much
about duels as I do about skill increases), some for legacy reasons.

Buying/selling etc: Green
tell/who: purple

Group/guild: more readable versions

Damage red to grey according to personal use

Shout/OOC (mostly I didn't like OOC being mixed in with auctions) This was
hard, OOC and shout are nearly the same, and shout is almost never as
important as someone else hitting me. So I lowered the saturation and
brightness and made them both reddish

Skills are yellow, which might seem overly dramatic, but as skill increases
happen infrequently and I care about seeing them, I thought I would make it
easy on myself :)

See what you think:

Tszaaz
