One question I have...what are some techniques you guys use for organizing
your spell book? Heh...

For my bard, they're just in order by level. This is easiest, since I can
really choose from almost any song, regardless of level. Makes it easy to
guess which page a song is on. I even left the spot blank for Melliflous
Motion (spelling..heh..). I've never bothered to buy that one. Seems
awfully dangerous...heh.

For the cleric, and the rest of my casters, the first 8 scribe slots are the
8 spells I have memmed. Then I try to decide which 2 are the next most
used, and put those in the 2 remaining scribe spots on pages 1 and 2. Pages
3 and 4 are spells I don't use often, but the possibility of use exists.
Page 5 are my cures, poison, disease, etc. Page 6 are the endures, poison,
disease, etc. The low level spells, ones I don't expect to ever use again,
are currently around page 20 - 21.
