Bone chips are pretty good money... Practically every necro in my guild
says "Time to go to Kurn's." when they run out of bone chips. Going price
is 3-5pp per stack of 20, and practically every skel drops 1 or 2. I swung
by there for chips for a necro friend (180 chips for 40pp) and then went
through the place sightseeing. Occassionally a skel would agro on me even
at 51, and I expect that they will at 60 too. Didn't see any named mobs,
but did see the throne room and the Burynai dungeon. Didn't get far enough
in to find the porter to the outside. I'll do that next time.

I saw the sweetest most innocent thing while I was in there that really made
me harken back to my youthful days in EQ. A 17th Iksar monk was on some
stairs Feigned and was being camped by a couple of skels. He had low health
so I killed the skels and got got to talking with him. He said he was
"camping a skel whos rare drop was a pretty nice sword." Well that got my
interest up, although I expected it to be a sword appropriate to the level
of the place... Turns out he was camping the rare drop of -- Fine Steel
Scimitar.
