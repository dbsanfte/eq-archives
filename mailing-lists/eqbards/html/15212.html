There were only about 30 missed notes in the latter logfile,
which probably means the sample is way to small to see a trend
for missed notes. Missed notes is probably one of the easiest
things to test though - just get your stats in the
configuration you want to test, turn on your logfile, then
rapidly stop/start a song over and over. Don't even wait for
the song to actually start playing, since you'll get the
missed note message afterwards. Then count up the number of
"your song ends" messages (successes), and compare with the
number of missed notes (failures).
