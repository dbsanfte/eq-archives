Subject: My opinion on the safest Freeport<->Qeynos route.


In my opinion, if you want the absolute safest route, go the Runnyeye
route. I've never had a bad experience in either, but the former is
definitely as safe as you can get if you're careful. There's very little
chance of getting trained or something spawning right where you're
standing. Just remember, in the gorge go CRAZY high up on the wall. You'll
be fine, trust me. Be anal about it, take 20 minutes to do it if you
wish... unless you have extreme bad luck or make an awful mistake you'll be
alright.
Just my opinion. It's not the fastest or the most convenient way but it's
the safest as far as I'm concerned.
