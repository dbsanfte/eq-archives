I would agree with the suggestion to make bear hide boots at least. I have
made and sold maybe 50 or so of these things at 5pp each. Also a quick run
to surefall will net you a mail run at the same time.

Lament is the best song you get other than Selo's until you are 11th level.
It will allow you to easily solo critters characters 5 levels higher than
you just look at because they can't take 3 at once. It allowed me to sol at
the bandit camp in Lesser Faydark 4 levels before anyone else I ever saw
there. No one else could handle the multiple spawns they would have to
face, but I could easily pull one out at a time and make boocou plat off the
bronze spears they carried. same deal in w karana with bandits if you are
careful. If Lament is resisted you just flee immediately via Selo's and
there's no problem.
