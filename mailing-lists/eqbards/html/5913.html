Subject: RE: What is a PvP zone?


PvP zones are areas where other players can attack you, even on a server
where that is not normally possible. These zones can be found in Qeynos,
Freeport, and Arena. There may be others I am not aware of. A warning
message appears when you enter one saying "You have entered a Player vs.
Palyer zone."

You get songs stuck when you hit the button just as your song becomes ready
again on the client side, but is still unready on the server side from what
I've seen. The two get confused and suddenly your song is playing
continuously. This can be annoying but a continual Bellow is fairly potent
as I found out by accident. You can usually unstick your song by rapidly
starting and stopping another song until it clears. Getting bashed or
stunned wil also unstick it, but that is not a recommended method generally.
:-)
