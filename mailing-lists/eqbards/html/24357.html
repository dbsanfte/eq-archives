Hey Tyunes!

I remember the first time I went there. The 3 or 4 of us were 50 and 51
(Kunark just out) and we did ok as long as we worked slowly. But at one
point, we got a few too many of them angry and we had to evac. I notice
that you are 36 and possibly some others in your raid were similar levels.
This could be why you had some difficulty. The social range of mobs is
directly linked to player levels. Even so, the social range in that dungeon
and that spot is some thing to contend with. I used lament to try to reduce
the social ranges on all my pulls so that we could fight as few as possible.
And finally, there are a series of "names" in the library (3 or 4) and
"nearly three hours" might not be long enough to cycle through all of them.
Also, you may not have seen him on tracking if other mobs had filled your
list and there was no room for him - sometimes I see mobs on my screen that
are not on my tracking list. The tambourine is a fine item and worth going
back for another attempt - good luck!
