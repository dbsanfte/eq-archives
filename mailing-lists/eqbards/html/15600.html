and online. When you play a character, it becomes a part of
you. Even on MU* that you don't pay ANYTHING, people get the
same feelings. They cry RL (even people who do not normally
cry at ALL) when they do not impress on a Pern-themed MU*
(PernMUSH, SouCon MUSH, DragonDawn, Dragonsfire MOO, Red Star MUSH)

they wanted. Even firelizard hatchings can become near and dear

they have become a part of you.

their consent...and very few people ever killed off their characters
unless they were having to leave the MUSH for one reason or another.
