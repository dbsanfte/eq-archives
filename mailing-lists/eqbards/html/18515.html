I play at 640x480, but then I also use a pair of LCD Shutter glasses to get
some realistic depth perception. Things look fabulous and the mind tends to
do antialiasing which makes objects look even better at low resolution than
a high resolution without the glasses. If you ever get a chance to demo
some of these glasses, I highly recommend it.
