From Cazic Thule, land of the lounge leezards.....

In the Throne Room, where leezards and monkies abound....

A fella named "Tae Templar" ocassionally carries Rubicite armor, and sometimes a nice Leezardscale cloak. His partner in crime, the "Tae Diviner" sometimes carries Rubicite Bracers (I am told), but usually carries the Leezardscale Mantle.

I am told also that the Rubicite Boots and Helm are carried by a critter upstairs of the throneroom. I haven't dared venture that far yet...

Lastly, the Rubicite Breastplate, which supposedly insanely adds 10 points to ALL your stats, can be found on the Avatar of Fear (I've been told). Amazignly enough, the Avatar is also the critter that sometimes drops the Demon Claw. I have not found out if he drops both at the same time or not. He is still just a wee bit high for my group. (Low 30's)

Good luck! I've seen Rubicite Greaves sell for almost 1000 plat, even though they aren't THAT much better than Plate Greaves.
