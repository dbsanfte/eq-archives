Go to North Desert of Ro. Lots of stuff there to keep you busy, also check
Oasis. Or try Misty Thicket, it has goblins, orcs, spiders, young kodiaks,
lots of things that will keep you busy up thru around 14th level.

Or you could even go back to Crushbone, at 12th level except for the royal
party you should be able to take down most orcs as long as you have a medium
sized party.

I went back at 16th level and totally controlled the whole dungeon (minus
Dvinn), with a party tho, we killed Dvinn 8 times.
