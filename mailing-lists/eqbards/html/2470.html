Subject: Interesting comment about Charisma


A fellow on an enchanter's board said he'd played around with
several enchanters of differing charisma during beta. One of
the effects he claims charisma has is at attracting a
monster's attention. i.e. just as high charisma makes you
move liked by NPCs, it makes you more hated by monsters.

Perhaps that could explain all the strange going ons about
bards being attacked in preference to others, even if the bard
is not playing a song? Maybe people can comment how often
they've had this problem, and list their charisma.
