Using my invis song in Mistmoore I have conned indifferent to every single
mob I have encountered thus far including will sappers. I believe that deep
inside the castle there are some casters that will buff themselves and other
mobs with see invisible but I have yet to encounter these myself. I was
invisible to every single mob you mention in your post for 3 days of camping
MM.

I generally have camped in the Courtyard and when someone calls train I tell
my group to gather round me and kick up invis. As the mobs pour out of the
castle I target and con every one, all indifferent to us. I have not yet
actually ventured INSIDE the castle so your mileage may vary although it has
been posted that others have invis'd themselves successfully all the way
into the music room from the front entrance of the castle with no trouble.
