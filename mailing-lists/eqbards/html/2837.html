Even singing it, if you don't understand the fundamentals of brass notes,
you'll have a hard time with that Discord. (And it's MUCH more effective
with an instrument anyway, don't bother singing it) It's a strangely
fitting first song for bards to learn their brass skills with. The whole
point of the "song" is to make a deafening racket, something easy to do when
you can hardily play a horn!

Must practice. Find a quiet, safe place, away from the radius of any
wandering NPCs--and leave yourself play a few hours. (Like the schoolchild
who finds a secluded place to learn their trumpet, away from any wandering
parents) I also went to a low level dungeon and just "practiced" on swarms
of greenies, killing them all. Just make sure you play it constantly for a
good long while. Now, hope you practiced wind, for your new song?
