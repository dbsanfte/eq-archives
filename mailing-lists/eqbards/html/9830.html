On lambent stone prices, I have yet to buy one. Here is another area where
guilding really shines like Sylly pointed out. I get tells from guildmates
very often saying 'I got you a lambent, let me know when you need one.' Even
the kiting druids were shouting to the zone for bards to loot them and this
on Xegony even! I have offered to pay for looting bracelets in Unrest and

capping the price for lambents and currently when I am in a dungeon or zone
with bard armor items, I make sure I or someone with me loots it before
letting it go to waste. Another bard in the zone hunting the item would get
first cut though. My plan is if we gather enough pieces, we will be able to
do the quests for bards who aren't having any luck getting them themselves.
And now that Gediprie has joined my guild, I have to look afte him too <g>.

firewalker boots to finish boots. The gems for these pieces plus guantlets
has cost me 1000pp. Then it was time to kill more HG's for money as well as
lambents, I was down to only a few gold in the bank! This leaves no roo to
pay for the lambent. Being guilded really helps though, just put out the
call for an HG expedition and I had a mid 30s to 40s crew in route
