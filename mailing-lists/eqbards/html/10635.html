Oh. I blew this one once. A clerical friend of mine and I were grouped in
our mid-twenties to kill griffawns in NK one day and I'm speeding around
looking for some prey. I stumble across this named ghoul that is wandering
the plains of NK. I kinda freak a bit, con it and it's white or blue to me
I don't recall. Well since I'm fighting with a Cleric I say "INC UNDEAD"
and punch it in the face. We kill it pretty easily and it has this "Bag of
Troll Guts". Well I too remembered the quest given in Qeynos so I ask for
and receive the bag. So later that night I jet down to Qeynos and give the
bag to the female in the bard guild that is looking for Troll Gut for
strings. She says "Thank you", and that's all. Then I realized later DOH!
I never tried right clicking on it to open the damn thing. Is it a 4 slot
container with troll guts in each slot? After all it's called a BAG of
troll guts. I've been kicking myself ever since. Try right clicking on it
to see if it will open like a backpack. I'm dying to know.

I don't recall her name, but the person is the vendor in the middle of the
lefthand wall when you enter the Qeynos guild hall. If you're patient you
can sit in the guild hall and hear the NPCs having a discussion along the
lines off "Shhh.. don't tell soandso what his strings are made of." Hail
her, ask about the strings and you'll start the conversation.
