1. Three without losing any effects, sometimes four with one effect
dropping for a few seconds
2. It works best if you hotkey every song you want to play, and instead
of looking at them to see if you can click song number 2 yet, just let
yourself fall into the rythm of it.
3. For battle it's anthem, chords and hymn; sometimes subsituting in
elemental rhythms if I don't need healing or dot. Solo battle I usually
twist anthem, warsong, and hymn, dropping hymn for a pulse of chords
once in a while. Out of battle I'd rather not be twisting, my fingers
need a rest and I like to chat, so I'll just leave hymn going. When
travelling I just put in the drum and play selo's, maybe twisting it
with one of the buff songs for some extra str when I'm encumbered.
