I've always had a problem with our damage shields actually. Unless you are
being attacked by multiple mobs, which with an enchanter doesn't happen too

options which produce more damage per 6 second pulse. About the only time
we've used the damage shields was in multi-bard groups. There's almost
always a better song to play than a damage shield, heck there probably
always is a better song unless you're just wading thru greenies.
