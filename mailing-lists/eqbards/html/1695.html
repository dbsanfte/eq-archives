If you have 4 mobs in a camp that con blue or greeen you individually lament 3
of the mobs that you don't want to fight, then attack the forth and back way
off. I usually intitiate with bellow so that I don't have to stand very close.
If you fail to lament any of the mobs, that mob will immediately attack you just
like you'd bellow'd it, and very likely it's friends will come with it if they
are close enough to the offended mob. Make sure you are lamenting with your lute
equipped (stringed song) so that you get the extended duration. I can actually
lament 4, but sometimes the first wears off too soon, so I go with three. I have
never had a green mob resist the lament with my lute equipped, even if it conn'd
as a tough green. Blues will occasionally resist it if they are the higher level
ones, and withblack/white cons I would expect a resist.
