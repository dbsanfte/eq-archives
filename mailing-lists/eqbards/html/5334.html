My armor and weapons total, with 3 weapons, is 53 lbs. I wear Crush's
tunic, very light AC12 and +8 to Magic/Fire/Cold. I also wear a lizardscale
mantle, used to wear a mesh mantle though, smae ac as leather, but far
lighter. I wear barbed sleves now, but they are kinda heavy so I may trade
them in an go back to mesh sleeves. I wear mesh pants, a leatherfoot raider
cap, same ac as leather. I haven't had any difficulties staying in fights
so far, despite my light armor. I have found that while I often take big
hits, I get hit less often due to more frequent dodges. It seems to work
out fairly close to the same as when I tried going all banded and its far
lighter. In all banded I was close to max weight with no loot and only two
weapons.
