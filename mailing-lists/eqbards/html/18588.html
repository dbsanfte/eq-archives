You may run into the same problem with guilds that I have. I found a great
guild, one that suits my playstyle and attitude. But almost all of the
guild members are on the US West coast and don't log in until I'm logging
out. Making things more difficult is that the 4 or 5 that play the same
hours I do are either Iksars or 10+ levels higher than me. Part of my
reason for wanting to join a guild was to make grouping easier. If this is
a concern of yours then you might be better off checking which guilds have
the most comparable level players on when your on, rather than which guilds
have the most members enrolled.
