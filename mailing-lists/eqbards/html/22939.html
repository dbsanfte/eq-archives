Lavastorm = No - I think they'll crank up the xp modifier for Najena, and

South Karana = Unk - No if they reason that Arena is only 2 zone lines away.

Dagnor's Cauldron = No - Gfay and Kaladim are both close. Kedge would

Lfay = No - Only 1 zone away from Gfay, and the spires. Although the

Feeroot = No - Only 1 zone away from Cazic Thule. Too close.

with light aligned guards? The points for, taking into consideration the

proximity to Lguk.

All of Odus = No - Between Erud for the lights, and Paineel for the darks,
nothing needed.

N Ro, S Ro, E Commons, W Commons = No - Proximity to FP, Neriak, High Pass,

death trap with little reason to try.)

Everfrost = Yes - A dark aligned camp would be most likely, since light
aligned folks can bind in Halas. Fixing Permafrost will also help, though.

Kunark and Velious - I just don't have enough knowledge of these zones. My
