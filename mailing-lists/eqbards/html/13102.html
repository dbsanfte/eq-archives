In my experience, MR isn't nearly as important as FR/CR. With 2 Rhythm
spells + Resist Magic, most classes are already at 140-150 MR, at which
point I feel to sacrafice more for MR is at a point of diminishing
return. Yes, you'll get feared about 1 in 3, but getting feared once or
twice a dragon doesn't really affect the outcome.

Your FR and CR however, is what will negate the area effect damage spells
from the dragon. As a class that cannot bind, you're only a contributing
factor as long as you stay alive. Your resist songs is what will keep
you and your tanks long enough for the clerics to heal.

Resist equipment that I always wear:

Crown of Froglok Kings (+20 CR/FR)
Symbols of Loyalty to Vox (+14 CR)
Lambent Sleeves and Greaves (+4 CR/FR/MR)
Guise of the Deceiver (+7 MR)
Dragoon's Dirk (+2 MR)

Resist equipment I carry around for dragons:

Earrings of Fire Reflection (+10 FR)
Plat Peridot Rings (+20 CR)
Plat Emerald Rings (+20 FR)
Gold Emerald Bracers (+14 FR)
Cloak of the Icebear (+20 CR)

The hard to get equipment (crown and symbols) are good items in and of
themselves. The resist equipment I switch in are either Jewelcrafter
made, bought from Gypsies, or in 1 case, easily purchasable off people
camping themselves a GZ. And really if you prefer lambent bracers to
Symbols (I prefer them because I like the band of silver on my wrists),
you can always substitute in Gold Peridot Bracers to save yourselves the
camp.

There's a few more things I can switch around, such as icy greaves and
such, but my resists are high enough to easily dispatch a dragon. None of
this stuff is planar, and almost anyone has access to them. The only
other thing you really need is a mm battledrum.
