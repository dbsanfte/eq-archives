Subject: Re: Proper use of Kellin's Lucid Lullabye


I found that it was rather ineffective (totally useless) at 15th
level. (thus dangerous since it attracts a lot of attention.) Only
now at 20th level am I finding it useful. It is great at crowd
control when you have a large pull of which several are blue/green.
If (a big if) every one concentrates on the most dangerous mob (also
the one most likely to resist) you have a good chance of lulling the
blue green mobs. They will still break out and hit you every once and
a while but it works fairly well. You do need to continually recast
it over and over. I don't try to weave at all when I am doing this.
So, I would advise not using it till you are higher level. You might
try it at around 18th since I had given up on it and didn't even try
using it again till 20th.
