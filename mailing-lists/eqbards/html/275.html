Joshua Owyang wrote:

> What's in the Lavastorm mountains?

Lots of fire drakes, fire elementals, fire imps, fire goblins, rock
dervishes, etc... all very tough, and some can only be harmed by
magic/magic weapons. However, I've heard tell that many creatures carry
gems with them. There is also a camp in that area that sells the much
sought-after combine weapons.

Connected to Lavastorm is Najena.... also very tough, but containing
good loot for a change. This dungeon is crawling with the undead, so be
sure to bring a handy Cleric with you!
