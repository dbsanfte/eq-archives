My group has been hunting here quite a bit, and it IS a very nice place to hunt. Lots of things
to charm, lots of space to run. There are three big things to worry about though, from my
experience:

- The pathing and warping in the zone is VERY bad. You'll be fighting a giant and suddenly
it's just GONE. Sometimes it warps just a short distance, which is no big deal,
he'll come running after you quickly enough. The real danger is when they warp a long
distance away, and then never come back. Never, that is, until you happen to walk
near him later, and he comes running with his 4 giant friends and the little cyclops
who wants to be part of the group.

- Hasten Quickboots or whatever the name of the little dude from the JBoot quest is
steals your kills.

- Sometimes there's too MANY hill giants. When you're in a group, one hill giant is tasty,
two makes them see how powerful you are as a bard. Three means run to the zone.
Four means run to the zone and try and take a screen cap. :)
