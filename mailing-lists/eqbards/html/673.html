However, there is a trick to the gorge, a path high up that the denizens of the gorge do not appear
to know about (I refrain from posting the spoiler but if people want it let me know). You must NOT
sing Accelerondo, as it is too easy to fall off the cliff (there are some tricky, but very possible,
corners). When you do fall, you need to beat it back up the cliff stay put, and heal. There is only
one place where you breifly cannot keep to the cliff.Be patient and everything will go fine.

I went from Kelethin to Qeynos as an eight level bard, I went via Highpass Hold and skidded stunned
to the zone, it was VERY close. On the return trip I died once in the gorge trying to rush through,
but succeeded with a calm approach.

The only other trick is, only EVER run the edge of the zone, do NOT take the roads.
