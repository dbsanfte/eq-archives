Subject: Use for Song of Twilight


Like Pixie Strike, the resists for Song of Twilight make it dodgy to use for
crowd control. What it is great at, however, is traffic control.

In many areas, controlling the placement of the mob is important. They get
pushed towards walls or bridges or just move away too fast when we are
chaining. So I stand in the chosen spot and use Song of Twilight to pull the
mob to me. The rogue I often group with loves it; I can pull that mob away
from the wall or corner so he can backstab.
