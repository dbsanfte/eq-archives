When you are ready for the undead bard do this:

1. Go on a Trak raid. If you don't die, get killed by a mob there in the
lair. Get rezzed but leave your corpse. Go chill, get xp etc. About 18 hours
later zone into Seb and use Corpse Locate song on yourself to target your
corpse. Then sing Solidarity of Vision on your corpse. You will bind sight
and have a clear view of the lair.
