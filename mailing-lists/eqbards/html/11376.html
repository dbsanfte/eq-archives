Lake Rathe is a nice area - the Aviaks give good experience - especially if
you get in a group where you can use the AE instrument attack. Eventually,
as others have said, you should be able to solo the areas.

East Karana is also nice - undead reavers and gnoll reavers - although
you'll find that some of the gnoll reavers are green but even some of these
give a little exp. If you solo the reavers, try not to pull more than one at
a time - will make for faster down time. Same thing for Gorge Hounds, which
are also in this zone and give pretty good experience. Just watch out for
the occasional evil eye and also the cyclops (Broon, Choon, Froon - don't
remember which one is the starter spawn there)...

I've also hunted with groups in South Karana - Either at Aviak City or just
randomly around the zone hunting Centaurs. If you hunt Centaurs be careful!!
They are very social creatures - and there are some that will kill you VERY
quickly - so have your drum handy in case you need to run (which will
probably happen now and then). At Aviak City - if you are able to get in the
group in the Penthouse, be careful what you play there, as the axis bug will
allow ground level creatures to hit you - just don't play AE songs there.

I've just now started venturing into places like Unrest - but the safest way
to hunt in places like that is to go with someone who knows the zone AND in
a group. Thats my 2c from my exp thus far in the game.
