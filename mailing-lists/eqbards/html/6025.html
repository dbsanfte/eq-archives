Well,

I would say level 22 and 23 were two of the longest levels that I had to
get out of. I was in level 22 for about 2 weeks and the same for 23.
What you need to do is to get into a good group in Unrest that is camping
the barroom or the Hand room. The xp is great and the spawn is pretty
frequent. And you will find that the group will love you to death. The
last few days that I spent in there have enlightened a lot of people to
the value of a bard in a party. Many times I either replaced a departing
warrior or caster and the group has exclaimed after the continuous battles
that this is the best group that they have been in. Hehe it's good to see
people learn the powers of a bard =). So persevere, you'll need some good
groups to get you out of level 22, 23 is bit easier. Getting past 24
seemed easy for me, since a majority of the MOBs in the 2nd floor con even
and below. BTW my bad luck came without prepurchasing a song.
