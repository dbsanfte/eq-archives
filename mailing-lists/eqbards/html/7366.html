>
> 1) You teach the other person, until their skill is maxed (i.e., it

> shows up as excellent)
> 3) The other person now has your skill + 1, so he teaches you (to his
> skill + 1, which is your skill + 2)
> 4) Zone
> 5) Repeat
>
> Eventually you'll max it.

Thanks to all who posted this method. It's a lot faster (and cheaper)
than the method I had imagined (which had to do with starting newbies and
using their skill points, with a large cash infusion). I figure it would
take about 2-4 hours to raise from one point on one person to excellent
on both (depending on zone load time, intelligence and attention level).

I wanted to note that it should, then, be possible to tag-team two (or
more) languages at once, for example:

Person A trains Person B in Language 1 for two skills
Person B trains Person A in Language 2 for two skills (simultaneously)

Then zone and switch languages, repeat, then zone back and switch
languages...

In fact, you should be able to train up to six languages at once (one for
each grouped member), although odd numbers would be hard to keep track of
(since you would need to cycle the languages, rather than just switch
back and forth). Still, it shouldn't take that much longer to do
multiple languages. Anyone on Innoruuk want to try it?
