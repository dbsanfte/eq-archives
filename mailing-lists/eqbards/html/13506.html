Steal Strength can be dispelled to, just use your target debuff song
with yourself targeted. 1 pulse will remove steal strength. It's nice
because you can sing it. This song is also extremely useful vs. druids
or mages. Get rooted or snared, maybe a DoT like creeping crud (little
dog barking icon), or even Tashani or Malise? all can easily be debuffed
off yourself. If you need to get rid of it faster you can pumice
yourself. I use the target debuff because you can use it on both
yourself and a monster, and the other only works on yourself only (and
party) and I believe requires the lute to be equipped to function (it
also strips 2 buffs though).
