PLEASE do not fall into this trap. I personally do not care about so-called
"spoilers". I would not be reading this list if I did not want info about
the game. The game absurdly poorly documented, making things like this list
a god send. Here I can learn things that otherwise would take me pointless
hours of frustration that could be spent actually having fun in the game.

will help all of you have more fun with the game. I strongly encourage
everyone else to do the same. If you don't like "spoilers" (in other words

looking for ore (which is NOT fun to me, I don't know about anyone else).
