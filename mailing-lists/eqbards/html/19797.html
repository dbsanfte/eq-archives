This is a REALLY late response but here goes:

Bards don't get flashing icons and never have (to my knowledge). Our group
members do. For a groupmate of a bard the icon will flash. For the bard
it's on solid. TRUST ME you don't want flashing icons. Icons start to
flash roughly 15 seconds before a spell effect wears off, this means our
icons would ALWAYS flash since our song duration is shorter than the
flash-warning time.

Hope this helps
