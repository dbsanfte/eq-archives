Subject: RE: Why did you start a Bard?


My friend Gryffin gave me the game as a (months off hehe) birthday present. He
and others were in Beta, and he thought I would like the game, and it would be
fun to have someone to talk with at work (we work together).

It was my first on-line game, and really my first serious computer game at all.

I have a real musical bent in RL, but no talent LOL, so a bard appealed to me.
None of the group I knew through my friend was a bard (they had a monk, cleric,
ranger, wizard, and druid) so a bard seemed a good choice.

And then I found out how difficult playing a bard is, and that's really what
cemented it for me haha. Plus all the lovely numbers and formulas and analysis
you could want (I am an engineer in real life *grin*).

Nearly two years later he and I are still playing, and we still talk about EQ
nearly every day!
