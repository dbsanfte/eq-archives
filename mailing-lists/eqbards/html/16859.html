Heh, I'm finding out that a safe spot in Mistmoore is just about as safe as
the "Safe Spot" in Fear ... but my favorite spot (and I'm 21 fighting with 2
other 21s - admittedly pretty twinked) is on the other side of the tower
base at the bottom of the ramp. If you hug that corner, the trains from the
castle to the zone won't get you.

There are two or three spawns right there that can be pulled singly, as well
as any of the lake spawns (if they're not camped). I still have yet to
figure out how to singly pull the little cavern to the graveyard (although
the druid harmony works ok as does my lull).

Also, after being terrified of using AE and aggroing the tower, I finally
tried it and I had no problems. Anyway keep Selo's handy and your little
magic resist song cuz those casters suck.
