This is kind of an interesting thing you brought up, regarding the check
or flag when people are there. The other day I was camping the top of
the temple and Archon with a L30 group (on my L30 character) and we'd
kill the spawns on the steps, mostly in melee, then when our puller
would pull from lower in the temple, we found the lizards would get
stuck on the corner by the stairs down. We found it annoying, but we'd
simply have 3-4 of us go to that corner and kill the lizard then run
back. We did this for a few hours and made great exp.

A guide messaged our group leader and said "thank you for not exploiting
the faulty pathing in this area of the dungeon". It was actually kind of
nice to have it mentioned, since we were a bit irritated at having to
meet our pulls half-way. I know the temple's pathing has been bugged for
ages, I guess I just wasn't aware anyone actually exploited it. Exploits
don't seem like a very fun way to gain exp to me. I'm not sure if
someone petitioned us in the hopes of moving our group from the spot
(seems unlikely, it was quite late and we had no visitors the entire
time) or they have a check, but it very well could be.
