My secret is just this: don't rush. Eventually you get faster and faster. you
just have to make sure you are over the slot before clicking to release.

On the subject of dropping something: I almost lost my tentacle whip by dropping
it. After wandering around for 10 minutes before finding it I learned a trick
for when you drop something. The instinctive urge is to turn around and go look
for it. RESIST THIS URGE. What you want to do is stop and walk backwards looking
at the ground as it passes. This way you cover the exact same ground and are
almost assured of finding what you dropped.
