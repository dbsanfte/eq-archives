At L5, I'd say a bard is about as much use as anyone in said party.
Possibly more useful. I don't think any melee'r is particularly great at

improve everyone's melee. As well, if it's safe enough, you could play
CoD and do a bit of damage that way as well. That said, why bother
grouping at 5? You can still get decent exp from firebeetles I think, or
spiderlings for sure. (freeport/misty thicket area).

Once the bard hit's 6, there's no question they are a great addition to
a group.
