There isn't a bard guild like the Soerbaird (Fennin Ro) on E'ci. There are
several E'ci bards who post a lot on this list. Off the top of my head,
Faras or Aloro are the highest level and they are in their mid-30s. Don't
know if any of the high level bards on E'ci subscribe to this list. If they
do, they don't ever post. Of the regular posters, here is the guild
affiliation of the ones I remember:

Galtin: Mystical Alliance
Laluni: Mystical Alliance
Faras: Crimson Legacy
Kitasi: Armigers of Mirth
Aloro: Flying Brethren

I can't speak for the other guilds, but there are at least four bards in the
Mystical Alliance that are of similar level to you. Send me a private note
if you want more info.

If there are other E'ci bards on this list that I have left out, please send
me your name and guild info as well. I plan on trying to organize guild
events over the Christmas holidays in Mistmoore and Lower Guk to help all
the bards in my guild get the lambent armor components. If you and other
members of your guild would like to help out it would be appreciated. I'll
post more details later on.

Galtin of E'ci
