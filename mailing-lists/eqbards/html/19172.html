Few tips:

1) Do not group with him
2) Charm him and make him attack his pet (he can't cast spells at all, pet
may even kill him before charm wears off)
3) Rinse and repeat

Then for variety, run off with his pet in hot pursuit, chain pet then run
back. Pet will get lost because it's so far away from him. Kill him when
you get back, he can't make a new pet so he's even more helpless than a
wizzie. When he dies pet goes poof.
