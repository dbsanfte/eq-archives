I think that mine is:

/pause 31, /cast <heal>
/pause 2, /stopsong
/pause 61, /cast <clarity>
/pause 2, /stopsong
/em taps his foot to the beat

to allow two pulses of clarity before starting anew. The tapping lets me
know it's time to hit the button again.
