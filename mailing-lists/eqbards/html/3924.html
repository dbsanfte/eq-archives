In decreasing order of usefulness:

Chain (stops fleeing ghouls, drybones, werebats, monks)
Accelerando (ZONE! ZONE NOW! Breaks root too)
invisibility (watch the train go by as your casters med)
Lullaby (when you pull more than you meant to)
Elemental Rhythms (fire and magic resists)

And of course the old standbys, Anthem and Hymn. I've heard
of some people using Binding to lower the AC of mobs so that
lower level people in the party have an easier time hitting
stuff that's red to them. It's area effect though so be
careful with it.

If you have Charm, keep it handy for the occasional kill
stealer. The hand and barkeep tend to attract a lot of
attention if you pull them out into the lawn.
