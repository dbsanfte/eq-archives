I got my Flowing Black Silk Sash last night, and I only had to camp an hour
for it. I got invited into the Frenzie group at about 7pm, we broke the
spawn in the room (3 Bloodthirsty Ghouls). The next spawn was the Frenzied
Ghoul, we killed him (quite easily infact) and the leader looted. Then I
saw the word "Sash".

My heart was pumping so fast as I had the chance of winning this fabled
item. The first person rolled and only got a 21. I then rolled and it came
up 90, the excitement of this was amazing. But, I was still hoping no one
beat my roll. I sat there as people rolled and low numbers were appearing,
33, 12, 39 and 28. I had won, I was now the owner of a loooooverly sash. I
equipped it and immediately noticed the difference during the first fight.
Sash + Celerity makes me attack very fast.

Well, there you have it. The quickest 20k I have ever earned. =)
