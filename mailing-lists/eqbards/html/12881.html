Actually, I love Enchanters. Give me a Cleric and an Enchanter and (for the most part) I really don't
much care how the party gets rounded out. Our crowd control is a poor second to the Enchanter's, and we
have to devote full time to it if it falls on us; I'd much rather have the Enchanter do it. If you're
high enough level to sing Mana Song, and the Enchanter can cast Clarity, you have mana coming out your
ears. Nothing like more than one column of buffs before you start singing anything! Heck, you can even
tank for a bunch of casters if it comes to that (and I've done it a few times). (Of course, their pets
are usually better than I am...)
