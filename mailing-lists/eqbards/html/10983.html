If you are 20 - try Highkeep. Start in the look out room (with a group) then
main room 22 - 24 then warrior room 24-28 then raider room 26 to 32. Go
into the keep and learn your way to the bank in the basement and to the
goblin rooms below the basement. Learn that way well because you will need
to run for the zone in career there. You can bind in the bank (actually any
where in the zone but the bank is convenient.

But spots for 20's also include SK Aviaks, Lake Rathe Aviak Guards

And if you are willing to risk it Unrest and Mistmoore - major trains of
high(er) level mobs.
