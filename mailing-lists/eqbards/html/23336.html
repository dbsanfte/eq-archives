Was camping the moat corners in Karnors this weekend. Had a warrior, a
second bard, wizard, cleric and enchanter in the group. I was weaving the
three chants (with Selos drum) during every fight. Based on the numbers
posted on the www.eqsongs.com website, I was doing around 172pts of damage
per tick (when all 3 stuck). As many people have stated before on this
list, this is a helluva lot more damage than I can do with weapons equipped.
Like Minkin, I was doing so much damage to the mob that it almost always
ignored the warrior and attacked me. Still amazes me though when other
classes ask me why I am not meleeing with weapons. Even though the game has
been out for almost two years, there are still a lot of people who don't
realize what all a bard can do.
