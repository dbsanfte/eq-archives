Not much to add, but here's a few...

Things you can get yourself:
----------------------------
Shoulder: Bloodstained Mantle (ac 6, +3 str, +10 vs magic) [approx 50 p]
drops from the undead knight in unrest, Crested Spaulders (ac 11, +5 sta)
[approx 150 p] drops from the avenging caitiff in MM
Earings: Opalline Earing (ac 2, +5 cha) [approx 15 p] drops from the undead
bartender in unrest
Waist: Troll Hide Belt (ac 5, +5 str, +5 sta) [got mine for 200 p, only one
I've seen for sale] drops from the froglok warden in upper guk
Ranged: Runed Bone Fork (dam 5, delay 29, +10 vs fire, +10 vs cold, +10 vs
magic) [1000 p on Tribunal] drops from the froglok scryer in upper guk

Things you will probably need to buy:
-------------------------------------
BP: Charred (ac 16, +3 str, +5 sta; renders as black plate) [approx 500 p]
Bracer: Silver Plated (ac 7, +5 vs poison, +5 vs disease; renders as silver
plate) [approx 150 p], Symbol of Loyalty to Vox (ac 7, +7 vs cold; renders
as silver plate) [approx 100 p]
Helm: Skull Shaped Barbute (ac 13, +35 hp, +10 vs magic; renders as silver
plate) [approx 350 p]
Back: Cloak of the Ice Bear (ac 8, +15 hp, +20 vs cold) [approx 125 p],
Lizardskin Cloak (ac 8, +5 dex) [approx 200 p?]
Arms: Azure Sleeves (ac 12; renders as no armor) [approx 450 p]
Rings: Jagged Band (ac 5, +3 str) [approx 50-75 p]
Feet: Charred Boots (ac 6, +3 agi, +5 vs fire, +5 vs poison) [approx 250-300
p]
