Favorite emotes? Since Minda is mischievious in nature, and more than a
little roguish, I will use these during downtime:

/e sidles up to <name> and slyly relieves him of his wallet.

/e hands <name> back his empty wallet saying, "I found this, is it yours?"

/e looks innocent.

I also lift boots, shields, armor, weapons...all the while protesting
ignorance of how such items came to be in my possession.

It can take anyone who has not grouped with me before by surprise
sometimes.. and those that know me grin and chuckle while Minda "rolls" the
newcomer for his "cash".

I reserve these emotes for necros and male dwarves, respectively:

For necros: /e paints yellow happy faces all over <name's> robe.

For male dwarves: /e ties pink bows in <name's> beard.

I almost always get roleplaying responses in return, and its highly
entertaining to see reactions ;-)
