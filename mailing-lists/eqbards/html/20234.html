I use AE songs in dungeons all the time. I am always told not to, I
ignore them. Sometimes you'll find out that the layout of the spot
you're in isn't appropriate for an AE song (i.e., you'll get trained), so
you add that to your list of places not to sing. Blame it on some weapon
proc. hehe. Largo's I've rarely had trouble with. Using the absolute
rule "no AE in dungeons" doesn't maximize your usefulness; you need to
balance risk and reward, which you can only do by trying it out.
