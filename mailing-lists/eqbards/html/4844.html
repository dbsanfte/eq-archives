WARNING!

In lesser fay there is an orc camp that has some tasty orcs. But it also
has the orc legionere from Innoruuk (or what ever is the equivilent of
Hell). I happened by there and healed up a dwarf who was in his 20's - 25
or 27. He says he just solo the orc leg and got xp. Then he whacked him
again. Turns out he was a cleric and he says I can kill this orc. You gotta
be kidding I say - I was like 18. He says he'll fetch him out and heal me.
Wow that was fun! (Just got under the xp cap for high level mobs I guess -
cuz I got xp we were not grouped and he healed me while I melee'd.) So if
you hunt that camp - it was like mid zone - have a good strong party ready
for the lege.

The good bandit camp in lesser fay is the Nybright Sisters. They drop
precious gems pretty often and also supply necklaces for a kelethin quest -
talk to the drunk - whoever he is. But you will prolly need help and it can
be a busy campsite. They were easy kills when I was 18 and I got xp from a
couple of them. I also had to endure whines from other campers - who did
not want to share with me. Any how the camp is by the zone wall way down
near the Mist Moore tunnel.

But the more fun thing to get in lesser fay is Crookstinger! He is a named
giant wasp and if you can find him he drops a nice magic dagger - with a
poison effect. Well, its nice for his level - which I'd guess around 10 to
12. I was just wandering down a path and see my text scrolling
"Crookstinger stings you for dmg 8" Wtf?? Crookstinger? I get ready to run
then con him green. Ohh, theeee Crookstinger! Now I remember Nummy!

Sylly.
