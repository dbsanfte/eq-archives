Subject: Re: How in the world do you stack? I don't think stacking works at all . . .


Its really very simple to keep 2 songs stacked, and not too hard to
get 3 going. Just watch for the line in your text box that tells you
the song effect just happened to scroll by. In full screen mode that
line of text is in blue, and fairly easy to spot amidts all the other
clutter scrolling by. Don't try and time it using the icons up in
the top right of the screen. Start with just juggling two songs to
get used to it, then try for 3 if you want.
