Mahlin's Mystical Bongo's (Unknown where this is found)
Percussion instrument - gives no statistic enhancements, but does appear to
give an 8% improvement for drummed songs. This is part of the Bard Epic
Quest, resulting in the Singing Short Sword.



Mahlin's Mystical Bongo's are rewarded by Baenar Swiftsong (South Karana
near the well) after you give him the head from Maligar's Enraged
Doppleganger (West Karana). You use the Bongo's to get the bottom half of
page 24. You could use the bongo's as an interim reward, but I would think
most bards will have better percussion instruments (Tambourine or MM drums
at least) by the time they can kill Maligar.
