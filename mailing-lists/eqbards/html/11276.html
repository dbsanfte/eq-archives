Greets!

1. Mistmoore - no clue. Mid 30's place afik.

2. Choon & Froon (gay clops?) live in West Karana on top of a hill with a
really nice view of the Oger Camp (don't get near even though they con
amiable - the Chief don't and he will harm touch for 400 and agro the rest
onto you) and the strolling Hill Giants. If you enter West K from the NK
near the river past the Stone Bridge, go about 1/3 of the way across the
zone (hmm, kinda tough to judge if you are new to the zone) then go due
North. Alternatively, you could follow the road thru north karana and enter
west k on the road. Keep following it and stay out of the hills. Skirt the
bottom of the hills (snarf the ghoul messenger boy, werewolfie, and any
meandering HG's if you want) and look for a shop where Brenzl McManus sells
his fletching goods (track Brenzl if you can). Right behind his shop - up
on the hill Choon and Froon. Yes, if you work argo radius, you can pull em
singles, I found that out when I tried to Lament and pulled a single.
Fear-Chain-DoT-DD, rinse, repeat. I prefer to have a helper and SoW. I
tried to charm one time and was resisted - but that was a couple levels back
and maybe did not wear all my cha stuff. They are prolly low 30's - high
20's?

3. Freeport bards guild - the guy upstairs can train you in the basics
of ID.
