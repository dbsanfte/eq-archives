Have you ever tried hand to hand combat? Go into a newbie zone and pick a
fight with an orc pawn or a gnoll pup. They cant hurt you because of your
ac and your hp rebuilds faster than they can damage you. But you will be
punching them for 1 point of damage. Now you should be getting into groups
where they are attacking monsters that are so much higher than you that your
normal weps are having trouble damaging them. Your role in that case will
be equiping an instrument and playing songs. So magic gloves and hand to
hand combat with monsters that you can barley hit with a Dirk or Combine are
not gonna do any good. Even lower level monsters that your regular weps can
hit will be hitting you and your party pretty hard. If you are in the
melee, you will be at risk of getting stunned and not having your songs be
used all of the time. And you will get beat up for no gain. My 2cp <g>

You would be better off standing off outside of melee and supporting your
party 100% of the time (no stuns to interupt you).

Sylly.

p.s. I could be wrong - I have only had 2 or 3 partys and monster combos
where I was able to do this stand off support role. but I have done the h2h
combat thing and it was pitiful!
