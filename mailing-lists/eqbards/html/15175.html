Ok here's what I've seen of this song so far...

1) Gives 10 STR
2) Gives 8 HP Regen per tick
3) Gives small rune against casters (haven't determined exact value yet,
must do more testing)

Equipping lute doubles the strength and hit point regen effects (have not
tested rune capability with the lute yet).

It does not stack with any of our damage shield songs, but it does stack
with VoV and CoB so now you can give 55 STR 30 AGI 15 DEX 8 HP Regen Attack
Speedup and a small rune when twisting during battle at level 50, not bad at
all!

Better still, between battles if you really must be the healer at that
level, it stacks with Hymn of Restoration as well.

Once I determine this rune thing I'll pass it along. Oh yeah, you can find
the song in Qeynos (where I bought it) and Kellethin (I heard it was here,
haven't looked for it there though)
