Hmm, the exe room, I need to spend more time in Guk (not) so my directions
are kinda rough. From the bedroom, head for the tower (left tunnel to
vampire bat room, left tunnel again but *hug* the right wall to avoid agro
the Greater Ice Bone Skels - or kill them - they see thru invis to undead.)
As you cross the bridge to the tower, you can jump off on the left side. Now
one way is to the lower tower rooms and the other way is to the jail and the
lord room further in. Head for the tower lower rooms. Off one of the rooms
is a small room with some cells (4) and troll prisoners (iirc). In the
center is the chopping block and iirc that is where the exe spawns.
