Subject: Easy Money with Bard Quest


I am pleased to provide a little detail about the bard quest in Kelethin and
how to make a little cash. Now there is no reason to be totally destiture as
long as you can get to Kelethin.

Last night I bought-out all the spiderling-silk in Freeport and it's
environs. It cost me a little over 2 silver pieces each, so the expense is
not too demanding. I filled eight backpacks and placed tham in the bank (64
silks). I then filled-up six more that I carried on me. This left two for my
own personal belongings.

The trip to Kelethin was uneventful. By playing my drum, the trip went
rather quickly.

Upon meeting with my "foreign" elf guildmaster, I exchanged the silks for
gold and drums. Each set of four silks (cost about 1gp) nets 1gp in reward
and a drum worth about 2.6gp to me. A total of 28 transactions were
completed. The net cash to me was about 72gp. Not bad for a little
scavenging. The other "incidental" benefits are the increased faction
standing with the bards group and the 1/2 bubble of experience I gained.
