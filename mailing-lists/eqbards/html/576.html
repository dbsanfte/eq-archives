This depends very much on the group, what they are hunting, and where and
how they do it. I've been in some very good large groups, including one
last night with 2 dwarf clerics, a gnome warrior, a wild-elf druid, and an
elf caster. We hunted orcs in the edge of the Oasis of Marr, a very
dangerous place. We were quite successful in terms of both experience and
loot, while ridding the world of some of the endless supply of orcs. Being
human I was the tallest of the group and neither the least nor the most deadly.

Good groups are usually happy to have a capable bard along. Group buffs,
fast running, and quicker group healing are much appreciated.
