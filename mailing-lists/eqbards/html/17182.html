I wouldn't bother with the Staff until it proc's. This happens at level 28,
only then will the Staff of Writhing be better that the Claw:

Using the Formula someone posted we can see that the claw is better (only
slightly):

Claw - 5 x 2 +1 = 11 / 19 = 0.578
Staff - 9 x 2 +1 = 19 / 33 = 0.575

The claw is only slightly better but considering it is lighter, and does
not have -9 charisma then it is by far the better item. I also prefer the
claws look over "The Ugly Stick".

Like I said though, once you get to level 28 (might be 27, I forgot), the
Staff of Writhing will be a nice weapon to wield as the proc can change the
way a fight is going.

Hope this helps.
