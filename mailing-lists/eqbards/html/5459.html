Subject: RE: Everquest account security


It is possible that someone hacked into your account. There are password
cracking programs out there. Not sure about EQ, but they are around for other
things. You might want to make sure you don't have a crackable password.
Where I work, we had a hacker problem and we were told to use a combination of
letters and numbers, and not to use a common name, or any variation of our
name or userid's in the password. Think of things that might easily be
guessed and DON'T use that.
