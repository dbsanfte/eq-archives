When I got DDD, I ran to shady and maxxed my brass. Then I tried using it
some (DDD) and like all 'immature' songs (ones you just got cuz u just
leveled) it was not the uber song I was expecting. And then I pretty much
went back to my old standards. And in the course of things, my brass skills
- although listed as 'master' in my stats - fell behind my levels. I was
wondering why screetch with horn was so crappy and it dawned on me that like
my 1HS, 1HB & Piercing, I need to max brass every level or so to keep it
maxed. My singing and string and percussion get maxed in my normal
fighting, etc. I prolly have slacked on wind as I don't invis much any more
- at least not like I used to.

So two points: Yes - lower level songs seem to get stronger as you go up -
even to me at 36. And max those skills every level - brass was one I let
slip and that's prolly why some brass stuff seemed so crappy when I tried
it. (A brass song with crappy brass skill obviously gets resisted more that
a string song with good string skill - duh! And the CoD being more 'mature'
than DDD adds to situation.)
