If you get charmed, click on your sit/stand button over and over, as many
times as you can. This will keep you from 'walking into his lair' until the
charm wears off. while you are sitting/standing, you don't move, or attack,
which is good :). However, other beasties can get in free attacks on you
tho... and you can't fight back until the charm wears off :(
