Subject: Melifluous Motion


We finally found a use for the seemingly-useless Melifluous Motion song!
We were prowling around in Solusek's Eye, and one of the party members got
stuck between an urn and the wall, unable to move. We tried levitating him
out; it didn't help. While waiting for a GM to respond to the petition,
and discussing whether we should just group-teleport out and come back,
someone thought of the Melifluous Motion song. The group wizard cast
levitate on me and the stuck player (since this urn was next to a deep pit
full of lava), we ungrouped and I grouped with just the stuck player, and I
sang a verse of the Motion song. Blink, and we're both elsewhere in the
room. Unstuck!

- Windsong Wanderer
