You need to be level 46 or better to enter the alter:planes.

Plane of Fear you can run into through the portal in the spec cave. But
there are usually alot of patrollers at the zone in spot.

Plane of Sky and Plane of Hate require a wizard to gate you in. The reagent
for the spell is like 200pp plus or minus. And there are usually lots of
patrollers near the zone in spot although I have made the run to the 'safe
spot' and camped out (ie not died).

You can gate or be gated out of any of the planes. Although Plane of Sky you
can just jump off the island and land in the water off East Freeport
(sometimes unintentionally). And I hear tell that there is a portal you can
run through to get out of Plane of Hate - but your guild would need to clear
CT's temple first for you to run to it. Have not seen that one first hand -
although we have cleared the temple - just did not bother to go look.

You are non-agro where you enter the Plane of Sky - so that maybe your best
bet. The main problem is you need to be 20 levels higher to zone in. Heh -
go try running into the TV in the PoF cave before you turn 46.
