You come in from the zone. Usually a goblin or three at the
hole in the wall. Just past it at the ledge, there are 2-3
more goblins. Jump down to the door. Open the door, 2 more
goblins. Next door opens to a room with a gate. 2-3 more
goblins. After that it's a bunch of long hallways straight to
the king room and Sol Mage room. There is (AFAIK) one Sol
goblin that wanders these halls, but they're otherwise
clear. You don't have to fight too many to get to where
you're going.
