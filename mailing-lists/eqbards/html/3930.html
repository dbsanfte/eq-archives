When I first got the Ayre, just happened on a group interested in hunting
Aqua Goblins at the bottom of the Oasis lake in Oasis! I stayed around the
goblin tower, and carried a Greater Lightstone. This way folks always knew
where to swim to hear the song. We had a few warriors and such, but a
Magician and his pet helped enormously is chasing those pesky fleeing
goblins.

It was just a riot, watching these enormous trolls gently swimming over my
head, and we all had a great time. Remember several of them saying they
never had so much fun in Everquest.

Bad thing, those aqua goblins are right on the edge of giving you any
experience. And it's kind of sad that this may be the only situation where
one can use the song in a group. Travelling with the Aquatic Ayre would be
plain suicidal (I've tried, and heard the notes of panic in friend's
voices). The radius of it seems so narrow, folks will drown if they depend
on the bard to give them water breathing in most situations.
