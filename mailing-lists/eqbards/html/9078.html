Hmmm from my own personal experiences, I think you may be onto something.
Until recently I was the subject of many "bard hate" episodes where monsters
would go out of their way to run over and smack on me, despite being in
conflict with someone else not in my group... often despite my not playing a
song (except for Selo's Accelerando - is THAT a taunt too???) or taking any
other action that I would expect to anger said monsters.

Recently, however, I picked up some classic rock and folk to mix in with my
primarily r&b and jazz sets, and I find that it seems to please many
monsters. Griffawns become very laid-back if you play "Fly Like an Eagle"
by Steve Miller, and I was amazed when almost half the gnolls of BlackBurrow
joined in on the chorus to the Beatles' "Help!" Oddly, the Crushbone Orcs,
who once attacked me on sight, now ignore me peacefully if I play "Dueling
Banjos" from "Deliverance" - must be that line about squealing like a pig...
