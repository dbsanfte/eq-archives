Okay

I did some testing tonight with the Libretto.

1. You must be grouped.
2. You must target the player wishing for something identified.
3. They MUST be holding the object (i.e. on their cursor)
4. You will get a message: You must select a target for this song.
5. They will get a message identical to the one you would have received if
you had been targeted and holding the item yourself.
6. You MUST target yourself to get an identify on your own objects.

There are probably some other anomalies that I couldn't figure out.
