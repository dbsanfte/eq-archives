Actually Pslam of Warmth is NOT a stinker, it is a great song. An
undocumented feature of the song is that it provides a group damage shield
(starts at 5 damage). The damage shield stacks with all other shields
(except cold based ones I have been told). If you think a 5 point damage
shield is not significant in your next group battle count the number of
times the mobs hit anyone in your party and multiply by 5 :) Also see
invisibility is great for spotting cloaked kill stealers, and it give
ultravision for the blind humans in the group.
