Lady Vox was slain in her lair by The Pantheon on the Innoruuk server.

We got 2 Mc Vaxius horns (me and Kaldivar), a Staff of Forbidden Rights, and
a Warhammer of Divine Grace. She also dropped a Book used in the Fiery
Avenger quest which we gave to Avarus of the Legions of Darkness (a rival
quild). He should be the first on our server to complete the quest and
obtain the coveted weapon.

The fight seemed much longer than our battle with Nagafen but I have not
checked my log yet to see the actual time. I was again in the 2nd wave.
This time there were *alot* of casualties and until the very end I was
uncertain of the outcome. She has that ridiculous complete heal but I did
not see her use it - maybe she did early on and went OOM. I managed to live
through the battle - perhaps because I did not melee until the very end.
Elemental & Guardian with MM Batle drum. No one in our party ran from the
fear and we were directly underneath her. And, oh yeah, there are a couple
surprizes waiting for you. Far be it from me to spoil your fun but when you
get there I think you will notice a difference.

Logging in was almost harder than killing the dragon. For some reason, the
patch server would error out downloading the updates over my ISDN
connection. In despairation, I connected my 2nd PC with a crap 28.8 analog
to my backup ISP and was able to get the updates. <sigh>
