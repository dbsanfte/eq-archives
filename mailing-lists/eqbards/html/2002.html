The dirk is quite possibly the best bard weapon available in
the high teens - lower twenties range, maybe even up into the
thirties. There are slower piercing weapons with better
damage for rogues, spellcasters don't really need melee

dual wield the heavier weapons favored by warriors and
rangers. If there's any weapon that has BARD written all over
it, the dirk is it.
