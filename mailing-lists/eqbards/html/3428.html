Well, here is something you could do for a real adventure and it would kill
two birds with one stone:

1. Travel to Everfrost and find Karg the Icebear. Give him 5pp and
polar bear skin (kill one or buy one from a vendor in Halas tunnel) and he
will make you a Polar Bear Cloak.
2. Go to Crushbone and trade the PBC for the Dragoon Dirk. A PBC
should be pretty rare item for this part of the world.

That would give you great travel experience and get you a dirk for a good
price. You might have to season the trade with like 10pp but in Crush you
should be able to get a Dirk for 20pp maybe 15pp if you get lucky.

In the meantime - I'd say use a giant snake fang for your wep.
