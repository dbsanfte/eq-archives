Subject: Speaking of outrunning Selo's


A word of warning for any bards looking to play in the Oasis of Marr area,
Lockjaw the crocodile is the only creature I have seen in EQ thus far that can
outrun a healthy bard with Selo's going. I'm sure there are others, but this
was the only one I have seen.

To quantify a little:

- I have tangled with Lockjaw twice (died once, zoned the other time only
because I was very close)
- I was healthy both times (no slow down because of low health)
- I did not have a drum in my hand in either case

Perhaps with a drum in hand you could outrun this nasty lizard, but I doubt it.
If anyone wants to go try running from him with and without a drum, be my guest.
