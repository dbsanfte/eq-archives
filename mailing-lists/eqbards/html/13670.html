DW Gobs - are hard to pull singles - unless you are level 50. So get some
help or maybe try this idea that was posted a while back (by Harmonic):

Go to Lake Rathe (why I cant say except maybe roaming singles can be found
there?) Get levitate and SoW. Track (ok have ranger friend track) DW
gobbies. Get right on top of them and agro them with Chords or other AE
song. Run for the zone. Have a ranger cast snare on one and zone out then
right back in. Find the snared one and kill him. Pray that he has a helm
and that you wont have to repeat this 20 times.

When I got mine, I was mid 30's and with a 40+ Enchanter, Cleric & SK. They
were pulling from the underwater tower in Oasis. They had broken the spawn
apart by the time I got there so I dont know what exactly they did. We had
to kill more than a dozen before we got an opalline helm.
