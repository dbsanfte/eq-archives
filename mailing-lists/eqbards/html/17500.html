Speaking as a fellow bard on Povar, my biggest warning at level 20 is NOT to
venture past the Courtyard (CY). Even there, hug the corner to your
immediate left as you enter. Your group will need an expert puller to pull
from the maze or the hallways. Don't use any AE songs....and if someone
shouts TRAIN, RUN, do not WALK to the zone and fling yourself through it. At
levels 23-25 I had very high death rates in Cazic, attributable to out of
control trains that spilled back into the courtyard or to really
disorganized groups. If it looks like you're about to be trapped, get out
that horn or flute and invis yourself as fast as possible. Feel free to look
me up in game if you wish.
