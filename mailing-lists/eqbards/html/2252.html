I did not seem to have the big problem with money that you seem to be having..., I bought it piece by piece from levels 9-11, had a full set since 12, I have no money making skills, EXCEPT, many, many friends made from helping them along the way. People payed me up to 3p per head (average 1p per head) for the speedy safe trip. Sounds silly but It worked. And with /follow you can do it easier than I did. This took away some levelling time, but it allowed me to finance the equipment to level much easier and more efficiently.
