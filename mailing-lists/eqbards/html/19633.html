By and large, most people are afraid to go to kunark due to binding and
traveling issues.

Experience in kunark dungeons is nothing short of amazing. We went to
Kaesora the last 3 nights. In 2 hours camping the Church area, I made 1.5
bubbles in level 44. We got a total 5 ransacker packs, and Xalgoz himself
spawned once, dropping the fang. The library area is awesome for levels
35-40 as everything is still blue. The drops are less frequent there but exp
is still great.

This is also a great dungeon for crawling. You can get most mobs as single
or double pulls. There are many wanderers, but they are easy to isolate and
pick off. There are well spaced areas where you can pause to clear the next
area. And the spawn clock is enough so that you can clear an area and the
next area before respawn.

I would recommend Kaesora for parties levels 35-45. In the time I've spent
there, I've only once, seen someone not in my group in the dungeon.
