The reckless efreeti spawns near the spear spike trap right outside
the gnome area. Facing the spear trap, turn left go into room with
small set of stairs. There should be a door here, lots of goblins
inside. At this point to the right of the door or thereabouts is
a secret wall, which leads down to the Efreeti. He is guarded by
two lava elementals. They have dmg shield that really hurt.

Alternately, you can walk past the spear spike trap, and hug left
wall to fall through a false floor which drops you right on his
head.

Harmonic.
