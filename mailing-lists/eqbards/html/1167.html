The best cash I have found is from killing Dervish Cutthroats. There
are camps in NRO that are usually camped out. There is one rather large
camp that is in West Commons that is not always camped out because only
good groups or exceedingly high levels can handle it, but as soon as you
get a group organized to crack it open, the vultures appear and expect
you to share it. There are a couple more derv camps in SRO that I have
found to be much less busy, probably because a lot of people don't like
to travel through Oasis.

The good loot is in the form of bronze weapons and cutthroat rings.
