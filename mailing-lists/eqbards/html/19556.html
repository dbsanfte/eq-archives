I think you were unwittingly a victim of the charm bug that I reported a
couple of weeks ago. I fought the giants last night and their agro radius
was unchanged. However, there is a known bug with charm that causes you to
get on the hate list of every giant in chase range. I have yet to encounter
the bug with my charm, but every time our enchanter charms one the bug
occurs. It basically turns the agro radius of all those giants into their
chase range and you get faction hits for every giant anyone kills in the
area. bleh! We had a GM take copious notes and report it. As soon as my
song pulsed on the affected enchanter I was added to all those hate lists as
well. My charm worked normally but maybe if their had been another bard it
wouldn't have. I could charm one gate spawn giant and he would come right
over to me. If I sent him against the other gate spawn giant I did not get
added to any other giants hate lists like the enchanter did.
