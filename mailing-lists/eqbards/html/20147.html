To get to the room where the asassain spawns in lower guk, you traverse
upper guk (all live) then enter the "dead side" of lower guk. But near the
zone to the dead side are some live frogs too. When the server is busy,
they are usually killed by folks waiting for groups. People usually get
invis-to-undead here. Then there are undead frogs between the zone and the
bedroom. You go past the bedroom and past some live vampire bats (hope that
they are camped) and undead frogs. Then past the greater ice bone skellies
- who will agro if you get near them regardless of your invis or
invis-to-undead. From there - go across the bridge to the tower and in and
to the left to the 2nd ladder you see and up the ladder to the
supplier/assasain room.

Yes, its *possible* but most folks who do it have run the route before in
their other characters.
