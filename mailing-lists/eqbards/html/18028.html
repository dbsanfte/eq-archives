Normally I use 1st person. During fights I switch to overhead. I only use
the butt shot view for swimming (try it sometime if your like me and have
trouble climbing back up on shore). The other two views look behind me to
the right and left. I use these when running to see if I'm being chased.

I prefer to target with the keyboard, but that doesn't always work so I am
forced to use the mouse a lot. Otherwise I only use the mouse for moving
items. Songs, my movement, opening doors, etc is all keyboard.
