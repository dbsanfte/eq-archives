OK speaking from experience here. Our underwater breathing (UB) song does
not override Enduring Breath (EB). If EB is up and you start singing UB, EB
will still be up when you stop singing. However, if you are singing UB and
you don't have EB, EB will not stick if someone tries to cast it on you.
You have to stop UB first in order to get EB to stick. While in Kedge, I
always have UB memmed. Whenever someone announces EB is dropping, I get UB
ready in case they start drowning. Saved a 50 paladin the other night
because of it. To say the least he was glad I had it ready.
