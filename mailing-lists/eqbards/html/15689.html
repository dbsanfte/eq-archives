Greetings Skarsol and Welcome!!!

Sounds like you have enjoyed a great spot for hunting. Might be time for
you to begin traveling to Antonica. There is a big world out there and you
should see it. When I was 15 thru 20, I did a lot of hunting in East Karana
and some in Highhold Pass and The Gorge of King Xorbb (Beholder Maze.) I
like to bind in Rivervale but I am wierd and actually like that Happy Music!
Otherwise Highhold or Highkeep are good spots. Highhold is good to learn
but kinda risky depending on how heavily camped it is. Highold from 16 to
18/19 is good in groups. Down in East Karana and over in North Karana,
there are bugs and stuff for 15 thru 18 or so - that you can probably still
solo. From 18 thru 20, gnoll reavers in East karana with a group or a good
partner can earn you your surname. It did for me. Also Lake Rathe Aviak
Guards are good for high teens and even low 20's. Then from 20 and up, you
can alternate between Highkeep pickclaw goblins and South Karana aviaks.
But by then you will be needing a group for the majority of your hunting.

Enjoy and keep us posted!
