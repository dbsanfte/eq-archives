UNREST TIP:
You jumped the railing coming out of the side door of Unrest, didn't you?
There is a not-very-well-known pathing issue here. If you go in through the
side door and pull something, do not jump the railing when you come out to
your group. If you do so, the mobs get confused and then go back in through
the round room and out through the back door. This has the effect of
pulling everthing from the rear of the house. Instead, when you come out
the door, take the time to turn to the right or the left and go down the two
or three steps to the yard. If you do that, the mobs will follow you. Some
Dark Elf told me that one time and I was skeptical but tried it out. IT
DEFINITELY WORKS.
