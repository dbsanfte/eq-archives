The "crack" series of songs (Cassindra's Chorus of Clarity and Cassindra's
Chant of Clarity) work differently from most songs - when you sing them,
the instant they pulse, they shoot that extra mana to everyone
listening. There is no continuing pulse afterwards. To get a pulse every
tick, you would have to keep singing the song constantly, but since this
isn't as efficient, most bards weave other songs into the mix.
