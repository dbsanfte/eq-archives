Welcome!

I'd say base out of Freeport and work East Commons, West Commons, for lions
(maybe green to you?) Y-kodis & Kodis, wisps for pp, and Snakes - asp's &
dark woodies - get a pair fo Chitan Shields if you got the plats - Snakeskin
tunic and Snakfang necklaces (from menkes tolbite in s qeynos - also stock
up on Chanda Miller boots and capes and sell them up in N. Ro and Oasis for
lots of pp - 10pp - sometimes 5 sometimes 15 - see what the traffic will
bear. Good ac and casters can wear em. Then hunt N.Ro and south thru
Oasis. Those zones should be good for you til high teens then off to
Highhold pass for some insane gnoll and Orc killing - spending some time in
East K killing bugs, lion mat's and pat's and more gnoll-reavers, etc. By
the time you hit your 20th level - the Aviak threat in South Karana and Lake
Rathe should still be in full swing!
