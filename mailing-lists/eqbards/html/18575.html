Group? Bah! Get that 2 spawn back in the cove in Lake Rathe and solo
the bejeezus outta it. Once it stops giving good exp (around 22 or 23.... I
*think*), head out the the Hermit's (or "Hermie", as I like to call him) Hut
in South Karana and do the same thing. After that? Easy. Head to the
gobbie mines in Ill Omen on Kunrak until yer about 30 (ye'll be soloing them
well before that). At 30, go visit the Sarnak fortress. There is a
reeeeeally nice spot just beyond the end of the path (after it goes past the
tower, and into the CY area) to the far right. It's a flat area near the
2nd level of the fortress, and *perfect* to pull to.
There is a lil' trick you can do to fight multiple spawns there, but as
it's prolly considered an "exploit". If ye get on the flat area next to the
fortress, go straight until you hit the zone wall, then move up the steep
part (to the right) until you stop. If you pull to there, then Screech, the
creatures will run through you to that lil invisble corner and run in place.
As I said, this is prolly considered an exploit, so use it at yer own risk.
Other than that, the recruits, legionairres and dragoons (only faced one
dragoon thus far, so they could be tougher) are quite soloable (low blues).
There are aome casters there also, but it seems PS is a bit slower than
their casting time, so I jusst avoid them :oP
