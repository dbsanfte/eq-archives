Regarding invis to undead.

I seriously doubt GM's or Verant staff told you that clouding does not
effect undead. We on this list have battled over it for a long time but the
facts support that clouding is a level based song in regards to the undead.
As the bard grows in level the higher level undead they can cloud. However
it seems the undead in Guk fall outside our level range for invis. However
by the time you reach 27 your invis to about every undead accept Kith and
Guk. I've seen level 40 bards walk unmolested through undead Kith with
invis. In other words for the 1000th time. Clouding IS invis to undead..
just not high level undead
