Look over the guides at EQDiva.com. It's a very good site. I believe that it has guides discussing
soloing. The biggest thing is that it is very slow. Here's my off the cuff thoughts.

Chant Kiting is draining because you're generally twisting while running around in a circle. SOW
potions or buffs speed it up if you can sing 3 DOT songs.

Fear Kiting is harder because you have to stay on the mob, get few resists and stay away from
wanderers. I found it comes down to location (where the mob doesn't run far and roames don't crash
the party). That and being able to twist in regen after you get resisted and beaten up.

Charm Kiting is very very fun. Again, location is important. I only do it outside and BW was a
favorite of mine. However, I was mightily impressed by a Bard ~52nd when I was in my 40's that
solo'd the Bar in SkyShrine. He snared two, ran to the zone-out, charmed one until the other was
dead and then zoned out. Too risky for me, but he did quite well in the train-free time I watched.
I stuck with the raptors outside :-).
