I've got a str of 86. I'm wearing 2 brass plate items, some banded pieces,
a leather apron, lockjaw's vest, I wield a Dirk and a combine LS most of
the time, I have 3 backpacks filled with assorted random stuff, carry 20
food and 20 water, instruments, I seldom go near cities any more so I'm
usually packing 50 pp, 100 gp, 200 sp (I give the copper to low-level
critters too, as soemone else mentioned)... I have some str buffing items
(so I think my normal Str is 91 or so), and with all that I'm fine. I can
get as much loot as anyone else and I won't get encumbered for ages.

If I started again I'd probably start with 90 in Strength, as far as what
to put in your other skills... go for them all ;) Spread 'em out nicely and
you'll be fine. It really comes down to how you're going to play your PC
and since that varies from time to time I recommend just making sure you're
not too lacking in anything.
