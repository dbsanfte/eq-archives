If you can get to the bedroom then you're halfway there. Get invised vs
undead and from the bedroom take the left tunnel out, hug the left wall if
bats are there and squeek by their agro radius. Follow the tunnel a few
paces until you see the chamber with the Greater Ice Bone Skeletons (GIBS)
in it. If you're on a crowded server they may be all pulled. If they are
there, know that they do see through invis vs undead. Hug the right wall
this time and you will just squeek past their agro radius. Follow the
tunnel a bit more and you'll see four or five steps leading up to some stone
walkways. There's usually two frogs stationed here. Up the steps, straight
along into some buildings where you'll shortly be forced to take a left.
Turn left 90 degrees and about 50 feet in front of you you'll see a ladder.
Climb that ladder (it's pretty short) and you'll be in the "tower" where the
Assassin spawns - it's a fairly small room with barrels in it. There's
either three or four spawn spots in this little room, and you get some
wizzies and stuff spawning. This room is not that hard for a group in the
low to mid thirties to hold if they aren't stupid. Once you get a grasp on
the room you can pull stuff from nearby up into this room for experience.
And as always in Lower Guk - no runners. Chain 'em.
