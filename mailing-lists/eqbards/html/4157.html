I have to disagree with this because of a very strong point that can be
brought up. Some people enjoy using EQ's /ooc and /shout commands for their
constant chit-chat and arguing. They may find it fun and entertaining, but
the rest of the people on the server or in the zone couldn't care less and
would really prefer it if they'd take it to IRC. (There's enough stuff to
keep track of in that little message window without trying to filter through
all the chatter) And before you say that we could simply turn off shouts or
ooc's - I'd argue that you shouldn't be forced to do that just to enjoy the
game. There are some VERY good uses and quite a few times that the shout's
are used properly (ie: "/shout Mino Hero at cave entrance!") that make the
tools valuable. I'd also prefer to not have an ignore list a mile long.. I
do like interacting with people and helping them as much as possible. If
they were put on my ignore list a month ago and I go to help them and can't
see anything they're saying I'm going to be slightly confused. :)
