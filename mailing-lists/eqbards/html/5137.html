I think everyone gets bored with the mindless combat. I feel forced to do
it for xp. I need the xp to level up so I can stay close enough to my
fiends to adventure with them. Three weeks ago - I was doing 1 level every
couple days. For past 2 weeks - I have barely made 3 bubs. For one week
not even a bub.

But the big reason to level up is to go to new places. You need to go to
new places to be afraid and see the new monsters. Even so, I wonder what
happens when you have seen everything?

And finally, I think the big fun in the game is the other people. Yes,
there are lots of dweebs. But I talk to everyone and try to do things to
make it fun. If you want more role playing - you have to play the role. I
meet a lot of players and I axe them if they rp and many will say they want
to but don't know how. I must admit - I don't really know how - this is my
first game like this.

I have a char. I run around and adventure. I try to help new ppl. I die
in bad places and have to find someone to rescue me. (Big Thanks to Kalvidar
and Plorare - both played key roles in helping get my bod out of somewhere
in Runnyeye it had no place being :-) For me, it's the interaction with
people not the AI that make this game fun.

Yes, there are problems. Let's wave the magic wand and "fix" all of the
problems. Even so -- you have to have other people to make it fun. It
used to be hard to get 6 friends to come over and sit around the table for a
night of gaming (or so I am told since I don't have that experience.) The
technology makes it easier to "get everyone together" but you still have to
find some fiends who wanna do the same stuff you do - then you go do it.
Whether it's camping, questing, powergamer xp, exploring, or whatever. But
one has to make the effort to make what's fun happen.
