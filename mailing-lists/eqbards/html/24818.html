I think Vox is harder than a lot of people give credit to. On Xegony; when
I'm in a place like Sebilis - I hear people "dissing" (on /ooc or /shout)
Vox raids where it often takes three or more chances to wipe her out. The
people doing the dissing are often members of larger power guilds - who
spent a lot of time killing Vox at a time in the past and now the guilds are
all too-high (even with second chars to do it)

More "Mature" players often respond in kind pointing out how many wipeout
said power-guild (or their predecessors - or the ones that member belonged
to at the time) had when they first went after Vox.

Vox/Nag is usually the first Raid experience for a lot of people; so you
have inexperienced people. A great many raids are pickup raids; so you'll
have people not used to fighting with each other fighting together. Or
newer raids made up of newer players getting to the 46-52 range for the
first time; and inexperienced raid leaders.

Even on a "twink"; an experienced raid leader will probably want to fight
something else; He'll want his 53+ friends to help; and there are places
with more loot etc.

And for us bards doing it for scales - higher chars are very useful not just
for buffing; but I've seen a Vox raid get wiped out (back in my 46-52 days)
when Ice Giants spawned while Vox was engaged. If a group or two of 53+ had
been in reserve to handle that - it wouldn't have been an issue.
