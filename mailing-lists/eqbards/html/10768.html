That does not sound like the same one I use. I would say stay to the far
right side of Upper Guk. Go into the water like you are going to the heart
spider rooms. Past them, thru the hot tub room, thru the room with the
square columns. Keep holding to the right hand side - down the halls. Cross
the bridge with the spiders and don't fall in - real pain finding the ladder
to get out. Thru the slime hall and soon there will be a hall to the right
and a torch pot and the zone to the dead side of lower. If there are a
bunch of high levels sitting around healing, you know a train just came
thru. When they go back in, you will prolly find some ded froggies with
greaves and gaunts. If some one shouts train, get moving to the zone.

Sylly
