Paw dungeon in south karana -- rarely has more that 10 people in it (on The
Rathe at least) and has decent loot. It's actually good (solo or grouped) from
level 6 to 18 or so. The down side is that it is a long way from anything. You
can sell at the gypsies in north karana, but the nearest bank is in qeynos or
highkeep.
