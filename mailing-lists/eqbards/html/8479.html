Greetings and Welcome!

The Mistmoore battle drum is a special instrument. It will make your Selos
Accellerando go a lot faster! 15% to 20% ? Noticably faster.

It come from a pretty high level monster in Mistmore - I think (makes
sense?) Anyhow, since there are not a lot of high level bards and since it's
pretty useless to other classes, these drums are either hoarded for those
who might some day do a bard or given as gifts - handed down from bard to
bard even. I got mine at level 30 from Kalvidar who was given it (got it
given to?) by Lyly - a high level cleric who looted it. (He had a spare so
he could give it away.)

The merch birdie in SK "Krak" hates me or hate half-elfs or something. I
tried all the tricks, charisma song, stand behind and sneak, all of the and
he still wont sell to me - but he does not have anything special for bards -
not for any one for that matter unless its some loot sold to him by players
in SK who wanted cash and dumped a rune or words that were useless to them.

Something you could get would be a mino horn - in fact a mino axe would be
pretty good for you until you can get a combine or dirk. Also a giant snake
fang was my weapon du jour for many levels when I was your age. Let's go to
Beholder (where the minos live) and get you equipped! They are pretty tough
so you need some help or need to buy it from the 15 to 20's who can hunt
them.

I play on Innoruuk so send me a tell when you are in the game and say hi!
Look forward to meeting you someday!
