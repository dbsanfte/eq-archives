Here is my hot key <1> specifically for charming in outdoor zones:

---------- ----------
| selos | | charm |
---------- ----------

---------- ----------
| attack | | who |
---------- ----------

---------- ----------
| sense | | track |
---------- ----------

"attack" is a social macro: /pet attack

"who" is a social macro: /pet guard me /pet who leader /time (yes, 3
things stacked in one. I use guard me to back off a pet who is agro on
party. Yes, /pet backoff would work too but I also use charm to pull and I
want the spectre to path to me as soon and as close as possible. The /pet
who leader is to tell my party I have a pet or to amaze lower levels with my
pet skellie or orc or bixie. And /time is handy to see what time it is in
game day for Kith. Tip: undead clerics make great pets. Dont know if they
would heal the party but they are too cheap to symbol buff. So may as well
let the othe rmobs beat them down and let them burn their mana working for
the 'good guys.'

"sense" is sense heading ability

"track" brings up tracking

I use Instill Doubt to annoy others when I am in wolf-form. You will get
the doggy barking and attack animation. Other than that, it's pretty much
useless to me.
