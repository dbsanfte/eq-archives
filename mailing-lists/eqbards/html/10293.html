Subject: Hard data on Clarity and Dissension


Just finished doing some tests on these two songs using a level 1 wizard as
a test subject. At 37th level Clarity restores 6 mana per pulse. There is
no residual effect, so this is a direct mana restore. Denon's Dissension is
not effected by having a horn equiped and is a direct mana drain, at 37th it
drains ~ 22 mana.
