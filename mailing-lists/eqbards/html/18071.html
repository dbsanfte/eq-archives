Subject: (Off-topic) Current RoK information:


Okay, this is a bit off topic, but us bards being the info-retentive people
we are, I thought I'd share just a few tidbits with you all.

1) RoK will launch on Monday, April 24th (time undecided) regardless of who
has their CDs or not. Those people who ordered prior to the 20th and do
not receive their CD before the 24th will receive a refund of all shipping
charges, plus a free month of game play. These arrangements to be handled
by email.

2) Some few persons have reached 51st, but were immediately demoted back to
50th. These persons are all Guides playing their play characters, so don't
expect to be able to level any time before the 24th.

3) You will need to have the CD and Registration Key in order to a) Visit
Kunark with your existing characters, 2) Level above 50th, or 3) Create an
Iksar character. You will NOT need the RoK upgrade to see Iksar players in
the rest of the world, or to purchase/trade Kunark-specific items from them.

4) I do not have a CD, and am currently not planning on purchasing one.
