Naeeldar, there are quite a number of places that high levels tend to forget
about. At later levels having grown up in Sol B and Lower Guk from 35-45
you get into sort of a mindset of pull-kill, pull-kill, pull-kill. Higher
level players sometimes don't use their imaginations about things to do!
Here are some suggestions of interesting things for high levels to do for
experience:

Kedge Keep. I'm not sure if 50s get great exp there, but 40-48s sure do.
Definitely a fun place.

Ocean Of Tears. The Seafury Cyclops in OOT that give exp to 50s and spawn
as fast as Moss Snakes. There's the Allizewsaur, a level 50 "dinosaur"
roaming an island there. He spawns pretty quickly and is pretty tough to
take down - so hard in fact a level 50 necro with a pet actually uses more
than half mana to do it. Now THAT'S tough. ;-)

Guards in Neriak, Oggok and Grobb. Heck for that matter if you don't care
about faction there are guards everywhere. You want a thrill? Go set up
camp down deep inside the town of Oggok and pull Bouncers. It's fun and
challenging. Heck Harmonic hit 50 killing Bouncers. Invis your way into
Neriak's Third Gate and pull gaurds. Makes you feel like a kid again!

Castle Mistmoore. There are mobs in there that give experience to 50s.

Brownie Guards in Lesser Fay. They were red to me last time I saw 'em at
41. I'd be pretty surprised if 50s didn't get exp there.

Paw has been tweaked again to be just slightly easier. Do 45-50s get exp
there? Little to none probably, but good 40-45 I'm sure.

Anyhow, don't forget some of the other places. I too get into a mindset of
"damn, solb packed. I'll run to lguk and check it out." But there are
other things to do.
