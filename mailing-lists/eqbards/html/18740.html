Now, my thoughts, my oppinions are below. They differ from others but

1) Bards are not primary tanks. Yes it would be _nice_ to have 3 or 4 more
AC but I'm not going to stay up at night over it.

3) ************** GZ Doesn't design armor. That's a different team

4) The singing steel BOOTS are AC 18. It's not like their AC Sucks they're
still better than anything else we could get before, including imbrued.
5) The "Magic" number for AC is about 800 AC. After 800 there seems to be

900 with items. We can get ours to about the same. With singing steel I

Why must we have the same ac as a warrior? We can kite, they cant. We can

they can't. Umm I'm gonna stop before that paragraph gets too long ;)

I'm not saying it wouldn't be nice to have more AC. I'm saying I'm not
having a cow about a *slightly* lower AC. Now, people complain that rangers
have higher ac than us. I PLAY a Ranger. They need AC about 10 times more

Am I envious? Sure! Am I going to die if I don't get an extra 4 or 5

Anyway... The armor is STILL Better than anything we could get _previously_
The only reason people are upset is because other classes are getting
