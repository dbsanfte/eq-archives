Subject: Soloing out of 30


Our recent discussions got me thinking about the rough time before 32 (when
the world became my oyster *grin*). 31 is easy, join a group at the Giant
Fort in the Frontier mountains and tell em you have the mana song in your
pack ready to mem, it will just fly by :o) However before that you have to
get out of 30, I HATE powerleveling, but in this one case it was way worth
it. Here is how I solo'd out of Hell_1 without dying once and without a lot
of trouble:

In Warsliks Woods ((at pos948,pos1514) there is a giant camp with 3 giants.
You can solo them with CoD/DDD/Largos/Hymn and they are far enough apart
that you can pick one of them at a time. The rest of the zone is mostly way
green with a few wandering blues, but I never got into so much trouble that
running (without zoning) didn't fix it. I love having the auto-pull effect
when you go back too, just make sure you approach from the right direction.
