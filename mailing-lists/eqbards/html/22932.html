I don't think I agree. I doubt that they're going to put bind spots right in
front of most dungeons, instead, I think they're likely to just add a few
spots in more central locations.

Some of your guesses don't really seem logical. For example, Why bother with
a bind in Lfay when you can bind in Gfay? Having a bind 1 zone closer isn't
going to increase MM trafic very much. Or WC? I doubt they'd add a bind
point for a teenage dungeon especially when freeport is 2 zones away.

I think they'll be aiming for mid level dungeons that are under used.
Definitely something near paw. Perhaps the stone bridge in SK or NK.
Something near unrest is possible, but not too likely seeing as how unrest
is pretty well frequented. The other biggie would be something closer to the
evil side of the world. Lake Rathe, Rathe Mountains, Cazic Thule,
Upper/Lower Guk. The arena is a bindable zone, but that's still a long walk
to cazic or guk.

Lastly a bind near perma wouldn't help in the slightest. The reason people
don't go is because it's so hard to get to, not to fight in. The closest
teleports are West karana near NK zone or Toxxula forrest. Neither of which
are very close. Personally I'd like to see a teleport ring or spire placed
in permafrost to make that side of the world a little more accessable.

Other likely candidates are kunark zones. Something close to Howling Stones
for good races and something close to Kaesora for non lizards both come to
mind.
