Subject: Funny story


Was in Charkok last night for the first time ever and a Sarnak Abjugator or whatever showed up. Our group was kicking it's rear till it gated out. Suddenly I'm standing in front of this thing (god knows where, remember never been there before) and it's beating the tar out of me.

Evidenly a BUNCH of my old guildmates were in the area because I started hearing cheers of "GO LYRNIA!" from all over the zone ;) So, taking the advice of all my old friends I fired off chains at the ugly thing, and high-tailed it away. Amazingly his bind point was in front of some castle thingie (which I wasn't about to go near) so I ran through a passage to the right and, luckily, hit a zone line back to Burning Woods ;) I immediately /guildsay'd (new guild, not old) that I was camping and coming back.

Entire group died except me. Was funny as hell. All my old guildmates (that guild kinda shattered, we're in several different guilds now) were sending me /tells "So, did you evade him?" Took me a minute to remember that when you get summoned the nasties say "You shall not evade me Lyrnia!" I was rolling laughing when the cleric appeared at his bind point just outside the zoneline ;) We went back in and CR'd then had fun for a couple hours ;)

Just thought I'd share. Was a funny moment for me (though scary! I generally stay low on hate lists (ancient Lyrnia Secret) so I've never gotten summoned before)

Lyrnia Jongleur
Bard of Norrath since Beta 2
