Subject: Charming, and Why I Won't Pull in Dalnir


Fighting in Dalnir yesterday, I noticed a couple things. First, for blues,
they all resist my charm a lot. A LOT. A smoldering goo resisted 8
consecutive attempts at charming. Others would resist 3-5 times. My
charisma is over 125, and I don't have these troubles with charm anywhere
else. The FM giants, the yellow and evens, tend to resist 2 times, and then
only occassionally.

The flip side, is the xp in Dalnir. Even in the hell level that is 30, 2
kills gives noticable movement. :) Just make sure you're in a good group,
even with Selos, the death run takes forever.

I won't pull in Dalnir. I think there must be a bard hate multiplier in
there. Had a druid pulling, and he brings 2 casters. He turns the corner,
and 2 heartbeats later, they appear around the corner, stop, and begin to
cast. Just take a wild guess at the recipient of all that DD loving. :)
Not the puller, oh no. It was the guy in blue who still had that weak,
level 20 manasong going. Full health to 2 bubbles before we do any
damage...heh. Too bad for them, the druid healed me. I then proceeded to
buff everyones MR to high heck, and I don't think they landed anything else.

But the second one still resisted my charm like crazy...I'm baffled. Charm
does not work in there like it does everywhere else. But I'm not leaving.
As long as you get a decent group...oh, the exp! Hehe...
