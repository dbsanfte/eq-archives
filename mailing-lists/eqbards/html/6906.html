it (from the mob's perspective) that they were literally chasing pixies.

they can't attack you. This might be considered too powerful except for the
fact F7 (default) targets the closest PC (generally that would be YOU) AND
the minute you attempt to do damage to the character, you are auto targeted,
hence these songs would facilitate fleeing and maybe a sneak attack/ambush
if you're in cahoots with others.
