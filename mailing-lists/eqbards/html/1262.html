re: damage, i like meleeing so i got my strength up as much as i could
(currently 107) and i hit for 20dmg with a 5dmg 1h slashing weapon, so it
does get better.

re: soloing, i never solo. i think the bard is built for grouping, so i
have one partner at the very minimum. of course this gets more important
the higher level you get, as blue creatures double attack for 80-90dmg. so
as far as being higher level and expecting to solo better, i wouldn't count
on it. and i wouldn't worry about grouping at higher levels, once you hit
level 32 your mana song is a big hit and quite a valuable addition to any
group.

originally i was in a full group and usually was throughout my time there.
the only problem is i was a bit ahead and missed the crowd, and cazic is
packed now.
