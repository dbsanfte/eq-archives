compatriots are eagerly looking for this stuff. We got our
first piece of medium bronze last night. It was off a L25

Archeologist. That would put it at roughly the same level as
the dry bones and nobles that most often drop small bronze in

can't use Accelerando/SoW, thus requiring a much higher level
group to survive there, much less get this stuff.

behind him (half of whom got caught in my lullaby because I'd
