Well, as some of you may have noticed, I had discovered what I had thought
to be a bonus that the Unicorn Horn has to wind-based songs. Yeah, I know,
wind songs don't do much with bonuses (can you levitate 20% better?), but
there are a few cases where it does make a difference (ie. Pixie Strike's MR

debuff, possibly others).

Well, after careful testing of my Unicorn Horn and a standard store-bought
flute, I have determined that the Unicorn Horn does add an addition 5% to
wind-based songs where it can actually make a difference. Standing in the
Freeport bank, I pixie striked myself several times with a store-bought
flute and my MR was reduced by 12 points. Then I pixie striked myself with

the Unicorn horn and my MR was reduced by 15 points. I believe my starting
MR was 48 at the time (I had removed some of my MR gear so that I could hit
myself more reliably).
