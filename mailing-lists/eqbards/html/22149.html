TOday I was one of a fortunate six who were in the right place at the right
time. I was working on my smithing in Qeynos when a Crier shouted that the
Antonius Bayle had put a price on the head of Mordavin, the Archpriest of
the Bloodsabres. Vegalys was to head the hunt for him and bring him in for
trial for his henious crimes, treason murder and more!
I gathered Arolpin, Snip, Noraa, Rizzlerun and Sechan in my group. We
learned from Vegalys that Mordavin has been seen in East Karana, and Sechan
gated us there. Then we were off! We who had the ability to track soon found

him at a farmhouse near the mountain foothills. We confronted him and
demanded he surrender. He refused at first so we attacked! Now mind you
though he was less then my own 52 summers he was at least 49! Icecomets flew

from Snips fingers while I sang three chants bound to destroy any clerics
concentration. He finally surrendered for we were a force too mighty for him

to overcome! Firmly bound (and gagged) he was taken back to Qeynos. Once
there Vegalys awarded us for our valour. I believe Snip gained a book,
Sechan a diamond studded cap. For myself, there was two giftings. First, the

shield of Antonius Bayle himself! Tis AC 75, an old family heirloom which i
will never allow to drop for tis bound to me by magic. In addition I was
awarded a title. No longer shall I be known in the realm as Elyana Shimada.
I am henceforth Elyana of Qeynos! Needless to say I got rip roaring drunk in

celebration. Then I went back to my forging.. somehow the pounding of steel
seemed to lessen my headache =P
