Get the best GeForce you can afford. Currently, the 2 best manufacturers
(IMHO), are Creative and Hercules. Others may disagree...heh.

GeForce 3 is the newest and best out there. If your budget is a bit
smaller, check out the GeForce 2 line. Buyer beware! Some of the "bargain"
card manufacturers creatively label their cards such as they're not
untruthful, per se, but you could end up with a slightly different card than
you thought you were getting, the difference usually being a slightly
different clock speed. Do your homework, read some reviews, pick a
particular model, and don't look back. :)

I bought a GeForce256 DDR a little less than a year ago. It's still a
superb card, I haven't found a game yet that's too much for it.

Good luck!
