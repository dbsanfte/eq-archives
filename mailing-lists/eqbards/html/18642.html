An alternate guild philosophy: be nice, be honest, be trustworthy. We don't control
information, we don't exclude people from raids, and we don't bad-mouth other
peoples' playing. On the other hand we also don't enjoy stupid people - which is
something that all pick-up raids suffer from ;P.

We have had many instances where the guild tag above one of our members' heads was
enough for someone to give consent for corpse recovery. I am proud of that.

We are in the third wave of high levels in our guild. The first wave saw people
switching to twinks a lot - their 50s are in the third wave ;P.

Many in the second wave left to form another guild. Some people thoughtfully and
respectfully posted to our web site and resigned. They were sincerely wished well.
Some left without a word - that wasn't a welcome attitude, but no one persecuted
them for it.

Now we have a lot of firepower to do more fun things....and many of us are immersed
in Kunark haha. We have about 50 planes-capable players but little interest in the
planes lately ;P.

There is a lot of appreciation for a bard's contribution and a lot of willingness to
work out tactics, which is great =).
