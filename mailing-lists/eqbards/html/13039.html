If you're doing Celerity for atk speed, you can always add in Chant for
str/dex for additional damage (str) and procs (dex).

Since you're meleeing, and you're fighting IGs outside where there's an
area no lower mobs move around in, use Chords of Dissonance for damage.
It will do more than bellow, and if you can manage to swap weapons/lute in
3 seconds for the pulse, it'll do more still (although with a borrowed yk,
might not be the best of ideas).

Note Chords over Denon's, as you mentioned additional resists with
dual-purpose songs, and Denon's is dmg/ac debuff whereas Chords is
straight dmg.
