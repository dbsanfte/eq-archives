Greeting Barrd! Welcome!

I am currently still exploring Kunark myself. I have a wizard 31 who got a
good group in the Frontier Mts - right by the zone to lake of Ill Omen.
They pulled stuff to the zone and we killed it. Also, saw some friends in
Timours Deep (sp?) - there are several islands with various things - one has
a cookie cutter copy of the SK aviak treehouse but its populated with
bigger, badder birties (spiroks) - some blue to me at 51 and down from
there. Might want to explore some.

As far as karnors, that seems to be a higher level dungeon. Again I dont
know much about it. Was there one time and saw level 50+'s training to the
zone and others getting trained out. Your mana song will be welcome in all
the groups you join but you need to be careful to stay in groups that fight
things that you can survive.

Good luck and good hunting, keep us posted on how you do!
