If you have a good group of friends to travel with, we all fought in the
Swamp of No Hope in Kunark from our late teens to early 20's. There's an
iksar marauder camp over by the zone to Trakanon's Teeth as well as a
Froggie camp hidden down a corridor (up on the right hand ledge heading
towards the marauder camp). Just don't attack Dugroz as he's an almost
invincible NPC needed for the Howling Stones quest.

The zone is real close in case anything goes wrong.

When that started greening out, we started pulling froggies from near the
frog city, and when we got bored with that, we fought near where the Ton
Warrior for the enchanter's iksar illusion spell spawned (right around the
iksar bandit camp close to the Field of Bones zone).

It's a great area because frequently we were the only ones in the zone. We
almost always (except for the Ton Warrior camp) had this place to ourselves.

Grats on 20!
