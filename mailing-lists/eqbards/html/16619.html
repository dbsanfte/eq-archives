I play on Veeshan also, it can get busy at times but me being from England
I get to play when the servers are under populated (1200-1400 ppl). I know
what you mean about experience though, I have only recently got through 40
(took me 3 weeks). An excellent place for exp at your level would be Dwarf
NPC's in Butcherblock, you only loose faction with the residents and not
guards. If you can get a 2 spawn dwarf camp you can sit there for hours
Charming them and then killing the winner using Fear/snare (or Kiting). The
spawns are 12 minutes usually but they are great exp. Also solo'ing giants
is another good option.

If not you could try Mistmoore, if you can get a group from your guild to
take you there and simply powerlevel you I can assure you that you will get
through 35 in a day. I made 1.5 bubs of level 35 exp in 3 hours and I was
grouped with level 45+ people.

you will get there, don't worry about it.
