the Libretto. I have used the Libretto on NUMEROUS occasions to determine
otherwise hidden information. Examples:
1) Ran across a ring lying on the bottom of a lake. Silver, and bonus to
charisma... Quest item? Hrm... Used Libretto to learn former owner's name.

2) Discovered that there are three different kinds of jum jum stalks, but
they all look the same until you identify them.

3) Only way to tell what Nybright necklaces you already have if you've
forgotten.
