Sorry, I'm not trying to be offensive. Its just that this topic really
steams me. When you constantly tell people how important CR is and they
ignore you only to die a pathetic death to a dragon, with twice as many
people as it would take those of us properly geared to do it, it gets very
frustrating.
Let me give you an example. Last week we went to kill Vox. 24 of us. No
problem. No deaths. We then went on to PoH. After about an hour there the
server crashed, and instead of spending the time breaking in again we
decided to go kill the freshly spawned Vox again. 24 of us. No problem. 1
death. This one death was a person who arrived late and didn't have time to
get CR buffs because we were already in her lair. From here we were feeling
pretty confident. Lets gate to Lavastorm and kill Nags. It was getting
pretty late so nobody wanted to waste the time running to town to grab FR
gear. (We hadn't planned on doing Nags, he only respawned when the server
went down).
24 people. Little or no FR gear. Barely killed him, more than half dead.
Normally Nagafen presents no more problems than Vox.
Sure, maybe you'll get a lucky roll every now and then on damage. Please do
not make the mistake of thinking that this means that resists are useless.
And if you think that equipping weapons and doing ~500 points of damage
makes a difference to a creature that has 25,000 hit points, think how much
more damage your party could have done if they'd resisted just a little bit
more damage, and had just a few more attack rounds.
