Tip #1 - be polite but persistent in contacting the head GM of your server.
It make take some time and some persistence and lots of politeness - and
role play helps too. Persist.

Tip#2 - move to kedge. Try for a drum off carolyn kelpmaiden - as good or
close to Selos.

Tip#3 - go to Naggy with guilds that need bards. At 42, you should be
pretty respectable in your ability to regen mana and provide excellent
resists - stack Elemental & Guardian with a drum <snif snif> equipped and
have them look at their stats. Promise not to melee and to do your best to
keep their clerics from getting feared and ae'd.

Tip#4 - camp MM and try for another drum.

I dropped mine one time and died on the spot (looking for it as a couple
clops beat on me) so that I would have the loc and be able to have my party
find it.
