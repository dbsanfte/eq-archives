I did the hand room last night in Karnor. When I started we had an

pulled the warlord and his bodyguards for example. Most amazing loot period
I saw. I about 3 hours (maybe less) we got Druid Gloves, Cleric Boots,
Warrior, and Cleaver of Ogguk, (I forget the class item names).

Then the enchanter left and I did crowd control. Eventually a very good
necro joined us (one who knew how to group). It worked great with the two

used Undead Charm as Crowd control.

while. I was taking some damage -but we had a good cleric - so I was found.
