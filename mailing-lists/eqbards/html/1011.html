You have to go into High Hold itself (another zone in HH
Pass). Inside in the back are two libraries. One has a man
selling wizard spells. The other has a man selling bard
spells (named Justin Rhymes or something, ha ha). There is a
bank in High Hold, so you don't have to risk any valuable
equipment and money on the trip over; although the place was
so camped when I went (the mobs there give no less than three
highly desired magic items) that I didn't even need
Accelerando and just walked in. The layout of HH Pass is
rather confusing, and if you don't mind getting a map online,
I suggest you do so. There are no 90 degree turns in the
place, and it's very easy to get disoriented.
