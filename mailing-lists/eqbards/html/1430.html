What's nice, and I know this comes late, is when you get Berzerker. It
provides a speed increase a tad better than Anthem at that level, STR
bonus, and a very nice AC bonus (9 points when you first get it). All in
one song. If you're willing to give up your weapons, and use a horn while
singing it, it increases speed, strength, and AC quite a bit more.

Also, you'll find use for Chant of Battle again. It stacks with Celerity.
Celerity is similar to Anthem, it boosts attack speed, but instead of STR
bonus, it's AGI bonus. You can cycle that with Chant of Battle, you get
attack speed increase, STR, DEX, AGI, and AC bonus, for the whole party.
