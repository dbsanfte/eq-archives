I agree. Basically, Verant feels the need to alter the
game balance by nerfing from character classes. This may
be part of a larger effort to help the pure melee classes.
In any case, I think they have come to the conclusion that
if they can do it silently, no one may notice or be
able to prove it. This lets them avoid any headaches dealing
with pesky paying customers.

The problem with this stealth nerfing policy is that it gets
characters killed who havent been notified of the changes and
attempt to do something that has always succeeded in the past.
As paying customers, I believe that everyone has the right to
be informed of any substantive changes that affect the gameplay
that we all pay for. That is why I suggest thated a clause on
stealth nerfing be placed in the Bard petition (Bards seem to
have been victimized a couple of times by this). On the other
hand, it may be more worthwhile to start a separate petition
demanding that Verant publish and adhere to an Open Disclosure
Agreement that commits them to release these types of changes
immediately upon implementing them. The "knife in the dark"
stealth nerfing policy is very unprofessional.
