Xegony does not use a master calendar to my knowledge for the simple fact
that we have too many personalities on Xegony. Cooperation is sometimes
limited and we have whole guilds of folks that simply don't play well with
others. Rule of thumb is whoever gets there first and god help you if you
get wiped out first try. Dragons on Xegony usually die pretty fast,
although

Vox is a bit off the beaten track and can be up a bit longer than most. I
would guess the aveage lifespan of a dragon on Xegony to be no more than a
couple hours. You may consider parking a toon that can track in Perma.

My advice is still to try and get into Ragefire camps for red scales and
watch for Gore as much as possible. If you can get into a Vox/Naggy raid,
do it. It will probably take you several levels before you have all the
pieces. Helping people with Gore/Vox/Nag/Trak/Rage gets your name known
and brownie points built. You will need them if you spot Vox up and when
you get ready to spawn Trak for your last step. My guild has some Vox
level toons and we need a frost book for one of our main paladins. If you
spot her up one night, give me a tell and we will see who  we have
that can come help.

We did finally get Kaas' epic finished this weekend. Ever had the luck to
be sitting in Trak's lair, waiting on the undead bard only to have Trak
spawn? What fun!
