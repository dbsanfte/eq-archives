If you go to Allakazahm's EQ web site, you can "register" and create
"characters" including a list of their base stats and current equipment.
(Base stats are actually optional.) Once you have your gear listed, you can
ask it to search the equipment db for upgrades based on what stats you want
to improve, ie: ac and mr or cha and in, etc. It can also filter the search
and such. Anyhow, it's helped me see all kinds of stuff that could help my
various chars.
