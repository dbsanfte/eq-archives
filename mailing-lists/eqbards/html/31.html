I just have to clear this up, and share how I use the song. First, consider
it a NON-combat song in many ways. If you sneak up on a goblin camp, and
spy four of the beasts, grab your lute and make three "sad" while they
loiter about. If you approach and attack the fourth, you will notice the
other three "sad" goblins will not join the fray. You can then back off a
bit and fight the sole goblin, effectively picking each one off one at a
time in this manner!

And who says Bards aren't built for Soloing?

Must say the song came in handy in the Qeynos sewers, as a way to pacify an
especially mean-looking NPC as I swam past. And O how I used it on my
journey from Kelethin, really made the Highold Pass a Cakewalk Pass.
