When I took my Bard there at about that level, I found the best thing was
to group with one or two tanks (Trolls and Ogres LOVE this), and just sit
back and play the healing song. The only thing that slows tanks down here
is healing - and if you can keep them healed (and throw in some occasional
damage like with Chords of Dissonance or Brusco's Boastful Bellow, so you
don't even have to switch instruments), they'll love you forever.

I got about half a level in one night just doing this. In fact, I went afk
to work on some paperwork and just left my Bard standing nearby, strumming
the Hymn. When the tanks were wounded, they'd sit near me, then run out,
kill some more, and come back. Spent about two hours like that and only
got hit twice ;)
