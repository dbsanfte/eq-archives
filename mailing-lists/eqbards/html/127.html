Valen is in South Karana more or less in the center, though it's east of Paw
as I recall. If you know the zone at all, there's a stone building in the
middle of all the flatness, in the centaur spawning area. Head north and
you'll find Valen.

I did all this in beta only to discover that the quest was broken, and Valen
wouldn't take the lute. Then I discovered that once you destroyed the lute,
you couldn't get a new one. Argh.

I have heard that it is fixed now.

For lower level bards, South Karana is mostly safe. Avoid the lions and the
undead and the raiders, but generally things are indifferent to you. North
Karana, on the other hand, is very dangerous. Keep your drum out and run
like the wind.
