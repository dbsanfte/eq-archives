I got my stuff in there (Barroom not King room I think) - about a 3 hour
camp - got my item 2 hours in but we stayed longer for ranger stuff and
general xp. Nothing too special - keep clearing adjacent rooms. Went back
another time to help rangers and stayed only 2 hours and a bit - that time
we did not see any bard items but mix of mobs was the same.

This is a cool camp - both spots King Room and Bar room. But it really
helps to have a guide. And of course a cleric (or equivalent healing power
in multiple druid/sha-people). It's a fight your way in and fight your way
out part of the dungeon.
