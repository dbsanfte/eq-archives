There are numerous locations in the game where, due to pathing AI, fear
kiting is a completely safe tactic. A good example is by the giant fort in

then start fearing it, it will just run back and forth while everyone whoops
up on it. Now if fear breaks then the giant will of course start attacking
you. I have never fear kited in the Overthere location referenced earlier

In reference to the fear kite tacticts in OT near the skyfire zone -- move
to the corner between skyfire and frontier mountains. If you fear mobs
there, they run up into the corner and run back and forth mostly up in the
corner.

I had a gm watch me for about 15 minutes up there one evening. I asked him
if he considered it an exploit, and he said "Nope, Great tactics!" Bear in
mind that the mob was never just running into a wall, it was always in
motion, and as long as fear wasnt resisted it stayed on this little path
where it ran back and forth.
