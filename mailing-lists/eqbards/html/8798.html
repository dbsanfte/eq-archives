The Solusek Mage and champions show up in the area around the
king room. After you zone into Sol A, pass the lava and go
through the hole in the wall, you'll come to a T intersection
at the ledge. If you go straight (off the ledge) there's a
doorway. Go through that door and follow the right
wall. There's one passage that will loop back (ends at a
window above a previous area), but if you skip it and keep
following the right wall, it'll take you straight to the room
with the Solusek Mage. The room immediately prior to this,
there's a door on the left which leads to the king room. So
it's actually not that bad a place to use as a base and pull
to.

You *will* need a group. Just clear as you go, and be careful
to give yourself time for when the room respawns. I had a
full L30-36 group overwhelmed in here because we had a 4
goblin pull when the room goblins started to spawn (two
healers too, never did find the second, probably healing
through the walls). This is the high level goblin portion of
Sol A by the way.
