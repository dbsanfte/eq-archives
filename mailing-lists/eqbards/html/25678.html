Concentrate on keeping your skills maxxed in the 20's and 30's. Going by
memory, it seems to me that the high 20's to mid 30's, the skills raised
quickly still. Melee skills particularly benefit from this, as during these
levels, you benefit more from haste, as more of those that you group with
have some kind of melee haste.

Also, I recall it was in my mid-20's, I parked in Arena for a day. When I
came back, I scrolled back, and there were always at least 2 skill increase
messages per "screen"...roughly, of course.

Nowadays, I've left myself sit for hours at a time, and I've felt lucky to
see 2 skill increases for the entire time standing there.

Do it while you're young, because unless you spend enormous amounts of time
doing nothing but working on those skills, you will likely fall behind once
you're in the late 40's.

Just my observations.
