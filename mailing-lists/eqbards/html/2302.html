I didn't make the original statement, but I'll tell you why *I* think it is
too dangerous. The bard song only affects party members within a certain
range of the bard. We've all experienced the case where a party member
gets out of range of Selo's Accelerando and then falls behind the party.
Imagine the same thing happening 100 feet underwater.

This is all complicated by the fact that it's very hard to see underwater,
and it's easy to get separated. Also, people are swimmers of vastly
differing skills. My swimming is maxed out from being in the OOT for so
long. Other people my level often have swimming skills of 5-10. Comparing
my swim speed to theirs is like comparing a jet ski to a flutterboard.

The song is great for a bard solo though.
