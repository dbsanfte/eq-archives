If you are 27th then you are using the wrong soings to solo. I am 29th,
and i solo all the time, and i NEVER use bellow. Just yesterday, i
solo'd a Full size mammoth (blue) and a Werewolf (also blue).

The way to solo at 27+ is chain/fear/heal. chain the mob so it can't
run, Fear it so i walks away, Whack it with your weapons, and heal

my HP, bellowing against it i would last about 6 rounds. With
Chain/fear/heal, i was able to solo it with about 50% hp left, probably
only that bad because my brass is not maxed, and i missed notes on fear
alot.
