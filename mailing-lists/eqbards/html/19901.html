I've made the trip 6 times now, no problem. Here's what I do.

I ride the elven warship past the BB shuttles and dive off when closest to
the barge island.
I swim to the barge island and wait for the barge.
I take the barge to Oasis.
Coming back is easier.
Get on barge in Oasis.
Ride barge to island, use invus to get on boat to Overthere
Jump off boat to Overthere and swim to beach.
