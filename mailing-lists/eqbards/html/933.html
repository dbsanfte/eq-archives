Depends on where you currently are hanging out. I was mostly in Beholder
around that level with a group. Hunting minos for their axes and a horn (for
later use). Also spent quite a bit of time in the North Ro desert. If you
are on Faydwer I would say try for Crushbone, the Lesser Fay bandits, the
Butcherblock Orcs, or maybe the Steamfont skeletons (I think your a little
high for the skellies though?). Near Freeport, I would suggest Desert of Ro.
Lots of wandering mobs. Near Qeynos I would suggest Blackburrow. I also hear
West Karanas has some good hunting grounds, though I have never tried there.
Actually tons of other places to go.
