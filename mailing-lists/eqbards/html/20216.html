In my guild, Jus Divinum (www.usdivinum.com), on Mithaniel Marr we have 3 50+
level bards that play frequently (my bard is my primary and I would rather play
her than any other character). We have several at lower levels too, and one at
50 who has an 50+ level cleric that he plays more now.

We also have several 50+ enchanters.

It's very common for me to be grouped with an enchanter. In fact, an
enchanter, bard, and monk are the core of our 6 person Fire Giant runs (WAY
fun). We also add a shaman, a cleric, and the 6th has been a paladin (because
he's my game husband hehe) but that is the spot to put another class.

Similarly, one group, or the core group for a larger Sebilis raid, almost
always has a bard and an enchanter.

The bard is needed to cut the down time. I agree with Lyrnia ( believe it was
her) that our strength lies there. But I have pulled in Sebilis, done crowd
control, been battle bard...just adapted to the conditions.

But one of the most fun incidents lately was Fire Giants. A FG wizard
teleported back to the throne room and summoned me (we were still clearing the
first few down by the lava). I realized I couldn't run out, so I charmed it
and pulled it back to the group ;).
