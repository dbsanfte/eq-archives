Subject: Re: Melifluous Motion -- another use


I have found another, possibly unintentional effect of the Melifluous
Motion.
Hardly worth the memorization, but when the song teleports you off a ledge
you can appear at the bottom unharmed. I say 'can' because if the cliff
wall is
slanted there is always that possibility that you could get teleported on
the
slant and immediately slide down, possibly taking falling damage.

Also, the song will teleport to the bottom of any body of water you
may be in. Uses again are limited, but if you wanted to see the bottom of
the ocean...
Or perhaps you are getting chomped by a shark in the OOT and need some time
to yourself before you die?
