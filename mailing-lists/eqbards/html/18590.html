Hi all. I'm in the midst of trying to do a new quest that has been
found in Firiona Vie. I was hoping that someone here could give me
some help.

If you hail Glinya Sweetpie (she sells baking items and food/water)
she mentions someone named Treekill. If you ask her about him, she
says something to the effect of he's been tring to court her and she
told him to bring her a Burynai Bunt Cake to put him off. Asking her
about the Bunt Cake, she says that there's no such thing.

There is a wandering guard named Scout Treekill. If you hail him, he
doesn't say anything, but the text mentions a helm hanging from his

Scout Treekill gets excited. What?!! Do you know how to bake a
Burynai Bunt Cake. Glinya said she would marry me if I baked one!!
She won't let me use her oven so I had to search for one. I finally
located one in some goblin mine, but I can't find the recipe or bake.
Please!! I would give you this bards helm for a Burynai Bunt Cake.

Here is what I have deduced from this passage. The Bunt Cake requires
the baking skill. There is an oven in the Mines of Droga where it
must be baked. Now, I am not a baker, so I'm asking here if anyone
might know the recipe for this? Is there anyone else who might be
working on this that can give me any leads?
