Here's a little trick I learned in cazic this week for
getting mobs that you recently had charmed off of you,
stop attacking and hi them with a pixie strike and
time it so you step back just as it hits. Kept them
from frenzing on me (as they tend to to coming out of
charm). Once they're off you resume combat as normal.
