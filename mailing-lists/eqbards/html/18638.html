Actually it will only switch to another target if that target is attacking
you, it's master. Otherwise it will just stand and/or walk around looking
dumb ;) Best way is to /pet back off then /pet kill (after targetting what
you want it to beat on)

Your pet could care less what's beating on your party. It DOES, however
care what is beating on you. If nothing is attacking you just doing /pet
back off won't do much good (other than to basically have the critter
'mesmerised')

Hope this helps!!!
