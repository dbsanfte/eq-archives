Obsidian Shard (and the Obsidian Flamberge) both have the "Obsidian Shatter"
effect.

The Obsidian Shatter Effect has these properties:
1) DD 30 DMG
2) DEBUFF: -10 AC for approximately 6 heartbeats (Tics)

It's not a DoT, but it is a Debuff. Therefore you will get the message that
it wore off.
