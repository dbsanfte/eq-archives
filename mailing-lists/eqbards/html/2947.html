To tell the truth... I have neglected to develop my "stacking" or juggling
skills. Now I am at a level and in situations where I REALLY need this. I
am also re-thinking how I have all my stuff setup - hot key palletes, etc.

But my point is - for all the young bards... learn to play 2 songs at the
same time - by starting one and then stopping it (it will continue for a
couple pulses) and starting another. Stopping it when you have two icons up
and restarting the first one, Rinse, Repeat.

In melee, I try to keep the Anthem de Arms up with either the healing song
or Elemental Rythmns and Bellow tossed in when possible - love seeing them
writhe in pain. I think I am really starting to see some really valuable
party aids. Not sure what they think - they used to like the after battle
healing - not sure if they are seeing the in combat help yet.

My trouble is missed beats and getting stunned drops the songs. Also I
guess I am clumbsy and lack rythmn to tap the keys with the pulses -
practice practice practice and get those skills maxed.
