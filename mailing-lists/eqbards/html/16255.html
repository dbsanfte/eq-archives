I used it on the Maestro (Hymn w/ lute, PR w/ drum, Niv's w/
lute, PR w/ drum). The only time I got attacked is when he

It does make you aware that your remaining hp is merely a
state of being - i.e. whether you're at full hp or half is
pretty much irrelevant. What determines whether you're going
to live or die is whether the rate you're taking damage
exceeds the rate you're healing.
