When I first got this song at 25, it did 4 points per hit. At
29, this changed to 5 points per hit. If this trend holds,
it's 1 extra damage every 4 levels. L25-28 = 4, L29-32 = 5,
L33-36 = 6, L37-40 = 7, L41-44 = 8, L45-48 = 9, L49-50 = 10.

Also, it *does* stack with the druid/ranger's thorn damage
shields. I thought it didn't because I only saw the "is
pierced by thorns" message. A druid cast shield of barbs on me
while I was playing PoW, and I also only got the "pierced by
thorns" message. But the damage was the sum of the thorns and
PoW. So only the message is in error, the damage is still
there.
