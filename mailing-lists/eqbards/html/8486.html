Subject: Trade of the Century..


[PREXUS-LEVEL 14 BARD]

For me at least. One day I was really bored and decided to do the Shining
Star of Light quest. Sure its Cleric/Paladin only, but what they heck I
figured, maybe I could get something for it. Time goes by and it starts to
gather dust.

One day while running to Quenos I see an auction in N. Karana..
..."For trade, Polished Granite Tomahawk, Mistmoor Battle Drums"

My heart skipped a beat and quickly went dead when I realized that I was a)
broke and b) probally not going to have anything a Level 50 Druid would
want, but being a masochist I sent her a tell. Polite snickering could be
envisioned as I went from offering a few things, to all my bronze etc. Still
no deal.

A while later I remember my SSOL. I propose it to her as "Im sure you
probally wont be interested, but if you have a Cleric/Paladin friend, maybe
they would like this.." Remarkably she replies that the SSOL is indeed an
item of worth and while she doesnt need it, she will check with her guild.
(Translation to me: I ask, but probally no).

Three days go by and I see her online, "Hello, I am the Bard who spoke with
you the other day. I was curious if you had spoken with your guild, and if
you would like to trade the Drums for the SSOL." ..I then began to await
the rejection.

But lo and behold she said she would trade! We met in the W. Commonland
Druid ring and traded. I equipped it, and ZOOOOM!!!!! Off I went, wow, I am
still amazed at the speed. At 13 I can outrun people in JBOOTS, and have
outrun people with SOW. Its absolutely amazing how much ground I can get.

Anyway, thats my tale.. hope it was entertaining.

P.S. To date I have been able to save myself and 3 other people in
situations where SELO alone wouldnt have done it.
