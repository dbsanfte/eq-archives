Author: Bill Costlow
Subject: Escape from Timorous Deep


Last Saturday morning I decided to return to Kunark
after two weeks of disasterous questing for lambant.

As the ferries zoned into the TD zone, the top level
of each barge vapped and I was standing in the bottom
of the boat.

As I tried to climb onto the big ship, I fell thru a
solid portion of the gangplank and into the water.
The ship took off at an incredible speed and I was
alone.

No guild help coming, I did an /ooc request for a loc
and swam NW to an empty island.

West 2 islands away is a spiroc camp (all conned red
to a 28 bard) and an ogre camp where the overthere
ship docks.

I approached the dock using shaury's and saw a lot of
folks camping ogres. Most conned blue and ... they
drop fine steel.

I got killed once by a shaman, bigchief something or
other but on returning I found that I could walk out
to the end of the dock without getting attacked.

Running thru the overthere and FM zones to get bound
in FV was a breeze.

Tyunes of Rodcet Nife
closing in on Hell level
