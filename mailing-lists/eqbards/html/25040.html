Most people I've talked to who game on Win2K generally have no problems,
however, if all you're doing is games, it's recommended to stick with
Win98SE. On the other hand, if you, for example, work from home, and your
machine is also productive (in a not fun sort of way...heh), then you may
benefit by the better stability and features of Win2K.

To boil it down a little:

If your gaming machine is used to cruise the web, and do the occassional
word document, or some such, go with Win98SE.

If you happen to play games on your productivity/work machine, go with
Win2K, unless you're happy with Win98, of course. :)

If you're not sure, games run better under Win98.

Good luck! :)
