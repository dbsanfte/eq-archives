Tzeetch - it's the very simple, and very old, Scrap Metal quest located in
the bowels of Ak'Anon.
Sanfyrd Featherhead (formerly Sanfyrd ontop), will accept scrap metal (in
loads of 4 unstacked at a time), which he will give you coins (a few
silver or copper) and a random item recovered from the scrapyard, which is
usually a rusty weapon, small buckler or Gnomish GlowRod (light source for
off-hand).
I believe that either 1) there is an incredibly small chance for a really
nice item, or 2) it was a fluke. Either way, I just won the lottery as far
as I'm concerned...
