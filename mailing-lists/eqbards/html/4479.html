At 33rd Level, you can still get some XP in Cazic Thule. Sure, a lot of
mobs will be green, but there's good loot, and for me, it's ALWAYS been
fairly easy to get a group there.

Mistmoor is another alternative. To me, however, Mistmoor Is a "Crusbone on
Steroids", meaning, a lot of stuff is green, a lot of stuff is red, and
there are a lot of trains. This is a good zone for us especially if you
don't have a drum yet.

Solusek B is very good for you level. EVERYTHING SHOULD be blue or up, but
dont even think about soloing there.

Lower Gukk is also good for XP, there are two sides you can enter, as well.
I have played zone warrior on the "live side", or mino side, where you can
usually make it to the zone as you dont have to go in very far to find mobs.
(On a side note.... you can also get one of the best Arm Armors I've seen in
this area, and its relatively easy to get, as well. MUCH easier than rubi
arms in Cazic). THe OTHER side is the "dead" side, noted for tons of Undead
Frogloks, Ice Bones, etc. This side has been much harder for my guild. I
wouldnt do this side without a group for sure.

There are the "singles" areas and mobs you could do... the basement in
Unrest, giants, griffs, etc. Depends on what you want!

I am sure there are plenty of other areas, too. These are just the ones I've
frequented.
