We're talking about making "social" buttons, right? If there's a more complex
macro environment than that for EverQuest, I definitely wanna know about
it...I'm the kind who constantly wrote script code for my ircii client, my
terminal program in the BBS days, et cetera.

To write social buttons, you go to the second Socials screen and right-click
a button, then give it a name and enter commands into the lines available for
the button. You can invoke ability buttons as "/do 1" (for the first button),
and combat as "/do 7" (for the first button), you have an additional "pause"
command, like "/pause 100". I forget if it's in seconds or milliseconds or
what.

You can also copy your socials to the custom user button area, and then link
them to automatic actions, by going into Options:Keyboard and adding hotkeys
to the "User Custom 1", et cetera, part. I use this to link Sense Heading to
the left/right arrows on the numpad, and wrote a macro for (for example) my
Monk which invokes Forage, Mend, and Loc every time I hit the forward key.

The spell keys are something like "/gem 1"
