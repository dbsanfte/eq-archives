Ok, I'll start something... I think of you all as friends
and could really use some advice. Its not bard related (well there was a
bard there!) but I could really use some input...

Last night.... Got invited to a Plane of Sky trip - my
first trip to the planes. It was not a guild trip but a bunch of people
just going. Everything went surprisingly well - we had 4 groups and few
deaths considering we had never fought together before. We cleared out
everything and were on the last kill of the night, the Heart Harpie I think
it is. The group I was in actually got the kill (2 druid, me (shaman) and a
paladin if you can believe that).

Well we all sit down to med, and the rest of the group is
OOC-ing for us to "shout out the loot". I hardly ever loot so I was
expecting someone else (i.e. my group leader) to do this. Well nobody in
our group did right away - I don't think. The rest of the group kept
shouting to tell them the loot was so I finally got up and looted the
corpse. All that was on it was an animal figurine - basically nothing. And
this was supposed to have the big loot of the night.

Immediately, several of the people started making loot
stealing accusations. And these were not calm and collected but ranting and
raving of the type I haven't seen since 8th grade. It was frankly quite
awful. I don't know many of these people well so I can see where they don't
have proof of my honesty but the extent to which they carried on and accused
was unbelievable. The fact that seemed to condemn me the most was that it
took "too long" (i.e. 1 minute) for me to announce the loot...

I have no way of knowing if I was the first to loot the
corpse or not. So perhaps there was something on it that someone else in my
group stole - but I honestly doubt it.

So my question is this... what do I do? I know in the
future I'm not looting a thing and that we should have had more clear rules
set up. Does this happen on other servers? Has this happened to any of
you?

Almost as disillusioned as Harmonic,
