There is always the option to send the Bards player guide to all the other major
EQ sites at the same time you send it to EQVault. That way everyone has it and
you don't have to worry about EQVault's policy of not linking to other sites.
IIRC, Allakazam is also requesting that someone send in an updated bards guide.

On a side note, I have the following EQ websites bookmarked that I check on
regular basis. IMO, each does one particular thing better than the other sites.


EQVault- Check here for updated game news, events, and to read the E'ci message
board.
Allakazam's site- This site has pretty good quest information if you need it.
EQlizer- I think this one has the best info on magic items and where they are
found.
Questors of Norrath- A Japanese website that has outstanding maps of almost
every zone. Even if you don't need/use maps, you should check it out just to
see this guys work.

I don't have all the links on my computer at work but if someone can't find them
they can send me a private message at bamadude@... and I will send the
URLs to them.
