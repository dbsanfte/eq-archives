At 24-29, it's 5 pts/tick w/o lute, 10 pts/tick w/ lute.
Plus 1 pt/tick for your normal regeneration rate standing
(2 pts/tick if sitting).

It gets upgraded by 1 pt/tick w/o lute, 2 pts/tick w/ lute
every 6 levels. So...

30-36: 6 pts/tick, 12 pts/tick
36-41: 7 pts/tick, 14 pts/tick
42-47: 8 pts/tick, 16 pts/tick

At some level your normal regeneration rate gets upgraded to 3
pts/tick (not quite sure when or if this is sitting), which
would put it at 19 pts/tick at L42. Sitting gives you an extra
1 pt/tick, for 20 pts/tick.
