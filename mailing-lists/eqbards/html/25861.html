I have tried doing this -- I've managed to make low-lag singing a bit easier, but missed notes in the middle of a social key are the great equalizer -- if you have more than one song keyed in a macro and you hit a missed note, you have to start the twist all over -- and you almost always lose two songs from one missed note

What I do:

Selos
/stopsong
/stopsong
/cast 4

each of my social hotkeys is set up this way. I have 8 genaric keys numbered 1-8 and then my regular keys: charm, selos, mana, etc. for my always memmed sets that are always in the same slots.

When you're in DL with 178 people in the zone and staging to kill Gore and running with selos feels like swimming in jello -- you'll see that socials are the wrong tactic to use for twisting songs. If anyone has found a better setup for social-hotkey song twisting, please post it here...
