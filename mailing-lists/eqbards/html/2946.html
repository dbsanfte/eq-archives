Greetings!

Misty Thicket is a great place to grow up in!

Rokenn Swiftsong hits all the high notes! Sound advice truly.

I would say though that getting Lysera's Locating song as soon as you can is
a good thing. And Selos and a drum - these have been my mainstay for many
levels. You can make lots of friends helping them find their temporarily
mis-located corpses - and even in a nice zone like misty - they can be very
paniced at the loss and you will be a great hero and friend for life! Never
charge for this duty - is my rule - donations can be accepted of course but
if you put a price on this service you may get asked to go to places that
you will wish you have never seen - eg: Runnyeye & Gorge of King Xorbb and
feel obligated to accept.
