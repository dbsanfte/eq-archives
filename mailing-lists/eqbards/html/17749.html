This is a well-known bug, being teleported into lava somewhere beneath a
lava pool (therefore not being able to surface). Why it hasn't been fixed
is beyond me, it would certainly take a load off the guides' time.

Another bug to watch out for (obviously not for a while for some) occurs
in Kedge. While fighting, if something gets knocked into the ceiling or a
wall, it will often teleport back to it's spawn point. Twice now I've
been the victim of a teleporting Swirlspine Guardian, who subsequently
started summoning people directly into Phinigel's lair. Not a fun thing,
by any means.
