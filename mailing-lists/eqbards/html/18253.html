This zone is simply amazing!! We have been going there off and on the last
few nights. We took 34 people last night on an expedition to see how far we
could go. The Black Reaver we pulled had four reincarnates before the Lord
spawned though. We got 3 Jade Reavers, a warrior only spear that just rocked
(sorry I suck at remembering stats) and a SK mace that is something like
19/25 +10 INT or something insane like that. From what I've heard the Reaver
can reincarnate up to 13 times before the Lord spawns, I was hoping for it,
that is a fun fun fight!!

After we spent some time clearing the yard trash, we moved up to the
building past the gazebos. We had a single rogue with us which was a good
thing, we had to gate him to Rivervale to train lockpick up to 200 because
the door to that building requires a 200 skill in picklock. Let me tell you
its scary as hell for 34 people to go into a room that only one person can
get us out of. We pulled into that room for a bit then moved forward. Our
puller found a transparent ramp so we all en masse went up there. We got
high enough on that ramp that falling off = death by falling damage before
we got to a house. Inside the house we got a 15 mob pull, we lost 2 people
but rezzed up and moved to the second house. At that point about half the
party thought it was too late and wanted to leave so we levitated off the
bridge down to the courtyard (major train action there) and fought our way
out. All in all that was probably the funnest thing I've ever done in EQ,
we had a great time!
