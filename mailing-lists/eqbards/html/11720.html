I haven't made a run on the castle yet but I have been able to go up
through the front door with invis. I think the best way to approach the
music room is to go invis up to the foyer. The first pull will be the two
gargoyles from the room. From there, the music room is through the hallway
between the gargoyle spots and behind the wall in ront of you, getting
through it going either left or right for a few paces.
Watch out for the will sappers in there, though. I think they can see
invis.

Greetings!
Yesterday I was allowed to tag along with the Esprit de Corps guild on Cazic
Thule server. We did an assault on Mistmoore castle. 4 groups of 30's to
low 40's (i am lvl 35) went in the secret way from the crypt up to the
bedroom. We cleared all the hallways and the bathrooom, then set up base in
the bedroom. Got the black silk gloves off the 10th Deathly Usher I looted.
Not as common as I was expecting.

Anyway, I had the gypsy musicians on track, but the groups were reluctant to
pull them. They said there were 5 hard mobs between us and the musicians.
I believe it. At one point our 4 groups were completely destroyed. The
spawns there are very deadly. We often were fighting 6-8 mobs. Perhaps
part of the problem was the groups learning to work together. Anyway, for
those who have yet to get the gypsy lute, be advised that this is no small
task.

On another note, I was able to get the Deep Water Goblin's Opaline helm on
the 3rd try. I knew nothing of these DW Goblins, so be warned that
attacking one will likely get you 5-6 plus their pets.
