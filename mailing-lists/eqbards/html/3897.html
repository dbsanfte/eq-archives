Ok, that;s enough space....
Our group has been finding Medium Bronze by the boat-load in Solusek B.
Oh, yeah, sure, it can be a nasty dungeon, but here's a few ideas.

1. We have found that medium bronze in Sol B is just as plentiful as Small
Bronze is in Unrest. I collected 5 pieces of Medium Bronze in less than 2
hours!

2. Who carries it? Just about any Kobold.

3. Sol B, right near the entry. Camp there with your group. Right around the
corner, start luring Kobolds, one at a time. A group of mid 20's with at
least once cleric SHOULD be able to handle a single kobold. If things get
hairy, you are RIGHT by the zone.

4. Whether they do or do not have bronze on them, repeat.

5. Season to taste. I just ended up giving 4 of those pieces away to fellow
bards who require medium armor. Who says you can't have Christmas in July?
