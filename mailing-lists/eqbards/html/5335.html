I guess this is a subject that will continue to pop up periodically as long
as this list is around. Once again, the level 3 song Jaxan's Vigor has
absolutely no impact on mana regeneration. This issue was answered by JM
Capozzi, a Verant employee, who used to post on this list. The song
apparently increases the screen refresh rate on the mana bar but the actual
amount of mana regenerated over time is always the same. All it does is
create the illusion that mana is regenerating faster.
