a duel-wielding dojorn is just nuts. this was my first trip there, so i was
learning. weird how his aggro works. we only had to deal with one
bladestorm and the noble. i think the other bladestorm got stuck the night
before when we tried. so i dunno what to do if both bladestorms are up.
after a couple failures we did come up with a good plan that worked. we had
about 5-6 groups up there. 1 group attacked the noble and the rest attacked
the BS. once the group who attacked the noble died, he unaggroed, and we
took out the BS a lot easier than with the noble tearing up everyone. after
we figured that out, we failed on the noble again. we had everyone wolfed
and shrunk and fought outside the windmill. but the pathing was too screwy
and a lot of people complained that they couldn't hit him while shrunk. the
last time we did it right. we did not shrink, everyone was wolfed and had
naltron and rune 3 (along with all shaman/cleric buffs, etc.) and we fought
inside the windmill. it kept him fairly contained, while outside he was
moving too much. and surpisingly 24 out of 36 people lived (i was SO close,
died when he was to the left of the N in Noble, barely any red left). was a
lot of fun, i am glad i stuck it out.
