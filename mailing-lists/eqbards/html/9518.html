I beginning to think there should be a level check on a critter when he dies
that his corpse disappears immediately if your level is more than a certain
amount greater than the mob. I was vehemently against this idea when it was
initially proposed by Verant. Its better than making everything 'no drop'
the way it is becoming now. This would chase away some of the higher levels
from lower level events however. I think cutting it off at the xp gaining
point would be too tight though, get 4 mobs on me that are just barely too
green to give xp and I may still be in trouble depending on the zone.
There's nothing I hate more than killing a new mob, only to find a cool item
for a monk or caster that is 'no drop'. Normally I could give it away or
sell it to a guildmember, making it 'no drop' just makes it useless. Quests
are becoming especially true for this. Do a quest and get an item you can't
use. If this is supposed to make us do quests that are for our class only
then it sucks even worse as there are many monk and paladin quests but
nearly no bard quests. 'No Drop' is no solution its merely a crutch used to
avoid a real solution.
