Word of advice - stick to the (right) wall. On the zone from WC - hug the
wall - and keep doing so. Be careful not to take falling damage -
especially around the RV zone. Get off the wall and run around it. Get
back on the wall and hug it. You'll see "stone" again. Get down - you'll
catch up with the trail and you can follow it a very short way to the HP
zone.

It's safer - and easier to keep your party from getting separated in the
dark and fog...
