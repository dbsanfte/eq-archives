Hatar was the 1st Sand Giant I and a group of other people MY level ever
killed. I was 20th I believe at the time. I had been in on other giant
kills, but with level 30 friends, so this was sort of a milestone. He did
have the Turban, but I dont remember it being non-visible. I thought it
showed up as a plate helm on the Troll who got it. Anyway Hatar is a fairly
rare spawn in Oasis cons white at 20th, and we had a really tough time
killing him with a full party of 18-20's. He hit as hard as a regular Giant
at the time. I dont think he could even hit me anymore at level 34. The odd
thing is he is the only Giant I have found who drops anything worth a rip.
