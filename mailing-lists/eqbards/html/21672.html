Mobs were blue to me at level 38-39. The warriors (sorry I forget the
names) were real easy. Just chant them to death. The casters were pretty
nasty though.

To get to the jail you must have invisibility. You enter it through the
secret door behind the crypt in the graveyard. Once you get in, you'll see
4 doors. There are also 2 other secret passages in this room. The wall to
the right has a passage that leads down and will be your safe healing area.
The 3 doors to your left are the cells. The 1 door straight ahead is the
switch to open the cells. To the left of this door is another secret
passage with stairs leading up to the tower. It is on this passage that the
avenging caitliff spawns (which is a very nice mob for both completing the
Wakizashi quest as well as for their loot (crested spaulers and the very
much sought crested helm).

At level 38-39 I could at times hold my own here, at other times I would
get so trashed. Same goes for the caitliff's placeholder. The casters are
the nasiest. I highly recommend a partner for this place. Doesn't matter
between tank or caster.

Also, the caitliff and place holder will wander into the jail, so I
recommend you kill them before clearing the jail. Just bring them down into
the safe area and kill them.
