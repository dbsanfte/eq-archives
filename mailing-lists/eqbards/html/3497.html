I am curious how do you try to join groups, is it simply by using /shout or
/ooc? You'll find that a LOT of players turn those channels off due to the
amount of spam and other inane chatter that goes on there...

Your best bet is to do /who 8 12 (assuming you are level 10) to list the
players within a couple levels of you - do /tells to them directly. Also -
to people nearby a /say is pretty effective.

I can only speak for the Xegony server - but I have only seen a few
instances of guilds that don't group with people outside of their group (not
uncommon however is people leaving an existing group if some group members
com online...)
