Broon is the named cyclops who spawns in the river north of the East karana
wood bridge. Kill him and his brother Droon re-spawns fast - so if you do
Broon - pull him out of the river other wise Droon will spawn right on top
of you.

Now if you don't attack Droon right away - he runs off to hide (disappears).
And then Broon spawns up between the HH ramp and the Beholder canyon.

If you do Broon (blue) and Droon (yellow) a couple times - papa Proon will
spawn and he's very red to me at 30.

Froon and Choon are the named cyclops that you can find in West Karana.
