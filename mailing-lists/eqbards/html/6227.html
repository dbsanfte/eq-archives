In a group these are all good spots:

Erud's crossing, and make a good amount of cash if you do from wisps. (bound
in Qeynos)

Lions in E. Karanas *yawn* or go after Gnoll Reavers. (bound in Highpass)

Mammoth Calves, orcs and Ice Skellies in Everfrost.(bound in halas)

Lizardmen in the temples of Feerot surrounding Cazic Thule Entrance, what a
blast, you will be sorry sorry sorry when you discover this place later and
realized you missed it. It is the perfect adventure at your level. (bound
in the Arena)

The Aviaks under the Aviak guards in Lake Rathetear, everyone is there for
the Aviak Guards and you shouldn't have to compete at all for the aviak
spawn. (bound in the Arena)

There is no good reason to be funneled into all the old boring heavily
camped spots in Norrath at your level, there is LOTS of adventure.
