I went thru Droga one night. We took 2 groups mixed from 51 down to lo-mid
30's. All green to 50's (iirc - maybe a few greenie exp's but essentially
all green). We worked it like a normal dungeon crawl and tried to move thru
looking for our 51 Wiz who wandered off sight seeing and fell thru a trap
either into the jail or near the jail. After 3 hours, we were kinda tired
and decided to gate out - never did find our wizard. Saw some neat places
and got one really nice thing for a druid (forget what it was staff or shawl
or something with wis+).

Droga is a huge maze. Get a map if you are so inclined or just explore it -
but it seems endless. It's one-way in (because you have to go down the
pit-trap and cant get out) and you need to find either zones to the mines of
Nurga or find the passages back to the entrance zone.
