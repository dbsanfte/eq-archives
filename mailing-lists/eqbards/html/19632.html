My guild plus an allied guild conducted a raid into Kaesora yesterday. All
told we hade 15 people there with me the lowest at 37th level. Raid had two
goals. One, to get as many coins as possible for the bards in my guild.
Second, to kill Xalboz, the boss mob who spawns in the church. Not everyone
got there at the same time so we marshalled at the library to wait for
everyone to arrive. All told we were there about an hour. During that time
it was nonstop pulling. The fallen crypt raider spawned once during this
time. Once everyone showed up, we moved to the lower parts of the dungeon
en masse, killing everything in our way. Once we broke the church area, two
groups remained at the bottom to take down Xalboz if he spawned. My group
moved back up to the library to harvesy coins. All told we were in Kaesora
for about six hours. Unfortunately Xalboz never spawned. However, the
fallen crypt raider spawned a total of five times. So every bard who
attended the raid got a coin. People who had been in Kaesora before were
shocked that the raider spawned so often. Guess the random number generator
god blessed us.

Like someone else mentioned in a previous post, Kaesora is a vastly
underused dungeon. We were there for six hours during peak hours on a
weekend. During that entire time, only two other people (both 50+ level)
entered into the zone. Not certain why there weren't more 30-45 level
people in the zone. Almost every mob in there conned blue to me at 37th
level so its a great place to hunt for my level. My guess is that the
server demographics for E'ci is top heavy. Probably not enough 30-45 level
folks around to cover all the dungeons designed for those levels.

Galtin of E'ci
