Subject: Wind-practicing spots


Personally, I have found many spots to practice my Wind and Brass skills. A
few choice locales that are easy to get to are:
1. Follow the right hand wall as you leave Qeynos, heading to QHills. As
soon as you zone into QHills you will be in a spot that no MOBs go anywhere
near. Make sure you have plenty of food and drink, equip the appropriate
instrument/song combo, and go see a movie while your character skills away.
2. As you leave HPH going into EK, follow the left wall to where it opens
up and allows you to go relatively far up the wall. No one and nothing EVER
goes there. Set the character up with the appropriate song/instrument
combo, and grab a good night's sleep.
As a good rule of thumb, any place in along a zone's edge where folks
usually don't go (for lack of MOBs to kill, merchants, etc) is a good place
:o)
