Either I misunderstood you here or you may be a bit
off. The scaler level bonus is (level-25)/3 (Note:
I'm not sure about post 50 but this holds true up to
50) for 1h weapons and is primary hand only (there is
a slightly different 2h damage scalar for 2h weapons).
I.e. at level 50 every time you hit with your main
weapon you do an additional 8 points of damage. The
easy way to test that this is primary hand only is to
DW two different types of weapons and log the results.
Look for your minimum hits not your maximum as that's
a more easily identifiable number. Your minimum hit
with primary hand will be 1+bonus and your min with
secondary hand will be 1.

-David
