I've used the song a lot, saved a *lot* of money, and never seen any
adverse reaction. The song actually casts an aura of enchantment on
yourself, attuned to a particular faction. You choose a target to set that
faction, and there's a glow on them as the magic touches them, but they're
apparently not enchanted in any way and don't react negatively.

The higher the price the more you save, into the many platinum of savings.
It also helps to load up with as many charisma enhancing items as you can
scrounge. I now have a silk evening shirt and a cats-eye amulet as my
shopping clothes and am watching out for appropriate earrings and other
items. They all make a difference that's real easy to test.
