Voodoo5 does apparently support 32-bit color but it still doesn't
support hardware T&L. Legacy DX6 games don't take advantage of hardware
T&L, and unfortunately that list includes EQ. However most new games,
and existing OpenGL games do benefit from the hardware T&L, putting the
Voodoo5 at a distinct disadvantage. It hardly seems worthwhile to throw
new money at old technology; 3dfx will find themselves not one but two
generations behind the competition.
