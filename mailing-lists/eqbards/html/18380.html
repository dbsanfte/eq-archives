LOL - Pina Colata? I think you mean Pinata ("Peen-ya-ta" -- Pin~ata - with
an "en-yay") but a good idea never the less. I grew up in the southwest in
the 60's (ugh i am really old!) and we always had a Pinata at birthday
parties. In case you don't know, it's a paper mache animal figure that is
filled with candy and suspended from a cord. Each child is blindfolded and
spun about just a bit - then pointed in the general direction of the pinata
to swing at it with a mini-baseball bat. Eventually someone makes contact
and it breaks open and scatters candy everywhere and all the kids scramble
for some. Maybe a druid in wolfform as the pinata and all the 'kids'
reeling from excess alcohol to simulate blindfolded - or some one who can
cast darkness is the pinata?
