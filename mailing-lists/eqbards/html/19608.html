At your level the best xp is to be found in the basement in mostly the pit
area and the ghost area. The bar room and the hand room will be very slow
xp for you at level 29. The tower is pretty good xp at your level except
for that fact that the number of spawns is lacking. There are only 3 or
so if the bar room is camped. In the pit there are about 5-6 that spawn
in the pit and about the same in the ghost room. Just note that the hags
in those areas are pretty brutal. 2 or 3 nukes would finish me of when I
was 28-29.
