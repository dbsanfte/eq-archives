I think most of you know this, but keep in mind that there are a lot of
situations in which where you stand when you are camping seens to directly
affect the spawn rates of the creatures you are camping for. I have seen
that if you stand to close to a spawn spot a monster often does not seem to
spawn for a long time. Also, I have a paladin friend who has been camping a
popular SK in befallen and when he leaves the room it spawns every few
mintutes, but if someone is standing in the room it spawns in it takes
significantly longer to reappear.
