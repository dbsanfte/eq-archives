Well, what sort of things are you killing? If you're in Sol B, yeah, you're going to get
resisted a ton. Chant of flame has 2 strikes against it. First, its a fire based dot, so it
checks a mobs fire resistance, and everything in the Soluseks has really a really high
resist to fire. Bellow and most of our other songs check a mobs magic resist, which
is generally lower. Secondly, Chant does damage *and* lowers fire resistance.
Dual effect songs are always more often resisted than single effect songs. You might
also notice this with Denons discord vs. Chords of Dissonance.
