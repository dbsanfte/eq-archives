There are 2 schools of thought:

Fighter/Bard: distribute points to Strength, Stamina and/or Agility. Most
people advocate Strength since it helps you hit harder and carry more loot.
Stamina helps you fight/run longer and influences the number of hit points
you start and gain per level. And Agility influences your AC and how much
you gain from each defense skill increase.

Bard/Fighter: distribute points to Charisma, Intelligence and/or Dexterity.
Charisma is rumored to help you when singing your songs so that they fizzle
less and are resisted less. It also gets you lower prices when buying and
higher prices when selling. Intelligence helps you learn skills faster.
This is the bard of most fantasy settings, Charismatic, Intelligent and
Dextrous.

For me, I hedged my bets a little. I have a Wood Elf Bard and I put most of
my points into Charisma bringing it to 100 and the rest into Strength
bringing it to 80.

Hope this helps a little,
