Subject: Adventure vacations levels 12-15 *spoilers*


I am finding areas are VERY class specific as far as being fun and profitable. I am posting some of
my discoveries as I find them to encourage people to explore them before they are too easy. I have
already posted about Fire Drakes in Lavastorm, Wisps in Eruds Crossing, Aqua Goblins in Ocean of
Tears and everything Kithicor (ALWAYS a good bet for mysterious adventure, there is a bizarre hidden
story in that woods, over on Crossroads of Norath/Discussions we have a little Kithicor fan club). I
am not including loot and only the most important survival instructions so it will still be an
adventure.

Feerot: (12-15): Get bound in the Arena, take a boat (do NOT swim) to the entrance to Rathe
Mountains (notice the gypsies you can sell your loot to when you get overloaded in the Feerot,all
the loot in this zone is HEAVY) and go from temple to temple outside Cazic-Thule. Hop inside to ask
if anyone wants to sell a Lizardscale cloak ("trash loot" on Rubicite carrying mob - AC 8, w 2.5, +5
dex 60-75pp) but be prepared to die if you do.

Aviak Village, Lake Rathe: 13-14: Get bound in the Arena, everyone there is waiting on the Aviak
Guards, and the other Aviaks are free for the taking. there is a shop to the south at which you can
sell.

Oasis(15+): Blue Camens and even blue crocs. Great exp but zero loot.

I will keep posting these places as I find them. And I would love to hear anyone elses discoveries
of areas that matched with their level well. I STILL have to go back to the Ocean of Tears and find
more, also I only quickly swung by the Barbarian Northlands and there is SO much to discover...
