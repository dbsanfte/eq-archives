I think the song selection is definitely dependent on the mob that you are
fighting and the situation. First and foremost I do not even bother to
memorize DD and COD AE damage songs. THose seem to be bad songs for
starting very large trains. Let me list the combinations based on
opponent. THis is for a level 24 bard, I 'll alternative swaps

Dry bone:
Hymn + Elemental Rhythms + Anthem

Skeletal Monk, Barbed boned, Carrion Ghoul, and other non caster:
Hymn + Bellow/Chains + Anthem

Hag:
Hymn + Guardian Rhythms + Anthem

Lullaby is thrown in if the majority of a bad pull con's even or below.
Make sure you have a cleric watching your health. I also have jig memed
for those extremely long fights. Trust me you and your buddies will love
it. Jig is played while resting because everyone thinks it boost mana
recovery (I don't agree but I will have to test it out, just wish we could
see the actual mana count).
