There is another way of getting on the rooftops that
doesn't include an exploit. I can assure you that if
a GM approaches you and asks how you got up there, and
you tell him either of these ways you won't get
banned or scolded.

You can either go via the way mentioned above, or you
can do it by jumping on the top of the door of the bank,
turning around, jumping on top of the rocks and then
onto the closest building. You can also do it via the
levitate spell in various areas of Freeport.

Regardless, I would nearly guarantee that if you were
to 'accidentally' fall of the world by being up there
you could count on the fact that there would be no
help from a GM or guide. But that's just my guess.
