I can pick up 50pp just in an hour or 2 in Lake Rathe, killing High Gnoll
Shamans and Embalmers. At night kill Dry Bones for small bronze plate and FS
weapons. I am palking about at your level too, this is stuff I did at 24-28.
There are ghouls in NK halfway between the stone and wood bridges that
consistently drop Bronze weapons, usually good for another 15-20pp. Bandits
on the river in SK drop the same. If your over 30th, Spectres in Feerot drop
Scythes worth from 4pp for Bronze to 13pp for regular, Opals, Bpearls, Boxes
of Abu Kar. These are just a couple of easy money makers. The thing is
making money is like working for a living, you have to approach it like a
job and make the effort if thats what you really want. After level 30 the
cash just happens with the mobs dropping 1-3pp each and FS weapons to sell.
You can always find a mob that drops a good item and farm it though.
