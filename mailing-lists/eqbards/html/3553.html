Subject: Kithicor annoyingness


Well, my alter-ego, a Gnome enchanter, was playing Mr. Postman on his way
to High Hold to buy a new spell, when he ran into the most interesting
situation. Being the naturally curious sort, he wondered what could
possibly be so important that it'd be locked in a cabin in the middle of
the Kithicor woods? So, after determining that the door was impassable
without a Rogue's assistance, he took a tour around the house. He noticed
a snake heading my way, so he stepped aside to let it pass, when suddenly,
he noticed it slide UNDER the house. How interesting, he thought to
himself, and crouched down to get a better look. Apparently the foundation
wasn't very solid, and had been a bit washed away in the omnipresent rains.
Crawling underneath the building, he poked around for a moment to see if
perhaps the chimney, which was still firmly attached to the earth, would
provide a possible entrypoint. Forgetting where he was for a moment, he
stood up to ponder, and suddenly realized that he was INSIDE! How could
that be? He checked for trapdoors or holes in the floor, to no avail.
Suddenly he realized the full enormity of the situation. The door closed,
locked, he on the inside meant only one thing: he was trapped. *sigh* Good
thing he's a spellcaster, and had Gate available. The bad news is, he was
heading for High Hold Pass, and was still bound to Steamfont. Sooooo, 4
hours later, he FINALLY made it to the Keep and bought his new spell. No
more wandering in Kithicor for him!
