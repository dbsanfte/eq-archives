I have a hercules dynamite TNT2 ultra, and I'm VERY happy with it. of the
tnt2 cards, i believe it's the most expensive, but it also has the highest
clock speeds. The picture quality is gorgeous, and everquest runs like a
dream.

The only "problem" is the lack of glide support, which isn't really an
issue with EQ (i tried playing with my voodoo2 in glide, and switched back
to the tnt2 because i liked it better), although it's preventing me from
playing Tribes (unless their opengl is finally ready). The days of
glide-only games seems to be over, so i'm not really too bothered by it.
