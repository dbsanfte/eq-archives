>Quest originates with a NPC merchant who hangs out _behind_ the druid guild
>hall in Kelethin (Soldiers of Tunare). The quest is pretty easy, if you're
>in your upper 20s, or have a group. He'll tell you that he needs the
>'plans' and 3 bars of enchanted gold and he'll make you the weapon. The
>gold bars are easy... usually plenty of enchanters willing to help out near
>Kelethin. The SG can be a pain in the arse - primarily due to his highly
>erratic spawn times (anywhere from many hours to minutes). The Kilij is a
>magic 1H slash, non-lore, Bard-Warrior-Rogue only weapon. It's 10 DMG and
>35 Delay. All in all, it's a good weapon - but it's very heavy, so if you
>use it in your primary hand, expect to take a big hit to stamina.
>
