My charm pallette has:

===========
||selos||--||sirens||
===========
||attk||--|| who ||
===========
||sense||--||track||
===========

attk is a social that is /pet attack
who is a social that is 3 lines:
/pet who leader
/pet back off
/time

The 'who' social covers 3 bases: tells group i have a pet ("My leader is
Sylly"), calms a pet that is frenzied on a group member, and shows me the
time of day for hunting in kithikor (actually I use this to get ToD - pet or
no pet.)

Selos is the secret to successful charming. Play selos all the time.
Selos, charm, then right back to selos. Remember that you can cast charm
while moving - use this power to your advantage. Avoid getting smooshed.

Sense is sense heading and track is tracking.
