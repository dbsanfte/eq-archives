My personal feeling is that Dexterity and Agility are two attributes
that are very often forgotten about but become EXTREMELY valuable during
the game and can help reduce frustration over character performance. One
determines the likelihood that your strike will hit, the other
determines the likelihood that you will avoid being struck. It really
doesn't matter how strong you are if you miss all the time, and it's far
better to avoid blows altogether than to worry about having enough HP to
withstand them, eh?
