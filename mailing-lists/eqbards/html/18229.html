I like helping an occasional beginning character,
especially if they are playing a bard ... but really
get tired of people begging. I was inspired by the
animated graphic on eqdruids to write this song:

"Twink this Beggar Newbie"

Oh twink this beggar newbie
The game is much more fun
When you've got a mithril two-han-der
And you're only level one

Hey twink this beggar newbie
It won't cost you much plat
He needs to have bronze armor
so he can kill that bat

Now twink this begger newbie
He doesn't want to fight
Just give him all your money
and log off for the night!

/e bows
