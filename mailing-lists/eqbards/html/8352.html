They're no drop to foil Lightstone hunters. Many people hunt wisps at night,
and once judged which wisps to kill by whether they had a glow or not. Now,
the wisps that used to have no lightstone at all have burned out lightstones,
so they all glow at night. They are NO DROP so that unscrupulous players
can't sell them to newbies (since they dissappear when you log, and are
worthless to vendors).
