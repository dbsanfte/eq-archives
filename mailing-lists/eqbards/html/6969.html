MORE FROM GEOFFREY ZATKIN
Here is some information you might find interesting.

Whilst there is no cap on the benefit of a statistic being over 200, the
extra benefit you get above this amount is very little. This means that if
you have 210 intelligence and only 90 dex, you would probably be better off
to go back to 200 int and have 100 dex.
It is not the case that you get no extra mana from having 230 int as
compared with 200 int, but it is a great deal less than you would get from
having an increase of say 170 int to 200 int.
