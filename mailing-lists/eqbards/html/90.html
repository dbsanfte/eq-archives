Ralborn:

I appreciate your concern, and I know that ten platinum is a hefty sum.
But, as I responded to you privately, the funds are not misused nor are
they squandered. Running a guild has its expenses, especially if we are to
provide collective services for our bards so that they might carry on their
work with honor and dignity.

As I mentioned to you before, lad, we provide the following services to our
members:
* Patchwork armor for all new students
* The first five levels of songs for all new students
* A hand drum at level 5
* A lute at level 8
* Bi-weekly rewards for the best in-guild ballad or writing. This week
it was a Banded mail tunic.

Also, we use the money to pay for other expenses, including:
* Staff for concerts
* Saving for a guild hall
* Prizes for other Soerbaird events

We do not use guild money for:
* Buying better armor or equipment for guild leadership
* Bribing news sites for better placement
* Paying necromancers to destory anyone who speaks negatively
about The Soerbaird

For the public record, all of our staff, called Ollave, have more than paid
their ten platinum, without a complaint, and most of them continue to
contribute into the guild treasury on a daily basis, in order to benefit
our students. I have seen several of them give the shirts off of their
backs and the weapons from their fists to help a deserved student in need.

Doerbaird, before you sally our guild's reputation again in a public forum,
I would respectfully request that you air your differences with me, as I
will be happy to hear any problem you have with our guild.

Listen and Live.

-- Jythri, Pencerdd of The Soerbaird
