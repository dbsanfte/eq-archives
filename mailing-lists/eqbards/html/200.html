I always keep both with me, because the drum is extremely useful with the
Accelerando, and the stringed instrument, of course, with heal.

The only difference that I know of between the lute and the mandolin is that
the mandolin is a little bit lighter, and of course has a completely different
musical tone.

>
> Lastly - I am always short of money. Most of my money goes to songs - my
> armor is pretty thin. Any suggestions for making money.


At your rank, one of the best things to do is to hunt orcs or goblins--they
frequently have weapons and/or armor that can be sold for a gold or more. Best
place to hunt goblins is probably in Misty Thicket near Rivervale, if you can
go there when it is not overcrowded. I've had lots of success there with money
making.
