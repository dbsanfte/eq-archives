Stats are generally not important. Many tests have been done and most
are inconclusive. The primary stats you should concentrate your
equipment on are AC and HP, then resists (although they are situation
dependent and bards can get by without them though that will obviously
negate using other songs you may need to be playing).

It is not a bad idea to get each of your "important" stats above 100
(Str, Dex, Agi, Sta, Cha). Str for ATK rating and to carry more, Dex is
supposedly (i.e. tests inconclusive or show very little benefit) linked
to missed notes on songs and increases proc rate on procing weapons, Agi
gets a huge AC boost at 75Agi and a moderate boost at 100Agi but above
100 it has diminishing returns, Sta gives you more HP (i.e. good) and
Sta over 100 means when your bar is empty you experience no adverse
effects whatsoever, Cha is supposedly (i.e. tests inconclusive or show
very little benefit) linked to lower resists on charm, mes, and lull
songs.

Absolutely feel free to post your equipment and money resources to
upgrade, it will definitely not be flooding the list hehe.
