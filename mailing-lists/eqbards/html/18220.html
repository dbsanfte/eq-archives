The giants also drop some funny weapons like a giant axe that is 45 dam, 150 delay, Orge, Bard, Troll
warrior only or a sword dam 10 del 42 I believe. The Brae giants are green at 45 up to the Patriarchs
and Steaps (sp?) which are blue at 51. The Brae are pretty easy and make up the bulk of the giants while
the Patriarchs hit DARN hard and really takes a good party to take out (we had 2 warriors levels 51 and
46, a 51 shaman with the new slow spell which works AWESOME, a 45 druid, a 42 rogue and me and we still
had both warriors under 2 bubbles of health when we got the AC25 tunic).

Of course the REAL good stuff drops on the Pats, but the steaps and brae drop the weapons and tunics that
you would want to sell to the vendors.
