You can damage them with your songs, and at level 10 chords plus punching
with magical gloves will do fairly good damage. By twisting Chords and Hymn
you could probably beat some of the lower level wisps fairly easily with a
lute. For easy magical gloves I would suggest running to Lower guk, scary
but if you are not agnostic and have not killed any frogloks you will be
ignored and can wait at the zone line for someone to get some near there
safely. Another possibility is woven/mesh/netted gloves. All are magical
and cheap now that monks have Gauntlet of Wu to acquire.
