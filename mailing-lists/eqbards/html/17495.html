If you're in Freeport, head out to the dervish camp in
West Commons. They spawn fast enough to support two
groups and drop bronze weapons. (And they aggro bards
more than the crushbone orcs ever did)

While you're there, see the armoror on the thrid floor
of the Freeport militia bldg and do the Reserve
Militia Tunic quest. They are only AC 9 but +3 dex
and look very cool. Like blood red chain. I had a DE
SK offer me 50 plat for mine the other night in
Lavastorm. Too bad it's no drop;-)

While you're in the commons, watch out for people
asking for help in body recovery -- most of them want
you to go to Kithicor forest...and it seems like most
of them want to go just before nightfall. If that's
the case, make them wait for morning.
