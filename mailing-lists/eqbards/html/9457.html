I am an expert with Sylly questions <g> perhaps I can help!

For each instrument skill, you need to put one point into training for it.
So, when you make a level where you get a new skill, go to bard guild and
train 1 point into the new skill. Then you can equip the instrument and use
it with songs the either require instrument or are enhanced by instrument.

Mino horn is wind only - old rumor said it was brass too but that is not
true.

Wind is one skill - flute and mino horns are wind instruments. Charisma and
the Aquatic Aria and our invisible song (level 20) are wind (invis requires
wind so I bind in the bank and keep a spare mino horn or flute in the vault
if I need to make a run to my corpse and want invis - spare drum too <g>)

Brass is another (different) skill - brass horns (trumpets) are brass
instrument. Denon's Dischord and Screetch are brass songs.
