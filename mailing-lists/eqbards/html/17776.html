Don't *ASK* if you can leave the chessboard. Program
a social to the referee so you send him/her a tell and
they know you aren't leaving the zone. I say a button
because you may be busy as you run...

It is easier to ask forgiveness than to get
permission ... especially when playing a widely
misunderstood class;-)

Oh yeah...this magican starts the duel WITHOUT the pet
and summon after the fight begins...pretty much the
convention but your opponent may try to argue
otherwise. Pets are somewhat variable in level and
you don't want him showing up with something that cons
yellow to him.

best of luck...
