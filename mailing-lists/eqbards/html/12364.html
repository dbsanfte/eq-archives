All the bards I know are good people. It's odd. Bards really
stick together more so than any other class I've seen. It could
be the friendly group mentality we have. A bard is truly a
different beast.

I noticed that a L50 cleric was trying to powerlevel a bard
the other day. Needless to say, I've seen very few bards
powerleveled. It's not an easy thing to do. Most powergamers
cannot stand being so weak, because in their mind if you cannot
heal, melee, or nuke then you are no good. The inherent bard
trait is to group, and to powergame you cannot do such.
Therefore you build this community of very intelligent, patient,
fun, and likable people. I've never seen two bards really hate
each other like I've seen wizards hate wizards, and druids hate
druids.

I love bards.
