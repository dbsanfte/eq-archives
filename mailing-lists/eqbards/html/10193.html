L29 enchanter Clarity was about a 30% reduction in med
time. L32 bard Clarity was about a 33% reduction in med
time. L40 bard Clarity was about a 38% reduction in med
time. These figures are probably +/- 3% or so due to
inaccuracies in eyeballing the mana bar, but it really seems
like a straight level% conversion.

I have yet to time the spells/songs stacking, but I have yet
to see any anecdotal claims that they *don't* stack. All the
anecdotes I've seen people give say one enchanter Clarity plus
as many bard clarities as you like will all stack.
