As for a pet assist hot key, here is my new one:

/assist <primary assist name>
/pet back off
/pause 15
/pet attack

You could easily add a "/g " message if you want there, but everyone I have
played with notices the pet's response to the /pet back off command. I'm
not sure if I could reduce the pause 15, but you definitly need a pause in
there.

It seemed to me that when I started charming higher level mobs, espeically
in Karnors, more often than not I needed to back them off party members.
Thus I added the /pet back off to my "pet attack" hotkey.
