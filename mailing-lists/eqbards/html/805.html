The best place that I have found for soloing at level 12 is West and North
Karanas. Pincer Beetles should be possible for you at level 12. But, if you
can team up with one good tank and one cleric, you can easily make North
Karanas your home for levels 11 through 14. Excellent for exp, but lousy for
loot. BTW, the beetles are much less trouble for the experience than Griffawns
(baby griffs).
