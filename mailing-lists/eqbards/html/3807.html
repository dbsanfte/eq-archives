Hail Fellow Bards,

[re: successful leveling]
I advise all bards overly concerned with the best weapons and damage dealt,
to review the gifts that you have been given. Nothing sickens me more than
seeing a forceful party in danger, while the bard is selfishly fighting.
SHAME ON THAT BARD! If you the bard wants to utilize your talents most
efficiently, group with 2 warriors (or a monk), 2 rogues, and 1 party member
that can do emergency heals. Stand back and play your heal with your lute,
twist whatever other song fits the occasion, and you can fight for hours and
hours, gain "circles of knowledge" quite quickly. A healthy group prevails,
so if you want to succeed and learn quickly, this is your best source. Use
your combat skills when soloing, or with weak groups, BUT always remember in
large groups, you ARE NOT a tank or a fighter, you are a talented musician.
