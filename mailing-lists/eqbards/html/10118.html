Sol A and Permafrost aren't dungeons you go to to find pickup groups,
IMO. First, anything interesting is considerably farther in past the
zone line, and secondly because of that, you need a group that knows how
to work together. Snaring is mandatory, as is a puller who knows what
the heck they are doing.

At the same time, they are 2 of the most fun dungeons in the game, again
IMO. Especially on older servers where Lower Guk is so camped you have
to book an appointment for a spawn spot, these 2 dungeons are probably
the only places where you will be able to explore dungeons the way I
think Verant really had in mind when designing the game. (assuming you
resist the temptation to go to a spoiler site for maps and mob
locations)

I missed these 2 the first time around, mainly because neither were
itemized until after they were way green to my character, but I've had a
great time in them with my new character, and am a bit sad now at 37
that they are getting too green for me.
