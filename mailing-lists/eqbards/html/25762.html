Aside from my epic, this was the most memorable quest I've done (even better than my lambant bracer quest where I had to kite 15 griffines before a charm dropped -- I bought my second bracer P). A power gamer is better off just buying a walrus skin drum but if you do quests, this is deffinately one not to miss out on.
These were actually the toughest coins for me to get. I went to Dalnirs and suffered many deaths (mostly because of bad pick up groups but I did meet some excellent players as well). I went to Kaesora several times with guildmates and never saw a failed crypt raider.
I paid 10pp for my Dalnir coin and 200pp for my Kaesora coin (I cringed but it was worth it). I made four trips into Kaesora and never saw a failed crypt raider. Both dungeons can be a tough place to hunt. I recommend getting your guild involved as it's good to have friends around places like these.
