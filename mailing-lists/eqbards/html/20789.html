> Singing Blues
>
> If you have more than one bard in the group (which I have had
> a bunch) can both bards uses Chords of Discord and damage the
> same mobs? Or are these songs limited like regular spells
> appear to be? [I'm pretty sure Hymn of Restoration does not
> heal faster, but that too is a question; any chance for a
> chorus to be more effective than a soloist?]
>
> GZ ANSWERS
>
> Most Bard songs do not stack on top of each other - once you
> have a song being sung to you, having more than one person
> singing the song does not give any further effect. With the
> way EQ Bardic magic was built, Bards are more soloists then
> chorus members. Any song with a duration (that leaves an
> icon) will not stack - so no, having two Bards using the Hymn
> of Restoration will not make you heal faster. Having two
> bards using Chords could be more effective then a single Bard
> singing it - monsters do occasionally save verses the song.
> It would probably be better for the multiple Bard party,
> however, to sing different songs.
>
> This being said, the following is a list of Bard songs that
> do not have a duration (they leave no icon), and can
> therefore be played by any number of Bards at full effect.
>
> a.. Alenia`s Disenchanting Melody
> b.. Brusco`s Boastful Bellow
> c.. Brusco`s Bombastic Bellow
> d.. Cassindra`s Chorus of Clarity
> e.. Denon`s Desperate Dirge
> f.. Denon`s Dissension
> g.. Lyssa`s Cataloging Libretto
> h.. Lyssa`s Locating Lyric
> i.. Melanie`s Mellifluous Motion
> j.. Song of Dawn
> k.. Song of Highsun
> l.. Syvelian`s Anti-Magic Aria
