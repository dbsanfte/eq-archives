Subject: Siren's Grotto


Pulled a guild-grind in the Siren's Grotto over the weekend. 3 groups of 49+ camped near the waterfall for an afternoon of non-stop pulling. Many, many conch shell horns and immaculent conch shells dropped...and a walrus skin drum.
lots of other stuff dropped too, including gems and a Wavecrasher (I lost the lotto for it but at least I got the drum).
Some advice for bards headed that way --
Psalm of warmth, pixie strike against watery/icey servants who cast drake-type AOE attacks
Enticing sirens swarm bards who use charm (but it also may be because I was the lowest in my group)
Our master looter was dropping runed shell necklaces on the ground along with non-quest gems. Runed shell necklaces have five charges of enduring breath on them and sell to vendors for 11pp and change. I picked up a fair amount of trash that day and sang all the way to the bank ;-)
