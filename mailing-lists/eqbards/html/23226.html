From the entrance (closest to Thurgadin) go right, right, left (at the junctions). It's a large cave with 4 crystallines, and sometimes a matriarch (she spawns between the back two mobs, and has a long spawn rate). Since the wurms spawn on the edges of the room, we just cycle through them (start on right, and go counter-clockwise). The middle of the room is safe, so you can pull there. If you look at the map at http://www.eqatlas.com/velious/greatdividemap.html the cave I'm talking about is the largest one on the map (approx /loc neg4400, neg1100).

BTW, we've been hunting here with some level 54s, and the wurms are still blue to them.

On the rarity....we cleaned the room once last night cause the Matriarch was up....a normal wurm dropped 1 spell, and Matty dropped 3 spells (Ms2, Spirit Quickening, and Melody of Ervaj). On any given night, we'll kill in the room for up to 4 hours (it can be boring), and see between 3 and 7 spells drop.

BTW, if you are on Tarew Marr, and are interested in buying Melody of Ervaj, I think our cleric or shaman have copies....give me a tell tonight and I'll find out for ya.
