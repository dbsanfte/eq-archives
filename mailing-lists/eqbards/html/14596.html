Seriously, do what I do when you see posts that disturb you: delete them.

Right now, you have a lot of monsters to fight that are close to your level.
Deal with them now. Play the game 'one level at a time' and enjoy. There
is nothing to be gained by worrying about stuff that is far away and
unknown. The dragon's you fight will be different from the ones that we
fight today just as they are different from the first ones that were fought
on all of the servers. By the time you get there, there will be new
weapons, new spells and a history of tips, tricks, and tales from those who
have gone before.

Verant _knows_ that they are in the entertainment business. My take is that
they _will_ keep "raising the bar" on certain zones/monsters to prevent them
from being "farmed" and provide challenge to the high levels. Sometimes
they may "road block" a zone or mob for one reason or another. Sometimes
they will make mistakes. But any time there is a change in the status quo -
you will hear complaints.
