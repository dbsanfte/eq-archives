The Cauldron is a bad idea for a Level 10. It is a bad idea for m at Level
15 unless I have a pretty good group.

Don't buy ringmail . . . buy banded armor. Much better, and probably
cheaper depending on the smith you get it from. As far as I know, makes
your character look exactly the same. I started buying my banded set when I
was Level 10 . . . completed it at Level 14 to the tune of about 120 plat.

Personally I made levels 10 and 11 easily in Blackburrow. Just kill all the
Gnolls, Patrolling Gnolls, and Giant Snakes you can handle. The place is
train central, but since it is "outdoors" you can outrun most trains . . .
or you can group and kill guards and elites. I made about 5 plat per trip.
