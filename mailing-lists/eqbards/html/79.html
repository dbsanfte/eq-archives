Subject: What's the Point?


Greetings. I'm Thrush of Freeport in the Veeshan aspect.

I find anomalies and frustrations in our chosen way of magic and seek
instruction from those more learned than myself.

It seems that when I play the drum those watching see me as if I were
strumming a flute and rather than the drum I hear the sweet sounds of
strings. Perhaps some perverse enchanter has cast a twisted illusion upon
us. Does anyone know if perchance the great powers of magic may some day
set this straight?

On another topic, recently I attained the 7th circle of skill and after
saving and even sacrificing a bit of personal jewelery was able to purchase
the scroll for the Whistling Warsong of the 7th circle. Imagine my dismay
when what I thought was newfound power had less effect though concentrated
on me than the old 1st circle song with which we all start has on me and
all others in my adventuring group. Can anyone encourage me that perhaps
some day my investment in the new song will be of some value?

Likewise, I find the Jig o' Vigor to be of no use. Have I missed some good
and valued application?

I look forward to hearing from you. May sweet harmony accompany your path
and Erolissi fill your life with love.

Thrush
