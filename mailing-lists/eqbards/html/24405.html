We get a new song at every level. Certain levels get two songs. At 27 your
life as a bard will completely change. No more annoying KSers. And you will
come to learn what few bards realize we can do. Charm Kite. This is
basically the strategy of charming MOB A to attack MOB B, repeat until they
both get down in health, recharm the one with the most health to finish the
other and then get xp for the kill. Repeat over and over again till you get
tired of soloing and raking in a nasty amount of XP. You could also try
charming that annoying PKer and send him in to attack a mid level NPC (ie
guards are always fun). That should wittle him down a bit.

IMHO bards basically fill in where needed. We are the cement that holds it
all together. Healing and restoring mana where needed. Buffing at times.
Cham taunting mobs off casters. Intermitent crowd control. Use your
imagination because bards will basically fill in any hole in a party.
