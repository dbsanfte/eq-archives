Greets!

While I cant give you specific tips on where in Guk to use AE songs or not
here are some observations of mine about AE songs in general.

Do use them if you are in a heavily camped spot. Unrest barroom and knight
room is also camped. Even if you hit the other mobs - if they are camped,
you wont pull them.

Do not use them if you are on train tracks or have lots of wandering mobs.
Unrest yard and lots of death beetle traffic. Also beware of z-axis "bugs"
- Aviac treehouse - if you use ae in top floor of tree house - birds on the
ground will attack you. If you use them underneath the trees, you will pull
all the birds out of the trees above you.

Do use them when you need them! For a long time I was scared of them for
fear of pulling too many mobs. But sometimes you just gotta use what you
have. A few too many kobalds last night and I played lullaby with a lute.
We managed to survive. When it got down to 3 doggies, I switched to
mezmerise. But with multiple mobs, you gotta use multi-mob crowd control.
