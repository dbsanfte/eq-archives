About 100 to 200 game yards past the ruins in the Lake
of Ill Omen coming from FV is an excellent spot.

On the far side of the mountains (left with the lake
on your right)is a ruined tower full of icebones and
assorted other fun stuff...not too hard to pull from.
In the low 20s stay on the lake side of the mountains,
then move to the tower. Lots of wandering fun stuff
on the tower side of the mountain so watch the AOE
songs...
