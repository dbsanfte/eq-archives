I would guess we should be using the Queen Klicknack as a clue. To get her
to spawn you have to kill both the Prince and the Princess and while they
are both dead she may (10% maybe) spawn. Yet the Queen is seen all the time
because the Prince and Princess are easily killed and give decent xp when
other Klicknacks are too green. There is no reason to think this level of
symbiosis in the spawning process stops at higher levels, it just becomes
more subtle. No longer do the mob names give away what you have to kill to
spawn a particular mob, but chances are if you keep killing things
eventually some of them may be figured out. I killed the Prince and
Princess hundreds of times at lower levels to get the Queen to spawn before
noting that she never spawned if I just kill Princes, or just killed
Princesses, but only if I continuously killed both. All this to get an
warrior friend who decided to become a jeweler some enchanted silver. If
Clerics would start looking for these triggers instead of just complaining
I'm willing to bet at least in some of the cases they will find them.
Course complaining is much easier I will be the first to admit.

Kitasi
