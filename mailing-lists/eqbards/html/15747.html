Congratulations on level 30! It's not long now until 32 when you'll become
every caster's friend. ;-)

It's excellent that you explored some of Lower Guk, it's a fun place,
however, I'd like to offer a friendly word of advice. Go elsewhere until
you get 35th or so (Cazic-Thule, Rathe Mts., Mistmoore, Paw), because from
35th up about the only places you can go routinely for experience are Lower
Guk, Sol B and occassionally Mistmoore and Paw. Believe me when I say that
you will get VERY VERY tired of these zones, and the earlier you start going
there the sooner you get tired of them.

As to Mistmoore, I do have some tips for getting drums. I don't have time
to type it up and the moment (cause I'm damn longwinded as we all know), but
I will do so today and post what I know. That's an interesting camp.
