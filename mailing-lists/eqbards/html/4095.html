> Where is Infected Paw?

Zone from North Karana into South Karana by crossing the stone
bridge. When you finish zoning, DO NOT touch the mouse or
left/right arrow keys. Hit auto-run to run straight forward.
After a minute, you will run right over the entrance to Paw.
It is in the middle of three (four?) spires on hills which
from an arial view are the claws of a paw. Then entrance is a
hole in the middle.
