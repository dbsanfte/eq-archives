Try Paw. It's usually pretty uncrowded, tho it's been increasingly more and more crowded lately.. : /

You can pick up, hopefully, some Runed Totem Staffs and some Snakeskin armor, as well as other loot there. The RTS' I've seen sold for 25 - 30 pp and the snakeskin gloves and facemasks are hot commodity items at 5pp each.

I'm at 15, my gf is a 15 mage, and we'd usually try to pick up 1 other player level 14 - 16 to join us near Rexx's room. It's a maze in there so try to find someone who knows the area. : ) Fight Commanders, Nobles, and the Named NPC Shaman for the loot I mentioned.
