Miriana hits the nail right on the head - having a solid guild makes EQ fun
again because many of the details are non-issues: loot, trust, skill,
communication and leadership. Be every so wary of the "need 5 more people
to form guild, come to chat...", and especially be wary of joining a guild
because of a name. Choose your guild based on how it meshes with your play
style and most importantly, with your "EQ attitude"! =)

-Tork@Nameless
