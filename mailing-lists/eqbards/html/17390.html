Rogue 1hs caps at 200
Rogue pierce caps at 210
That's 2 levels of effectiveness better with a piercer
in your offhand than a slasher. Also an EBW is more
effective in the main hand than offhand (from the
static level-based bonus) hence it makes more sense to
me for a warrior, ranger, bard to get it before a
Rogue. Alternative example...your group loots a
Sharkbone Warhammer from kedge (8/26 1hb +5wis very
light weight) and the monk wants in on the random with
the cleric/druid/shamans...sure he can use it but its
worse than his hands by that level and just better
suited in a wis caster's hands.
