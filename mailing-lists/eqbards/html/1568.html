I would recommend sticking to the walls. It is unlikely anything will
attack you in these zones at the walls. Go to the mountain - and go up as
high as you can. When you get to East Commons go to the right until you hit
the mountain. Run along the top of the mountain. Zone to W Commons. I've
never been attacked by griffins, NPCs or Sand Giants on the North wall in W.
Commons (but the Sand Giant do hang out on the Desert wall)

Continue running along the "right wall" in West Commons until you get to a
canyon. Go into the canyon and Zone. Do the same in Kiticor - run along
wall - but after a bit - don't stay up top - you can get fall damage and you
can miss the Rivervale Zone.
