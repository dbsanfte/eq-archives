The belt is indeed the same speed as the FBSS. With FBSS you'll get the same results with all your tests
as you did with the BoC. The belt has no "special" ability which puts you at 75%. There is a CAP on weapon
speed. It is roughly 55% of the original weapon speed. Meaning if you stack just about *any* attack speed
buff on a FBSS, BoC, Swirlspine, CoF, RBB, etc it will always cap out at that 55% of the original weapon
speed.
Not sure if you noticed, or if its even practiced on your server, but enchanters no long use swift like
the wind for this reason. Its just a bigger waste of mana, so they use Augment instead. Its a lower level
spell, costs less mana, adds less speed than SLTW, adds AGI and AC.
I'm not sure how you came to the calculation of 75% speedup. You have listed that all the delays are
capped at 20 with Item + Bard songs. (Also note monks were complaining of having this delay cap on them,
thus innate monk haste went in soon after).
20 (maximum HTH delay)/36 (original delay) = 0.55, 55%.
