Didn't see any interesting loot or named mobs.

Me (level 40 bard) and a level 45 enchanter went there, explored the whole
tower from top to bottom for about 40 minutes or so.

When you get to the first floor, where the badgers are (when you enter the
tower, you're on the 2nd floor), there is a dropoff that you can take to
more badgers. In this basement there is alot of water and tunels. It
seemed that the badgers couldn't swim, so if you found an isolated island,
the badgers couldn't run away. (forgot to load up Chains).

Somewhere in the basement is a teleporter that takes you back to Field of
Bones, by the entrance to the tower. We never did find a way back up from
the basement to the first floor.

If you have a necro or SK with you, they'll love all the bone chips you can
collect in there.
