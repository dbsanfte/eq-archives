Once you start dual wielding you will notice stamina begin to play a role in
your longer battles. Lighter weapons mean less stamina loss. Remember you
can always switch weapons mid battle...so If I were you I'd probably dual
wield the PGT and the whip with the dirk in the ranged weapon slot. If the
battle starts to get long and your stamina is dropping...then swith the
tomohawk for the dirk.

other night...curious to know if I got a good deal (not that money is a
problem after level 17).
