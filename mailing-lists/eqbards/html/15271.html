Subject: Did some testing....


The Gypsy Lute (the one for the Lambent BP quest) gives +5% to songs in
addition to +5% MR :)

There is an interaction bug with instruments. If you have an instrument
equipped and pickup in your mouse pointer another instrument of the same
type (ie string, wind, brass,percussion) you will lose the instrument bonus.
if you pickup an instrument of the a different type (say Lute equipped,
horn on mouse pointer) you get the full instrument effect. Having an
instrument in your inventory of the same type has no effect on the song you
are playing (ie no fire beetle eye/lightstone effect).
