I have always been content with the "power" of the bard class in relation to
the other classes. However, since Verant seems to be amenable to improving
the power of the hybrids, why shouldn't we try to make our class better than
it already is? In the last patch, all of the hybrid disciplines got
improved so no issues there. However, all the other hybrids just recieved
an upgrade (new 50th level spells) to their abilities to close the gap in
"power" between hybrids and non-hybrids. Bards did not get a similar
upgrade. Perhaps Verant didn't think we needed it? Who knows?

My proposal on haste stacking was based on the idea that it would be a way
for Verant to increase the power of the bard class without adding new songs,
raising skill caps, etc. IMO trying to get a new 50th level song or getting
skill caps raised would have little to no chance of ever getting approved.
Getting our haste songs to stack however might have a better chance. We
won't know unless we request it.

If one of the options I listed were actually implemented, it would make
bards even more group-friendly than they already are. Even if you think we
are prefectly balanced as is, would any of you NOT want that to happen?
