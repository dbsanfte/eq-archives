Dalnir's is easy to pull...

here's the trick...

use a flute/mino horn/what have you, to lull with (don't know if it really
helps, but seems to)
oh, and my unbuffed cha is about 108, buffed i sit between 116 and 148,
depending on buff/who/level/etc.

now that you have your targets (up to 3, sometimes 4) lulled, target the
nearest one, and charm it. do a /pet guard me and stand there, until it
decides to wander over. be ready to be beat on a bit. shuffle down the
hall, recharm, rinse/repeat. warn your group that it will be a pet when it
shows up, so don't start nuking until it decides it wants to turn you into
blueberry soup.

Charming a pet, and not getting too far ahead of it keeps it off the 'long
way around' path where it can collect friends to come make you unhappy.

If you get a resist, well, IF you are really lucky, and you are in a 'safe'
pull spot (i know, there aren't many, but you got there without attracting
too much attention), fire a charm off. AS SOON AS IT LANDS, sit your butt
down, and camp. You have 18 seconds leeway before charm breaks (hopefully),
and 12 seconds for the mobs to run around and come agro on you. With the
pathing in Dalnir, you can make it maybe 1/3 of the time, especially if they
decide to take the really long way around.
Second option: curl up in a ball and wait to die. Don't mob your group!
They'll be much more likely to have you around than if you train them.
