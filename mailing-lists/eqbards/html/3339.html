Pretty frequently actually. Each undead camp seems to have
several individual spawn "spots." Each "spot" can spawn
anywhere from 1 to 7 (most I've seen) undead, always of the
same type. The druid stones in North Ro operate the same way,
though you may be right - I don't recall ever seeing ghouls
spawn there in anything but groups of two.

There are wisps in Kithicor too - very rare but occasionally
you will see them. The place actually reminds me a lot of
Lesser Faydark, but with a lot more fog. LFay also has orc
and undead camps, and a ranger(?) outpost, as well as the
faerie and brownie villages. It is also a zone well worth
exploring from L8 on up if you are into avoiding crowds.
