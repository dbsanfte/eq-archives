Greetings all,
What I do when I get bored is change to Vallon Zek, a pvp teams server. I
am experimenting with a few different classes, but I have 2 bards at level 5
(started half-elf bard in Kelethin <only place to start a half elf bard
there>). I have 2 bards because I started a half elf bard first on the first
week of release and couldn't get any Medium size armour in Kelethin. So
that bard tweaked (hehe a level 5 tweaking a level 1) my Wood elf bard and
she leveled up quickly.

The point is if some folks find that they get bored on their regular server,
doing the same old same old, then try a pvp server. I have decided to try
to make an Troll Shadow Knight now. His mission will be to kill casters
(most casters are Human and Elf based). He will be a sort of anti-PK pk'er.
His MO will be to shadow step and hunt down those medding casters :)

Ok, ok....that is just an idea based on frustration, but it does add a whole
new dimension to the game. If you have friends or guildmates that you could
convince to meet you with you on a pvp server, then you could watch each
other's backs, and hunt PC's and NPC's alike. And be hunted :)

I am now level 40 on Cazic Thule server, and quite sick of waiting lists and
the like. I also have a hard time with the Hill Giants, because I get them
fighting and some group or other will move in and kill em off for me :( I
just want a short diversion. Nothing permanent.
