A well-played bard is ALWAYS useful to a group. At 5th an excellent choice
for weaving is Chant of Battle and Chords of Dissounce. Chant will boast
the parties AC, STR, and DEX. Chords will damage all the critters you are
fighting. Just besure to use the 3rd person view to stop chords if an
unwanted guest (like a wandering guard) happens by. At 6th you will have
hymn of restoration which will make your cleric and tanks love you even
more.
