Subject: Song Stacking


I put a post on the Concert Hall boards which shows stacking rules for
most of our party-based songs up to my level (57). For each song, it
lists all the songs that a) it overwrites, b) are overwritten by it, and
c) mutually block each other. There are also charts at the end which
show the rules for the two major song groups (attack and defense). This
is useful for me just building a single song list, but is even more
useful if you're stuck working with another Bard. I'm not reposting it
here because it's pretty long and it doesn't format well on this board,
but if you would like to check it out, see the thread:

http://pub5.ezboard.com/ftheconcerthallbackstage.showMessage?topicID=8358.topic

One thing I'd like to note even if you don't want to look is that Melody
of Ervaj (level 50 stackable haste) overwrites most of our resist
songs. They can't be sung if MoE is up, and MoE will just replace the
icon. Just an FYI.
