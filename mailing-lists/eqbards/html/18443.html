I used the combo quite effectively to get me through Hell 1 in the gobbie
mines in Ill Omen. Just Chain 'em, then Screech, rinse, and repeat. Also,
if you pull more than one MOB, make *sure* you get the Chain to stick, then
hit Screech, and watch the other critters run off, leaving you alone with
your victim (and with the way things aggro in Ill Omen, if you fear the
group off, then move backwards a few yards, that should effectively put you
out of the rest of the groups' aggro range so you can kill them one by
one... oh yeah, when healing, don't sit down unless you want them to 'port
right on top of you). Keep in mind, this is not an exact science, as
sometimes the whole group, or portions may come back, but if you do it
enough, it will work most of the time.
Also, Screech is AE, so be careful that you don;t get any other MOBs
caught in it (learned that lil lesson while fighting Icy Orcs in Perma...
damn that Iceberg :oP )
