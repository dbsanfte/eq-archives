Dont bother with chant right now.

1. Since its your current level song, resist rates will be insanely high.

2. You have no other good percussion song (unless you are playing chant of
battle) to mix with it.

3. Chords does more damage with a mandolin and you can use the mandolin for
big heal too.

You need to adjust your songs to your group.

Use anthem if your group does not have an enchanter. Use Chant of Battle if
they do (and the enchanter is hasting)

Using Binding if your group does not have anyone doing slowdowns (shaman or
enchanter)

If your group is light on healing, go to all instrument and play heal,
chords, and XXXXX (take your pick: anthem, denons, binding, psalm...).

As bards we have to adapt to the group. Most people believe their class
should be played one way. No matter what, and won't change. Since we tend
to be worse at whatever they are doing, then it is our job to adapt to that
and change.
