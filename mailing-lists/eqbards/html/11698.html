Cazic-Thule. If you're 28th through 38th-ish I implore you to try
Cazic-Thule. I've said it before: There's a learning curve to the place,
but if you can get into a competent and reasonably balanced group in the
throne room, Archon temple top or alligators at the green and blue pools you
will be amazed at the experience and loot. If you're unfamiliar with CT and
need a hand figuring the place out *please* just ask. I cannot stress how
well I did in this place from 28th-37th. I made TONS of experience: A full
level one Saturday at 31st and, and all of 30th in three evenings. All the
while I was staggering under the weight of fine steel weapons and 1-4pp
drops.

Two nights ago I was hunting Spectres in Feerrott and was in a fair amount
of danger for pretty minimal exp. My group was complaining about this so I
said "Hey, Cazic-Thule is right here, let's go in there." To which two of
my members replied along the lines of "Oh, I don't know Cazic well, only
been in there once or twice." so under my urging we went there and took the
upper throne room. My group was utterly amazed. I got one and a half
bubbles exp. in the two or three hours that we were there before I logged,
and I'm 37th! My group *did* benefit from my 9 levels of experience and
Lament though (I was pulling).
