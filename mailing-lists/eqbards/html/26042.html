The location of all spells/songs is on http://eq.crgaming.com/ under the
spells link.

You will find all the luclin bard spells in Katta Castellum...the loc is: 640
-330 and the name of the vendor is "Sellar Mulletune"

It's the lamest of all songs, though, completely useless to you. The ONLY
thing it does is let a non-magical weapon hit monsters which can only be hit
by magic weapons. Since even decaying skeletons drop magic weapons, by 9th
level you certainly should already have one...and what's more, almost nothing
you fight at 9th will be invulnerable to non-magic weapons yet.
