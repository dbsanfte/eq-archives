instrument (obviously), and after bumping up a level it's even ok sometimes
with just singing it. My favorite thing to do is to go to the orc camps or

world of some orcs and dervs. It also makes a nice screen shot to see a bard
playing a lute in the middle of dead npc bodies everywhere..
