Try runnyeye - I was with a group of players over the weekend there that
were no lower than level 21, there are plenty of Goblins there that will
give you XP at your level, just don't expect to do that zone solo as the
Goblins go crazy and round up HUGE trains regularly.

At level 19 (which is what I was) I died 3 times and ended up leaving
frustrated because I had less XP than when I started and not gotten a single
piece of black iron, which was my goal in being there. It seems that being
the lowest member of my party, and therefore the weakest, caused me to
experience the old "monster magnet" syndrome again - should be a good place
for you though at 22. I know that on my server there were never more than
around 20 ppl in the zone at any time during the day and often closer to 10.

Plus everyone is aware of course that there is a much sought after weapon
(not going to give away) there that is better than the BBC stat-wise and it
is the place that blackened iron comes from. I saw a bard run past with
selo's and a drum last night in HPH all dressed in black iron and it looked
VERY cool - I felt like I had just met Johnny Cash ;-)
