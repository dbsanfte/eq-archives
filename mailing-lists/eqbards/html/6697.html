Not true from what I've seen. With no spellcasting they are as good a tank
as a bard if not better. Add even 18 seconds of invulnerability to that and
they definitely outlast us. The cycle time is long, about 5 minutes, but
being invulnerable for 18 seconds out of a 90-120 second fight effectively
increases their hit points by 10-15%. Since they have as many hits if not
more than us already, and the same armor plus a shield... It adds up
quickly to their being able to absorb way more damage than us. I have duo'd
with my dwarf cleric friend several times and when he gets a mob's focus he
always ends up taking far less damage than I do, especially in the shorter
fights. Imagine the following, 4 wizards, 1 cleric. Cleric casts smite,
then divine aura and gives the wizards 18 seconds of nuking. In same
situation the bard would take a hell of a lot of damage trying to solo tank
the same critter the cleric could.
