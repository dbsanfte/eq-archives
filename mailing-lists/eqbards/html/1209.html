Subject: Hunting Places


I saw someone mention Guk as a good place to hunt. For the levels infered, I
assume Upper Guk was meant. If you have hunted the live frogluks in Upper
Guk and have gotten bad factions with them, you now have a real problem
getting into Lower Guk at higher levels. You must cross Upper Guk to access
lower Guk for the undead frogluks (and all the really cool goodies down
there, like did you ever want to be a dark elf bard?) once you get into the
30's levels.
Our take on upper guk is that it was intented as a evil races dungeon
primarily since they are born with bad live frogluk factions. A person in
our guild has that problem and he has to get someone to invis him to get in
and out, everytime. This sounds simple til you start counting deaths also.
The only people who must kill live frogs are the hapless paladins, if they
want to get one of their holy swords, the Ghoulsbane. How do I know this? My
husband is a L40+ paladin and lives in lower Guk after painstakingly
doctoring his factions thru getting a ghoulsbane. I do see others in game
hunting there and the few I have talked to had no idea that lower guk
existed. This is so far the only split dungeon we have found. So think very
hard before you hunt upper guk.
Brilinnia on Xegony
