If you're by Freeport my recommendation is Northern Ro. One of my favorite
places. Orcs, desert tarantulas, scarabs, snakes, madmen and eventually
cutthroats, zombies, mummies and dune tarantulas. The commons is nearby too,
and you kind find a lot to fight out there if you like a little more variety
(and don't want to carry so much milk/water).

Oasis is nearby also and is good place when you get higher fighting the
various loot-poor gators (doesn't sound like you need the loot).

Near Qeynos there's Everfrost, I'd avoid Blackburrow (which is way to
crowded for my tastes) during peak times.

If you find yourself in the Butcherblock mountains, there's the orc camp
which didn't seem to be as camped as it used to (Bertoxxulous server).

I've been playing a lot of other characters and have a small low level guild
who tries to maintain some low level characters so we can play with some of
our friends who have just started, so I've been hunting at this level
recently. Hope this helps.
