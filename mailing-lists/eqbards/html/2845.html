>I have a bank of hotkeys set up as follows:
>
>Top Left = Melee slot one
>Top Right = Melee slot two
>Middle Left = an inventory slot (which will at any given time hold either a
weapon or an instrument)
>Middle Right = an inventory slot (which will at any given time hold either
a weapon or an instrument
>Bottom Left = an inventory slot containing a backpack which holds
instruments and keys
>Bottom Right = an assist command hotkey.
>
>Changing from weapons to instruments is straightforward. Assuming both
melee slots are filled with weapons, and that only one of the two middle
slots have an instrument, then
>take weapon from Melee slot one and put it in Middle Left, then pick up
instrument and put it in place of the weapon in melee slot two and put that
weapon in the Middle Right
>slot. To change instruments--right click on backpack in the bottom left
slot---backpack opens--change instruments. I think this is more or less how
most bards are doing it. I
>learned about this from one of the nice folks on this list. I have never
dropped an item using this method and I don't really see how I could. Good
luck!
