Say you have whistling warsong playing. Stop playing it, and you'll notice
the icon stays on the screen a while. Start Anthem de arms, and you'll have
two icons on the screen. Go back to playing whistling warsong, then back to
anthem. Keep juggling this way, and you can keep those two icons on the
screen at the same time, thus gaining the benefits of both songs.
