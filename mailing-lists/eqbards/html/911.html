Aye, you're finding the earlier version of the problem I've had in low 30's
lvls.

One solution is to find (or create) a semi-permanent group. People you are
with most of the time, who will agree to keep you as a member of the "core"
group.

Throughout the game, there are points where one class's star dims, and
another's rises. Spell casters are hot for first level or two after new
spell level. Bards are hot after certain songs and a few abilities (such as
tracking at 35). You just have to keep going until you hit another hot
level for bards. Your next one should be at either lvl 18 (discord) or 19
(sleep).
