I think the problem is that most of us have already re-evaluated our

effective thing you can do is be a mana/hp battery. The Cantana/Nivs/Mana

Unfortunetly we are all too used to being effective in a variety of ways,
not just one way. Even if you melee while singing the "battery" songs, you

compare them to groups that are balanced.

people don't have the right to tell them that the rules they have made for
the bard class squeeze the fun out of it at higher levels.
