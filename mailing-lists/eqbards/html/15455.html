Get thyself to solb, in the window and/or pool room with a group. dont move
yourself until you get lvl 46, (when the gks start becoming green). it took
me all of 4 days to get out of 40 and 45 in the pool room. starting at the
window room and counting the spawn points up top, there are 14 gk spawns,
that if are broken properly, you can have nonstop pulling all night. i cant
say enough about the exp received in these rooms. bad pull? there are 4
zones fairly close for you and your group. also you are usually out of
harms way with the crazy bat/ldc trains.
