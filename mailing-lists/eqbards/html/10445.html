I have a suggestion if you want to earn mega-cash very quickly. The
raider camp in the se corner of N Karana is fantastic for a bard of your
level. At 19 I can solo the camp and cannot keep it cleared, using lament
to pull the bandits out one or two at a time. The bandit wizards and druids
can be a tough fight, but the humans and barbs are easy. You can get bronze
weapons off the humans as well as red bandit sashes. The red bandit sashes
are a great money maker. You turn them in to Chesgard in S Qeynos for the
bounty. With Carillion the bounty is 5-15gp, and a bronze weapon or round
shield worth another 15-33gp. All in all 2-5p per sash plus a smidgen of
xp. Add in a bronze rapier and you can get as much as 9pp off one green
human bandit. I made 50pp there two nights ago in about an hour. I usually
average closer to 30pp an hour there, but I had some fortuitous spawns with
more bronze than usual.
