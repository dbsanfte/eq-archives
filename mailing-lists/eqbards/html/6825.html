CCC does indeed raise your faction to amiable for the duration
of your visit to that zone. However, you cannot click every NPC
you see and bring your faction to amiable, you can only affect one
faction at a time. Attempting to CCC another faction, reduces the
first CCC you did back to it's normal level. You can test this
in any city on guards and merchants, since each belong to different
factions. Once you do CCC it's there until you zone.
