CONGRATULATIONS!

Last night when the East Coast router was down, Corellon could not get on so we
chatted in Yahoo instead. The disadvantage was not being able to look into his
eyes. The advantage was no interruptive shouts, casting, ooc, auctions etc.
*grin* We discussed the honeymoon and both agreed "PG" and he said he is not a
minor *really, a BIG concern on my part* so we can play it a little more adult
*grin*.

As for the RL thing, we both share the same fear. We got into it a little, out
of curiousity, neither of us wanting to lose the fun of the roleplay. He guessed
I was 21 *bwahahahahahah* I don't think knowing he was off by 25 years was the
best news he heard all night, but on the other hand the roleplay at the time went
well. And we had a good time travelling back to Freeport, but got disconnected
once there as he smiths and orders started pouring in as soon as he showed up on
/w and I got about 10 requests for binds and had bows and arrows to sell and and
and - it was like being in a busy city - I hated it *grin*.

I still don't know if it has an effect. This morning started out okay, then he
announced he thinks our characters are on the wrong servers and we should bring
the humans to Vallon since he doesn't want to be a warrior and lose his favorite
weapon to another player (MEN!). I was TOTALLY disappointed as I like my
Barbarian shaman a lot, even if she is only 4. And before we had a chance to
talk, he went afk, then returned with "gotta go" which is more abrupt than
normal. An old friend had just popped in out of nowhere. So I suddently felt
like it HAD mattered, and also feel like I"m overly paranoid.

The good news is I switched to Quellious, met up with one of my bridesmaids and
her ranger friend, we hunted and the more I thought the more I realized it was
just bad timing. Well, this couple (though not anything but friends they protest
overly much all the time *grin*) is also human on Vallon so I think I'll honor
Cor's request and we'll move our humans there only instead of being a cleric to
his monk, I'm going to be a Druid and Sibonay's little sister. Her friend,
Tenadar on Quellious is a monk, Andus, so we'd be matched couples. And now it
sounds kind of fun. As all four of us roleplay, we should have a blast.

And if it does affect the roleplay then it is better to find out now, right? The
problem is if one partner wants it to evolve into more than it can ever be and
the other does not. As it can't on my part, that was my concern (leading him
along) and that being settled is probably important. And if he starts sleeping
instead of playing with me, it's probabaly a good thing too.
