Well, first question I have is, do you have a full set of patchwork? That
is one of the first things you should try to get.

Next, if you can afford it, buy a minotaur axe. Those run 4pp, but are
well worth it.

I'd try hunting on Orc Hill. Orc Hill is near the orc lift, which is the
lift in Kelethin that is on an incline. If you follow the path from
there, continuing on after it ends, you should see a hill surrounded by
orc tents. This is a great place to hunt up to level 9.

There are lots of orcs on orc hill. The most important thing to know is
how to run to the guards, if you have orcs on you. That will save your
life. With your speed song, you should be able to outrun the orcs.

When you get an orc, try and run on top of the hill, so other orcs won't
wander by and join in on the fight. They all spawn at the bottom of the
hill.

Level five is a hard level in greater faydark, but once you get to level 6
you'll find things a lot easier, that's when you get your healing song.
