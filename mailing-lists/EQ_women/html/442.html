I have a topic for discussion, actually. Since this is a women's list what
better place to talk about the number of men that play women characters on
EQ?

I don't care what gender that people play in the game, but what I'm finding
particularly disturbing is the amount of men that pretend they are women
characters then start the typical "romancing" song and dance (be it fantasy
or with the intention of bringing it to reality) of the male characters (or
in some cases, other female characters). For example, I know of a man who
plays a female wizard on my server who started telling me how "bonded" they
felt to me...how well we connect. All the while, my male friends on EQ are
starting to wonder about the odd friendliness that the char exhibited toward
me...but the guy didn't want our other EQ friends to know it was a guy
playing a girl. I've extracted myself from that situation but know of other
male characters that he/she has started to be extra friendly with. When I
let those male char's know of the deception they are typically appalled, not
so much from a homophobic standpoint, but from the idea that they were
deceived.

I've heard that from other guys too...they start hanging out with a female
character and weeks after flirting online they discover that it is a guy
that they are with.

Again, the idea of flipping genders isn't too disturbing to me..it's how far
they carry the deception that gets me a bit riled up. And...I find that a
lot of the male characters are really hesitant to believe that I am a woman.

Just wondering if any of you have had good, bad experiences or just general
comments?
