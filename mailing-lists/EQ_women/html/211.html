Things that I have become aware of:

If you're still connected to your ISP when you drop from EQ, than you could
have a bandwidth problem (too much stuff streaming through too small a
connection, essentially), a resource allocation problem (video or RAM memory
overload), or a connect with too many hops along the way. What is your connect
speed, video RAM and RAM size, and have you noticed any sluggishness when
you're connected at those times with your browser or other 'Net applications?
I personally need a new processor, because the amount of data I'm getting
through a very fast connect with a good ISP is bogging my system down (even
though the rest of my computer is "rodded", memory, graphics card, and
HDD-wise). So I occasionally dump too.
