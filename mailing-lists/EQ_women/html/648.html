There are (rare) but out there hybrid-guilds

- tend to be >75% adult
- help each other
- have enough type-A personalities and "free timers" to get planar and run
thier own raids
- people who are not type-A acheivers are not kicked out
- have someone doing trade skills in almost every skill

There will be a few bozos, a few really selfish hogs, often an insentisive
person or two, and am immature kids or two (often tho the parent is in the
guild so you can get them swatted if they go too far). But usually none of
the above will be too flagrant.

The politics between the guild and others at planar/raids is still there,
but due to the adult community feel inside the guild it is less inside the
guild (not gone - people ARE people - but most of your guildmates will
display a social skill level above kindergarten when resolving the
politics).

I have heard of three (different servers) - and have now managed to join
one.
