It is probably best to join an existing guild, an existing guild will have
more experienced players who can help you learn the game. Running a guild
is a lot of work!

Retrieving bodies when you are level 10 or higher can be difficult. If you
think you're going to die it's a good idea to quickly type

/l

this will tell you your current location. When you die, write down the
number, then you can use /l to see how close you are to that location
while you're trying to find your body.

Bards, Necromancers, and Shadow Knights can locate corpses with spells. If
there is one in the zone they will often help you if you are polite.
