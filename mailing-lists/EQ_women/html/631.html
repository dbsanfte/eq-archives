Hi everyone,

I've been playing since Christmas 1999..a little over
a year...and my Paladin just hit Level 50. It was a
very exciting moment for me. I am still to this day
amazed at the lands I'm just beginning to explore and
the creatures that I'm just now "old" enough to kill.
I never have a difficult time finding a group nor does
my level 50 Ranger fiancee. The game is really a lot
about networking. Very much like the real world. At
higher levels you will go into a zone and say "Hello
Karnors Castle" (for example) and half the castle will
holler back "Hello Lily!". I never thought I would
have so many friends and acquaintances. To me that's
what EQ is about. The people I've met and the good
times I've had with them. I don't see EQ ending for me
because it's not only a game. These folks are my
friends. Just to let you know I am able to balance EQ
and RL. I have a full time job and a family that lives
close by and I even clean my house! :-) I'm 38 years
old and I've found something that keeps me young at
heart. EQ.

Lilyana
