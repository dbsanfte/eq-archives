Hey there!

The scariest thing about being in North Queynos is getting out of North
Queynos, hopefully you can find a nice druid or wizard who will give you a
lift.

Over in Erudin you can fight in the Warrens which should be just about
right for your level. I haven't fought there myself however.

One tried and true place to hunt is Oasis in antonica. The crocodiles
there are non aggressive until you whack them. Start off with Caimen (the
weakest crocs) and work your way up from there. You can also group and
fight orcs. North Ro is also good xp at your level, Dervish Cutthroats are
good xp.

When you get a little higher perhaps journey to Luclin. Pauldal caverns is
great xp to level 23 or so, and you get to fight cool fungus beasts.

For a better weapon, I suggest you save your money carefully, and then
travel to the Bazaar in Luclin. There you will find players selling all
kinds of amazing stuff, and you can usually find incredible deals. These
days, it's generally much more effective to buy a weapon than it is to
kill a monster for it.

Good luck and have fun :)

- Christina
