Hi everyone, I'm the list moderator, and I thought it was only appropriate that I introduce myself and my characters as well.

My first character is Inora a dark elf necromancer. She had a lot of problems making cash, and found it difficult to solo past 18th. I eventually put her asside temporarily, so a magician friend could level to adventure with her. They will start adventuring again very soon. I found Necromancers to be a very fun class to play, and they are very good at soloing. It is difficult being a dark elf necromancer though, because so many NPCs want to kill you!

My second character is Iuna, a wood elf druid. I've found druids to be really fun--they have an interesting mix of magic. My druid is currently 20th level. I think she will be my favorite character for some time.

My third character is Svarta. a dwarven paladin. She groups with a couple of my friends. I find taking a little dull sometimes though. She's very much a group oriented character. The rest of the group consists of a bard and a wizard.

In real life I'm a 26 year old Canadian who works as a software programmer. I've been playing role playing games, and computer games, since I was twelve or so.

I'm happy that so many people have joined this list, and that so much great chatting has been going on.
