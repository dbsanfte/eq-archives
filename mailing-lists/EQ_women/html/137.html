My game mate was playing a cleric when we met. As he progressed in levels
he started to experience exactly what you described. By level 16 he was
burnt out. Now we are on the Test Server and he plays a monk, I am a druid.
We love this combination. I discussed your letter with him and he wants me
to tell you it is not a female thing, he is very, very male ( jock even) and
he experienced the same thing. Personally, I think the partner/group makes
all the difference in the world. I also believe that if you cease to have
fun then change what you are doing or with whom you are doing it. I cannot
stress enough how much difference the right partner/group can make for ANY
class.

Just MHO,
