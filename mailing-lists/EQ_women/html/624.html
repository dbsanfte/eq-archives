Happy New Year, ladies!

I just wondered how long the shelf life of EQ is? I have played since
24 August 2000, and I tend to feel that I could carry on playing for
around a year, so long as I could get groups. EQ does not strike me
as a game I could easily play casually though, so I probably wouldn't
play fewer hours per week over a longer period.

I think the decisive factor would be in-game; it's a miserable
occupation if you can't play as part of a group, so if it comes to
the point where my ranger cannot get into groups, it will probably be
time to move on. I suppose I might try another class, but I am not
that interested in doing so.

Many of you have played far longer than me, and some have retired. I
should be very interested to know how this worked for you.

One further point; are any of you intending to play any of the new
games slated to open up in 2001? eg Shadowbane, Dark Age of Camelot
