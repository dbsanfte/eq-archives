Welcome!  Started a Barb Shaman on Firiona Vie to see how the roleplay was going.  Not enough of it, but a few. 
 
Then was lucky and DAoC right off the bat.  It costs a gold piece to start a guild (and 500 gold to get a guild emblem for cloaks -youch) and gold is 100 silver which is 100 copper.  The whole guild was wearing old tatters found on low level skeletons but we did get guilded.  So I'm in Blade of Enric on Percival playing Albion.  My primary is a cleric, but last night I went to Guinevere, also an RP server and started a dwarf Viking (will be a Skald aka Bard) and LOVED it. 
 
Back to She-Wolf's message.  It's the first time in my MMORPG experience I've ever had hit-points.  I could fight and fight and fight and the lower my health got it just meant the lower level mobs I took so I didn't take any greif from them (got to use my hard earned copper for real armor as no guild there) and let it build back up.  They have combat styles, too, sort of like EQ Monks, which take extra energy but hit harder.  I think I may try an Armsman secondary in Albion instead of a sorceror (though sorcerors get speed and running around in Camelot is SO SLOW!)
 
Anyway, I finally understand the appeal of warriors.  Man can they take a beating!
