Hey everyone!

I got level 50 with my enchanter on Brell Serelist yesterday. Then I
joined a expedition to take on Nagafen (first try on the server). We got
through the first fire giants ok. Then we got through King Tranix the
fire giant SK ok. Next we got to Nagafen herself. She had a unique fire
giant wizard guarding her. In the first attack, we took down the wizard,
but Nagafen killed everyone. We then sent several more waves attacking
Nagafen. We unfortunately never got her below 60% health. I lost about 5
bars of xp dying about 10 times. Some people lost up to twice that much.
It was a really exciting fun experience though. The scariest thing about
the whole thing was that many big players had their corpses sitting right
under Nagafen and it was possible we'd never get their corpses back (mine
was there too). But, brave warriors through themselves at Nagafen while
we dragged our corpses away so they could be looted.
