Specifically, I play a druid character that has been rendered
essentially
useless because of changes in the way the game is applied to the
character and its level (spells, etc). I only play that character
and am not interested in starting another from scratch just because

Second, I have heard that Sony is trying to "level out the playing
field" with respect to these changes. What you have done is leveled
my character o the point that I cannot play without finding a large
group.

I did not intend to play with large groups, now or ever. And even
when I do find a large group, my character's group value is useless
to them. I can offer them nothing of value because my character now
cannot contribute
anymore either by direct attack or by "buffing".

For example, the general feature called "mana" is severely defective
in that it takes eons to regenrate. To counter this defect, I relied
on a spell that helped "mana" regenerate faster, named Kodiaks
Endless Intellect. This spell has recently been "nerfed" to work
only on high level characters. Now, I sit and stare at a computer
screen, bored out of my mind. Where's the pleasure in playing your
game when all I do is sit. Its a waste of my time.

Third, the issue of "mana", and the amount of "mana" that spells
require should be reassessed. The game is for fun. I am not having
fun. I can only cast 3 or 4 spells before the "mana" runs out. Then,
it takes a lifetime to regenerate "mana". I could go fry up a pound
of bacon before my "mana" regenerates (I did that last night).

Either make "mana" regenerate faster, make spells use less "mana", or
let us "pay" for Kodiaks Endless Intellect. Whatever you do, you've
got to do something, I am reluctant to play anymore.

Fourth, it appears that many NPC's have been enhanced in some way.
Many things that used to "con" light blue or green now "con" dark

myself, even without Kodiaks Endless Intellect. Now, I die every
time.

static. One plans and strategizes based on the parameters of the
game. When the parameters continually change, as they have been, one
cannot strategize and the game becomes a waste of time and money.

The way I look at it, I am a customer that is now not getting what I
originally paid for. Please correct these problems. If you would
like a comprehensive list of other problems, I can compile it for
you. The ones that Im complaining about are the worst offenders.
