Guilds are wonderful... well, I guess if you get into a wonderful guild. :)
I'm a dedicated to Ilsik Haucil on the Mith Marr server, and I've had a
great deal of fun. The people in the guild are helpful, will rez you if you
die, will support raids you want to go on, and will just play for fun
anytime. There are only about 35 members in this guild, very small, but a
wonderful bunch of people.

They started the guild because a bunch of friends who happened to play on
line all the time wanted a more tight knit group. They are that, indeed,
and I sure hope to become a full member soon. I think to find a guild you
should probably group with people in certain guilds, ask them how their
guild is, and just find some friends who you like their playing style and
who you get along with. With IH, whenever someone wants to play, there is
usually a guild member or two on line, so you never have to go alone. :)
Also with IH, though, they are very selective about who joins. You have to
play with various members, get someone to sponsor you, and then play with
various players more so they can see if you'll fit in. I like IH because
they don't let just anyone in... they have a certain play style which is
very efficient and organized, and I like that very much.

Oh, and I'm the one who's boyfriend got me hooked. :) Only 1 bubble from
19!

Good luck!
