Rangers do rock,

I have a level 52 Ranger (Pixy) as you know Icey, and I can say without a
doubt that rangers rock really hard...up to about level 40. From level
40-50 they are good. After 50 they start to become weak in comparision
with other hybrids/tanks.

Rangers are in particular rocking for small groups. I hunted from level
20 to 50 with my RL boyfriend (now ex sigh). It was really quite easy for
us to get to 50 together. We grouped sometimes in larger groups but the
majority of the time we just hunted together.

We got most of our xp hunting outdoors. Harmony is great for single
pulling, then we just did tank/heal. Like all tanks rangers depend on
equipment, but if you have decent equipment, rangers tank great up to
level 50.

Personally I've always loved the elegance of Rangers. My first high level
character was a druid, and I recognized that snare and harmony were
awesome, so playing a ranger was the obvious next step.

Post 50 I find that paladins and shadowknights are both very interesting,
but not quite interesting enough for me to level one =) I have a baby
Paladin who is level 15 who I enjoy playing though.
