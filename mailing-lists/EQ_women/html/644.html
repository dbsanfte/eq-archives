Hi Again,

I am also a player from the start of the game.....I still remain
enthused by EQ.
Perhaps because I have not power-leveled my characters. I have several
with which I rotate playing, my highest is Deneyu Firnloch, a Barbarian
Shaman on Lans T'yvel at level 20. I solo most of the time, partly
because I can play in only short periods of time and partly because of
hating to end up in a group with a bunch of pre-adolesent slash and
dash. I buff those I meet along the way, assist when I see a person in
difficulty, but I do not worry about making levels....just in having
fun. I explore
EQ mostly with my level 16 Enchanter on E'ci, Yadienu... I even visited
a bit of Velious with her. How long will I remain a player, probably
quite a while longer....It continues to surprise and enchant me. I am a
40-something single mom of two, I am a member of SCA, I am a teacher a
supporter of youth organizations. I think that the secret in EQ, is to
take your time at the game, not rush up a ladder to the top, only to
find you want to jump off :) Patience is a virtue in EQ too. Good
Hunting to all and may your hearths be warm with the joy of well lived
lives.
