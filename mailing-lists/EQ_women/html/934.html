Well. For the suggestions offered by you ladies, let me thank you,
although I doubt I'll be using them. "Life is good" for me, too...as
my wife has gradually weaned herself out of EQ's grip.

For the record, I agree:
1. EQ, as with any pasttime, is harmless - in moderation
2. The game itself is not "evil"; SOE is not Satan
3. What people do with their time is their own business
and...
4. The vast majority of EQ players play responsibly

Unfortunately, my wife did not.

She began playing playing in Sep 01, and by May 02 was up to about 18
hours a day, 7 days a week (with some - few - exceptions). Her life
consisted of rolling off of the couch at noon, slumping directly to
her PC, and battling mobs until dawn. She would not leave the house
for 20-30 days at a stretch (seriously, not even step foot into the
yard, let alone get into her Lexus)

Meanwhile, I worked about 12 hours a day (active duty military), plus
a 1-hour commute to-and-from work. When I got home, all the chores -
and I mean ALL - fell to me: cook, clean, laundry, home repair,
shopping, trash, entertainment of our daughter (5), bills, etc, etc.

On weekends she suggested I take our daughter "out somewhere"...so
she could play uninterrupted. Later she would say that her playing
while I did chores all day made her feel guilty.

For six months, I went to bed alone, listening to her typing away
amid all the EQ-sounds. In the morning, as I got dressed for work,
she'd be there slumped on the couch.

Occasionally, I'd read her character logs while she slept, because
I'd wonder what a 36-y.o. woman could possibly have to discuss with
teenage and 20-something males for endless hours...

My friends suggested throwing her PC out with the trash. My MOTHER
IN LAW (!) suggested I move out (she has 3 EQ-addicted daughters).

Eventually...inevitably...we had a confrontation over EQ; I said
she'd become a BUM, she admitted the game controlled her life. I
made it clear that I'd be there for her when she's ready to rejoin
RL; meanwhile, I'm living my life too. Now, at 39, I'm in the best
shape of my life (run 30 miles a week), have finished my 2nd masters,
ready to retire from military next year comfortably. Life IS good.

She continues to play, but her playtime has greatly diminished (about
10 hours a week now, mostly due to our daughter beginning grade
school).

Sorry for the long post, but when I read the original message, it
struck a nerve. To you ladies who play EQ responsibly, I'm sorry if
you were offended. But I hope you can appreciate my feelings, and
the reasons for them.

I'll keep my future rants in more appropriate forums (EQ Widows,
SpousesAgainstEQ, OLGAnon.org)

Peace
