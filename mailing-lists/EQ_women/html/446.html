The way my husband and I balance our EQ life and real life is that well.. we
both have a characters on EQ and have had for over a year but we made a pact
that we play together so our characters are close to the same level. We
both like roleplaying and gaming so it helps. However, we too spent too
much time on EQ and took a break for several months and became involved in
other things. We then went back to it later and had even more fun. We also
limit ourselves if we get to involved in it. I think that EQ is very
addicting and it can ruin a relationship if someone is always on-line and
the other person has no interest in it at all. You have to balance your time
together and sometimes it means spending time doing things you may not
normally do. You might create a character to play with him on-line and in
return he spends time doing a hobby you like, or just going to dinner,
movie, etc. EQ is NOT the real world, and taking off from work to play is a
bit scary... but I can see how it can happen. I hope you can find a
balance, it is difficult.

Kat
