My boyfriend played on Vallon a bit and I watched him. He made a dwarven
cleric and leveled very quickly to level 9, which is the level at which
you can fight anyone from 1st-17th level. He then joined frequent dwarf
raiding parties that attacked the elf newbies, and killed lots of them.
For some reason, the dwarves and gnomes seemed to have no problem
attacking the elves, the but elves never seemed to launch counter
offensives.

I was a little surprised to admit I enjoyed watching those battles, human
vs human has a real draw to it. My boyfriend's role was generally to
blind and root elves so that smaller dwarf warriors could fall on them and
kill them quickly. However, I also got to see my boyfriend be killed by
parties of elves ocassionally. For example, when fighting the sisters in
lesser faydark, some elves started blinding and rooting him and his
partner so the NPCs killed them. Leveling there seems to be very hard.

Ultimately, I didn't think I'd like Vallon, and my mind hasn't changed.
It seems all too vicious to me. The race wars concept justifies attacking
anyone of another race for no reason. I don't really want to kill other
people for sport, and I know I'd find it very frustrating if people killed
me, trained me, blinded me while I was fighting etc. It takes hours just
to get your corpse back some times on Race Wars.

- Christina
