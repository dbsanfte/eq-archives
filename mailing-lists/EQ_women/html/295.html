I am 27 and I have an on-line "love" in the game too. We are both
firmly aware of the person behind the character. He is a man with 2
kids and a wife. I am a woman with a husband. We are just
role-playing our characters.
Sometimes I still feel a bit "wrong" about our flirtation, but really,
I know there is nothing to it and we live in totally different
countries (he is from Canada). So. I really don't know if I have
helped or not. I say make sure you and he are both aware of the real
person behind the pixels on the screen.
