Dear Jennifer:

I am a single parent going to college and working part time. I play EQ with my
boyfriend (separate computers, different cities, hehe) about two hours a night.
I put the kids to bed, and the time is mine. I do not play during the day on
weekends unless my kids are with their dad.

I have brought one character to 15, and one character to 21, and I have a couple
of other newbies that I grew tired of. I play essentially one character, and
it's enough for me. Before my boyfriend and I started dating, we both played
ALOT more than we do now (my kids were usually asleep by the time I got home)...
fancy that! <grin> I know for a fact it was a nice little escape world for me.

I do not care that I have been playing this game for almost a year now (more if
you count beta) and I am still at level 21. But then, I have competition in
alot of other areas in my life, and it's nice that this is one place I don't
have to worry about refreshing my house every day or keeping up with my MUD
mail. My boyfriend and I usually find someone to hunt with, or we meet new
people and make new friends, or if we really CAN'T find anyone (very rare), we
do a quest.

BUT we also do not play when the kids are awake, during work hours, or on
weekends when we have special plans. And if he had ever told me to come to
Norrath if I wanted to see him, I would probably not be dating him. Bit
different when you're married, though. =(
