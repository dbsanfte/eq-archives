Hello all sisters...
Well, after playing a goodie-two-shoes cleric since I first got into the
game, I decided recently to check out how the other side lives and started a
female troll shadow knight. The swamp was a new experience alright, and so
was the famed troll regeneration (watch those HPs zoooom up!). But the
funniest thing was when I flirted with an ogre warrior in Grobb (some big
bad 'un with a last name). After all the recent discussion here on flirting
and females getting items, I decided to try out my trollie charms on this
ogre. All I did was to comment (in my well spoken troll dialect) on what a
fine basher he seemed to be and emote ":bats her eyelashes at [player]" and
all of a sudden I received 1 platinum! For a lvl 2 trollie SK, this was
indeed a fine gift... I guess trollie women have something to teach my
dwarven cleric when it comes to turning mens heads because my cleric never
received such a fine gift... :)
After this encounter, my trollie character is convinced she is indeed
attractive, but I doubt she will carry her flirting further than the
eyelashes at the moment. Still it's proof that it's not just the wood elves
that can make a killing by flirting... :) /em smiles fiercely
