I, personally, have enjoyed the revamping of the
zones. Once you reach the higher end of the game, you
find it sort of loses it's challenge. For those that
feel this way, it has been a blessing and has
heightened their game play.

Though some of the zones are harder and require a
larger force, or a higher level to enter, there are
many higher level folks that will be greatful for
that. There are many lower level zones, but once you
near the top, you found yourself stuck in the planes
or having to have a raid force for about everything.
I never ran into a lack of places to go when lower
level.
