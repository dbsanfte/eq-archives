Hmm ok all about Luclin in a few easy words.

Beautiful new graphics. Friendly kitties. Beastlords. If you don't have a
lot of time to play, I would suggest making a new Vah'shir Beastlord, that
way you get to experience the new class, the friendly kitties, and the new
graphics all at once.

Luclin is seriously hard on your computer system, but hey, wasn't it about
time you upgraded anyway? =o

If you're on a budget, and need to upgrade, I can make some
recommendations.

1. Geforce 2MX graphics card. You can get these for like $50-100. They
are low end for Luclin but they will get the job done. If you have money
get a Geforce 3 TI 200, or a ATI Radeon 8500.

2. Ram. You can get 256mb of ram for about $25 these days. You want to
upgrade to 512mb or so (which is about $50). For most people you just want
to buy one 512mb memory chip and stick it in your computer. Some computers
don't like mixing ram, but that's ok, just throw out whatever was in there
originally. Of course, not all kinds of computers use the same ram, so you
may have to go back to the place you bought it for the upgrade to make
sure you get the right ram.

The most fun I have had in luclin honestly has been playing with my
character's look =) Looks like it's going to be fun though.
