Sever politics hits when you get to higher levels.  It has to do with scheduling raids, loot rules, etc.  Once the server matures to a certain point there are more people wanting to go on raids than spots to accomodate them.  On Brell it was at the point that guilds camped the boards to be the first person to sign up for the next raid right as the clock rolled over.  Then there are cliques of people who won't play with people.  There's fighting about loot (I think that is the worst - I HATE it so much). 
 
By 50 everyone knows everyone else.  People have formed friends and enemies and your reputation makes a HUGE difference (HUGE!!!!) in how you get to play.
