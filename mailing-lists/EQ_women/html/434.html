Since I considered healers as buffers as well, the Bards ability to use
song for the same effect Shamans and Druids use spells had me classify
them that way. They are the characters that strengthen the groups
abilities, especially by removing downtime.

We had two bards in our group last night in Cazic Thule and had ZERO
downtime between huge MoB battles. I kept Embrace Death up which
converts my health to manna, having a similar effect to Clarity. But
with the Bards healing songs I never lost enough health to have to Drain
Life so could use my far more potent "Shock of Poison" spell. That was
the plus side. The down side was that fighting for me was REALLY BORING
since I required none of my special skills. /assist 2 2 2 /assist 2 2 2
/assist 2 2 2 2 all night long (2 is my poison hotkey). And with no
downtime, there was not roleplay or chat. <sighs grimly>
