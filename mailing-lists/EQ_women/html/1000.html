Salutations!

Weddings in EQ can be a lot of fun as well as other
non exp grinding events. We had a trivia contest and
kind of a scavenger hunt once in my old guild for
prizes. It was fun. I've seen dueling contests in
guilds set up by levels and even contests in things
like swimming. All fun for me, but then I get tired fo
grinding exp all day.

I had my cleric's wedding in the big chruch looking
building on the moon . . . don't even remember the
zone name off the top of my head. When my cleric got
married it was by the sleeping dragon. This one was
really planned out with each of us and the cleric
having a number of pre set buttons for certain parts
without having to type so much on the spot. I wrote
out vows and customized them for EQ and for our
classes . . . cleric and ranger. It was fun.
