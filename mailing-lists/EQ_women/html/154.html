Hi there, and welcome to EQ Women! This mailing list is
for women who play the game Everquest to meet each other
and chat.

You can post to the list by replying to this message,
or by emailing "EQ_women@onelist.com".

Please feel free to post an intro message with your
character's name, race, class, level and server
(and anything else you'd like).
For example:

"Hi everyone! I play Iuna, a 17th level wood elf druid
on Mithaniel Marr. I'm looking forward to
chatting on the list."

If you ever want to unsubscribe, go to the ONElist web site, at
www.onelist.com, and select the User Center link from the menu bar
on the left. This menu will also let you change your subscription
between digest and normal mode.

Have fun,

Christina
