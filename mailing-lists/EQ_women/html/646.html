I really don't know very much except what 2 of my friends have told me after joining and leaving The Sisterhood.

One left because of political strife within the guild. I think this happens to every guild at one point, though, so it isn't a negative thing necessarily. My own guild went through some major political strife over the summer, but it came out much the better for it in the long run.

As far as my personal experience with The Sisterhood goes, I found them to be polite, helpful, kind, brave, exemplary players.
