Subject: Marriage


As my character is marrying on Sunday I must confess to a few minor qualms. In
particular, er, how does one roleplay a honeymoon *smile*. Unlike many who
develop relationships in Norrath, my beloved and I are unknown to one another in
RL. For all I know he weighs 300 pounds, guzzles beer, and is hygenically
challenged *smile*

There are times if I wonder if this is a positive thing to be doing. I mean,
what if he is some starry eyed adolescent and I ruin him for life *grin*. I know
MY lines are clearly drawn in fuzz (okay, I think I know the difference between
reality and fantasy though I swear I saw a Pixie Trickster in the airport the
other day *grin*).

Anyway, this is all fun and games and I am having fun. But there is this part in
the back of my mind that wonders. Because unlike reading romance novels (blech -
persish the thought) there is another person involved in my little fantasy and I
must never forget that.

Any other hard core role players out there struggle with these ridiculous
issues? Any other players over the age of 25, for that matter *smile*
