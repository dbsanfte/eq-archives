Hey Everyone,

The time for the meeting has been set for Monday August 16th at 6pm PST in
East Freeport on Brell Serelis.

The meeting is an opportunity for us all to meet eachother and chat.
You can either make a newbie character just to attend the meeting, or you
can use your existing Brell Serelis character. While Evil races are not
allowed inside East Freeport, the newbie zone is not patrolled.

My character Luna will be hosting the event, and you can send her a tell
if you have any questions.

After the chat, there will be a subequent newbie hunt for any who are
interested. This will be an opportunity to group and hunt together. East
Freeport has an excellent newbie zone. North Ro, which borders East
Freeport, has numerous creatures levels 1-4 for hunting.

Please note the boat from Butcherblock takes about an hour in total travel
and waiting time, so be sure to boat early.

I realize not everyone will be able to attend during this time, but this
was the best match available. I'll try to have another event, and make
sure that it works with the time schedule of those who can't attend.
