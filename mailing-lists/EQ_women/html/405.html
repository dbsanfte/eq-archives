Subject: Solo xp for 35th level necro


Oh if you do have to solo and need good xp for your necro, I suggest you
try butcherblock. Level 35 is actually really hard for a soloing necro,
especially with the recent nerfs to DOT spells. In butcherblock however
there are a wide range of dwarven NPCs, some that will give very good xp
for your level. You don't want to attack anything that isn't blue. I
believe for example that south of the docks area there are some NPC
dwarven guards that should be blue to you, but I would strongly
recommend you get a SOW before trying them, I haven't fought them myself
and they might be really tough.

Asking other level 40+ necros on your server should also help you find
good sources of xp. Most people are happy to talk about where they got
their xp 5 levels ago or more.
