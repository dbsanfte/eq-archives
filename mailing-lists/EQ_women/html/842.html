A tell between us might go something like this: Me,
"Dear heart, how do I get to "place x"?"
Him, "Where are you now hun?"
Me "I don't really know, but I think I'm in the "so
and so" zone."
Him, "Ok dear no problem, just go east."
Me, "Which way is east?"
Him, (after delivering some kind of arcane analysis of
the weather and cloud patterns or Norrath) "and that
should take you right there."
Me, "???"
Him, "Or, you could just try sense heading."
Me, "You know my sense heading is broken."
Him, "No, it's not broken dear, just keep trying it."
Me, "I'm telling you this thing is broken, even when
it gives me a direction I'm not sure if I can trust
it."
Him, (seeming more frustrated now for some reason)
"Just keep trying, it's not broken."
Me, (with an appropriate silance and a sigh) "OK."
Him, "Do you want me to come help you?"
Me, "No, I'm fine, just lost that's all, you know I'm
always lost, kind of funny really, I'm the ranger who
has no sense of direction."
Him, "Yes, funny, ha ha, Now, just keep pushing sense
heading."
Me, "OK, but I'm telling you it's broken."
Him, "It's not broken!"

And so on it goes . . .
