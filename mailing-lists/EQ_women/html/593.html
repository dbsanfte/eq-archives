The "rangers suck" issue started at 50 when rangers could deal out proportionately more damange than any other tank, but could take less damage and therefore they were constantly draining all the mana of the clerics and dying a LOT.  Verant recently raised the caps on their ability to avoid getting hit (which rangers should excel at over other tanks) and there is hope that this will balance the play.
 
BUT, rangers are not really "tanks" in that they cannot take damage.  They are mellee class because they dish out INCREDIBLE amounts of damage, esp. at 50+ (I watched Sylvanar solo a mob in Fear and KS from the casters everytime).   We need to learn when to backout of fights to lower ourselves from the top of the aggro list.  We need to always pull from a distance (that's why we have such good archery skills).  I'd like to see us get even better archery weapons so we could stand back away from the tanks and just play archers, but the damage done now doesn't compare with duel weilding 2 fast weapons with a haste belt and haste buff.
 
Yeah, it's a great class.  You just have to max out your AC (hard withough plate) and play smart.  That, and have a good attitude about dying and getting teased ;-)
