Hi Beth,

I have been playing rpg's for almost 30 years. I have been a computer
addict for about 15 years. The two just seem to mesh so very well. I
started online gaming with The Realm and moved to Meridian 59 where I met my
r/l best friend. Hopefully he will soon have his computer up to speed and
will be joining me in EQ. Btw, he just turned 60.

I come from a predominantly matriarchal family and the elder women have
always been strong role models. My mother is 96 and still works full time
in her craft shop. Nobody ever taught any of us that age was a limiting
factor. lol

Now that I am disabled, and spend most of my time at home, I am very
thankful for worlds such as EQ. EQ gives me contact with the outside world
that is beyond my limited r/l world. I live in a very small community in
r/l so the larger one of EQ is very precious to me.

Oh, happy news: Kachine and Mondre were married in Rivervale, Bristlebane
on June 19th. They are very happy and will be hosting a big party later so
all of their friends can help them celebrate their love. They were to be
married July 10th but just couldn't wait so they eloped. When the party
date has been set there will be an announcement here so that all of you who
are on Bristlebane or just want to come visit Bristlebane, can come have
some fun with them. There will be some games and lots of food and drink.
Get those alcohol tolerance skills up for the drunken relay races. hehe

Thank you, Beth, for asking about my experiences with gaming. I wish more
of my generation would give it a try. I think it is a great stress reliever
and such a wonderful opportunity for expanding ones world.

Good hunting and stay safe,
Janice
