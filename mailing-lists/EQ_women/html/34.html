Ladies,

As an older women in real life, I find it amusing to watch the juvenile
attempts at flirting that some characters try.

My experiences have been varied. I have been harassed by a young dwarf,
who was crude, profane and persistent during the first few days of
production in March. Needless to say, I learned how '/petition' worked and
he was slapped down and repeatedly warned by other players, guides and GM's
alike until he learned to quell his urge to poke any and everything that
walked by.

Also, I have observed another Fennin Ro female player, who shall remain
nameless, use OUTRAGEOUS sexual overtones to sell her wares. One wondered
if it was just leather armor she was selling, but it is interesting to
watch her use sex as a way to control male players. She has been doing
this since production and I have recently played in a group in Unrest with
her and she does keep the patter up. You would think she would get tired
of it and concentrate on the game, but it seems that this IS her game,
so.......I watch and wonder. I have met two men who are playing female
characters and was able to detect it immediately, and they admitted it in
private tells. I have kept their secret, but over time they have improved
their 'femininity', so I think that few others know that they are male in
RL. I have been saddened to not have made friends with other female
characters on-line in EQ, when I have had wonderful lady friends on other
on-line games and miss them terribly. Thus, my hopes soared when this
mailing list was created.

I am always up front about my age when I am asked, but I am not too forth
coming when I am asked for my email address or for personal pictures. I
always say that I am an ugly, fat, old, rich women and that usually puts a
stop to the pushiness.

I have taken on the role of 'Auntie' to a few fellow players. One who is
married on EQ. He is baffled by his woman friend
and her behavior and doesn't understand periodic moodiness. He always
thinks he has done something wrong. Another, slowly budding, only wants to
relate to women on-line who live half a world away from him in real life.
It is so much safer emotionally and has more richness to his fantasy if
there is no chance of meeting.

I think the half-elves, high elves and wood elf women get hit on the most
since they seem like such 'pin-up' girls to the young male players. I
think that men are biologically over balanced from visual stimulation in
real life, and these types of female characters are the epitome of a male
fantasy girl-thing.

On that note, do you notice that we are the Girls of Norrath on some
web-sites, but they are the Men of Norrath? Think that is a subconscious
need to control, by belittling???

Anyway, that is my two coppers worth. Hope it fuels some discussion.

Dornita and Dornar
