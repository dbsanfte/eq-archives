I'm a former guide and I think that EQ's model for CS is flawed, but
probably in pretty much the oposite of what most people think!

I think in EQ too much is done in terms of item restoration due to bugs,
and resses in terms of death by bugs. The best way to stop that is to
ensure that people don't die or lose items because of bugs. The second is
to design the entire game model so dying or losing an item is not so big a
deal. Unfortunately EQ makes it so losing an item, or dying, is a huge
penalty so people demand customer service when this happens because of a
bug.

People abuse guides and GMs all the time also it's really sad. I know my
own guild on Tholuxe does this. They trick guides/GMs into ressing them,
or porting them around, it's no good.

MMRPGs are really young, hopefully we will see them evolve so guides are
only there to answer questions, and not to compensates for technical flaws
in the game.
