I don't have a problem with twinking new characters as a way to more
quickly get them up to a level where they can interact with other existing
characters. I've personally just yesterday started one major twink. We
always seem to have a shortage of clerics, so I created a dwarven cleric
and equipped it with my 36th level character's backup armor. This means
several pieces of bronze (including chest and helmet), lizard cloak and
mantle, bear hide belt, and everything else banded. And a mucking bit
2handed blunt weapon called Midnight Mallet that I picked up at an auction
(11 damage, 44 delay, sometimes squashes 1st level monsters in one hit).

The dwarf's name is Staar. In the description area of the character I wrote
"Twinkle twinkle little Staar, what a twinked-up Dwarf you are. Armored up
in shiny bronze -- that stuff's not from Orcish pawns! - Windsong"
