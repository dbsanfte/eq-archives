> > My favorite pen and paper RPG is without a doubt Ars Magica. I can't
> figure out why it hasn't become the most popular RPG ever actully. The
> thing I like the most about it is its magic system, which ... well I won't
> go into the whole thing but it's super cool. Wait, here is a link which
> describes it, so you can read about it if you want.
> >
> > http://azathoth.gg.caltech.edu/troupe/ars/magic.html
> >
> > What I really like about this system is that it is a two dimensional
> system. In Everquest, for example, a spell is an 'Evocation' spell (and you
> have an evocation skill). In Ars Magica, if you wanted to throw fireballs
> around you'd have to train up 'Creo' (create) and 'Ignem' (fire). But, as a
> side benefit, you'd also be better at extinguishing fire, and creating
> water. This makes for interesting and complex magical dynamics.
