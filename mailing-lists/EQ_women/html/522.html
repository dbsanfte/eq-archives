Hey Sandy,

I'm on Brell Serilis. Best class and race depends a lot on your playing
style, they are all fun in different ways =)

I'm in a guild called Rain which my ex-boyfriend runs (ack, we just broke
up last week, I'm still not used to calling him my ex =( We are still
friends but I don't know how things will work out for us and the guild in
the long run.

I have a level 54 enchanter. I love enchanters because they tend to act
to alter combat more than to actually kill mobs. They mesmerise things,
they speed things up, they slow things down, I guess I'm a bit of a
control freak =) My enchanter is a female high elf named Luna. Enchanters
are not much fun at low levels though. Most of their spells become more
useful at high levels, at low levels they are mostly a waste of mana.

I also have a level 52 wood elf rangers. Rangers are I think one of the
most fun tank classes. They are very versatile. I'm finding at level 52
that being a ranger isn't as fun as it used to be though, rangers seem to
peak at around 40 I'd say.

Tina

Other fun classes:
Bards, at low levels they are basicly tanks with neat abilities.
They get healing song and speed song fairly early
Druids, they get lots of fun spells fairly early, and are good for both
grouping and soloing pretty much forever
Necromancers, very powerful, and have lots of neat tricks. Pets are
fun.
Monks, don't need a lot of equipment, and very effective in battle.
Fairly simple to play as well.
