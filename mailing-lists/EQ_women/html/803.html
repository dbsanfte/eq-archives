Basicly it's up to you to decide what is and is not acceptable in how you
interact with people in the game. You can create your own emotes in the
following way:

/me slaps Joewoodelf

That will make the game say

Bangvyne slaps Joewoodelf

There are no easy answers to personal interactions, you need to figure
that stuff out yourself. My advice is have fun and try to not
unnecessarily hurt people's feelings, while at the same time ensuring
people respect your boundaries.
