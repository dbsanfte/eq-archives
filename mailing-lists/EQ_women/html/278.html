Subject: Guild Stuff


Hi everyone,

First, thanks to everyone who showed up last night to help get the guild
creation process going. The guild still needs to be approved, and that
might take another week.

I think I may have jumped the gun a little in moving the guild creation
process forward so quickly, but I wanted to get us all able to play as
quickly as possible. I'd like to encourage continued discussion of what
we want as a guild. In the mean time, I'd like to use the Sisterhood of
Erollisi as our guild. If ultimately there is a clear message that this
is not a good guild for us, we can then all move to a different guild
(hopefully just getting our named changed). I encouage people to post
alternative names to this list for consideration.

I've been very interested by the discussion of the historical context of
being a priestess of a love goddess. I have a few comments with regards
to this.

First, I've heard expressed concern that being a Sister of 'Love' will
spark inane comments from little boys. This is a valid concern, but in
and of itself I don't think it's sufficient to invalidate the suitability
of the name. I don't like restricting what I do based on how I feel
inane little boys will act. Lets face it...little boys are little boys.

Second, regarding the mythological context of Erollisi, I think there is a
lot of room for us to define for ourselves what it means to be a follower
of Erollisi. I don't feel we're locked into being ritualistic
prostitiutes.

Regarding chastity, I think it's fairly clear that the guild members don't
want to have any assumption or implication of chastity on them from a role
playing context. To this regard I'd like to withdraw my suggestion of
chastity as a basis in role play.

Regarding Men, I also think it's clear that people don't want them seen as
the protectors of the sisters. I'd like to withdraw that proposed role
for them, and the title of Warder which might imply it (I'll try to come
up with another title).

Lastly, discussions can go on forever and eventually become pointless 8)
To that regards I'd like to put a limitation on the discussion of guild
name and guild charter to Saturday. I'll do my best to listen to what is
said on this list, and reflect it in the name and charter of the guild.

The real purpose of this guild is to give us an environment with which to
play EverQuest together, so lets not forget that! 8)

- Christina
