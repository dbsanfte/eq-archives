These sorts of things happen online. You can try and pursue the matter
further to try and get this person in trouble, but it's rather hard to get
a complaint to result in actual punishment. Unless you are seriously
offended and upset by comments like this online, it's probably best just
to ignore the teenager in question. Remember, you can ignore anyone with
the following command:

/ignore name

When someone is on ignore, you can't hear anything they say.
