The Ebony Blade is found in lower guk live side. It's a pretty cool
ranger only sword, that procs a root that does some damage. It's
considered a good low level ranger weapon.

Around 30-40 level many prefer switching to an Electrum Bladed Wakizatci
(or two). This sword has a better damage ratio, and is very light and
fast. This is the one you need the fangs for.

Around level 40-50 most rangers try and pick up Yakeshas and a flowing
black silk sash. Yakesha is a very popular weapon, with the same ratio as
the EBW and a nice 75 point damage proc. EBWs are IMHO almost as good
however, and you're fine with a pair of those at 50.

A good two handed sword will have better damage output than dual wielding
up to around level 35 or so. It depends on your weapons though. Some
good two handed ranger weapons are:
- Axe of the iron black (Found commonly in Kunark, sold for a couple
hundred plat I believe. This puppy really owns, it is good to 40th level
for sure. Pick one up.)
- Shiny Brass Halberds (SBH) and Blackened Iron Bastard Swords (BIBS)
are good up to level 30 or so.
- I'm not really up to date on all the crazy Kunark weapons, I know
there are a lot of powerful ones around now
