Hey everyone,

I recently got 36th level with my enchanter on Brell Serelis. I'm a
little in shock in I'm only now starting to comprehend that I've been
power leveling like crazy. I think it's time for me to back off a bit,
because I've been just way to into this character.

I'm currently doing jewelry on Brell and I'm the only jeweler who can
make the coveted Platinum Ruby Veil (+7 wis +7 str) and Platinum Sapphire
Necklace (+7 int +7 str). I never though I'd be in that kind of position
on a server it's kind of weird. It won't last for long, but for now, I'm
the best jewler on the server.

The guild is fairly slow. We have probably 6 regular players I'd say
(people who log in on a daily basis). We have a few other more infrequent
members. There is a wide range in the guild in terms of levels it seems.
I'd like to encourage anyone interested in exploring things on a new
server to consider trying Brell. Now that the server has been up for
almost two months it seems to have stabalized a bit. The newbie zones
aren't as over hunted as they were. Because there are less people on
brell, the hunting areas in general don't seem as camped.

I plan on starting a new character on Brell soon, a ranger or bard I
suspect. I'm very tempted to make a ranger because I acquired a Gigantic
Zweihander in a trade (great 2-handed slashing weapon). I really like the
spell-songs of bards as well though. I see this character as being for
mainly grouping.

I hope everyone is having fun playing!

- Christina
