Is ok. No harm done. I've run mailing lists for the past 5 years, and not
allowing attachments saves alot of aggravation on the part of the list
owner; and it's not just viruses that become a problem. I have never run a
mailing list off a service such as onelist before, but I don't believe
onelist had this option until the last few months with this Happy99 virus
and then with the Melissa virus.

I know some people on the EQ lists like to share screenshots from the game,
but the best way to do that is display it on a website and send out the
address, or tell the list you have one and then send it through private
email to those that ask for it.

The best rule is what I've read from others already, never open
attachments. If you get something that you requested, you still need to
virus check it before opening it.
