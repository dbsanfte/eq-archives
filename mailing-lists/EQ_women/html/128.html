Subject: Shamans and Clerics


I would just like to tell everyone out there who is a Cleric that they
just might like to try out a shaman. I spent 18 levels being a Cleric
and 5 being a Shaman so far. I was always frustrated with my
limitations as a Cleric and took on way too much responsibility for
other people's lives, which made grouping a very un-fun experience. I
love being a Shaman so far. ( I've made 2 new characters in Quellios.
Bloodless and Blacksatin) Go ahead and laugh at the names, it's the
best I can do on such short notice! I agonized believe me. Someone even
called me "campy"!- whatever that means. I'm only 25, that term's
before my time.
Anyway, I've played on that server for a total of 3 days and have
noticed, not just me - also complete strangers, that most people on
that server are really helpfull and nice. Or maybe just the people in
Halas as that's the only town I've been in. I encourage you guys to try
it out as I have been so happy. I don't die constantly and I don't have
to sit out the action all the time. I get to actually participate as a
part of the group instead of staring at my spellbook all the time with
nothing to do but chat. Not that chatting is at all bad, but I want to
have some fun too!
I have noticed that a lot of us have said that we play clerics, it
seems more than any other class. (Or maybe I only notice the clerics
because I play one.)I hope this doesn't offend you all, but I wonder if
we have chosen to play one because we are by nature nurturing and
helpfull, or because we have been raised in our society to act servile
to men. (I'm sure I've raised a few hackles-sorry!) It bothered me that
I would be one of the only women in rl playing a female character in
any given group and I would be forced to sit and heal all the male
warriors. I grew resentfull. I'm wondering if many of you have done the
same thing, or if this just says a lot about me.
Thanks! :)
