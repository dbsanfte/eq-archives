Server politics for me had to do with being a high level character, and
usually are only something that directly touches high level characters.
At high levels your focus shifts so instead of soloing, and hunting in
groups, you become more focussed on raids. These are coordinated
activities involving 12-40 people to achieve some common goal, like
killing a dragon. At this point, every server naturally develops politics,
which govern how people are or are not allowed to participate in these
raids, and when they can participate. With large groups of people all with
different desires, it's hard to come up with a common focus on what is
"fair". This is politics, and it sucks and there is no point, which is why
I think it's best to just completely ignore it for your own happiness =)
