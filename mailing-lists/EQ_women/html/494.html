Well, my partner and I play wood elf rangers, and have played them steadily
together for over a year now. We have a kind of Penn and Teller routine going,
and have given our characters quirks (he thinks he can fly or at least safe fall,
so he jumps off things alot, I always wince and announce to the zone, 'How many
times do I have to tell you rangers DON'T FLY' which always gets chuckles), I get
lost in dungeons and everyone indulges my slightly breathless and wide-eyed look
at life. We're very efficient as a 'combat force,' but alot of times we lose
track of fighting to trade oneliners. <grin> We also toss insults at dark elves
(we tell them it's in character only, most are really wonderful) and oh, the fun
we've been having with lizards. And lots of them have been having fun back.
Mostly we like to point out the difference between succulent, tender, city-bred
high elves, and tough, stringy, hardworking wood elves. One lizard even took
notes and confided that gnomes are wonderful hors d'oeurves.
