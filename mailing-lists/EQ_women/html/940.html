Dave,

This is the way I look at the situation...

-Your wife played 18 hours a day, neglecting life in
general, not sleeping or communicating with her
husband or family, or pretty much avoiding them. Did
it occur to you that there was likely a problem before
she started playing EQ? As with any sort of game,
book, or even television, it can be a wonderful tool
to relax and get away from the trials of life. Some
people get carried away with it and you must look at
it from an outside view. What made her feel like life
was so much more bearable in the EQ world? Have you
considered that there may have been a communication
problem before she started playing? That maybe
something was bothering her? Did you even ask? Or
perhaps you did...

As for snooping through her files... This tells me
that you had lost trust for her and beyond that,
communication. As far as I am concerned, this problem
really had nothing to do with EQ other than it being
the medium she used to escape whatever the problem
was. It could have been anything, really.

Instead of hopping on an EQ forum tossing out advice
or warning, consider what it is that you really are
telling us. Buecause I am not so sure that it occures
to you that 9 chances out of 10 the problem was hidden
there far before she started playing EQ and it just
then became noticable.

I have been happily married for 7 years and during
that time, I have found myself getting deep into
something (Artwork, reading, webpages... never EQ)
when I was experiencing trials in my marriage. After
we would work things out and get our communication
back, I found that those things were not nearly as
intriguing to me and I would much rather spend time
with my Husband.

So tossing the medium (EQ in this case) aside, a far
better warning would be "Be careful not to let
communication and happiness with your family slip
through the cracks for so long that you get wrapped up
in another world". Instead of saying, "She turned
away from her family because of EQ!" Say "We stopped
communicating and grew unhappy so she turned to EQ"

This is not putting the blame on you, for
communication takes 2 or more people. It is just as
much her fault as your own.

-Sorgaine
