Hey Beth,

A new server (not the race wars server) will be going up in the next two
weeks. That would seem to be the sensible server on which to try this, so
no one is directly advantaged from previous connections on an old server.

In terms of a city, I don't think it's necessary to have an official guild
city. It may be useful however to have an unofficial city, so that those
of us who wish to group while newbies can choose to group in that city.
I'd personally recommend Kelethin/Felwithe. My experience has been this
is one of the best newbie zones, and one of the best social zones.
Butcherblock, Lesser Faydark, Unrest, and Crushbone also offer a great
range of close by zones to adventure in. Really I think almost any newbie
zone would suffice, there are great places to adventure everywhere.

I personally plan on playing either a High Elf or Human Cleric or
Enchanter (can't quite choose!) I also plan on being either a blacksmith
or a jeweler, and will do my best to provide either armor or jewlery at a
low cost to my guildmates.
