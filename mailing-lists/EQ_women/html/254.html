In all honesty, I would just like more choice in clothing in general. Not just
getting to choose whether our clothing is revealing or not, but what we wear
just in general. I would enjoy being able to buy skirts for feminine characters

and pants for more athletic ones. Why must clothes be limited to starting
garments and armor? Being able to choose clothing in general would also help
more characters look distinctive, rather than all the same. With such a limited

choice of faces, anything that can help differentiate one character from another

is a Good Thing, imho.
