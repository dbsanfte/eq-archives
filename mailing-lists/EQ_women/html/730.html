Subject: Ring of Fire in Acrylia


I thought I'd just briefly share my experiences with a cool new Luclin
encounter called Ring of Fire.

Essentially you go into Acrylia, which is this cavern filled with
grimlings (tiny cave people). You step into the sacred ring of fire and
guardians spawn. As long as you have a person standing in the ring of
fire, guardians will spawn fairly regularly.

After a while eventually a boss spawns, who when you kill, he drops some
cool loot.

If you stay in the ring of fire more powerful bosses will eventually
spawn.

After about two hours a boss spawns that takes about 24 level 60s to
kill. He drops some really neato stuff, including a 50 ac chain
breastplate, and a flowing thought 3 ranged item, and an all/all
flowing thought 3 mask with 100 hp/mana and a bunch of stats and 7 resist
all.

The next boss is even tough, requiring about 40 people to kill. He drops
the same loot as the previous boss.

We've never managed to make it to the next boss, we may try tonight.

Anyway it's pretty fun, and it's a neat idea, I suspect there are things
like this all throughout Luclin :)
