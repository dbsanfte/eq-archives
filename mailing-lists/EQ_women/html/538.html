ROFLMAO - just wait til you get group teleport.  The druid's life is "TAXI"!  And if you're a cleric and get res?  People ask my cleric friend to travel 8 zones away to rez them. 
 
I have rules now that really help.  I usually start the game with about 20-30 minutes of teleporting.   If on a raid, it's my job to pick people up before and drop them off after.  Once I'm grouped, my group gets my time and my mana.  It is not fair to leave a group to teleport someone and then come back.  Not to mention it is impossible if deep in a dungeon.  I'll SOW anyone if I have SOW up (which is almost never as I usually keep SLN in my buffing slot and I only have one except on a raid then I drop my "save my ass" slot and put group chloro there).   However, usually the rangers always have SOW up (fewer spells to fill their slots) so I almost never get asked anymore.  (My bard, in full lambent, however gets asked all the time which completely cracks me up).
 
So my advice is to remember you can always say "no".  When younger and soloing, I would only SOW if I had meditated to full.  If someone was willing to wait, I'd SOW them.  If not, forget it.  I also will not do it for someone who is not polite.  I had one paladin ask me to group with him and when I suggested that at 25 he's get little experience with a 52 he said "oh, I know.   Just SOW and invis me."  I refused.  I told him he needed to learn manners and consider this refusal a lesson. It takes so little to say "Could you please help me.  I need SOW and invis to join my group."
