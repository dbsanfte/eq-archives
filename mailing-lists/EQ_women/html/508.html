One simple way to measure the effectiveness of a weapon is by its
damage/delay ratio. For example, a fine steel two handed sword is 12 dmg
43 delay for a ratio of 0.28. A fine steel longsword is 6 damage 28 delay
for a ratio of 0.21. This means you'll do about 30% more damage on
average with the two handed weapon. Bashing does help to close that
difference, but my experience has been that it's a small amount of your
total damage output.

At high levels (50+) it gets much more complicated because the minimum
damage a weapon can do is based primarily on your level, and not on the
weapon, which gives an advantage to small fast weapons.
