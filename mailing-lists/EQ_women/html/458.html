Okay, to be honest, I don't balance well at all. It's a chronic
struggle and I consider EQ an addiction with a WHOLE LOT of
rationalization why I can play it. If he is playing the raids on Fear
and Hate and the dragon raids then he really is committing himself for
the duration of the raid. At the earlier levels one can log on and log
off pretty freely as there is less coordination with other people. But
the raids require commitment.

If he IS level 50, he doesn't really have to play every night. He is at
the maximum level. One suggestion would be to set aside one night a
week as "date night" where the two of you spend time together. And,
yes, you might look into getting your own EQ account. It really doesn't
cost that much. Not so you can spend more time playing with him as much
as so you can learn a bit more about the environment and community.

Or just hang out with him. There is enough downtime in the game that I
often read a book while other watch TV while playing. It is interrupted
conversation, but it is possible to have.

At any rate, good luck. I don't know that there is a solution,
honestly. If I had a real life group like SCA I might enjoy that more.
I suspect it is a LOT healthier. <sigh> Oh well, good luck.
