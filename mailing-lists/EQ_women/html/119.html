Janice,

I love how your characters have such distinctive, fun and interesting
personalities. I'm not exactly sure what my character (Guerrilla on
Tuneare) 's personality is yet. I guess I'm developing it, but you've given
me some good food for thought.

I've met mostly nice and only a few rude characters on line. More of the
rude ones tend to be male characters and I suspect that the rude ones,
whether their characters are male or female, in RL are probably those 13
year old males you mentioned. Two of the nicest female characters are my
guild sisters and they are for sure female in RL. What I've noticed about
EQ characters, both female and mostly male, is that some are very selfish
and obnoxious. They are impatient and lead their comrades into dangerous
places too often. They take more than their share of the loot. And they
never say thank you. Somehow I think that most of these characters are
young males in RL, but they could be older (some people never grow up) or
female (good and bad are not limited by gender).

I chose to play a female to begin with on EQ because I figured I would have
enough to learn about the game environment and my race/class without having
to worry about role playing a gender I'm less familiar with than my own.
Perhaps later I'll try a male character and see how it works. Do any of you
real women every play male characters? Do you recommend it and if so why?
