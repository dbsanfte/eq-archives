Playing with your real life partner can be very convenient!

My (ex) boyfriend and I played together from levels 20-47 with our
Ranger/Cleric combo. We hunted very efficiently. It was nice because we
could log in and hunt for half an hour effectively, we didn't have groups.
It's kind of like soloing, but you're actually with someone.

Also with a real life partner you can communicate much better. No typing,
just talk, you'd be amazed how much that can help =)

We would still do social grouping as well. I had another high level
character (enchanter) so we could do the cleric/enchanter combo while
grouping. That was very nice, it was easy to form groups, by just adding
in a couple casters and tanks. I could also play my ranger instead of my
enchanter if we needed a tank more than an enchanter.

One bad thing was I became very dependant on playing with him. If I wanted
to play and he didn't want to play I would get snippy. Having to find a
competant healer to replace him was always a pain! So if you have
different playing styles, it can cause problems.
