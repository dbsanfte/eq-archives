I have to be really strict with myself because I know
that EQ takes up so much time. So, I only let myself
play maybe 5 hours a week during the semester and just
let myself play the 12 hours a day if I want to during
the summer and winter break.
I guess it's really a one or the other situation. I
know if I'm gonna play EQ I'm not gonna want to get
off when I'm supposed to so most of the time I just
don't let myself play. I guess I'm a little obsessive
when it comes to role playing games.
The whole time thing about EQ: Yeah, I've noticed it
takes way longer to get anything done in this game. In
UO I could get wherever I wanted to go lickety split
just by gating, but EQ, sheesh, it's a half hour
commitment just to run through 5 zones. Ridiculous. :/
I think that it's just the downside of makeing play
dependant upon other players, you spend more time
trying to coordinate times to be on together and just
getting a darn group together.
