In another game I played (UO), I DID get married. My RL paramour hated it. I
enjoyed the attention (yes, lack of romance in RL was probably a huge factor)
and the pomp and circumstance and FUN of being able to be a medieval Lady with
her Lord. I actually dragged lots of people who were power gamers into my
little mental playground, and they have told me they are the better for the
experience. To this day, my online "husband" remains a friend, and he and I
talk about our significant others (his wife is expecting in July and I have the
baby present waiting to mail), the joys of parenthood, our respective fields,
and just, well, life. I do caution anyone that this experience isn't always
the case. I have had friends who have been less fortunate and hooked up with
some real psychos. On EQ I am not married. My wood elf is barely out of her
adolescence, and has some very protective guild brothers besides--which, by the
way, is LOTS of fun. She does have a hunting partner, and the two of them
together make Abbott and Costello look serious, but it's all in character. I
find it's safer, and easier on my real life relationship that way.

Sara, Povar
