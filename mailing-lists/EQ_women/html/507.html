Christina Norman wrote:

> I'm not a Paladin but I'm good friends with a 53rd level Paladin.
>
> Paladins actually have exciting weapons of almost every type. Off hand I
> can think of excellent 1h blunt, 2h blunt, 1h slashing, and 2h slashing
> weapons. Accordingly, I'd suggest trying to train them all to some
> extent.
>
> If you don't want to train them all, I think one handed slashing is
> probably your most defining weapon type. Paladins are skilled shield
> bashers. Ghoulbane, a relatively easy to obtain Paladin weapon is one
> handed slashing.
>
> However without an exceptional one handed slashing weapon like Ghoulbane,
> most of the time you will benefit from using a two handed slashing
> weapons. They are more commonly available, and generally do far more
> damage, then normal one handed slashing weapons. Exceptional one handed
> slashing weapons are usually paladin only.
>
> A good lower-priced two handed slashing weapon is an axe of the iron
> black. They are easily found Kunark weapons. BBCs are common Antonica
> 2-handed slashing weapons as are shiny brass halberds, and should be
> cheaper than an axe. GZs are also good.
>
> For one handed slashing, until you obtain ghoulbane, there really aren't a
> lot of great one handed slashers. I'd suggest keep your bash and slash
> maxed with a fine steel longsword and a shield, then switch back to your
> two-hander once both skills are maxed.
>
> Hope this helps =)
>
> Tina
