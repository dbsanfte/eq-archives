Generally what you do is plan the place and time of your ceremony in
advance. Then petition a few hours early for a GM/Guide to wed you.
Depending on the availability of someone to do it, they will either just
change your last name, or they will perform the whole ceremony. It's a
good idea as a backup plan to have a friend able to perform the ceremony
(probably a cleric or druid) in case there are no GMs or guides available.
Try and hold your wedding during prime time when it's more likely a
GM/guide will be on.

The ceremony is really what you make of it and what you want out of it.
You can always simply say you're married, and then petition for a last
name change. If you haven't taken a last name yet, you can just use the
surname command to give yourself your partner's last name.
