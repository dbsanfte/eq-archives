Getting to level 50 takes time. I had about 40 days of played time on my
character by the time she made level 50. Do /played sometime and see
where you stand. It does take a long time to get to 50. I play this game
way too much, and I know it.

Real power leveling is really very boring. I hunted levels 17-38 in aviak
city in south karana. Levels 38-42 I got in lower guk running the bedroom
circuit (there is a nice cyclic path you can do over and over again that
is very good xp). Levels 42-48 I got killing dwarven NPCs in
butcherblock. Levels 48-50 I got killing freeport guards in freeport. I
should note that none of this NPC/guard farming put newbies in danger. In
all cases there were guard NPCs left that they could run to when they
needed protection.

I didn't just do boring, repetitive, killing to get xp. I also grouped
and had fun and did cool stuff, but probably 75% of my xp came from these
repetitive XP farming activities. If your goal is to get to 50th level
fast (mine was) you should try stuff like this, if you are trying to have
fun you should just try and have fun =)

Starting new characters is always fun and exciting. With a little bit of
twinking they can usually have a fairly easy time at those early levels,
and advance very quickly. You get to try fighting in new fun ways. You
get new ability quickly. It's great!

Rangers are really fun to play, and up to level 22 they get new abilities
really fast. They solo very well, and they are I think the most
interesting tank to play.

For a level 35 necro, if you have good friends for grouping I would say
hunt in lower guk. I don't know how crowded your server is, but necros
are great in guk. Start at the zone entrance and kill the froggies there.
Eventually move in deeper. Lower guk is great xp for a necro up to level
45 or so, and you can even get to 50 just hunting there. I understand on
most servers lower guk is really overcrowded and has many trains though.
On my server (brell) it's still relatively uncrowded. Try it though,
because necros totally rule in lower guk.

- Christina
