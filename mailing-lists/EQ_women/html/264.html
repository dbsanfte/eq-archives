A couple of comments on the guild ideas as given... first, why would sisters
dedicated to the Goddess of Love be chaste? The idea seems wrong to me; the
opposite seems much more likely. The other thing that bothers me is the idea of

the Warders - why do a group of warrior women need men to protect them? Kind of

implies that even tough warrior women need a big strong man to help them, you
know? Maybe we could come up with some other way men could belong? Maybe the
men could belong to a brother order, The Brothers of Mithaniel, with a close
association with the Sisters of Erollisi?
Other than those two quibbles, I think the idea in general is sound and I would
enjoy belonging to this guild on a new server.
