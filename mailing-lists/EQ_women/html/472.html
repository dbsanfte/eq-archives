Ok, this might just be a rant, but since it's non hostile I'm going to post it here =)

WOW Iksar are so cute. The first time I ever saw one I was like...oh my gosh! It walks around and swings its little tail and talks and everything! It's like the best pet!

And Kunark is very pretty. Lots of fun new zones, with cool looking critters. It's pretty obvious to me you could hunt in Kunark and never leave, and get all the way to 50. It seems a self sufficient continent.

Kunark is dangerous though, I have talked to a lot of people who brought their mains over and got stomped by something.

The solution to all this is...make an Iksar shaman! You get SOW at 9th level and you can explore like crazy. Team up with a friend and make an Iksar monk, and you have a great 2-person leveling/exploring team especially for low levels. FYI Iksar have regeneration, and the highest wis of the shaman races, making them the best shaman and monk races IMHO (though they aren't as cute as Duna).

(Rumor: Iksar do not get the inherent haste that human monks get at level 20+)
