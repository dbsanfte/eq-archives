I bring my characters to life through my writing.  Ice has a complete history that described the moment of her "birth" into Norrath (when she ran away from home to avoid an arranged marriage with a Wood Elf she loathed).  Each adventure became the next chapter in her life.  I stopped writing at some point, and that may be part of what happened.  I was just too busy playing and the group I had joined up with really didn't do a lot of story writing like my previous group had (but there were lots of OTHER problems with that group and it split up badly).

At any rate, just for yucks and if you're interested, I just finished the rest of Ice's tale up through my Religious Retreat and personal meeting with Tunare (we laughed all night at what a foolish Druid Ice is, always throwing away what she wants the most ;-).  This coincided with my taking a week hiatus to reconnect to my real life and, duh, start writing again ;-)  Writers write, they do not play EQ all day every day ;-P
