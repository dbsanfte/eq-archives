Ranalla,
I envy you being a 14 lv druid, when I tell people I am ONLY lv 6,
nobody wants to group with me! :(
As far as grouping, I have only been in 3 different groups for any
length of time (1-6 hours)
The first group didn't know what they were doing. Of course, neither
did I but they abandoned me in Lesser Feydark AND didn't split the loot
fairly.
The second group I was with was great! They were mostly lowbies (more
experienced players with fairly low level characters). Lots of fun, and
they really helped me out with strategies and not getting lost (dumb
blonde syndrome!) I don't believe in twinking (sp?) but it is nice to
get advice from more experienced folks.
The third group didn't go so well. Each person had their own agenda,
and couldn't agree on where to go and what to hunt. I didn't stay with
them long.

My advice for grouping-make sure everyone agrees on plan of action
before you start-including where to go, what to hunt, any quests people
are on, and order of looting.
