Hi!

I've been playing on E'ci for just over a year now. Unfortunately, wicked
things like work get in the way and prevent me from playing as much as I'd
like! I started because one of my roommates played, and I used to be
an avid MUD'der. So it was natural for me to fall into EQ. And I'm
addicted to starting newbies as well. Currently I have:

Rixte, level 42 monk - human
Ereen, level 24 magician - human
Meika, level 24 druid - um, wolf?
Jaxiana, level 15 paladin, high elf
Irdith, level 10 necromancer - erudite
Sarizy, level 9 shaman - leezard
Heike, level 8 warrior - barbarian fisher and baker
Megany, level 5 bard - wood elf


I love my Rixte the best. There is nothing like the sheer glory of a
monk. And all three of my higher levels now live in
Kunark - only Meika ever touches home soil, and that's usually to run
errands for everybody else.

Rixte and Ereen are both in the same guild, Caer Sidi. Rixte usually
plays with guildmates. Meika (and sometimes Ereen) are really for playing
with RL friends who also EQ. I'm an officer in Caer Sidi, and it really
is a great guild - it's quite small and closeknit. Because of differences
in levels we often don't get to group together anymore (when we started
the guild, everybody was about the same level) but it's always nice to
talk to everybody on /guild. When it comes to guilds, there are so many
different choices in what kind of guild to go with. For me, it's about
the people in the guild. Not about twinking, camping, raiding, but just
about people who really care when you hit that next level.

Rixte is an adventurer and explorer. Camping mobs just isn't as much fun
as exploring new dungeons! Although mind you, there's nothing like
spending 2 hours and 20 real life minutes feigned on the floor of Kaesora
while your group attempts to mount a rescue party...all because you got
impatient with waiting and, um, fell down a big hole with lots of nasty
spiders who wanted monk for dinner. That was a great day!
