I finally got together all the things I needed for the
tunic. I never did find the forest drakling scale but
I found someone who had one and offered to sell it to
me and asked what I would give them. I told them that
I didn't have any idea what anything cost but would
pay them whatever the fair price was. They told me
that they used to get 50pp but that they would sell it
to me for 20pp and that to be nice they would let me
have it for 10pp if that was all I could afford. Well
I had the 20pp and I didn't want to take advantage of
anyone so I gave it to them. (Yes, I'm a sucker)
:o)

Then I took all my stuff back to the Ranger guild
house and gave it to the Ranger who gave me the quest,
I forget the name. He took all the stuff, told me

know whether to laugh or cry . . .
:o)

there are not enough slots in the box to fit the stuff
and the tunic.
:o)

Anyway, I went back out and started gathering stuff
again. Still need to regain a couple items, including
that silly Forest Drakling Scale, but now I'm paranoid
about the exact approach to take to not lose
everything again. Apparently, I did or said something
wrong last time.
:o)

Oh well, live and learn I guess, but it certainly
didn't make me feel any better when I was telling

Anyway, just thought I'd throw that out in case anyone
