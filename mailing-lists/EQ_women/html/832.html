There's a ranger haven on the mainland outside of Qeynos, IIRC.  That's where I got married.  The name escapes me (haven't played in a long while) but someone on the list should know of which I speak.  It's very nice.  If you have any concerns about guests' safety, ask some of your 'wedding party' to be 'ushers', ie., escort your guests from Qeynos.  Or have a wizard 'port 'em in.
 
My fiance and I were both rangers.  We were very effective at taunting; once one'd take too much damage, the other would start taunting madly.  'Course, the roles may have changed in the past year...
 
Guilds, eh... they're nice if you've joined one because you regularly adventure with many of its members, but I never personally advertised for a guild.  I tended to let my escapades take me where they would (as an elven ranger when Povar was first new, there was A LOT of escapade) and ran into my guild brethren when hunting.  When I began looking for them when I signed on, I knew I was finding a "home".  Just my two coppers'.
