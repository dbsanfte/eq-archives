Subject: RP Server in Test


For the RP lovers out there that have quit playing EQ, the new RP server,
Firiona Vie, is up and running in test (i.e., you have to use eqtst.exe to
play). They have new rules: trivial loot, race/class/deity alignments that
prohibit grouping and guilding with non aligned players, language barriers (no
common tongue at all), everyone is ROLEPLAY ON, and only one character is
allowed.

I played it the last two nights and had a BLAST! Everyone in Everfrost (never
did play Shaman past 9 before) was roleplaying. I hear that wasn't the case
in GFay and Qeynos but that the RP'ers were pretty strong and insistent and
the spam quieted down. The chars will be wiped out once the server goes live,
but I kind of like Katrice and will probably keep her. I love the Shaman
potions they can make now :-)

A few powergamers have decided to come to FV to see how the new rules affect
people, and as long as they don't uber guild the server and camp the dragons
and make the high end game SUCK I don't mind.

Anyway, the rumor is the server goes live the day DAoC is released. I'm
planning on getting DAoC, but I may just not cancel my EQ account quite yet.
AO, on the other hand, BIG YAWN! Made it to 10 and quitting. Good try, but
the game is just too buggy *sigh*.
