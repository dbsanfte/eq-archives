We are a sisterhood. Only women may join. If we find out that a
man has joined, he will be deguilded. (And trained. Just kidding.)

Anyone can "serve and protect" but not to the degree that women are
capable of. We vow to cover for each other when someone hears a
THUMP from in the playroom. We vow to tutor each others youngsters
in the ways of the game. When they are 18, they must seek their
own guilds. We vow to watch our sisters' backs when they smell
smoke in the kitchen. We vow to be there for each other when
someone needs to talk. We vow to never leave a comrade's body
unrezzed or unobtainable.

Finally, this is only a game. We vow not to take it so seriously
our egos could be threatened. Raids are not mandatory. We will
set buffing schedules for those who need to solo early in the AM or
late at night.
