Subject: Healing, SOWing, etc.


I tend to heal anyone who is in danger of dying in
combat, but if a person is not in danger I generally
don't. I haven't had a lot of people ask me for heals
though. If I am asked for heals, I'll usually say I'm
low on mana (if I am) or throw a single heal on them if
I'm full.

On the other hand, if I am full of mana and I'm in the
mood, I sometimes heal or buff people randomly for no
reason at all. It's an easy way to be nice to someone,
and make new friends. People seem in particular to
appreciate getting Spirit of the Wolf.

I spent three hours last night in Kelethin disguised as
a tree. I went about bolting orcs, healing, and buffing
newbies as they valiantly tried to face off orcs. That
was a lot of fun. I didn't get any XP, or any treasure,
but I had set asside the time to do it and it was fun.
A few newbies were freaked out by this moving tree. Others speculated as to whether I was an enchanter or a druid. I saved several people's lives, and they were very greatful. A few newbies role played with me, as I presented myself as 'The Great Tree', a protective Kelethin forest spirit. All in all, it was fun. I recommend you healers and buffers give a shot at some community service some time, it can be rewarding 8)
