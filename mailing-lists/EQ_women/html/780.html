You need a GM if you need to change your surname to match his. The only
real service GMs provide is changing your surname when you are married.
They also sometimes help make weddings more fun by performing the marriage
ceremony.

As for gifts, well that's up to you. A great place to look for wedding
gifts might be the Bazaar, you can buy almost anything there and usually
at a great value.

Any ring can be a wedding ring but PC jewlers can make rings that are
actually called 'wedding rings'. The cheapest wedding ring is an electrum
carnelian wedding ring, you could probably get one made for about 5pp but
it would be a special order. You can also get Diamond and Fire Opal
wedding rings that will be more expensive (then can be as expensive as
1000pp). Like real life, with wedding rings you need to figure out how
much you want to spend, then talk to a jewler, and they'll be happy to
take all your money.
