I know I have no interest in playing a boy at all. I don't like the way
boys relate to eachother. I know a bit about this because I have
interacted with people who assumed I was a boy (even though I was playing
a girl character). I just get weird vibes whenever a boy is relating to
me in an obvious "boy" way. I don't know why, it's just icky.

If you play a boy and act girly, you come off as being fruity, and in
Everquest this is something that people either find funny or annoying, but
it's not something I think that can be just ignored. A few times I have
played my boyfriend's cleric, and continuing to act like myself, it's just
weird I think.

I think if you are a 'quiet' type, it can be easier. I've noticed that
some of the boys who play girls who do it the best are the quiet types.
If you're not constantly emoting your feelings, or actively stimulating
conversation, you don't send out as many gender cues. I theorize thus
that a quiet person will probably find it easiest to play the other
gender, and will not cause waves when they do so.

I am not a quiet person. I am constantly emoting, talking, chattering,
whatever. If I have to stay still for very long I usually end up running
around in circles for no reason.

I once played a friend's bard for a Nagafen raid. I decided to have some
fun with that and played him in a very stereotypically gay way. I made
sure everyone knew I was playing him. I didn't do anything rude, I just
phrased things in a sterotypical way, expressed a lot of affection to
other boys, made positive comments about hair and clothing to girls, silly
stuff like that. People generally went along with it and had some fun,
though I probably made some of the boys uncomfortable. I think it is kind
of funny that even if we know the gender of the person behind the
character, we still tend to relate to them strongly as the gender of the
character. I believe someone else commented on that, saying that they
didn't feel comfortable flirting with their husband when he was playing a
female character. Kind of interesting.
