One good way to group is to try small groups. Lots of people seem to
think that you should try and get groups of 5 or 6, but groups of 2 or 3
can work really well.

As a druid, you are an ideal partner for a tank. Pure tanks like warriors
and rogues are usually happy to group with a healer.

I use to dislike twinking, but over time I have come to understand it. I
have twinked myself. For me twinking is simply to avoid aspects of the
game that I no longer find as entertaining. Usually with twinks, as with
any other players, it's fairly quick to figure out if they're decent
people or not. If someone is nice, whether they are twinked or not,
they're nice.

Tina
