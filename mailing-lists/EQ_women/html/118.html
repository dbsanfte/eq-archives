As a level 9 paladin, I have been flirted with some and responded some.
Mostly all of us are kind of busy running around killing, buying and
selling, etc. though. I enjoy the flirting perhaps because until maybe this
year I had never done so in RL. When I was young (I'm 48 now) I thought I
was ugly and I was too serious and intelligent to flirt or want to flirt.
That was in the initial days of feminism when we rejected a lot of our
feminity in the name of equality. Then for about 15 years I was way over
weight and no one was interested for a long time. Recently I have lost 50
lbs and become physically very attractive because of the weight loss and
because I've learned how to enhance and appreciate my natural and innate
beauty. Men respond at all levels including opening doors for me as well as
actually coming on to me.

Anyway, it's been fun in EQ getting some attention similar to that I now
get in RL. On the other hand, I am fairly happily married (17 years now)
and am not interested in having any affairs. On EQ that could only happen
if I gave someone my real name and contact information, which I don't
intend to do. I have found a steady, however, who is very nice and
romantic, saying chivalrous things to me, giving me trinkets, and helping
me find my corpse, fight, etc.. Like most women, my RL husband, is not and
never has been romantic. Nor have any of the men in my life ever been. The
rpg environment can lend itself to that chivalry, romantic thing so many
of us fantasize about. I very much enjoy hearing his compliments as well as
complimenting him. We usually do that when one of us needs to leave. It's a
great way to end the game evening. I also have genuinely learned a lot from
watching him play and playing with him. I suspect he's more experienced at
EQ or role playing in general than I am and/or that he is a fairly mature
person in real life. So my compliments are genuine, not just romantic
frivolities.

He's never asked me about my RL except to ask if I was really female. He
doesn't know my age or marital status nor do I know anything about him.
Wonder if it will ever come up or if we will continue to just interact
within our game roles. Certainly I don't intend to bring it up.
