Hello,

I am Dornita and Dornar, two characters on the Fennin Ro server.

Dornita is a 19th level High Elf Paladin who comes from a very rich
Felwithe family that is in the shipping business. She is a clothes horse
and only wears the finest armor and accouterments. She has a personal Chef
named Pierre who packs her picnic baskets with the finest wines and
eatables. She is very snooty and does not deign to talk to the plebeians
that
wander around Faydwer whining about their lost corpses and hawking their
trashy loot. She has a crush on Lord Jyleel, but he only tells her that he
is sorry she is not a defender. She has taken vows of purity, duty and
honor to shape her behavior, and often lays down her life for her group.
She is patient and not greedy. She solos very well, but is an extremely
slow leveler. As a paladin she is a mini-tank, mini-cleric and
very-mini-caster (undead only), but she has maximum skills in swimming,
fishing and sense heading, as well as the 5 ranks of magic use.

Dornar is Dornita's wood elf cousin. She is a 14th level Druid and a
pretty good tailor. She is a member of a hunting club, so she only gets to
come out on Monday nights and Thursdays when she groups with her hunting
club. She sometimes sneaks out when she falls behind the levels of the
other members of the hunting club, so she can catch up, since she is a very
slow leveler. She would be on all the time, but SHE PROMISED TO BE GOOD.
(But she is such a good solo character, it is very hard). She binds,
levitates, swims underwater, befriends animals, runs like a wolf, and hides
like a tree. She likes to explore and is very good at one handed slashing
and offensive magic use.

We look forward to meeting the other women of Everquest.

D and D (pun intended, hehe)

Ps. Yes, I am a women in real life, too. Probably one of the oldest
players of EQ.

Hi there, and welcome to EQ Women! This mailing list is
for women who play the game Everquest to meet eachother
and chat.

Please feel free to post an intro message with your
character's name, race, class, level and server
(and anything else you'd like).
For example:

"Hi everyone! I play Iuna, a 17th level wood elf druid
on Mithaniel Marr. I'm looking forward to
chatting on the list."

If you ever want to unsubscribe, go to the ONElist web site, at
www.onelist.com, and select the User Center link from the menu bar
on the left. This menu will also let you change your subscription
between digest and normal mode. This list is for
women only, so please respect this policy and unsubscribe if you
are not a woman!.
