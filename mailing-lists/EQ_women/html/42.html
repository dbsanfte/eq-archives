Greetings fellow gamers! It is nice to see that the stereotype of the
RPG fan is not always accurate! We are not all nerdy teenage
boys with nothing better to do then role dice :)

My main character is Aaldiana, a lvl 17 high elf cleric though she
does now have a little sister named Kina, who is a lvl 5 wood elf
bard. Both of my characters are on the Bristlebane server.

As for some recent topics:
For the most part, I am grouped with all males, both in game and
IRL. My personality just tends to lead me to that. I am an
outrageous flirt and have a great time doing it. I do not however
use that to any kind of advantage, not intentionally anyway. I just
RP and see where it leads.

My husband does not care since it is only an RPG. Both
characters do have SOs, though they are actually brother
characters of each other, so obviously the other player and I are
what mesh, and we have a great time doing it. We also trade
emails all the time. I can truly say that he is now one of my best
friends even though I've never met him in person :) We have been
playing together since Aaldiana was lvl 4. We are lucky to not only
have found each other, but there are also two other guys that we

joke that due to our frequent deaths, these guys have now seen me

I am proud to be a founding member of a brand new guild,

angle on the game too. I look forward to seeing how this aspect

Well, that's enough babble for now. I look forward to hearing more
