New subscription to the list here.

I don't know what's usual for an introduction, but here goes:

I'm an (alost) middle-aged computer person/working mom. I've been RPGing
since I was 11 years old (started in pre D&D era), and since D&D, AD&D,
Traveller, ICE, GURPS etc ... all came out when I was in high school to
college I pen & paper RPed/GMed/DMed a lot back then. I ran an all-female
group around 20 years ago in my college hometown which at one time included
my then 80-90-year-old (passed away now) conservative German immigrant
grandmother. She would visit and I really got to ask "Canasta or D&D?". :)
I also ran mixed and male-only (except for me) groups.

Due to time contraints of job and children I haven't done pencil & paper
games for at well over a decade, but do play on-line! I've played other
on-line RPGs (but not UO for more than 1 week) for years, and am now
playing in EQ. Since I never have enough time ... my characters "never
amount to much". But I have fun! My almost teen daughter has played
on-line with me in two other RPGs (supervised), and helps with the EQ
character creation. If I can ever get ADSL here, we'll be a mother &
daughter team on-line. :) Sometimes I think we have as much fun coming up
with the ideas for a character as actually playing them.

I like to experiment with character ideas and right now have no "main"
character. So far I like Enchanter & Bard. Though my Necromancer is
surprisingly fun to play. I sort of enjoy the amazement (do they even
belive me?) when I claim that it REALLY is a female player behind this
female character. I know thay don't belive me when I tell many of the
college age males that I've been playing/running RPGs longer than they've
been alive (and I'm female). ;) I mainly play on Erollisi Marr, but
occasionally play on the Test Server.

- Lora
