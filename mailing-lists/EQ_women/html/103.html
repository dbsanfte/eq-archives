This is a very good point.. I have done that much.. I hunt with someone so
when we start new characters together then we have the other one pass along
some coins to at least get starting spells.. I didn't even realize GM's
would give you a "leg up" kit, it's shocking actually. We do good to get
GM's to answer us.. I did get to talk to a programmer once though.. he's in
charge of the design of the spells.. He showed up on Erud's Crossing when I
was there alone.. I thought I saw someone then did a /who and I was the only
one there so I went to investigate of course, after asking him he told me he
was testing out new spells..I didn't actually get to see those or anything..
we did talk about several things though. I was surprised he did stay to talk
to me for a several minutes.. among other things was how you have to med
with that dratted book open.. I did get my 2 copper worth in about that.. he
said at least it was better than beta where you couldn't even chat when
medding.. that it stayed that way until a few other programmers decided to
play spell casters and they didn't like it.. he did say the meditate button
would be obsolete at some point and would just auto kick on when you open
the book, which is good for blondes like me who sometimes sit to med and
then run off to do something in the house real quick (gotta get that stuff
done at some point ladies.. hehe) and come back to find I forgot to push
that darn button.. hehe..

Another thing we did talk about was the friend list, I commented that I've
made many friends in test that I hunt with at certain areas and like to keep
in touch and find out where they are to see if I can find a group if my
partner isn't with me. I have to sometimes remove someone to put a new
friend on cause of the 20 limit.. he said yes it was something he wanted
changed.. and even asked how many I thought would be enough for me.. I said
at LEAST 2x as big if not more and he agreed.. hopefully that'll change at
some point. This guy had a some great stuff on.. when I asked about where
to get that sorta thing he said I couldn't.. that it was things he'd made
himself to test out.. must be nice to be able to do that.. All in all it was
a good chat and I was grateful he took the time to explain some things to me
about why things do what they do in the game.
