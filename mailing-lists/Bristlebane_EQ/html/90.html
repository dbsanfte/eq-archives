Coming to the crossroads from Kaladim, you see a split, left and right.
take the right path and when it turns first, keep going straight into the
hills. Follow the ravine until you see a tall golden tower. From time to
time three skeletons will spawn here. Keep going and the ravine will split
into a T intersection, take the left way and immediately take the first
break to the right and the ruins are just ahead. I hope that is clear
enough! hehe.
