On Saturday Oct 9th 1999 between the hour of 9pm and 10 pm eastern Lord
Brutl Lionsroar, The Lion of Halas, shall marry our sister Myrisa Truegard.

The marriage is to to take place just inside Western Karana near the river,
shortly after you zone from North Karana. Dynasty guild members can find
more information on the marraige at the Clan Brutal web site:
http://www.swafo.com/brutal or the Dynasty guild website at
http://members.xoom.com/bjhinkel/dynasty

All Dynasty Guild members are encouraged to attend this wedding as a show of
mutual support for our sister and for the future of both our fine guilds. A
large party shall commence upon the speaking of the vows and sealing of our
mutual guilds fates.

I will be leading a party of guild members (and anyone else that is
interested) from the Gates of Qeynos to the wedding site in Western Karana
near the river. Any and all are welcome - the route is safe for all levels
and will be a lot of fun. Big party expected . Zeffery will be there to cast
SoW if needed. Party will start forming at 7:30 pm eastern at the gates to
Qeynos. We will be leaving on or about 8 pm eastern for the wedding. Any
guild members in Halas or Everfrost needing help getting through Blackburrow
and on to Qeynos please email me (patty_halas@...) or give me a tell
in game.
