Bits of advice for these email lists...

Don't reply-as-attachment or forward-as-attachment to the group. I don't
think people getting the digest version will get these attachments.

If your email box starts getting spammy, consider signing up for the digest
version (compiles the whole day's group emails into one message).

If you reply to a specific topic, consider editing out some of the extra bits
so your message stays as efficient as possible.

**If you are going to give a SPOILER or discuss a QUEST, please state so in
the SUBJECT. This gives people the chance to skip your mail if they want
to.**

People getting the digest version will often seem to be "behind" the
conversation.

Each person's email address appears in their message, so if you want to
contact them directly about something you can do so.

Happy hunting!
