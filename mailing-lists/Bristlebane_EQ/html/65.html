Greetings people, I am Kahnan. I am a warrior of noble heart from the depths of Kaladim. With the powers vested in me by the father of dwarvenkind, Brell Serilis, i hunt down and kill the evils of the land, assist the helpless, explore the unknown, and get drunk. I love to chat about the times and ponder the workings of the universe anytime, and you may be asking me to keep quiet as i gab between swings of my mighty halberd while we hack at those foul smelling minotaurs. I am just, i am honest, and i've been told i brag heavily while stumbling about in a stupor. My loyalty is golden and share what i have. I despise greed and arrogance. I believe respect is the key to peace.

I am seeking a fellowship of friends to join in adventure throughout Norrath, i guarantee my company will be pleasant.

Kahnan of Kaladim
