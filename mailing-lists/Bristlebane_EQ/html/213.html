Subject: Enlightenment Gathering


There is to be an Enlightenment Gathering on the Bristlebane server on
Sunday, June 13th in the ever-popular Tunnel to North Ro Desert in the East
Commonlands. The events will commence 3:00 PM Pacific (5:00 PM Central). All
races and classes are welcomed to come and share in this festival of
knowledge.

(So far these are the events planned to take place. More may be added later.
The information for this gathering will be updated daily at
http://www.stonepool.com/hands. This event is sponsored by a newly forming
guild, The Hands of Quellious.)


Philosophy Discussion

All are invited to debate in a somewhat ordered discussion of the gods, war,
ignorance, and other important issues. (Of course this will be done in
character and will be a great opportunity for role-playing. We wish to also
argue some of the player written Norrathian history contained on the web
page above.)

Trade Discussion

We will devote a bit of time talking about each trade, including research.
Hopefully we will answer any questions and peak the interest of those who
havenï¿½t discovered the wonders of the different trades. (If you wish to lead
the discussion of a particular trade, especially if you are experienced,
please contact me, I will post a running list on the web page in a few
days.)

Language Lessons

This will mainly be devoted to sharing the normal race languages so that we
may all understand each other better. Any citizens wishing to share their
knowledge of the elder languages is certainly welcomed hold free classes,
but we ask that there be no selling of languages. Participants my tip their
teachers but (If you wish to share your language please contact me, I will
post a running list on the web page in a few days.)

Scavenger Hunt

This will be for some of our younger attendees (anyone 7th level or lower).
Participants may form groups if desired but must share their prize. All the
items will be found close to the tunnel (only in the zones adjacent to the
east commons). There will be prizes for all that partake in this quest.
(There will be a story to go with the hunt.)

Open Market

And of course as with every event in Norrath, there will be the selling of
goods. We would like mainly self-made goods to be sold here. Though the
selling of acquired items is not prohibited, we encourage tradesmen to make
a bigger showing. (The method of sales will be announced later on the web
page.)

the Enchatress on Bristlebane.
