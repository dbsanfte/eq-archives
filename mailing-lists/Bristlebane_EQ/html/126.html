I am a 7th level paladin in qeynos. I get a great deal of XP in the
sewers (aqueducts), you just have to know where to look. There is a
detailed map available on EQvault, at the entrance to the
bloodsabres' lair there are 4 skeletons that respawn regularly and
ALWAYS have items. I can take them on and get about 1/2 of a segment
on my xp bar from the fight (I would advise fighting them with 2
people, I can take all 4 at once by using my paladin lay hands
skill), as well as picking up anywhere from 2-5 gold. Also, kill the
large(not giant) spiders in the aqueducts. Their venom sacs sell for
4-5 gp and their silk sells for 1 gp. Also kill the shady
mercenaries, they give xp for lvls 6-8 and usually have dark black
capes that you can wear for AC 3 or sell for 4 gold. I even found a
skeleton one night that gave me a magic ring +5 HP. The gelatinous
cubes also give good XP and occasionally have random items that are
valuable.
ALSO, hang around the ruins in Qeynos, the skeletons and putrid
skeletons there give good xp. Aside from these, the Karanas and
Blackburrow are the staple xp places around qeynos. Hope somebody
finds this info useful; if anyone would like to add to this
information please do. If any 6-9 see me online and would like to
adventure, let me know.
