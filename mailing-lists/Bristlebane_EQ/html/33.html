myself to ye all. I am Lexcanium, a Human Paladin who has currently attained
the tenth numerical rank within his training. I can be found, generally, all
throughout Norrath, as I wander the land often, making my way from one place
to the next. Most often, as of late, I can be found on the beautiful
continent of Faydwer slaying the evil Orcs of the Clan Crushbone, which, by

a bloody pulp.

while, I am usually always free for some friendly chatter. Fare thee well,
and may we purge the wonderful lands of Norrath from all evil in given time!
