Nay mate, you don't wanna go at nine that place is RUTHLESS. The single
creatures aren't bad but they tend to come in trains... i've seen 4
necromancer acolyte's, 2 neophytes, 1 normal necro, half a dozen pets+,
two greater skeletons, 1 lesser mummy and a ghoul show up when you pulled
one creature with a dagger in the hall right beside the enterance... i
avoid that pit until i'm near level 14-16... (on the bright side corpse
retreival is easy =) ) Befallen is in the West commonlands... head out of
the Newbie gate at freeport and go through east commonlands on the left
hand wall... stay on wall in west commonlands until you see a hut, then
start looking down towards the base of the wall ... if its night you'll
see the big glowing maw of befallen... day times just look for a hole int
he ground... luck.
