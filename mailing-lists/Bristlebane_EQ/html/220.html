i have been getting positive feedback about having a weekly gathering at the
swashbucklers cave in east commons(also entrance to it from north ro). the
one change is to have it on sundays instead. most of the feedback i have
been getting is saying that more people could make it on a sunday, instead
of a saturday. so unless there is another big event being planned on a
specific sunday(*see below for details), from now on i propose to have a
trade festival every sunday at 12 EST from now on.

purpose of the trade festival:

-MAIN PURPOSE-
1)to buy and sell our wares. looking for a smith making banded? selling your
handcrafted bows? trying to sell that polished tomahawk at top dollar?
rubicite leggings? LJ tunics? bone armor? bring your rare and handcrafted
items.

-OTHER POSSIBILITIES-
2)for guilds to possibly talk about themselves and invite other playes to
become members.

3)have language lessons.

4)have rich players or guilds sponsor events with prizes.

this will only take off if we let others know about it. so spread the word
to all your friends, and to others while on line. every sunday at the
swashbucklers cave at noon EST.
