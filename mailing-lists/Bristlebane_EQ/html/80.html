Fellow troubled cleric,

I can only share with you my experience, but from what it sounds like there's
a simple fix - get bound to Freeport, find a nice big group that needs a
cleric like yourself, and go hunting tarantulas and mummies in the desert.
Ward undead works well in this case, and there are many groups formed all of
the time by the fishing huts. As for getting bound, ask Drugar, he bound me
to Freeport for only a platinum (and had the kindness to wait while I
stumbled about the maze that is Freeport city). If you do venture into the
deserts, I warn you to watch out for that character Dorn. He beat me up good,
and his buddies didn't look very friendly either.

Good luck,
