OK, here is the FULL scoop on this.

Halflings have the *racial ability* to sneak and hide. I don't know what
"skill level" this translates to, but for illustration lets say it is 75.

Depending on your class, you may or may not get the *class ability* to sneak
and hide. If your class does get it, it will be at a certain level (your
guild trainers will show you what level) and you cannot put more practices
into it until that level. Furthermore, using the skill will not increase the
skill until such a time as your class gets the skill. Argh, hope I'm being
clear.

More on this... lets say you have the racial skill at 75, and your class gets
the ability to learn the skill at level 10. At level 10 the skill would be
capped at (level*5)+5 or 55. So even though at 10th level your guild will now
train you in it, they wont because you are already maxed out in it.

I don't know any of the specific numbers, nor how often this wierd stuff
happens (although there are a number of races that get sneak or hide), but
this is how it has been explained to me. Use the skill if it helps you, but
don't expect it to improve at all until such a time as your guild would train
you in it.
