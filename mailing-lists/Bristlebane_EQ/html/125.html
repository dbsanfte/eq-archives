I am Heidrich, a 7th level Paladin. I reside in the city of Qeynos.
Of late, I have spent the majority of my time slaying lions and
grizzly bears in the Karanas with my fellow adventurer Rastaman, who
is an 8th level warrior. My point is, we started adventuring
together when we both hit level 5. We found that I got 65-70% as
much xp as he did for killing enemies in a group. Now I do not know
what the exact figure is, but that is what we found. That is why he
has progressed to level 8 and I still remain at 7. However, the
hybrid class benefits, in my opinion, far outweigh any extra xp-
gaining time.
