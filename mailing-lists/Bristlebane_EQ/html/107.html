Hail Bristlebane players!

I'd like to add something to the Bristlebane community, so here's what I've
learnt about skills.

Your skill cap is 5 + 5 x current level. So, a level 1 character has a skill
cap of 10 in each of his skills, and a level 10 character has a skill cap of
55. You raise your skills by using them (once you have attained the basics),
so yes, they wil go up if you practice/use them until you reach your level
cap.

Sneak is a tricky skill. Every time you use it you will turn invisible in
your external view and you will walk real slow but this does not mean that
it is working. I have heard that the only way to tell if sneak has worked is
to ask another NPC if you dissapeared from their view. I think the same is
true for the hide skill.

Armistar, Ranger of Tunare
