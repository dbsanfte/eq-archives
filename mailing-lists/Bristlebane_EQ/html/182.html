Required is a loose term here. Since a bard can always sing the same tune.
However, using the wrong instrument or singing a song that "requires" an
instrument doesn't produce the same effect as singing the song with the
proper instrument. For example, when singing Hymn of Restoration, the
Bard's healing song, you will gain roughly 3 hit points per "round".
However, while playing a stringed instrument (which is the "required"
skill), you will gain 5 hit points per round. Also, the effectiveness goes
up according to the Bard's abilities with the necessary skill.

I hope this explains it better.
