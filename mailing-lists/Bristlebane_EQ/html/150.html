Subject: Bristlebane North Qeynos Auction/Trade Show


I am going to see how many people we can get to participate in an
outdoor auction/trade show near the city of Qeynos on the Bristlebane
server. This was done on another server and was a rousing success.
I would like feedback on this; I am going to schedule for this Sunday
the 25th at 12 noon PST. All good-aligned citizens of Norrath would
be invited to trade or buy goods. Hopefully this way we can bring in
some rare items from the far reaches of Antonica as well as Faydwer
and Odus. I will be checking the replies compulsively and replying
to anyone who would like to help expand the scope of this event. Do
OOCs in every zone you are in, no matter where you are, as this way
we can get the participation of those who do not check EQvault or the
Bristlebane mailing list. Here are the specs:

North Qeynos Auction/Trade Show

Server: Bristlebane
Date: Sunday, April 25th @ 12 noon PST
Location: Outside North Gate of Qeynos

All good-aligned citizens of Norrath are welcome
Bring any items that you think will be good sellers,
as well as LOTS of money to buy the neat things that
your fellow adventurers will have. See you there!!
