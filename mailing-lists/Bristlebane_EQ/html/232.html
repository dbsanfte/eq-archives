In the tunnel from North Ro to East Commonlands, there is no danger for a
necromancer by NPCs. I am a Dark Elf Necromancer and go at will between the
two zones via this tunnel. I've even traded with the only NPC there and
have not been attacked. A gnome such as yourself should be more liked than
me, thus I don't think you should have any problem. Thus your only concern
is getting through Freeport to the tunnel, which isn't very hard either. If
you have Gather Shadows, then use it and you won't have to worry about
guards or any other Freeport citizen. Head south from the South gate of
Freeport (actually within East Freeport zone) and enter North Ro, then
follow the right zone wall and you'll find the tunnel easily. Keep going
until you find an open area with the Swashbuckler inside (and hopefully
everyone else that is there to trade). The alternate route is to stay
invisible and make your way from East Freeport to West Freeport and then to
East Commonlands where you can follow the left zone wall and you'll find the
tunnel inset in a hill. Shouldn't be too hard.
