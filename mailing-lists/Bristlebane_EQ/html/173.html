Ok... earth elementals have the most hit points, but
generally are the weakest offensively; fire elementals
are the best offensively but generally have the fewest
hit points; airs are generally the best defensively,
and have hit points and offense somewhere in the
middle... also airs have a nasty habit of casting
invisibility on themselves (which serves no useful
purpose that I can see, and makes it durn hard to know
when they have stopped following you... as pets
frequently do). Waters... I really have no experience
with, so can't really comment on them.
