Well, almost right, but not quite... Everyone has two
combat skills in addition to their chosen weapon
skills known as offense and defense... offense, as it
rises, allows you to get the most out of your weapon,
increasing both damage potential and likelihood of an
attack succeeding. Defense, on the other hand, allows
you to get the most out of your armor, and as it
increases, so does the effectiveness of your chosen
armor.

Therefore, a level one character could, indeed, put on
a full set of plate mail, but it would have nowhere
near the same effectivenes as the exact same suit on a
20 level character, but would still be a darn sight
better than any other level one's armor class running
around without plate mail... so good armor is
definately of use to lower level characters, just not
nearly as effective as it will become as the charatcer
develops.
