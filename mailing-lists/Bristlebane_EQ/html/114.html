Subject: Disturbed Guards on the Wall look on as...


...

Two guards lean over the battlement to wittness a scuffling figure in the
dust and dirt near the fortress walls of Freeport. They must listen closely
to hear his dizzy mutterings..."... ratty ratty... h-h-h .. r-ratt rat rat.
h-h-h.. shhhhh... must... shhhhh.. still... still... ratty... h-h-h..."Lying
weak, disfigured and still in the desert dust the sickly figure feigns-death
in a desperate attempt to lure a possible meal. Too weak to move, too weak
to beg, the hint of survival instinct is the only clue that intelligence once
resided in the wretched, blood and dust heap of a man."shhhhh... shhhhh...,"
the sound is like a swamp outgassing, until... "Arr-ha!" Sudden and quick the
form closes on it's prey like a predatory plant... weak arms and legs curl to
the chest to encircle the curious visitor. And then, too weak of fist or leg
to seriously injure his prey, and lacking the needed teeth to be menacing,
the reeking pile of human waste wrestles furiously with the mottled
beast!"Grrrr.. Grrrrgrrrrrr.... h-h-h-h.. grrrr grrrr. RAA... grrrr..
ahhhhaaaa! grrrrr...," the wrestling match goes on! And then, seizing his
rat-assailant firmly between his two boney hands, and with a ferocity never
seen by innocent eyes, the broken man begins beating his captive over and
over and over again against his own head! ...beating the biting and clawing
beast until his own forehead and face is covered in blood, who knows whose...
and the match goes on. "Arrrr... grrr...." ::squeek:: ::squeek:: ::squeek::
:squeeeek::Dear God, the guards each think quietly in turn. They've seen
examples of the effect of poverty upon mankind, but this is insane! And then,
in a gesture of supreme jest, one guard reaches over and does the quick
"push/pull" gag on his friend.
