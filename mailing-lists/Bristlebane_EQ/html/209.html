Subject: possible weekly trade festival...


i would like to suggest a weekly trade festival at the swanky swashbuckler's
cave. (it is located in east commons, with an entrance to it also from north
ro) i think that this would be a great place for players to bring thier rare
or hand made items and try and sell or trade them. looking to sell that
rare polished tomahawk, or that hand made high quality bow? looking to find
a smith selling banded armor, or trying to find that peice of bronze plate
that you are missing? just found some rubicite armor and want to fetch a top
price for it? i think that this game is already awesome, but we can make it
better with weekly events like this, or other events. i suggest it be held
every saturday at 3PM EST. this should be a time when most players can get
on.

other possibilities for this weelky trade festival can be a place for guilds
to try and recruit other players. players can get together and discuss
quests or what certain lore items are for (for example i have a pirates
earing, a lore item, and have no idea what is it for). maybe rich guilds
and/or rich players can even hold some contests with prizes.

if this sounds like a good idea, please reply to the list with your comments
and/or let players know about it in the game. verant has given us a grand
start, but it is up to us to make it even better!!
