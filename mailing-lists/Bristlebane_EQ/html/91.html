Subject: Some races/places have all the luck


After dabbling in a few races in a few homelands, I really start to hate
Freeport and the commonlands.

Did you know that a Troll turn in 3 items (two found on a 1st level monster
almost all the time, and another available on 4th level monsters occasionally)
and get an item worth 2+ gp? And they can do this repeatedly? Not to mention
that the 1st and 4th level monsters will often have other items worth 1+ gp on
them? Where else in Norrath can you find 1st level monsters dropping cloth
armor?

I had to hunt high and low in Freeport area for containers, yet in Everfrost
it seems like every other gnoll pup or goblin whelp drops a box, bag or belt
pouch. Oh, and there are 10,000 decaying skels which seem to have a 1 gp
weapon about 25% of the time. Also, of the races I've seen it seems that the
Barbarians have the easiest shot at armoring themselves. Cubs for bearskins
are about 4th level or so, and are everywhere. Humans have to wait to kill
tougher pumas. The Faydark folk have to fight over the 1 wolf that seems to
spawn every 10 minutes.

Oh well, just my little rant for the day.

On a lighter note, my most excellent traveling companions and I have set out
on a little adventure away from Freeport. Currently we are on Faydwer and
have seen many cities and many zones. I highly recommend travel for anyone of
sufficient levels.

Phoeble, Wandering Monk of Freeport
