There are definitely more wisps in some zones than in others.

On Antonica the zones I know contain wisps:

Misty Thicket (infrequent)
West Commonlands (frequent, but one dangerous place to hunt)
East Commonlands (frequent)
West Karena (frequent but scattered)
Qeynos Hills (infrequent)

I am sure there are more, but these are the ones that immediately
come to mind. A VERY effective way of hunting wisps is to pair a
combine-wielding ranger with a cleric. The ranger can be the tank, cleric
can heal, attack with spirit hammer and burn the wisps. The advantage of a
ranger is even in zones where wisps are not common, he can track them.

I like hunting wisps at night, where you can pick and choose those
wisps that give off light vs. the ones that dont. I personally get about 1
greater lightstone to 4 lessers when hunting wisps. You can also tell if a
wisp has a lesser or greater lightstone by the radius of the light.

Lago the Wisp-Hunting Bard of Bertoxxulos(sp.)
