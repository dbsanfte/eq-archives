Q: Please tell me if I've just got to insist this way or if wisps in other zones will carry greater lightstones more often?
A: Greater lightstones are not more common on different wisps in different places. It is totaly random. I have hunted wisps in Gr Fay, Misty Thicket, Karanas, Q Hills and Kerra Isle.

Q: Are there zones with more wisps?
A: In my opinion, the best zone is Kerra Isle. Have to watch for campers, though. Try to either group with some campers or see if you can join up with a ranger for tracking.
