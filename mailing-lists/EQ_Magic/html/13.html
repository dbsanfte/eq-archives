> Many unelightened warriors have walked up to me and asked to be

I have two mage characters- a 7th lvl High Elf Magician and a 5th lvl
Barbarian Shaman, both on the Rallos Zek server.

With the 7th lvl Magician, as long as I have the mana and the time, or the
extra supplies, I will summon food/water/bandages for free. I feel (and
this is my own personal ethic here) that it is of questionable ethics to
charge for this service. A few reasons for this.

1. When the person camps, the food is going to disappear, so it is not
really, IMHO, a tangible sellable object.

2. I feel I have an obligation to help other "good" races in their
endevours. Providing free food and water is an easy way to do this,
especially when hunting out in an area removed from civilization.

3. When I am in a jam, I don't want to be expected to pay for help.
Likewise, when others are in a jam, I do not want to charge
them.

I have not gotten the Bind Affinity spell yet and have not decided how I
will handle that. I think that can be looked at differently, since it is a
huge mana soak and it is something that makes someone's life easier. It is
not a bare necessity.

The going rate I have seen for binding tends to fall in the 5g.
neighborhood, although I have seen people advertising to do it for a plat.

With the 5th level Barbarian Shaman, I will cast buffs and heals for free
and often unsolicited. A little random kindness and all of that. It also
helps me practice my spells and gain goodwill in what can be a fairly
rough neighborhood for a newbie.

Just my two cents.. :)
