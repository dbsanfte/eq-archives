It is yes Ice Comet.
The NPC specifically states that he will reward you
with the Rune of the Astral and the Rune of Frost.

This is my understanding of the quest. The level 49
wizard who wants this spell must first go to the NPC
and obtain the book that gives riddles to the
locations of the spokes of the wheel, and then is
given a container to put them in. He must go then and
collect ALL of the items himself. They are No Drop, No
Rent items, as is the book and the container. So it's
one long night for that wizard but the reward, I
think, most would agree is worth it. I believe one
must simply combine the container once all the spokes
have been placed in it, and hand it back to the NPC,
typical of most quests of this sort.

I have come across one of these spokes myself, simply
named a Rod, wizard only item, does nothing by itself,
and it was lying on the floor of the room I found it
in, not loot, and protected by a few simple creatures
for a level 49 wizard to handle. The most difficult
part it seems, is deciphering the clues given, and the
descriptions they give for the locations can be stated
as vague at best.

In short a long, complicated, and quite worthwhile
quest for those few wizards who have reached 49th
level. Now that you've done it, pray that you have a
high enough research skill, and a good roll of the
dice :)
