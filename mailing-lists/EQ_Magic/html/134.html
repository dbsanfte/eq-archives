Soloing has its good side and bad side. I have been in a few really good
groups that I have enjoyed being in. But I find it hard to find a group I
actually want to join. I think it is safer to solo than to play in a group
that doesn't work well together.

Ever been in a group that wanted to go into an area that was too high
level? How about a group that where there is no real leader and people
attack things wily-nily, thinking that someone is covering their backs,
only to find out that there are now 6 yellow monsters for a 4 person
group?

Good groups are hard to find. I don't mind if I level slower because I
have to sit and rest more than I would in a group or because I can only
take blues instead of yellows or reds. But I did very much mind losing 1/2
bubble or more everytime I died.

I understand that the game is suppossed to be challenging and is meant to
be geared for people to work together. But sometimes, I would prefer to
work alone and I don't think I should be penalized for it.
