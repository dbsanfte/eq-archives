personally, ive never seen the wizard class as a seeker of items and
knowledge any more than any other class. it even says in the manual that
wizards are a damage-dealing class, that is their specialty. ive heard the
arguement before that this class or that class should have this, the fact is
that wizards can nuke a monster in short order, faster than any other class.
the only thing that balances that fact is that they have to regain their
mana afterward, a slow process. to speed it up would unbalance the game, i
feel. i have a necro, and i can speed up my mana recovery. however, the
majority of my spells are damage over time (dot), so i NEED a tank- i cant
go toe to toe with an opponent very long. necros are also hated in most
places, and as a tier'dal necro i have it doubly so.
final point. who do you think would win in a duel, a L? wizard, or a L? of
any other pure spell caster? i would say the wizard, just for the power of
the spells. with a pet it would be different, but im saying all things
being equal.
