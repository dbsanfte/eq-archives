Fix your notice:

http:\\www.tip.it/wizard
^^^^
Will not work on any decent browser (unless you count IE 4.0 as the only
decent browser)... HTML *DOES* specify that the `/' character is the
separator.

As a result, on any www page, the above produce
http://www.some.other.location/page/\\www.tip.it/wizard
and the result is... well a 404 Not Found error...
