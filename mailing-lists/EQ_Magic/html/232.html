Pets are ONLY in highhold. Get used to this type of thing. You were given
those illusions for a reason... you are going to need every one of them.
Several spells are ONLY in Erudin in the Library... Mostly all of your
higher level illusions and Illusion Halfling. The enchant metal series is
only in Neriak's Third gate Library. A few spells are only in Ak anon, and
a few more only in Felwithe. For the most part, the thicken mana series is
ONLY in the brownie camp in L Feydark. You will be visiting just about
every zone in Norrath, looking for spells. Best idea is to make good
friends with a druid...lots of travel.
