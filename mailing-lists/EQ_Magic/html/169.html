A sapphire platinum necklace requires a platinum bar (~100pp) and sapphire (also
~100pp), so the base cost, materials only is 200pp! Don't expect a jeweler to
make one for you without asking for at least 500pp.

The sapphire necklace is +7 str/+7 iq. Not bad at all. Pricey but the item is very
powerful.

The Rose Ring is "cheaper". Platinum again at 100pp, plus a mere quartz at a pittance
of 1pp :) - expect a sell price of 200-250pp. It creates a ring of +4 intelligence.

Given the progression, I expect the skill required for these items to be about 100,
i.e. being done by only a very few jewelers; most will drop the trade as it becomes more
and more expensive to train oneself.

I think that jewelry is the money sink Verant was aiming for. Items are now competitive
for their price, and the investment required will weed out jewelers as levels
progress. Now, if tailors could make special leather armors... or tinkers good
items.
