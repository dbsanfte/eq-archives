Well the reason this is a necro ability only is, a necromancers specialty
is the manipulation of life energies & in the changing of living energy into
something different. Such as changing someone's life energy into your life
energy thus the life-drain. Or giving life to something dead. Its only
natural that the necro would have figured out how to change his own life for
more of the mana that they covet so much. Wizards on the other hand do covet
mana but is not a master of manipulating life energy. He turns mana into
brute force & damage its not subtle like necro's are think of a wizard as a
broadsword & a necro as a scalpel if a wizard tried this they might just
take all there life. A cleric on the other hand changes mana into life he's
not very good at taking it away or changing it into anything at all.
Magicians change mana into objects they don't really manipulate life at all
either. Enchanters are the masters of illusion no life manipulation there
either. All other classes borrow spell from these classes. It was a bit long
sorry but I think it is correct.
