One ritual that is very important to this group is what
we call "Engaging the Brain". This ritual begins with
the Reading of the Group Description. For example,
"Everquest list for wizards, magicians, enchanters, necromancers and other
magic using classes (clerics, druids, shamans). where we can excxange info
on spells use, quests, and items related to our class; combat tactics, race
diferences, bugs and new ideas to improve spells and magic and all other
related things are welcome."

The key to this ritual is the first word in the group
description. Engaging the Brain involves meditating
upon this word, and seeking to understand it before
randomly spamming people one does not know. If a
person performs this ritual correctly, it will become
clear that the word "Everquest" denotes a popular
massively multiplayer online computer game which does
not at all depend on the phases of the moon. Other words
& phrases which might have offered clues to this fact include:
quests, combat tactics, bugs, race differences, class.
