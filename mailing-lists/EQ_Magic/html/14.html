Well said. Yes, just the sheer casting of the spells helps the castor. And
something so simple, to the mage/Shaman, such as creating food and water is
a HUGE deal for someone who can not do these things. And hopefully, as you
stated, when we do need some help, this person will remember our kindness
and step in and do something simple to them that is not so simple to us like
Swing that Sword or cast that Massive DD spell to save our butts in a time
of crisis.

Also, as you state, if we log or camp, the food and water we have provided
to them, no matter HOW much we gave them, dissappears, so it doesn't seem
right to charge for an intangible, especially when Server TimeOuts are still
fairly common (at least for me)

Now, binding on the other hand, as you said, is a HUGE mana drain and a HUGE
benefit to someone. You see people selling armor and weapons that they have
made or found all the time because others find that it will benefit them.
So charging for binding doesn't seem out of the question because it is a
permanent effect.

Question, does someone have to submit to being Bound or can you BIND them
without their consent? i don't knwo why you would do this, just curious.
