Here is my 2 Cents regarding Druid Soloing :

Lake of Ill Omen is one of the best places for this. I got there
around Level 16, and I am currently level 23 (half away from 24), and
it is awesome.

I must admit that at level 16, I grouped with others and killed
goblins and skels, and if we had a good group we did some Sarnark
recruits. This got me easily to level 20. After which I started
soloing more often.

With Sabertooths in the Lake of Ill OMEN, these are my 2 main
fighting styles:

A) Cast Snare, Cast DOT (Damage over time spell) then run around,
when DOT runs out, cast it again, maybe blast it with a Careless
Lightning here and there. But mostly use your DOT.

B) Cast DOT, Cast Snare, Cast FEAR ANIMAL, Run after the Sabertooth
and wack it with your weapon, when your DOT/FEAR/SNARE runs out,
recast it, and continue till he is dead.

Both the above work great, I find that RED's resist alittle to much,
so I only stuck with Yellow and below. Using your track, it is easy
to find a lot of sabertooths floating around. The best place for
solo'n I think in Lake of Ill Omen is at a place they call the
PILLARS, its easy to find, just give a shout, most people are nice
here.

Don't worry about banking, cuz you can just bind your self near a
zone, either at Frontier Moutains Zone, or Fironia Vie Zone, and then
Teleport over to ButcherBlock, do you banking, then Gate back and get
back to killing.

If you ever get bored here, just head on over to Warsilk Woods (its
about 1 er 2 zones away) and there is a great Giant Fort there. I
know what you are thinking, GIANTS are you crazy, but I went over
there for a bit around level 20 - 22, and it is one of the safest
spots I ever found, even though there is tonnes of Giants lurking
around. If you get into a group, the Fort layout is pretty simple,
and the spawn points are easily visible, you can pull 1 at a time,
and kick butt. At level 20, most where Yellow or Above, some green,
but again, they it was easy to control the amount of Giants you pull
to your group. I think Solo'n here might be easy as well, as the
Giants usually stick to there spawn points, and only a few walk
around. Another great thing about this zone is that it is usually
empty except fer 1 or 2 group killing giants (at least on Torv thats
the way it is)

Anyways, that is my opinion. The first time I went to Kunark at
Level 16, I was very worried, but after gaining level 23 kind of
easily (i believe i've only died like 2 times), I think the next
character I make will be coming here as well. Later all.
