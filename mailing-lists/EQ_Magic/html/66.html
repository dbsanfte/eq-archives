Ok, several of our low level spells are broken or useless.. And there
is talk that they are to be replaced.. It seems to me that a useful
replacement would be a low level spell that gauges accurately the ability
level of a summoned pet..
I mean we are supposedly the masters of our own trade, and yet we don't
even know most times upon summoning a new pet what level it is.. This
makes no sense.. I understand the point for randomness, but not for
ignorance..
A simple spell that popped up a text message that gave at least the
critters numerical level.. Would be great.. Better (but less likely) would
be a quick gage of base abilities like given when looking at an item..
Personally I think HP, Atk, and AC along with level and maybe special
abilities would be perfect, as it would put to rest all the debate over
whether or not the different types of elementals are really different..
I'd love to see this go into effect.. As a programmer I think this
would be one of the less difficult suggestions to implement (it's just
reading info already in the system and reporting it through text.. No major
mods needed).. I'm thinking a petition is in order.. But have no clue how
to get one started...

Any ideas/comments?
