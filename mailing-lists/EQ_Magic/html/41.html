<b>Specialization  New Functionality<br>
<br>
</b>The various Specialization skills will be implemented such that if a
spell is cast in, for example, the abjuration family, the
Specialization  Abjuration skill will be checked (if present) and if
successful, will decrease the amount of mana used to cast the
spell.   The Specialization skills are as follows: 
Specialization  Abjuration, Specialization  Alteration,
Specialization  Conjuration, Specialization  Divination, and
Specialization  Evocation.<br>

<b>Scheduled Changes<br>
<br>
</b>Identify: The identify spell was originally going to reveal the
statistics of an item (Damage, Weight, Weapon Speed, Ac, etc.).  We
decided, however, to leave in the Beta ability of right-clicking an item
for information.  The identify spell will soon be used for revealing
the lore, if any, associated with item.  Lore will only be found on
high magic or Artifact level items  an item with lore will typically
be rare. <br>
<br>
Detect Magic: This spell will be replaced with a new spell sometime in
the near future.  All players who have purchased this spell will
have it automatically updated into their spell books.<br>
<br>
Whirl till you hurl: This spell works as intended and at full
effect.  There is a display error, where a ∩┐╜monster∩┐╜ will not be

Illusion: Air Elemental, Water Elemental and Fire Elemental: There is a
display error that causes Enchanters who cast the illusion to look like
Earth Elementals.  This is only a display error  all NPC

will be fixed shortly.<br>
