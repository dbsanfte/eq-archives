your visible/invisible state isn't considered. Scenario: Find a monster in
a train (preferably the head monster) and hit him with the weakest spell
you have. Check that he keeps on moving. Then cast invisibility.

Then see the monster come back straight at you after the hapless quarry
has zoned.

(affecting a monster in any way places you in its consider list)
