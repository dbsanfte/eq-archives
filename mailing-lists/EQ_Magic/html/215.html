According to Jason Kelley:
> Ive just hit level 12 with my human magician and was wondering if
> someone could tell me what a good creature is to kill loot and
> experience wise at this level?

Wisps. If you're on the East Coast, try hitting the West Commonlands
(lotsa wisps). If you're on the West Coast, jump on the Qeynos-Erudin
boat and land on Kerra Isle. Summon your trustworthy magic weapon,
and you're set.

West Commonlands has a good supply of wisps, plus some additional
excitements, such as young kodiaks and the occasional kill-stomp
giants and griffins, while Kerra Isle is boringly monotonous (one
main spawn point for wisps, with a cycle of four wisps, a second
somewhere else, with one wisp for the same duration. Don't touch
the beetles, the Kerrans kill you for this, don't touch the spiders
they all gang on you, and there are lots).

If you play off-hours (not during prime time), Kerra Isle is sometimes
desert, so you can hog the two spawn points all by yourself. If you
have other wisps hunters, rotate the spawn point, or alternate between
the spawn and the zombie sailors in the water.

An enchanter friend spent the entire level 12 & 13 there, levelling
each day (playing three-four hours), and getting about 40pp/level.
