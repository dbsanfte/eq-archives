You are very much incorrect on the kill information. There have been
several posts by Brad discussing switching from individuals to group
totals for kill determination. As it stand currently damage is
calculated based upon individuals, not groups for determining who gets
the kills.

The below is commentary clipped from the archive section of EQ Vault
(direct URL is http://www.eqvault.com/archive/19990627.shtml look in
28JUN date for the info), which clearly shows they are calculating on
individuals. As Brad mentions they are looking to move to a group
aggregate for kill determination but I have yet to anything showing
they have made that move.

Brad on Kill Stealing
Verant VP Brad McQuaid responded to a comment on the subject of Kill
Stealing made by Aetherial on our General Board. Here's an excerpt from
the original post followed by Brad's complete response:
I understand your point about kill stealing the OTHER way (ie groups
stealing from individuals) I don't believe it could be as serious a
problem as currently individual casters stealing from groups. The
reason is simple, your developers obviously have taken great pains to
make this a group oriented game so given the choice I would favor the
groups. To favor only certain classes in their ability to solo is a
serious imbalance. Going the other way simply levels the playing field
for all.
Hrm. Maybe we'll try it the group aggregate damage way, and see how
people like it and whether it helps with kill stealing or not. I'll
just make sure my programmer comments out the old code so he can put it
back if necessary :)
Give us a few weeks and I'll try to get this change implemented on a
trial basis.
-Brad
