You may want to take a look at the mana calculators on
http://www.baelish.aust.com/everquest as you get higher in level using
items which add to either your wisdom or intelligence will have more
benefit. For example by getting ride of my silver jaded rings (+5
mana/HP +1 AC each) and switching to two star rose quartz rings (+2 int
each) I was able to get a total of 18 additional mana as opposed to the
10 provided by the rings (8 additional mana in my favor). Not to
mention the added benefit of having a higher intelligence (faster skill
raises, etc.).
