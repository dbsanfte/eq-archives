When I used to solo with my gnome necro in crushbone(level 6+7 at the time) I
would always stay right in that big open area right as you exit the tunnel
entrance. Tons of centurions spawn there and on the other side of the river
and whenever I would get attacked by a larger creature by pet would buy me
those few precious seconds for me to make it to the zone. Besides if
something big enough to kill you wanders here it is already part of a train
so you dont have to worry about people getting mad for training to zone. Also
if someone shouts train to zone or something to that extent feel free to jump
in the river and hide there is nothing shameful about not standing up to
creatures 6 levels above you. Hope this helps.
