Subject: General Question about getting items into inventory.


Ok, I know that this is a General question but it is also related to magic
because of the summoning spells many of us get.

This was the history of how I learned to get things into my inventory.
Either off of things I have killed or my dead body doing a loot.

1. I would left click the item in the left window and drag it to an empty
slot in my inventory and drop it there. And when I got bags, I would open
the bag and drop it into an empty slot.
This method was very slow and time consuming, especially as I got
more armor pieces and had to individually place them in each slot on my
body. Also, stackable items gave me problems becuase it would be picky if I
didn't click in the EXACT place to stack.

2. YEAH! I learned that if you just put an item over your picture and drop
it on "you" then it would automatically either go into your
inventory/bag/stack or on your body in the appropriate place.
This was HUGE and saved a lot of time and clicks, but I still was
doing a lot of click on the left, click on me on the right for each and
every peice.

3. OH YAH!!! I learned that if you RIGHT click an item on the left, it
automatically drops it into your inventory in any available open slot, even
inside bags.
This does not work for armor items getting onto your body, though.
BUT, you can fill up your six slots real quick and then just pull them out
of your inventory and drop them on your picture which reduces the left/right
action A LOT!

Ok, now on to the question I guess, When you are summoning items
(Food/Water/Bandages/etc.) The item gets places on your cursor. From that
point, I click my persona button, drop it on me character, hit ESC, and then
resummon another item, click the persona button, drop it in my inventory and
hit esc... and so on..

Is there an easier way to do this from the Wide screen?

Bayne, Shaman Barbarian frusturated with the tediousness of summoning water.
