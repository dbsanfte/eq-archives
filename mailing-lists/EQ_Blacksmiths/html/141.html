Hi All,

This is going to sound like a really odd question, but I'm very curious as
to what the attitudes and opinions are of other blacksmiths in the game. I
play on Innoruuk server, and just recently got my blacksmithing skill up to
104. I was contacted by a potential customer who inquired about my prices
for a banded tunic. I replied, but it took him several minutes to respond. I
assume that he was negotiating with other blacksmiths, because he messaged
me a few minutes later to tell me I had good prices and that he would buy
one. I made the transaction, but was then contacted by another blacksmith
who seemed outraged that I sold my wares for so cheap. Because he knew of
the exact price, I assume that the seller had also been speaking to him and
that the price was discussed. So apparently, this blacksmith felt that I had
undercut him out of a commission. He then told me that the prices were fixed
among master blacksmiths, at roughly one half chain. When I asked who else
was using these prices, I was told everyone but given no specific examples
of well-known blacksmiths who agreed to this price-fixing.

I dont necessarily disagree with setting prices. A lot of money goes into
getting skill up; much more than simply buying a full suit of banded (am I
the only smith who started wearing banded well after many other people of
similiar level?), as well as the cost of failures, which I still have quite
a few of and which eat up profit rather quickly. But I don't want to be
overcharging for these items and losing customers to people who obvioulsy
have a much better clue than I do.
