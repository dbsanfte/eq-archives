A couple of things about price 'fixing' and those doing or trying to do it:

First of all, what they are doing is just like any union does... Let's make
everyone the same, so that everyone can make the same money... They might as
well be NPC's for Tunare's sake!

HOWEVER, your (higher level players) type is one of the reasons they may be
doing it... Yes, you can easily afford to buy banded, or chain, or even
plate mail armor... As you said, you really have no need for plat except for
learning things like smithing... so, no skin off your nose... You are higher
than level 20 and easily make more money off of better mobs...

BUT, you have to realize that some of these smiths are MUCH lower than
you... they poured their time and efforts into smithing in the beginning
instead of gaining experience like others did...

There are some I have seen that are only level 7-10 characters that are
master smiths!! Now I myself didn't know you could do that! But for these
players the only way they can make 10-20 plat a day, like you do, is to make
a good profit on the armor they make... And, they know just like the oil
companies in RL that if they stick together they can control the market...
I have even heard that when they 'blackball' another smith that when he
tries to sell armor, the immediately post prices MUCH LOWER (sometimes
taking a loss) just so you won't be able to even sell yours!

Now, I hope that the prices don't keep coming down and down and down
either... because it is costing me a FORTUNE to learn smithing (I am up to
level 54 now... woo hoo!) and I WILL be trying to factor that into my costs
when I make a piece... I figure if it costs me 100pp to get good enough,
then I need to make a 'profit' of 1-5 plat on each piece of banded in order
to break even within the time it takes to make 30-50 different pieces of
armor... OR, I can charge MORE and do it even faster... Hey, after all,
Enchanters do it, so do tailors... Where does it say we should 'give it
away' to people... people appreciate what they work for and earn... not
what's just given to them... So yes, be cheaper in price if you feel you
must to sell your wares, but don't give it away at cost... because some of
us don't have 100pp sitting in the bank...
