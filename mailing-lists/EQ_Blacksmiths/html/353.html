that for all of our well being we need to band ( no pun intended ) together
to make this work. If you want to sell, or even give away the occasional
piece for cost or slightly above cost at least keep it under the table. I do

believe me. Sell Bob a set of bracers for 8pp because he's down on his luck
or whatever reason you choose, but tell Bob to keep it to himself, but tell
him what you would like in return is for him to advertise for you, and in
return you will give him better pricing, this works!!! I was in gfay and did
this, sent out my auction, next thing I knew "Bob" was shouting about what a
great smith and guy I was on my prices etc etc, I made 5 complete suits of
banded within about 90 mins, made 600pp, and in return I gave "Bob" his
tunic at cost.
