The reason people differ on Lanterns vs Skewers is price. Lanterns are
expensive but raise your level faster. With Skewers, you wont gain very
fast and fail a lot, but they are cheaper to make. You should probably test
it both way to see which you prefer. It is going to be different for
everyone. I say this because WIS, INT, and CHA all affect how many you
make, how fast you go up and how much it will cost. I personaly think agree
with Paul's order to do things, but you should decide for yourself.
