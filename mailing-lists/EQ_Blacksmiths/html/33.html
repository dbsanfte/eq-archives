Ok as everyone else mentioned Neriak sounds like a good place to buy
supplies if your a DE, Troll, or Oger.

If your looking to get some exp in the process and have some fun. I know
that during the beta the Mudites in the Beholder zone sometimes gave ore as
treasure. I was fighting them in a group of level 14+, and they were
hitting us HARD. I'd say get to about 16th to fight in Beholder and even
then watch out for the Gorge Minotaurs and evil eyes.
The Mudite's can blind and they hit pretty hard. They have Elders that can
cast spells, and I have never seen them running around by themselves.
Always were in a group when I encountered them.
