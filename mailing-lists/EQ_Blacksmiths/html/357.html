1. Metal bits requires 2 small ore chunks and 1 flask of water. This is why you are getting that message.

2. Some areas are better than others for certain trade skills. For example, Surefall Glade is great for fletchers and bowyers. Qeynos is not very good to smiths. It's a long way to find ore and you'll have to decide whether or not it's worth the effort. As an example, I myself play an ogre in Oggok. We have some molds and one forge but no ore. (We also have no file or scalar molds which makes using the available smithing guides useless). The troll city of Grobb next door has ore, but no molds or a forge. So basically when I was learning to forge, I had to constantly run over to Grobb to buy ore, then go back to Oggok to use the forge. I still have to do that actually as I have to make all my own sheets for armor as no one sells them. It's a hassle but it's been worth it. My skill is now over 100 and I'm one of the only master smiths in the Oggok/Grobb region, meaning I have the entire ogre/troll market practically to myself.
