Well, I have managed to answer my own questions regarding this. The answer
is at level 136, all items seem to give me the trivial message except
cloaks. In case you are wondering, a great place to level your smith up
from 122 to 135 is Serpent's Supply in High Pass Hold. There is a forge
inside the place right next to a vendor with needle and thimble molds and a
vendor with water in the same building (since I brought my own metal bits, I
have no idea if an ore vendor is nearby). This arrangement is important
because sewing kits are a container and won't fit in a backpack or other
container. This arrangement allowed me to literally turn around every few
seconds and sell my sewing kits to a vendor. After leveling up, I returned
to Freeport and had much better success making armor. Oddly, the mantles
still had a 50% succes rate even though they are trivial. Thankfully,
success rate on other items is very high now. I did blow like 30pp before I
noticed I wasn't getting the failure message for making cloaks (ran out of
money) but rather the "these items do not combine..." message. Doh!!! I
was using 1 water, 1 mold, 3 metal sheets. Not really my fault as my guide
says 3 sheets as do 2 other guides I found at various sites. I found one
site that says 2 sheets (is this correct?) I managed to sell a couple
pieces of armor before logging out and will try this tomorrow (1 water, 1
mold, 2 metal sheets).
