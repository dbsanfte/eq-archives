One thing everybody must learn, prices can ALWAYS go down and NEVER go up. Don't
take never literally, though. We need to raise prices on banded. 7 for gorgets,
8 for bracers, 11 for masks, 13 for helmets, 12 for sleeves, 12 for mantle, 20
for mail, 20 for cloak, 12 for belt, 19 for leggings, 11 for boots. These prices
give us significant profit on 100% success and help even out the profits for
when we fail. If ALL of us sell at these prices and stick to them (don't let
them drop you more than a plat), then the 'low priced' banded sellers will be
very scarce indeed. Then you can see the 'wait an hour' frustration on the
buyers end. If someone auctions they are willing to pay 5 plat for a banded
bracer for over an hour, they'll be frustrated enough to give up and pay the
damn 8 plat. If we have a set price that majority follows, then we will still
sell. While the discount smith might sell 10 gorgets at 2 and a half plat profit
each, we'll sell at LEAST 5 gorgets in the same amount of time for 5 and a half
plat profit each. They might be making 25 plat, but we're making 27.5 and have
less chances for a failure :) If we group together and work as one group of
dedicated smiths, we can only win. If we become bitter enemies over who has the
lowest price, the price war will just destroy us all. We will ruin the value of
smithing. NO MORE DISCOUNTS!!! :)
