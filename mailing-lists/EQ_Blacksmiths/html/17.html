There are two books for blacksmithing, one is "blacksmith" something
(sorry, forgot) and the other is called "weapons and armors" (or very
close). Dunno which one explains it but you should buy them anyway,
they just cost over 1gp.

If you want the answer here, you have to buy sharpening stones (not
sold everywhere) at 3 or 4cp a piece and then put the rusty weapon
and a sharpening stone in a forge (available somewhere in most
cities, ask around) and then hit combine. If you're lucky you'll get
a taarnished weapon which will be a couple delay points faster! Not
bad. You might however fail and then loose both the wepon and the
stone (lost anyway).

For me sharpening weapons became trivial at a smithing skill of 22. I
think it means that my smithing skill won't increase from it, dunno
if it means I will never be more successful. I succeed most of the
time anyway.
