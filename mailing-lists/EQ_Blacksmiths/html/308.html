I agree with the pricing problems of banded... I too have had to watch some
smiths selling banded armor at what is basically the cost of materials!
(not the actual cost which would take into account the time and cost to
learn smithing)

>From time to time I too have sold some cheap, but it is usually when I have
made way too many of something and had it on me way too long... So, I need
to get rid of some of the 'old' stock...

But I think the real problem with the pricing comes not from overstock, but
mostly from impatience. People think that they should be able to /Auction
1-2 times and that if nobody answers them, they want to go to the next zone
("where there are more people") to try to sell there... Then, when nobody
wants to buy right away, they start discounting it to the cost!

People, you have to understand something about selling... IT TAKES TIME!!!
Just like the smithing itself does...

If you have ever been in a fine clothier or jewelery store, you might have
noticed that there isn't a whole lot of customers 'shopping' around
inside... They are there selling a quality product for a good profit... that
is the way they make their money and keep their reputation of a fine
product...

Much different then Sears or some warehouse store that is basing sales on
HUGE Volumes because they (vendors) get huge discounts on the items
(Materials cost)...

Well, remember folks, this isn't Kmart!! Don't try to sell at Kmart
prices!!

I often hang out at the banks or gates of a city selling my wares... and
there have been times when I sat there for 5 min or more without selling a
thing, or even getting a query as to the prices and such! This can seem
veeeery frustrating... but look at it in a different light:

Last night I spent about an hour selling banded armor... and guess what? I
made about 30 plat in profit (over costs of the materials, not actual
costs - I am about even now... hehe...) How long would it take you to earn
30 plat in, say, Unrest? or NRo? Probably at least twice as long, and only
if you got some good loot off some mob! If you can just relax and sit back
and sell armor for a couple of hours, you can make about 30-50 plat per day
just off of that... Then you won't mind going to those places where you earn
lots of experience, but get no good loot... Because it will allready be in
the bank!

So, remember... Keep those prices up there for a GOOD profit... and RELAX!!!
Take the time needed to sell... Just because there are only 14 people in
NFP and nobody buys from you in the first 5 minutes doesn't mean that 5
minutes later the SAME 14 people will be in that zone! They change all the
time.... just look at the names... Oh, and if you see some that are there
for a long time, they are probably selling too!
