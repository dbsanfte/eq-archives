be it in e-mail, message boards, or whatever. It's my name, I don't really care. If you do,

prove a point

I'm not especially known for banded on my server, I'm at an equal platform with everyone else.
If I can sell at a price, other people can too. So, when I sell at higher prices, that proves
to other smiths that they can as well. Instead of undermining my price and making less money
per sale, they can go to a different zone and sell for the same price I am. Sales are out
there, and if the market isn't oversaturated then they always will be. Why get less money and
contribute to market oversaturation at the same time? Both are negative things to a smith.
Charging less money, we both lose, charging the same price, we both win. I don't see how
somebody can justify competition between smiths as 'a good business strategy'. If they charge
the same price as me, they'll make more money than if they charged less!! I make more money
since the price raise, it shouldn't be different for anyone else. As for new smiths who
haven't mastered the trade yet, I have no idea where you're getting your idea from. Why would
any reasonably intelligent person charge less for an item when they're in a higher need for
money? This is what you're telling me. Since they need money, they will charge a lower price.
Usually I put more logic into my arguments, but I can't even touch that because it simply does
not make sense. Newer smiths would want to charge higher prices so they can get more money so
they can spend more money on practicing their smithing! I'm assuming that every new smith on
this board is in favor of the price raise. If you're a new smith and you aren't in favor of
price raising, frankly, you have your head shoved right up your ass. You're ruining the market
you're just getting into by selling at lower prices, and you're making less money now and in
the long run. There is no justification for that, it's just plain idiotic.
