1) I would put in as many points as you can (Up till 21) to start. It's
okay if you can only put in 14.

2) Next, make Metal Bits until you have a bunch. If you didn't put 21
points in at the beginning, you will reach 21 making Metal Bits. Don't sell
them because you'll need them for everything except banded.

3) Make scalers till 41.

4) Make lanterns till 68.

5) Make skewers till 90's. Once you get to the 90's, you can make every
piece of banded except leggings and cloaks fairly well. If you want to stop
for a bit and regain some cash, this is where I would do it.

6) Keep making skewers till 115.

7) Make pots till 122.

8) Make sewing kits till 135. Now, you can make every piece with ease.

It took me about 75pp (I put in 17 points in the beginning) to get to skill
90. It took me 2 days (About 7 hrs total playing time) to get there.

Hope this helps.
