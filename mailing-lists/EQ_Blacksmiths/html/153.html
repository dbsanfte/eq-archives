Subject: Bottles for Lanterns


You can get bottles in Freeport in the same
building you get the Molds but from another
NPC. Most NPCs that sell bottles also sell
booze. The bottles have the same icon as
red wine and are usually at the bottom of
their list of wares they normally sell.

The lanterns themselves are exactly the same
as the large lanterns you can buy from NPCs.
