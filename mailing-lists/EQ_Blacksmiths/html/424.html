The point is not to have all armor deteriorate, just Smith/Tailor made
armor.

If Rubicite armor deteriorated, that'd just be dumb, and increase camping
for items to an even greater degree.

But if ONE type of armor were to decay, banded should be the one to do it.
Why? Game balance. Economics. Etc.

Just track the amount of time a piece is in game. Not that difficult. Make
it last a good long time. Say 30 days. (24 hours a day, so in actuality,
if you average 4 hours a day, the banded would last you six months of
playing EQ.)

Have it degrade a bit, not totally . . .

I think it's a great idea.
