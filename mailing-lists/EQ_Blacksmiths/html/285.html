You can go to www.eqvault.com and get the Blacksmith's Guide. But here are
my suggestions:

Save 20 points and dump them into the skill, if not already at 20 (your
guild will tell you that you need advanced training after 20, which means
you have to start spending some money)

From skill 21-27, sharpen weapons that cost over 1g to sell. At this point
you won't get any skill increases from daggers and maces.

From skill 27-41, start making scalers. You will need metal bits (2 small
blocks of ore + a flask of water), scaler mold and a flask of water. These
are great because if you fail, you still get an item to sell.

From skill 42-115, make skewers. Metal bits, skewer mold and a flask of
water. You will rarely make these, but this is the cheapest way to get your
skill up.

I would wait until skill 60 to begin trying to make Banded Armor. At this
time, you should be able to make Gorgets and Bracers with ease. That should
compensate you for your time spent jacking up the skill.

I took me 3 12-hour days and over 200pp (i lost count) to get my skill from
30-100. It WILL aggrevate you, but this skill can be one of the most
rewarding.

Good Luck!
