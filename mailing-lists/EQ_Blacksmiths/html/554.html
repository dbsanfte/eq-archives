Actually, in the long run, Lanterns are cheaper... Why? Because when you
make one, you can sell it back! When you get to the point (level 41) when
you are trying to decide, you will not have success with Skewers for a LONG
time, and therefore will just be throwing away money completely...

At least if you are making lanterns (which do cost a bit more), you will
have more successes and will be able to sell them back to NPC's to help
offset the costs...

In the long run, I found it cheaper to do lanterns first (until trivial at
70) and then start making skewers...
