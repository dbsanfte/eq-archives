To Jason: Why is a 15pp profit too steep? They could pay 15pp for a
ringmail tunic and get less ac and more weight or pay 90pp for a chain tunic
for the same results or wait for bronze. It's not a matter of how much I
make, it is a matter of how much they are willing to pay.

Ok, 40pp is a tad steep. There is a certain line people have to draw. If
people charged 40pp for a tunic, they'd probably charge 300pp for a suit.
You can make your own suit for less than that. It takes 150pp to get to a
reasonable master skill (early 100's) and another 60 to craft your own set.
This would take time, but, saving money is worth it and they can also sell
their own banded afterwards.

People will never (not even on a new server) pay chain prices for banded
because if they had the money to do that, they could make their own banded
or go to unrest and pick up ring/bronze.
