Subject: Creating Magical Armor and Weapons


Here is my 2 cents worth on this subject. I say no to enchanters being able
to enchant ore for us. I agree that the enchanters would become the best
class to make magical items. Let them do what they do best and make pretty
jewelry for us to wear.

How can we make magical weapons and armor? I like the idea of the mold.
Also maybe introduce special ore that can be used to make enchanted weapons
and armor. Of course this ore would have to be dropped by creatures and not
bought from NPC's. Or maybe combine different ingredients into the ore like
snakeskin for poison resistance, lightstones to give of light radius. Maybe
use a combination of all the above to make unique or lore items, like a
special mold, magical ore, snakefang, and a lightstone to make a special
sword that causes poison and gives off a lightradius of a lightstone.
