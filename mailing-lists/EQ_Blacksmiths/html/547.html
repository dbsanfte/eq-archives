I think the problem with pricing and manufacturing costs on the new
weapons is due to the disparity between what Verant thinks the weapons
are worth and what the players will actually pay for them. The store
prices on most fine steel weapons is from 30-40 pp in the shops, but you
can buy them by the tons on Karana for 4-8 pp each from players. That
won't cover the costs of our molds, much less taking failures into
account.

Most people I know want magic weapons by the time they are 15th level so
they can hunt critters you need magic weapons for. Prior to 15th level
it doesn't really matter much what you use for a weapon. I think the
weapons are a nice idea, but Verant needs to rethink the component costs
and get them in line with reality.
