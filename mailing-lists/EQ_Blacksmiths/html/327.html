The problem with fixing prices is that there are not enough smiths on this
list. There may be 20 spread out over 20 servers. Whoopee. IF this
mailing list was legitimate, then fixing prices would work. (Would the EQ
government get on our case for collusion? hehe.)

I tend to sell my banded for fair prices. The market in greater faydark
(Fennin Ro) is steady. There are the occasional low-priced sellers, but
they're almost never a smith; probably some warrior who just got himself a
bronze breastplate. I generally stay around and sell armor for an hour or
two and make around 100pp.

Believe it or not, smithing is still lucrative, albeit not as good as when a
server first starts. The real profit is made from items like boots, helms,
sleeves, pants, and tunics. Boots cost about 3pp to make and I sell them
for 10pp consistently, and they're easy to make. Pants and tunics are easy
for me as well (skill 134) I sell them for three times their cost (16pp and
18pp, respectively). I tend to scowl at masks and belts because they cost 2
metal sheets and I can only sell them for 5pp and 7pp, respectively.
