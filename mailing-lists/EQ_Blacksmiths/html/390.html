But in EQ you CAN be a Master Fisher, Tailor, Blacksmith, Fletcher, Jewler
and Baker with enough time and money. The argument is that Enchanters can
Master Jeweling better than any other class, and that gives them an edge on
the market. Also, their high intelligence gives them the advantage of
learning the other trade skills quickly. This combination yields a very
deadly merchant who can clean up an entire zone. I know of one Enchanter on
Tunare who, within a matter of minutes of logging on, has about 50 tells for
his goods and services.
The only thing that we non-Enchanters can do is learn multiple trades so
that we can market ourselves better. I do agree with you that we should
befriend these Enchanters. It can only help us.
