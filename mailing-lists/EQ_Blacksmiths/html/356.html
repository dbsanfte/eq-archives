Its looks, plain and simple, some people care more about how they look than they do about having the most efficient weight/ac armor ratio.

There are basically 2 types of people. Type A, which cares a great deal about stats and couldn't care a bit about looks. And type B, which care more about looks than stats.

I'm a 36th lvl DE warrior, and I'm definitally a type B person. As soon as I was able, I aquired a full set of blackend iron chainmail. Which is lower AC than banded and A LOT heavier. Sure the weight was annoying, but I looked much cooler than the person standing beside me in full banded. After a couple weeks of wearing full blackend I discovered that small bronze was the same weight but more AC. At which point I decided to get me a full set of bronze, but after seeing countless other people in full sets of bronze I decided I really didn't like that look. But since I was at Unrest I decided to get some anyways. What I ended up with was bronze helm, tunic, bracers, leggings, and still had my blackend arms, gloves, boots. This turned out to be a nice looking combo, so I thought, and I had several other people stop and tell me the same. Now many many levels later, I have Rubicite (red plate) helm, arms, bracers, leggings, and Charred (Black plate) tunic, boots, and gloves, weilding a black 2h sword. Charred isn't as good as Rubicite, but the combo of the red and black plate looks extremely impressive.

To each their own, some people care more about an extra point of AC or an extra 2 points of wisdom more than they care how they look. Some will sacrifice a couple AC and a couple points of an attribute so that they can have nicer looking equipment.
