This is the third message about how I am
increasing my blacksmithing skill, right now
it stands as 102 (Master).

I believe in my last message I was at skill 66.
At that point I was doing lanterns which quickly
became trivial at 68 along with Studs, Sheet
Metal, Cookie Cutters and Large Containers.

Since I successfully created a piece of Sheet
Metal I decided to attempt a banded gorget at
level 68 and was successful!! My first piece
of banded is now fastened securely around my
neck.

After this I decided to finally start doing
skewers since they only cost 226cp for each
attempt and next higher cost non-trivial item,
Sewing Kit, costs 576cp per attempt. I started
getting a decent success rate and my skill level
was rising in about the same fashion as the other
items I was creating. Also about every
10 skill points I would attempt to make the
other items to see if they had become trivial.
Unfortunately some of the items require ceramic
lining, a pottery skill, so I had to start
working on my pottery (it rose from 1 to 8 :).
The only thing that has become trivial from 68
to 102 is Steel Boning. During this period I
spent a total of around 55pp. This includes
around 5pp for pottery and I kept the
metal cooking items for increasing my cooking
trade skill which amounts to about 6pp in sell
back costs. An additional 10pp was spent doing
the attempts to find the trivial levels for
the other items that resulted in failure. So,
if I just stuck to doing skewers it would be
around 34pp. This brings my total to around
100pp to get to this level or about 1pp per
skill point. I should mention that the character
is a human warrior that put all his extra points
into strength and stamina.
