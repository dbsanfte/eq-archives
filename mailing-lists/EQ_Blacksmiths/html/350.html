Just so you all know, I read something a few e-mails ago that I would like
to address.

Something along the lines of "if sold to the NPC who will then sell it back"
or somesuch. About chain and ringmail. On any account, if you sell an item
to a merchant by accident, and mean to buy it back, but scroll down it's
entire inventory and find nothing, that is because it's not "on the shelf",
so to speak.

You need to buy out other items on the merchant to have more items in it's
inventory come to show. Don't buy the items that the merchant normally
sells....they have endless stock of them. Only those that people sell the
merchant.

I once accidentally sold a runed totem staff to a merchant, but it wasn't on
the merchants "shelf". So I bought out item after item after item until
finally there was my staff. Well, the merchant wanted 61pp for it, and I
said fuck that and bought another for 20pp. Ah well.
