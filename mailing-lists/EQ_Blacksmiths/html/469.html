Well, since you helped me, I will help you. I am a Kelethin native.
Sharpening stones and Ore are in Steamfont, 2nd windmill. Small molds are in
Steamfont and Butcherblock, both are merchants right outside the respective
cities, Ak'anon and Kaladim. You can only get medium molds in Kelethin, so
you have to take a little trip.
