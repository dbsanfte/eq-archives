Metal Ore and sharpening stones can be found in the following places:

Neriak - The place with the fish...some guy by a dart board.
Steamfont - One of the windmills close to the gnome city
Freeport - The Forge (not the one in the marketplace)
High Pass Hold - Haven't actually made it to this one alive, but everyone
tells me it's there.
I've also been told that there are some high level hunting areas where the
critters drop the ore when they kick the bucket.


These are the only four locations I know of. Another point of interest --
East Freeport has a shop that sells only rusty weapons. I've been there but
can't remember where exactly. Instead of using all my training points I
spent only 2 points and made the next 21 levels by sharpening weapons and
then selling them. Most of them i sold back to the shop at a loss, but I
managed to sell some two handers for 5 gold each. It cost a lot more this
way..but since my wife is a tailor I didn't have much use for the money at
the time.
