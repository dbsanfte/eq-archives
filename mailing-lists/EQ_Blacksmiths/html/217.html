And, in a role playing game, people usually have somewhere to store things.
A house, a secret place no one knows about, etc. The Bank is a way of
implementing this, although the one thing it provides that other
role-playing type games (or life way back when....) is security. Personally,
I don't keep that much in my bank account. Copies of extra keys to Befallen,
a couple of semi-rare items in process of selling or waiting for a guildmate
to come get (like a derv ring, a couple of spiked collars, and a mino axe),
and coinage. That's basically about it. I don't keep extra weapons or armor
or food and drink for when I buy the farm. That's what friends are for -
help me do a corpse retrieve (assuming I need help). And, if I need a weapon
after I die, I'll kill a couple skeletons in the newbie areas and get some
weapons before I head back out, which I usually don't even worry about.
