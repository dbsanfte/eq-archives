Subject: A good place for smiths


Well.. I've been pretty quiet on this list so far and have just
observed everyone else. Not too many people have mentioned the little
town of Rivervale. This town really has a lot to offer. It is a pretty
good trade town if you ask me. You can buy small molds for
blacksmithing, it has a forge, and you can get ore off the muddites in
beholder. I know the ore off of muddites is NOT something the lower
level people can attain easily, but that is the only drawback i see to
the town. There are very few smiths here, so I'm sure a smith with
descent skill would make a killing selling banded to all the furry
footed people. I think Rivervale has every type of trade skill
available in it. Potters wheel, kiln, forge, oven, and a brew thing.
Just a suggestion. (Hopes to see a smith selling banded in the next few
days because his character would surely buy some =0)
