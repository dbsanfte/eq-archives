I started out with 19 points, then made files till it was trivial (like 23 I
think), then I switched to scalars and now I have a skill of 29. I was
under the impression that you do scalars till you max in that, then switch
to skewers.

That is what I know on the process. Something I have not heard anyone talk
about ever is your faction standing and how it effects the smithing process.
Being a wood elf currently I thought the best place to go to get ore would
be Steamfont. But all the gnomes there are apprehensive of me which means
bad prices. The only other place I knew to go was N Freeport, so I made the
long journey and low and behold the humans regard me indifferently. I ended
up saving a Silver per ore and 2 copper per water. Now on its own that
does sound like much, but thing of how much ore you have buy. Every 100 ore
you buy you save a platinum.
