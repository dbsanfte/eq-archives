Put 20 points in to blacksmithing from the start and you don't need file
molds till you want to make studs (I have found them in the wood elf town,
but that is where I am from.). You don't want to make skewers yet either,
you will waist a HUGE amount of cash for little skill gain. Instead make
scalers and then large lanterns till they each become trivial, and then jump
to skewers. I believe most of the molds can be found in freeport.
