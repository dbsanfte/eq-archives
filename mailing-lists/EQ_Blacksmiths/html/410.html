Sorry no one will group with you. Enchanters are a very powerful class.
While you do share the buffs with Shamans, your debuffs spells are the best
in the game. Not to mention your illusion spells allow you to walk into any
city and not be KOS. Enchanters are powerful, especially in a group where
their abilities will shine.

I still say that not all magical item creation should be dependent on the
enchanter though. Yes, enchanters should be used to create most magical
items, especially the more powerful ones. If a tailor just wanted to create
poison resistance armor, why couldn't he or she just fashion it out of
snakeskin and use the inherent properties from that? A Fletcher could use
special feathers of a creature on a arrow to increase it's distance, or a
smith could use a lightstone in a tunic to give it light radius. Simple
magical items such as these should not require an enchanter.
