> Find some ore, and make some metal bits. Say four stacks worth, as you
will fail a few times making these. Make some scalers with the bits, then
make more bits. Once bits become trivial, make a full backpack or two of
them. This is major carpal tunnel syndrom time, but it's necessary to do.
Keep making scalers until trivial. Move on to lanterns, they cost a little
more, but you fail a lot less, and don't lose much money. Scalers are last
until your skill hits 115, then you can try pots to 125(?) then sewing kits.
Although I found that pots and sewing kits don't give you very much skill.
Once you hit 125 in skill just sell all armor.
