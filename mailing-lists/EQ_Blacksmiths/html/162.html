I just thought I would throw my oppinion into the mixx.

I have to agree that I think price fixing is wrong. I do like having a
rough idea of a standard price that the majority as a whole charges. If
someone wants to go lower thats their right. The problem I see is that EQ
is totally unlike the Real world in Economics. If I bring a piece of ring
mail to an NPC vendor I get more money from them than I get for bringing
them a better quality piece of banded mail. Thats just stupid on the AI
side of the NPCs.

Suposidly in the big update wthat Verant will be releasing in 6 to 9+
months, they say that they will be doing a major revamp of trade skills to
make them so they don't suck. I certainly hope better pricing for items is
included in that.

However, untill that time comes, and it is a while away, its every players
right to sell or give items away at what ever price they want to sell them
for. Even if they want to take a loss on the product. Perhapse they feel
that they can gain skill faster by selling their product for below cost
(yet more than NPCs will give) as a suplement to their continued skill
training.
