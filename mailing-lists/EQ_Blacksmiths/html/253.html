buy the two smithing books.

Start with either bits or sharpening weapons. That should get you to low
20's. Then switch to files or scalers. that will get you to high 30's or low
40's. From there switch to skewers but you wont ever succeed. Your skill
will still go up. Once around 55 -60 start making bracers or gorgets. do
that till you hit about 70. then move to boots. dont attempt tunics till 90
skills, legs 95 skill, or cloaks 120 skill or you will just fail a lot and
those are the costliest items.
