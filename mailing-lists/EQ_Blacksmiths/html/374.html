About being able to enchant sheet metal for armor....

Yes, I love the idea. I think it would be great to get some kick ass armor
like that! But then....it wouldn't be fair. Think about it. Enchanters
already are the best at Jewelry Making. It is (pretty much) damn foolish
for anyone BUT an enchanter to learn the skill. Unless, of course, you also
play a high lvl enchanter who you use for your own enchanted metals.

By giving enchanters the ability to enchant sheet metal, thereby creating
magical armor, this make's enchanters have far too much control over the
market. Jewelry making and Blacksmithing all would depend upon enchanters?
No, I'd rather not have that.

Besides, Magical Armor should be something special. IMHO, I believe there
should be quests for Blacksmith's, where the reward might be a mold that
comes enchanted with some random quality. This would allow EVERYone the
opportunity to do this, and make us not be dependant upon enchanters.

What do you all think?
