I was online with a new smith the other day, and she didn't want to waste
her points, so she just bought a sharpening stone and started sharpening
weapons... She increased VERY fast (while we were talking she went from
level 1-15 in about 5 minutes!) and at the same time she was getting to keep
the stones many times too... (she had saved lots of old daggers)

She then started making bits and that was where I left her... She messaged
me about 10 min later to tell me she was already over 21 now and was
starting on files.

So, you can increase very quickly now without having to put 20 points into
it... If you don't have lots of extra points to spend that is.
