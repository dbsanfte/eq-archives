Well, seems Verant finally put the banded gauntlet molds in the game.. Sort
of. They "forgot" to when they did that big patch earlier in the month, and
"remembered" it later. The molds may be found in Kelethin and Steamfront
along with the normal banded molds, but for some reason Freeport's medium
mold vendor (East Freeport, near the docks) doesn't have them, at least not
that I saw. They MAY be located with the weapon molds, I'm not sure.

Anyhow, so far they seem to be as hard as cloaks. With a 143 skill, I had a
25% failure rate. The gauntlets do NOT show up on the player model, so if
your customer wants them for looks then he's SOL.

I've been selling them on E'ci at a good clip at 12pp, in line with the
Smiths of Norrath union rates. A few complaints ("*whine* ..but banded is
only 1pp per AC! /whine") but once they find out it's as hard to make as
cloaks they're sympathetic.

Stats are 7AC, weight 3(sm) and 4(med), don't know about large.
