How long did it take you to get to level 3?? I did it fairly quickly...
Only took a couple of hours! In fact, I had level one done in less than an
hour..

I was up to level 5 within the next 3-4 hours of play time (over a couple of
days for me) with a little help from another cleric friend (Thanks Devrill!)
who gave me a couple of pieces of patchwork armor (she was learning
tailoring) basically at cost and or free... I gave her pelts (and still do!)

So your point of not being able to take on toughies in the first level is
true, yes, but you also don't NEED to really... But you won't DIE because
some Orc Pawn starts slamming on you either... your armor is likely to help
you get back to the guards a LITTLE easier if it's banded or plate!

Then, when it comes time to start hunting Centurions and the like, you will
be up on the game again, with higher AC and such... That's all.. If you
like that, then fine... I found myself going out and getting killed ON
PURPOSE so that I could have more food and water without having to pay for
it! Guess if I could have just gotten say, 100 plat right off the bat I
wouldn't have to waste time killing myself... hehe... But it was good
practice on looting my corpse.... hehe...
