I would like to see bank accounts where you could transfer items and money
into other peoples accounts. It would make trading and selling items with
other people a lot easier. What if you had a friend in Freeport that could

post a price. If someone wanted to buy the item, they would pay the money
and get the item. The money would then go into the sellers bank account.
