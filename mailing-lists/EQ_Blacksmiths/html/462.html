Plate costs should be balanced between the cost an NPC would BUY them for,
and the cost an NPC will SELL them for... example: if the NPC will BUY that
Breast Plate for 50 but wants to SELL it for 200, then your price could
(should) be half way between those two - 125p - or higher... IF your costs
don't outweigh it...

In the RW, most manufacturers make an item for $1.00 in costs for both parts
AND labor, but with overhead, middlemen and retail costs, and profits for
each along the way, the actual PRICE of the item is usually around $9.99 or
more... Learn from RL folks... Sure we probably couldn't sell our Banded
for 10 times what it costs (actual) to make it, but hey, 3 times the amount
is not that bad...
