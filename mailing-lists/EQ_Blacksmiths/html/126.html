I'm on Fennin ro, and have checked out prices (been about a week and a half)
from Qeynos to Freeport. I've seen many of the prices you listed, but those
would be the best end prices...

Bracers for 8-10 is accurate.
Tunic and cloak for 20 is accurate
I haven't seen legs go for less than 20pp.
Most everything else I've bought for 12-15pp.

And, It's been a week and a half and I don't check out prices anymore. I
bought all my stuff from the people who had it.


QUESTION: I've heard Medium banded is lighter than Large banded. I'm a
barbarian warrior, and someone told me I needed large so I bought some large
stuff (bracers, helm, mantle, boots, etc). Then I got a medium tunic and
legs. Still need to get a cloak and mask.

Problem is, I can't carry squat now... With the basic essentials (armor, two
mino axes, and 3 backpacks plus food), I'm at like 80/119 weight. Wondering
if I changed my large banded over to medium banded if it would be worth it.
And, can Barbarians wear anything large and anything medium? Someone told me
they can wear SOME medium stuff, but all large worked. Seems stupid to me,
but I'm not sure anymore...
