Subject: RE: Where to find sewing kit molds in GFay?


Make pots! They will get you to 122. I failed a lot less with pots than
sewing kits. And if I remember correctly you only lose about 2g per
successful attempt. You need standing legs mold and pot mold for these. They
can be found at the same merchant that has the Skewer and Scalar molds in
Kelethin.
