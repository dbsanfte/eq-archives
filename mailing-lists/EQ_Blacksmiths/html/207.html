Well I have to disagree with the other replies ). Start with Scalers. Don't
stop scalers til the become trivial, whatever skill that is. Next are
Lanterns. I tried skewers and was wasting ALOT of money. Lanterns boosted my
points a good bit. When Lanterns become trivial, then switch to skewers. One
last personal recommendation, take skewers all the way to 115, where they
become trivial. At this skill level you can start making banded armor with
good proficiency. You still fail on items at times. Keep this in mind. The
cheapest banded item you can make, gorgets and bracers, each cost you over 1
plat. Skewers only cost a little over 1 gold. Practicing on skewers is alot
cheaper on your purse than practicing on Banded.
