I started smithing with 10 skill points. I have lost 115PP 9GP, 6SP, 9CP in smithing so far (or spent this much to achieve level 84, depending on how you look at it). This is after I've sold my finished products. My average "loss" per level, from 10 to 84, was: 1PP, 5GP, 6SP, 7CP.

I've spent countless hours standing at a forge. I didn't put a price on that, nor the 2 books I've purchased or the 10 skill points I wasted in the beginning to get this started. With the market flooded with cheap banded, and now bronze becoming the armor of choice, I feel that I've gone as far as I should in this endeavor.

Following is the breakdown of how much I spent per item. The numbers are almost exact, if not exact. I was careful to write down my successes and failures. If they're off, it's probably only by a fraction, unless I miss-typed a number into my calculator.

I've listed prices as 4 digits, first is PP (may be multiple digits), then GP, SP, and CP (all single digits).
Ex: 0,0,1,3 = Cost to buy water.

Skill level 10 through 20 making metal bits
Attempts: 62
Made: 31
Failed: 31
Cost per attempt: 0,0,7,6
Total cost: 4,7,1,2
Average cost per level (10-20): 0,4,2,8
(NOTE: Selling metal bits for a loss would lower the cost here, but metal bits are needed in bulk for the next 100 or so levels.)

Skill level 21-40 making scalers

Cost per level (21-40): 6,5,0,4
(NOTE: Scaler molds made "Large Container Lid Mold" when I was at this level. They didn't make the files until I passed this item (it became trivial)).
