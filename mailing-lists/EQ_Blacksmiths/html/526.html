Another nice place for this is in Kelethin. If you have metal bits on you,
more the better. If not, a trip to Ak'Anon will get you the proper
materials.

From Felwithe, going on the path towards Kelethin. The first lift you come
across, go up it. On that platform, on the other side of the central tree
trunk, there is a merchant that sells all the molds needed for Sewing Kits.
Water can also be bought on this platform. The ramp going up, which the
vendor selling the molds faces, is where you want to go next. Coming to the
top of this platform, the forge comes right into view.

This is where I did all my forging, through my level of 164 (so far). I've
found it to be VERY convenient. Close to all medium armor molds, and fairly
close to small armor molds (outside Kaladim). Only thing hard to find are
large molds, but those are really only good if you have a troll/ogre selling
base.

Anyhow, there ya go from my experiences.
