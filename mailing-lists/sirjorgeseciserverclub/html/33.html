An even BETTER spot for mid to high teens seems
to be Highpass Hold. I did all of L17 there (made
L18 yesterday), and the end for this area is still
out of sight. I've earned nearly 100pp in loot since
I got here. The South end of the zone is orc land,
and the spawn rate is incredible. I recommend you
absolutely DO NOT go to the south end without a full party.
As an example, I was there with 2 rangers and a
Necro this morning, all near my 18 level. In one spawn,
we got a Fanatic, and 3 warriors. I was the only
survivor, and then only because I ran to two guards, then
dove into the river. ( the rest of my party was dead
before I split, of I'd have stayed to fight to the
bitter end). The north end is Gnoll territory, and this
is where the PGT and Cracked darkwood shield are
gotten. A group of four can survive here indefinitely due
to the slower spawn rate. Another fine aspect of
HighHold is the scalp quests. I can raise my faction with
the Freeport Militia (very important to us
Paladins)with NO bad hits that matter. <br> Seems too good to
be true? What's the catch? Getting to HighHold is a
deadly proposition. Since Plane Of Hate poisoned
Kithikor, traveling in the wood is very risky. I met a
zombie trooper there that killed me so fast I never had
a chance to hit the Lay on Hands I had hot keyed.
If you can invis, go through the Innothule and
Ferrot. It's a LONG, LONG run, but at least you'll get
there.<br> Hopefully I'll see ya online tonight.<br><br>
