I am Toradin of the 34 season. I enteredHigh Pass
at lvl 17, and have been either there or High Pass
Keep since. These 2 places are great for exp and
money. Once you get into the Warrior room the Gobs start
to drop small Bronze and lots of Plat.<br> The only
drawback is trying to get into groups. The Pass and Keep
are both quite popular and just about always full; so
you may have to be put on a waiting list.<br> The
good news is that you can get bound in the Keep and it
also has bank and merchants, so you never have to
leave if you don't want until about lvl 40.
