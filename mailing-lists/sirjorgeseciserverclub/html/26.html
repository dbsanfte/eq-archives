I most recently adventured in Oasis, south of
Desert of Ro and found that a group of 6 lvl 16 chars
can make great experience. Our particular team had a
great balance between casters, tanks, and hybrids but
this balance may not me required in order to benefit
from this zone. You must be carefull and sat away from
Sand Giants (killed me in three shots, lvl 16 wiz) and
most of all deepwater goblins (1 cast and I was
toast). Things to hunt while in this group are orc
warriors and priests, dry bones, mummies, and madmen. We
were able to handle orcs that were red to us. Great
exp. If you are in the area send me a
