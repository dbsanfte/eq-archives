Ya ever find yerself in a bind for places to
stash all yer stuff? Bank vault's full of backpacks,
and ya got no free space left when ya stumble across
a screamin' good deal on a Shiney Brass Halberd?
Guess what? While that 16/48 implement of destruction
will NOT fit into a backpack, it WILL fit into a large
sewing kit! Not only that, but if you're traveling, that
little ol' sewing kit only weighs 0.1 vs the wt of 3 for
a back pack. Also, a large sack will hold anything
a back pack does, and only weighs in at 1.0 Do the
math friends! Large sacks and sewing kits make that
long haul easy to load up for.<br><br> Foleen<br> 18th
