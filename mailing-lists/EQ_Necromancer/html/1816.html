What I did at 16-20 was just put my point into reasearch and didn't do any
research. At 20th I put as many points as I could into it and researched my
16th level spells at 24th I maxed my skill and did the 20th level spells.
Once I hit 29th, I looked for higher levels necros, usually around the next
spell level to see if they had the spells and if I could buy them or if I
had the words, would they research them for me. I also max my research out,
so that when I had all the current spells for that level , I would try and
research them myself and give them out to younger nec's for free. I have
heard of too many horror stories of higher level necs trying to research the
hard to find words only to fail and still have to work with a pet a spell
level or 2 below them. When I hit 34th, I found a 39th level nec that was
willing to combine the words for my 34th level pet and they also had COB and
wave of enfeeblement. For that I gave the nec 100PP as a donation, well
worth it to be able to use my 34th level pet and cob at 34th level. I am
only 35th right now, but already have all the words for my 39th level
spells, so when I hit 39th, I'll find a high level nec to combine them for
me and pay them quite nicely to make my spells for me.

Vanelin Blackheart
35th level DE Nefromancer, oooops I mean Necromancer
