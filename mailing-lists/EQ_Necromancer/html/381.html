Dark Pact is a level 8 spell that takes from your life and gives to your
mana over a certain period of time. It's very useful, especially for
meditating when you have very low Mana. It speeds the process up
dramatically. Just don't let it drain your life too far.

Also, as somebody else here stated, using it for an almost constant
correctly-timed LifeTaps is most amusing. :-) But, be careful, you can also
tick the creature off enough to where it'll frenzy you.
