There is the book that the "combining" is done in. Then there are 4 books
with spell components in them. Each in a different language. You will want
to buy these (Or cheat and get the lists at the research site...)

You will have a list of 2 to 4 components that you put in the research book,
then click combine. If you succeed, you get a spell.

and non-stackable. It does not seem to make a difference which you use when
making a spell.
