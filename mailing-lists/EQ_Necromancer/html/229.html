for those that do not know, instead of having your pet sit, you can give the
command /pet stand up (even though he is allready standing). This will keep him
from wandering off or "guarding" an area.
My problem with having a pet sit down is that they will not defend you if you
are attacked (especially nice when your meditating). If he is standing, he will
defend you. Just watch out though, if you fight somewhere and he comes running
to help, when the fight is over, you have to give the command "/pet follow me"
to get him back to your spot or he will continue to stand in the spot that the
last action took place.
