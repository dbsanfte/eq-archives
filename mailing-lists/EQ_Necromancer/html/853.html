Another pet command I like it /pet guard here That one can get bad, because
your pet wont help you unless you tell it to. The reason I like this is for
when you want to pull monster to a very certain spot. As you are running
back you pet will run after it and engage it where you dont want it too.
When you get back to where you want it to fight you hot key /pet attack with
the monster targeted. This has worked well for me.

/pet sit is importain. For you pet heals faster siting.

/pet follow me to make it come. /pet guard me is another dangerous one. Any
thing it think is going to attack you. It will jump when it get close to
you. Weather you are meditating or ready for a fight. Fighting oban Drakes
you never use that command for you will be fighting 10 or more of them at
once.

/pet settle down stops your pet from fighting. Unless you or your pet is
getting hit.

/pet report health very good hot key to have. "I have 12 % of my health
left" thats when you know you have no choice but to stay in battle and pray.

now being a new necro you also need to know some tatics. I have made it to
level 24 this way. Stand far from your opponent. Dot them first with your
biggest dot you have then sick your pet on it as it get close. hit it
onemore time with another dot other then the one you just hit it with then
get in battle. hit it for a bit. When you are low. Step back and Drain some
hp. Then get back in battle again. Keep doing the engage..... disengage
drain hp till it falls dead. Now dont try and kill it too fast. Alot of
necros make that mistake. If you dish too much damage too fast your pets
taunt just wont work, and you wont be ablie to step out of battle to drain
hp. but dont make the battle linger too long or yore pet is dead as well.
You will get the feel for this over time. Welcome to the world of the undead
and there master's US!!!
