Your pet can make you rich. Use it to kill mass amounts of greenies and
loot til your hearts content. Try for high resale and stackable stuff. If
you have the faction to walk into RV, then goto the lake and kill tons of
trout and the occaisional Pirhrana. Without a pet I can make 2 plat an hour
easy and all the food you can eat. RV lake is good for 6th. You can even
turn in the pirhrana teeth for xp if you have the faction to talk to Fiddy.

silver each. Turning in the Pirhrana teeth gives you almost 2 gold each.
I'm sure there are many areas where you can make fast money off loot. If
you pick a good area, you will have trouble looting as fast as you pet kills
stuff.
