Nope you do not get KoS if you go to the bank in Freeport. I know because I
go there and I am a gnome Necro myself.

For the guild well when you go out of the west gate , turn right (if you are
facing the desert) and follow the wall.... (the wall will be to your right)
Just when you will meet the wall in front of you (the L part of it) you just
go through the wall in a secret passage.... The music changes and I like it!
anyways.... Just keep going straight until you reach a room where there's an
exit in front of you and to your right...... go to the one on your right and
just follow the path... The necro guild loies ahead.

Have fun!

Hope this helps!
