This is easy, just buy red wine at a bar, and take it to Lokar Tobiath (the
scribe) in the Library in 3rd gate. The faction adjustments are:
+DarkBargainers +DreadGuardInner +DreadGuardOuter

guards and merchants. Now if I could only figure out a way to do the muffin
