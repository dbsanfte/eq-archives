Kill Lrodd in Befallen... many times. At level 11 he shouldn't be
much of a problem. Make as good a pet as you can and just camp there and at
the SK site. Very quickly you'll hit level 12 with a minimum of fuss. That
or head to Feerrott and go to three skeleton runes that are right next to
each other, just watch out for trains.

As for a guild I would be more picky, there are a lot of loser
guilds out there. I suggest making friends and playing a lot with them,
maybe even form your own guild or make friends with a good guild. Do a lot
of research, don't jump at the first chance you see.
