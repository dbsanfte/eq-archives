1) There is no easy spot. ALL shadowmen spots spawn
between 3 and 6 shadowmen, some you can't see. You
will have to have a group to take them.

2) Good guards were recently upgraded in magic
resistance. There really are no good guards to pull
anymore. Rivervale wall guards are a maybe example,
east common guards are another maybe example. Find
corrupt guards to hunt.

3) honestly, I would recommnend 29 and the new spells
with a group to do the quests. I did them at 28 solo
on a young server and died 11 times doing all of the
quests. It will be easier on an established
server...but.... :)
