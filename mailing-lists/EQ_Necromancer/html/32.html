You are giving a wooden box with empty slots. What you do is put
one eye in each slot. What you have then is your wooden box filled with
eyes in every slot. Then at the bottom of the box inventory box, there's a
button called COMBINE. Click it and if you have the right kind and quantity
of beetle eyes, it will create a new item. This item will be called, "A box
of fire beetle eyes" and cannot be opened. Then you head to your guild
master and GIVE the box to him. Then you've completed the quest.

However I must state that my "reward" was pretty lame. I think I
got a few silver and a sliver of XP and a tad of faction increase. In other
words, I gave away a gold piece for crap. I heard that the reward is very
random though too... but essentially I think you are better off just selling
them for money.

Malark, Dark'Fier Ravens, Karana
