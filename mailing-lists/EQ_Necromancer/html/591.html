I have found the spell, esp in combo with dark empathy to work very nicely
against asps. Since asps poison strongly, I send in pet after I cast a
darkness spell. I then try to get off a disease spell. Then I just alternate
mend bones and dark empathy. With this I was able to defeat lev 9 and 8 asps
at lev 9. With this method I am also able to defeat almost any lev 10 non
poisoning MOB in ecommons. I have killed lev 10 Y-Kodiacs, orc centurians
and lions. Or you can just use mend bones and when the pet is near death,
you can step in and tank for your pet.

What I like most about my necromancer is that there seems to be more then
