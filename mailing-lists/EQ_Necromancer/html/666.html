Well, you picked a race/class combo thats a little harder to play but it can be done.
First there are two ways you can do:
- Stay until lvl 8 in odus, get invis spell
- or if you want to explore go to queynos but beware ! If the ship docks jump into the
water there should be a secret entrance to the sewers (i dont know where exactlly)
Theres a little town in the sewers where you can get all necessary things.
Further you should find somebody to bind you in the north queynos area.
To get your faction up with the queynos guards you should go to BlackBurrow and kill
some gnolls (a lot) also dont forget to cast that steal strength spell on other gnolls
that are killed by others. (help them and get a good faction hit, for the little mana
use a good deal)
The Necro quests i hear and tried are crap. Dont waste your time on them.
Armor ? you are kidding ? hehe well try some cloth armor or when you are better get
some woven or netted armor.
Weapon ? If you are lucky someone sells a giant snake fang in your area (good piercing
weapon in the beginning) Or you can try to get your hands on a 2 h blunt weapon. (most
of the better weapons are 2 h blunt)
You can also try to train all weapon skills you can use. They go up pretty fast because
of your high int.

Other thing you should consider: You will be allways killed on sight.
