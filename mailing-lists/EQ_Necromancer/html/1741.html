Our pet heal spells are different than normal heals. They dont heal HP, but
they heal as a percentage of HP. The first one heals 6%, and the 29 renew
bones heals 16%. So on a level 1 pet, or a level 49 pet, renew bones will
heal in 6 casts. Mend bones in 16 casts. They dont really wear out as our
pets get more HP :)
