I'm level 30, I started my reign of terror in the Qeynos Hills off and on at
about level 26, killing Holly, and the Millers and the 2 guards by Surefall.
At level 29 I could take Cros Treewind about half the time, I can take him 2
out of 3 now, still not worth the risk though :) Those damn Druid DD spells
*hurt* I'm just thankful that I have these mobs to kill while I wait on
Varsoon, I pity the goodly races that have to sit in this zone doing
*nothing* while they wait on his daily spawn. Some things to watch out for
if you plan on doing this. Guards Nash and Leopold wander, and are still
red to me (does anyone know what level they are?) And Cros and Holly have
this obscene tendency to cross paths while I'm fighting the other one. :)

On a seperate note, big thanks to those who recommended I pursue Vampiric
Curse, this spell just plain *rocks* I was able to separate the Millers
spawns and keep taking them one at a time by alternating between allure of
death and vampiric curse. These 2 are about level 25 or 6 and spawn every 5
minutes, a veritable xp fountain for evil races. PLUS, Verant finally fixed
it so you only receive KaranasResidents Faction hits for killing them,
rather than Qeynos guards (the 2 of them are poachers and are hated by the
surefall residents, it didn't make sense to lose faction from the
surrounding area by killing so called villains) They both drop Bronze
weapons, great roleplaying fodder. I tend to give them away to the first
level 5 or below that runs up and shouts "HAIL THE PRINCE OF HATE" or some
such evil nastiness. :)
