I was in fact able to finish a quest while invisible. I was in Qeynos and
everything there is ready to attack. I was trying to raise faction, so i
was doing the quest where you give 2 gold to Den Magason by the docks.
After I gave him the money I got some blank scrolls that I didn't need
because I was only doing the quest for faction. I had a friend with me that
was hailing him to keep him from wandering away. My friend said that after
I gave the NPC the 2 gold the first time that I became visible. I still had
the gather Shadows icon and no one was rushing over to kill me. Other PC's
could see me, but the NPC's couldn't. I was able to sit there and give him
enough to get to threateningly before my Invis finally did wear off. The
odd part was that I did NOT get the warning that I was about to appear and
thus I had no time to run away and I did die. But the point is that I WAS
able to give gold and receive quest items back while invis. This was about
1 month ago. Since I was visible to PC's and didn't get a warning, it seems
to me that they intend for you to become visible and because of that, it may
have been fixed in any one of the patches since then. I will try to do
another quest invis and let you all know if it still works.
