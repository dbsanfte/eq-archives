Last night, I finished the quest for the Robe of Enshroudment (necro only
robe from the sol ro temple) It basically took me all weekend, I got the
werebat wing from Unrest first, by simply zoning in and auctioning that I'd
pay 5p to loot it off someone's kill, got one in about 2 minutes (the
easiest component by far) Next on my list was the globe of shadow, from the
Nokta shaman in upper guk. This fellow spawns just past the Nok Shaman, by
the ancient croc and he is accompanied by 2 shin knights which were blue to
me at 35 and it took about 4 kills to get the globe. The large shadow silk
was a pain in the butt, as shadow wolf pelts are a bit difficult to come by
quickly...what I did was zone into East Commons and shout "20p for each
shadow wolf pelt brought to me" the newbies went nuts and I don't think
there was a wolf standing for more than 5 seconds. Got my pelts in short
order. Luckily our guild has a decent tailor so he made the shadow silk and
it was time to get the mask of shadow from the shadowed men. I picked the
spawn in Nektulous, since it was close to the temple and I knew there
weren't any big beasties that would come interrupt me. There were usually 4
of them at the spawn, they repop quickly and it took about 3 spawns before
one dropped what I needed. In all, a relatively easy quest for a pretty
decent item (9AC, +4int, +4agi, +4 sv fire, +4 sv cold) and it looks very
cool, pure black...almost looks like your character is missing a texture. :)
I've already had numerous comments on how good it looks. That and it's nice
to not be one of the FBR/Oracle clones running around.

Grizumpy Bloodmaw, the fashion conscious necro.
