Greetings again folks,

The spell is cast directly on the opponet as LifeSpike is cast
directly on your opponet, so you just target the undead as a regular victem
before casting. As for the price of the spells, I think you paid about
average as I get the same prices with a Charisma of 80 in the city of
Qeynos. Vampiric Embrace I think as a general rule should be at the bottom
of one's to-buy list, but could be handy if you find yourself weaponless
(lost your corps), and will have to fight hand-to-hand. Sometimes you just
can't help it. In that case, cast Grim Aura as well, but try to avoid
fights at all cost of course.
