My wizard friend swears by the Everfrost zone. At
your level you will have moved up to the goblin
scouts and will soon be in the mammoth calf range
(with a large group). However, check your faction
with the barbarian guards. It would be impossible
to have any fun there if you are KOS.

Other ideas would include BB and west Karana. In
BB you are up to gnoll level (say away from
patrolling, burly, guards, guardsmen, elites and
commanders ... but move up from left to right on
that list as you gain levels). By level 9, start
hitting the giant snakes. You and your pet should
just be able to solo one. These will take you to
level 12 or more. By that time, either group for
the bigger BB kills or move to Splitpaw in South
Karana. It's the same old gnoll thing but the
nobles have nice loot, as well as the gnoll
shamans. I found west Karana way too boring and if
you aren't a tailor ... what's the point of
killing the bears and lions? The loot stinks.

You CAN make levels over there and keep hanging
with friends, but the post about going to Freeport
is correct. It's a little easier and there is a
better monster per person ratio there. Don't even
think of it until level 8 unless you've made the
trip before, or you have a guide. At level 8 at
least you can use gather shadows and wander
somewhat in order to find the zone points. When
you reach 8th, make a post here I'll be glad to
give you directions for a trip across should you
want to do it.

Good luck either way.

-Haytred
