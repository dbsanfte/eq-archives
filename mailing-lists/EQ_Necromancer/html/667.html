Well Unfortunately my Necromancer isn't quite that high level yet, however,
I do have other spell casting characters that run into this problem.
Actually I already have the problem a bit at level 10 with my Necromancer.

Generally if I am in a group Gate is not memorized. If something comes
along that will waste the party, we start running and my pet holds it off
so we can get a head start. This tactic can be used when fighting solo too
and frees up a spell slot for one of those other spells that are more critical.

The only time I keep Ward Undead in memory is if I am in an area that I
know will have undead, and I don't mean just decaying skeletons. Currently
I fight in South Ro alot. If I am killing orcs I don't bother with Ward
Undead, There just aren't enough undead in that area. If I move to the
sand part yes it becomes a great spell to have.

I trade off and on between Siphon str (in a group) and Impart Str when
soloing. Though I will probably end up needing to drop this to fit in the
Heat Blood line.

If I am in a group I am usually not in melee that much. Hence I don't
bother to keep my Grim Aura going. I do leave my lesser Shielding in
place. Its just a pain to let it wear off.

I wouldn't bother wish shadow step. It may have its uses in PvP or if you
want to get to the bottom of a high wall and not take any damage, but mobs
target too well for this to really be that effective.

I guess we can always hope that when they release the expansion in about 6+
months that they will redo the interface and make those spell icons a bit
smaller so we can fit more in the same space.
