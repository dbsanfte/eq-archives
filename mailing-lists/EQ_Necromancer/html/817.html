You want to hunt Jonah Bucker in Lake Rathe. He is in the South West corner in an inlet. You will see an inlet that has a small island sitting in the middle of the entrance. If you follow this inlet it is going to split. On one side is a double Aviak Guard spawn. On the other side is JB. You will see a shack, thats JB house. He is good for 17 and 18th lvl then hit the single Aviak Guard on the SE side of the lake until 21st or 22nd. Decent money and great exp.
