east freeport... don't go to north freeport until you have hunted orcs intil
you were sick of it... then hunted more orcs untill your dog, cat, kids,
wife, mom, dad, and the rest of your neghborhood are sick of you hunting
orcs... than maybee you can get into north freeport without being killed.)

still have to periodically go back to Neriak to bank. (the bank in freeport
is in North Freeport...)
