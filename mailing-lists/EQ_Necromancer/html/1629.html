Well first off, you are not kos in all of freeport, i think it is just near
the paladin and cleric guild. I always go the nro to freeport way to get on
boat and never have had a problem, but you can stand on the dock all day
long and no npc will bother you. DE's have a deal with the freeport militia
that is why we can walk freely around (for the most part).

Once you get on the boat, sister island is the first stop. I hunted there a
lot at your level and made quite a bit of money :) My favorite spot to set
up on the island looks like a little tear drop coming off the island where
none of the sisters go, so I could always med or pull to this area with no
problem. As soon as the boat turns and is lined up the edge of the island
on the right, I would jump off the boat and swim to this point and commence
with the slaughtering. You are kos to the people at the dock, so stay away.
