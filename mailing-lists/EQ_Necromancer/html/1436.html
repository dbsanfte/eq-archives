Both items are a piece of cake to get.

To get the scepter in lguk, how i get to it is go through scryer room
towards jail and hang every right until you zone. You'll need to make sure
you take a right when you go up some stair like things with spiders on them.
Once you zone, travel to you take a left or right, take a left and soon
you'll be going up some stairs near a waterfall, turn in room on right, pick
up scepter, and your done.

To get the scepter in mistmoore, take a right where the gargoyles spawn and
follow path till you see a gate on left, go through gate straight into tome
(building), pick up the sack on the casket which is a flower, go straight
back out (going straight in and straight out is important because i hear
there are traps on either side of the casket, but was unwilling to try this
out for myself : ). Go out the door from the tome and go behind the
building you'll see a named shadowman, give him the flower and you have the
scepter.

The hardest part of the quest, in my opinion (although I never died doing
it, but another nec lost a lot of exp doing it) is getting the globe of
shadow in guk. I was and still am 30th, (i've going back and forth from
31st killing shadowmen) so that may make a difference in how we finally
broke into the room. But to get to the spawn, I went to lguk were we got
the scepter and at the top of the waterfalls, follow the water up stream,
you'll need breath of the dead for this, and hang every right, you'll
eventually zone back into guk. When you reach the end of the river you'll
see an island in the middle of the water with 3 froggies on it. There are 3
planks on the island, the one that goes in the direction that you were just
swimming is another entrance to lguk, the one to your left you don't care
about (or at least i didn't) and the one on the right is where the nokta
shaman spawns. Our group had to get a high level nec in to help us break
the spawn, because we weren't able to cast pets at the entrance to this room
since we were too close to about 6 froggies (the ones on the island and the
ones in the room), once we broke the spawn, it took about 3 hours to get 3
globes.

Good luck
