Head to Netkulos Forest. Summon a pet, memorize sense undead. Then
'/auction Buying 20 bonechips for 3pp'. Then cast sense undead...run to
your target, kill it and loot. Rinse and Repeat. After every 5 minutes,
throw the auction back up. Anytime I run and low and do this...it takes me
an hour max and I leave the forest with about 200 bonechips. Good luck.
