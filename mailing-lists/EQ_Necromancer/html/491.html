'fraid you wont find much around Qeynos. The best thing for you to do
is make a trek to Freeport. That's undead haven there, I swear. You've got
numerous little ruins outside, plus Befallen (watch out for horrible trains
& that damn Ghoul & Ice Boned) you can put that Ward Undead to use. Beware
of fizzles, however.

As for around Qeynos.. eh.. Karanas is the best place. Just kill the
grizzlies and spidies. It'll take longer, but you're almost guranteed the
experiance from them so long as you're not double timed. Just remember to
smack 'em with poisen, maybe Clinging Darkness and/or Fear, and leave enough
mana for a Mend Bones or Gate. :-) You're not going to get much more around
Qeynos. I left the place at level 10 for Freeport, got 56 gold in three
days there, a greater light stone, two magical (Cut-Throat Insignia)
rings..... and lost level 10. *grin* Even the Orc Centurian around the
entrance to the city of Freeport carries leggings at times, which you can
sell for gold.

Freeport is nice, but very dangerous. Those damn young kodiaks hate you
and will attack you on sight. They're pretty tough at this level,
especially with a giant spider or two in tow. Both of which roam around the
immediate area of Freeport, one zone from the city. Good exp, just watch
your back. Also, just because a zombie /con`s blue doesn't mean it's an
easy kill. I made it out of a fight against two of those with a sliver of
mana and life. Granted, I had no pet at the time, but geeze they hurt.

Ghouls and the Ice Boned Skeleton takes magical weapons & spells to hurt
it. Your ward undead can kill it the Ice Boned if somebody else is
distracting it so you can get all of them off, but not face-to-face as it
will stun you repeatedly. Ward Undead on the Ghoul is a waste of mana.
It'll resist virtually every one of them. Don't -- Fight -- The -- Ghoul
:-)

Also, the desert is a great place. The Giant Scarab Beetles are very
easy kills -- I can sit and meditate while my skeleton beats the snot out of
them (just remember to poisen them & keep a watch on your skelly's life) --
and gives okay experiance. Zombies roam the desert, as well as skeletons.
/con _everything_ first, especially the skeletons. Many of them will /con
red to you.

Good luck. Qeynos at level 10 is incredibly boring...

~Mysk
