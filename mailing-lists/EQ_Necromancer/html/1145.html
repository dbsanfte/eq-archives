Grab your little piles of bones and put your travelling shoes on and head
for Guk. It's down in Innothule swamp next to the troll city. There you'll
find tons of little froggies just dying (sorry for the pun, couldn't resist)
to give you their woven armor.

A warning, though. Don't do it solo and check your pet with the coat girl
at the door unless you're extremely talented at keeping it under control.
I've seen so many pet related train deaths in that dungeon that those cute
little skeletons ought to come with a warning label from the surgeon
general.
