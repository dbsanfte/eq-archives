On Mon, 12 Jun 2000, John Sanders wrote:

> Can anybody recommend any other good solo areas for me at 21st lvl?

If you have a fast machine, Warsliks woods right by the zone to Overthere
is great for 20-25 or so. Lots of dark blue to yellow targets. Likewise
the goblin ruins in the north part of the zone. At 24 you can take forest
giants from the fort (before that you're still ok taking white or easier
wanderers). Warsliks sucks badly if your framerate doesn't keep up, but
other Kunark zones aren't as bad.

Sarnaks in Lake of Ill Omen are probably too tough to solo well, but you
can likely take goblins from the caves there. Sometimes hard to get
singles though.

Kurn's tower in Field of Bone is perfect as well - tons of L15-19 or so
skellies and buryani.

I haven't found any good darkness/fear spots in Kunark - your strategy is
mostly dark/dot/heal pet, or sometimes dark/dot/dot/run such that you can
control the path of the MoB (but then DoT's only do 2/3 damage).

In the old world, Unrest was great for me at L18-L20 or so, but only in a
group that can take a room inside. There are some good spots in Cazic in
the maze where you can fight safely and pull singles most of the time.
Better with a partner, but still doable.

Permafrost is absolutely wonderful - if solo, fight the light blue stuff
in the tunnel before the flag room. If you get 2-4 friends, you're ok
pretty deep in.

SK and Highkeep are hellholes. avoid them unless you have a lot of
patience for 12-year-olds.
