From Lake ZONE .. . .

1) go left follow wall (hugging it on your left side) until you see the
gypsy camp (tents) on your right . ..

2) keep going straight passing the gypsy camp on your right (you will now be
hugging the wall on your left side) kind of jump to the other side so your
still going the same direction but the wall is now on your other side, you
will know what I mean once you are there.

3) you will pass a small "village" bunch o' huts as you go on... keep
hugging the wall on your right until you come to a pillar looking thing w/ 3
named BUGS around it. They are like L8 I think .. .

4) turn left at the pillar (the stairs of the pillar point the way to
ferrott) and run straight all the way staying in the middle of the "mountain
pass" (if you go straight or right at the pillare there are MPC troll/Ogre
merchants)

FEERROTT is a green looking zone passage dead ahead about 1-2 min run w/ SOW
from the Pillar

just bind at the LAKE, MOUNTAIN zone entrance on the LAKE side (cyclopses
suck) and GATE back if you get lost and try again. ;-)
