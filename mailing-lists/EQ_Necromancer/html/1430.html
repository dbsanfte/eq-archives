A good way to look at delay is in seconds. So a weapon
with delay of 50 will attack once every 5 seconds,
while a fs dagger with delay of 19 will attack once
every 1.9 seconds.
Give your pet a very slow weapon and you will notice
that monsters will attack twice or even three times
before you get to attack. Start a 1st level warrior
and test this. Give him a rusty scythe and go fight.
Then give him a tarnished dagger(sharpened rusty,
should cost like 5 silver total) with half the delay
of the scythe and go fight. You WILL notice a big
difference.
