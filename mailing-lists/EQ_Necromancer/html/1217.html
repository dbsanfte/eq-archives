Group and split. There is a base 10% bonus to experience for any groups.
Whether it is 2 or 6 10% is added to the total exp of the kill before the
split. If 2 of you hunt as a team you each get 55% of each kill instead of
50% of overall kills. Adding more members drops this bonus a bit and makes
2 man teams the most beneficial pure experience wise, but larger teams can
kill more faster so it make up for this.

Even a 2 man team should drop risk and downtime greatly.
