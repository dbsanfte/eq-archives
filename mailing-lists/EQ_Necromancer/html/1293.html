You should be able to get off at the dock ok since the npc that meets the
boat is indifferent to all races/classes. If that makes you nervous though,
jump off the boat after you set sail from the bb docks just on the other
side of the wall which is a much shorter journey to unrest than jumping off
the boat before the docks.

You'll need to watch out for dwarf guards and you should be able to sell to
a druid that is kinda on the way to unrest if you jump off the boat after is
leaves port. Just con the druid prior to selling.
