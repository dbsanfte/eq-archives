I spent almost 5 levels in lavastorm. There are mobs there for all levels
but it is an extremely dangerous place if you arnt careful. First of all,
everything there will resist heat blood so you only have 2 DOTs to stack,
second the fire elementals are very aggressive and quite powerful sometimes.

I had yellow cons at level 17 on them. The FEs fire shield will kill your

pet more often than not also. The imps are fairly easy prey around level 15

and the basalisks are doable too but dont solo them. They will stunn you or

cast stone breath on you and then your dead meat. The good thing is its
close to neriak and you can lure from the Najena entrance and can escape
readily by zoning.

Good Luck
