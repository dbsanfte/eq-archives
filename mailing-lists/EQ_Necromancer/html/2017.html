No. The previous message was talking about a pet from the L24 spell. No
cheap weapon is good enough to increase that pet's damage.

For levels up to about 16, however, it still makes sense to give your pet
a rusty 2H Sword, Halberd, or Scythe. Personally, even at L21 I give my
pet a rusty weapon because I prefer the look.

Pets do the better of their natural damage and the damage of the weapon
they hold. They ignore the delay of the weapon.
