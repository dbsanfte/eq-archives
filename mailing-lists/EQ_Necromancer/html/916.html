It is a research ONLY level 20 spell.

It gives the user 18 seconds on invulnerability (NOTHING can ham you, though
you can be stunned) During that 18 seconds you cannot cast a spell. As far
as I know, there are no required casting components.

There is a long regen time between casts, and when you first memorize it
(though I MAY have hit a bug... I zoned while it was still on, and it was
available immediately again...)
