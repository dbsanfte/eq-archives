see my previous post, then go to S Karanas and tool some aviaks, they're
heavily camped but you should be able to make quick xp and decent loot. It
gets a tad boring after a while, but you can spice things up by zoning to
lake rathe to the south at night time and kicking some undead ass (drybones,
greater zombies) also, if you don't mind hosing your Qeynos faction, you can
fight treants in the southwest corner of S karana. I went from 20 to 24
fighting egrets/darters/rooks (aviak) and at 24 I can easily take out
harriers that are yellow/red for a nice half bubble of xp each. If you
aren't comfortable soloing yellow/red mobs, you can always hook up with a
warrior group and beat up avocets. at 22, I was in a *great* party, 2
barbarian warriors, a barbarian shaman and a human rogue, we were
slaughtering avocets left and right. great xp and loot. The avo's will drop
black pearls which jewlers buy for a hefty price. You can easily reach 29th
lvl without ever leaving S karana. You can sell items to centaur merchants
at their village, the only drawback is food/water and banking which to my
knowledge you have to go to either Qeynos (don't fight treants if you're
taking this option) or Grobb (lake rathe/rathe mtns/Innothule/grobb) Good
luck!
