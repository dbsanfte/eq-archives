Restless Bones and Banshee Aura are available in the SK guild in
Grobb (Troll city) or in Oggok. Feign Death is available in Neriak (not
exactly sure where, but I think maybe the Enchanter's guild). Hungry Earth
and Umbral Messanger are two of the spells that you have to create I believe
since nobody seems to have found them. Start saving up those practice
points for Research and Languages.
