Hello.

If you have only just made your char and haven't gotten any levels yet, I
would suggest simply deleting your char and making an identical new one, and
going straight to your guild and giving the note.

As a 2nd option, you could make a 2nd necro char and give the note to a
friend and then have the friend give you the note. Though I think it would
be easier and faster to go with the first option.

Also, unlike the average race, Dark Elves like necromancers alot, so you are
very welcome in the city. This make early levels of being a necromancer
easier. However, being both a necromancer and a dark elf will not make you
popular outside of Neriak. Thus, I have elected not to go into ANY other
citys but instead hire people to do my shopping.
