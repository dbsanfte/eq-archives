Congrats! :)

As far as specs in Oasis go, you should be able to solo one...however you
can't pull singles off the island until you hit 46 or so. So until you can
do that safely, avoid being the only one fighting them as it's rather rude
to kite one then train the rest of the island to zone everytime ;) a nice
darkness/fear/pet combo will finish one off easily, as with any outdoor mob.
Just get one to yourself and keep him feared and slowed while your pet does
the dirty work.
