Heh, I dropped from 24th to 22nd when Kunark came out. Exploring was fun
but not profitable.

Fortunately, I hate soloing. I was able to do well with partners up to
full groups in Warsliks Woods (goblin ruins, outside of giant fort, and
the tunnel to Dalnir) and Lake of Ill Omen (goblin and sarnak groups) at
L22-29, then move into Overthere.

MistMoore courtyard and various rooms in Unrest are good in the low 20s,
and upper Guk is fabulous if you have a group, but you'll never find a
pickup group there.

People say good things about sisters in OOT, but I never used them.
You're still in a good range for OOT gargoyles and aviaks though.
