>I'm now a LVL 6 DE necro, and would like to know the best places to solo
>by lvl till about 20th or so.


If you have enough faction to walk through Rivervale, then I would suggest
the pirahna in the lake there. They are great xp up through level 10 and
they spawn quickly. They also drop a tooth that you can turn into Fiddy
Bobeck right there for another big chunk of xp, a gold, and some RV factions
(RV merchant faction which is hard to get), and sometimes an additional item
like a pouch, food, or beverage. You only have to be dubious to turn in the
teeth. While your waiting for the Pirahna to spawn, you can kill the trout.
All added up you can make 4 plat an hour if you can keep up with the spawn
rate.

Great xp, good faction, excellent money maker, and unlimited food, its a
great place to go at your level. The only downside is that this place is
slowly catching on, but isn't a problem yet.

Dicey

P.S. Beware of the cleric guild near the Kith zone, I have never been able
to fix my faction with them. you may want to plan on going here at 8th
level when you get gather Shadows. That will give you time to kill some
Deathfist orcs to get enough faction to walk safely in RV.
