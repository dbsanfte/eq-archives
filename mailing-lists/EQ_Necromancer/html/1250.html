>On another note, does anyone know a trick for getting around the limit
>of 20 names on your friends list? Especially since it is local and thus
>all your characters are drawn from the same list.

The only trick I have managed to use is to create a social that basically says:
Friend2
/who all <name>
/who all <name>
/who all <name>
/who all <name>
/who all <name>

It gives you an extra five friends to keep an eye on. I suppose if you
have enough social space you could make a Friend3 social as well.

What really annoys me is that if you start up on a 2nd server, your friends
list carries over. How many friends do you have on the 2nd server that use
the same name as that of the first server. Personally I wish the friends
list was specific to each server, however verant dosen't appear to think so
as I did a feedback on this quite a few months ago.
