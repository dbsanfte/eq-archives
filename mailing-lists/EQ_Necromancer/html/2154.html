Goblin Headmaster is on the Oracle Island. The Oracle island is right off
the end of the dock on Sister's Island. Sister's island is the one stop the
boat makes FROM Freeport.

Run around the edge of the island until you find a "Y" looking oblisk. The
Headmaster spawns between the Oblisk and a Goblin camp with a ten in it. He
spawns closer to the tent though.
