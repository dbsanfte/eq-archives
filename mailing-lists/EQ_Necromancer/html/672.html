i would really just like to be able to have spells ready in case of
emergencies, without hurting my ordinary ability to dish out damage. for
example, i almost always have ward undead, even if there are only a few
undead around. resting after a battle and getting attack by a zombie is no
fun, even though they are rather weak opponents. even a high level,
ordinary skeleton can do some damage if you dont have a pet and/or ward.
just for comparison, i got trained by 3 skels, without a pet, and i was able
to kill them all. by the time i finished i was severly low on hp, and just
about out of mana (enough for gather shadows). if i wouldnt have had ward,
i would have been dark elf pot pie.
i also like to keep either gather shadows or gate on hand, but it would be
nice to have access to both. also, i almost always use dark pact, and the
extra mana has saved my buns many a time. it would just be nice to have the
ability to use a good portion of the spells i have, instead of needing to
choose a small handful. im sure any spellcaster can agree with that, but
since we are already limited in our ability to take damage, i think the
versatility would help, even if it doesnt really make us all that much more
powerful.

belphenor
