Subject: Invisible Necro Guild..?


Hi All..

A few days ago I read on one of the web servers about an "invisible gnome
warrior guild." Basically one low level gnome warrior was struggling when a
higher level one came by and gave him some very good (for his level)
equipment. His parting words were to return the favor to another gnome when he
was able to, which he swore he would do.

After reading some messages in this list a few days ago about travelling to
the Innothule Swamp and Grobb I thought I'd take a wander down and see what it
was like down there. I traveled in shadow fearing the monsters I'd encounter
on the way, little did I know a fellow Necromancer was in a zone along my
journey and happened to see me pass by. He inquired about my journey and
offered to lead me the rest of the way and bind me to Grobb which he did (my
first binding from home, yeah!) and even refused my offer of payment. If
hunger (in the real world) didn't pull me away from EverQuest I would have
taken him up on his offer for a tour of the zone.

For someone who was twice threatened and harassed by characters "roleplaying"
I am happy to see other friendly characters out there looking out for others.

I'd like to propose the creation of an invisible necromancer guild.. I'll
certainly remember the kind actions of a fellow necromancer and hope to return
the favor some day to another necromancer in need. Our Guild isn't easy and
certainly those of the Dark Elf race have an even harder time. Sharing of
knowledge on forums like this one is a good thing and helpful to all,
hopefully kind actions is another thing we can start doing for each other.

Many thanks go out to Manick @ Innoruuk for his help and assistance.
