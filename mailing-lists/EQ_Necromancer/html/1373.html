I do most of my PVP in the Arena zone, (this is a blast by the way, a whole
new way to have fun in the game...tired of camping for that rare loot? Go
to arena and beat some people down/get beat down.) You can bind in the zone
regardless of class, so downtime is very short (I usually have someone kill
me when I'm oom, so I don't have to waste time medding) There's no looting
and no xp loss, just fun :) Necros are a *very* powerful PVP class,
unfortunately you can't summon a pet in the arena (he kills himself
immediately) but even without him you can kick some ass. I beat down an
equal level enchanter, a magician, a wizard a shaman and a warrior that was
5 levels above me.
