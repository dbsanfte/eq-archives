> weapons still. Some guides suggest not giving them weapons past level

Dual wield is different from double attack. At L29, you'll be able to
summon pets that can wield two weapons at once, and then you must give
them 2 1H weapons for maximal effect.

Below that level, give them weapons only if it will increase their damage,
which is very hard to do at L16 (perhaps a FS 2H sword), and cannot be
done at 20 or 24, so don't bother.
