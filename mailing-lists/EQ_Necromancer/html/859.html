The 3 dot combo works gret for me... Fetch with darkness, Send pet to tank,
when he is about at 80% or so hit with heat-blood, let pet tank and possible
heal him a little if it is a particularly nasty mob.. Let pet get to about
50-60% and hit with heart flutter... Let pet drop to 40 then go melee...
Sometimes you will pull the mob to you, but pet will usually still taunt it
off and personally when I am at full at pet is below 30% I'd rather have it
smacking me... Plus sdarkness slows them enough if you do pull it you can
kite them and let bash it in the back till it evens out the damage and turns
to the pet...
