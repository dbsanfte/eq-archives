I made lvl 12 in nro exclusively, basically hunting scarabs and tarantulas
(desert, not dune) and taking the occasional mummy if the occasion arose.
The key to success at level 11, is keep casting your pet until you get the
high level one that has bash, test him out on something weak (fire beetles
etc) if he bashes, he's a keeper, if he doesn't reclaim energy and try
again, he should /con blue.
