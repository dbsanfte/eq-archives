They're in the everquest_manual.txt file in your \everquest\ directory.

/pet
sit down
stand up
attack
back off
guard me
guard here
as you where <- I've never noticed that do anything.

be _sure_ you /pet follow me after you tell it to sit, as it will not get up and follow you unless you do. Thus, you could lose your pet, especially in a big zone.
