>1)
>Is the only way to make a pet attack to click on the pet and then say "kill
a large rat"
>because I would like to select the creature that I want him to attack and
say PET KILL or PET ATTACK and that did not work.

You can use the command /pet attack , to make it attack your current
target. :-) You can also bind it to a key. Take Social (such as wave, but
not nessacarly that one), and right click on it. A screen comes up that
lets you edit it. Make the command in there /pet attack

Then all you have to do is click the button. :-)

>2) I saw some necromancer in Unrest and he had this skeleton w/ him and the
skeleton would fall into a pile of bones after saying something like "Taking
new form >master" Then later the pile of bones would float back into a
full skeleton. HOW WAS HE DOING THAT????

/pet sit down

Just be very, very careful to tell it /pet follow me , before
you start walking again. If you don't, the pet will stay there where it
"sat". You don't have to tell it to /pet stand up , just /pet follow me ,
which will make it stand up and begin following once you're a certain
distance from it.
