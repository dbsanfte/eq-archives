Don't forget you'll need to get a rusty weapon everytime your skel
DIES.

At that level don't hesitate to step in there from the very
beginning with your skel and help kill it. Usually you can beat up
something pretty good double teaming, then stepping back and draining, then
stepping back in. Doing this you should be able to handle yellow
spiderlings and beetles fairly easily. Just watch for teamups and
gangbangs, ESPECIALLY with the spiders. Always look around between strikes
to make sure there's not other spiderlings bearing down on you.

Malark on Karana
