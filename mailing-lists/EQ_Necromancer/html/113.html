Ok, this is how to go from Qeynos to freeport or freeport to qeynos. This
was done at level 9 by a bard, the only part of caution will be a couple KOS
places. This is from a Gnomish Necromancers perspective.

Starting out in Qeynos you leave to the Karanas. You hang to your left all
the way. Once you reach the bridge you go to your left again. You might want
to watch out for the Cyclops fisherman adn the druids. But once you get in
the hills stay up in them as much as possible to the left. Keep going and
you will reach beholders maze. In the maze you stay to the right UP ON the
wall as much as possible. Go slow and don't rush. Many a peopl ehave fallen
to their demise. IT is good to be invis all this time also. Once you go far
enough along the right wall. You will reach the entrance to RunnyEye
Citadel. In there stay to the left wall and you will come out in Misty
Thicket. In misty Thicket I Would recommend going straight forward until you
find the wall. You should stay invisable especailly at this time because
there are guards in this area. You will pass through a gate in this stone
fence and follow the road, taking the left fork. You will come to a tunnel.
This will lead to riverdale. Go invisable before entering Riverdale, as they
will kill you. Though if you want you can kill a lot of goblins in the area
and your faction will eventually get good enough to enter without
invisability. But once in river dale find the entrance. I remeber it as head
right around the big circle building and there is a gnomish lady selling
stuff in a stand and I go down the path right before her. Not that that will
help on other servers. But hey Always check out those maps on Stratics. Well
once you get out of riverdale you will be in Kithicor forest. Stay to the
left and keep following the wall. This zone is fairly harmless, however I
am not sure how all of the inhabitance will react. Some are friendly to me.
There are also large animals I believe, and they will probably be
aggressive. Now once you zone, you will be in The Commonlands. You should
run along the road, dodging hill giants. OR you can stay to the left a bit
and you should miss them. However beware of along the left wall there are
Dervish camps and undead shrines. Bewarned, so you don't stumble on them. If
you stay near the left you keep going, oh beware the druid shrines also that
are near the dervish camp and near the teleport gate. They have something
against necromancers. Well stay to the left and eventually you will zone
into the other commonlands. Keep going and you will come to a zone to the
left. Do not go into there unless you want to go to the Nektulous forest.
Keep going and you will run into a vast waste land. Keep going and you will
zone into West Freeport. From their I am sure you can find th egates. Good
luck..
