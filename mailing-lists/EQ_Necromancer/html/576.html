I would definately go to Lavastorm, and you can stay there for the next 4
levels, at least. The experience is great, the loot is wonderful, there are
words to be found here, and it is grossly underpopulated. Just make sure
you either have a summoned dagger for your skeleton or it is level 12, since
there are many magical creatures here. It also helps to have a RTS with
vampiric embrace.
