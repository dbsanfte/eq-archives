Subject: Seventh Level Itch


This is a question for experienced folks, and some advice for newbies.

7th level as a Necro to date was the most frustrating level for me (Dark
Elf, not sure if that makes a difference). Probably the main reason is that
a lot of the creatures you used to fight for good EXP now /con green to you.
Almost all large spiders, quite a few shadow wolfs, and a number of bears
/con green and don't give you any EXP when you kill them. This leaves skels
and tree snakes (Nek Forest) as the only reliable EXP you can find. To top
it off, since you're maxed out in spells and armor at this point, you
usually don't have much reason to kill except to gain EXP to level up, which
just makes it more monotonous. So, any newbie players to the Monks, be
warned that the 7th level will make you want to quit the class (It almost
did me), if you can tough it out 8th level has some nice benefits.

The question for experienced players is: is this common for anylevel before
a new spell rank? I.E. does level 11, 15, etc. seem to take forever? Or is
it just level 7?
