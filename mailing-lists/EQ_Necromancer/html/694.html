Easy, go into Nektulos and buy bulk. I usually make a weekly stop
in Nektulos and auction "Buying Bonechips in bulk! 5gp for 20, 1.2 plat for
40, 3plat for 80" or something like that. I don't mind the prices because
at that level we should all be carrying around enough "petty cash", at least
I am. I usually keep a good 12-20 plat on me for bonechips, SOWs (I pay for
them, I think its only fair), and other niceties. Usually it takes no
time at all to rake in about 120-160 bonechips (you carry four backpacks
right?) then head off for another week of fun.
