I would go with Treants in SK from 20 - 25. A full group takes them
with no problem... 2 Necros can do the same. Not only is it good EXP
but the Loot is great (Can get heavy at times).

From 25 - 30 I suggest Druids in EK or Rangers in WK. Once again
Great exp and Loot.

For 30 - 35 Ulrich in WK is the best, this is a prime spot for
Necro's and druids. He is on a 6 minute timer and is tough at 30 and
a breeze at 35.

For 35 - 40 start taking Einhorst. He is in the same camp as Ulrich.
Keep in mind you must kill ULrich before pullin Ein or you will be
using FD alot.

If you dont have problems in groups for Treants & Druids or getting
the Barb Spawns then you can go from 20 - 40 in about 2 or 3 weeks of
good game play.
