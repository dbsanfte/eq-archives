as some others, It is the most fun to roleplay. However, I do wish you
would consider the Troll race over the ogre... it is true that on paper, the

troll is far superior to the ogre in any gameplay situation. Get your
strength to 125-130 and then put the rest into int for 70-75... And regen is
often written off as impractical in the later game... I can not tell you the
number of times you will be jumped while killing frogz and Lizziez an tings,

de ograyz. An ebberbody noz dat onlee da trolliez iz da real shady
knights... ograys gud basherrz cuz dey no smart, lik marines. Den da
trolliez can take wat da ded ograys wuz fightun. An da dark elfiez is gud
tu! Dey tough ifun you fry dem, but dey boil gud! An ifun you grab dem on
da neck, dey mak gud clubz! But no be dum ogray...
