I believe

Scowls at you, ready to attack is the KOS all the time message. If you are
too high a level and they are smart enough they'll still leave you alone
though undead and elementals have a VERY low morale loss rating (undead in
fact never run I think) so they almost always attack.

Glares at you threateningly is sort of KOS, meaning I think they have a
lower agg range. In Rathe Mountains I can get close to preservers that con
this to me, but if I attack a bear or something while they are nearby or I
get TOO close they go into agg mode. I think basically their agg range is
reduced and if you do anything other than walk/run by they attack.

Glowers at you dubiously is non KOS but non vending. They don't jump you,
they don't get mad when you beat on stuff (unless they are assigned to
protect whatever you are beating on) but they won't buy/sell anything and
they won't do questy type things either.

Looks at you indifferently, nonKOS and will do vending and quests

Lokks at you warmly is non KOS and vending and questing and means you are in
good standing.

Looks at you amiably is the same only better. I think this also means you
get discounts at vendors and stuff, but I don't know for sure.
