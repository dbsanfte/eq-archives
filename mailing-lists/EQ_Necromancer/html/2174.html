Prior to getting to level 20, I hunted on Sister Isle in OOT. Once I
reached 19/20, I began camping Ratraz in Neriak (I'm a DE, btw). Between
the two of them, I was able to get most of the words I needed for my level
24 research spells. I still had to auction for a few, though. Once I
accumulated the words, a level 50 necro at the bank was gracious enough to
combine my words for me, giving me my level 24 pet without fear of losing
the words.

My fellow guild members help me out a bunch also. Whenever I find that I'm
in need of a particular word, I send the message over /guild and usually
within a short period of time I find what I'm looking for. And my guild
isn't all that big either. At most, 11-12 people online at once.

But the official rule of thumb is, if you need level 24 and up words, hunt
level 24 and up mobs. Odd thing is that I was finding level 24 words on
the Sisters and Ratraz, who are most definitely BELOW level 24.
