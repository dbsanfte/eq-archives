>Whre else could i gowhere i can fight gambles without trains?

East Karana. Gorge hounds, Carrion Spiders, and Crag Spiders. Crag Spiders
have a nasty venom. If you use a pet, watch his HP after the fight. Stay
on the north end of the zone. I pulled to my pet sitting on the slope of
the border between the bandit camp and the Gorge zone. I spent a lot of
time there and I was never bothered by anything there except once Droon
killed my pet while I was away pulling.

Also in EK is Milea Clothspinner who is never camped, but she doesn't
spawn as fast as you would like. Milea can be found on the valley floor on
the way to High Hold. She spawns in a short tunnel and doesn't wander.

When you get a few levels, you can try Squire Wimbley. You can take him
when he cons yellow, but he hangs out with Sir Morgan who is 30th. If you
see someone camping Sir Morgan, or you see a griff kill Sir Morgan, go for
Wimbley, he is pretty easy for a Yellow and great XP. Squire Wimbley spawns
by the bridge and waits a couple minutes before walking over to join Sir
Morgan, this is a good time to kite him along the beach. Wimbley has a very
fast spawn rate. You probably wont have enough down time to control his
spawn before he walks over to Morgan. 2 necros can control Wimbleys spawn
if you can get someone to give you a head start by killing Morgan once.

Dicey (Lived in EK for 4 levels)
