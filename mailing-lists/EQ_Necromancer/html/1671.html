Ugh. If Oasis is boring and crowded, the solution is NOT Lake Rathe or S
Karanas Aviaks. Just as crowded, just as annoying, and just as boring as
Oasis.

15 or so is where groups or partners get really handy. Spiders, snakes,
and puppies in N Karanas are plentiful, but a poison curer is just about
required. Minos and muddites in the Gorge are good, but probably require
a partner or small group as well.

Kodiaks (non-young) in W Commons are probably about right for you and you
can solo them in all likelihood.

Everfrost is going to have orcs and ice-boned skellies for you. Rathe
mountains has a couple of options. You can pick up ice- and dark-boned
skeletons if there's a group at the ruin taking the big skeletons. In the
south of the zone there's a bunch of drakes and basilisks that should be
very good (but don't get too close to the sphinx).

Unrest is actually very good if you have a decent group (at least 4 people
recommended, including one tank and one wis-based caster). Yard trash
will still be blue, and pulling ghouls and mummies from the first floor is
easy. Pull to the back wall and you'll almost never get trained on.
