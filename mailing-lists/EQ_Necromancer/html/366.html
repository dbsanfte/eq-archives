Once you tick the creature off, numb isn't going to do a whole lot, it's a
preventative spell, say there are 2 skele's that you want to take down, but
you can only take one at a time. numb one so it won't attack you while you
kill the other one. Be careful though, more often than not the spell will
fail when you most need it and both mobs will be angry at you.
