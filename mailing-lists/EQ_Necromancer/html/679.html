Well I liked Steamfont right at the zone entrance to Lesser Faydark, there are lots of Skeletons that will CON Yellow or Black to you and you can solo them w/ your pet and Ward undead. Also you can fight Large Plague Rats there and Drakes if you feel daring. Pumas are there too, and not many people. plus the ZONE to lesser fay is right there so you can run to the ZONE if IT hits the FAN . ..

I went from 8th lvl to 12th lvl here in about a week . . . now that I am 12th I am in unrest and I killed Death beetles last night for about 2 hours and am 1/2 way to 13th now. :-)

One thing to look out for in Steamfont is LARGE PLAGUE RATS and SKELETONS are friends and will TAG TEAM YOU, so only attack one if the other is not close by. The Large Plague Rats are a little tougher than the Pumas, so I would start out with Pumas and Skeletons if you have WARD UNDEAD . . . ;-)
