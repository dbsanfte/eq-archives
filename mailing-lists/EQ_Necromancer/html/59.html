Ruins, but you're going to have competition. Make sure you have Bone
Walk before you go there, too.

Just leave Qeynos, go up where you zone into the hills, and follow
the _right_ wall of the zone. You'll come to a big open mouth cave. That's
Black Burrow. Go past it a little ways. The ruins are up to your right.
Be careful of the Putrids as you'll get hurt pretty badly probably, and the
Dread Corps will probably kill you. Don't let 'em team up on ya, for sure.
Oh, an Varsoon wont attack you unless you attack a skelly around him, or
talk to him. In which case you're a gonner. :)
