The trip to Freeport from the Qeynos areas is harrowing at best for a Necro.
Not only will the beasties try to do you in, but many of the NPC farmers
will chase after you. I finally just said screwit and waited until I hit
8th so I would have the benefit of gather shadows. The way I went from 7th
to 8 rather quickly was basically soloing blackburrow. There's so many
people in there it can be difficult to actually get a kill, but this also
works for you in that trains are pretty rare now, and when there is one,
there are many targets for them to focus on, allowing a hasty retreat.
