I have 3 buttons setup that I use regularly. "pet attack" "pet stand up"
and "pet follow me" about the only one that needs explanation is the "stand
up" this command will make your pet stand in the same place, rather than
follow you all over the dungeon/zone. I've found that a good strategy
(while soloing) is to have your pet stay in one place and train things back
to it, using clinging/engulfing darkness to slow the mob down while pulling
it.
