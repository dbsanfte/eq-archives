Next quest in Neriak.

The menial tasks quest in neriak os followed by other tasks quest. You are told about
it once you return the full box of fire beetle eyes. This quest includes assembling
more beetle eyes, but from beetles that are only found on Kerra Island, you only need 4
of those. You're told that population of Kerra Isle is protective of those beetles,
also you're requested to bring a tusk of some elephant creature which roams the plains
of west of Qeynos.

Also, there is a low-level (similar to menial tasks) quest in Freeport. Don't remember
the details, but they are given once you hail the Necro guild master NPC underneath
freeport. You really don't get much for it either.
