My human necromancer had little trouble making the journey to Freeport from
Qeynos, but I did have Gather Shadows to assist me. I waited until level 8
for this reason specifically. The guards in Freeport actually look at me
"apprehensively" which I *think* is better than the dubious looks I got in
Qeynos, though I could be mistaken. There are the odd shop keepers who will
look threateningly, I just make it a habit of conning every npc I come close
too.

The necro guild in Freeport is in a condemned building in East Freeport,
through (what else) a false wall. There are nice maps of the 3 sections of
Freeport on Eqvault under city guiles that I've had open during my first few
days in this new city.
