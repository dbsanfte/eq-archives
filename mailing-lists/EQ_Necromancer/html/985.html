Well you have done the same as me. I hunted in so ro till level 15. Then I
was dieing for something new. Well I found it. Lake rath. Its near the ogre
home land. You hunt Aviak Guards. Look for the island that has a cave to a
zone called the arina. Just out side the cave is 2 towers with solo Aviak
guards on them. You pull one down with a big dot and fight inside the cave.
If you run into trouble you zone to the arina. Its allot of fun and you make
some good cash as well. Then just go left out side of the cave to the next
island and at night there is undead spawns. You get rts and skulls and gold
stud earrings off the gnoll embalmers, high shaman's. Look out this place
gets really hard but the loot is wicked.
