In Freeport necros are barely tolerated... but a lot of merchants
will just look and spit on you. A good thing to do is go to the East and
West Commons and slaughter orc pawns and centurions wholesale. They improve
faction little by little, but as a necromancer some of your options will
always be limited. However if you follow Innoruuk you should be able to get
anything you want in Neriak.

From Freeport head into the East Commonlands and then head west to
the West Commonlands. One of the zone walls is a desert wall, stay on it
and follow it till you get to the Befallen entrance.
