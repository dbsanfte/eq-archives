Ok concerning Runed Staves. There are Totem Runed Staffs found in paw off of
two monsters. Err or two spawns. On Spawn is like some Rexxon Rex or
something like that. He is a high shaman I beleive. You drop down at the
first bridge and he is up some stairs there. However beware of the gnoll
commanders and nobles. Rex is level 16. Next is near the five shaman spawn.
There is a door kind of behind it. In there spawns 2 nobles and inside that
little room are 2 more doors. The door on the left spawns gnoll captain and
a gnoll shaman. The captain is generally level 16. Then in the right door is
a named Shaman spawn plus a normal shaman and a noble spawn. The named
shaman has the runed totem Staff. They only appear once in a whiole. I spent
like 4 hours and only got 2. And those two were right in a row. This was
from the named shaman spawn. Occasionally a High Shaman Grisk will spawn. He
is level 17 I believe. But usually is is a level 16 spawn. These guys have
Runed Totem Staves. +3 int (I think) +30 mana (I think the bonuses I am not
sure on..only stats) it is a 9 dam 37 speed AND magical. That is same speed
but more damage then a minotaur axe but it is also double handed. Well that
is a good staff. They should sell from 20 pp to 30 pp. 25 pp is fair. I have
seen them go for 40pp which is a rip off. Go to the Karana plains and you
can get them for from 25pp to 30pp. I have seen level 25 wizards coming in
trying to get these staves. They are pretty decent.
