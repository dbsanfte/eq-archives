By far sister island is the best place to hunt for xp and money purity belts
bring 1.2pp a piece when sold to npcs and around 2pp-5pp when sold to
players. the xp is very good as the spawn rate is perfect for resting
between spawns.. I solo'd from 12-15th there. and made several hundred
plat while doing this. BTW you would be killing sisters or Eroslilli or
something like that. a good thing to keep in mind is to bind yourself to
Freeport in front of the vendor on the dock because you will be so loaded
down you will hardly be able to move.
