I have beaten red giant skeletons at 22 in Lake Rathe (with great
difficulty). There aren't enough of them though, and the cyclops are a
pain.

The druids can be good - again a necro can just about solo a red one (low
red anyway), the wandering ones drop up to 5 plat and FS weapons sometimes.
Best tactic I found was to send pet in, cast cancel magic to get rid of
their damage shield, heart flutter followed by heat blood, the fear and
drain (with allure on). You will normally get dotted during the fight so
keep your health up.
