I'm still a low level necromancer at lev 15. But even now I find it much
faster to just buy bones from newbies. I would imagine this will hold true
until the bone type changes to warbones (rumor I heard) at some point.

I am a dark elf and when I gate home to sell at bank and return outside I
just auction for 20 bones for 5 gold. If that fails, 20 bones for 1 plat.
Usually I get 3 or 4 takes and 60-80 bones right off. For 2-4 plat which I
can make back much faster and with more fun then I could run around trying
to gather 40-60 bone chips on my own.
