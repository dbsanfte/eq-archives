You can sometimes find them on merchants too. I've seen them on Felwithe
merchants all the way to merchants in Halas. I usually grab them when I can
since I understand these are random turnouts too. They range up to 4pp a
piece usually. Expensive yes....but not so costly as you start fighting
creatures that drop plat.

Other things I have found useful to get runes. (I would assume most of you
do this already):

-- trade the words or pages you find for runes.
-- inform your party members that you are looking for runes. I'm always
surprised how many non-casters are unaware what those little black things
are for. :) I always get the "oh..you WANT these? here!"
