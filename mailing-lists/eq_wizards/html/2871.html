Wizard Practice Guide.
You'll need to get the the Concordance of Research from Mrysila, who's in
the Gypsy camp in North Karanas (the loc is -672, -162). If you're at the
silver bridge or the Wizards Gate in North, follow the path away from the
river. You'll go over a hill and past a guard tower. The Gypsy camp in
past the tower (Be CAREFUL! Hill Giants and Griffons seem to like going by,
and through, the camp on regular basis.) To get the book, give her a Great
Lightstone.

The book is just like the Wizards Lexicon. Take the ingredients listed here
to make a Azia or Beze practice Rune. You then take the created set of
practice runes and place them in the Wizards Lexicon and hit combine, and
you'll get practice in research

(BTW, the book says you need to have a quill and parchment. This MAY only
be for other classes, but I will verify this ASAP)
