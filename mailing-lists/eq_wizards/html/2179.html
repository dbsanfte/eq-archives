If I am with a group of lvl 20-22s, and go after something that is lvl 26 or 27. I would expect to have our ass's handed to us. But, if I am with a group of a few lvl 29s, and a few mid-30s (lvl 35-36), and the high lvls want to go after a yellow or an even in the group, everyone should be able to help in some way, but with how things work (espically magic resistance) that is not the case. You would think that if the group had

- a lvl 29 wizard
- a lvl 30 cleric

- a lvl 30 ranger

Right now people 8 lvls below me can group with me, but if that lvl 21 wizard cant help at all in my group ( I am lvl 29 ), for the things that I am fighting, why would I want them?
