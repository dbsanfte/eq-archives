Subject: Elkantar list of things to remember as a Wizard v1.0


1. Whenever you are engaged in battle, wether you think you can win
or not, always type /loc before or during battle. That way when
or if you die you can scroll up in the chat window and get a
general idea asto where your corpse is.

2. Never run after losing your sticks(warriors). The beast will catch
you anyways and all you will do is waste a couple hours looking
for your corpse cause it is not with the others.

3. Before you train up from leveling. Make sure you have researched
what skills you will really need for the next level.


Hope these help. Feel free to add to this list.
