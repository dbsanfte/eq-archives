> Kerra might be hard for you at your level. The Wisps are a gold mine (they
> have a random amount of cash on them, up to 3g once, and carry
> lightstones ~1pp and greater lightstones ~4pp at times). But the Kerran
> wisps range from level 10 to 12, and can only be hit by magic weapons.
> Get a mage to summon you a dagger before hunting.
>
> Otherwise, it IS a perfect spot. I spent 6 hours of playtime at level 13
> there, and made it to level 14, with 45pp in bank. I know a friend who stayed
> there for level 14, when about 1/3 of the wisps became green, and he left
> at level 15 with over 100pp in bank.
>
> Up to three players, you can set a good rotation on the main wisp spawn
> point (cycle of 4 wisps). Don't camp the 2nd point, if you can find it
> (spawns 1 wisp during the same cycle). But it's a boring activity.
> Once, I had the entire zone for me. I couldn't even kill wisps fast enough
> and I died once when I got three of them (deep blue "risky") on me.
