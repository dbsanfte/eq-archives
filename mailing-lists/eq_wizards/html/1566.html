Here is the poormans way to make a tarnished dagger. Put one sharpening stone
(4cp), and one rusty dagger (free) into a forge and click combine. At a zero
skill level you have roughly a 50/50 shot of making a tarnished dagger. It
won't seel for anymore than a rusty dagger, but it raises your smithing skill
for the heck of it, and it gives you something to do with all those daggers you
get fishing. They sell for nothing anyway.
I believe the stats on the tarnished dagger are 22/3as opposed to 24/3.
