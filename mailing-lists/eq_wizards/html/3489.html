You can find all the details on Graffe's site (www.graffe.com) regarding the
Jboot quest. The most complex part being the camp for the Ancient Cyclops.
Forget about finding it in SRo...I've tried. Best I've heard is the island
in the Ocean of Tears. It's a little swim away from the Sister Island. I
hear it's a pretty long camp - most of the time spent killing the goblin
placeholders.

Once you get the quest items, you need to partner up with a druid/ranger to
catch a little gnome runner over in Lake Rathe. You can have your druid
locate and hail the runner to keep him occupied for you to run up.

Good luck, if you find an easy way to do it, let us know! :)
