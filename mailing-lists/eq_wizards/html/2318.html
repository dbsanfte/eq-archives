spells in moderation. Let the tanks deal some damage before you start

Okay, I guess it is bad I come on here to give my observations, but here

Well I have discovered the wonders of doing the most damage to a Monster,

stand so far back that I don't even get the combat messages or sounds, and
that doesn't even work all the time. Yes I'm kicking ass and taking
names... but at what cost... (It was great when a dwarf was complaining

Next I have discovered the POWER of the druids... What I'm about to relate

masters of anything after this little show of power? btw at the end of the
test she did 260 hps dam for 140 mana and I did 395 hps dam for 240 mana.

more hps dam in a slightly shorter period of time. Not bad but nothing like
i would expect from the masters of damage that I expected and still do
expect the wizard to be. (she pulled a monster off of me with her spells
once and another time she pulled the monster on herself after a set of
spells [it saved vs 2 of mine I had cast doing only 36 and 39 respectivly])
(these were wolly calfs, and orc Mounteneers/shamans)
