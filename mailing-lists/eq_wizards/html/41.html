I have read that Okeils does 2 pts of dmg each time
the target (preferably either you or a party member)
the spell is cast on is attacked. I have no way of
confirming the dmg but it is admittedly small.

The benefit of this spell is it adds up over time so
it is a great spell for melee that you anticipate will
carry on a while. e.g., when your party is fighting
many monsters. Its a way of doing continual dmg and
unlike the AE spells, the monsters dont smell you out.
