I am very happy as I have just escaped level 30 hell, where you have to do three times as many kills to get same xp. Now I hope I don't die and lose a whole bub of xp, as happens in level 31. Anyway, I almost didn't buy Thunder Strike spell because I heard it was resisted a lot, like the terrible Force Strike spell, but no, it is great. I have casted many times on red mobs and hit for full damage, only an occasional resist. And it is fun to see the mob knocked back a bit when it hits. I have used Inferno Strike on the same mobs, and have seen it partially resisted, but Thunder Strike always hits for full. And it goes up 3 dmg per level like the other spells. I keep it loaded at all times now.
