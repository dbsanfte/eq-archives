Learn from my mistake. I concentrated on piercing upto level 14 and
maxed out the skill. With a Giant Snake Fang it works very well.
Once you advance, however, you will start encountering people with the
beautiful Runed Totem Staff, which (from memory) is 8dmg, 47 delay,
+5HP and +5 mana, AND allows you to fight magical creatures. It's a
2h blunt weapon. I had neglected 2H upto then and am STILL trying to
get it maxed.
So my advice is, get a nice worn Greater Staff (or another decent
beginner staff) and use that until you can afford a Runed Totem Staff.
The Runed will cost you between 20 and 30pp (usually) and is superior
to almost anything else out there.
