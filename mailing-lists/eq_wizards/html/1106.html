Finally, and answer!!!

To use the skull you simply have a one handed weapon equipped or two
free hands. Place the skull in an open hand and Viola!! +20 mana and +2 SV
Magic. I believe you can use two skulls as well but you will have no melee
weapon. This is going to be my practice in a group. Or I may keep two
skulls on me and use them in emergency for that quick Gate away or that
finally spell to blast the mob to hell!.

Enjoy your skulls!
