The fastest way to learn or teach a new language is to set up a hotkey box that is full of text. Put the text in /gsay also. Group with a friend and then just spamm spamm spamm by continually pressing the hotkey number that your language lesson text is on. If you do this for about 10 minutes or less you will see the improvement come rather quickly. Also, you can only teach someone a language one point better than you know it. When they see the "your language skills have improved " stop coming, it is time for them to spamm you in that same language, keep going back and forth and soon you both will be excellent in the language. Once it was nessesary to zone between lessons, but I think they were gonna fix this.
