First of all I would like to tell the people that complain about druids and
necros being so much better at soloing (or anything) to just forget your
wizard and go play a druid or necro. And also, I would like to state what I
am dissapointed with in the game.
1) The fact that the /ooc chat lets everyone discuss the solutions of quests
or the fact you can buy quest items. I think anyone should be banned if
they give the solution of a quest out in game, I know we cannot stop the ICQ
contacts or web sites that do but most of the solutions are givin out in
game anyways.
2) The camping issue, I think verant should change spawning points of
monsters to a random location in the zone, or something similar and not let
tracking find NPCs. I know that will hinder ranger's importance but I don't
see the logic behind tracking an NPC that's not an animal.
3) The role playing aspects of the game are very weak, I know that's what I
long for. When AC comes out I am buying it instantly, it's the type of game
I am looking forward to. Although I wish they would put a class system or
race system in.
4) Classes in everquest should have more uniqueness, I don't like the fact
that wizards are just the masters of DD spells. I think we should get
something special, anything really. Like special wizard hats or something
of the sort.
5) Everyone complaining, Verant seems to be shifting toward listening to
people whine about "I cannot camp this critter because mister X big and
powerfull is" I think that camping should be something frowned upon in
general and I definitly think they should randomize spawn points and make
mobs have the urge to walk around more and stir up trouble.

Thanks, just my 2 cents.
