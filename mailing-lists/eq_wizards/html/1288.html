Well I am hoping that this is not so as my level 10 wizard is developing
more. I do know that we will be highly useful to a group once we get our
escape and portal spells. I am pretty sure that any high level group will
have a cleric (to cast group divine aura) along with a wizard (to cast group
teleport) in sticky situations.

Other things I have seen is that wizards (at least at level 12 with
lightning shock) can be superb kill stealers. In crushbone there is
commonly a level 12 wizard camping the trainer for his coveted shiny brass
shield. In 3 or 4 lightning shocks the trainer is dead and no group can
hope to get the kill that quickly unless they have a level 12 wizard as
well.

I am sad that wizards dont get any additional damage shields after o'kiels
radiation at level 4, and other than that dont really have any group buff
spells at all. I don't really know about the higher levels very much, but
wizards dont seem to have a whole lot to offer the group than teleport
spells and a bit of direct damage. Other classes might very well be more
useful in a group.

More feedback would be appreciated fellow Wizards. Please post.
