u can follow the wall if u want, but watch out for 3 things... the first
being that u have to cross the river. at lvl 22, u shouldn't have a problem
with this (the piranahs should all con green). the second is along the wall
close to najena u will run into the neriak entrance. here, there are 2
dragoons that are in the lvl 40ish range, so u better skirt around them.
most of the other guards in neriak will con green to u at lvl 22, with the
occassional dragoon at the bridge (at 26, i con all the guards green on the
bridge most of the time). the third problem is the guard that sits in front
of the najena zone. i con him green most of the time, but occassionaly, a
dragoon will sit in his place. if he sits in his normal spot, u'll have to
go invis to avoid him.
