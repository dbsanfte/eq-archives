Just discovered that Elven Wine gives a small mana regen boost. My alcohol tolerance is so low though I take an INT hit when I use it, so I only do it when mana is really low, so by time I am FM my INT has recovered too. Anyway, it is like a poor cousin to Clarity, but helps a bit when you are soloing and want mana back ASAP.
