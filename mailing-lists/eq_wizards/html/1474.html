Hail Wizards!

As a fellow seeker of knowledge I have travelled the land from Faydwer to
Odus, crossing the breadth of Antonica on foot. I noticed some information
in your tome that I can expand on to add to our knowledge. Mostly I will
add information on the different color robes that can be purchased. It is
interesting to note the Erudin does things differently when it comes to
Robes. In all other cities, there is only 1 color of Jr. and Sr. apprentice
robe sold and they can be worn by all magic using classes. In Erudin
however there are 3 different color robes that are sold and each one can
only worn by 1 of the magic using classes (enchanter, magician, and wizard).
I will detail this information below.


>Items Info/Location
>---------------------- --------------------------------------------
>Blue Robe Erudin, Qeynos

There are 2 types of blue robes, light blue and dark blue. The light blue
(jr. and sr. apprentice) are found in Qeynos and can by worn by all magic
users, while the dark blue (jr. and sr. apprentice) are found in Erudin and
are Magician ONLY. Also I think that only medium sized classes can wear
both of these robes.

>Red Robe Erudin

The red robe is Wizard ONLY, and I think only medium sided classes can wear
them (sorry gnomes).

>Violet Robe Freeport

Again I believe this is for medium sized classes only.

>Yellow Robe Ak'anon (Though I think only gnomes can ware them).

Yes only gnomes can wear. I confirmed this.

>Senior Apprentice Robe +1 mana only no extra regeneration/+? AC

Armor class value on Sr. robes are 6. Also, the Erudite robes are better
than the others as far as I have seen. The red robe I purchased is +2mana
6ac rather than the +1mana 6ac green robe I was previously using. The other
robes I checked in Erudin had the same +2mana value as well.

Those are my corrections/modifications, and now for some additions:

Junior Apprentice Robe - 4 AC
Gold Robe (jr. and sr.) - Erudin (this robe is Enchanter ONLY and can only
be worn by medium classes)
