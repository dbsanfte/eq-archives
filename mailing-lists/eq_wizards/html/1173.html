The three I have apart from these are (in order of usefulness)

1. Lesser Shielding. As a Wizard you will want to buy any shielding
spells you can get your hands on.
2. Halo Of Light. Creates a headband which has AC 3 and gives better
light than a Greater Lightstone.
3. Resistant skin. Improves resistance vs. poison and disease. Only
use it when you're fighting creatures that can poison or disease you,
of course, but very useful then.
