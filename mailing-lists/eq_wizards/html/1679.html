I hear a lot of players say that it is wrong and I agree. I hear few say
leave it alone! I and a lot of other players left twinking alone in UO and
look where that game is now. Same for Diablo. It does effect me and my
character cause it will lead to every other player having to twinkle just to
play on even ground. Then all the honest players will be left behind like
UO.

Twinking the Definition....Giving high level gear to a low level character.

I agree that "low level" is not lower level. I a 10th level wizard gave
items to a fighter who is 7th level. Things I can not use but he can and it
helps our party. He being only 3 levels under me. This just makes sense. But
when a 20th level character gives items high in value to a less then 10th
level character that is simply foolish and exploitable.

Now at 5th level I did find a mighty magical item that someone had
mistakenly dropped. I belive this is fair in the game. But to have a
character outfitted by other, in a guild or no, is simply cheating and will
ruin the balance of the game.
