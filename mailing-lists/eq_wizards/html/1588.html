Well as far as spell selection I would say definitely Cast Force and
Enstill. Those are the 2 main one I use for my soloing. I have heard alot
of people say that Cast Force is worthless since the patch, but I only get
resisted in it about 1 out of 5 times. To me those are excellent odds for a
spell. Sure sometimes I have to run, but more times then not I get the
kill. If they resist Cast Force I have found that Flame Shock (110 dmg)
does the trick. I have set my spells list up witht he following spells down
the line:

Shieldskin / or Shielding when I lose it
Gate
Shock of Flame (yes lvl 1 spell) (16 Dmg)
Enstill
Shock of Ice (38 Dmg)
Shock of Lighting (83 Dmg)
Flame Shock (110 Dmg)
Cast Force (173 Dmg)

Hoep this helps . And I might be posting my spot sooner than expected. Hit
22 last night <grin>
