We have had threads on this before but I would like to pass on to everyone
how I made about 15 plat in about 2 or 3 hours last night. I had travelled
from my home in felwithe across antonica and was battling the gnolls of
blackburrow. Well at level 13 which I was last night (till i leveled to 14)
I easily found a similarly leveled party to fight the gnoll elites and
commanders. Even with 5 members in our party the loot each of us got was
incredible. The gnoll elites and commanders each have the chance of having
a bronze weapon (2 or 3pp), studded or spiked collars (1pp to store, more to
players), and the commanders additionally have the chance of having onyx
earrings (2 or 3pp to players) and I think bloodstone rings as well.
Occasionally you would get more than 1 of those items at a time and one of
the fellows in our group said that he once got a bronze weapon, spiked
collar, onyx earring, and bloodstone ring off a single commander. The best
I have gotten is a bronze weapon and a spiked collar (which was off a single
elite).

It was intestesting to note that although blackburrow is very crowded (and
laggy in some areas) there were not all that many groups fighting elites and
commanders, so we had plenty to kill. Once you hit level 12 if you are in
the area this is a good place to earn cash if you are in a group or can find
one.
