Concerning O'Keils Radiation:

Sometimes when you attack a Goblin or Orc Centurion a nearby low level (Whelp or
Pawn) or two will be attracted as well, when this happens O'keils Radiation can
take them out without you having to pay them any attention at all. Yes it is a low
level, low damage spell but it effects anything that attacks you each time it
attacks you, and it's a pretty cheap spell to purchase as well.

I use O'Keils Radiation to hunt Orc pawns and Goblin whelps, it uses little mana
and between it and my staff i can usually take one out in a couple of seconds. By
the way dont discount these guys, I have found backpacks, good armor, and other
items of cash value on them. No they don't provide exp once you get past the
early levels but in numbers they can pay for that extra spell you want so badly,
and its something you can hunt solo.
