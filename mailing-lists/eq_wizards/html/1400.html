Hail fellow presdidigitators,

I know this may sound like blasphemy here but ... does anyone else here think that the Wizard, Magician and Enchanter Classes are kind of sad all by themselves. I think a combined Magic-User of those three classes would have been a much more interesting character to play as then we would have been a great buffer, we would have had a pet for soloing if we wished and pure nuking power for groups. In exchange for a powerhouse like that with that many good solid options for play, I would have been willing to take a negative percentage hit to my xp like they do with paladins, rangers etc other multi-class character.

I have never played a Necro (not in my roleplaying style), but from what people say, they are fairly well rounded and in game terms they provide a nice roleplaying oppurtunity and fill a void for "evil misunderstood" type class, like the shadowknight.

Of course the Magic-User Class would have to have some spells removed, but a lot of the spells are also common.

Just some wishful thinking guys and girls, dont take it to seriously or to heart...it will never happen anyways! ;-)
