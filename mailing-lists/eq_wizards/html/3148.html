Anyway, I've been able to find a pretty good spot lately for xp in Cazic.
It seems to be a good spot for a small group because it avoids the bulk of
the trains. Some of you might know the area that looks like a big crater?
I think the crater area itself had about 4 or 5 spawn points...plus it has 2
corridors on either end where and experienced tank can pull from. With a
group with 1 Wizard, 1 Cleric and a couple of tanks...you may never need to
run once during your whole time there. I think this area maybe good from
levels 24-30. A couple mobs are still red to me at 27. Extra challenge
comes from the MoB healers who make it extra tough to kill any of the
creatures when they are around...best to switch targets and nuke the hell
outta them. :)
