Keith Wiseman wrote:

> Hunting grounds for a low-level Erudite Wizard?
>
> 1. Tox Forest (across bridge) hunting Kobolds and Skellies. If you find a
> Ranger to help, you can actually level fast, otherwise you'll be camping at
> the Kobold camps and fighting for kills, or wandering and hoping to find
> something. Unless they changed the spawn rates since I was there several
> weeks ago, this can be slow compared to other newbie areas.
>
> 2. Kerra Isle. Level 6 is (IMO) about the time you can start venturing here
> but need a good group to do well. Usually there is a lack of tanks in this
> area of the world which makes this place more difficult than it should be.
>
> 3. N. Qeynos then Qeynos Hills - I stayed on Odus for a long time before I
> ventured across the ocean to Antonica. When I finally did, my jaw DROPPED
> at the site of N. Qeynos newbie area. There were kills as far as you could
> see since it is flat and practically treeless. I was so amazed at how much
> easier it would have been to level here never having to 'search' for kills
> like Tox Forest. Even with the increased population, the ratio of animals
> was still MUCH higher than Tox.
>
> 4. Erud's Crossing - Once you get over 10, you can start to consider
> stopping at the Isle in Erud's Crossing and hunt Wisps and Spiders. This
> island can be a little dangerous but you can make a TON of money here
> getting Greater Lightstones. This place is definitely Wisp Heaven!! I made
> a ton of money here in my low teens and have been way ahead of the money
> game ever since. I recommend grouping here like most places. Spiders have
> nasty poison so beware picking fights. I can give more strategies on this
> place but I'll shut up now ))
>
