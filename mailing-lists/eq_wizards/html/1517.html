Agility affects your armour class.
Dexterity affects your chance of recovering when you've been interupted. I
put my 5 extra points into this at character creation (into an already
dexterous gnome), and at level 8 it seems to be making a slight difference.
I've talked to Euridites my level who regain concentration 1 or 2 times a
night, whereas I'll regain concentration 5 or 6 times on average. Mostly
these recoveries aren't occuring in battle, but when I cast to quickly after
running. Overall I'm very happy with the points spent in dexterity and I'm
even thinking about blowing some cash on derv rings to improve it more (at
least until those +int items come along)
