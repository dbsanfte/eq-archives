Ok as promised when I reached lvl 24 I would reveal my location for
solo-ing. well I hit it early sunday but ended up back at 23rd last night
after dieing 3 times in Cazic Thule. Where is this secret location you
might ask? Well it is non other than Lake Rathe. The Lake support Aviak
Guards that are great exp and loot for soloing. There are several Island
inside Lake Rathe and have different variations of the Gaurds, ie., ! guard
Post with 1 Guard, or 1 Guard Post with 2 guards or 2 Posts with 1 Gaurd
each Post...... So choose you destiny. I would HIGHLY suggest not taking
on the 2 guards at once unless you have someone with you. Even at lvl 24 I
find it very difficuly to fight 2 solo. Reason being is that they are Lvl
20-24 but Hit like Lvl 30 Mobs.

Also a bit of warning.....If you do not have high swimming skills you best
get them up there fast. The Lake supports a Sea Monster (lvl 53 Mob) that
hits for over 100+ per shot. Now there are boats to tkate you from island
to island but mostly I swam. Started with a low swimming and now have 120
swimming in 4.5 Lvls.

Well Good Luck, and hope this helps all those stuggling to reach 20+.
