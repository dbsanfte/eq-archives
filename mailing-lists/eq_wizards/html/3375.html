One full group of early 40's can waltz through straight to her(you can
actually just pull to the music room). You need to be sure that your group
knows what they are doing because if you just take the first 5 40+ people
you can then you may end up with a very tough corpse recovery. Here are my
thoughts on what you want for classes:

1 Wizard(because we rule, heh)
1 Cleric
1 Shaman
1 Warrior
1 Enchanter/Bard(depending on whether you are tank heavy or caster heavy)
1 Paladin/Magician(depending on which one you took above)

Now this is a similar composition to what I hunt with on a regular basis and
we have no problem going deep into Mistmoore, granted we have been hunting
together since level 12 but if you all understand your roles you should be
fine.
