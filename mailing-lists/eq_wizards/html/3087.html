Yeah, I've been too chicken to gate over there too. Although the
levitate/invis trick I heard sounds promising. Anyway...I've traditionally
taken the LOONG way there following all the walls. Forgive me if I get the
names of the zones wrong (I hope there are those here who would correct me)

-- Go to Oasis...follow the right wall until the back side of the zone. You
can't miss this huge zone area back there. I think there is an orc and derv
camp back there. Greenies but I steer clear anyway.

-- S Ro. Follow right wall again...you'll encounter orc camps along the
way. Greenies again. First zone you encounter will lead to Innothule.

-- Innothule Swamp. Right again. I don't remember bumping into anything at
all here. First zone

-- Feerot. I usually go invis in this zone. Might be a good idea to go
invis along the whole journey. Anyway, Cazic is in this zone. But you have
to watch for Ogre guards along the way. Especially the infamous roaming
Bouncer Hurd. So I steer clear by following the left wall till I encounter
the river. a short swim across to the other bank. I would say about....30
more yards along the left wall is the temple entrance guarded by two green
to blue lizards. Viola.

It takes some time to get used to the place. But invis will be your best
friend here heh. That's my method....the "Lim'Dul Chicken Express".

I haven't found a place with a very detailed map. Although I currently
