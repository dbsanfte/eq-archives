A lot of them are available from the server selection screen. If you click
on news, it will give you the updates going back quite a while. Farther
back than that, though, I'm not sure. You could try the official Everquest
site; they might have some older ones. And you can also try Everquest
Stratics. They have archived news. If you scroll down to the very bottom
of their main page, there is a link to archived news. I don't know how far
back it goes, but the last time I needed to look something up I seem to
remember it going back several months. Their pages always list the updates
that the patch server prints out, so you might find them all there. They
are at http://eqss.stratics.com/ Hope this helps.
