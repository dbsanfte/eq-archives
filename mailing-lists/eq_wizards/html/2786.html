Oh.. heh =)

Here's how I do it.. *shrugs* ...I invis at the entrance.. just so when I'm sitting there, the Ogre guard doesn't smack me.

I root one Terror.

If the root sticks.. I run to the entrance.. and fight the single Terror that follows.
If the root doesn't stick.. I zone.

Sometimes.. in the middle of the fight.. the second Terror shows up.

If this happens but I'm winning the fight against the single Terror, I burn into it.
If I'm losing the fight against the single Terror, I bail.

Also.. another thing to think about... (depending on how crowded the dungeon is) ...if you kill both of the Terrors right there and then.. they'll spawn at the same time again. BUT.. if you kill one.. and keep the other rooted for a couple minutes.. or have it chase you around or something for a couple minutes... then when the first one spawns.. you'll have a couple minutes before that second terror spawns again.

Anyways.. hope that helps. =)
