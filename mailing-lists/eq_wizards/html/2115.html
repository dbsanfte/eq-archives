Subject: Force Shock & Stun


I just wanted to clear up one thing about force shock that I have seen
several ppl make references to regarding it's stun capabilities. It does
NOT stun the opponent as most of the spell listing sites have indicated (and
as some ppl have assumed here). It will knock the opponent back a step or
two, but there is NO stun involved (yes, I was disappointed when I got force
shock and found that there was no stun).


SPOILER-SPOILER-SPOILER
There is a lvl 20 spell that we get that does stun your opponent though. It
is Tishan's Clash. It will stun your opponent for about 2 seconds (much like
a skel might stun you), and does approx. 30dmg. The recover time for it is
about the same as force shock, so you cannot cast it several times in
succession if you were hoping to keep the opponent stunned. The mana cost on
it is very high though, so I don't really see a valid use for it (if the
stun time were longer, it would be a nice spell, but 2 seconds just isn't
long enough).
