If you are a non-PvP server and all -PvP, you do not need to worry about
hurting other party members.
BUT, using AE spells is dangerous anyways, if you cast it on a mob that has
no hate for the tanks yet,
you are bound to get them onto you.

But, as for the use of AE spells and how they are good.

- Lets take the lvl 24 Column of Lightning spell, 130 mana, does 130 damage
to the mobs around it.
- Lets say there are 2 mobs, you cast that, for the cost of 130 mana you do
260 damage(130 per mob).

That is nice damage / mana ratio no? .. but you need to make sure your tanks
are building up hate on all the monsters before you start to use AE spells.
I just like to stick to my lower lvl DD spells when in groups.
