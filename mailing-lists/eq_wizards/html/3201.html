For most skills, it's 5 * (lvl +1). So they max at 65
when you're level 12.

For offense and defense, I think it's 4 * (lvl + 1).

For actual combat skills (e.g. Piercing), I believe
it's 3 * (lvl + 1).

Trade skills, like Brewing, don't have a level cap.
But, anymore, those are really just for role-playing
at higher levels when money is meaningless. (Except
for low-level tailoring, and arguably smithing, and
enchanted Jewelcraft, of course).
