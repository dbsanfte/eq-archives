If you are going to be either human or Erudian you will need sphere of light,
otherwise save your coppers for a few levels.

When you hit level 4 you need to buy
1. Shock of fire
2. Root
3. Gate
4. Fade (To Raise Alteration)
5. O'Kiels Radiation (Cast on Friends not enemies. You don't know how many
people cast it on enemies. This will also help raise Abjuration. Its hard to
remember to cast shield until it wears out)
6. Glimpse or sense magic if you didn't need to buy sphere of light. (to raise
Divination)

Put one point into piercing and save the rest of hose skill points for later.

haven't figured it out yet, at least not without camping .
