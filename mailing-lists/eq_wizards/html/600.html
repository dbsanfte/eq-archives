The Ak'anon map is in the manual. I think its listed as the library of
magencia or something like that.
You get there by entering the city and follow the tunnels to the waterfall.
Take a Right. Walk through more tunnels until you see the main shopping
district (4 merchants under tents) follow the righthand wall until it opens up
into a chasm. Walk through the chasm. On the right side of the chasm starts
a long upward ramp. WALK up the ramp and on top lies the Library which houses
the wizard, enchanter, necro guilds. In the center of the library is a common
area with two merchants. One sells low level common spells the other sells high
level common spells. Off the center are three rooms, one for each class. Two
merchants and a guild master in each room. Wizard guildmaster is the start of
the telescope lens quest.

I hope this helps
