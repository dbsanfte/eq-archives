I agree with everyone else in here who has any sense about the issue....Kill
Stealing does NOT accomplish anything. People *remember* you...it's a
self-fulfilling prophecy. You get yourself a reputation that you are a
KSer...you'll NEVER get a group. I've seen it happen where certain KSers
where literally shunned from certain zones as soon as they were spotted.
Nobody grouped with them and nobody helped them when they were in trouble.

Like many others here, I have had NO problems finding a group. It's been
kinda easy since 16 or so...a couple /tells or one /ooc or so can usually
grant me an invite. It's gotten so easy to find at least ONE person I've
helped before in the past...either I binded them in the past or simply said
hello to in another zone. C'mon people...is it so hard to make friends even
online?

You will need to group eventually...I have some 30+ level friends who are
everything from mages to warriors. They tell me it's near impossible to
solo even a blue creature anymore....groups will become essential. If you
have a bad rep later on...tough luck for you. I've said this before:
Wizards are ALWAYS welcome high level groups. These same buddies of mine
tell me they sometimes look for at least 2 Wizards for their groups.
