According to Nat Russo:
> Just made it to lvl 6. I am presently in North Qeynos and wondering where a
> lvl 6 wiz should go for good hunting. Any advice as to what I should do at
> this level?

Good areas at your level:

Blackburrow. If you learn how to avoid the trains, it's very easy topside
till level 8 or so. As a wiz, you'll hate the deeps, so I sugggest
migrating after that.
At level 8 or 9, migrate to Misty Thicket. Safe area, guards not far, and
good faction. You can level to 11 there, gaining good faction, and
maybe the squad ring (a 80hp heal item, which can save your life)
Put then all the cash required into a magic weapon; dragoon dirk (will be useful
at all times), runed totem staff, anything.
At level 11 or 12, go back to Qeynos area, bind yourself at the docks (if 12),
and go to Erud's Crossing, and hunt wisps. You can go to 13 easily,
and make a good stash

Then, it's time to start broadening your horizons...
