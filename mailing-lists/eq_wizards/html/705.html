<< Question 3: When I was still 12th level I cast Bind on
myself and my friend. I got a
message that I feel bound to the area (or something like
that). My friend did not.>>

Self-cast bind will work anywhere in the world but if you
want to bind someone else they have to be in your group and they have to be
in a city zone. You can bind someone anywhere in Greater Faydark because
Kelethin is located in that zone but if you want to bind someone near
Steamfont you have to actually enter Ak'Anon because the city is a separate
zone.
