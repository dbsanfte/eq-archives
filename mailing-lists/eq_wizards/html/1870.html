The reason why your level 20 spell shock of force is resisted so often is
because it has a dual effect. It not only does damage but it also stuns the
opponent. Any spell with a dual effect is resisted a LOT more (ask
enchanters because almost all their spells have dual or triple effects). At
level 24 we get a DD spell that does about the same amount of damage as
shock of force, but does not have a dual effect and is thus not resisted as
much. It also costs less mana to cast.
