If you are interested in the Location of the Halo of Light Spell it is in the Wizards Guild in Felwithe.
When you enter the guild you see Tarkas (I think that's his name) and there are two passages out
of the room left and right...they both circle around but it's less confusing if you take the Left
passage. At the end of the hall make a left and at the corner make another left...go through the
door, and the first merchant on the right sells the Halo of Light spell.
