>What is the next logical step beyond a cracked staff? I am a lvl 3 wizard
>(just started TZ) and am only familiar with the 2H blunt. What should I go

All through the lower levels 1 through about 10 maybe 12 I would, and did,
work mainly with a tarnished dagger, cracked staff, and worn great staff.
Once I got to 12th I headed over to paw and was able to get an RTS. This
all happened during the first week of the game.
Now RTS are much more common, I see them auctioned off for 10 to 20 plat
all the time (average 15pp).
Its generally the next step up if you want a magical weapon. If your
really looking to upgrade, see if you can get a Fine Steel Great Staff, or
a Fine Steel Dagger. Maybe I just haven't come accross them, but I don't
think I have encountered a FS Staff.
An RTS should last you a while. Once you get to the upper 20s or 30s start
looking for an observer staff or some of the other magical 1 and 2 handed
staves in the game. Also a Dragoon Dirk is pretty easy to get once in the
mid 20s. I could solo acquire one relatively easily at 28th.
