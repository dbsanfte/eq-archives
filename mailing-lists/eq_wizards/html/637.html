couple of suggestions...

In my party.. the designated leader always yells out the run command. If he yells it out in all caps.. everyone runs. No questions asked. Other than that... my group never runs.

Also... about tanks... I tell my tanks, that if they think they're gonna die... before the battle is done.. then run. I tell them, just start running, and tell me youre running. That way I can prepare my gate. I have a much easier time running, than our tanks do. So.. I give them the freedom to choose whether to stay or die.

About journeying back home with the fighters... If you think there is a possibility that they'll encounter trouble.. then ask em if they need your help on the way back. Most tanks are pretty confident. ..cause at full health.. if they know they dont have a wizard, and are going to take a beating.. they can usually zone safe enough. Thats why I usually leave them.

Just MHO.
