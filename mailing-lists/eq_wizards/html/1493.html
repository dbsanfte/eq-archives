Also the other thing to take into consideration when purchasing/using this
spell is that if you send the eye out to look around you have to be
prepared for monsters to train to you. I have not personally experienced
that happening (because I use it very rarely if at all), but I have heard
of others who have used it in the dungeons only to pull a bunch of nasties
out on themselves, and everyone else, in the process. It is kind of a
novelty spell, if you ask me, with no real use at this point. If they made
the eye an entity that monsters would not train on it would be worthwhile,
otherwise I would just wait. Had I known I wouldn't have purchased it myself.
