First. Make sure you keep all three skills maxed at each level (P, 1hb,
2hb). Its not hard to do, if you start from the beginning, and you will
be glad you did in the long run. As for your question, the next logical
weapon would be a Giant Snake Fang. You should be able to obtain one
for 4-5gp. After that you will be working for a Runed totem staff.
They sell for 10pp on a mature server, but will probably go for much
more on TZ. Hope this helps.
