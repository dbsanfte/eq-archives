I believe mesh is not wearable by mage types. Woven armor is though it
typically is 1AC less than leather, and each piece weighs less than 1. You
get can get woven armor off of the Frogloks of Guk. These are the types
that carry it Sentry, Sentiel, Guards, Tuk Warrior, Apprentice, Novice,
Neopyte, Witch Doctor, Tuk shaman, and a couple of the other frog types.
Mesh armor is on Gaz Frogloks and higher. Guk is a dungeon near Grobb. As
far as price depends on how close you are to Guk. Normally you can get it
for 1 - 2 plat a piece at Guk.
