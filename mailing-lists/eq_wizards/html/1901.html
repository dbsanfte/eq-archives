> since I could just a + mana ring for a whole lot less. Also in wearing

A plus mana item will add the specified amount of mana to your mana
pool.
A plus intelligence item will effect your mana indirectly. A good rule
of thumb is that for every extra 5 points of intelligence you will add 1
point of mana per level
