Hmm, the only thing I can think of is that you are casting too often whilst in battle. You should let your Ranger take most of the hits. Unless my group is in trouble I usually don't cast more than twice on any specific creature. Even then, I usually cast the first time right away and don't cast the second time until the creature has under half the hit points remaining.

As always (and as stated here many times), make sure you are doing a /assist on your Ranger friend.

Rule of Thumb: The Mages should always be the last to die. The tanks should be willing to die for their Mages. If your tank is not willing to die for you, then he shouldn't be grouped with a mage. However, don't take your tank for granted and don't panic and run when he's in trouble. You must work together!
