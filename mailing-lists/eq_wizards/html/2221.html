I agree, good point. On that note, for some of the younger wizards, you'll
find a percieved hatred of our class in certain areas...notoriously because
of our abilities to strike from afar, and more likely because you are
playing with others who are ignorant of class balance.

Places that are sometimes hostile to Wizards include BlackBurrow around the
Queynos area, and Crushbone over near Felwithe. I would say those in BB
were more hostile to our class...more sensitive to issues about steal kills
and such. Of course, there ARE those wizards I have seen that have used
their powers to exploit others and place shame on our class. I'd like to
name names...but.......nah. :)

Once I hit 16...it was amazing how many groups start looking favorable to
our class. Start looking in areas such as Highhold Pass and Unrest...
people are always ready to allow a Wizard in the group. So don't dispair
folks...the wait isn't THAT long. :)
