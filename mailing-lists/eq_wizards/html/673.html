Verant IMHO has done an *outstanding* job of balancing
the game and the various classes. With this in mind I seriously
doubt they would "neuter" the wizard class or make any changes at all
without serious forethought.

But I do think we might see these kind of downgrades (along with
upgrades) pretty often. MUDs do this all the time, and EQ is
a lot like a MUD. Verant seems to pay much closer attention to game
balance than UO ever did, thats a good thing.

With all that said, my guess is that if they do lower the high-
end wizard damage, then they'll give wizards something else to
make up for it. I have to admit the kill-stealing thing is a
valid complaint - it IS easy for wizards to steal kills. But
looking at the evidence so far, having played in beta, I'd say
Verant knows what they're doing and I trust them not to screw
us over.

Just my 2 copper,
-Steve
