Oh, if I was you, I know a great place to power level, but it's Kerra Isle
between Queynos and Erudin. Just sit by the shipwreck and take on the zombie
sailors. I rocketed through 11-13 with them. However, you'll need to flesh
out your group some to do this, because the initial wave is 3. If you delay
looting corpses you can then take them on 1 at a time or 2 at a time. The
place also spawns 3 wisps, and because you can lap the island in under 2
minutes, they're readily found. Good cash, and great xps at 10-11th level.
As well, not a lot of people go there. Well, that's my experience.
