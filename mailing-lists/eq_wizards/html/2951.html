> Final unrelated question: I know this has been covered before, but I don't
> want to dig through hundreds of digests. :) How do I find my way around the
> Cazic maze from our teleport pyramid? How do I get to the courtyard?

Go West young man! Hehe - basically thats it. When you show up, you are
facing west already. Just keep heading that way as close as possible.
Yes, it will fork slightly north and slightly south, just keep the trend
west (IE - if you veered a little north on the last fork, veer a little
south on the next). Note - I have my inviso spell hotkeyed, and as
SOON as I'm there, I press F1 (target self) and then my inviso key. I
usually get invisoed about 75% of the time, but that other 25%, you get
hit by one mob, that RAPIDLY turns into more. Either gate out (if you
are lower level, or unsure of the maze) or I just run like MAD thru, and
keep going out, and south thru the double doors out the zone. Of
course, I never show up there not full heath, with my (X)skin and
(X)shield spells up, and sow if I can (jboots now). Even at level 35,
it is still a risk, as you can get stunned, and blocked in between mobs
on both sides (usually only if its a returning train to the temple in
there though).

Good luck, and it really isn't as scary as I make it sound, but if I do
that, you are less likely to die and have your corpse somewhere lost in
that maze. That REALLY sucks...
