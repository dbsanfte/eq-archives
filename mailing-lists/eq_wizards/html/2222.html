Let me explain - Verant hasn't implemented specialization skills or the
alternate planes, we have almost nothing for armor or hitpoints, our melee
skills are pathetic and our powers don't become greater than other classes
until 40-somethingth level. Plus, the single greatest way of leveling up -
killing har red mobs - is unavailable to us, because our spells are often
resisted completely by these creatures. So, instead of grouping with
powerful warriors to beat up on mobs that will actually give EVERYONE in the

WE CAN'T TOUCH HARD MOBS LIKE EVERY OTHER CLASS CAN!

characters started while I was at level 12 PASS ME at level 13. It's not as
though I never play, it's just that I CANNOT GAIN MORE THAN HALF A BUBBLE
EXP PER HOUR, on average. That's powergaming, soloing in the Karanas vs.
blue beetles and the occasional white/yellow.

Needless to say, I'm very frustrated in playing this class right now. The
only reason I remain a Wizard is stubbornness. Everyone says it gets better
at higher levels, but the fact of the matter is that we'll always be a
second-rate class unless Verant fixes specialization, plane travel and
spell-resistances of higher-level mobs.
