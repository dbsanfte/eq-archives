The bolt spells require that the bolt be able to fly from you to the target
without obstruction. For instance, if people are standing in the way of your
target the bolt will miss and will not do any damage. Bolt spells do more
damage than normal direct damage spells, which are not affected by
obstructions.

AE is an area effect that can be cast at a certain range from your target.
PB is point-blank affecting only those creatures that are close to you.
