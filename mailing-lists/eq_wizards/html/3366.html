Just as a suggestion and for saving time on your behalf, up until about 16
I'd say, maybe 12, hunting is all the same for every class. Just shout out
in your zone for suggestions on what to hunt, since almost all classes can
hunt the same thing at that level. Once you get to around 12 then it gets a
little trickier and more wizard specific, and that's when you should ask us.
Btw, as a dark elf I did lavastorm from 12-14, oasis 14-20, then aviak
guards to 22, and I haven't soloed since then.
