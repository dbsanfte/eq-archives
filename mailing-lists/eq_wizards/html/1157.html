This is a bit off topic, but since all of our E-Mail addresses are
easily obtainable from this list, I thought you should all be made
aware. This message was posted on the EQ-Vault News page.

Email Scam
I've received numerous
emails from
people who have
received a message from
someone claiming to be
from Verant
Interactive. In this
scam email you are
asked to email your
EverQuest login name
and password in to
them. The email looks
something like this:

Hail Everquest
player. We
regret to inform
you that
your name has
came up in
one of our
meetings
concernig your
legitimate
account. Your
account must
be viewed by our
staff as to
review the
purchase of your
account. This
may be
nothing at all
but it is required
that we do not
overlook any
false payments.
You will not
be kept off the
server unless
you do not
respond within a
short period of
time. Your
login name and
password
will be
required. Please
respond with
this information
as soon as
possible. Thank
you, Sincerely,
Everquest
staff. Please
sen the email to

eq_producers@...

It is unknown how the
perpetrator of this
scam has gathered so
many of EverQuest
user's email
addresses. But it is important
that you do not give
out your login name or
password or your
account's safety will be
compromised.
