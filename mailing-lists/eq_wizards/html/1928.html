A "Lore Item" mention means you cannot have more than one (on yourself and in bank).
A small "*" sign after the name means "newbie item", and you can't sell, trade or
drop the item. It cannot be looted out of your corpse, even with a /consent!.
An example is the Apprentice Robes: once purchased, they're yours, and you keep them.
