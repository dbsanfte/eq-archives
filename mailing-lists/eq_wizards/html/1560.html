At level 9, you can solo blue-conning Desert Tarantulas in North Ro.
Typical loot per tarantula: Chitin (4-5s), Spider Silk (1 out 2-3, 1g7s),
and Poison Sacs (1 out of 5, 4g5 each). I usually make a platinum per 2/3
bubble of XP. And I can reassure you, I'm alternating between the worn
great staff and the dinky tarnished dagger (to get a tarnished dagger,
find a smith, or contact a scout of Tunare - one of their quests yield
a tarnished dagger).

I am now 10, and I already have bought since I got there the 3 levels 12
spells I mainly want: Shock of Lightning, Bind Affinity, Cancel Magic.
After I've bought Resistant Skin, the cash I'll be making will go into
extra items purchase, should I find one of interest offered for sale.

Once I hit 12, I'll immediately start purchasing all level 16 spells I want,
and then resume getting cash for items.

Strong hint: Buy the spells first. Take fishing if you need to (about 1pp
in 30-40mn, but boring as hell).
