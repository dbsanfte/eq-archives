What I do, is wait for a bit. Let the tank dish some damage. Then if the tank is losing, or its time to start dishing damage, I cast root FIRST. This doesn't damage, and wont attract the monsters attention more than the dmg that the tank has already caused. Watch your text window.. if the monsters feet adhere to the ground.. burn.
