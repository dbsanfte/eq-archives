Walk through the entry tunnel from your "zone in" point. It will open up a
bit and have walls on right and left. Pick a wall and stay close to the
zone. Stay along the wall as the trains that go by will not see you there
unless you are silly enough to attack it. From there send a scout a bit
further up to pull out death beetle and tormented dead...jack o lanterns and
gtr skels. DeaTH BEETLES will con yellow around level 10.
