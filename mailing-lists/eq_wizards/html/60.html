Your evocation will almost always be at the max possible level, unless you
never hunt. You'll notice that right after you gain a new level, your
evocation and channeling will quickly go up by 5 and then will stop. This is
because the max your skills can reach is based on your level:

(level * 5) + 5

So, at level 3 for instance, your evocation will max out at 20 (3 times 5 is
15, plus 5 is 20) and will not rise again until you level again.
