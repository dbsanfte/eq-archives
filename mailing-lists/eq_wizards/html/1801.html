Subject: adjustments for Wizards?


There is starting to get some interesting suggestions for tweaking the
Wizard class. Keep 'em coming...way better suggestions than that whole "ban
the twinky" idea. :)

Here's something that I found disconcerting last night. I was with a bunch
of warriors, druids and others inside Dvinn's palace last night. It was my
first time back there in a long time and so I was pleasantly surprised
everything was green to me there. Most of the others in the room were
almost uniform in level 14-19...maybe about 10 of us in the room.

Anyway...it has been a dream in my young Wizard life to give some payback to
Dvinn for the hassle he had given me when I was younger. I was one of the
higher level guys there being 16lvl...and being the only caster in the
room...everyone was depending on me to get the damage in while everybody
else tanked.

Lo and behold...the fighting started and ALL my spells were resisted. We
managed to take him down anyway...and everybody praised me for my help...but
I was so embarrassed I brushed off all the praise and just sulked in the
corner. :)

I know we've addressed the subject of our spells vrs tombstones before, but
we are an embarrassingly useless class against red level characters.
Everything just bounces off. Wonder if Verant should see it fit to adjust
that a little...maybe even if we just knick the tombstones a *little* it
would be nice. Even the lower level tanks were contributing at least.
