Hello,

I am also a L10 wizard on Bristlebane. I am having an enjoyable time
grouping with one other player, usually L10 Ranger or L10 Rogue.
Kill something big, sit down and chat awhile. I recommend the undead
pawns on the chessboard, but be careful of overcrowding. Last night
some monk tried to claim all the kills by hitting them once then
yelling kill stealer when we tried to get one out of five. I once
again proved that it is a waste of time to try to take a kill from a
wizard. (smiles proudly)

Anyway, its a matter of taste, but I like to hit a big thing and then
rest. Killing a lot of weaker stuff for exp is tough with all the
campers out there.

Let the tank do most of the damage, and then if he gets to hurting,
just phaser the MOB to the ground. Lots of fun! Then recharge at
your leisure. A little thought and that gate spell will be
unnecessary. Find a regular partner who thinks like you do.

This level I have gotten noticeable exp with almost every kill, so I
must be doing something right.

See you on Bristlebane!

Calyn
