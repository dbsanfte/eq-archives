as a wiz) tonight and discovered(from a tip) something very interesting...
At higher levels everyone's DD spells take a while to cast... Our secret
weapon is our level 12 DD spell shock of lightning. It is hardly ever

buddies went to their lower level spells, I would interrupt them every time.
