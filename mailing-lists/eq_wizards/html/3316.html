spawns are well timed so if it's camped, you're ok. You also will need
invis vs. undead and the bedroom to be camped (vampiric bats see through

undead. And you CAN'T stack both. So if the hand spawns and you're
there, don't even think about anything, you're dead 100%. Just try to
grab the rod before you die so you'll drag your corpse with the rod.
