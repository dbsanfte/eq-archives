Sphere of Light - Its useless even for human or erudite wizards, a Fire beetle eye or torch does just a good a job and its really a Magicians conjuration spell.

Eye of Zomm - Another Magicians conjuration spell, opens locked doors (which is a bug, removing some of rogues effectiveness) and causes monsters to run straight for caster even if eye is a mile away. Please.

Fade - Usefull only to raise ones Skill points, doesnt put you far enough away in a reliable direction to make it worthwhile and you shouldnt need it at that low of a level.

Sense Magic - Ahem. Nothing to say here. Gone.

O'Keil Radiation - The 2 hps/hit damage is too small to make any real difference to either us or warriors we cast it on. Would be a nice DoT spell if incorporated to our Shielding spells, making even our defense Offensive.

These are just off the top of my head and you will notice that they are predominantly our "utility" spells and that they are pretty much worthless.
