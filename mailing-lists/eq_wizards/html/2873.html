I do hope you are dark elf then. Dark Elfs rule the area. Befallen,
where you can get some nice items (including, if I remember right, the
damask robe), is hotly contested between the dark elves and the halfling
druids, but the Dark rule the Commonlands, Ro, cutting off Freeport from
any possibility of levelling (there are always a dozen or more DE or
trolls on "frontier duty"). But Dark Elfs more consistently CONTROL the
area with Najena (hey, who's gonna have the boots?) and its gossimer,
Lavastorm (good area), and Solusek (where you can level up to 50).

Wizards are very appreciated in race wars as they can root. Unlike mobs,
an opponent will size forces very quickly, and can run to the zone if
outmatched. Hence, root, or enchanter's whirls.
