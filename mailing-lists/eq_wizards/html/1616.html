Here is a quick formula to determine the "power" of a weapon. Just divide
damage by delay (Damage/Delay). The higher a number is, the more damage the
weapon can do in a given amount of time. I believe a worn great staff is
6/40 which comes out to 0.15. A tarnished dagger is 3/22 for a score of
0.14, and a rusty dagger is 3/24. This comes in last scoring a 0.12
