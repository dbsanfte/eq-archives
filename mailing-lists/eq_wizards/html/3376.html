A very hard place to get to indeed. Take a Bard, or an Enchanter with you
for lul or for mez spells. higher the level the better. I would of said a
monk, but as I am sure most monks agree, they would only cause more trains
than aid.
I don't recomend just heading there invis. It would be best if you can get
a group all above 40 and clear the place as you go. A cleric is probably a
good thing to have here as the damage can pile up quick. Keep magic resist
buffs up at all times.

I do believe the wreath is the common drop, but I am not positive. A
better item for us is the runed cowel from Lower Guk 2ac 5wis 9int. It
happens to be the rare drop, but personally its an easier area to get to.
Just a much longer camp. Expect to wait 6hrs min and even then its
definatly not a sure thing.
