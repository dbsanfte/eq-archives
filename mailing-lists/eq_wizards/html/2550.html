ok, ive spent the better part of the last 3 or 4 weekends in cazic... and
here's my story:

at lvl 21, u should be ok to go in (with a healthy group of course). i
wouldnt venture past the courtyard tho (at 24 or so, you can maybe head over
to the throne room where u can pull bigger mobs and also have a chance at
some rubicite). u, being a wiz, should nvr pull. let the tanks pull (and
make sure they know what/where to pull from). at 21, defenders are gonna
con blue, white, and yellow to u (i think.. may go up to 24 which would con
red). there are some other mobs in there in the 18-24 range as well, but
dont know them by name (seems like there is about 20 different lizzy types
in there). another good pull would be the sents (sentinels or something
like that). they are lvl 24-26 i think. always pay attention to whats
around you though. anyway, whenever somebody yells "train to the zone"....
get out or cast invis (or just keep ur eyes open real wide). if if the train
doesnt get u on the way TO the zone, it can get u on the way back. and some
of the mobs they train are lvl 30ish and higher mobs (protectors,
justicators, etc..)... and not just 1, sometimes they will train 5 or 6
high lvl mobs which wipe out all the lvl 25 and lower guys before they can
even stand up :) last, you will be zoning quite often, as there seems to be
lots of trains, and groups always end up picking up more mobs then they can
handle.... but when u zone into feerot, watch out for the ogre guards.
they will come right up to the cazic zone and smash all the light races.
dark elves, trolls, and ogres are safe.
