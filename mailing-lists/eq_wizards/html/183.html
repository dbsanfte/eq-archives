Ok, I know that O'Keills Radiation seems useless.. and in
generel it is. However, let me give you an example of how it saved my life,
once. We were hunting in Unrest.. (party of 5 or 6 level 11-14s).. We're
doing fairly well.. avoiding the really tough ones, etc.. etc.. Our tanks
are doing ok too, but only one of us has a magical weapon, and non magicians
in the party. Anyway.. we get attacked by a dark boned skeleton. This
thing was hitting us for HIGH damage, and we almost decided to run from the
very start. The warriors had the thing down to half health, and it resisted
almost EVERYTHING I cast at it. So.. I hit all of our tanks with O'Keils
Radiation. 2 dmg here, 2 dmg there.. eventually they started adding up..
and the health of the skel started dropping quicker. I guess what I'm
saying is... if you have 4 people hitting a monster that is resisting your
spells... you can use O'Keils radiation on your tanks.. and it WILL get
through.

Just MHO.
