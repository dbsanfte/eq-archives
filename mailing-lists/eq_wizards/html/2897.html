The Tome of Research is received as part of a quest that in in North Karana.

Below for more specifics (spoiler).














































Go to the gypsy camp in the middle of North Karana and talk to Mysteria
(something like that anyways). She will tell you she is the keeper of the
Concordance of Research (which is equivilent to the lexicon, but for
research). Give her 2 lightstones (seperate). One for the Research Runes
vol 1 and one for Research Runes vol 2. These tell you how to research.
Then give her a greater lightstone to receive the Concordance of Research.

Note, as of this moment (unless they just fixed it) research practice does
not work. The recipies are incorrect or something.
