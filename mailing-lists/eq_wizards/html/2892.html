you are with a party wich know how to get monsters from you if you cast it.

a 3 or more fighters wich engage a monsters on 1 on 1 basis so when you
cast a AE spell it will do low damage to all 3 mobs wich have already build
some hate to fighters. AE spels are not efficent if you hit less then 3
mobs as mana to damage ratio is much worse then our DD spels. The main
problem is if your group is attacked by more mobs then you have fighters to
intercept with, as any mob wich was not been hit at last some times will
imediatly turn on you.

If you are in group wich is attacked by one big guy and some green ones
come along, you can cast a AE spell on your puller and then wait a second
that your group take off all guys wich come to attack you.. ps advice dont
try this if your group is not a well known people in casual groups people
