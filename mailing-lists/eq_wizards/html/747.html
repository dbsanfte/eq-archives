If you don't take risks you'll advance but slowly. At level seven I'd
suggest sitting in front of BB in the Queynos Hills and killing the gnolls
that spawn there. Doing this solo STILL might get you killed at 7th. Wolves
are another good choice but you have to be careful of Cros Treewind. Do the
Root N' Shoot strat, works and will give you some running space. remember
that if the creature breaks the root, simply recast root. Do not attempt to
nail it with spells while it's nibblin' on yer bum. That's my experience.
