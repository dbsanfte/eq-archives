Allure of Death

This is an upgrade to the Dark Pact spell. It costs a meager 5 mana to cast and
is another one of our many Alteration magic based spells. This spell drains 8
life from you every round and exchanges it for 8 mana. As is tyical for
upgraded spells, this one lasts longer then the last version, Dark Pact. Now
what good does damaging oneself for mana have? A bit suicidal perhaps you
think? Well we Are Necromancers! As your health withers away and your mana
returns, start using your lifetap line of spells and heal up... With almost 100
specialization in Alteration Magic, I practically lifetap back as much health
as I lost to gain the mana, very useful. There are 2 particularly nasty
combinations with this spell beyond the lifetap casting. 1) Group with a Bard,
their heal song should offset your damage taken so that you in effect have a
super mana regeneration rate so long as bard plasy and you have allure up. 2)
Get Regeneration cast on you by a Shaman.... same effects as #1 above. The
downtime you may have had before is halved or less while using these
combinations. And to confirm this, Allure of Death does NOT turn you into
skeletal form, though once rumored to do so. A later upgrade called dead man
walking will do so.
