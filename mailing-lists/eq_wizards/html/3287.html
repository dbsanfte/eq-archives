According to Jason Smith:
> Warriors have a natural 95 resistance to magic I hear, I was wondering the
> same thing.

Not. Warriors have a bonus equal to their level/2 vs magic (so, a naked
level 20 warrior has 35 vs magic, a level 50 has 50 vs magic base).

However, warriors have more choice in items vs magic. With an untwinked
level 30 warrior, you can have over 100 vs magic from various reasonable
items.
