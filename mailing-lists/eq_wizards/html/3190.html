First, make sure your Alteration skill is maxed out. It may take a little while (Root newbie mobs) but it will be well worth it. Second, Root is really only used in 2 situations. # 1 Soloing. Root, Shoot, Loot.
It's the only way to solo. #2 Group Dungeon hunting. If a mob retreats, you must root it. At your level, root should almost be a trivial spell to cast, and while it never becomes very reliable, it is one of your
bread and butter spells. Get that Alt skill maxed!
