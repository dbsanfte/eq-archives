Create a Dark Elf and find the where the rod is located and learn the path to
it. It is in the water, so once you get past a certain point, you jump in the
water and swim to the rod. I have never seen any guards in the water around the
rod. Once you have the rod, gate out. Hope to god that invis stays long enough
to get to the water. I actually had it wear off in the middle of the city, but
was lucky enough to find an 'out of the way place' to recast it.

Hope this helps!
