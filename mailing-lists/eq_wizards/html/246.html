From the gates in Felwithe, follow the path to the Y, take the left hand
path. Follow that to the next Y and take a right. Keep going until you hit
another Y and take a left. That will lead you to the BB zone entrance.
Once in BB, I would suggest going over by the docks. Just follow the path
to what is referred to as the Crossroads. It is actually a Y, take the
right path, follow it past the city and to the Docks. Hope that helps ;)
