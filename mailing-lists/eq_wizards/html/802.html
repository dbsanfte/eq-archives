Subject: Learning languages


Someone said you have to put a practice point into a language before being
able to learn it.. not necessarily! I managed to go from 0 skill in Dwarvish
to Excellent after 40 minutes of on-again off-again spamming at the Kaladim
Docks. The trick is this: you won't see a skill for Dwarvish or be able to
select it as a language until the next time you zone. After you zone, the
lang(s). will be in your list.

Vash, 10th level Wizardess, Felwithe, E'ci
