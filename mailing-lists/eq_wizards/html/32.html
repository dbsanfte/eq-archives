Shadowwalker,

there are a couple that i know of, these are in CB.

1) Prayer Cloth of Tunare (it's a shoulder pad that Wiz's CAN wear. AC 3 +3
Wisdom)-

Here are some keys to solving this quest. The main NPC's you need to talk to
are the Dwarven Smith and the Elven Priest in the Slaver
Shack (West Side of CB)
Items you'll need are the Magic Ringmail Tunic (From Emperor Crush) and the
Bracers of Battle (From Orc Warlord)
Start by talking to the Dwarven Smith and use some key words like Emperor,
Taskmaster, Warlord etc..

2) Screaming Mace (don't think Wiz's can use this but i've seen it sell for
over 100P)

This is a continuation of Quest 1 above. Once you get the Prayer Cloth make
note of what the Elven Priest says to you. You will have to
give up the Prayer Cloth to get the Screaming Mace though :)

good luck.
