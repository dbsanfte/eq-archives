I know the feeling. It wasnt untill lvl 16 ( I was still feeling weak at lvl
12, even tho I did more damage then the others ) that I started to feel "ooh
yeah I am powefull" type thing. At lvl 16 I can dish out 102dmg in 1 spell,
at lvl 17 it is 104. The lvl 16 DD spell goes up 2 points of damage per
level, which is nice.

But I definatly know how you feel, for the longest time I felt like a "magi
without a pet" but now that feeling is starting to change. In the end we get
the best direct-damage and are the most mana-efficent when it comes to
dealing direct damage.

We also get a wide range of teleport and group teleport spells. Wizards and
Druids are the only two class's that can get those, and we get more then the
druids (inter-plane travel spells). So things do look better for us in the
long run.
