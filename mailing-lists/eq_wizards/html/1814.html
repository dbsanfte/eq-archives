It's an AE spell which means that it can damage several mobs at once.
Only use it when your tank has a few monsters on him. The mana cost
is higher but since it can damage several mobs at once, it's a good
deal if you use it in the right situations. It's a good thing to
always have one AE spell loaded in case you get swarmed by mobs. I
usually have one AE, one Shock and one Bolt spell in my top three
slots.
