Most assuredly. There's no guards to be found around Guk other than near the
entrance to Grobb. There's an npc trollslayer wandering about but he ignored
me, and I know there's another troll npc called Zipthipple or something but
didn't see her. When you enter Innothule swamp from South Ro take a left and
follow the mountain. This will take you to Guk, cave opening, and quite
large, can't miss it. If you continue along the mountain you'll come to
Grobb. Don't go there. Nasty. guards will nail you bad.
