Here's the deal, Netted is the same AC as cloth armor, and the same wieght as
small cloth. So if you are a small race, don't waste your time. Woven is
heavier than cloth. It generally has a 1 or 2 AC advantage over cloth per
piece. Woven is found rarely on Frogloc Foragers, and Kolbold Hunters in
Innothule. Innothule is also a great place for loot (armor, giant snake fangs,
no people) and experience for a level 10 person. It is about a fifteen minute
walk from Freeport. Before you go there, purchase all the bear hide armor
pieces from the Millers in Qeynos Hills. You can't beat Bear Hide AC and I
believe it is wearable by all classes(If somebody could verify this, I'd
appreciate it) You may also want to hunt some Elite Guards in BB before you
go. Their Neckbands sell pretty good in Freeport. When/if you head back to
Qeynos, stock up on Giant snake fangs. There are like 3 giant snakes in the
Qeynos area, and you can't walk 5 feet without stepping on one in the Freeport
area.
