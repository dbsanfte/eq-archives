Subject: Re: Looking for (a use for) Glimpse and other Vision spells


Has anyone found a use for these? I cannot seem to locate them.

Ok that is my cynical reply :). I can actually say that I found a use for
these (other can increasing your divination skill for when you get
invis--hehe). I have at times used these spells to get a closer loot at
bandit camps and such to better see how many enemies are present (and who
has weapons?), but in general these spells are not worth keeping memorized.

I wish that these spells were more a little more useful, but the amount of
magnification that you get from glimpse and gaze (its the same spell only
gaze lasts longer and you can cast it on others) is just not very useful.
If they perhaps doubled the magnification that you had with it, it could be
useful (scanning the horizon for hill giants maybe?).

Other than that, I would really like to see some more vision spells with
more uses. Perhaps a night vision spell that gives us better low light
vision (perhaps similar to ultravision that the dark elves get). Heat sight
is nice but only useful for humans and erudites.

Anyone got some ideas for cool and useful vision based spells? Perhaps make
it so that if a monster attacks your eye of zomm, it at least won't then
come after you? I mean come on that really makes no sense. You are a
monster and see this little eye moving around. You decide to whack it cause
it's annoying you, and somehow you immediately know that the little elf 2
miles away casted it? Things like this make spells like eye of zomm
completely useless. Anyway.. I don't really know what my point is, maybe
that we need to get rid of useless spells or have them improved.
