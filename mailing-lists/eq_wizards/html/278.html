Couple of other places...

North Ro - You can take dervishes and madmen with a group.

Oasis - Again with a group you can take caimans and crocs and the occasional
ghoul that spawns there.

East Karana - With a full group you can take the carrion spiders, gorge
hounds, lion patriarchs and the gnoll reavers. This is a tough area but well
worth it(it is where I am hunting at 15th and have been since 12th).

Unrest - Get a group and rock on, enough said.

Befallen - Get a group and stick to the 2nd level(greater skels and such).
Another rocking place, just a little more dangerous than Unrest.

Paw - This is good up until around 16th and then it gets stale.

West Karana - Miller's farm, you and a cleric start taking down scarecrows,
great exp, crappy loot.

Southern Ro - Hard to get to but tons of ghouls, mummies, spiders and other
stuff. One of my favorite spots but you have to travel through the sand
giant spawning location in the Oasis to get here.

I hope that is enough for now, if not let me know and I will name some
others.
