I just recieved a work around for the no drop/no trade problem with
wizard robes. It goes something like this. Have your Dark Elf buddy
buy a robe at his guild. Group up and find a low level mob. Give the
robe to the mob, and then kill it. Whoever wants the robe, loots the
body. Good luck and enjoy your new dark red bathrobe.
