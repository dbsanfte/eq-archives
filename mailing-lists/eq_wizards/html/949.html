Shock of Ice does less damage than Firebolt but the mana to damage
ratio is better. Also, since it's a shock spell you don't have to
aim. With the Bolt spell, you need direct line of sight, which can be
a nuisance in groups (if someone steps in front of the mob while
you're already casting you lose the mana but don't do any damage). I
hardly used Firebolt after I got Shock of Ice. The only times I used
it is when fighting mobs that resist cold.
Once you reach level 12 you can get Shock of Lightning, which starts
at 78 damage.
