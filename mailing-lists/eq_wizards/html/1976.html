Flowing black robes are kinda tough to get. You have to go into the dungeon Najena in Lavastorm and kill Najena herself to get one. I don't know if there are any other places to acquire them in Norrath because I haven't been everywhere yet. Myself, being a 14th level wiz, am unable to compete in the dungeon too far from the first room. There are greater skels, ogre guards/officers/captains and tentacle terrors, black widows, earth/mist ele's and such. You would need a good sized (4 maybe 5) group of folks your level and above. By the time you hit level 25-28 I think its pretty easy going from what I can see, but you would still need back up since we are melee-free pretty much at that point. The robe is random on Najena from what I understand as well. And there are 3 (? correct me if I'm wrong) NPCs back there.. Najena, Drezla and one other..... that is if you get that far. Might be fun to try.. but bind yourself outside the dungeon zone so you don't have to run a zillion miles to get back if Najena is the only place to get this item.
