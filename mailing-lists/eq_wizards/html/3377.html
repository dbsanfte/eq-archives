Hi all,

List sems to be dieing down so I thought I'd share my experience with
lava.
I was in soulsek A and trying to go through the window instead of taking a
chance with the swinging blade trap. I was also invisable at the time.
Well I got stuck in the window. Suddenly I popped out and fell backwards
into the lava pool. At the time I had major shield, Leather Skin and
Elemntal Protextion on me. At first I nearly paniced but then I started
banging my 6 key. It was my hot key for gate. The whole time I was thinking
that this was the end, and I'd never get my corps back. After two tries, I
got a gate off. When I materialized at m bind point, I had 22 hp left.
From what I hear, I can consider myself extreamly lucky to have lived. I
think I would have been a lot luckier if I had not fell in at all.
