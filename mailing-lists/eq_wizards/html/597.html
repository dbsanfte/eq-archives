>I am going to assume you can get to Lesser Faydark, after zoning into Lesser
>follow the mountain to your left(this is the zone border) until you come to
>another zone entrance, this takes you into Steamfont Mountains. Once there
>follow the path until it branches off left and right(a gnomish watchman will
>be at the crossroads), take the left hand branch. Continue to follow this
>path until you come to a large set of windmills, you will see a few watchman
>and another gnome near here. At this point you should see a large mountain
>on your right, follow this mountain(keeping it on your right) until you see
>what appears to be a mine entrance(there should be rogue clockworks, ebon
>drakes and harpies as critters in this area). The mine entrance is where you
>will find mino's.
