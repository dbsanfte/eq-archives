For low twenties, a group is needed. I have seen pairs of 23s pulling, but
I only succeeded in making trains when I tried that.

Camp in the lake area on the hill or in the alcove on the other side of the
tower from the Lesser Faydark zone. Then have someone pull from the
coutyard and lake or from the canyon leading to the graveyard. If you are
in a balanced group try pulling some stuff from the graveyard.

Watch out for the Ssynthi (floating NPC book behind the morgue in the GY) ,
it drops the robe of the keeper, but it also is a nasty faction hit against
the temple of Sulusek Ro to kill it.

Also, avoid standing right next to the tower. I have been told that is
where Mayong Mistmoore himself spawns, and the tower can be pulled
accidently if you are careless.

Spell wise I use Tishan's Clash to stun runners (almost all familiars run
when hurt) and casters. I help out the melee guys with a shock of flame or
force shock here and there. Be sure to nuke gargoyles hard, they seem to
have a high AC which makes all melee fights long. If you can group with an
enchanter make sure they use tashani and mes frequently as it is easy to get
swarmed and magic resistance seems high on some of the spawns.

Great exp, 1-2pp per kill at times, fast spawns, but VERY dangerous.
