Subject: Wizard Guild on Karana


I am currently in the process of creating a Wizard Guild on Karana.
We have half of the members necessary to send in our info to Verant. We have
many plans on the basis of this guild.

Sick of having to deal with the KS'ing reputation that Wizards have? We will
help others who are having problems with ANY KS'ers and promote Wizards'
abilities in a very positive manner.

Sick of having less-than-ideal equipment because no one cares that you want
to go to location X to get item Y? I have grouped with many Wizards at once,
we get the items that we want that no one else cares about. We have
developed very effective strategies that doesn't have any down time that you
think a group of wizards would have. Also, I believe that there is anything
wrong with GIVING fellow Wizards hand-me-downs (making sure that the item in
question isn't too powerful for person in question).

We always GIVE extra runes or extra spells (which ever the person prefers)
to those who need them.
This is the only problem that Wizards have with joining this guild. Some
feel that they are given more runes by being surrounded by those who don't
need them. I totally agree with them.
I totally feel, however, that this disadvantage is far out-weighed.

These are just some of the main focuses of our guild.
If you have any comments or suggestions or desire to join, PLEASE email me.
I love to hear what people think about this guild.

Also, if you want to start a Wizard guild on a different server, email me
and I will give you all kinds of info and help to get you started.
