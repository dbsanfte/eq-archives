#2 My Philosophy is this. If all the rest of the groupo has fallen, and
there is one tank and you. Fight with the tank till the enemy is dead or
till the tank looks like he/she is not going to make it. If they arent
gonna make it Gate the heck outta there. You have to remember, we have
built in weaponry and armor, they do not. If we live then we can do
everything in our power to hold off what ever killed them in the first
place while they gear up to exact revenge.
Almost every group I have been with tells me to run for the hills when the
tanks and clerics are almost destroyed. They find it much easier to return
to a hostile place where bodies lie when they have artillary as backup.
