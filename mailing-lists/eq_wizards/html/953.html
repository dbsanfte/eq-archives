It all depends on how you hunt. If you are a solo wizard(which is not
impossible at 16th) then Shieldskin is better, if you hunt in groups then
just stick with Shielding. Shieldskin will absorb 100 points of damage but
it does have a time limit(I am not sure how long). Shielding only gives half
the bonus(+50 HP for this spell) but it also helps you with Magic Resistance
and a better AC. All in all they are both good spells but it is up to you to
decide which one fits your style better.
