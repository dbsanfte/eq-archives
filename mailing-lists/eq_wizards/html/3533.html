You will want to head up to North Karana and fight the
beetles up there. You can hang out by a zone line and
kite them all day. You should be able to take out
White Pincer Beetles rather easily. Do not worry
about rooting them. You can easily out run the
beetles without SOW...you may have to jump a few times
to get out of their range quickly (if they get too
close for some reason). I have taken out yellow
beetles but did not do this very often due to resists
on my SOL spell.

Good luck...and its great XP.
