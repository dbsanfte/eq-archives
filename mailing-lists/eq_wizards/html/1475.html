To get to the freeport docks without facing the guards, go to w.freeport and
while facing the guards, head right and hug the wall. Eventually you'll find
a secret passage. Go through that (it can get kinda confusing) and then
you'll eventually see some water. Go in that and then when you get out into
the open, take a left and follow the wall. You should soon see the docks.
