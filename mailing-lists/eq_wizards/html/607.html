I totally agree with you Chris. Also... another thing that is misleading... is the fact that Verant was limited to a certain amount of lines in that bar of experience graphic. So.. sometimes it takes another (for example purposes only) 100 exp to make the next little yellow sliver of exp to show. So.. lets say that one person has 95 experience already put towards getting that next 'sliver' of yellow to show.. and the other person only has 10 towards it. they fight something.. and get 10 experience each. The first person moves passed the 100 mark and his next yellow sliver of experience shows, and the second person has to wait a few more fights to get there.

My suggestion would be to get a couple people who are the same level... only group with people your level... then go fight a 'bunch' of things... enough for a couple bubbles. You'll find that you all gain about the same ammount of bubbles.
