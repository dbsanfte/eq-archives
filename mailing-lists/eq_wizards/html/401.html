Folks,

this list has become quite popular. I've been reading some frustrations
from people that there is just too much Email generated from this list.

So I'd like to offer up a few alternatives to receiving a couple of hundred
emails a day

1) You can subscribe to the digest version.
To do this you can either login at http://www.onelist.com and change your
subscription method
OR
you can send an Email to eq_wizards-digest@onelist.com - Switch your
subscription to digest

2) You can set your account to not receive mail and read the archives
on-line instead.
To do this you have to login to your account at http://www.onelist.com
Once logged in you will see a list of groups you're subscribed to. Click on
"No Mail"
for EQ_Wizards and submit that form.
Once you've done that you will no longer receive an mails. To read the
archives you have to
login to onelist, click on the link eq_wizards then click on the link View
Archives.

Hope this helps alleviate some of the pain for some of you. Here are some
additional Email addresses
you can send to -

eq_wizards-owner@onelist.com - Sends Email to the list owners
eq_wizards-subscribe@onelist.com - Subscribe to the list through Email
eq_wizards-unsubscribe@onelist.com - Unsubscribe from the list
eq_wizards-normal@onelist.com - Switch your subscription to normal
