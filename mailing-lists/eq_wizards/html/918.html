Subject: Skill Level Formulas **Spoiler**


I saw these posted on a message board, and thought they may be useful.
I can't guarentee the validity of them other than that they are
consistant with my numbers.

Class Specific Skills (Evo, Channel, Div, Abj, Etc.) = ((Level + 1) * 5)

Offense = (Level * 4)
Defense = ((Level * 4) + 1)
Weapons (Pierce, 2/1h Blunt) = ((Level + 1)*3)
