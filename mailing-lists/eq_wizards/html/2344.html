There is a difference between an exploit and a cheat.

A cheat requires you use a flaw or bug in the program that wasn't intended
by the designers to your own advantage.

And exploit is taking advantage of a good situation when it arises.


Cheating is wrong and can be punished by a GM, exploiting is not explicitly
wrong, but it may or may not go against the spirit of the game.
