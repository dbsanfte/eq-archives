At level 21, I was happily soloing blue aviak guards and even the occasional
blue conning dry bone skeleton in lake rathe. I might have had to run about
50% of the time from the skeletons unless there was someone there to give me

from the aviaks. There are so many things to fight at this level it is
unreal. The people that complain how they can't solo should either 1) find
a group or 2) explore a bit more.

Personally I like to group. After soloing from level 22 to 24 I was bored
out of my mind. In a group downtime seems so less significant because you
have someone to chat with. Also grouping affords much greater safety than
being alone.
