I am a Starfall, a 4th lvl WIZ on the Rallos Zek server. I spent the time
to MAX ALL of my non-trade skills at every level. The proper formulas are
listed below.

Combat Skills ( MAX= ((lvl+1)*5) )
(Hand-to-Hand,Piercing, Throwing, 1-H Blunt, 2-H Blunt)
Defense ( MAX= (lvl*5) )
Offence ( MAX= (lvl*4) )
All Other Non-Trade Skills ( MAX= ((lvl+1)*5) )
Bind Wound, Beg, Alcohol Tolerance, Sense Heading, Swimming, etc...

I have not had the money to work on any trade skills other than Fishing
or the occasional Baking and Tailoring. The Fishing skill seems to Max at
((lvl+1)*5).
