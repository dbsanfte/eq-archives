Subject: Gemcutter use found


Since the discovery of the Gem Cutter gnomes have
wondered its uses.

The gemcutter can be used in place of the Darfumpel
part of the shadow night quest containing a faceted
Hyacinth as a quest componet.

Take one gemcutter and one uncut hyacinth and put into
a jewelry bag. Have a high-level jeweler combine these
items and you receive the faceted Hyacinth.

Darfumple (npc) charges 1000pp to facet the stone so
using this, shadow nights could probably get it done
cheaper from pc sources. This is the only quest I know
of that it works on but it is surely compatible with
other stones...
