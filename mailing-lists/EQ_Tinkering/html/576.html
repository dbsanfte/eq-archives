If anyone on this list reads a vendor to you, they are misinformed. The only
way to make pie-tins is through smithing or pottery. Being a master potter is
a huge advantage for you, as you can make the pie tin and make the compass.
That is what I will be doing tonight.

When someone says "it's on the vendor next to the blue building in freeport"
they are mistaken. Because you can buy the tin sketch (1pp and change) from
the vendor to make the tin, but then most will just sell it back to the vendor
since they are not in demand. Thus why you see it occasionally off a vendor,
someone actually made that tin and sold it back.

Your best bet is to become a master potter. Preferrable an Enchanter with
Benevolence and Illusions so you can get bare base price on everything from
jewelry to clay.
