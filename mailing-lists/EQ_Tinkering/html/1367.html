Subject: Making aqua lungs and what I charge...


Hi Folks,

With a tinkering skill of 102, depending on your stats you can expect to have
a failure rate of about 50% on aqua lungs. With a component cost around 225 that
means your cost would be around 450. I would have prefered to sell them at 600,
but people balked too much (a lot depends on the maturity of your server and
how many high levels you have). So, I wound up selling them for about 550, which
has become a popular item for folks headed to Kedge Keep, and nets me 100 plat.
However, I have no problem with someone trying to get upward of 800 to 900 plat
on them given the chance and the 'right' customer...heh-heh.

With a skill below 100, I would not recomend trying them yet. Mainly because you
can get your skill up to 102 with the probes, and you will have to charge around
700 plat or so for your lungs to break even with a failure rate in the 65% range.
