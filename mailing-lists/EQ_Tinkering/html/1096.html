-- Welcome to Gnomestock '99 --

The purpose of this event is to celebrate the discovery of the Third Book
of Gimbleston. This book contains many useful tinkering formulas, as well
as some more words of wisdom from Gimbleston himself. While the location
of the book is still only known to a few, those few have been generous
enough to share its contents with all gnomes. This act of sharing shows
true gnome spirit. I and many others are forever indebted and grateful to
this person.

Some events at Gnomestock '99 will include:

- Fishing with collapsable fishing poles and animated bait.
- A small fireworks display. (snicker)
- Free beer and booze for all.
- High Diving at the great wheel.
- And just some general, 'Gnomeing about'.
