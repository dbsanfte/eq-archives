If you complete the scrap metal quest, it is one of the possible rewards
you can receive. You must bring 4 pieces of stacked scrap metal to
Synford, (sp?) the NPC gnome who resides opposite the entrance to the
mines of malfunction. You can get the scrap metal from the two different
varieties of clockworks that are located near the mills (north and east
from mills to be exact). Red V also drops scrap metal occasionally but he
is a much rarer spawn.

A few notes on this quest:

I recommend obtaining a dimensional pocket or spirit pouch as each piece
of scrap metal weighs 7.5 - very heavy indeed considering you need at
least 4 of them.

The scrap metal is NOT no-drop so you can buy it from any player hunting
the clockworks.

When hunting the clockworks, it's a good idea to pick up the rusty black
boxes as well. You can turn in two at a time to Manik for chump change,
minute exp and faction. Although you might not think that's a big deal,
but one of the faction increases is with merchants of ak'anon - same with
scrap metal quest.

As of this moment I've completed the quest about 20 times and still
haven't received the trash compactor nor the gnomish glow rod (another
possible reward according to Alakhazam's). Question to those who have the
rod - are the rods lore? If not, could they be the rods that need to be
sewn in the tattered cloak to make the thermal cloak? Just an idea... :)

The only odd reward I've received so far (mostly get rusty weapons) is an
item that looks like hematite but apparently is some kind of ore - I've
never seen ore that looks like this and I have no idea what it's for
(however I'm not a blacksmith and perhaps it is just a common piece).

Hope that helps.
