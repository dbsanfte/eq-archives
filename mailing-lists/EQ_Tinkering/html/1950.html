To sell cams, dont auction that you are selling them, auction for a good
fletcher. Once you find one, tell him that you do can do cams and that you
can give him good prices. Most highskilled fletchers have a gnome tinkerer
to provide cams for them, and they stick to one person, thats why they are
hard to sell. Me, I got myself to 185 skill fletching and use the cams
myself, or will when i have maxed it which i cant afford because of this
damned tinkering :)

To sell fireworks, make a whole bunch of em, take em with you, and just use
a few in a dungeon or somewhere where there is a lot of ppl, that will make
em sell, same with spyglasses, just use em, best way to sell em.
