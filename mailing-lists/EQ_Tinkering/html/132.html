I have found pie tins in two good places. They sell
for around 9gp each.

In GFaydark take the Orc Lift in Kethkin(sp?) up.
Across one bridge or up one ramp (can't remember, need
to go look again) there is an Inn. The vendor in the
Inn sells pie tins if you scroll down just a bit. My
wife (Mariposa) found this.

In Freeport North, if you turn out of the bank and
take an immediate left there is an un-named building
with two women in it. The woman on the right sells
pie tins. For about the same price.
