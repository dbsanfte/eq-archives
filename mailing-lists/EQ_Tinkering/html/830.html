I took out the 'ol fishbone ear ring tonight and did a bit of searching in OOT. The zone is HUGE, so I didnt finish before I had to quit.

So if you drew a box with these coordinates you would get the area that I have searched:

2000, -6000
2000, -10000
-5000,-6000
-5000, -1000

Make a box out of these and that is the area that I have searched so far in OOT.
