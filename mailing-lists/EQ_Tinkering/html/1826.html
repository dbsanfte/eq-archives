There's a known bug with illusions where if you zone in after someone has
cast an illusion, that person will appear to be their normal height no
matter what their illusion is. Typically you see this in dungeons where an
ogre (troll/barbarian) uses a guise of the deceiver to make himself into a
dark elf that's ogre height.
