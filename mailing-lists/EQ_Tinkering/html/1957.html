About selling Cams and Fireworks...

At first I had trouble selling Cams (I haven't really tried anything else
yet 'cause my skill isn't high enough and I run out of money), but lately
I've been able to sell all I could make and even have trouble keeping them
in stock. (I blew all my money on my lvl 24 spells) I'm not really making
money on the skill yet though. I am hoping that changes with spyglasses.

I have had wonderful success selling one cam for 20pp or two for 35pp. What
is everyone else's price point on these? At 40pp for two cams, there aren't
as many people that want to buy.

Fireworks have been a big hit. I even have people I've never met asking me
to make them. I've sold them for 1pp for the 4 pieces you get with each
attempt. I even made 12 fireworks (48 pieces) for someone once for 25pp!
It's kind of a pain to get the bat wings (there is a vendor in ECommons) but
they seem to sell well.
