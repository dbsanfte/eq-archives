Subject: EQ Survey


Hello.

I am a student at Queen's University in Canada who is taking part in
a research project that is studying Everquest. After spending
numerous hours (or perhaps months would be a better way to put it!)
playing the game we have put together a brief survey, which should
take no more than ten minutes to complete. We are trying to get a
better understanding of the relationships which develop in the vast
land of Norrath. If you are interested in participating merely cut
and paste the questions below and e-mail your replies to
7jss1@... Any comments or questions would be welcome and
appreciated. The survey can also be found at
http://qlink.queensu.ca/~7jss1/eqsurvey.htm

Thank you,
Pteridan (Jon Smithen)
Knight of the 14th seasonPlayer questions


How old are you?

Are you male or female?

How long have you been playing Everquest?

Is Everquest the first on-line roleplaying game you've played?

Why did you start playing Everquest?


Avatar questions

What level, race, class, gender is your main avatar?

Do you have secondary avatars?

What levels, races, classes, genders are your secondary avatars?

Why did you choose the classes you did for your avatars?

Do you play on a PvP server?

Are you in a guild?

For what reasons are you in a guild (or alternatively for what
reasons are you not in a guild)?

Do you attend guild events?

Do you plan to ahead of time to attend guild events?

Do you normally group with

1) People you meet that session
2) People you knew prior to playing Everquest
3) People you met through Everquest
4) Guildmate you found in your area
5) Other (please specify)

Have your grouping patterns changed as you've gained experience at
the game? How so?


Time questions

How many hours a week (on average) do you play Everquest?

How many hours do you play each session?

How do you normally spend your time in Norrath?

Are you on a constant quest to level or do you take time off from
gaining experience while playing?

What do you do when you take time off from the mobs?

While trying to level do you ever take time out to help others?

If yes (to the previous question), in what ways?

Did you know the other avatars prior to helping them?

Can you give any reasons why you chose to help others?

Have you ever died helping others (outside of a group situation)?


Calls for help

Do you ever help newbies?

If yes (to the previous question), how do you help newbies?

Can you give any reasons why you helped them?

Were you helped as a newbie? (This does not include help from friends
you met prior to playing)

If yes (to the previous question), how were you helped?

Do you respond to the /yell command?

Does your respose to a plea for help depend on any factors? What are
those factors?

Are you more likely to help members of a specific race? gender?
class? Why?

Was there any time in your avatars life when you were more likely to
help others?

If yes (to the previous question), what brought about the difference
in game play?

Why do you think that people help others in Everquest?

Comments? Further thoughts?

Please e-mail to 7jss1@...

Thank you for spending your time completing this survey. If anyone
would be kind enough to meet our research team on-line (either in
game, ICQ or IRC) for discussion we would be very grateful for the
opportunity.
