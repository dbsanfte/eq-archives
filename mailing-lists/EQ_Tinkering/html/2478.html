Actually, you can use them empty or full when using them to make rebreathers.
 
They still sell back to the store for 190p whether empty or full.  I've found that aqualungs are quite useful in kedge, too.  They're cheap to make, last a while, and useful when used up.  Just have members buy one and put it in a general slot, and make a hotkey for the slot.  If you kill things that dispel, and all they have is enduring breath, they could very well die.  Having an aqualung handy in a general slot means they just click it to get EB back.  It's also useful since they don't have to remove a head item to breath..
 
Hope this helps.
