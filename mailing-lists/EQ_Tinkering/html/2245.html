This item can be created by gnomish tinkers. Combine the following items in a
Toolbox to create the gemcutter. This item will become trivial to make at
skill 104.


Metal Rod
Diamon Dust
Standard Bow Cam
Metal Fastening
Firewater
Grease

This is a great tool for all those jewelry makers out there. It will increase
the quality of gems which in turn will reduce the chance of failure when
using these "upgraded" gems. All you have to do is pick up a gem, and then
right click on the gemcutter, you will be returned with a high quality gem.
