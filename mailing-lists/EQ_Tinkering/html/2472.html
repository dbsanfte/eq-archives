An empty Aqualung sells back to the vendor for the exact same amount a full
one does (about 160pp if I remember correctly), so make sure you let any
potential customers know that they will be able to regain some of the cost
when they use up the Lung. You could also offer to buy back the empty lung
yourself at around that price, as it can indeed be used to make a
rebreather. Another option is to sell the rebreathers at a discount to
anyone who provides you with a used Lung. Good Luck.
