Those Killer Sharks are still red to me at 36th.
If you want to find them. Go to the Island that the boat stops at on its
way from Qeynos to Erudin. I think thats Kerra Island. Anyway, run along
the coast to your right from the dock. You will see what looks like poles
sticking out of the water. Those are masts from a sunken ship. There were
some green undead zombie sailors there. Go directly out past the sunken
boat and you will find the killer sharks.
Warning. I saw more than one when I was there invis last time. And when
my invis poped right next to them, DOH, with my 165 swimming I didn't have
time to make it back to land before they got me. Hitting for 70 to 90
damage per hit, with multiple attacks.
