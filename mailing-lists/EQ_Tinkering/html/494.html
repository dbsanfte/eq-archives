Subject: Some thought on the fishing pole


During the beta, the developer for the trade skils said that
for Tinkering it was VERY IMPORTANT to read between the lines
in the Journals of Gimblestan. If that's a given lets look
at the description of the Spyglass.

"...The extra Metal Rod gives it a good shell while the bottle and
metal twine hold and amplify the vision..."

I've taken this to mean that because it says extra the collapsable
pole must have at least 1 metal rod. I'm also assuming since it
doesn't say extra or additional on the metal twine or bottle that
they probably aren't a component.

Of course I may be way off base. As soon as I have more money
to experiment with I'm going to start trying to make this one.
