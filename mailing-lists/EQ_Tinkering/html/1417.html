If there were no tinkering cap.. every character with a few thousand plat
lying around (40th plus folks in general), regardless
of race, class, etc. can roll up a 1st level gnome (or if we leave limit
at 16th level, then power level a small gnome necro to 16) and get them
maxed on tinkering in a few days. This would crush our market. If you
had a 50th hi-elf enchanter, there's no way you'd feel like starting a new
gnome mage and take him to 40th JUST to tinker (it may have a factor in their
choice if they're choosing a new character anyway).

I'm 35th level, been tinkering since 29th. This is my first character and
I dont have lots of cash (mostly thanks to tinkering). If the cap is removed
I'm confident the market will crash worse than it already is.

enchanter.

I am certainly bias, because my meager savings only lets me advance in spurts.
If 50 master tinkerers show up on my server I'm probably screwed. All my
