There are ONLY sharks in OOT in the water. They are usually around level 18,
and scowl, ready to attack at everyone, but, for some reason, they will
still not attack you, unless you hang around one for too long. They all look
like hammerheads, but, for some reason, there is no other kind of fish or
animal in the water. The ocean bottem is rather borng in most places. It
usually looks just like a totally flat plane, so it will usually be easy to
spot an abnormality.
The icean floor is not really as deep as you might think though. You can
easily go to the bottom and search it for a little bit with a high enough
swimming skill, but I also highly reccommend a fish bone earring.
