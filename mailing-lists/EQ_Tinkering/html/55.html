Quest #1 Telescope Lens quest. Originates in wizards guild. Obtain 4
spare telescope lenses and return to guild master. Reward in beta was a
level 24 spell.

Quest #2 Observer staff quest. Originates in steamfont. Kill 30ish level
Eye, return cornea (lens) and get a staff of the observer in return.
