There certainly seem to be nothing wrong with the will
for exploration among us gnomes. This is as it should
be, gnomes ARE the most curious race of Norrath.

numbers of all races, and not all of us do even bother
to tinker.
What I have been pondering about is a way to spread
the word about what we are searching for. This way we
may find our treasures and get to our conclusions
earlier.

What if Fulcrum or some other spokesperson among us
posted it on EQvault, Stratics, Allakhazam, Everlore,
EQlizer and so on, that we are searching for tattered
gnomish cloaks and diamond dust. We may even put out a
reward for it.
Please tell me what you think about it.
