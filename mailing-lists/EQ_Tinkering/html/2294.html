Well, you're still in luck from 125 up (don't know the trivial point) you
can make catapults.

1 Shaped Ashwood Recurve Bow ------26.0pp
1 Gears ---------------------------------------------7.4pp
1 Metal Twine ------------------------------------1.0pp
1 Sprockets ---------------------------------------7.4pp
1 Firewater -----------------------------------------0.1gp

Also about 42 plat per attempt, also sells back for 38pp

The Bow is made through Fletching Skill
1 Ashwood Bowstave
1 Plane
1 Hemp String (N.B. String is important, it won't work if the bow is made
with Linen or Silk)

I started these at 122 and got to 125 without them being trivial. I
haven't gone beyond that yet.

The resulting Catapult weighs 0.1, equips in the offhand slot, and is
useable by All/All. Having said that, I have no idea what it does. Doesn't
seem to be a weapon or give any benefits. If anyone figures it out, I'd be
happy to hear it :)
