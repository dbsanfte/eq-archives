The four trade skills messages you can get (that I know of at least hehe):

-You have fashioned the items together to create something new!

Made a new item

-You lacked the skills to fashion the items together.

Failed making the item but the components and quantities you used were
correct.

-These items don't combine in these quantities to produce anything.

Wrong formula or quantities, remember not to stack components that are
stackable such as water.

-You have fashioned the items together to create an alternate product.

You failed making an item but the game gives you back 1 or more of the
components you used.
