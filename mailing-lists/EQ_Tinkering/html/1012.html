The library is the mage/enchanter/wizard guild. It used to be the
necromancer guild as well but it was getting rediculously crowded so they
moved the necros down to the mines of malfunction. As you go up the ramp to
the guild, stop at the top and look at it. There is actually a sign that
says "Library Mechamagica" above the door.

The secret wall is the only corner that does not have a (visible) area for a
specific guild. Since there are obvious areas for mages, wizards, and
enchanters, just walk through the one remaining wall that isn't taken up.

Frankly that secret isn't nearly so perplexing as the location of the rogue
guild. I had to start a newbie wood elf ranger to track the guildmaster
before I could find it.
