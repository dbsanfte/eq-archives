Just a word of warning, I wouldn't get your hopes up too high for tinkered
pets. I'm fairly sure that Verant made an official statement that there
would never be such things. I also know (playing since beta 2) that there
have never been publicly available tinkered pets in the game. Given their
concerns about balance, I find it highly unlikely that they would include
tinkered pets. Think of how much more powerful a gnome wizard with tinkered
pet would be compared to a wizard of any other race.
