To answer Zanren's question, you can enhance your
int/wisdom with several pieces of jewelry (+4 int
rings, plat sapphire necklace, bracelets of woven
grass, earrings +2), plus oracle robe (which adds both
wis and int), wolf fur slippers, gatorskin leggings,
stein of mogguk, pet focus items (for magicians only),
runed circlet etc. Sorry I'm trying to go by memory.

In addition, you can have a high level enchanter cast
brilliance and insight to add additional int and
wisdom. With all those items in place the stats can
really add up.
