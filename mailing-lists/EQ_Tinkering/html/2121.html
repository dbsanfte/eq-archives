Hola gnomes of Norrath, tis I Calador again.  I appologize for my absence, it took me a while to track down Ursoo again, he has been very elusive, probably due in part to the fact that I haven't been on as much lately because of work.  Anyway this weekend, I caught him online, and got the real names of the parts he found, and got him to agree to bring them to me so I could have a tinkering friend of mine try his hand at this recipe.  Xeus was right about the gear thing, Ursoo aparently just looked at them and assumed they were simply gears, in reality, they are Size 6 sprockets.  Let me reiterate the parts we have:
 
Inferno Shock (Wizzie Spell)
Metal Twine
Metal Rod
Gizmo #6  (may need to be replaced by a gizmo #4)
Large Lantern
 
(things get a bit strange on the rest)
 
(13 X) Size 6 sprockets
(5 X)   Metal Bits  (used in smithing)
(4 X)   Ambers
 
Yes, the last 3 parts used multiples, and were stacked, which is the only way they would all fit in a toolbox anyway.   This recipe could be tried with only 1 of each of the last 3 now that we know it uses sprockets and not just gears, but I figure knowing the way the rest of tinkering works, that the multiples would be right, to drive prices up for making these.  I intend to get back to Ak'Anon, and find a friendly gnome to help me test this, and the optional recipe using a Gizmo # 4, but I am in Eastern Karana now, and do not know when my travels may take me back that way.  I hope this is a viable recipe, and will create something, anything, maybe even something that will help fill the skill gap between aqualungs, and rebreathers.  Again, I appologize for not getting this information to you sooner.   Good Luck.
