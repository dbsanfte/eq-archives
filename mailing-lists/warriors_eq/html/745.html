Warriors are not solo'ers, plain and simple. We require a cleric to be
really usefull. And at later levels we need a wizard to do the majority of
the damage. BUT at the same time, wizards need us to keep the monsters off
of them so they can cast their spells. All in all I gotta give verant 2
thumbs up on their design, the classes are pretty well balanced, no one
class is really any more powerfull than any other class (due to each classes
weaknesses), every group pretty much relies upon 3 things, a tank, a cleric,
and a wizard.

As for the PvP server, well, wizards defintially have some advantages
there....but thats another story I don't care to get into.

as for us being weak offensivly....well thats debatable, at least when
talking about group play non pvp. Sure we don't actually dish out the
damage, but we make it possible for the casters to be able to dish out the
damage, so looking at it that way, we have tremendous offensive powers. As
they say, a good defense is the best offense, and NO BODY can match a
warriors defensive tanking power :)
