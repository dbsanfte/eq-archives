Taunt is ineffective if your casters are dealing to much damage with spells.
They do large amounts of damage in a short time keeping a mobs attention.

The reason the 2 hs is better in a group is if the wiz controls himself in
the early parts of combat you can keep a mob taunted on you the whole time
because of the damage you are dealing with your big sword.

Then when the wizzie kicks in the heavy damage the mob takes off running.
So in the perfect combat you take all the damage...cleric only has one
person to heal...and the group moves on to the next combat faster.

It also helps that you do the pulling as well. Keep a bow on hand for
pulling from a distance.

My $.02
