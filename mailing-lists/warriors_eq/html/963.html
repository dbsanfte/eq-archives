Subject: The Best Class is.....


There is nothing better than life as a barbarian warrior in Norrath! In
previous lives I have been a Paladin, a Wizard, and a Monk (not here in
Norrath, but another world/MUD). There is no better way to experience the
world that is Norrath than as a warrior deep in the heart of battle. I have
no need to role-play, as I live my role every time my group goes out on a
hunt. With 500+ hitpoints, two long swords (one combine, the other fine
steel), and no fear of death, I can think of no better true killing machine
on the battlefield.

Sure magic users have powerful magic spells that cause a great deal of
damage in one shot, but it is warrior that must maintain the fight while the
magic users rest to regain mana or run like frightened rabbits to zone when
they get hit a couple of times. A good fight for me is my group geting
jumped by five of six MoBs, each capable of killing any one of the magic
users with ease. I wade into the front and just start hacking and taunting
until everything is dead in front of me. Oh sure, I might be down to 5 hit
points (yes it happens), but the group will always remain alive.

One day I will lead the attack on that silly dragon camped in Sol B. No
other class can truly call themselves a tank, and no other class would be
stupid enough to led an attack on the dragon and expect to live.

For the Honor and Glory of Rallos Zek;
