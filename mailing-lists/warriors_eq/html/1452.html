Well I believe the lore item weapons you will only be able to get one. You
will be able to get 1 PGT, or 1 Painbringer, but not two of each. Also
Ghoulsbane is Paladin only so you won't be able to even get one of those.
Start working on your piercing right now and get a Dragoon Dirk, they are
better than a combine longsword. I was able to take out Dvinn with a level
19 enchanter and get my Dragoon Dirk instead of buying it (I was level 20)
but you can buy one for around 20 plat. At level 21 I haven't seen my
strength bonus yet. I have been hitting for the occasional 14 since level
17 though with a combine longsword.

You may need to check your numbers though. I have a 110 in Double attack
and dual wield. I double attack maybe 75-80% of the time and dual wield
happens maybe 30%, so 44-48 dmg per round is probably not correct. You
really should spend the money now for another combine weapon though. You
can solo scarecrows at your level with two magical weapons. Also if you
play a large race the magical slam is one of the best skills a warrior can
have.
