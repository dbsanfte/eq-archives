OK assuming you can board the boat in Freeport this is how you do it. At
the first place the ship stops STAY away from the docks. The woman wood elf
guard will board the ship to KOS. The only thing that saved me first time
around was the ship moved. So stay to the left of the ship or on top of the
high deck.

The Dwarven guards conned dubious to me, so I was able to get off at the
docks. ALL other Dwarves conned threateningly and ALL elves were ready to
attack. I can not really describe how to get to faydark since I do it all
by sight.

Once you enter faydark if you stick to the zone wall and go left you will
hit Crushbone if you stick right you will hit the other faydark entrance.
Dark elves are safe in Crushbone. The gnomes were also neutral to me so I
could visit their city as well.

Good luck!
