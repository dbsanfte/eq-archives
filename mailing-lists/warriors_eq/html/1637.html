Yes, the scarecrows are not bad xp, but it is actually slower than with a
half descent group in Unrest. If you can get a group in unrest killing yard
trash, which is in abundance, you can level much faster. You can even take
some of the lower level house items depending on if you can get some of it
in ones and twos. Soloing is not the way of life for a warrior, they are
meant to be the meat of a group. You will come to rely on your group more
than anything else very soon when there is almost nothing you can solo and
still get xp.

But yes, the scarecrows were left out of places you can get xp at an ok rate
and solo.

Just thought I would share this. I've notice that not too many folks hunt
the scarecrows at the Miller farm in West Karana. While the money is not
that great, the ep is pretty good, and there is usually no one there so you
have them all to yourself. There are 4 that spawn in the fields of the
Miller farm. It is rather safe hunting, as the scarecrows do not help each
other (no trains) and there is nothing in the area that is dangerous to you.
I have never seen a Hill giant at the farm, and at 18th lvl that is the only
wandering mob in the zone you really have to watch for. Soloing for a
warrior is really tough, but I was able to gain some good ep, in a
reasonable time, in relative safety while solo in these fields.

I was able to fight 2 in a row before having to rest. Just make sure you
have lots of bandages!
