The bard's songs are their 'spells' or magic.� Most all songs are 'sung' -
that is you scribe them, memorize them and then you can 'play' them.� If you
do not have an instrument equipped, you will be 'singing' them.� Some songs
are enhanced by the use of an instrument and require a specific instrument.�
Some songs you can only scribe if you have sufficient experience (level) and
some instruments you can only play when you reach a given level.
�
The first instrument you can use is the drum.� Ask around and see if you can
trade someone your lute or mandolin for a drum.� In any case sell to another
bard either the lute or mandolin - they are both string instruments and you
need only one - not both.
�
Level 4 gets you the Lyssiera's Locating song and you can help people find
lost corpses.� There is not as big a market as you'd expect for helping
people find corpses.�The newbies who lose their corpses have no money and
the higher level players who do - often do it in really tough�places in
zones you have never been too!� That can be quite an adventure - especially
if you are only level 4.
�
Level 5 is a great level to reach.� At 5, you can sing and play with the
drum Selo's Accellerando and feel quite invulerable as you can out run most
anything.� This is great for exploring.
�
At 6, you get the song that can heal the whole party. And at 8, you can play
your lute to enhance the healing process.
�
So, I hope that piques your interest in bards -
