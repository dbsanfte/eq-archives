<< My question is what skills/weapons should I actively pursue more than

My advice:
never put more than one practice into a melee weapon
skill
learn to use a bow and learn to fletch (both expensive and time
consuming but worth it) - archery requires investments of bunches of practice
points, fletching requires time (something to do while resting) and money
(lots of botched arrows) but the bows you'll eventually be making will rock
(much better than any store-bought).
fairly soon it should no trouble for you to get another melee
weapon skill maxed; 2HS is probably a great bet especially until you get dual
wield; I have little experience with blunts
tailoring is a waste of time (fun the first time you
put your outfit together, but store-bought leather/rawhide is significantly
better and you'll be wearing ring mail/chain soon enough; although people
made a profit off the skill in the past when it was a novelty, I seriously
doubt anyone will make much more from it)
