Subject: Camping


Its Verant that created the campers. They have made it so that the good items only spawn once in a great while. These items are worth having, and worth spending time to obtain. So when I get strong enought to get any particular item I really really want, I camp the monster that drops it until I get one, and chances are that there will be someone else in my party that needs the item to, so we have to camp until they get one too, THEN we move on.

I do admit camping is a serious problem in the game. But mainly the problem is that there are so many guilds who FARM items for their lower level guild buddies. By farming I mean camping getting their item, camping, camping, camping, getting their item, camping camping camping, (loop), until they have enough to give their entire guild.

Fine example was last night in Cazic, a SK and a couple caster types were camping for rubicite gloves. It was rather obvious when they actually got them because the SKs gloves turned red. The gloves are the only item that this particular mob drops. After getting his gloves he continued to camp. Now why did he need to camp there after he got his item? THATS where the camping problem is.

I've purposed this solution to verant on their internal beta testing beta board which the general public does not have access to, but nobody from verant even bothered to reply:

Simply make it so that the higher level items can only be looted once by any given player, perhaps twice (so they can have one for trading purposes), or 4 times for items that you can use 2 of, ie earrings, rings, bracers, 1h weapons.
