Indeed, the Centaur brothers offer *8* intriguing mini-quests to provide
outstanding warrior-only armor. Someone tipped me off to their
existance as someone has to you, and I whole-heartedly encourage each of
you to go down to SK, take the time to have a lengthy conversation (and
get all the details on the quests) for yourselves. Each of the armors
has two main components needed to be made. A somewhat rare, somewhat
random, no-drop item found on a mostly random level 35+ mob in a certain
area, and a rare gem (or gems).

There are no magical enchantments on the armors I've acquired
(breastplate and helm). The gems for the items range from Ruby
(vendor-available, cost 130PP and up depending on charisma), to
Sapphire, the game's rarest gem. The clues left by the Brothers are
very clear and easy-to-follow in all but one case. Doing the quests...
not easy. :)

Dalkin Orcslayer
