Well, I think if you go on the basis of a person who just entered EQ and
didn't have a guild or anyone else assisting... what would actually be
attainable. I found it much easier to get items and such once I joined
my guild because of the level mix, but before I joined I had to really
plan out what I could afford, where to hunt, etc. Some things like
bronze can be bought by going to zones where the upper level folk go and
just hang out because some times they are getting a little overweight

be mentioned. Prices for bronze items oyi.. if you are getting large
seems to be lower but that medium will be a higher prices since it can
be worn by more races. Small sizes are of course cheaper. I'm not a
good one for attaining prices from since most of my armor has been gifts
(crafted *drools* all from trades and my char's wedding gifts)
