At level 15 I am just getting into the daul weild/double attack mode, but
Tom's info looks very appropriate. My primary reason for looking to switch
from longsword primary and mino axe secondary isn't speed or damage its
STAMINA. There is a current thread running on this topic - be sure you
check it out. Mino axes are HEAVY, and weilding two weapons already takes a
lot of stamina. Combine this with the fact that the length of your fights
increases with the level of the MOB and you run into quite a few situations
where you are runnng too low on mana.
Last weekend I was soloing some orcs in the south part of Oasis while I was
waiting for some friends to come so we could hunt some crocs. I engaged a
lone orc and pulled him down to the beach. While I was fighting this orc
(and doing pretty well - he was conning blue) I heard the DREADED sound of
an approaching giant (You know the sound if you've heard it! It's one of the
few things that gives me an adrenaline rush EVERY time in EQ!). Not wanting
to be squished like a bug, I tried to take myself and the orc closer to the
zone boundary - without getting into the ocean - and I got a LITTLE too
close to the OTHER orc that was in the camp. To make a long story short I
managed to avoid the Giant, but I couldn't flee the orcs without running
into the giant. I killed off the first orc and still had around half my
health...I ran out of stamina before I ran out of health with the second
orc, and I was pretty damn confused! You see I was SWINGING, but I wasn't
HITTING anything. I checked SEVERAL timesto make sure I was still in attack
mode and properly targeted (you guys know that feeling??) and then FINALLY
realized I was just plain ol' tuckered out. Anyway it came down to the last
couple of blows, and I DID die...but I learned a lesson too. Mino axes are
pretty cool (and I love the way they look - 2 axes is even cooler!), but
swords are much less demanding on your stamina.
Right now I am trying to see if I can improve my Piercing skill (I think its
around 20, lol!) by sticking a Fine Steel Rapier in my secondary weapon
slot. I'll let you all know how that works!
Thanks fer your time...sorry if I rambled!
Grukklukk of Bristlebane, lvl 15 basher
