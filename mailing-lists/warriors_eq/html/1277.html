What does everyone think about possibly giving Warriors a MINIMUM amount
of damage done? For example, Im level 16 wielding either a Dwarven 2Hand Axe

Combine LongSword and Tentacle Whip. Most of the time, I hit for 28, 29, 30

1-10. Personally I think that sucks. Now, what if Verant were to give Warriors

with the Combine LS.

I think this is a reasonable idea and in some cases would be better than
5-10 damage extra. Just think, NEVER hitting for 1 damage again. I personally
think in a realistic sense, a big Barbarian Warrior with 130STR wielding a huge
Dwarven 2Hander hitting something for 1 damage is kind of pitiful. What do you
all think?
