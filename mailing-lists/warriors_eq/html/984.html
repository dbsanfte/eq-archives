help me when I was jumped by the dry bones skeleton the first time. They

Once in the oasis area, I realized I still had three full backpacks dragging

I climbed up the hill overlooking the inn and looked around for errant

Well anyone reading this can guess the rest. At least I sold all my extra

find some.

I have decided that I hate giants more than griffons, and one day I will
lead a hunt to destroy every giant in the world of Norrath!!
