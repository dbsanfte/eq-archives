This was my response to the latest petition update:

>Remove the Cap on Combat Skills (offense, defense, 1H slash, kick, dual
wield, >parry, etc.) which comes into effect around level 40.

Instead of raising the cap (something that won't affect most warriors
anyway), how about increasing the chance to hit a creature? It would be much
more beneficial if I could hit a creature more often at skill level 50
instead of waiting until level 200 for that same chance to hit.

>Allow Warriors to advance 6 points in Combat Skills per level instead of
just 5. At >any given level they would be 20% better at melee combat than the
hybrids.

Nice idea, but I feel that's unfair to non-warriors. Again, to get better at
melee combat than the other classes, see my suggestion above.

>A unique skill that no other class has. Many players are in favor of Weapon
>Specialization (reduced delay and increased damage). Use the same rules as
>specialization for Spell Casters.

I'm surprised something like this wasn't implemented in the beginning anyway.
However, I fear it might be too broad to allow one to specialize in all 1H
Slashing or all 2H Blunt.

Something else that would work well would be either a permanent immunity or
increased percentage chance to resist going into a berzerker frenzy in
combat. Either that or the ability to stun monsters like they can stun us.
