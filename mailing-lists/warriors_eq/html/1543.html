Really hard. Unfortunately, there aren't very many
magic weapon options. Here's the possibles:

Combine Longsword: 6/28 0.214
Combine Scimitar: 5/24 0.208
PGT: 6/26 0.231
Barbed Leather Whip: 7/28 0.250

These are the most readily available options. There
are other weapons that might be available to 30th
level characters (both lootable and affordable), but
they're much rarer. For example, I finally found
someone wielding a Gladius (7/29 0.241) just over the
past weekend. Others are reported but not commonly
known where to locate, or commonly available on the
markets.

Piercing expands the field somewhat:

Combine Rapier: 5/23 0.217
Combine Spear: 6/27 0.222
Dragoon Dirk: 6/23 0.261
Ashenwood Spear: 6/22 0.272

These are about as available as the 1hS options above,
all in the vicinity of (or under) 300pp(ish).

Kinda disappointing, huh? Once you look at it, there
really *aren't* very many weapons available...
