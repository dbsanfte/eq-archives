Actually, the Sith was an ancient order of Dark Jedi founded after the first
great division of the Jedi Order. They were named so after enslaved a race
known as the Sith and becoming merciless warlords. Most of the Sith were
weeded out by the Old Republic in later years, but a grand temple still was
hidden on Yavin IV, presumably where Darth Sidious and Maul learned their
art. There is only one Dark Lord of the Sith at any time... Sidious is
beyond that title. The Dark Lords are the only ones to use red
lightsabres. Maul was the current Dark Lord, and Vader later became a Dark
Lord.
