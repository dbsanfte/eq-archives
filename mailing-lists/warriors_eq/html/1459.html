Head toCazic Thule for cash. I know it is probably heavily populated, but
you can get alot of FS weapons from the defenders and sentinels which should
still give you experiece as well. The warders will carry FS also. Just get
yourself with a cleric and pull ones and twos from the tunnels and "try" to
get the ones that have FS. I have made over 100 plat a night in that place
with just a few hours worth of work. And that was during crowded time
periods.

Also, the temple at th end of the maze is a GREAT place for cash/exp. But
you should have a pretty good group to go into that area. Definitely have a
well rounded group i.e. 2 healer/2tank/2nuker. You will gain xp and cash
very fast in that area.

As far as weapons are concerned. Try to get yourself a demons claw and
tentacle whip. The PGT is nice, but the knock to your agi is terrible when
the bezerker strength goes off. The VERY low speed/damage on these items
allows for non-stop attacks. For armor... I would recommend saving your
cash for Steel plate until you can get into some rubi/mithril/silvered. I
had similiar problems with people constantly camping the rubicite so i
decided while they were sitting there gaining nothing but wasting a tone of
time. I would just spend my time reaping cash/xp from the lizards and buy
my armor. Also get as much bronze as you can in the mean time wihthout
spend alot of time/money doing it.

Another good money pit is solusek a. You should be able to go there with a
well balanced group and get around pretty well. Be wary of the traps and
get mobbed by hordes thoug. Everytime I head to a place like that i have a
druid or wiz that can group gate or succor us out in an emergency (they
always happen *grin).
