Sometimes you have to wait but usually you don't have to wait too long. Two
nights ago, some guy shouts "help! I give 12pp to anyone who can find me
and lead me back to qeynos" So, I figure hmm piece of cake and set out
looking for him. He gives me a loc and I go there to look for him and it
turns out to be the middle of the West Karanas bandit camp. I know that
place pretty well so I did not run right in and die or anything but took
note of this prankster's name. Last night I am on with my other char and
see him in South Qeynos shouting about something and I give him a /tell:
"help I am lost ill give you a plat to show me the bank..."

Well, he bites on it and while I go about my business, he runs around
looking for me. "where are you?" "I see mermaids" "do you see me? I am at
mermaids inn now - did you go inside?" "now I see a pond whr r u?" "hey
stand still so I can find u..." After 5 or 10 min of wasting his time, I go
"nm, found it!"

So moral of the story is: take names and kick butt later if necessary.
Sometimes you need to be creative, otherwise just /ignore.
