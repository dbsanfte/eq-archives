Will a mob kill your whole party if it is attacked by just one individual?

Yes and no. I've had an interesting experience while being partied with a friend
who plays a rogue. Most of the group has problems with the tough mobs and will
die if it starts attacking the party .... except for the rogue.

Here's an example. While doing the screaming mace quest we were camped in the
tower of Crushbone waiting for Emperor Crush to arrive. Assured that Ambassador
Dvinn was at the zone killing newbies, we continued to wait without any concern.
Suddenly Crush spawns ... no problem, we begin the whacking. Then, about 3 or 4
seconds later, Dvinn spawns. Wham Wham Wham, one down ... etc until 5 party
members were dead, none even made it past the throne.

Except for the rogue who sat there plain as day, probably messing herself from
the fear. He never touched her, and in fact conned to her as indifferent I
believe. She was able to do corpse retrievals for some of us as we all waited at
the zone.

Soooooo, I think as long as the mob is dubious or auto-attack toward everyone in
your group, then yes, he will kill the whole group. I guess it pays to take a
dark elf or rogue along with you at times.
