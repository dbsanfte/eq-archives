Mark...When you right click on the main combat menu, a pad with six buttons
pops up. When you first log on, they will be named Skill #1, #2, etc for
each of the six keys.
One of those keys should bring up a horizontal set of keys on your screen.
All your skills will be listed there. Find bind wound, make it a hotkey on
the left side where the hotkey bank is. Then when your health falls beyond
half, you can click on it (providing you have bandages in your inventory),
and you will drop down and begin to Bind Wound. A yellow message will pop
up on your text screen when it is completed. If you move at all during the
bandaging process, it will discontinue. The same if you do it on someone
else. After you or someone you've targeted reaches half health, the
bandaging will no longer take place. Hope this helps.
