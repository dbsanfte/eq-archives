You don't have to read somewhere, go look. All NPC
merchants have set stock they're selling (ore, spells,
armor, whatever); and then, at the bottom, they'll
have whatever people have sold to them. I don't know
how long these other items stay in the inventory
before disappearing, or if they'll ever disappear
(until sold), but they're there. You can test this
yourself; walk up to a merchant w/o something, sell it
to him/her, then talk to them again, and you'll see it
in their for-sale inventory. This is why most
merchants in North Ro and Freeport have Cutthroat
rings for sale, etc...
