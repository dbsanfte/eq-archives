Attack and AC do not cap at 1000. I've seen 1100+ AC on a level 55 warrior
and over 1000 attack on a warrior at 51. The 1000+ attack needed 255+ STR
and wolfform at 51, don't think an inkie will ever get STR that high though.
Not sure what your resists question is about. Aim to get them all around 60
un buffed so a resist buff will take you to 100. Once you get resists above
90 most non epic in nature mobs (gods, dragons and such are epic) won't be
able to get a spell to stick on you.
