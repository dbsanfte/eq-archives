Guildless at 42 with all that gear?? <boggle> The jagged bands are a bit
aged. I like the +5 ac +55 HP rings myself, but for raids you will want +sv
rings also. As an inkie (or any other non large race) I recommend working on
getting your dex high also. Smaller races don't have the str/sta of the
large races, but with crits being based on dex, I see the smaller races
critting alot more to make up for the lower damage and lesser hitpoints.
Getting down to Guk and camping an FBSS should be on your list of things to
do soon also. 42 is a great level for that. Blackjacks make a good 1hb
weapon, and the price is fairly low for the value.
