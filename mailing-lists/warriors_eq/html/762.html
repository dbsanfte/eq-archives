hmmm I agree and disagree.

I mean I use to hold to the same idea (and I even suggested it last night -
lets just fight IN the room with the bonecracker and 2 necros, if we die we
die, but atleast we tried). I always thought that the traditional dungeon
crawl, that is go from room to room exterminating the enemy, is the best
way. BUT EQ doesn't allow that, things RESPAWN, so your back is never even
marginally safe.

Then I thought about it logically why do humans and demi-humans dominate the
world of Norrath and not orcs or goblins who seem to exist in profusion as
well, and are physically tuffer than us (we use weapons to hurt them and
wear armour, there natural strength and thick skin is good enough)?

It occurred to me its because we are smarter than them. If "pulling" can
neutralize part of the enemy attack then I think its a good thing, I think
its a good thing because it demonstrates WHY we rule the planet - we are
smart (comparatively speaking). Its also a solid tactical move, minimize
the enemies ability to attack you, have them come one at a time or have them
strung out.

So you have never pulled? or never been in a party that has?
