Maybe in real life stamina is a limiting factor, but not in the game. And
when you do run out of stamina in EQ, yo umay miss more but you are still
able to hit. A warrior with no stamina is a little less effective. A mage
with no mana is worthless - period. Instead of coming over and help bang on
the creature I would rather them go sit and meditate so they can have enough
mana to help more.

Again, you can not compare real life settings with what is in EQ. In real
life you can be in full plate and wield a sword, I would rather carry a
bazooka (equivalent to the wizards damage spell) and just get it over with.
A warrior, *in EQ*, should not be able to do as much damage as a mage, or as
quickly.
