The letter that I posted was not my writing, I read it from the EQ'Lizer
site. I am also trying to get into touch with the writter of the letter to
enlist his help. I have only almost attained level 31. But in the time I
have played, and having interaction with some other players (warriors and
monk) I am a firm believer in the warrior is/will be not worth having in a
group of high level players. Sure, he will always be able to tank, but
other than that, he will have no participation in a group. The proverbial
punching bag. Every skill we have is shared with a hybrid group. We get
nothing special at all. I do not want to be a cry baby, but this just seems
unfair to me. Something to make the warrior stand out as the class that you
need to have when in physical combat would be nice. I mean isnt that the
true calling of a warrior? Many of the ideas you have submitted to me so
far are really good. And I will put together a complete list of everything
we come up with. I will not send this to verant until we get enough to make
a good selection from. Then when I send it to them, it will be "this is
some of the options that we have come up with... what do you think" type
attitude. They should listen to us, they listen to everyone else. Why
alienate one class when they are bending over backwards for others?
