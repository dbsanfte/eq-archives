safe.,Jes member that if it runs ta corner an
goes poof it'll reappear at top o ladder an start goin down an proally bring
some friends back.. On way ta fire room in
the corridor one spawns at zone wall, two at area where ya turn, one at bottom
of ladder, at top of ladder, in an alcove
jes near ladder top an one straight down ta fire room.
Mix of under 20 an lil ova 20 can pull ta fire room, or do flag room. In fire
room two spawn, slowly but they spawn.
From fire room to flag room three spawn on the way there. No memba place
exactly. In flag room three spawn up front,
Two to left archway an one to right archway. Through archway in back two spawn
there. To tunnel ta right one spawns
near there usually a caster, but not always. Through tunnel on right by gate
three spawn, one usually a caster.

Through archway to left is a door. Two spawn in that room an at bout 20 they be
blue. Either they be regular gobbies
or one be caster.
Through double doors to right is another good place fer 20 or lil over. Now me
say lil over because if you have to run ya
gotta go through the flag room if ya get in trouble. Two spawn there.
From that room there is a door that leads to the achiologist (proally spellin
wrong) Three spawn afore you get to
archiologist. Usually got evoker or a diviner amongst the three. The
archiologist room. One spawns to the right, Three
on stage, one on bridge. Under bridge be two that spawn that ya can see, Under
bridge to left usually three that
spawn. The archiologist room hard to clear. Is obtainable with mix of low/mid
20's but because of the one ta three
casters is dangerous.Spawinin from what m can see happens in threes in the
room. Archiologist will spawn on stage
near a pedistal lookin thing.

Now instead o going ta the door if you go down hallway there be preacher room.
Two spawn against wall, Three on
stage (preacher in middle). Three spawn behind the stage. Again there be
casters that spawn.

From the flag room go through the right tunnel through the gate to end of that
area an ta left be a movin boulder. That
be alchemist area. Ya gotta fight ya way through an is tough. Most everythin
blue ta me at 24. If ya not careful ya be
in spot where three near gate will come through gate. Total of bout 5-6 spawn
