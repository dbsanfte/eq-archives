Jiya guyz. First let me say that I am really happy to be seeing actual
discussion on this list rather than flames. Now to add my Quarter after
buying two dime candies and three penny candies, let me state this: It DOES
matter what kind of armor you have on, *BUT* it is also the AC that matters.
Here's an example. When I started my ranger I was wearing mostly patchwork
armor, then I moved up to leather. Take a leather gorget as the example. AC
3. A studded leather gorget is the SAME AC value, but adds +1 STA and +1
STR. Watch your AC when you switch them. It will drop by about 5 points.
Mine went from 210 to 205 at level 9 even though they both had the same AC.
I gave up the +'s for the higher AC since, at level 9, STR doesn't count for
diddly squat except for weight allowance. Don't belive me, try it yourself.
Take something that has the same AC value but is of a different material and
watch your AC drop.

Jynxx
