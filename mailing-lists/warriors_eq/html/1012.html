As a side note. If one of your group is engaged on combat by and
mob...named or not....then once your party member is dead the mob will come
after the next closest party member and so on till all are dead or zoned, or
you kill the mob.

When a giant attacks a party member...first thing I do is DISBAND! Same goes
for anything that can wipe out the whole party. We disband and then regroup
when the danger is passed. Then the whole party doesn't have to run to zone
to regroup which can save considerable time.

This can upset some people so it is best to make sure everyone understands
before it happens. To many people die becuase giants can destroy a party
before anyone realizes the trouble. 90+ damage per hit makes short work of
most players especially after being stunned.
