--- Patricia Halas <patty_halas@...> wrote:
> Has anyone ever thought about the skills we have to
> maintain;
>
> Dodge
> Parry

These maintain themselves, as do Offense and Defense.
If you're in a fight, they maintain.

> 1H slash
> 2H slash
> Dual Wield
> Bash/Slash
> Kick
> Archery

These you have to 'work' somewhat; by remembering to
switch out weapons after level capping one or the
other. Bash/Slam and Kick I figure aren't so hard; my
Troll has those hot keyed and they're *always* being
used in combat. The weapons are the hardest to
remember to maintain, especially when you're in tough
fights and want to switch to the 'better' weapon, but
want to keep capping the other.

The best way I've found to work archery is to find
someone who doesn't mind if you tag his targets with
some arrows, and then do it.
