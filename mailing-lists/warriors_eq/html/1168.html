I am in agreement as well.... Short of the fact that warriors are needed in
high level groups. Let me backtrack a little bit. Warriors at the lower
levels are far superior to hybrid classes in general. But at the higer
levels, the hybrids have gained so many abilities and extras that the
warrior is overshadowed by the attributes of a hybrid. Also, a hybrid can
fulfill the same role that a warrior can at those higher levels just as well
as a warrior can. A warrior also has very little ability at higher levels
to use his taunt, bue to lack of damage inflicted to a mob. I find this
happeneing to me at level 30 already. How bad will this be at level 40?
The lack of a warrior being able to get a mob off the casters will make his
worth in the battle less. Therefore groups will be more inclined to chose a
hybrid class as their tank. This will cause a loss of people actually
playing warriors in the game, leading to the downfall of the class as a
whole.

As for wizards/warrior argument. Warriors are not supposed to do the damage
a wizard can do. we are there as the shield so they can lay down the damage
at will....

If you get into a good group that is working well together. Where everyone
in the group knows there place in the group organization. Wizards become
the secondary source of damage, not the primary. If they nuke to soon, they
are mobbed, they have to hold their spells in check until the right time to
burn down the mob. Yes, they do put some small burns into the middle of the
battle, but they shouldnt let loose the great damage spells until late in
the battle. This is the true place where warriors shine. But even at that,
by the time the battle nears where its time for the wizards power, the
warrior has done little except take alot of damage. IMO we should be able
to help with some of the beginning portion of battles more than what we do.

I am thinking that the majority of us agree on the critical hit issue. How
do we feel about the specialization skills? Also, remember that we cap all
of our skills (that didnt cap earlier) at 200. Some of the hybrid classes
continue to be able to gain in skills past that. I feel we should be able
to remove that cap on our skill and continue to progress in them. We are
warriors, we never stop learning our art.
