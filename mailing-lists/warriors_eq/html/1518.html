Only thing I would change out is the druid. I prefer a shaman with me.
Regen (level 24 shaman spell) is one of the most mana efficient ways to
heal people. A shaman at 24 or above can go almost OOM during the fight,
then throw regen on the tanks, and the tanks will be 80% or better by the
time the finger wagglers are FM. And I do agree with Dyvim, until you fight
with an enchanter, you have no idea of their power. They don't start coming
into their true power until level 16. With one spell, they can change the
odds in a fight. And if you fight something that has a pet, once you kill
the master, an enchanter can one shot the pet.
