From what I have noticed, your slam and bash skills are
the same, only the bash will use a shield if it is equipped.

raises. If you don't have a shield equipped and try using
the bash skill you will get a message saying you need a
shield. The slam will work, however. I am working on my
bash/slam skill now with dual weapons instead of kick
because I have noticed it does interrupt spell casters much
more than kick.
