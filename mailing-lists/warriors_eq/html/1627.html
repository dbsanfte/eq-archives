Yep that's right. SHIFT + 2 will take you to the second 'page' of hotkeys
for example. Now here's my addition to the hotkey discussion. Socials. I use
emotes and socials a lot, so I decided to try something. On your main EQ
screen (without pushing F10) there are some menus on the lower right. The
one to look at is 'socials'. The menu is filled with all kinds of boring
socials 'afk' is one, 'hail' and 'wave' are others. Well what I did was
erase them and write my own. If you edit your socials, you can add about 6
lines of commands like a macro. I use it to hail a traveller in a different
way, by introducing myself. So the edited social looks like this:
> hail
> /say I am Jynxx, officer to the Council Of Grey.
> /say If there is anything I can do to assist you, just ask!
Then I name that something like 'intro' and make a hotkey for it. By editing
socials you can do anything that you can command to do. Just for another
example, I have one that tells me who in my guild is online. I have it so
that it reads:
> /who council of grey all
I have that bound to Hotkey 3 Page 1. I just thought I'd share with you guys
what I think is a great use for hotkeys, especially if you roleplay a lot.
To me that's what this list is about, and I have definetly gained a wealth
of knowledge from all of you. Thanks!
