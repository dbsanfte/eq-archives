I ALWAYS have two characters in every RPG game I have ever played Solomani
the Knight and jade (or Jayde or Jaade) the duelist.

I nearly always start with Jade but end up switching to solomani for a the
long haul. I also always end up liking solomani better.

Reasons I like to play a female, being a guy;

1) They look good and are nice to watch.
2) I find it easier RPG'ing a female then a male, maybe its because I am
imitating what I have seen and not reflecting what I am.

Also when I play a male the real me tends to come out a LOT more than with a
female. This isn't always bad but it can be if you are playing an evil
male. Its easier to RPG when you are forced to think about everything you
say and do, I see the character graphic as prism that distorts the light of
my personality. Sounds weird, but thats what 15 years of RPG does to ya ;).

3) My fianc� plays the character (its a shared character) no where near as
much as me, but she does play Jaade. This adds to the multiple
personality/erratic nature idea I like to do with Jaade. I think (as does
my fianc�) its kind of weird for her to play a male (but O.K. for me to play
a female ... go figure). We usually never tell our in game companions we
have switched so dont assume its a guy steering Jaade if you ever meet her.

that suddenly there transvestites or anything. Pickup any comic book, all
