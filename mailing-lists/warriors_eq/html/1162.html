Subject: Warriors vs Wizards


Many of the Warriors here have complained that they cannot do damage as fast
as a Wizard.

THEY ARE NOT SUPPOSED TO.

Now all of you real life ninjas and steel sword swinging fools just calm
down. Read carefully.


Warriors get to wear platemail and have many more hps than a Wizard.
Warriors also have a higher Offense, Defense, and Weapon skills. This all
means that a Warrior can stand toe to toe against an NPC.

Now lets make a Warrior have the same ability to deal damage as a Wizard.
Now we would have a class that has the best armor, the highest combat
skills, the most hp's AND the ability to deal the most damage.

Why would anyone want to play a Wizard if a Warrior could do as much damage
AND be able to take blows in melee?
