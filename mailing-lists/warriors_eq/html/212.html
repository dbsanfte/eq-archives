This is the way I use my weapons:

When fighting blue or white monsters I use my 2h sword because it can inflict mass damage, and I am fairly successfull at hitting blue and white mobs.

When fighting yellow or red monsters I use my dual wield with fast weapons, they don't inflict the mass damage like a 2h sword, but you get a lot more swings in. And since yellows and reds are much harder to hit, I have found that I do more damage just pecking away with little weapons and a lot of attacks vs a slow 2 hander attack which seldomly hits for max damage against reds anyways.
