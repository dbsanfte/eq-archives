Scrawny gnolls at entrance of BB three gen spots pretty easy picking.. will get
you to 8 easy.. painfully but easy.. there is a guard that wanders at the base
of the entrance.

When you get in a group go inside BB and stay on the top level. If you go at
night watch out for that first step oyi... there is a big hole in the middle
and water and this buggar in the water too (ouch!) fell in it the first time I
went there.

If you don't mind the dungeon atmosphere BB is great exp... I prefer the plains
so can't tell you much more...

On the plains hunt some larges spiders, and in a group some polar bears (some
you will be able to handle on your own) Just stay close to Brandl ( i think
thats how you spell his name) when you hunt alone.. pull them in and you will
be able to run if you get in trouble.

Later on the plains you can get in a group 8/9 ish.. start going for orcs
troopers... lepoards.. giant spiders.. just watch out for ice bone skeletons (
you will need a magical weapon so just run ), shamans and mounts.

Now I'm level 14 and have been doing mammoth calfs alone and in groups ..
started them n groups when was 11. and can handle sharmans and mounts with one
other person (love the bash skill for disrupting spells - it seems to work
better than kicking got it to 70 currently). I must say having a sharman to
give you SOW and heal you is wonderful.

Good luck,
