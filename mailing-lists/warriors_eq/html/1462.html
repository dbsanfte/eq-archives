I wouldnt start heading to Solusek A until around 25 and up. Maybe even
higher. I didnt go anyway there until level 30. Solusek B is much more
dangerous. I just set foot inside at level 30 and the first thing I saw was
red. May have been bad luck to see a powerful Goblin right off the bat, or
maybe that is the way the whole place is. Do not know, I didnt go any
farther.

As for the demon claw. You should (I would think) depending on the server
your on, and how many high level characters are on the server be able to
pick on up for around 200 - 300 plat. The shouldnt be for trade only items,
and they can be obtained around level 32 on your own with a group. Though
you DEFINITELY better have 2 clerics and someone at least around level 35.
The mob is a level 35 SK and has a 800point harm touch from what I have
heard. He never got me with the touch. He also smacks ya around a little
harder and alot faster than a Sand Giant.
