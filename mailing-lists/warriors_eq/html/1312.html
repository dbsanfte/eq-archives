Trolls, Ogres, Dark Elves (Probably Lizard Men too when they get added)
Barbarians, Humans, Erudites,
Wood Elves, High Elves, Half Elves.

will attempt to bring misery and pain to those who follow the light. Both my
friend and i are roleplayers and enjoy the feeling of playing the bad guy.

to be a warrior thats fine! Heck thats GREAT! We just need people.
