Subject: Surprise at the Barbarians Warrior Guild.


I am Thuxtis the Mighty, a Gnome Warrior who recently arrived in Halas
for some cold weather adventuring with his guild. After a lot of great
hunting, I returned to Halas to drop coin and loot, and decided to stop
at the Warrior guild to be sure that they would train him. Thuxtis had
recently made 14th level and was eagery looking forward to the magical
15th.

So there I am at the guild (and yes they will train gnmoes), I just
happened to look down the list of skills to train and I see:

Dual Wield
No Charge

Huh? Thuxtis rubs his eyes and looks again.

Dual Wield
No Charge

Well, You don't have to tell Thuxtis three times, so i reach up and
click on it, then press the practice button. "Your Skill in Dual Wield
has Gone up". Awesome... practice, practice, practice, practice.

How did this little gem of a change slip through? My friend Ramkota
informed me this morning that at 13th he could practice it.

So the Moral of the story is for all you 13-14 level warriors (and it
wouldn't hurt for the 12th to try as well), grab a SOW and RUN to the
nearest guild to grab this skill.
