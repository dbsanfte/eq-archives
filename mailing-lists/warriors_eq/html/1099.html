Subject: Spoiler for THEX MALLET


Ok i know this isnt the best warrior weapon but some people have asked about
the quest and i now have how it is done. dnt read on unless you want to get
spoiled.















----------------------------------------------------------------------------
------------------------------------------
OK this is a quest designed for evil characters so all you goodie twoshoes
may as well forget about it.

1. Ok first off you have to go to the necro/SK guild in Neriak and talk to
the head SK (Ive forgotten his name but he is pretty easy to pick out.) You
have to talk to him and he'll give you the quest to go kill the paladin in
the lavastorm mountains.

2. once you kill the paladin and return his prayer book to the guild leader,
he will ask you to take a note to a member in the West commonlands, Gix.

3. Give gix the note and hell ask you to fetch him the hammer peices from
befallen. The first two are located on the elf skeleton at the beginning of
level 3 (level 16-20 ) and the third is located on the Commander mummy in
Befallen. He is a REALLY rare spawn ( I still havent seen him ) and he drops
the thrid peice, bring them to gix and Voila you get the thex mallet.
