To get to guk from around freeport is easy, take the tunnl to North Ro then
walk toards the huts by the ocean....simply follow the coast right up untill
you hit the oasis. Once there keep following the coast past the dock, right
up untill you see an inn to your right. walk in a straight line past that
inn....as in start at it facing the door and go behind it. watch out cause
there are tons of ginats raoming around the oasis. Once you zone into
southeren Ro hang a left and follow the wall all the way to the next
zone...innothule swamp. To get to guk follow the left hand wall and youll
eventually run into a small cave, thats guk. Depending on your level you
will wanna stay in Upper Guk, there are tons of forgs to kill of all
different level as well as a section full of Heart spiders, (thats my
suggestion ... they give great loot and the giant heart spider drops the
chitin shell sheild AC 15 10 to resit poison and weighs 4 pounds.) just be
carefull cause trains are a constant plague in this dungeon
