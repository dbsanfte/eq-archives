I believe that warriors are necessary in a group only to the high teen-low
twenties. When most hybrid-magic user classes get to that level they can
either root, or get a pet that will engage the creature while he or she
casts (wherein they usually don't need long because they deal out the most
damage). I am a lev 17 warrior and blue cons can take me down to 1/3 hp, it
then takes me a half hour to heal back up. While I have a cleric who is
taking on white cons with little problem. I have to agree that warriors are
necessary, but only to the level before casters come into their own. And fyi
I have a fellow mate who is a level 18 wiz with all his points put into int
and he has only 100 less hp than I have. Another overlooked characteristic
is that casters can hit magic only creatures from the begining, while
warriors have to run around for weeks trying to save up the money for a
special weapon (wich are way overpriced), and most of the time we end up
running from green con's because we can't hit them. I only use my warrior
now to get money and equipment for other characters and roleplay.
