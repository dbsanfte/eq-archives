Warrior Petition Update

Iain has updated the recent Warrior Petition (see yesterday's news):
Many people have come to sign the Warrior Modification Petition. I and many other
warriors appreciate your support. As of right now 841 votes have been submitted.

I have run into a slight problem. Just minutes after I submitted the news of the petition yesterday. I came across a warriors mailing list that had done some debate and come up with a solution. The petition was modified after the first six votes to reflect something much more like the solution warriors_eq@onelist.com had come up with. The official petition on the site was changed, however many people did not read the official petition on the site, but just voted on what they read on the news submitted to this site.

If you voted yesterday, please take a few minutes to go back and make sure your opinion is still the same. The official petition (which will not change) is shown below.

After much heated debate on the Forums I am proposing a solution to make the warrior become king of melee combat.

1.Remove the Cap on Combat Skills (offense, defense, 1H slash, kick, dual
wield, parry, etc.) which comes into effect around level 40.

2.Allow Warriors to advance 6 points in Combat Skills per level instead of just
5. At any given level they would be 20% better at melee combat than the hybrids.

3.A unique skill that no other class has. Many players are in favor of Weapon Specialization (reduced delay and increased damage). Use the same rules as specialization for Spell Casters.

This would mean that at beginning levels warriors show small improvement at melee over the hybrids, but by the time they hit the teens and the hybrids are getting their spells the warriors are starting to out-melee them. They would be hitting much more often and they would be much harder to hit.

If your position has changed, simply email me with the account you signed up with,
your position, and comments (optional) and I will manually go in and change them.
