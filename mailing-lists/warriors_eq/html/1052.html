Uhmm - Fippy is the low level npc in nq - of that I am certain! Now Flippy
is probably the one you speak of and I have seen Verant play other tricks
with similar names to entice you into fights way over your head. GM's can
take over NPC's and play them against players - not sure what their policy
is but they have the power. I have heard that they will do this to break up
farmers who are not sharing spawns at camp sites.

You can hail Gnasher Gnoll as well and talk to him. He is part of 2 quests
out of Qeynos. And much lower level than the uber Gnolls in BB.

There is another named gnoll who lives on the farm in West Karana. His name
is Furball Miller. You can talk to him too. (Hint: just don't blindly
follow him into the corn fields.) Also talking to his parents you get his
sad tale. He is adopted. I think this is some unfinished quest as he has
relations back in BlackBurrow or Split Paw.

In South Qeynos there is a quest to kill Trumpy Irontoe - an obnoxious short
guy. In QH, you will see a Crumpy Irontoe who is much higher level. I
think Verant wants to fool you into trying to kill him thinking its Trumpy
(low level) when really it's Crumpy (high level) and you will get your butt
kicked (unless of course you are high level.)

Moral of the story: con everything and read the words as well as look at the
colors.

Snyak.
