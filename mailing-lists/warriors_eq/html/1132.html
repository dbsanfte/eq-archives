With my warrior and some friends i attempted to solo a blue sand giant. I
had a cleric of the same level with me and a magician as well ( all level
37.) SO i begin the fight and it starts going bad right away. There i am
hitting him and seeing his Hp bar drain waaaay slow. I figure i can stand
here and take his punishment maybe 1 minute longer. So my friends step in
and heal me up and we take it together quite easily.

Ok Then the cleric acts all tough and decides he wants some 1 on 1 action
with a giant for kicks. Ok we say. I stand back with the magician as the
cleric proceeds to root / wrath and hit the giant to death. He finished off

thing.

There you have it. A cleric at level 37 takes on the giant, a warrior of
same level WITH buffs cannot. Not much else can be said. Oh sure i know its

defense.

even to me with my cleric.
