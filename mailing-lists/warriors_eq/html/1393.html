Level 8-12,grouped with magic weapons.

I have not done this with a warrior, but I see no reason why it could not be
done. If you can get some sort of weapon that does magic damage you can
start taking out wisps around level 8 if you have a partner. I fight these
in the commonlands. If you can't afford a weapon that does magic damage,
find a magician and pay him to conjure up a dagger for you. I took my necro
and a friends mage at level 8 and we have been killing wisps as our primary
staple. They usually drop at least a gold, they also drop lightstones(8
gold) and greater lightstones(3.6). As level 8 characters we pulled at
least 40 plat in five ours of play.

Level 12-?, grouped
In Southern Ro there are a few Derv camps. There's also some in North Ro
and West commonlands. Dervs range from about level 9 to 14, so you will
want to be grouped. They drop some change around maybe a gold or so. They
also drop rings pretty often which can be sold for about two gold a piece.
75% when they have weapons they are bronze weapons which sell for 2-4 plat
each.

Level 12 solo
There are some Lizardman warriors and mystics which spawn outside of the
Temple of Cazic Thule. The carry some loose change. Their tails sell for 4
gold, I believe. They also drop bronze weapons and mystic dolls.
