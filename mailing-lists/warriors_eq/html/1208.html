Subject: Possible changes to the Warrior class I'd like to see considered..


1) Increase of max skill per level from (5xlvl)+5 to (6xlvl)+5
This would give a Warrior a 1 point advantage in weapon skills at level 1,
scaling
up to a 40 point advantage in weapons skills at level 40 when compared to
the
hybrid classes. Not a lot, but enough to make a decent difference.
If the current skill caps remain the same (level 40) then a Warrior would
cap at 245, compared to a Hybrid at 205.

2) If the above (1) is not acceptable, then increase the skill cap for a
Warriors
weapon skills to level 50. This would do nothing for the Warrior at levels
lower
than 50, so I'm not really to enthusiastic abot this idea, but hey, better
than nothing.
This would have hybrids capping at level 40 with a 205 skill, and Warriors
at level
50 with a 255. But up to level 41 they would be equal as they are now. I
realize
Minks are the masters at hand to hand... but not with 2 Handed Swords... in
the
above two items, I'm only talking about the weapon skills, and not Hand to
Hand,
Offense, Defense, Parry, Dodge, etc...

3) Start Warriors with 'Bind Wound' at a default level of 50, that can not
be increased
untill attaining level 10. Similar to the way the racial special abilities
work.

4) If the above (3) is not acceptable, then raise the bind wound limit from
50% hits to
75% hits or more. This would make the skill more usefull, and make the
bandages
worth the over 7sp they cost. Though this would not aid Warrior's themselves
in any
way as a class, it may make the skill usefull enough for more Warrior's to
adopt.

5) Weapon Specialization. Done like the magic users specialization skills.
You would
have one specialization skill for each class of weapon (1HS/2HS/1HB, etc..),
and it
would decrease the delay of the weapon, not by a lot, maybe 5-10 points at
most.
However, the first skill to get to 51 (see where this is looking like the
mage skill?) would
then become your "Preferred Weapon" and attacks with that weapon would cause
more
damage. Again, nothing unbalancing, maybe 10-20 points at most. Some may
think these
numbers are high, but remember that if this was introduced, it would
probably be a 30th +
level skill, and these numbers are the max on a scale.

6) Thinking about something I heard earlier about Warriors having problems
catching fleeing
foes, while a mage can Root and finish it off... How about a game-wide
(*all* classes) skill
of Sprint? It would drain stamina pretty quickly, so it would not be a
prolonged thing. You
could base speed on the Dex stat (which would be good for Monks and Rogues)
or have it
a set speed for all. The better plan I think though would be like
swimming... the higher the
skill, the faster the sprint, up to a maximum velocity of approximately 3/4
that of a Spirit of
Wolf endowed character. This would serve to purposes... to catch fleeing
opponents, and to
give yourself a little adrenalin induced start when forced to flee yourself.

Just some thoughts... If anyone has any input, I'd like to hear it...
