Your skills increase each time you use them no matter what the level of your
opponent.

You can stand over a spawn spot and swing away at rats and snakes for hours
to build a new wep skill.

Experience points are tied the level of the opponent though. But they are
tied to other factors as well. I can swear that I have killed stuff that
conned green (lower than blue) and have gotten xp from it. Why? I have no
clue - maybe because you get a quota for each kind of mob in a zone? Or
something. I'll max out on killing young lions which are/were blue and then
have to slay a pesky bear which is green and get xp off the bear but not the
young lion, go figure.

As a rule, when I solo, I try not to attack yellows ('...quite a gamble...')
or whites ('...looks like an even fight...') and of course not reds. I'd
rather take more easy kills than suffer the heal time between long melee's.
But I wonder if the gain in xp is worth it?
