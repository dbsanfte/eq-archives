I can feel the pain that both of you are having. The group of five that I play with
just came from CB after killing everything in there but Dvinn many, many times. My
experiences were as follows:

1.) High levels constantly camped the eqipment mobs. We were there for a reason, to
get our Cleric ONE screaming mace (needs two items). In order to do that we had to
log on at odd times just to get the two items we needed. At times there were
multiple groups from the SAME guild, camping the mobs nearly a weekend at a time. It
took the better part of three weeks to get TWO items.

2.) Most all of the solo high level campers were spellcasters. I can recall 2
wizzies, 2 necros, and a mage. All level 20+ since they had 2 names but other than
that, they were anon so we could not determine level. Why were they there? I have no
idea. They all played solo, and they all dealt more damage than any grouped party
without a spellcaster, thus winning the kill.

3.) At the time we had a group of 5 characters: a lvl 15 pally, a lvl 15 warrior, a
lvl 14 warrior, a lvl 15 thief, a lvl 14 cleric. While we wre there, there the
levels even went up to further add to our damage dealing power. Despite this, at no
time were we able to get a kill when ANY other party contained a wizard. Even if
they were solo wizards only one or two levels above us. Typical scenario ... we have
the spawn, we attack and damage the mob to about 3/4 or 1/2 damage when *fzzzzt* a
spellcaster nukes the mob, trains it to him and then finishes with a few more
spells. We would always chase,but we could never get the kill when this happened.

4.) A solo spellcaster can fry a mob in about 30 to 45 seconds with 5 or 6 strong
spells, followed by about a 5 minute 'meditation enhanced' downtime. A solo warrior
can kill a mob in about 1 to 3 minutes with many, many hits, followed by up to 15
minutes of pure butt sitting. Anyone else think this is slightly unfair for
gameplay?

We had kills stolen many many times and nothing frustrates you more than bleeding
from a mob that is stolen from you in mid fight. We tried getting spellcasters into
our group, but at our level, most were doing better playing solo. From all of this I
gathered that two things need to be done.

One, if Verant wants to give groups a better advantage than solos, then by all means
allow group damage to overcome the 'single greatest damage dealer gets the kill'
rule. Or bump it up somehow. It doesn't have to be much but for god sakes ... people
having fun as a team should be rewarded for their organization and coordination. It
requires effort and patience to play well as a team. It requires nearly nothing to
play well alone.

Two, wizzies need to be toned down on solo just a little. I have nothing against
powerful wizards. I have EVERYTHING against solos who steal other people's kills in
mid battle. Or even if they are grouped for that matter.

Can anyone think of a GOOD way to prevent these type of activities? I cannot. We've
discussed it in our group many times but could not come up with much. Any input on
this post, or the two previous, would be great to hear. Maybe it will cheer me up
some.

-Rosmorte
