To start a guild is a long process...

1. get 9 people recruited into your guild
2. get all 9 to send an email with the guild name as the subject, and their
login names, char names in the body
3. send an email to verant yourself wil the same info as well as the guild
Charter.
4 wait for 2-4 weeks for verant to get back to you.
5 if they accept your guild then you will have to contact a Gm at a certain
time. then they will give you as the leader the ability to add people into
your guild.

thats the basics.
