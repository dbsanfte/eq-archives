A solution to camping shouldn't be to complicated.
A high level magic weapon spawning on a unique NPC cannot spawn if the item
exists in the "spawn area" AND the item must be initiated by a quest.
Unique NPCs should also spawn in a random location in the zone.

Farming is the main problem....I think. I have no respect for farmers. If
they won't take turns on rare spawn item I will kill-steal it. I am a
dark-elf after all. I don't necessarily like doing this, but when a group
has been farming a spot for days and refuses to share the spawns there isn't
really any option. So when the creature spawns....my group attacks it. We
try to get it first and then accuse them of kill-stealing...which is true
depending on your perspective.

I just think that farmers ruin the spirit of the game. I never camp a rare
spawn item for more than an hour or 2 spawns whichever comes first. I
respect those who camp a spot so they can have that awesome weapon they
really want but can't afford....but I depise those who camp a spot so they
can sell the items. It just isn't in the spirit of the game.

Just my opinion. I know some of you won't approve of my method of dealing
with farmers, and you are entitled to that.
