For all of you interested in the math, the lack of importance between levels
in groups for high level characters makes sense:
Take the 2 cases mentioned, the first with a lvl 20 and a lvl 24.
The lower lvl char is getting 20/44 = 45.5% of the exp, the higher char
24/44 = 54.5%.
In the second case, a lvl 6 with a lvl 10 we get:
6/16 = 37.5% for the lower lvl char and
10/16 = 62.5% for the higher lvl char
So if you look at these numers it makes a LOT of sense that the difference
in levels isn't as significant as you get into the mid 20's and higher.
