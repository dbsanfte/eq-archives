Hail sister!

After weighing the options of many of the magical weapons out there and the
cost/return value of each, I decided to spend the money on two combine
longswords right now (I am level 18). A surprise benefit I found last
night: I can slam creatures that are immune to normal weapons. I couldn't
do this before when wielding only one combine sword (and I don't have any
magical bracers, wristbands, or gloves). I notice every so often I will hit
for 14 as well (combines are 6*2 + 1 supposedly, but I do get an extra now
and then). The biggest reason I decided to purchase is combine longswords
is the return value. I paid 54.3 plat for each of them, and I can come
close to getting that back by selling to another player. No other magical
weapon has a higher return (unless you get them yourself).
