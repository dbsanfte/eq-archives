Good call, fletching while sitting waiting on HP regen
is an *excellent* time to fletch.

I wouldn't make a bow, unless you're going to fletch
your skill up into the 70s-80s; the bow you can make
at sub40s would be a Hickory bow, and it has *nothing*
on the Elm bows at the next level. It's very
inexpensive to buy an Elm bow from another fletcher,
should cost you 5-6pp. Pick Hemp string for more
damage, Silk string for more speed (lower delay);
efficiency ratios for either work out the same.

The best way I found to work archery up is to hang out
where other groups are fighting mobs that're blue+ to
you; and shoot into their fights. I've never had
anyone mind; there's no way an archer will killsteal
like this, and it helps make the fight easier for the
group tanking it.

Pulling is much easier with a bow, let me tell you.

Good luck!
