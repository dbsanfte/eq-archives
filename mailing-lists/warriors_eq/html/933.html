Killing something 4 levels above you is not hard. Any good group can do it.
Also, now that I'm 22nd level, often the groups I'm in (and if you hit 20
and don't group all the time, you are wasting your time IMHO) vary in level
by more than 4 levels. I've killed giant skels (level 22-25) with a group
all at or below 20. When I got my SBH, no one in the group was above 20 and
the orc that drops it is at least 24th. Brad has always said that twinking
catches up to you, and I believe it. A friend had a 17th level halfling
warrior and decided to pass all his gear on to a wood elf ranger he wanted
to try (full set of ring, mino axe, tentacle whip). He had the hardest time
judging what to kill and he was levelling so fast his skills didn't stay
up.

There a very few items I would pay for that could be found as loot. Find
out where they are located, go there, and get in a group. The only money
I've spent since level 13 when I bought a regular battle axe (at the time I
made 13 very few FS weapons were being found on CT) the only cash I've
spent was about 80pp for most of a set of banded and 6pp for snakeskin
gloves (no matter how often I've been in Paw, I never find snakeskin). Been
kicking a fair amount into guild so I have about 90pp at level 22 (money
really isn't that important to me) and am trying to meet a fletcher I know
who will sell me a shaped ash bow for 60pp. If evil races could fletch easy
I'd be making my own by now, but hey, Verant all plays good races (in beta

bronze from some guild mates who have been hunting Najena heavy, or may
