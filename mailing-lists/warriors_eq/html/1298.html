Treynon on EQVaults posting board brought up a list of specialization
suggestions for Warriors:

Monk and Warrior Specialization Skill Version 1.0

Background:

Monk - Human only and honed in the arts of war, this class serves as one of
the few fronts men who war against evil. However, it
comes with a severe monotonous feeling of only using his fist, special
attacks and light armor (not to include carry very little tohelp one?s AC).

Warrior - Almost ever race have warriors in their ranks and these are the
"muscle" of any army. They are weapon masters and
are glorious in their shiny armor and unique weapons. Having the most hit
points (not very impressive considering a high level
wizard can toast a high level warrior quite easily) and best damage when
using a weapon of their choice.

Nature of Specialization:

Hybrids vs Pure Tanks - It comes to many high level characters that the
hybrids overpower the pure tanks. This is not to say that
most warriors and monks have completely no skill whatsoever but it is just a
fact.

Casters vs Pure Tanks - In the world of LAG, tanks have a really hard time
chasing casters to take them down. Some of this new
skills will help ease this conflict as to disallow utter bullying of the
tank class.

Specialization Skills (5 total, same as those with casters):

Rules - All skill can reach a max of 50 but then the warrior or monk has to
decide which he or she wants to go with for the rest of
the character's development.

Critical Hit - This skill allows warriors and monks a chance to inflict more
damage then usual in a hit (double damage). The
probability of this happening is determined by the specialization skill. At
51 points, the chances are 5% and increases every by 1%
every 10 points to a max of 90% (for future expansion levels).

Enhanced Attack - This skill increases the attack ratings and speed of
either a monk or a warrior allowing for better hits. For
every 2 points of increase the attack rating increases by 1, and for every
50 points of increase the delay of their attack reduces by
1. At 51, the attack delay is reduced by 1 and attack rating increases by25.
Enhanced Defense - This skill increases the defense rating (AC) by 1 point
every 1 point increase in this specialization skill. At 51,
the AC increases by 50.

Enhanced Resistance - This skill increases the all the resistance of a
warrior or monk by 1 point every 10 point increase in this
specialization skill. This helps especially when he or she is destined to go
against magic, poison or disease attacks. At 51, allresistance increases by
5.
Enhanced Recovery - This skill increases the rate of health recovery, along
side with the original recovery of that race. For every
10 points increase in this skill, the health regenerates an additional
point. However, you have to be sitting down to have this
regeneration to be effective. At 51, a warrior or monk can regenerate and an
additional 5 points.
