Yes, 3 tanks is nice. But when you get into higher level mobs, you HAVE to
have nukers of comparable levels. Otherwise even getting healed and
whacking away like mad on the mobs will still not bring you close enough to
winning the battle. Normally this is because other mobs join the fray, and
you have to take the mob out fast, and move on to the next. You can take it
down semi quick with 3 tanks, but then the creature runs, and you have to
chase it. If you dont he grabs 3 friends and now your hosed.

2 tank (warrior/hybrid - at least one warrior.)
2 healer (cleric/shaman/druid at least one cleric.)
2 nuker (any 2 nukers will do... but nice to have a necro/wiz combination.)

Only way to have it.

I have had the opportunity to operate in this type of group. We have been
able to take down things that would have detroyed groups 2 levels higher
than we were. Why? Because of teamwork and knowing the roles that each
play in the group. Never had a better feeling than taking out a zealot, 3
fans, a couple heralds and a few defenders/warders all at once. Then
immediately following that up with a stone golemn. Was a great night, we
worked well together. Just my 2 copper.
