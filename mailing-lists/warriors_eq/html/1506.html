Subject: best grouping


In your experience what has been the best group make-up that you have been a
part of? If possible give a little input on why it was the best group
make-up. I'm concerned here with character classes, not level. Interested
in group sizes anywhere between 2 and 6. Of particular interest to me is
what it's like to group with an enchanter.

I've mostly played in very small groups. I usually play with one other
person in my group. A cleric was nice to group with in the pre-teens since
he did comparable melee damage and could keep me healed up. In the teens I
began grouping with a magician. He used a water elemental for a pet which
is supposed to be about the equivalent of an equal level troll warrior. I
would let the elemental tank for me and I would stand at that perfect
distance where the mob attacks the elemental and not me, yet I could still
get my hits in. Since the elemental regains HP at about 10 points per round
as opposed to my two it meant less down time. At level 12 the magician had
a DD spell that did 90+ damage, very nice when it was needed to finish
something off quick. He could also summon bandages so I get healed up to
50% for free. As a two man team the magician was much better than the
cleric because we could dish out much more damage in less time, and with the
pet taking much of the damage healing was not really necessary. As an added
bonus the pet could play the part of sacrificial lamb while we zoned when
things got nasty.
