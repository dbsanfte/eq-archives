When your group gets into a fight with multiple opponents - some who con
higher than you'd normally try solo - you need to concentrate your fire
power. Your party needs to all gang up on a selected bad guy and kill him
first. The trouble is coordinating - especially when they move around and
have the same names. You cant tell by looking which "a bandit" is the one
you have targeted and which one the tank has selected. Especially after the
first one is killed and the tank selects the next one.

So, what you do is target your tank like you are gonna issue an invite and
then use the /assist command. That will automatically change your target to
the same target the tank has selected. Now attack and you are assured that
you and the tank are hitting the same target.

Make sense?
