What we need is a reason to congregate in a tavern and tell stories that
would also benifit out character. I suggest adding Gambling. Think about it,
gambling has been around for centuries in one form or another. Players could
gather around an NPC to play against the house or Players could carry a (EQ
theme) Deck or cup of dice to play against other chars at any time or place.
They already have something that is close to what would be needed and that
is the trade screen. Players could place the bets in a slot, click ok, and
then the dice would throw or a card is drawn and whoever wins would
automaticaly win the bet. If they wanted to take it further, they could
implement gambling as a skill, making the chances greater for the higher
skill ya have(not sure about all the implications there though)
I have been tossing around this idead since beta and I think it would be
great to draw us together for drinks(if you are drunk maybe gambling skill
is affected some way) and get us telling stories and maybe even make (or
lose) a few plat.
What do ya think?
