Bronze armor is good amost pieces are close if not the same to steel plate,
tho much much heavier. Small bronze plate is readily available from many
different places. Medium bronze is rare, and I have no idea where to get
it. Large sized armor is only found in one place that I know of. Not all
pieces of bronze armor are worth obtaining due to the weight. So when you
decide to try to get this type of armor, be aware of the other various types
that are close or provide buffs in lew of bronze plate. Some pieces may
nopt be as high ac, but will be better for you die to weight/buff
advantages.
