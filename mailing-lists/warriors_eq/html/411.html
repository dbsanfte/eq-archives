If you play out of Qeynos, you can talk to Capt Tillian of the city guards
(on the top floor of Grounds of Fate) and he will tell you that he hate
gnolls and you should bring him gnoll fangs which you can get off gnolls in
BlackBurrow. When you give him the fangs (one by one or one each of the
give slots ie: not stacked) he gives you xp and moonstones. You can sell
the moonstones or take them to the bartender in the back room of Fish's Ale
and give to him in exchange for BB Stout (an illegal gnoll brew.) Then sell
the BB Stouts for money - I usually drink one for the heck of it or
sometimes carry some back to BB incase I need to offer someone a reward for
helping me or whatever. It's not that far out of your way when you make a
bank run to hit the bakery and fish's ale first.

So, I do this as often as I can and one day the bar keep says" "oh oh
running low... can you take this note to the brewmaster and he will give you
some more BB Stout to bring back..." So, I take the note and srcibble down
the name of the brewmaster: Gnasher Furrgut. Ok, turns out that another
quest has the head of Gnasher as a quest item and most monks are out there
trying to slay Gnasher. Finally, I come up from BB after some hard fighting
and see Gnasher running past so I run after him and get close enough to hail
him and he stops and talks. I give him the note and sure enough he gives me
some real big bottles of BB Stout.

Cool I think now what? Hmm I guess I shall kill him and get his head too.
So I unsheath my trusty short sword and give him a few whacks which gets him
pretty mad. I glance over to my hp and ruh roh! Still below 50% from the
earlier fights and Gnasher does me in and I drop a level! Dang! I owe
Gnasher one now!!

So are quests worth it? This one gives you xp, cash and some depth of
character (ie makes the game a bit more interesting) beyond bashing gnolls
all day long. So far it's the best one I have found and you can do it while
you are doing your normal power leveling thing.
