First, from some comments you made I can see you're
another player who doesn't understand the Everquest
armor system.

AC IS NOT EVERYTHING! AC is the second half, and the
lesser half, of the armor system in Everquest. The
first half is damage absorption. The armor sequence
is:

Naked > Cloth > Patchwork > Leather > Ringmail >
Chainmail > Banded > Plate

Each level of armor absorbs more damage than the
levels before it. If you doubt, try it. Fight
something on your level naked and see how badly you
get hit; every shot will come in at max damage. Put
your armor back on and see shots hitting for 2, 5,
etc...

Look at Rangers, who can't wear better than banded,
fighting along side a Warrior. Ranger in full banded,
dual wielding; gets hammered and has to be healed much
more often than the dual wielding Warrior in full
plate. Warrior takes less damage per shot. Put the
Warrior in banded and watch him start taking damage
like the Ranger, needing more heals. PLATE IS WORTH
IT.

Plate is a requirement to be an effective tank past
20ish. Now you have to make some concessions for good
buffs (I still wear *my* Spiked Collar for the +3
Strength; but it's just a collar), but you want to be
in mostly plate.
