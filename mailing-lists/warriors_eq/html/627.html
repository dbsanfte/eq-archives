I played a paladin, and they have not got the raw power of the warrior BUT
that LOH was very nice, and I could actually solo stuff a lot more. The
paladin is a good self-sufficient fighter, with the ranger probably an
equal, there lack of spells being made up for by there forage ability (i.e.
self-sufficient).

ALSO that 9 levels behind thing is a misnomer. If a level 20 paladin casts
courage on himself it will be BETTER than a level 15 cleric casting the same
spell. Sure the paladin is casting a level 1 spell, but its cast at a level
20 ability.
