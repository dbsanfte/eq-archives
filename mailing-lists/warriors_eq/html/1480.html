> Monks and Warriors have similar, but not exactly the

> approximately 10-30 points higher on AC for each

> EQ uses these sorts of tiny differentials as the

> advances (3-5 point increase purely on level alone)
> there are easy ways for EQ to 'normalize' the AC vs.

Ok, if AC is all that matters, why do a pair of
characters at equal level and Defensive skills have
such vast differences? One a Ranger in full banded,
the other a Warrior/Paladin/ShadowKnight in full
plate; the Rangers consistently take more damage from
fights than their heavily armored fellow tanks.

Still don't like that test? Try this one: take *your*
WAR/PAL/SDK and get a full suit of plate to match the
full suit of banded you're so proud of. Go fight a
few blue mobs in your banded armor and note the
strength of the blows that connect against you; how
much single shots tend to do, how many low damage
shots you receive, and how much total damage you take
over the fight.

Now put on the plate and try another few fights
against similar mobs (level and race). You should
notice fewer shots dealing maximum damage against you,
more shots doing low damage (2-10 where before it
would have been 40-60 on each connect), and less total
damage at the end of the fight.

You think you've found some secret uber-combo (ala
Tekken or something, up-up-down-left-right left and
right punch together to produce the MegaFireball) or
something; this game has shown no instances of such
imbalances.

Do you truly think they managed to get all the way
through four phases of beta and the past 4-6 months of
final and never notice that plate, as you are
alledging, is useless?

Thinking you've found some hidden truth; that banded
is marginally (5% or less) effective than Plate but
has a fraction of the weight ... folly. Sheer folly.

Plate matters in a fight. I'll take plate armored
tanks over those in banded seven days a week and the
rest of the month too. I have watched the performance
of Rangers (since they *can't* have better than
banded) higher than my level for months now; they're
stuck in banded while I'm in plate, and I take less
damage when tanking. I've watched warriors who aren't
plate armored yet, and I take less damage than they
when tanking. And I only have an 80 AGI, so it's not
that.

But hey, whatever you want to do. If you're in my
group though, I won't appreciate the higher percentage
of the healers' mana you'll be taking to recover from
the greater damage you'll be taking. I suspect they
won't either.
