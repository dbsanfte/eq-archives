I have been in fights lasting almost 10 minutes in length. Wizards and
healers running out of mana, and me being completely out of stamina. I have
something like 97 or 98 for a stamina. I usually run out around the 4 or 5
minute mark. Then everything is just so slow it doesnt matter what happens,
your dead if you try to run. Usually I have 2 Paladins in my group, so I
back out of battle and just sit down for 15 seconds or so. Does alot for
the yellow bar. Then back into the fray.

Stamina is very important to keeping your attacks fast, and your defense up,
and for running in hairy situations. So never try to get so low that you
can barely move. Been there, and died for it. This is why groups are
important, and need to be well balanced. Without being able to take a
break, or have invigor cast on you, your not gonna last the fight out.
