I know how frustrating this is. I was 12 or 13 at the Chessboard in butcherblock
with about 3 other people, one cleric and 2 other hybrids . A 13th druid showed
up and was able to outdamage us and was clearly using us as his tanks while
taking all of the experience. We asked him if he wanted to alternate kills, or a
number of other solutions that would benefit us both. But he clearly wanted to
get every kill he could grab from us, while he took no damage from them. He even
told us he would leave when he reached 14 so he could get his next rank of
spells.

So we decided that the next king would get it's attention and no-one would
attack it, so when he started blasting it would go for him. He tried training it
on us and we just kept fleeing from it so it was focused on him. Then he trained
it on someone on the otherside of the board and stole the kill from them.

If verant is encouraging us to work to gether, the code needs to be changed so
that the experience goes to the Group that does the most damage, not the
individual who does the most. This allowed this druid to steal them from us and
use my party as his shield. If we as a group were the determining factor in who
gets the xp, then he would not have been able to do this. This would also
encourage players to form larger groups to overcome problems like this on our
own and not have to petition them. In this situation we could of outdamage him
and taken the kills that were ours and he would have gotten frustrated and left,
or worked on getting a lerger group on his own that would have forced us to
leave. I'm sure there could be abuses with this rule change, so please let me
know what they are.
