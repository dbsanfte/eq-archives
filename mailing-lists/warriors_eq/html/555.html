Subject: Now about 1hs vs 2hs Re: Rangers and warriors


You make some good points. I will admit that when I started I was focused
on dual wield...I realized my mistake at 12th level but by that time it was
to late to work on my 2 handed slashing skill. My ranger alternates between
a fine steel 2 handed sword and a mino axe/shiny brass shield combo. I am
keeping both skills maxxed at every level.

My opinion on the 1hs vs 2hs is this. Warriors need both. For blue mobs
the only way to fly is 2 handed with double attack at higher levels. You
hit very often for high damage, and with double attack you are a devastating
hack'n'slash machine. For higher level mobs that con yellow or red, the 1
handed weapons shine because of their speed. You are not going to hit high
level mobs as much, so having 50% more swings gives you a better chance to
do damage, even if you do less damage...some is better than none.

I am interested to hear others opinions on this. And what do you think of
trying to get my 2hs skill of 14 up to 80 on my 15th level DE warrior?
Worth working on? Or will I have a hard time increasing skill?
