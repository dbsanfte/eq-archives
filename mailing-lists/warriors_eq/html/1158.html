Mages have a limit at what they can do though. Sure they can hit with a
spell for 1200 damage, but they have mana that will eventually run out. By
what you are suggesting, a warrior could keep swinging and swinging for
1200 damage per attack without losing that ability after prolonged use (did
that make sense)? The critical strike is probably a good solution, as well
as the specialization skill - but in no way should a warrior be able to do
as much damage as a caster.
