BB is a mad house sometimes.

The situation you may want to watch out for is "helping" someone kill a mob
when the person you are trying to "help" wants to do it solo for the xp. If
you join a fight in progress, you may inflict more damage and get the kill
and thus the other guy does not get the xp from the fight.

What I do is target the mob and then target the other guy. Assess if he
needs help by looking at the difference in hp - this may be the 2nd or 3rd
mob he has been fighting and even though it's a scrawny, he maybe low on hp
and would like some help.

BB is where some people go for loot - others go there for the high MD.
There are usually lots of kills laying around free for anyone to loot. And
even some dead guards and elites just laying there waiting for the looters
time to expire.

If someone gets so bad with insults, I just go: "don't make me /ignore you!"
