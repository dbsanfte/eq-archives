> In west karanas, there are usually 2 at the bottom of the hill where they
> camp (north side of the zone.) Sometimes one will be a spellcaster. We
> gang up to nail those. If we go after the whole camp, we have one or two
> groups and gang them.
>
> There are 2 schools of thought:
>
> Kill the weakest one first to reduce the numbers.
>
> Kill the strongest one first to reduce their power.
>
> In any case, you want to use good group tactics. Select a "tank" and all of
> the party target the tank. Have the tank engage the bandit first and let
> him get some hits in. Then use /assist command to make sure everyone
> concentrates on the same target the tank has selected. When you have a
> group fighting another group, the /assist command is very helpful to insure
> that you all are selecting the same target and concentrating your powers.
