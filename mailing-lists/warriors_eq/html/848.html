--- Jim Peterson <jim@...> wrote:
> Then beat us to it. Geez, what do you expect? What

This just leads to more in-game crap. This kind of
thing almost makes me wish +PVP was the required
setting (almost, but not quite; because reality in
other games proves people will just use PC Killing as
a source of amusement and bullying, and not a serious
in-game mechanism).

I have no problem with someone camping to obtain an
item. Key word here, *one*. They show up, wait their
turn, get their item, move on. In this fashion, it's
like an amusement park ride, everyone takes a turn.

Camping to get multiples is just lame. Very, very,
very, very lame. It takes away the fun in a very
serious way. I can't compete for the commonly
mentioned items because the hard-core 'see if I let
you get one before I and my friends kill-steal it away
from you while we laugh' groups are determined to set
up and hold the spot.

I'm probably going to have to pay plat for items I'd
like my character to have, which really takes a lot of
the fun out of it. A lot of the fun. It would be
*fun* to go adventuring, fight through the dungeon,
find the spot/npc everyone rumors as the source of a
mighty item, and then defeat him/her/it in battle for
a great reward. That would be *fun*. But strolling
through the dungeon that people are keeping cleared,
finding a huge crowd anxiously waiting for the npc to
arrive; that's *not fun*. Especially when they're
rude and irritated that I dare have the nerve to want
to come and get an item for myself, which is what the
response almost always is.

Worse, it's certainly not fun to buy it. I probably
will, but it's not going to be the same at all. Not
at all.

One of two things needs to happen; Uber monster spawns
need to be instituted (like SGs in NRo and Oasis, or
the Mino Hero in Steamfont) that will make farmers pay
with XP and blood for their patches, or hard coded
'lore' item limits. And if this makes it hard to get
a paired set of PG Tomahawks, tough. We tried it the
nice way, and it's not working; so you'll just have to
use one PGT and one of something else.
