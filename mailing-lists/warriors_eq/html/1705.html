Ok I have a couple of insights into this from a high level warrior perspective:.

1) My warrior at 54 still tanks very well with the 58/59 Pallies & SKs I play with.  The SK can still outagro me but thats always been true.  Its not even close how much better I can take damage when I can actually draw agro.
2) They didn't eliminate the the proc agro effect...they reduced it (right in patch message). This impacted ALL classes & not just warriors. To be honest in 5 hours of Howling Stones last night Ididn't notice the difference (i.e. with my Bloodpoint & werewolf form I still saw plenty of agro).
3) Warriors were only "useless" the first 6 months of the games existence...I know I experienced it first hand (same armor, weapons & hps as everyone else with zero distinguishing skills) .  These days with decent warr specific armor & weapons we tank very well at most levels.
4) The idea that warriors don't contribute significant offensive damage is an old idea.  With the uber hps high level MOBs the damage we contribute over time is quite significant.
5) Raising the defensive caps for the other classes actually makes them stronger in the groups I play in.  That means my groups survive more. I kind of like that.
6) The mid 30s-40s is a tough spot for warriors.  Mostly because the other tank classes (Rangers/SKs in particular) have similar agro capability to ourselves at this point but little experience in the value of their role in a group.  By the mid 40s very few Rangers, SKs & Pallies are actively trying wrest agro away from a warrior...at least if they have any brains.
