Subject: making hotkeys


You can make a hotkey out of just about anything.

You can not make a hot key out of a weapon, but you can make a hot key out
of a backpack slot. You must then, always keep your weapons in this
specific slot. I had my first experiment with this using a bard character a
few days back. hotkeyed a backpack slot and my Melee1 and Melee2 slots.
It's works pretty well. Sometimes looting can cause a minor problem though.
Most of the time it's OK though. These don't act like regular hot keys,
where you can just click 3 or 4 to make them work. They act like inventory
slots. You click on a weapon to pick it up, and then drop it in one of your
other "Hot Slots".

Hope this helps. If someone has an even better way that I overlooked I'm
all ears.
