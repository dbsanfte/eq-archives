Rattlesnakes bite with weak poison so you shouldn't have a
problem with these. Asps and Darkweed snakes are a
different matter all together. They have a higher poison and if
you get down in hit points while poisoned, you will probably
die before the time the poison wears off. I suggest not to
fight the Asps and Darkweed snakes unless there is a
cleric available to heal you. The poison from the darkweed
snake took my ogre from 200 hp to death before it wore off at
level 8.
