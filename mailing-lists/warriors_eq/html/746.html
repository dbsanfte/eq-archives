They seem to have made the Tentacle Terrors way more aggressive and stronger
as well. I am level 21 and they are blue to white to me. My friend, a
level 30 ranger was helping me raise my 2handed skill (I just got a BBC)
with the TT. He would draw them out (they are green to him) and take all
the blows. They would consistently reduce him to one bubble and myself to
half my hit points.

The day before they were a piece of cake, now there killing machines.

They seem to have changed the linking also with the bonecracker. It used to
be you could pull the BC and the two necros would not come (as long as they
were green to the puller). Now they all come no matter what.

AND finally blind spell pulling no longer works either. If you target a
mob, then run to another room (i.e. the mob cant see you) you could cast a
spell and the mob would come running. This no longer works.

Just thought I would share that with you all.
