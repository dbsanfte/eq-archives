Great Divide, and go to Tizmaks or Kodiak caves, or if you have a caster partner, 'Clad, Kitty Ridge (the ledge overlooking the sea, , near the Tower, where almost nothing wanders and you can safely pull snow cougars). CC is an idea too, again with a caster partner. You can ALMOST solo it at that level, if you have regen, SOW, and a good bow, and the exp is very nice, I soloed orcs at the top at 36 and 2, 3 of them were worth a blue. If you have a GOOD connection and a good party, you can try the orc fort, but if your connection is less than perfect you'll get some nasty lag, and if your puller is less than perfect you'll get some nasty trains. Let a caster pull here with snare.
