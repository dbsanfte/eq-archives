Well, in my journey to Guk and when i managed to kill the giant heart
spider, i was level 12 with my cleric and was with 5 other level 15
chars...so it would be a really big risk for you to try with out 2 more in
your party, they range from level 18-21 i think...they poison as well and
its a nasty dose of venom...so be sure to have soem form of healer with you.

Dont forget you wont be figting the Giant heart spder alone...itll be with
one more small heart spiider and in the adjacent rooms there are 7 more
spiders, dont worry they are too far away to swarm, just dont try to
run....make sure you can take out the giant first. Oh, the spiders dont
Attack on sight either...but will if you are low in Hp as will most
creatures.
