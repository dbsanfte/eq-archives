sean, yes you are in the right spot for killing the spiders.....

be warned i was in a group of 6 level 15 chars...and we had a tougher time
than one would think. First off you have to kill the two spiders you see
first, yes ther eis two you just cant see the second one untill you walk
in....drawe them out to where the croc is....so no other spiders jump in on
you . when you dipsose of them, go to where they were and hang a right.
youll see a larger heart spider and a normal one. Gotta kill them both as
well. The larger doesnt give you the sheild tho, you have to sit and wait
for the next respawn which will be 2 normal hearts. then kill them, then sit
there and wait again. THE NEXT spawn will be the greater and a normal heart
spider, watch out the poison from the greater consues around 250 hp
roughly....so make sure you got some kind of healer....once you kill it,
whoever loots the body will get the shield itself.

Once more the spawninggoes like this.
Larger/normal........./normal/normal..............greater/normal...........n
ormal/normal..........repeat.

hope that helps.

Oh the Giant is about level 18-21 and normal heart spiders can be from 11 to
15.
