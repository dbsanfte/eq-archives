Ok one horror story and one honor story. First the bad news:

There we were... camping the Undead ruin in East Commons - why we have no
clue (no loot and no xp either.) Then the 2 ghouls spawn so we ooc for some
help and a dwarvin pallidin or maybe cleric joins the party. We melee the
ghouls and in the midst of combat this pallidin/cleric loots the first
ghoul. We kill the second one and the cleric immediately says "got to go"
and disbands. Not 5 minutes later he is auctioning off a bronze sword. I
give him a tell and he confirms it was off the ghoul. Talk about an a-hole.
His name is "gander" and it was on the Innoruuk server. If a hill giant is
ever chasing you, be sure to run right up to 'gander' and ask for a heal.

Now the good part:

There we were... formed a party and we head off to camp the dervs in North
Ro. When we get the camp by the mountains, we see another group working it.
So, we ask them if we can have a turn and wait for our turn. Things are
working good each crew taking turns and actually glad for the rest and heal
time between turns. So then the other group leaves for food and drink and
to sell stuff. Turns out there was another guy hanging out there and he is
like a 15 or so cleric (we are 10's and 12's warrior, bard and pallie.) He
asks us if he can help by lulling so we can pull dervs out one by one. Wow!
Sure thing! So he does and casts heals and such as we need it. We take
some turns with him but mostly he says he just wants to help us. So this
goes on for long enough for us to fill loots 2 times and run to sell and
come back.

Finally he says he has to go help his guild mate and we work the camp for a
while. Eventually, we get a 3 spawn and they all con yellow to our highest
party member. So we give our friend a tell and he is kind enough to come
back and help us!

So for all the bad times you have in Norrath - hang in there because you are
bound to hit some good times as well! This guy's name was Badin and he is a
member of the Legends guild. And he truly makes them Legendary!
