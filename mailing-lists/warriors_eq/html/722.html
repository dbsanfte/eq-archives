You bring up a valid point. But the reason for random spawn is to make the
game more enjoyable to people as a whole. Also, the spawn would have to be
completely random in time. I Just think the spawn should be on maybe 3 or 4
different types of mobs, all around the same level, all spawning in 3 or 4
different zones, and all spawning at different random intervals. This
increases the chances the items will be around, and makes it a little more
difficult to camp it. Also, there should be more of a chance that the item
is not actually on the mob since there will be more appearing more often.

For instance, you want a rubicite breastplate. For those of us who know
what and where it comes from, its pretty easy to wait for the spawn to
appear. Then get together poeple and whack it. (dont get me wrong, its not
an easy task to kill... just to wait for it.) On the other hand, if that
same creature spawned in 3 or 4 different zones, and only spawned at random
intervals... The campers would either have to spend obscene amounts of time
sitting on one area hopping to get lucky. Or they would just have to travel
back and forth to the 3 or 4 different zones to get their kill. This also
increases the chance that the spawn will appear when they just left the zone
they were waiting in and give someone else a chance at it.
