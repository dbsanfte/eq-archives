I gotta disagree. Remember, the problem with camping is the 'farmers'. If
you rnot sure that Tommy's gonna show up anytime soon, chances are you ar
egoing to get bored and move on. By randomizing the loot, you actually
remove the desire to just sit and wait. Of course, if you want to sit for 4
days waiting for that one time the Tommy shows up, be my guest, but I would
call that a bit obsessive-compulsive .

What randomizing loot will do is make it unproductive to try and go and get
such and such item. The game becomes less of a pursuit of gaining items,
and more of a pursuit of gaining adventures.
