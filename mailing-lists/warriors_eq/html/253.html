According to the folks at EQ, the higher levels get more of the XP, this is
to encourage people to stay with their same level and to dissuade "tanks" in
the traditional style (i.e. the 31st level warrior standing there taking all
the hits while the 5th level warrior hits for all the XP.) According to
them, the gap between levels gets longer the higher level you are, so that a
25th and a 32nd level can get about the same xp, but between an 11th and a
5thm the 11th would get ALL of the xp. That's the scheme as far as I know
it.
