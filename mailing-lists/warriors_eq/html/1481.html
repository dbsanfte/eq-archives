Ok... have you actually looked at the AC advantage that Plate provides
over Banded? Have you actually _asked_ a Ranger in full Banded his AC and
then compared it to yours (assuming in full Plate)?

Here's the numbers. Armor sets add the following amount to your 'base' AC
from level/agility/defense skill. (Note that there are some modifying
effects. e.g., Armor provides more AC for characters with high (90+)
Agility.)

Full Plate: 110 AC
Full Banded: 77 AC

AC differential: 33 AC (not including modifying effects)

That is where the difference in damage arises, not from the type of armor.

If you were to replace all of these Plate pieces with Magical Leather
Armor that totalled up to 110AC you would receive the _exact_ same
protection as if you had Plate on (level and skill being equal).
