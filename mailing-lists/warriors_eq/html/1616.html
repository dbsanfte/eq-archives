Hmmm... Areas to hunt... Well... depends on your level of course. And
whether or not your looking for some specific kinds of loot. But my
favorite place of all time from all my levels was Cazic Thule. Nothing like
pulling from the maze to your waiting group for the slaughter. XP was very
good, and the downtown was non existent providing you had a well rounded
group with casters that know what they are doing. You could non-stop pull 2
to 4 bluw/white/yellow out of the maze and just haul in the loot/xp. Its
also imho one of the best zones to hunt in cause its outdoors which allows
SoW to be used.

So if I had to put together a where to hunt for what levels list it would
look something like this.

1-5 Definitely Everfrost Peaks. The loot for low levels is the best in the
game. Plus there is absolutely NO shortage of things to kill.
6 to 12 Misty Thicket. Good xp, relatively safe, lots to kill. I wasn't
that fond of the loot tho.
13 to 15 Beholders Maze. Good xp, good loot. Downside is its very
dangerous.
16 to 22 Unrest. Great loot, good xp. Bad side again is trains, and very
dangerous.
22 to 35 definitely Cazic Thule. Cant beat the xp or the loot of this place
for this level.
36 and up. This is a toss up. I think I preferred solb due to the ease of
escape if you get into trouble. The loot is hands down some of the best in
the game. Xp can be made pretty non stop here as well if you get into the
right groups. My second choice would be lower guk of course. This place is
great loot again, great xp as well. But very dangerous cause your usually
deep in the dungeon when things go bad with no way or chance in hell or
running to a zone.

And since I am probably one of the worlds worse writters...... I might take
