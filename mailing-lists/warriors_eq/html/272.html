*Spoiler* This is not a spoiler on how to get the Executioners Axe,
but the whereabouts of Gnasher.

Gnasher Furrgut (I think thats his last name) is a named gnoll
that hangs out in Qeynos Hills. It is part of a quest that when
completed, you get to watch the Executioner (a barbarian woman
in full platemail with the horned helmet) open a can of whoop-ass
on a tavern barkeep in Qeynos. I actually hit the barkeep a couple
of times while she was fighting him to see if I would get a faction
hit when he died but since she killed him, no faction hit.
