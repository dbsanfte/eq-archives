Gentlemen and ladies,

The final recommendation from warriors_eq@onelist.com (128 members) is two
enhancements for the warrior class:

1) Increase the cap on combat abilities (1H slashing, 2H slashing, dodge,
parry, riposte, dual wield, double attack) from 200 to 255.

2) Allow weapon skill specialization along the same lines as magic users.
This would allow a warrior to specialize in one type of weapon for reduced
delay and increased damage.
