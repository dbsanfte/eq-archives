I completely agree with you. I hate this type of play from people who, by simply
moving on, could make the game a lot better for everyone else.

There are some great ideas in this thread. I have a couple of thoughts on the
subject also:

First, there are currently no level restrictions in this game. Why? Nothing
ticks me off more than to see a low level character running around in full
banded with some awesome weapon ... it practically SCREAMS of twinking. On any
other game you play of this type there is usually a level restriction. Something
like, not being able to hold or use the weapon unless you are that level, or not
being able to save it when you log off or die. Please, please, please .... get
this into Everquest.

Second, how about some sort of flag for an ultra rare item. If you have one ...
you cannot have another (unless you lose/sell the first). It may not fix
everything, but if a small group is camping for objects ... they WILL eventually
have to leave to get rid of them because they wouldn't be able to hold more.
This would limit those people running around and grabbing 5 rare items during
one super farming session ... just to sell them off. It's not so much the
farming that would be helped, it would be the reduction of the lines at the camp
sites.

Just my two cents on the subject...
