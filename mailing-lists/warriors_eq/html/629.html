Bah! All those people who talk about uniqueness have not played high enough
levels to compare.

A warrior IS unique, but it does not kick in until much later.

I have not played until level 50, but I did here Brad quote that a human
warrior (or was it barbarian warrior, not sure) gets something like 3000hp
at level 50. 3000!!! That will be a HELL of a lot more than ANY other
class. Its not just a couple hundred hp, its a lot more.

Basically what makes us unique is we can take punishment like no other
class. We can also dish out continuous damage. but we are NOT the mob
killers, wizards are.

My ONLY whine is the "warriors are the only class that can wear all armour
in the game". This is just dishonest. Certain kinds of magic plate can be
worn by ALL other classes. This I REALLY wish they would change. I think
the only classes that should be able to wear plate are Warriors, Paladins
and SK's. I also think the BEST plate in the game should be ONLY wearable
by Warriors.

Its not like we have a lot of weapon choices, we have 1 ultimate weapon
which is unattainable until atleast level 40 or so, its also an axe. So
atleast give us some warrior only armour.

O.K. ill shutup now.
