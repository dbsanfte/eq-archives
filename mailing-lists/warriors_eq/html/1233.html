Funny to hear that you did not go into BlackBurrow. There is a wide variety
of levels in there but generally its good for 6 - 10's I'd say (your other
stuff implies that you are quite a bit higher than 10 - esp if you are
killing Gryfawns in EK.) The elites (which are pretty hard but under 15 I
think) stay buried pretty deep. And the trains are pretty easy to avoid.
Some groups will make it their business to stop trains. That is they wait
for the train and attack it for the kicks. It's a great place for groups -
even 8, 9, 10's grouped have a great time in there. And it's the first
really good (for that level) place to get loot.

The worst part of BB is that it is buggy if you die in there. I have died
down on the second level (lower) and come back to not be able to find my
corpse. Even the bard says it points to a wall and there is nothing on the
other side of the wall. So, I start asking in /ooc and someone tells me
they see it on the first level (upper.) Sometimes the guy who dies cannot
see his own corpse but the guy standing right next to him can. And then
sometimes it gets overlayed by terrain textures and if you are lucky - there
will be the toe of a boot or something sticking out that you can click on to
loot. Other times you die and your corpse will be on the bottom of the
water - even though you died on land.
