I wouldn't go so far as to say that dual wielding is our Prime skill. I've
got a BBC, 2h slash, that I use when fighting primarily blue critters due to
the massive damage it can inflict. But when fighting red critters, I switch
to my Demon Claw/Barbed Whip dual wield...reds are hard to hit and when you
are wielding a big slow 2 hander you don't to a lot of damage, but dual
wielding against red, they are still hard to hit, but you get a lot more
swings in so you end up doing more damage..... at least this is how it
seems to me.
