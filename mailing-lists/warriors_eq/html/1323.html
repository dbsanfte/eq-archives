Hey all,


Just wanted to share with you a sweet kill me and my friends got last night.
We were in Mistmoore and out came the lord himself. There were 6 of us. A
level 49 cleric. Level 43 warrior (Me) Level 47 Wizard, level 47 Rogue,
level 49 necro and a level 45 Ranger. We decided why the heck not give him a
try even tho he conned red to everyone in the party. Well it was quite the
battle. By far the craziest i have ever seen. In the end only the Necro and
the wizard were left but somehow they managed to pull it off. I tried my
best to stay in melee with him and was doing quite well untill the cleric
had a fizzle. But then it was ouch time. As soon as i went, the Rogue was
next and followed by the Ranger. The cleric somehow managed to stay alive
for a while longer but couldnt handle the beating. When i finally made it
back the necro was at 1 quarter life and the wozard half. Talk about your
kick ass battle, Ill never forget it. When my friend who was just watching
sends me the screens, would any one like them?
