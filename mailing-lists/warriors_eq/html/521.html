To get to see her, someone has to complete a quest: Head of Gnasher Furgutt.
There is a named gnoll that needs to be killed and his head needs to be
given to the Captain of the Guards. He then dispatches the executioner who
walks over to Fish's Ale and kills the barkeep who was Gnasher's partner in
the illegal brewing operations.
