At level 23, my favorite location for both loot and exp was the Ruins of
Cazic in the Ferrott. At your level, you could get groups in "the
courtyard", "maze", "pit", or maybe the "TR1" (short for Throne Room 1). As
you progress in levels, you can move to other sections of Cazic like TR2,
the "Steel Golem pool", and "the temple". I was in Cazic til about level
32-33 having a great time, making good money from Fine Steel drops and plat.
Ravenscale(which is tradeable) drops in several locations.

I'd highly recommend it as a great place to level, accumulate wealth, and
learn the higher level dungeon survival tatics (like dodging trains in the
courtyard =) )

Anyway, good luck with your quest!
