OK look, I am not flaming anyone at all so please don't use that term with
me. I am stating a fact: I don't want to hear someone's opinions on why
warriors suck on a warrior board for them to tell me how great their cleric
is. Who cares that it was a cleric? It could have easily been a Wizard or a
Necro, there's no difference. The point was that this is a warriors board to
help people who want to play warriors, not for people to discuss the
differences between clerics and warriors and decide who is better at what.
Lastly I do want to quote you on something though...
"And believe it or not probably the only few classes you COULD compare would
be
wars vs clerics
wars vs pallys
wars vs SKs
wars vs monks"

Ok time to play a game...anyone ever watch Sesame Street? They have this
game where one thing doesn't belong in the group of other things...like a
screwdriver, a hammer, a saw, and then an apple. Get the point? Monks:
fighter type. Paladin: Fighter type. Shadow Knights: The word Knight speaks
for itself. Cleric: Spellcaster. Clerics were never meant to be fighters,
that is why there is a hybrid cleric/fighter and that is a paladin. Clerics
are priests. Again, this is not a flame. I am happy that you find joy in
playing a cleric, I really truly am. I play a monk, a Magician and a Ranger
all quite often as well. But I don't come on this board and tell everyone
how much more fulfilling it is for me to play my monk rather than my
warrior. I am sorry that I might be taking this so personally, but I can
compare it to you walking into an all african-american convention and
shouting that you hate black people. Imagine the response?

Jynxx
