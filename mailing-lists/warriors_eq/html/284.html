Seeing as how you are a troll (good choice) your hp are gonna go up faster
than all other races which is a bonus cause you want haveto wait around so
long, my advise to you is to kill rats, decaying skels, syoung water
mocasins untill level 4 or so. Then if you can find one other level 4 or 5
char, go for normal froglocks or a weak froglock fisherman....oonce you hit
5 you can probably solo them as well as the occasion swamp alligator, once
on level 6 head to guk but dont go in too deep, stik the the larger froglock
tads and sentinels....good exp for you and the occasional woven/netted
armor, (which sells great to any monks or spell casters. Oh you can get
netted off the small froglock tads as well.) then once you hit 10 id suggest
making your way to the south desert of ro, find a group and kill dercishes
and orcs, at around 13-15 go for the oasis and kill crocs, caimans, and the
occasional Deepwater caiman....just watch out for the giants,
...........after that the rest is up to you.
