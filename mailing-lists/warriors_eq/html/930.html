Yup a bad thing, but you can still find areas to work in. I refuse to camp
for a lore item or whatever, but my friends and I will frequently camp
around a spawn area and Kill Kill Kill.

You can find us loitering around the Drevs in West Commons, the second
pillar in Oasis, or the Orcs in HH. You just have to work out a deal with
the groups already there.

Most groups will share. I find it also handy to give other groups a helping
hand (if they will let me). Gives me practice using my 2H sword, and since
they are blasting away with magic there is no way I will get the experience.
Then the next Orc pops up and my group destroys him and we get the
experience/loot.

Groups that work together have fun and a chance to share the
experience/loot. Groups that spend there time stealing others kills are
probably not happy in the long run and are better off ignored.

Just my lowly opinion.
