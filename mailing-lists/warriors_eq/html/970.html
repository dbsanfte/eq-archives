--- suzkim@... wrote:
> Is there specific items - armor/weapons - that a
> warrior should try to obtain at different levels.

None of these suggestions include fighting against
massive item-farmers (those who camp for long periods
to get multiple drops of a cool item, like Shiny Brass
Shield, etc...).

1st - 5th, work on getting patchwork and the rusty
weapons that correspond to the skills you want to work
up. Most warriors recommend working up both 1h-s and
2h-s; you may want to also work, at your preference,
1h-b, 2h-b and/or pierce.

6th - 10th, work on upgrading to leather/rawhide
armor, with the odd piece that's low-level magical.
By 10th you should be able to have a minotaur axe
(1h-s) and/or a FS weapon(s) for your first two or
three weapon skill preferences.

11th - 15th, start collecting banded and magical
armor, work away from any rusty weapons, and start
working on combine and/or magical weapons in your
prefered slots.

16th - plus, it varies. You'll start working
exclusively with groups, or spending a lot of time
begging for heals. You'll be hanging out in areas
where money and/or valuable or useful item drops start
to happen with greater frequency. By this point you
need at least one magical weapon.

Look over the warrior skill progression and note which
you'll be interested in; decide if you like kick or
bash, if you like dual wield vs 1h/shield vs 2h.
Advance and item yourself accordingly.

Good luck.
