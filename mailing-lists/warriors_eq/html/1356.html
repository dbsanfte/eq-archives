Patty,

When i hit 17 i found myself looking to get cash as well. But i didnt want
to skimp out on exp either. I was sick and tired of befallen so i decided to
go test my luck at Lake Rathe. Well lets just say i was there for a long
long time and still am. During the day look for the occasional gnoll
embalmer or high shaman (Shamans have runed totem staffs while embalmers
carry magic skulls and crysknives ) Or hunt any of the aviaks you see, the
drop bronze weapons and cash or words for research, all sell for a few gold
or plat. At night the undead come out and they are somewhat tough so get in
a group and kill the drybones for small bronze.

Thats what i did around your level.
