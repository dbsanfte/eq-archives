The best group I have eer been in was one consiting of
2tanks (2 true warriors)
2healers (cleric/druid)
2nukers (enchanter/wizard)
We were all level 24 to 26 playing in Cazic Thule. A well balanced group
can take multiple reds/yellow/blues/greens. The best thing about this kind
of group is the tanks can distribute tha damage very well between them
(providing they know what they are doing.) And their is a cleric to help
tank as well. The clerics would alternate acting as a 3rd tank while one
would just sit back heal/dd. When he was out of mana, that cleric would
take the place of the first tanking and then he would take over as healer.
the mages.. Well they just blew things up efficiently. Rarely did we (the
warriors) have to try to pull something off the nukers cause they burned to
fast. Its a good feeling to get 3 levels in 2 days with this kind of group
WITHOUT powerleveling or being lame. The bottom line was downtime was VERY
minimal and we could take very large groups of critters NON-stop. As for
grouping with the Enchanters... You havent played a group until you group
with higher level enchanters. Their debuff/charms are simply INCREDIBLE.
If I were to pull absolutely too many mobs, he would just charm one and turn
it from 10vs6 to 9vs7. As well as slow up the attackers so they are acting
half as fast. Less attacks equals less damage to tanks equals less mana
used by clerics equals more life for the tanks equals more damage they can
inflict equals less mana used by the mages. Sorry for the run on sentance,
but you get the picture.

Hope this helps you a bit.

Dyvim Kincaid
Warrior of Cazic Thule.
