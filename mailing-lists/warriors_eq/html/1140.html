**SPOILER ALERT**










Brutechopper is obtained from an NPC named Lindyun (or something similar) in
Innothule swamp. She is a troll, and didn't have a problem with my dark elf,
but any barbarians or dwarves should probably /con her before getting to
close. Talking to her will reveal she has a love of rubies. If you give her
one she will give you one of two items. Brutechopper or Midnight Maul. They
are both lore items and having either one in your inventory means she will
not give you either one.

I do not know where to obtain a ruby other than to buy one for 130p-175p
depending on faction and charisma. If anyone can tell me what mob might
drop a ruby I would be grateful.
