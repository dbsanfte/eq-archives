OK, finally got my reposte skill maxed out, took me a little over 2 levels, but FINALLY got it maxed :) Upon some testing this is what I found out:

When you reposte, you get another swing. This doesn't mean you'll hit, but you get another swing, and I'm assuming your chance to hit is calculated as with any normal swing. Reposte really kicks when you are wielding a big 2h sword like my BBC :) Let me give you a couple examples:

1. In this fight I was wielding my demon claw and my whip, in a good round (I concider a round to be the time between taunts, and an attack is a swing or kick), I had 12 attacks, 2 of which were repostes, 8 of which hit for 12,14,7,6,10,8,13,10, total of 80 points, 10 of those coming from a sucessfull hit with reposte.

2. In this fight I was wielding my BBC, in a good round I had 6 attacks, 1 of which was a reposte, 4 of those attacks I connected for 13, 42, 38, 27, total of 120 damage, 42 of those coming from a sucessfull hit with reposte. (note: due to slow speed of the BBC I usually only get one attack (2 if double), and about every 3 or 4 rounds I get 2 attacks (4 if both doubles) with it, this was one such round)

3. In this fight I was wielding my BBC, in a good round I had 5 attacks, 2 of which were reposte, 4 of those attacks connected for 10, 41, 27, 26, total of 104, 26 points coming from a sucessfull hit with reposte.

conclusion: reposte rocks, anything that gives you extra attacks is a good thing, expecially something that also prevents you from taking damage :) But it really seems to make a difference when you got a big heavy hitting weapon.

Hope that helps some
