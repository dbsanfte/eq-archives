Why would you want the price of the item to go down? The economy in EQ is
being blown by people who "farm" equipment to sell for cheap prices just to
farther themselves a tiny amount. IMO this sucks. I remember when you
could sell a mino axe for 10pp. Now I see them sold for 4pp and laf at how
rediculous this is. I also realize this is due to the ease of gaining a
mino axe now. But for things like a BBC, SBH, or Tomahawk, why drive the
price down and ruin the worth of the item. Half the fun in the game is
going and getting something for yourself. Buying items is definitely a part
of the game, and should remain an available option. But when you get people
camping items just to sell them for profit, it becomes very anoying. I have
tried to get a BBC for about a month before I gave up because of campers and
kill stealers. Hard to express the anger you feel when you sit killing and
waiting for 3 plus hours only to have another group move in and steal the
kill to gain what you have been patiently waiting for.

Im off the track here. The point is, why drive the cost of something down?
You should want to keep the items worth something until the majority of
people in the game can get them for themselves. Otherwise you will see lvl
3 and 4 characters running around with weapons that are level 15 or 20. And
they only paid 4plat for it.

Just my opinion I guess...
