People often think that the troll regen rate makes the the ultimate warrior. That may be true at first, but at later levels you get to the point where you have to have a healer of some type in your group, or you will die. And at that point the troll regen is almost pointless. If you want brute str Ogre is the way to go. Their str, sta, dex, and agil are much better than Trolls, not to mention ogres have the most points to distribute. You can easily get your dex and agil above 90 and still have your str and sta WELL above 100. Ogres are also smarter and wiser than Trolls, so they also make better SKs and shamans....with one exception....trolls regen rate works very nicely with the canabalize spell.

Just my 2 cents :)
