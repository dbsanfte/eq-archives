Patty, I have tried for about 48 levels to put hotkeys to my weapons like
you have. I have had 0 success. Can you please explain to me in detail how
to make a weapon hotkey so I can change between my 1hs/2hs weapons faster.
As well as make hotkeys for my jboots and guise.

Also, the web page is great, I just have a few suggestions. The always
taunt thing imho is incorrect. If you continually are taunting, the mob
will be mad at you sure. But when the wizard hits him with a DD that really
pisses him off, you won't be able to taunt hard enough to get him off your
nuker. So I suggest only taunting every now and then. You should be doing
enough damage that you are pissing that mob off to begin with without the
taunt. Then when someone does get him agitated and he turns to them, you
can easily use taunt to turn him back to you.

Also, the difference between 1hs and 2hs damage ammounts will be pretty
significant at the lower levels. When you reach higher levels, duel
wielding 1hs's can be of a significant advantage when you have close to 10
damage weapons that proc effects and are fairly fast. Specially with
celerity of swift like the wind =) I still almost continually use 2hs
myself though as my primary weapon. Haven't found my Ykeshas yet =)

Other than that great page. Good information and keep those youngin's in
line.
