I am a lvl 16 Barbarian warrior, and here are my advises:

You should decide already now wether you want use a 2h weapon or a 1h
weapon. Personally I went for the 1H.

This will make you a bit less effective untill you reach about lvl 15, when
you get dual wield and doubble attack. This will from there on be pretty
much the same, if not better than a 2h weapon.
Since you are a Human warrior, you cannot use bash, and 2 1H weapons or a 2H
weapon and bash due to that you will need a shield. Therefor you should keep
on using Kick. Bash never will hit it for you, if you dont decide to use a
shield and a 1h sword, who I havent seen any warrior over lvl 15 use.
Also keep Taunt and Sense heading maxed, these are two good skills to use in
the future. A warrior who cannot taunt the beast whacking the wizard to
pieces are no warrior at all and will loose.
Bind Wounds is expensive to work up, but I have now learned it is a very
usefull skill and should be worked on. (have a magician summon bandages for
you)

It is also recommended that you have a primary weapon skill, and a
secoundary. At later levels if you find an awesome weapon you be sure you
can use it! I would personally recommend working 1h slash as primary, and 2h
slash as secoundary.

You never need to put more than one skill point in any skill, but some
skills can be a pain in the ass to work when they are under 10 skill. I have
never put more than 9 in a skill, and I now have almost maxed out Doubble
attack and dual wield at lvl 16 (soon 17).

A place for you to hunt now at lvl 7 and all the way up to lvl 15 or 16 is
BlackBurrow.
I did it up to lvl 16 there.
