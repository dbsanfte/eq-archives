It is very unproductive to use higher levels weapons as a low level
character. You will never be able to effectively (meaning use it at your
level of experience) use it until you attain the level at which you could
have possibly acquired the item. This is some newer code implemented to
help reduce twinking. You are just not skilled enough in the art to use
this powerful a weapon. Do not get me wrong, you will be able to use it,
just not as well as you should be able to.
