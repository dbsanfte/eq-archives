Subject: Armor AC and Absorbtion test conducted...


In my experience the only real effect on damage absorbtion is AC.
I did some experimenting on the same character. I got my AC to 384 without
buffs using a mix of bronze plate and banded, and agility bonus. On average
hits I took were less than 10 points, about 1 in 5 hits would be up near the
max of what the creature could do. 50 for darkbones, 40 for ghouls and so
on.

I then switch to my magical armor which includes alot of leather items and
ringmail that give stat bonuses. I then got myself buffed by a druid and
managed an AC of 383. There was no significant difference in the amount of
damage I took. An average hit does minimal damage....and 1 out of 5 hits
does heavy damage.

Once I was in plate and banded and one I was in Leather and Ringmail. With
the same AC there is no appriecable difference in damage absorbtion.

This won't finish this arguement but at least it adds some fact to it.
