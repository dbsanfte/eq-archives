in beta I dueled many wizards. Simply put, if they use root, warrior dies, unless you get real lucky and resist a lot. In many of the duels, since they were just for fun and mainly with friends, they agreed not to use root. The results were rather surprising. If they tried to use their big damage spells I would usually win due to a couple resists and couple interupts, and just smackin the hell outta them with my GZ. BUT if the wiz would use lower level lightning spells they would win becuase they cast so fast I didn't inturupt much, and even if I resisted a few, there were a few more on the way.
