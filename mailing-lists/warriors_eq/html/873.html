Here's the best way to handle the skill of Sense Heading: First under
Options goto Keyboard and find the User Defined 6 slot. Get rid of
whatever's there and make it Keypad 8. Now, go back to your Abilities list,
and hotkey Sense Heading onto the last hotkey box on the left-hand side of
your screen. Now, whenever you walk forward (assuming you use the keypad for
control), you will automatically practice Sense Heading at the same time.

Sense Heading while Fishing? You bet! When you fish, take a seat next to an
NPC (presumably another fisher). As you are fishing, practice Beg on the
NPC, and Sense Heading all at the same time.
