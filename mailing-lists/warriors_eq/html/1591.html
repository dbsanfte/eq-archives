Matt-
I have a 30th level barbarian warrior on Veeshan. I can only tell you what I've chosen
Max out 1hs, 2hs, taunt, kick, slam/bash and I am working on archery (this has helped me take down runners).

Some have said take up piercing. When you get duel attack and duel weild only for the fact that a piercing weapon is faster and will help max these skills out.

I find that 1hs with duel weild is more effective in certain circumstances and then 2hs in another. 1hs gives you speed of attack. 2hs is good for greatest impact (recently got a crit hit of 100.. now that was sweet). If you use a 2hs or 1hs will also depend on your group make up. I do carry all my weapons with me so at times will switch depending on the circumstance.

All my reading and such have said to keep both maxed out. Some reasons are the performance of the weapon others for the variety of weapons you can use. I didn't work on my 2hs until I was at the 28th level and it was very painful to max it out.

Good luck!
