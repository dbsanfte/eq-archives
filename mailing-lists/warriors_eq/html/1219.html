The only point I would disagree on is that critical hit should be an
automatic skill like dodge/parry. The assumption being that the kick of
bash could potentiall be the critical hit. As a warrior you are going to
always try and do the most damage you can. With "critical hit" skill it
depends on how skilled you are is how often you succeed. Kicking an orc in
the gonads qualifies as a critical hit. Also, you said that you would "know"
when the right time for a critical hit is.....how could you know that? I am
not sure what you mean by this.

Just to reiterate. I think the critical hit skill should be checked
automatically against each and every strike. The percentage chance for a
critical hit is proportionate to the skill level. It would be something
like dodge/parry/riposte I think, though I am not sure how those checks are
calculated. The damage caused by this strike should be based on the weapon
you are using, perhaps the damage rating x 2. So a longsword would be 12
points (6 dmg?) and a 2 handed sword would be 24 (12 dmg). The end result
of this skill is that warriors become the masters of melee. We still will
never do as much damage per round as a mage of the same level, but we will
be more desirable as a melee class than the hybrids.

Now that I have finally thought of a complete solution I am going to submit
this is as feedback just to dispute all the proposals I don't agree with. I
will still participate in our petition of course, once we all agree on a
solution. But for now, this is just so Verant is thinking about our
problems.
