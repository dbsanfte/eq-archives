I don't want Warriors to get Critical Strike, at least not as it has been
presented on this list. While we could use a combat boost, I'd rather we go
another way.

I don't want another skill that works all the time without any strategy as
to when to use it. I'd rather be able to have a skill that I can use when I
want to. I want to be able to choose to use my skill to change the outcome
of a battle

I don't want another skill to replace kick/bash. We will then just use the
most effecient skill all the time.

I'm proposing again my Battle Cry. For 20 seconds, you get Double Attack
Speed, +25 STR and +25 AC. What would be even better is if EVERYONE in your
group gained the benefits of the Battle Cry. Something this powerful would
be limited to once per day, just like Lay Hands.

I also believe that the Warriors should have a general combat boost, maybe
6*lvl+5 for the combat skills.
