Subject: Weapons - IMO a level 15 Warrior


I have maxed out 1H Slashing and have 2H Slashing, Double Attack, and Dual
Wield all in the High 50s.

I am using a combine longsword and a fine steel longsword for my dual wield,
and a fine steel 2H sword to build up my 2H skill.

I've tried mino axes and such, but I just don't like the delay/damage ratio.
2H Slashing is ok for the damage, and with double attack getting better I
get plenty of hits in during an attack - HOWEVER, nothing I have seen by any
warrior deals death as plentiful as my two long swords with dual wield and
double attack. It is wonderful to change camera view just to watch as the
two swords fly. Attack, Attack, Attack, Attack, Bash, Attack, Attack,
Attack, Attack, Attack, Attack, Bash!!!!

Oh well, will have to see how good I am with my 2H Sword once I max out the
skill. Like I said it works well now (24 to 25 a hit - with double attack
up to 50 per round), but I still think my two longswords rule. Saving cash
now to buy a second combine longsword.

One last thing; talking to a 21 level warrior last night, the BBC is to him
the best weapon he has seen. Of course it is a 2H weapon, so 2H Slashing is
important to keep up.

This is all my opinion as a level 15 Barbarian Warrior Princess.
