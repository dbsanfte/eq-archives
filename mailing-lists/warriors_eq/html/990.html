Crushbone is the home to the Crushbone Orcs <../beastiary/orc.html> . This
zone is located in Greater Faydark <greaterfay.html> . If you are coming
from Felwithe <felwithe.html> you can hug the zone edge to the right. When
you see the fires in the distance you will realize you have come to the
entrance to Crushbone. This dungeon is designed for characters 5-15 with the
castle area holding some mobs to please characters in the low 20's.

There are several major portions of this zone. The front area(near the
entrance) is designed for lower level characters and contains mostly low
level monsters(5-8). Up on top of the hill you can find some monsters in the
8-12 level range. To the left of the entrance and over the bridge you will
see what looks like a small hill. Beyond that hill leads to the mine area.
The monsters back here range in the 8-10 level range. If you were to turn
right at the entrance and follow the wall you would be led to Crushbone
Castle. This contains the meaty monsters. You will find many mobs level 8-15
inside of the castle. You will also find most of the name NPC's at the
castle area. The emporer and the ambassador are by far the highest level
mobs at Crushbone(levels 18 and 19ish).

Crushbone is the originating point of at least two different quests. These
are the Crushbone Belts/Shoulderpads Quest <../quest/crushbelts.html> and
the Screaming Mace Quest <../quest/screamingmace.html> . There are several
good magic items to be found in this zone as well.
