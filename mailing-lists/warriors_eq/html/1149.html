I think the difference between melee combatants and casters magic should be
thus: Melee fighters should be able to do just as much damage, if not more,
whereas a caster may strike from a distance, and strike multiple targets
with area effect spells.

I am the president of a national group that fights with live steel weapons.
I've been fighting with long swords, 2-handed swords, axes and maces for 10
years, and I am very comfortable with just how devastating these weapons can
be. No matter if you are struck by lightning or are cut in 2 by a large
axe, you are just as dead!

So perhaps the direction we need to seek, and the difference between these 2
types of characters. melee and caster, is not how much damage they do, but
how. A caster should be able to reach far away, and strike multiple
targets, while the great warrior should only be able to strike at one target
at a time, but be able to mow them down like wheat.

armor and swing a real blade ( and trust me, it will give you a whole new
perspective on your RPGing!) check out our website!
