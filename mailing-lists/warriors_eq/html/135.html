Patty,

geez i dont know what you would be fighting at level 12 that needs a magical
weapon to hit it, only stuff that cant be hit by a normal weapon in befallen
are elf skeletongs, ghouls, and iced bone skels, other than that you should
consider fighting there, creatures like mummies, greater skells, and necros
are abundant and easy to take out. Also in befallen you can stick on level
one and constantly kill skeleton lrodd who is about level 12 or 13. Other
than that you can try to make it to lava strom witha group and fight drakes
and basilisks....they are good exp and loot. if you want to adventure more
take the trip down the the innothule swamp area, lots of frogs down there as
well as lizardmen, both of these give so so loot.

Or if you are in a strong group head to the Oasis of marr. start killing
Crocs, Caimans and if you are feeling lucky try a deepwater caiman. they
dont give good loot but are great Exp
