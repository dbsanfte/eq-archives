I see your point but I disagree. I think it should be TOTALLY random unless
a quest is attached to that item. So the executioner axe is on a level 45
mob in a dungeon. That means the executioner axe could appear on ANY level
45 mob in the game (within reason, necromancers wielding it in Najena
doesn't make sense). Probably add the restriction that it also must be a
dungeon mob.

The only reason I dont like your idea is simply, people will camp at all 4
spots. People WILL spend obscene amounts of time trying to get that item.
For a solid week easy I saw the same people over and over again waiting for
the Tomahawks.

I think your solution just spreads camping. There are people with lots of
time, people WILL spend days at a spot waiting for an item if they know that
SOMETIME the item WILL appear here then it WILL be camped.
