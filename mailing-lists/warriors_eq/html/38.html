I hunt from Qeynos and just went through 7 and 8 - my longest levels so far.

There is an old Qeynos warrior saying: "West Karanas for xp; Black Burrow
for loot." (Well, it's 2 weeks old anyways :-)

At Lev 7 you should be ok in BB as long as you stay smart (do not go into
the tree on the top floor!) You have to kill whatever you attack - if you
let one run away, he comes back with 3 or 4 of his pals! If you run with 3
or 4 chasing you, you have formed a "train" and the top floor dudes don't
like it when someone drags a train up top and then zones. Also, if you
attack the "caboose" of a train, you stand a good chance that the whole
train will turn on you. And then there are kill and loot stealers and
whiners - the PC are almost as bad as the NPC at times but if you learn your
way around and pick the correct fights - the loot is good. You should be
able to get some 2 h weps in BB after a short bit of work.

Out on the Karanas, stay away from the bandits and scarecrows and anything
else that cons red to you. You can build great xp on bears without any
hassle from Cros or Holly. Then work the young cats and by late 8 you
should be able to solo on some of the big cats - con everything and read the
writing as well as looking at the color. Use the ruined cat pelts to make
patchwork armor. Sell the rest.

Good luck and good hunting,

Snyak.
