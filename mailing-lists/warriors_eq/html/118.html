Hi there,

I am a level 13 warrior (Flair) that hangs in Crush Bone alot and this happens all the time. After awhile you learn to just start ignoring people after politely telling your side of the story. The thing is if you aren't trying to actively kill steal & you occasionally do it by accident I think you are ok.

For example, last night when I piled onto a slaver no one was tackling...the thing I didn't know was that a mage had already rooted it and was waiting for his tank to back him up. Did I kill steal? Yes. Did I mean to? No. After a lengthy discuission between me & the mage (windu) we worked it out. The thing is we both work the same area regularly & we loosely know of each other and have been through this drill a zillion times so we had the patience to talk it out.

In short as Jimmney Cricket would say "Let your conscience be your guide". If you do not actively seek to kill steal then I wouldn't sweat it. I'd apologize when when you screw up but if they refuse to listen & throw a tantrum, walk away and add a new candidate to your ignore list. If you work the same area for a period of time people will learn boundries anyway (and with it respect)

Good Hunting!!!
