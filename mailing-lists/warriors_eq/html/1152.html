Depends on the size of the fireball now doesn't it? Could be that fireball
is about as annoying to the giant as a bic lighter! And think of this, how
much damage can a pen knife do to you? Think you could be killed by one?

Now, we can look at EQ as a fantasy setting, or we can make it realistic.
If you want absolute realism, then yes, there is now way a single man with a
sword would be likely to kill a giant. In fact, to use your argument,
giants should be killing everyone with just on stomp, period. Not the 2 or
three it takes to kill me. We would also have to do away with that romantic
image of the Knight in shining armor slaying the Dragon with a single blow
from his sword or lance.

I tend to agree that they should probably have damage modifiers for HUGE
mobs that would reduce the base damage inflicted. These should be applied
to both physical weapons and spells though. Let's assume that base damage
is against med to large creatures. Of course, the reverse should hold true,
that smaller creature would receive more damage (all those elves, hobbits,
gnomes and dwarves beware!). This would make dragons and giants even
tougher then they are now, while making the combat with creatures of similar
size more realistic. I've seen what a broad sward can do, And like I said,
whether your head is cut off or you're reduced to ashes, your just as dead.

LefGrebo
