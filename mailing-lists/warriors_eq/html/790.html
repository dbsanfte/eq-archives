I have navigated High Hold pass several times with no problems
because of all the campers there. Coming from East Karana
if you always turn to your right when you can you shouldn't
have a problem. The spawns at the zone entrances are
usually handled by campers. You do have to watch carefully
for the turns, a couple are hard to see. I don't suggest using
SoW while there either your first time, because you could miss
a turn and end up in the water :)

I have went through the Gorge and the Evil Eyes always was
able to target me, even when I was up on the cliff.
