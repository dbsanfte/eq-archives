West Karanas has 2 bandit camps - nasty unless you have the fire power. Your
party should. We can take 2 bandits with a warrior#10 <mailto:warrior@10> ,
palidin#10 <mailto:palidin@10> , rogue#13 <mailto:roguge@13> ? maybe 14 and
its fun. We also did 3 and it was exciting. Sometimes there will be 5
bandits in the camp but usually there is a pair outside the camp. They vary
too - smetimes fighters sometimes spell casters. Some times good loot
otherwise good fun.

Also there is a brigand out there that requires a real gang to beat through
his full set of chain. And ogres that I have not yet seen and scarecrows
but also not sure how they'd con to your group and not sure if you could get
them one by one or if they swarm. They are over our heads.

If you hug the south shore and zone into N Karanas, you can hunt some good
stuff there but watch for the Gryfawns - unless you can take them. That
might be one of those spot where everything is either too high to fite or
too low for xp. A group of us 10's go there and hunt very carefully and
zone if we get in over our heads.
