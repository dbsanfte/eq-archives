There are a couple of things that could be added to the warrior that may
help. The critical strike is one of them, somewhat akin to the backstab.
It should require a button, and should require a recycle time and render
kick and bash/slam inoperative. Another option would be to lower the
recycle time of bash/slam and kick, and also changing so that you can kick
and bash/slam in the same round. Another idea that has come up would be to
have a triple attack. This would be nice, but it would have to be very
late in levels, maybe around 40. Right now after level 15 the warrior only
has two levels to look for where they actually gain something: level 25 -
disarm, and 35 - riposte (if I am wrong on these please correct me:) ).
There should be an additional skill at 20 and 30. How about Bind Severe
Wounds that let you heal more and up to 75% of your total health (would use
the same skill as bind wounds, would just replace it). How about a Sleep
skill? Yes you heard me, Sleep. Doubles your healing rate but turns your
screen blank for that period of time, kind of like you get when you are
blinded. Would put us in more danger than the spell hurler that is
meditating. These are just some suggestions that I have heard and thought
of myself. Maybe if we got enough together and sent them to Verant as a
petition, they might listen. They seem to be listening to alot of what
other players are saying.
