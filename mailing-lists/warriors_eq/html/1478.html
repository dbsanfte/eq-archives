Monks and Warriors have similar, but not exactly the same AC. Warriors
are always (with proper equipment) approximately 10-30 points higher on AC
for each given level. This may seem insignificant, however, EQ uses these
sorts of tiny differentials as the basis for damage calculations. Given
the way AC advances (3-5 point increase purely on level alone) there are
easy ways for EQ to 'normalize' the AC vs. Offense to determine relative
damage levels for enemies of similar level.

Monks are _not_ as good tanks as Warriors because of this difference.

As for Studded, that is merely the trade skill version of Leather Armor.
There is nothing 'better' about it. I think its nice to have Studded
because it looks nice when you examine someone. But there is no
additional advantage over Leather.
