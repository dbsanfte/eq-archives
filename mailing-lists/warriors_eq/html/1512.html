Actually if you see the berzerker frenzy message it is too late to run then.
I really haven't noticed anything special about it, except that it raises
strength and lowers agility. But thats it. It takes experience to know
when to run. As a general rule if I get below half a full bar of health and
the creature isn't close to the same (in a solo situation) then I will turn
and start running. That leaves enough hit points to hopefully make it to a
safe spot. Berzerker frenzy is like giving a caster 20 extra hit points
when they get down to 35% of health :)
