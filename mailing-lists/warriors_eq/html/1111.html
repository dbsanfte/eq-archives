Mark,
From the small window mode, select the button on the right labeled combat (also
try abilities). It should be a button listed in that menu. Left click and hold
on it until you 'pick it up'. Place it in one of your empty hot slots on the
left side of the screen.

To use it, click on the person you want to use it on (or click on your name to
use it on yourself). Get close to the person. Have bandages in ANY inventory
slot. Click on bind wound key.

The better your skill is, the more damage you can heal. However, the biggest
limitation is that you can only heal someone up to half of their hit point
level. After that, they will have to heal normally. You also will not get
success if the person already has over half of their hit points left.

If you do not have the button listed (which I don't think is possible. Every
warrior starts with it.) then you will need to train it at least once. If it
does not go up, try putting two more trains in it until it does 'work'.
