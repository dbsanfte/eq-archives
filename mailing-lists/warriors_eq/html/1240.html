Actually Dvinn isn't that powerful (unless something has changed in the last patch with his new dagger) and people in the 18-20 lvl range have a field day with him (my estimate is he is 20 or 21 based on cons I've heard). I play CB an awful lot so I relate this from my own exp.

The fact is that a mage will outdamage a warrior every day of the week on a creature he is equipped/able to hit (is it within 4 levels for non-fizzles for wizs?), is standing still & has a tank on them. The difference in levels doesn't matter as long as the wiz can hit it. Whether this right or wrong this is how the game is set up (i'd be inclined to say its still wrong too). You would think that higher level chars would be able to inflict greater damage on dark green cons than someone attacking a yellow or red...

However, if you took the same wiz (Mr 18), pitted him against a mid-twenties critter (ala a skeletal monk or something), guess who wins? The warrior (lvl 36?) because all his (the wizs) spells fizzle while the warrior still hits. So the warrior has a relative advantage over a wizard in "the blind spot" between where the wizards ability ends (i.e. things he can't hit) & your own capacity to kill (in this case probably creatures of level 22-34). This of course means you can only outgun people at least 5 or so levels below you as a warrior.

But when I'm camping the CB castle I just let Dvinn & Crush kill solo wizards (oh this is fun to watch). If there is a team of wizards I just leave. I absolutely hate the KSing &*(&##'s!!!
