I think a combination of the two is more accurate. The GZ answer explains
how the total amount of EXP is awarded for a kill.

Different levels still get different percentages of experience based upon
their level.

I'm going to modify Bruener's example in light of GZ's answer and combine it
with your scenario:

Mob exp: 1000
total lvls in group 59 (34 + 25)
Group EXP bonus 100 (10% I'm making this amount up since I don't really know
what it is)
EXP/level = 1100/59 = 18.64

Thoren (34 warrior) gets 634 exp (18.64 * 34)
Mikal (25 Bard) gets 466 exp (18.64 * 25)

PS. I've seen an experiment that someone ran to see if the human race got
any exp breaks/bonuses. They did not. A human warrior had to kill 14
firebeetles to level from 2 to 3. A dwarf warrior had to kill 14
firebeetles as well. A Human hybrid had to kill 22 Firebeetles as did dwarf
hybrids. There is no exp bonus for being human. There is one for being a
warrior.
