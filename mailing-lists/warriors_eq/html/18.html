Hi james, i have a level 14 warrior dark elf, but i have ventured all over
Norrath. Ive been to qeynos and i must say that that area has spots for
cash, or spots for exp, but its hard to find one with both. Being level 9 it
would be good to group with another level 9 char and go to the sewers, as
there is lots of smugglers and thugs there. Good exp and so so cash. To be
honest im not partial to BB at all...actually i think its a rather poor
dungeon compared to all others i have seen ( befallen, Najena being the
best.) wait till you are level 12 or so then go for Gnoll Guardsmen and the
occasional Elite. that will be nice exp, dont know about cash. Then when you
hit level 14 or so go up to the everfrost and permafrost zones, go hunt ice
goblins in a group of 3 people. Its amazing for loot and for exp trust me on
this one. If you are up for adventuring, make your way across the Continent
and head for the Freeport areas, lots of good places for both loot and EXP
here. Such as The desert of Ro, Befallen, West commons, many orc camps and
Dirvish camps all throught those areas. Give it a try!
