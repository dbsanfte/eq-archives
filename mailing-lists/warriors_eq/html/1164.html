I whole heartedly agree with you wayne.

The game is about balance. Some people are saying that a group of really
high levels do not need warriors. I say thats BS my friends always want my
warrior in their group. Not just becuase im company mind you. Because they
are spell casters. If im not there, the MOB would latch onto them and
interrupt their casting.

Sure we could beg verant for one more skill .... critical hit , which is a
very good idea. And i like the idea of it being like ripsote and parry.
Makes more sense. But the fact of the matter is this: We are there to
prevent our spellcasting buddies from being hit by creatures so that THEY
can dish out the damage. I have never had anyone complain to me and say look
man we dont want you in the group anymore cause you doont dish out the most
damage.

Every group needs a good strong warrior.
