You can buy them in the shop that sells gems in Qeynos - blue door adjacent
to Ironforges - (back room of Ironforges) North side of city on the way to
the zone that comes out by the clock. 70pp if you have really good charisma
(a gypsy in South Qey sells a Silk Evening Tunic that will add +5 or more
cha - 4pp) You want STAR rubies not regular ruby. The 2nd woman in the
shop sells the star rubies.
