sigh.. Things are not as bad as you guys think.

Warriors CAN still tank better then paladins and sks. We were never meant
to be the ultimate damage dealers. It has just evolved to everyone thinking
we have to dish mad damage to keep the critter on us to do our job. The
only thing they have hosed us with is the ability to taunt in higher level
situations. And to top that off, they remove the taunt to our proccing
weapons.

No one has the amount of HP we have, no one has the AC we have. No one can
eat damage and stay live like we do.

Find a group where people play their classes instead of just go kill things
and you will see what I mean. If the ranger is getting his ass kicked
because he is outdamaging us, then he is not doing his job and is putting
the whole group in danger because the cleric is spending their mana to keep
him alive. Remember, a 600mana rez is alot more mana efficient then 3 comps
and a bunch of smaller heals to keep that ranger kicking. If the paladin or
sk is tanking, then they are not doing their job because they are not taking
the damage as well as a warrior. It is all about how you play your class,
not what abilities you have or dont have.
