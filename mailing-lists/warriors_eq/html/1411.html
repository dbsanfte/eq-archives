While highpass may be good for loot, I've always thought it a little too
risky for 16 unless you are in a group. I prefered hunting at the bridge to
north-east Karana on the eastern side. While this won't get you a lot of
loot, you will level quickly. For loot around lev 16 find a group and hunt
bandits, they drop bronze weapons and shouldn't be that hard for you. HHmmm
for loot alone if you have a magic weapon you can hunt wisps on Eurids
crossing, or if you don't have one you can hunt the zombies there. Paw is
also a good place to go, you could kill Nobles there and get both loot and
exp. Oh, and if you can get together a party the gorge is wonderful for
both as well, just stay clear of the evil eyes.
