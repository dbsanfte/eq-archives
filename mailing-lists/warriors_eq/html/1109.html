I have been to Mistmoore. In fact, I have been all through the castle. But
I was invis and invis to undead. Some of the horrors that can be found in
there is pretty amazing. I ant wait till I have a group that can handle
that exploring that place. The first and only time time I was there, I was
level21 or 22... cant remember. We stayed outside, right next to the zone.
but even then, it got very ugly very fast. Suffice to say i was full health
(somewhere around 710hp) and died in less than 5 seconds flat. Even with a
full group at my back, they didnt have time to cast a single heal i got hit
so hard and fast. I cant remember what hit me, a deathly usher or herald i
think. something like that. regardless, the time we spent there before
that was very very good for xp and loot. you just have to be very careful,
know when to zone, and always use your different camera angles to watch for
other incomming mobs. I actually plan to head back there very soon now that
I am much higher level and able to handle a little more than 2 casts from
one of those nasties. The shield you mentioned comes from there as well,
called the Mistmoore Shield I believe. Bugged shield as well, when you zone
to certain zones, or store it in the bank, it just disappears. it is a
known bug, and apparently they are working on it. just no resolve as of
yet.

Hope that gives you the info your looking for.
