Sure ... I have suggestions. But the question is what do you WANT to put the
skill points into. Ever thought of a trade skill? You are at a good point
now to be able to support most any of them. I won't get into those here.

Tip one:
Bind Wound - get it, use it ... it will save you many minutes of sitting on
your butt later as you get higher and higer amounts of hit points. It will
only get you up to half life, but that's a long way when you zoned out with
a sliver of red and you have no healer type with you. It will also reduce
the bitching you will receive from your group members for always having to
wait on your slow healing rate (when in actuality you have twice their hit
points). A few points will really jump start this skill.

Tip two:
Alcohol Tolerance - It helps reduce the negative effects of alcohol. Since
alcohol will pump your strength and stamina (? I think), then this will
significantly help you in fights. The downside of alchol, reduced agility
and the wobble/vision things are supposed to be much reduced, or even not
present as your skill gets higher. I have not done much with this skill but
there was a post to this group a while back. Maybe someone can repost it. I
think the sender included what benefits he saw at different levels.

Tip three:
Any other skill that is used heavily that you may not have already trained
up in, including; swimming, sense direction, and secondary weapon skills
that you might need for emergencies.

Good luck,
