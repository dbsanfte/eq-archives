In RPGs, my heart and soul has always fit that of a warrior. But, having
played a warrior to level 12 on Zek, and having suffered countless setbacks
there, mostly but not exclusively involving PKs (one time 2 yellow wizards
wiped my level 11's full hps out in less than ten seconds after rooting),
I've come to the conclusion that warriors are, more than anyone else, forced
to wear and use the juiciest items to obtain xp while at the same being the
most vulnerable (after we've used our hps to get xp, non-hiders are out of
options without spell-casting bodyguards) and least dangerous/flexible/useful
of the classes. Furthermore, if/when we do get an advantage against an
enemy, we're really bad at actually finishing them off (escaping from us with
impunity is a big option).

I've heard some very encouraging things from some of you more successful
folks contradicting my conclusions and I'd like to hear more. I'm not so
much concerned with the PKers as I am concerned that a party of four wizards,
a magician and a necro might just be slowed down by a warrior tank (best
defense = good offense + we seem to suck at offense more than most casters
w/pets suck at defense, etc.) Many other classes could fit into that group
and be less of a drag, I think...

I'm not at all interested in theories or how things look on paper from people
who've farted around like I have to 12th or even 16th level. I am more
interested in hearing how things are actually working/not working for us in
the grand scheme of things before I can seriously committ to trying to make
my warrior work in this game and on RZ.

Thanks,

-A disgruntled former barb
