OK.... Now my 2 cents on what to give to warrior class. Last time I will
participate to this thread. It is getting out of control, and becomes
boring when by now we have no resolution or agreements. We do have a few
who are on the same page as to what needs to be done tho. (Of course this
is just my opinion.)

1. Remove or raise skill caps. (Or change them all together.) Instead of
the standard level * 5 +5 = cap (I believe 200 was the skill cap
previously.) Something more like level * 5 +10 = cap This would make a 40th
level warrior have a skill cap of 210 not 200. All other clases will be
only 10 skill points behind. How much difference does 10 skill points
actually make at that high of a level? Also, raise the skill cap for
warrior to 255. This would cap out our skills at level 49. (Who knows what
the caps will be for above 50 levels with the expansion.) Personally I
would be satisfied with removeal of skill cap and no bonuses to it.

2. Specialization. (Just like other have mentioned have it akin to the
mage specialization skills.) What the advantage is, at skill 51, that is
your main specialization. The other skills cap at 50 then. The advantage
speed up weapon use and 1.5* damage increase. Not alot, just giving us the
ability to inflict a little more damage. I mean think about it. I can hit
for around 49 a slash with a BBC, time and a half on that would be 73 or 74
points. Plenty of damage. Warriors are not ment, never were ment, never
will be (at least in the EQ world) the main damage inflicting character of
the game. This will allow us to more effectively hold attention of mobs and
possibly even (dare i say it?) solo a little more. This skill should come
into play around level 22. This skill should also be checked like
parry/riposte/dodge and such so it is not an every attack damage deal.

3. Sleep Regeneration. When we sit on our duffs to regen health, we should
have something like meditate for mage classes. All it does is time and a
half our regen process. Again, that is not alot, but it speeds up our
downtime. Pitfall to this skill, the screen goes black, and we can not see
a thing. We also have a 2 round penalty to "wake up" from our sleep. This
is a huge negative to it, which will help promote group playing NOT soloing.
And should also make it more viable for us as a request.

4. Increased taunt. At higher level taunt does not work as well as it did
as lower levels. Why? Because we do not cause enough damage to make the
mob turn from the mages to us. Taunt makes the mob think we caused it
damage, if they increase the amount of damage the mob thinks we cause when
we use taunt, things will be a little better in regards to using taunt.

These skills are not to off balancing and do not give a huge advantage to
the warriors over anyone else. What they do is give us the ability to
possibly cause a little more more damage. Raise our attack and defense to
places that will make us shine a bit more. Allow us to get off our butts a
little faster. And make us the melee class we are supposed to be.

Sned me feedback on this, and I will see that it gets into the hands of the
people who will be able to listen to our requests and possibly give us help,
or denial or our requests. At least I am pretty sure I can get this to the
correct people =)
