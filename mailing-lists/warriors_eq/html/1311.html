A full group below 24 can get large bronze so long as you have a 24th level
wizard with you. There is a 'rare' NPC that spawns with the creature who
drops the large bronze (a 26th or 27th level cleric instead of a 22nd or so
level warrior). This makes the fight very dangerous (a 22nd level paladin,
2 24th level warriors, a 24th level ranger, and a 24th level wizard managed
the fight). She also seems to be spawning more often as of late, or at
least was when I finished getting my full set of large bronze at level 24.
I started camping that spot at level 21 or 22, now I have to go back to
that zone at 26 to take out a few more special NPC's there. Large bronze is
also very heavy, a full set, a BBC, and a ash bow tip the scales at about
138. This doesn't leave me much room to carry alot of loot.
