This arguement still doesn't make sense to me in a realm where some of the
opponents are taller than houses.

I don't care how big your axe is, you aren't going to be able to do the
damage that a caster can to some creatures, like a hill giant.

You argument just doesn't make sense when you apply it to the setting in EQ.
Sure I can hack someone's head off in a single swipe and a mage could blast
them to ashes. However, that same sword swipe will be nothing more than an
annoyance to a giant, where that same fireball from the mage will do
devastating damage.

Making the warrior a huge damage dealing machine was never the intent of
Verant and isn't in the spirit of EQ's balanced group oriented world.
Verant needs to allow us to have an advantage of the other melee/caster
hybrids is the main point of our discussion I think.
