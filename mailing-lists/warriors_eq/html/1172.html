But the Warrior does have a limiting factor: Stamina. Once again, I will
draw from my experience in wielding broad swords in real life. After about
3 to 5 minutes of constant fighting, in 100 lbs. of armor guess what, you
are wiped out and need to rest a bit. Just like the mage has to rest while
dealing out those spells, we warriors should need to rest after fighting in
all that armor and swinging that 3 to 5 lbs. sword around, 9 lbs. in the
case of great swords.
