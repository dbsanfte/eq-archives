Good advice from the others so far. Here is how I sometimes deal with em.

First ... in character or not, kill stealing is kill stealing. I hate it and most
other players do too. I happened to be in the snake pit in BB one night and there
were four people there. Three of us had been rotating kills for about 30 minutes
or so. The fourth one came in ... a female human paladin. She proceeded to take
all snakes she could, including jumping in on our fights and looting the corpses.
It was annoying to say the least. When questioned about it she replied, "Sorry
... gotta make level." Okay, excuse me for being rude here but what the hell?
Like the rest of us DON'T have to make level?

The three originals ended up grouping and taking every single kill and loot for
about 45 minutes until she left. It was fun and we laughed at her the whole time
during group chat. Not to mention we made some good exp even though grouped, and
some moderate loot also.

Moral of this story: When it's only one person annoying you ... form an anti-jerk
group with the sole purpose of driving them away. It works because your group
will always do more damage, thus getting the exp, AND the loot.

Good hunting,
Faderic (and Rosmorte)
