Subject: Protest petitions and power


Ok, I am new to the warrior list, though I have been raising a stink over on
the Vault's boards about warriors. I believe there are some issues that need
to be discussed. I also believe that petitioning for changes with out a
dialog is not going to be successful.

First some technical thoughts. We all have seen that skills go from 0-255,
which equals one byte. I believe that raising the skill cap would be a
non-trivial task on Verant's park, requiring a write (or at least a
recompile) and altering all the player records on all the servers. I would
think they are doing this for the expansion pack, since the level limits are
supposed to be increased from 50 to a rumored 75 or 100.

I also think I know why they capped combat skills where they did. I have
noticed that my attack skill was at 510 at 21st level. At a guess (and if
someone could confirm this I would appreciate it) that Attack caps at 999 or
1000. Again there may be technical issues that prevent that from being
easily changed. I predict that there will be some MAJOR server upgrades (and
probably associated down time) before the expansion pack comes out.

For those of you who did not see this on EQVault, this is supposedly an
email that Brad sent on warriors:

********************************************************************

Recently I e-mailed Brad McQuaid about the Warrior class. I asked him if he
was listening to everyone about the warrior class, whether or not they were
going to make changes to it, and what he thought were their strengths and
weaknesses. This is what he had to say:

"I personally feel warriors are in good shape, but we've been thinking about
adding weapon specialization, probably a warrior-only skill. It will take us
some time to get that implemented and there are some things we need to
finish first that have higher priority.

Do keep in mind these tidbits about the warrior that not everyone is aware
of:

1. They get more hit points than anyone else
2. They have access to more weapons than anyone else
3. They have more freedom to wear items than anyone else
4. They have more special class-only weapons and items than anyone else
5. They have higher skill caps than anyone else."

*********************************************************************

Now I have debated the merits of the warrior abilities and advantages
endlessly and think we all know that these boil down to a fair plain
character class. A couple of comment from me (and any comments from you all
would be appreciated).

The ability to wear more times seem to be the saving grace over rangers for
warriors before rubicite is available. I have an AC around 470 with mostly
bronze armor. For now rangers cant compete with that.

I am now 22nd level and I have yet to get my first "special class-only"
weapon or item. Nor has a 26th level warrior friend of mine and we have
fairly good connections. If there are more warrior only items I would love
to see and better yet get them. (Brings up a question: are there any good
warrior web sites out there?)

As to the skill caps I have only just now started to look at these in the
bigger sense of what effect they have on attack. I can tell you this, I
compared my self at 21st (barbarian warrior) to a 21st level wood elf ranger
and I had an attack that was 45 points higher (510 VS 465). I wonder how
much of our feeling that we are not masters of combat comes from the fact
that the default setting for battle messages. We see our misses but not
others. We only see their hits.

And a thought about battle spam. In a six partly group with 2 or more tanks
it is just about impossible to tell how you are doing. What do you think of
having the text color for your battle messages being a different color? Say
purple or something.
