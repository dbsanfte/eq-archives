Now to answer your question about Drosco.

Warning *spoiler*.....scroll down to find out what quest Drosco is
connected to.


























Seems like ol' Drosco was a member of the Bloodsabers
(the group in the sewers that worships Bertox). One of the
NPCs in the Shrine of Bertox in the sewers will tell you that
Drosco has been leading the guards to the Shrine and that
he must be killed. I believe you return the letter or the
amulet to the NPC to solve the quest. Note that this will
only work for evil aligned characters. If you are good, you
will have NO chance to make it to the Shrine.
