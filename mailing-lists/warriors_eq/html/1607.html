Ok what I have personally experienced goes like this. The experience is
boken up into lvls. You take the exp gained by a mob and divide it by the
total lvls in the group (example: Mob exp: 1000; total lvls in group 40;
1000/40 = 25; so each lvl is worth 25 exp's; so a lvl 10 would get 250
exp's.) So the person with more lvls would get more exp because it takes
more exp to lvl. This theory is in no way concrete.

I read a posting by either Brad or John that as long as theres one other
person with you then you get a 10% exp bonus. The bonus doesnt increase
with more members. SO it would mean that it is more efficient just having a
partner then a whole group.

Im not high level but I have a pretty good story.

One Friday night at about 3am est I was out wondering around the East
Commons looking for something to do. There were only about 15 people in the
zone. I came across an orc camp and decided to kill orcs. I was in need of
slashed belts and figured this would be cool with noone else here. At lvl
14 I can take just about anything in the zone and I wanted to see if I could
clear the camp of 4 orc spawns. 3 of 4 spawns were blue to me but seeing
that I have close to 500 hp's I wasnt worried. The battle was very close.
I had forgotten how many hp's the orcs had and when theres 4 on you your
hp's go down quick. I killed the last one with about half a bub of health
left and sat down to an unexpected long rest.

A bard strolls into camp and askes if id like to join him so I could heal
quicker. I said hell yeah! From that point on we would take turns killing
the orcs (ungrouped) and then join again when I needed healing. I was there
in case he got into trouble (he was lvl 11) and he was there to get rid of
my downtime. We had it down to such a science that we were doing both camps
at the same time. When ever he pulled to many he would run them around in a
big circle as I took em off him 1 by 1. Also , he didnt want the belts so
he gave then to me.

We finally called it quits at about 6am. I had made lvl 15 plus a bub. Had
about 25pp in cash, and 4 backpacks full of belts.

It was one of the most enjoyable times I have had in 7 months.
