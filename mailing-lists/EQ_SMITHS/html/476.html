To make a dye extract you need to have a ALCHEMIST combind one of the
following items (it just lists the items and will not say what colors they
make) along with a flask of water...
Permafrost Crystals, Evergreen Leafs, Sarcoscypha Fungus, Russet Oxide,
Jack-o-lantern Fungus, Iron Oxide and Charcoal.
Each one makes a diffrent color extract.
You then take the extract (it is not clear but it sounds like this is also
by the alchemist) and combind it with 1 to 3 resins to get a diffrent shade
dye (Charcoal extract with 1 resin gives a diffrent shade of the same color
then Charcoal extract with 3 resins).

You them add the dye along with lacquer in the forge when making steel plate
(it specifies steel plate so I guess there will be no tinted banded) and it
will be perminantly tinted.

Jar of Lacquer - 1 gold, 1 silver, 8 copper - Steamfont Windmills - Prices
may vary.

Smithy Hammer - 14ish plat. Found in Hallis and Freeport (so far, probably
other locations as well). Note, this is not the same as the dwarven smithing
hammer or evlish smithing hammer.

Buckler = Water + Smithy Hammer + Buckler Mold + 1 metal sheet.
Round Shield = Water + Smithy Hammer + Round Shield Mold + 2 metal sheets.
Targ Shield = Water + Smithy Hammer + Targ Shield Mold + 1 folded sheet of
metal.
Kite Shield = Water + Smithy Hammer + Kite Shield Mld + 2 medium quality
sheets of metal.
Tower Shield = Water + Smithy Hammer + Tower Shield Mold + 1 medium quality
folded sheet of metal.

Note: this book mentions superior craftsmanship and enchanted shields from
other cities.

Plate armor = Smithy Hammer + leather backing + water + appropriate mold +
appropriate metal.

Visors, Collars and Bracers = 1 medium quality folded sheet of metal.
Helms, Pauldrons, Girdles, Vambraces, Gauntlets and Boots = 2 medium quality
folded sheets of metal.
Breastplates, Cloaks and Greaves = 3 medium quality sheets of metal.

Note: I have not seen leather backing yet, probaly on tayloring merchant or
taylor made. I'll look into it later.

Metal Rings = water + small brick of ore + file
Chain Jointing (sp) = Metal Rings + file + smithy hammer + water
Note: Have found no formula yet but Chain Jointing is used in making Field
Plate and Full Plate.

Field Arrowheads = metal bits + water + file
Hooked Arrowheads = file + large brick of Ore + water.
Silver Tipped Arrowheads = silver bar + file + block of medium quality ore +
water.
Steel Arrow Shafts = water + file + 2 large bricks of medium ore.

Tayloring:
silk thread = 2 spiderling silks
leather backing = silk thread + low quality wolf/cat/bear skin
