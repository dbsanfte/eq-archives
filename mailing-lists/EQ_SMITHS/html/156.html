Studs require one metal bits, one water flask, one file.
Files require two metal bits (unstacked), one water flask, one file mold.
Metal bits require two small ores (unstacked), one water flask.

You'll have to check the basic blacksmithing book for the ingredients for
steel BONING (note the spelling).
