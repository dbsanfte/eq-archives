Yeah, but don't get me started on that. I died like 5 times in one night in
Neriak, and had to run back from Oggok every time. If it wasn't for sprit
of wolf, I'd have probably started another character. You should start
thinking about the trip there too, unless you want to make lanterns from 0
to 80 skill, and only intend on making large banded after that.

I've personally given up on smithing until they actually give us something
to shoot for. I've taken up fletching though. True, you can't make any
money at it, but at least you can be pretty sure you'll have a goal until at
least level 30 or so. That darkwood bow staff and cams to go with it are
expensive (about 450pp just for components if I remember correctly).

Well, off the soapbox...
