70 P is a good start, but not nearly enough to get you to skill lvl 80
(where it starts becoming profitable enough to pay for itself, or least so I
hear; I'm a skill lvl 43 blacksmith right now). Although making scalers can
increase your skill, I'd highly recommend making lanterns until lvl 80.
They're cheaper, you'll have more successes with them, and you can sell them
back to merchants for about half of what it cost you to buy the components.
