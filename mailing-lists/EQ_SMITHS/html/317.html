Items do indeed go out of use. Players stop playing their warrior and
start up another character that will need his own banded. The level 30s
that finally get their replacement for banded armor often are not interested
in haggling, meeting, and reselling banded to players for 2 or 3 p
gain (vs selling it to a merchant) I know a couple of people at level 22-23
that don't even bother selling FS weapons to players because it is a waste
of time, it is the same with banded. (and no, these players
are not master potters or brewers)

What we need is the ability to make steel armor, or at least
bronze, that way we can cater to more than 1 level range of players.
