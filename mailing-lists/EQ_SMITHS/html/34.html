Whoah buddy, slow down! Hehe, sorry I had to say it =) Anyways, based on my
own personal experiences as a bard/smith... I found it MUCH easier to put 2
skill points into smithing every level, no matter what your level... Believe
me, it will pay off in the long run... At level 3, if you can spare the
coin, I suggest starting to sharpen weapons... after that, you should (I
think) start making "metal bits", and then go onto bigger and better things
from there...
