Most of my experience comes from being an ogre in Oggok, so here's what I've
found in that area.

Oggok:
forge in Metil Armer shop

Feerrott:
basic armor and lantern molds and 2 books (basic smithing and armor &
weapons) near the bridge to the Rathe Mountains

Grobb:
ore (all sizes) at a merchant near the shadowknight guild (Nightkeep)

Neriak:
file and other (not sure which) molds in the Foreign Quarter (2 ogre women)
ore (all sizes) at a merchant in the Commons from a merchant in an aquarium
type room near the bank (The Burnished Coin)

I had to go all the way to Neriak for misc. molds - ogres and trolls beware
of roaming NPC's with bad attitudes. I died several times in Neriak simply
because I made a wrong turn, so don't know what else may be there.
