I sold a full suit of banded for 120 plat today. I made 70 plat profit. I said
my price is higher than most, I said I wouldn't sell for lower, and I gave the
buyer the option to shop around and find a better price or to buy from me right
then and there. Well, I sold the suit :) People pay the prices, and the more of
us that sell for a higher price, the rarer the cheap banded, and the more
willing they are to pay our price instead of shopping around. I'm not trying to
be greedy, but my time invested into smithing is worth more than a few plat per
sale, or in some cases, actually losing money. I won't continue to lose money, I
am not dropping my prices. I sell fine at 130 per suit (will drop to 120), 6 per
gorget, 12 per mask, 14 per helmet, 8 per bracer, 13 per sleeves, 14 per mantle,
20 per mail, 19 per cloak, 13 per belt, 19 per leggings, 13 per boots.
