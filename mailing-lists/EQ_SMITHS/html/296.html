Point 1. BS. The customer is to paying for the failures, and my time for that
matter. It's called OVERHEAD. (example) If I were making cloaks, and the
failure rate was really high, even for a 150+ smith. Say like you only made 1
cloak out of 10, do you really think people would sell cloaks for 10-15p each?
Get a grip. My time, materials, and the persons personality go into the price.
The last factor can lower the price if they are nice and don't look twinked, and
say they can't afford the cost by a couple of plat. Then I'm happy to lower it
down for them. The Aholes, that come off saying hey, your greedy.. I'm not
buying from you. Then 30 mins later when they can't find it for less or at
all.. want to buy from me.. hahahah I won't lower it a copper.. I might just
raise it a few plat.

Point 2. Over all armor costs.. I have a completely TWINKED smith. I'll be
the first to admit it. I have a 25+ warrior, and I used cash from him and
purchased magical items to get my smith character up in smithing skill. I run
my smith for the SOLE purpose of making money to feed my warrior. (Have you seen
the prices of steel plate??) and to help my wives mage buy spells. But, I don't
NOTHING to hurt the smithing trade. I won't lowwe my prices to 50p for a full
suit of banded or sell components for 1p over cost. I sell at a fair price to
both me and the customer. If you get on the Erollis Marr server, I'm known as
"the banded guy". Lots of people buy my banded at my prices. I don't have to
work hard at all to make 300-400p a day on the weekends.. and 100-200p on the
week nights.. profit. Then I go off and spend it in the market place (PCs) and
adventure... I'm not a full time smith.

Point 3. I'm NOT going to help another little smith ever again. (On Errolis
Marr anyways). The last 3 people that I have "helped" get to 100-110 smithing
so they could begin to make money turn around, and undercut me and try to steal
all of my business. That is crazy. There are TONS of people wanting banded all
the time. We could all sell for the same price and make plenty of plat. but
no. They have to try and undercut me. Well, to bad for them, that people know
when they are getting a good deal and alot of people will only buy from me, and
word of mouth is powerful... Sorry young smiths on Errolis Marr, your on your
own. To bad things have to come to this.. I hate seeing people try to make
banded at 50skill wasting TONS of money.. and not really getting anywhere.. then
spending hours on top of hours trying to sell gorgets and bracers.. (This is
something I did in the begining cause I didn't know better) then a very friendly
smith pointed me in the right direction. This is why in the begining I made it
a point to help smiths out... Only take a couple bad apples....

Lastly, I think most smiths here, that are actually thinking about the whole
picture have about the same prices. 4-5pp for Gorgets or Bracers (Items that
take 1 sheet) to 17-20pp (items that take 3 sheets) I think my prices are right
in the middle.. I saw some smiths selling (2 sheet items) helms, belts etc for
8-12.. I sell mine for 10. It's pretty basic.. 1 sheet = 5pp 2sheets 10pp
(mantles can go for 12p cause of failures) 3 sheets 15-20p depending on item and
failures. (For faulires see point #1)

Ok, this is lastly.. I just wanted to ask a few other smiths out there that are
140+ at least.. How do you see your success rates for Mantles, Cloaks, Leggings
and Tunics? Do you feel that when the last patch came out they changed the
Cloak %? I used to fail alot on cloaks, now I hardly ever fail... Even at 150+
I fail on Masks, Belts and Leggings the most.. and Masks, and Belts are
Trivial...
