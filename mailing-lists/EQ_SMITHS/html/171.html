In the Gorge of King Xorrb (sp?). The muddite minors are about level 12 to
14, the elders are from 14 to 16. At least that's what I have found. It's
best to bring a party and sit near the zone in case of trouble from the
other baddies there.

If you go to fight them, be careful of getting mud in your eye, it will
black out your screen for a few seconds and your to-hit chance is
drastically reduced. Not to mention it will scare the poop out of you the
first time it happens.

The muddites will drop ore of all sizes, but it's heavy. So if you aren't
high on strength,bring some pack mule tanks with you.

Good luck,
Rosmorte
