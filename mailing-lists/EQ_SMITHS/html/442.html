The progression is metal bits (21), scalers (41), lanterns (68), skewers
(115), pots (122) and finally sewing kits (135). Everything you need to
make metal bits is in N Freeport. Everything else is in E Freeport until
you are ready to make sewing kits. When you reach skill 122, pick up and go
to high pass hold to the Serpent's Supply. There is a forge right next to
the vendor with needle and thimble molds. This is kinda important for your
sanity because sewing kits are containers and take up a whole inventory
slot. The postition of the vendor means you can simply turn around and sell
off your kits to make room for more. Personally, I had alot of trouble
making armor profitably until I maxed my skill out with the sewing kits.
Also, note that many of the guides out there have incorrect information on
how to make cloaks. The correct formula is mold + 2 metal sheets (not 3!) +
water.
