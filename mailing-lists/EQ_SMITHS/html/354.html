I was an ogre at one time, and I've seen a few trolls killed in Oggok. The
only place I actually saw it was in the shaman guild area. Trolls are
definitely kos there. I've seen troll bodies in the shadowknight and
warrior guilds too, but I'm not sure if trolls are kos in those areas. So,
stay out of Komarr's Caves (area J on the map in the EQ guide). I'd
actually suggest just going straight to the forge in Metil Armer (area K)
and then straight back out the way you came. There's not much else to see
in Oggok anyway.
