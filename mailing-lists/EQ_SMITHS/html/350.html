Well, its a bit odd that you posted that question here (as studded has to be
made by a tailor), but ok.

First the studs must be manufactured (by a smith).
Total supplies need for one set of studs:
6 small chunks of ore
1 file
4 flasks of water

Convert the small chunks into 3 metals bits, then place the bits, file and
your one remaining flask into the forge and prey for a success. Repeat this
process until you have the required numbers of studs (ranges from 1 for the
non-implemented mask to 4 or 5 for pants). Go get a High Quality Pelt
(wolf=small, cat=medium, bear=large), and the pattern for the item your
making. Place the pelt, pattern, and studs in your large sewing kit and if
ye gods of random number generation smile on you you'll get your piece of
studded (which usually has identical stats to storebought leathers). If you
fail you lose it all. Kind of strange that one can ruin metal studs with a
needle, but there it is. -sorry for the small rant, but I practice both
smithing and tailoring, and would like to see at least the missing patterns
implemented.- Hope that info helps.

Vinval
