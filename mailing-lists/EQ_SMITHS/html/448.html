Well, there are new items for smiths to make since this mornings patch.
Time for people to collate our info on where, what and how much. So far, I
have uncovered 3 new items we can make. The molds are available from a
vendor near the entrance to Kaladim, in Butcher Block. The new items are:

Forged Morning Star, 1HB, 8 damage, 38 Delay
1 water flask + 1 metal sheet + Spiked Ball mold + Hilt Mold

Forged 2 Handed sword, 2HS, 12 damage, 44 delay
1 water flask + 1 metal sheet + Pommel mold + Hilt mold + Heavy Steel Blade
mold

Forged Bastard Sword, 1HS, 7damage, 33delay
1 water flask + 1 metal sheet + Pommel mold + Hilt mold + Dual-edge Blade
Mold

The cost of the molds from me (I have high charisma and the dwarves con
amiable to me):

Dual-edge Blade Mold 1p9g1s8c
Heavy Steel Blade Mold 3p8g3s6c
Spiked Ball Mold 2p5g5s7c
Hilt Mold 6g3s9c
Pommel Mold 6g3s9c


These are pretty good weapons except for one glaring problem, they can't hit
magic only creatures. So far, the response to them on E Marr has been
deafening silence, hehe. I have made one of each of these and auctioned
them in the Freeport area. No one showed the slightest interest in them
except other smiths wanting to know how they were made. IMHO, great
twinking weapons but prolly not viable to produce unless there is a way to
make them magic (then tons of people will want them). I don't think I'll be
able to sell any of them for the 8 to 10pp they are worth. What do you guys
think?

So, anyone notice any other new items we can make, particularly new armor
(or know when or if new armor for us will be implemented). Any other
locations for the molds? Any thoughts on what we should charge for these
things if I'm wrong about the demand for them?
