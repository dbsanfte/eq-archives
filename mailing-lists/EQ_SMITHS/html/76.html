Subject: Power Smithing (what I have learned so far)


Well I decided to become a smith, as my character about dead-ended on
the tailoring route. BTW, I am
on the Rathe Server (Archonon)

I started with a 10 skill, and now have 40. I have spent 62p doing this
and have the system figured out
pretty well. I have 100p left to spend, afterward I will have to go out
and earn some spending cash or
sell some stuff off..... (I think its more fun to go earn the money
however)

From x-20 points of skills just make metal bits, and don't sell them!
When you cross 20 skill just make
as many as ore you have left on hand.

Then fall into this rotation.... buy and use enough skewer molds to use
up all the metal bits you have
assembled (try to do a min of 20-30 per cycle). When you run out of
bits, go make more, on the order
of 100 or so (meaning 200ore + 100water + extras for all the failures)

Then just turn them all into skewers (I have made 9-12 skewers). You
will fail all the time, but you still
gain skill points from failure. If your lucky you can sell a skewer back
to make up some lost cash, but
not much. Never sell the metal bits, its better to just bank them until
you can afford to smith more.

The key is too build up a big supply of bits so that your skewer
creation attempts run one after the other
as this seems to affect the skill rewarding.

So far the average cost has been 21-24gold per point of skill.

11 copper per water, 56 copper per ore, 89 copper per mold.
