Josh,
I cannot answer all of your questions, but, being a beginning smith in Qeynos, I can
anser some of it.

1.) There are basic smithing items all around Qeynos, except for ore and sharpening
stones. For these items, the closest place I've found is High Hold. At your level it's
a risky but not impossible trip. Below these answers I will give you a run down on the
best route, and who to see once you are there. For molds, and books, check in town (one
of the shops ... the Tin shop I think), and also the cottage in Qeynos hills, and one
of the cottages in East Karana.

2.) I've asked this question myself and didn't like the answer. I believe it really
depends on how many failures you will tolerate. The answers I have heard are above 40
skill you can TRY. At 80+ skill you can DO.

3.) Good question ... on my server they are all over the charts. From 5-20pp per piece
usually. Good luck getting a standard price. And remember that Qeynos smiths can charge
slightly more due to the lack of local materials. Again, I have not made any, but I
heard banded gorgets were the item to start with?

4.) No weapons ... I know it sucks doesn't it. I've heard that you can make pots, and
other miscellaneous items. Not seen molds for them yet myself though.

To get to High Hold from Qeynos:
-If under level 15 or so ... put EVERYTHING into the bank except food. You won't be
able to kill mobs on most of the maps so you will be avoiding combat. If you die or get
lost .... who cares, all of your items are in the bank.
-Leave Queynos north on the road. Zone into Qeynos Hills.
-After zoning in, follow the road to the marker, take the right path (going east) all
the way along the road and through the valley until you zone into East Karana.
-Continue following the road into East Karana to the first road marker, turn right,
following the road to the south until the road ends .. and you are running on the bare
plains. Go south all the way to the water.
-Turn east, following the water all the way across the map (a long way). Zone into West
Karana.
-Continue on this zone along the water. You will come to a large white bridge. Do not
cross it. Continue again along the water heading east. There will be one raider camp
along the way, just against the water. Go around it by swimming, or running into the
plains jut a little.
-You will come to a second bridge, this one will be wooden, and shaped like a large
linked chain. Turn south and cross this bridge .. in the middle you will zone into
South Karana.
-After exiting the bridge, follow the water left (again east) until you can just see
the mountains coming up. In the water is a large NPC you will want to avoid called
Bruune. Skirt areound him and go up the mountain face until you cannot climb any
higher.
-Turn South and follow the mountain all the way to a valley when you must run down. Do
not go into the valley ... it zones into the Gorge.
-Instead, go up the mountain on the other side, and once again go to the top, and
continue running south. You will eventually reach a corner where you will have to curve
around and run west. Continue running west along the mountain top to the next valley.
-Looking into the valley you will see road winding up on the right hand side. Take this
route and follow it up all the way around the mountains (quite a hike). You will zone
into the High Hold map.
-After zoning be careful!! There are gnolls and orcs on this map that are high level.
You most likely woudn't survive a fight with them so wait until they move (they seem to
patrol the area) and then sneak by. You will have to follow the left hand wall around
all the inlets in the rocks. If you see lights .. you are getting close to the Keep.
You will come to one inlet that leads to a tavern/inn. Come out of this and contiue
left to the next inlet which should be the hold main entrance.
-Go into the hold, zone to it's map, cross the coutyard and go into the keep. follow
the hallway down and back to a large chamber with columns in it.
-At the back of the chamber is a set of steps going down. They will lead to a mine
entrance. A miner just in the entrance sells sharpening stones as well as ore. Leaving
him, as you return to go back upstairs you should see the female bank teller also.
Place your stuff in here for safe keeping.
-Follow the route back home or do like I do and explore until you get killed, whereby
you respawn in Qeynos and go to the bank to retrieve your items.

- Don't get discouraged if you die ... I littered many of these maps with my corpse ...
you can too. :-p Try to use a concept called 'bordering' as much as possible. Mobs
don't seem to wander close to the map borders (mountains or water). Use this to your
advantage by running right up against the side of the map.

Note: When looking at the main hold entrance if you turn around, directly behind you,
you will see a path leading up into the rocks. This path actually takes you back to the
other side of High Hold that exits to the Kithcor Woods on the way to Freeport.
