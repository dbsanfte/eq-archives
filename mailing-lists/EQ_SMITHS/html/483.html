Hello Fellow Smiths

I am Shaepe, a high elf enchanter on Tholuxe Paells, and the
proprieter of Shaepley Armor, located in the tree high city of
Kelethin.

I've been having lot's of fun working on my smithing skills on a new
server, banded is actually in demand. When I was able to make my
first few pieces, not enough other players had any money to spend on
fancy armor, so sales were slow, but starting yesterday (server 1 week
old), WOW! I was kept very busy, and feel I have finally achieved
enough skill and customers to make a profit with my smithing. I found
I got much more response when I changed my auctions from "WTS small
banded gorget, bracer ..... 1pp per PC" to "Heros of Norrath, make
sure you fight in style. Buy Shaepley Armor. Small banded gorget,
bracer...... now in stock. 1pp per PC" and other such variations.
Putting my name in the ads really helped folks remember me, and I
started getting tells at other times, asking for armor.

My skill is now at 124. I started with sharpening weapons, then did
scalers, lanterns, skewers, pots, and now sewing kits. I tried my
first simple banded pieces when I reached a skill of 70. I do pretty
well with most pieces now, except those stubborn leggings and mail
pieces.

My question to you all is, now what? I thought about making forged
weapons (already trivial), but was disappointed to find the cost of
making the weapons (without failure) was more than what I could sell
them for. I want to be able to make the new fine plate armor, but
don't know what I should do to prepare for that. What skill level
should I achieve before then? How do I get that level? (more banded?)
Are there items I should be collecting now that I will need later?

All thoughts greatly appreciated.


Shaepe
