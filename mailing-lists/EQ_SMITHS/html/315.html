The problem with smithing and diminishing prices is the fact that once an
item is created, it usually never leaves circulation. Most, if not all
banded is passed down and sold to others when it no longer has a use. What
this means is that eventually, there will be little to no market for banded
AT ALL. What smiths and other crafters need is some sort of item degrade.
This is the only way to keep the trade skills working indefinitely and the
only way to remove items from circulation.

Also, another major problem with trade skills and the economy as a whole is
the fact that Verant said they would not allow for you to sell back to a
merchant at a profit, then went ahead and did it anyway. I was one very few
people that was a Master at Brewing and Pottery before "The Patch." Now
everyone and their mother is a brewer and a potter simply for the exploit.
Can you imagine what would have happened if this was true for smiths and
jewelers??

Verant needs to stick to its word about no profit from selling to merchants.
Already, no one sells good items any more for plat because plat is worthless.
The current economy just leads to more camping and kill stealing.
