Tim,
The guy you are looking for is IN highpass keep, in the middle of the
highpass zone. Upon entering the keep (after crossing the courtyard), go
toward the back to a room with large columns. From the back of that room, or
close by anyway, is a set of steps leading down to the prison and mines. Take
the steps down, turn left around the corner, and you should see some guy who
is a Miner. He sells all types of ore, plus sharpening stones.

In you need a bank, turn around and just before heading up the stairs you
should see a woman on your left. She is the banker so you may deposit you
items until your return to Qeynos.

Happy smithing.
