Well, yes and no. You can buy lantern molds in the Feerrott, but I'm not
sure what skill level they max out at. I'm assuming you're only interested
in scalars/skewers to increase your skill. So, it's a lot easier and
especially safer to stick with metal bits and lanterns until you max out on
them. You can buy large banded molds in the Feerrott as well.

To smith other items, you will have to eventually go to Neriak (or raise
your faction with Qeynos or Freeport, haha). The problem here is that the
ONLY safe place for Ogres and Trolls is in the Foreign Quarter. To get ore,
you have to venture into Neriak Commons, which isn't too dangerous, but one
wrong turn will ruin your day. Also, hanging out in Nektulos is hazardous
to your levels.
