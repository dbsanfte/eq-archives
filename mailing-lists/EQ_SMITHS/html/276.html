Yes, I have to agree.. Smithing is getting rediculas.. People don't understand what
and how smithing should be. First off is the time it took you to get to 110 or what
ever you got to before you started on banded. Secondly, there is your time. Your
time is worth money. If you stand around making and selling armor for 5 hours. Thats
5 hours of your time. I raise the price of my items a bit to add that in. Thirdly
there is failure rate. Ever smith will agree some items fail more then others. I'm
at 165 smithing skill and I still fail on stinkin masks and such more then I should.
Lastly of course is the cost of the materials. We all know that buying materials with
a high cha is the best bet, and all customers must assume all smiths have a 250 cha.
Well, I did create my "smithing character" with min/max smithing in mind. 110 Cha,
and 120 Int base. Since then I have raised my Int to 147 and Cha to 133 through
magical items. But this dosen't have to be passed down through to the customer. This
is like stream lining your factory. You do it for my profit, not to give the profit
to the customer. I have a really basic formula for prices.. (This has NO scientific
basis) 5pp per metal sheet an item takes. So, Boots, Bracers, Gorgets 5pp Masks,
Helms, Sleeves, Mantles, Belts 10pp I fudge on the cloak cause of the failure rate, I
charge 15pp for the cloak and again, I fudge for the Tunic and Leggings, for failure
rate and for popularity on the Tunic and Leggings. I charge 20pp for those 2. Now, on
almost all items I have left myself a SMALL margin (in my mind) to lower the prices
depending on the customer. I usually won't go to low on leggings, cloak or the 5pp
items. Well, if your ever on the Erollis Marr server in the freeport area.. you'll
more then likely hear this auction...

Medium Banded Mail Armor 4Sale! Gorgets, Bracers, Boots 5pp Masks, Belts, Helms,
Sleeves, Mantles 10pp Cloaks 15pp Tunics, Leggings 20pp (Full Suits 100pp)

Well, happy smithing to all..
