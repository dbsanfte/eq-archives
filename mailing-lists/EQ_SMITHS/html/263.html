Well when I learned my first lump sum was 100pp. I didn't bother with
sharpening but rather dumped 20points into the trainer. I then made Metal
bits LOTS of Metal Bits I needed them for EVERYTHING until around 85-95 when
I could actually make some money making banded. You DO NOT want to learn
using banded it costs too much to fail.

After Metal bits where trivial I started making scalers with all those bits
I had. After bits go triv though I failed a fair bit at them still. So I
only made 20 at a time then made scalers to improve my skill before making
20 more. After Scalers went trivial I started making Skewers. I failed ALOT
at skewers at first, there might be an item that is a little cheaper to fail
at but I am not sure? Anyway Skewers took me all the way to 115 after about
90 though I started making the easier banded items. Bracers and Gorgets.
After abou 100 I went to making banded on an as needed basis. I stayed with
skewers though for skill gain.

Now if someone will buy things at a price I can make some money at including
the fact that I am a busy person and my time is worth something as well as
my 220pp I initially invested I make almost anything if people try to haggle
I simply tell them I am not a charity and am not here to give them a deal I
am here to make some pp. I am selfish and I don't care. I am after all a
dwarf...

If you where to make a character just for making banded it would likely not
cost you as much but I thought what sense does it make to have some frail
wizard making armor?? They might damage themselves doing that if I am going
to make armor I am going to do it as Dwarf!!! I seem ro remember though that
elven chain was usually pretty good wasn't it?
