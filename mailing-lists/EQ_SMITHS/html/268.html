That will never happen, there will always be a person or two who will
undersell the others, and then the customers will want it for his/her price.
However, these people will forever be in the minority, since they will
quickly realize that they can not supply enough of the items to make a
decent profit. Even if they never fail, they simply will not make enough
money for them to want to continue to invest so much time into it. And even
if do? Well, kudos to them, as they have done what the player driven
economy is all about, undersold their competition. Sounds a bit like real
life doesn't it? But think about it this way, even if you do half the
business of those types, and you are charging double the price, you are
still making the same as them for half the work. As for people who always
want to buy cheaper and cheaper, well guess what, it's not going to go that
low, at least not consistently. Those that ask for such abysmally low
prices have probably seen someone sell for that price, either one of the
above mentioned lowballers, or someone who simply no longer needed the
items, and wanted to get rid of them quickly. If they continue to seek such
a low price, they will probably eventually get it, of course they may be in
their high teens, low twenties and still in mostly patchwork....
