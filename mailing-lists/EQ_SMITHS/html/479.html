To make leather backing you must have a small sewing kit. First you
put in 2 spiderling silks and combine to make silk thred. Then you
put the silk thred and a med. quality pelt of desired size into the
kit and combine. That is how you make the leather backing . Also a
note my tailoring skill is only 26 and i can make the backing about
half the time so get the skill up some if you do alot of plate mail.
