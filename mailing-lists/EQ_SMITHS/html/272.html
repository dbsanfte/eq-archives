The best thing about sharpening weapons for skill is that if you fail your
skill roll, you only lose the sharpening stone, you keep the weapon.
I think you should always make the easiest item that is not trivial. Start
by sharpening rusty daggers, then move to short swords, on up
to rusty two handed long swords which I hear help until skill 32.

You will only be sharpening about 3-4 weapons per skill point,
very cheap.
