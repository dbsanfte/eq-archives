In all the posts I have seen on this topic, I have seen one Items you
can make neglected completely. At the time I was doing this, I was
simultainiously working on Tailor and Blacksmith. The Item I am talking
about is stell boning, used for tailoring to make reinforced leather. This
is nice low level armor. It is better AC, as well as lighter than regular
leather.This requires the use of bricks of ore, but the costs don't work out
too differently from studs if you work out the math. I tried making stell
boning 20 times at lvl 26/27 smithing, don't remember which for sure. I only
succeeded 8 times, but got to skill 33 off just those attemtps. I think this
works out to be both cheaper and quicker than other things I have heard
suggested, and you can try to sell these to Tailors, too. I haven't played
that character in a while, so can't give any more info than this, but I hope
that this helps someone .
