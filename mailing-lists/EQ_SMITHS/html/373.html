Wow, a whole 2-3p? Good luck.

The pelts are EXTREMELY rare. As a tailor who has been searching for those
pelts, I can say that I don't think I would sell a pack for less than 20p.

that cheap.

In fact, the going rate on my server is 5-10p to make the pack for you if
you supply the pelt.

When I go around trying to find pelts, I offer 1p for the raw pelt. So far
I've been able to buy 5. That's after 4 weeks of searching. Every time I
enter or leaave a zone I auction for them, and soemtimes if I spend a long
time in a zone I'll auction again. All that, and only 5 pelts have turned
up. I've also spent a large portion of time killing bears. Not one single HQ
skin has fallen out of a bear I've attacked.

I pass all my skins on to my friend who is a better tailor than I. After 15
attempts at backpacks, we've only made 2.

Perhaps you need to rethink how much you are willing to pay.
