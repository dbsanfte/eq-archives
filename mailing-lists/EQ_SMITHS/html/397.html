Subject: Re: Finding patterns for studded leather armor


There are only one set of patterns for all tailoring. The patterns you use
for patchwork are the same ones used for making studded and reinforced
leather. The difference is the pelts used. You use ruined pelts for
patchwork, MEDIUM quality pelts for studded leather and HIGH quality pelts
for reinforced leather.
To make studded leather you need a large sewing kit, a pattern for the piece
you are making, a MEDIUM quality pelt and the number of studs that piece of
armor requires.
BUY the book on tailoring and read it as it tells you all these nice little
details about tailoring.
