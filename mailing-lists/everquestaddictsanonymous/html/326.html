I've never had a Shammy, but if you play
conservative (with SOW), you should be able to get away from
trouble. My only suggestion would be to find good groups
and stick with them as long as you can. That's how I
got most of my xp with my druid. A great place at
that level is LOIO. Sarnak or even goblin cave groups
would be good. You may still have some luck with the
orcs in HHP, but they may be too low for you now.
Unrest is fast paced and good xp and loot, BUT be
prepared to die a lot EVEN though you have sow. Anyone
with SOW is very welcome in Unrest groups. <br><br>The
new zone, Stonebrunt would be good for you too, but
it's a long trip, and no good if you can't find a
group, though at that level I think you can hunt the
highe end mobs in Warrens. <br><br>LOIO is best, a
little later you'll want to head to SK for Spires groups
(another fast xp and good loot spot), and then to the
Overthere around lvl 30. I went to OT at lvl 29 and got
through lvl 30 there. I still go back every now and
again. Good xp, horrible loot, can get boring but worth
it if you are in desperate need for xp. <br><br>Good
