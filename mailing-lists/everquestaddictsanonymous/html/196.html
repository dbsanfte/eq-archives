You probably shouldn't pick an Iksar just as much
as you shouldn't pick Troll, if not more. Iksar is
hated by more NPCs than Troll is, and Iksar hunting
grounds arent very good either. But its your choice,
although i highly recommend you go with a non-evil race
(i.e. NOT Dark Elf, Ogre, Troll or Iksar) and a
non-evil class (i.e. NOT Shadow Knight, Necromancer). If
you go with a good race/class combo it'll be much
much better, and i truly mean that. However.. if you
REALLY want to play Iksar, i'd go with Necromancer or
Monk.
