*yawn* Ok, i'll get serious then. (But if you're
not convinced from our passion about the game, then
maybe we have to appeal to our explanation of how great
the game play is). <br><br>Combat is only a part of
this game (though, i suppose, every character does a
lot of it). Casters mostly focus on spells, but you
haven't shown much interest (yet) in those classes, so on
to melee combat. Most combat is auto-combat. Click
to attack a certain enemy and your char
automatically swings its weapon to try to hit. You can hit,
miss, avoid getting hit, etc. depending on your skill
in the particular weapon type, your offense or
defense skill, and your char's dexterity/agility etc. You
still control moving around AND you can still choose
and cast spells (if you are a class that has spells).
You can also control and use special abilities, such
as backstab, kick, bash, slam (depending on what
race and class you are). At certain levels, certain
classes get automatic melee abilities, such as dual wield
(you can use 2 weapons), dual attack, dodge, parry,
etc.-- at very high levels, there are even more
abilities. MONKS get more special combat abilities, such as
eagle strike, roundhouse kick, feign death, flying
kick, etc. <br><br>Some weapons have special abilities
of their own, which will manifest during
combat.<br><br>At first, I though "auto-combat" was lame-- why
can't i just have a "hit" or "slash" button? But then i
learned that EQ is a social game, and it helps to have
auto combat so I can yell to my party during battle.
While bashing an Orc Oracle with my mace, I'm hit with
a poison spell and can yell to my cleric parner
"I'm poisoned, help!!!"<br><br>He can disengage from
melee, and cure my poisoned state, heal me up if needed,
and resume bashing. I have come to love the combat
style. Look, i've played a lot of games, as i'm sure we
all have. If you like combat, if you like adventurous
games with brains, if you like to socialize at all (and
even if you don't-- EQ is still good), if you like to
role play, if you like to be constantly amazed by new
creatures, items, battles, spells, places, stories,
people.... you cannot pass up this game. <br><br>If you like
N'sync concerts, barbie dolls, smoking crack in a dark
alley, playing canasta (sp) with your grandparents on a
saturday night, flying helecopters in Korea, changing
diapers at a day care for a living, dressing up like a
woman and performing a song review 3 nights a week, or
reading the Wall Street Journal on the commuter train---
this game is STILL probably for you!!! (Except for the
someone smoking crack in a dark alley, which who needs
crack when we have Evercrack, i've met all of the above
people on line). All walks of life, all races, genders,
classes, lifestyles enjoy this game. Some people get into
true roll play, some people spam the zones they're in
with "WHAAZZZZUUUUUPPPPPP????" The bottom line is that
the game rocks, AND with the new patches constantly
working at fixing (mostly) the game to make it better,
and new expansions every year to increase the game's
attraction (can you say Shadows of Luclin????!!!!), it is
literally a game you could play for Ever(quest).
