Great idea. Here's what I know so far:

Be careful in Butcherblok. All guards, and all NPC's at the dock *will* KOS,
but there are ways around it if your swimming's up to par.

Forget about the western half of Antonica until you're strong enough to make
it through Highhold pass alive. The guards in Rivervale(Death to all
halflings) will KOS, although the 2nd time this happened to me, they conned
"look at you threateningly" rather than "scowling and ready to attack", so
faction does improve(Kill orcs. lots of em).

Kithicor Forest: The Merchants in the first cabin to the right would sell to
me. /conned "apprehensive". The inkeep at the next cabin to your left will
KOS, but only if you walk through the door. On my way back to loot myself, i
was able to open the door, see him staring at me, and /summon safely.
