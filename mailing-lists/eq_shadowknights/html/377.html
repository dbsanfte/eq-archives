Grimrott is in S Karana just over the bridge. There's 2 spawns of him, one
where he's in full armor and carries the scythe - about level 30-32. And
another where he's naked, but sometimes carries the scythe - about level 28.
As an SK you can (or should be able to) go up and speak to him, by doing so
he tempts you into trying to kill him heh, saying if you defeat him you'll
get his weapon.
