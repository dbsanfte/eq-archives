Subject: Lord Grimrot Quest


The Grimrot quest is part of a series of interlinking quests given by an npc in the necromancer's guild in Qeynos. It starts with a task to kill Drosco, a ghoul nearby in the aqueduct. You then give Drosco's letter to the npc and get a quest to kill Frumpy Irontoe in Fish's Ale. Give Frumpy all the mead he asks for, follow the hints he give, and soon he will run away frightened. If you get him in a spot out of sight of the guards he is easy to kill. If you get his head take it back to the guild and you will get in return a skull to take to Lord Grimrot.

Yesterday I happened upon an aviak killing Grimrot's pet skeleton, but that is the closest I have come to seeing him. Did manage to see some Grimrot dialogue though.
