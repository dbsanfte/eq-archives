I think if you were a newbie and wanted to start out as a shadow knight,
the dark elf is the perfect choice. One thing is, they are pretty common,
from what I see, even more so than Necromancers. There are ALWAYS Dark
Elves around, so it is easier to make friends and find alliances than the
other dark races. But there are those still who pursue anything that will
make your gameplay less enjoyable, so make your choices wisely.

Human Shadow Knights seem the be the rarest of the SKs around, or so that I
have seen. But this is one reason I enjoy playing it, the challenge, and
the fact there isnt much of my kind around.
