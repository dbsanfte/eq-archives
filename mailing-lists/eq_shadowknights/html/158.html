Blackened armor can be obtained at Runnyeye. At level 10, I had full banded
armor except for the cape and helm. The helm I had was steel plate and the
cape was leather. Then I decided that the plate helm looked ugly and sold it
to purchase blackened armor (tunic, pants, belt, crown, bracer, sleeves,
glove). The blackened armor would be the best equipment in terms of coolness
and AC. But when you reach a higher level, you would then be obligated to
switch back to banded (because the blackened is heavy and the loot you start
getting becomes heavier also) or bronze for the AC.
