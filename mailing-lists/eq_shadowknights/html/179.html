Depending on your level, Lavastorm is never the place to hunt for money. At
low teens, I suggest you go to Najena to fight skeletons. Those skeletons
give better loot than the ones in Lavastorm considering the toughness and
downtime. At later levels, orc camps in Ecommons are good for experience;
which you will need to kill the Dervishes at high teens. The Dervishes are
what will get you most of the money.
