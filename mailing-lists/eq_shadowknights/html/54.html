Well, it depends if you want to play for roleplaying or stat wise. Stat wise
darkelf shadowknight is probably the 2nd worst race/stat combo in the game
(only worse is erudite paladin). You're primarily a warrior so you will need
high str and stam, both of which the darkelf has little of. My best bet for
race/class combo is ogre, I pumped all mine into int since he already had a
large str/stam, but some into agility or dex would be ok too.
