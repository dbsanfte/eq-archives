Hail dark bretheren,

For all I know, the beetle eye quest and the paladin quest are the only
ones for us in Neriak. They are important in the aspect that they raise our
faction with The Dead so that other quests may be acccesed, such as the Ring
of The Dead quest, ( provides a Gate to Neriak ). If nothing else, that box
is a free container to hold 10 beetle eyes. =) I want to do the mail
delivery quest when I hit lvl 30 ( and can invis ) to see the faction hits I
get. If quests are your thing make a barbarian, Halas is FULL of quests and
a few of them are cool and rewarding. If you are going to do the beetle
quest, do it now. Soon you are too high a level to get it from the Necro in
the Lodge of the Dead. Be carefull what other quests in Neriak you do,
ANYTHING that hurts your standing with the dead must be avoided at all
costs.
As far as the team servers go, I will under no circunstances make an SK
there. It is rough enough being one on Rallos Zek up untill you aquire
sufficient power. The team servers are made for mages and mages only, and
since the SK is my favorite class I do not see myself going. I did log in
as a newbie warrior in Nectulos, discovered 234 other newbies in the zone,
and returned to my beloved Rallos Zek. The only TRUE roleplay server, where
your allies are the ones you make and not the ones you cannot attack.
