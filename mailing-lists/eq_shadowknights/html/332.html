With regard to the pet issue, I saw the same thing in dueling as my SK back
in my CandyLand days on Veeshan. Now, every once in a while on Zek, as a
Necromancer, I see the same thing, so its not just a SK issue. I'm not sure
whether its a bug, but I've also heard that pets will sometimes be less than
willing to attack something that cons red to them. My latest experience with
this was with Codil the Naked Monk outside Freeport. He was only yellow to

change some code, at least on Zek. If we can summon a pet, we should have
complete control over it and it should attack anything we direct it to,
whether that something is a Large Rat or a Hill Giant.
