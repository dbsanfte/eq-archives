Subject: I have sent a feedback suggestion to Verant


Taking the view of a SK being an anti-paladin, I asked for a review on the
SK balance regarding Harm Touch vs. the paladin's Heal. For both classes,
how about harming / healing the total of their unaltered life force (hit
points total) ? As an alternative, consider simply slaying outright / fully
healing (or within 1-10%) their level or below, and for their level or
higher no more than their unaltered hit point max. I added to both
suggestions that neither should be resisted, or both should have equal
chance by level for failure as this is a 'divine gift' (IMHO).

Perhaps the chance for failure would be related to faction. that might make
for nice role-playing ;-), e.g..: Been whacking a lot of evil crits getting
your faction up to walk into town ? Hmmm might not be favourable to the
ghods and your deadly touch might fail at a critical time. Same with Heal
for paladins, and perhaps even more so because of their goodie-goodie
ethics; whack a bunch of innocents for some item and, whoops, dang heal
fizzled, 'Rodcet Nife has frowned on you' - hehehe.

Thoughts ?
