Hi All,

I'm not sure that this survey will really be useful. I mean its pretty
obvious us SKs will want more powerful spells so we can crush the other puny
classes like the bugs they are ; ) ...who among us would oppose this?

If we want things to change, the important issue to raise is WHY we need
more powerful spells and abilities.

For example, there has been some discussion about the matching of lay hands
vs. harm touch. Personally I see the balace as being pretty good. First of
all, we have the advantage of pets to help us... Paladins don't have them
and all that extra damage they do adds up. Secondly, I feel that being able
to do 700 damage in one shot without a saving throw would probably have some
serious game balance implications. Sure it might be a little fairer for
dueling, but you could take out some very tough creatures with that ability
that you wouldn't have been able to before.... this translates into much
easier xp for us and ultimately more SKs that reach higher levels faster.
The result of this is an imbalance between the classes. I think that this
is the reason why Harm Touch has been somewhat penalized. Is there anything
about this you guys think I'm missing?

I would also like to say that I think the abilities we get are pretty good.
I participated in beta so I had an opportunity to play some other classes
like warrior and shaman. They each have things that are cool about them and
things that suck. Warriors can take down tougher monsters for their level
but you end up sitting around for a half hour healing after each fight if
you don't have a healer. Shamans can heal but they are pretty weak compared
to the warrior. These tradeoffs are what make the game interesting.... for
each class you get to exploit some cool things about it but you have to cope
with the not so cool things. ShadowKnights have similar advantages and
disadvantages, but all in all they are pretty fun to play. It all depends
on how you want to play the game or the style of playing that you have the
most fun with. I think this is the way it should be.

So, in summary of my ramble, I think that Verant put a lot of time and
thought into balancing the classes in the game so that they would be fun to
play. If we want to change them, we had better come up with some good,
concrete reasons that make sense. We can't just go to them and say "This
sucks, change it".

Just my $0.02...



Kudzul
