This is just my opinion, but it all depends on how you want to play.
Ogres -group.
Trolls - solo.

Humans and Dark Elves in my opinion don't make very good shadowknights.
They have higher int than Trolls and Ogres but have nowhere near the Str.
We are 3/4 warriors 1/4 necros so I wouldn't worry to much about int. Also
they can't bash unless thay have a shield and all the Shadow Knights only
weapons are 2h. On E'ci it seems that most Shadow Knights are Dark Elf
followed by Humans. I guess people just like the look of them better. I'll
list the advantages and disadvatages of all of them and just pick the one
you want to play.

Ogres-Infravision, bash, highest stats except int.
-Only can go to two towns(Ogre and DE), Exp penatly.
Trolls-Infravsion, bash, regeneration, 2nd highest stats except int.
-Only can go to two towns(Grobb and DE) Biggest exp penatly.(My
opinion, hardest character to play)
Dark Elves-Ultravision, highest int, can go to all 3 evil towns.
-No bash, low stats, exp penatly
Humans-Exp bonus cancles out Exp penalty, can travel much easier than the
others.
-Low stats, no bash., no infravision


Everyone has there own opinions on which class is the best, so just pick
the one you will get the most enjoyment playing.
