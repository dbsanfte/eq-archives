Point well made. Yet, there is in fact a PvP server with unique rules not
found on any of the others for PvP ballance. Ok, I am calm again. Let me
ask this of you:

How do you know what was intended upon design?

We ARE ok in player vs monster. My gripe is that I want ALL of my powers
to be brought to bear when commanded, that includes the pet. Duels exist on
the other servers as well and I have to forget about my signature ability
while in one. That is foolish. Regardless of what was "intended". We are
the only class that cannot use all of our powers all of the time. How do
you justify that?

I will not complain again if the pet would just CONTRIBUTE, it makes THAT
MUCH OF A DIFFERENCE!! A single interrupt on a wizard, just ONE will allow
me to flee or even consider fighting her! Instead it just stands there
looking silly as I die. Against a Ranger or Paladin the pet has no
offensive value BUT, do they know I am an SK if they haven't seen me? Will
they take the time to consider it and realize its green? In either case,
opening with the pet puts THE PET in their target window, not me. I can now
use Darkness and Fear without expecting a Blindness to get me because I need
to be trageted first! Suddenly my level 8 pet becomes a true treasure even
when I am 21st level waiting on the next one. As it stands, an SK in a
duel is a foolish SK.

Roleplay IS infact what this game is about. Teaming up and facing the
unknown. There are times though, when the gloves need to come off. When it
comes down to true grit and a keen strategy. It is THEN that we are hurt.

I still love my DE female SK. If I were stacking stats and not playing a
role I would always take the Ogre ( not knocking on The Horde or
anything...heh ).

Hey, wizard DD casting times have dropped. How does THAT figure into
roleplay? If you feel the game is being reduced to hack and slash, you're
right. Bitch at Verant.

When is Microsoft releasing their game?
