Yes, I also disagree when people say Ogres and Trolls make the best Shadow
Knights. At higher levels, one would use alot of mana for the various
reasons. If you play a Shadow Knight as a warrior, then yes, Trolls and
Ogres are WAY better. However, before engaging into combat, I would not cast
Engulfing Darkness and start bashing. At later levels, spectres hit for 90s
and it is impossible to tank anymore; thus, we are required to be more
strategic than just continuously bash. Therefore, one would use Darkness >
Fear > Heart > Blood > Strength > Diease (swap usage order according to
situation, and repeat some of the DoT to renew them if possible) before
engaging into melee. As seen in the technique above, one requires to have as
much mana as posssible; this is when the Human and Dark Elf Shadow Knights
have an advantage over the large races. Sure, at low levels, Ogres and
Trolls are better simply because there is not many DoT to throw around. But
once stacking is possible, you want to drain as many HP from the mob as
possible before melee. The reason is because you cannot tank anymore without
a cleric at high levels. To all the Dark Elves out there, do not be
discouraged - there is a reason why the Darkness spell slows down the
target, it is to allow us to stack more and cast before engaging into melee;
do not bash like the Ogres and Trolls.
