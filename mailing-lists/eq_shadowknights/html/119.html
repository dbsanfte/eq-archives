Ok I think I'll shed some light on this subject. Intelligence is not how
SK's mana is decided. Enchanters, Magicians, Necromancers, and Wizards mana
are decided with Intelligence. For Clerics, Druids, Rangers, Shamans,
Paladins, and Shadow Knights, Wisdom is the factor that decides the amount of
mana you get...Higher wisdom..earlier cast those spells at lvl 9. Hope this
helps.
