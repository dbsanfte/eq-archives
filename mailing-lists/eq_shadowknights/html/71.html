Hey Lloyd,
Xillith here, I am relatively new at the SK myself but I have
realized a few things:

1) SAVE THE HARM TOUCH! You will need it in a pinch and nothing
freaks out a PKer more than the big SHAZAM!

1a) The touch does as a rule of thumb (not recorded anywhere) Level x
10 dammage + 1-3 pts per level. So, 11-13 dammage per level SK per
touch. At fifth level, I dropped a human fighter who was "yellow" to
me from 3/4 life to dead, BOOM!! He had no idea I was an SK until
that moment of truth, so savor it. Remember though, the touch is
treated as a spell and CAN BE SAVED AGAINST for partial or NO
dammage!!! So be carefull around Dwarves and other such highly
resistant little crappers cause you will waste a good ability and wait
24 game hours to get it back!!

2) Use anon. Harm touch is your ace. If averyone thinks you are a
warrior, they will never see it comeing. Save it for the END of the
fight as a COUP DE'GRAS. Using it too early could scare your prey
away.

3) Do NOT use all of your training each level. When you are ready to
use a skill craft, dump the points of your choice into it and watch
you expertise take off!

4) Before commiting to a skill craft, make sure your home town
supports it. Example, Neriak doea not have the complete list O' junk
needed to make bows. So, Dark Elves seeking fletching will be very
dissapointed when they discover that they need to be at least 13th
level to cross the terrain alone needed to get them to a complete
store only to be either attacked on sight or ignored by the merchants.
I have no idea what the Troll and Ogre lands offer.

5) Take crap from NO ONE!!! Join a guild, and display your colors
proudly. LIE ( you're evil so who cares) get into a duel and fry your
opponent with the " BIG WHAMMY".

6) Realize that you are hated even more than the necromancer! Yep,
you are EQs' enemy #1 and ALWAYS WILL BE. So expect to die and die
often. Therefore, buy bags at your first opportunity and keep your
stuff in there (bags cannot be looted). Also, if you are about to
loose a fight and you have a weapon you like, open your inventoery and
move it into your bag!! Your killer will be pissed off when the
armour or weapon that they wanted is safe in your bag and out of their
reach!!!! You cannot protect any cash you had though, thats life.
Have discipline, leave a few empty spaces in your large sac or
backpack for your prized equipment. If you disagree, consider the
cost of replacing a Two-Handed sword over a friggin bear pelt you
scavenged!

JOIN A GUILD!! REALLY!! DO IT!! Not an @sshole guild either, shop
around and remember, you are who your friends are, and that could mean
life sucks!! If you play a female Dark Elf (me) JOIN THE BLOOD COVEN
and tell 'Brin' the Grand Matron, that Xillith sent you. I need the
bennie points :)

SO! Kill proudly, kill often, and play a Dark Elf female because we
are the coolest. Enjoy EQ, everyone else does!!
