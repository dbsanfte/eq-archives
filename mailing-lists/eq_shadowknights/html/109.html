well,
I can only speak for why i play the shadow knight and how I believe the
shadow knight should be played. To me the SK is a dastardly devil, cruel,
ruthless, and a true threat in battle. But at higher levels he is weaker
hand to hand than the warrior, as he should, and at all levels he is an
(aguable) match to the paladin. I haven't met one that I haven't killed, if
killing the charecter was my intention.
The SK was never ment to go toe to toe with the light warrior, he was
ment to bide his time and strike when the paladin (or any one else) is the
weakest, in battle with another foe, or with the SKs pet and shout
"Muhaaahahahahaa!!!" as the other player cries "Why? Why? I like Dark
Elves! Aiieeeee..."

ahem

So, the bottom line is theatrics and role play. One can play the
dastardly fiend, or one cannot. It is that simple. If you seek a persona
for PK or staight war gaming, play a wizard or warrior. Two dimmentions are
for you. If you see your charecter as the Scourge of Freeport or The
Warlord of Neriak , a charecter loved by friends and LOATHED by all others,
the SK is for you.
The key is Role Play not PK. You will find that other players will not
feel so bad WHEN they die if they faced XILLITH, Lady of Pain and Dark
Destroyer. I even made some interracial friends by PKing them with an Erol
Flynn air >:) (even paladins...heh heh) They come back for more and
challenge you to elaborate duels and grand wars. I rescued one Paladin from
a kodiak only to let him die by my hand because "Only Innorruk deserves
they glory of this kill !!" and he loved it!!
On a side note, only being looted by the one who kills you is nice. I
despise looters. If Xillith kills and loots your charecter expect an offer
for fair trade immediately, unless you are rude. As the Bard Leatherwolfe
on Rallos Zek. I killed her, with a buddy, and took her kite shield, (heh
heh) a kite shield is quite the setback so i ofered to return it for trade.
I recieved a Mino axe for the shield and a tarnished spear, and gained a
friend to hunt in Lavastorm. YEP, we hunted together. Why not right?
Innorruk was sated.

So role play and love this class, it is the best.
