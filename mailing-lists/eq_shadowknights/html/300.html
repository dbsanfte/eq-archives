First of all, the paladin's spells are just as likely to be resisted as
ours. I've been rooted and blinded before, and it's very, very difficult to
keep someone under both for any significant period of time. 5 rounds to
"finish off the pet" will be more than sufficient to get out of at least 1,
if not both. An actual problem is that sometimes pets for SKs don't work in
PvP. The important part of the darkness spells is the slowing more than the
damage. Starting a fight with siphon strength, which I've never seen
resisted, does a 20 point switch in strength balance and gives the SK an
advantage in melee. When the fight comes down to melee, which they all
eventually do, the SK is going to deal more damage much more quickly. I do,
however, concede the paladins have bows part.

I have not ever lost to a paladin, and I don't attack anyone who is going to
or has just got their corpse. It's not that I play an honorable character,
as much as respect for another player. My main point to all of this is that
I do not believe SKs are weak, which you agree with at the beginning of your
message to a degree. I also do not believe SKs are significantly more
powerful than paladins, by the same token. I'm saying we have a comparable
level of power, except, perhaps, for HT being resisted.
