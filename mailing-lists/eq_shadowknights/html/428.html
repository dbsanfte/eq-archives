OK

It starts with being able to chat with Gizdean Gix in West Commons. If you
cannot do this you cannot have the ring or scithe.

1) Give Gix the following message:

"utalk adarev otcin"

He gives you a note.

2) Take the note into Befallen and give it to Skeleton Lrodd. He says some
gak and releases Commander Windstream. Windstream is a big zombie who is of
ill temperment. I do not know what level he is. I do know that he was blue
to a lvl 33 SK (me), and is built like a brick S*** House.

3) Windstream spawns by the false wall in level 3, by the chair. He doesn't
roam that I know of. Kill him and get the stem of the Thex Mallet. The
stem is a container, so allow a slot for one.

4) There are two other parts to the mallet. The head and the handle thingy.
They are only found by the elf skelleton that stands by the locked door to
lvl 3. If the elf skell isn't there a dread bone will be. Kill the dread
bone and wait, the spawn rate is about 6 or 7 min. (yea, I had alot of free
time *frowns*)

5) OK, now you have all three parts to the mallet. Put the head and handle
in the stem and combine. The Thex Mallet is created.

6) Boogie back to Neriak Third Gate, to the Lodge of the Dead. On the
second floor is the SK Loveal (sp). Give him the mallet. He will either
give you the Reaper of the Dead: Lore 2H slash 12 40 +4str with one charge
of Complete Healing(YAY), or the Ring of the Dead.

Mind you, I did the quest about 18 times. I am warmly regarded with the
Dead. In fact, I stoped getting favorable faction hits (could this be
why?). Regardless, the Reaper sells to merchants for over 10plat and the
complete heal (much like Lay Hands) is a priceless ability for our non
healing class.

I only sold one charged Reaper, and that was to a very senior human SK.
PLEASE dont use it to twink light races!! It is for US and US alone. If I
ever see a High Elf wizard "lay hands" on himself I will loose my mind,
because I will be pretty sure how he did it. But i firmly believe that once
you take the time to wait for Gix, get the mallet, and make the Reaper you
will be very reluctant to part with your full heal.

The SPENT Reaper sells to players from 80 to 110 plat on rallos zek. It has
outstanding stats and is in my opinion up to par with the Grob Cleaver and
it has a better "in character" look to it. I made about 1.5K plat selling
spent Reavers to players and sold a full one for 600(rofl). This quest is
one I will do many times, even though i have the Ring.

THE RING:

In a phrase...kicks ass.

One charge of bind affinity, rechargeable by a merchant in the lodge of the
dead. 80plat. I do not recall the name of the merchant, but if you ask
them "what is the ring of the dead" one of them will offer to recharge it.
The recharger is a female one the first floor of the Lodge of the Dead. Now
if it sounds like a DE only thing, you're right. The ring is SK, Necro and
dark elf only. (sorry all you Humans Ogres and Trolls, you get to walk a
little farther from bind point....hehe)

Now here I am, a lvl 34 SK bound IN Permafrost. The Ring looses some of
it's usefullness since I often run with Clerics who can Resurect me back to
my body. BUT, for those solo excursions and groups with only druids/shaman
or rangers/paladins to heal...it is a real gem.

SO DON'T GIVE UP!!!!

It is out there, it is a working quest, and it is SOOO sweet.

Also, The Dark Reaver is a weapon to die for folks. Get it get it get it.
Hunt Guk until exhasted, by the time you can get it from the Assassin you
will be a Froglok killing expert and the owner of many new levels and items
to boot. Guk is made for us, no joke. Practice your evocations so that you
can Ward Undead right beside your necros and clerics, casues Dead Side is
where it's at AND we get Expulse Undead at 39 which is just iceing on the
cake.

For all you young SKs out there, DONT GIVE UP!!! I almost quit in disgust
many times and I am on a PvP server, so I have that to deal with at times as
well. But I love my charecter so much I stuck with it. The SK suffers SO
much at low levels, but once you crest over 20 and get your dual attack, and
then 22 where you get some minor healing and other usefull spells if you're
rescourcefull...there is no looking back. I often have 130 dammage sets if
I DONT reposte, and no dual wield weapons anyone can get untwinked at my
level come close to compeating. Our pet now contributes in PvP (THEY
LISTENED!!) and I find my class being more welcome in groups than the
paladin or ranger.

PvP? Dont get me started. We rule. Warrior with big whacker and pet =
move along to next target. Our ability to Shadowstep and lay layer after
layer of DoT is truely dreaded, and the harm touch means I dont have to
personaly kill them all the way. PvP with other tanks is tough due to their
hit points and resistances, but I eat casters for lunch (except those damn
enchanters, they suck! But Pumice Stones are a spiffy solution to Charm)

wow, I am just going on and on!!

Enough. Lets revive this board and get the newer players in the swing of
the best class in all of Norrath, the dreaded Shadowknight!!!
