I'm a human shadowknight based out of Qeyos, about the only one i can
see that does not walk around with their "anonymous" flag on. (Baddies that
do that, that make themselve anonymous just piss me off. Nothing personal
but I figure we need to at least see each other.)

I've just been killed five times in about five minutes. This sucks. I know it is
supposed to be hard, difficult, but this almost goes overboard. Getting my
corpse took an hour, or I should say getting all of my corpses took an hour.
And this is worth it? I get a pet, a crappy harmtouch (I think the math is
pretty clear on this) the inability to bash with a dark reaver (my fault for picking
a human, but I just don't want to be an ogre/troll/darkelf), some spells that
don't look particularly special until I'm 50th level (and for those of you who think
that we do get good weapons compared to paladins, the best weapon in the
game, firey avenger, is paladin only), and the pleasure of being waxed by any
inkeeper that comes along. I realize that some of this presents rolyplaying
opportunity, but christ, what are the payoffs?

What I'm looking for is a damn pep talk? I'm seriously considering dumping
myself and starting over as a paladin just because it has got to have a better
payoff. Somebody convince me not to. Please.

Maybe I'll just change races. Maybe I'll quite whining. (I said this was a rant,
afterall.) Nah, I probably won't quite whining.

Silkke
Veeshan (maybe)
