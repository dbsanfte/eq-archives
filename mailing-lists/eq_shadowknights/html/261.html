Xillith:

Combine Two Handed Swords cannot be purchased by Dark Elves. Because the
vendors that sell them do not like us. However, a Shiny Brass Halberd is
already better than a Combine Two Handed Sword, so there is no need for you
to get a Combine.

When you are level 20 or higher, you will see yourself doing more damage
with your Halberd. However, if you are above 20, I suggest you go to
Befallen and camp for the Bone-Bladed Claymore (which could be put into a
bag).

Rune Totem Staves can be obtained easily at Gnoll Shamans at Lake Rathe, you
can also get small bronze armor off of Dry Bone Skeletons at the same place
too.


Psion:

Some players could have easily gotten you a Fine Steel weapon for 5pp. Right
now, however, I think you should start saving up for banded armor. After you
have a full suit of it, a BBC (200pp) should be your next goal. When you
have reached mid 20s, you should go to Najena to collect large bronze pieces
off of Ogre captains. At high 20s, you should go camp for the Journeyman
Boots that is also in Najena (Jboots are not tradable; therefore, you must
loot it yourself).
