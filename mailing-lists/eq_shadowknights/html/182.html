Do NOT buy magic weapons. Many creatures drop them. the shiny brass
Halbred is dropped by the named orc in HH pass, East side. Split Paw shaman
drop Rune Totem staves and the named Gnolls in Paw have magical daggers,
this is a newbee dungeon too.

Dont want to use a dagger? TOUGH. Magic is VERY hard to come by,
especially the heavy tank weapons. Make due with what you can get. I had
to become proficient in 2H blunt because I happened across a Rune Totem
staff when I killed a High Elf in Nectulos at level 9. Immagine the
confusion when people asked me for cures and I had to explain why an SK is
using a staff.

Be smart.
