Verant has not changed anything in regards to fletching supplies in the
"evil" lands. You still cannot buy ALL of the components you need in
Neriak, Oggok or Grobb. You will have to either develop contacts who can go
into town to buy you what you need or you sit around with a wasted skill.
There is supposedly a centaur merchant, who tolerates Teir'Dal, in a centaur
village in the Karanas. I would not venture there until you are around 15th
Level or have a strong party to travel with.

There is also a shop outside of Oggok that sells bowyering components but I
have yet to locate it. I have a friend who dumped 20 training points into
fletching and decided to forego crafting arrows altogether and went straight
to bows. It is expensive but it is all we can realistically do for now. My
suggestion is, if you have your heart set on being a fletching SK, you
select a human to play as so you have access to the components (provided the
human merchants even sell to you)...
