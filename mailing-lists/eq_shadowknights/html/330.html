Xillith:

Actually, in Player versus Monster situation, we excel. Because we have a
pet, ~250 damage DoT (level 30 Darkness and Blood) and melee, Shadow Knights
can deal the most damage in the shortest amount of time compared with other
hybrids. However, when one considers the amount of time the Shadow Knight
needs to heal after the battle, one would then see our weakness. Although
warriors has to do the same thing, they do not take an experience penalty.
At this point, one can clearly see that the Shadow Knight will take the
longest time to level up.

In addition to the above, I believe Xillith raised a good point about the
pet being jumped on by every monster in the dungeon. Most of us will agree
that the pet is completely useless in the dungeons, and might even get us
killed in the process. Now, take into consideration that the pet, who is
suppose to balance out the 'kick' of a Ranger or Warrior, might also take
some of our experience along with them, one can then see that we got a class
that needs to be redone

In conclusion, take note of the following:
- exp penalty
- bad factions
- useless pet indoors and somewhat outdoors
- the only hybrid that cannot heal to compensate for the downtime
- resistable HT
- Darkness spells are resisted ALOT even on blues, more on yellows and reds
(I had it resisted 4 out of 4 times on a low blue)

Some may say it gets better on higher levels, but what I've stated applies
to all levels (and this same thing is said from another 48 Shadow Knight on
the same server, so all this is based on anything before the 49th level
spells). Is it worth it to go on? I would not. But would one want all that
time that went into the SK to be wasted? No.
