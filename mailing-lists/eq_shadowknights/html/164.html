From my experiences, a Shadow Knight cannot be played like a ranger or a
paladin. Because we cannot hit for as much damage as a ranger, nor do we
have heals to heal ourselve after battle, we need to use other tactics to
win. The way to do this is to strategically use the darkness, fear, syphon,
disease spells that we have. From this, I have a tendency to relay more on
magic than others. In other words, intelligence plays a heavier role in our
class than one would think. Dark Elf, being the most intelligent of the
given races, thus provide a tool for victory. In addition, Dark Elves Shadow
Knight looks sooooooooooooo cool with black armor and a scythe (too bad the
Dark Reaver is not a scythe . . . ).
To compare Troll with a DE, Trolls get killed on sight in some places,
whereas Dark Elves are spared from death. Nevertheless, they each have their
own good. But in the end, it all depends on how you play and how cool you
want to look.
