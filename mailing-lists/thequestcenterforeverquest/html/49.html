Ogre Meat Cleaver Quest <br><br><br>1) Talk to
Chef Dooga to get the quest. Dooga will give you some
meat to take to The Gobbler in Neriak.<br><br>2) Head
to Neriak Foreign Quarter and give the meat to The
Gobbler. The Gobbler will give you an Ogre Meat Cleaver on
occasion.<br><br>Dark Shield of Ebon Quest <br><br><br>1) Talk to
Hekzin in the Rogues guild of Neriak to get the
quest.<br><br>2) Head to Nektulos Forest and collect 3 moss snake
skins and 3 teeth. <br><br>3) Return the items to
Hekzin and he will occasionally give you a Dark Shield
of Ebon.<br><br>Ring of the Dead Quest
<br><br><br>1) Head to Befallen and kill Elf Skeletons to
retrieve the Thex Mallet Head and Thex Mallet
Hilt.<br><br>2) Head to the 3rd level of Befallen and kill
Windstream to retrieve the Thex Mallet Stem.<br><br>3) Put
the Thex Mallet Head and Thex Mallet Hilt into the
Thex Mallet Stem (container) and hit combine. You will
create the Thex Mallet.<br><br>4) Take the Thex Mallet
to Lovehill in the Neriak Necromancers' Guild and
you will receive either the Ring of the Dead or the
Reaper of the Dead.<br><br>Tell me if ya need
anymore.<br>Good Luck and Happy Hunting!
