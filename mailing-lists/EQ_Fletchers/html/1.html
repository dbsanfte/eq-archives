Subject: Bow making


Well folks at a skill of around 60 you should be able to make Rough Elm/Silk
bows...
At a skill of 80 you should be able to make Rough Ashwood/Silk bows
I have yet to make a Carved Elm/Silk bow

Each time you increase the type of string (hemp to linen, linen to silk) you
decrease Damage by 1 and Delay by 4

each time you improve the bow staff type (hickory to elm, elm to ashwood) it
seems that you increase damage by 3, range by 25, and delay by variable amount

Carving appears to decrease delay by 5 when using a whittling knife according
to stats given to me by a very highly skilled fletcher (mid 90's)
