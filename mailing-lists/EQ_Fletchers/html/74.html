To my knowledge there is no archery range in-game. The skill must be
increased by using it.

When I was level 5 I put 14 training points into archery. I then used
archery as my first strike against all opponents and continue to do so each
time I level until my archery is maxed. I am now a level 11 ranger and my
archery skill maxed at 60 before I reached the first bubble toward level 12,
so I know this method works.

With a good "jump start" archery will max each level with use.
