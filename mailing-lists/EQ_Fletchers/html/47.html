Yeah, the simple truth is that Fletching still rocks. I finally got my skill up so elm is trivial, and I am seeing more money than I've ever seen in my life. Sure, 7pp may not seem like a lot to most of you, but to a poor little wood elf in Kelethin, it's a fortune. And, even if I never get my skill up to 200, or even 100 for that matter, I've still got a pretty decent bow and that patch HAS made it a lot more convenient to make trivial arrows for personal use. Not to mention the bottom line of why trade skill were put in in the first place, to enhance the role playing experience. The ONE thing that would just about make me cream if they let us do is be able to name our goods. Either like in UO, where it would just put your name on it, or to actually be able to give it something creative, instead of "Rough hickory recurve," or what not. Not only would it enhance roleplaying, but it would improve the market as well. For instance, if I wanted a darkwood bow, and I knew someone who had one, I could just find out the name on it and track him down.
