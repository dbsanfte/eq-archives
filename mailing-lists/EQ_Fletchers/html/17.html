i recently spent some time building fletching again...

at skill 82 the combo Field, Bone, Shield, Small becomes trivial
at skill 102 the combo Hooked, Wood, Round, Large becomes trivial
the cheapest combo i could find that gave skill at 102 was Field, Wood, Wood
Vane, Large (approx 1g7s)
then at 122 (when above becomes trivial) i moved to Field, Wood, Bone Vane,
Large and it still cheapest... (approx 3g2s)

unfortunately the arrows made are rather less than usable so sell them
back... you lose about 25 to 33 % but keeps ya going a bit longer...
