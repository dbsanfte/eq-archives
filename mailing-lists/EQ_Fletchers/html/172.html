For those who have been asking about how to level, I started with all basic
parts, large nook, wood shaft, regular point and round fletch. When this
became trivial I upgraded one piece to the next expensive type and
continued till that became trivial then upgraded another piece.

The nook and fletch upgrade increased the distance value of the arrows I
made. The shaft and tip increased the damage of the arrows.

The basic parts made a class 1 arrow. By using two medium parts I now make
class 2 arrows. I am currently using a medium nook, parabolic fletch, bone
shaft and hooked tip to make class two arrows and at level 74 are still not
trivial.
