You take 1 dwarven wire, 1 spiderling silk, 1 micro servo, and 1 wood chip, and place them in the bag. You hit combine, and you'll end up with a "Pack of materials" on your cursor. You give this to Maesyn, and he will give you the Trueshot staff. You then put 1 trueshot staff, 1dwarven wire, 1 micro servo, and 1 planing tool in the kit and hit combine. If you succeed, you get a trueshot bow, if you fail, you get your planing tool back. So, to make the bow, you need the following: 2 dwarven wire, 1 spiderling silk, 2 micro servos, 1 wood chip, and a planing tool. I would recommend getting multiples of these (except the planing tool) to make the bow. I have heard of a 110 making it on the 4th try, and a 112 making it on the 6th, bit I (140) failed 10 times, and I even talked to a 196 who didnt get it until the 4th try. Good luck!
