The best way to raise your archery skill is find a group of high level playerfs who dont mind you hanging around them ungrouped, and shoot arrows at their kills. Use a hickory bow, so there's no risk of stealing their kill or anything, and so the mob won't attack you. If their mobs are red, you'll increase in skill really fast. Just be sure to get permission first, or you may piss them off. I got my skill up quite a bit following a group of sand giant hunters in Oasis, it was really fun to shoot at the bastards, too.
