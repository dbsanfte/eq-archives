Subject: Wisdom Does Effect Trade Skills


One major case in fact- EVERY jeweler (master and above) out there has a set of
equipment for jeweling- wisdom being the main factor after skill to determine
failures, then intelligence to raise skills.

I tried this with my cleric- started by brewing, but also on some low level
jewelry, baking and tailoring stuff. I was able to reliably make items that
other characters could not make reliably, even though these other characters had
much more dex (145 vs 110), agi (138 vs 85), and between 10-20 skill points more
than I did. My wisdom is 178, thiers 70.

If you don't believe that, ask a jeweler or go check out the enchanters board
(www.eqcastersrealm.com)
