Karvar has a very excellent suggestion about having a "Trade skill" suit.
For those who don't know, there are two (and only two... not DEX, or AGI)
stat's that affect your trade skills. (Wis)dom and (Int)elligence.

Intelligence increases your chances of getting better at any skill (gaining
a level) for each attempt.
Wisdom increases your chance of being successful at an attempt for any
level.

Bob has 95 INT and 85 WIS

George has 85 INT and 95 WIS

Bob is successful at making [whatever-arrow] with a fletcing skill of 120
about 2 out of 5 times

George is successful at making [same-whatever-arrow] with a fletching skill
of 120 about 3 out of 5 times.


Where it took George to get his skill to 120 in about 3000 tries (about a
week and a half), it only took Bob to get his skill up to 120 in about 2500
tries (only a week).

BOTH Failures and Successes count towards your level.

...These numbers are just hypothetical, but you can see how they affect your
skills (all skills, not just trade skills)


[Cha]risma also plays an important role, mostly to your trade skills though.
The more charisma you have, the higher merchants will pay for YOUR stuff
(items and loot) and the less you will have to pay to buy from the merchant.
This combined with your faction standing can really make a difference in how
much PP you spend on that next batch of arrows. The likliest place to have
the highest faction is in your home guild, but try around and get the prices
from other cities and merchants.
Me, I'm in Surefall Glade, not far from Queynos and I've been noticing that
prices for me in Queynos are starting to catch up to those in Surefall, but
I always get a good price check first before I sell.

Hope this helped ANYONE out there at all...
