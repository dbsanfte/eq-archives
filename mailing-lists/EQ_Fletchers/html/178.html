Well, I havn't been making bows to sell lately but I always used a rule of
thumb of pricing the bows to double your cost of materials. Because there
is always a chance of failure, this will cover your expenses incase you
screw up. Now, if your making trivial bows to sell, you might want to lower
your price. The only problem that I have with doing that is that you might
undercut someone else, that has a lower skill than you, that is using the
same rule of thumb.

I also recomend that rule of thumb to people when buying. If they know the
approximate cost on the materials (keeping in mind that people have
different charisma), don't pay more than double the cost of materials.
