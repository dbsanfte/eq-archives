The increases were slow for the first 5 points (took at least two backpacks
full per point), but they "seemed" to pick up after that. From 193 to 197
actually took only 2 backpacks total to get. The last few points, however,
again took 3+ backpacks each point to gain. I did notice major
success/failure differences when I upped my Wisdom some. I also put on
some Int gear to help with the learning of the skill. Best advise I can
give is to get as much Wis and Int gear as you can when you work on your
fletching. The increased Wis will allow you to succeed more (thus costing
less in failures), and the increased Int will save your wrists some pain
since your skill will increase (slightly) faster.

As far as what to make, stick with Ceramic vane arrows. They do not go
trivial and they are the only arrow I know of that makes a profit. If you
can get your skill up and make a profit at the same time, I say go for it. :)

Good luck on the Darkwood Bow. After I reached a fletching skill of 200, I
immediately attempted to make a Compound Darkwood Bow. I failed the first
two tries. On the third try I was successful (thank goodness). This just
goes to show that even though the skill is maxed out, it is not guaranteed
that you will succeed every time.
