Do not forget dueling and PVP combat. The bow is a warrior types ultimate
defense. When you are rooted, switch to your bow. You can still do damage at
range and if you time your shots you can disrupt a spell. They are also useful
for pulling enemies to you and for killing those that are running away. Also, a
group with one caster who can root and 5 archers can take a small breather from
normal combat by switching weapons. Also good in normal combat where one of your
members is hurt. If he backs off, he can use the bow and usually not build up
too much aggression.
