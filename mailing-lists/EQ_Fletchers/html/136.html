LOL. I am really starting to get fed up with Verant. When I first
started playing EQ, I thought it was the greatest game ever made. The
thing that thrilled me the most was that it wasnt a pure hack-and-slash
game. It had things in it that were even lacking in most of the old
pencil and paper games! It had a wide variety of trade skills that one
could participate in if they didnt want to spend all their time going
out and killing stuff. I immediately got into tailoring. After a
while, I got sick of hunting down green wolves for hours, to end up
making a few gold selling pw to newbies, and decided to go with
something new. Being a ranger, I took up fletching. I had found my
calling, it seemed. I clicked until my fingers bled, and finally got my
skill up enough to make my first hickory. It sold almost immediately.
It sent on similar to this for a while. Making a little cash here and
there, bringing my skill up, and taking time out to go adventuring every
now and then. Suddenly, things began to change. When my skill was at
60 or 70 or so, they put a patch on that made it cost 5 times as much to
up my skill. So, I took up pottery to make money and give myself the
edge to try to catch up to the lucky ones who got their skill up around
200 before the patch. I was still making money selling elm, and
eventually ash, as well. Then the trueshot comes along. Suddenly, I
can't seem to sell ash anymore. I do the quest myself, hoping to make
up for it with tremendous profits, and I fail 10 times in a row with
skill 140. I think to myself, well, no matter, I'll do more pottery to
get my skill up enough to make one. One day, I go to buy sketches, and
they've gone up in price to where profits are now impossible!

Sorry about the length of this rant, but I've been pissed. They
say they want a player based economy, but it semms that with every patch
comes something to make trade skills harder! They were hard enough as
it was before all of these patches. Now you'd have to be insane to take
all the time away from adventuring and leveling and making money to sit
around clicking combine with no hope of ever getting any return on your
investment. Of course, they havent done a thing with Jewelcrafting!
This is what really irks me! Enchanters are the only ones who can
really do this successfully, and they're making thousands of plat off of
it, while us fletchers are struggling to get back half of what we've
spent!

I'm seriously thinking about quitting the game, and as soon as
Asheron's Call comes out, they'll have a really hard time keeping me in
Norrath.

Slig, Povar's disgruntled Fletcher
