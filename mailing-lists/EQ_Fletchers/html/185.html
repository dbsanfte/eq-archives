I primarily use the 1 Dam 150 Range arrows, and am a L33 Warrior using the
Compound Darkwood - 19 Damage one. I see a maximum currently of 26 points
of damage from my arrows, and hit quite often for 23, 24 and 25. This is
the most that I have ever seen, and while I see it more often the lower the
"Con" of the MOB, I do occaisionally see it versus RED's and YELLOW's. I do

I will keep you posted on my results.

A tip here for you fletcher / archers.... Carry the components to make
arrows with you to fletch as you go on the road. The components to make 100
arrows only take up 4 slots unfletched, and 5 slots fletched. I normally
fill up (2) 8 Slot containers for a good hunt and supply arrows to the
others in my party that use them... Of course I use my bow as a weapon too
so I go through a lot of arrows on a hunt. When pulling, I normally back up
to the ambush sight pumping arrows into it as I go, and can have a couple of
hundred points in it as it arrives. Also if I am not pulling, tell the
puller to hit auto-attack as they say "INC" and you can use the /assist
command to get a target on the MOB, then flip off your auto-attack as it
turns on and shoot it as the puller brings it in. Takes some coordination,
but works pretty well. Lastly, if you have to back out of combat because
you are hurt, flip off the auto-attack, back off a few feet, and fire into
the melee with arrows. This works great for any warriors and rogues, and
the hybirds that are low on mana or want to save their mana for later.

As you can see, I am not one of those types that would say "I only use it to
pull" when talking about my bow.
