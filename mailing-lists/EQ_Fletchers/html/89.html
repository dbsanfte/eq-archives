Raising your skill is really simple. All you need to do keep doing the cheapest thing until it's trivial. Then, find the next cheapest thing, and do that until it's trivial. Repeat until your fingers bleed. Keep in mind, though, difficulty is only based on the most expensive part, NOT the sum of the parts, so always use 3 6cp pieces, and 1 other piece.
