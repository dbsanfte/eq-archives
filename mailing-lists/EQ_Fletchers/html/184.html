Unless Verant does something a bit different about arrows, there's
not much sense in making (in my opinion) anything beyond a damage 1 arrow
unless someone specifically asks for you better and is willing to pay for it
(which happens very rarely).

Now, the sensible thing, it would seem...is to make arrow damage a
multiplier of bow damage...given that arrow cost basically doubles for each
additional damage point. Otherwise, it's simply not cost efficient to use them
.
With the present system, bow damage + arrow damage = total damage.
A damage 1 arrow costs, say 1sp, and adds 1 damage to your 19 damage bow
for a total of 20 damage max.
A damage 2 arrow costs 2sp and adds 2 damage to your 19 damage bow for
a total of 21 damage max.
For the 2sp I spent on a 2 damage arrow doing 21 max damage, I could
have made two 1 damage arrows with a total damage potential of 40.

And of course, it gets worse the higher you go...spend 8sp on one 4 damage
arrow with a total potential damage of 23, or 8sp on eight 1 damage arrows with
a total potential damage of 160?

This of course, discounts crits, but those are not significantly higher to
the
point of justifying the ridiculous cost.

The other sensible change would be to simply adjust the cost of making the
arrows more in line with their actual value.

If Verant is really interested in making fletching a viable trade,
(bowyering is)
they need to make one of these adjustments, and I heartily encourage my fellows
to join me in suggesting a change.
