I recently encountered a player, who was even level with me, and 10pts
better in archery. So out of boredom, I challenged him to an archery only
duel. We wailed on each other for a while, each of us getting the nice
critical hits ... but my bow was better, so I was hitting for more average
damage. However, after several rounds of arrow zinging, we were fairly
even. Want to know why? Cuz this individual had discovered (so he claimed)
the master archer (NPC of some sort) who taught him a researchable skill (so
he claimed) in "split arrow shots". What is that? HE WAS HITTING ME WITH
2 ARROWS IN ONE ROUND !!! He even managed to execute a TRIPLE shot!
Apparently, rangers can now double attack with bows !
After much coaxing and pleading, I was not able to get ANY info out of
him; as he claimed to be sworn to secrecy. Needless to say, I was vastly
disappointed that another player would not share a secret of archery with
his fellow ranger.
So all I can do is put out this All Points Bulletin.
I don't know if he is playing a trick on me. But he seemed genuine
enough. Fellow rangers, we MUST all get this ability. Can you imagine the
satisfaction of firing a TS or Darkwood bow, hitting critical for
(57)damage, then nailing the Mob with a second arrow for an additional 29
dmg ... in the same round?
Let us all unite and find the master... if he exists. I know we are
working on enchanting the TS bow right now. But I'm sure one of us will
come across some clue for the "Split Shot" ability in that whole process.
