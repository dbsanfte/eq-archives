We had a somewhat nasty debate about this on the eqrangers list. I was the lead advocate in charge of the opinion that this bow should cost around 200pp. Other people were saying that you should have someone bring you the materials and charge 10pp for "clicking combine." Now that it's been around a little while now, they sell for 150-175 on Povar. I'm not too happy about this, as that leaves no reason to buy an oak. I basically figure that this bow should be sold at a price relative to other bows in its abilities. It is better than an Oak bow, certainly (sure, the oak is 25 more range, but the better stats more than balance that out). So, why should it cost the same as an oak bow? Well, there's no cost of materials, you say. There's no cost of materials for a piece of rubicite. Would you expect someone to give that to you for 10pp? It's all about the time it took you to get the item. Fletching is very expensive and time consuming, so if you can make this bow, that means you spent a long time getting your fletching up to a point where it is possible. This time should be considered equivalent to the time spent camping an item. I think 200pp is a great price, and if you can get 250 for it, by all means charge it! This is a magnificent bow which is not particularly easy to get right now, so charge what you can (we need some way to make back all that money we lost getting our fletching up). You can always lower your prices, but you can never raise them! Keep this in mind, as if you start selling for 100pp, no one will ever be willing to pay more than that on your server.

Slig of Povar
Defender of the righteousness of Fletching!
