Well, how do you camp? Be prepared for long and boring waits my
friend. If you are camping a rare spawn that's all you can do. You
are usually focused on a certain item for a certain quest, so that's
what keeps your interest up. This is where friends or guildies help,
as you know they will ask for your help on their boring camp. I'm in
the midst of the Ivy Etched Armor quests and I have several camps
ahead. One guy is an 8 hour pop, if he pop's! My buddies gladly
come along, and there's usually a few people around to chat with.
And there's always gems!

So, I would say look at what you need for what you want, and plan for
it. Many guilds take in new members in your level range, join up and
tell them what you want to do. Odds are there will many people
willing to help you, as long as you help them.
