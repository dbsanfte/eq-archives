Actually, I have been PLed a little. I had people come up and offer to PL
me. I learned what they were capable of and it helped to hone my grouping
skills. I knew what I had to do as well as what they had to do. I learned my
limitations. I always try to suggest strategy to anyone I PL. Generally
PLing is NOT good for a firsttime player. It's best for when you already
have a character that has leveled up well. Then you can just level up
another quickly. I don't really like it when people ask me to PL them.
