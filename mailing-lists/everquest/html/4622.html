Its in the Freeport/Nek area. Starting from FP, zone
to EC. Hang on the right wall till you zone Nek.
Stay on the trail, cross the bridge, take the first
left bearing trail after the bridge. At the end of
that spur, bear a bit left until you are at zone edge.
Zone to lava storm. Sol A zone is kinda in the
middle of lava. To line up on it go straight in and
you'll see two pools of lava close by. Go exactly
between these and do not turn. You'll come to a large
pit of lava towards the back of the zone. When you go
down the ramp that you'll see, the first door is not
sol A, the second door is not sol A, but a little
climb up the hill you'll see another doorway. That's
sol A.

Watch out for the Wizard Gobs. They are pretty mean.
