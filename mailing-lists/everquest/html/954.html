To everyone worried about money at low lvls, why? If it was me I would just
try to lvl instead of trying to make money. Money will come. Once you get
to your mid 20's to late 20's almost everything drops FS weapons and up to 4
or 5plat in money as well. Although I do remember the days when you could
kill dwarf NPC in BBM and get an axe that sold for 22p Xg everytime you
killed them. 3 spawns every 12 minutes. 1 person could make over 300pp
every hour. Not too bad. They are lvl 35 btw but no longer drop the good
axes.

Another great place for money and EXP is Neriak (sp?). If your around lvl
40 or so and can get a group together you wont be disapointed (at 40 you
would need a pretty well rounded group at 50 or so , btw still get exp here
at 54 all the guards are still blue, you could do it with 3 or 4 people. Go
in the city and head to 3rd gate zone but dont zone in. Fight at the zone
in and pull from Neriak Commons. 18 spaws here that we could kill in 24
minutes. All of them dropped a sword that our enchanter could sell for 11p
and some change, also dropped a shield and usually a second FS weapon. Have
fun know this is a little off the lvl range for what you guys were talking
about but might help someone.
