There are actully 5 types of runes that drop.  Minor muramite runes which are for lvl 66, Lesser Muramite runes which are for lvl 67, Muramite runes which are for lvl 68, Greater muramite runes for lvl 69 and glowing muramite runes for lvl 70.  The first 3 are all tradeable.  The last 2, greater and glowing are no drop.  The first 3 are pretty commen drops off of nameds throughout most of OoW zones, the last 2 only drop in the higher end zones, or off raid mobs.
