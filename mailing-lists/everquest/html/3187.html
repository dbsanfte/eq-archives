you should definitely work on all weapon skills...  just keep an eye on you skill, as it goes up, so that you know when to switch to a different weapon.  the formula for weapons skills is this:  (level * 5) +5  so, at 11, your skill should max at 60.  as soon as your skill hits 60 with one weapon, switch to a different skill.  do this each level, if you can.  i neglected to do this with my ranger, and am now paying for it.  i am trying to raise my 2hb, and 1hb from nothing, and am level 42.....  which means, since i solo, i take much more dmg than i should, because of being so slow to kill the mob with those weapons....  when you get dual wield, it will be easier for you to keep 1hs, piercing, and 1hb maxed, as you can slip 1 of each type into a hand, and still do a considerable amount of dmg, while increasing 2 skills at once.  hope this helps..
