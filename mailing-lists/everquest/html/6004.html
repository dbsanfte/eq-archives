You're just level 10 now. Shaman, at the higher
levels, don't melee very much, if at all. You'll be
there to buff your group and debuff and slow the mob
mostly. So you want to make sure your spells aren't
fizzling every time you try to cast them. Power
leveling will increase the chances that your spells
will fizzle more often.

Also, if you zoom up there in levels, you may not
quite know how to play your character. The game gets a
bit different after level 25, and changes greatly
after 50. So you should take your time and enjoy the
game, and learn how to play your character well. :)
