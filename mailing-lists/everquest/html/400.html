Hi

I found it quite easy starting off as a dark elf necromancer, even though
most people seem to think they are impossible to start with. The spells to
get to start with is locate corpse (saves you hours when you die, I found
Nektulos forest to be very confusing to start with). Most people would say
get the 1st level skeleton spell, however I never bothered with it. level
1 to 4 is easy enough, you will die a few times, but not that often - the
biggest problem is the beetles who may gang up on you.

Sense the dead is also useful - save every bone chip you pick up, don't
sell them - you need them for your skeleton at level 4! Disease cloud is
useful - doesn't seem to damage your target much, but they don't seem to
heal either - and it improves conjuration which is good for your skeletal
pets.

The biggest problem with nektulos is that there aren't any shops there -
the first thing you should buy is a backpack.

That's it really - if you need any help and I'm online feel free to ask me
any questions, I'd be pleased to help. I'm bound in Qeynos at the moment,
but I can give advice easily enough. When you reach level 8 or so and you
start adventuring into the commons, don't go near any human npc's - they'll
attack you on sight. That's the biggest disadvantage to being a dark elf -
nobody likes you.

If anybody in Qeynos needs help finding any corpses, please ask me - I'll
be glad to help, and I only ask for some food and water as payment :-)
They really should start selling food and water in the necromancers guild!

Good Luck

Mored
10th level Dark Elf Necromancer
