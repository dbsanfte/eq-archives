I forgot which spell ability casting darkness has but use that one. That'll
be your main aggro drawing spell (along with Terror/Fear-in the upper 50s).

hits for the group so if a wep has anything with STA/AC/HP on it use those.

and no stats. Like I said earlier the main ones you should focus on are
STA/AC/HP and STR/DEX to a lesser degree, anyone with WIS and CHA is pretty
much useless to this class.
