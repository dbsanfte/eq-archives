A lot of difference. The Ti has a much faster redraw rate so graphics will
look cleaner and more detailed when you turn or move. Also faster
processing and memory so it cuts down on internal system lag. A better
video card can sometimes compensate for a slower system. Also, the Ti is
made from titanium which is much more heat resistant than previous cards.
So the card doesnt get as hot and there is less chance of failure later on.
Overall it's a good card for the money. I plan on getting the 128MB Ti for
my P4 once I get moved into my new apartment.
