I don't know your level but as higher level you wont find those groups all
the time. Hell, it's even hard when you are in a guild. I'm one of the
leaders in a guild that has 150+ members and most are 50+, however, you will
notice problems when you get higher and you've noticed that EQ ain't that
fun. You wanna do the real stuff like dragons, planes and more "uber" stuff.
That's when guilds comes handy. At lower levels it's more like a community,
but still great tho, you will get to know alot more people via your guild
and hopefully you will join a good guild. As for me, i like to chat with
folks all the time, i wouldnt manage to stick to EQ (already hard as it is
with AO this close) without a guild.
