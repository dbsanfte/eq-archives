In game marriages are more common than you might think. My wife and I had our Ogres get married almost 3 years ago...it was less common then. We had GMs,guides,etc... all there. Since then we have been to lots of weddings. Sometimes between people that know each other in r/l other times only in game.
You don't have to have to notify a GM before hand but if you want something special you should....after the wedding you can petition to change last names so they are they same. The marriage means what the couple want it to.
