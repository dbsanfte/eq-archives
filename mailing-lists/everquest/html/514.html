Ok... If your pretty good at travelling, you can get to my favorite place to
fight at slightly-low levels. You need to go from Kelething to Kaladim
first. I've never done this and heard that it can be dangerous. Once you get
to Kaladim, I would suggest finding someone who can bind you there. Then
take the boat ride (approx. 40 - 1hr full round trip) to Freeport. Then
again get bound there so you don't have to ride the boring boat again. (FYI
- I would not suggest moving around on the ship a lot, while it is moving
itself. I have gotten stuck before between the mast and the wall on the 2nd
level of the ship, and was a real pain waiting 2 1/2 hours for a GM around 4
AM.) Then you might want to find a person(s) heading in your same direction,
as this would be your first time. And watch out for griffons and hill giants
when passing through east/west commonlands. They seem to "disapeer" when
flying sometimes, and then pop-up behind and you fall to your death almost
instantly. From the west commonlands you zone into a forest called Kithicor
(I think?). Then just follow the zone border to the right, and when you get
to the rocky mountainside just go to the base and there should be the
entrance there. Then don't forget to get bound here to. Then there are
multiple things you can do here. There is a dungeon named RunnyEye (Which is
usually for higher levels [20 or so]) but there are 3 goblin camps here. And
1 orc camp. At level 4 I would just stick to the Klicknak's inside the great
wall, then at five go past the wall and look for shadow wolfs, and sometimes
balck bears. At 6-9 I would say just start solo'ing the goblins. Verant
seems to have up'ed the loot on them and now as a level 8 paladin. I can
bring in 10 - 15pp a day without so much work. The EXP here is great.
