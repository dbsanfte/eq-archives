Well, you can go to Paludal Caverns on Luclin. Take the entrance from
Shadeweaver's Thicket, though. First, you can start by killing the weakest
creatures near the entrance. Around 15th, you can move in and fight the
plerg fiends until the XP starts slowing down. After that, move in deeper to
kill the phlarg fiends. That can easily sustain you until close to 20th
level. I think I managed to hunt there with my ranger off and on until
around 22nd (I bounced back and forth from Lake of Ill Omen to Paludal
Caverns).
