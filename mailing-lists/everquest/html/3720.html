Patch of dire wolf fur = 3 tufts dire wolf fur (foraged in Eastern
Wastes, Velious) embroidering needle 46
Woven dire wolf fur = 3 patches of dire wolf fur, embroidering
needle<=46
Leatherfoot Bags (100% weight-reducing)
infused platinum thread(s), woven dire wolf fur(s), acorn oil
wrist pouch: 1 woven fur, 1 infused platinum thread
small pouch: 2 woven furs, 1 infused platinum thread
shoulder bag: 3 woven furs, 2 infused platinum threads
haversack: 4 woven furs, 3 infused platinum threads
Leatherfoot Wrist Pouch, Wt: 0.1 2 GIANT slots, trivial >159
Leatherfoot Small Pouch, wt 0.2, 4 GIANT slots, trivial <=250
Leatherfooot Shoulder Bag, wt 0.4, 6 GIANT slots, trivial >202 <=250
Leatherfoot Haversack, wt 0.6, 10 GIANT slots, trivial >250

celestial essence = celestial solvent (vendor-sold), spell research
component anyone can combine, does not require skill


purified water = 2 water flasks, celestial essence<=117
celestial temper = purified water, blue diamond powder136

Brewing - combine in brew barrel acorn oil = flask water + Misty Acorn
Misty acorn is a ground spawn in Misty Thicket, also foraged in Misty
Thicket; trivial 102
