> The bazaar is a really kewl place ... as long as there's not too many
> people there :-)
>
> Satchels cost a little under 2 plat and they hold 10 items.  You can buy
> as many satchels as you like, but remember that folks can only see the
> first 30 items you have for sale.  I've got a bunch of satchels in the
> bank for storage space.  You have to activate every item you want to
> sell specifically, so you an use the satchels for anything.  They do
> weigh a tonne (practically) so the bank is the only useful use for them
> outside of the bazaar.
>
> Oh a side note .. there is no weight penalty to movement rate in the
> bazaar.  So you can safely haul around 3 (or more) of those 25 WT
> satchels!
>
> You get the satchels from the first merchant on the right in the big
> building between the 2 banks.
