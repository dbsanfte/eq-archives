Talk to Hasten Bootstrutter who can be found in the Rathe Mountains. He has
an extra pair of Journeyman Boots, but would rather trade than sell it. You
must seek out the following items and hand them to him.


* Shadowed Rapier

* Ring of the Ancients

* 3250 Gold

The shadowed rapier can be found all around Norrath dropped by those Shadow
Men. I suggest getting this piece last since it will disapear from your
inventory when you camp. For the Ring of the Ancients, it spawns on an
Ancient Cyclops in South Ro. Also make sure you hand him 3250 Gold and not
325 Platinum as this will not work.

These boots do not have to be worn to be activated. Just right click on them
and they will bestow great speed to the user, almost like the Spirit of the
Wolf spell.
