I haven't been to Felwithe in a while, but every city has a person in
their guild area that gives out a quest for "Newbie" armor and
weapons. Just hail everyone. When you see something in brackets
that is the response you /say to them. The right person will give
you a box, then you ask for patterns and they list the items you need
to collect. Write it all down. This is much better armor than I had
at that level! It will sustain you until your 20's at least.

And to go between Felwithe and Kelethin just follow the road. It's
not too dangerous. Just don't fall off of the platforms!
