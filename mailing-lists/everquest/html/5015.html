One problem with the war is it can screw up the zone until the server
is reset. They have patched it but it still breaks, though less
often. But it is fun!

If you want to increase your brewing cheaply just gather Payala fruit
in Shadeweaver Thicket and combine it with water. It's a ground drop
so anyone can find it. Just look for the blue topped trees. It
makes Shar Val essence, triv at 180+.
