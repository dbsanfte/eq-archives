Subject: Trueshot Chainmail Ranger Quest armor


The Trueshot Chainmail armor is a newbie armor quest in Greater Faydark.  You can find all this information on Allakhazam and EQrangers, but I offer it here to you just for comprehensive purposes.  This armor is wearable by half elves and wood elves, but only if your deity is Tunare.  The quest starts out in the ranger guild in Kelethin.  Hail Samatansyn Flamecaller to start the quest.  He will give you a Trueshot Armor kit which you will use to put specific items in and combine them to make the mold for the armor.  Then take the pattern he gives you for the armor piece you wish to create and combine it with the mold you made in a forge.  My ranger is level 10 and has not failed on a single piece yet.  I just helped a level 1 ranger craft the coif and he succeeded so I don't believe it takes any smithing skill to finish each item. 
 
NOTE: Most of the items can be purchased from vendors around Kelethin so before you spend time killing mobs, try buying them first, unless you just want the xp.  Some of the orc specific items are No Drop, which I'll indicate by (ND).  The bricks of crude bronze are best purchased off other players, or as I found out you can trade Crushbone belts for them. 
 
 
BOOTS:   (all items purchasable)
- 3 bricks crude bronze
- 1 severed orc foot
- 2 spider legs
 
BRACER:  (all items purchasable)
- 1 brick crude bronze
- 1 skeleton tibia
- 1 pixie wing
- 1 gypsy wine
 
COIF:  (all items purchasable)
- 2 bricks crude bronze
- 1 Royal Jelly   (drops off giant wasps)
- 1 spiderling eye
- 1 ration    (NOT Iron Ration)
 
GAUNTLETS:  
- 3 bricks crude bronze
- 1 Orc Ulna (ND)
- 1 Orc Tibia (ND)
- 2 Orc Fingerbones (ND  Note: These are rare drops)
 
LEGPLATES:
- 4 bricks crude bronze
- 1 Orc Fibula  (ND  Note: Very rare drop.  Drops off Orc Slavers)
- 1 Orc Clavicle (ND)
- 1 Lightstone
 
SLEEVES:   (all items purchasable except venom sacks)
- 2 bricks crude bronze
- 2 giant wasp venom sacks
- 1 large window abdomen  (ND)
- 1 white wine
 
TUNIC:
- 5 bricks crude bronze
- 1 Orc fibula (ND very rare)
- 1 golden bandit tooth   (note: not sure where to obtain this or what it drops from, but I suspect the bandits in NK probably)
- 1 pristine forest drakeling scale  (note: should be purchasable but I haven't seen one yet)
- 1 Orc Ribcage (ND)
- 1 Orc Eye (common drop, can also purchase)
 
 
 
I suggest doing the gaunts, the legs, and the tunic last since the fibulas will come the hardest.  This armor has decent stats and should be good up until mid 20's.  You will have to go to Crushbone for the ND items, but other than that, it's a simple quest. You can check out the stats on Allakhazam.  It's nice looking armor too.
 
Enjoy!!
