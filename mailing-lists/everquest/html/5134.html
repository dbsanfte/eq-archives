Hello Tracy welcome to the world of Everquest!  One thing I highly reccomend (and I'm sure you'll all agree with me here) is move the auto-attack button.  I use the ` key since there is little chance that you'll hit it by mistake.  I also suggest going to any PC gaming store and getting the Everquest Guide (the Kunark version since it's more in depth and focused on newbies).  The game is very confusing at first but after several hours you get the hang of the game, the lingo, and whats looked down upon/whats highly accepted.  After reading the guide you'll have an idea of whats going on the game..and what people are saying, you'll hear things like, "Can I get a SoW?  Lvl 12 pally LFG.  TRAIN!"  those type of things heh heh.  Well Tracy have fun learning and getting around in the world of Norrath - Happy Hunting!
