Welcome to Norrath! I hope you having fun bashing bats and spiders in
the newbie area. Never fear you don't have to stay there forever.

You are in luck. They added armor quests to the dwarf city recently. Go
back to your guildhall (the room you started in should be your guild)
and hail NPCs until one of them answers you and part of the answer is in
brackets [...] repeat the bracketed words to them as a question. For
example, somebody says 'We have been having trouble with [orcs]', you
say, 'What orcs?' to get them to continue with the script. If you find
the right one for your class you will get a box of some sort and a bunch
of patterns or molds, not sure of specifics for your class, write down
everything the NPC tells you to get and how to combine the stuff to get
your armor. If you want a full suit, it will take some time but during
that time you will make a lot experience.

You can try looking up the quests on the eqtraders.com quests page
www.eqtraders.com/quests/quests.htm

What server do you play on and what is your game name? I'm sure someone
on this list may be able to help you out some in-game with more playing
tips.

Good luck!
