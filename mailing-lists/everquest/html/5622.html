> This has nothing to do with the LDON adventures though. This is
something completely separate that they implemented for the 5th Year
Anniversary. Many people have already done this quest. The prize is
a Staff of Endless Adventuring which serves absoluitely no purpose
except to shoot off fireworks. There are different kinds though.
Each staff has its own set of fireworks. The type is based on the
character creation date. What you get is a Collector's Sack. Then
you find the 10 items the book suggests and put them in the pack and
hit Combine. This will create a Sealed Collector's Sack which you
give back to the NPC who gave them to you. He will then give you the
staff in return. The quest is fairly easy to anyone over level 50 as
all the mobs will con green. If you're a non-porter type, it may take
you awhile to run from zone to zone. If you have a porter friend who
hasn't done the quest, they can take you around and you can help each
other get the pieces. I finished the quest in about 3 hours with my
55 druid.
>
> The items you need to collect all start with "A Collector's..." You
can't put just any item in there. It has to be the Collector's items.
The items are in no particular order.
>
> 1. A Collector's Lightstone - drops off willowisps in West Commons.
> 2. A Collector's Fire Goblin Skin - drops off goblins in Solusek's
Eye (Lavastorm Mountains)
> 3. A Collector's Undead Froglok Tongue - drops off undead frogloks
in Lower Guk (Innothule Swamp)
> 4. A Collector's Mistmore Granite - drops off gargoyles in Mistmore
Castle (Lesser Faydark)
> 5. A Collector's Shark Tooth - drops off sharks in Ocean of Tears
> 6. A Collector's Preserved Split Paw Eye - drops off gnolls in
Splitpaw (South Karana)
> 7. A Collector's Water Ring - drops off aqua goblins in Dagnor's
Cauldron
> 8. A collector's Skunk Intestines - drops off skunks in Toxxulia
> 9. A Colelctor's Kerran Doll - drops off Kerrans in Kerra Isle
(Toxxulia )
> 10. A Collector's Scythe - drops off spectre's in The Feerrott
>
>
>

spell effects turned on and Opacity to full. It's worth it.
