You can rename your ini file to include the new servername and then when you launch EQ it will have all your original configurations.
 
If you have launched EQ since the merger, there will be a default ini file named UI_charname_newservername.ini. Just delete this one (or rename it save_ or something if you don't want to delete it). Then find your old UI_charname_oldservername.ini and rename the oldservername section to the newservername.
Do the same thing to the charname_newservername.ini and an charname_oldservername.ini as well as bazaar and barter files you can rename to get all your old prices and stuffies.
 
Good luck!
