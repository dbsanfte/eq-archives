I just got the download finished and popped over there to look
around. In case you thought the auto download should have grabbed it
with last patch, no such luck. Had to use advanced and do a thorough
search to get the files. Took only 1.5 hrs., but that was with nobody
disturbing the machine during download.

I liked the look of the entry area of the zone. Judging by the number
of various bodies around the entrance portal marker, I would
recommend invisible for any travelers with even the sleightest doubt
about faction.

The zone looked nice, and a quick track scan indicated a nice variety
of quarry ranging from dark blue to red for level 33. Rolling hills,
sparse woods, lots of big boulders which may hide mobs. Looked open
enough for kiting, but even near the entry point the mobs were
visible and fairly thick.

I got a bit of lag, but I need to defrag and tune a bit before I know
just how to handle the zone.
