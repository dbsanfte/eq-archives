> To clarify that a bit, the lag meter monitors the condition of your
> connection. I don't really know what the various numbers mean, but as
> your bar goes red and shrinks, you may expect to be disconnected.
> This may be at the game server or your internet connection. When you
> are disconnected you go "link dead." Inconvenient most of the time,
> deadly if you are in battle.
>
> If you are in a critical situation and your bar keeps going red then
> back to green, it is often wise to move somewhere safe until the lag
> problem goes away. The better your system the less lag you get on
> your end, but there are still connection and server issues which will
> give you some lag. Another aspect of the game to learn, Lag
> Management.
>
> Barto Proudfoot
