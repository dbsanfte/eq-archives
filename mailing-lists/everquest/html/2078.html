If you die at levels below 5 you should resurrect with five days of
food and water. If your corpse had nothing on it, it may have
disappeared, but your own anwswer is most likely. You misplaced your
corpse. Dying at levels below five is set to be low-penalty (you lose
very little and get food and water when you come back) to make the
early learning a bit easier.

There are tools to find corpses, sold by merchants. However, it is
best to learn to die with finding your corpse in mind. Try to look
around at landmarks when death is clearly coming. Try to be near some
object you can find easily when you come back. And unless you don't
mind having to do this often, learn to fight in such a way as to
seldom die. Some gamers like to take the risks leading to frequent
deaths, but I prefer to hunt with care and die seldom. Seems more in
the spirit of the game to me.

Good hunting to you.

Barto Proudfoot
