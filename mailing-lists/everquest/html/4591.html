You can try Paludal Caverns fighting recondite bandits and those phlarg
fiends or whatever they're called that cast disease on you... Or even try
killing the mushrooms in PC. Or you can try killing gnolls and orcs in
highpass. Even bandits might give you xp at this point. The only problem is
that they changed the pathing for gnolls and orcs so instead of staying at
their spawn point, they wander off to the guards and can sometimes attack
you when you are fighting the bandits. Bandits sometimes drop pearls which
are good to keep for components for cleric and mage spells. If you ever need
to be summoned by a mage, it takes a pearl. Pearls are also used by
enchanters for making vials of viscous mana. Oh, and the weapons the bandits
drop are pretty good too. Dalishea is the highest level of the 3 bandits.
The dwarf is the next highest.
