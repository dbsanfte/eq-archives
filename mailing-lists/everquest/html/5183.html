I think if you fight in EW it's best to say near the bridge to IC and to be
careful. there can be lots of adds. One good method I saw a druid using was
to snare a mob then cast camo on herself and run back to the bridge. That
way other mobs didn't see her running.
Also, if you can use the spell to charm an animal you can charm a wolf or
kitty to help you. Only problem is when charm wears off. If things go bad
you can run accross the bridge to zone.
I believe I hunted in DL at that lvl, but I'm a cleric so I couldn't solo.
If you have Lost Dungeons of Norrath it's really great xp. I know you can't
solo, but the xp in group is worth it.
