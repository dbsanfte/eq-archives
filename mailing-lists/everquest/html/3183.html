I was hunting in SK, and trying to buy fs weapons. Very few hunting
there, so lots of wandering Gnolls to pick off. That happens when the
Gnoll hunters are not many at the spires, some gnolls wander off.
If the spire camps are busy, but the groups are not large enough to
take every gnoll that pops, a tracker can locate wanderers and pick
up a gnoll or two.

And so, learning that none had fs for sale, I went to the area near
the hermit. Sure enough, I picked up a wandering gnoll right away. He
dropped a fs warhammer, which I took home to Rivervale. I broke it
into ore, and made my hq metal bits. Grabbed the parts, went to the
Vale forge, and gave it a shot.

I now have my Vale Sewing Kit. Pretty exciting. I will have to head
out for Velious soon to start gathering the pieces for the Halfling
100% weight reduction bags. Should be some big coin in those.
