You should also hold on to pixie dust. there are two quests for it. One for
the rangers and one for the rogues.
There is also a quest in the warrior guild where you bring the guy a bat
fur, a bonechip, a spiderline eye, and a ruined wolf pelt. He gives you a
random item. sometimes armor or a gem that sells for a decent price. But you
have to have killed enough orcs to have good faction with him to do the
quest. Hail him and he will give you the quest. I wish I could remember his
name... he's in the place accross from the store that sells fletching stuff.
Pixies drop decent loot sometimes too. And there is the mail delivery quest
from Felwithe to Kelethin. Sometimes the guy you deliver mail to gives you
up to 5gp. I wish I could remember the name but my brain is just dead right
now.
