Subject: Tracking Brutes in Warsliks Woods


When I first moved to Warsliks Woods to hunt Brutes, I tried to track
them. I kept finding myself tracking over empty places. I could not
find the Brutes.

I later was advised to look into caves for them. I do believe I was
right over them all of the time.

So, if you track Brutes in Warsliks Woods, and can't find them above
ground, check underground. I am camping Brutes in the cave located at
3800/3800. The tunnel to Dalnir.
