I tried one task.  There are 3 Task Masters in POK.  And I believe at least 1 in every home town.  Just hail them.   They will give you a list of tasks to choose from.  Rezize your task window to see the rewards.  These tasks are not easily soloable, but if you get a group you can finish them fairly quickly.  My first task was to collect 4 bone chips and kill 10 reanimated mobs in Chardok.  It took awhile but it was good xp and a 58pp reward.
