When I was playing my dwarf warrior, I found that CB
and the surrounding orc camps were the best place to
level fast. Not only do you get XP for killing the
dratted things, but you learn pulling, group work --
if you happen to find a good group -- and you can get
XP from the dwarves for turning in CB belts,
shoulderpads and bracers.

With my new Vah Shir rogue, I'm finding the best way
to level quickly is to work on my throwing skill and
break camps. The Loda Kai camp near the mountains is
proving a great place to do both -- I throw, get a
couple skeletal brigands, kill them, heal for two
minutes, and then repeat.

I was levelling my dwarf in about...maybe not an hour
but certainly two if I put my mind to it and I was
levelling my rogue in at least two. It's bit harder
now because I'm level 12 working on 13, so it simply
takes longer.
