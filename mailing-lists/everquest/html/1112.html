I use the root and nuke strategy.
Another fun thing to do for faction or items.
With my cleric I run around hittng polar bear cubs once and get a whole
train of them on me (when I am much higher lvl than them). Once I have about
5 or more I wait til they are all close to me and cast Word of Pain. Then
They all die instantly. So then I gather up their pelts. some of which can
be turned in for quests.
I do something similar with my wizard and orc pawns in Freeport. I use a low
lvl dd spell to lure pawns to me and get a whole bunch around me. This is
good when I am encumbered and cannot chase after them. Once I get them
gathered around I use an AE spell. if they don't all die, I stand in place
and attack the ones that are close to me. If they run I turn to another one
and fight it. Eventulaly they all come back to me. I keep fighting til they
are all laying dead around me. then I loot their corpses and gate back to
Hallard's to deliver pawn picks.
Got my faction up to apprehensive so far.
