Well, according to the official rules it's only KSing if the other person
engaged the mob first. Attacking a mezzed, snared, or rooted mob that you
did not engage first is KSing (if you get the kill). Sometimes it gets into
gray areas. For instance if a mob comes and attacks you after someone else
aggrod it and the people who were originally attacking it don't come to get
it or help you and you have to kill it to defend yourself then it's not
KSing as the mob is considered abandoned.
Some people still do camp checks. If you camp a spot for a long time people
can still come in and kill a mob that you are waiting for and the GMs can't
do anything about it bc it's not against the rules. The reason for these
rules is bc it's too difficult to figure out who was camping a spot first.
The only time I have moved in on a camp that someone else was at was when a
lvl 65 was claiming the entire undead section of lower guk and I wanted to
hunt there for xp. My group moved into one room and the jerk tried to pull
mobs that we were already fighting. (he started pulling the entire dungeon
over other people and then killing them).
