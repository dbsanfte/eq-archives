> Here's the basics for you. For the first few levels,
> you'll mostly just run around and whack bugs with a
> club. Try to raise your One Hand Blunt and One Hand
> Slashing skills (using scimitars for the latter).
> It's
> a very long term consideration, but if you ever do
> reach lvl 50+, you'll want to quest for your Epic, a
> scimitar.
>
> Once you start getting a little more mana to work
> with
> and a few more spells, you can start practicing a
> technique called Kiting. (you can start doing it
> really effectively as soon as you can cast Spirit of
> the Wolf) Kiting is casting Snare on a single
> monster,
> having SoW on yourself already, then running away a
> distance, cast a DD spell, run a little more, cast a
> spell, repeat until the monster is dead. Ideally you
> want to keep the MOB at the very edge of your
> casting
> range, at very least far enough that they can't hit
> you. Using that technique you can kill yellows and
> even in some cases monsters that barely con red. (do
> your research on eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com before
> you
> even DREAM of attacking a red conning monster)
