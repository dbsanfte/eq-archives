Your welcome..so you've got a lvl 21 bard? I once had a lvl 27 bard (I've
played just about every class except Shaman and Ranger) and let me tell you
at 27 you get a nice song..Song of the Sirens I beleive it's called which
works just like a charm spell. Anyway at 21 you can get excellent exp at
Padulal Caverns at the bandit camps. I highly reccomend hunting there, no
other zone can compare to the exp you get there at your level. Once you hit
26 everything starts to green out. If you don't want to try that out again
try Neitherbain Lair or even the Karanas. Once you hit 28-30 Split Paw Lair
is a good place to hunt along with the other zones mentioned in the pervious
posts plus Sol A and Sol B in Lavastorm Mts.
