If you turn off server-filter you will lag more. The serverfilter makes the
game remove the texts you have chosen not to see on the server side. If you
remove this all text will still be sent to you (such as other hits etc.) but
your client will not show them to you. If you turn the serverfilter on the
server wont even send these texts to your client.
