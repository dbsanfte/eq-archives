Well, if they aren't camped there are the aquagoblin camps in butcherblock
mountains. They drop a few items that sell for a few plat each. If you hunt
rats in freeport they drop plague rat tails that sell for just over 1 plat.
You can also take up a tradeskill like brewery and make money selling heady
kiolas (trivial at 46). You can probably solo the bandits in highpass (right
near highkeep). They drop bronze weapons and gems that sell for a few plat.
I think Netherbian Lair is also good for loot but I'm not sure on the levels
there.
