Blackened Iron is nice to look at however.. Banded is a much better choice
for a rangers standard issue under level 25. Blackened iron is heavy and
has less AC. I'm not saying Banded is the best overall, however its one of
the only armor sets a ranger can wear.. besides the silver plated bracers
and leggings. But, if you can get your hands on those.. you can start
acquiring the ivy etched gear. There is one exception.. Blackened iron
boots are great to have if you cant get dwarven workboots. Sure they have
one less ac as the banded, however they can kick for magic.. that always
comes in handy. =)
