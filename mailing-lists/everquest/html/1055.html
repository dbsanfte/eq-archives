There are 3 goblin camps that I know of in BB. First one if you are coming
from Gfay run till you get to the buildings (the 2 with vendors) and take a
right after the first building and just go straight out there is one there.
Goblin Wizards can spawn here and can kill you pretty fast if your not
careful.

Second one when you come to the next building after the one mentioned before
this (called the cross roads on my server, 2 guards and one dwarf in silver
plate Nyzil Xxxxxxxx) go straight past this building and the road will curve
around to the left and you will see the goblin huts off to the right after a
short distance on this trail.

Third one is when you come to the cross roads guard shack, coming from gfay,
take a right when you get the next guard shack take a left right off the
trail and you will come to another goblin camp (goblin warriors, no wizards
here).

On a side note if you go past the goblin warrior camp you will come to a
bandit camp, careful here as the lvl range is large and they have a pretty
large argo range for their lvl. I think they can go from 9 to 14 (peg leg
is 14 or so). Spawn is pretty fast. 1 roamer here spawns on the wall
across from the camp and can argo the camp when he runs out to get you. His
name, Qued ( I think). He is my arch nemesis. I hate this guy and I think
to this day he is still responsible for the majority of my deaths. I cant
count the number of times I was fighting a goblin or a bat or something and
this SOB came up and started backstabbing me with his fists. I am sure I
died at least 30 times to this guy.
