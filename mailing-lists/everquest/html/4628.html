Many, many options for you at level 11. Mind you, I did most of my
pre-30s leveling in the old world, before most of the expansions
came out:

- Crushbone (orc city in Faydwer, north of the elf city Kelethin)

- Estate of Unrest (kill yard trash to mid teens, then group inside
or pull outside from 1st floor).

- FIELD OF BONE (iksar newbie area)- its perfect for leveling up to
about 20ish, depending on the area you hunt. Over in front of
kaesora is great for you.

- Kurn's Tower: Esp as a paladin, when you get your vs. udnead nukes
and/or a ghoulbane, you will ROCK against undead, and this place is
full of 'em. You can solo here, or join a small group and watch the
exp fly by.
