In my experience playing my Shaman on the Saryrn server a decent balance of
both your Mana pool as well as what ever it is you use to generate your mana
(Wis for priest classes and Int for the rest of the casters) can not lead
you wrong. IF you remember more of one or the other could be a good thing
or a bad thing. My mistake with my shaman was getting more of the Wis
items to build up my mana, however I always tried to find stuff that
included an addition to my mana with "x" amount of mana to try and balance
it out. Unfortunately the price of things in the bazaar tended to deplete
my funds for tradeskills like "Alchemy" (still can't believe the prices for
making those durned potions) but the more my wis ultimately led to the
better my success rates in making my tradeskill items. But I digress. Most
people will tell you put more into the mana pool and forget about the mana
regenerator ability. In Ya'lls case Intelligence. Others will tell you the
exact opposite cause they say that you will regenerate your mana pool faster
as well as increase it per level. Myself I prefer the balance of both equal
of my wis to the addition of whatever the mana plus is included on the item.
