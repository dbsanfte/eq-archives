Personally, I love monks, and love to have a well played one in my group.  Some of my closest friends are monks, and they play their role very well and are an asset to any group they are in…  Including their ability to pull and melee.  Some of the things in the last patch were designed to make them a more powerful class.  However, I do seem to have had some problems with bards.  Only met a couple that could play their class and be a benefit to the group they were in.  If you don’t think that you can be a benefit as a certain class, then maybe you should look into creating a different character.  But remember, all classes have their limitations, and it is how you handle those limitations that makes the character an asset.
