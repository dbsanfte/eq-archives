itself off. After much exploration I found that my graphics card had

the video card again. No video output.

I was planning on a new card, anyway. We are limping along on the old
video out, and I started the disheartening search for a pci card of
some value.

Imagine my elation when I found a gforce4 in pci format for $116!

Lacking a free $116 right now, I am still limping. But more than I
dared hope for is available.

started shopping.
