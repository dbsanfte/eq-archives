Nick,

There are better weapons out there but I believe they are obtainable
later on in levels. As an example, the Observer Staff is part of a quest that
usually Level 30+ can complete. But you can also purchase the item but
again, I doubt a level 10 could have accumulated over 200-300 plat. I am
not saying it is impossible, but what I am saying is that what you have now
is excellent and should last you until at least level 20 or 30.

As for the Sense Animal spell, you can obtain that at any Druid
Guild. I believe the closest one to you from Halas is the Druid Guild
at Surefall Glen, I believe that is the name of the zone. If you ever need
any of the Animal Spells, the druids carry all those spells.

Hope this helps.
