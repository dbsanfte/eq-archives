Second, I have a SB Live! also and have music in Certain
places but you should check the Options when you log on. Not the
Initial Screen but the main screen when your character is in the
Zone that you are playing in. As soon as you log on you will see the
Windowed Screen with the text box at the bottom. Look up on the
left side of the screen and you should see the Options button. Click
on it and it should give you a box that has Display, Filters, Sounds,
Etc. Click on the Sound and make sure that the music is turned up.
When I installed EQ, mine was turned all the way down and I had
to turn it up to get the music.

Hope this helps and again the 1st point was not a flame but
just a comment.
