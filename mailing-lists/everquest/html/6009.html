One more thing to think about.

Assuming you get PL'd to 40+, your skills will only be maybe level 20 if that. In order to raise your skills you have to practice them on mobs that you still get xp from. Your skills don't increase unless the mob is at least light blue. Skilsl like divination and conjuration can be worked while sitting in POK just repeatedly casting them over again. Skills like Abjuration and alteration will go up just by repeatedly buffing people, which you can also sit in POK and do. BUT, the big skill, Evocation doesn't go up unless you're actually killing mobs. And that's where it's gonna hurt you.

I find as a druid, my least commonly used skill is divination. Divination is Invis and See Invis. spirit of Eagle and Flight of Eagles has See Invis included. so you may want to work Invis because it comes in handy in the higher levels.

As a druid, probably your most commonly used combat skill is Conjuration since you will primarily be using DOTs. Mix in a heaping of Evocation for nukes and you can raise them pretty quick, but only if you're getting xp for the mob, and as has been pointed out, that's very hard to do if you're PL'd

Skills, skills, skills, skills, skills, skills, skills!

I hate to be the one to say this but you really should focus on your battle skills. Trust me, at the lower levels that is a perfect time to get the skills built up. I can understand wanting to go in and just beat the heck out of everything and being uber and killing it all in one shot.
