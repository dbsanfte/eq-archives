There is NO working method to cheat yourself more plat.  Your chars money and items are stored on the server that you play on, not on your machine.  For that matter, Verant keeps close watch on all hacks available and CAN detect and track usage of them.  Any use of hacks/cheats/exploits will quickly get your account banned.
 
If you wanna horsie, do what I did; go mug Hill Giants in Rathe mountains for a couple weeks.  You'll need to be at least level 30 or so depending on class, and the KSing and training there can be very hard to deal with, but it's worth it when you ride out of the stables shouting "Yee-HA!  Ride 'em cow-elf!)
 
The cheapest horse cost my Chanter 8499p at max charisma, The cha price-cap has been raised since then, so they may be a bit cheaper now.  Just be sure to get a charisma buff before you buy, it can make a BIG difference in the price of a horsie!
