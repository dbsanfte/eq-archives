Anybody play the beastlord class? I've played one to level 13 and it is ok. The beastlord himself is about as good of a fighter as a bard except they can only wear leather armor. His pet is a good tank though with a neat taunt proc and the beastlord has cleric level heals for the pet. He also gets shaman spells too. It's a lot like a magician except with more of an emphasis on healing and buffs. I don't see why the beastlord pet couldn't be a main tank in a group. I kind of wish they got a few extra spells though most of their best spells you don't get until level 59 or 60. I wish there were weak lower level versions of some of their key spells like maybe a spell like the enchanter's augmentation spell that increases attack speed %15 or something.
