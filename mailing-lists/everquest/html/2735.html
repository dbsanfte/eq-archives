      I have to agree with the nice gnome here.  I had a Geforce 2 mx and it was all fine and dandy.  I also had a geforce 3 TI 500 but I couldn't play EQ.  I bought a GeForce 4 MX 440 and the graphics are far superior in my opinion than that of a geforce 2 mx.  The reason the Geforce 4 TI looks better is that it has 128 MB of DDR ram not 64 MB DDR ram that the geforce 4 mx has.  The geforce 2 only has 64 MB SDRAM.  There is a big difference between the two of them.  By the way I am running EQ with a full clip plane and it works just fine.
