They are for two quests:
Tesch Val scrolls in South Qeynos. Give one to Edvard Tommels near the bank
outside Office of the People. You may need to collect 4 different ones but
I'm not sure. The reward is Karana's Tear a Sta +5 earring. Lore.
and the other quest is for Gem Inlaid Gauntlets. Here's the info from
eqdb.allakhazam.com
This quest starts out in North Qeynos at Crow's Tavern. Inside, an Erudite
librarian named Umvera Dekash wants you to return to him 4 different LORE
Tanned Split Paw Skins, which are dropped randomly off Gnolls in Infected
Paw and look like scrolls (not the old style tanned split paw hides that
looked like spells). The skins can be identified even if you cannot read
Gnoll, as the number of the scroll will always appear near the beginning of
the first sentence (1-4), not to mention that you will not be able to have
more than one of each in your inventory.
After returning the 4 skins to the librarian in Crow's tavern you will
receive a book of the translated split paw skins. Umvera will then tell you
that you need to have the book bound by Jheron Felkis, who can be located in
a spare room upstairs at Velith & Bardo's bar in East Freeport. Once bound
the book magically rewrites itself into a series of letters written by Ishva
Verishe Val to his subjects called "A Paw Book".
Return the bound book to Umvera in Crow's and he will give you a sealed
letter to take to Durkis Battlemore in Butcherblock Mountains. Durkis can be
found in the guard hut just before Kaladim, on he path from the docks. In
return for the letter Durkis gives you a set of Gem Inlaid Gauntlets.
