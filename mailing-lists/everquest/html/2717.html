J. Smythe wrote:

> How does one get to Crystal Caverns? I have always wanted to go but dont
> know hw to get there. I am a 36 ranger can I play there?


1st, go to http://www.eqatlas.com/velious/easternwastesmap.html and have
a look at the map.

locate "6" (in the center of the map): that's the orc fort. you need
invis (or some good cleric buffs) to go in there (some things are still
blue to my lvl 53 char).

go to the entrance of the fort, it is facing north. the way into CC is
in a small house in the back of the fort. when running inside the fort,
i normally take the right way around the house, because i believe the
small house is more to the right and not on the same height as the
entrance.

the small house has a lattice(/trellis - not sure which is the right
word (english isn't my native tongue) you have to open. inside is a
porter gem, you have to click, to enter CC.
