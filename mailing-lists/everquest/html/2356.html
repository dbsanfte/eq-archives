Ok,

here is my suggestion.

Launch your machine and close all programs.
Go to the Windows\System directory and RENAME the file
DINPUT8.DLL to DINPUT8.old (Right click on it and
choose rename, type in the new name and hit enter)

Direct X 8.05A or better - you can either download
this previously or after the rename.

Now, reinstall DirectX and after the installation
reboot the computer. Close all running APPS and NOW
try to run Everquest.

Let me know how it goes.
