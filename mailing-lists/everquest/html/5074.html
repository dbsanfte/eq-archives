Hey folks,
It just occurred to me that I haven't said much about the rules on posting
links.
Someone just reminded me of a rule I should have stipulated.
When you post a link to something, please have a description of what the
link is to (preferably mentioning EverQuest).
Just posting a link is something spammers do, so if you do post a link
without a description, people are likely to mistake it for spam.
I'm not picking on anyone or anything. It's my fault for not setting this
rule earlier. (well, not really a rule, but more of a request).
