you don't use the "Elemental Grimoire", but instead the "Concordance
of Research". check the links below to learn how to obtain those.

also i think you won't be able to increase research until you hit 16.
the first level where you can spent one training point in it. so any
components you use are just burned.

on my webspace i have a copy of the "Everquest Magician Resource".
it is the old unmodified version of the origianal pages. there you will find
all you need about (mage and) practice research:
http://muog.de/emr/

practice research is at
http://muog.de/emr/practice.html

if you want to do research only for your (mage-)spells, it is better to
wait! besides: level 34 to 44 "research only" spells were added on
some vendors, when Luclin was introduced (this also includes wizzy
and chanter spells).

there is no such "feature" for necro spells, but i did the following thing
with my necro, and i also recommend this to the other pure casters as
well: wait till you get to level 30. some research only spells aren't really
needed to get to that level (a necro can survive pretty good without a
pet at those levels). then train your research as high as possible (i think
PP will be the limit for non-alt chars).

also don't forget about bazaar! many research only spells or components
are sold there ... but check/compare the prices.

one more thing about research components: i "scan" merchants in popular
zones from time to time. for example, it takes about one hour to check
ALL merchants in PoK, but it is worth it: either you can sell the components
or the spells for quite some good money (10 spells for 400pp each; takes
about 3 to 4 days to sell them all on a vendor in bazaar).

i hope this helps a bit... good luck
