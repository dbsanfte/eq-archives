Sure I still get lag in a few areas and I don't have full cliping plane.
But I have it as a resonable distance and its pretty seemless. Some zones
were really laggy when the Luclin was released, however, now its pretty much
seemless in old, kunark and velious zones. Some aspects of Luclin lag
occasionally but nothing to complain about.

I do find that if your running a card with Anti-Aliasing the spell graphics
really tend to have an effect on the performance of the graphics card. I'm
sure many of you have noticed when you have spell graphics on and a Mage/Wiz
does a Fire Rain spell most of us are instantly hit with mega lag. Same
thing happens toward most spells when AA is turned on. Leave it off and it
runs nice and smooth.

I know the specs are on the high side, but I'd gladly help anyone in
pointing them to low cost solutions.
