Heres one idea: You have the natural ability to forage. Use it. Forage
cinammon sticks and you can sell them to other players. I had a player pay
me 20pp per stick! So for 2 stacks I had 800pp.
Also, you can go specific places to forage specific things. Kithkoor has the
hard to find oak bark. If you fee like it sometime, sit just inside the zone
to kith (in case you need to run if undead come) and forage til you get
some. It may be time consuming but the payoff is worth it when you can sell
it. Morning dew is also in high demand. You can also sell foraged vegetables
to non-foraging races for baking. Baking may be worth your while. You can
also work on brewery using vegetables. Once you are good at it you can make
heady kiola to sell to tailors for raw silk. You can also offer to track
things for a fee.
At your level foraging in gfay is your best bet.
Also kill as many pixie tricksters as you can. their wings sell to venders
for a decent price.
Oh, and I played on test server the other day and they have all sorts of new
creatures in gfay. drakelings, evil treants, etc. If and when they become
part of regular servers you may be asked to track specific mobs like
decaying skeletons (which were in short supply).
Most importantly, be friendly. I have found that being nice to people has
been profitable. People gave me money just for showing them where places
were.
