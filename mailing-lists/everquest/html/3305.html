Basically you have a proc on your weapon..  depending on what it is,  you need to be a certain level before it will proc for you.  If what you have is a velium mace,  it won't proc until you are level 25 & by then it is pretty much a useless weapon.  you should look up what weapon you have on allakahzams,  & it will tell you when it will proc for you. (a proc is extra damage,  like a velium weapon procs velium shards for about 25 dmg)
