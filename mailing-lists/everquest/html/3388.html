Got on in the bazaar at a good time, with few enough people that the
lag was not terminal.

Buy a trader satchel, load it up. Find a spot on one of the trading
platforms. You cannot trade if you are not on a platform. Type /trade
and set your prices. Hit List Items so others can scan the list. Now
either sit there and chat or afk and do something else.

I sold for an hour, but no buyers. Then I scanned the shopping list
and found a hq brute hide for 5pp. Seemed a good deal, so I bought
it, banked, and logged.

This is a fabulous setup once you get it set up. Find a spot on the
platform and your toon can sell all day while you work or sleep. Just
the thing when you actually want to eat in real life. A real meal, I
mean, not just gamer food.
