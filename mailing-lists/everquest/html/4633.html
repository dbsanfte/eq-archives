Yeah, a couple of things about that. My cleric is not
level 20 yet, so spells don't use much manna,
especially the blasts. I can literally cast as
quickly as the interface will allow, buff, heals and
mostly blasts and have yet to see my manna go below 4
bars.

Solo my fights usually go, root, blast 20, blast 20,
root, blast 20, blast 20 until they finally break root
then 1hb 10-20, blast, 1hb 10-20, blast until the
beastie is dead. I end up with 4 bubs of manna on a
yellow con this way :)

With enough manna a cleric is actually a formidable
fighting machine /grin. Anyone who wants to duo in WC
with this cleric on Fenin Ro should look up
"bouncyfrog". I usually play on the weekends.
