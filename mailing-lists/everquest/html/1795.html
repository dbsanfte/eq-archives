strangely enough i play a lvl 15 druid on Terris Thule, too <G> she looks like a twink though, because i've spent so long
trading and selling in EC.

some low to mid level must haves (in my op):
Moonstone Rings (3str/30mana) maybe 100pp for a pair
Foreman's Tunic (10ac/3str/3wis/0wt), 100pp or so
Platinum Armbands (3str/3wis), 150pp or so
Platinum Ruby Veil (4str/4wis), 200pp
Lizardscale Mantle (3int/3wis/5ac), about 100pp

notice the trend? i'm a wood elf, so strength is always a good thing, and wisdom and mana for druids is always nice.

some non-essentials that are still nice to have:
Icepaw War Claws (1hs 9/24 3agi), 250pp or so
Velium Round Shield (15ac i think and 0wt), 150pp but they go cheaper a lot

the Testament of Vanear is a nice quest item, but 15 is a little low to get it without help. it's 10wis/10mana, and my
friend did it solo at 24. the worst part is it's nearly a 7 hour spawn rate and the guy that drops (dylln starshine in High
Hold Pass) has a high level buddy that will kill you dead.

good hunting.
