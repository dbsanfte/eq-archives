I am running EverQuest on a PIII 800mhz with 1GB of RAM and a ATI Radeon 128MB 4X AGP video card.    I never saw any lag on my system pre-Luclin.  It's only when they souped up the game engine for better graphics that my system started lagging.  Thus the upgrade to more RAM and a better video card.  Now I run on a cable modem.  There's virtually no lag on zones that existed prior to Luclin.  There is still lag on Luclin, but I can turn the clip plane down to nil and turn off spell effects (which are annoying anyway) and get almost no lag at all.   However, same system specs with a 64MB video card and  was getting major lag in Bazaar.  Now it's pretty smoothe.  I plan on upgrading the processor to a 1Ghz or better next month so I am sure even Bazaar will be fine.
 
I still contend that a higher end video card can compensate for a low end processor.  But when you've got both together, EQ should be flawless.
