You need to recruit first. To get guild tag created, at least 10 people
must be online, at the same place, for a GM to count noses. He'll then
create the guild-tag, and make the guild-former the guild leader. The
guild leader then (can) invite in the other 10 people.

Note that to KEEP a guild, 10 accounts(aka players) must be in it (not 8
characters!).

Guilds are a per server thing, so guild fubar on 1 server could have no
relation to guild fubar on a different server (though many of the large
guilds form themselves on all the servers)
