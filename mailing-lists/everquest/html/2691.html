Well, I started practicing 2hb in my 20's with a Buryani Leg ripper I bought
of a player for about 50pp... Fear kiting animals is a good way to get
skill-ups with odd weapons, as it is fairly safe to do solo so you don't
feel a fool in front of other players with the amount of misses you get to
begin with.

Then I moved on to a Froglok Crusher, then a Drakkel Forged Sledgehammer
(these can be bought off vendor for about 110pp if a player has sold one to
the vendor), and now a Drakkel Icegrinder. My 2hb is currently at 186 at
level 47, and it would be maxed but I hardly used it  from 35 to 45 as I was
leveling in a hurry in Dawnshroud.

Anyway, my weapon skills at 47 are 1hs, 1hb, piercing all 200, 2hs 190, 2hb
186. I expect to max both 2h before 50.
