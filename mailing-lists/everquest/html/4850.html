Well I have like 5 chars and range from 25 -45 and I
can tell you don't believe any money making scam out
there.

The people who sell UBA stuff are part of large guilds
that get RARE drops and can make tons of PP.

For the casual player, as mentioned there are some
things you can always sell and make some Decent PP
from but even at 45 in DL mobs only drop 3-5 PP per
mob

When you get into low 30's or a strong group of 25ish,
you can go to SOL A and kill the foremans tunic and
kobold boots. THose sell for like 50-75pp and most
times will sell.

But there are no sure ways of making tons of PP, under
level 40 for most people.

Good luck
