I would dare to say that most of us learned the game by playing or using knowledge gained from other MMO's.
but yo uasked how to make more room in your inventory?   The only way to do this is to buy/get bags, boxes and/or other containers.
this goes for your bank/broker slots as well.
 
the Broker is a place where you place your items to sell.  First you must have a container (like a box or bag or something)
placed in the broker slot.. you then put items into that bag, and then set the price at which you want to sell the items.
if you want more slots, you will need to buy a bigger house.. so don't worry about that yet. =)
when your items sell, then when you talk to the broker again, he'll give you the money that you earned.
 
there is also a way to sell your items directly though your house, I'm not going to explain it here because I don't
want to totally confuse you.. but just to know that the option is there. =)
