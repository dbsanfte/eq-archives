checking if Wuoshi is up (normally this poor lady is killed every week). and if you start hunting in CS (another quad kiter ... :-

Thurgadin as a base, you can start to work on your Kael faction with killing Dwarves in icewell keep (this is sort of higher
level stuff). so after some (long) time, you can have SS and Kael faction.
