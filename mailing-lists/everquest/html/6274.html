There has been much debate about what level to start aa's at and at
what level aa's seem to move the fastest. In my experience in EQ I
tend to get my first 6 aa's at level 51. The first 3 I spend on run 3
on any class of character that I start. The next 3,if it is melee I
spend on regen 3, if a caster I spend on either wisdom or intelligence
depending upon the class. Then I level up to level 60 and work my
aa's again there. I have been known to work aa's up to 100 at level
60, the aa xp seems to move a little faster at level 60. As an example
in Sol Ro tower at level 60 you will get an aa about every 8 to 10
kills. I will say again the unrest instancezone off of butcher block
is great xp. Easy single pulls and you have a chance at a nice upgrade
at the end. You just have to make sure that your average level is
within 2 levels of the lowest member of the group.
