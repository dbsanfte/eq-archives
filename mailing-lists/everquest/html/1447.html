I'll try to take these one at a time. =)

Sneak lets you sneak around a mob * warning if you pass in front of them you
will most likely be seen * its useful for a number of situations. exp. If a
merchant will not sell to you.. sneak and move up behind them, then right
click as usual to start up a buying session. Be sure to consider a mob in
both situations.. it should tell you they are indifferent, else otherwise
they can see you.

Prior to backstab... Its not really necessary to attack from behind for you
gain no benefit without the skill. Yet its good practice. It's also easier
to know when a mob is mad at you so you can evade. Remember when grouped
with others take full advantage of using Evade ( turn off attack and click
hide ) if successful the mob will turn away from you and you can continue
attacking =)

Disable traps is a under used skill. There are a number of traps through
out the game. Yet most of them were rendered harmless.. (example.. the
swinging axe in sola - and the dropping stone in solb ) You can train it
anytime and anywhere by simply clicking on the skill button. Yeah I know
that sounds dumb but that's how its done.

Remember when your a rogue your hide and sneak buttons work differently from
other classes.. You can enable both of them and move around unseen by
nearly every mob in the game. Comes in handy when pulling your friends
corpses from dangerous areas. Or joining a group that's deep in a dungeon
undetected by the mobs that have respawn =)
