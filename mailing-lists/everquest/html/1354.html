Kill the goblins. Kill lots and lots of runnyeye goblins. This increases
your faction with the people in Kaladim. Oh, and killing the BB bandits will
increase your faction with the citizens of Kaladim. Possibly the Stormguard
too. So get a group and pick off the bandits. Beware that PegLeg and his
group undercon. They may be green but they hit hard and fast. They tend not
to stay rooted very long. They like to gang up on you. So get someone who
can harmony/mezmerize or snare. Then kick the crap out of em one at a time.
If a lesser spirit spawns when you kill Peg Leg do NOT kill it. It is bad
faction with shamans if you do.
Also, keep the Runnyeye Warbeads to turn in to the guy in the same building
as the guy who takes the belts and pads.
