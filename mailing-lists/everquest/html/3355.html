Made my way to Kunark, and decided to try Warsliks Woods for brutes
to begin my adventure. As a Halfling tailor, brute hides are a
valuable next step in tailoring. My son recalled finding brutes in
the tunnel to Dalnir, so I went there. Once I do an initial harmony
to draw only one, I am able to root, dot, dot, root, nuke with
surprising ease. With a respawn of about 15 minutes I can keep
farming these for quite a while as I build my tailoring skills making
Halfling Vale leather armor.

Experience is not great, but since I will be working on trade skills
as I farm, the time making lvl 34 will be well spent here.

Once I have gotten tired of farming here I will try Frontier
Mountains.
