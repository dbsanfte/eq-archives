You go from a ship in Stonebrunt, but instead of working on a timed schedule
like old world boats, you simply click the door and it zones you over. I
thought it seemed like it ran pretty well on my 450 mhz, actually, and was
one of the most "old world" zones out of any expansion since Luclin. Jagged
Pine is a bit rougher, think NK on crack, or a hyped version of DSP. The
critters are aggressive and generally powerful. The humanoids in JP are
somewhat easier, in fact my warrior soloed at the poachers at 51 and 52.
Some of the various loots from the zone are nice, some weapons and jewelry.
The poachers drop pelts also, never lower than LQ, so for more advanced
tailoring, this place is heaven.
