When I was a level 14 druid someone on the list suggested Lavastorm.
I was there until level 19. The loot is pretty good, cash anything
from poor to fair, and the hunting interesting. Getting there was a
special adventure for me, as I am KOS in Neriak. I went in chamo and
levitated, turned left as I entered the forest, and kept to the zone
wall. There is a river half way through the zone, which I levved
across (not being a good swimmer) and kept on along the wall.

I really liked the rock dervishes. Good cash and they dropped ore,
which sells well but is heavy. I would pick up a load until I was
encumbered even with strength, then port to a selling point. If you
can find someone to port the stuff out at a discount, the ore would
be profitable.

I mostly solo, so that is what I did there. Lots of fun.

Good hunting.
