Not me.  I prefer grouping.  It's faster xp than soloing in my opinion.  As a 65 druid, I can solo a lot of things, but it takes too much time to kill them.  Root, snare, dot, dot, dot.  Sit and wait.  Keep that up til the mob is dead.  In a group, most times I only have to worry about snaring and dotting, and maybe back up healing.  It's not as labor intensive, and the xp moves faster with constant pulls.  Unfortunately, lately, it seems like it's been increasingly more difficult to find good groups so I end up soloing most of the time anyway with LFG on.
