To get this EQonelist EverQuest TIP LIST off the ground, I will submit my
first two TIPS:

GENERAL TIP

1) When trading with NPCs in a new city or town (or any town for that
matter) /con ("consider") a bunch of the trading NPCs and see which ones look
at you the friendliest. You will get better prices for both sales and
purchases of items from these NPCs than ones who look at you indifferently or
negatively. This can save you up 30%!!!!

GENERAL TIP

2) Never /shout "HELP!" - Shouts are heard throughout the entire zone and
most players that could help you realize that the zone is huge and you are
unlikely to be near enough to rescue, thus, they will rarely bother to even
look. Always use /say "help" - Then, whoever hears it will know that you
are in the immediate area and will often come to your aid.
