Hi Mike,

I'd recommend the Testament of Venear quest, for one
thing. The resulting item adds +10 to wis. The only
problem is you have to camp a guy who's almost always
camped and who has an 8 hour spawn time. My druid
(lvl 47 at the time) did the quest in 5 hours, but I
got very lucky because 1.) no one was camping the guy
when I got there, and 2.) he spawned after only 4
hours of my sitting there.

Go to www.eqdruids.com to find out the particulars of
the quest .. I don't remember the names of characters
off the top of my head :)

Let me recommend finding where the guy (Dylinn?
Dylan?) spawns and binding there, and checking every
once in a while to see if he's there :) Sitting there
is no fun whatsoever.

Good luck fellow Tree-hugger!
