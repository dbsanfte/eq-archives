If you have Luclin you might want to kill Lesser Shades in shadeweaver's
thicket for shadeling silks and swirling shadows. swirling shadows are used
in smithing and sell pretty well. If you can take down Shak Dratha they drop
shade silk and condensed shadow (used in smithing to make arrowheads which
are used for high level fletching).
Also, hold on to low quality pelts and high quality pelts. Even medium
quality pelts. They are used in tailoring and pottery (lined poison vials
require quality pelts or zombie skins). You also might want to hold onto
gems you get if you ever want to do jewelcraft. Farming gems can save you
alot of coin.
