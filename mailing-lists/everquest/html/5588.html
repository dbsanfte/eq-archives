The boats are really slow and awkward to use. You are
better off swimming.

As far as plat.

1. Buy 2 opal encrusted steins to boost your charisma
when selling.

2. Farm fine steel weapons. They sell for around 5pp
with 105 charisma to vendors. Places to farm FS
include Sol A, Lower Guk, Runnyeye etc.

3. Acrylia caverns in velious. Farm fine steel, velium
blocks, spider silk, gems etc. Sell velium and silk
to players in bazaar. On Vazaelle you can sell velium
blocks of various sizes from 40pp to 90pp per block.

4. LDON adventures yield good plat and you gain exp at
the same time.

5. Loy. Go to Dulak's harbor and kill pirates. They
drop 4-5 pp plus weapons that sell for around 5pp to
vendors and the occasional high-end gems. Diamonds,
blue diamonds etc.

6. Farm tradeskill items. Spider silks sell for around
20-30pp per stack. Spiderling silks for around the
same. Animal pelts sell pretty good too. For spider
silks hunt crag spiders in east karana. Kill
spiderlings in everfrost or innothule swamp. For pelts
my favorite place is the karanas and stonebrunt
mountains.

Hope this helps.
