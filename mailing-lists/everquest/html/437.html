Bouncer Hurd (ogre) and one of his other bouncer buds (ogre) patrol around
the outside and outskirts of the Cazic Thule dungeon in Feerott. (Hurd is
not actually inside the dungeon itself) He quite frequently, along with his
bud, comes INTO the corridor safe zone area where you zone into Cazic itself
and starts killing everyone who is trying to heal who zoned out of Cazic
from the notorious trains.

Hurd is a VERY nasty, VERY hard and fast hitting ogre whom I have only seen
killed by like a 35+ mob. I did see a 42 level paladin in full plate armor
fully buffed with a ghoulbane kill him solo, but that was only because I was
standing behind him and putting greater heals on him until I ran out of
mana, and then two others players jumped in at the end to help finish him
off. And if Hurd's bud is with him, you are in double trouble. I was
sitting and medding at the zone when they both showed up and literally in
less than 2 seconds, stunned me, double teamed me and took out about 3/4 of
my hps (I am level 39). If I had not been at full health and been 2 inches
from the zone I would've died. A friend of mine died to Hurd and his
bouncer bud 4 times in about 3 minutes....he was bound in the corridor right
outside the cazic zone, so each time Hurd killed him and he reappeared, Hurd
and his friend killed him again. He was NOT a happy shaman that nite.

And yes, Hurd is the one with the crude stein, but not always. It's a rare
spawn from what I am told. I was lucky enough to get one from that paladin
I kept healed and buffed so he could solo Hurd.
