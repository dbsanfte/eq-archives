<<You have five lines to work with in a macro and my
greater heal spell is in slot 1:

/sit off
/g Casting Greater Heal on %t. Stay close friend.
/cast 1 >>

I learned this trick off a cleric message board .. in
addition to the /cast 1 line, I add two more lines:
/pause 10
/cast 1

This way, if you fizzle, it casts GHeal again as soon
as is possible without you having to cast it again or
in cast you miss the fizzle message. If the first
cast takes, it just tells you You can't do that while
casting, or some such.
