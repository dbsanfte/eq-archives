I've had this happen to me as well.  It's NOT possible for others to loot or drag your corpse unless you give them permission using the /consent command.
 
More likely what's happened is that your corpse is inside a tree or object or even slightly underground.  If you have the /loc where you died, stand there and use the /corpse command which will summon it to you (if you're close enough).  If you don't know where it is, try gaining the help of a necromancer or bard, they have the ability to locate corpses.
 
You can also buy a Chipped Bone Rod in Felwithe (I don't remember which shop, sorry), when used, this item will point you towards the closest corpse you have in the zone you're in.  You can then run in that direction until you get close enough to summon it with /corpse.
