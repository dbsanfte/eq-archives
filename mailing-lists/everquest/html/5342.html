If you go to East Commons and kill the Willowisps they drop a lightstone or greater lightstone as well as a little coin. Don't put it in a backpack leave it in one of your inventory slots and it will improve your vision. I've had one on my beastlord now since level 4 and it hasn't burned out yet. Oh and the greater lightstones sell for about 6pp depending on your charisma
