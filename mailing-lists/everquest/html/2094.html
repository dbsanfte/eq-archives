> Here's the actually policy if your wondering:
>
> What if I can't see my corpse?
>
> Corpses are sometimes "invisible" after a death. A player may ask a bard
or
> necromancer to verify that indeed their corpse is invisible, (the bard or
> necromancer can see it, but the player cannot), as sometimes where a
player
> "thinks" they died, is not where they actually are. This is especially
> common in larger zones like Greater Faydark. Once a player has verified
> their corpse is indeed invisible to them, usually a quick log out and log
in
> corrects the problem, if it does not, give the Guides a /petition and they
> will come to assist.
>
> How long will my corpse last?
>
> Level 1 - 5: 30 minutes real time. Corpses need to be retrieved
immediately
> or they will decay.
> Level 6+: 24 hours of online time, or 1 week of offline time, whichever
> comes first.
>
> *Note: Empty corpses that are level 30 or above characters will remain in
> game for 3 hours. It is best advised to get your corpse right away. If
that
> is not possible, remember that one-hour in game is the same as seven hours
> out of game. Also, be advised that your corpse decay is still advancing if
> you leave your computer at the character select screen or play on another
> character on that same account.
>
