In my experience on The Rathe, the only level 2 players you will see in full bronze got that full bronze from one of their older siblings, who DID it the hard way to begin with...worked their way up the ladder until they were high enough to acquire such good items. I currently have only one persona in EQ...a cleric who is almost level 40 now. She acquired her exp and items the hard way...fighting and dying for them, saving enough plat to buy them, camping for hours and days on end for them, and trading some of them for others. Because when my current persona DOES get a sibling, you can bet that is whom they are going to go to. A Twinkie? Yah, you betcha. But don't forget....they were originally gotten the long, hard way.

Been there...done that....not gonna do it again.
