Think about it this way....
 
Both SK and pally epics are 2HS.  So it's good to keep that skill maxed
 
Yet, pallies get Bash.  In order to use Bash you have to carry a shield, in which case 2HS is not possible.  So it's good to keep 1HS up.
 
SK's I think get a similar ability, or maybe even Bash, but I dont play a SK so I dont know for sure.  But 1HS is also important to SK.
 
On the other hand, pallies and SK's usually work as pullers in groups, so they have to have good Taunting abilities, and sometimes weapons they have can help with taunt by doing more damage.  Go with the weapon that helps with that regardless of 1Hs or 2HS.
 
Just my two cents.
