Thats not really a new command .. its been avaliable since the final beta.
Comes in handy when you want to loot your corpse in a dangerous area. Find
someone to make you invisible and use the /corpse command over and over,
till you can drag it to a safe area allowing you to loot it safely.

The only other command that i can think of is the /pet sit down command. I
never found it in the book for some reason. ( maybe its just me ) That
command lets you place your pet in a spot where it will just rest. It will
not attack other creatures around you unless you are attacked. Rememeber to
tell it to follow you when you are ready to go, or it will just sit there..
