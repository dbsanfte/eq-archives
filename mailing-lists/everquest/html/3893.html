As for learning how to play EverQuest, I know how the newbies feel. I am
fairly new, and if it wasn't for my boyfriend to teach me everything, I
would be so lost. Just remember these few codes:

/ooc - Sends an out of character message
/shout -Sends a shout

If you have any questions what something means, just shout it or send an out
of character message and someone will help you out. I still have to shout
questions. You will catch on quickly. If you have any questions, feel free
to email me at: Thunder_Cat_4283@... and I will do what I can to
help you out. Also, here are a few short hands:

PST = Please Send Tell
WTS = Willing to Sell
WTB = Willing to Buy
LFG = Looking for Group
