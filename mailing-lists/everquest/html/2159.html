Yes. Make sure you have camo memmed. FM is very very nasty. The brutes con
blue to my lvl 35 cleric. And they hit fairly hard. The main problem with
hunting them is the possibility of ads. So you will need to harmony.
Snare would be helpful. kiting would be dangerous in FM bc of ads.
Especially the larger ones. I suggest that if you enter FM from LoIO you go
left rather than right to hunt. over to the right are goblin casters. I
didn't see goblins casters much over to the left.

High quality ore drops off of pickclaw warriors and raiders in
Highpass/Highkeep (I can never keep it straight which is called which). You
would need a group at your level. Since you are a druid, people would really
want you in group there. Sow can be cast. And if you have regen and other
beneficial spells they will love you. Especially since you can heal. If you
have coin to spend, go to highkeep and auction to buy high quality ore.
There are usually some players who will part with it. I think the small
bricks go for around 5pp and the large bricks around 10pp.
