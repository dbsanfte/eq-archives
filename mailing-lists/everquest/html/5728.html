I have found silk to be one of the best ways to earn plat for us youngin's.
Spiderling Silk, Spider Silk, Shadeling Silk, and Shade Silk sell for 1-5
plat each in the bazaar. You can carry 20 in a stack and they are so light
you never worry about being encumbered. Many places have spiders,
bonecrawlers, widows, etc that drop silk so you should be able to find a
place to farm that is friendly to you. I am using a human (level 8) at the
moment and he has been haning out in the commonlands. Once the spiderlings
go green I start on the bigger spiders. Not only am I making $$ I am
improving my skills the whole time. Loot all the spider parts, sell
everything to local vendors but keep the silk. When selling to vendors look
at their stock. Occasionally I find silk for sale for a gold or two, buy it
all! When you have enough silk sell it in the bazaar. See what others are
selling it for and price it the same as the lowest priced guy. Demand is
high enough I always sell out in under and hour.

With my cleric I use my spells to kill since you can get better at casting
even if you cast on green creatures. I have noticed the my melee char does,
on rare occasions, improve skills on green chars. This is contrary to what
I have read in a few places, but it is so rare you might as well say melee
skills never go up on green.

If your on a server that allows for more then one character, make one char
and send it to the bazaar right away. That char will now live in the bazaar
so it does not need skills or equip. Gather silk and put in shared bank.
Switch to bazaar char and sell, drop plat in shared bank and swtitch back to
main char. Keeps you from running so much.

Gloomingdeep silk is an exception. Have not sold any in bazaar, sell to
vendors. Don't buy if you find it.
