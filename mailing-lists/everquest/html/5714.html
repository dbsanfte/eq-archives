As far as I know, there is no way to check faction.  I've asked this same question before.  Even gone so far as to send/feedback about it in game that there needs to be some way of checking.  You just kind of learn as you go along I guess.  Needless to say, as an Iksar your faction probably will suck in most places outside of Kunark, and even some places INSIDE Kunark since the Iksars and the Sarnaks are against each other.  And if you ever want to get into Freeport you gotta kill more orcs than you'd ever care to.    Most of the time, if there's a quest you want to do, you can find it on Casters Realm or Allakhazam's and they'll give you a faction fixing guide  in order to do the quest.
