A good, well balanced group of mid to high 20's can take on the weaker
Giants at the Fort in Frontier Mountains, moving inside as you get into
the high 20's low 30's before moving on at about 35.

You can also try the Giant Fort in Warsliks Woods, which is lower level
than the one in FM. A mid-high 20's group can own the Warsliks Giants
easily, though the drop rate isn't as good as in FM, and the general
loot value is lower.
