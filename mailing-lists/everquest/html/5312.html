If you ask these people who have 100k plat and more they will all pretty much tell you the same thing.  It takes hours and hours of work, and whatever you do...don't spend what you have.  It's kinda like buying a car.  You work your job, you put a little aside each paycheck to save up until you have enough to go out and buy the car.  The other thing is that the people who have this kind of money are high level and go on lots of raids so they get a fair split of the loot which always seems to give them nice gear along the way.  That means they're not having to spend their money on gear.  If you tried to do this at around level 20 it would take you awhile, mainly becuase the amount of loot and the type of items you get off mobs aren't as nice as the loot and items you get off higher mobs.  Suffice to say, I don't see a lot of lower level toons riding around on horses... unless that person has a higher level alt with the plat to spare to buy that horse for the toon.  I'm like you, I've never had more than 5k at a time.  I thought it was a red letter day when I broke 2k. Then I spent 1500pp on nice armor for one of my lower level toons and there went all my money.
