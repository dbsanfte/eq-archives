Silk is for tailoring. Something else that sells well are hides,
especially hopper hides. I hunted all kinds of hoppers and their
hides can be used for a lot of mid-level tailoring. And leather
padding is needed for a lot of the planar armor and sells great.

I suggest you have a look at EQTraders. There you can find all the
tradeskill stuff and see what else people need.
