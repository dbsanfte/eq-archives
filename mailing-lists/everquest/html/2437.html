Yep, you can minimize EQ, you can play 2 EQ accounts at a time on the same
machine. It is one of best tools for healing, porting, damage shielding, C3
slave. You have to have a fairly strong machine to run 2 accounts at once,
but my machine can handle it and I have a good stable Internet connection.
The writer of the program is still playing EQ also, so we've seen updates in
the EQW version number, I think the most recent is 2.21. Any earlier version
will not work. One thing I loved was getting all my chat colors just the
right color. You can edit you ini client file and then refresh EQW and get
everything looking just as you like.
