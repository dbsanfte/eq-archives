Fast way to level?

Don't waste time looting, only kill fast.
Fight close to the guards, know when to run to the guards and give up the
fight.
Guards = friend, learn how to use them
Pick your fights wisely.
Don't waste your time grouping, every level can solo from atleast 1-10.
Do not chat in tells, ooc or shout, it will just waste your time,
concentrate on killing.
Don't zone or go to town, it takes up too much time.
Learn your health and mana. You won't have to med/sit to 50% just to get in
one kill.
Learn the right time to die. Remember, after death you come back full health
and mana. Very little if any exp lost on death from level 1-5. Your supplied
food and water on death the first few levels, atleast you use to.

If you played the game a while, you get bored at times and do different
things. I have raced when new servers come up to see who could get to level
10 the fastest. I have power leveled and have been power leveled a lot.
Recently took my brothers Iksar monk from level 1 - 46 just out boredom over
the past holidays. ( it is really unbelievable how much Iksar can regen with
the right gear and regrowth ). If you have played the game, and know how it
works, I see nothing wrong with PLing. Mater of fact, I can find it very
gratifying, exciting and fun.

Oh well, if this is your first character, strongly urge you not to some of
the things listed above, but that's not you asked.
