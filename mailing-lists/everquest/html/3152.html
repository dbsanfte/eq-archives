If you go to the moon and go to the bazaar zone, just around the corner from
the banker is a row of venders. The first vernder sells peridots, stackable
coffins and non stackable coffins. The shadow knight and level 49 necro
spell is the non stackable kind. The small stackable coffins are called
Tiny Jaid Inlaid Coffin, not sure what the other one is called, but they
will always be sold at the same place. They both look alike except 1 will
have the small little number in the corner for how many you have. They look
like a long, narrow, light brown box. You can't miss them. Look at the
price. I know the stackable ones cost approximately ~85 platinum and the non
stackable ones are ~175 platinum. iirc.

Of course you could go to dark elf city and buy them from the cleric guild
in the 3rd gate, just make a newbie, but then you have to transfer plat and
all that junk.
