Yes it can.  EverQuest has multiple camera angles.  You can do 3 different overhead angles and one from behind.  Use the F9 key to change camera views.  Default view is always first persion.  Use your mouse scroll wheel (if you have one) to zoom in and out.  F10 removes UI files so you can see full screen.  The minus (-) key on your numeric keypad will take screenshots and save them to either BMP or JPG files in your EverQuest screenshots folder.
