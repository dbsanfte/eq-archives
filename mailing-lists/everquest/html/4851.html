At your level, farm grikbar fur and teeth in Steamfont. It's not super cash,
but at your level it's respectable enough. With halfway decent cha the fur
goes for nearly 1 PP a tuft, and the teeth for about 1/3 a PP. I managed to
get 25 PP from lvl 5 to 8 with my froggie warrior here without even trying.
