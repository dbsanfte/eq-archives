Hi! and "Welcome to EverQuest" :)

You may be interested in the tailoring trade skill. This mainly involves
selling the items you have tailored to other players. I like this skill
because most of the tailoring material is dropped from animals which you
can gain experience killing, so you don't have to take time out fighting
mobs to go back to town just to practice your trade. Using info from the
two links below, I was mass producing and selling weight reduction
backpacks for about 100pp each at level 15.

http://boards.station.sony.com/ubb/everquest/Forum4/HTML/000675.html
http://www.eqtraders.com/

Good Luck!
