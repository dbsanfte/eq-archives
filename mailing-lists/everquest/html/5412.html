There is a skill cap.  It is 5X your level + 5.  So for example, if you are level 15, then the skill cap is (5X15) + 5 = 80.  The skill cap for level 15 is 80.  Also some skills don't progress after a certain point.  Swimming, for example, doesn't progress past 200.  Once you hit 200, that's it.  Regardless of midifiers, higher levels or whatever... it never gets any better.  Also, druids have Specializations after level 30.  Your first Specialization will progress up to a certain point, but every other specialization stops at 50. 
 
So I hope that answers yoru question.
