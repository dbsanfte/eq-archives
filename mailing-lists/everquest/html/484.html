The dimensional pocket is quiet useful at some times.

You can summon a bag that only has four slots; however, whatever you
place in those slots no longer adds to your weight. Therefore you can
carry loot (especially heavy weapons) on your person without encumbering
your character. You can also give these pockets to others with the same
effect. Comes in handy when you have tons of looted weapons that you would
otherwise leave behind due to encumberment.

I must warn you. If you camp, while holding a pocket, the pocket and the
items contained within will be destroyed. As long as you bear this in mind,
and sell or store your loot in a safe place before camping, you will be ok.

Another problem: if you have frequent server disconnects - be aware that
when you return, unlike other items ( as in your pet that will sometimes be
there when you return ) your dimensional pockets will not.

I not sure about the staff of tracing but i here its pretty good.
