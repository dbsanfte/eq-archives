You have to put the sense heading button in the same place for all
your characters for this to work well. Be sure not to place a
hotkey/social in that spot that you dont want to go off automatically
(like spells or something). I also map senseheading to all the
hotkey pages that I use to the same location for all my characters,
that way if you change hotkey pages, then it will still be set right,
and it will remind you not to place a hotkey in that spot later.

Hope this helps some.
