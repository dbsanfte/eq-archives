The proper name for them are Journeyman's Boots.
 
Basically you have to give like 3500 gold to a dwarf in Rathe Mountains along with something dropped from the ancient Cyclops and a Shadow Rapier to get the boots. 
 
Keep in mind, the only benefit of these boots is the speed.  And it's not as fast as sow.  Thee is only 1 AC on the boots and no other stat mods.
 
The effect is permanant as long as the boots are worn as far as I know, but they do not stack with sow.  In other words, if you have sow cast upon you, you wont see a noticeable change in speed with J-boots PLUS sow... you will just see the increase from the J-boots TO sow; you will run at standard sow speed, not the two combined.
 
Hope that helps.
