Also, if any of the items for the quest are stackable they should NOT be
stacked when handing in to the quest npc.

So if the quest involves handing in 4 Batwings, then make sure that the
trade window with the npc has 1 batwing in each slot.

Use ctrl when clicking on a stack to select a single item from it.

Hand in the first item by clicking on the NPC while holding the item on
the cursor, but hand all the subsequent items in by clicking them into
empty boxes in the trade window.

If you click them all on to the NPC directly then stackable items will
end up stacked in the trade window and only count as 1 item handed in.
