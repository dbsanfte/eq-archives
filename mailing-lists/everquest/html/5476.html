Hi Peg,
 
You have started a thread here that is very intresting.   Like others I enjoyed reading about your early exploits, and now reading about what others went thru is so cool.  I started with a wizzard.....which wasn't the best choice for a first time player.  But I read the Lord of the Rings, and Gandolph is powerfull, sooo I chose a wizzard.  I came up in freeport and immeaditly got lost.  Then I ran out of food and drink, they didn't start you with much back then.  I never saw a McDonnald's or a Safeway, and I'm dying from starvation.  I asked another player where to find food, and he said just let your character die and you'll get more, I was horrified.  I too had to ask how to open doors....tried saying open sesimee, didn't work.  But when I tried to hail an orc, he killed me, and food problem was solved, temporarly.  Then I ran the wrong way and the game froze for a few seconds, then it said I was in East Commonlands.  By that point, I was so frustrated that I allmost quit.  But then I attacked a skelli and won, I was sooo proud of that kill. 
 
The point of all this is we all went thru what you are now.  And there is no totally right or wrong way to play, because you can't possibly accomplish all there is in the game.  We have all made mistakes, and there are ways to correct them later.  The main thing is to have fun.  So somebody power levels you a bit.....enjoy it, it's another experience in the game.  And if you attack a willowisp without a magical weapon.....well, join the club.  I think we all made that mistake at least once.  You will have times that everything goes wrong, and other times it all goes right.....just like real life.  That's what makes this game so much fun,   and before you know it, you'll be telling a newbie about the letter you lost, and laughing about how much you didn't know "way back then".  Some players are mean and cruel, so be carefull, but most are friendly and willing to help you when things get "impossible".  When you are lost, most will help you find your way.  Just like this "e-loop" many are trying to help you now.
 
So, relax, and enjoy, and tell us more.....you write very well and it's a pleasure to read what you have to say.
