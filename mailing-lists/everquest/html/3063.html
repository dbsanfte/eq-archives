Honestly, for the most part, money does not really start to get easier until you hit the low to mid 20's, as at that point, depending upon where you hunt, mobs start to drop fine steel and bronze weapons, not to mention chainmail and bronze armor.  There are certain areas, and mobs you can camp at most levels, from about 6 on, that drops a bit better gear than others.  The only other way to gain much in the way of pp at the lower levels is to drop every cp earned into a tradeskill such as smithing, or tailoring, to get it up to over 100, where you can sell stuff to other players, such as player made backpacks, and banded armor.  All it takes to find the right mobs is experimentation, and research, eventually you will find what you are looking for.
