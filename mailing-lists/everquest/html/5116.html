At 51 I would think you could handle Dulak fairly well.  I could solo there with my druid pulling singles or quad kiting at 49.  It's decent xp. As far as AA points go, in your AA window there's a slider bar where you can set the percentage of XP earned to go for levels or AA.  Most people I know will set it at 50% so half the XP goes toward leveling and the other half to AA.  This takes awhile to get AA though.  One guy I know waited til he hit level 65 and THEN started working on his AA points.  As far as what to spend them on, I'd suggest the General skills first since you'll most likely get more immediate use from them.
