Rangers can solo EW to a certain extent on some of the animals
there. Fear Kiting works wonders. Necros can also use a similar
tact by fearing and the sending their pet in after the mob.

While EW can be soloed, you have to be very careful of dervishes and
giants. First off, even at level 55+ dervishes will be much lower
in level but can still hurt you plenty. The hit hard, and can
quikcly gang up if there is more than one in the area. The second
danger is the giants that roam. While these guys are easily
quelmed, they also hit hard and can gang up. If you are fighting
one of these and a dervish or two join in... well you better be a
tank with massive HP, be a necro with a pet, or have an extra pair
of shoes in the bank because you are going to be on a CR soon.

Also, EW has one additional danger that many people do not think
about. If you are fighting near the Kael ramp you don't want to
linger. Hanging around the Kael ramp too long spawns the Death
Squad and these things will tear through anything that is not on
their faction. Just a heads up.

Drago
