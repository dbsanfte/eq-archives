Just in case this is a serious question, I like to use some of the
rooms in Freeport as a twinking place. Some are on upper floors,
seldom visited. I usually only do this when there are not a lot of
people on, to reduce the probability of someone picking up my drop.

I get my twinker and twinkie in the same place, make the drop, log
twinker off, log twinkie on, make the pick up.

For the most part I keep this in a rp mode by having twinkie and
twinker be cousins, one passing to the other such things as relatives
might to help each other out. No super-twinks. A bit of a push, but
it works for me.

My primary and first alt are from Rivervale, and I found a few good
twink spots there as well. However, one of them I moved to Freeport
and had bound there, so my higher level character visits him in
Freeport from time to time.
