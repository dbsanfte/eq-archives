I think that craft items should be more beneficial and thus more
valuable. At present, there is little motivation to craft unless you
have a gaming predilection to do things like that. At present, by the
time you have leveled up enough to aquire materials, plus leveled your
crafting, the items you make aren't as good as what you can hunt or buy.

A real in-game economy would add to the fun. Especially if it would be
good even for casual gamers. I can't always find the time to play
enough to level and aquire stuff in ways which seem economical. There
should be provision for a good gaming experience even for very casual
gamers.
