I'm somewhat inclined to agree.   I, however, have not been one to ever complain about Verant or Sony.  I take it with a grain of salt.  I've never been cheated on EQ, I've never had crappy customer service, I enjoy the game, I am an addict.  When I die, I'm temporarily upset, but then I go on and that's that.  I don't blame anyone else for anything.  It gets nothing accomplished.   I understand the reason behind charging for EQIM and if I think it'll enhance my EQ gameplay or help me in any way, then sure, I don't mind forking over the extra cash for it so long as I feel I can afford it.   On rethinking an earlier statement I made about not wanting to pay for it, I look at it from the perspective that AIM, ICQ, Yahoo, and MSN Messenger services are free.  They make their money from other things.  If you'll notice they all pretty much carry banners and advertising.  And as with most companies if you don't want the advertising and banners you have to pay extra.   I'm sure if Sony/Verant wanted to EQIM could have banners etc, but that would ruin it for a lot of people so in order to keep it banner free, you're paying for something specific.  That's all.

 

You could argue it either way, but now I'm inclined to think that if it will enhance my EQ game time, I'll pay for it. Maybe that's me.
