At your level, give willowisps a shot. they range from 9-11
You can sell lightstones to merchants for almost a plat, and greater lightstones for almost 4, or you can sell to players for around 6pp. They give pretty good exp too, you should fly up pretty quick
best place for wisps off the top of my head are:
1) West commons, in the area between toll booth and druid ring
2) the isle in Erud's Crossing. Take the boat from Qeynos -> Erudin and there will be a stop halfway
3) Qeynos Hills, at the area right after you zone from qeynos up to the road
