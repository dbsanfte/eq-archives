I would encourage you to work on all your skills,
you'll not be sorry! The epic is two 1hs weapons. Of
all the skills for my 30 Warrior, I've enjoyed the 1hs
the most due to the duel wield we get at 13, and then
double attack at 15 makes it even more fun!

However, I've come across some wonderful bargains for
2hb and 2hs weapons that had amazing stats and were
limited to warrior only or just a few other classes
and sellers kept discouting the prices due to having a
limited market. I landed a really fun 2hb weapon with
super stats and some haste for a few hundred plat. I
was forced to work on my 2hb then, but it's been worth
it!

I know a lot of melee classes that will switch out a
weapon they aren't as skilled in during groups,
especially when the situation is well under control
and there are more than one melee to help taunt. This
provides an excellent opportunity to help work on
those skills!

Have fun!
