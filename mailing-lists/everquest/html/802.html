Being a rather high level warrior.. I get inspected constantly by lowbies
and I inspect/am inspected by people my level all the time. It in no way
bothers me, and if it does, it just shows you're getting into the game a
little too much. The only time this should ever make you feel uneasy is if
you're on a PvP server because they're scoping for your items to see if they
should kill you usually. If this honestly bothers you, I would imagine you
the type of person who is claustrophobic or who gets upset if someone bumps
into you in the subway or something along those lines. Eventually, you'll
take it with an air of pride must likely, because it's usually just newbies
gawking over your equipment.
