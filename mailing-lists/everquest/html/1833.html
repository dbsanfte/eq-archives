Ahhh a cleric .. what a noble class, I prefer rogues myself. Anyway,
for newbies I recommend that you hang out at the dwarf fishing camp
and wait for someone to work over the named dorfs there. Hey drop
serious weapons and ringmail armour. You may be able to get them to
cough up some of the goods, since selling them locally will be tough
as they are ruining their faction with the Citizens of Kaladim.
Begging never hurt either.

As a new cleric I agree with some of the statements made below. Being
BRAND new, just solo around your starting point and loot everything.
Sell that stuff and save all the money. You will need it for spells
obviously and you'll also want to pick up some better armor than the
tunic your guild gives you. I wore leather armor for a very long time
until I had saved enough to buy something else.
>
> When you do get higher in level and can start buying some better
items, work on buying stuff that is +wis. This will help a lot.
Farther down the road at much higher levels you will want to work on
making your AC and saves better, but you shouldn't have to worry
about that too much for quite some time.
>
> Everyone has different opinions on how to play, but the important
thing is to have fun. Find some people on your server that play in
your area. Usually you'll start seeing familiar faces.

> I have a 38 dwarf cleric on The Nameless. A few tips:
>
> 1) Max wisdom - not all subscribe to this, but you have an inate
strength already as a dwarf. I put my other points to bring up
either dex or agility, whichever lower
> 2) Find a regular partner to help you to level at the low levels -
you can solo first few levels, but I found grouping with one other
PC helped
> 3) As a cleric, go to Kurn's tower around level ten - lots of
undead to nuke on when alone and clerics were always welcome in
groups there
>
> Use the other yahoogroups specifically for clerics for more help
and advice: EQ_Cleric@y... and EQ-Clerics@y... - Some of the folks
there are quite, er, vocal and opinionated, hehe. But we all know
that Dwarves make the best clerics - you picked the right race.
