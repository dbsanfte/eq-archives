Hello, yes as mentioned a few times begging is useless. You have pretty
much wasted that time working on your begging skill. Why do you have
direction sense? Norrath is a huge world..you will soon answer your own
question on that. And safe fall, just keep doing what you are doing to
build it up, practice Mend with Safe Fall whenever you take too much damage.
Remember you can only use Mend every 6 minutes. I suggest working on that
skill the most..if your skill in Mend is 5 then it will only work 5% of the
time. Plus a couple things to remember as a monk do not carry more than
14lbs..do it and your AC will suffer. Balence your weapon skills meaning
use a 1hb in one hand and a hand to hand weapon in the other hand or
whatever. Once you get Feign Death practice that skill as much as you can,
it will save your life many times. And also not all the characters are
white, Eurdites are of course black and the dark elves, trolls, vah shir,
and froglocks are their own colors/races. Well that is it Happy Hunting.
