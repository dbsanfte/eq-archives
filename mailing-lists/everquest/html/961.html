Subject: New Fennin Ro Guild


Hello all!

We are looking for several adventurous souls to join us in forming a new guild on Fennin Ro. Our purpose will be to enjoy this game for all it's worth -- to help each other, to teach those new to Norrath the skills they will need to survive. Rules will be simple -- things such as never beg, never ks, and always help your guildmate first. We will also be focused on life in Norrath being more than just the battle -- other skills are given to you for a reason. Want to learn how to tailor? You've got to start with the basics, and if you and a couple of guildmates want a nice quiet night, why not go out together and gather spiderling silks to get going? Or get together for a trip to Steamfont to pick up some supplies for smithing?

We will also be planning raids for various levels, a couple of parties... and maybe even a wedding or two. )

So if you're interested in helping us create this guild, just to have fun, either leave a post here, or email to akane@... with your character name, level, class and race, along with an email address where we can reach you.

Look forward to hearing from you! )
