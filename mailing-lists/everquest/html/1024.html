Subject: EQ research


Dear EQ players,
I am part of a 6 person research team at Queen's University in
Canada that is analyzing Everquest. More specifically, I am looking at
the way people roleplay different genders in the game. I am interested
in the reasons why people choose to play characters of different
genders. The survey is a small part of a large paper that we are
writing for a course. As part of our research, we are conducting a
survey on EQ players. If you are interested, please take the time to
answer the following questions and email your responses to
7AEW1@.... Additionally, if you are interested in
answering more questions in depth, please indicate your interest in your
reply. You are not obligated in any way to answer the following
questions. Thanks for your time.

Sincerely,

Naidan (Amy Wilson)


GENERAL INFORMATION

1. What is your gender in real life?

2. Have you ever played a female and a male character on EQ?

3. What level is your primary male character and your female character
(please indicate which is your male character and your female character
in your reply)?

4. What race(s) are your characters?

5. Why did you choose these races?


MALE VERSUS FEMALE
6. Why did you choose to play a male character? a female?

7. What are the advantages to playing a female? a male?

8. What are the disadvantages to playing a female? a male?


EXPERIENCE QUESTIONS
9. Has anyone asked you what your gender is in real life?

(If yes to question 9) Who? Did you know the other player well?

(If yes to question 9) What character were you (ie. was it your
male or female character?)

(If yes to question 9) How did you respond and why?


10. Are you treated differently by other characters when you play a
character opposite of your sex in real life?

11. Do you think you know who is playing a character on EQ that's the
opposite of their sex in real life? If so, how?

12. Do you group more with your male character or with your female
character?

Thank you very much for taking the time to fill out this survey. If you
would be kind enough to meet us online (either in game, ICQ or IRC), we
would be more than happy. Please indicate if you are interested in
meeting with us in your response (please email responses to
7AEW1@...). Thank you for your time!
