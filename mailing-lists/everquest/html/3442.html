Hi everyone,
I just discovered this quest tonight for any ranger. I've got
the first two done and am working on the third.

SERVER: Solsek Ro
Class: Ranger
Min lvl: Unknown, mostly newbies I guess
Location(s): So far Greater Feydark and Steamfront Mountains
Starting Point: Faydark's Champions
Contact: Samatansyn Flamecatcher

This quest involves getting official Feydark Champs armor. (Coif,
Bracers, Breast plate, leggings, ect.)

Your replies to Samatansyn Flamecatcher's questions in order are...

'what training exercises'

'I am a ranger of Tunare'

'I want to craft (name your item)

You will be given a box and a pattern for the item, once you gather all
your ingredients, combine them in the box you were given, (without the
paper looking thing he gives you, keep that separate.) You will have a
nice little square now.

Take this and the paper looking thing, (That's your mold BTW), to a
forge and place the material, (your square), and the mold, (the paper
looking thing), in there and combine them. You will get a new piece of
armor!

Tonight I got the coif and the bracers and now I'm working on the
sleeves. Here are the ingredients you need for the first two.

Blocks of crude bronze can be found in Steamfront Mountains off Kobold
Scouts or at the Grikbar camp, they drop pretty plentiful so grab as
many as you can carry because I feel you will need them.

The Skeleton Tibia can be acquired off a Decaying Elf Skeleton

The ration is just that, so don't get the Iron ration.

Pretty much most of these items can be bought throughout the city, I'll
be on a bronze hunt in a few days so if you need some and can't get any
let me know, I'll be Durgaa.

COIF
2 blocks of crude bronze
1 Royal Jelly
1 Spiderling Eye
1 Ration (Not Iron Ration)

The last 3 are easy to get, you can actually buy them.

BRACERS (Pattern only makes one! So grab enough for two items!)
1 block of crude bronze
1 skeleton tibia
1 pixie wing
1 Gypsy wine

SLEEVES (Pattern only makes one! So grab enough for two items!)
2 blocks of crude bronze
2 giant wasp venom sacs
1 large widow abdomen
1 white wine
