The first questionw ouldbe, do you even have Planes of Power installed on your computer?  If not, then you need to go out and buy it before you can even activate the zones.
 
If you have it installed, make sure you've entered the CD key to enable the zones on your system.  If you've done that then all you need to do is look for the books.  There is one in West Freeport behind a walk through wall in a hidden tunnel.  There is one in Nexus opposite the entrance to the Bazaar.  There is one in Firiona vie on Kunark inside an underground tunnel that also leads to the Dreadlands.  There is one down the path from the Newbie lift in Greater Faydark.  There is one off the path in Misty thicket.  Just look for them, you'll see them, or have someone lead you to them.  Just make sure you have POP installed and its activated.
