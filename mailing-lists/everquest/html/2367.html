Your own newbie zone is usually the best place to start. You learn
the most with the least risk. Also, leveling is only part of the
game. Explore trade skills, shopping from vendors, and trading with
players. Take enough time to learn all you can from your first newbie
experience. Once you have leveled your first character, you may want
to power level some of your alternate characters. Or, taking the long
road to leveling for alts may appeal to you.

Take your time. Every level has it's own challenges and pleasures.
Have fun on your EverQuest.
