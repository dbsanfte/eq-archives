I don't want to discourage you Denis, but I can't think of a good reason to
ever play a Human. Maybe you should try another class.

My first character was a cleric and I got tired of
sit-med-heal-sit-heal-heal-med routine. So at level 52, I quit my cleric and
tried a warrior. "The repetitive nature of Melee, Kick, Melee, Kick" seemed
appealing to me. I love my gnome warrior, she is level 56 now.

Every class has boring stages, but if you are bored at level 7, I think you
need a change. Try to read up on some low level quests at
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/ <http://everquest.allakhazam.com/>  . You
also might want to go to some different zones. Seeing a new zone for the
first time is very exciting. Start a new character in a zone you have never
been to before, go look around. If this game is new to you and it is boring
already, maybe the game is not for you :-(

Oh well, good luck my friend!
