So this is just a question to find out what kind of hardware you use to run EverQuest.  I suppose a Ps/2 counts as well since you can technically run a version of EQ on it, but I was more curious as to everyone's computers.
 
Me, personally, I have two accounts.  I have two PCs I run EQ on.  One PC is a PIII 1.2Ghz with 1GB of PC133 SDRAM, a 128MB ATI Radeon 4X AGP card, a SB Live! Gamer audio card with digital audio out, a set of Creative  FPS2000 4 point surround sound speakers, and a 19" flat screen Samsung monitor.  Great sound and great graphics, but lags a little on Luclin and the newer zones.
 
the second system is a P4 1.6Ghz with 640MB of PC133 SDRAM, a 64MB ATI Radeon 4X AGP card, and using the onboard AC97 sound for the time being.  It does do digital audio out, but I only have a set of old Labtec speakers.  Also, the monitor is a 17" Gateway E200 monitor. 
 
I use Roadrunner cable to connect so it all works pretty well.  I don't plan on upgrading the PIII any more, but I will make more changes to the P4.  I plan on putting it up to a full gig and a half of RAM and upgrading to a 128MB video card and a SoundBlaster Audigy card.  So when I get the money as they say...  :)  Hopefully next month!
 
Anyway, that's what I use.
