I've only been playing since Sat., but I'm loving it
so far. I've got a lvl 11 sorcerer on her way to
becoming a wizard at some point. A few of my guildies
from EQ1 are there too and we've started up a guild.

It's not EQ1 with better graphics, that's for sure.
It's a totally new game. In some ways it's easier than
EQ1 and in some ways it's more difficult. All in all,
I'm having a blast being a true newbie, running around
and getting lost, and just exploring again.

Death is easier to deal with, leveling seems to come
much easier, quests are all over the place and they're
worth doing, so far the community seems nice, money is
kind of hard to come by, but that's not such a big
deal since the quests tend to reward with decent
armor.

Give it a shot :) I'm on the Najena server.
