EverQuest is a massive online multiplayer adventure with various
towns, cities, lands, dungeons, islands, and even planets. Well, you
can play on the planet Norrath and it's moon, Luclin. There are also
different planes of existence such as the Plane of Knowledge.
It's a roleplaying game somewhat similar to Dungeons and Dragons. You
can play various races and classes and do quests and fight monsters
to gain experience and items.
This description of mine doesn't do it justice. You should check out
http://www.everquestlive.com for more information.
