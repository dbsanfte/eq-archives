Subject: Camping Crystal Caverns


I have been camping Crystal Caverns for a few weeks. I usually pick
off orcs at the bottom, working the tunnel to town. It allows me to
get a few orcs, and find a safe place to med. On the whole, a good
camp, with very good cash returns.

One problem has been claim-jumpers. As a halfling druid, I usually
hide between hunts. Since people can't see me some don't feel
compelled to recognize my camp. This has not been a frequent problem,
but I have noticed that not being visible is a challenge to the
legitimacy of my camp.

For the most part, I find there to be enough orcs to go around most of
the time. Still, to have someone complain that I am claim-jumping is a
bit anoying.

Usually I just go bag some terrors until the interloper leaves. Or
gets killed. Some bite off a bit too much, I am ever so sad to say.
