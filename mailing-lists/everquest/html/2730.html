Go to Frontier Mountains, in Kunark, and get into a Giant Fort group. Great
exp, nice loot. Druids are always in demand -- "sow please."

Gatorscale leggings are nice Druid legs. 7AC, +4 wisdom, +15 hit points. You
get them off the Ancient Crocodile (level 30-ish), way back in the back of
Upper Guk, or you can buy them at auction for maybe 300-400pp, at least on
Tarew Marr. The Ancient Croc has been heavily camped in the past, but I
don't know now.

The Mammoth Hide cloak is a nice druid piece for the back. AC 7, +4 wisdom,
sv cold +5. It drops off the ice goblin alchemist in Permafrost (level upper
20s). It sells for about 400 pp or so on Tarew Marr, I think.

For the arms, adamantite armbands. AC 3, strength +3, wisdom +3. You can get
them for under 200pp if you are lucky. They drop in Howling Stones, I doubt
you want to go there to fight at your level.

Froondor, 41 Wood Elf Druid of Tarew Marr
