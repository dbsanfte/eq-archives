Rico,

I have a 38 dwarf cleric on The Nameless. A few tips:

1) Max wisdom - not all subscribe to this, but you have an inate strength already as a dwarf. I put my other points to bring up either dex or agility, whichever lower
2) Find a regular partner to help you to level at the low levels - you can solo first few levels, but I found grouping with one other PC helped
3) As a cleric, go to Kurn's tower around level ten - lots of undead to nuke on when alone and clerics were always welcome in groups there

Use the other yahoogroups specifically for clerics for more help and advice: EQ_Cleric@yahoogroups.com and EQ-Clerics@yahoogroups.com - Some of the folks there are quite, er, vocal and opinionated, hehe. But we all know that Dwarves make the best clerics - you picked the right race.

Use Caster's Realm and Rayne's cleric site for cleric reference information. Also Allakhazam's for a lot of general information.
