Yep...it sure is easy to build up trust. Just make friends, just like you
would elsewhere. Pkers generally don't try to make friends and hang out w/
you. They just rush up when you're weak or obviously much lower level than
you.

They can't get anything in bags.

They can take things you are wearing, but it's only 1 random item..so if
you don't want to lose something cool, bag it b4 u die.

Guards will protect you if you're right next to them, but don't attack.
Let them hit you, and the guard will step in. However, they won't do
anything about you getting casted on or shot w/ a bow from a distance.
