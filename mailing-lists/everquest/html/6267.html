I have one for my cleric as well and I found that (other than medding

be the first food to get eaten.

As for the old debate of which is better, a Drog or a Horse, all I will
say is just keep this in mind, on a horse you constantly have his/her
head in your view and can make targeting a little difficult if you are
just manually clicking on your target. With a Drog you don't have the
head in your view, unless you are surrounded by a bunch of gnomes!
