It's possible that you do not have good enough PoK faction for these NPCs to
continue and actually offer you a quest. A possible solution is the "sewing
needles for Willamina" quest. Talk to Willamina (loc neg 415, pos 1160), and
she sends you off on a Fed Ex quest involving 9 other NPCs, all in PoK.

You finally get a book from her that, in addition to some minor stats, also
contains something called a "faction modifier", which I am assuming modifies
PoK faction to the point that others will give you quests, i.e. continue
their conversations further.

I have written down the names and locs of all the NPCs, if you want them.

Froondor
