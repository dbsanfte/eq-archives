Without a doubt take out the dblue first. The
reasoning is that with less mobs up you have a better
chance of running should you have to. With multiple
mobs hitting you, there is more of a chance you will
be stunned should your health drop to a level you feel
that evasion is necessary.

The other reason is that the faster you can kill one
of the mobs, which will be the easier, then you can
focus uninterrupted dps on the harder mob.

One last thing to concider is that the LONGER you
fight the more chance of adds from wondering mobs.
Make the fights quick and manageable. If you kill the
easier, you can drag the harder one to where YOU want
him.
