I have done the shovel of ponz quest and I have all of the
ingredients for the other 3 focus items there is one for each pet
type. There is a torch, stein, broom, and shovel you have to get
these from Najena they drop off of mages there. for the shovel you
need a ruby, eyes from a gargoyle(you can buy these), hill giant toes
and the shovel from najena take them to the temple of solusk ro and
turn them into Vira and she will give you the shovel of Ponz. you
dont lose it and you cant trade it it has stats and boosts your earth
pet. you just need it in your hand when you cast your earth pet then
you can take it out and use another weapon, or you can just leave it
in your hand ....I wouldnt consider doin this quest until lvl 20 and
have some friends with you ...Najena can get sticky some times.
