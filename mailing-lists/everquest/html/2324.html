Got some sites you might want to check out
Allakhazam- a very good site for looking up quest information
http://everquest.allakhazam.com

Eqtraders-- tradeskill info, gives recipes and where to find
ingredients, good place to look up Vahshir quests
http://www.eqtraders.com

Everquestraids-- site to get raids together. Mostly used by the big
guilds to schedule epics but it's also used for lower level raids.
You can look up guilds for your server from this site, that's how I
found my guild.
http://www.everquestraids.com

EQ links-- a general info site. You can look up links to other sites
on almost any EQ related topic.
http://www.eqlinks.com

There are many more sites out there but these are the ones I use most
often.
