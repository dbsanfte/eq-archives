"Gargoyle Island in Ocean of Tears. Get a level or two, plus 60-80
gargoyle eyes here in a few days to a week of hunting and earn
yourself a cool 500-700pp or more. The eyes sell for 9pp each. The
thing to note with Garg Isle is that there are gargoyles and
skeletons. Kill a garg, a skel will spawn, and visa versa. Keep
killing the skeletons or no gargs will spawn! Also, some monks find
it fun to get a train of skels then run up to a garg, feign and let
the skels fight the garg down until the skels are dead, then pop up
and finish the garg. It works well, but I found it more efficient to
simply kill the gargs. This is also the place to learn to run around
in zoomed out mode. Hit F9 four times, zoom it all the way out and
then run around the isle. Takes some getting used to, but it's the
only way to go to spot the gargs when they spawn. This is a lonely
few levels, but the money is worth it."
