You don’t know about the mage? Well……
Mage AKA the Magician is the greatest pet class in the game; our pets are
second to none. We are also great summoners, able to summon food, water,
equipment and lots of other useful stuff; we are also the 3rd best nukers,
under wizards and druids. The big downfall is that we are totally dependent
on our pet, if they die there is a good chance that we will die too, unless
we can get a gate off, but usually that doesn’t happen, our pets are very
powerful. That’s about it, overall a very good class, good for soloing and
in groups. I’m very happy being a mage Any other question regarding the
Mage feel free to send em my way
