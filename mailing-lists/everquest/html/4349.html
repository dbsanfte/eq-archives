Subject: New frogloks race


I started a new froglok character tonight on the Tunare server.  I created a Froglok Shaman named Threetoed.
 
When I hit level 20 the name will be Threetoed Treetoad.  :)
 
The race is pretty cool.  shamans start off with 85 wis and I allocated 25 points, the most I could, to wisdom giving them a base wisdom of 110 which is pretty good.  They also have good stamina, but very low charisma.  A 55 charisma. Yeouch!
 
Anyway, no more recruitment letter, you can dispose of it after you Hail the trainer.  He gives you a starting quest which leads you on a consecutive series of quests which all help you to learn your way around the zone.  Eventually you go out into Innothule Swamp where you have to collect certain pieces.  At some point, as a shaman, you have to collect a troll raider's head and turn it into one of the Garrison officers.  the troll raiders are around level 8-10, and right now I'm only level 3 so I've got a ways to go before I can finish this part of the quest.  I hear it leads you on to another quest about a letter, and finishing that you have to retrieve a traitor's tongue.  After the traitor's tongue you've finished all the newbie quests. 
 
Also, I started the shaman newbie armor quest called the Yun Initiates Armor.  The pieces available are:
 
Helm, Bracers, Boots, Armguards, Legplates, Gloves, and Breastplate
 
Unfortunately, I don't know the stats on these pieces yet.  It's all new to me but so far I've been having fun playing a froggie.
 
I'll let you know what else I find out.
