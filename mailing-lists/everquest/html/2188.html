I soloed some in Lake of Ill omen with my cleric at your level. But I agree
with previous poster. A group will be a lot more efective, or a twinked monk
partner (and hell there are a million twinked out monks).

If you wanna try out solo, you should get sow and c/c2 and load your undead
nukes. Just aim for undeads , the other nukes arent mana effective. Since
the cleric nuke lines are marred with very high casting time you most of the
time need to use root and shoot to kill.

It will work, but not the best efficiency. I used to solo while shouting for
group, that way if i didnt get one i still came home with some exp.

Good luck.
