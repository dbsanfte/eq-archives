i thought about getting a horse for myself (even with having jboots).

the 1st time i checked prices, i thought there must be a mistake:
129Kpp

well, i don't have much CHA. some time ago people started to sell
"Opal Encrusted Steins": slot PRI/SEC, CHA+25, MANA+10. you
can buy them starting at 25pp up to 45pp each in bazaar (TM prices).

with this my CHA get over 100, and my standing with SH isn't too bad.
now the 129Kpp horse sells for a bit over 100Kpp.

i equip all my chars with those "Steins" and do some of the SH
faction quests. faction and CHA does have a big impact on prices.

and this is not only true for buying stuff from merchants, but also
selling gets better results.
