I concur.  I decided just to see what they were like to get one of the guides marked "Make Plat the Quick and Easy Way" or something like that.  It turned out it was nothing more than a recipe book for batwing crunchies, fish cakes, and fish rolls.  It gave specific detailed instructions on where to buy the ingredients, how to combine them, and so on.  The idea was to get your baking skill up to about 135 so you could make most anything and then sell it back to vendors or to other players to make the plat.  However, I found  if you sell it back to vendors you don't get near as much money for the product as you paid for all the ingredients, especially with what you lost on failed attempts so it didn't turn out to be profitable in the end.  However, I found this could be remeied by charisma enhancing items or buffs.  A pair of Opal Encrusted Steins which have +25 charisma each can sometimes get you a plat or two more.  Otherwise, you can sell tacks of what you make in the bazaar and reap some reward for them. 
 
At any rate, my baking skill is now up to 100.  So I guess it was somewhat worth it.
