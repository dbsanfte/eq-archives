Not pathetic. Just new. Tax season? My brother in law is a cpa, and
at the end of tax season he looks like he has been beaten up.

Try looking at alakhazam or caster's realm for something on quests.
As to names, try out ones that are easy to type. makes it easier on
other players. My first druid had a long name. This one just started
as Barto. My warrior, Bartoo. My magician i called martondo, a bit
longer but easy to type, and the best I could think of at the time
that the game would accept.

Get some maps from the eq atlas, a link from alakhazam. I have quite
a few printed and in a binder for easy reference. better than getting
lost.

when playing solo you have to find your own play style. I tend to be
cautious, exploring with care and engaging mostly things that are
blue. My son's play a bit more boldly, less concerned with getting
killed. Though the one that is a mid-40's shadow knight has become a
bit more cautious after learning how expensive higher level deaths
can be.

Hey, and look into trade skills. a nice part of the game for those
who find it interesting. Some very high level quests require some
good trade skills, so they can be worthwhile, especially if you enjoy
pursuing the advancements. Not the same thrill as hack and loot, but
some of us find this aspect fun.

good hunting to you.
Barto Proudfoot
