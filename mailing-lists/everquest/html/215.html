Observer's Staff

This staff can be obtained as part of a level 30+ quest. The staff can be
used by priest and mage types(wizard, magician, enchanter, necromancer,
cleric, druid and shaman). This two handed staff carries a damage of 12 and
a delay of 39. It also grants +5 intelligence, +5 wisdom, and +30 mana. As
if that was not enough it has charges of the enchanter spell Sentinel which
will warn you of others drawing near you when you are meditating.

Runed Totem

The runed totem staff can be looted off Shaman Ren Rexx at Paw. It is a
favorite among casters. It is a 2 handed staff which has an attack value of
9, a delay of 37 and also grants +5 mana and hit points.
