This is really a matter of opinion really.  Monks aren't any more capable of pulling singles than any other class.  In fact monks take just as much aggro when pulling as anyb ody else.  It's only monks who are capable of successful feign death that can pull singles.  If feign death fails, then the monk better be running the other mobs away from the group or he can get the group killed just as well as anybody else.  Monks who are good pullers know how to FD pull as well as what to do if something goes wrong.  Monks as a class make good pullers, probably some of the best in the game, but it really comes down to individual players.   If the player is not a good puller, then doesnt make a difference what class he's playing.
