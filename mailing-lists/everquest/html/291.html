GENERAL TIP for TIP LIST

One of the best hunting grounds for 2nd through 5th lvl characters is the
Barbarian region of Everfrost Peaks near town of Halas. This area has hordes
of mobs that give good experience points and probably the best loot found on
low lvl mobs including cracked staffs, backpacks, pelts, bags and weapons as
well as silver. When starting a new character, I highly recommend making the
trip to Everfrost Peaks for early leveling.
