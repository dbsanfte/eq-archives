Generally speaking, you don't get as much money when you solo.  You
get a lot more money when you group.  Sure, you probably don't get
everything for yourself but because the groups can pull fast and
kill 'em, you generally get more money that way even if you are doing
alpha looting.  If you can get into a group, you probably can make
money that way.  Try the aviaks group in SK.  You can probably do it
at 19.  And yeah, Lena's correct, if you can get a few more levels
you can group in paw or whatever cuz they drop a lot of FS weapons.
