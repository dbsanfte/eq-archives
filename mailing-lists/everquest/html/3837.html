I personally started a ranger before the "Trueshot Chainmail" quests
came out, but I started getting that armor around 8th level and now
have a COMPLETE set. It took a while and some work to get all the
armor, but it was well worth it. At 15th now and have over 100 str ,
100 sta, and 100wis without having had to dumped all the training
points into any of them..I increased cha, dex, and int and
wis..because after 9th, a ranger is also a druid and need wis for
mana.

Put a few into each, but remember that the Trueshot Chainmail
increases STR and STA for EVERY piece. I have included the stats
below. Take it from someone who had the ringmail that was weighting
him down and now has the relatively feather light trueshot chainmail
and loving it.

Almost every piece increases the STR enough to cover the items weight
and in some cases more.

And if you do these quests at the appropriate time, everything should
provide you with experience. Crushbone will be your chosen hunting
ground because 80% of the stuff you need drops there.

If you are or soon plan to be, a big orange hostess treat, then
ignore my comments, because NOOB armor is beneath most twinkies even
at low levels. ;)

Boots: STR+2 STA+3 AC5
Bracers: STR+1 STA+4 AC4 -need 2
Coif: STR+1 STA+3 AC5
Gauntlets:STR+1 STA+4 AC5
Leggings: STR+2 STA+3 AC6
Sleeves: STR+1 STA+2 AC5
Tunic: STR+2 STA+6 AC12

Enough rambling from this lurker :) Have fun all
