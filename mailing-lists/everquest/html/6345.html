You're probably looking at it from the point of tradeskills, like baking or tailoring or whatever.  A list of components you can combine to get an end result.  When actually all you need you've probably passed up without realizing it.
 
Caters Realm has a list of spells for each class.  One of the options it gives in the spell list past level 50 is Researchable or not.  If so, it gives the items needed to research it.
 
I believe Allah's does the same thing.  All spells that are researchable require a parchment and a quill.  You combine them with some other items in a researchers kit to get the spell.the kit, parchments, and quills should be available at just about any mage guild.
