I am assuming by this narrative that you are a melee fighter and not a caster, and probably under level 10?
 
If you are a melee fighter then I'd say go for the blue one.  If you can kill him you'll at least get some xp before you die.  If you are under 10 you won't lose xp so the xp you just got you still keep. 
 
If you are a caster try slowing /snaring/mezzing/rooting one or the other then pull the still mobile one away and kill him.
 
In either case, its always good to have sow just inc ase you need to run if thigns go frm bad to worse. 
 
Now though if you get a Temp from a high level cleric, you can probably take on both orcs without losing too much health.
