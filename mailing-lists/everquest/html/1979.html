Well said Lance! I am in total awe of SoL. It is like a whole new game. I
want to go and visit EVERY Old World zone again. I was running through
Dagnor's Cauldron and I could not believe they made a very ugly plain zone
look so beautiful. I mean I thought this zone was just grayish rocks with
water in the middle. Well, not now. It looks so very good. I now want to go
to every zone just to view the scenery.

Btw, I got a new computer just for the SoL. I had an old W95, 300 MHz, 256
RAM that ran the Old World ok, but now with my new PC, wow, what a change. I
have P4 1.7 MHz, 80 gig HD, 512 RAM, geforce 3 400 and run this on a DSL
that downloads around 1,200 kbs and uploads ~ 800 kbs. Last night in the SoL
I was zoning in 20-30 seconds ( glad they fixed this yesterday ) and very
little to no lag. The ping bar looked good all night, with left side max
around 200 and staying in double digits and on the EQ side around 2,000 max.

I couldn't be happier with SoL. I can understand Verant wanting to get the
product out on time, but I can also see the customer side. But I would have
been happy enough just with the new zone graphics and the new AA experience.
This new experience really offers the +51 player a lot of cool skill to
shoot for.
