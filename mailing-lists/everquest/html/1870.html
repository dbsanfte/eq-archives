Sorry for the short post before.

Find out what server your friends use and be sure
to start there. For your first character please do
something simple. Once you know the game and your
way around the game then experiement a bite.

Warriors, cleric and druids are good for you to
learn the system with. Any of the magic users can
be great if you have friends to be online with.
It can be alot harder if you are alone or soloing.
The 'best race' depends on what you are playing.
Humans make good warriors and not bad at others.
several races get higher intellegence which is
great for magic users. Actually it helps any class
for skill advancement.

Make friends annd conteacts .. everywhere you go.
The idea is to always having friends to run with
and share the adventure with. Meeting people starts
with talking and helping others. Be sure to ask if
someone needs help before you jump in. Much as you
want to help someone who looks like they need it be
sure not to take someone elses kill. You will see
people standing around someone who looks in real
trouble .. doing nothing. Thats because they are
waiting for the person to accept their help. Its
also a good idea to be sure you can help before
you jump in. jumping into a fight WAY over your
level only gets one more person killed ..YOU.
