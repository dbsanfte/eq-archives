Woohoo!! Congrats, rogues are a fun class to play.


> 1) How does "sneak" work exactly? Can I sneak across someone's line of
> sight? What is the difference between "Sneak" and "Hide" that comes
later?

Sneak, when successful, makes you walk "quietly" (slowly) by/near mobs
without
being seen. However, usually if you are in the mobs line of sight it
doesn't
work that often. It's great for sneaking behind mobs to get past them.

Hide, by itself, makes you invisible but you can't move. If you Hide AND
Sneak
(forget which order you need to do it in; i have it hot keyed :) ), you can

bascially walk around invis. :) Be sure to keep both skills MAXED. It's a
real life and corpse saver.

I have fun hide/sneaking up to mobs and attempting to pickpocket them. :)


> 2) I do not yet have the "Backstab" ability. Does it do me any good to
> attack monsters from behind without the ability yet? I have been making
an
> effort to do just that, but should I not even bother?

Contrary to belief, hitting a mob from behind does give extra bonus. About
2 years ago Verant posted there is an extra (if slight) benefit from
attacking
behind; lower AC? While fighting mutiple mobs, people have noticed that
they
get hit harder from behind than in front. Stick a green mob behind you,
and
note
the average damage. Then turn around; the average damage is less. This is
why most
ppl try to get the mobs all in front of them instead of having some behind
them.
Though the mob templates differ from characters, the principle is the same.

Now when you get the backstab ability, you'll really wreak havoc! :)
