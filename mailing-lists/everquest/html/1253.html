Su,

In most groups that I hunt with now, we use the king looter system, where one
person loots everything and then it is divided out when someone leaves the
group. If a major item drops, we normally have it go to whomever will use it
first. If no one will use it as a primary item, then it is rolled on by the
group. We allow people to trade up, when better items drop. Some days we might
only see one or two major items drop. Then other days, we have seen multiples
of items drop and everyone in the party get something good. It is always that
luck element that decides the quality and quantity. Nowadays, I am more
experienced oriented and fun oriented than loot oriented. Most of the places I
camp do not drop items that are useable by my class or drop items that are
better than the items I might already have. I do not like loot hogs or ninja
looters, but I am not really concern about loot as long as the system is fair
and everyone understands and agrees to it ahead of the time. :)

It has been ages since I been in a group that use alpha loot or loot to X plat
or whatever. Normally, I really like the king looter system, since it allows me
to concentrate on supporting the party and getting mana back up. ;)

very good with abbreviations.
