All you need is a router and enough Ethernet cables to connect each computer
to the router, which in turn connects to your DSL modem. You can find a
four-port router from Linksys or D-Link in most shops that sell computer
hardware, and they're not too pricey. Configuration shouldn't be too
difficult, as a lot of consumer routers come with an installation CD that
will help users to configure their computers and the router to share an
Internet connection.
