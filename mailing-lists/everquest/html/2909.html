One of the best boosts to my tailoring skills came in East Karana. I
think they were craig spiders. Whatever their names, they were large
and red and black. Dropped lots of silk. Made lots of silk stuff. I
was gaining experience at the time as well, so found it all the more
satisfying. Plus, the weight of spider silk and the silk stuff I made
was not all that much, so I did a lot of tailoring in the field. Not
so easy to do that with heavy hides.
