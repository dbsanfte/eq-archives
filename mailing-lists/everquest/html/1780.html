At level 5 its 30mins. Whether your online or not. What I might suggest
for the future is to know your surrounds: roads, landmarks, certain shops,
etc.. Stick to the roads, it might not be the most direct route, however
they will lead you to your destination. As far as finding your corpse,
invest in a Chipbone Rod or two and place them in the bank (remember that
when you died your nekid - store them in the bank or they will be useless
:). You can find them normally at a merchant with shaman supplies and
potions. They are a few platinum, however are worth it if you don't know
where your corpse is. Simply target yourself ( or whomever your trying to
locate a corpse for ) in the zone where your target died, and right click.
This will cause you to point in the direction your corpse is located. It
has a few charges and works quite nicely.

Else do a /who bard, or /who necro. Both of those classes get a corpse
locating song/spell before they reach lvl 6. In most cases, if not all,
they will be happy to help you find your corpse for little compensation. I
still keep a rod on me, I never know when a friend of mine will be stuck
somewhere, not knowing where they died. And its nice to be able to find it
for them.
