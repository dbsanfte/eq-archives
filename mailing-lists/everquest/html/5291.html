One thing that I noticed: if you have the very same type of arrows in
your ammo slot and in your quiver, the arrows in the ammo slot will be
used until it only contains one arrow, then the arrows will be pulled
from the quiver. If you have a different type of arrow in your quiver
than is in your ammo slot, the last arrow will be pulled from the ammo
slot (leaving it empty) and no arrows will be pulled from the quiver.

Hope this helps.
