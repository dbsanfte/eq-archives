Oh my, 5-6 nukes? I forget what level you are but 5-6
nukes will most definitely kill mana as well as draw
aggro where then you won't be able to get the mob off
you.

I have a mage and a druid and after months of not
playing my mage, I started him up again only to die
several times. I was getting to caught up in the a
similar pattern where I was nuking too much and once
you get a mob that hits for 75 per shot on ya, we
don't last tooo long.

We have the second best pets in the game and should
let the pets do most of the work. I remember being
under 25 and being able to handle mostly yellow mobs
and I am not a twinked char. Once you get Renew
summoning , learn to use it wisely. YOu can heal your
pet for 200 hp and sit while watching its health.

I strongly suggest if you are having trouble to get a
partner or group for a while. A druid or cleric is a
great team where you can both buff your pet and they
can heal it as well as you. Both with Kei would be
dangerous.


Let us know if you need more , dont' be afraid to try
strategies, find one that fits your playing style.

Feel free to email me off list and I will be more than
happy to help you.

Rob
