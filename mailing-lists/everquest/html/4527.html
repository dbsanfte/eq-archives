You can get by with the processor. Upgrade to al least
512 megs of ram, and also upgrade the videocard. You
should be able to get a Geforce 2 gts or a radeon 8500
64 megs of video(minimun) for cheap.

In the meantime go to the options and turn the luclin
models off. Don't turn on texture caching because
sometimes causes graphics anomalies. Turn particle
effects (from the in-game menu) to low or off.

Hope this helps.
