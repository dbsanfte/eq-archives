Since I solo a lot, I had to find ways to get exp and money without
grouping. My choice of a druid helped in this. Knowing I would be
into the game often but for relatively short periods of time was my
primary reason for focusing on the solo arts.

I found ghouls to be profitable as they often dropped bronze items.
Now I often hunt aviaks in sk, as they have good cash drops and also
drop bronze. There are lots of wanderers in sk, so easy to solo
without the inconvenience other birds jumping in as at kfc.

I also bag wandering gnolls in sk. I do occasionally join a spire
group to get lots of gnoll loot, but have found aviaks give me better
cash flow.

When I get bored with the routine of birding, I go look for a spawn
somewhere on my item wish list. Don't really camp them, just pop in
to see if they are up.
