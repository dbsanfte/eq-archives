at your level you should go for a robe with AC, and MANA bonus. also
a HP modifier wouldn't hurt. when reaching 30 to 35 i would go for
a robe with INT, AC. at my current level (and INT over 200) i look
for a robe with INT and AC but it also should have resistance and MANA
stats. actually the more stats it has, the better ... ;-)

if you have access to allakhazam and can search for equipment, be sure
to turn off NO DROP, and GM ONLY items for your level ... it is almost
impossible that you will get them. (and it always hurts knowing what
items exist in game ;-)
