If you are looking for MT (as a raid function) theres only one choice -
Warrior. If you mean MT during exp groups both Pally and SK works fine.
During some special mobs during raids SK or Pally works as MT on the ones
that dont have a ridiculous damage output, but on big fish you choose a
warrior, period.

During high end raiding id say Pallys are a bit more useful than SK but SK
are also quite good, especially the mana/hp spell that gives you 100
hp/mana, this is loved by the clerics. Both classes are nice choices as add
tanks cause of the ability to grab aggro fast unlike the warrior who is
dependant on melee damage for aggro both SK/Pally can easily hold aggro
without wepaons just using spells.

Conclusion:
If you want to be the MT for a guild on most raids - go for warrior. If you
want to tank some and be a lot more versatile but at the cost of tanking
ability , go for any of the two other classes.
