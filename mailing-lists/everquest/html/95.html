Yes.. There are two

Wolf-hide Armor and Bear-hide

You have to talk to the Millers (Brother and Sister)who are elves sitting
outside of surglade falls (sometimes they wander around, but they are always
in the area).. Boabob the brother will sell you wolf-hide armor. He will
make you a Belt, Boots, and a cloak( the cloak requires you to have the
correct faction - or be an elf). The sister named something like Cellesta
will not talk to you at first. You must tell her that her brother boabob
sent you, and she will make you Bear hide armor. They will explain what is
required for their services..

I think that this is what you need.. but im not sure :

Wolf-Hide

Belt AC 3 - Ruined wolf skin and 5gp
Boots AC 4 - Medium wolf skin and 10gp
Cloak AC ? - High wolf skin and 21gp

Bear-Hide

Belt AC 4 - any quality bear skin except ruined 10gp
Boots AC 5 - medium bear skin 15gp
Cloak AC ? - high bear skin 21gp

i havent had the correct faction yet to buy the cloaks thats why i dont know
the AC.

And yes they can be worn by all classes and races.. great for us magic
users.
