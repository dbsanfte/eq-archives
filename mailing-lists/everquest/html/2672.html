let me thing back what i did at your level. i think i was in that range
just before velious was availible, because of this there might be also
some more possibilities, because of luclin.

i believe i spent my time in frontier mountains at the giant fort until
34 (with a group, but it is easy to get into one at the fort). then i
did cougars in iceclad, and wolves in great divide (both of them solo).

at 35 to 37 i also joined some groups in crystal caverns and tower of
frozen shadows. but at those levels i started to solo walrus and
ulthorks (in the north part of eastern wastes, they are the 'weaker'
ones; the southern ones are for lvl 42 to 45 groups).

at 39 i soloed my first frost giant scout in great divide. but i was
lucky, because it was sick or just died of old age ... after three
deaths, i decided not to touch them solo again for some time. actually
it was 44 when i started my personal war against all sorts of frost
giants ... ;-)

i filled the levels from 42 to 44 with hunting young shardwurms in great
divide ... something that gets really boring at 43 or 44, especially if
you hunt them solo and don't have candy availible.

two things you should do:
1) get your research spells from katta ! the merchants there have all
the research spells from level 34 to 44 for mages, chanters and
wizzies (research only spells, not quest spells). i don't know if
necros have something similiar.
2) at 44 you should try to get jboots. you really need them and can do
this at that level without any help !

as said at the beginning, there should be more options with luclin, but
i don't have too much knowledge about luclin (i only know the way from
nexus/shadow haven to katta ... i think i already forgot it, because i
am bound there, and i have my personal lvl 40 wizzy for transports :)

i almost forgot: you should check out "The Magician's Tower". this is a
forum for mages, with many hints, where to hunt at which level. point
your browser to: http://pub6.ezboard.com/bthemagicianstower

on my personal web space, there is also the copy of the "Everquest
Magician Resource" (this is at http://muog.de/emr). this is sort of
an official copy, but because i told the original author that i won't
add new things to it, he didn't distribute my link to other EQ sites.
so even if those pages may be outdated now, this copy will remain there
and still maybe useful to fellow mages.

well, good luck,
