I've played every wis caster and a rogue and a warrior.  I haven't played any int casters.  I tried a bard.  After all is said and done.  I went right back to the wis caster.  The druid.  I think people will choose a natural affinity for one type of character over another.  I guess it depends on personality sometimes, but I find the druid is the easiest for me to play.  I don't know why.  It just is.  I even played a rogue for nearly 40 levels before I realized I got more enjoyment playing the druid.  bards are probably one of the more difficult characters to play becuase of their song weaving abilities.  They have elements of both casters and non caster classes.  Because of this they are one of the most well rounded classes for all around purposes.  So if you think your personality is such that you can handle the difficulty of playing a bard, then I'd say go for it.
