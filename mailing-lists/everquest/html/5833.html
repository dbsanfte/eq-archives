It's not your fault, the NPCs are pretty stupid.
 
I check Allakazam.  It will usually tell you what to say for each quest.
 
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/
 
Also keep in mind who you are affects what they tell you.  An NPC may tell everyone the same story, but not everyone will get details if asked.  I have had NPCs tell me about a quest so I go do it and when I get back with the stuff they ask for they tell me to come back when I have earned their trust (raised faction).  I have also had NPCs ignore me totally but if I give them items I know they want (from reading Allakazam) they thank and reward me.
