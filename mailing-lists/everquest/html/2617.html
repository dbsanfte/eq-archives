Subject: Building a wish list


Since I have yet to subscribe at Allahkazam, I have used an
alternative research technique to work up my wish list. I am still in
the middle of that research.

I go to the caster's site and use the druid equipment list. I grab
any info on items which look good to me. Mostly stuff with wisdom and
ac, and the more positive buffs the better. Once I have collected all
of the items for a particular slot into one file, I use Allakhazam to
fill in details. This goes farely quickly if I use two windows and
move back and forth. Once all of the data is collected I organize the
info in the file by level to attain. Tailored and quest items are at
the top of the list, drop items following.

Lots of work, but I have been doing a little every day. I have
learned a lot.

Allakhazam has a new wish list system for subscribers. If you want
tailored info but don't want to do the work listed above (or any
other method with similar results), the subscription is a good value.
