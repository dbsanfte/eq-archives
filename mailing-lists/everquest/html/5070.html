Welcome back David!
Legends of Ykesha comes with new zones, new items, new tradeskill recipes,
and more bank space! not only do you get double your bank space, but they
have now made it so you have 2 additional bank slots that are shared by all
of your characters. There is even a shared coin bank part. Ykesha also added
a new inventory slot for charms. All of the charms I have seen are no drop
and are either quested for or dropped from npcs in the Ykesha zones. Oh,
Ykesha also added a new playable race-- Frogloks. With a special dye only
from Ykesha you can dye your armor whatever color you want. The only bad
thing is that one of the evil races got kicked out of their home and now
share neriak with the darkelves.
Lost Dungeons of Norrath added new zones and instanced dungeons that only
you and your party can enter. No more fighting over spawns. You can choose
your adventure type and have a time limit for completion. Inside the
dungeons there are chests and barrels and such that can be unlocked and
opened or bashed into opening up. You need to be careful of these as they
are often trapped. Dungeons also added item augmentations. Via quests or
killing mobs you can get magical stones that when combined in a little pool
thingy on a pedastel (found in the Wayfarer's camps) you can augment an
item. The first one you get is the Adventurers stone, which increases the
enchantments the more you interact and advance in your status with the
wayfarers.
It's really quite neat. :-)
