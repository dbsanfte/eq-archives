Still breaks? Not that I've seen on mine. I can get any mob to take over
the zone anytime i want, just takes time to get them to attack. There will
be times that the wrong mob attacks, you just have to kill the attacking
force and go back to hitting the named to get the right ones to attack.
Btw, one secret would be just hit the grimlings if you want either bears or
wolves. Most everyone knows how to get the bears to take over, its just
most get lost trying to get the wolves to win. The trick is once the bears
take over, just keep hitting the screechmaw at the chiefs hut and the wolves
will attack the north, then keep hitting him till the wolves attack the
south..then you have wolf only zone. One down side is the bears attacking
the wolves once they lose their home, has happened before. Just have to try
to make it close to the wolves cave and kill the 5 attacking bears...and
make sure you count. Miss one and the bears will win if the wolves already
have the north cave. As for the grimlings...you have to hit the wolves then
the bears in that order.
