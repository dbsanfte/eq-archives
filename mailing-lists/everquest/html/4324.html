You wouldn't believe the number of people who have asked for help finding
the Registrar in Shar Vahl. It's hard to explain where she is, but I will
try. Lets start with the bridge that leads to the thicket zone, not the
hollow... there are two bridges. Standing on the bridge to the thicket, run
into the city. Pass the black female vender - do NOT turn left or right, go
straight past her. Then turn right. There is a little passage that looks
like a tunnel. Go through that. You will then turn up in a little market
square. The Registrar is in one of the rooms in the square. I know it's
confusing, but that is the best way I can explain it. Let me know if you
need any more help.
