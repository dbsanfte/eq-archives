The good thing about rogues is that they are non-casters so your skills will go up whether you're in a group or not.  doesn't matter if you solo or if you want to group.   The rogue epic weapon is a rapier  - a piercing weapon, so you'll want piercing to be your main skill.  Particular in higher levels when you get backstab.  One of the rogue AA skills is called Assassinate, its a backstab that can do up to 30,000 damage on your opponent.  So keep that in mind.  With applying poison to your weapons you get bonus damage points as well.
