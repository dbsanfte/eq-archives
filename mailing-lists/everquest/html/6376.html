This is actualyl the BOT key quest which takes place in Plane of Storms. 
 
As you've noticed the quest is in two parts.  If you've already turned in the beard, bone, and sash, you have to follow the prompts and Askr gives you another bag.  Then you need to put the two medallions (fromd ifferent camps) in the bag and hit combine.  then hand the bag back to Askr.  That will get you the character flag and gain you access to Bastion of Thunder.  If you just tried handing the medallions back to him without the bag it wont work.  My guess is that you're going to need to camp the named giants again and get two more medallions, get another bag from Askr and try to finish the 2nd part again.
