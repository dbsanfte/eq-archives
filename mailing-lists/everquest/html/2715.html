There are several good areas to try. I've been to Dalnir in the
Warsliks Woods, the Mines of Nurga in the Frontier Mountains and Sol
A in Lavastorm. All of these are boosted experience areas, usually
+13%. So you get an extra boost. Groups are hit or miss as these
places are under-utilized since Velious. I went from 30 to 34 in the
Overthere in 3 days, and there's plenty of groups.

And if you're a permium member on Allakazam's site you can tailor a
seach for just +wis items for Druids. I find it's a great help. But
a Druid friend of mine says he really see's no point in upgrading
armor, since after 30 you're mainly a caster and kiter!
