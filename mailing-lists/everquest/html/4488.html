you could try grouping in Rathe Mountains in a Hill Giant group. Xp there
is good up till at least 51. At about I think say 48 or 49 you could
probably solo the HG pretty well. My 53 Shammy was there not long ago and
the HG were more green than Light Blue by then but the Cash is great and you

resell back to him will get rid of the smaller coins tremendously and yield
you a return of around 39 to 40pp per stack.. I've done this many a time
when ever I needed to get cash up for my tradeskills supplies.
