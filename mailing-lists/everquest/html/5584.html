I had the same problem in one of the expansions

This is what I did to fix it:

1. Start the EQ patcher
2. Once its done with its patching, click on advanced
3. Click on complete file check.

The game will then search the files, most likely find a couple that
were wrong, and the game should work fine from there.

Past that you may want to try reinstalling the game, or contact
customer service.
