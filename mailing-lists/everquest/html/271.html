To get music with the SB Live! you have to ensure you can download the
sound fonts. To do this go to Audio HQ and run the SoundFont program.
From there go to options and ensure that your static caching is around
10Mb or so. It won't use it all up, it just needs to know it can load
in a 3Mb sound font.

My SB Live! works great and I just love how the music sounds. Very good
soundtrack indeed. The 3D sound support really helps to track down
those pesky monsters as well.
