We are saying the same thing, and yes a monk's job is
to not get aggros off tanks and to not get ripostes.
I think its six the one a half dozen another whether
to just FD during the fight or to pick appropriate
weapons so that the poor tank can hold his aggros.
Doing it the way I do I never get hit (except once on
the pull and position about half the time of course),
which is my goal :)

I think its a matter of taste whether a bard is the
puller of choice in PoP (and I don't see the point of
discussing a matter of taste like that).

Bards are cool, don't get me wrong, but monks are
still the best at steady safe pulling I think. Yes
its true that other classes can pull too. I've seen
many a would be puller mess it up though... that's
just a matter of practice since I've been doing it for
over 4 years now. I never get my groups killed.

But I do wish there were a role for monks like there
used to be... damn nerf.
