You will get better as it takes longer to level and you will have more time to get your skills up. Weapon skill has a major effect on your attack abilities. The maximum skill you can have in a weapon type I think is Level * 5 + 5, so the highest skill you could have in 1hs at level 11 is 60 which is much higher than the 25 you currently have. Generally you should try to get your skills up in all available weapon types as you might get your hands on something really nice and you will want to have at least some ability with it. So yeah you should alternate between the weapon types and try to be good at each one.
