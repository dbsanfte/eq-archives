Subject: Re: Piercing vs. Blunt


Doesn't matter. Since your Int is so high, you will have no trouble maxing
out what the few melee skills you are allowed to use... and if you
specialize in one and ignore the others you will be at a MAJOR disadvantage
since some mobs are more susceptible to different attacks, i.e. a Giant
Turtle might take minimal damage from a 2H blunt but a Piercing weapon will
poke it's little eyes out.

Get one of each weapon for each skill you have that you like and alternate
them to see what works best. Don't forget about Hand To Hand, if you can use
that, either! When you die you might have to bully your way past something
on the way to your corpse, and freshly dead, naked, and in the middle of
West Karana is NOT the place you want to work your skill up to 1 from zero.
