Personally I would head over to North RO if you are
around level 10. There are two good Derv camps there
which give good exp. You can then head to Oasis 12+
but some roaming mobs are a bit nasty, like IceBone or
Drybone Skelies, for got the exact name now, and not
to mention the Sand GIant here or there but the Orc
Highway is a great place to learn how to group
effectively.

I would not recommend Paludal Caverns before 16. Most
won't want you and soloing will be rough. However, at
16, this is the best place by far for exp up until 25.

After 25 you could get a partner or small group and do
Unrest or Aviaks in South Karana. From 25-35 the High
Keep is a good place but gets rather full fast.
Around 33+ you can go to the Overthere and get into a
group at either the Sky zone or the Ledge which is at
the FM zone. (Please see Eqatlas.com for
abbreviations)

You can fight in the Overthere till 38-40 and after
that you can go to Dreadlands.

good luck
