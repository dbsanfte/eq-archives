If you head north where the river comes to the mountain, take a look thru
the ice and you will see the cave. Sometimes you will see a goblin swimming
at the mouth of the cave. The cave itself is inside the mountain, so as you
swim it will eventauly open up above the surface of the water.

I believe that the NPC Necro's con was aggressive and he was red at lvl 16.
Be careful when approaching him. BTW there is very little light, and it is
a maze ( oh what fun... hehe)

I never zoned so i dont believe its a new zone, just undiscovered.. the
cave itself is quite large (or it seemed that way) but was relativly empty.
This is what i saw:

1.. House with 4 icebone skeletons
2.. Shrine ( zone point )structure protected by 2 icebones skeletons
3.. Human NPC Necromancer with pet - red at lvl 16
4.. Possible Giant.. (type unknown) - I never saw one myself

And, there could be more to the cave that went unexplored, because i had no
way of passing the Necro at the time. I was informed of the cave by a fellow
player. She stated that she had seen a giant, but alass i have not.
