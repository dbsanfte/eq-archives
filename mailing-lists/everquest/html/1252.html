I hear where you're coming from. As a druid, I sometimes have to heal, sometimes nuke, sometimes damage shield and DOT, but rarely, if ever, melee. So I run into the same problem of not being near the corpse when it dies and the group you're with is FFA. I really don't mind free for all loot, provided that everyone understands no one needs to get greedy. By the sound of it, you were hunting in Highkeep, a personal favorite of mine. Most groups that are not ffa usually go alphabetically and you keep looting until you get a total of 2pp, a bronze or FS weapon or armor, or some combination of ore (down in the warrior and raider rooms).

I don't think any looting order is any better than others. I think it completely depends on the people you're grouped with. Some people can do the ffa loot and no one ever loots too much and it works smoothly. And in the alpha loot, as long as everyone's honest, that works well too.

Cooperation is the key.
