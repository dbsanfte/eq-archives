I went to the new Jaggedpine zone tonight, as soon I got through with
all the patches. It is beautifully done, and well populated with
mobs in my level range. I'm not sure about merchants, but there is a
banker. Some people were having trouble binding there, so you may
want to go to Qeynos first.

For all you cityfolk you get there through Surefall Glade. It is in
the caves behind the falls. And there also appear to be some new
quest people around. And make sure you wipe you feet! You're in my
house now.

The new UI looks good, and the spell icons are great. Now I don't
have to figure out which buff is going, or which one I'm missing. It
did need some rearranging, but overall it's an improvement.
