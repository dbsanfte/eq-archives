Subject: Mystical Paths -- Autofind


The autofind function can be faulty at times depending on your UI
and yourgraphic card. Make sure that EQ knows what graphics card
you have by going under options in the start-up menu. The path
sometimes does go through walls. When this happens, use the
autofind key again to get a new path and it may correct itself.
