Ye forgot wood elf! I have human, wood elf, and Iksar warriors...all have their strong points. Even at 55, my "woodie" rarely needs to ask twice for a port, and while her stam can be a a bit low for her main hand weapon (Wurmy), I put a light weapon in off hand (Journeyman's Walking Stick) to balance it, which also helps her keep aggro...the tauntage from its proc is awesome! My human, while blind as a bat without some help, has been fun to roleplay and anything that gives me a second chance to take the spires and Paw is good by me. The Iksar is but a hatchling....although I plan to take it to gfay and work on it's faction soon, what I have seen of it is they make great warriors as well, well rounded, quick to heal, and FUN!
