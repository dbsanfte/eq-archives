Yes. You can lose levels. Once you get above 6th
if I remeber correctly. You only can lose a level if
you lose enough experience to lower your experience
bar below the current level minimum. You lose
experience when you die. So don't die! Also, just because
you leveled, don't change your hunting area right
away. Get yourself a cushion of experience. How much?
Depends on your level. Currently, I am a 24 warrior and
if/when I die, I will lose three blue bubbles of
experience. I have yet to notice a loss of stats/skills from
losing a level (went from 19 to 20 and back way too many
times) but your skills will not advance to the maximum
of the higher level until you regain it. I.e. one
hand slash is at 121 out of 125 (level x 5
+5)(24x5+5). I am barely 24, I die. The experience loss is
enough to take me back to 23. My skill stays 116 even
though it is above the max for that level (23x5+5=120).
It will not go up any until I regain the level which
increases the cap. <br> The guards will not know that you
killed that particular person. If you had any faction
adjustments from the kill (freeport militia goes down,
knights of truth goes up, unseen hands goes down, etc)
then those groups like or hate you just a little more.
Always pay attention to faction. /c when in doubt! I was
kos in ButcherBlock alll because I worshipped Rallos
Zek. Now I'm amiable!<br> As for the epic, you need to
be very experienced. Say 50 ish. Ask the npc's in
