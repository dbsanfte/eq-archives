Monks are a powerful but tricky class to play,
especially in their teens, before they get feign death. They
can outdamage other tanks and since have virtuall no
armor they can taunt monsters onto them, which means
death. Unless they are grouped with a warrior that knows
to pull stuff off them, they need to communicate
their role to the group. <br><br>Also they can be a
pain for healers because they are taking too much
damage or just die fast.<br><br>Players who know how to
play the class well really shine, and I've seen
several outstanding monks, and a bunch of really bad
ones. For that reason the class is never going to be
really popular. It's a good idea to work on your
throwing kill, so you can pull <br><br>You also need good
friend to hold your loot for you since you can't carry
more than 15 lbs.<br><br>On the upside there aren't
too many classes you can outfit so
cheaply.<br><br>Also, at high levels they are favored as pullers and
almost required for really hard places like the planes.
