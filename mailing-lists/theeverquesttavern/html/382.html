Greetings, I hope this answers a few of your
questions...<br>1. Wood Elf Ranger - my personal favorite. I have a
lvl 42 wood elven ranger on the Mith Marr server.
Rangers a very fun and complicated class to play, as well
as versatile. As a melee class, it is our duty to
protect the weak spellcasters in our group from harm.
However, we do not get all the convenient teleport spells
pure spellcasters get.<br>2. Erudite Wizard - Wizards
are the masters of dealing the most damage in the
shortest amount of time. They also have great travel
spells like druids. Just a warning: if you are going to
play a wizard, be prepared to die a lot and spend a
LOT of time meditating. Additionally, Erudites have
horrible night vision.<br>3. Halfling rogue - Don't know
much about this class/race combo but I see a lot of
them around. The halfling rogues i group with seem to
enjoy playing their character.<br>4. High Elf druid -
High elves cannot be druids...perhaps you meant Half
Elves? Statistically, half elves make the worst druids
(lowest wisdom score), but what the heck...it's a game,
have fun =)<br>5. Dark Elf Necro - Necromancers are
extremely powerful - they can heal themselves, cast
powerful Dot (damage over time) spells, summon a skeleton
to fight for them, and do many other things. The
downside is that nobody outside of their own city and the
Ogre and Troll cities likes 'em. <br>dark
elf-necromancer
