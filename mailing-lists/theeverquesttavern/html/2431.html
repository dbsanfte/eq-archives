The problem with EverQUEST, is that there aren't
enough quests that can be done. Many quests are certain
race, class or faction only. Most classes have a few
newbie quests, maybe an intermediate quest, a highlevel
quest and an epic. Others have very few-- (where are
the wood-elf druid quests?? Start by collecting rust
weapons? ack-- do it once for fun, then that's it-- if you
wander to Felwithe you can get the Symbol of Tunare
quests from the cleric guild-- tough to complete, but
you get a cool, upgradable item for your adventures
into dangerous Lesser Faydark)<br><br>Many quests that
exist are too painstaking to complete-- ie Anyone got a
Jaggedpine Cloak (or whatever it's called)?<br><br>There are
some great quests for high xp and loot for around
level 10-- CB belts and pads not only give great xp,
but you get loot to equip or sell, AND you can keep
farming belts and pads to sell for plat. Same is true for
the DF belts in EC/WC -- quests in Freeport give you
great xp and gold. <br><br>Choosing wisely your hunting
areas and being good at auctioning are keys to getting
plat (also some tradeskills, but I wont go into that
here). I'm not sure about Pallys, but I would assume
that there must be somewhere you can go to fight
undead that drop FS weapons (sell to merchants for about
5pp and to players for about 10pp). Maybe Unrest.
<br><br>Giant camps in Kunark (Forrest in WW and Mountain in
FM) are good places to group-- you have a shot at the
Forrest Loops and Foreman's geat, plus most stuff that
drops off these giants (ie legchopper) sell for GREAT
plat at merchants.<br><br>If you go through life just
fighting and selling stuff to merchants, though, you will
not get rich quick. Unfortunately, it takes putting
in the auction time to get serious pp.
