I agree that SK spires is a great place to hunt
in mid 20s-- even at low 20s if you can get into a
good group. I started grouping there with my druid at
lvl 22, and was welcome for heals, Damage shileds and
SOW, as well as nukes (that got resisted a lot at that
level though). For a low 20s char-- the loot isn't bad.
You can get the scrolls for the gem inlaid guants,
get some FS weapons occasionally, get a dagger of
dropping, and SP armor (doesn't sell that well anymore,
except the gloves which still go for at least 15pp, at
least on Xev). <br><br>At upper 20s, you can find
adventurers willing to go inside PAW, where grouping in the
"safe rooms" are great xp, if not a bit boring. Don't
forget "KFC" (which we lovingly refer to as the aviak
town in SK). The aviaks are of varying levels, meaning
you can solo or group fight them for many levels.
They drop some quest items, so there's always people
ready to group on them. SK is a great place to kite if
you're a druid, and I would kite aviaks around when
soloing for great xp. You can also charm an elephant to
help you out-- I had great fun letting my elephant
pets beat down the little chickens. <br><br>I did do
Lake Rathe a few times, just at the gnoll/undead camp
on the other side of the SK zone, which was a great
camp, but not easy to get a group to go there (at least
in my experience). <br><br>After 27-- you can go the
boring route and head to FM and then to OT on Kunark. FM
is acutally a good place to group at the giant
fort-- some good loot can drop and giant killing is fun
for all. OLD WORLD spots for high 20s to 30s usually
means dungeons. Cazic Thule is great, but not easy
access. Rathe Mtns is almost within reach, and you might
be able to get a group of giant hunters there once
you hit 30. SK gnolls is your best bet. Unrest is
still viable, but you have to go after some mean mobs.
Mistmore is viable, but only at the ent area at high
20s/low 30s. Stonebrunt is good hunting, but it's way
over there, so bring your own group. If you're on
Kunark, don't forget about Warslik's Woods-- Forest giant
camp, gobbies and iksar, and not crowded at all.
