Iksar SK is a nice choice. I have a Dark Elf SK
and he rocks. As an Iksar I think you only have one
chioce as far as a diety. The only drawback is that you
are hated everywhere so banking could be a problem if
you decide to leave your island but it is not
impossible with a lot of faction work. Iksars also have a
higher natural regeneration rate which will save you a
lot of downtime.<br><br>I say choose a class that
fits your playing style and personality. If you think
you are going to solo a lot I would recommend Necro,
SK (if you want to melee), Druid or Mage. They seem
to me to be the easiest solo classes. As far as race
goes, it is really a matter of preference. Race might
make a difference initially, but at high levels its
almost a non-issue. Chances are if you are like most of
us, you will try many combinations of races/classes
before you commit to one or two. Be warned, most of the
people around here seem to be Druids so expect to hear a
lot about how great Druids are.
