in the infernal dungeon of Soluseks's Eye, I rushed
from Cazic Thule to meet him. There, we took on some
stuff and were about to kill a rare spawn when we ran
into 3 infernal goblin wizards...My group (I - a lvl
31 ranger, a lvl 33 druid, and a lvl 35 bard) were
utterly slaughtered. I walk back from Freeport (not that
far away from Sol) and get my corpse. Then, we get
ambushed by another horde of goblins - easier ones but
they were numerous, and we were forced to zone into
Nagafen's Lair. Just as we zoned into Nagafen's Lair, a
train of greater kobolds hit us. Greater kobolds are
about lvl 33-35 and double attack for 70 damage. My 2
teammates zoned back into SolA. Why didn't I zone? Because
the zone barrier had turned into OPEN SPACE - and it
wouldn't let me zone!!! So there I was, stuck in the zone
getting killed by 2 kobolds...When our group finally
regained composure (and picked up 3 more people to make a
full group), we returned to SolB to get my corpse and
to hunt greater kobs. When i zoned into SolB i
couldn't find my corpse!! It had fallen thru the world,
and i couldn't summon it. So...i spent about an hour
fighting kob, naked with a FS 2hander (borrowed from
groupmate). I petitioned a GM but none were online at the
time... Well i eventually got sick of it and logged off..
