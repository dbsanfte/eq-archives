Well you'll probably want to put all of the
copied files from the disk to your desktop. Then use
WinZip to unzip them - if you have winzip installed,
double clicking on the files will usually prompt winzip
to open them. Once the files are open, just move
them to the EverQuest directory in your Program Files
folder. Click "yes" when the prompt asks you if you want
to replace. That should do it.
