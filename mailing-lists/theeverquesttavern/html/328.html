Subject: Asheron's Call Beta stuff-EQ anyday!


Well, I recently heard news of the Beta for the
upcoming new OMMRPG Asheron's Call going public via
Computer Games Magazine. I figured I would give it a peek
so I grabbed a copy (About $7) of the mag and tried
out AC. After an hour of updates, I finally got in.
Yes, I answered the Call, but I probably should have
hung up! =P Frankly, the game was quite crummy. Nasty
lag, bad graphics, unnerving interface, miserable chat
system, the works. I was amused with a few things I might
like to see in EQ such as millions of choices for
looks and no zone lines (Actually, there are zone lines
(Portals)to get into "dungeons", but none throughout the main
world) Still, I would prefer EQ any day. Some might
enjoy it, but it isn't my cup of tea and I'm sure many
others will stick to Norrath rather than pack their
bags, delete their characters, and move to
Dereth.<br><br>Just my thoughts =) I have just got done playing AC
for a little while and the boredom drove me to vent
some opinions! hehe<br><br>PS I'm aware that AC is
only in beta, but it is due to release in one month,
so there isn't much that will be changed now.
