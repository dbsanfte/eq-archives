The Taskmaster is near the slave pits right
beside the Castle. You'll need a group to get him
because there are a lot of Legionaires around there and
they can be tough (especially for someone with very
few Hit Points). So when you get into Crushbone shout
that you are looking for a group or ask if you can
loot the Brass Earring from a group that is hunting
there. Just remember, don't go alone!! Around and inside
the castle is very dangerous for someone under level
12. It's very easy to get trained (ran over by lots
of orcs) in Crushbone. I'm sure you won't have any
trouble getting a group, as enchanters are very helpful.
^_^ Good Luck!!
