Tough situation-- here are my thoughts:<br><br>1.
Yours is a lesson to EVERYONE travelling through the
Kithicur forrest at night-- DON'T!!!<br><br>2. Have you
thought of checking out which servers might have more
people on during the times you play?? If I'm in PST and
play normally from 2-5 am, a server that's mostly
American (PST-EST) may not be as good for me as one that's
mainly European--<br><br>3. You should be able to get
back up to decent armor/items/weapon fairly easily.
Non-twinked, my druid char didn't have THAT great stuff at lvl
14-- in fact, most of my cash went to spells, and at
least you don't loose THOSE when you die. If you like
the char, it's hard to give it up. I'm not sure how
good Shammys are at soloing as you go up in levels.
Never had one, and don't see too many on my server, in
fact out of 80 chars in my guild, there are only 3
shammys. <br><br>3. As for great solo artists-- I've heard
that NO warriors solo well after a certain level. My
experience for solo-ing is as follows: Necros are great,
especially in undead areas, Druids are increadible, BUT not
until you become a kite and port master, high teens--
you can port to good hunting locations instead of
running/boating. My mage is a surprisingly good soloist-- pets are
very handy once you learn to master using them in
conjuction with spells. Pallys are ok soloists, though only
in undead situations. The best soloist i know is a
Troll SK-- he seems to STILL be able to solo evens and
some yellows (with HT) at lvl 33. High melee stats,
natural regen, spells and pet. Evil race?? makes it more
fun to roleplay. Only real problem my friend has is
that he can't bank in most places-- BUT-- there are
still a few unique merchants he can sell to-- he can
bank in Runnyeye, and his STR is soooo high that he
can carry TONS of plat around for a long time. He was
discouraged at first, but found how many places in
S.Antonicus (close to his home) he could hunt, and then he
headed out to OT where all evil races are welcome and no
good guards are around at all. <br><br>Just some
thoughts-- good luck!!!<br><br>Xela
