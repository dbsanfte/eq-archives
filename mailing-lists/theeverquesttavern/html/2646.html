I don't know... lvl 13 seems like an aweful young age to get
married. I mean, as a caster, you don't really have a lot of spells
yet, and your spell skills are low. As a melee, well, there is just
so much to do and learn. That's the time when you should be out
adventuring and sowing your wild oats, but now you have to worry
about your "soulmate" getting eaten by a bear or stepped on by a
giant?? And how are you going to feel if you pull a train down on
your wife and she bites the farm?

I saw wait until you're older. Go out and make a (sur)name for
yourself, see distant lands. If she lets you go and you return, it
was meant to be, right? You hear it all the time-- young couple gets
married, but the realize they want different things. He wants to
study Al'Kabor's secrets in Erudin, while she wants to vanquish Hill
Giants for profit. Long distance romances just don't work either,
even if one of you is a druid or wizard. Best to wait 'till lvl 30
at least. Unless you're a halfling. In that case, your life
expectancy is not very high, so you might as well breed like the mice
you are as soon as possible!!

To do it right, you need to master jewelry making, and make your own
ring too. It means so much more.
