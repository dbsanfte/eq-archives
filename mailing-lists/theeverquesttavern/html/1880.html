What to do??<br><br>Are you any less of a player

chars are twinked even though they have cloth armor and

of my knowledge and experience playing this game. I
know about quests, mobs, zones and fighting strategies
(though class specific strategies, especially with my new

comes to taking on Red mobs even if twinked. Also, even
if you twink caster armor, they still have low HP
and AC, and are going to get crushed by mobs. Case in
point-- i grouped with a twinked lvl 10 wizzie in CB, who
had TONS of mana and kept unloading lvl 8 nukes on
Legos-- also kept getting agrod and dying because 5 hits
and she was dead. (which is pretty good, because as
an un-twinked chanter, 2-3 hits usually kills

least to be sucessful at lower levels. <br><br>Last

can be for sale. Low levels can find way to "twink"
their own money supply, buy selling bone chips/bat
wings-- starting trades (I know a low teen mage who
twinked himself after getting to the point of tailoring
those backpacks), etc. Personally, I play for fun and
it's not fun to me if there is not challenge or risk.
