To break it down into layman's terms, the game relies heavily on data
transfer between your PC and its server. Therefore, connection speed
is paramount. ;) Dial-up is one of the slowest connections you can
have (if you use AOL I pity you).

If you really want to spend the money, you could upgrade to DSL. It's
roughly about the same price you'll pay for AOL (depending on where
you get DSL from), or if you have Cable service available, get that.
Dial-up transfers up to 56K per second. DSL and Cable are 10 times
faster than that and more if you have a good modem and cable.
