Kharlisht gave a good suggestion. Also, once you get up your 20's and you get Camoflage, you should think about running through the Warrens and go into Stonebrunt. Plenty of Kobolds to get XP and loot off of too. On my server, Solusek Ro, Stonebrunt is usually pretty empty. Had the whole zone to myself from time to time. Have fun.
