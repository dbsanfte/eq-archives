1) Make sure your computer meets the recommended
system requirements for playing EverQuest. I would not
trust the "minimum system requirements" to provide any
sort of enjoyable experience with this or any game.
"Recomended system requirements" is what they designed the
game for. "Minimum system requirements" is what the
marketing guys put on there to broaden the potential
customer base (regardless of the fact that the game sucks
when played under those circumstances).<br><br>2) Get
Direct X 7.a if you don't have it already.<br><br>3)
Stop all other applications or memory resident
programs while playing EQ, such as Norton Auto Protect,
ICQ, etc. I have found this helps me
personally.<br><br>4) Make sure you have the latest drivers for your
video card.<br><br><br>More info can be found at
