Wisdom and AC will be your two most important
statistics as a shaman.<br><br>There are some different
theories on this, but as a shaman you'll later be casting
spells that have high aggro value - mobs will often
times beat on you a lot. You will want lots of AC for
this. Base agility raises your AC more than agility
raised later by spell buffs or item buffs.<br><br>Also,
wisdom is your spell casting power. I don't think you'll
be able to put all 30 points into wisdom, but put a
lot in there.<br><br>In addition, you'll get
statistical buffs, so keeping your stats high will never be a
problem, as long as you have mana.<br><br>Iskandar<br>51
