Ahh the good ol reports... I usually hate doing
them but if its on something I like then its
fun.<br><br>Just go up there and have fun doing your presentation
and putting it together... sometimes the simplest way
to go is always the best. As for handing out packets
and goodies; its a good idea but remember to keep
your audiences attention you should hold on to them
until your almost finished or they'll spend your
presentation time looking at all the stuff in the packet
instead of paying attention to you. I lost many audiences
by not doing this and I always got aggrivated when
they wouldn't listen to me because I handed out my
best part at the beginning.
