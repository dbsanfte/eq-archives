As with all (and yes ALL) classes, monks have
their drawbacks. Monks, by nature, are not a worldly
class. Therefore, they do not wear much armor or carry
anything with them deemed unnecessary. Monks lose a
defensive bonus if their weight ever exceeds 14, so pack
light. All good monk armor weighs next to nothing. A
good idea would be to get woven or netted armor from
Frogloks, or at least the gloves. Magic gloves count as
magic weapons with hand-to-hand. While monkeys can use
blunt weapons, a true monk finds his strength in his
own body. Monks are also the masters of feigning
death, which can save the whole group. For listing
purposes, here are the benefits of a monk:<br>Damage, lots
of it, and fast as a naked barbarian in
Everfrost<br>Really excellent looking abilities like the roundhouse
kick.<br>Feign death, you will find a place in groups very
easily with this skill<br>Mend wounds-monk only, full
heal (at least I think it's full heal)<br>Safe fall-
again excellent for pullers<br>Never have to worry
about money, there are very few things you will ever
really need after cured silk or better armor<br><br>Now
for the downfalls:<br>Remember that last statement?
You'll really never have the money for anything. Monks
are probably the poorest class on average. Take your
loot in plat or gems only. You have to watch for that
over 14 stoneweight penalty.<br>No armor means more
damage taken, if you don't dodge that is<br>Until you
get feign death, there is not a terrible lot of call
for monks<br>There are very few monks in the world of
Norrath, means that you will have to find information from
outside sources (everlore, allakazam (sp?), etc..) unlike
druids, where there is one always around the
corner<br><br>Remember that monks are not tanks, they have no taunt
skill and cannot take the damage like a tanker.
Personally I have found my monk to be one of my most
enjoyable characters to play, simply because I don't have
to worry about earning enough cash to by that magic
weapon or expensive armor. All I need is a good pair of
gloves and some bandages. (I know some will disagree
with my anti-weapon for monks stance, but then I don't
have anything wrong with a bo stick or other such
things. I just like the Jackie Chan style action. When
you rip a gnoll commander's head off with your fist,
it just feels better than with a big
stick.)<br><br>Nukai Oakenwulf<br>Druid of Karana<br><br>Roen<br>Monk
