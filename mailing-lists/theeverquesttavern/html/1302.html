Here's another Qeynos/Halas area quest dealing
with gnolls. I'm not sure it works, but it's been
bouncing around Everlore a little bit lately. <br>We all
know the "patches of gnoll fur" that we get from
killing gnolls (from pups to elites), right? Well... find
yourself a large sewing kit, put 4 of em, (not stacked, as
with any trade skill) and hit combine. If what I've
heard works, you should get some sort of shoulder armor
(has some save v. cold on it, I think). Take this to
someone in the Halas rogue guild, and you can exchange it
for xp, faction with barbs, and a barbarian rogue
weapon, which I think could be sold for a little bit. I'm
not especially sure on any of this, but gnoll patches
are incredibly easy to come across in blackburrow. I
will investigate this quest a bit and report back
soon. I hope it works, because I could use some good
faction with those crazy barbarians.. heh...<br><br>Nukai
