...I have a story to tell...<br><br><br>The fire
crackled as Bane cast a fresh log into its midst. He could
not keep it burning long, for to do so would bring
unwanted attention to his camp. He only sought the warmth
and comfort of the small blaze. It was another days
journey that would bring him to his destination. Knowing
the route he must take however, would make this trip
seem like ages. The deadly forest of Kithicor was the
center of many tales. Mostly of lost travelers, and
tortured souls. It is said that to enter this Cursed place
in the dead of night would assure you a place in the
afterlife. Bane scoffed at such superstition. <br><br>He
traveled at night, as he felt most at ease within the
shadows. He had learned, from<br>an early age, how to walk
unseen and unheard within the darkness. It had save his
life on more than one occasion, that was
certain.<br><br>As the fire died down, he prepared to get at least
one hour of rest before continuing his travels. Fate
however, always seemed to have other plans. It was only
minutes later that Bane became astutely aware that he was
not alone.<br>He figured the best thing to do was to
remain completely motionless for as long as possible. At
first he thought that there were only two, but then he
realized his situation was much graver. There were four in
all...two to the east<br>and two to the north. By the sound
of it they were heavily armored and by the smell of
it they were goblins. Probably a scouting party. But
why so far from their lands? By the manner of their
movements he quickly determined that he was<br>still
undetected...this was his chance.<br><br>With one fluid, soundless
movement he rolled away from his camp and into the dark
shadow of a nearby tree. Held confidently within his
grip was the Deathbringer Rod. A grand weapon he
acquired months ago in a far off land. Though apparently
just a staff, this wooden<br>weapon did far more
damage than most razor sharp swords. The pure magical
energy used to craft this weapon, is the primary reason
it lives up to its name so well. Many have fallen to
its mighty wrath, and four more were about to join
their ranks.<br>As he sat motionless, poised for attack
the first goblin entered the clearing. It was far
from an impressive sight, actually quite
comical...<br><br><br><br>-------------------<br><br>Please feel free to add to this story. Always try to
