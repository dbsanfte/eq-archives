The new Firiona Vie server is set up to be a
roleplay server. This may or MAY NOT be a good place to
start. It will be a good place to create and have fun
with a charachter you want to roleplay, but this is
not any easy place to be a new player. <br><br>On FV
there is no "common" language, and you start of
basically understanding only the language of your race.
This makes it difficult to travel at first, and to
communicate with others you may need/want to talk to. (ie. I
play a halfling on FV, and would love to buy some
enchanted jewelry, but don't know enough elf to make my
desires known-- lots of elven enchanters where I am, and
there are NO halfling enchanters-- darn!!).
<br><br>Also, the ruleset on FV is very different from the
regular set, with limitations on shouting, auctioning,
looting, speaking, grouping, and getting experience. Also,
I think that FV is the only server where a
character takes on an alignment. <br><br>"Regular" servers
(not including PvP ones) are more of a
community/social experience than a roleplay experience. If you
really are new, my suggestion would be to make a char.
on any other server to get a feel for the game. You
can then ask others specific questions and you'll
hopefully get some help. Then, when you've gotten a hang of
it, you can switch over and make a char. on FV server
for some good old fashioned role play
fun!!!!<br><br>X.
