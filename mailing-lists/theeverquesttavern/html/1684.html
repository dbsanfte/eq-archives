Don't forget about THE best Kaladim quest-- belts
and shoulderpads from the evil orcs in Crushbone!!!
Can't Pallys also do the Corflunk's head quest? Kaladim
is full of good low-mid level quests, and then some
higher ones that send you on great adventures across the
worlds. (Much better that the ONE lame quest for wood-elf
druids in Kelethin-- "uh....go and get me some rusty
weapons. That will make Tunare happy, cuz we don't like
pointy things that are rusty. Oh, except
scimitars...we're ok with those, I guess.")<br><br>IMHO-- the game
is called Ever"QUEST" for a reason. There are tons
of quests you can get in any number of cities.
Talking to NPCs to figure out who they are, how they fit
into the world, and what they'd like you to do for
them, is a large part of the fun. AND-- some quests are
just plain great for quick XP. At certain levels, the
CB belt and pad quests will powerlevel you. The
point, though, is that questing can be a lot of fun, and
give you some direction and purpose. This is
especially great if you like to roll play a little.
