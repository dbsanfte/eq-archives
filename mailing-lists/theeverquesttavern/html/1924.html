Woot is kinda short for "WooHoo!!" --Said in a
"way to go," "good job!," "WOW!!" kind of
context.<br><br>If your station name is rejected, that probably
means someone else has it. That is not the name of any
of your characters, it's just like an account ID.
Everyone has a Station Name and a password to access your
account. If you go to someone else's computer to log into
EQ, and type in your Station name and password,
you'll pull up your account. <br><br>Weapons-- many
casters lament over weapons. Most people will say that
weapons don't even matter to casters. At low levels,
anything will do-- cracked staff is your best bet until
you're ready to hit magic MOBs, then you'll need a magic
staff or piercer. IMHO, 2HB weapons are a waste. There
are few 2HB weapons that will give you the stats you
can get carrying an item in your off hand (ie. Stien
of Maggok). Later in the game, you'll want to hold
to stat increasing items in your hands most likely,
and rarely switch to a weapon--UNLESS you get a great
stat increasing weapon. My suggestion, pick piercing
OR 1HB, grab a weapon and go. OH-- on Xev, you can
pick up a Dagger of Dropping for 5pp or less-- pretty
good lower level dagger, with a low lvl proc of
weakness, I think. <br><br>Next item-- by "clan," i think
you mean guild? Depending on your personality and
playstyle, you may get alot out of a guild. I liked the
soletary life w/o a guild for a long time-- but having
people in your head that you get to know over time is
pretty great. There are lots of bad guilds out there,
though-- takes a lot of work to make them fun and
prosperous-- good luck!!!<br><br>Xela Orcbane<br>Druid of the
