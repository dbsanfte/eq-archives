A bit of advice. If it's your first time playing
I recomend a warrior, just to get used to the
game.<br>Warriors are pretty much the simplest class, and probably
the easiest to play in the early levels.<br>After you
get more familiar with the game and how combat works,
you can switch to another class.<br>As for race,
Barbarians make good warriors, and their town's pretty
simple, but they're kind of hard to get started with as
far as their hunting grounds go. Unless you use the
right strategy, you'll die a lot before making level
2.<br>Freeport has 2 good starting areas, but the lag's
intolerable. So I recomend starting in Qeynos. The lag's not
too bad, and there's plenty of good hunting from
level 1-3.<br>Oh, and have your friend show you how to
hotkey your attacks.
