Subject: Which sites I use for what.


I was interested to know which sites people use
for what purposes. Here is how and why I personally
use each
one.<br><br><br>www.castersrealm.com<br>Spells, jewelery, research, patch info, hot forum topics.
I use this site a lot because I'm an enchanter.
Also, the webmaster always posts summarized responses
from Abashi (Gordon) on various hot forum
topics.<br><br><br>www.eqmaps.com<br>Weapon stats, maps, NPC mob info, and more. I use this
site all the time. This is #2 on my list only because
they don't have spell info, which I need. I personally
believe this site has the absolute best layout, search
features, cross-referrencing, and updates. Amazing
site.<br><br><br>eq.stratics.com<br>The only thing I really use this site for anymore is
the "Class Referrence" page which lists the skills
for each class. Other than that, I never really visit
anymore. I am not that interested in weddings, new guilds,
trade bazaars, and the other various community issues
that seem to constitute the bulk of this site's
news.<br><br><br>www.maximumeq.com<br>Price refference. It might not be very accurate, but
many times it can give you a rough estimate. I also
like how the weapon/armor listings are listed from
worse to best. You can sort the item searches on EQMaps
various ways also, but sometimes Maximum EQ is just a
quicker way of doing
it.<br><br><br>www.brellserilis.net<br>I only use the forums here. But these forums are
specifically for Brell, my server. Many great discussions can
be found here regarding plain/dragon raids, big
guild matters, etc. I encourage everyone to find the
"self-declared official" board for their server and check it
out.<br><br><br>everquest.allakhazam.com<br>Quests. I like this site for quests because all the info
is edited and placed by the webmaster. So all of it
is either confirmed, or open disclaimed as a
possible rumor. But if I can't find it
here...<br><br><br>www.everlore.com<br>...I go here. I know some people that think this is
the best EQ site around. I think it's definately
good, but I prefer to go to various sites that
specialize in certain things. The Quest section on Everlore
is quite expansive and up-to-date. Players
contribute driectly to the info. But I find it more
difficult to actually find the info I'm looking for here.
Half of the posts in the quest section are questions
about (not answers to) some quest. And some answers
that you do find have a contradicting post from
another player on the same page. Kinda
confusing.<br><br><br>Eq'Lizer - gameznet.com/eq/<br>I stopped going here after
EQmaps got rolling. But every now and then you'll hear
of some high level item that EQmaps hasn't added to
their database yet. Then I'll check here. The guy who
maintains this site is a level 50 SK (I
believe).<br><br><br>I also check out various class-specific sites when
I have a question regarding them. www.eqforge.com
is a great resource for Rangers, Shaman, and Shadow
Kinghts. In particular, the Shaman's Crucible (under
eqforge.com) has the only good Alchemy section I know
of.<br><br><br>I'm interested in hearing your thoughts. What sites
do you use for
what?<br><br><br>Nelmunbar<br>Enchanter on Brell
