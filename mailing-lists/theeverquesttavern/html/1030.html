Well a guildmate of mine recently purchased a
Voodoo 5 5000 with 64 MB of VRAM. He runs full
resolution with all features turned on/maxed with no lag
whatsoever. EQ runs more smoothly on Voodoo cards because
they are Glide compliant. Prophet II cards run on
NVidia GTS Geforce technology. Their drivers are known
to be quite good, and most people like the full
screen anti aliasing they offer (smooths out
pixels/polygons/rough edges on graphics), but EQ tends to run better on
Voodoo cards. So really it's either way, whichever you
prefer and are willing to buy.
