If I were you, I'd go to Rivervale or Freeport
next, I'm not too familiar with higpass and highkeep,
but I think most of the stuff there starts around
15th level, may be wrong though : )<br>To get there,
go from Qeynos hills to West Karana, as soon as you
enter WK take a right and run til you get to the shore.
Then follow the water for a very very long time.
You'll pass a stone bridge in NK, theres a bandit camp
pretty soon after the bridge so be carefull. Soon you'll
come to a wooden bridge, cross that and follow the
path to highpass hold. The path splits up, but theres
a marker to tell you which way to go. You can bind
anywhere in the highpass zones which is quite nice. The
run takes a while, but following the shore will make
it alot safer. A good idea might be to /ooc if
anyones running that way and ask if you can tag along,
theres always people making the freeport qeynos trip and
most are quite happy to help i find : ) If you do
decide to go further on to Rivervale or Freeport just be
careful not to go through Kithicor woods at night, its
rather nasty.<br>Have you tried Kerra Isle? Tons of exp
and loot there for someone your level and its not
usually crowded. Its the first stop for the qeynos
boat.<br>Well, this has been quite long winded hehe.. hope I
helped out a little : )
