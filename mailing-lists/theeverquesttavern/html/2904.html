Befallen is a good zone for a toon of your level, stay
close to the zone when fighting and you should be
alright. as you raise in level work your way in and
bring havoc to the creatures that lurk in the compound
stay away from the well of the Damned because the fall
would either kill you or make you vunerable to the
25th level shadowknights on the 2nd level, above all
try to group for better protection. I'm a firm
believer that you should know all you can about a zone
before comitting yourself to venturing in it. Buy the
Everquest Atlas (The Maps of Myrist) and you will have
a good map of both levels.

Luis
