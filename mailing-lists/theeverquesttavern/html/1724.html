Subject: Hunting Grounds


Just thought I'd like to share my favorite
hunting grounds, as well as hear from some others about
they places they frequest for XP and loot.<br><br>I
have discovered the joys of the Warrens. Besides being
relatively quiet, with rarely more than a half dozen people
there, you can safely hunt there, even solo, from about
level 7 to 30. Being a dungeon, you recieve an XP bonus
there as well. And the faction CAN NOT be beat. You
only lose faction with the kobolds themselves, while
gaining faction with both the Heretics and the good
Erudites.<br><br>Not only that, but the loot tables there are off the
charts. A mid-teen character can easily make 150pp+ in a
day's worth of fighting there. Bronze weapons drop like
mad, so does cash. Better items when you reach around
20-25 drop off of the named mobs. Crown, Scepter,
Cloak, etc. of the Forlorn, lots of class-specific
items, and some pretty decent armor, both Cold Iron and
Soft Wicker, drop throughout the zone.<br><br>The best
classes to bring in there are probably monks, shaman,
warriors, shadowknights, necros, or really any true
casters. Lots of INT items in there, as well as mana
boosting and WIS items. There are also quests in the
Warrens specifically for Heretics and Paladins from
Erudin.<br><br>Only downside of the zone is the random spawns of mobs
who are a bit TOO high for younger characters to
handle. Twice last night we had to zone because something
wandered up to us that was at least 5 levels higher and
resisted nearly all our spells. Other than that, not too
many problems. Even the pathing in there is pretty
predictable.<br><br>Oh, and farmers/loot whores shouldn't bother going:
if you're too high level to get XP, or the creature
you killed was FAR too easy for your group, you don't
get to loot the rare drops that creature might
have.<br><br>Let's hear YOUR fave place to hunt. What do you like
about it? Where's it at? Some tips for newcomers?
