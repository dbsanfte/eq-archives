Figured, I noticed those that posted stories and
I shall post an interesting story.<br><br>This is
not one about such grand ol' things such as ye of the
large and grandeous cities might tell. There are no
dragons, or demons. No grand monsters, and no evils
slain.<br><br>Yea, this is a tale of laughter and embarrassment for
such as I. I, known as Fatedhue, Wood Elf Druid
resident of Kelethin. He of the 4th ranking.<br><br>I was
travelling through the forest stopping the foulness of
Tricksters and Pawns. I was also harvesting the natural
bounty of the forest when I passed a maiden most passing
fair.<br><br>This was something I oft wondered at and I told the
lady... "Indeed, tis quite a wonder at the art of this
world that generates such beauty as that of the wood
elf female!"<br><br>Oddly enough, this said female
bedecked o' her self in leather asked me, "And of High
Elves?" I answered her, "Yea, laddie. High Elves are fair
but only passing fair compared to the beauty existant
in Wood Elves. For what can compare to beauty forged
in nature. High elves be of lofty buildings, where
we be of lofty arbols."<br><br>I passed on my way,
continuing in my chores for personal inroads-- as well as
assisting those of the Tunare Rangers.<br><br>Later that
night, faine did I pass said lady again, and I peered
more than passing glance this time. Alas my
embarrassment!!! I noted her more closely as HIGH elf... and not a
laddie of my fair arbols. She was a LADY of
Felwithe.<br><br>I apologized to the lady for my comments. I
profusely apologized for my drool-- I'd fallen from the
heights of Kelethin once too much. Tis' indeed more
embarrassing to insult a laddie or lady, than to fall in
drunken stupor to the floor of Kelethin.<br><br>Yea,
indeed. That is my tale for those around. I am a humble
storyteller-- of no match for those Minstrels of Norath. I
thank all for listening to my humble tales.<br><br>Fare
all well,<br>FatedHue, Druid of the 4th, Resident of
Kelethin, He of Drinal.
