Anyone ever pay attention to druid agro ability
in a group?? Everyone talks about clerics and
wizzies agroing by heals/nukes, but there is a dynamic
for druids that i haven't yet figured out. Here are
some observations, though---<br><br>Snare/Ensnare
makes mobs mad, but they are easily taunted from it.
<br><br>A tank with a druid DS is far less likely to loose
agro, even by heals/nukes. <br><br>Druid dots don't
seem to cause much hate AT FIRST, and generally don't
cause a mob to break agro on a tank and come for you.
Exception-- get too close and/or start melee. Example-- a mob
is pulled, everyone bears down on it. I hit it with
Creeping Crud and Immolate, cast a damage shield, wait a
bit to make sure someone else has snared and that
healers are ok, and move it for melee and nuking up
close. I walk in, take a few wimpy druid hits, and the
mob totally ignores the Warrior and monk and comes
for me. I take a few hits for the team and back
off--mob goes back to hitting the DSd warrior.
hmmmmm.....<br><br>Fear spells (druids' on work on animals) don't seem to
cause much hate-- probably cuz once it finally breaks,
others have done so much damage that that hate overrides
it. <br><br>healing and nuking are the same for
druids as clerics/wizzies-- if you hit/heal to hard,
you'll pull them off in many cases. I NEVER heal above
50% health-- i see clerics wasting GrHeal all the
time-- the secondary tank lost 2 bubs and isn't getting
hit anymore, but he gets healed-- or the tank is down
1 1/2 bubs of life and gets healed with Gr heal.
Isn't that a waste??? PLUS--healing a nearly full
character is more likely to agro the mob on you (if a mob
has a character down to 40%, it's less likely to
leave that guy AT ALL, especially after the recent
patch affecting this situation--in my experience).
<br><br>Quick way for a lvl 30 druid to die (just like for a
wizzie)-- start unloading with combust on a freshly pulled
mob. I've seen druids do it, then run around screaming
"get it off me, get it off me!!!!" <br><br>A wizzie
has to be patient and wait for the right nuking
opportunity. A druid has so many other options BEFORE he
starts nuking, that he doesn't have to get bored--
snare, DS, DOT....<br><br>My 2cp worth-- anyone know of
a Hate list?? (ie. what actions cause the
most/worst hate).<br><br>Xela
