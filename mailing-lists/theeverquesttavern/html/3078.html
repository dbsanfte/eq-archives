The cd key is tied to the account name and password. A subscription
card or credit card won't help you get past this hurdle. If you knew
the person who sold the software, you could ask them for the name and
password. But it's doubtful you would get it and even more risky as
their information is registered as the owner of the account. Your
best bet would be to either buy the 9.99 cheapy basic EQ to get a
fresh cd key or find the new jumbo pack which contains most of the
expansions along with the basic eq game. It is supposed to retail for
29.99.
