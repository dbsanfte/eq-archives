Subject: Here's a tip.


If you're an Enchanter, keep scintillating colors
memorised. It's a great spell to have in a group. But even
if you're soloing, or just fighting single
opponents, use it anyway. It's a FAST casting time, stuns
them long enough to let you get other spells off, and
doesn't cost much mana.<br>Languid pace is a real
lifesaver too. Cuts their attack speed in half. =)
