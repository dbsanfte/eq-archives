Yep-- hit the "-" key to take them. Remember that you can hit F10 to
get full screen shot, and F9 first to change the angle to get more
diverse pics.

The are stored in your EQ file as EQ0000001, etc, or something like
that. They are saved in .BMP format, so I usually always make them
over into .jpg after I log off. BMPs take up lots of space,
especially when you are "camera happy" and forget that you've got
hundreds of SS (screenshots) hanging out there.
