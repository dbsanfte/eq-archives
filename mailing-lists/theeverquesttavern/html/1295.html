I too have the look of almost all leather, save
for my arms and forearms. Unfortunately this is a
curse that we druids have, we look pretty much the same
from lvl 8 until lvl 45 or whenever we can start
getting our hands on planar armor. <br>My arms are bone
armplates, which give a chain look, and my forearms
alternate between leather and bare, depending on whether
the graphics pick up on my reinforced bracer or my
Chipped bone bracelet. It is possible to break out of the
leather look with a Brown Chitin Protector (gives a brown
plate look to chest) or for halfling druids, there is a
scarab armor that gives a green plate look (it's a quest
out of Kaladim, if I'm correct, but I've seen the
helm, good lookin for a lil' guy). As for legs, I've
heard that barbed leggings (or something like that)
gives a black plate look, but I am unsure of
this.<br><br>::Nukai shrugs::<br><br>Nukai Oakenwulf
