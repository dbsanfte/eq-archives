scapegoatism (for lac of a better word/or non word) is/has been prevelant umong state societies for over 8000 years
its so much easier to pick and something and make it the "evil" than to truly examine our own lives and culture for potential faults. humans (or all mammals for that matter) are addictive my nature, or you could say are driven internally to find patterns of behavior and lean on them. anything can be an addiciton, from the ritual you do when you start your day to the things you read, eat, the excersize you do. also anything that involves social behavoir as in chatting with friends can be very addictive. being social is also an intinctive drive. this is one of the great difficulties in breaking unhealthy habbits like smoking for instance in that it often involves having friends who also smoke and sharing and being friendly while doing so. if someone doesnt have much of a social life when they begin playing then the involvement that EQ requires with others as you get higher in levels could fill that need and being in a guild can for some create a psudo family.
perhaps it would be more productive to examine what is missing from society rather than attacking games, music or any non traditional group activities that are filling in that void.
