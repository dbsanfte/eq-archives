Holy crap... I didn't have anything close to 100
plat at lvl 12.... keerap... I was too worried about
wondering how I was going to be able to upgrade my spells
in 5 levels to worry about what I was wearing or
wielding. Come to think of it, I used a non-magical cracked
staff paired with a wooden shield until I was level 18
or so. Don't know how I got away with it, but I
did.<br><br>But that will all change, all change. When you hit
the 30s, this is when you can start getting your
hands on some decent money. Do giants for cash, gnolls
in south karana, go dungeon hopping in Unrest or
Runnyeye for some stuff that'll sell. Even Cazic Thule is
a decent place to head to, try and win the rolls on
ravenscale or lizardscale mantles and such. Mid 30s take on
the Avatar of Fear, and sell those claws... whoo!
<br>But anyway, don't be worried too much about cash at
level 12. As long as you can survive, you are doing
great!<br>Keep your spells upgraded at all times, and get a
magical weapon of some sort. For paladins, the price of a
Ghoulbane will be dropping soon, merely due to the
introduction of a not terribly difficult quest to get it.
Locate your nearest druid and see if you can convince
him to get you one. Be extra kind. A level 35 druid
will have no problem getting around to all the
locations for the quest, and should only have trouble with
the creatures involved in Najena (because it is
indoors). A ghoulbane is an excellent weapon, and I would
advise you holding out until one is made available to
you. From there, take on Befallen or Unrest to put
that dismiss undead proc to work for you.<br><br>Nukai
