Don't overlook a few key spots in Dagnar's-- for instance, the gobbie
fort close to Unrest. A few times when Unrest was too crowded or we
were sick of trains, we'd head outside to this camp. Gobbies range in
level, and occasionally the Tidal Lord will spawn, which is great xp.
They drop some average loot, but they constantly drop arrows, so you
can load up there on the way to Unrest too.

Lots of good places in old world-- some are out of the way, though,
and you might want to form your own group. Najena, SolA, SK, High
Pass, Everfrost, Oasis, Upper Guk to name just a few.
