**(Necro advise) I personally always save enough
mana to Feign Death and Gate. Always have FD (if you
are a necro, SK, or monk) memed, always have Gate
memed, and invis is also a good one to have memed. If
you are about to die and something is aggroed on you
then FD. Gate will be interupted so use FD to get them
off of you then when they walk away gate. FD still
works for 15 min (I think) so you can lie there for a
while if you have to.<br>**Making a pet can be good AND
bad in a dungen. An extra tanks is always nice BUT
pets will follow runner isn't killed quickly with the
pet behind it, then the pet will have other things
attack it. When your pet dies those things will come
after YOU. I had a pet chase a runner and he died and
sent me 3 gators, 2 Zelots, a Judicar, and a Sent. We
had just finished fighting 4 gators and a pro so we
were low mana and health. The cleric and Mage died and
the rest of us barely made it. Because of this, I'm
leary of using a pet in a dungen because in a melee
it's hard to keep track of your pet.<br>**ALWAYs
ALWAYS tell your group if you are AFK. One time the
shaman who was the group healer went AFK without telling
anyone. Things went bad really fast and she and the tank
died while she was AFK. She was quickly booted from
the group.<br><br>That is all the advice i can think
of right now. Hope this helps!<br><br>~Aeday
