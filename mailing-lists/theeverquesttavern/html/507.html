There's a few dozen class and race combinations,
faction ratings depending on your class, race and
religeon that can be improved or worsened, and hundreds of
different items and spells. It's a level based game with a
wide variety of skills, including combat and trade
skills. You can use some of the skills to create your own
items for sale or personal use. You can also exchange
most items with other players. You can either play
solo, or with up to 5 other players. If you group with
others you split the experience automatically, and share
the faction hits and bennies. There's also an option
to split loot automatically.<br>It's a very big,
highly detailed campaign world with hundreds of npc's,
many of which give you quests to go on. And has
challenges for levels as low as 1 to as high as 50,
depending on the area you're in. Also in my opinion it's
graphics aren't anything to sneeze at.<br>I recommend a
warrior at first, just to get used to the game.
