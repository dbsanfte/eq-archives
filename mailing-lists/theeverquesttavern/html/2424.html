At level 7 you can go so many places in old world
that they can't be listed. It just depends on where
you are and what you want. Soloing, grouping, seeking
unique loot, running quests, making plat, getting best
xp, working on tradeskills in between fighting...
what is your objective?<br><br>For non Iksar, Kunark
is really for lvl 20ish and up, so no worries yet.
Be warned though-- Lake of Ill Omen, Frontier
Mountains, and The Overthere are some of the best zones for
xp and grouping from 20-35 and beyond. Velius is
really for 30ish and up, so no worries there either. You
can survive without Luclin, most of us did until
recently. <br><br>Someone said Mammys in EF for a lvl 24
and up? Yes-- caveat is that you are a druid or a
ranger, or team with a druid or ranger. From 25ish-29,
druids and rangers can solo Mammys for good xp and great
loot. They have track skill, and generally prefer to
solo-- because they can. If you're a pally trying to
find and beat Mammys alone... good luck.<br><br>Many
dungeons in old world have XP bonuses. If you want XP
quick-- drag some friends to places like Befallen (i
think), Najena and Runnyeye. For fast action and group xp
(if you don't die too much), get to Crushbone or
Blackburrow. East Commons is pretty safe, close to town, lots
of guards and vendors nearby, people at tunnel who
will buff/heal you, and some orc camps and wandering
beasts. Bonus if you are working on tailoring--- kill
spiders, lions and bears. <br><br>If you're a pally-- make
Unrest your home from about 17ish on up to 30 and beyond
if you want. Full of undead-- groups in mid to high
teens start outside the house or in the courtyard, then
head in to the front door, the side door or the "Round
Room." Then upstairs, and finally to the
basement.<br><br>Theoretically, you could stay on Faydwer to lvl 40 by doing CB
and Butcherblock (the dwarf bandits and higher level
orcs at the shrine), Dagnor's Cauldron (gobbie camp
close to Unrest), Unrest, and Mistmoore. Oh, and don't
forget about Steamfont-- very overlooked, but there are
some good hunting spaces, including the dragon
skeleton area (skellys), Windmill area, outside the Mino
caves, and inside the Mino caves.<br><br>I've heard you
can get from 1-30 without leaving Paineel newbie
zone, Warrents and Stonebrunt.<br><br>BUT-- how boring
would that be??<br><br>Good luck, and make sure to drag
some adventurers to some underpopulated zones for some
fun!!!
