Subject: Beginner notes on Rallos Zek


I played a Cleric on Rallos Zek up to 12th level.
I do not know everything there is to know about
PvP, but I can offer some advice.<br><br>Get the spell
Flash of Light and have it memorized at all times in
potential PK areas. Put it on a Hot Key. If someone flashes
you, you can still hit the Hot Key and flash them
back.<br><br>Always try to keep your target box empty. Hit the ESC
key often as a habit. This will allow you to
auto-target anybody trying to blast you with a spell. Then
you can Flash them without having to spin around (or
look up) to find them.<br><br>Keep Divine Aura
memorized. You should probably be doing this even on the
regular servers. It will give you a valuable 10 seconds
of immunity to zone, bag your stuff, or
whatever.<br><br>Take your valuables off during long medding sessions.
The items that act as "Kill Me Please" signs are
Chest, Legs, Arms and Head. Boots and Gloves are fairly
generic and do not attract a lot of attention. You could
wear the best belt, earings, rings, mask, gorget, and
cloak in the game...and nobody would know since they
usually do not show up on your character model.<br><br>If
someone inspects you, target them, back up, get rdy to
fight, run, or die.<br><br>Bank often. Losing items to a
PK is the worst part, but no sense losing a lot of
money as well.<br><br>Keep your health above what you
usually would on a normal server while medding. For
example, if you have 3 bubble of health and only one
bubble of mana you'd usually just sit down to med. On a
PvP server, I'd spend that last bubble of mana to
raise my health to 4 bubbles if possible. Nobody has to
know you're OOM. But they can clearly see your health
status.<br><br>Talk to people. They will often tell you if there is a
naked mage (the plague of PvP) in the area. Steer clear
of that area. If you've been told so-and-so is a PK
and then you see him and he says it was a lie, I
wouldn't believe him. Better to be paranoid than
dead.<br><br>If you see your friend getting rocked by someone and
cannot help them (he and the PK are both way above or
below you in level) try to loot your friend's corpse
the INSTANT he dies. You can then give your friend
whatever item you took back, and prevent the PK from
getting anything. I'm not sure if this works anymore on
Rallos, but it did when I played there.<br><br>The spell
Stun is very nice. If you can time it correctly, you
can begin casting it the same time as a wizard starts
his casting. Stun will usually cast faster, and break
their concentration.<br><br>These are some things I
learned during my time on Rallos. I hope the
help.<br><br><br>Nelmunbar<br>Enchanter on Brell (because I got tired of being paranoid
