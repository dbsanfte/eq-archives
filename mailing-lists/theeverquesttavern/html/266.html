Finally, after much putzing by the gods, the
guild of Innoruuk's Bane has been formed on the
Mathaniel Marr Server! Innoruuk's Bane is a guild devoted
to conquest against evil and hatred all over
Norrath. Ogres, Trolls, Dark Elves, Shadowknights, and
Necromancers all stand for virtues that are against those of
Innoruuk's Bane and thus may not be members. As the name
suggests, the guild is primarily against the Tier'Dal and
wars with the Dark Elven race will be frequent and
unmerciful. For all of those who feel the same way about the
Darkness that is spreading over our beloved Norrath,
please post a message here saying so, or send a /tell to
Durran, Jeruss, Oladahn, or Trellan on the Mathaniel Marr
Server. May your heart guide you to the right choice so
that we may save our world from these horrendous
evils!<br><br>Trellan Hawksight, Wood Elf Ranger of 22 seasons.
