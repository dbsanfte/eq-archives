Druids and Wizards are commonly referred to as
"taxis" because of their teleport spells. Usually,
however, the term is applies to those Druids and wizzies
that spend a LOT of time teleporting for cash. 10pp a
seat, for most normal rings/spires, is about right. Hot
spots like the Dreadlands, Eastern Wastes, etc. cost
more because of the level needed to teleport there and
the difficulty once you've arrived. NEVER try to TP
to Dreadlands until you're 35+. Even then you're
going to have some problems when you
arrive.<br><br>Tyral Stormfang
