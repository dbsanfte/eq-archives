Subject: How to Join The Dreamweavers!


Welcome to our Online Guildhall.

To Join the Dreamweavers you need to

1. Read our Charter and make sure you meet our qualifications.

2. Read our Rules, to see if you agree with them.

3. Fill out our Online Application Form. This lets us know you are
interested and is required for all members to be added to our roster.

4. Find some Dreamweavers in EverQuest and Chat/Group with them. They
will become your 'sponsors'.

5. Contact an Officer online for an Interview. Let us know you've
applied and who you've grouped and spoken with.

After that we contact those Dreamweavers and ask how it went.

Our decision is based on compatibility. If you would fit in with our
family you are invited. We do this to maintain guild harmony and
ensure our members continued happiness.
