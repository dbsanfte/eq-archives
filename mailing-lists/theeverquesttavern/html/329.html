Subject: The Temple of Solusek Ro


After the new zone was added, I decided to check
it out. I thought it was a very interesting and
creative idea. If you weren't aware, this zone is not for
hunting. On the contrary, it is actually a zone entirely
devoted to quest distribution. This zone gives quests for
necromancers, magicians, wizards, enchanters, bards, and
clerics. The zone looks really great with all sorts of
lava spouts and rivers all over (Be careful! Fall in
the lava and you might find yourself trapped while
you are burned to death!) Unfortunately, all of the
quests are sort of high. For example, each of the quests
for clerics that will give you a piece of cleric only
armor, requires 2 or 3 enchanted platinum bars!
Unenchanted bars cost about 125pp EACH! Oh well, guess it's
time to do some leveling, hehe.<br><br>Those of you
not listed among those sponsered inthe Temple, do not
despair! Monks are warriors already have their quests
(Sashes/headbands and crafted) Rangers can obtain their new armor
quests at the ranger guild in Kithicor, Shadowknights
get theirs in Mistmoore, Rogues get theirs in Cazic
Thule, and druids in the rathe mountains. Still don't
know about shamen and paladins....<br><br>There's my
little update. Go check out the new Temple. It is pretty
cool =)
