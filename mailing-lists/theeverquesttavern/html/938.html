Hide is also useful for enchanters to break their
charm spells without having to invis.<br><br>Some notes
about Hide for those that get it as a racial ability.
Your skill never improves from level one. You will
often fail with it. You will never be able to see
yourself when you Hide, even when you fail. But a good way
to tell if it worked is to target an agressive mob,
then Hide. Then /con the mob. If it now /cons
indifferent, the Hide worked, otherwise it failed.
<br><br>This does not apply to all Kunark mobs, since some
/cons there are screwed up and many monsters will /con
agro to you even though you're invis, and some of them
aren't agro at all. Brutes in Warslik's Woods for
example. They /con agro, but aren't.<br><br>Hope this
helps.<br><br><br>Nelmunbar Mirraghost<br>30 DE Enchanter
