Warrens is way far away from my neck of the woods
(Faydwer). I've been living in Unrest for the last six
levels or so. Very fun place, but takes some getting
used to. Notorious trains, weird pathing, mainly
undead. Been getting a lot of FS and bronze weaps, lots
of runes for various casters' spells. Also, if
you're a rogue, you can get lots of supplies for
poison-making -- zombie skins for vials, grave mold, Amanita
Phalloids, Dark Bone Marrow for the poisons themselves. And
the only source for a mortar and pestle in Faydwer is
(sort of) on the way, on the island in the lake in
Dagnor's Cauldron.<br><br>But again -- you must have a
group (and a good one) after about level 15. You'll be
able to solo the hedge, for example, but that gets
kind of old and slow after a while. Get someone who
knows what they're doing, and you'll get a level every
two days. Enjoyed it much more than
Crushbone.<br><br>Of course, there's no place like home for a bit of
variety. Lots of things to kill in Butcherblock Mountains,
and lots of guards and helpful PC's to help out if
you get in trouble.<br><br>Kharlisht of
