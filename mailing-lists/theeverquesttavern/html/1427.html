I have successfully made it into the warrens on
several occasions. It sounds like you are either having
trouble with Habbib in the Paineel newbie zone, or you
are going too far into the Warrens. Habbib is a pain,
but you can outrun him most of the time, especially
if you have SoW. When I visit the warrens with my
druid, I usually kill Habbib and let his corpse lie
there to help out any non-heretic newbies trying to get
to the warrens. Note: the warrens is a lvl 6 or
above dungeon. I would advise waiting until about 7 or
8 so that you have no trouble with any of the
skeletons guarding the paineel zone. Other than that,
