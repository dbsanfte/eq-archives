Oh, don't get me started on this. I've only been
playing about 6 weeks, but I already see what twinking
does to the game, and it annoys me. It's kind of an
arrogant thing that no self-respecting DM ever allows in
pen-and-paper D&D, but that Verant evidently doesn't have a
good solution for.<br><br>I don't twink, and not just
because my main character is only 10th level. I hope I
don't do it -- I think if I do, it means the game has
become boring at lower levels, and I should probably
stop playing. Because that's really the issue, right
-- no one wants to go through levels 1-10 again, so
you twink and power-level to get to the interesting
stuff. Maybe if Verant made life a bit more exciting for
"old" noobs somehow, that would reduce the need for
twinking. . . .<br><br>I watched a 5th level Bard dismantle
the women bandits in Lesser Faydark one night, and
was totally wowed. I asked to inspect, and discovered
that EVERY SLOT was filled with a magic item. Cripes,
I had _one_ -- a suit of Dwarven ring mail that
someone had pretty much given me (sold it to me for 5 gp
or something in Freeport) (he wanted 5 pp, but that
was all I had). <br><br>Do I like it when someone
twinks me? Sure, because it's cool to get magic items,
but more importantly, because it takes place in the
context of the game. When the friendly Dwarf sold me the
ring-mail, another character standing nearby said, "That was
a really nice thing to do." And it was. Why'd he do
it? Glutted market, maybe, but also perhaps because I
was a role-player. I've found that other characters
(usually high-level) reward that. But that's another
discussion.<br><br>What do we do about it? I've wondered if one character
per server would solve the problem -- that way, you'd
have to get twinked by other people, at least, and
that would promote Verant's goal of encouraging group
play. I suppose hard-core twinkers would just buy a
second account, but that would also work to Verant's
advantage. <br><br>Thing is, twinkers see nothing wrong with
the practice (obviously). Go to the class-specific
Rogue discussion board on Everlore and check out the
post "How should I twink my little roguie?" Gee, maybe
