Subject: 19th in Cazic Thule


I just got level 19 last night in Cazic Thule
myself. I'd say 19 is generally too low to be there, but
the circumstances allowed it. There were 2 high level
groups in the front area pulling things. They didn't
mind if we helped is disposing of the "trash" as they
called it, Warders, Defenders, and those low level
casters.<br><br>I was in a full group. Myself (Enchanter 18),
Shadow Knight 18, Ranger 20, Shaman 20, and 2 Wizards
both 20. A few of the things we were pulling were red
to me. So instead of watching my spells get
resisted, I would help in other ways. I'd of course Tashan
the Mob (unresistable). Then I'd usually Quickness
the tanks and Mez any extras. Shaman was on Heal
duty, as his DOTs weren't really necessary with 2
wizards in the group :) Memory Blur came in handy for me
many times also. One of the Wizards wasn't too good at
timing his burn and got frenzied on a lot.<br><br>We had
to zone a couple of times, but with SoW it wasn't
bad. I ended up getting 19th level there. Then the
high level groups started thinning out and the
remainders were pulling stuff they couldn't quite handle,
which was exceedingly dangerous for us. So we decided
to split.<br><br>Overall, a very good night. But in
general, I'd say Cazic Thule is not intended for people
below level 20 :)<br><br>Good hunting to you
all.<br><br>Nelmunbar<br>Enchanter on Brell
