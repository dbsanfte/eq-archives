All of your pictures will be called "EQ000001" or
something to that extent. They are bitmaps, so they take up
a large amount of memory. If you do keep some
screenshots, you might want to convert them to jpg format. All
you do for that is go to "File" then "Save As". There
will be an arrow under the box where you name the
file. Click the arrow to bring up some options, and
then click on "JPEG interchangeable format" or
something like that, just look for jpg. JPGs will be about
10-20% of the memory size of
