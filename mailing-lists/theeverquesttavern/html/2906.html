The good news is that the skeletal excavator's you have to kill to
get the foreman to pop are found on the first floor. They are not
behind any doors, so it's just a good ol' find 'em and whack 'em
event. When the foreman pops, he does a zone wide saying and appears
towards the back on the first level, between the two skulls in the
wall. Kill him and Sir A will spawn in the same spot.

Befallen is decent. I still visit it with my 28 war once in a while.
On the way to the 2nd level, that is when you will find much more
sks, necros, and mummies. They will swarm you. But since this quest
can be done with out going down there or through any doors, you
should be fine.
