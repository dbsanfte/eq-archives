Subject: Escape From Aviak Island


My friend Mordiki and I headed to Aviak Island
last night in the ocean of Tears. Mordiki is a Ranger,
I am an Enchanter, bot level 19.<br><br>Short
description of the aviaks on the island:<br><br>Starlings -
green, very easy for level 19<br>Turaco - green/blue,
fairly easy<br>Tanager - blue/even, ideal<br>Quezatol -
usually red, too difficult for just 2 people<br><br>We
could actually have killed a Quezatol, but the danger
and resulting downtime were too great to warrant
it.<br><br>Once there, we started pulling, but it was difficult
because another group of 4 players was grabbing all the
Turacos and Tanagers. Starlings were giving us crap loot
and no exp. And we refused to even try a Quezatol. We
had an escape plan in case we did pull a
Quezatol:<br><br>1) Mordiki informs me that doom and destruction is
incoming.<br>2) I levitate myself and wait for Mordiki.<br>3) I
levitate him while he's incoming.<br>4) We take off over
the ocean.<br><br><br>Well, eventually it happened.
Mordiki pulled a Tanager and a Quezatol spawned in front
of him as he was pulling the Tanager back. So we
perfectly executed our brilliant plan and took off. Then I
switched views to look behind us, and discovered that the
damn birds were right behind us and standing up, not
swimming. It's as if they were running along the water.
This was bad. Then I realized that I had no clue what
direction we were heading or where the hell we were. This
was really bad.<br><br>Mordiki tried casting Ensnare
on them and I tried Mezzing them, but you cannot
cast spells on a monster if it's in water and you're
not. We kept running. <br><br>Then Mordiki starts
getting attacked. I thought "How did they catch up to
us?". I look around, and even though I'm running, I'm
not moving forward. We hit the zone border, an
invisible wall in the ocean. This has officially become a
nightmare. So let me recap:<br><br>We have no idea where we
are in the ocean.<br>We cannot see land
anywhere.<br>We are being attacked by red birds.<br>We cannot
run.<br>I cannot cast spells on the birds.<br>Mordiki is at
2 bubbles of health.<br>It's raining. (couldn't see
crap)<br><br>I cast Rune on Mordiki to keep him alive, then the
birds decide I should die for that. I get pimp slapped
down to 2 bubbles. We break and run. We get separated.
I figure it cannot get any worse at this point.
Lord Nagafen could take a giant dump on me and it
wouldn't get any worse.<br><br>Then the Gods smile upon
me...I see land! (I'm agnostic, but whatever) I scream
for Mordiki to track me. He says he has me and is on
the way. I reach land and prepare myself for our last
ditch effort.<br><br>I see him running up with the
birds in close pursuit and I successfully Mez the
Quezatol! Then the Tanager that was with him comes running
over and starts bitching me. I quickly drop to one
bubble. I febbly try to cast a Mez between his hits. I
almost get one off but then he hits me..."You continue
your casting."..."A Tanager has been enthralled."
WOOHOO! I quickly sit down and memorize Memory blur. My
hope is that I can Blur them before they awake. I Blur
the Tanager first by accident. The recycle time on
Blur is WAY too long. The Tanager breaks mez and
begins to walk off. I get scared, because I mezzed the
Tanager SECOND and he's already broken free from it.
Memory Blur cycles. I cast. Quezatol break Mez and
rushed me. He is on top of me swinging. "A Quezatol
blinks a few times." He strolls
off....<br><br><br>Enchanters rule.
