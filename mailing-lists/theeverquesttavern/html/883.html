If you've never played the game before, Warrior
is a decent choice. Race doesn't really matter.
Warriors are fairly straight-forward to play. Once you get
the hang of the menus, combat, the chat channels,
etc. you may want to change classes. If you want to
experiment with a casting class you could try a Druid or
Necro. They both have a wide variety of spells. Playing
them up to level 5 should give you an idea of what you
like.<br><br>After playing these, ask yourself:<br><br>Do I want to
be offensive or defensive?<br>Do I plan on soloing
(playing alone) a lot or grouping mostly?<br>Do I prefer
to cast spells, melee, or some
combination?<br><br>Once you get used to the game mechanics and can answer
those questions, come back and ask us what class suits
you best...if you haven't already found one you like
:)<br><br>Hope this helps.<br><br><br>Nelmunbar
