Anywhere you go it is always good to have an
ideal formation of balanced characters, especially in
dungeons. I would say at least 2 characters need to be
hardcore tanks, but 3 or even 4 will work. Warriors,
paladins, rangers, and shadow nights are usually the best
at tanking. Then you need some magic users. In my
experience the two necessary magic users are clerics and
shamen. Shamen have spirit of the wolf, which makes your
group able to escape from most any battle if needed,
plus their buffs are great. Clerics should buff and
heal the tanks ad nauseum. Enchanters work well too -
mesmerize lets you pick on the mobs one at a time. Wizards
are pure offense and can smoke mobs like no one else.
Magicians are nice - food when you run out is handy. Necros
are pretty tight too. Bards are underrated and can do
the jobs of many at once. Try to always be in a group
of 6, and perhaps befriend another group and watch
each other's backs. Get some experienced pullers,
don't get in over your head, and communicate. As far as
in Cazic itself, I've never been, but any place
named after the God of Fear has to be horrific. Check
out Allakhazam's page,
