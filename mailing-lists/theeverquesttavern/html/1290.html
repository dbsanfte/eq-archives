The smugglers will attack you when you hit
Dyllin, if any smugs are near him, just use the failsafe
harmony on em. Note: there are some wandering smugs that
were red to me at 24, watch out for them, and if you
get in a spot you don't like, run up the hill to the
left of where Dyllin spawns, it exits directly in
front of some guards at high keep. Dyllin is not a
spellcaster and hits for less than 20 per hit, easily.
