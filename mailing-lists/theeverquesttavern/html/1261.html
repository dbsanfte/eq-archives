No real quests to get any armor yet. You can pick
up some patchwork armor pretty cheap, which is
decent at low levels. It's made by players using the
Tailoring Trade Skill.<br><br>Then you can move up to
banded, which is made with the Smithing Trade Skill by
players. Usually it's around 1pp per AC. You can probably
be in full banded by level 10 or so. <br><br>Once
you outgrow that, I estimate you could be in full
bronze by level 15. Bronze randomly drops off of level
30-ish mobs - a good place to try to get some is if
someone is killing the barbarians in West Karana. Bronze
should last you quite a while; once you've outgrown it,
you'll know what to do next.<br><br>Iskandar<br>53
