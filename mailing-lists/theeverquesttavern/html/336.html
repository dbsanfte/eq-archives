The Temple can be found off the zone Lavastorm.
Its entrance can be sound on roughly the opposite
side of the zone from the exit to Nektulos. Wander
over there and you will find a huge lava pit. There
are three caves carved in the inner walls of the pit.
The Temple one is the one at the edge of the zone,
good luck! <br><br>Trellan Hawksight, Wood Elf Ranger
