Creating aggro for me? NO problem:<br><br>1.
Snare. The beasties hate it.<br>2. Flame Lick. They hate
that just as bad. For some reason Flame Lick creates
more hate than just about any other dot until you get
into the 40's.<br>3. Taunt. Lots of it. All the
time.<br>4. Journeyman's Walking Stick. Procs Tashani, which
creates a HUGE amount of aggro. Really pisses 'em
off.<br>5. Don't stop hittin' 'em!!<br>6. Taking damage. As
a ranger, I'm going to get hit alot. Not as much as
most, because my AC is pretty damned high, but still
more than a warrior, pally, or SK of my level. Taking
damage from a creature creates hate. The lower you are
on life, the more likely they're going to continue
whaling on you until you're dead.<br>7. DS. I always
throw a damage shield up on myself before a tough
fight. My barbcoat + thistleshield does 8 damage each
time they hit me. Doesn't sound like much, but it adds
up. The mob hits me 20 times in a fight? That's 160
points of damage.<br><br>That's about it. Rangers are
VERY good at creating and keeping aggro. You just have
to time your casting and taunts right, and you have
to have a group that understands how to manage their
own aggro.
