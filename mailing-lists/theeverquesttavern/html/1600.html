Hoo boy.<br><br>I don't think I've seen quite
this much false info flung around in quite a while.
I'll see what I can do to repair it.<br><br>1. You do
not begin to lose XP until 6th level. As it is, you
can't drop below 6th level. That would mean XP loss in
the 5th level, which is impossible.<br><br>2. Skills
are only "lost" if you drop more than two levels. For
instance, someone "delevels" (attacks a high level mob over
and over, dying over and over, and losing XP over and
over until they drop in level) from 54 to 26. Their
skills drop until they using them at the max of 28th
level. However, when that person regains 27, their skill
automatically jumps to the max of 29. This will continue until
they've regained the level they originally
lost.<br><br>3. Healing, buffing, or otherwise casting beneficial
spells on ANY mobs does NOT work. Healing a wolf while
some newbie is fighting it might scare them off, but
in reality it isn't doing anything at all. This was
put into place to prevent both XP kills and to keep
high level characters from buffing out Orc Pawns and
watching them murder newbies.<br><br>4. There are RUMORS
of hidden faction when killing bears or wolves, but
they are just that. Rumors. I've killed hundreds of
bears AND wolves, as a ranger, and my faction with
Jaggedpine Treefolk and such hasn't changed a
bit.<br><br>Hope this clears a few things up for you
all.<br><br>Tyral Stormfang<br>Captain of the Knights of
