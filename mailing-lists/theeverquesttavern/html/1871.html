I've never really liked twinks, but never though
to ask "why?" After thinking about it, they don't
make me as mad as before. Why twink? If it's to get
through the "low" levels and get to the high levels
quicker, then go for it. I won't, because I like to enjoy
each level. And if you twink and go, I probably won't
have to experience you for very long anyway. If it's
to run around Crushbone and shout, lvl 8 druid with
200WIS LFG, then just stay home. <br><br>To me, MAJOR
twinking is like cheating. It's like cheat codes in some
games, or reading the back of the book. Getting
something that someone else doesn't have to make you better
than the rest. If you don't have the patience or
ability to do it yourself, the regular way, then I don't
have time for you. <br><br>BUT-- here are a few things
I don't mind: If you twink to get to lvl 20 because
all your friends are higher level and you want to
group with them--what's the harm? You hurt yourself
because you don't enjoy the lower levels and you probably
don't have enough experience to be a great player if
you rushed through the levels. Also, minor twinks are
just part of EQ society-- get a piece of
armor/weapon/item for being a good person, no harm in that. At lvl
10, I was given a velium mace from a guy who had gone
to Velius for the first time-- not a great weapon,
though better than what i had. It was cool. I was still
in mostly leather armore (with some tattered still,
i think). <br><br>As an aside-- my guild does not
Twink our members. We will offer high end items at
discound rates, but will not equip lower level members
with all the best stuff-- and our members like it that
way. I remember getting an item that cost about 800pp
from a guildmate-- i had only about 500pp in the bank
total, and i handed over every last pp in the bank for
the item. Took me awhile to recover from that, but I
had the item and had paid a price for it. Twinking?
maybe-- harmful? I don't think so. I could have farmed
Mammoth tusks for a week to get the actualy money, but
instead I was out helping guildmembers and others, and
leaving the mammys for others (who can get good xp for
them) to hunt.<br><br>/rambling off<br><br>Xela
