Subject: Leaving EQ


Just letting you all know that you probably won't
see me because I have decided to leave EQ. I think
the game is fantastic and it is probably my favorite
game of all time. Meeting all sorts of people and
adventuring together is fantastic, but I just feel the game
takes up far too much time. You can't really get on for
15 minutes for a short session, you have to play for
hours at a time to succeed. Also, if you want to take a
break from the game, you fall far behind your friends
and can't hunt with them anymore. So, I've decided to
move on and do some other things in my life ;) Don't
get me wrong, I think the game is great and I wish
everyone in Norrath a fond farewell and good
luck!<br><br>Trellan Hawksight, retired ranger of Tunare.
