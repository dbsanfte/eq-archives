Subject: Great quest for clerics, druids, shamen!


Okay, I'm sure some of you out there know of, or
have done this quest.. but I will post it for those
out there who don't. I haven't contributed much
thusfar, so I figured I might as well
start.<br><br>Testament of Vanear<br>Secondary Slot Item<br>+10
WIS<br>+10 Mana<br><br>Go to Moodoro Findharn in Qeynos
(he's in Crow's Tavern in North Qeynos)<br>Give him 2
gold. He should give you an empty testament of vanear.
This is good.<br>Give him 4 gold, he'll give you a
card (either a queen, king, jester, or something
else). If it is a jester, give it to him and receive a
page. If it is one of the other cards, destroy it and
give him another 4 gold until you get the jester. This
is half of the pages, place it in the testament and
hike it to highhold pass.<br>When you arrive in
highhold pass, find the tavern that is surrounded by
water, I forget the name.. sorry. Hop in the water,
there is a hole in the wall under a bridge, swim
through it. You should arrive in an area surrounded by
smugglers. They were apprehensive to my druid (at the time
lvl 24) and some conned red and some green, so be
safe and don't attack any of them. Travel farther into
the smugglers area and you'll come upon an area with
a pottery wheel and a kiln or oven. This is where
Dyllin Starshine (aka your target) spawns. He is
approximately an 8 hour spawn, so you either have to be lucky
or patient to get him. He conned green to me at lvl
24 and was easily dropped with about 200 points of
damage, perhaps less. Loot his body for a page and an
envelope. The page goes in the testament with the other
page, hit combine on the book and voila! The envelope
is part of another quest, but I am not sure what it
is.<br>I was up waiting on Dyllin until about 4 AM, but I
didn't start until midnight. So.. a night missed in exp
got me 10 wis.. very useful.<br>I highly advise this
quest for any cleric shaman or
druid.<br><br>Enjoy<br><br>Nukai Oakenwulf<br>Druid of Spiritus
Valorum<br>Tunare's Chosen of Karana
