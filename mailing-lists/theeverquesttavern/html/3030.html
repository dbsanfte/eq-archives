Unless you really have your heart set on playing a Vah Shir (the Cat
characters), then I would buy as little as possible, probably just the original
and the Kunark expansion. That will end up giving you enough zones to try out
and almost all of the races to try out.

There is no use putting down $50-60 (especially since half of what you would
end up buying you can't use for, at least, a few months anyway) on a game just
to find out that you don't really like it. Buy the original and play for a
little while. See if you like it.

The only thing that Planes of Power will give you starting out is quicker
travel (you have to be lvl 46 or above to actually move beyond the first zone
there). You couldn't do anything with Scars of Velious at all since it was
created as a higher levle expansion (lvl 30 and above).
