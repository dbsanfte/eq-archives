At level 8-- if you're still in/near Gfay, here's
a good way to make some money on established
servers (ie. NOT FV server).<br><br>Kill BATS-- sell
stacks of bat wings to druids, chanters, etc who can
cast Lev spell. Higher level chars will pay 5pp for a
stack of batwings (that's what I pay, at
least).<br><br>Kill Skellys-- sell stacks of bonechips to necros and
SKs. Not sure how much they sell for, but I'd assume
it's close to the bat wing price. <br><br>Hunt Skellys
in any Faydwer zone. Even if the skellys don't give
xp anymore (ie. decayed), kill any that you see
holding a staff or shield. Cracked staffs sell to
merchants for 1pp, which is good for your level-- i think
shields sell for about the same too. Dwarf skellys in BB
are higher level and may still give xp. Regular
skellys may be too high for you just yet. <br><br>HUNT
ORCS!! Go fight orcs on orc hill, near the CB zone, or
in CB. The drop lots of stuff you can sell, so make
sure you have extra bags. Auction off all CB belts and
pads. Belts can be sold for 1 or 2pp each, pads for
2-4pp each, depending on server, time of day and
market. I had a twinked guy buy 5 pads from me for 5pp
each. I lvled my chanter up in CB from lvl 9 to 12 and
made lots of money off the mobs there-- not many
chanters there, so explain to your groups how they can
best use you. (Also, I actually turned in the belts
and pads for the quest, and still made some money by
selling the misc. items you get for the quest).
<br><br>Once you hit your low teens, the best bet is to do one
of the following:<br><br>NRo/SRo-- Derv camps--
dervs give ok xp and often carry rings, gems, misc.
items and BRONZE WEAPONS, which sell for plats.
<br><br>Befallen-- It keeps changing back and forth, but if the DEs
are there-- it's good loot. <br><br>WC-- derv camp
and orc camp there, but the key here is to hunt
wisps, bears and spiders. Stack of spider silk sell for
a lot in Ecomm. Killing bears could result in HQ
bear pelts, which sell for 20pp easily on XEV (or you
could use them to make Backpacks to sell for 70pp or
more). Wisps sometimes drop GLS, which you can auction
for about 6-7pp, OR turn in for a quest to the
gypsies in SRO (if you are evil--or have DE illusion), or
in NK (if good). You get xp for turning in each GLS,
and you get a concordance of research, which you can
sell back for 9pp!! As a chanter, the best bet is to
kill wisps all day long, fill up bags of GLS and LS,
head to SRO, use illusion DE (when you get it) and
make tons of cash. <br><br>OH-- people say that OOT
(ocean of tears) is GREAT xp and loot, but i haven't
hunted there much except gargoyles and cyclopses at
higher levels. <br><br>Xela/Mandir
