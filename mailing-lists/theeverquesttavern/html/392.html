Subject: Shadowknight Races


There are 4 choices for race when you want to be
a Shadowknight.<br><br>Human-All around decent
stats, but nothing spectacular. Humans have no night
vision so you will need a lightsource (At 22 they gain a
spell tht gives nightvision. Humans are not the most
imposing, but they do have more choices on where to travel
because they are not as hated as the "evil" races (Troll,
ogre, Dark Elf) <br><br>Dark Elf-A classic race choice
for a shadowknight is an evil race, such as the Dark
Elves. Dark Elves are weak, so the heavy armor of a
shadowknight might bog them down, but quick and good at
hitting and not being hit. Also, they have a very high
intelligence if you want your shadowknight to be especially
proficient in spellcasting. Dark Elves have ultravision, the
best vision in the game, but are going to be hated by
EVERYONE except ogres, trolls, and ofcourse Dark
Elves.<br><br>Troll-Good at smashing things, basically. Very low
intelligence, but extremely powerful in combat. They have
plenty of strength to carry the heaviest equipment and
stamina to stick it through a fight. They get good night
vision, a stunning attack, and most importantly,
regeneration. Regeneration allows a troll to regain hitpoints
TWICE as fast as other races. However, trolls will be
hated by everyone so don't plan on visiting many cities
any time soon. Basically, trolls are strong, tough,
and horrendously stupid.<br><br>Ogres-Pretty much the
same as a troll. Some minor differences are they get
better night vision, better all around stats, but not
regeneration. They are hated like trolls, tough like trolls,
strong like trolls, and only the slightest bit smarter
than trolls.<br><br>So here are your choices.
Basically, what it all comes down to is choose the type of
character you want to roleplay. Do you want and mysterious
and intelligent shadowknight who is quick and agile,
bringing terror on their foes? Dark Elf. Or, do you want a
hulking brute with no knowledge of subtlety and
preffering straight combat over their still useful spells?
Troll or Ogre. Just choose what you think would be mroe
fun, and then don't worry about stats. All characters
even out stat-wise in one way or another, so don't
worry about it =)
