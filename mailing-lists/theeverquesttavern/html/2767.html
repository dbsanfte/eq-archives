Ah. If you're in Qeynos now, you might think about going to the
Warrens, on the Isle of Odus. Take a boat to Erud (Erudite city), and
head south. You'll have to go through the Evil city of Paineel's
training yard to do so . . . that's not usually a problem, however.
Zone into Paineel, go forward until you see the HUGE FREAKIN' HOLE
(don't jump or fall into it, you'll probably lose your stuff), and
turn left. When you see daylight ahead of you, stop, align yourself
with one of the trees out in the yard, and motor for it. Once you're
well into the center of the yard, you're OK. Then turn right and look
for the tunnel to the Warrens and the Hole.
