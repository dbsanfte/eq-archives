Keep the AC 6 bracers and lose the Serpentine. The bonuses are trivial
to your proc rate (unless they are tremendous bonuses) and it's not
worth losing the AC over.

Agility measures how fast you can increase your defensive skills, and
adds a little AC. Being a Ranger you should have a pretty high AGI
score anyway. Again, the bonuses aren't worth the loss of AC.

The only exception would be if you need to get a stat over a hump,
e.g. getting an attribute to over 75 or 100 or 200. Even then, it's a
hard argument to lose AC.
