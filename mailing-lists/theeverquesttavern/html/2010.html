There are some robes you can get at lower levels
but they don't have very good stats (usually don't
have any INT). There is a quest in Felwithe to get a
GOSSAMER robe. It's a white robe that is quite pretty
(especially on high elves ^_^). You need to get the BRASS
EARRING from the TASKMASTER in CRUSHBONE. When you get it
you give it to the FEMALE HIGH ELF SLAVE (in CB) and
she gives you a note. You then take the note to NIOLA
in the MAGICIAN's Guild in FELWITHE. Niola gives you
the robe then. It has no stats but it is AC 8 which
is pretty good for a newbie. ^_^<br><br>As for
jewelery making I hope you have a way to make a lot of
money. It's very expensive!! It's the most expensive
trade skill to learn. At first you won't be able to
make anything that spectactular but eventually you
will make a lot of money back selling the jewelery you
make to players that need the stats. I assume you are
an Enchanter? It's easier if you are because you can
enchant your own metals. There are lots of good trade
skill guides online. You should try Allakhazam,
EverLore or EQ Statics. Personally I'm a master Baker. LOL
So I can't help much with jewelery. ^_^ Good luck to
you though. Master Jewelers are always wanted!!
