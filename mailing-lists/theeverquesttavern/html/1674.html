At higher levels, everything seems
crippled.<br>(Most of my comments are from reading and from friends.
So, forgivenesses are in order on some
points).<br><br>Rogues do a lot of damage but can't tank. Monks are
similar, although Rogues have more problems b/c they need
backstab. Rangers tank better but still-- I'd group them
just slightly better than Rogues and Monks in tanking
and they do a lot less damage. However, their spells
are pretty cool in different situations. (SoW,
snares, etc).<br><br>Warriors tank well but end up doing
like only what-- 10% or less of total damage at higher
levels? Wizzies do a ton of damage, but can't find a
group worth their salt and can't solo very well even
with Root and Snare. (A properly timed wizzie deals I
guess 25% of the damage and can waste their mana if
need be to pull ppl's behind out of the fire in a
pinch).<br><br>However, the more I see Wizzies in play-- the more I
realize that they are the hardest class to play.
Enchanters are also very hard to play but have soooo much
utility-- they're never without groups. Which makes things
easier. Plus they have very easy to define roles for
timing. Mezz, Haste, Clarity, etc. Wizzies have to learn
timing and that's HARD. Very hard. Knowing when to hit
that mob for 250 points of damage can be tricky.
(sorry to be so wordy.)<br><br>Necro's are GREAT soloers
but dang... in a group-- sometimes they aren't more
than mana batteries. They're okay still around 30 but
I've been told around 40... they and Shaman start to
slow down. Shaman at least still have lotsa stuff
groups can use. SoW, Debuffs, Buffs, etc.<br><br>Druids
are good all around but can a lot of times feel
useless. We don't do anything super-well and lotsa ppl do
things better than us. I can usually find something to
do in a group though. And if need be, I can solo-- I
just hate doing it and don't do it well b/c I never
practice it.<br><br>What class am I missing? Covered
Shaman, Rogue, Warrior, Monk, Ranger, Druid, Wizzies,
Enchants, and Necros. Ahh.. Magicians. Great pets-- but
each has their flaws. Root is both a blessing in a
curse. Great when overwhelmed... pitiful when one at a
time and snaring. Etc etc. Bards-- great in groups--
decent solo. Bards are good all around characters but
twisting can get tiring.<br><br>Over all, I'd rate class
balancing pretty good except for Enchanters and Bards. But
I love those two groups too much to complain. Grin.
Those who've grouped with them -- know. =)<br><br>A
combination of any classes makes for a fun group-- as long as
it's a full group. I've had a group of Wizzie, 2
Druid, 2 Shaman, and an Enchanter and we killed faster
than any group I've ever been in. (Group
bouncing/kiting)<br><br>Play whatever you're comfort with. Some ppl keep
telling me about the weaknesses of their characters at
higher levels. Least complaints come from Bards and
Enchanters. But man... almost any combo of melee and casters
can work. (under normal non-uber mob situations-- ei
Dragon Raids, etc).<br><br>I love groups-- full
groups... 20% bonus to exp and more if you're grouped with
hybrids b/c of the way they add to exp when there are
hybrid classes. My advice of course is "Group group
group and just enjoy your role and make friends." If
you know your role well... you should do well.
Friends will look for you to do things.<br><br>shooman
