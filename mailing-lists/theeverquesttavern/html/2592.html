For the most part, Plat guides and PL guides are not worth it unless
you are really new to the game and can't find web sites (most of
which, like Allakhazam's has all this info, you just have to find
it).

A friend of mine got one, thinking it would give him all these
secrets, but it didn't. In fact, some of the info. was outdated and
just plain wrong. Best bet is to check out EQAtlas or Allakhazam's--
check out the zones you're in or near, look up the quests and check
out the comments made about them-- sometimes you might have to post a
request for info-- ie, Is this a good xp quest? For what levels?.
Other than that, come to places like this, and just test out quests
in the game. It doesn't take much time to "hail" NPCs in cities.

Now the good stuff-- DF quests (both of them) are great quests, and
they give different faction depending on what type you want. I hear
tht the one in NFP (pally guild?) is better xp, but the one in the
with the Captain of the guard is really good, with decent coin for
under lvl 15. This is a similar quest to the Crushbone belt/pad
quest that comes from Kaladim. Great quest all the way to about lvl
14 as far as loot, faction and xp. And later you can /auction belts
and pads for some good plat.

Best bet for xp these days is to hit any dungeon with an xp bonus
(see Eqatlas, but most dungeons now have some kind of bonus). If you
are a solo class, go for it, but note that dungeons are indoors, so
druids are limited. Either bring some friends or look for a pickup
group.

My rogue is now nearly lvl 30 after a month of off and on playing.
Started in BB mountains, went to CB and did the belt/pad quests to
early teens. Went to EC/WC to do orc and derv camps. Went to Oasis to
do orcs to high teens. Then went to High Pass to do orcs to 20. Then
to LOIO to do Sarnaks to 24, then to SK/Paw (xp bonus) to 28, then to
SOLA (xp bonus) to 30, and now in OT and Dalnir (xp bonus). I'm not a
solo class, so I looked for good grouping places (LOIO and Oasis) to
get my skills up.

BTW-- DERV RING QUEST-- in WC you will have to turn in 4 orc pawn
picks to one of the toll booth guards- make sure to have good faction
with him (ie turn in DF belts to his captain in WFP). He will then
give you the ring quest-- He gives a backpack to fill with 8 rings
and combine. Give it back to him and he gives you a coin. Take it to
the FP guard house and find a smith there (can't remember his name),
turn it in and get a Freeport Militia Tunic (9ac and +3dex i think).
You'll get tons of FP faction for this and some xp too. The tunic is
not great, but I was running TONS of people through this quest on FV
when it first opened up-- a GREAT BP on that server.
