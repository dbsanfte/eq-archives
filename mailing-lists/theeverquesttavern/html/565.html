I know a couple of these:<br><br>SoW stands for
Spirit of the Wolf, a shaman spell that enables you to
run like a jackrabbit. Chances are you've had this
before - an icon of a boot surrounded by blue stuff
appears in a corner of your screen. It's a really good
spell, lets you run away from monsters and aids in
corpse retrieval.<br><br>When someone "zones", the
person moves to a new zone. A zone is a region of the EQ
map. You know you enter one when you get that
"LOADING...PLEASE WAIT" message in your chat box and you didn't
fight anything. An example of two zones is North Qeynos
and South Qeynos. Zoning is pretty effective strategy
- if you're in a losing battle, go to the nearest
zone so you can run away - if you make it, the monster
will leave you alone.<br><br>As for PoD and lego pad,
I have no idea - chances are someone else does.
