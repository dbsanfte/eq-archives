Ahh...I know what you can do for great
Experience. First off go hunt in BlackBurrow and kill gnolls
on the top level. Make sure to collect all the gnoll
fangs you can find. They are No drop, but bring them
back to the warrior guild in Qeynos give them to the
Guard captain guy on the little ledge overlooking the
arena in there. He will give you great exp for those
levels, very good faction adjustments, and moonstones for
every fang you give him. The exp is really great
though, i was able to gain half a level just with about 6
gnoll fangs at level 3. So at Level 4 you should still
get great exp. In the end you can sell the moonstones
to the merchant for 1.8 silver a piece, which is
pretty good cash.
