You could go with honor loot - people loot
whenever they think it is their turn. The only problem is
that casters spend less time meditating and more time
looting, plus nicer loots are luck-of-the-draw.<br><br>I
prefer master loot for most things. One person loots all
of the corpses and does a /split 0 0 0 0 (splits all
of the platinum, gold, silver, and copper pieces
looted from the mobs killed by the group) whenever
someone leaves.<br><br>For the nicer items, alpha loot
works pretty well. The names of the groupmates are
alphabetized. The group leader assigns a number to each person
and uses /random 1 x - x being the number of group
mates.<br><br>I usually do my best to give preference to the
classes who can and will use a certain
item.<br><br>Example - Gaelle, Shenjairo, Hittue, Autumn, Tristanie,
and myself are grouped up. A Sionachie's Partisan
drops. It's bard only. Since there are no bards in the
group, it's randomed among everyone.<br><br>Autumn 1,
Gaelle 2, Hittue 3, Iskandar 4, Shenjairo 5, Tristanie
6.<br><br>/random 1 6<br><br>**A magic die has been rolled by
Iskandar.<br>**It could be any number between 1 and 6, but this
time it was a 3.<br><br>Hittue then gets the
Sionachie's Partisan.<br><br>Iskandar<br>51 champion of
Prexus
