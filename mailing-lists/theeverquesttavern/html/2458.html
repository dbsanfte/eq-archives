Hey there-- first off, you must post your
server!!<br><br>OK-- lots to do at lvl 20. Unrest is certainly a good
option, especially for pallys and clerics, though
warriors, chanters and druids/shammys (for sow mostly) are
welcome. <br><br>Wizzie is one of the only classes I have
not played AT all (i think wizzie, monk and SK are
only ones, and only a little as warrior and bard), so
I can't help you as far as solo spots.<br><br>As
for Groups--- If your server has an active population
in Stonebrunt, that is a good place (usually a low
pop area, especially now with Luclin). Is anyone sure
on the population of Luclin zones?? Some are getting
more and more popular, and a wizzie may be a great
addition, expecially since there are no pure caster classes
in the Vah Shir kingdom. <br><br>OK-- here's what
you want. Get on the boat and head to Kunark-- LOIO
is the ultimate place to hunt from 20-26 and even
beyond. It is also /ooc /shout spam zone, so be prepared.
I was there just last night to get my rogue from
lvl 19 to lvl 20-- which i did easily-- on Xev. There
were about 80 people in the zone and groups galore.
Sometimes the standard "20 rogue LFG" doesn't work, and you
need to head to the action places -- Mills, Sarny
Fort, Gobbie Mines, etc. and see if there's room for
you. The action is fast, and unless a group is paying
close attention, they will not see your shouts. Plus,
some of us turn off the /ooc and /shout so that the
spam doesn't keep us from seeing the important battle
spam. <br><br>Other places for pick up groups-- HIgh
Pass is still viable for you at lvl 20, find an orc
group near the Kith zone. Later you can go fight in
gobbie groups in High Keep. Oasis is still an option--
lots of groups there fighting orcs and the different
variety of crocs/caimen. You may be able to find groups
in South Karana or in Lake Rathe (the gnoll/undead
camp at the SK line). Aviak groups are somewhat common
in SK, and then you can graduate to Gnolls at the
spires. <br><br>With my druid, I joined spires groups
early and all the gnolls were red to me. This is ok for
some classes-- i was still valuable as a buffer,
healer, part time mellee, damage shielding, and some
nuking. Wizzies have it tough though, as the reds will
resist your nukes, and make you basically ineffective. I
found this was true with my mage, but at least I had a
pet that could attack and I could case a good damage
shield. Chanters can Tash and still get spells off---
<br><br>There are lots of other places, but depending on your
server, they may not be well populated. If you're looking
for easy teaming, you can get from 20-35 in Kunark in
the Lake of ill omen, Frontier Mountains and The
Overthere. Problem-- you have to take the stupid boat and
will have to leave eventually because the FV dock area
does not have all the spells and items you'll need to
keep going from 20-35. <br><br>Plus, you'll want to go
to other places-- dungeons and such-- for the fun of
it. Best fun I had is going to a popular zone and
send shouts to people that you want to go exploring
another, less popular zone--- you'll be surprised at the
resonse. That's how I got a group together in EC the first
time I went to Najena. <br><br>Ok--- NOW i'm done...
