Subject: FV to LOIO and the DL run to FV


Alright... here's the low down.<br><br>I spent 20
to 30 as my druid in LOIO and I've made the trip
from FV to LOIO a couple hundred times.<br><br>And
since I got the gate to DL I've made the run to FV
about 30 times and to the area outside KC about 50
times.<br><br>FV to LOIO is easy. If you've got sow and
invis/camo-- all you have to do is run to water fall and
follow river to zone. Ignore everything. Nothing's
faster than sow there. You'll make it to zone easy. Most
things can't see invis there except for two things. An
occasional Drogvar (sp?). Stop... go around it. Those are
mean and might stun you. You're faster than it with
sow.. but why take the chance?<br><br>Spiders near as I
can figure it... can only see you if the Caster
spider happens to cast See invis on itself or a friend.
Doesn't happen often but does happen.<br><br>Now, the run
from DL to FV is tricky. There's a couple of ways to
do it but the easiest is SoW and Invis/Camo. Don't
Camo yourself till you get to the tunnel from the
gladed area to the ice area. Before you enter the
tunnel... cast camo. By the time you reach the other side--
it'll either have dropped or it won't and you should be
able to make it all the way to FV without it
dying.<br><br>A lot of people prefer to use levitate here but I
never bother with it. I just sow and camo and run for
loc 300, -5500. That's the tunnel into FV. When you
go on the mountain... have a gate ready in case you
mess up.. nothing's faster than you with sow... run
and count out 1, one thousand, 2, one thousand...
etc. All the way to 10, one thousand. Stop, spin right
(to do the fast cast technique-- allows the server to
know you've stopped a second earlier than it might if
you didn't spin), and then cast gate.<br><br>But as
long as you avoid Drovars, Giants, Spiders-- you'll be
okay. They're far enough between and easy enough to see
that I've never had to gate out but once in about 50
tries. Once Camo dropped too soon and I ran from KC to
FV zone with 4 mobs on my tail BUT... like I said--
nothing in DL can catch you.<br><br>I've had Paly and SK
friends do this run from DL to FV with just sow and
levitate.<br><br>shooman out<br><br>PS.. my comments in no way regard
Gore. If you're foolish enough to try wolf form through
DL OR you attack Gore, my advice will not help you.
