Okay, if you're a DEF, you should be close to EC
(Nec forest, one zone away). Go there and kill orcs in
a group or zone again in NRO and kill the
tarantulas. You and one or two others should be able to
handle those just fine. Soon as you hit about 12 or 13,
I recommend Cuthroats in NRO at camps 2 or
3.<br><br>Heck, as a necro and using a fear, kite, skel tank, dot
combo... you probably could solo-- But I don't recommend
it.<br><br>Why? B/c at higher levels, grouping is the way to go.
EQ is a group oriented game. Groups help you get
over humps and make things more fun. (provided you are
a good grouper and that takes practice and other
players to teach).<br><br>I play druid and love to group.
I've been worried b/c at higher levels (40 and above)
it gets to the point that a good group really only
needs 2 melee classes, 2 clerics, a bard and an
enchanter-- with either the bard or enchanter being
switchable with some other class-- probably nuker as long as
there's some mana regenerator in the group.
<br><br>Versatility has it's price and have a friend lvl 46 shaman
who verified this... oh well.<br><br>That's why I
recommend you learn to group early. I've developed a decent
reputation as a fun and good grouper and that should help me
later on (assuming I ever have the time to play EQ
