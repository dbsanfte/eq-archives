Hmm...what do you mean by warrior-mage? One who
is proficient in both melee battle and spellcasting?
All the classes in Everquest are quite balanced to
some extent, and the closest things to warrior-mages
are hybrid classes (i.e. Paladin, Ranger,
Shadowknight). Paladins are a combo of a warrior and a cleric;
ranger is warrior/druid, and SK is warrior/necromancer.
Keep in mind that hybrids are at least 75% warrior.
Clerics and druids are considered to be "priest" types,
and the necro is a mage type. If you plan on
powerleveling your character through the early levels, the
shadowknight will be the mosty difficult, and the ranger will
be the easiest. Go Rangers!! woohoo =)<br><br> -
