Cazic-Thule is a great spot. The easiest way is
through South Ro. Find the southwest wall of South Ro -
there will be a zone line here. Zone into Feerrott.
Follow the right wall closely. Stay only on the right
wall and keep an eye out for a break in the wall. This
will be the zone in to Innothule. Come forward and
follow the left wall. You'll come to a river - swim
across. Once on the other side, you should see some
stairs. Invis yourself if you can. Up the stairs is the
entrance to Cazic-Thule.<br><br>Also good at that level is
Frontier Mountains in Kunark. A bit hard to describe
directions - perhaps you could have a friend show you the
way.<br><br>Iskandar<br>51 champion of Prexus
