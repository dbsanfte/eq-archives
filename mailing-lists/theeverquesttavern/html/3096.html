Police Search For Daughter Of Teen Found Dead

KENNEDALE, Texas, 12:29 p.m. CDT August 23, 2003 - Kennedale police held a
news conference Saturday at 10 a.m. to reveal more about the death of a
16-year-old girl.

18-month-old, Crystal Alisha.


Police turned their focus to the victim's daughter. They believe the
18-month-old girl was with her mother when her mother was last seen alive.
"Even the family members that we contacted don't have any idea where she
might be," said Kennedale Police Chief Jim Rutledge. "She was last seen in
Dallas with her mother in the early part of August. Investigators have been
working in that area of Dallas trying to locate the little girl, and now we
re asking the public for assistance."
Police found 16-year-old Christina Robletto of Dallas in the trunk of a
burgundy Mercury Grand Marquis on Tuesday. Now, they are trying to locate
the victim's 18-month-old daughter, Crystal Alisha.
The Police Department held the news conference to release a picture and
information about Crystal.
Rutledge said no family members are suspects at this time.
