When you hit level 20 put one training point into
each Specialization. Whenever one of your
specializations goes over 50, it becomes your Primary
Specialization. After that, the other ones are capped at 49. The
Primary one can continue to raise up to 200 I
believe.<br><br>So put one point into each. Decide which skill is
the most important to you. And make sure that one
hits 50 before the others. If you want, you could
memorize a level 1 spell of that skill and just chain cast
it for like an hour to max it out.<br><br>Check here
