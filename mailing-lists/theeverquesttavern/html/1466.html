Subject: An Everquest based Interactive Story!!!


Hey everyone! Awhile back, when I was hopelessly
addicted to Evercra--err Everquest, I began writing a
story based on one of my characters. Considering how
far I usually get with a story,or rather, how quickly
I stop writing before I get far at all, the story
took off and the ideas flowed! I had the whole story
planned out, but then I hit a writers block... how do I
keep writing when I don't know what to say? Well,
that's usually the way of it, I guess. With that, the
project died and I kept the story in my archives... until
recently. A few days ago I came across a website,
www.stories.com. The site is rather innovative and I quickly saw
potential in it for myself. So, yesterday (or was it the
day before?) I went ahead and posted 2 of my stories,
including the one linked
below.<br><br><a href=http://www.stories.com/main/interact.php?action=view&item_id=148315 target=new>http://www.stories.com/main/interact.php?action=view&item_id=148315</a><br><br>Unfortunately, my writers block on both stories is still in
place and I need some help.. Therefore I've set up the
stories to be interactive.. Basicly an Interactive Story
is one where, at the end of each chapter, the reader
is given several options. As the story progresses,
the reader will eventually reach a choice that hasn't
been written yet. At this point, the reader is given
the opportunity to add to the story, further
developing the plot. I've done this to see what people come
up with. New ideas might help break down my writers
block.. if it doesn't, then at least it'll be fun to
watch a story develop at the hands of whoever reads it!
Anyway, I'll also post the first small section of the
story in just a moment. If you like what you read,
please go visit the Interactive Story version at the
link above and read the rest. Then, PLEASE rate it..
feedback is important and without a rating, I'll never
know what people think. Also, if your interested, feel
free to contribute to the story! I won't delete
anything at all that's written by someone who takes the
story seriously and has played enough EQ to know what
they're talkin about.<br><br>Darkmyst - A once great Dark
Elf himself
