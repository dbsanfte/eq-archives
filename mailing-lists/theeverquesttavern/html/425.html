Glad to have you, and glad you will be able to
play EQ =) Regarding your question on the best
character to be, it is entirely up to you. There are more
than 60 race/class combinations that can all be very
interesting, useful, and fulfilling. In choosing your
character, consider things like whether you want a fighter,
a healer, or a caster, or a combination of these.
Also, choose whether you wish to be a good race or an
evil race. After deciding these, there are still many
other choices to choose from. Probably the best thing
to do if you cannot choose a single character you
like best is to try a few of them and then decide what
your favorite is(You have 8 character slots per
SERVER, meaning you can make more than a hundred
characters total over various servers ;) ). Keep in mind,
most classes are very similar at earlier levels, you
probably won't recognize the advantages of them for a few
levels. <br> <br>One other thing I wish to mention, don't
worry about the statistics and numbers, choose the race
you like the best despite the fact the stats may seem
all wrong for the class. Gnomes have the worst stats
for a cleric than any other race, but I still love
playing a gnome cleric and I'm just as useful to a group
as a dwarf cleric =)<br><br>Have fun choosing your
character, it will effect the rest of your characters life
so make sure you enjoy it!
