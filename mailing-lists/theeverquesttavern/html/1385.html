Suggestions<br><br>1) Stay under 15 pounds of
weight. The instant you hit 15 pounds.. get rid of
something or go sell. You lose your monk bonus AC. To give
ya an idea... some level 11 monk friend of mine
dropped 200+ AC points when he hit 15 lbs. (Something
like that)<br><br>2) Practice mend and Feign death
CONSTANTLY. You want to mend whenever it's available -- cause
before you master it.. it can cause you damage with crit
failures.<br><br>Feign death is important b/c it helps you with pulls...
monks often are given the role of puller and since they
do lotsa damage.. sometimes they need to feign death
just to get the mob back to the tank.<br><br>Also,
you'll want to practice Safe Fall. It's just too useful
in a variety of things. Running ragged from a pull
(SK aviaks come in mind-- monks with good safe fall
and feign rock as pullers).<br><br>3) RP comes from
whatever you want to bring together as your monk. I play
very impish silly sorts with a sense of inner being.
That's why I always do something Elvish.<br><br>shooman
