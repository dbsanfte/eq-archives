Subject: Notes on Powerleveing others.


Generally speaking, whomever does the most DAMAGE
to the mob will get the exp for it. So if you want
to powerlevel your lower level friend, feel free to
ultra buff him/her and debuff the mob, it will not be
counted as damage towards the mob. (for exp purposes
anyhow) Great spells for this are Quickness, Drowsy, high
level Skin/Talisman/Daring series. Stuns help
too.<br><br>For example, if you are a high level enchanter
helping out some random newbie to powerlevel, you could
do this:<br><br>1) Quickness/Cloud/Beserker Strength
the newbie<br>2) Tashani/Languid
Pace/Disempower/Whirl/Flux the mob<br>3) after the newbie does over half
damage, blow the mob away using DD.<br><br>And the newbie
will get all the exp.<br><br>Try to avoid using DOTs.
They are not effective to cast at the end of a fight
obviously. And if you cast them at the beginning it is hard
to judge how much damage the mob has taken from the
newbie, and how much was from the DOT.<br><br>Heals are
another good way to help :)<br><br>Hope this
helps.<br><br><br>Nelmunbar<br>Enchanter on Brell
