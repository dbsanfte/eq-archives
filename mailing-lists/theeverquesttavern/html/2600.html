There's such a huge difference between playing a game like FF and a
game like EQ. There area differenct reasonas to get "addicted," that

hooked-- it's not about "beating" the game, because that really can't
be done. It's about that next level, those next skills or spells, the
next item, the next 10 or 100 or 1k or 10k plat.

People have done crazier things than 7 hours of EQ play-- especially
when it comes to camping and raids. There are 12-24 hours of straight
play easily. I'm sure you'll find people to ditch work, school,

of time are all around. Check with the club/group EverquestAnnonymous
(if it's still around). There used to be some posts there all the
time about what addicted people did for EQ.
