Some GMs will tell you it is not possible to move
a character from one account to another. They
generally as a rule do not do it. However, it can be done
and I think it is judged on a case by case basis.
When my husband and I got our second computer we got 2
accounts and moved one of our characters over to the other
account. As far as trying to sell characters...that could
be why they do not do this often. Both our accounts
use the same credit card information and we dealt
with a VERY nice GM, so it was a fairly easy process
for us to get the second account. BUT, a friend of
mine that very week was told that this could not be
done. So she started over. She was very distressed to
find out it was possible after she had already deleted
her character from the other account. Also, you will
have to buy your own version of EQ to install, as you
cannot use the same registration number for more than
one account. :( Good luck. I hope this
helps.<br><br>Sadia Willowynde<br>High Elven Enchantress<br>Master
