Here's a good tip -- don't follow the roads
through Lfay to Steamfont. When you zone in and are about
to leave the tunnel, align yourself with the wall on
the left. Just go straight at the same angle as that
wall, run for about 45 seconds, and you'll see the
Gnome huts with some weird equipment and robots
standing around. Just beyond that is the tunnel leading to
Steamfont.<br><br>Then follow the path -- go right at the first
crossroads, left at the second, I think. A right turn at the
second takes you to some Kobold camps that might be good
hunting for your level. <br><br>That being said, I would
go to Butcherblock instead of Steamfont. Much easier
to get to, more guards around, better
MObs.<br><br>Kharlisht
