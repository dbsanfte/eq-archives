Just a reminder of some best BANG FOR YOUR BUCK
things in Norrath:<br><br>Get your tailoring up and make
backpacks-- you can get at least 70pp for them, and they
usually sell pretty well in either Gfay or EComm (at
least on Xev)<br><br>Spire groups in SK-- Gnolls are
fast action, so you'll get good XP, AND they often
drop plats for even full groups PLUS FS weapons (about
5pp each to merchant) AND Split Paw items. Most sell
for around 10pp at auction if you have time, EXCEPT
the gloves (which have wis and dex bonus) can sell
for around 20pp.<br><br>MAMMYs in Everfrost!! This is
where I made ALL My money. Best if you are a ranger our
druid over lvl 20, but if not, get one of these to
group with you. I started at lvl 25 and it was tough
soloing them, but i could do it and get xp all the way to
lvl 30. Mammy's drop tusks, usually one, sometimes
none, but sometimes 2. After getting faction up in
Halas and finding the right merchant, I was selling
them for over 9pp each. They are huge and heavy, so I
would fill up, run to town, sell and gate back for more
action. Had several sessions making 100+ plat. If i had
teamed up with a cleric, chanter or tank, it would have
been faster XP, but I would have had to share the
tusks. <br><br>In the 30s, there is no better place that
Rathe Mountains hunting giants. As with the Mammys,
there is serious competition for mobs here, and lots of
high level soloers looking for easy cash. At level
30-38ish, this area can be challenging for a group, provide
good xp AND a TON of money. Full group can get up to
8pp each Plus FS weapons and the occasional
miscellaneous items. <br><br>Kunark (LOIO, FM, OT) is one of
the best places for XP, but the loot there is
generally random, rare and terrible. BUT-- if you don't
like to auction stuff, then Kunark is the place to
hunt and be patient. When a Leg Chopper, Giant Tree
Flayer, or armor drops from the giants, go sell it
immediately to a merchant. The above items sell for over
30pp, with the large Foreman's tunic selling for 67pp
last i tried. Just gotta wade through all the tump
stumps---<br><br>Ok-- there's a little info-- I always forget to throw
in info for under 20s, because i can't remember what
GOOD mobs i fought then.<br><br>Xela
