HI there,<br>I'm a druid on Quellious.<br>I found
that getting as many +wis items as possible early on
is quite helpful. Primarily I went to each area that
contains alot of skellies or creatures with weapons.
Saving items and selling. I kill only greens at this
point as it's the easiest to collect the weaps. Another
thing, I do this early early in the morning, usually
there isn't any other person in the area I am hunting,
so as to not take the kills away from the levels the
mobs are blue to. when I gain a couple hundred pp I go
to the EC auction at a busy point of the day and
auction for +wis items, stating my buying level. It's
amazing the number of people who are carrying around
items that they don't need or just don't want to have
as weight. I've gotten some incredibly good deals
because people don't want to carry the item any more.
<br>Any way, what I guess I am trying to say is that
rather than concentrating on leveling at first I
concentrate on getting the necessary equipment for my
character. It is something that pays off. My level 21 druid
has a 164 wis now. Makes medding sheer heck, but
still worth it. <br>*grin* Jessica
