Subject: Death and Carnage in Lower Guk!


I have now begun my trips into Lower Guk, I have
the full belief that this is one of the most deadly
areas in the game. Not only are there the undead goul
frogs... but live ones as well! So if you ruined your
faction with them, things will be alot harder. The first
few things just at the dead side entrance to guk are
live frogs... just 3 but after that its all ghouls.
these frogs vary from about lvl 30 to 34, as you go
deeper in you run accross bats and spiders (wich will
attack) so you cannot just get through with invis undead.
The place i have been currently camping with a guild
i am in, "The Aggressors" in the tower. In the
Assasin room to be exact. So far i have aquired no
treasure so far there, other than lots of pp. I have lost
lotto after lotto on the Guise of the Deciever :( This
is the mask that turns you into a dark elf!!! I will
keep the site posted on these short little trips.
