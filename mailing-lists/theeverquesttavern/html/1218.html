Go to eq.castersrealm.com - along the left hand
menu, near the bottom, will be a link called Client
Wizard. You can pick and choose the colors you want for
the different messages in game. Then you get a series
of text, which you copy and paste over the text
lines pertaining to that in your eqclient.ini file,
located in the Everquest directory.<br><br>Only about 1/3
of the lines are customizable using the client, so
don't just blindly copy and paste. Instead, find where
the text you created at Caster's Realm cuts off. Then
paste it only over the matching part in your EQclient
file.<br><br>Hope this helps.<br><br>Iskandar<br>51 champion of
