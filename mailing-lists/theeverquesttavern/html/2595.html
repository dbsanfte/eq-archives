Rondo Dunfire's Tongue, however, is one of four needed for a Dwarf
Rogue-only quest. Well, you can do it if you're not a Dwarf Rogue,
but you won't be able to use the item you get. The other tongues are
from three other Dwarf bandits, all in Butcherblock Mountains.
Barma, Crytil and Keldyn are the other three Dwarf Bandits. Turn in
their tongues to Diggins in Kaladim (but not Qued's or anyone
else's!), and you get a Parrying Pick (AC 7, Dwarf Rogue only). It's
really useful only until you get Dual Wield at level 13 (?), at which
point you'll want two weapons anyway.
