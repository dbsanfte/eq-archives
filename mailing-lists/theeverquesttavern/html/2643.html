Good luck. The Brewers are extremely hard to find. I leveled my Ranger
there from 6-13 and only saw a handful of them.

They are a rare spawn. The one spot that I remember seeing a few is
crossing the bridge and staying to the left. That small strip of land next
to the water (that leads to the underwater ladder that takes you to the
Elite and Commander Room areas) is where one will eventually pop. Just keep
killing anything that spawn on that strip.

The other spot is somewhere near the Elite ledge.
