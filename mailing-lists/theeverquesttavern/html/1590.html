Sniffle...<br>::wipes away tears from the
scarring::<br>I have always had respect for clerics, just as I
have always had respect for all the classes. In the
case that you feel used, ask yourself these questions:
Am I in a group that communicates to me (i.e.
listening when I say I'm OOM)? Do I get a fair split of the
loot? Am I doing my job, without having to worry about
my own personal safety?<br>These questions work for
all the classes. From monk to warrior to druid to
wizard, it depends on playing fair and communication. If
a group communicates well, you should have no
problem with being "used". As for those who run up to you
for a heal (or SoW, or clarity, or whatever the
trouble might be), tell them to wait on you, and you'll
see what you can do. You may still be "used" but at
least you're not readily available.<br>My thoughts on
clerics:<br>Wonderful wonderful people.<br>Nothing like an experience
resurrection in a hell level, ever had that feeling?<br>Heal
and buff. That's what you do, and you do it well, so
stick to it!<br>And I really do sympathize with
clerics. As a druid, I have to. Just as I sympathize with
mages, wizards, shaman, and necromancers (to a limited
extent). Since I share some of the abilities and,
therefore, responsibilities of these classes, I share in
their plights.<br><br>Nukai Oakenwulf<br>Rabid Cleric
Hunter of Spiritus Valorum
