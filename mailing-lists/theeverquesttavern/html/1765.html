EQATLAS (<a href=http://www.eqatlas.com target=new>http://www.eqatlas.com</a>) and start printing the

around, and the grids on them allow you to figure out
your /loc.<br><br>It's been a while since I've been to
OT, but I do remember it's a little tricky to get to.

somewhat hidden valley that leads to OT from FM.
