You probably won't be making much money for a
while, being only level 4. When you're a coule of levels
higher you can take on gnolls and trade their fangs for
moonstones, and then trade the moonstones for blackburrow
stout. You can get pretty good money and exp from doing
that. Check out the quests on everlore for the full
details, names etc. Around level 8 its a good idea to hop
on the boat and go to kerra isle. Everything gives
good exp and you can get some nice loot like giant
snake fangs and lightstones, you'd need a magic weapon
to hit the wisps however. Theres a really nice quest
where you save up your greater lightstones and trade
them to a gypsy in Norht karana for a tome which you
can sell for about 8pp. Hope this helps you out a bit
