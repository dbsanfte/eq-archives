First off that dwarf paladin would be pretty
awesome. There aren't enough dwarven healers in the game,
they're really underrated and really good. Your first
character should be a warrior, again, so you can learn the
combat system. But after that a dwarf paladin would
rock.<br>Servers - the newer servers, such as Luclin and Lanys
T'Vyl are full of newer characters, they generally have
less people on at any time. The number of people on a
server affects lag time, so if you're worried about lag
join a server with a few amount of people during peak
time (equivalent to primetime in TV land). But these
servers generally have younger characters with less
experience than others. The older servers - Fennin Ro and
Veeshan especially - tend to have the worst lag but the
most serious players. Pick whatever you want.
Personally I like Prexus - medium lag and medium level of
seriousness. But it's your choice.
