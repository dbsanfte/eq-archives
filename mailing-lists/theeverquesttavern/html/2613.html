First getting married means changing /SURNAME. Only a GM can do
this. You petition a GM to do the change. Normally this means an
area that is not used much for the event like Arena or Temple of Ro
though I have seen Surefall and heard of Xorbb's Throne(charm and

get a band of bards to play on the side for dancing(levitation helps
here) But make sure a few times that the GM will be there. They
dont keep records of this stuff so petition and make sure that the Gm
you are talking to can personally do the service.
