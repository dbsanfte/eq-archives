"NO DROP" means that you cannot get rid of that
item by giving/exchanging it with someone else, either
a player character or a nonplayer character. You
also cannot drop the item; i.e. click on the item,
then click on the area of the screen that shows the EQ
world. If the item is a dagger, then a dagger should
appear on the ground near where you were standing when
you dropped it. No drop is used to avoid twinking,
when a higher level character gives his items to a low
level character of his. If a magician loots a powerful
warrior-only sword, he may create a warrior so he can use the
sword. But if it's no drop, all the magician can do is
hold on to the sword, put it in a bank, or destroy
it.<br>"LORE ITEM" means that your character will only get one
copy of that particular item in his EQ lifetime. Take
for example the Bloodfire, an extremely powerful
warrior-only sword. It's 1 handed, so a warrior could use 2 of
these with dual wield and become very powerful. But if
the sword is lore, then the warrior can have but one
of these. Does this help? Sorry so
