Ok, first thing I want to say is that I have not
played a monk so I am certainly not an expert, but I
still think that I can help with some of your questions
because I have grouped with monks and have some friends
who play them.<br><br> -Hand to hand vs. using
weapons? Monks are hand to hand fighters - perhaps you may
develope a weapon skill for the fun of it but you will be
more affective going hand to hand. You can get a pair
of magical gloves such as Netted gloves and you will
be able to hit creatures that can only be hit by
magic (like wisps). Netted gloves are pretty easy to
get-go to everlore.com to get info on them. Anytime I
have ever grouped with monks they have never used
weapons.<br><br> Is Round Kick better than Tiger Claw? I cant
really help you there, sorry. I would say to develope
them both-keep both skills maxed. <br><br> How much
weight? I believe the amount of weight you can carry
without penalty is 14 pounds (one reason you probably
dont want to use a weapon). Monks have natural AC that
will suffer if you are carrying too much weight. I
dont know if it affects your attacks or not. Remember
that coins have weight and will encumber you if you
are carrying alot.<br><br> Well, I hope this helps -
