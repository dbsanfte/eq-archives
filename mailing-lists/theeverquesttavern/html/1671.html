First, check with the Hearwood master in your
guild-- he will give you the rusty weapons quest. Not a
great quest, but it's the only one you'll get from him.
I would go to the ranger's guild to get the quests
they offer, and maybe even the warrior and rogues
guild, if they'll give you the quests (I can't remember
if they do, but I got XP from teaming with some
rogues and warriors who completed those quests with me
in the group). <br><br>It is sad that there are not
many lower level quests for wood-elf druids. Once you
start ridding the Faydark of the vile orcs (which
should be your calling in life, if I can preach the word
of Tunare here...) you will start to find Crushbone
Belts (and later CB pads once you venture into CB
itself). The belts are VERY valuable at lower levels, for
you can travel to Kaladim to show the warriors there
that you too are in opposition to the evil orcs. Give
the appropriate dwarf proof that you have vanquished
4 orcs (by giving him 4 belts) and you will be
rewarded richly in experience and by an item that you can
either use, or sell for profit (druid spells don't come
cheap!!). <br><br>Also, check with the High-Elf cleric's
guild. At early levels, most of these snooty clerics
won't have much to do with you, BUT remember that both
of you serve the great will of Tunare. You will get
a quest to hunt down the evil undead in Faydark
(decaying skeletons) and bring back 4 sets of bone chips.
After that, you will be give a much more difficult
quest involving the rancorous ghasts of the Lesser
Faydark. Good questng indeed, and not many of Tunare's
druids know of this union with the Clerics of
Felwithe!!<br><br>Good Hunting to you!!!<br><br>Xela Orcbane<br>Druid of
the 24th Season<br>Defenders of Mithril Hall<br>Xev
