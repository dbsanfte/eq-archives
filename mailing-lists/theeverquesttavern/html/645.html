Subject: It's all about perceptions.


Here is just my personal opinion on why nobody
should be complaining about how "their class is
weak".<br><br>People choose classes based on what they THINK the class
will be like. Sometimes their perception of what a
"Wizard" or "Monk" is varies quite a bit from what Verant
thinks. Then at some point these people realize that
their class does not live up to what they thought it
should be.<br><br>Now, at this point, most people will
switch classes. Some people "stick it out" in the hopes
that their class will somehow get better at higher
levels or that Verant will change the class to meet that
player's expectations. I have no remorse for those people.
Playing a character you do not like is foolish. The
instant you figured out you didn't like your character
you should have switched. I played a Cleric to 14 and
a Monk to 12 before finding my favorite class
(Enchanter). Did I think the Cleric or Monk classes were weak?
Hell no. I just wasn't having the playing experience
that I thought I would, and came to the conclusion to
switch characters. I had fun up until the end with those
characters, so most of my time with them wasn't really
wasted.<br><br>The most common post I see on the various forums goes
something like this: (very simplified)<br><br>"Verant said
[class] are the masters of [whatever] and they aren't!
[Other class] is way better at at it than we are! I'm
level [insert high level] so I know what I'm talking
about!"<br><br>My question is this: Did you finally come to this
brilliant conclusion only AFTER reaching level 50???? It
took you 50 levels to realize you think your class
"sucks"?<br><br>Ok, I'm being excessive with the level 50 example,
but I hope you get my point.<br><br>Another
possibility:<br>So you started playing a Rogue let's say. You get to
a certain point and it becomes clear that being a
Rogue is not what you had in mind....but there are no
other classes that appeal to you out there. Then I
think you are stuck. Your only options are:<br><br>1)
Change your perceptions of Rogues (unlikely)<br>2) Learn
to live with the differences in your vision versus
Verant's vision.<br>3) Play another game.<br><br>Many of
these people try #2 and can never fully accept the
differences in vision. They eventually get pissed and either
quit or start filling the boards with their heated
posts to vent their rage. I feel sorry for these
people, and I hope they eventually find whatever it is
they are looking for in a game.<br><br>The last
possible scenario I can think of is nerfs. If you started
a Necro and enjoyed it all the way to 50 then some
new patch came along with what you think is a
horrible detriment to your class...well that sucks. I can
feel your pain. My only suggestion then would be to
post some intelligent requests on why the change
should be revoked or some possible reparations that
could be made.<br><br>I hope this is read by people
with an open mind. If even one person out there gains
any insight from this post then I feel it was worth
the potential flames.<br><br>Good journey to you
all.<br><br><br>Nelmunbar<br>Enchanter on Brell
