At lvl 6, Orc Hill and the surrounding area will
still give you good xp. Try the orcs that spawn near
the CB ent--they are a little higher level and the
nearby zone makes hunting safer. Also, while Legos may
be too tough for your level, there are other mobs in
CB that are good. Try heading over to the Slave
Tents (not the slaver pit by the castle). There are
fewer Legos there, and there are a wide range of Cents
and Slavers too choose from. You can get keys from
the slavers and set the slaves free for additional
XP. <br><br>Beyond that-- you can head out to
Steamfont and team up with gnomes there-- though most of
the stuff you'll fight is wandering instead of at a
static camp (although there are 2 Kobold camps that you
should be able to take with a group). BB has lots of
different camps, though I didn't hunt there until at least
lvl 10 (at the chessboard), and later (at the bandit
camp and orc shrine). <br><br>Have fun-- CB is a fun
and fast area. Many lower levels "grow up" there--
starting at the entrance areas, then to slave camp, then
to slave pits, then to castle front, then to Throne
Room.<br><br>X.
