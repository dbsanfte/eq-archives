Faction with Oggok doesn't matter, except to make
it easier to get to Clurg, the Ogre bartender. Clurg
has his own faction. I have found no way of raising
faction with Clurg other than fulfilling the Stein
quest....which you can't do until you have a high enough faction
with Clurg. Catch 22. Enchanters get around this using
Alliance/Illusion spells.<br><br>The details of the quest can be
found on other sites. My suggestion would be to get an
enchanter friend to do the quest for you. The Stein isn't
No Drop, so he can give it to you once the quest is
complete. All the items involved are No Drop (maybe the
Kiola nut isn't, not sure). If you wanted to be nice
and help him, you could kill the Koalindal fish in
Qeynos for him, but let him loot. The fish is only like
level 3, but you take a severe faction hit with the
Qeynos guards for killing it. But I'd personally just
start a mule character in qeynos, get him to level 3 or
4, and use him to kill the fish. My brother has a
level 4 Cleric named Larinchi whose only purpose in
life is to kill the fish when I need another Stein
