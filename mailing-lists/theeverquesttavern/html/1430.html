OK - here is a guide to the messages you get when
you /con - not including the faction message (ie.
"looks at you amiably/warmly/dubiously, threateningly"
etc.).<br><br>For Creatures that /con even to you - <br><br>When
you are Lvls 1-12 -- "Looks like an even
fight"<br><br>When you are Lvls 13-14 -- "Looks kind of risky - you
might win"<br><br>When you are Lvls 15-24 -- "He
appears to be quite formidable"<br><br>BLUE
/Cons<br><br>You = lvl 1-12 (Mob = 1-3 lvls lower) -- "Looks like
you would have the upper hand"<br><br>You = lvl 13-14
(Mob = 1-3 lvls lower) -- "Looks kind of risky, but
you might win"<br><br>You = lvl 13-14 (Mob = 4-5 lvls
lower) -- "Looks like you would have the upper
hand"<br><br>You = lvl 15-24 (Mob = 1-2 lvls lower) -- "Looks kind
of dangerous"<br><br>You = lvl 15-24 (Mob = 3 lvls
lower) -- "Looks quite risky"<br><br>You = lvl 15-24
(Mob = 4 lvls lower) -- "Looks quite risky, but might
be worth a try"<br><br>You = lvl 15-24 (Mob = 5 lvls
lower) -- "You would probably win this fight--it's not
certain though"<br><br>GREEN /Cons<br><br>You = lvl 1-12
(Mob = 4 lvls or more lower) -- "Looks like a
reasonably safe opponent"<br><br>You = lvl 13-24 (Mob = 6
lvls or more lower) -- "Looks like a reasoanbly safe
opponent"<br><br>I didn't put the messages for above lvl 24, but can
if requests are made. The guide is good, especially
at lvls 13 and up, as it lets you know which BLUES
will give you more experience. You get more the closer
to your level they are. Also, remember (as i'm sure
you've all found) that just because a Mob or mobs are
green, doesn't mean they wont attack you on site--
especially if they hate you OR you are sitting. <br><br>Hope
this helps -- <br><br>Xela<br>Druid of the 17th
season<br>Xev
