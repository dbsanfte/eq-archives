It really comes down to a question of what you
see your role in a group as. A bard will never do
alot of damage with just hand to hand, but if you're
in a group with a couple tanks, by all means put
away that sword and equip your drum and play away.
You're probably more valuable to the group as a sort of
caster, twisting chant of battle, chords of dissonance if
you can safely without pulling more mobs, and be
ready with the Hymn of Restoration if needed and Selo's
if your party has to make a break for it. You'll
want to try to keep both hand to hand and your chosen
weapon skill/skills maxed as much as you can, (not an
easy task as you level quickly for the early levels).
After the tanks put some damage on the mob, however,
you can step in and start swinging fists without
doing enough damage to taunt them off the tanks. Chords
of Dissonance seems to taunt them nicely though,
which could be good or bad.<br><br>Some invaluable
