Eventually you'll settle into a routine, playing maybe a couple hours
per night (if you have a real life, that is). That's why folks like
Shooman and me don't level fast -- if you want to get to the 50s or
even level 60, it will take many days of playing time. Here's a
kick -- a month or so from now, type /play when you're in the game
and see what it says.

The green bar you're referring to is the connection status bar. This
is a description someone gave on the main EQ board:

"The green (sometimes yellow, sometimes red) bar with the 4 number
sets is the lag-o-meter (or lag bar). The leftmost and rightmost
numbers are your server side and client side ping (meaning how fast
your connection to the game server is). The middle two numbers are
packet loss percentages and should stay as close to 0% as possible if
everything runs fine(90% or more = LD usually)."
