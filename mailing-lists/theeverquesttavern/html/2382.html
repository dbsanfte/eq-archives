Sorry, not on Quellious . . . but wanted to point
out that if you're in Butcherblock, you're in the
same zone as the docks with the boats to Freeport. No
need to go into the Dwarf city (Kaladim), although
it's an awful nice place.<br><br>Depending where
you're at in Butcherblock, to find the docks: If you've
just zoned in from Kelethin/Greater Faydark, take the
road in front of you. You'll pass between some huts
with no guards, then come to one with three armored
Dwarves around or inside of it. Turn right -- this is
commonly known as the crossroads. Then just follow this
all the way north, and you'll come to Kaladim,
passing two or three more huts with guards. Either go
into Kaladim, or keep running on the path past
Kaladim, and you'll eventually come to the docks.
<br><br>Just watch for the Aqua Goblins coming out of the
water.<br><br>Kharlisht<br>Rogue of 27-odd seasons<br>Drinal
