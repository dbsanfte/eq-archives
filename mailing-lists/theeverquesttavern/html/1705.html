I have two computers, and a separate EQ account
for each of them. Used the same CC no problem, but I
had to get a second game, it was the easiest option
for a separate computer as I saw it.(I got the new
$10 classic pack, just the original Norrath world, no
Kunark or Velious on that one.) <br>If you only have the
one computer, but want to have a second account on
that, you don't need to go out and buy another copy of
the game, but you WILL need to call customer service
and purchase a new CD-Key for the second account. A
friend of mine did this, and I can't remember exactly
how much it cost, but it was about the same price as
a new game w/ the expansions, maybe a tiny bit
less.<br><br>Short answer: one credit card regardless, One CD Key
for EACH account (and that you get either by buying a
new copy of the game or calling Verant Customer
Service.
