sounds like you may be real new to this game, so
i will cover this in some detail. if you have any
questions afterwords, feel free to ask. the rusty swords
are rewards you get when you loot a corpse. when you
kill something, click on it and you will recieve any
money it has plus a box will pop up showing you what
the creature is carrying. click on any object and you
can transfer it to your inventory. if you right click
and hold it on an object, a box will pop up and show
the name and stats on the object you clicked on. not
all creatures have objects on them, some just have
money. so, do not be surprised if you have to kill and
loot from quite a few mobs. when you get all 4 swords,
take them back to the person who gave you the quest.
click on 1 sword and it will transfer it from your
inventory to your mouse arrow. move the sword until it
covers the picture of the person you want and click on
him. a box will appear with the sword in it plus
openings for 3 more objects. place the 3 remaining swords
in the empty spots. at the bottom of the box are
smaller boxes that say trade (i think) and
cancel.<br>click on trade and the 4 swords wil be given to the
person and you will recieve some exp. and usually a few
rewards. <br><br>as far as finding your way around goes,
the best advice i can give is to go to eqatlas.com.
he has very good maps that you can print off for
free. i find his zone connection maps to be extremely
useful in finding you way around at first. good
luck.<br><br>elryche<br>41 barbarian shaman
