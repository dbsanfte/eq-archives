The Mageborn guild (unless the name is already taken)
is growing, but cannot be formed without YOUR help.
If you don't have a mage, it's okay, just start one.
We will be generally geared (at first) to low lvl
characters. I am also working on developing a
charter.

We the Mageborn of Norrath hereby form this society
of elite, to bring power to the residents of Norrath.
Our great world has lived long enough under the thumb
of the Gods and Dragons of great infamy. This era
shall be at an end. Change shall rock this world to
its very fabric, and we shall form a new Norrath under
the guidance of the Mageborn senetors.
1. To be a member of the Mageborn one must be of
the class Mage.
2. Any policy of Mageborn may be ammended with a
3/4 senate vote.
3. Co-exist peacefully with the guilds of Norrath,
but afford them no special treatment, for no good deed
goes unrewarded.
4. To declare war upon a guild, a minimum of 10
members must be present and a 2/3 vote must be in
favor of these actions.
5. To become a member of the Mageborn Senate one
must be above the lvl of 20 and must be elected by a
popular vote.
6. In achieving this popular vote, one shall then
change his/her surname to Senator, that all may know
of this station.
7. Any complaints, made by the citizenry of
Norrath, shall be investigated, and any offender or
group therein shall be summarily court martialed by a
member of the senate and (a) warned (b) punished or
(c) removed from the guild.

along pretty well =P talk to you guys online
