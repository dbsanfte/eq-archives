My enchanter had a macro command for casting three spells in combat. The
first was a Tashani, then then a debuff spell, then the choke spell, then a
/say of Now choking %T just for fun!

Try this macro out in a hot key.... place your spells of choice in the spell
jewel positions and change the /pause timers so that you get enough time
between spells. This is also helpfull for clerics as you can use the %T
command to tell the person you are healing to STAY PUT! I use similar macro
buttons for letting everyone know the enchanter is in trouble and needs
help....."HEY! Get this %T off of me! NO! NO! NOT THE FACE! Crap, this is
gonna hurt!" works really good.

/caste 1, /pause 65
/caste 2, /pause 70
/say Burn baby burn!, /pause 10
/caste 3, /sit

I do not remember the macro commands for skills such as beg, but if someone
does, please post it.

Thanks,
