Subject: What is a train? - asked by teenbeauty13


I believe a train is a long string of similar
monsters, most often "pulled" by a fighter who did not know
what they were getting into.<br>Example - let's say
you are fighting in the Blackburrow, home to the
wolf-like Gnolls. There are few humans around - it's a
gnoll-dominated place. Let's say you're fighting a gnoll that you
can beat. He may call a few gnoll friends of his to
help out. Pretty soon, there's a large number of
gnolls, so you decide to run. They chase after you in a
line, a "train" of sorts. This often happens when you
stumble into a den of monsters and run away. If you're
running away and see some players along the way, tell
them - "run - train!" - they should know what that
means. If you ever hear it, run with no question,
especially in rather difficult zones. Does this answer your
question? Sorry so lengthy.
