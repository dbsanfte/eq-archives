try to get a screaming mace or a fine steel mace.
the fs mace will run you about 4-5pp. do not pay more
than that since vendors buy them from players around
3-4pp. the screaming mace will cost you quite a bit more
and may be out of your reach for quite awhile unless
you are twinked. the really good one handed blunts
start at around 1k pp. you might give some thought to
changing over to a piercing weapon though. our epic is a
very nice spear, the spear of fate.
<br><br>personally, i wouldn't spend much money on anything that
doesn't give you a wisdom bonous. in a few more levels
you will be doing far more damage with your spells
than with weapons. my shaman is currently a level 35
and can cast a 253 pts direct damage spell 9 times
before running out of mana. her slime coated spear only
does 22 pts for one hit. once you get root you will
not need the club as much. the only time i enter
combat any more is just to practice my combat skills or
until i can re-root. <br><br>elryche
