First things first, you are playing a Shadow Knight, and if you
haven't already figured it out, you are what is called a "hybred"
class. You are a cross between a Warrior and a Necromancer. At lvl
9, you get spells. You can ask someone in the game if you need help
with that, just go to your guild in Neraik and buy them. (should
only cost about a plat or two at that lvl) Dark Elfs are hated my
ALL races except troll, Ogre, and Vah Shir (someone correct me if I
am wrong with any of this BTW). You can bring up your faction (how
the NPCs in other cities like you, or don't like you) by killing most
monsters around the area. For example, if you wanted to go to the
cities of Kalidim, Felwithe, or Kelethin (all on Faydwer) you would
need to go to the zone Clan Crushbone and kill orcs which would give
you positive faction with some citizens of that city. If you can get
your faction up enough with the guards that you can enter the city
you can try to do quests that are given by other citizens for
factions with merchants, guards, etc. One more thing, IF you decide
to go to CB (crushbone) and fight to get your faction up,
BEWARE!!!!! They lower your faction with Indigo Brotherhood, which,
to my understanding, is a BIG part of Dark Elf faction and if you
kill to many you wont be able to get into your own city to do quests,
train, or anything else. Hope this helps.
