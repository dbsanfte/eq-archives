Steamfont Mountains. This place is prime hunting
ground for a pair of adventurers. Lots of Lesser Ebon
Drakes and Rogue Clockwork Spiders and Harpys and
Minotaur Slavers. Not to mention the good supply of
elementals(Steam, Earth, etc.) <br> I hardly find more than 20
people in this zone at one time. i'm on the XEV server.
i can't say how it fares for other servers seeing
as how i don't play on them, but i think it should
be the same throughout. <br> i leveled up from 11 to
12 in a matter of about 25 minutes. i was very
excited. of course i had a friend buff me with Skin like
Nature so i was to the advantage of other lvl 11
characters. <br> I play as a warrior and i had a warrior
partner. i wish i had a spell caster partner instead. i
found that i could do no damage to those steam
elementals. (FYI- Elementals will join in a fight if one is
fighting. They flock to the scene like no bodies business.)
So i tried to avoid the elementals. <br>
Furthermore, i have to state that Steamfont has now become my
favorite hunting ground. I used to like Crushbone,(i like
