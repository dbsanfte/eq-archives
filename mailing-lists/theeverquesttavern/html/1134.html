Well what race are you? Qeynos Hills and East
Commonlands are nice areas for that level if you are around
either Qeynos or Freeport. If you're a wood elf, there
are some areas in Greater Faydark to hit, perhaps the
bandit camp near Kelethin. Don't know how tough these
bandits would be for your level, but from now on, you'll
want at least 1 partner, maybe a mage or tank class
(warrior, ranger, etc.). They take the damage and use melee
while you sit back, heal, and ensnare. Just don't do
either too much or too early, lest you want to get
beaten on. :)<br><br>Screenshots are available in your
C:\Windows\Program Files\Everquest folder. They will be called
EQ000000, EQ000001, etc. They are bitmaps, around 1MB in
size each, so you'll want to save them as JPEGs if you
