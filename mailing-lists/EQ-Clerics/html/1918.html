Well, I'm almost done with level 45 finally :) Want to share my EQ life last
night.

I was in Sol B, the King room. We had to pull the priest room and the Champ
room first as we were the first ones there (full spawn). After buffing everyone
I was don't to 40% mana. We took the first 2 rooms easily. I guess I should
tell you who we had in our group. 50 Monk, 40 Warrior, 41 Warrior, 42 Ranger,
39 Druid, and me. We start spacing the kills in the Kings room so we can take
them one at a time. A couple other groups come in and they take the Champ and
Priest rooms.

Well, after we had spaced the spawn in the King's room, I would constantly be at
full mana before the next spawn, so I melee'd along with everyone else, I cast
Retribution, I ran around the dungeon doing res's (make almost 300 plat from
doing them- I don't charge friends, but these 2 guys I can't stand died so, even
though I really don't like them, I res'd them for money hehe). I was doing
this for several hours when someone pulls 2 LDCs, a Bat, and a Lava Beattle
through the area- the priest room get's wiped out (except one who made it to the
zone), the Champ room made the mistake of thinking they could pick off the
straggler (the Lava Beattle). Well, they get all the bugs back and they evac.
Well, we're sitting up in the King Room nice and safe. Zone pupolation went
from 39 to 18. One group was in the bug area (other side from where the bad
pull came from) and one was in the noble area.

We,re sitting there talking within our group "Hope the King spawns this time",
"Hey, wouldn't it be cool if the priest spawned too?", "Ya, with the BP". I'm
thinking, okay, so far we've had it easy, but the King and Priest at the same
time is tough! I say, "If the priest comes up while the King has spawned-
everyone but [our main tank-level 41 warrior] on the priest or he will heal the
King until we are all dead."

Well LO AND BEHOLD! ..... it never did happen, that would be a really
interesting story, huh? But I had a blast being able to swing my mace, cast
some DD, run around pulling (hehe), and make a bit of money!
