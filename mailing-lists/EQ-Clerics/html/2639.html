One very useful way to use atone is for passing through area's with mobs
to get to a group. On more than one occasion I have had to get
through lower guk live side to the king area. Typically I'll get an
invis, but sometimes nobody is around to cast invis. What I do if I get
attacked is root a mob, get a fair distance from atone, and move on. It
can be dangerous but can work.
