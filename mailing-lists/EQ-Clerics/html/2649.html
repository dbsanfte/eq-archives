>>This is a hidden quest given by Gavel in the Temple of Solusek Ro. If you
>>hail him and say "What of Sacrament", he says that the Ingot and Icon are
>>merely legend! He also says that if you could bring him both Ingot, Icon
>>and
>>2 magnetized platinum bars, that he could forge something very special:
>>
>>Ingot of the Sacrament:
>>Go to the Rathe Mountains and find the Undead Ruins. To get there, as you
>>zone in from the Feerrott, take a left at the first 4-way intersection you
>>come to. Keep the mountain wall on your left and you will eventually come
>>upon a gold tower with undead, this is NOT the spawn. Keep going until you
>>see what looks like a druid teleport spot (ruins) with undead; this is it.
>>Here spawn the giant undead skeletons. You are looking for the rare spawn
>>that looks like a giant dread bone skel (dark brownish-red in color). He
is
>>a low 30th lvl mob and will be quite tough to solo. I, a 34th Cleric, and
a
>>27th Rogue took him easily. We even had another normal giant skel wacking
>>on
>>us, so a group of 3 or so at 30 should do. Also, these guys drop "A
>>skeletal
>>finger", which is a Necro quest for Shadowbound armor. You could use this
>>to
>>attract the company of a necromancer to aid you in destroying the spawn.
>>
>>Icon of the Sacrament:
>>To get the Icon you must travel to Mistmoore. At the back of the Graveyard
>>there is a secret wall (back left, facing the graveyard). Pass through and
>>you will be in a small, square room. There is yet another secret wall
>>immediately to your right as you enter. Go through and you will find
>>yourself in a room with 4 doors. The only door that can be opened is the
>>one
>>you are facing as you enter. But be warned, opening the door causes the
>>other 3 to open. And unless there is a party camping there, there will be
a
>>nasty creature behind each. There is only 1 wall with 2 doors. The Icon is
>>behind the left door of this wall. It is lying on the ground and looks
like
>>a voodoo doll. Also, this is a 6-hour spawn. I like to call this one my
>>"12pp solution". Why? Because that's how much a potion of invisibility
>>costs
>>=) I traveled to Mistmoore and asked if there was a party in the
graveyard,
>>and there was. I asked if they had been killing the things in the secret
>>room, they had been. I asked if they had picked up the doll behind the
door
>>and they told me they had done so about 2 hours before. So....I just
logged
>>and came back 4 hours later, which happened to be around 6:00AM. This
>>worked
>>out great because nobody was in-zone at that time. Drank my invisibility
>>potion, strolled in, opened the door, picked it up and got the heck out.
>>
>>Magnetized Platinum Bars:
>>Purchase 2 regular Platinum Bars from a Jewelry shop or from an Enchanter
>>(hint: create an Enchanter and dump all points into CHA and check the
>>prices). Take these to Ostorm in the Temple of Solusek Ro (he will be
>>upstairs through several doors on the right side of the Temple). Give
>>Ostorm
>>1 bar at a time and he will return it to your inventory as a Magnetized
>>Platinum Bar.
>>
>>Give Gavel the Ingot, Icon and both Magnetized bars (I separated the bars)
>>and he will give you your reward:
>>
>>Caduceus of the Sacrament
>>Magic Lore No Drop
>>Weight: 8.0
>>Type: 1HB Dmg: 12 Del: 32
>>Cleric only
>>
>>I constantly hit for 28's with this thing (89 STR). When I cast Frenzied
>>Strength and Yaulp II, I get some in the low 30's. Truly awesome weapon
>>that
>>is relatively easy to get.
>>
>>Good Luck!
