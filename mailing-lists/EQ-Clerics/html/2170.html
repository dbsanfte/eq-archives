There is a paladin (I can't remember his name, I'm sure someone else has it)
in the paladin guild which is behind the clerics guild in north Kaladim.
Give him four bone chips for money, exp and an item. I used this to level my
cleric from 10th to 13th. Its a great quest when combined with the camping
of the dwarf skeletons around the skeleton tower and the druid ruins in
Butcher Block. You can give him bone chips in stacks of 20 but in
desperation to make money I always just gave him 4 at a time.
