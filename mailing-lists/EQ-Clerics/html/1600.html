We get group heal spell at level 34 - though it does not heal too much heal
for the 180 mana it costs to cast - course it is great when everyone in
group needs a little bit healed. Also - party members need to be pretty
close for this spell to work on everyone - works better in a dungeon than in
fields as everyone is a little more clustered together.

I used RTS up until about level 30 - when I finally was able to get observer
staff. The quests for the testament of vanear and paw of opalla (both +wis
higher than observer staff) are extremely difficult for dark elf to obtain
as they take place in the "goody-goody" race cities =)

Armor-wise: Bronze works in a pinch but is TOOOOO heavy. Steel is lighter
and same AC as bronze, but you really should be trying to get the items with
+wis stats such as gator scale leggings, BS tunic, mammoth hide cloak,
adamantite epolets (shoulder slot) etc. You might look a little newbie-ish
but the +wis is necessary for cleric.

Just my opinions :)
