Well, this might be a crowded area, but I always make good exp with the
birds in SK. By 25 I can also solo the undead embalmer etc in Lake
Rathe...I would try to lull (or is it calm now) them first though.

The knight room in RE is also doable with 3 or more ppl at this lvl. (Tip:
always jump into the water if the situation gets out of hand)

Drez, the ogre capn, bonecracker in Najena are all good too with a full
party around this lvl.

Permafrost is nice too but that place can train easily if you are not
careful. Try camping the preacher room at this lvl. You would still get
exp and maybe a ruined circlet in the bargain. (Tip: always try to mix
camping and exp gaining together...would make things alot easier on yourself
in the long run ;)

Evil eyes in Beholder. The spawn is very fast and you would still gain exp.
You might just be able to camp this area solo, if not, then you would just
need one other person with you. Again, you might just get lucky and get
some poish bone bracelets in the bargain for your trouble ;)

Divinn in Crushbone. I dunno, this might be green to you now but I vaguely
remember killing him with 2 other friends around lvl 20+ and gaining
exp....You would need 2 other person to be safe in this area in the tower.

The DE guards in front of Lavastorm. I hunted here with a monk friend. He
would go pull the guard and I'll just heal him from way way back...those
guards hit hard and are hard to kill. However, their loot is good and u can
always zone into lavastorm if thing gets out of hand.
