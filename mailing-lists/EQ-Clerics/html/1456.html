I play 4 charachters, my highest and favourite my 25th level cleric - with
that are a 12 Ranger 16 warrior 21 Mage.... I add that info for this
reason... my non-cleric charachters group with clerics and so I get a view
from both sides.

My adice to the level 9 is this.... set the strategy for the group at the
start.... easy enough to say group with the same people - not always
practical, and though I have regular hunt partners and am reasonably well
known at early levels people are leveling at different rates and group make
ups can change quickly.... The cleric calls the shots - when to attack and
when to run.... signals during the fight that their mana is getting low...
that they are medding... that they need a mob being pulled off them.... tells
the group ro run.... I find that groups will very quickly understand your
worth and defer battle startegy to you if you keep it simple.... if they
don't time to look for another group. I too like others consider myself the
last line of defence and will hold the mob while my group gets away, I don't
want to be doing this because of repeated stupidity of a group or group
member that starts another battle before I the Cleric am ready... TELL them
to WAIT,

At the level I am now I will rarely group with starngers.... they copuld kill
me or I could kill them... for a Cleric to be at his best the group eeds to
have complete trust in him, that comes over time.

Develop the strategy and style of play that you enjoy most... for me shooting
out a DD can be better than a heal drawing the mob onto me and relieving the
party member being hit... we have decent hps and mele defence if not attack
and can TAKE some hits for the group while the mob is finished... tends to be
a tactic I use when there are 2 or 3 of us, ie takling the dmage that
otherwise would have been taken by my group member and which I would have had
to heal. LOTS to think about being a cleric... MUCH more than any of the
other charachters I play, and without any doubt I believe the cleric to be
the most influential and essential party member,,,, play it well and you are
a STAR.... make mistakes as we all do and you are a Villain... what other
class has such profile ;-)
