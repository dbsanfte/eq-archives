Tank Buffs: Resolution, Shield of Words, Symbol of Pinzarn (Naltron is not mana
efficient IMHO for the small yield in HP for tanks at that level), appropriate
Resist's for beastie you hunt, and keep Extinguish Fatigue memmed to cast if
you have mana, long melee really consumes Stamina for tanks especially if they
have haste items equipped.

Caster Buffs: many Clerics dont buff casters much......I do. Resolution, and
Pinzarn at least and any resists they require.....these buffs may save their lives
as their nuking might attract unwanted attention....enabling them to move to
safety and give tanks time to taunt off the attacker.

Your High Level Buffs stack nicely with almost all the Shaman Stat buffs as well ;)
