IMHO, you should've put your pts into wisdom and if you were going to put
some into another stat, agility. Normal plate is too heavy, and has no
wisdom enhancements, only AC. You want items that give you wisdom. And at
high levels, you shouldh't be getting hit much, if at all, if your tanks are
doing their jobs. I have sacrificed AC for wisdom, and have no regrets.
Agility will keep you from getting hit as often, also. Understand now,
this is at HIGH levels. Which is what your goal should ultimately be.
