That bit about the 100 pt damage spell is not quite correct. I have played a
Wizard, and at level 12 you get Shock of Lightning, which does damage in the
low 80's. You don't get a 100 pt damage spell until level 16. At levels 14
and 15, clerics can actually do more damage to the undead (although not to
other mobs) than a wizard.

People hate wizards because they can killsteal, but you know, it's like that
old joke: Why do dogs lick their b***s? Answer: because they can.

In Unrest, and other undead dungeons, clerics can killsteal, and a couple,
including the notorious Soulbasher (may Brell curse his name and fizzle his
spells!) do so regularly. We curse wizards because they commit sins...that
we are usually not capable of. But when we are capable, a few clerics will
be succumb to temptation and be killstealers as well.

It's funny. I didn't killsteal as a wizard, but switched characters because
of an impression that no one really wanted a wizard. As a cleric, I have a
new appreciation of wizards. My thing is healing, and having a wizard in my
party can vastly reduce the damage I have to heal, because the mobs get
taken down more quickly. Less downtime, less mana expenditure, safer fights
against tougher mobs -- a better time is had by all. I really appreciate
having a wizard in the party.

The whole killstealing thing is due to a very, very bad design decision by
Verant. I.e., that a group's total damage is ignored in assigning credit for
kills, and if an outside player has done more damage than any *single* group
member, then he gets credit for the kill. Brad posted a note about this
design decision to the Vault message boards, that was reprinted widely on
the news pages. Verant seemed to feel that if a group's total damage was
counted, then groups would steal from individuals.

Duh! Now we have exactly the opposite problem. And why, in a group-oriented
game, are we catering to the least ethical, most unsocial, nastiest solo
players?

I ran into a guy -- yes, a wizard -- named Odisius, in Unrest on Fennin Ro
the other night, who was soloing and killstealing from groups. He said he
had no real interest in playing the game, but was only trying to level to 20
so he could sell the character/account on eBay.

This is the kind of person the current damage-assignment algorithm benfits.
Aaaccckkk! This is dumb, and make the game frustrating for those who really
want to play and enjoy it.

I am going to send Verant some screenshots of Odisius sceaming obscenities,
and describe his behavior, along with a plea to rethink the kill-assignment
algorithm. Everyone should email Verant with the same request, talk it up on
the message boards and newsgroups, and maybe Verant change the code and end
this whole killstealing debacle.
