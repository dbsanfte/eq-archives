The Paw of Opella is a Held item(either hand)
It gives 9ac, 9wis, 9 agi, 5sta(I think) and has a negative Cha attribute.
Awesome item in my opinion.

there are three steps to doing the quest.(they do not have to be done in any
specific order)

1) Go to Qeynos Sewers and Kill Vin Moltor(beggar) and he will drop a
severed Paw. This is a container with 4 slots and a combine button.

2) Go to Befallen and Kill Gynok, the random spawn NPC mummy and retrieve
the Platinum ring(This ring has a pos and a neg attribute)just so you know
its the proper plat ring.

3) Go To Kerra Isle, Kill High Priestess Mitty several hundred times, she
drops most of what you need. You will get the Copper, Silver and Gold rings
from her. Again each ring will have a pos and neg attribute.

To get the paw:
Take all four rings, place them in the Paw container and Press combine

Congratulations you are the Proud owner of a Paw. ;-)
