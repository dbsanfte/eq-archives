The benefit to Revive is that you can save a lot of down time if one or more
of your party members dies by bringing them back right away. No waiting for
them to run back. You should bind yourself close to where you are hunting
so you can get back fast and revive the others. It's an incredibly useful
spell to your party members. Especially if you are travelling across the
continent and you have members who are bound only in cities, if one of them
dies, you can get them back quickly.
