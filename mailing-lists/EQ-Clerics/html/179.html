My ghod man, you've made it to lvl 15 without hotkeying spells? Now
that's amazing! =)

Anyway, you had it right. On the mem'd spells on the top left side
of the screen, click and hold down the left mouse button until it
becomes a button itself. Then drag it to a hotkey slot and place
it just one pixel to the right and one pixel or so down, then lft click.
For some reason (at least on my screen) it never catches if i place
the icon right over the hotkey square. I have to place it just to the
right and down for it to latch on.

I never tried this, but if you have no spells memm'd on those spell
slots, you can also try lft clicking on the empty spell slot until
it becomes a button. Then just mem the spells in the slots that have
associated hotkeys.

Note: on my slower computer (amd 200), when i try to mem spells,
the response is so slow it sometimes clicks on the spell instead
of creating a button. Just do the trial and error trick. Also,
be sure you're in windowed mode (not full screen or transparent
screen).

I hope this helps,
