Well it might get worse at higher levels (I am only 28) but I only do
MED/HEAL when we have way too much action or a bad number of healers to
burners/bashers. As a general rule I melee at the start of a fight (as
long as I have lotsa mana) unless its a caster, then I am stun stun look
for heals stun stun. I also throw a nuke in if the burner is low and a
runner is taking off or stun and let the meleers do their business. BTW
stuns are very useful they halt a MOB that is running, doesn't cancel
chains or snares, interupts a caster and stops it damaging for a round
and with some of the things we have been fighting they can hit for 60 or
more a time and several attacks a round.

It also helps to get in a group with an enchanter above 29 and a bard
above 32 ;p
