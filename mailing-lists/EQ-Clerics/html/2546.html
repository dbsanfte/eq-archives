Might I suggest you try this in Cazic? =)
Just the other night I heard somebody shouting asking somebody to buy all their FS from them
at 2pp each. Since you can sell them at 5pp each, it's a good deal. There is a lot more FS in Cazic than there is in unrest and it's a lot further from good selling grounds. This is especially a good spot if you play an Ogre or a Troll and can sell in Ogguk... since most of the people in Cazic have to go much further to sell. I know that I usually end up leaving FS lyin on the ground in Cazic because it weighs me down to the point where i can't even move. It works out to be a good deal for the people in Cazic because they can sell all their FS with a 2pp/per profit and not have to run back to sell their stuff, and a good deal for you as well because you make 3pp per piece. I don't think this would work as well in Unrest since there isn't nearly as much FS available.
