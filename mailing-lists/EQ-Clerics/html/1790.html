Gynok's place holder spawns at the zone and does a shout to the whole
zone, you will see a giant rat shouts "some garbage about waking me or
something" it then wanders thru 1st level, then 2nd level then 3rd
level. So unless the spot is camped the only way be sure is to go thru
and kill anything that seems to be moving, and since the place holder
may have made it to his final resting place, everything on the third
floor, then go back up and camp the zone killing everything that spawns
and does a shout.

The place holder can be anything that spawns in Befallen, but I have
seen/heard Giant rats, putrid skeles, a skeleton and ghouls.
