I moved thru 22-25 pretty quickly solo'n the 3 named bandits in that cave
across from High Keep in HHP. It's pretty easy for a cleric to spread out
the spawn to where you have one about every 5 mins. And if things do get
ugly, you have a zone and guards right there. There's also a vendor & bank
in Keep. The bandits drop some coin, but more importantly the occasional
bronze weapon and gemstone. Once you get the spawns spread out, you want to
work inside the cave, since there's a roaming guard outside that will steal
your kill.
Once they don't give good exp anymore, just move into Keep. Sure it's
boring, it's a great place to level. And loot includes plat coin, fs weapons
and small bronze armor as you move down into warrior room and then raider
room. And while you're waiting in line for a group, there a named prisoner
you can solo back in the jail area that will give you exp til about lev 32.

Pantheus
lev 35 on EM

PS: Another nice thing about workin in the HHP/HHK area at these levels is
you're close to the spawn for the Testament. Once you get your bandit spawns
timed out, you can easily run to smuggler room to check for the gaurd
between spawns.
