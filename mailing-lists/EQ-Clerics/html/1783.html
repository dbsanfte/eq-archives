I know this from MANY hours in Befallen waiting on him. He or his
placeholder spawn right at the entrance. Not a little way down or anything
else, immediately inside the zone. He then immediately shouts and walks to
the 3rd floor. There are several random spots he can go to on the 3rd
floor. To get him to spawn again (he seems to be between a 20 and 30 min
spawn) you have to find and kill the placeholder on the 3rd floor. You will
know when you have the right one, because they *usually* do the shout again
when you attack them.
