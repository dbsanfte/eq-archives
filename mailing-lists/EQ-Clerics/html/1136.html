I agree with most of what Geoffrey said. It is difficult to balance the
classes in relation to each other. And clerics ARE well balanced.
However, I still think that clerics should not be able to be stunned
while Divine Aura is up. I don't agree with Geoffrey that to change this
would give clerics a "get out of jail free card." Many times, there is
nowhere for the cleric to go even when he's invulnerable. And often he's
too hurt to get to a zone fast enough to matter. In some cases, casting
Divine Aura merely postpones the inevitable. Since you can't cast or
fight, all you have time for is to find a nice cozy corner to die in.

One exception: with DA on, you can sit and memorize gate (unless you get
stunned, of course!), and hope that you're lucky enough to get it to
work after DA wears off.

This being said, I think someone should tell EQVault to update their
cleric guide. It says that while you have DA on, you can cast Heal on
your group members and help them get away from the mob by meleeing the
MOB, which will offer enough distraction for your friends to hightail it
to safety. Ha ha! Wouldn't that be nice?
