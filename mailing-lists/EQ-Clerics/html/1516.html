What I've found to be the (unfortunate, imho) case of late in Unrest, is
that it's safest to kill inside the house. The major downside to this, at
least in my case, is the major lag I pick up just by stepping inside the
house. It's pretty much gotten to the point that when I've seen it the
safest in Unrest is when there are groups camping the tower, the barroom,
the reclusive ghoul magi room, and some lower level groups in the room at
the side door and the back door room. The pathing in Unrest is so buggy
that if you try to pull something to the outside, chances are that the rest
of the house is going to come with it. That's half the reason I moved over
to Mistmoore once I hit lv 21.
