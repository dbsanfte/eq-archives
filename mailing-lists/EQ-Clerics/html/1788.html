Ok, guys, I'm camping Gynok now. You must be sure to kill the placeholder
or he won't spawn. The spawn spot is right at zone entrance. It can be
anything from a rat to an IBS to a ghoul. The putrid skel and ghoul
placeholders TALK when they spawn. First thing you have to do is go to
level 3 and kill everything down there. That's only way you can be sure to
get the placeholder. It can be an IBS or anything. So after you kill
everything down there, youwill hve killed the placeholder and then you just
have to camp that zone spot and kill everything that spawns. Not every
placeholder will talk. The IBSs and rats don't talk. The placeholder will
ROAM after it spawns and make its way to level 3. That's why you have to
clear everything down there first to be sure you get it. Otherwise it will
never spawn.
