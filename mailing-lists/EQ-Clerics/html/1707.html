When I was in Kerra Isle at 24, she was green to me, and the druid I was
grouped with was also 24. The druid was getting xp (while I was not, oddly
enough, but I've seen that happen before with greens). Based on what I saw,
she's probably around 18-20. She is basically a cleric of about lv 14, as
she will cast center, holy armor, and can smite quite a few times. Also she
can heal herself with Healing, so as a lone cleric it can be a long
mana-burning battle, especially if you don't kill the other two pristesses
on the side. She really doesn't have a lot of HP, but the hardest part
about killing her is the two Kerran Pristesses that sit on each side of her.
They can cast light healing on her, but with there being two of them, they
can heal her quite quickly. I'd recommend having Holy Might ready and a
partner, and both be around 20-22 before you venture trying to kill her.
Not that she's really tough, just that her placement can make it tough. :)
