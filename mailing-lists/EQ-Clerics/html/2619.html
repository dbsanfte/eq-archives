I'll beat the High Keep drum ;)

Worth the wait for a group. Has places for you to get great exp from 24 to
35... and that's not the best part. The best thing about Keep is Keep
itself. Guards handy if things get bad... zone handy if they get worse...
vendor handy so you don't hafta waste a scrap of loot if you don't want...
bank handy so you don't hafta throw out coppers to stay unencumbered... and
food and water sold there if you don't feel like conjuring some (like who
does anymore anyway?<G>). The only other thing you could ask from a zone
would be that you were the only one that knew about it! <G>
And while you're waiting for that group... you might be able to solo the
named prisoner in the jail area or the named bandits outside in High Pass.
