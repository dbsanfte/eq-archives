When i am running around the Norrath i always look around the people and
check for their health. If they are fighting i stop and throw a heal on
them. If they are sitting and very low on health i throw a heal on them.
However, if i am in group i rarely check the health of others unless it
looks very obvious that the guy is losing AND all my group members are
safe. Also, if i am hunting in a guild event i am extra careful about
my fellow guildmembers health but still might be looking out for others
who may be in trouble. And for revives i feel sorry for the people
especially if they are binded far away. I have never charged for a revive
and never will. Often i even offer a revive for them if i see them slayed
by Bouncer Hurd or some other foul beast.
