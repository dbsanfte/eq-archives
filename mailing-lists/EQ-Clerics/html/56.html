Hail and well met.
I am currently a lvl 15 cleric an the Rathe server. My
recommendations for a weapon would be to try to find a mob that
carries a staff. Great worn staff is an excellent 2h blunt weapon,
but do not neglect your 1h blunt skill as you will be in dire need of
it at higher lvls. I usually switch between my 1h and 2h to keep both
at an equal lvl.
As far as spells goes I recommend getting as many of them as
possible. With the possible exception of Cure Blindness which I have
yet to find a use for.
