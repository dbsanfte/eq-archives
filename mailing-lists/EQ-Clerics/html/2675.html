Upper Guk is a weird bird. The base level is around 20th level for
the vast portions of the dungeon. The upper floor of upper Guk is
around levels 18-22. Probably somewhere around 20-24 for the lower
level of Upper Guk (down the stairs, or down the slope, depending on
which way you went). There are certain level cut-off points which a
group shouldn't go beyond. One of these is the Shin Lord room. A
group of mid-20s can own upper guk, but once they get to the Shin Lord
room, they would get the foo knocked out of them. The other room is
the Priest room. Again, a group of mid 20s could rock, but then get
slaughtered in this room. It's at these points that a group of high
20s is required.
The ancient croc room is about as far into UG as you can possibly
get. If you look on a map, there is a small room that leads off a
large lake. This little room goes nowhere, and the lake is at the
northern-most part on the map. This is the croc room. From what I
know,the croc is low 30s in level. The other saltwater crocs are
similar level. Apparently, the crocs do not aggro. While I suspect
a full group of mid-high 20s could take the room, getting there is the
main problem. There are two ways to get there. The dungeon makes 2
big circles, basically. The way on the left goes through the Priest
room. A group of mid 20s could not get through this room alive. I
have not been the other way.

Good luck.
