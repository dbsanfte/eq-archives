At level 40 I have no problems with mana.
I'm
using Superior Heal for tanks, and greater healing for caster types.
Even grouped with level 50's I can keep them healed to full without mana
problems during a fight. With 181 wisdom at level 40 I have 1528 mana.
If I have an enchanter with me and he casts insight on
me my mana goes up 1600+. The key is mana management, never heal
someone too early, make sure the full effects of your heal are going to be
used and not wasted. The only exception I use to this rule is if
someone who drops fast starts getting hit. (I.E. Wizards, Enchanters.) I
tend to let loose with my quick heal spell (GH) a lot sooner than I would
on a tank.

Also, if you ever get in a group with a Bard and Enchanter, the mana song,
clarity combo is insane, the mana regens so fast you hardly ever are oom.
