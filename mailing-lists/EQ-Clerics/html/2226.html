It's been my experience that the majority of all mob attacks on a
cleric (due to them healing) can be avoided. Just dont be too quick with
throwing a heal. I never heal anyone in my party if they have taken
lessthan 2 bubs of damage. Depending on what is attacking them I typically
wait till they have taken 3 bubs of damage. This doesn't apply to
wizards, I always heal them almost as soon as they start to drop.


If the puller is running back to the group and will need a heal as soon as
they are in range, I always tell my party to get ready to clear me,
healing someone right as the party starts to engage a mob is almost always
guaranteed to get you attacked.

I always said clerics had the best taunt in the game....:)
