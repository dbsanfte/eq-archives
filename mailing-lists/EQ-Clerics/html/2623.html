Symbol line of spells adds to the persons maximum HP total. The top end one
is Symbol of Naltron, which adds up to 400hps I guess (maybe more, can't
remember). This will make complete heal more effecient on your tanks, as
your 50th lvl warrior will probably have over 3000hps, when you cheal him
for 400 mana, you get a pretty good ratio.

Skin Like Nature (SLN) is the druid version of Resolution basically, but
it's apparently better as it adds more HPs and rumors of a small regen
effect. Work out with your druid how he wants to do his buffing. I always
through resolution on everyone, it's cheap and helps out. On tanks, I put
resolution, shield of words, and symbol spell (usually pinzarn as it's
cheaper 1pp per cast, naltron is better, but costs 10pp, get's expensive).
Depending on situation, I will also throw on resistance debuffs, but usually
only for big events as their duration is mediocre at best...

Hope that helps...
