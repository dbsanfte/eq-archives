If it's any consolation to you, it will get better... =)
Unrest is the lowest level zone of all what I call "high pressure zones"...
by high pressure i mean the trains and the fighting can get pretty intense.
Don't get me wrong, all zones have trains and can have intense fighting...
but certain zones are big xp/loot zones and have much more danger... Unrest
is the lowest level of these zones. As a result, you get a lot of people
there who haven't really "grown up" yet, as it is the first high pressure
zone they've been in. You also have a lot of people who have matured in EQ
and know how to group... but when you do a pick-up group you never know what
you are gonna get...

On the other hand when I am in a group like Cazic where most people are
level 30'ish.. almost all of them have this experience and know their roles
in the group and how to handle different situations. That's not to say you
won't get bad groups in Cazic (or Guk, Sol, etc) but I think your chances of
getting a GOOD group are significantly better... that has been my experience
anyway.

This is just my theory =) Who knows, maybe i'm way off base <grins> but
based on my own experience it seems to be the truth...
