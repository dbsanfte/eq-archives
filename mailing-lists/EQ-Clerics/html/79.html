Subject: Let's set things right.


If you have recieved this message multiple times, I apologise, but it was
necessary to reach everyone possible.

I am writing today to try to correct an injustice that has been in
existance from the date of release. Many may feel that this issue is small
compared to many of the other problems with the game at this point. However,
I feel that I am missing a large piece of the game because it appears as
though I am not allowed to fletch.

From all that I have heard and seen, there are only two places to buy the
necessary components for making arrows: Kelethin and Surefall Glade. As an
evil character (Dark Elf, Troll, Ogre) it is near impossible to get these
items, and even more difficult to get them in quantities which make the
trade skill worth practicing.

Part of the irony in thi situation is that there IS an Ogre in the
Feerrott which sells Bowyering components, but any fletcher will tell you
that you need to increase your skills with arrows before making bows.

In order to help resolve this situation, I wish to show the EQ Developers
that the players do have a voice. I propose a 3 step plan for you to help
solve this problem.


1. In game, /feedback, and state that you would like to see a Merchant who
sells arrow components in a conspicuous place in Neriak, Grobb, or Oggok

2. Go to http://24.0.101.19/eq/ and sign the petition that has been put up.
All that it asks for is a character name, race, class, and server. This list
will be sent straight to the EQ Developers as extra firepower for the cause.

3. Tell all the EQ gamers that you know to follow steps 1 and 2.


In order for this to work, we need everyone's support, not just those of the
darker affiliation. No matter what race or class your characters are, please
participate to show that we can make a difference.

Ankhorahil of the Rathe
