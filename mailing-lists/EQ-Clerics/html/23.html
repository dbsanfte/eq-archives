Well personally, I have been in one duel myself (against a fellow guild
member), and I actually won (WOO HOO). Anyways, he was about 2 or 3
levels ahead of me, and a magician. My advice against spell casters
would be just to fight as normal against them, and try to disrupt their
spelling, and after they bombard you with their spells and drain their
mana, heal yourself. This would make their magic casting in vain, and
give you the advantage. If you have plenty of magic left, smite them,
or use any other powerful magic against them to drop their health down,
and simply melee them until their dead or unconscious (anyway you
prefer).
