The SSoL is a funny thing. It is probably worth more than it seems. Similar
weapons for pure casters (e.g. Water Crystal staff) I've seen selling for
100pp+. The SSoL is basically a mediocre weapon, but with a pretty useful
effect. Many clerics cannot do the quest (or don't want to spend time
building faction). To them, it may be fairly desirable.

I traded my first one to a lvl 30 Teir'dal cleric in return for a pair of
crested spaulders (AC11, +6sta), and I think I got the better deal. So I
would say: make your way over to East Commons or Nektulos and you may get a
good price.
