Hey guys, after 17 straight hours of camping that stupid placeholder for
Gynok Moltar and STILL no Gynok Moltar, here is what I saw:

1. The placeholder spawn consisted of putrid rats, IBSs, regular skels,
ghouls.

2. NOT every placeholder that spawns talks. Some of them do and some of
them don't, and I mean some of the SAME type. Not all putrid rats that
spawn talk, or IBSs or ghouls. I've had some of the rats talk and some not,
some of the IBSs talk and some not. When they talk they say something
like..."Aiewwwwwww, you have disturbed my sleep and now you won't rest" or
something or other like that about disturbing their sleep or rest.

3. The placeholder spawned right at the zone entrance inside Befallen every
18 minutes or so.

4. WATCH OUT FOR TRAINS. Stupid trains seemed to always happen when it
was time for the placeholder to spawn, and several times, I almost LOST the
stupid placeholder (especially if it didn't talk) and had to chase it down
and kill every single dang thing in the train to be sure I got it.

5. If a ghoul spawns, which it frequently does, ROOT it and kill it fast,
cause it runs FAST downstairs. I had to chase a couple of em down to 3rd
level cause they were so fast and almost got away from me. Before my DD
undead spell could cast it was out of range.

Hope this helps. Any other questions, just ask.

Mik
