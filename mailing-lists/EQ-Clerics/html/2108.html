Subject: Unrest is so unrestful!


Does anyone have any tips for solo-ing in Unrest? I was there last
night on the test server. I'm a bar away from 21. I start out in a
small group. On the test server, there are around 15 people max in
Unrest at any given time. So most people either skirt the outside,
or camp the front yard, and have someone pull from the house. We
were pulling from the house, and the place was like grand central
station.
The best time I'd ever had in Unrest was when we had a 30+ level
druid in wolf form pulling for us. We took out the entire second
floor without any problems. But if the person pulling is low enough
level, they get way more than they bargained for.
Well, I had to leave for a bit, and when I came back, the group was
full, so I decided to solo. I cast invis to undead on myself, and I
would pull a ghoul, or a DB from the first floor. The ghouls were
usually blue, sometimes green, but I always got xp for them. The
Lurking mummys were tough, but I could solo those, but usually if I
/yelled for help. But I tried to stick with ghouls and DBs.
I figured that I could mem calm and calm extras, and would just be
able to pull one mob at a time. I got nearly half a bar solo-ing, and
it worked pretty well. I ended up dying 3 times last night though,
from other peoples trains, and I ended up with slightly less xp than
when I started. Anyone have any good advice for solo-ing this place?
