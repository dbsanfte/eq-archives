Well I am still a level 16 cleric. lately I have been hunting in oasis with
me and a level 17 warrior. Pretty much we have figured the system down
pack. He obviously tanks it and I dont do anything except hit it and heal
him. I never use any other spells. I did use Smite on the DW Croc or Ca
but it just seems to waste more mana and we have to wait a bit longer so I
can med. So now we just bang away and when he needs I heal I heal him.
Then there is no wait time for me to med because I have about 75% mana left
after the fight and we do pretty good with me casting Smite. I noticed with
multiple clerics in a group one would usually be the dedicated healer the
other would be the cleric who casts Smite and the emergency healer in case
the other cleric gets hit. We as clerics are primary goal is to keep our
members alive. Sometimes its a hard job to keep.

Hi. I'm new to this mail group but thought I would put in my 2cents here. I
am a level 10 High Elf Cleric on the Rodcet Nife server. I regularly group

job is to sit back and heal. I jump right in with them and watch their
