I have been in unrest since level 17. Our group
always seems to come back there for some reason.
Although, we have had tons of close calls, I have
found this my very very very favorite place for
gaining ooooooodles of experience. I think the main
thing is to have a very well balanced group. At level
17-19 we did mainly yard trash. Death beetles,
ghouls, greater skeletons. From 19-20, we did alot of
dark bones and dry bones. From 20 up (with a full
group of course) we tackled hags and the occasional
barbed boned skeleton (they hit like a semi and if you
pull more than one, you might think about making a run
for zone). Now, at nearly 28, there isnt much that we
cant take. Our group ranges from level 23 to 27 and
we can take on sizable trains.

As Anne Falbo-Ortiz (Topaaz) can tell you, she has had
many mis adventures in this zone. But, for me, it is
fun and I dont plan on leaving till level 29.

The main thing is, learn the ropes of the place. Try
to keep the same group and when looking for tanks,
advertise for one that is experienced in pulling.
Hopefully you'll get one.

To this day, we still stand along the back wall while
the younger groups look up at us with awe in their
eyes. (I got a big head, so sue me). They have become
familiar with our group and know that if they end up
in trouble they can ask us for help.

So far every one of my "gals" (characters) have gone
to unrest as soon as they earned the right of binding.

SOrry for ramblin =)
