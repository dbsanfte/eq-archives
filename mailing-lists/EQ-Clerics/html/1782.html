Hello,

I'll clear up a lot of junk that you might find on everelore. Gynok
definately spawns at the zone point. He starts there, if he is being
camped he dies there, if not he slaughters 1st floor players then works
his way down to the third lvl. If you see a Ghoul spawn there, that is
the place holder so kill it. Otherwise the Ghoul will run Gynoks
pattern, being the only truly mobile spawn in the zone. Gynok won't
spawn while that Ghoul is on the loose.

Read everelore.com for theories as to what makes Gynok spawn. My 29
druid has killed him twice by luck. Once after Gynok spawned on my
Clerics head while medding at the entrance, the other time the druid
picked it up on tracking and gated to the spawn point and followed the
corpses to the 2nd lvl. It was lucky in that 90% of my time in Befallen
that spawn is camped.

Don't think a 22 cleric can solo Gynok. Clerics are lousy soloists
although Gynok is undead.

I traded one of my rings for a Guardian Shield (+8 wisdom). Don't think
i have the patience to camp Mitty for two gold rings while not getting
xp.
