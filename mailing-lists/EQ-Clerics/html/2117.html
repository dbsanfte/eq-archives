I spent some time soloing there, but I still prefer grouping. I was using a
tactic I called "The Cleric assassin". Basically sneak in invis, pick one
target, kill it fast and get out ASAP to rest. I liked better killing them
on site, but it's not possible when they're not alone, you then have to be
very careful pulling, using calm, etc. Don't pull anywhere close to the
trains path.
At 21 stay on the first floor, look for mummies and dries mainly, but dark
boned are still good. Ghouls are not that good but you might have to clear
them. Keep up all your buffs up (daring, armor, symbol, endure fire and
magic). If a druid or shaman is around try to get a SOW.
That's pretty much it. Don't be too greedy. Exp is good for each kill, so
get out each time it doesn't look good.
