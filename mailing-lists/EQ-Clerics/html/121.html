Hail and well met brother Cleric,
I play a high elf Cleric lvl 15 now on The Rathe and have a few
suggestions for you. First in Butcherblock there is the chessboard
with 5 spawns of undead pawns, bishops, rooks and the king. Spawns
above pawns are random and some have spells and pets so be careful
until you learn what is there. Second also in Butcherblock is the Orc
Oracles. Orc centurions and oracles spawn here. There are 3 spawn
points here. Third is the bandit camps in Lesser Faydark. I don't
like the one with the Nybright sisters because everyone goes there.
There is a lesser known camp with 3 bandit spawn points. These
bandits spawn bronze weapons sometimes so you can make some money
here. The orcs have rawhide leather and some run of the mill weapons
and armor. The chessboard is mostly bone chips but sometimes rusty
weapons. At your lvl I would suggest bringing a friend along till you
get used to these places or offer your services there. Hope this
helps.
