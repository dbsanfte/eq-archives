Howzabout Oasis!

4 levels of gators (low to high): caimans, crocodiles, deepwater caimans,
deepwater crocodiles.
undead: dry bones, ghouls, mummies
plus the occasional 'water boys' (madmen who drop lots of water flasks)
or on the west wall, orcs!

I soloed there up into my low 20's..........be warned, the loot is VERY thin
but the XP is great...

Just watch out for the Sand Giants and idiots trying to fight spectres and
training them up/down the beach while trying to zone....
