> Paw and Mistmoore are still too high/tough.

Mistmoore is NOT too tough. I just gained 25 thru 27 there, and previous to
that I gained 22 thru 24 in Cazic.

All you need to do is get in a good group and hold the "corner" in MM (this
is just around the tower, right before you reach the tunnel to the
Graveyard.), from here the puller can clear out the tunnel and the lake/hill
spawns with the occasional garg from the tower above. If you are in a high
level group 24-30 (avg lvl 27), then you could easily hold the GY as well.
Just clear the tunnel first, and then move to the GY. Remember to sit just
to the *right* inside the entrance. There is one "glyphed" spawn here and
two across the yard. Then there are some behind the building, but you can
pull one to two of these at a time. Also, the nice benefit is every 25-30
mins, the Avenging Caitiff (lv30) spawns and a good gorup of 26+ could
easily take him solo.

In Cazic, just hold the yard with a low-to-mid 20s group and pull *lots* of
stuff from the Maze. Not much to it, just make sure the puller knows
his/her way around the Maze.

An Enchanter who knows how to use Mez can be invaluable in both of these
circumstances, because if your puller or the group next to you's puller
yanks too much from either the GY or Maze, then you could actually handle
them one at a time.

One a side note, the loot in MM is poor unless you move to the GY, where as
in Cazic almost all but the lizzies that /con green to you and perhaps the
Warders, will drop an FS weapon if they wield a weapon (which can be quite
frequently).

Hope this helps some =)
