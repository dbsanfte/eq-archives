Yes, to get to Steamfont from Felwithe, just follow the path to lesser
and stay on the main path. There is a shortcut, since the path kind
of takes a long way to get there, but if you stay on the main path
and don't take any side-roads, you'll get right to Steamfont. You
should be able to fight near the entrance, or follow the path to
Ak'anon, the gnome city. The guards can help if you get into trouble.
There are 3 dervish camps in N.Ro. If you leave them alone, though,
they are are indifferent. So if you don't bother them they won't
bother you. At derv camp #1 (east-most camp), Dorn B'Dynn hangs out,
and will make mince out of you (he is a Dark elf), so stay away from
that one. Picking off the random stuff in N. Ro is probably good. I
see a lot of people around 10-12 in North Ro. You can also sell the
giant snake fangs to young spell-casters for maybe a plat.
