After comparing my class to others, I must say I am happy being a
Cleric. The best weapon we could get (in my opinion) would have to be
the observer's staff. It's 12 DMG, 35 Dly, 5+ Wis and 5+ Int. That's
pretty close to what some of you have been looking for. I am quite
happy with this weapon, but I must say I really don't need a better one.

The reason for this is that as I gain more levels, and venture into the
harder dungeons (Cazic Thule etc.) I realise that my duty as a healer is
above all else. I find myself healing our tanks and casters more often
than anything else. However in Cazic, since the lizzies are smart,
they'll tend to attack the healer on the first or second heal you make
(if there is more than one), so my advise to you, would be to
concentrate on buffing yourself with armour, wisdom, and invulerablity
spells. ;)

I personnaly would love to see Cleric only weapons. Even if it isn't
the greatest, we all, as healers would have something to show that would
be unique to us.
