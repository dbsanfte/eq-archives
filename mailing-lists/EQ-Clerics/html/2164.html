The best suggestion that I can give you is to keep pushing forward... when I was a cleric in my teens I was very tempted to make a new character because in all honesty, it just wasn't very much fun at that time. Once you get your level 19 spells though, things become a lot more fun. You up your DD spell to smite which does 80-some damage per hit and you get what has become one of my favorite spells, Holy Might. Holy Might does 30-some damage and puts a pretty long stun on the MOB. You also get nice buffs at 19, Bravery and Spirit Armor. Then at 24 you can add Greater Healing to all that... The teens really suck for a cleric, but now that I'm level 27 I wouldn't trade my cleric for any other class... stick with it and just keep trying to find groups... because it is in groups that the clerics power shows. We simply aren't meant to play solo as clerics...
