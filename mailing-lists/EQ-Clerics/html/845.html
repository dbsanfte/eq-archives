Anne,

From my understanding here are the issues:

1. Don't respond with the original message attached.

Pro: Saves bandwidth. People don't have to download 10K worth of message
to see 2K worth of response.

Con: Someone coming in the middle of the thread won't know what you are
talking about. As you can see, I am a member of this believe. I think that
if you use good judgement then you won't have to worry about it. Just don't
quote a 2 page e-mail just to make a 2 sentence response.

2. Don't use anything but plain text.

Pro: A lot of people on these lists use *nix or some other form of test
only mail readers. Things like html and RTF only detract from the message
and sometimes make in unreadable. I support this believe. I would rather
have everyone be able to read my message than to have it look good to the
30% who can support html.

Con: It makes the post look better (sometimes). I don't think this is a
concern.

I believe those are the two issues that concerned you. This is my
understanding so it may not be totally right. One other thing, you may want
to make a more descriptive title to your thread. Something like 'List
Etiquette' would be appropriate. I would change it now but it will screw
with people who are deleting this thread with a filter.

Hope this helps.
