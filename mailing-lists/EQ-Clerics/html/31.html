I have had a few experiences dueling, due mainly to the fact that I don't
abide with kill thieves. All three times I came out on top because I was
prepared. I sat down and memorized flash of light and root along with my
regular arsenal of spells. Those two spells can turn the tide on any fight
up to 3 levels above you. First you blind them, that way they have no Idea
where you are. Then you root them, walk around behind them and beat on them
or stand back and nuke them. The tactic I used on a spell caster was
simple. I challenged him and as soon as he accepted I cast divine aura on
myself. He mana hosed himself in the first 25 second and I had free reign
to beat him soundly about the head and shoulders. Just my experiences in
dueling.
