Clerics can easily beat any pure type caster class of their same level.
All you have to do is cast stun/ and the best offensive spell at your
level.

What i typically do is stun the chaster, then run behind them quickly at
an angle, then start nuking them. When they unstun they take some time
to turn around to look for you, when you notice this, stun them again.

It really works well. I havent dueled too much but i'm 9 and 1 vs casters
in duels.

> I have a few thoughts on PvP and clerics. I play a cleric on Vallon Zek and
> occationally battle other players. In my experience, clerics are not good
> offensively, but rather good defensively, especially grouped.
> Once a necromancer cast dots on me (engulfing darkness and boil blood I
> believe), plus fear. I dropped HPs rapidly, but managed to cast a couple of
> heals on myself, and then the DOTs wore off.
>
> Can clerics generally survive a fight versus casters by healing themselves?
> If I cast 4 greater heals on myself I can heal for 1200+ damage. Although I
> am not sure, I doubt a wizard will have the mana to deliver 2000 damage at
> my level (24), and some of his damage might be resisted. Assuming I have
> full armour, I should be able to root and melee the caster to death.
>
> Other useful spells are blindness and stun. I use the low level variants of
> stun since they take less mana. Divine aura is useful since the attacker
> doesn't always observe that I am invulnerable, especially casters, since
> they don't get any damage information (="blabla hit by non melee for 140
> damage" etc).
>
> Buying an egg shaped pumice is also a good investment, although it is quite
> expensive. It is a magic item sold by some vendors (in Neriak, the one near
> the bank) and has 5 charges of "pillage enchantment", that will cancel
> several effects like charm/DOTs (and buffs) at the same time.
