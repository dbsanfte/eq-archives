Hammer of Wrath is a summon spell that you'll get with your 3rd round of
spells, at level 9. It's 1h blunt, 6 damage and 32 delay. It's also
considered a magical weapon, so it will enable you to kill willowisps. The
thing about it is, the hammer is no rent, no drop, so that means every time
you leave the game you'll have to summon a new one in the next time, and
that you can't give the hammer to anybody else. That help ya out? :)
