These places can be very tough, but when I was in level 35 I was getting
mega XP in Lower guk and in SolusekB. Have to of course be in a good group
for both, as those places can get out of hand easily.
In Lower Guk, you can hunt in the bedroom, undead side. In Sol B I would
reccomend hunting right near one of the zones to Sol A, there are 3 I
believe. Don't fight at the main zone to Lavastorm that is train central
and you will get some pretty nasty stuff there. Both of these dungeons are
really crowded most of the time, when I was 35 it wasn't to bad, so I don't
know how bad fighting for spawns would be now.

You can also see about getting into a Avatar room group in Cazic. Perhaps
Deepwater Goblins and Spectres in Oasis and Spectres in Feerott.

These are the places I remember being in that level area i hope this helps

But hell levels are going to take awhile to get through especially if you
don't have a lot of time to play often. The fastest I have seen anyone get
through 35 was 3 days and that was with playing constantly ( he wasn't
working at te time and was home all day). Then on the other hand it took our
group Paladin I think a month of playing each night and a lot of the
weekends to get through 35.
