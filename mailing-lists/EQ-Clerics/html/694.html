Can I be hypothetical here for a second?

Imagine you are walking down the street, a car hits you, you die, and
miraculously, a doctor is able to revive you and bring you back to life! Are
you going to have full health? No way! Are you going to be extremely weak?
You betcha!

Granted, it was a REALLY nice thing to be able to be resurrected and have
your full mana and HP on your character, and being ready to go after you
loot, but if EQ is about Roleplaying, and about trying to be semi-realistic
in some cases, ressurect was not broken in the last patch, but instead, it
was made to be a little more beleiveable/realistic.
