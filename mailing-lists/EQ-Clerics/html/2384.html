I think 14th level spells are like 7 plat apiece. Or thereabouts.
You will want to buy:
healing
smite
bind affinity
expulse undead
invisibility to undead (it's really cheap, anyway)
cancel magic
and symbol of transal (+hp buff)

Symbol of Transal will require cat's eye agates to cast. They are
2gp apiece. So, pick up a few of those as well. Your group members
will thank you for this buff.

The less useful spells are halo of light, endure disease and endure
cold.

As a side note, if you turn in a rabid grizzly pelt to Priestess
Caulria in N. Qeynos, she will give you endure disease (a 7 plat
value)
