Whoa! You must have had a really bad group. I lived in Unrest from level 16
until level 20, made lots of loot, didn't die any more than I did elsewhere,
and came out with a full set of bronze armor. I moved on only because I
thought I was getting a little stale, and I knew I was going to have to be
level 24 or 25 before I could safely fight in the barroom or the tower, or
level 30 before I could go to the basement. And when I hit those levels, I'm
a'comin' back!

Unfortunately, a lot of other people like Unrest, too, which is why it isn't
unusual to see 100 people in that zone on Fennin Ro, evenings =(.

It's true, the trains can be amazing. But one nice thing about Unrest is
that the lawn is so wide open, you can often get out of the way of the
train, which you can't do in Befallen or Guk, or even Crushbone often. It's
awe inspiring to watch two festering hags, a werebat, a greater darkbone or
two, a couple of mortuary fungi, a tentacle terror, the dwarven spirit and a
gaggle of tormented dead, darkbones, lurking mummies and death beetles
thunder past you, headed for the exit!

== Gilead, dwarven cleric of Fennin Ro
