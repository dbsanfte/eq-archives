I've seen this strategy before. You need three spells. (I used this with
Mummies in LFay) Root, spook undead, and ward/expulse undead.. Stand back,
root it. If it runs at you, or when it breaks free, spook it, then root
again. Use your nuke to take it down. You can usually take down yellow MOBs
without taking more than 1-2 bubbles of damage. (You'll probabily have to
tank the last little bit when you run out of mana)
