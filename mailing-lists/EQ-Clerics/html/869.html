blind, fear, holy might, smite, and root. all are good also remember to
have pro magic, poison and disease up. I know thier DoTs use it.. ya might
want to try and get the SSoL before the fight as well as that will help
remove any DoTs that the shaman might put on ya.

Get Stun, and Holy might in mem (if ya have the time that is) and only use
them when they start to cast... stun is a fast casting spell. holy might is

a little longer. blind works well. If it is used right before the fear
they will run away and not know where.. that could be good for ya.

Root might not be all that good for ya as he does not need to hit you to
kill you... also have dispell magic ready, just remember it will start at
the top.
