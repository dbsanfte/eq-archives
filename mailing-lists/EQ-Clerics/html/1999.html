Kill a rabid grizzly in qeynos hills and turn the pelt in to Priestess
Caulria and get a 14th level spell, Endure Disease.

Kill the zombie (infrequent spawn) in the sewers and get a +5 charisma
amulet (order of thunder).

Also, kill the skeleton knight in Qeynos sewers yields a +5 hit point
ring (limestone ring).

You can also buy a +5 charisma (silk evening tunic) from the lady in
front of Lion's Mane in Qeynos (looks like a cloth tunic). It doesn't
show anything on the character, but I adorn it and my +5 chr amulet
before selling things and buying things at any merchant. That +10
charisma really helps. I was going to shoot for a +10 chr raider cap
from Rivervale, but they changed that quest, and I can't wear it now.

You could do the bug collection box quest from Rivervale, which might
yield a bracer of beetlekind AC 2 Strength +3, Fire +10, Intelligence
-3 Strengthen (10). I'm working on that one now. All I lack is the
wasp wing. Coincidentally, I found giant scarab egg sacks for sale
in Kaladim from the guy at the entrance to the warrior guild. I don't
know if it was because someone sold it to him, or what...

Not sure what else..
