Scarecrows in West Karana.
You'll probably need a friend or two, though. You won't be able to
solo them at this level, unless you're really good. Another REALLY
good thing is rabid wolves and grizzlies in Qeynos hills. Turn in
one rabid grizzly pelt and I guarantee you you will be addicted to
killing this things. That is some amazing experience. And the loot
is good, too. The rabid grizzly spawns in the valley just north of
the guard tower. There are usually two or three in the zone. Just
make sure you watch out for Holly and Cros. They will kill you if
they see you attack them.
