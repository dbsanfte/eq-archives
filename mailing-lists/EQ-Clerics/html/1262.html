only ones in Erudia, and likewise the only ones in Kerra Isle. (not
wisp isle).
A lot of Test regulars don't want more people. Me, I welcome it.
Sometimes it's hard to find a group. Sure test may be somewhat
unstable, but we get all the latest patches, and in the evenings its
fairly reliable. We deal with it. But we are a happy lot, and we
just sit back and laugh at you people that have to deal with camping
issues, or kill-stealing, or lag.
Heh.

different executable. At most, there are a like 250 people. A word
of warning, though, Race War is being tested, but only a few people
are participating in it.
