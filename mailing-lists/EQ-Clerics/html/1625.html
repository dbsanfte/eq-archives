ACtually, a manastone trades 60hp, for 20mana.

during combat, the rengen/chloroplast and manastone combination work pretty
well (as in if Im -kinda- low on mana, I manastone away my first 2 bubb's of
health to get mana, and let that health regen.)

Also, as clerics, we are the only ones that can "powermed" with the
manastone. What I mean by this (and when it really becomes useful), is at
level 44, you get your last 2 hp buffs, Resolution and Symbol of Naltron.
Cast both of these on you (to you get you the most hitpoints that you
possibly can). So next time you are at 10% mana, manastone your health down
to almost nothing, then complete heal yourself. Manastone again down to
almost nothing, complete heal. Repeat. You will be to 80-85% mana in under
a minute... great way to get mana very quickly, and is something ONLY a
cleric can do :) (without aid by others)..

Just make sure to pay attention when you manastone your health down that low
(especially if you are getting packet loss).. a manastone death DOES take
away experience..
