Here is a rough summary of where I got most of my experience on my
quest to get 50.

I started as a halfling cleric.

Level Zone

0-12 Misty thicket
12-16 Beholder Maze
16-21 Oasis
21-25 Unrest
25-29 S.Karana/Lake Rathe
29-34 Mistmoor
34-47 Solusk B
47-50 Specters in Oasis/Ocean of Tears, Tiny Miller in W.Karana
50 L.Guk/Hate/Fear

After Misty thicket I hardly soloed at all until I hit 47 then pretty
much soloed to 50. Tiny Miller is great exp if you don't mind being
hated in Qeynos and the Karanas (j boots are strongly recommended).
You can also buff the young clerics doing scarecrows while fighting
Tiny :) .
I was also always broke until I started fighting in Mistmoor then the
steady income started coming in.
