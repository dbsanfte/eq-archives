Subject: A quick way across W Karanas zone.........


To make your trip from the Karanas to Qeynos Hills shorter (and avoid
walking across one of the widest zones in the game), here is what you
do.........I have done this many times..........

WARNING: Do this at your own risk, it this makes your corpse disappear,
don't holler at me!

1. Zone into W Karanas from N Karanas along the southern edge (heading
west), you should be walking along the top of the ridge right by the river.
2. As soon as you zone into W Karanas, walk about 20 feet forward and take a
90 degree turn to the left (now heading south)
3. Walk down into the river and keep swimming south until you hit an
"invisible wall" and are no longer moving south.
4. Start moving slightly to your left, scraping along the "invisible wall",
you are heading back east now.
5. When you get close to the N Karanas zone line, you will "fall out of the
world", and be placed on the complete OTHER (west) side of the W Karanas
zone, very close to the Qeynos Hills tunnel.
6. Voila! You have avoided walking across the zone. Again, don't blame me
if something bad happens..................Verant may fix this one
day.........

I am unaware of a way to reverse the process, just gate back or
whatever.............
