The one that dropped off Nag the other night says "Dragon Tooth", no color
listed. It is designated a magic item and expendable, with one charge of
Death (summons a 49 level necro pet ONE time). Talk is that it is an
awesome item to powerlevel a low character, provided 5 minutes after you
summon the pet, you don't see the message "Server coming down now, get to a
safe place and log out now." or you don't go LD or disconnect from the game.

The ones that are colored are the dragon scales...the Red Dragon Scale and
the White Dragon Scale, which are used for quests to obtain items. Nicole
got her awesome cloak by doing the quest, and one of the items needed was a
White Dragon Scale, which we got off Vox. Nag drops the Red Dragon Scale.
