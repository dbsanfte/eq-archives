Symbol of Ryltan requires bloodstone and adds about 150 HP. It's a great
spell, it saves lives. You can often get the Bloodstone in lavastorm for a
few gold each from people hunting there, but a lot of times it is more
convienient to go in to town and just buy a bunch of it. I found it much
more affordable than Symbol of Transal based on money availability at the
time.
