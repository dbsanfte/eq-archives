I would definitely say that 14th is the bare minimum that a cleric
should be going in there. I went at 13th and vowed not to return
until I was at least 14th. As it turns out, I journeyed to Qeynos at
that time, and now I just made 16th and haven't been back. I have
heard that the aggro range was increased greatly, though.
I think there are a lot more options for players at this level aside
from just Unrest. I made 16th killing Scarecrows in West Karana.
Solo'ing blues was iffy, because they hit so hard. With at least 1
partner, I was generally ok. And with 2, we could fight yellows.
They give great xp, btw, and for some reason, they don't aggro, even
though they "scowled at me, ready to attack." So you can walk right
up to them.
When my friends weren't online, I hunted Tovax Vmar in Qeynos Hills,
occasional wisps, and the barbarian poacher (forget his name).
My friends found Everfrost, near Permafrost, to be a great place to
level from 13th to 14th. Fighting Orcs, and mammoth calves. You can
get xp here til about 17th. When my group all gets on again, we are
talking about going into Splitpaw. When we all get to our high teens,
we might fight West Karana Bandits. (we need to get revenge on them,
anyway) There is also a ghoul camp in WK, but I haven't fought there
yet, but I suspect it might be a prime location to hunt at the higher
teen levels, too.

So, bottom line is, there are plenty of places to hunt aside from
Unrest. Many of these I mentioned are not very popular, either, and
could be hunted without much interference.
