I personally prefer Mistmoore over Unrest in almost every case, now. One
notable exception, however, is that as a cleric you will be _very_ hard
pressed to solo here effectively. To judge how well you would do with the
weakest mobs, go into Crushbone and try to solo D'Vinn without his dagger.
If you can kill him easily, then you'll have no problem there with a single
Initiate Familiar. Problem is, most of the spawns come in at least twos,
some come in threes.
And anything under 10 people killing things around the base of the tower
will result in trains at least as ugly as those in Unrest. At level 20, you
may want to stick to Unrest for a level or two. Mistmoore has an ugly
tendency to turn on a healer the moment they finish their first heal spell
in a battle. And when a blue initiate familiar is beating on me double
rounds, sometimes hitting for 31-40, it makes my job at healing a little
tougher. With a proper group, though, you can gain xp super-quick. I made
24 from 23 in at most 6 played hours, at least an hour of which was sitting
in Lesser Fay waiting for the Ancillae train on the other side to clear out.
Keep in mind that some things there can see invis, too. Hope that helps ya.
