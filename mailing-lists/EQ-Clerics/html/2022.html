Kill Dylin Fireshine in Highpass Hold. Get page 30 from him.

Go to Qeynos and find Moodoro Finharn in North Qeynos either in or
near Crows Pub. Say "I want page 34". He will make you play a card
game. for 4gp he will give you a card. If the card is the jester,
give it back to him. He will give you page 34. Give him 2gp and he
will give you a 2-slot book. Put both pages in the book, and hit
combine. That's it.
The quest actually originates in Erudia, but someone on here tipped
us off that you didn't actually have to go to Erudia to get the book.
That, by giving Moodoro the 2gp, he would give it to you. With that
little tip, it's incredibly easy to get, and well worth it.
