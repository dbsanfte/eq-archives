Subject: Complete Heal


Does anyone else think Complete Heal is an almost useless spell in
combat situations?

This spell has one of the longest delays for a cleric so it is very
dangerous to use in combat situations. I think the only cleric spell
that takes longer to cast is shield of words. The only time I found
this spell usefull was when I was grouped with a L50 warrior since he
had so many hp superior heal was only healing one bub of his health.

Last night I was in LGuk and our group was camping the froglik King.
Now this guy hits hard and fast (140 double attacks) and I was casting
superior heals one after another just to keep our tank (L50 sk) alive.
There is no way I could have gotten off a complete heal during these
fights.

It also doesn't make much sence to me that it takes longer to
completely heal someone than it does to ressurect them.

I think all of our healing spells need reduced casting times, It's
very frustrating when your tank looses their last 2 bubs of health
when your trying to get a heal off just to see thoes blue sparkles
around their corpse.

Just something to think about.
