What 1 point of mana converts too depends on the level.
The formula for mana calculation is as follows
((wis/5) +2) * level

So if you at level 20, have 150 wisdom your mana would be

(150/5) = 30
( 30+2) = 32
(32*20) = 640

You could take this further and determine what a +5 wisdom item would be
(155/5) = 31
( 31+2) = 33
(33*20) = 660

So a +5 wisdom item added to your inventory would give you 20 more mana.
It doesnt seem like much but trust me, it does add up, esp at higher
levels.

That same +5 wisdom item at level 40 would give you 40 more mana
and at level 50, 50 more mana.


(I typed this up real quick so if my math is wrong please correct me :) ).
