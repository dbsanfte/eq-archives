well, i must say it doesn't get any easier to choose as it goes along....I
always have gate and divine aura memd....a DD spell, a healing spell, at
least one buff, and typically stun.....Alot depends on the group you are
with and whether you are hunting undead or not....also whenever i get a new
heal spell keep 2 memorized as the new one usually take s a lot of mana, for
example when i turned lvl 24 i got greater healing....i am now lvl 31...from
24 until now i used both healing and greater healing...at this lvl my mana
is much greater as well as the amount of HP my tanks have, so reg healing
does not help much except with the pure casters since they don't have as
many HP.....
I often will alternate buffs throughout, cause i can change them quickly
while medding, i will beef everyone up with bravery sit med and mem gaurd,
then cast on the party with that, so i have more room for spell that are
going to be used during the battle.
Best advice i think it to try different things, see what you're most
comfortable with and you will find a balance with your spells and what you
require to function as an effective cleric.
