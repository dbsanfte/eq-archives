Unrest is VERY easy to solo if you're a cleric and can provide some great
experience. My lvl 22 halfling cleric has been soloing there since lvl 14
and the experience is great. I would honestly tell you to start killing dry
bones on the 2nd floor or stick to lurking mummies. Dark bones and ghouls
don't give near the experience they used to for someone of your lvl.
Lurking mummies sometimes con even to a lvl 21 if i remember correctly which
is always good experience, and the dry bones will almost always be yellow or
the occasional white. Either way they both give great experience, and if
you have a decent character you will be able to solo each of them without
any trouble. You can also solo carr ghouls now, but there is about a 30%
chance of ya having to make a run for the zone if things go bad with these
guys. No.. I'm not lieing to ya as many people on this list will say. A
cleric of your lvl CAN solo yellow and *low* red undead. Yellow are very
easy though and the exp bar will shoot up.
