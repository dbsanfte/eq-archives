While I'm at it, here's a good newbie tip :
the skill of divination, for all clerics levels 13 and below have no
really useful spells that use this skill. The only two are flash of
light and sense magic. When you get to 14th level and finally get
invisibility to undead - a useful divination spell, your divination
skill is liable to be in the single-digits. Can you say "fizzle,
fizzle, fizzle"? So, do what I just did, and put an item on your
cursor, and cast sense magic, over and over and over again. It will
go up. Before that, I tried casting flash of light on myself and then
cure blindness, but that took forever! You can also cast flash of
light on another player, and it won't affect them. Either of these
tricks will work, but the sense magic thing is much easier and
quicker. I finally maxed mine at 70, so I'm ready to go for my next
level!
