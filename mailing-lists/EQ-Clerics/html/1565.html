From lvl 9 on I went to the mummy shrine in Lesser F. Great Experience until
14 or 15. Be careful at first what you pull. By standing in the right spot,
use of root and ward you should be able pull and take yellows easily. Use
the wood elf camp as your base and you can run back and forth for safety.
Often there will be other clerics around that can pitch in if things get out
of hand. Be careful though as you are the lowest level where I would risk it
and even the orcs round there might beat you to a pulp.

Good luck
