I play a lvl 31 DE Cleric on Erollisi Marr. Now my faction is good in
Highpass/Highkeep since I levelled from 18-29 almost constantly in those
two zones.. My faction in Qeynos isn't brilliant.. I'm not attacked
but I can only sell to Tubal Weaver (who I guess has Highpass Faction
not Qeynos) Everyone else refuses to sell to me. I guess I need to
kill more gnolls (yawn).

Anyway I have just completed the Testament of Vanear quest yesterday and
only need the gold ring for the paw quest. So it is definately possible
for us to do it. Though I have been of the habit of trying to up my
factions in all cities I can since I know how much of a pain it would be
having to go back to Neriak to sell each time.

Out of the two cities I would probably work on your faction in
Highpass/HighKeep first.. since by doing so you are also upping your
faction with Corrupt Qeynos Guards (and possibly the normal Qeynos
guards but I can't be sure on that)

Just kill orcs/gnolls until the guards let you in.. took me a few days
to get enough faction to be able to sell there..

For the paw quest you don't really need good faction at all.. it is all
completable without having to go into any big city that you would get
killed in.. You have to go under one.. but as long as you manage to
sneak into the Qeynos aquaducts via the hidden entrance you don't need
to enter the city.. Also get a teleport to Tox forest when it comes to
camping Mitty it would be worth the cost.. fast and no sneaking through
Erudin/Qeynos..
