Screaming Maces are good. I used a BF hammer from around 14th to
around 19th. I really liked it. When I hit 19th I bought a screaming
mace. The quest for the mace is really annoying. I tried to get one
myself, and found one to buy. Oddly enough, 2 weeks later, someone
gives me an Enamelled Black Mace. :)
Personally, at your leve, I would suggest the Shining Star of Light.
An old friend sold me my screaming mace for 50pp, but I suspect they
are still close to 100pp on most servers. You can have the SSOL for
30 gold, and a bit of work. Stats are not quite as good 6/28, I
think, but the cleanse effect is nice. I would suggest to get this
and to save your money. If you are not solo-ing as much, your weapon
matters less and less.
Also, you'll want to start looking at getting a Testament of Vanear,
instead of the SBS.
