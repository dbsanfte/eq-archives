Hail fellow Clerics,


I have witnessed and read about many adventurers dueling to
test their skills or to settle a dispute. However, I have
never seen a Cleric duel nor have heard of one.

ourselves to Take a life? Clerics are far from being cowards,

So I ask, are there any dueling clerics out there that can

adventureres that are of lvls higher than yourself.
