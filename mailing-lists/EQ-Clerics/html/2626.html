Pinzam use jasper, only less than a plat, Naltron use peridot, it runs for
around 10pp. Make sure you always cast resist magic, resist cold or fire
on the dragon raids. Resolution gives about same ac/hp bonus as SLN, but
it takes much much less mana. However, SLN also gives regen. Druid has
no armor buff, shield of words is the best ac buff in the game, but takes a
lot of mana to cast (300). Make sure you know cleric's role in a dragon
raid. Mostly do heals and resurrects and buffs, staying out of dragon's
range. Also, wear as much resist magic, cold fire stuff as possible. That
extra 40 wisdom don't do you any good when you are running around
mindlessly after you take a dragon roar. Also, Vox raid is different drom
Naggie raid, as clerics are needed for their stuns to stop her complete
healing. I would recommand going to www.castersrealm.com to check out all
the cleric spells, before you do anything. 6 death for dragon raid is a
lot, most people only die 3 times max on average raid. Make sure you have
one of the divine or both memorized and active at all times. Just a few
things i want to point out, for now.
