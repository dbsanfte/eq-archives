Guard is an upgrade to the Holy Armor/Spirit Armor line of spells, and can be used in conjunction with the Center/bravery/Daring, etc line of spells.

Symbol of Rytalm... I don't know the exact HP increase but I believe it's near 100 HP...
and this may depend on your level. I think that I casted it on myself when I first got
it at level 24 and it gave me 80HP... but I"ve casted it on others and they say they got
more.. I'd say around 100 give or take a few =)

As for the greatest Taunt... root usually works pretty good... other than that I'd say Greater Healing is one of the best Taunts in the game (a usually unwanted effect). I have also had MOB's turn on me for using Holy Might. If it succeeds they take enough of a beating from others that they don't worry about me, but often if they resist it they will turn on me.

You say that 1HB isn't going to cut it, but I actually think it's best to stick with 1HB. I used a staff of observers for a while, but I have found that I make myself most useful by maximizing my wisdom and I can do that much better with a 1HB (especially if you get a tainted morning star with +6 wis) and holding a charred guardian shield (+9 wis) or a paw of Opella (+9 Wis) or a Testament of Vanear (+10 wis, +10 mana). Reguardless of whether you use a 1HB or 2HB you aren't going to be able to do significant damage compared to your tanks and wizards, so don't worry about it... instead concentrate on maximizing your wisdom thus giving you more mana to do what you do best... Heal! =)

You CAN increase your 2HB to where you are now with 1HB but the increases will come VERY slowly.. especially if you try to improve 2HB on lower level monsters. Use practice points if you really want to do this. One other side not, we only get 4 skill improvements per level for melee skills like 1HB and 2HB... that's a penalty to clerics... tanks get 5 skill imps per level in those skills (which is one more reason not to worry about doing melee damage... after level 10 or so you are going to be way behind other classes in the melee skills).

As always, everything i've said is IMHO =)
