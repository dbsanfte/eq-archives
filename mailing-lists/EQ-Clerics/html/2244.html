You're right about it being hard to travel around, but I look at it from a
RP'ing perspective. Like the first time I snuck through Rivervale....what a
rush! (actually, there was no sneaking going on, got a SoW and booked to the
zone) BUT, as most of you know, the best dungeons in the game are accessed,
or close to, EVIL cities (Cazic, Guk, SolA/B, PoF). This has saved me more
times than I can count now. ie. Cazic Thule: Big train comes and everybody
zones to Feerrott...guess what? Bouncer Hurd is on the other side slapping
down every good race that zones and forces them to go back to that nasty
train of lizzies. But not me, 'cause me and Hurd go way back. Heck, I even
buffed and healed him for NOT killing me (j/k) Anyway, there are definate
advantages of being evil at higher levels. From a RP'ing view, I love it. If
you have never played an Evil race, I would suggest giving it a try to see
the opposite side of the coin. Nothing better than running for your life as
soon as you get off the boat in Butcherblock ;)
