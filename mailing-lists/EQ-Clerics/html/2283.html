The game has changed a lot since I started (first week of release) so
the items I had at various levels are a lot different that what people
have today. I didn't know anyone that was high level that could help
me out as there were very few high level chars and places like lower
guk were largely unexplored. Not to mention that permafrost wasn't
even itemized.

When I was L10 I hadn't ventured out of misty thicket and was wearing
patchwork/cloth with no magic items at all (except my hammer of
wrath).

By L20 I was fighting in oasis and was wearing mostly banded armor
with a few patchwork pieces. Still no jewelry beyond dervish rings.
The one good item I did have was the crook from lockjaw when we were
actually able to get the kill.

By L30 I think I had mostly bronze plate from fighting in unrest and
lake rathe and possibly some +4wis +2st rings, BI medallion and
polished bone bracers (can't exactly remember). Also might have had
an enameled mace and mistmoor shield by this time.

In the low 30's is when I started to make some money from mistmoor and
when I moved to sol b at L34. Then the good stuff started coming in
:).

Now compare this to what people have out there today and you can tell
it's a lot different and I'm not one to judge yourself on. I've
always felt that it's not the items that make you a good cleric.

I sometimes feel sorry for new players out there now. There are so
many twinks running around in awesome equipment it must be hard seeing
someone that con's green wearing way better equipment than you are.

Just remember, A naked L30 cleric could beat the crap out of a L20
cleric with awesome equipment :)
