Well, I successfully attained my ToV this weekend, and I am a DE Cleric. So,
it is possible for us evils to do this quest ;) The hardest part of the
quest, of course, was waiting on Dynil (sp?) to spawn in Highpass. I was
hunting in SKarana, so I made frequent trips to and from HKeep, and on one
such trip lo and behold, he was standing there. After pimp-slapping him and
taking his page of the book, I met up with a friend in Qeynos who invised me
and I went to Moordoro's place. Only took me 7 tries (28gp) to get the
jester. Needless to say, I was quite thrilled. If you are in your teens, I
recommend doing this quest because this is a great Cleric item (+10 WIS, +10
Mana) that is relatively easy to come by.
