I have to agree with Mr. "I Don't Need a Shrink to Tell Me What to Think
" in that you should either be an evil Cleric (Dark Elf) or a humanoid
cleric. Dwarves probably have the best stats for a cleric, but being a
dwarf on the racewar server will be a hard road, because you are teamed
with the gnomes and halflings. Poor bastards.

As for what humanoid race to pick, it TOTALLY depends on your
personality. Are you happy with sitting back and healing (Erudite) like
a pure cleric, or would you rather tank a bit and smash some face
(human), a fighting cleric. Your choice.
