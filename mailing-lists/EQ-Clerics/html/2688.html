This is the information I've gathered about Gull, but it's possible that
it's wrong... but I think it's right. Reguardless it sure hasn't helped me
get him... Gull is I think the hardest camp of all of them. At least all
I've done so far (i haven't done the bracers or the Glacial Bear yet).

There are 14 regular aviak spawns on the island.
There is a 15th one that spawns rarely... I've been told it spawns
every 8 hours, but I cannot verify this. I can however verify that there
are in fact 14 spawns and rarely a 15th one. I just can't verify the spawn
time on it. The 15th spawn is on occasion Gull... but very very rarely.
I've been told that this spawn spot will change every 8 hours even if nobody
kills the place holder. I've also been told that if you have a ranger on
the island tracking, the placeholer will always be the last bird on the
list. The person who told me this said that he knew of at least 2
instances where Gull had spawned when nobody was on the island.

This is all I know... I've been trying for Gull a LONG time and have had
absolutely zero success with it. Sometimes i wonder if he really exists.
