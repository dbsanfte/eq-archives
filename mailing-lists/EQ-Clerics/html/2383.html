In order of importance, get these spells: (according to me, anyways)

Healing
Bind Affinity
Expulse Undead
Symbol of Transal (need Cats Eye Agate as the reagant)
Smite
Cancel Magic
Invis to Undead
Endure line upgrades

I have found that the endure line are spells I rarely use. As well, smite
is ok for solo'ing, so if you solo, definite must have. Cancel magic is
good in Beholder, for when those muddites blind you or a member. Bind is a
must have, as now you don't have to trek across norrath to get your corpse
if you remember to use bind. As well, people will pay about 1-2pp for a
bind. I know, clerics shouldn't charge for services, blah blah blah. But
it is a mana costly spell, so I will rarely give it out for free.
