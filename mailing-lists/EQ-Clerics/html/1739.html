I camped there for 30 hours total to get the ring, most of that time I
was with one of the following: lvl 35 cleric, lvl 24 druid, lvl 20
druid. I also got other druids/clerics popping in to see if she was
camped. So it is possible to group (but will lengthen your total camp
time considerably if the others are after rings too)

What I did to help disuade KSing was to give any extra rings I got to
another kerra that I wasn't going to kill. That way if someone came
along that needed the copper or silver rings I offered them them in
return for looting rights on the first gold ring. Then just told them
which kitty to kill. That worked nicely for me. Just remember to give
the kitty your ring then loot Mitty to get a replacement since you can't
carry two at once (being LORE).
