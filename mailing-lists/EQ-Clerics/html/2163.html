At level 10, I was soloing at the Undead Ruins in BB. If the zone is as empty as you say,
you can level rather quickly by beating up on the dwarf skeletons there, collecting
their bones and taking them to the Paladin guild for more xp and loot. This is better
than the tower because none of the mobs are linked. Also, if you
are able to find a tank to group with, the Goblin Warrior camp is good xp at your level
if the chessboard is camped.
