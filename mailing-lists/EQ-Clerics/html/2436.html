I would add that you will need just above 'amiable' to get the quest.
*Just* getting to amiable won't do it. You have to hit amiable, and
then do a few more quests to raise it further into amiable.

I would also suggest, if you do the blessed oil quest, you bind
yourself in one of the places. That way, you can just gate back and
save yourself at least half the walk. For instance, bind yourself
right in front of the priest that gives the quest. Get the quest, and
then walk to WK, deliver the flask, and gate back; wash, rinse,
repeat.
