Let's get some content flowing on this list shall we? Let's start with why
you want to be a cleric.

With me it was a simple choice. I like to be able to help people. There is
nothing like coming upon someone locked in a life or death battle and
swaying the tide to their side. I don't do it for any reward other than the
thank you I receive. It is good to know that you helped.

I also like the fighting abilities of the Cleric. Although not as good as
the Warrior or the Paladin, Clerics can definitely hold their own.
