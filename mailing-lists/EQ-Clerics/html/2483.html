The Triad (Composed of 3 guilds: Knights of Eternal Good (KEG), Emaf'Aitch
(MFH), and Clan Icewind (CIW) on the Rathe server has been slaying both
dragons, Vox and Nagefen, on a consistent basis. Originally, Vox has been

summoning a level 49 necromancer pet. She dropped this item pretty often
it seemed, at least 1 tooth every two kills, sometimes 2 teeth on one body.

drops a Red Dragon tooth as well (Many peeps, including me, believe this is
a new item drop for Nagafen, that was recently introduced). Many of us are
holding onto these items for now, in case they are needed for some sort of
"super" quest (Hmmm... could these be needed for the fabled Fiery Avenger
quest they are revamping?).

I'm not much of a trader, so I couldn't comment on what a reasonable trade
