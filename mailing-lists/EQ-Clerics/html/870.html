I play on Rallos Zek and I have always found Stun to be a waste of time you
need the Mana.

Some suggestions. Flash of light is an awesome little tool, if it works. Its
fast and uses little mana. He also has cure blindness and he would be stupid
to not have it memorized. If he is a lower level it can last a reasonable
time.
However if you use blindness move behind him to add to the disorientation.

Use a one handed weapon if you can. The faster hit times will help keep his
spells interrupted.

My suggestion and it is only one possible tactic is stick to blindness,
cancel magic and healing. Your offensive spells really are not that good
mana/damage wise.

Blind then get in close and hit him with your club. He will try and DOT you
and slow you down with spells. Then bash the hell out of you. Keep hitting
till you are half down on mana. Then blind and heal yourself. Your heal
spells are far better mana efficiency. Then keep hitting.


Good luck and let us know how it goes. If you are two levels higher than him
and can't take him. Then you have a problem. One is risky. At the same I
would favor the Shaman. But clever tactics might win the day for you.
