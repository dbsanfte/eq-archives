One hint I have... a great way to clear the zone quickly and with not too
much mana useage is to run through the zone training as many goblins, rats,
snakes, etc that you can get to agro on you and leading them all to the NPC
Paladin camp... heh. This is assuming you are high enough level not to get
killed doing this... i am 35 and had no problem. Just get as many things
as you can to agro on you and lead them to the pallies and let them clear
the mess for you. Quick and easy. Just make sure that the Dwarf paladin
is there... if he has gone off chasing a rat, none of the others will attack
for some reason... but as long as he's there those 4 will make quick work of
your train.
