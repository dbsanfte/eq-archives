GUARD = upgrade to spirit armor line of spells.

Regarding 1HB vs. 2HB - At your level I would stay with the 1HB skill if you
have not trained at all in 2HB. Reasoning will be that your melee is going
to significantly decrease as your levels progress. Also, work on trying to
get a charred guardian shield as soon as you can (+9 wis). Also there are
some +wis 1HB weapons that you will be able to obtain once you get a little
higher in levels. (Tainted Battleworm morningstar = +6 wis) If you MUST
use a 2HB make sure to keep your 1HB skill up if possible.

Regarding Taunts - Taunt skill takes time to build up to be effective. The
best anti-taunt a cleric has when getting the crap beat out of him/her is
Divine Aura. Just cast DA and usually the mob will head back to the tank.
=) If not.. this is a good time to start running!
