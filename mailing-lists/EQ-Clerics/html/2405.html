Since there has been so much talk about Rubicite replacements here...
here is something I would LOVE to see! I'm not suggesting it should be cleric only, but if it were we would be the envy of Norrath.. lol. I would love to see a piece of armor similar to the Rubicite Breast Plate which instead of having a healing Aura would instead have an "aura of clarity" (for lack of a better term) and would help replenish manA. This would be an incredible piece of armor for a druid, cleric, or shaman... even for a ranger or Paladin, though I imagine rangers and Paladins would normally opt for higher AC and or HP. I don't know if anything like this will ever happen, it may be considered too unbalancing... I would love to see it though. I think that would be as highly sought out (and should be as difficult to get!) as the jboots...
