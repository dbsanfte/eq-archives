I strongly suggest you stick to one hand blunt. First
of all, 2h blunt will cause your stamina to drain
FAST! Also, there are many cleric only one hand blunt
weapons floating around. Althout the damage is not
great, it all adds up. It also gives you the
opportunity to hold some great wisdom item in your
other hand. SOme prefer the testament or paw, but I
prefer a charred guardian shield. +9 wisdom plus AC,
it's perfect ;)

The symbol line is awesome but does get really
expensive to cast. It will be a favorite among your
party. Currently, I am at level 36 so I have symbol of
pinzarn. It costs nearly a plat per cast. I go
through approximately 250 of these per week. But then
again, I make decent money so it does not bother me
all that much. Symbol, guard(holy armor line) and
valor(courage line) all stack nicely together.

I hope this helped a little bit.
