I just got my Caducous (sp?) of Sacrement last night. The ingot does
spawn on the red giant skeleton in the undead ruins of the rathe
mountains. He's not that easy a foe as it took me 3 banish undeads to
kill him, he was blue to a L39 cleric. I was there for mabye 15-20
mins last night and saw 2 of these ingots droped so the spawn is
fairly quick on this one.

Once you get this ingot and the icon from mistmoor you also need 2
enchanted plat bars (why we need enchanted plat to do our quests I'll
never know) that you turn into magnitized bars in the temple. Give
these to Gavel and you have a nice new 12 dam 32 delay 1 hand blunt
weapon (although it just looks like a morning star).
