<devil's advocate>
True, but how much of a tank/solo class is a cleric compared to a paladin?
And if there were to be a cleric super-weapon, would it be 1h or 2h? I know
personally I'd want 1h mace or hammer, but there are plenty of people who'd
want a nice 2h staff.
</devil's advocate>

That aside, I agree, I've not found much in the way of a 1h blunt weapon
that does more than 9 dmg, and certainly none that have those sort of side
benefits. I know I'd love to have a weapon that alone just added another 7
wis to my character. I've just read on EQ'Lizer that "two or three"
paladins already have them, and they're about lv 18. That screams to me
that either the game balance is about to be completely upended by a single
weapon (just my opinion from seeing the same sort of weapon implementation
on MUDs that I played), or that they're going to get something even better
that takes a higher level to accomplish the quest. All in all, I agree, I'd
like to see some more powerful 1h blunt, something with more than 9dmg
(maybe there is something like that, I just haven't heard about it yet).
