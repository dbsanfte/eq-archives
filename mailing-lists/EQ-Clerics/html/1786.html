I have done this quest and have the competed PAW.

The key thing to know about his place holders is they talk. You will see somthing like "a rat shouts arrrrg who has disturbed my sleep". This can be any creature. I have seen rat, putrid, and a ghoul. Gynok says this when he spawns as well.

Gynok and his place holders spawn on the top floor by the zone line. Then they walk down to the 3rd floor.
So the first thing you need to do is go down to the 3rd floor and kill everything. Then head back up to the 1st floor and kill each place holder. Whatch for that key phrase.
