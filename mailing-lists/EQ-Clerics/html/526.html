Oh my oh my oh my! I have found many many wonderful roleplayers in my
travels through Norrath! The funny thing is, I never actually
roleplayed a day in my life before EQ! It's a shame to see you so
disappointed! I have learned much and traveled plenty. I have met
kewl dudes as well as serious roleplayers. Where do I stand in all
this? Probably right in the middle. I'm not a "kewl dudette", yet I
am not a serious roleplayer (give me a break, i'm just learning the
ropes). I enjoy speaking and acting in character as well as out of
character. It just depends who I"m grouped with. I think this game
(aye, it is a game, draw the line!) is tailored to suit everyone's
needs, depending on what those needs are.

Just my 2 coppers worth!

Adira SoulSaver
