I'm level 30 the spells I currently use are
1. Heal
2. Greater Healing
3. Holy Might (I use this on NPC's that heal right as they start to cast
their heal, it usually gives my party enough time to
finish off the baddie).
4. Smite or Wrath
5. Root
6. Divine
7. Guard
8. Bravery

If i'm soloing I use
1. Healing
2. Smite
3. Wrath
4. Root
5. Divine
6. The newest version of Divine (forget the name)
7. Guard
8. Bravery


When I first got holy might (I belive it was level 24) my roots tended to
get interrupted more while soloing, I would use the stun, root, nuke
tactic from about 24-27

Of course we have more buffs available than the two slots I use for buffs,
I just cycle through the buffs but always leave guard and bravery memed
between buffs as they are the two most useful... (I use symbol buffs if
we are going to fight something more difficult)
