There is absolutely no use for such a spell as Halo of light IMHO. When you complete the Screaming Mace quest (Crushbone zone) you will probably have two items which both work as a halo of light. Both Screaming Mace and Shiny Brass Shield provide light to times when i travel in the dark. Don't spend any money on such a useless spell as Halo of light. But if you are not anywhere near Faydwer then if you absolutely must have some light for the dark times go for it.

For other lvl 14 spells i have Healing, Expulse Undead/Smite all the time memmed and Bind Affinity, Cancel Magic and Invis vs Undead are often memmed.
