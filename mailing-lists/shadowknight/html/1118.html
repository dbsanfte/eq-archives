Kane is harder to impress than the Corrupt Guards.

The only 2 ways i have found that raise his faction,
is the Trumpy thing (takes several hundred Honey Meads
to raise faction by one step), or turn in the Bayle
List parts.

Even when i turned in just part one, i gained faction
with him. And he was actually first on the faction
list, as opposed to being last in the Trumpy one.
Since the first part of the Bayle List is the easiest
to get, i recommend doing it whenever you happen to
run thru HH.
However, if you turn in part one, then try to turn it
in again, he may not care, since he still "has" it.
All 3 may have to be given in order for you to be able
to get faction from just the first again.

Worth some experimenting with though.
