Once you get 12th level, Dorn's dervish camp in North Ro becomes prime
hunting. At 12 you should have a few blues, plenty of whites (which you can
beat), and some yellows. The flagpole derv can be pulled alone, but the
other two in the camp are linked. It's a great spot for dark elves because
Dorn kills any light-siders that get too close or try to pull a derv. I've
been there since 12th level, and I'll be there till 15th. Go try it out. Go
into NRo, and his camp is over near the fishing village right on the water.
