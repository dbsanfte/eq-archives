I play a lev 9 Ogre SK and in my opinion, the best SK's I've seen are
trolls. Because of their regen they seem to get experience faster than
ogres and have an advantage in str and stamina over De and human SK's.
The higher int of de and humans will help in overall ability increases,
but even as an ogre, I've maxed out everything I've wanted to every time
I've leveled so far simply because it takes me so long. If I were going
to start a SK over, I'd do a troll and dump my extra points into either
int and agiliy or int and dex. they already got plenty of str and stamina
and charisma is for those pretty good races as far as I'm concerned. Also
in light of the fact that I have so many misses when fighting and that
Vampiric embrace seems to be dex dependant now, i'd probably go with Int
and Dex. As far as places to go, I don't know about the DE or human
playgrounds at lower levels, but I've like both the Feerot and Innothule
once I stopped getting lost. The easiest way to do that is just stay
close to the roads or boundaries. By the time you get locate corpse, you
probably won't need it. By then you know where everything is and where
you died. Anyway, Good luck with whatever you choose and be ready to
level slowly...
