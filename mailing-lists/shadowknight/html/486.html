If you guys like clinging darkness, you'll love Engulfing!! It is a major
upgrade that lasts much longer and does more dmg. It does cost mroe mana
though so you may want to keep both memed until about lvl 24 or so. That way
you have that emergancy runner stopper.

Advice to people reaching lvl 22. You're Evoke skill will be next to nothing
having not had a spell to do. Cast a pet and then cast Ward undead on it to
get yuor skill up. It took me about 3 hours, but is well worth it if you
plan to hunt undead. Also, cast Endure Cold since this will keep your
abjeration skill up I think. I found to my horror that it is still on 55 for
me at lvl 27! What a slacker! 8)

1H vs 2H - this argument will go on until the end of time. My thought? Use
both! Carry both types of weapon around and keep them maxed out. This will
prepare you for all situations and also help you to find which you prefer
using. Which is better is almost totally weapon based. 2H is generally
better then 1H, but I would preffer a PGT to a FS 2H any day!

As for HT, I too used to keep it for an emergancy, butfrmo about lvl 24
onwards it isn't all that great so I try to comromise and use it as often as
possible while looking for the best time to use, multiple opponents etc. At
lvl 27 , 271 dmg just isn't all that great. the max dmg I've done in a round
is 102 ( double attack 40, 48 and a Vamp for 14) so it only takes a few
rounds to make tht sort of dmg. before lvl 24 though it is often a
lifesaver! 8)

If you get a chance to party with a rogue do it! Between your ability to
attract monsters and their backstab you'll do well. I partied with a lvl 27
rogue when 24 and the mob never even looked at her despite backstabb for
60's +

At lvl 20 or so when you start to get more financially well, build you're
bind wound skill. I have found this to be invaluable. I've maxed it at 100
which is as high as it goes. It heals about 25 or 30 HP per bandage at that
skill which is quite nice to cut down on down time. It is good even when
with a healer since it saves them mana and I'm sure everyone's had a battle
where their whole party is under 1 bub and no one has mana and someone yells
Train! 8)

That should just about do it for the moment. If anyone has any questions
don;t hesitate to ask! we're all here to help! 8)
