the BBC is deep in the dungeon of befallen, occasionally wielded by a troll
shadowknight of around lvl 16. however, that area of the dungeon is very
nasty, full of necro npcs and a whole host of undeads. for soloing, i
recommend you be at least lvl 25 (23 or 24 for troll sk) to make sure you
can reasonably avoid hits from most attackers. if you group, a full sized
group of lvl 18 and up should be able to handle themselves. just be sure
that you grab several glass keys from the ogre sk on level two before you
come down, because it is bad news if the one with the key loses his link and
causes the key to disappear!

as for the paladin in firestorm, i believe he appears at random locations
throughout the area. this makes fighting him rather frustrating, because he
typically gets killed by fire elementals very soon after he appears. he is
in the vicinity of level 9.

to the best of my knowledge, you can't MAKE snakeskin. you have to find it,
and for the life of me i cant remember where it is at exactly right now.
