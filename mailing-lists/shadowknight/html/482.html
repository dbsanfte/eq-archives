Subject: Shadow Vortex


I had a friend cast Shadow Vortex on me to see what it
does. It lowered my AC by 7. She didnt say whether
hers went up or not, so im not sure on that part.

For certain tactics: Darkness/fear.. great.. i use it
all the time.. you can get several free hits in..but
as warned before, make sure you know where he is going
and that he doesnt have any friends.

I also use fear when i am fighting more than one
creature at a time.. i cast fear on one (no
darkness..i want him to run far away :) ) and hack
at the other. the one i am beating on will either be
close to death or dead by the time the other one comes
back.

Fear acts weird in the Arena though. it causes the
target to warp. One time my friend cast it on me and i
warped to one of the spires. i was then hit by 633
points of non-melee damage then fell to my death :)
I bugged it of course.

Shadow Step i have had problems with. Either it puts
me right next to where i was, puts me thru a zone or
dumps me into a body of water. Not to say it isnt
helpful, i have just had bad luck with it so far.

And Harm Touch. When fighting anything that is magic
hit only, be prepared to be disappointed. BUt on most
other MOBs , depending on level, it does seem to work
well. Sometimes you may have to wait until the targets
HP is below half before it will have a full effect.
When i hit Broon with it (level 22ish Cyclops) to try
and pull him off of my friend, it did a whopping 36
points of damage. My BBC did more in one swing than
the HT. Broon totally ignored me and continued to
smack around my friend. Eventually he turned on me,
giving my friend time to cast and we beat him. But HT
was worthless that time.
It can be a last resort however. Usually i do save it
for an emergency. And when it comes down to hit, i hit
the hot key and pray to Bertoxxulous. MOst times it
gets me out of trouble. (example: goblin guard in
runnyeye. was the last one out of a train, he was
pounding on me. waited until he was below half, so was
i too, then HT. He died..i didnt. without HT, i would
have been kibble). But this is not failsafe of course.
I have heard some say that they use HT as soon as they
get it. I see that as a waste. But whatever toots you
flute.
