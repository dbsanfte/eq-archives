Subject: Corpse Retrieval Tip


Here is a handy tip for those of you who die in line of sight of KOS NPC's. I discovered this with my druid character who can cast camo. Get someone to cast invis or camo on you. Make the /corpse command a hotkey. Get near your corpse and drag it along to a safe spot in the zone by contiually clicking the hotkey with your mouse. Your invis will not wear off until you right click on your corpse.
