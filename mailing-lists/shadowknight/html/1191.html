Human SKs of Bertoxxulous do indeed start in Qeynos.
The karana residents hate you though, so dont think
hunting in them will be all that much easier, you just
wont have to worry about the guards. And i couldnt
step foot into Qeynos Hills without either Holly
Windstalker or Lars Mcmannus killing me.And if Cross
Treewind gets you in his sights, youre done for.
But fixing faction with the guards of Qeynos was
extremely easy. Lots and lots of gnolls.
We can freely train in Freeport.Both the Militia and
Paladins are Dubious at first. But the Militia faction
i seemed to fix rather quickly.
We can enter Neriak,most are Dubious. But we cant
train. They dislike us because we are human. The only
KoS seem to be the Warriors. Even the ogres and trolls
in the foreign section dont attack.
The bouncers in Feerrott attack. Not sure about the
trolls.
