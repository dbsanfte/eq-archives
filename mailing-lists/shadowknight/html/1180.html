The only possibility is to try the quest for the
Visiting Priestess who hangs out in the Ogre Captain's
room.
Warning though, if she sees you attacking anything,
she will attack. Killing her lowers faction with the
Clerics of Innoruuk and some others in Neriak you
probably dodnt want to tick off.
She is still DUBIOUS to me even after all the Najena
killing. So she might not be linked. But since she
asks if you are Najena's Champion, could be that it
will help.
However this quest is very demanding. She needs the
various types of crystal staffs scattered around
Norrath.

As far as the Bards go..i havent heard of anyone
significantly raising MM faction with them.
The only successful kill was Princess Lenya. And she
was moved, though we have an idea where she is now,
just havent gotten around to doing a full blown
search.

If anyone wants to give it a try, go into HH and ask
Mistress Anna about Princess Lenya. She asks who sent
you. If you tell her "someone else" she sends you to a
DE NPC in the same area where Lenya used to be..
We couldnt get her to talk (DUBIOUS) so we ended up
killing her.
She had a container with a COMBINE button and a note
written in Dark Speech with instructions and the VERY
basic whereabouts and fate of Princess Lenya.

The container and note appear to be part of another
quest somewhere but have yet to find its origins.
