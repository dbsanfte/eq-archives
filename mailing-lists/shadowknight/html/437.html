HP regen jewelry: there was a rumor that Bloodstone Rings helped life
regen. Since then I have come across a few, and when you look at its
properties, it says nothing about magic. I even tried wearing it and
watching my life, and it regen'd normally. Either this was a scam
someone came up with to rip off newbies, or there is a different kind
of Bloodstone Ring out there.

Armor: Get Banded. You can buy Banded from a PC way cheaper than you
can chain from a NPC. Plus banded has the same AC, but is lighter and
it looks the same.

Those are all the answers I have for now.
