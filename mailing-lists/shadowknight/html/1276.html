The math is as follows:
Level 1-40 Same as always
Level 41-50=10 x level + 1 + (10 x # levels over 40)

In other words there is an additional 10 HP per level after level 41. 10 at
41, 80 at 48, 100 at 50. This is confirmed data from a number of sources and
testing.
