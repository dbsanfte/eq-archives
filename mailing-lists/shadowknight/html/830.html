The closest place to get fletchie kits, my green friend, is to travel to the
Centaur Camp in the southern plains of Karana. It is a large place but if
you travel north and east until you near the river you will find it. Beware
of the aviaks that inhabit this realm however as they do not like trolls for
some odd reason. I'm not sure if it has anything to do with the fact that
when you are older you will eat them or not? Anyways, if get to the river,
you've gone too far (loc of camp is 200, -2300 because I know how much of a
pain in the arse it can be to find). The fletchie kits should be around 1
plat or 2 (probably closer to 2 for you, my charismatic friend). The Centaur
has a name and is in one of the four centaur houses there. He also sells
equipment for bowyery for when you decide to take up that trade.
