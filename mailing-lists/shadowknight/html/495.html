From: Grant Vanderhule <vandergm@...>

dont worry about the BIBS and GZ quite yet.. most non-twinks are unable to
obtain these until high 20s to low 30s levels. :)

as for harm touch, like i said in a previous post.. you gotta be kind of
devious to make it work. it is not really a DD, but a melee attack that hits
for magical damage. it is most effective when used from behind the monster
or while it is trying to flee or stunned. this is why fear is such an
excellent spell..

as for spells at 15 and 22.. buy em all i say, they are cheap by the time
you can cast em (they are the same as necro spells of 1/4th your level). but
the most important spells: at 15, fear and clinging darkness are the MUST
BUY spells. the rest have their uses, but these two are required for the
combat strategy of DOT > slow > fear that works so well for us. at 22 your
best spells will be engulfing darkness, the upgrade to clinging darkness,
and vampiric embrace, our first buff that we will actually USE. oh dont
complain about VE you trollies, a dark elf with a 1h weapon is quite
effective with it ;) also-rans include shadow step at 15 (the game's best
duelling spell, so long as you learn how to keep your bearings when using
it) and deadeye at 22 (if you are human, you finally get to see in the dark!
if you are not, it also doubles as detect invis).

by the way, those of you who say voice graft at 30 is a useless spell.. BAH.
its by far the most hilarious spell myself or any of my group buddies have
ever seen :) there's something about hearing the mob you are fighting
screaming for help and whining about how cruel you are..

pets: they are not your tank. they are there to get a few extra hits in
edgewise. and this damage adds up, especially if you give it a weapon. (i
believe that animate dead and higher necro pets are able to dual wield, but
i can't confirm this..)

also... i've heard that some DE sk swear by quicker 1hs and piercing weapons
rather than big slow 2hs weapons. this is because dark elves can rely on
their dex and agility in a fight to be hit less often and deal max damage
more, while the trolls and ogres use 2hs mostly because it emphasizes their
incredible strength that much more. me, as a human, i use both ;) also, a
dark elf sk with a fanged skull stiletto or thulian claw and VE drains life
at an incredible rate.. it might be something worth looking into :)
