I do believe that Shadow Knight is great class with (in my opinion) a
few glitches.

On one point that Steven brought up, though, i must burst his bubble
some.

At this point in time your wife's SK will NOT get a lifetap spell.
The only spell available to us as a lifetap was Vampiric Embrace but
this isnt the case. Many have tried this spell in everyway imaginable
and no HP is transferred from the target to the caster. All it seems to
do is raise your attack for about 20 seconds.

And as far as bashing and slamming. Thats Troll and Ogre specific.
Human, Gnome, Dark Elf and Euridite SKs never get slam and need a
shield to bash. But i personally dont see this as a problem.

And anybody who can wield a halberd will do 24 dmg. Thats not SK only.

The only things i feel could be improved is the Harm Touch should not
be resisted and at level 15, start getting HP drain spells that
actually work.
And at level 30 we can use Voice Graft. YEEEHAAAA!!! I can see myself
spending a couple plat on that one. This should definitely be replaced
with Gate or something worthwhile.
And Endure Cold and later Resist Cold dont make as much sense as having
a universal shield spells.

And yes, i feel the pets are fine as they are. Though if they got
upgraded, i obviously wouldnt complain.

The comment was made by someone a while ago that these changes would
make the game easy. I fail to see this. I too dont want an easy
cakewalk of a game. But i would like my character to have equal skills
to other classes.
