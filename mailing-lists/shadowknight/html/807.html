Yep, did it Saturday night. As you leave the Lake
tunnel, turn left and follow the wall till you see the
gypsie camp at the pass opening. Go through that pass
and turn right. You will go past the Inn and a couple of
huts (you will be behind them if on the mountain side)
follow this path and go into no other passes and you
will walk into Feerrott.
To know you are going the right way:
After the Inn, you will come to a pass on your right
(skip it) and then pass an altar with 2 named beetles,
then a crossraods/pass to your left and right,keep going
straight. You will then zone soon after the crossroads.
Total time from zone to zone is about 2-3 minutes.
