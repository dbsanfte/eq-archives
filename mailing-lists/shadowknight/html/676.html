I believe that you're right on the assumption of weapon purchase. The BBC
has a harsh AGI hit, but it a weapon that is accesable to players that want
to loot it. Buying a combine 2h sword is expensive and usually you can get
your friends to help you just get the BBC.

As for the Dark Reaver you do need a full party of 39 / 40 types if you want
to get it, and it's a rare drop at that. You can find it on the market if
you like. But you're looking to pay somewhere between 1500 and 3000pp. To
some people that might seem like a lot of money, but since a lot of people
have started second characters the AGE of twinking has begun. I ran into a
level 7 SK with full plate and a Dark Reaver yesterday. heh.

Seriously though, getting the BIBS is a lot easier then the GZ. Karg is too
hard to find, and generally doesn't stay alive when you do find him. As an
SK it's hard to loot a lot of items because it takes forever to get
anywhere. When I wanted to get a BiBs for my Shadowknight I just sent my
wizard to camp it instead (being honest). Why would I want to have to run
from Nek, ec, nro, oasis, sro, innothle, feerot, rathe mount, lake rathe,
sk, nk, ek, beholder to runny to camp for such an item? I could take a
chance to invis through rivervale but that would REALLY hurt if that wore
off, or some PC casted a spell on me that took off invis.

On a side note, why does our Vampiric Embrace do WAY less then a Necro's?
Same spell should do same damage. It's our ONLY way to get any HP back and
it's in battle. Rangers, Paladains can heal fine, and they're based on
Druids and Clerics. We're based on Necros and can't even do a simple life
tap. Food for thought.

Princess Xaera
