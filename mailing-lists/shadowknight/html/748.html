Dear Mr. Hook,

A couple things to talk about, with you in particular.

1. What were the spells that SKs had removed from them back during beta,
before the game went final? Like Boil Blood, Banshee Aura, etc.

2. Why were those spells removed? If we can get some insight into Verant's
reasons behind the removals, then it will make it easier for us to come up
with a "least-offensive" change that we (the SKs) would like.

3. I think it's great that someone from Verant is on this list and not just
listening, but actually polling us on what we would like to see changed.
Additionally, I do so enjoy seeing this list alive again. :)

4. I understand that no new spells can just be added without a corresponding
loss of some. But I think we need to hold this as two separate
conversations -- i.e. first we need to decide possible spells (or
spell-lines) to be added, and THEN concentrate on what we'd give up for
them.

5. I would personally prefer damageshielding over lifetapping. If you think
about it, lifetapping is one of those non-melee spells, meaning that you
have to step back out of combat to really effectively cast them. Being a
tank-type class, that really isn't feasible for us, and I think this is
being overlooked. A damageshield, on the other hand, is like VE, you cast it
before the fight (when your puller says INC) and then it just works
throughout the fight. Sure, shielding doesn't give us healing like a lifetap
does, but I actually agree with Beld after thinking about it -- giving us
real healing will (a.) draw fire from other hybrids, and (b.) overbalance
the class. Shielding won't change anything unless you're actually getting
hit, and then it'll be very nice to see 8hp a pop (or whatever). Think about
it folks, as a tanking SK, which would you rather have -- a spell that can
heal you but you can't cast, or a spell you can put on beforehand that'll
work without you worrying about it? For me, the choice is clear -- damage
shields all the way.
