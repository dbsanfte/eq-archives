I'm not sure about how crowded your server is, but Blackburrow was a good
spot for me from 15-17. You can be bound in Qeynos and there is a Barbarian
merchant that you can sell to in West Karanas. Another good place is
Unrest. You can fight blue mobs in there until the cows come home, just
stay in the back of the house area to keep away from the trains. Some aqua
gobs and orc camps can be solo'ed outside in Dagnor's Cauldron as well. I
never found a merchant to sell to evils out in Faydwer though, so I would
sell to other players at a reduced cost. Be warned however, in both cases,
you will be far from any evil city bank and the coins can weigh you down
quite a bit. In long stays abroad, I typically use my copper to buy
summoned bandages and other such things that I don't really need, but it
helps keep my weight down. Having a few thousand coins was not uncommon
(but it's nice when you do return to town and bank your 30+ plat you've
accumulated).
