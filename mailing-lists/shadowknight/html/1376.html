After finally getting to 22, I was amazed at how easy it was to even solo,
or to have fun in a group with either a druid or a shaman. Outside with a
druid who can harmony to just pull 1 mob, then engulfing darkness or snare
to slow them down. Add the druids DOT, slap on a fear and have fun chasing
the poor mob around while you, your pet and the druid whack on it.

I am only level 24 and a human with a natural 90 strength (equipment and
personal buffs bring that to 122) and I am constantly double attacking for
40-50 each hit with a Dark Reaver. I have even scored the occasional triple
attack.

My advice is to get a dark reaver for about 400pp. Hard to save up for but
well worth it for the 20 damage 42 delay.

Don't forget about your pet. If you are in a reasonably safe area make sure
your pet is there. at 22 mine has about 200 hit points and hits for 14-16.
This is like an extra dot as well as something that gives you a few seconds
head start if you need to run.
