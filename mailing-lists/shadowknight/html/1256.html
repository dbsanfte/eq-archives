No one can play an agnostic Shadow Knight. SK's and Paladins are the soldiers of
their gods - religion is their reason for existance. Just like you couldn't be an
agnostic priest, it wouldn't make sense.

Dark Elf SK's can worship only Innoruuk
Human SK's can worship Innoruuk or Bertoxxulous
Troll SK's can worship Innoruuk or Cazic Thule
Ogre SK's can worship Innoruuk or Rallos Zek

No matter your class, I'd pick Innoruuk, just on the chance it makes you more
agreeable to other evil races...
