Even though I do feel, as I'm sure you all know by now, that HT and
some of the spells do need tweaking, I must defend the SK class.

It has always been said from the beginning that we will NEVER be as
good a tank or fighter as a Warrior. We will NEVER be as good a caster
as a Necromancer.
This should not take you by surprise.
And you're only level 10? You're still a baby to the class, along with
myself i will add.
I have a friend who is level 20 SK and he's a major butt-kicker. We do
come into our power, but patience and hard work is required.

And if you dont want to role-play the character, you chose the wrong
line of work.

I am a level 12 Human SK. I have seen the Necromancers I travel with go
from a level or 2 behind me, to 3 or levels ahead of me. And it is a
little disheartening. But, this is the curse of the hybrid.

But even these now higher level Necros like a little more muscle in
between them and the creature wanting to dance on their foreheads. They
cant cast if they're getting beat on.
There have been many situations when even their pet has been killed and
i'm whats left to take the beating. Which i seem to enjoy in a weird
and twisted way.
I still save their butts as often as they save mine.

And when they are out of mana and cant cast those spells that we are so
jealous of and a train or high ranked creature decides to appear? Whos
left? Us :)

Anyway, in short, 10 is too young to decide. If you dont have the
patience to wait until you come into your power, then SK is the wrong
class for you. I can see my powers slowly, but surely rising.
I have no doubt I WILL be feared.
