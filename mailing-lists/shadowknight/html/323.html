I have a nice Rough Ash Recurve Bow that dishes out 17 dmg with standard arrow.
Cost 34 p though, so probably stick with the elm until your skill is 80 +

I heard a rumour of fletching parts that can be bought in HH but have yet to
find them. You can always get a friend to buy them for you.

I wouldn't not recommend using archeryy full on until level 9 or more. Then grab
one of those needle thingy's which are like daggers but use the archery skill.
they are the best way to raise your skill since they use no arrows, so are much
cheaper. Don't do much damage but good for practicing.

Also, arcehry is good with either SoW or clinging darkness for kiting. I kited a
Bull Elepahnt that conned yellow to half health without taking a hit before
closing in and fighting it.

With Arcehry kiting and pets, Yellows are easily soloed by SK, even up to lvl 20
