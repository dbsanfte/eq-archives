If your interested in roleplaying a DE then you can get lots of material
about how DEs, or the Teir'Dal, from the 'Library of the First Born'
(http://firstborn.eqhq.com/). There is a whole role playing section on
there that makes good reading.

Overall, act arrogant. Remember that as a Chosen of Innoruuk and one of the
'Firstborn' race you have the right to do as you wish. Add to this the
Honor of being a Knight of Innoruuk, blessed in His Eyes. I like to add a
little dedication to my god into this as well, where I will go out of the
way to seek the death of others, or die in the attempt in an overly arrogant
manner. Works great on PvP servers 8)

There is also lots to read on http://www.neriak.com
