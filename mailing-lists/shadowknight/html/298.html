>the BBC is deep in the dungeon of befallen, occasionally wielded by a troll
>shadowknight of around lvl 16. however, that area of the dungeon is very
>nasty, full of necro npcs and a whole host of undeads. for soloing, i
>recommend you be at least lvl 25 (23 or 24 for troll sk) to make sure you

>come down, because it is bad news if the one with the key loses his link
and
