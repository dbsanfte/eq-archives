i also FINALLY made it to 22 today. and here is the raw deal on the "new and
improved" vampiric embrace: you cast 30 mana, and while it lasts you MIGHT
occaisionally draw about 6 hp from the target. add to this the fact that,
like all other random weapon effects, this is based on dex. so if you are a
troll or ogre, you can more or less forget ever even getting that 6 hp once.
and it doesnt even really last very long. is verant intentionally picking on
us SK players, or do they just not get it?

as for engulfing darkness, despite the high casting cost of 60 and that it
casts a bit slow, i found it works fairly well. like so many other things
the actual duration of the spell is fairly random, so sometimes it lasts for
a long time and sometimes it wears out quick. at least it works better than
clinging darkness so far.

like Trian said, the least disappointing spells for me at this level are
ward undead and deadeye. now while i can hit for 35 damage twice a round
with my bone bladed claymore, and ward undead does about 41 max, it isnt all
that useful other than an additional attack against undead. deadeye is quite
useful to us humans at least; i can see! i can SEE!

i didnt have as many problems casting the pet.. but somehow it doesnt seem
as bright as our lvl 15 pet. the bone walk spell makes a pet that will NOT
attack something unless it attacks you first or when ordered, but i'd swear
that the convoke shadows pet took the initiative and attacked a deepwater
caiman without me telling it to.. i'll keep messing around with it and see
what happens.
