Subject: my opinions on power issues


well, if they want to make ALL classes playable and fun, they cant have a
single class be overpowering at all levels. therefore, assuming that SK
becomes THE most powerful class (or close to it) at high level, they should
start out weak to make up for that. (in general, once you get to high level
you will BE high level much longer than you were low level). for instance,
at low level, warriors kick some major booty. they can solo fairly easily,
are quite capable of dishing out damage and absorbing it too, and have a lot
of str so they can wear heavy armor and carry a lot of loot. but once they
get to high level, they become little more than cannon fodder to keep the
nasties off of everybody else long enough to win the fight. on the other
hand, a wizard is tremendously weak at low level, often unable to survive
for long outside a group that knows what its doing. but once you get high in
level, the wizard gains godlike powers of destruction and mayhem that make
things scatter before them in fear. (thats the idea, anyway 8)). i think SKs
are closer to the latter category.. remember, while paladins can heal, and
rangers get a few modest DD spells, SK is the only class in the game that
can wear heavy armor and weapons AND cast significantly damaging combat
spells once they get to high level. most people would be deathly afraid of
somebody in full plate with a 20 damage vampiric sword, can zap you for 501
damage, cast heart flutter, and teleport around and go invis when you try to
fight back. and rightfully so :) remember, what you dont know sure can hurt
you! (a cookie to whoever guesses that reference!)
