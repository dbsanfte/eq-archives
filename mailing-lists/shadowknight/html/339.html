I did not declare that anything was a bug exploit, I merely pointed out that
these daggers are a pathetically easy way of raising your Archery skill. Due
to Verant's EXCELLENT history of balancing this game, I find it extremely
difficult to believe that they would put a component in the world that would
allow you to raise your archery skill 5 points in 5 minutes when normally
archery takes an extraordinary amount of time to raise unless you use it
constantly. Yes, I did raise my own archery skill 5 points in 5 minutes (it
was already at 72) while testing it to see if it worked. The ability to
raise any skill, especially a combat one, that much in such a short amount
of time must be considered a bug or something that Verant does not realize.
If the daggers/pine needles were throwing weapons that went in the ranged
weapon slot and used up one each time, that would make it a little bit
better, but they are not, so for 8cp or something silly like that you get
this item to raise your skill to its max with one nights work or less. As
for waiting to hear back from someone of authority, I agree, that is why I
and all my compatriots have /bug'd this item. Your statement about them
using the archery skill due to the aim factor is just as much conjecture as
my statement, however. And by the way, the fact that they have such pathetic
damage makes them the reason that they are that much more powerful at
raising your archery skill.

Anyways, I just hope that Verant fixes this major game-balance problem soon
before everyone and their momma is a master archer. :) I worked long and
hard to become as good at Archery as I am, and it seems silly that someone
can pick one of these items up and in a few hours of practice become a
master archer.

Sincerely,
Jeremy Pollack, Shadow Knight, Solusek Ro
