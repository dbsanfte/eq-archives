continue to attack you, and by doing so, all the other enemies will
see through it as well. I have never seen it run out, it doesn't
put up a spell effect like invis, you just hit the sit button to stand
up, or meditate, or whatever. Also, you will want to practice the
feign pull, it is very handy and your groups will love you for
camp breaking.
