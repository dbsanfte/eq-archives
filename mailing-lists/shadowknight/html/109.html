Yes I have done this quest. The paladins do drop the amulets, but not
always. If you get one, consider keeping it, becuase it gives +10hp and
+5char. I sold one for 4pp. If you decide to give it to the bloodsabers
you get a little xp and an item -- in my case it was a rusty broadsword.

Be careful though when hunting them, because there is one that has killed me
3 times already even though he conned green. As a matter of fact he killed
me and my buddy sk (both of us level 11 at the time) at once. This guy (I
forgot his name) only punches for about 8 damage but does so increadibly
fast. He was from the temple of life. I have only found orders of thunder
on my victims. Never have I seen a prayer bead.

Now, about finding them. They seem to spawn randomly, but about every 10
minutes at least. The best place to wait is by the QH zone border, becuase
they spawn there and run someplace (T1 I think -- but maybe only if you are
attacking) and back. As far as I know they are about he only NPC's running
that path -- so con them from a distance and look for 2 names or whether it
says too far to inspect. Note that the farther you have to chase them, they
farther you must go to the zone if you have to retreat.

You can lure them easily, either with a spell or with an arrow, but catching
them and striking first with a melee weapon is very hard.

It can be quite frustrating waiting around for them so I don't do this quest
anymore, but I still strike one down if I happen to see him.
