Ok, here are me 2 PP...
The damage done on HT is a little low. I agree that if
you get a full group of high level SKs together that pop
HT all at once there could be abuse. HOWEVER, most SKs
don't use HT offensively and there aren't that man that
group together (at my level anyway). Most use it as a
last ditch effort to save their ass. I was one of those
until Mr. Beld gave me advise that changed how I look at
SKs. This stems from the fact that at low level you are
paranoid about not having HT ready to use. Most don't
out grow that view. Also, getting that many SKs focused
on one MOB is death for that MOB anyway. :)

I would say increasing the amount of damage to 15*level
is a reasonable increase. This is because most times you
will not be attacking 1 MOB, but several. The overall
effect of 1 MOB dying will not overbalance the
situation. It may allow you to win, and if you die, you
won't get the XP from killing the HTed MOB anyway. It
will help a solo SK tremendously (heh).

On resisted HT...
In a group against a RED MOB that is 3/4 or better
health my HT is resisted alot so that instead ot 141 HP
it does 36 (ak!). On the same level MOB that is at 1/2
to 3/4 health the damage increases and the number of
resists went down as well. Same level MOB at 1/2 or
less had no resists and full damage every time. I had
several very patient groups that helped me with this
over the last few days. I did this to see exactly what
tactics would work best for me to use it in.
This tells me that to start combat with HT against a
higher MOB is a waste. Strategic use in group is best.

Brian,

You say giving SKs Lifetap and/or other spells would
make SKs into tank Mages. In effect, we are just as
Paladins are tank Clerics. This is not such a big issue
because clerics are not as limited as mage classes
equipment and ability wise, so to transfer the abilities
to a Paladin is not adding too much that a cleric
couldn't already do.

Why not ty this instead... make the SK into an Anti-
Paladin (which we are supposed to be really). Reverse
all cleric spells that heal for a Paladin and give that
to an SK as DD along with all the buffs that a Paladin
normally gets. After all we are not healers, but we are
the strong arm of evil and are to protect our kind with
the gifts of our dark gods.
Heh.
