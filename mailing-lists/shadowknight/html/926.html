Subject: New twist on Weapons and Armor ...


This of course is only a suggestion and most likely
never be implemented. But here it goes.

How about weapons and armor granted to those of
specific deities. For instance, not only is the
Pestilence Scythe for WAR,SHD,NEC, but it is for
worshippers of Bertoxxulous only.
Sure SKs and Necros of other deities can cast all the
same spells, but worshippers of Bertoxx have a special
relationship with death and disease. They should have
exclusive rights to this type of a weapon.
Why should a warrior of Tunare have access to the
Pestilence Scythe?They are supposed to abhore that
type of power.

Of course this is only an example. All religions will
have their own special little things, and this wont
apply to all weapons and armor, just certain ones.

And for all the Agnostics out there who will complain,
tough. Thats what you get for taking the easy way out.

I just think certain items need more history behind
them. Right now everything is either class/race
specific. Religion is totally ignored. This doesnt
make sense since religion is a huge part of the game.
Lets try adding something in there that gives us more
of a reason to follow a certain deity.

Anyway, just a random thought as i sit here at work
bored to tears.
