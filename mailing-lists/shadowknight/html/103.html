It _should_ make up for it, however there are other mitigating factors.
The pets are not generally as useful as necro pets, since these pets lag
behind the SK's level by a significant amount. A 10th level SK's pet is
probably not that threatening to the type of monster a 10th level SK will
attempt to solo. This is actually somewhat of a problem with SK spells in
general -- they're not useful at the levels they are actually acquired.

In addition, SKs are evil, therefore they're often KOS in many lands. This
means they are already working at a significant disadvantage to paladins.
This makes travel particularly dangerous for SKs, and to make matters worse
they don't get ANY spells to assist travelling long distances or to heal
themselves. An invis or gate spell in addition to a lifetap would REALLY
be nice to pick up at level 15 or so.

Once again, my position as a game developer probably provides some insight
into this. It is quite obvious from Smedley's public comments that they
are aware of the bugs/complaints. Lack of an individual response (or even
a public position on more widespread grievances, e.g. vampiric embrace not
working, rogue issues, and SK HT) irks some, but if they do nothing but
answer e-mails or post position papers then they are likely neglecting the
task of actually resolving issues. For example, it's pretty obvious that
the past few days they've concentrated on networking/packet loss/ISP issues.

Probably the wisest thing to do is write a very intelligently done essay,
post it to a Web site, message board, etc. and also e-mail it to John
Smedley politely. If he doesn't respond, so be it, however I'm sure he'll
read the e-mail and take it into consideration.

To me the big issues are:

1. HT does not balance against LoH. To make this fairer/more balanced, I
think they should either:
- not allow it to be resisted (partially or completely)
- make it analogous to LoH in terms of damage
..or..
- allow it to be cast at a faster rate, three times a day

I'm against the latter, since it then becomes a tool instead of an escape
mechanism. I like the idea of HT being used for epic battles and sticky
situations instead of just using it during the normal course of a day of
adventuring.

2. SKs are KOS in many lands, so this should affect the balancing somewhat
(or we just accept that this makes them more difficult to play and so be it).

3. While SK pets do offset some of the negative aspects, they are fairly
weak and not as useful as many players believe.

4. Since SKs are KOS in so many areas, they should have SOME type of "safe
transport" spell at their disposal. At a minimum invis, preferably a gate.

5. Like warriors, SKs do not have the ability to heal at an accelerated
rate. Instead of comparing them to warriors, however, I think it is more
valid to compare them to paladins and necros, their closest analogs, both
of whom have healing type spells. A simple lifetap would help out a lot,
and I think would actually make the game more interesting, since you would
have SKs that roam the land sucking the life from creatures in order to
sustain their own health.
