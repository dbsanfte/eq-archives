(Setting macros for anyone interested: Select the start F10 view, select
> "Social Skills" from Lower right panel. decide on one to do away with and
> right click on it. The dialog section will display a text box to enter the
> Button Caption and the macro window below. Type anything you want in there
> (like /loc or /shout I am bringing a train of nasties to the Zone, please
> pardon any inconvenience this may cause...) Click the done button. This
new
> "Social" button you have created can then be dropped onto your hot keys
for
> ready reference)
