I have seen Dark Elves in Qeynos so it is possible.
That is accomplished by killing lots and lots of
gnolls in Black Burrow.
Some of the people on the dock are kind of touchy
though. After doing several quests in Qeynos i became
KOS on the docks (Trumpy and his buddies hang out
there). I had to wait for the boat to show and pay
someone to invis me so i could make it to the boat.

The big problem for you is when you get to Erudin.
They didnt like me, but they didnt kill me either. You
may not be so lucky. You may want to consult other
Dark Elves on their faction in Erudin.
There may be other islands that have Kerrans on them
too, not just Odus.
