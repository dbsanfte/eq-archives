I'm a Dark Elf (DE) ShadowKnight (SK) that wants to get from Neriak to
Crushbone to meet a friend. I know the way. My question pertains to
faction standing and KOS. As a DE SK, where can I go safely and where
must I watch out.

Matt
Email: bair@...

You have to steer clear of all Dwarven, Wood and High Elven Guards, it's
rather simple really as you can just follow the road avoiding first the
dwarven guard posts in BB, then following the road in GFay, avoiding any
fairies you see (unless you're tough enough to kill them) as well as any
guards you might chance upon. Also if you do go to CB I would not actually
fight the Crushbones, because if you have not yet you'll find that they only
glower at you dubiosly, and therefore none of them will ever attack you
unless you kill a few and lower your faction.
