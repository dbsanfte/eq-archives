Yeah I also play a dark elven SK, and as one there are a few things I've
noticed that are useful as a dark elf, especially since most think that DE
SK isn't such a good choice. Yep it's hard, but it has some real payoffs.
The high INT keeps your skills raising like crazy even into the 100's as I'm
experiencing now, and allows you to do more creative things with your mana,
like back off out of a fight when soloing and let your pet beat on the
monster to finish it, with you healing it with mend bones (15) with all that
extra mana you've got. Since a dark elf SK can't have too high STR
(especially if you distruibuted points to other things like STA and DEX),
item-wise you'll want to try to get things that raise STR. You can use
either 2-hand or 1-hand weapons, 1 handers are useful because having a
shield ups your AC,adds to your parry rate (17), gives you the ability to
bash, and may also have some useful attributes to it (like the darkwood
raising STR by 9, or the chitin shell raising poison resist by 10). If you
use a 2-hander, then you'll get great damage, it just isn't as ideal as a
troll or ogre tho because of their ability to slam (bash without shield).
Aside from this, you might also listen to what Beld said about being careful
with HT etc.
