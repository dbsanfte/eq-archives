Subject: Guild on Cazic-Thule


There is a guild forming on the Cazic-Thule server consisting primarily
of (but not limited to) Shadow Knights and Necromancers.
All SKs and Necros will be considered.
All evil races will be considered, regardless of class.
And in certain instances, other race/classes may be considered if the
character is roleplayed evil.
As im sure you have guessed, it is evil aligned.

The name at this point cant be given out until it has been accepted by
989.

The very basics of our charter:

Our Lord Bertoxxulous is gaining strength, preparing to return to
Norrath with a terrible vengeance, and send the wretched Lighbringers
into eternal Darkness.
Our mission is to bring war upon the Lightbringers, feed their corpses
to our Lord, and cast a Shadow upon Norrath, creating a gateway for our
Lord to pass through.

Now our real life goal:
Basically we just want a good group of people who like to roleplay, go
out and kill stuff and have fun.
We do plan on eventually doing guildwars, hopefully leach some dynamic
quests out of Verant and once we get settled (starting a guild is
actually quite a bit of work) give quests to fellow guild members with
some good and/or interesting rewards. (of course these will have to be
obtained first, but we'll be working on it).

More info will be available after we are official. But if any are
interested at this point, feel free to email me, i will contact you and
give any info i currently have.
