Subject: Update on Fletching


For the first time since the patch that altered fletching, i gave it a
shot.

Now, i dont know if this was the way it was immediately after the
patch, but as it stands, its not bad at all.

For every set of arrow components you combine (on success of course)
you create 5 arrows.

The skill rate seems to work the same as when we were combining single
components as well.

I didnt get to buy the components myself, so i dont know what the cost
is now, but as far as how it works and what you get out of it, it seems
like a good deal.

I am still trying to find out where evil races/classes can buy
components. If anyone can enter Ogguk, you may want to check there,
since Grobb doesnt seem to have the components as i previously
believed.

Thats all..thank you and have a nice day
