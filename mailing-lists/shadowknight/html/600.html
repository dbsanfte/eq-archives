i play a troll SK, and at level 9, with an int of 77, and
a conjuring of ~10, channelling of ~20, i could cast my
pet spell. the mana calculator is badly off for hybrids.
now at 11 i have around 60-70 mana. to find out how much
mana you have, just cast the sense undead spell over and
over, until you get insufficient mana. then multiply the
number of times it went off by 5, and you have a rough
guesstimate of the amount of mana.
also, for bash, train one point in it, you get it at 6,
and if you train one point in it, you can press slam, and
when successful, you will bash for 5-7 dam.
