This is where Beld's advise has really helped. With my
regen, a blue elemental or a black drake is a possible
victory. I wait til it is at half life if I can, center
it in my view and hit the hotkey. I have yet to see it
not do full damage when used this way, and I have yet to
die this way as well.
In group, it has saved the life of my friends many
times. The casters go nuts on DD spells and won't let me
tank (I like tanking heh) since it frenzies on them.
When I notice they are in trouble (since I am watching
my Slam and Disease Cloud buttons to become available
again) I hit it and they frenzy on me instead. Then I
hit Taunt to keep them there while they regen. Very nice.
I no longer feel weak if I don't have HT available
either. I just pick my targets more carefully, and
position myself to where I am least likely to be
ambushed. I learned very quickly that the doors to

is a zone point right behind me if I get into trouble.
