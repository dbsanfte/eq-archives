You need the mold, the hilt, and the electrum. Then just hit combine, no
skill needed.

You get the hilt by giving Arlena's lute to Grimrot. He also gives you a
note, which you take to Dekam N'Tazi in Qeynos. Give him the note, he gives
you the mold. Then get some electrum, have a friend enchant it, then combine
the hilt and electrum in the mold.
