Well, I bought my Cleaver from another SK that was
upgrading to a better weapon. Got it for a steal (I
think) at 85PP. The weapon is not in that great a
supply/demand though because it is evil race "fighter"
only.
The weapon is great! Is the only one I use now. Of
course, if you are human I don't think you can use it. :(
As for 1HS, try to get ahold of a PGT. HHP is the best
place to look for one that I know of.
Another good one is the Barbbed Whip. Najena is the
dungeon for that. Bonecracker drops it I believe. Just
auction in Lavastorm and the Najena zone border for one.
You may just get lucky.
Perma has the best 1HS weapons in the game IMHO. If you
can get to Perma alive I would suggest that. But that
can be hell if you die. :)
