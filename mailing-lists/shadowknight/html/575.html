Sorry, missed your questions first time around:

Which axe quest? The grobb cleaver is the one I know of. It is a great
weapon, but you have to kill a lvl 20 NPC (he's not too tough, though ... a
lvl 20 enchanter and I (lvl 20 at th etime) wiped the floor with him).

The Hukulk Helm quest gets you the, duh, Hukulk Helm. It's not bad ....
think it gives some +str too. It makes you lose faction with the indigo
brotherhood, however, so if you are not careful you will become KoS in the
inner two parts of Neriak. A SK can do it at least once without becoming KoS
(a warrior can't ... my friend found out the hard way) but it wouldn't hurt
to raise your indigo brotherhood faction first (give LT caps to, umm, Narex
V'tam I think his name is. THe guy in chainmail in the warrior guild in
Neriak commons.).

Not sure what other quests. I'll write more if I think of more. You can
check the underground of Freeport, too, but none of those give anything
good.
