I'm level 26 now, and yes, I'd have to say that level 22 was pivotal in my
career as an SK. You get Engulfing Darkness, an upgrade of Clinging
Darkness. Whereas CD does 20hp over 30 seconds, ED does 90hp over 60
seconds, a big difference. Plus you get Vampiric Embrace, which some say is
almost useless, but it goes off more and more depending on your Dex and your
level (not to mention sucking more damage). Level 22 spells also include
some more Vs Undead type spells, making Unrest a lot easier (i.e., go get
some small bronze now if you haven't already).

I used 2HS for a long time (first 24 lvls as an SK, and all 31 lvls of my
warrior before that) and it was a big decision but I decided to go to 1HS. I
now use a demon claw and a chitin shield. With our dark elven agility, I
squeeze another 23 AC outta that 15 AC shield, not to mention the
bash-then-cast strategy helps get a LOT more spells off. If you're gonna be
solo'ing, use the shield and 1hs, because you're fights will take longer but
you'll end up less hurt. In a group, go for outright damage dealing with a
good 2H sword. You'll have heals and backup, so just concentrate on kicking
ass, rather than hiding behind a shield. As a side note, VE does seem to go
off significantly more often with a demon claw than with a zweihander (not
sure it makes up for the damage difference though).
