- reclaim energy: 2
- mend bones: 3
- voice graft: 1

on the other hand...

i'm quite sure that i would cast the lv30 lifetap fairly often to ruduce
downtime and kill runners (i see "miss! miss! miss! miss! miss! far too
often when i wait for the sliver of a bastardly frog's hp to be sucked away
by dot. i'd be willing to bet that mobs under 20% or so get a massive agi
shot as part of a "frenzy" phase).

so, i can't complain. especially since i honestly didn't expect our class to
see any changes past the 49 pet change many months ago. also, i don't have
much sympathy for those who take the "i suck in groups", "these spells don't
help in groups" line. consider yourself lucky that you have the manpower
available to finish each battle in a timeframe that makes your dots
"useless"...it's a simple trade off in my opinion. a higher level of safety
and the power for larger encounters with reduced spell effectiveness vs.
high risk, limited total fighting strength and spells which realize their
full potential. furthermore, i've never been called "useless" in group or
been refused acceptance based on my class. in most cases i'm readily
accepted if not in demand as our versatility can balance most any party when
played right.

if you're running into people who don't respect your abilities or strengths
because you don't visibly attack a mobs hp with big dd or critical
hits...you really need to find some better people, some people whose
understanding of combat goes beyond watching the hp bar or gangbanging mobs
in a 6 on 1.
