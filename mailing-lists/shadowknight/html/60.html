i fear that i cannot disclose the exact location of the tomahawk, for we
have all sworn a solemn oath not to give out its location to anyone! (this
would likely cause a major rampage and flood said area with a hideous number
of people camping for it). personally i only found it through luck (if you
can call being killed by its wielder luck :)) and when we came back to get
revenge.. well, we were greatly surprised.

the stats on this thing are 1hs, 6 damage, 26 delay, magical attack, and
casts 'berserker strength' randomly during a fight. it also weighs 6.5
pounds, and can be used by pretty much any of the melee combat-oriented
classes. the berserker strength is defined as follows: it adds 19 str,
subtracts 10 agi, and while you are affected you are INVULNERABLE to melee
damage. i am not kidding, you actually become partially invincible while
this is in effect! the downside is that this effect is cast randomly during
combat (you may be affected 5 times in one fight or not at all), and the
effect is very shortlived and chaotic.. i've seen it last almost 2 minutes
one time, and fade almost instantaneously another. even with that drawback
tho, imagine a warrior dual wielding a pair of these and sinking into
berserk fury range.. you can almost see the blood fly. just imagine a
magical dwarven battle axe that casts divine aura and a strengthen on
steroids, and you get the picture.

now, while i really cant tell you the exact location, i WILL say that this
weapon is always wielded by a gnoll named Grenix Mucktail. for
well-traveled shadow knights this should be ample information for you to
begin your hunt, but others may have to explore a bit before they figure out
where he may be located ;) good hunting, and may all who stand in your path
feel the rage of Innoruuk!
