Dark Elves can easily jump on the boat at Freeport, sail to butcherblock,
jump off early, and go to greater faydark or wherever.
To return, you simply have to swim around the blocked dock area, and ensure
that you don't get too close to a certain High Elf npc who stands near the
steps up to the dock you need to get to to get on the ship (jump onto the
highest point possible and the npc will not attack you since you won't be
close enough). Once you're up, you simply wait for the ship like anyone
else, there are no more npcs to get in your way.
Unfortunately I doubt any of this would work with Trolls or Ogres, since the
npcs who do not attack DE"s probably do attack Trolls and Ogres.
Con the npcs to find out for sure tho, although invis is always the safest
bet.
