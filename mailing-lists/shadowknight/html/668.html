Wow, Brian Hook posting too our little email list, very cool. Keep up the
good work Brian, you seem to be genuinely interested in the pulse of the
gamer and the state of EQ as a whole. As far as web sites, several have
been listed on this list (if you check the archives, you can find them
pretty easily). But, even these sites are rather preliminary and
incomplete. For quests, the best place to look is Allakhazams web site
under the regular quest locations for SK's (i.e. Grobb, Ogguk, Neriak, FP,
Qeynos, Southern Karanas, etc.) For strategies and tactics, check out the
EQ Stratics SK board. Pay special close attention to any posts by Beld
Demonblade (that guy KNOWS what he is talking about).

I can relate what info I know and have heard from other SK's. This is
pretty standard for any non-twinked character. Basically, when you reach
the highest level of the range, you should have all of the equipment listed.
This is for the 2H Slashing SK's which are most common. Others use Piercing
or 1H Slashing, but I don't know which weapons they are using.

Levels 15-20:

Weapon: Grobb Cleaver, Brutechopper, Shiny Brass Halberd, or Bone Bladed
Claymore
Armor: Banded or Blackened Iron with some misc. stat enhancing armor mixed
in (Barbed from Befallen, Spiked stuff from Blackburrow, LJ vest, etc.)

Levels 21-30:

Weapon: Blackened Iron Bastard Sword or Gigantic Zweihander
Armor: Bronze with misc stat enhancing armor mixed in

Levels 31-40:

Weapon: Dark Reaver
Armor: Anything better than bronze (steel, rubi, mithril, etc.)

Levels 41-50:

Weapon: Dark Reaver or better (what's after it?)
Armor: Beyond your wildest dreams (Plane of Fear stuff, I guess)


Scragg Wraithbone of Bristlebane (22nd level TSK)
