while i dont have a loc of the villiage (i never navigate that way
anyway) try doing this to get there from the bridge: from the bridge,
turn left and follow the coast until you pass a bunch of raiders. a bit
past them, the coast should take a sharp turn to the south, with a tree
inside the turn. follow this turn in the coast straight inland, and you
should end up either at the centaur villiage or close enough that you
can see it.
