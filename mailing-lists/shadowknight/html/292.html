the easiest 2h magical weapon for us to obtain is a combine two handed
sword. you can buy one from gypsies in oasis or north karana for about 108
plat. it may sound a bit steep to a low level sk, but it is a lot easier to
make the money and get that than to get a bone bladed claymore or
darkreaver. you just walk up to the store and buy. (ogres and trolls may
need somebody to buy for them, depending on wether or not the gypsies like
them).
