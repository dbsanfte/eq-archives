>At level 22 We do get a couple good spells.
>Engulfing Darkness being probably the best combat
>wise. At least at first, you can stack it with disease
>cloud and clinging darkness. Eventually neither
>clinging or disease cloud will work on anything, but
>at 22, it still worked on most things i was fighting
>at the time.
>Vampiric Embrace isnt nearly as pathetic as i was
>anticipating. At first it only drains 12 points of
>damage. But it goes up 1 point every 2 levels.So at
>24, it did 13, at 26, 14 and so on. PLus it can go off
>even when your swing misses. So instead of a big
>nothing, you do 12 damage and get 12 HP back. It is
>unpredictable though. I can go thru 2 fights and not
>have it go off at all, or it can go off 3 or 4 times
>in one battle. But i cast it before every fight.
>And the last of the 22 spells that i use is Deadeye.
>This grants Infravision, which dark Elves, Ogres and
>Trolls dont care about in itself. But, Deadeye also
>lets you see invisible players. So no more sneaky
>Wizards creeping up on you.
>Ward Undead i havent used seriously, but this may
>change when i head to Unrest or a nostalgic trip into
>Befallen. Though i have a feeling it will be resisted
>alot, but im used to that now.
>
>So, your question about becoming very powerful at 22,
>the answer is no. More powerful, yes.
>But level 30. Heat Blood. Bwhahaha. If youve ever had
>that spell cast on you, you know how good it is. Plus
>Invisibility, at long last.
>Dont expect to be invincible at 22 by any stretch of
>the imagination, but you should start to see how good
>things will be getting.
>Plus at 20, double attack. This has changed melee into
>a whole new game for me.
>
>now as far as 2H slashing vs. 1H slashing.
>If you want one to be chosen over the other, my
>opinion is 2H. The reason being is that so far, all
>the really great weapons in the game that a SK can
>actually use, are all 2H Slashing. Dark Reaver, Giant
>Zwizzlestick (or whatever its called), FleshRipper to
>name a few.
>So definitely get your 2H going.
>But i wiil say, fighting a caster with a 2H bites.
>They are slower than most 1H slashing, so the caster
>gets off more spells, which is bad news for you.
>Unfortunately, my STR isnt enough to carry both around
>with me, so ive been sticking with 2H for now. But
>eventualy will switch back to 1H to freshen it up.
>
>Hope that helps you out.
