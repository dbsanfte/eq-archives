Subject: Changes, what I'd really like to see


Although the lifetap line would be nice, and I wouldn't complain,
there are two issues I think are more important:

1. Base pet aggro on the level of the owner, and not on the level of
the pet. There would be fewer complaints if the green training and
lion attacks would stop. Then, regardless of power, the pet would
be useful in more situations.

2. Harm touch should never be resisted on even and under cons, and
should continue to be useful up to about 3-4 levels above us. I know
that we can't have a dozen SK's wiping out griffons at 20th level, but
sometimes, especially at lower levels, it just gets ludicrous.

I just wish I could fight a rook/avocet without a lion/wolf/puma
distracting my pet, and rely on my harm touch when soloing a
reasonable opponent. (Blues resisting and me dying is just silly)
