The Abbasi is not a bad weapon, 9/25 + stats. But it is only
marginally better than a Ykesha, and has no proc. Dex will make your
vampiric embrace go off a little more, but I wouldn't worry about Dex
much at all unless you have a Ykesha or Soul Leech (dunno about the
Drinker of Blood from Sky), and definately don't sacrifice AC for
dex in any case.

If you can get it, Bloodmoon is the ultimate small race SK weapon,
and currently is arguably the best weapon in the game. It is 18/28
one handed, but dropped off Innoruuk.

As a small race, you should always carry 1H/shield in addition to
a 2H weapon if you choose to use 2H most of the time. At levels 45+
you should use an EBW/Ykesha/Abbassi (Of course bloodmoon if you get
it) instead of a Dark Reaver, and only use 2H if you have a Razing
Sword, Soul Leech, or a Mithril 2H.
