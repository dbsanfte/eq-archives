As far as where to find a 2h sword....its hard to find one in town...i'd do
an ooc or auction request and see if anyone has one to sell you....expect to
pay around 2 gp....you can buy regular steel ones for 26 platinum in the
back of the shadowknight guild in Neriak or in the blue flame store in the
Commons of Neriak.

As for your practice points....I'd use them to advance a different skill
like smithing or tailoring....other than that, yes, I'd save them....being a
dark elf your intel is so high that your skills raise extremely fast....I've
got around 25-30 skill points saved and i don't plan to use them on anything
for quite some time. maybe for double attack later down the road.......

hope this helped a bit....
