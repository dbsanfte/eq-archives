Subject: Finally Some Upgrades!


Well it looks like Verant is finally listening to the calls of the all SK's
out there.
Our final pet, which was originally Haunting Corpse, has been upgraded to
Summon Dead, which is simply a higher-level pet.
Another of our final spells, Word of Shadow has been upgraded to Word of
Spirit, try and complain about that one :)
The final change is up to some debate at least for now, we gain a brand new
spell called Shadow Vortex, replacing Intensify Death. Until we know if
Shadow Vortex is "better" than Intensify Death, there's not much we can say
here, except that certainly sounds cool.
Note also from the last patch, that Boil Blood is replaced with Dooming
Darkness (not terrible, but probably not an upgrade, although it does seem
fair, as someone pointed out a while ago, that someone who can melee the
hell out of you shouldn't be able to have an extremely fast DoT spell as
well), and that Banshee Aura has been replaced with ShieldSkin (kind of an
interesting tradeoff, lose a spell that causes damage to the enemy when it
hits you, to gain a spell that causes them to do less damage when hitting
you).
Now if only they'd add a decent SK-only 1H weapon...
