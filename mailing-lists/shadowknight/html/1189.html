i've yet to see any direct benefits of worshipping innoruuk. but it may help
a human sk out with the de. by the way, worshippers of innoruuk are 100% kos
in the plane of hate. yay!

as for starting stats, i have yet to see a convincing demonstration of the
benefits of agi. with a lv50 shaman buff the difference is negligable as far
as i can tell. however, the effects of strength are immediately noticable as
it increases your atk, which increases your chance of hitting for "normal"
damage (dmg*2+1), increases your max damage at a rate of about 1 point per
10 atk and increases your minimum damage. it's also believed by many that
atk determines your hit % as well...i haven't observed this to be true, as
my hit rate has been a rock solid 66% for the last 6 levels that i've been
keeping logs.

my sk always has and will likely continue to focus on:

ac - never have enough. less important in a group situation.
str - more damage. more important in a group situation.
int - more mana. more taps. more feigns. less important in a group.
sv magic - resist assorted dd and most "effect" magic.
sv fire - trolls have a base resist of 5. many, many npcs have fire attacks.
agi - more dodge, parry, riposte. no noticable difference <+20
dex - more procs. reaver already procs enough to "always on".

in that order. dex would possibly overtake agi if procs become more
neccessary.
