Subject: THE place to hunt


Well this place is called Unrest. If yer level 16-high 30's you will have
something here to kill. A full group of 18+ can kill most anything if they
have enough clerics.

To get here you have to get to the freeport docks, Sail to yhe butcherblock
mtns, Jump off the boat before the bay, best thing to do is find the road,
get an invis and hire a guide if u dont know the way.
And of course you want a bind. You will have to get to greater faydark. The
home of the wood elves. You can get bound right at the zone. be careful of
wondering guards irf you get curious in that zone.
After you get bound, Go back to the butcherblocks and follow the left wall.
You will avoid guards and get to Dagnor's cauldron. This is the zone that
takes you to unrest.
Now, Run along the coastline, the zone wall is very difficult and you will
take lots of falling dmg. You will see a dimly blue lit tunnel about halfway
around. This is unrest.
Unreast is an estate with a large mansion, a big courtyard with a 14th
century style hedge maze and, a few lil tricks.
At low and mid teen levels, i would reccomend going on the right side
of the estate near the gazebo. You will find Death beetles, tormented dead,
Jack o lanterns and a few skeletons.
Later teens you will wnat to group and pull from the first level mansion
and if you are in high teens and have a good FULL group, pull Carrion
Ghouls, Dry bones and lurking Mummies.
The things to look out for are Skeletal moinks, barbed bones, Greater
Dark Bones, Zombies of an unrest noble and MOST OF ALL, The festering hag.
She is a 30some level spellcaster from what i have seen. I have killed her
but only after she spends all her mana. at 20th level two spells from her
can kill me.
This is a great placee for loot. Many things carry small ringmail, spell
components, Platinum pieces and occasional bronze Platemail, vrey sought
after on this island. But unfortunaltely fer the ogers and Trolls, Its
small. Well i think i gave as much info as i can. Good luck if ya come
here.

I got 17-20th level in a week here and am skyrocketing up.
