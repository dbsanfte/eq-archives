now i've heard several people say that if you use archery you should have a
quiver, because arrows are pulled directly from it if you run out of arrows
in your ammo slot. however... being something of a bastard, i use LARGE
SEWING KITS as my containers. they weigh very little, and can hold anything
a backpack can hold, even a few things that a backpack CAN'T hold (like
larger weapons). the trick is making sure that you dont accidentally hit
combine... now, when i store arrows in a large sewing kit, ammo is drawn
from it automatically as if it were a quiver. and i am assuming that the kit
weighs less than and has more slots than a quiver. this is most likely a
bug, but a fairly minor one (with little real abuse power) so i havent
bothered to report it yet.. altho i pass it on here ;)
