Welcome to High Difficulty, Cronik. It is High difficulty for a reason. Here are some answers to your questions.

1) Food, water, weapons. Food & water can be purchased in Ogguk, Grobb, Neriak, or from other players (summoned or regular). Guards in Ogguk & Neriak con Dubious, but merchants should be at least apprehensive and will sell to you. Just stay away from the Tavern in Ogguk cause the cook there will KoS you. (I worship Innoruuk so mileage may vary) For weapons, your best are through quests or other players and combine weapons are a waste of money, IMHO. For 2 Handed, do the bone chip quest in Grobb then sharpen the rusty 2 hander you get at the Ogguk forge (stones available in Grobb). When you reach around 18th or 19th level, do the Grobb Cleaver quest from Carver C. In between, try to buy a FS 2 Hander from another player. I bought mine for 5p. Use the money you saved on weapons to buy some good armor. For 1 Handed, go with a mino axe (bought from another player). Magic weapons should not be necessary for 1 handed as 2 hander should be your main weapon (use 1 handed only for skill building), but get Polished Granite Tomahawk if you want one.

2) NPC's in S Ro will sell to you. It heard that they have recently started carrying combine weapons, but I have not personally verified this.

Hope this helps.

Scragg of Bristlebane (22nd level TSK)
