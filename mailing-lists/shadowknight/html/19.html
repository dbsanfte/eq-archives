Archery and Shadowknight

What does everyone think?


I think we make great archers for many reasons. It rounds out our
distences ( with out spells) so that we can more fulfil the back and
support roles if we have lots of tanks in the group. If we have to stand
back due to to much damange (usually because we disease the target) then
we can still hit the target and contribute. I myself have a lovely rough
Elm bow, and a skill of 45+ so I quite consistantly hit for 14 damage,
which is quite good for someone who is sitting back and healing. When we
get out pets, we can let teh pet tank for us while we archer away. The
pet will no doubt die, but then we don't have to split the xp for it :)

The downside is of course the cost. There is very little chance of
taking up fletching due to Rangers totally dominating that trade and
quite disliking SK's. However you will easily be able to buy a bow from
them, mine cost 5p, and there is one slightly lesser bow for 2p. Arrows
can usually be bought for about 9c each, which is quite resonable. But
it will take a lot of effort. I must have gone thruogh close to 1000
arrows by now, but I beleive it has been worth it.

What does everyone else think? Any other experiences?
