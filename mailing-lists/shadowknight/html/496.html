well, i didnt say voice graft was USEFUL, i said it was just great
entertainment :) a lot of people dont realize that you can make things other
than your pet speak with it. summon pet, cast voice graft on it, then click
on ANY npc that is within speaking range and just say something. the npc
will speak instead of you. this is great fun in many cases, especially since
you get invis at the same time. (try sitting next to a guard in a town and
have him start barking orders at a newbie who walks by. call out to people
by name and watch them scratch their heads in confusion :D).

VE starts doing 12 damage at lvl 22, and the damage increases 1 point every
other level. so at lvl 30 you are draining 16 life every time it hits, and
by 50 you are draining 26 points. this doesnt sound like much.. but remember
this is in ADDITION to whatever damage you do with your weapon, and those
points can quickly bridge the disparity between 1h and 2h weapons, since it
will go off more often with 1h attacks. while it isnt the greatest spell in
the world, its the first practical buff we get, so go ahead and use it when
you get the opportunity.
