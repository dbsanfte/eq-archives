Using a mana calculator from the net I found out I have
a 14 mana pool. Increasing Int by 2 points I think was a
waste of points. Why? Using a mana calculator this is
what I found out.
Level Int:60 Int:70 Int:80
9 14 16 18
10 28 32 36
11 42 48 54
16 112 128 144
22 196 224 252
24 224 256 288

You get 1 point more base mana for every 5 points you
increase Int by. Each level you add the base mana to
your curret total. So, my advice to you all is not to
buy Int only items. Get Int (+5 minimum if you didn't
increase int at the start) AND mana increasing items.
Jaded Electrum Bracers: Mana +10, HP +10 and AC +2 each
bracer. Keep them on you but not equiped when battling
as you need the AC more. Equip them only when casting.
Unless the AC reduction doesn't bother you. :)
I do not know what other types of items are out there
that can help mana pool and not lose AC.
Anyone on this list that does please let me know. :)
