as the story goes (pieced together from many beta sk players and numerous
snippets of brad-talk etc...)

in beta: melee fighters and hybrids were extremely powerful. sks were very
powerful. a troll sk was considered the strongest 1 to 1 class in the game
at lv50 by many.

is this true now? absolutely not, in my opinion. i tend to think most anyone
would agree. unfortunately, the idea that we are gods at lv50 lingers to
this day (most people don't even know that boil and banshee were taken from
us, our skills cap low (bash, dodge, archery etc...), we have approx 50%
less hp than a warrior at any given level [my TSK will have just over 1400
at lv50. as opposed to the 2100+ of a troll warrior.] etc).

so, our penalty lingers even though our strengths don't...and...to give you
an idea just how severe the penalty of playing a TSK is. i created a human
monk and a troll sk, put them both in the same zone and had them both kill
the EXACT same mobs (lv1 bats) the troll gained approx 60% less xp than the
monk each kill and continued to xp at the same -60% rate for as long as i
played out the comparison (level 3).

tis too bad i can't transfer all the xp from my tsk into a monk...he'd
likely be about lv60 (after factoring in the exponential increases in xp
reqs as levels progress) =)

brad/verant justifies this with "you were told this race/class combination
was of HIGH DIFFICULTY in the beginning". yeah i was and that's all good,
but EQ is the only place i've ever faced high difficulty without enhanced
reward.

in the end, i'm happy with my character and i'll continue to play him...but
some explanation of exactly what about our class is so godly that we deserve
last place in leveling speed would be nice and likely very entertaining.

kg - at least i can still solo the ghoul lord.
