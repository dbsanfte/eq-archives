From: "Krukhan Garabash" <krukhan@...>

- dexterity, attack skills (1h/2h slash, offense) and the "strength"
of the mob you are fighting all seem to have an effect on your hit
rate. in my experience, dex doesn't play a big role in this as
anything short of a major increase (10+ points) has had no noticable
effect.

- i recommend 2h slash for beginning characters (a 2h sword will kill
many newbie monsters in 1 or 2 good hits, greatly reducing the
target's chances of countering) and alternation between 1h and 2h as
necessary or when you reach a skill cap.

- i can see how 1h skill could rise more rapidly that 2h as a 1h
fighter would strike roughly 25% more in each battle than a 2h user.
though, in my experience, the rate of increase is pretty much equal.
