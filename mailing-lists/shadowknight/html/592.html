Some thoughts on this advice.

First of all, no lvl 9 SK can cast their first pet. Being a big dumb troll,
it looks like you may have to wait until lvl 11 though. You either get more
mana or things begin to cost less as your skill gets up, though. At the
beginning of lvl 9 my DE SK couldn't cast his pet, but after channeling and
... conjuration? whatever the pet skill is, got maxed near the end of the
level, then suddenly he could cast it at lvl 9.

As for intelligence vs mana stuff ... your point is valid for the time, but
once you get a few more levels, well, you'll first off get a larger mana
amount per int point of course, plus you'll find that there are less and
less pure mana bonuses, at least that aren't for pure casters. I would
rather have dexterity bracers than and extra ten mana and HP at lvl 23.

Dexterity is also a good one to get up. As a troll, yours may be hopelessly
low already, but mine was a 75, now it is around an 85 or 90. My bloodclaw
procs a TON now, and I love it. In a duel the other day, it proc'd *6*
times, a total of 300 dmg just from the proc (probably a bit less since 30
of it is a DoT that probably doesn't stack on itself). This was against a
lvl 23 (I was 22 at the time) SK wielding a 2hs weapon and we didn't use
spells or PvP tactics (it was just a straight up fight).
