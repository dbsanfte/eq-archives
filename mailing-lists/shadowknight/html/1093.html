I agree that most SKs will be kinda disappointed when
they actually get these spells.
But realistically, what else from the Necro spell book
were they going to give us?
They already dumbed down our attack spells at higher
levels, so believing that they would give us more
wouldnt make sense.
And other than an upgrade to Sheildskin, Necros dont
have much for buffing either.

Like the rest of our spells they are going to be weak
for the level in which we get them.
Use them as you see fit, or dont usee them at all. For
most of us, the latter would put us right back in the
same spot we are in now. So no harm-no foul.

I was really down on SheidSkin when they changed that.
And though i would rather have Banshee back,
SheildSkin isnt nearly as pathetic as i thought it
would be.
I use it to pull casters or things with their own HT.
And the SheildSkin usually eats a good part of the
initial casting.

So i dont think this will be as wonderful as some are
hoping, but i dont think it will be as bad as some
think it will be either.

Bathory D'Allatu
