on the 1hs vs 2hs issue, i personally tend toward 1hs, tho i keep both
skills maxed for my level. right now, my 1hs weapon, the polished granite
tomahawk, just completely blows away any 2h weapon i can either afford or
reasonably obtain on my own or with my usual group of companions. also, i
greatly prefer the speed of the 1hs weapons.. they do less damage, but if
they swing almost twice as fast you are ultimately getting more out of it ;)
coincidentally, 1hs allows you to use bash, which is very helpful both for
breaking spellcaster concentration, and seems to help in your own casting as
well. (try casting just after you do a bash, it seems to gain you a second
or so of casting time). by the by.. if ever you do decide to group up and
get yourself a polished granite tomahawk, do NOT group with somebody you
dont know. bad story, and one i may relate later..
