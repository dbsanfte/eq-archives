He laughs again and runs off.

Someone claims Voice Graft has RP value? Ummm...if you

Damage shield like Banshee would have been great.
A boost to VE would make sense. I stand by VE, even in
its current state. No you cant rely on it to heal you,
but it is a great in-combat spell. Damages and heals
some in addition to your swing. Its free basically
damage/heal. Cast before a fight, so no in battle time
wasted. And when it hits, its guaranteed. No
variation. At level 34, when it hits, its for
18DMG/Heal everytime.
