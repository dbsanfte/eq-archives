Subject: New Spells Apparently Being Implemented


Lately I hit the Necro/SK guild in Neriak (after the patch, to check spells
even tho no changes to SK spells were listed in the update).
I noticed that we SKs can now "use" (although we can't, since they require a
level of "61") at least the following three spells.

1) Infectious Cloud (now this could be useful, at least compated to what
we've got)
2) Spirit Armor (my mouth was open when I saw this one, we might actually be
getting a nice buff)
3) Augment Death (this puzzles me, a level 34 necro pet buffing spell, while
I suppose it would be useful, I was actually hoping they'd do away with the
pets and give us something else, ah well this could make the pet more
worthwhile...)

I talked to a GM about why they required level 61, and was told that any
spell requiring that level meant it was currently being implemented. Whoo
hoo, I'm excited, at least about Spirit Armor...
