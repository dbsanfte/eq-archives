I'm trying to collect good faction with the SK guilds, to date I am
warmly in Neriak, warmly in Freeport, amiable in Qeynos, and amiable
in Mistmoore. I thought it would take forever to go from glower up
to amiable killing Brothers Chintle,Estle, and Drash. Then a friend
offered a suggestion from her work on the Stein of Moggok: the
koalindl fish gives bloodsaber faction.

I was level 31 (not a good time to die), and bored of killing the
Millers and guards, so I got a bind in Surefall, and headed to
Qeynos under a cloak of shadows. Remember, Clerics and Paladins
cannot see invis, so I safely snuck into their temple and to the
pond where the protected fish are. Once their, stay by the stairs
into the water, and pull them with a spell. It will take about
3-5 fish to max your faction. Also, the guards will not come to the
water, so you will be safe until you decide to leave (when you no
longer get faction messages).

You should have seen the look on the newbies faces when a dark elf

I said "Really?" and pretended to be concerned. Another offered help
getting out, which I declined. He then asked for money... *sighs*
