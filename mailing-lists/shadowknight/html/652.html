Freeport Quest that involve Opal Darkbriar....
1)Hogcaller's Inn: In the warrior's guild, you are
given a quest to investigate the Hogcaller's Inn. End
result is info of a traitor named Opal. This I believe
is the Opal in the Arcane Science building.
2)Groflah Steadirt's Death: Netuk Phenzon at the
shadowknight's guild says that Lord Innoruuk has need of
your services. Your [mission] concerns a man named
Groflah Steadirt. Groflah has come into possession of a
piece of parchment that was taken from one of the Dismal
Rage's allies. You are instructed to find Groflah and
kill him, while returning the note to Netuk. Doing this
will improve your standing with the Dismal Rage and Opal
Darkbriar (most likely the Necro version) and lower it
with the Knights of Truth.

Ultimately I think it is actually the same Opal
Darkbriar, but the Arcane Science one is a traitor.
There is a third place Opal Darkbriar spawns which is in
the Slums of West Freeport on the second floor of one of
the buildings. She is usually under a minor Illusion
spell and looks like a locked chest (i think). In this
form, Hails do nothing, and I have no clue how to get
her to drop the illusion. Anyone else see this?
Has been a while since I have ventured above the sewers
in Freeport, so I am not sure how accurate this info is.
