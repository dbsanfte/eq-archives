Aim is everything in archery. If you are aiming at your target, and the
shot misses, it may hit some other NPC on the other side of the target. If
an NPC steps in the path of the arrow, the arrow may hit that NPC. If
either of these situation occurs, you'll have one angry NPC on your hands.

One of my other characters uses archery a lot, and this has happened many
times (one time the arrow went right through a gelatinous cube to hit a
beggar behind it, who proceeded to kill my character very quickly).

I make it a point to not use arrows if there's a chance I could hit
something other than the intended target. (i.e. No guards, giants or
griffons, etc within my character's sight.)

Also, if your target turns and runs in another direction, the arrow will
most times change its path to follow them.

Good luck, and be careful with those arrows!
