Actually you need 2 different parts of the Thex Mallet
from the elf skeleton. He drops the head and shaft i
believe. The 3rd part is on a 25ish zombie in befallen
named Commander Windstream (or something like that).
That would be the handle, which when you right click
on it, opens like a container, with a COMBINE button.
You put the other 2 pieces in there and COMBINE. And
low and behold..the Thex Mallet.To get him to spawn,or
so i hear, you need to turn in the note from Gix to
Skeleton Llrodd. To get the note you need to say some
phrase which i dont know, but i believe its a SK
guildmaster in Neriak that tells you what it is.

Good Luck with it
