Sir Lucan is in the top floor of the Militia baracks
(the first building you see as you enter West Gate from
the Commonlands, in a room by himself.

The person you are to meet/kill is the Shield Merchant
in the last hut in West Commons. This guy also wanders
around that part of the zone. If I remember correctly
that is. I was part of a Paladin Holy Sword mailing
list and it involved going to find someone in WC to give
them a note. They in turn gave you a note to return to
someone else. I think this also involved turning around
very quickly and following someone that spawned behind
you, and killing them.

The paladin half came down to:
Getting a note from the Marr Temple, giving it to the
Shield Merchant in WC to warn him about something,
getting a note to return to someone else, killing the
above NPC that spawns, taking his head or another
something from him back to the Temple.
This is quick and dirty, and may also have changed
somewhat. To find out for sure, go to the Paladin list
archives on Onelist for Holyswords@onelist.com (may want
to search for that as the name of the list is fuzzy) and
scan through it. It may take a bit, but it is worth a
try if you really, really, really want answers. :)

Hope this helps you out. It has been a while since I
actually did anything Paladin related so things may have
changed. Good luck.
