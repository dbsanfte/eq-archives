I thought I'd sure a few of the details about the life leech spell so you
know what you are aiming for.
If you don't want to know then don't read

*******
Spoilers
*******

















the life leech is 280 lifetap with 315 mana cast (about 2 bubs for me with
112 INT)
recast is about 2min and 2 .5 casting time.

It by no means turnes you into a healer, but does add another weapon to our
arsenal. I would consider it to be another form of Harm Touch but used much
more often.

The Quest itself is easily the hardest I have heard of so don't expect to do
it straight away. The soulfire is a childs play in comparison.
You must be lvl 50 to talk to the guy giving out the quest but you can get
the details and start earlier then that. The spell is lvl 49.
and start saving your money, need 3 K to do it 8)

Good luck!
