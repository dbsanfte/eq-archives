Yes there is a secret enterance to freeport. You walk into a section of wall
that is indistinguishable from the rest (except you can walk through it).
There is a series of tunnels underneath that go throughout all three
freeports. It is found in East freeport. I have 2 loc's written down, don't
remember which. (-356.70, -93.32) and (-893.84, -1253.82). It might be
easier to find by creating a human character in freeport and following it
out. The exit is under the docks to the south. You go down the stairs, under
the dock and in the corner by the fisherman you will mfind a door. Go into
this warehous, down the stairs and under the stairs. Under the stairs you
will find anot her secret door (cant be seen, just walk into the wall).

I am now a level 16 Sk and have killed every orc I could (for a while in
Crushbone and then in the commonlands). I am now able to walk the streets of
Eastern and Western Freeport unmolested (the guards con as Dubious). I was
only attacked by a Mage NPC by one of the zones to East Freeport. North
Freeport (where the bank is) should be a little Dicer though as the Paladins
live there.
