Hey all,

I guess my opinion on this Harm Touch thing is I don't think
anyone's getting shafted. EQ has been extremely well balanced from what
I've seen and half the fun for me is taking a game that's designed a
certain way and finding my own ways of dealing with whatever hardships
I'm presented with. I feel like trying to ask the dev team at this point
to make the game easier for me is pretty weak. That was the point of the
beta. Part of the reason I bought the game was because it seemed like a
challenge during the beta and a game that is challenging is much more
interesting than one that is easily mastered.

I also heard a story about people complaining about the Sand Giants at the
Oasis saying they were interfering with their play. My feeling about that
issue is: duh! thats what they're there for. I felt like those people
were whining too much. And while the issue of balance is a better issue
to discuss than how to blatantly make the game easier, I still feel that
it's slightly whiny to complain about a character that is intentionally
more difficult at the lower levels. Let's face it, at the highest levels,
we're going to be the most powerful players in the game. Wizards will be
able to do more quick damage, but in a long run fight, SKs are the only
ones that can dish it out AND take it. And if there's any such thing as a
soloable character at high levels (which I doubt), SKs are going to be the
closest thing to it.

So my perspective is to leave the HT alone and rise to the challenges that
the designers gave us. After all, we did choose our characters.
