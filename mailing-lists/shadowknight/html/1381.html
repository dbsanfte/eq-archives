As far as I know the siphon strength spell is not cumulative. But remember
to cast it on each MOB you fight. You will still only gain the strength
bonus once.

think of attack delay as the number of seconds between attacks. so a 7
damage 30 delay weapon will attack once every 3 seconds. a 35 delay every 3
and 1/2 seconds, etc. Buffs and equipment can reduce that delay (haste
spells, quickness, FBSS) and that reduces the delay of your weapon(s). Also
you can give your pet a weapon and armor. I usually find a rusty 2 handed
sword and watch my pet hit for alot more.
