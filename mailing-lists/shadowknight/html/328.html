Kiting is not an achery terms, but can be applied. It is when you ran from
something while attacking it and thus not being attacked yourself. So that with
a bow and if you have SoW (Spirit of the Wolf), you can run from something,
turn, shoot and then keep running without being attacked yourself. Quite a slow
wy to do it, but when done right is quite safe. will allow you to hunt yellows
and low red's your self, somewhat.
