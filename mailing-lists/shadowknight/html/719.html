To answer Brian's question, yes I definitely think a Lifetap would be better
than VE, but at the same time I'd rather we kept VE and simply threw the
pets out the window in exchange for weaker lifetaps. One thing everyone here
seems to agree on is that the pets are simply a mess, far too weak, etc, and
it would be hard to complain (and damn we've been complaining forever) if we
finally had some USEFUL AS HELL spells. I mean paladins get healing, rangers
also get healing, both because the class they come from (Cleric or Druid)
has those lines. What are necros famous for? The lifetap line, and yet sks
can't use a single one, aside from one which randomly goes off when you
slash, and therefore can only be minimally effective. This would let us heal
(tho in a different way) and at least I'd feel like there was a lot more
balance if that were the case. I also don't see why it would be so
dreadfully unbalanced if us sks could use these lifetap spells, especially
since rangers get raw DD spells and raw heal spells, and yet here we are
sitting with par dots and useless pets (and no buffs I might add). As much
as I love Verant, I can't help but think that the SK class was one of the
last created and therefore a little rushed, some of the things we have just
seem to make no sense when compared with what the other hybrids (our target
of comparion).
If you like details this is an idea that's been floating around my head for
a long time now:

Rank 1- Toss Leering Corpse, Replace with Lifetap (the level ONE necro spell
that steals 4-5 hp, less than minor healing)
Toss Reclaim Energy, Replace with Cold Light (even tho this would be
largely useless Reclaim Energy DEFINES useless)

Rank 2- Toss Bone Walk, Replace with either a lifetap (maybe not tho, I
wouldn't want to be unbalanced), or a line we don't have at all like
Grim Aura.
Toss Mend Bones, Replace with Poison Bolt (not great, but beats another
useless one)

Other Ranks- Toss the pets, replace with X. Well when I got my rank 3 spells
for the first time I was actually satisfied with what I had for the vast
most part (being a dark elf I didn't need Deadeye but realize that the
humans do etc). Rank 3 is also the last one I've seen so I can't really
suggest for the others without having tested them, although something that
seems obvious at the moment is that Shieldskin at 39 can't be too useful
(casters are using that at 16). Another thing I've noticed is that our final
rank seems to be incomplete (only 5 spells?) and that one of them is a
virtual clone of Deadeye (what's up with Shadow Sight?). That final rank
needs to be changed if any do. The bottom line is that whatever final
decisions are made, the SK class simply is not complete in it's current
state.

Well was that a rant? I guess so, but oh well it felt good =)
