i completely agree, i would personally like to sit down with the game
balance programmers and get THEIR explainations of how paladins and SK are
evenly matched. however, i think i can understand a few possible reasons why
things are as they are:

ok, so at lvl 9 a paladin could probably beat up a lvl 9 sk good, on account
of the paladin has 3 times normal hp (due to all the healing), whereas we
get a spell that bites him for 5 damage and suck away some of his str. but
eventually i think we accelerate in power faster than paladins.. by the time
we get to lvl 50 in our best equipment, we would be fully capable of beating
the snot out of a lvl 50 paladin in his best equipment. (also, we dont have
to kill the lava dragon for a darkreaver ;)) upon checking back to the guild
periodically i have seen a great many necro spells being imped for SK use at
high level, including some of the powerful DOT spells, spirit armor, and
expulse undead.

also, while i admit that HT vs lay on hands isnt very fair, remember that HT
IS rather powerful. most melee combat classes cant boast the ability to
knock off a third or a half of their target's life with a single attack and
still be able to go ahead and wail away with nasty implements of pointy
death. my suggestion is not to boost HT, but to TONE DOWN lay on hands. the
ability to heal that much is just not reasonable; altho i have heard of
people having lay on hands interrupted, while it is not possible to
interrupt HT that i have seen, it being instantaneous. can anyone confirm
the possibility of lay on hands to be interrupted?

there is a very good reason why HT can be resisted: imagine about 20 lvl 10
sk all charge a griffin and simultaneously lay a harm touch on it, causing
it to die instantly. now somebody is going to get a crapload of xp out of
that, and it'd be a hideously unfair trick for us. even more lame than the
little bard accelerando-run in circles-bellow thing! i admit i become quite
annoyed seeing HT resisted or soaked altogether, but i try very hard not to
fight things that are guaranteed to resist it when it can be helped anyway :)
