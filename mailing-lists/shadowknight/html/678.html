"twinked" in my mind is when one of you or your friends high level characters gives you items or money that you couldn't reasonably obtain at your current level. I, myself, bought a FS2HS at around 10th level for 5p. It was a good deal, but good deals aren't twinks. Now if someone sold me a Dark Reaver for 5p, then I'd consider it a twink. Gifts and such are not considered twinks IMHO unless you could not obtain them yourself.

Lyguna gives you the Brutechopper (or other random item) through a quest. Do not kill her, she is a nice Troll (although a bit of a heartbreaker of you ask Hulkuk). Just talk to her, she'll tell you what she wants (a typical women, hehe).

BTW, don't go into Guk unless in an experience group. It is a deathtrap, apparently designed by Verant for taking levels away from people.
