Lord Grimrot is in the little graveyard just south of the bridge.
He spawns in two forms, skeletal (25th level wielding a rusty scythe)
and human (35th level, wielding the Opal Scythe). He is a Shadowknight,
with a nasty Harm Touch.

Beware! NPC SK's can harm touch MORE THAN ONCE, or at least Drelzna
can in Najena. I've seen her do it 3 times in one fight, and that can
be really, really bad.
