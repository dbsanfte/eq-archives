Few replies here 8)

I'm power gamer alkl the way with a 50 human SK and a 48 barb rogue.
Just got myself some more Umbral so I can get my AC up around 860 which is
nice. for any hunting above 45 + your tanks should have 800 AC minimum and 850
+ preferably.

Warriors still make much better tanks, especially after they get plane gear,
but SK's are way more versatile and have more options. They are all the best
for general CRing due to both invis's and FD. I used to think darkforged was
pretty average, but looking at the AC on some of it, it seems worthwhile to
get. Remeber, AC is everything.

At lvl 20 you might want to try unrest, sol A, cazic, UGuk. You should also be
trying to find a good group of regular players. don't be discouraged if they
make mistakes, but watch to see if they learn from them and be better. Nothing
will get you to 50 quicker then having a solid group you can always party with
who know how each other plays.

FG's are rather simple for us now due to lots of experience. 2 groups is about
the minimum to try them with though. 4 is preferable for the learning process
until you know what to do. If you have 48 + enchanters they will be able to
charm one FG which will aid the fight a lot. otherwise you should have 3 group
conentrating on 1 FG and 1 group tanking the other ones off the casters /
cleric. After the first 3 (remember to charge across the bridge for that one)
you can have your cleric bind just there which will speed up CR's heaps. make
sure the rebind when they get out though. The worst pull you should get is 4,
and that is later on towards the end. Also, make sure you use a lvl 50 to pull
the throne room. they have a better chane of pulling just a couple of FG's
instead of a lot. if all else fails, try FD pulling.

As for a dragon, not having a bard is going to hurt you. A lot!
You need to have your mr at 100 or 150 + and fire resist at 150 to 200 +. On
everyone. so start collecting your jewellery and items to get up to those
numbers. they are vital. forget stats and AC and mana and everything until you
are at those resists or you will get creamed. general groupings is 2 cleric
bard 3 tanks for at least 3 groups and the casters in the rest. the melee are
tghe key to winning the fight. once all the melee are dead, the fight is over.
Naked casters charging the dragon will onlylose them xp so make them stop asap.
grab the corpses and form up for another try. due to the recent changed,
dragons are now oneshot only. by the time you are ready to try as 2 nd, they
are at full health, so its pretty much all or nothing. but you should try and
even if you fail you willlearn from it and gain experiece.

In both cases you should have a loot system set up before hand. nothing sours a
raid more then arguments over loot afterwards, and worse still, ninjalooting.

Planes would take few pages to detail, so I'll leave you to experient. if
you're still having trouble ask again 8)

Hope this helps all the people I answered.
