If you're looking for an easy way to some decent armor you might want to see
if you can creep down to Lower Guk and pick up some Shin armor. There are
often corpses around just waiting to be looted since it is no drop and
anyone there already has it or doesn't want it. It drops of Shin Ghoul
Knights, although at 20 you certainly won't be killing them yourself (just
mooching the loot lol), although people shouldn't mind. Silvery-Plated
Bracer is a nice thing to have, mainly because of the look of course (poison
and disease resists are nice too since it augemnts our natural resists as
sks), and the fact that it isn't too expensive.
BI doesn't have good AC, but personally I put on certain pieces once I
started to get really sick of bronze (to the point where I hated my char and
refused to play unless I looked sufficiently cool).
The BIBs is great for the 20s, try to have a Reaver by 30 tho (you won't
regret it, being the DE you are hehe).
As for where to hunt, I soloed or grouped the Aviak Guards in Lake Rathe,
another option would be Unrest, although that might not be great exp since
you'll have to acquaint yourself with the dungeon (unless you alrady know
it).
