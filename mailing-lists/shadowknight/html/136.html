I think a couple of my problems were that I wasn't role-playing an SK -- I
chose SK because it was a cool class, not because I wanted to necessarily
be evil. Being a human was a compromise for me since that meant I wasn't
KoS in as many areas as, say, a troll SK (which must be one lonely dude).

You have some good points -- a 10th level is nothing in the game, so I may
not have given the SK a fair chance. The problem is that I think part of
the game his horked when it comes to XP and level increases. Since I level
more slowly than my other party members, I'm always behind on levels. This
is amplified by the fact that higher level party members get more XP! So
eventually I'm stuck switching to a lower level party or just never getting
experience -- we started out equally, and they are at 12th level and I'm at
10th level (about a 20% difference in XP, even though we've been partied
the whole time).

The other factor that I alluded to is that I'm basically a nuisance to our
three person party, barring the role-playing aspects. I'm not an effective
spell caster, I can't heal, they constantly have to worry about me being
KoS, and I don't have bind, gate, or invis. I'm a worse tank than the
necro's pet, and this isn't a skill thing -- I'm maxed on 1HS and 2HS, have
good STR and STA, and wield a Mino Axe. So I can dish out damage, but the
necro's pet misses less, is expendable, doesn't get diseased, doesn't need
to be healed, and does as much damage as me.

So that leaves HT as my only real saving grace, but the wizard in the party
can dish out 4x what my HT does in one battle.

Which means that I'm effective at preventing the spell casters from being
attacked, but in terms of any positive contributions to a battle (other
than as a human shield), I really don't have much. I do some token melee
damage, and that's it.

Brian
