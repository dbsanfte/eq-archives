good luck to you :) a few tips on the zombie to make sure you are prepared:

do NOT use area effect spells for the fight. he'll spawn right next to
kazzel.. and making kazzel angry is not healthy.

zombie uses AE fear, so get the magic resist of everyone there as high as
possible.. no good being feared into a cyclops and having more mess to deal
with.

dont try to fear kite the zombie :) he will teleport.. and then start
summoning you one by one.. not pleasant.

we were shocked when the zombie i spawned had a two handed sword :o it only
turned out to be fine steel, but even so it looked pretty cool for this
gigantic skeleton to be chopping us with a 10 foot long sword :)
