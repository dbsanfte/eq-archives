Typical gear. Well, I am a lvl 24 Human SK. /played is 33 days -- maybe a
little high for my level but not TOO outrageous for a SK as far as I can
tell comparing it to others. Rundowns on different items for the last ten
levels.

Weapons: I wielded a mino axe until 15, had a tentacle whip for a few
levels, then picked up a barbed whip for 43pp (all my savings) at 18. I used
that for I think two levels, then at about 20 I was camping (ugh!) jboots
with a group of friends all five to eight levels above me and we got a
bloodclaw, which was given to me. I learned piercing and use it still.
Currently realize that with the great deal of SK 2hs weapons that I had
better learn it, so I am SLOWLY learning 2hs with a store-bought claymore. I
have no funds to buy a real 2hs weapon so I will likely try and get a BBC or
something myself (with my friends that is) when my skill gets better.
Ultimate goal is to get a Ykesha or Wakazashi (which, btw, how are SK's
supposed to get a Wakazashi when the whole quest is now in Mistmoore and,
hmm, we get bad faction in Mistmoore-- but that is another point altogether.
:) and have a 1hs/shield to use against casters and a good 2hs for
straight-up damage.

Armor: At 15 I believe I finally had almost a full suit of banded. At 24, my
armor is a med bronze breast and legs. Barbed arms for the dex that I got
for myself three days ago, banded face, raw-hide cloak (anyone got an extra
lizzie cloak on sol-ro for sale?), enchanter made rings, earrings and wrists
(get them at cost as the enchanter is my RL friend and we all play together
and help each other) to boost either int or dex to the max depending on the
situation (int to learn 2hs, dex for everyting else), no mask (grot mask on
its way), savants cap (you ever seen a human in a trashcan, I mean, plate
helm? God-awful ugly) and banded boots. I picked this stuff up slowly,
usually not going out looking for it, but when someone was selling it cheap,
or, in the case of the legs, someone sold it to a vendor.

Toys: Too expensive. Except for my crude stein which is my absolute most
cherished possesion which I received from a lvl 50 Paladin in exchange for
bog juice the day before someone from Verant told the recipe to everyone.
(Not my favorite day ever in EQ when I had the market cornered, after two
weeks of experimentation, on something that every high level paladin wanted
and I could, in all SK-spirit, gouge them for, and the next day everyone and
their mother knew about it. Dang!).

Bank: Not much. An Ashenwood short spear that I picked up from Drelzna three
nights ago when trying to get boots again (finally got them. 55 hours

Have maybe 90pp total, 80pp of that I made today when exploring the OOT and
