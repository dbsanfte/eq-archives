One of the reasons I don't like Lifetap is that I have to disengage the
actual combat to cast the spell (are lifetap spells quick?). With VE I can
cast once and stay in the battle. Also I've noticed that it counts as a 3rd
attack (I've doubled and still had the VE effect go off). If they could just
up the damage and make it go off more often that would be great with me.
Maybe give VE at 15 and a upgraded version at 30 (is there a upgraded
version???).

Also on pets, I think they have a use, but only up to about the third circle
of spells (lvl 22). I was still soloing quite a bit at that point and the
pet was nice to have. I do think that they need to dumb a few of pet utility
spells though and replace them with something that might someday be mistaken
for something useful (Voice Graft, Mend Bones and Reclaim Energy - I have
never used any of these except to see what they do). Personally I'd like to
see us get the Grim Aura line of spells.

Anyway, just some thoughts.
