Subject: one problem thats been bothering me


now, for the most part i'm a "happy sk". i enjoy playing the class, my
friends seem to enjoy having me tag along, and i tend to dismiss most of the
complains as whines or just uninformed rants.

but one comment keeps coming up that i find it hard to just dismiss offhand.
that is the fact that sk, along with the other hybrids, have an experience
penalty. and the question is, why? most sk (DE, troll, and ogre) have
additional racial xp penalties, making them by far the slowest levelers of
the game. but at high level, we really are not that much more powerful than
anybody else, if at all. in fact, necromancers and druids have no
discernable xp penalty by comparison, and, well, are incredibly powerful.
now that warriors have had a lot of beefing, and their xp remains the same..
the xp penalty does seem to make less and less sense.

an xp penalty assumes some sort of "reward" for reaching higher level..
which there doesnt seem to be for the hybrid classes at this time..

not a rant, just an observation on the ONE complaint of others that i am
unable to readily answer :)
