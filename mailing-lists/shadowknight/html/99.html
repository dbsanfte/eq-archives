I fully admit, and am glad for it, that SKs will indeed be a class to
be reckoned with.

However i must stick with my previous comments on HT. Sure if a few SKs
got together and HT'd a Griffon to death quick and easy, they would get
good exp. But that would be it for another hour and a half. If thats
what they want to do with their HT instead of keeping it for an
emergency where HT is the only thing that'll save them, thats up to the
individuals involved. Its not like they'll be able to walk around and
HT everything to death right after that.

And as for LoH getting interrupted, i have heard this about HT,
although its never happened to me.
I would like to hear from any SK on this.
If this is true, then we're back to square one. LoH 1000 healing, HT
500dmg, but resisted by the creatures you would actually use it
against.

And we all know that a toning down of LoH just isn't going to happen.
We could campaign the rest of our lives and it'll never even be
considered.
So what's left?
Tweak HT.
HT is only powerful WHEN it works. Which seems to be less and less as
we gain higher levels and fight bigger creatures.

And i still feel that it should work every time.

But aside from our differences of opinion, i would like to hear what
you think would be a good way to get the Dev team's attention with our
various concerns.

Talking about these topics on message boards and mailing lists is OK,
but this wont accomplish anything as far as the game is concerned.
Lets do that pro-active thing i hear so much about.

Thanks for responding,
