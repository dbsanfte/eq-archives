I had planned to try dueling a friend and cast Siphon on them to see if
it stacks on them, or if it wears off quickly, but just havent got
around to it yet. But I will post my findings as soon as i do, unless
someone beats me to it.

Now as far as casting wasting a swing with a weapon, true it will, IF
you time it wrong. I always cast right after i swing. When the next
round starts, im swinging again as normal. When i time it like this, it
doesnt seem to take away any of my melee.

And i did notice that when you cast Siphon, your ATTACK goes up the
same amount as your STR does.

Plus what does it hurt to cast it. Its not a spell you absolutely need
to win a battle, so if it gets interrupted, who cares? But its good for
practicing Alter (i believe). This will help in the future when we get
more useful spells.
