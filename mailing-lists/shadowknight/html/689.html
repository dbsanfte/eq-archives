The ruby that Lynuga wants is just a store-bought one. I bought one at Shiny
Tings in Neriak for about 160pp, you can also get them in Highkeep (but
we're KOS there) and other cities too, I'm sure.


**Spoiler below, not for the faint of heart**
























There are possible things you can get for the ruby once you give them (one
at a time) to Lynuga.

Brutechopper (most common reward) -- 16/50 2HS axe

Midnight Mallet -- don't remember stats, mid-level shaman 2H hammer

Ivandyr's Hoop (rarest) -- This is an earring that casters love, it's AC6
Int6 Wis6 I think, this is the jackpot. If you get one, feel very very
lucky. I got one as a newbie and traded it for dwarven work boots, and the
kicker is that I got ripped off! <G>

PS: You can sell back the Brutechopper to an NPC for 60pp and MM goes for
more. So it's like playing the lottery where 100pp buys you a ticket (160
for ruby, 60 back) and if you get a Hoop, look forward to crossing some
things off your wish list. :)
