just a few quick comments:
we DO get an invis spell, gather shadows, at lvl 30. yeah, it is awfully
late to be getting a travel related spell, but by then we're getting other
nifty spells as well. also, not ALL of our spells are useless at the level
we get them. at lvl 15 clinging darkness makes you considerably more
fearsome: not only is it a DOT spell that helps you kill things faster, it
also slows them, meaning you can run if you need to, and prevent THEM from
trying to escape. the pet spell was actually useful for lvls 15-17, as it
was keeping up with me as far as hit rate and damage was concerned, and
sometimes surpassing me. (remember that a monster of your same level can
probably beat the crap out of you unless you are lucky or a lot smarter than
it, so just because its several levels weaker than you doesnt make the pet
COMPLETELY useless..). fear is also useful, under controlled circumstances.
i.e., do NOT use fear on a froglok in guk unless you want to take a
screenshot of 30 ton knights before you die.

i am also concerned about the vampiric embrace issue, and sincerely hoping
they get their act together and give it lifetapping ability rather than str
drain by the time i reach lvl 22. after all, i already HAVE a str drain
spell, and a ranged one at that. if i dont have a cleric around, i am SOL if
i get beat up badly. and i'd rather not have to wait until i get a
darkreaver to be able to heal myself :)

as for your ideas on harm touch, i think i prefer method one. if you raised
it to the level of LoH in terms of damage, it would be blatantly
overpowered. most creatures you fight probably dont have that much more than
20 times your level in hp, and you'd see SK slaughtering nasties left and
right without breaking a sweat. a non-resistance policy may allow for some
abuse issues, but at least would put it more on the same footing with LoH.
