Subject: armed pets


pets of any level will use ANY 1h or 2h weapon you give them, so be careful
and dont accidentally give your pet your well balanced 2h axe :) pets cannot
dual wield tho, so it is usually best to give it a 2h weapon. they can wear
shields, but only if they wield a 1h weapon with it. however, i never give
mine a shield, since it can bash anyway and shields cost money. i usually
just give my pet a rusty weapon i pull off of something, or if i'm feeling
particularly extravagant, a bronze weapon.
