Jyle is, 35? You won't be killing jyle solo. He is 30 or 35 and also has a
30 or 35 (actually, 30 I think for both of them) warrior guard. Jyle is a
druid so you can't kite him. But the Errolisi sisters are another story.
They are actually pretty low level. 10 maybe? I don't remember. They were
green to me by the time I got there. There are some named sisters however
that are 25 or so and are quite good experience and generally not camped
seeing as how they are "good" If you can take the two that are in a hut
together you can also make good money seeing as how one of them (larissa I
think) has a 10-plat longsword every time (8minute spawn).

As for crushbone, yeah, trolls are kos there by default. I don't know how
hard it is to change that (I don't know any trolls that have tried). If/when
you do go there, the slaves are very low level, like level 5 maybe 10. I
often killed them in a single double attack at level 20 with my reaper of
the dead. Do not try and kill the little gnome magician though unless you
are 30+ (maybe higher) as he is a really nasty bugger.
