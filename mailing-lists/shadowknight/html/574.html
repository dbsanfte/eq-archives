A couple of considerations.

I don't think it matters when you pick up archery. I didn't until 15th.
Since 75 is the max, it doesn't matter.

You will be able to hit stuff with it just fine. I have no problem at all at
23rd level.

Yep, kiting is boring, and you'd need to carry a few BOXES of arrows with
you. I don't kite.

Pulling--I always use my bow when pulling, especially outdoors. Yes, I use
darkness, but as I run in, instead of just running straight to my group, I
just turn around once in a while and fire off an arrow, run, fire, run ...
etc. By the time I get it in, I've usually knocked off 50-150hp. It hardly
takes any more time, and that is that many more HP we don't have to deal
with.

Runners -- when OOM or with afast runner, it is nice to put an arrow in
their back.

That said, archery is not a *critical* skill, but it is one that is very
nice to have. I only use an elm bow still (14dmg max) and I still do quite
well with it. It will take you some time to master it (depending on how good
your int is (de/human or troll/ogre?)) and just how much you practice it. I
play with a group of friends, so I was able to practice it quite a bit when
I picked it up. Took maybe a week or so to get it maxed (same as when I
picked up piercing at 21).
