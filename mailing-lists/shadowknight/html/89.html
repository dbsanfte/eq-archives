Subject: Concerns With SK Class


I am new to this mailing list, so if this ends up being a repeat of
messages sent out by others, i apologize in advance.

I play a Human Shadow Knight named Bathory on the Cazic-Thule server,
currently level 11 and residing in Qeynos.

I have noticed personally as I gain levels and have heard from many
other SKs of all races and levels that there seem to be problems with
certain areas of this class.
My main concern is with Harm Touch. This is supposed to be the
opposite, but EQUAL of the Paladin's Lay on Hands.

1. Now lets crunch some numbers here.
-LoH potentially heals upto about 1000HP
-HT at its best, at level 50, will do 501 pts dmg
- 501=1000 ? Not last I knew
Now here's my thoughts on this. I do feel that our HT should do equal
dmg that LoH does healing. Now many feel, including some SKs, that this
would be too powerful. And i can see that point. But my question is,
why do Paladins get such a strong healing spell? If HT is supposed to
be the equal of paladins LoH, why isnt it?

2. HT is resisted way too often for comfort when fighting higher level
creatures. For a "special" feature, it doesn't seem too special when
you need to do that 111 pts damage because you're getting stomped on
and you see the message "target has resisted your Harm Touch".
Lets go back to LoH. How often is that resisted? I review many message
boards, and i have yet to see a single complaint about LoH being
resisted.


3. Partial Damage. Ive seen myself and heard from many others that even
when it doesn't get resisted totally, it will do only half damage. And
an even more disturbing report is that on occasion it will do only
19dmg. Unless you're having trouble with that pesky large rat, 19dmg
isn't gonna do a whole heck of a lot.
LoH? Again, i've heard no complaints on this.

Not to mention of course, for something we can use only once a day, it
should work and work to its full potential every time.

4. I feel, to make HT a viable weapon, it needs to either, do equal
damage as LoH does healing, or boost its power some but have it
available more than once per day. And in either case have it NEVER
resisted.

Those are my main concerns.

My other concerns I feel are less important, but important still.
I feel the spells we received could be boosted some, or at least have
available some spells that are more useful.
The first set we get are useful, but kinda lame. OK, i can deal with
that, because its our first set. But at level 15, I see no reason why
we shouldn't have a HP drain spell and a shielding spell. And have a
new drain spell every circle after that, especially since i have yet to
hear any success with Vampiric Embrace actually working the way it
supposed to.
And if Necros get gate at level 5, giving it to us at level 22 or so,
isnt too much to ask.

Some may say that Paladins spells are lame too, so why am i
complaining? Well, lets get back to the fact that our damage spells are
resisted by most of the creatures that we'll be fighting. I don't
believe Paladins have this problem with their healing and buffing
spells. So we should have more useful and effective spells to
compensate for this.

Its not my intention to make SKs all powerful or equal to Necromancers
in casting abilities, but I do feel we are not equal, as we should be,
overall.

The fighting abilties, I have yet to find a reason to complain. We
eventually get all the main features of fighters except Kick and
Duel-Wield. I have no problem waiting for them as long as I do get
them. We can wear any type of armor and use any type of weapon except
for a very few.

Now let me state that I do indeed like being a SK, i like being evil,
doing evil quests and not having to be nice all the time. And I fully
accept being hated by NPCs and occasionally KoS.

I have already submitted a Feedback Form via Sony Station and I know
others have as well. But it seems a bigger kick in the butt is needed
to get the Dev team to notice this. I would like to stage a massive
write in by SKs of all races and levels on all servers.

I am open to suggestions on when and how this should be done. Whether
it would be more effective while on-line, using the /feedback command
on a Friday or Sat. night getting as many SKs on-line to do it as
possible or just getting as many as possible at their convenience to
submit thru Sony Station's Feedback Form.

This ended up being way longer than i originally thought, and i'm sorry
if i bored you,but these are issues that I feel are important and after
speaking with many other SKs, i know i'm not alone.

Any feedback or suggestions are welcome, whether they agree with my
opinions or not.

Thanks For Hanging in there,

Bathory
