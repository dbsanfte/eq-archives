to'biath the drunken scribe in neriak wants red wine, not fish wine :)

i believe the 3 DE merchants in sro will give quests that raise faction in
neriak.

as for the necro guild in qeynos, there are two ways to get there:

1). in the arena of fate (the pvp area of qeynos, by the warrior guild)
there are some bones lying on the ground. follow them underneath the arena
of fate, and past some prison cells, where they will come to a halt at a
wall. pass through the wall and follow the tunnel, where you will soon come
out in qeynos sewers very near the bloodsaber's guild area. if you are KoS
by the steel warriors, and not high enough level to get invis, this method
WILL cause you to die messily, and possibly in a place where corpse recovery
is difficult.

2). explore the qeynos sewers until you see the words "Beware the
Plaguebringers!" scrawled on a wall. this sign means you are near a hidden
entrance to the bloodsaber guild; look for a false wall here, and wander
around behind it until you find the guild itself.

you will recognize the guild area by passing ranks of stationary skeletons,
then passing through a spear gate into a room lined with red rock, several
surly looking evil guys, and a very impressive pillar of blood in front of
and above you :)
