Just my experiences with archery.

I have a Rough Elm Recurve Bow which has a base 13 damage. With the 1 dmg
arrows, I can do 14 damage to beasties. I also have an 80 skill in Archery,
having practiced and practices (and practiced and practiced) till my thumbs
bleed. Now that I have a good skill and a good bow, I find it quite
worthwhile. But I would not reccomend it for anyone that is shy on spending
a LOT of time working on getting it up (I probably fired 3,000+ arrows in my
training). My bow does almost as much damage as a mino axe, although it is
slower (48 I think maybe 51, compared to 38 for mino axe).

As for fletching ... there is nowhere on Antonica to get fletching supplies
except surefall glade. The only other plalce I know to get bowyering
supplies is in Oggok which is not an option for humans (DE, Trolls and Ogres
only). Even ones that worship Innoruuk. :(
