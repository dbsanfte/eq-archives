Although 2 handed slashing is decent with a sheild and does give us the bash
ability I feel that since we Shadow Knights never get dual weild, 2 handed
slashing is a better choice for a Shadow Knight as we do get double attack.
Now this is one of the areas where a Dark Elf Shadow Knight truly shines
though...Due to out naturally high Int I have found that I consistantly max
all all my combat skills before leveling without fail everytime. My Shadow
Knight character is level 11 and this has held true everytime. Orges and
Trolls that I have spoken to cannot claim this as their Int is naturally
quite low and does play a big part in their skill advancement. This being
the case I really see no problem at all with keeping Both 1H Slashing and 2H
Slashing maxed out through out your characters growth.
