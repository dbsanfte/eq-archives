actually.. human sk is NOT easy. in fact, it is probably THE single hardest
class/race combo in the game.. and if you are a worshipper of toxxy.. talk
about hard times :) at least the qeynos SK guild kicks ass..

the reason for this is that our stats are mediocre, and despite whatever
info is out there our faction is NOT significantly better anywhere than the
other SK races. human SK can still only get into 3 towns (freeport, qeynos,
and neriak), which is par for SK of any race. really our main advantages is
that we are physically a bit stronger than dark elves, are more agile and
intelligent than ogres and trolls, and we have one of the highest charisma
ratings in the game (buying things cheap is a definite plus). with all my
jeweller buddy's special equipment, i can get cha over 100 ;) if you want to
make a first-time sk, be a troll. your xp gains will really suck (penalty
for combo class AND penalty for large race) but the hp regen REALLY helps
you, as well as your mighty trollish strength. plus trolls have some of the
best SK quests that WORK at the moment. so basically, be a troll unless you
seek even greater challenge :) just my 2 pesos
