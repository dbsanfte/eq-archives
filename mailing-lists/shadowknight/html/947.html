Some guards do see thru invis. I hear the Dwarven
guards are pretty good at that as well. Plus they can
outrun SOW. How such stumpy little legs can move that
fast is beyond me...

As suggested previously, con them...if they give you
anything other than indifferent, they can see you.
Gather Shadows is REALLY flaky. Maybe i just have bad
luck, but it will wear off sometimes even before the
spell effects are finished swirling around me. And in
some places, its just horrible. In N Ro, oasis, and S
Ro, im lucky if it lasts 30 seconds. Cazic Thule and
Guk also dont treat my invis with much respect.

I would STRONGLY suggest against binding in Oggok.
Ogers dont like humans regardless of the fact that we
are SKs. And the Bouncers see thru Shadows all the
time. Plus remember that when you hit your bind point,
its usually after youre dead. No spells, no Invis at
all. So if for some strange reason an Orge is near
your Bind point, you may get stuck in a very nasty
death/spawn/death loop. No pretty.

Feign death. Has saved my but a few times. BUT..it
does have a casting time of 1.5 seconds, so it can be
interrupted. And it doesnt always work. As a matter of
fact, it has worked for me maybe only 25% of the time.
However this may depend on how close you are to the
MOB(s) that are aggro on you.
It seems to work pretty well in Guk. I went LD once
and came back to my group being gone, a shaman, undead
knight and some warrior who was hitting me for 80s. I
finally got a Feign off and it worked even with them
beating on me. It lasts forever as long as you just
lay there. I believe the aggro eventually leaves the
MOB, so you can theoretically, get up and walk away
after they are out of sight. Try that one at your own
risk though.
Cazic Thule on the other hand.. I have much less
success in CT with feign, though it has worked. They
just arent fooled as easily as the froggies
apparently.

Good Luck

Bathory D'Allatu
