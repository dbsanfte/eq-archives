ok, to get the bone bladed claymore i suggest a group with an average level
of at least 20. if you bring many people lower level than that, you may not
survive the waits between the spawns of the troll sk, even tho you will have
no trouble just killing him. i recommend that you dont waste money on a
combine 2hander if you plan on getting the bbc unless you have a great deal
of money to throw around. an ordinary 2hander or fine steel 2hander will
serve you well until you get the bbc, but remember to bring along some
magical weapon to fight the ghouls and ice bones you are sure to find down
there in befallen.

specialize is a skill that allows a spellcaster to choose one of the sorcery
skills (abjuration, divination, evocation, conjuration) to "specialize" in.
this basically means that when you specialize, spells that use that skill
cost less to cast, cast faster, and may have greater effect once cast.
specializing in one group of spells doesnt hurt the other magic skills tho,
i dont think.

as for the tentacle terrors.. have you tried having the party cleric lull
one right before you lure the other? i havent fought there often, so i dont
know how well lull works on those things.
