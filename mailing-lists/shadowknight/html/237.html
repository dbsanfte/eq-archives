Subject: Guk and Befallen


there are 3 major routes across the continent between Qeynos and Freeport:
you can go thru the gorge of king xorbb, which passes rivervale, but this is
a rather unpleasant trip for evil folk. you can go thru highpass, which
while maybe a bit more dangerous if you are under lvl 20, is both faster
than the gorge route AND doesnt involve npcs that are KoS to shadowknights.
both of these routes end up in kithicor forest, and if you continue past
this you get to the west commonlands. befallen is in this zone, along the
southern zone wall. it appears as a small cave near the base of the mountain
(with a large dune in front of it), and if you approach it at night, it glows.

the third route is to go to south karana, past the aviak villiage, and enter
lake rathetear. from here you can go to the mountains of rathe to feerrott
jungle to innothule swamp. guk is in the swamp, about halfway along the
eastern zone wall. while i know this route exists, i have never gotten
around to passing this way myself, and i've heard that the mountains are
HUGE. however, i'm fairly certain that it would be quicker to take this path
to guk than to go thru kithicor and freeport if you are headed there from
qeynos. on the way to guk, you will pass both the ogre and troll cities, as
well as the lost temple of cazic-thule in feerrott (a mid to high level
dungeon where you can obtain some very nice loot). the main problem this
poses to human shadowknights is that neither the ogres or trolls will let us
in, and the nearest bind point to guk for us is freeport. in the cases of
both befallen and guk, it'd be safer for you to temporarily move to freeport
as a base until you are done with both dungeons. the only other alternative
is to remain bound to qeynos and hope somebody there has a spirit of the
wolf free should the unexpected happen.. which it will, as both dungeons
have high train traffic.
