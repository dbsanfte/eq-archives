I can tell you that my wood elf druid character started out as indifferent
to the stormguard and quickly became amiable when i kiiled a few dozen orcs
and goblins. Whether you can become non-kos or not remains to be seen, but
this would depend on your level since your max faction increases as your
level increases. Some of you who have adventured around blackburrow might
have noticed that eventually Holly Windstalker (or maybe Cros Treewind - i
cant remember) becomes indifferent if you have killed enough gnolls. This
happened for me around level 10.

I also saw a necro jump ship today in the butcherblock zone to avoid the
guards and he was in the 20's. I would assume then that it may take quite a
while to raise your faction.

I also want to mention how much faster my druid has leveled than my sk.
Maybe this is becuase he is my third character and I have more experience
playing the game, but I started a week ago and I am level 9 already. Seems
like it took twice as long to make it that far as a human sk. Playing a
shadowknight is fun but there really is a serious penalty.
