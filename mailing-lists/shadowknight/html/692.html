I spoke with our magic system designer and he does not believe those spells (Infectious cloud, Spirit Armor, and Augment Death) are
supposed to be for Shadow Knights and thus might be a data entry error. He's double checking these.

I'm also talking to him about possibly changing some other aspects of SKs to make them more viable in the low/mid-levels.

One problem that SKs face -- and one of the reasons that they are so difficult to play -- is that they were tuned on the assumption
that you would be playing an Ogre or Troll. As such, human SKs are quite possibly the second most difficult class/race combination
in the game to play (after Erudite Paladin).
