Subject: Making pottery


At the wheel, take the correct amout of clay, the sketch and 1 water.
Hit combine.

If you succeed, you will get what looks like a box.

Take this 'box' to the kiln and put it in with the correct firing sheet.

Hit combine. If succesful, you will have the finished product.
