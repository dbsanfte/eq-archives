> >How I make 60pp an hour in Halas (as a barbarian shaman with 71 cha):
> >
> >2 large blocks of clay = 1gp 1sp 7cp * 2 = 2gp 3sp 4cp
> >1 large bowl sketch = 1sp 2cp
> >1 water flask = 1sp 2cp
> >
> >total cost for unfished mixing bowl (no pie tins here) = 2gp 5sp 8cp
> >
> >1 firing sheet ~ 1sp 2cp (I don't remember this figure, exactly)
> >
> >total cost for finished mixing bowl = 2gp 7sp
> >
> >Average selling price to NPC in Halas ~ 8gp 5sp
> >
> >Net profit per mixing bowl ~ 5gp 8sp
> >

> >achieve 19 successes in under 10 minutes (with 148 skill). Let's say
