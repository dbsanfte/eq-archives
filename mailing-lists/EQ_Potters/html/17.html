Subject: Best place to do pottery


Hello

I dunno about Halas but i think the best place to do pottery is Kaladim. The
kiln and pottery wheel is right next to a pottery supplier. Although you
have to buy the sketches at Kelethin or Freeport, these are stackable and
take minimal space. I mastered pottery in no time (making pie tins galore)
at Kaladim. Water can be purchased at one of the guardhouses just outside
Kaladim.

Kelethin is also a reasonable place to do pottery, since the whole city is
one zone, you don't have to load anything to get all your supplies and to
get to the equipment. You might get lost tho )
