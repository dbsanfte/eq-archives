2 backpacks in the top 2 slots, 18 water and 18 large bowl sketches in the
slots directly below them. Keep your own food and water in the top two slots of
the left backpack. Fill up on large clay. Make as many mixing bowls as you can,
store them all on the left sides with an empty spot to the right of them. Any
empty spaces (besides ones to the right of unfired mixing bowls) should be at
the bottom of the right backpack. Go buy sheets until there is a firing sheet
opposite every unfired bowl. Go fire 4 bowls, sell, 4 more, sell, 1 more, sell
(if 100% success which isn't too rare). Buy clay, make unfired bowls, buy
sheets, make 6 bowls, sell, make 3 more, sell. For me, that gains about 9 plat
profit and that's with a low charisma and low faction. You can make a lot more
as a barbarian shaman with a naturally high charisma (adding cha jewelry and
your cha buff, you'd be rich QUICK!)
