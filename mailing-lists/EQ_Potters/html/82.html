In East commonlands, in the separate building by inn 1 (the

Sometimes Merra is stuck on the roof, the easy (and slightly
cheesy way) to take care of this is to create a disposable
magic class. It takes all of 2 hours to level to 4th. Then, any
time Merra's on the roof, just run him out and cast the burst
of flame at him. He'll be down of that roof and smack you
for 130+ in no time :)
