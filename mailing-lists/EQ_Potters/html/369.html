I haven't seen a wish list in a while so here are mine.

<disclaimer: I am not a potter. I was considering it once and this list has
so little traffic I never unsubscribed>

Some sort of clay pot as a requirement for some alchemy.

Molotov cocktail: requires clay pot, fire water, vodka. One charge of
area-of-effect fire.

Portable still. Lets you brew on the road. Kind of like the spit for baking.
Any time you use the still there is a 1% chance it breaks.

clay bulb arrow tip. Filled with magical powder lets fletchers make a few
new kinds of arrows. Blindness, snare, and some small debuff would work
well.

clay golem. 1st level pet. I think Verant has reasons for not wanting pets
useable by everyone (hence they removed clockwork pets) but I still think
its a worthy idea.

soul protector. Very expensive. Maybe needs to be enchanted by a high level
enchanter or necromancer. One charge of "soul protection" which lasts for
maybe an hour real time. When you die while under the effect of Soul
Protection you don't lose any experience. I know this really isn't feasible
but I can dream.

caltrops. essentially an area-of-effect snare.

clay mask. When worn makes your faction indifferent with some groups (maybe
all merchants, or human merchants, or MerchantsOfQeynos).

ceremonial armor. No AC bonus but looks cool. Available in different
colors - bronze, steel. Might be limited to non-planes armor that already
exists. Would be perfect for those special events when you want to look
good, and not your silly piecemeal self.

blank tablets of scripture. Can only be used by a cleric. When used, the
cleric loses 1% of his experience and gets an item called "tablets of
scripture". It can only be used by someone of lower level of the same faith.
Using it boosts that persons experience by 1%. So if a 32nd level cleric of
Tunare used it he would get a "tablets of Tunare XXXI". Clerics would make a
fortune from twinkers, especially once they hit 60th level and didn't have
anything better to do with their xp.
