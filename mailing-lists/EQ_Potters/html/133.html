Someone in the last digest asked about spit sketches. At last check, there
is no such thing. Spits were added to the game a few weeks back for cooks.
Already made, they cost 3 gold (or less, based on charisma), and are sold by
the same merchants who sell the smithing molds for baking (pie tins, etc.)

For *other* pottery sketch locations, you can check out
http://members.aol.com/niamist/locations/pottery.html
