There are two ways: The Fastest way, and the economical way.
The Fastest way: Have about 13pp ready,start making ceramic lining until trivial
(about 36)Than Pie tins until about 115.This is the fastest way to raise the skill
and start making money.If you dont have about 13pp than make different things after
36,because Pie tins will fail about 95% of the time at skill 36.When you are selling
or buying,it helps a LOT to have charisma enhencing items or spells.(Medal of
merit,SOS spell for shamans)After skill 115 Mixing bowls are good money makers.But
the most money will be made with Pie tins.
i pay 1silver 1 copper for pie tin sketch,pie tins sell for 6 gold and 4
silver,(Medal of merit +5 Char,SOS +15 Char,Char doll +3 Char)
