It is possible to make twice or even three times the profits of brewing,
simply because the pie tins have such a high resale value.

My dwarf with a charisma of 55 (pathetic I know) buys the raw ingredients for
182 cp and sells the finished product for 593 cp in Kelethin

My wood elf with 105 charisma (made specifically for pottery) buys the raw
stuff for 125 and sells for 653 cp.

Before I started I scoured the city and found the merchants that sold best to
me.

But for the wood elf that is 528 cp (or 5 gold 2 silver 8 copper) PROFIT on
each tin!

wheel and klin with more than one person.
