Ack! Don't use HQ Bear pelts. They are used for making 10 slot backpacks.
If nothing else you can sell them to a tailor for 10 plat each. The skins
that work for lined poison vial are:
a.. low quality bear skin
b.. low/med/high cat pelt
c.. polar bear skin
d.. puma skin
e.. white wolf skin
f.. grizzly bear skin
g.. zombie skin (very useful - stackable)
h.. blackburrow gnoll skin
i.. mist wolf
j.. black wolf skin
This info comes from AllaKhazams web site at
http://everquest.gamestats.com/. Click on Skills, then Pottery, then on
Pottery Recipes.
