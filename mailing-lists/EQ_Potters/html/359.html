I've heard all sorts of prices for vials. Regular 1pp-5pp, and Lined
2pp-6pp. It costs around 6gp for Regular & 1pp for Lined to make. It is
really up to you what you wanna sell them for. I've maxed out this skill so
my prices tend to be on the low side. And I think the high side is kind of
ridiculous if you want to keep any customers. I've found about 3 rogues
that I sell to regularly. When I collect enough zombie skins & think that
I'll make a round of vials I give them a tell and see if they are interested
in buying and find out how many & what kind they want. To find my customers
I usually check /w rogue in the zones and ask high level rogues if they are
interested. The market for vials is so small you'll be wasting your breath
auctioning them over the zones. Though you may pick up an enchanter or two
that way.

Good luck!
