2 backpacks in the top slots (upper left has my food and water). Sketches and
Water in the middle two slots. Fill the rest with clay. Make your unfired bowls,
get your firing sheets. Then you'll have 4 open spots to put mixing bowls in. If
you buy 18 sketches and water instead of 20, you'll have 6 open slots the second
time on each run-through. I do this in Halas where I don't have to travel far.
Unfortunately, the lines are too long to make this profitable anymore. Well,
compared to anything else anyway =P
