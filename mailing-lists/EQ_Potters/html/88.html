Here's my method...

You need 42 free slots (20 sketches, 20 flasks of water, 40 blocks of clay =
42 slots.) 2 regular inventory slots are used for flasks and sketches. the
other slots are my 6 packs (total 48 free slots). I add my pottery book and
7 different types of food, including water (being a ranger that's usually
berries, grapes, vegetables, roots, pig meat, pods of water, fishing grubs
and muffins). Leaving 40 slots for clay.

That way I can buy clay with impunity and not have to worry about buying too
much.
