Well, I haven't done this yet, 'cause it'll cost money and time but...

The wheel has 6 slots
3 of these are already spoken for :
Sketch
Water
Block of Clay (whichever size)

now the other 3 can be one of: empty, clay, glass shards, metal bits.

leaving 64 combinations * 3 sizes of block of clay = 192 possible
combinations.

This is assuming no item uses 2 different sizes of clay block.

exhaustive testing should find either a) the right recipe or b) that it's
broken, and doesn't make anything.
