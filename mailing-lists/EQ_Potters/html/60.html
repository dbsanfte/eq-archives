How I make 60pp an hour in Halas (as a barbarian shaman with 71 cha):

2 large blocks of clay = 1gp 1sp 7cp * 2 = 2gp 3sp 4cp
1 large bowl sketch = 1sp 2cp
1 water flask = 1sp 2cp

total cost for unfished mixing bowl (no pie tins here) = 2gp 5sp 8cp

1 firing sheet ~ 1sp 2cp (I don't remember this figure, exactly)

total cost for finished mixing bowl = 2gp 7sp

Average selling price to NPC in Halas ~ 8gp 5sp

Net profit per mixing bowl ~ 5gp 8sp

On average (no exageration) I can make 20 attempts at mixing bowls and
achieve 19 successes in under 10 minutes (with 148 skill). Let's say that
it takes me 10 minutes because I'm working slowly.

19 bowls * 5.8gp = 110.2gp

About 11 pp. This makes about 66pp per hour. That's if I'm not working at
peak speed... but to be fair, if there's a line for the wheel or kiln, or if
I'm talking to a friend at the same time, that figures not going to be that
high. Most of the time, though, I do this at non peak hours, which means no
line _or_ friends... :)

I go mixing bowls all the way. I'm only level 9, but I almost never have
less than 30pp on me any more.
