I feel your pain --- the same thing happened to me when I first got into
pottery using jars. Here's what I ended up doing.

Put the unfinished bowls into backpacks. It's not a container until after
you've fired it in a kiln. Get another backpack full of firing sheets.
Put everything else into the bank, and just carry a backpack of unfinished
bowls and a backpack of firing sheets. Fire bowls until you fill up your
remaining inventory, and then go sell. Repeat as required.
