Every named NPC in Everquest belongs to a faction, or group of beings. You
as an individual (race, class, charisma, religion) and by your actions (what
you hunt, the quests you do) affect your standing with every faction.

You should pay attention to what the paladin says. He told you that you
have not proven yourself to the temple. That means he doesn't trust you,
and isn't going to say another word until you improve your faction with the
Temple.

Go out and kill things till you see a faction adjustment that lists that
particular temple. Then go out of your way to beat that particular critter
type up. After ten or so kills, go back and check and see if he'll trust
you further. If not, and you really want to help him out, go out and
continue the slaughter. Eventually he'll tell you how you can help.

Be aware that if you are a different race, class and/or religion, this might
take a whole lot of effort.
