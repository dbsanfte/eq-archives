Hi Tiny,

EQ will take over your life run while you still can, MMMWWWAAAAHAAAAHAAA
Seriously, welcome to the fabulous world of EQ and I hope you can
explore the whole of Norrath eventually.
There are many places to get info on quests. The best seems to be
www.allakhazam.com You can get a quest list by zone or item on that
one, I like it. Don't try to do a search--it's only for thier premium
members but all the content is accessible without paying just have to go
differently.

Another site you should check out is eqtraders.com They have info on
all of the tradeskills and what to do with all the 'useless' batwings
and fish and other stuff I sold to merchants when I first started. Fire
up your printer and print out as much of that site as it will print.

A third site you may want to check out is everlore.com They have info
on all classes, advice on playing the class etc.

For maps, allakhazam has most of them and check out eqmaps.com Don't
forget to buy some printer ink before you start on these LOL
There are alot of zones on Norrath and you will want to have maps of
almost every zone you go to so you don't get lost.

Don't get discouraged, there are quests for newbies.
