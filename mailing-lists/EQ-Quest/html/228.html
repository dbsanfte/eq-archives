It's very easy actually. Get a level 20 magician, wizard or enchanter
to group with you. That's right... group. I guarantee you will have
quite some success.

See, all those kill stealers wait for you to engage the monster before
firing away. This makes it so they don't have to root it. You group
wizard or magician can do over 145 points of damage on a single shot.
Make sure you time it so you can engate the creature once he starts
casting. If a kill stealer fires off a spell after this happens, it's
very sweet. You group caster will be able to do more damage, even if
he is much lower level, because he will have a second opportunity to
burn before the kill stealer does.

You need help acquiring an item, but it is generally considered good
manners to help the caster aquire his items too. In many situations, a
caster has a very difficult time in getting items unless the monster
in question is very very green unless he kill steals it.

Also, you can probably get a caster or two to join you if you go for
two spawns of the PGT instead of just camping for yours and then
leaving.

I know you may not want to deal with this. But, the fact of the matter
is, if a caster needs to get an item, often the best way is to go for
something like the PGT which they can get easily and then sell it so
they can buy what they can't get in more difficult situations.
