There are quite a few advice websites out there the best ones are
www.eqtraders.com and http://everquest.allakhazam.com

Those will lead you to other sites if they don't answer any questions
you have.

General Advice: Keep all those useless items. All spider and
spiderling silk, all bones, all bat wings, all fish scales. All are
usable for other things. Silks are used in tailoring, bones are used by
necros i think, bat wings have many uses, fish scales are used in spell
casting. If you are going to use them yourself, those items can be kept
in stacks of up to 20 and sold to other players. My all time high was
when I got 20 pp for a stack of bones but that is rare, more likely to
get 4-7 for those.
I could go on and on about such item: pelts, belts, shoulderpads, all
items have a use just have to figure it out. Eqtraders has a
comprehensive list of how to use items and where to find what you need.
