Subject: Anti-kill Stealing Tactics


In my experience, the Enchanters in the game are the very best at preventing
kill-theft.
Most Classes are easy to stop.. Wizards, on the other hand are more
troublesome. Their
rapid-kill nuke spells are easily abused. Enchanters are wonderful at
stopping that.
Mesmerize not only scrambles the hate list, it blanks out the record of who
did the most
damage. After a Wizard fires a spell, Mesmerize it, and then whoever does
the most damage
out of the REMAINING hit points will get the xp. If you are lucky enough to
get a spell
off before the Wizard, Charm is most effective. The Wizard usually wastes
all his mana
before he realizes the creature is charmed, and can take NO DAMAGE from him.
If you use
this tactic beware: when the charm expires the creature will hate you like
no other.
Be ready with your handy Mesmerization, and all is well... You or your party
will get the
kill (and xp) while an impotent, mana-drained Wizard has to Meditate for a
good while to
recharge...

I am very interested in other ways to handle the more frequent nuisance of
kill-stealing.
Any other tricks/strategies are great ammunition for those of us just trying
to have a
little fun.
