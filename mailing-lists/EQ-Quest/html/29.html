Being a new Erudin enchanter I sought out on my fist day to talk to every NPC in the town. One of the most simple tasks I found was to recover a bood from the Library and give it to this paladin. So I walked a little way to the library and asked the Librarian for the "Testament of Vanear" She informend me that it had been lost and told me the last know person who had checked it out.

This began the series of events that has led to my reletless attempts a rcovering a book that at first seemed simple and unimportant. I managed to track down the book in a near by town but once I had the bood I found out that two pages had been ripped out and were missing. This has inturn led to many treks across the continent of Antonica to locate the missing pages. So I travled to the location where I was told that one of the pages were stolen a few hours earlier and that the assailant was nearby. I also found that the other page was being help by a Eruidian. Knowing that I was not quite strong enough to recover the page that had been stonen I headed back across the continent to speak with the man about the page he had in his possesion. After a lucky hand of cards and several gold later I had the book and one of the two pages in possesion. With my curriosity peaked I again set out to locate the person who had the other missing page. Now it has become apparent that this book ties the events in several city together and I can not help but wonder what the book will tell once restored. I now have to kill a band of smugglers and a corrupt city guard before I will be able to restore the book and read it's contents.
