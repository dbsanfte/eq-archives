You talk to Diggins inside the entrance to the mines.
He will ask if you are a member of the Mining Guild 628
He asks you to bring back runnyeye warbeads < 4 of them>
when you bring them back he tells you that you can
earn the Parrying Pick.
You need to geet the tongues of the Dunfire Family
who are with the ButcherBlock Bandits.
They are not all together tho ya need to run around a bit
to the camps.

Crytil and Keldyn Dunfire are located in the bandit camp adjacent to the
Goblin Warrior camp in the Butcherblock Mountains...Along with a nasty Cheif
Bandito called Peg Leg. Barma Dunfire cam be found at the bandit camp
located near the Scarab Pillar,not far from the last 2 houses on the trail
to Greater Faydark. Rondo has taken up with the orcs in the Citadel of
Crushbone,and can be found in a tunnel not far from the entrance,along with
a pair of Orc Slavers.

A ranger will help
The names of the Dunfires tongues you need are
Crytil, Rondo, Keldyn and Barma
bring them back and give to Diggins
and he gives you the Pick
He also tells you how to get another Pick .



The tongue quest is a FORKED quest, that is why not many people know much
about it. There is one side of the quest which is to get Qued's tongue, and
all the Dunfire's tongue, and you get a parrying pick. The other side of the
quest is killing all the people at Peg Leg's camp (which includes Peg Leg,
Stump Rundil, Qued, Margyl Darklin, Keldyn Dunfire, and one other guy, I can
remeber his name) then you talk to a guy in the mines in norther Kaladim,
named Diggin. I have no idea what you get for completing this part of the
quest. Peg Leg is level 12 or 13, all the rest are levels 10 to 13. You need
a really high faction to get this quest, The reward Item is a Parrying Pick



Killing bandits is the only way of increasing faction with 628 you must have
at least a faction of amiably to get the quest(I lvled twice only killing
bandits before being able to get the quest). After i recieved the parrying
pick 628 (it is dwarf/rogue only) you are instructed to see matar who is
close by next to bank. He tells you to look for someone near the docks who
knows the wearabouts of boog Mudtoe. I have not found this person but have
found out that this particular Mudtoe resides near an island off of the
sister island. You will have to swim! When you are on the boat you can look
off of it and see gornit. the mudtoe is on the same island as gornit

This is all the information I have on this quest. I have not done it and
cannot verify any of this.
