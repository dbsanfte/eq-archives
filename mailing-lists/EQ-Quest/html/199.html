Trial of a Murderer
by Mushin
This event will involve the Murder of another character, the subsequent man
hunt for the accused man, and eventually a trial to determine the mans
innocents. Currently the event is in the preliminary stages of planning.

The Event Coordinator is looking for people to fill the following positions.

Victim: You must be willing to have you Character killed (only once) and may
loose an item and money. This is because you will not know when the attack
will happen. We are hoping to have several possible victims to give the
murderer a variety and to ensure that the killing happens randomly when you
are not expecting it. However you will be free to fight back and are allowed
to defend yourself.

Murderer/Assassin: Your job will be to kill one of the possible victims. You
will not know where they are and will only have their Names. Should you
fail in your attempt to kill the victim you are to attempt the murder of
another name on the list. Once you are successful you are to loot the body
and take one item. You must keep this item on you character and can not sell
or give it away.

Lynch Mob: The Members of the Lynch mob will be required to track down the
Murderer and return them to the scene of the crime for Trail. There will be
no limit on the number of people in the Lynch Mob

Jury:
The Jurors will decide the Guilt or Innocence of the Murderer. We are hoping
for a Panel of at least 6 Jurors. (you are allowed to create a low level
character to act as a juror specifically for this event.
