Some time ago, I had a friend who screwed up and became KOS
in Freeport. I looked all over in an effort to find something that
would help his faction and came up with donuts. I remember that one
of the things that was suggested was to go to HH Pass and kill Orcs,
for that would help his faction. At the time, this did not seem to
do the trick.
Well, I don't know if my friend just didn't know what to do,
didn't do it enough, or if the thing just didn't work then, but I can
now say for sure that killing both HH orcs and HH gnolls will help,
but the BIG payoff is running their scalps to the local guard
captains. Give them a single scalp and you will get exp as well as
faction gains for several different hard to modify groups - including
the Freeport Militia. The beauty of this one is that there are no
faction losses, as are most of the other +Militia quests of which I
know. You also get between 1 and 6 gold pieces for doing this, so
it's good for a reasonable chunk of change as well. I turned in
about 25 of the scalps and went from apprehensive to indifferent.
