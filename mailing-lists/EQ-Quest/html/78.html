Subject: Charisma and it's Effect on Buying and Selling

Charisma most DEFINITELY has an effect on buying and selling. Even one
point. It's a linear scale. I built about 40 different throw away
characters and tested things like STR to ATTK bonus and AGI to AC bonus
AND Charisma and pricing. A Charisma of 74 vs a Char of 70 reached a
1-2% savings in price and garnered another 1-2% increase in value of
goods sold. This doesn't seem like much but then again, it was only a 4
point difference. I will build an Enchanter (Max. Char 115) and give
him my two magic items (when I get the second one) to bolster it to 125
and see what the difference is then. Will post results back here.
