> I lifted the following text from allakhazam's Web site hope it helps:
>
> On the second floor one of the buildings in a small group of buildings to
> the EAST of "The Lion's Mane Tavern" and to the south of "Nesiff's Wooden
> Weapons" and the "Bag and Barrel", you will find a man named Vicus Nonad
> with a bad cough. After talking to him for a bit, he will tell you that he
> is a tax collecter and needs you to make his runs for him. He will give
you
> a box, in which to place and combine the taxes, and a scroll with the
names
> of the people. To get the taxes from the, simply hail them and say "Please
> pay your taxes." Doing this will give you a very small lowering of faction
> with Qeynos merchants.
>
> Upon going to Mira Sayer, In Qeynos Hills, and asking her to pay, she will
> begin to cry. When you ask her why she is crying, she will tell you a
> halfling named Flynn something or other stole her tax money. He is in
Crow's
> Pub in Qeynos. Go to him, hail him, and ask him if he stole Mira's tax
> money. He will affirm this, and tell you to say "I am a gnoll loving
> weakling and am not worthy to comb your feet" After doing so, he will
shout
> it, and the entire zone will hear what an idiot you are. He'll give you
the
> money
