The Sherrif doesn't give you the ring. It's the deputy down stairs in the
dinning room in the bank. It's the deputy standing closest to the stairs.
You say "Am I one with the wall?" If you have earned it he will give it to
you.
As for recharging, I believe you just give it to the cleric in the cleric
guild, and it returns to your inventory immediately. I am not 100% sure on
the cleric, I just watched a guy fighting Nillipuss do it, I never saw him
say anything.

You turn the necklaces into the sherrif, and the orc belts into the deputy
out on the wall in Misty Thicket, you also earn faction for killing those
orcs and goblins in Misty. I also hear that completing any quests in this
zone also gives you faction. One in particular is to go by polar bear
cloaks/skins (I forget which one, do the quest and he tells you which one
you need), and ways, go to Halas, and I believe you can buy these items from
the vendors or from newbies that are out killing low level monsters. If you
fill up your entire inventory with these and bring them back and turn them
in one at a time (or however many he requested at a time) you earn cash/exp,
and faction. I heard that 8 bags full is supposed to be enough, but I don't
know anything for sure, this is just what I have read, and heard from people
that I have met who have this ring.
