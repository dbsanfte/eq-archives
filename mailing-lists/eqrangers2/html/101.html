There are a few good spots for Loot and leveling.
One of my favorites has been the Estate of Unrest.
This place is located off of Dagnor's Cauldron which
can be reached via Butcherblock Mountains Zone.
Dagnor's itself is very dangerous to be in at less than
lvl 20...as Aqua Gobbies are highly aggressive and
ruthless. Best path in I have found (until you get Camo) is
to enter Dagnor's and run to waters edge and run
along it until you get to far side (south end I
believe). Zone for Unrest is a large rectangluar opening in
the mountain wall.<br><br>Unrest is basically a 3
story house with basement and a large yard that is host
to MANY undead. Trains are frequent and deadly here.
Get in a group...kill yard trash (Death beetles, Jack
o Lanterns, tormented Dead, and greater Skellies)
until you get to high teens. If you play wisely and
stay alert...you will level quickly here. Loot gets
better as you take on harder mobs and includes armor
pieces, much coin, gems, bronze weapons and misc stuff
like zombie skin and fungi that sells for a couple
gold to a plat each.<br>Everything aggros here so
don't move around much and stay close to walls.
Oh...you will die here...more than once...but the exp is
worth it IMHO.<br><br>Other places good for your level
is Lavastorm, Blackburrow, and
Befallen.<br><br>Trueshot quest can be done anytime...collect your items
now...and plenty of them. I would reccommend that you do
not try to mfg the bow until you get to skill of 140.
You can do at anytime, and I know those that have
succeeded with low fletching skills...just you failure rate
will be high.<br><br>Good luck...<br><br>Quynnelleon
