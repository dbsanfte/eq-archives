Subject: Short Sword Of Morin


for all you newbies that are looking for a good
weapon under lvl 30 that is basically FREE. Get some
help hunting the ghoul messenger in WK to get the
letter off of him. turn the letter into Morin(can't
remember his full name)in Kithkor forest and get the sword
in return. It's ranger only, lore and no drop, so
there really isn't any price on it. it's 6 atk, 22
delay, +3 str , and I think the last one is +3 wis or
agi. I was lucky enough to get mine at lvl 12(a kind
ranger already had his and I was at the right place at
the right time) and I still use it at lvl 41. I've
had it for 2 years now, i've grown pretty attached to
it. I'm currently looking to finally replace it with
something better, but I like to work for my stuff, not buy
it (unless it's player crafted). <br>so for all you
newbies, get the SSOM. if they were actually able to be
sold, they would probably run at least 1kpp. So get
one, it's free.
