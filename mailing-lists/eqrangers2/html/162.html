Only guide I can give is the one that I did on my own. 27 is a good
level to go to Everfrost and learn how to fear-kite on the mammoths
there. Only really good til about 30. From there you can go to
Iceclad and kill snow cougars the same way, be very careful at first,
they'll resist a lot. I killed them all the way up to 39!! 40 was a
rough one for me, I went to DL and grouped. Since then I've been in
Eastern Wastes killing every animal I see: Dire wolves, Woolly
mammoths, Tundra Mammoths, Woolly Rhinos, Tundra Kodiaks, Snow
Cougars, Snow Griffins, Elder Snow Griffins and Walruses. The
smaller mobs like the wolves and cougars are only really worth XP up
until 46 or so, the southern edge of the zone has blue mobs up til 49
and 50 though they're fewer and farer between the higher you go. Now
at 49 I'm going up to Luclin to kill rockhoppers in Dawnshroud, I
hear they're good up until about 56, maybe even higher. Good luck
and happy hunting, hope I helped out some.
