Hee hee. Welcome to the game. Ranger is a good
choice of character, altho you'll find yourself spoiled
if you start up a new character. <br><br>Learn to
use the consider function ("C" key or right click
after left clicking a potential enemy), and learn to do
it from a safe distance. Things which are "ready to
attack" are best steered clear from at low levels, even
if they appear even to you, because they often have
friends which are also ready to attack you when your back
is turned and you are busy whipping the first one.
Also learn how to /loc yourself when you are getting
beat on so you can easily retrieve your stuff and loot
if you do die.<br><br>Practice TRACK and SENSE
HEADING as much as you can, TRACK especially is a
valuable tool later and makes your ranger very welcome in
groups as you level up.<br><br>Can't say much about the
hunting where wood elves grow up, I am a human ranger and
so have hunted the Qeynos and Western Karana area
during my adolescence. Good luck to ya, if you ever
start a new character check out Torvonilous, its a
great server.<br><br>Stormhugh<br>Ranger of the 11th
