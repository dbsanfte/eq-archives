Let's get this club rolling! I'll try to help
with my view on fletching.<br><br>I just passed
fletching 70 with my Level 10 Ranger. Fletching is one of
those trade skills where all you need is money to get
your level to rise. I have alternate characters with
various trade skills over 100. None over 200
yet.<br><br>I find what really helps getting skill levels to
rise is persistence and intelligence. You should only
try various skills that are trivial at about 5-10
over your current level. Any higher than that, and you
will be wasting $$.<br><br>I fletch while medding. I
haven't made any bows yet. I primarily fletch for the
arrows. I also made a 6-slot quiver with 22% weight
reduction using tailoring skill. Quivers are handy. They
give you autoloading.<br><br>One problem I've had with
fletching (archery in general) is finding supplies when
traveling. You need to do your home work before you
travel.<br><br>I would like to hear (on this club) about what
others are up to in Ranger hood.<br><br>Brewcamper -
