OK, Here's my take on this..<br><br>When in a
full group with a legitimate tank, a Ranger is
supposed to be dealing out damage without drawing hate so
you don't turn into a mana sponge for the healer. For
this, I switch to a 2 hander (currently I'm using a
Nature's Fury). I know this isn't as sexy as sporting 2
lammy's but it keeps my health in the red and my healer
happy. I guess one exception to this would be a
Wurmslayer cause it has such a high delay that it's like
carrying a 2hander.<br><br>When you are acting as a tank,
the 1 handers are the way to go. As a general rule,
the faster the better, the lowest delay in the off
hand. Lammy and Lupine Dagger are UUber until you get a
swiftwind. Anything that procs will only help in keeping the
hate. Admittedly, I don't have the greatest 1 handers
yet (Wurm Tendon whip 12/26 I think and a Yak Club
9/24) but I'm camping the Lupine until I get one).
<br><br>Another circumstance is when you are fighting a caster
mob. I have found that the lowest delay weapons
possible are the best, just to interrupt more. This is
really evident when you are fighting a cleric mob with
Complete Heal. Nothing is more frustrating than almost
killing the stupid thing and Bam! back to full health.
When you have call of sky cast on yourself and a DEX
buff from a shammy, watch out! Proc and interrupt
CITY!<br><br>There's my 2cp worth ;-)<br><br>Tetzu<br>48 Ranger
