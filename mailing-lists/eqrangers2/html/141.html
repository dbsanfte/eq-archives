For me as a Ranger I use my bow for pulling mobs.
At lvl 40 I crit for 61 and regular damage is 25 to
30. Now you can bow kite using snare and shooting
until the mob dies but this takes a lot of arrows and
time. I use a dmg4 range 150 arrow most of the time but
have a fletching skill of 202 so all old world arrows
are trivial to me at this time so I rarely have any
failures when making even the dmg8 arrows but they are
very expensive to make, about 80pp per 100. My archery
skill is at 180 right now. To get there all I had to do
was shoot arrows, lots of arrows. When pulling I have
a shadewood bow and the dmg4 arrows that have a
range of about 300. My guild leader is a lvl 52 ranger
and crits in the 300's. So the hits come with time
but the bow is mainly, at least for me a tool used
with tracking to pull mobs for myself or the group I
am in.
