Greetings Zensidhe<br><br>The spells that you now
have are all that are currently available for a Ranger
of your level. Make sure to practice each spell area
up ( Evocation, Alteration, Abjuration, etc) as they
will serve you well later. Take special note of your
spells Flame Lick and Snare...these are wonderful for a
Ranger learning to Kite or act as group puller. A
flame-licked and snared MOB is an angry MOB that you can drag
back to your camp to give it a beating. Very reliable
for that level range. Snare is one that I use
religiously.<br><br>Lull Animal reduces the agro range of the animal you
cast it on. This helps if you are in an area that is
highly populated with animals that aggro easily. You
won't see the effects unless you are really
close...just note if you get a message saying that you were
interupted or such...else it should work fine.
Personally...I found very little use for it. <br><br>As for your
new spells...start buying them early...like around
level 13 or 14...specially if money is tight. Its
always nice to have those new spells with you when you
DING rather than having to leave and fetch them after
the fact.<br><br>Good Luck...the better spells are
yet to come.<br><br>Quynnelleon<br>Ranger of the 24th
Season<br>Bertoxx Server
