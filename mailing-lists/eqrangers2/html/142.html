Like the previous reply, I too use archery for
pulling primarily. At level 44 I critical for 68 using
the good ol' Trueshot longbow and 6 dam rng 50 arrows
(Class 3 Steel hooked). I also have a higher level
friend, a 54 Ranger that I watch crit for upwards of
3-400 damage. The new Luclin archery spells help out a

between 1 and 68 damage. Archery is definitely a skill
you want to work on. Warriors, Paladins and Shadow
Knights have their critical on melee, ours is Ranged, a
definite plus. If you are fortunate enough to get a
Tolan's Darkwood Bracer that right-click summons arrows,
