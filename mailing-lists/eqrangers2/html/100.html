Snare will consistently be your most valuable
spell in EQ as a ranger. It won't stop a creature in
it's tracks (unless it's almost dead), but will
significantly slow the mob down.<br><br>In groups, a ranger
should be snaring every mob. This not only helps when
retreating, but makes it harder for a mob to aggro on your
caster when he casts that big nuke and jumps to the top
of the hate list...<br><br>Later on you will get
root, which is good for different situations. It will
plant the mob in place, with a 10dd effect. Great for
chain pulling, bow kiting (used with snare too) and a
bunch of other stuff that you will figure out with
time.<br><br>As for sticking close to Faydwer, I would go out
adventuring, checking out new zones. You don't relly find out
the magnitude of EQ until you start travelling. Many
places you might want to visit are: Freeport-Eastern
Commons-South Ro combo (all close together), Qeynos
hills/Blacburrow/Everfrost (also close together) Toxx/Befallen etc...bunches
of places to see.<br><br>Have
fun<br><br>Tetzu<br>Morell-Thule
