Heyas,<br><br>Well, this seems to be a pretty
good topic of converstation. As far as I know, STR is
the only way to increase the damage done per hit..But
more importantly is the way that you play during mele.
With Dual wield & double attack & Kick, you can deal
out just as much damage as a warrior (minus Bash).
Keep this in mind, because the biggest thing you
should be thinking about during Mele is the Hate
list.<br><br>Because Rangers have weak AC, we get hit much more often
than Warriors with plate. If we are constantly dishing
out TONS of damage & drawing the attention of the
mobs (hate), Healers have to spend lots of mana
keeping us alive. If we are not playing primary Tank
(hopefully you aren't)It should be your job to do as much
damage as possible WITHOUT drawing the hate of of the
mob. That way a healer can keep the main meat shield
targeted & concentrate on healing him. One way I do this
is to actually stop attacking for a few seconds so
that the Warrior can taunt the mob off of me, then
start hacking once he turns. If you are fighting
multiple mobs, it's the Rangers job to "peel" mobs off of
chanters, clerics & casters when they aggro on your wimpy
little buddies.<br><br>The next most important thing to
do is SNARE. You should snare every Mob in sight.
That makes a huge difference in the long run. Mobs
have a harder time aggroing on your casters, & if you
need to retreat, snared mobs will never catch you.
(then there's the fact that they stop fighting and
stand there getting beat up when they try to
retreat).<br><br>I read once that playing a ranger is like dancing a
dance. It's our job to stay alert & be aware of
everything going on around us, filling in where needed. Let
the meat shields get beat up. Act like a bodyguard to
your caster buddies. You'll make a lot more friends
than if you hit occasionally for 30 with a
1hs.<br><br>Just my 2cp<br><br>Tetzu 39 Ranger
Morell-Thule<br><br>P.S. DEX is most important for getting crits in
archery. Plus, when you get a weapon that Procs, it will
proc more with high DEX
