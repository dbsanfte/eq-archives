It's not a banable thing. And whether or not it's bad depends on you. I have
been given items that were higher than I might have normally and have been true
power leveling. I don't see any problem with it, but it's just me. It doesn't
ruin the game for me or hurt anyone in the process. I know some people who
think getting a gift from another player is wrong if that gift is too high for
you (I got a Staff of Writhing at 20, can't be used until 25). It's a personal
preference, and I don't think you are actually power leveling, there is
actually a faster way if you have a higher level there to help you, and that's
power leveling. Just decide how you feel about it and go from there.
