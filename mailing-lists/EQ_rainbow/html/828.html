Hail!

The wedding is tomorrow, Saturday, Oct. 21st and the grooms can hardly wait.
Here, are some more details for those who plan to attend:

The ceremony itself will start at:
4pm Eastern
3pm Central
2pm Mountain
1pm Pacific
9pm GMT

It will be in the Toxxulia Forest zone. It will be near the little dock with
the rowboat.

From the druid ring, travel SW.

From the wizard circle, travel NW and cross the river. There is a bridge.
After crossing the bridge go almost due west to get to the dock.

From Erudin, travel SW till you get to the water, then head directly south.

(For those using an EQ Atlas map of Toxxulia Forest, it is the point labeled
number 7)

WARNING: There is a necro named Elial Brook (level 6 or 7ish) that hangs out
near the dock. He cons ready to attack to most people. We will need someone
to kill him regularly to protect any low levels attending the wedding. His
spawn time is 6 minutes.

Fentus and Kerik will be doing ports prior to the ceremony. I should be on
around noon Eastern on Saturday to begin setting things up, so give me a
tell if you need a port. For obvious reasons, I will not be able to port if
you are running late.

Guests from other servers are encouraged to create Rodcet Nife characters if
you wish to attend. It would be best to create Erudite characters. Taren
will be available at the entrance to Erudin to buff and sow you for a safe
journey to the wedding / reception site.

Taren and Gwynap are catering the reception. They have prepared quite a
feast with plenty of spirits too. There is also talk of getting fireworks.
It should be cool.

I am very dubious of the GM showing up to do the ceremony. I was told to
petition tonight (Friday) to remind them and also to petition on Saturday 2
hours prior. However, our very own Kythwin has agreed to serve as minister
if the GM should be a no-show.

We are expecting 10-20 people at least, and think it will be a lot of fun.
We will have at least one person from another server and also, Harbinger
from Band of Gypsies plans to attend (BTW he says hello to everyone in
Mystic Alliance).

While setting up an EQ wedding is easier than a real one, it has taken a lot
of work nonetheless. Thanks to everyone who has helped. Eizen and I really
appreciate it.

Hugs and kisses,
Kerik and Eizen
