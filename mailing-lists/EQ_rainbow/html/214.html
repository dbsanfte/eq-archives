Joseph-

Just re-read the second paragraph of Christina's post. Do you see why I
suggested it? I originally laughed out loud at my thought of calling the guild
Mystic Gaydar, but then Mystic Alliance seemed pretty good. Not that every
GLBT man and woman feel called to associate with each other, but obviously a
bunch of us here do... that's mystical, spiritual, mysterious, magical... and
everything wrapped up together. No religious affiliation is taken from the
name either. Although angels and archangels, etc. sound nice, most people did
not want to inject Judeo-Christian ideas into Norrathian culture.

It's almost certain that no one name will be the ultimate to everyone
involved. Arriving at a consensus fairly soon would be great as I am excited
and wanting to let everyone I know about it. Having the name to relay with the
rest of the information is important to start building a guild identity and
presence.

Thanks for your ideas-

Christine
