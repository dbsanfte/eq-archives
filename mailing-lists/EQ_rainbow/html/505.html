this is something i wrote not long after i came out of the closet and i
thought i would share it, if it is similar to anything else you have
heard or read it is only a coincidence and i did it unintentually


It began in the fall and the dead leaves started to cover things up.

When winter clouded up the skies, and snow blotted out everything, I
almost felt safe.

But when spring came along I realized that the snow wouldn't be around
forever.

And now that it is summer I am glad that the leaves and snow of fall and
winter will never cover me again.
