Hi folks:

A month ago I posted a message here about the new
gay/lesbian/bi/trans guild on The Nameless server which was then
called "Stonewall Pride." After some deliberation (and some wierd
nerfing which happened to our guild after the last patch) we decided
to abandon Stonewall Pride and move everyone into the previously
existing gay/lesbian guild called Nameless Love. This was at the
kind invitation of Tsinen, the head of Nameless Love.

Thus gay/lesbian EverQuest people on The Nameless server may wish to
join us in the Nameless Love guild to enjoy a safe and friendly
gay/lesbian environment.

Many of the new Nameless Love members are players of a lower level
since we have all only recently moved to The Nameless server from
other servers. We love to group and hang out.

We welcome all gay/lesbian/bi/trans EQers and hope to see you soon!

Dandee Lyon, druid

Ps. Many thanks to Nameless Love for inviting us into their fold.
