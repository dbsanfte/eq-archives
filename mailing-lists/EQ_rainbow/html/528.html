Subject: My exp with the GM Event this weekend


Ok so they finally had a well thought out event, I thought at least. I had
the pleasure of joining in on the defenders of High Hold Keep. As I heard of
the events, I immediatly went to High Keep to join in the defense guild.
From that point on it was Patrol and take posts and wait..until it began
with a whisper in the woods saying "War War..." They were plenty in number,
coming from Neriak through the commons and nektulos. Needless to say I
roleplayed this one big time.. I was even able to take down 1 inkie and 1
troll that was in the invasion on my own. In the end, I saw Neresei
wandering around and sending shouts.. she was a High level Dark Elf PC and
was real tough.

There was even a instance of someone casting Ice Comet on a crowd of us..
that was devistating. There was plenty of Gm's involved as there were many
NPCs sending out threats .. and as one threat by Neresie told us to give in
to Innoruk's children, I opened up my big mouth and sent a shout telling
Neresei that "You fool, your demise will be in the name of Mithaniel Marr".
Well that was a mistake lol as she and her generals sent shouts speaking of
my bones as they needed them... They were after me. Well I was by riverdale
and with a group of us attempting to defend riverdale.. Next thing I knew we
were swarmed and I was prime target.. they tore me to shreds.. with one
final shout I claimed "my death was not in vain for the light shall
prevail". "Loading Please Wait.."

So upon corpse retrieval.. I went to get my body and rest in riverdale, by
that time Riverdale was taken over by them... Thats all I know as I had to
camp for the evening..

This was truly a great event IMHO.. I do feel bad for all the people that
lost levels due to this.. but hey I didn't mind the exp loss at such a fun
price..

How did the raids go on your servers ? I heard they were server wide. Im
curious to see if they actually took over High Keep.
