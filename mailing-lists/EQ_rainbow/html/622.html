Hi as I've said before I'm a guide on Rodcet so let me tell you exactly
how to deal with this kind of harassment!

First of all, always use the /report command to log the homophobic
comments. Also note the name of the person and the time the incident
happened.

Then petition immediately, petition "I was harassed by [name] and I have
logged the harassment".

When you speak with a guide, say you want to speak with a server GM about
it. Only a server GM can review the logs. Guides have to witness
harassment to be able to do anything. Server GMs are not always on so you
may have to be patient and persistant over several days.

Once you speak witha GM you want to say something like "On [date] at
[time] I was harassed by [name] who made homophobic and insulting comments
to me. I logged the harassment with report. This kind of action must
not be tollerated in Everquest, and I would like your assurances that you
will find review my report, find this person, and speak with them so they
know that homophobic harassment is not allowed in Everquest."

The GM must then find the person when they are online in order to speak
with them. You can aid this by putting the person in your friends list,
and petitioning whenever they are online, continue to petition in this
fashion until the GM tells you "I have reviewed your report and spoken
with this person." Persistance is key! GMs generally act on petitions,
and they will not seek out people unless they are online, and they don't
generally look for people.

I hope this helps.
