Here are some thoughts on our decision making process for the name of our
guild:

1. There is no obvious good name that everyone will love. It would have
already been suggested by now.

2. Online discussion takes way way longer than real life discussion.
We're not all sitting in a room together chatting. A conversation that
might take two minutes in a room can take a week online, as people
exchange ideas.

3. Votes in online mediums take at least a week. First someone has to set
a deadline for submission of named to be voted on. Second, someone has to
extract all those names and post a list. Third, we have to wait 48 hours
for people to vote.

For all these reasons, I would like David to select a name based on our
discussion, but not governed by any official selection process (like a
vote). I also think their should be a cap on discussion, and that cap
should be soon (like now perhaps 8)

I think we generally agree that the most imporant thing is that the guild
exist with some name.
