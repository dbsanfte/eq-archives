Hello all,

It was great to have 18 of us together on chat last night. Our efforts at a
replacement name were humorous if not productive.

I had an idea for using a latin name for the guild.

Gloriae et Liberatatae

'Gloriae' means fame, renown, glory; glorius deeds.
'et' means and
Liberatatae means freedoms, liberty, independence.

The initials would be GL (same as the initials for gay and lesbian. Also,
the B and the T in liberatatae could represent bisexual and transgender. I
just like the idea of having a hidden meaning, but one that we recognize and
remember as we think about the diversity of our own group. Kind of like the
meaning of each color of the rainbow flag.)

The guild's name would also represent our own desires for personal and guild
glory. And the desire we have to be free and to make sure others are free
too.

Other names based in latin:
Pedes Genialis

pedes - foot soldier
genialis - relating to enjoyment; joyful, gay.

Pedes Liberti (soldiers of freedom)

I know these sound militaristic, but after all, we do carry weapons and kill
things.

Societas De Sapho - Association Of Sapho
Societas De Genialis - Association of Enjoyment (joyful, gay)
Societas De Liberti - Association of Freedom

I kind of like the idea of using something Latin, but at the same time I
want something that sounds good. Don't know if any of the above fit that.
What do you all think?????
