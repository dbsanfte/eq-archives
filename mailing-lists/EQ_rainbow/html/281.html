Here are few tips for those on the list that are new to Everquest. THIS
list of 'rules' has saved me more often than not. Hope they are useful to
you:

1) Someone yells RUN! - YOU RUN and DONT ask 'What?' - If you ask what,
you usually DIE and then find out. Example - I was fighting something in
the desert...someone yelled RUN..I turned too look...I was stunned to see a
Sand Giant chasing after someone near me..VERY near me. They zoned...Sand
Giant saw me and Squashed me like a bug.

2) If you are in a party and someone yells RUN..follow the above rule.

3) If you are in a party and someone or everyone just isnt listening or
working together...DISBAND and leave those losers to die..because is you
don't, you will DIE with them.

4) If you can buff someone, do so. Making friends can be fun and
profitable. They will remember you and probably help YOU out someday.

5) Never be afraid (or too cautious) to try a new place to hunt or explore.
Meat and Potatos of EQ is exploring..fun fun fun!

6) ALWAYS get a bind in a new city if you can. You'd really hate having to
run all the way to a new continent just to get your things.

7) CONs the bad guys before attacking! You may live longer.

8) AVOID making a train and leading it by others as you try to ZONE FOR
YOUR LIFE! They WILL remember you and made get even later.

9) Good rule of thumb - put at least 4 gold, a weapon, 4 water and food in
the bank...maybe a backpack if you can. Nothing like dying and coming back
poor, defenseless and hungry.

Welp, those are some things that have served me well. If they help you too,
COOL!

Love ya all!
