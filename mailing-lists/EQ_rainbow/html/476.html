Hello all,

Last night, we had 5 Mystical Alliance people on Brell at the same time and while
we didn't group together, or even see each other, it still made things a lot more
fun because of the conversations we could have while we were healing and med'ing.

I just want to encourage more of you to move over to Brell. I know a lot of you
have high level characters on your current servers and I can understand your not
wanting to leave. But many of you have lower level characters and you are the ones
I want to invite over to Brell.

I gave up my level 13 cleric to make the move and I know what it is like to start
over, so I have a special offer. If you want to make the move, let me know and I
will do the following:

o I will help equip your character. Once you figure out a class and race, I will
work to get you some equipment that would be suitable for a character below level
10. I will help get cloth for casters, and rawhide and patchwork for melee types
and weapons too.

o I will help you power level. My lvl 17 necro has a healing spell that takes HP
away from me and gives them to you. I can keep you healed up. Also, once you kill
a creature down below half, I will help you finish it off. You will get the XP and
loot. This should really keep your downtime to a minimum.

In just a few hours, we can have you up to level 5 or more. While I can't speak
for other guild members on Brell, I wouldn't be surprised if some of them might be
able to help you level and give you some 'hand me down' equipment as well. And
depending on your level, you might also be able to group with various of our
members as we have characters in many different levels.

So, if you are lonely on your existing server and don't have too high a character,
please come on over to Brell and at least give it a try.
