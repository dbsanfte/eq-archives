Subject: I'm playing again


After a long absence from the game I'm playing again, kind of.

As some of you may or may not remember I quit EQ about 5 or 6 months ago
after breaking up with my boyfriend who I played with constantly before
that point. That plust a general disgust with server politics and a
new job caused me to quit cold turky.

I have a close friend who switched to one of the new servers Tholue Paelis
(probably mispelled). I decided I would go play a newbie druid on there,
and I hunted up to level 16 or so. My friend is actually in an uber-guild
of sorts on the server, which of course conjures up all sorts of negative
imagery. He put me in the guild just so I could listen to guildsay and
it's been an interesting listen to say the least, very teen boy.

After playing a druid for a little while I started to realllllly miss my
enchanter from Brell (54th level). My friend offered to lend me a level 52
druid to power level myself. Despite warning bells going off in my head, I
figured, what the heck!

So I started power leveling my brand new first level enchanter, which is
an interesting experience to say the least. For those of you who aren't
familiar, high level druid power level characters by buffing them, putting
a damage shield on them, and chloroplast. With this a 1st level character
can kill a 12th level mob. It took me about 3 hours to get to 16th level
this way. I continued to tank up to about 30th level at which point it
stopped being effective. From that point on I've done kiting using memory
blur to allow the druid to do all the damage, but allowing my enchanter to
then take the kill. I'm 39th level now.

Now, all of this is not really playing EQ in the sense I normally have.
It's been kind of fun though simply because I haven't done anything like
this before. My goal now is to get 46th level so I can hunt with my
friend's guild, as they go about killing dragons, and Venril Sathir, and
other such "uber mobs".

But, it all feels very cold in a way, I don't really know anyone on the
server, and the people in the guild don't seem like people I'll really
like (except for my friend). I wonder sometimes if after I officially join
the guild, if I will end up quitting the server a couple days later =) It
could happen.

I've gone back to my old server to visit and that's nice, I always run
into some old friends who say hi and chat with me. There are people I
still care about on that old server. But, it seems like I have a barrier
that prevents me from playing there. I just know if I play there that
server politics will rear its ugly head again.

So for now I will power level my little enchanter on Tholuxe, and see what
it's like playing there.

Christina
