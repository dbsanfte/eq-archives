Subject: Fun on Rodcet Nife


Not sure if it was mentioned on EQ_Rainbow, but last night we had a big
Mystic Alliance guild event on Rodcet Nife. It was called MegaPlat and
consisted of a lottery (the grand prize was 665 plat) and raffles of various
items (armor, jewelry, weapons, etc.)

We had around 20 guild members on at the same time and that set a record for
us, I believe. A lot of people have been moving over to the Rodcet server
and this event was a great way for them to get some nice equipment.

Joe and Rob did a great job of coordinating and running the event. Thanks
guys.

If anyone else wants to create characters on Rodcet, we invite you to come
join us. We'll be happy to help you out as you start new characters. We're
quickly making Rodcet the San Francisco of Norrath and you are welcome to
join the fun.
