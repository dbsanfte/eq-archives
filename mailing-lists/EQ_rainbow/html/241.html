Subject: SERVER LAG and DROP OUTS


Here's something to help all of you having troubles connecting or staying
on. First, let me introduce some of you to the wonders of TRACERT ! With
this handy dandy little utility you can see if any of the places we all
have to bounce around on while trying to get on Everquest is up or
SERIOUSLY laggy! Tracert is already on your machine (If you have windows
3.1/95/98 ). Open a DOS window while still in Windows and do this:

1) Once in the dos window you will see C:\WINDOWS|
2) Type cd\ and press enter
3) Now type TRACERT and then one of the following:

208.236.12.25 (This is the E'ci server, but doesnt matter even if
you are Brell )
or
chat.everquest.com then hit enter (Got these address from EQ)

4) After hitting enter, pay attention to the numbers that appear below.
This info tells you HOW MANY places you are bouncing around in to just get
to Everquest. Also, you will see numbers like 15ms or 17ms. This is the
time in MILLISECONDS it is taking to go from one NODE (bounce )to another.
IF you ever get an area that says: "REQUEST TIMED OUT" then one of the
places you are bouncing to is down or just SO slow it doesnt carry a signal
fast enough to keep your computer from just dropping the whole connect
attempt.

I hope this helps you all out! It does me! Let's me know if I need to
YELL at my ISP or EQ!
