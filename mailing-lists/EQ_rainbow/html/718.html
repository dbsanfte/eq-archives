Subject: Using roger wilco with EQ


Yeah, the question came up how to get on it. If you don't have it,
this is a freeware program that downloads in about 2 minutes, sets up
in about 5, & then takes a varying amount of time to adjust & tweak
to get your sound card to work with it & transmission to go well. It
allows you to hear & transmit voice messages while running another
program, including EQ -- basically a voice chat room. Once you have
it all set up, you just open it before you go into EQ. You'll want to
click 'join', & then you want to join this channel: 216.37.33.36.
After you've done that, you just leave roger open & go into EQ like
normal. We've had a few of us going on it for a week or 2 now.
