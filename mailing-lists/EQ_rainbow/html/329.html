Hey Joseph,

Try creating a new character. Don't give up on your old one, but tryign a
new character can give you a fresh perspective on the game. It provides
you with an alternative when you feel bored with your wizard. If you're
sick of medding try a rogue or warrior or monk. Bards can cast spells,
and don't need to med. Paladins and rangers and SKs don't need to med
much.

Items in Everquest are highly overrated, in particular for mages. They
are mostly there for status. EQs social hierarchy is based on levels,
plat, and items. In terms of your effectiveness as a wizard, items
matter, but not that much!

One of the things I really like about Brell right now is that everyone is
low level. It's so crazy compared to Mithanial Marr where there are many
50th level characters. I'm a 21st level enchanter, and I am the highest
level enchanter on the server (as far as I know). Most people are under
20th level. Of the over 20th level people, most are soloing
druids/necros/wizards. Cazic Thule only had 5 people in it when I stopped
by the other day. It's a very fresh environment. No one has any gold or
platnum jewlery. Newer servers can offer a great environment to play your
character where life does not revolve around rare expensive items.
