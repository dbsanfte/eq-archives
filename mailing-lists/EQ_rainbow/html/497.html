>I've heard that as well, I've also heard people say that a level 49 wizard
>successfully camped jboots very recently. I'm not sure whether it is
>factually true that high level players can't camp jboots any more, or
>whether that is just a rumor. It isn't hard to imagine a high level
>character killing drelzna 10 times in a row, not getting boots, getting
>the note, and thus concluding that it was impossible for them to get boots
>any more (when they were really just unlucky).
>
>I'm a little suspicious that there is a level cap on who can get jboots
>because that would be a totally new kind of approach to camping. Until
>now what a creature drops has not been affected by who kills it. The fact
>that creatures sometimes use items that they carry suggests that what a
>creature carries is generated at spawn time, and not at time of death. If
>that is the case it would be difficult (though not impossible) to at time
>of death change what the creature drops. I'm a computer programmer, I
>tend to look at things from the perspective of "how hard would that be for
>me to do". Programmers are lazy, they prefer easy fixes over hard ones.
>
>I'm not saying it isn't true, just that I'm not convinced as of yet.
>
