Hi I'm Christina, and I started this list, and to quote Big Gay Al, I
think it's just SUPER that so many people have joined recently 8)

Regarding the guild, I think it would be a great idea to have it on Brell
Serelis. You have to start some where new to make things fair for
everyone. I have a 9th level enchanter on Brell. I have been power
leveling. Her name is Luna. I also have a 30th level druid on Mithanial
Marr named Iuna.

Some people are concerned about leaving their servers. Don't! My
suggestion is that we have two official guild adventuring times (i.e.
Thursday evenings, and Sunday afternoons). That way you can play on Brell
during those times to hang out with your amazing queer guild friends. You
can play on the other server othe times. The bigest benefit of course is
that when one of those servers goes down, you won't be spending hours
staring at the server list waiting for your one server to come back!

I just created a guild on Brell for my other mailing list, EQ_women. It
was pretty easy, and I was approved within 24 hours. The guild is called
Sisterhood of Erollisi. I don't want to be guildmaster of two guilds
though, so I hope that someone else will volunteer to run the queer guild.

As for the name of the guild, instead of a subtle "hey does that mean
queer?" name, I personally like a name that is symbolic of who we are.
How about "Unity". It's short, it's simple, and I think being unified is
a great general concept for us to present, as gay lesbian bisexual
transgendered and supportive straight people. I'd suggest calling us
"Rainbow" but lets face it, that sounds silly for a guild name. Anyone
else have any ideas?

There is obviously interest in a guild, so I'd really like to see one get
made. It's hard in an environment like this to really elect or appoint
someone to run a guild. So, what I'd like to suggest is that if you're
willing to be the guildmaster please reply to this email to the list and
say "I'm willing to run the guild". Also list three reasons why you think
you'd be good at running the guild (i.e. previous experience, no social
life). Rather than have a vote which doesn't make much sense when we
really barely know eachother, if everyone is ok I'll just pick one of the
people to do it. Really running a guild isn't that big a thing, you just
have administrative duties, the most important thing is that you'll play a
lot on the server. The important thing is that we get the guild going.

Anyway, I must get to sleep, good night!
