I'm not sure of Lockjaw's level either, but I was part of a group that
killed him once. We had a 19 troll warrior, an 18 druid and me, a 17 druid.
We druids could not cast any DD spells on him because he was too high. We
simply damage shielded our warrior and healed him till we were OOM while he
beat the thing. However, even though we were successful at getting the kill
(and the experience), about 2 or 3 other ungrouped people were beating on
him too. Without the others beating on him, we would not have won.

LJ seems to have a random spawn and I think one of the other gators must be
a place holder for him. I've gone hours and hours without hearing of an LJ
sighting and then he'll spawn twice in 15 minutes. He can spawn as far south
as the orc camp near S Ro, and almost as far north as the N Ro zone and
anywhere along the shore in between. Because of some nice loot, he also has
one of the shortest lifespans of any creature in Norrath. I have read that
he is about level 24 or 25.

The best bet to find him is kill as many gators (crocs and caimens and their
deep water cousins) and have 2 or 3 trackers spaced evenly along the coast
trying to pick him up. Then you better be ready to move quick or someone
else will get the kill.
