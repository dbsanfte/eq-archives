Hello my extended family. I have a story to relate to you all that may
be strange and it may make you think. It may make you think about how you
see and view yourself.

In 1995, January, I was getting ready for my vacation in England. I
had never been over seas before so I had to get my Passport. I'm SURE you
all remember the January, etc. where the Govt. kept shutting down due to
money matters. Well, here's the story. To get a Passport, I had to cough up
my birth certificate. This was a little bit of a problem because I was
adopted when I was merely a few weeks old and my mother had never gotten a
birth certificate (Im 34 and live in Tennessee..the state where all I had
to do to get a Social Security Card was to produce the 'FAMILY' Bible in
which my name and birth date were scrolled). Ok, I thought, no problem, I
will just contact NASHVILLE (Our state capital) and get them to send me a
copy of the record. After not hearing from them for a week, I decided to
call. They could not find the record and asked if I have ever gone by any
other name. I told them that I was adopted at a VERY early age but my
birth name had originally been Johnathan Stephen Graves. She thanked ma and
the search was on. In a few days I received an envelope via post with a
Birht Certificate for my ORIGINAL birth name. Bewildered I called them back
and they told me that NO OTHER certificates were there concerning little
old me.
After this I spoke to my mom...she FINALLY reveiled a secret to
me...she had never gotten the paper work from my bio-mommy and thus my name
had never LEGALLY been change at all! YEP! That's RIGHT! I had lived under
and ALIAS for over 30 years! I had student loans...credit cards...etc. in a
NON-Legit name! Well, I had to straighten this out right away! I had
NON-Refundable CHEAP tickets to LONDON and I WASNT gonna miss out!
When I arrived at the local court house and explained what had happend
and that I was actually changing my name to a name I had used for YEARS,
the clerks and Judge laughed themselves silly! Indeed, it was funny! I
think of all the SPY movies Ive seen over the years where the hero's laid
out a huge amount of cash for a new ID and I had dont it for NOTHING! ROFL!
They say your life is usually a reflection of both your inner self and
those you pull to you..and are around you. I think this means that we all
need to too under the table..around the corner....dont take anything for
granted..nothing is as it seems. Life is too wonderful to be that boring!

I love you all! And have a great day!
