Well, our guild has been approved! My thanks to all the newly arrived
bears who helped us pass the first hurdle of guild approval.

We are now ready for actual guild creation of "Nameless Love" on The
Nameless server. The GM for our server has Fri and Sat off of work, so
we will meet Sunday instead. We welcome everyone who wants to attend
this special event! After all, how often do you get to be there for a
gay guild creation? :)

We will gather at November 12 Sunday 3pm Central time. Because of
Verant's new annoying guild creation rules, everyone MUST be
"physically" at the same location for this event! This time, I've
picked the tunnel entrance in the Northern Desert of Ro. This location
is relatively close to teleport sites, it's safe for both newbies and
dark races from random attacks during the socializing, and there's not
too much other chatter in the zone.

Please have your character there promptly at 3pm. If necessary, move
there prior to Sunday and then camp in the spacious tunnel to await the
celebration. If you need teleports beforehand, send a message to
SoulDreamer in game and I can hook you up with a druid or wizard.

I'll bring plenty of Brandy and Underfoot Triple Bock to share with
everyone. (I wish I could brew some elven wine for this special
occasion, but I've never had any luck foraging the morning dew
ingredient!) I also have some bear meat available for bear sandwiches.
(mmmmmm. bear sandwiches. *woof*) If anyone wants to hunt at the
desert oasis for alligator meat, I'll have the sauces and spices ready
to fix up some tasty pickled gator. We'll also try to have party favors
available for everyone: armor, weapons, jewelry. Not much in the way of
twink items, I think, but all useful stuff for budding adventurers.

All future announcements about the guild will be posted to our website
at http://www.sweetvision.com/~namelesslove under the discussion board.

I hope to see you there on Sunday! My thanks to everyone for the help
they've already given to make this event possible.
