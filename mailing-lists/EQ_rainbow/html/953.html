Stonewall Pride

Hi folks:

A gay guild is forming on The Nameless server at this time and we
invite anyone interested to join us and be part of "Stonewall Pride."

"Stonewall Pride" is the tentative name of the new guild (yet to be
approved by Verant). We hope to create a guild based on a welcoming
gay / lesbian / bi / trans fellowship where any of us can play EQ
without being subject to the anti-gay prejudice that we frequently
encounter in Norrath.

We're still formulating the charter and operating mode so you can
definitely provide input regarding your ideas about a gay guild or
your experiences in a gay guild such as the renown Mystic Adventurers
on Rodcet Nife.

We're currently using a Yahoo Club for contact -
http://clubs.yahoo.com/clubs/gayandlesbianeverquestfolk. We're
having a meeting in the chat room of that club on Monday June 11th at
8pm Central time (6pm PST) to discuss "Stonewall Pride" and how to
get it running smoothly.

Many of us have moved from other servers to The Nameless so we mostly
have lowbie characters at this point - not aware of any over 20 yet -
so start up a character on The Nameless and join us!

Best wishes to all,
