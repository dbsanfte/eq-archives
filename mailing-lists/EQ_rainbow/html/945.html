Friends whoms names are too numerous to mention:

This email concerns the semi-recent and the recent up to date series of
events that has been hitting Norrath for a while
now.

So when did this all start?
Bloody Kithicor, last year, if anyone remembers. Innorruk sent forth his
daughter into Norrath to spread chaos and havoc.
She succeded, and after a few bloody days, royally trashed EC, WC, Kith
and Highpass. She also succeded in cursing
Kithicor with hte Undead Night spawns. Last but not least, she managed
to succede in opening a Portal to the Plane of
Hate.

What she also managed to do was to get herself killed. And after that,
you really don't hear much from the Prince of Hate
camp for a while.

Jump ahead a few months into the future.
A band of cheerful good-doers started to explore the jungle continent of
Kunark. Unfortunately, one of them was
abducted and held prisioner by an Iskar the name of whom I can't
remember off hand. The thing here is, however, that it
really upset her father (Who happens to be the King of High Elves) to
the point that he started calling in favors from other
races, sent an army across the seas and restablished the outpost named
after his daughter: Firiona Vei.

The Evil Aligned sat back and watched this with growing interest. They
set up their own little base on Kunark, allied
themselves with Venril Sathir for power, money, new spells, ect, and the
chance to continue to stick it to the Light Races.

In other parts of the world, a few months down the line.
Around Qyenos groups of Rude Individuals started showing up. I can hear
you sayit now, yes, it is qyenos, and everyone
is rude over there. But these guys.. were a special breed. About the
same time that they showed up merchants started
getting the stuffing kicked out of them and general discord was
experienced by all. Then, after a week or two, the Rude
Individuals became Loathesome, and Specters started showing up in the
Catacombs of Qyenos. Those whom were
Loathesome started KoSing anything within reach. (and they tended to
advance spawn: kill one, a bigger one would pop,
until I was getting exp from them in my high 40's.)

Despite their best efforts the plot was discovered that they had tried
so hard to conceal. Turns out that Bertoxxolous, the
Plagebringer, the God With No Allies, had been trying to open a gate
into Norrath from the Plane of Decay. The plot was
foiled, and a few people were hanaged. (And a ranger was given an AC 50
shield as a reward)

Back in Kunark...
Firiona Vei is released. Much joy is had by all. (Except those in the
Outpost, who got a solid kicking for their involvement
with Sathir)

In the Northern Desert of Ro..
A band of happy go lucky Gnomes and Dwarves go exploring, and manage to
find themselves a whole new continent. See
what happens when you give them too much sugar? In the process of
discovering this new world, they stumble across
three new groups to be warned of: The Coldain, The Kromiff and Kromzek,
and the Claws of Veeshan. Later exploring
leads travelers to find The Sleepers Tomb, guarded by Dragons galore.
The Sleeper, well, is sleeping. He's the offspring of
Vox and Nagafen (and apparently Vox and Naggy shouldn't have mated, as
it was that act that created this all powerful
Dragon and got themselves thrown out of the "new world" to live as
outcasts from the Ring of Scale), and insanely
powerful.

Unfortunately a few months later the Guild Afterlife manages to wake him
up. The Lead-Lead GM shows up and says
somthing along the lines of "Norrath is not ready to face the
consequences of what you have awakened today."

That line throws up warning bells, does it not?

Then things go back into a calm, restfull state. Until...
Back at Qyenos, the city of warm, loving, thoughtfull people, well..
they managed to find the High Priest of Bertoxxolus.
And promptly hanged him.

This fact did not set well with the Plagebringer. To the point that he
sent his influnce into the Karanas, spreading a massive
Plague upon the land that corrupted the crops, the animals, and created
a new breed of Bug: The Swarmers. But it was
also at this time that someone else came back into play..

Lanys T'Vil.
The daughter of Innorruk came back, in a different body but with the
same mind and most of the same powers. Now,
Neriak gets a little touchy on this. See, part of them beleive she IS
who she says she is, while the rest think not (due to the
new body). Even better, Inny's little girl had a falling out with
Himself. He wants his powers to go to a new vessel on
Norraath, she simply wants to keep them.

What's more is that she is seeking out 3 parts of an ancient staff that
terrifies even the Teir'Dal - and she has one part of it
allready, after delivering a solid kicking of the Forces of Light.

And there seems to be one other, minor detail. Since she and daddy had a
slight falling out, she has opted to choose a new
Deity to give her allegance to (as it is rumored). His name is..
Bertoxx.

Good Old Bertie...
As rumor and some evidence points, himself wants to open a gate into
Norrath - and to hell with the Pact of the Gods that
was to keep them in their own seperate planes. In essence, he's thrown
out the rulebook and because of this, everyone
wants to play a bit and shape Norrath into their own image.

Runneye..
This really doesn't hold much for the main series of events but it is
worth noting. The Goblins of the Runneye Citadel have
managed to throw out the Evil Eyes that have ruled over them for so
long. And while they were at it they killed their king
for his compliance with them. New management is in place, and this bodes
ill for Highpass: the Pickclaw Goblins are at the
heart of this chaos.
