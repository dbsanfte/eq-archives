One thing most people don't know is that when you create a new character,
your character's name is submitted to a random guide who must either
approve or deny your character's name. I believe if no guides are present
it's automatically allowed, but if a guide is around they have a chance to
approve or deny it.

I was guiding on Rodcet the other night and I got a name approval request
for "Charismaqueer" a high elf enchanter. I denied it of course. I then
watched in some amazement as, over the next 20 minute period, this guy
tried every alternate spelling of charismaqueer he could think of.
Karizmaqweer etc. It was kind of amusing to just keep denying him his
name over and over again as he tried to out guess the "computer program"
that he probably figured was denying his name.
