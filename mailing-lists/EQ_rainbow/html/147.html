Hello everyone,

Since we have new people and some may have missed a few of the earlier
posts, I will summarize what we've got going on tonight.

We are hoping to get 10 or more people on chat tonight to use the new
automated guild formation procedure. The guild will originally start out on
Brell Serilis, the new role playing server where many members of the list
have now created characters and started over.

The chat channel will be "Unity" and the password will be "rainbow" in all
lower case. We hope to meet at
9:30 PM EDT = 8:30 PM CDT = 7:30 PM MDT = 6:30 PM PDT.

We must get 10 people into the chat room/channel at the same time and must
all type
#guildapprove (I think that's what we type but will verify before the
meeting).

You will have to stay in the room until we get all 10. We have quite a few
who are going to be on so it should not be a problem.

After that, we will wait for (hopefully) about a week to become an official
guild on Brell. We strongly encourage people to play on Brell as we will be
trying to make it the 'San Francisco' server of EQ. However, we realize that
some people will not want to give up their characters and their friends on
old servers. (Note: Some time soon, there is supposed to be a quest that
will allow you to move your characters to another server. We will certainly
post details when we get them.) We expect we will be able to open other
chapters of the guild on the various servers, even though we strongly
encourage people to put at least some time into playing on Brell.

I have given up all my playing time on Veeshan and don't really miss it
(even though I was just 5.1 bubbles of XP away from having Bind). I just
created a new character completely different from my old one (from a cleric,
I am now a Necro--quite a contrast).

Because no one else stepped forward (in fact I think you all tool a step
backwards), I became the guild master by default. I would really like to
have a co-leader and would like the co-leader to be female IRL so we have a
balanced guild, genderwise. The last thing the world needs (even the world
of Norath) is another male dominated power structure. So, please, some woman
volunteer... no experience necessary... I certainly don't have any
experience running a guild, so don't be intimidated.

Some ideas I would like to throw out:
One person suggested a longer name than just 'Unity'. Like 'Citizens of
Unity'. I've seen no feedback yet. So, either send your ideas to the mailing
list, or bring them up tonight. If you can't be there tonight, please send
email now so your voice can be heard.

I have drafted a couple of paragraphs of a fictional history of the guild
and will post to the list soon. I'd like for others to edit and/or augment
it and then post back the modified version so someone else can do the same
thing again. This way anyone who wants to can contribute to the fictional
history of the guild. Does this sound like it would be a good idea (and
hopefully fun too)? If so, then I will post the lame stuff I have written so
far or someone else can create one from scratch.

What other activities do we want the guild to do? Do we want to have a
treasury that can be used for loans? Do we want to collect old items and use
them as hand-me-downs to the lower level players? Do we have volunteers to
do these things?

These are just a few of the things we can discuss tonight, but sometimes
doing it in email ahead of time is easier.

I also painstakingly researched the many postings to this list to create an
HTML file that lists the people and characters as well as the servers we all

email it directly to your email address, bypassing OneList. The file is just
HTML and there are no viruses in it.

I'm getting really excited by this now. It would be enough just to be in a
guild, but a guild filled with LGBT and friendly allies is even better.
