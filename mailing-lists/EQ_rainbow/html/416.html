Subject: URGENT to Mystical Alliance members on E'ci


It has been brought to my attention that the guild has been set up by someone on
the E'ci server without my knowledge. Last night, I petitioned to ask 1) how could
that happen? and 2) to please delete and recreate the guild with me as the guild
master.

After some further research by Sandy, he has found out that it is indeed a GLBT
guild.

My problem is that the person who got this set up did not inform me or this list
and we don't know exactly who the guild master is. While I do not have designs on
being a powerful multi-server guild master, I feel that it is inappropriate to try
to get the guild set up without authorization. Especially when we had someone from
this list who volunteered to be the guild master. I also don't understand how
Verant did this either, but that is an issue I will take up with them.

The upshot is, unless I hear from the guild master very soon, I am going to
proceed with having this guild deleted and recreated. The leadership will then be
transferred to the person who volunteered on this list (I have to check my notes
to see who it was).

My feeling is the guild belongs to the people on this list and that this list
should be used to communicate general information about the guild. There is no
room for someone who 'hijacks' the guild and doesn't share the information with
the people on this list.

I hope that what I have said above is reasonable. If anyone feels that it is not,
please politely let me know.

On a related note, I roved around between about 7 different servers searching for
a GM to set up the guild and found none. I will continue this and let you know as
soon as the guild is set up on any of the other servers.

Thanks,
David
