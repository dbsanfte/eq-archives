I haven't played a druid recently, but I don't think that the loss of
kiting has seriously underpowered the class. Kiting simply allowed druids
to level extremely quickly, and kill very powerful creatures. However, in
the post kiting patch fix, druids are still a great class they just don't
have this advantage over other classes.

Druids are still a great, versatile, class. They can tank pretty good up
to level 20, backing up their tanking with bolts, heals, and damage
shields. They are also excellent healers in any party up to level 40 or
so. Their evac ability means they're still usually welcome in really high
level parties. Their bolts are weaker than that of pure casters, but they
are still pretty good. Their teleports and SOWs are very useful, great
for exploring, and very useful to friends.

So my overall conclusion is that druids are still one of the most fun, and
useful, classes in the game.

- Christina
