Life's Little Reflections


1. Never raise your hands to kids. It leaves your groin
unprotected.

2. I'm not into working out. My philosophy is no pain, no pain.

3. I'm in shape. Round is a shape.

4. I'm desperately trying to figure out why Kamikaze pilots wore
helmets.

5. Do illiterate people get the full effect of alphabet soup?

6. I've always wanted to be somebody, but I should have been more
specific.

7. Ever notice when you blow in a dog's face he gets mad at you, but
when you take him in a car he sticks his head out the window?

8. Ever notice that anyone going slower than you is an idiot, but
anyone going faster is a maniac?

9. You have to stay in shape. My mother started walking five miles a
day when she was 35. She's 70 now and we have no idea where she is.

10. I have six locks on my door, all in a row. When I go out, I lock
every other one. I figure no matter how long somebody stands there
picking the locks, they are always locking three of them.

11. One out of every three Americans is suffering from some form of
mental illness. Think of two of your best friends. If they are OK, then
it must be you.

12. They show you how detergents take out bloodstains. I think if
you've got a T-shirt with bloodstains all over it, maybe your laundry isn't
your biggest problem.

13. If you ask certain people why they have deer heads on their walls, they
usually tell you it's because they're such beautiful animals. I think my
significant other is
very attractive, but I only have photos of them on the wall.

14. A lady came up to me on the street, pointed at my black leather jacket
and
said,
"Don't you know a cow was murdered for that jacket?"
and I said "I didn't know there were any witnesses. Now I'll have to kill
you too".
