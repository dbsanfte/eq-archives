Don't do anything you're not comfortable with. There are lots of ways to
avoid unwanted sexual situations in the game. Relate to the person as a
friend, overtly, dismissing advances as a joke. Tell the person flat out
you're not interested. Make up some suitable background story, like your
spouse died, or was lost at sea, or something that allows you to dismiss
romantic possiblities without insulting the person. You could also just
say out of character that you're not comfortable with in-game romance, and
that you're just here to adventure and have fun.
