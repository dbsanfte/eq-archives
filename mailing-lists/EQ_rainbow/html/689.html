Hey, after reviewing that thread on the Newbie Zone, it turns out
it's
already been done, & with an element that might make it even more fun
-- all of the contestants used first level characters started
specifically for the contest. They also allowed heals & binds, but I
personally think this would detract from the fun & the point of my
original idea -- to see how far we could make it with nothing. Oh, if
anyone missed it -- the idea is for a bunch of newbies (either a real
char., or 1 started just for the contest) to run nekkid from, say,
Kelethin to Cabilis (or another equally suicidal destination) with no
heals, buffs, binds, or SoWs -- just whatever rusty weapon you're
willing to lose & offensive magic for the casters. Get as far as we
can, die young, & leave a beautiful corpse to decay in a far away
land! Maybe we could get together some prizes for the Ultimate
Survivor, Most Spectacular Death, etc. So, if anyone else is
interested, post a reply here or on the mail group!
