Sorry we missed you when you visited us on The Nameless. There are
quite a few of us there so it's a pity we failed to hook up.

The guild has not folded. Oh no! In fact, it has not officially
made it out of the starting gate yet. We're still trying to get 10
people on at the same time in the same zone (it's like herding
cats!) - we came very close last week but this week we will succeed!

On Monday June 18th between 6 and 7pm Pacific Time we will be in
Butcherblock outside Kaladim where WOLFVEN, our Fearless Guild
Leader, will invite us into the guild and do all the official guild
set-up stuff with an EQ GM. Anyone who wants to help out could just
create a dwarf character on The Nameless that evening and come to the
gates at Kaladim. That will ensure that we get the right number of
people to get going. We will be most appreciative and you'll have
helped create a new gay guild. Yay!

Our guild will be based on welcoming gay people to a safe, friendly,
and fun place to play EverQuest.

All of us have characters on other servers and we're all conflicted
about leaving behind high levels characters, plat and goodies but
from our experiences grouping together so far on The Nameless I'd
have to say it has been totally worthwhile.

Hope to see all of y'all this Monday.
