There is a gay / lesbian / bi / trans guilds on The Nameless called
Nameless Love. It has a small core of active players and a much

characters in other guilds (mostly The Nameless Of Mercy). People in
this guild communicate using the Yahoo Club:
http://clubs.yahoo.com/clubs/gayandlesbianeverquestfolk although

although several players in Nameless Love on The Nameless server also
play on Rodcet Nife in Mystic Alliance.
