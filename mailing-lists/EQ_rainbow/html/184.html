Subject: Some more thoughts on the guild name


I'd just like to say some more things about the guild name, based on my
observation of our discussion.

There is concern that the guild name will look appropriate for any race
and class. I.E. a troll shadowknight might not like "order of the pink
bunnies" and a high elf paladin might not like "defilers of the
light".

People don't seem to want a guild with gay or lesbian or any other direct
sexual orientation reference in it, to avoid harassment.

Most seem fine with an implied indirect reference. However, I don't
think there has been an implied indirect reference that has really held up
well. Some people feel that an implied or indirect reference should be
part of our name.

Some people don't like latin, some people do. Some people want to use
words that sound ok in a medieval context. It seems that plain english,
non modern, words are the most accepted linguistic basis.

I think a good name for our guild would be one that is in plain, non
modern, english. It should stand alone as a name that sounds good to
someone who doesn't know we are a GLBT guild. It is good if this name
also has some GLBT reference or meaning. The name should not be
inherently 'good' or 'evil' to avoid an implication of goodness or
evilness on the guild members.

Keywords that seem have been suggested that seem generally accepted by all
include:
- Unity (Unified, United)
- Alliance (Allied, Allies)

I hope this helps people in suggesting or considering names. This is just
my perspective on our conversation.
