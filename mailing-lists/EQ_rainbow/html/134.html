Gang,
Today - I had a run in with one of the GMs. You're not going to believe this,
but it's shades of Ultima Online all over again.

We all know that character names are restricted by bounds of good
taste/decency/keeping with the spirit of the game. So you can imagine I was a
little surprised when a characer named "Cokaine" shows up. Not only that, he's
using /ooc to carry on private conversations all over the place on Brell.

I /tell him to please use a channel for side chatter. I won't repeate the
response I got. I warn him to cool it, or I /petition. Use your imagination on
the response I got - and it was to store my goodies in a locked box either!

I give up and /petition. This guy was really all over the place. A few minutes
later, a GM pops up and tells me to not make false complaints! I let him have
both barrels, and hard. A minute later he comes back and tells me that in fact,
Cokaine is also known as Vampyre aka Jason White. He's a GM!

I asked why he could get away this conduct. The GM says "he's had a bad day -
his wife died yesterday". C'mon - do I have "Hello I'm a stupid 14 year old"
written on my forehead?

Needless to say, I've sent a formal complaint in. I'd suggest that if you get
someone who's just a bit too obnoxious, don't be suprised if it's a GM.
