Subject: Guild Stuff


Hi everyone,

A lot of people have suggested having the guild on multiple servers. I'm
fine with this. I'd like to emphesize however that we need an official
home server for the guild, and I think this should be Brell Serelis.

One thing I've learned about guilds is that larger is usually better.
Unless you're with a close knit group of real life friends, it's unlikely
that more than 20% of your guild will ever be online at any time. Of that
20%, only maybe one in three will have a character in the same level range
as you. This means a guild needs about 76 members before you can feel
confident there will be 5 other people on the server at any given time who
you can group with. Now, you don't need to group with that many people
all the time, but the fact remains that the more guild members you have,
the easier it will be to group and adventure together.

By having an official home server we can encourage people to play there.
That doesn't mean deleting your characters on other servers. It just
means that you have a character on Brell Serelis, and you play that
character either regularly or at the suggested times (when possible). All
of us playing on the same server enables us to socialize more easily with
eachother, and get a better sense of community.

Having guilds on other servers as well is 100% ok, so long as they don't
divide us. I encourage people to run mirror guilds on other servers, so
long as they don't encourage people to migrate to their server (instead of
Brell). I also hope that people won't feel that playing in a mirror guild
is a good substitiute for playing on Brell, but is instead something they
do in addition to playing on Brell.

- Christina
