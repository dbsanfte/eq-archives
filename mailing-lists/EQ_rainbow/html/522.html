Subject: I'm getting married!


I never thought I would get married in EQ, but I am, and I'm actually
really excited. This is on Brell Serelis with Luna my 50th level
enchanter. She will be marrying Duna, a 45th level monk.

A while ago I posted to the list about Duna, basicly lamenting the fact I
had a crush on her and I was sure she was a straight girl. Well, she's
not as straight as I thought. Since that time we've slowly developed an
online relationship (another first for me), and I guess the culmination of
that relationship is our characters getting married.

This has proved very interesting to me from a queer coming out
perspective. Our characters have been dating for a while now, but they've
both been in the closet so to speak. I was fairly comfortable with this
arrangement, but Duna decided at some point that we should get married.
Well, I'd actually already proposed to her, but I didn't think she'd say
yes. Since then she's been coming out to people somewhat randomly. We've
started /em'ing kissing eachother in public. So far, no negative
reactions. Most people think we're joking around I think though. I
really don't know how things are going to go in the long run. This all
only happened last evening.

Duna is very brave about all this, I think she may also not entirely know
what she's getting into. She's quite young (16), and doesn't seem to have
a lot of experience with queer stuff, and accordingly has none of the fear
I have associated with coming out. I think its amazing she wants to do
all this, I want to do it too, but I would never have dreamed of
suggesting it to her.

The final details of the wedding have not been set. There has been some
talk of having it in the throne room of King Tranix the fire giant, which
would limit attendence to those level 40+. We may have it in a more
mundane location. Regardless, I will let everyone know, and if you can
attend I would love you all to attend.
