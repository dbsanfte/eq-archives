Subject: Low level twinks


As a guide, I usually get one petition a week from someone whose twink's
body has decayed. Unfortunately there is nothing I can do. I am not
personally opposed to twinking, and I feel genuinely sad whenever a person
loses their items and equipment to corpse decay.

For those of you who don't know, the corpse decay rules are that from levels
1-5 your corpse decays very quickly (less than half an hour) so twinking
a character level 1-5 is very dangerous.

I have twinked characters myself, but generally don't give them anything
of importance until they hit level 6.
