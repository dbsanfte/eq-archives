Well, I have some good and some bad news about the guild.

The good news is that the mystery and problems with getting the guild set up
on E'ci have finally been resolved. The bad news is we are probably going to
have to recreate the guild on Brell and Rodcet. Here's what happened.

Once upon a time, we submitted our guild with the revised name "Mystic
Alliance". It was approved and I set it up on Brell Serilis. After setting
it up, I noticed that while I had asked for "Mystic Alliance" to be set up,
they made me the guild leader of "Mystical Alliance" (not that it is
MysticAL not Mystic). I didn't really worry about it figuring that somewhere
along the line, the Verant folks had added an extra AL to the name as I've
seen plenty examples of their bad typing in the past.

So, then we get a request from players on E'ci to get the guild set up.
However, we find out that the Mystical Alliance guild is already set up
there. After some investigation by the GM Kyala, during which I ask her if
there is a guild on her list called "Mystic Alliance" and she assures me
there isn't and that in fact, I am the verified leader of "MysticAL
Alliance" she tells me she will delete the "Mystical Alliance" and recreate
it with me as leader.

After two months, I find out that she has not only failed to delete the
guild, but is not the GM anymore. (Personally, I think she may have been
short-changing Verant on her hours because I could almost never find her
when she was supposed to be on. Maybe this led to her being fired -- I can
only hope.)

The email I sent to Kyala was forwarded to the new GM who, miracle of
miracles, actually was online last night when I went to meet her to get the
guild thing cleared up. It still took an hour, but when she finally got to
me, she checked the list and found out that "MysticAL Alliance" was in fact
assigned not to me but someone else. I then asked if "Mystic Alliance" was
listed. Initially, she said no but then she checked (I guess) some other
list and found that in fact there was a listing for "Mystic Alliance" after
all.

So, long story short, the original Brell GM who set us up made a mistake and
then Kyala compounded it with misinformation, but finally the GM named
Sal`Varae one E'ci has fixed it. However, that means that we have the wrong
guild name on Brell and Rodcet and our web site.

Sadly, we just got the Rodcet Nife guild leadership transferred over to Joe
(Fentus) a half hour before this mystical mystery was solved.

On Brell, I would like to get the guild recreated with the correct Mystic
Alliance name. I leave it up to the players on Rodcet to make that decision,
but I would recommend the same thing there so we don't have to worry about
the 'real' guild leader guy of Mystical Alliance coming along and making us
delete it if he should find out it is a glbt guild.

Also, I need to make arrangements to get the guild leadership transferred on
E'ci over to whoever is going to be the leader. Haven't heard from you E'ci
guys in awhile, email me directly and let me know when we can meet up in
game.

As to getting the guild set up on the remaining servers. Apparently, the
GM's are not really checking to see who the guild leaders of record are
before they set the guild up. So, if you have volunteered to be the guild
leader on another server, go ahead and petition to see if you can get them
to guild you. Just make sure you use the name "Mystic Alliance". Then let
everyone on the list know what you've done and how they can get into the
guild.

thanks and hugs to all.

Dedric, gnome necro, lvl 21 on Brell
