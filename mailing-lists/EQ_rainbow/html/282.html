Hey great list Sandy,

Id like to add a few more hints to this list..

When you Con, be sure to pay attention to wording. ie, cocopuff views you
amiably, warmly.. or threateningly, scowling ready to attack. The colors
help to see how dead you can be for trying to kill the person, but this is
key in determining if you are KOS by what they say and you being their
doomed prey. There has been many times where i was KOS because i saw red
and didn't read the con wording.

For Magicians, use hotkeys for your pets. Ie, create a hotkey that tells
your /pet attack, /pet backoff. This way you can send pull and command your
pet with one button. Its very easy and very effective and can save your life

Put the loc as a hot key. This way if you are doomed.. example you are
getting mauled and you know you can't make it to zone and are going to bite
it, hit the /loc key to get your location before you die. This way you can
write it down and make your corpse easier to find. This is a lifesaver trust
me..

Always be generous when you can..I am mage and plenty o people ask for a
bind, and bandages, daggers food water.. so on.. When I can, I do it, I
never charge but always accept donations. (key word if i can or i am waiting
for someone, ill do it-kindness pays good rewards and frienships and the
occasional good item are helpfull

Be SOCIAL!! These people you meet in Norrath will prove helpfull to you in
game.. i have met people who gave me things just for speaking to them... so
kindess is always rewarding. There is plenty of anti-social people out there
and you never know, that person may save your hide oneday. I have helped out
plenty who are in need and they have helped me out some point later on in
game..Remember .. THE KARMA WHEEL SPINS FULL CIRCLE......

Here are few tips for those on the list that are new to Everquest. THIS
list of 'rules' has saved me more often than not. Hope they are useful to
you:

1) Someone yells RUN! - YOU RUN and DONT ask 'What?' - If you ask what,
you usually DIE and then find out. Example - I was fighting something in
the desert...someone yelled RUN..I turned too look...I was stunned to see a
Sand Giant chasing after someone near me..VERY near me. They zoned...Sand
Giant saw me and Squashed me like a bug.

2) If you are in a party and someone yells RUN..follow the above rule.

3) If you are in a party and someone or everyone just isnt listening or
working together...DISBAND and leave those losers to die..because is you
don't, you will DIE with them.

4) If you can buff someone, do so. Making friends can be fun and
profitable. They will remember you and probably help YOU out someday.

5) Never be afraid (or too cautious) to try a new place to hunt or explore.
Meat and Potatos of EQ is exploring..fun fun fun!

6) ALWAYS get a bind in a new city if you can. You'd really hate having to
run all the way to a new continent just to get your things.

7) CONs the bad guys before attacking! You may live longer.

8) AVOID making a train and leading it by others as you try to ZONE FOR
YOUR LIFE! They WILL remember you and made get even later.

9) Good rule of thumb - put at least 4 gold, a weapon, 4 water and food in
the bank...maybe a backpack if you can. Nothing like dying and coming back
poor, defenseless and hungry.

Welp, those are some things that have served me well. If they help you too,
COOL!

Love ya all!
