There are a couple advantages of magical armors (netted, mesh, rubicite,
etc). They do weight less (you know that one). Also, anything worn that's
magical can generally be worn by all sizes (magic stuff is sort of
one-size-fits-all). Also, magical gloves and magical boots allow you to
punch and kick creatures that can be hit by magical attacks only, like
willowisps (very nice for monks). However, a full set of magical armor
won't make you hittable only by magical weapons ;)
