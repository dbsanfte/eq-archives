> Hi everyone, my name is Christina, and I created EQ_rainbow. I'm happy to
see that 16 people have joined us in just 24 hours. It's hard to tell with
something like this how quickly and how many people will join. I'm going to
use this message to say briefly why I created this list, and to tell you a
bit about who I am.
>
> One of the first things I noticed in Everquest was that it prohibited
anti-gay guilds and harassment. At the time I just thought "cool" and
brushed it off, but it kind of sunk in after a while. It made me feel
comfortable as a bisexual woman, knowing that (officially at least)
homophobia was prohibited.
>
> However, in playing the game I noticed also that there was no queer
visibility. I have seen a lot of heterosexual visibility, in particular
I've seen a lot of het online marriages. I've thought sometimes about
defining one of my characters as a lesbian, but it's seemed like a daunting
task.
>
> By forming EQ rainbow I've hoped to meet other les/bi/gay/tg everquest
players, outside of the game, in a relaxed chat environment. I'm hoping
this will help me realize, and all of you realize, that we are out there,
playing that game, on every server!
>
> Just to give some biographical information on myself, I'm a 26 year old
bisexual woman from Toronto Canada. I work a computer programmer. I do
volunteer work here in our amazing queer community, and I love it.
>
> I hope this list will be a positive space for all lesbian/gay/bi/tg
identified Everquest players. I also want this list to welcome straight-ide
ntified and queer-positive Everquest players.
