Hey Reuben,

You don't need to farm armor yourself, I recommend buying it from merchant
players. You want gossamer/demask armor. If you can't get that get
woven. Concentrate on leveling and making money and you will be able to
get great armor for your magician.

If you don't have a runed totem staff, farm one from the gnoll shaman in
Lake Rathe, or buy one. As for better staffs, i just don't know! I
usually buy most of my equipment rather than farm it.
