Subject: Leaving EQ


Hello everyone,

It is with a certain amount of sadness that I must announce that I will be
leaving the world of Norrath. There are many reasons for my decision, some
related to the game and some related to real life, but they have all added
up to make me decide it is time to leave the game. I've not played now for a
couple of weeks and have not suffered any withdrawal symptoms, hehe.

There are several implications to my decision that affect the Mystic
Alliance guild. First, if you are trying to get the guild going on other
servers, please /petition the GMs and ask to get the guild set up as soon as
possible so that I can help you if it should be necessary.

Second, I would like to send a message to the Verant person in charge of
guilds to have someone else made the guild owner of record. This means I
need a volunteer. If we get no volunteers, it may make things difficult
setting up more chapters of the guild later.

Third, I need a person to be the guild master on Brell Serilis. Any takers?
We need to reform the guild on that server anyway to get the correct guild
name.

Lastly, my two main Brell characters have some pretty good equipment and
cash that I would love to give to members of the guild. My level 21
necromancer, Dedric has full raw silk armor, RTS, Dagger of Marnek (sp?),
lots of containers, some level 16 and 20 spell scrolls, other misc. items
and over 100 PP in cash. I also have a lvl 4 warrior named Rainbow who has
partial rawhide armor, some nice FS weapons, a mino axe and some other misc.
items, plus a little cash. (Note, that the highly coveted name of Rainbow
will also become available, so let me know if you are interested in making a
new character with that name). I would love to give all my stuff to my
guildmates, so let me know if any of you are interested. If anyone wanted to
start a necro on Brell, now would be the time ;)

Finally, I would like to say that being a part of this mailing list has been
a lot of fun and has enriched my life and my game-playing experience too.
While the list has been fairly quite lately and I have the feeling that some
of my friends may have left the list, I plan to continue to stay on the list
just to hear how things are going with all of you. You're a fun bunch and I
do really care about you.

Hugs to all,
David
