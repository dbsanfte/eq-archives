Bat wings, along with other components that are required for various spells,
can be purchased for sure in the Erudin Mage Tower. I usually drop in there
with my druid to pick up a ton of fish scales (for underwater breathing) and
cat's eye agate (for my pure casting friends who need it for shielding
spells). I honestly have found every regeant anyone has ever needed in that
place!

Money at earlier levels is always a little tough to get. Once you hit about
20-25, the mobs you're fighting will start to drop plat on a regular basis.
I think one of the richest mobs in the game has to be Hill Giants so far,
usually carrying between 10 and 70 plat (which auto-splits nicely in a big
group). Now I am sure Vox and Nagafen might be loaded too, but they are
usually bursting with really cool weapons and other rare items. =)
