As seen with the recent posting of the poison formulas a Master Potter has only one market to sell to, Rogues. (Kind of like Fletching's limited market, only worse.)

Having been asked by a Rogue friend to put another now useless Mastery to work we set out to a well stocked supplier and loaded up on equipment to use at the wheel.

Fifteen Plat later we were no closer to making a Vail or Sealed Vial, and too frustrated to attempt the Plat+ style.

Someone has to know the correct mix by now. After all, it was on the Test Server before release.

I can state what DOESN'T work for the Vial since I started taking Mix Notes by the time we started getting low on coin.

The following combinations WILL NOT make a Vial:
(Sketch and Water are assumed requirements)
Small Block
Small Block and Glass Shards
Small Block and Metal Bits
Two Small Blocks and Metal Bits
Two Small Blocks, Metal Bits, and Glass Shards

Block of Clay
Two Blocks
Block and Glass Shards
Block and Metal Bits

Large Block
Large Block and Glass Bits

The last two groupings (Block and L.Block)are guesses on what we used.
