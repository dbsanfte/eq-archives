Your character can only have a 15 conditions affecting him or her at one
time. If you are going into a battle with something that has a really
nasty condition (like when fighting necros or crits that charm) Get a
Shaman load a bunch of buffs on you. Then you will not be bothered by
the harmful stuff. You can try this yourself. I am a lvl 24 Shaman so
I cast all my buffs and filled all the slots up(5 stat buffs, 5
resistance buffs, 2 sight buffs, my ac/hp buff, my ac only buff and SoW)
Then I cast Envenomed Breath on myself. I didn't get the usual resist
message, but I didn't take damage and I didn't get the poison icon.
