I have not played in about a year and a half so this

At level 30 or so ( I have gotten three characters to
this level, war, druid, Wizzy,)


First of all at this level and a warrior you should
really be in a group. That said here are a few spots I
liked

Forest Giants in that zone next to the Lake of Ill
Omen, I forgoten the name of that zone even though I
have homestead rights there. Basement of the Unrest,
you need a GOOD group. This might not be so hot. The
last time I was there the zone was filled with 16th
level char and there 50th level mains getting a
powerlevel. Kobolds in the "window room" in that zone
with the Naggy (the red dragon). Frogs in Lower Guk.
I think 30th level is also a good time to try Velious
(sp?) you know the Ice zone with the wallrus and
bunnys and 40+ level giants. That zone on the other
side of the Warrens might be 30th or so level.
Probably more fun for a Druid than a warrior. There
is another zone in Kunark with those gyro goo things
too. I know that discription sucks. It is in Kunark
and it is a dungeon. Very hard to find it is small
cave entrance and garded by a fake pool of water.

I think the 30's is one of the best levels in the game
for exploring and having fun. I have twinked my
Warrior just by going to the 50th level zones and
picking up nodrop stuff. If your not dressed like a
twink lots of player will help you. Well it used to be
that way. IMHO the levels past 50 are too time
consumeing.
