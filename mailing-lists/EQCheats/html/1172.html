MMmmmmmm..... Mortificator....

Loads on a named Lizzie, Mortificator Syythrak. He is a high 30's low 40's
Lizardman Shaman. He hits for 90 points a pop and casts Envenomed Bolt.
The Staff is his rare load. Normal load is the Syythrak hide jerkin, a nice
monk item. So, find a bunch of monk pets (and a ranger pet if you can) and
head down to Rathe Mtns. Oh be sure to bring your tent as it will be a long
camp since Ol Mort doesn't have a set spawn point.
