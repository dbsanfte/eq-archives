Subject: An Actual Exploitable Bug in Norrath...


It's just a minor thing, but there's a skeleton standing in the teleport/pot
room of Timorous Deep. It can be cast upon, and you can gain skill casting
upon it, and you can "tap" it for hit points, AC, attack, and whatever else
you like, if you're of the classes who do that sort of thing.

But it never fights back, and never dies.

My necro is bound in the pot room, so she can teleport everywhere. I raised
her Evocation skill (which was horrible) by going there and casting Ward
Undead on the skeleton repeatedly, medding in between. When I need to heal,
and am near a city where the pots go, I gate back and do a Leach and life
taps on it, as well as one of the hp-mana spells on myself. I leave fully
healthy and charged with mana.

Speaking of raising your casting skills, remember to ALWAYS practice away all
your mana right before a rez. When you're rezzed, you LOSE all your mana, so
mem that one spell you have in the category where your skill is still like
10, and cast it until you use up your mana, while waiting for a rez.
