As a programmer, I know the hazards associated with third party
Programs fudging your data. The way EQ is set up, any
modifications you make are reflected in YOUR computer's memory,
and maybe even your display, but NOT on veran'ts server. You
FEED commands to Verant, the actions are resolved on the server,
and the RESULTS are sent back to you. You may modify the commands
sent, or the results on your screen, and it won't change what
happens to your character on verants servers at all. It is a great
way to preclude tampering. You may change your screen to show
50000pp, but try to buy something.... The next screen update will
show your real totals... Your character and all interaction with it
are done BY Verant on their own servers. You have NO real contact with
anything that must be modified for any kind of cheat to work.
