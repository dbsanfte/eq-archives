Two islands due west (Ok maybe not west, there is an east/west flip that
occurs somewhere on the OOT, if you just got off the boat at the sisters
island, head straight down the dock until you get to the end of the island
hang a left, 2 islands straight that direction.) there is an island with
a named giant, and MANY cyclops. At the 'back left' tip (Depending on if the
flip occured that's either south west or north east) of the island is a
campfire and the following:

1) Halfling Rogue (Captain something) Has the Bracers
2) Dark Elf Warrior?
3) Gnome magican
4) *-Elf Warrior?

They are semi-linked but I've managed to kill them all without triggering the
Elf. Kill the Magician first as he's a spellcaster. Two Mid twenties cleared
the island and kept it clear for 4-5 spawns. The bracers are lore, +5 strength
Also you can get an earring hat can be returned to the sisters as a quest.
