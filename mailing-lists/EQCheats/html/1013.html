There was a quest that was taken out of atleast one server due to
exploitation where you were given an item by someone in the halfling town to
give to a guard who wanders from point A to B and then dissapears once per
game day. What someone did is attacked the guard and ran to the person who
gave the quest, and then died. Someone standing next to the person who gave
the quest would then hail the guard locking him in place. You then could
receive the quest, and give the item to the guard gaining xp and faction.
People went from first to 12th level like this in a matter of hours before
being banned. It's possible though unlikely this quest (Or any quest like
it) is still in the game.
