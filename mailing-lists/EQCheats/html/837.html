A recent patch, about a week and a half ago or whatever, added deepwater
goblins to this zone. Well, the goblins are mid 30s mobs and are shamen, so
they have wolves for pets. As some may know, if you kill a pet, but not the
master, 2 can spawn in place of the 1 pet. So the other day when I was
hunting there, I saw literally 30-40 pets following a goblin who got out and
about on the land. The wolves drop no loot but they do give okay exp for
people in mid to high 20s.
