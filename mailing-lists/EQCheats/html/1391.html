I went there this weekend with me (Level 30 Ranger),
Level 30 Paladin, Level 33 warrior and a Level 20
Paladin. We were overkill for everything, even the
king is blue to me. The only problem was the Level 20
Paladin was causing trains due to his low level.

If you are a 32 shaman (not sure if you mean you, and
a 32 Shaman) I would bring a Ranger/Paladin/Warrior/SK
to do the main tanking. You will more than likely get
jumped by a few elite goblin guards which could get
rough. Especially if you get Borxx, you want to
interrupt his casting, which a ranger or warrior can
do well with dual wield/double attack and decent
weapons.
