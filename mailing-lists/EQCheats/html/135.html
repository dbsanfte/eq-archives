I can pretty much guarantee one of two things.

1. There is nothing magical or worth platinum on the island.
2. There are magical items, but you must be 50th level to attain these
items, and they aren't worth the effort.

The reason I can guarantee these things is. If there was any magical items
or things worth platinum, the island would be full of campers. This is
unfortunately the way the game works, so if you find an empty zone, the best
you can hope for is that the monsters give good experience. Because there
probably isn't any decent loot.

I could be wrong, it just might be a place that no one has been too, or
bragged about. But these places I'm sure are few and far between, if they
even still exist.
