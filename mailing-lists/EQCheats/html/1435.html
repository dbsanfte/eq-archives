Subject: RE: Guard Killing - Getting Trained


Verant's official stance on this issue is that people can train you to protect
their lands, etc. basically as long as they can think up a good roleplaying
excuse to do it. This includes when higher level characters train larger MOBs
through newbie zones (including giants, spectres, cycs, and guards). Now, a lot
of the time, people fighting in these areas know (by way of previous death) that
these MOBs get trained regularly.

Where you were fighting there is nothing to harn newbies, so you weren't doing
any harm, so I really don't have a problem with it. However, any "evil" race
has few options for banking or going into a city where they are not KOS, your
"violation" of their city is - in GMs mind - a justifiable excuse for training
you. This goes both ways, I've seen Dark Elves kill the wandering guards around
just about every city- Greater Fay, FP, Karanas, Quenos, etc. that have been
trained by "good" races. Funniest site I ever saw was a level 8 or so High Elf
Cleric train the Wood Elf Ranger Guild Master to 2 DE necros killing guards- he
cast Divine Aura on himself and jumped off the city platform and ran right to
these guys- twice. Needless to say, he also dying in training the Ranger
Guildmaster to them, but I've never seen 2 level 35s die so quickly!
