Those numbers are fairly close. Here are the accurate numbers.

Affliction does 135
Scourge does 520
Plague does 1524

Go to http://gladstone.uoregon.edu/~jwindshe/usg/ for a DOT damage
calculator. The basic feeling in the Shaman community is that the
disease line was only good for kiting before the patch. Now however,
the disease line is completely useless. Plague is the last in the
disease line that used to do 6000. However it was ONLY good for kiting.
On the mob's agro list, DoT spells register as though you did the full
damage when the spell hits. Consequently, if you hit plague in a group
it was like hitting the mob with 5-6 Ice comet spells at once. Can you
say "Instant Agro?"
