Actually, my group spent the greater part of yesterday camping the preacher.
I found out a few things have changed in perma:

Evokers now, in addition to thier nearly unlimited mana and chanelling skill
of 255, have access to a plethora of new AE spells capable of taking a full
party of level 21-23 to one bubble of health. (these are blue evokers to
me, a 22 level shaman)

The preacher now has greater heal, and uses it on itself with reckless
abandon. Pretend you are killing the same gob five times over and you have
a pretty good idea of what it's like.

In the four or so hours I was in perma, the preacher spawned twice (which is
much less than it used to), the prelate never spawned, and the rest of the
spawns were filled with evokers, priests, and reg. gobs.

The first time we killed the preacher he had the RC, the second time he had
the morning star. (Which I think is INCREDIBLY good luck, since I have
killed him about 20 times and this is the first time he's had the morning
star)

Oh, and just a quick note to you 40 level people who have never been in
permafrost yet think you know everything -- the ALCHEMIST has the mammoth
armor. The ARCHEOLOGIST has the pack and the dented brass mask. They
DON'T spawn in the same place! ;)
