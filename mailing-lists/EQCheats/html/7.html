I have a level 9 Dark Elf Necromancer and what I have found that give decent
experience is killing skels in Nektulos Forest. It is slow, but they always
spawn at temple or pyramid all the time. Without fail, and if you go later at
night when there is not a lot of people, you could gain a lot. Also killing
the occasional lesser mummy or zombie helps as well. Doesn't seem like much
loot, but after sitting there and killing for a long time you acrue a large
amount of silver and copper which gets to be a lot. The highest that I
remember getting is about 350 copper and 150 silver. Not bad, plus I am a
necromancer and I have over 75 bone chips to use. Just a suggestion and if
you are not evil, the guards could kill you on site. I would only try this
when you are level 8 or above for a necromancer. Not sure about any other
class.
