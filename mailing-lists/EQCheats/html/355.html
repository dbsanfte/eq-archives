To get the Midnight Mallet you buy a ruby and give to a troll NPC lady called Lynuga in Innothule
swamp. She roams about and is hard to find. She also gives an earring and the brutechopper and its
random what item you get. All three items are lore. A ruby costs about 161pp for a troll with
charisma 45. You might get the items if you kill her too but shes not too easy (maybe lvl 35 or
so).

The bone armour you get from the ogre shamans in west karana. there is 4 SK guard ogres (lvl
20-23), 2 ogre shamans (lvl 25-26), and an ogre chief (lvl 34+ shaman). Of course all the SKs and
the chief have their pets. They keep eachother buffed and sowed and they will use their harmtouch
if attacked. My team of six lvl 25-30 died in less than 30 seconds.
