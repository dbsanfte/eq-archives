Here is a quest i completed a while back:
Runner's ring
+2 AGI
+2 STA
I equiped this on my 15 Paladin and it gave him about an extra 30 AC
I could not start the quest because I am not a druid I suppose but I took my
lvl 19, at the time, ranger, Killed Nillipus, who took me down to 2 bubbles
of life. He spawns by the bridge in rivervale sometimes on one side of it
sometimes more toward the entrance to misty thicket. He spawns about every
10 minutes, real time,. I killed him a few times and looted plat, brownie
parts, gems, arrows, and best of all jumjum stalk. I gathered up 4 jumjum
stalk and took it to Reebo Leafsway at the druids guild by the farmers field
and gave them all at once to him. He gave me some plat and the runners ring.
I tried to give him 3 and he gave me a bunch of carrots. I gave my ring to
another character of mine and gave him 4 stalk and he gave me a bunch of
carrots once and a compass another time. So I am not sure if this a random
drop or actually something to count on. It has been told to me that if you
let Nillipuss run around before you attack him that is when he picks up the
stalk, supposedly swipes them from the field, but i have killed him right
after he spawned and he had one on him then also. Well its something to try
anyway. Good luck.
