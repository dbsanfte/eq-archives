Varsoon was yellow to me at 24, so he was level 25 or 26 that time he
spawned. He has a nasty DD spell that he likes to cast a lot, as well as a
couple of DoT's and a pet. He hits for about 40, and drops jack.

The problem is "Varsoon the Undying", a zombie, that spawns when you kill
him. Varsoon the Undying was level 30 (even con) when I killed Varsoon, and
he hits for 70 - 80, double. I don't think he is a spell caster, so I guess
you could kite him it you are careful. Just watch that you don't kite him
into the guards, some of whom will make short work of him.

It is hard to solo him, because you will probably be low mana and health
from killing Varsoon and getting hit with his DoT's. I suggest a group
approach for this reason, or an agreement to the effect of, "I'll kill
varsoon, you get his zombie", so you are full health and mana when you take
him on.
