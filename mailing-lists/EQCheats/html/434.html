BIB is on the goblin Knight. At the bottom of the first
ramp, you come to a door, a tunnel going left, and a tunnel going right. Go
right at the bottom. Follow left wall around, past the water, into the nest
room.. there's a spot on the wall.. either green or purple.. can't remember
off hand. Anyway.. the Knight spawns there, but be warned, it's a VERY RARE
spawn.. I've been there waiting for 4 hrs and didn't see him.. Others say
it's random, sometimes they can spawn back to back within a half hour..
sometimes it takes up to 6 hours or so. He always has the sword though.
As far as blackened pieces go, elite guards and up will drop
them..
