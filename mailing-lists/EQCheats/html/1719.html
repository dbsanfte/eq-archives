I have a toon of every class and race, and Paladin seems the most useless,

> hunt / get good drops for a 14th level Bar. shaman.

You should go play in Paludal Caverns by 14th level, it has a higher
experience bonus than anywhere else in the game at any level. You should be
able to level every couple of hours.

I think you have cure disease, in which case you can go hunt Fiends.
Otherwise you should join a Bandit group, but be careful because you're
slightly low for that and can't take/deal the hits like a Troll shaman. The
Fiends don't drop decent loot, but the Bandits drop armor you can sell for
multiple platinum at the Bazaar. You can also get free Uber buffs if you hang
around the Nexus either asking Ubers or waiting for someone to offer them.

I hope you've figured out how to root and DoT your victims. That's the big
Shaman soloing technique. Root, Slow, DoT, then maybe DD if you have enough
mana. Be ready to re-root. If you get KEI in the Nexus, you can even root and
Direct Damage them to death, easily. At your level you can't cast fast enough
to use up your mana if you have KEI on. With my Troll shaman I also melee
them a bit each time (I solo yellows) just to keep my melee skills up, but
trolls are stronger and have regeneration built in. I can melee stuff my own
level without even casting on them, have always been able to.
