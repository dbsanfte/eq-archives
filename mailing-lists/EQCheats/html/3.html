Subject: XP Booster Hints


Here are some great XP booster hints.

Beetles are slow, all beetles. With a wizard you can usually solo a gamble
beetle. Hit it and run. Hit is again and run, if careful you will not get
hit and should be able to kill it. If not try and go hand to hand if it has
very little health left. Works better with Spirit of Wolf cast on you.

You need magic to hurt Wisps. So do all the other creatures in the game.
Once an enchanter hits 16th lev cast beguile(charm) on a green (they all
will be) wisp. With your wisp pet have it attack a full grown griffon in
North Karana. The lev 35 type of griffons. Sit back as the wisp wittles
away at the griffon. The griffon can not hurt the wisp. Meditate during the
fight and when you have enough mana cast beguile on the wisp again. Keep
the wisp charmed. After a LONG battle the griffon will die. You will get
upwards of 2 bubbles of XP! The loot can reach in value of several plat.

Two bards. Not sure what lev they need to be. One needs the group sleep
song, and the other need the group hurt song. Find a group of red
creatures. One bard sleeps them all, the other bard hurts them all. After a
long battle both of you would have killed 2+ tomb creatures for great XP.

Not really cheats, but useful.

There are plenty of AI cheats in the game. Ones where you force the
creatures to run in circles or certain patterns thus never able to hit you.
Keep a look out for those.

Ken
