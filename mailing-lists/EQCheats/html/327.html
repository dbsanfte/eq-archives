Subject: Unsubscribing instructions


Alright, I saw someone moaning about not being able to unsubscribe in one of
the posts in the last digest I got, so I'll tell ya. Go to www.onelist.com.
I'm 90% sure you had to go to that web site to subscribe to the damn list in
the first place anyway... and they made you pick a password for your email
address. Log on with your email address and password and you can either (A)
unsubscribe or (B) change any list to DIGEST format, which puts up to like
20 mails in ONE email so you NEVER get spammed. Try it out. In fact,
remember this for any lists you subscribe to in the future; most listservs
have a DIGEST option to reduce spam. I subscribe to over TEN lists and never
have to worry about my mailbox being cluttered because each message is
clearly marked with the name of the list in the subject line, and says
Digest. Good luck to you all, pass this knowledge along to anyone you know
who likes mailing lists.
