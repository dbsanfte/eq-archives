Okay, in West Karana, I found the merchant Gindlin Toxfodder
at -3000 -13300. He sells a mortar and pestle, suspension (5 gp each)
and an "old Delivered Book" (2+pp) The mortar and pestle is a
container with six slots. The book is the recipies for poison.

Basically, each poison is going to take some legwork.

Poisons of the Desert:

Regalis
- Vial
- Suspension
- Urticating Hairs
- Froglock Poison Gland

Asp Poison
- Vial
- Suspension
- Two Asp Poison Sacs

Lixt Wing Dust
- Lined Vial
- Suspension
- Two embalming dusts

Poisons of the Snow

Crystal Eritus
- Lined Vial
- Suspension
- Two Crystallized Marrows

Cyclan Butil
- Lined Vial
- Suspension
- Two Cyclamine Corms
- Snake Venom Sack

Sweet Lathyris
- Lined Vial
- Suspension
- Two Ichors (found on the giant wooly spiders in Everfrost)
- Giant Wasp Venom Sack

Poisons of Fire

Basalisk Poison
- Lined Vial
- Suspension
- Two Basilisk Eyestalks

Putrid Desil
- Lined Vial
- Suspension
- Two Putrid Biles (a friend saw them for sale in Freeport)

Festering Nettle
- Lined Vial
- Suspension
- Two Ashroots
