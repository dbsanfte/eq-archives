The Crude stein if from killing the Bouncers in the
Ferrott zone. Don't think you can get them any other
way. They are in the high 30s to 40th level range and
hit extremely hard. Over 100 points per swing, attack
fast and have a brutal kick too.

Look for Bouncer Hurd and Bouncer Flerb, there may be
others, I am familiar with those two.
