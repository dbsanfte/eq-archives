Subject: Re: Stats on reinforced or studded leather


Look at it from a Smith's point of view... It costs quite a bit (mostly
time) to make both studs and boning (for reinforced), and it's much harder
to recoupe the money for them then it is for banded (remember those
failures?) Studs usually have to go for 1p each just to break even (less
for higher level smiths, but they don't want to waste the time). So, if
something takes 3 studs and leather, it's hardly worth it. Try to find a
smith that will make you some if you want some.
