Doesn't look like anyone told you where Milea is. When you make it to East
K, head east across the zone until you get to a Valley. This is the same
valley that leads to High Pass. I am pretty sure the north south loc is
zero. You can also follow the path from the bridge, just go towards High
Pass. When you get to the entrance, Almost everyone will take the path
going up. You need the one that is level, or down. She is near the very
end. you will have to run a long way, maybe 10 min, down that valley to get
to her.

As for getting past the raiders, you can also try passing them by swimming
under water or during the rain. The rain will lower the distance that they
can see.
