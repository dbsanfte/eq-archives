notified about monster (update has been sent from server what such

in EQ uses bounding boxes tracing, and im sure it is made by client, not
server. So, because you can shoot your DDs through walls and monsters in
most cases cannot move through walls, it can be ultimate cheating.
Target any monster in dungeon on client, and nuke him from behind a wall
without any opposition. Of course, server do not send ALL monsters in
zone to client, so you dont know their IDs and cannot target them at
all, but you possible can target any mob in ~30 meters radius.
