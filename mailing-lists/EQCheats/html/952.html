I dont know if you guys know about the Dagnor's Cauldron safe spot or
not so I'll post it.

Its good for wizards lvl 18-22

here is what you have to do... go to the goblin camp on the left of the
unrest entrace tunnel
and just blast one of the goblins (if there is a shaman blast him
first).. run back to the tunnel
and go to the end of the tunnel... now start going back to the entrace
step by step and check if
you are in range to attack the goblins trying to blast them with your
spell(they will be stuck in
the roof, over the entrace and wont go inside after you)... then once if
get in reach to attack
them just start blasting them and you will kill them easily... the Tidal
Lords take a little more
time to kill since they are RED to you you will have to burn all your
mana , go back to the end
of tunnel and med to full mana untill you start blasting again.

when you kill a goblin just go near the entrace untill you get in range
to be attacked by them
with their bows... once you get HIT or missed once you will have another
goblin targeted... go
to the end of the tunnel again and kill it. do it over and over and you
will gain LOTS of exp..

also if you have the cancel magic, cast it on each goblin before start
attcking them... it will
reduce their chance to resist your spells since it will remove the buffs
the shamans cast on them.

made it there from lvl 18 to 20 in 2 days.... 7 hours of play total.

I think only wizards will be able to exploit this bug successfully.
