The Bronze is also a fairly common drop of the goblins in Butcherblock
Mtns. There are some wandering goblins, but also wandering orges,
which at your level are lethal. Go with some backup. There are 2
goblin camps as well (not the aqua goblins, those are light blue
skinned, stick with green skin goblins).

As Laurie pointed out, there are Cultural Forges, and regular forges.
You may be using a Cultural Forge (they would say something like Elven
Forge, or Dwarven Forge in the title vs just Forge). Find just a
"Forge" and try again on the sharpening. It is only one sharpening
stone.
