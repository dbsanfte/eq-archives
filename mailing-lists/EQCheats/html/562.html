Sure... many mobs carry the blocks of ore... hey, and they only weigh
15lbs!! Heck, you can carry 3-4 of them if you have good Strength!! That
will save LOTS of money... yeah, right... I would rather leave them behind
and carry a FS or Bronze sword that weighs less and is worth MUCH more...

I really think that if Verant keeps making skills LESS profitable (or no
breakeven), then they will be encouraging players to quit doing skills all
together, which means that even MORE players will be out in the 'battle'
zones...

I would think they would want to ENCOURAGE people to learn skills rather
than the opposite... I think their reasoning (which is probably money
related) is kind of ontrack in that they don't want a level 5 player selling
Banded all the time and being able to buy 'high end' weapons and armor which
would normally be out of reach of his level...

Verant should ENCOURAGE people to practice their skills... Give them a
Faction adjustment with the NPC's or something... Give them some EXPERIENCE
when they go from one level to another (or every 10 levels)

The same thing goes with languages... Why would MOST of us want (or need) to
learn different languages (like Dragon)?? Are we going to talk to dragons
with it?? No... But it would be a nice gesture if after learning a new
language (to expert level) that we got a little experience for it...

After all, what IS experience? If we only get it from battle (or some
quests), maybe they shouldn't call it experience... Maybe they need to call
it Battle Points instead...

It sure seems strange to me that we would NOT get any experience gain for
things like Practicing a Trade and/or Learning a language...

It's just a shame that the ONLY way you can gain experience is by killing
things (even in most quests you must kill something)...
