Sorry guys....
I have to side with Verant on Nerfing the Pottery/Brewing type skills.
If you guys want to sit around doing these monotonous, boring vendor-type
skills, than I guess that is your prerogative...BUT I sure don't see where
you guys should be making 8-10 times the profit in an hour that a person can
make out fighting monsters.
Agreed, they seem to be going a little overboard with there 'corrections'.
I don't think it should be impossible for you to make money selling made
items to NPCs, but the amount of money that you make should be very
comparable to what I can make out there gathering loot off of MOBs. My
impression (please feel free to correct me if I am wrong!) is that you guys
are the same group of people that made hundreds of millions of TONS of
surplus Tailoring-made items in UO - you chose to spend time working up your
Tailor so you could provide your mage with enough money to buy reagents.
True?
The point I'm trying to make is that you guys were taking advantage of the
system...using in imbalance in the pricing structure to make a LOT more cash
than you should have been able to make by using trade skills. Be happy that
you got to take advantage of the system as long as you did. But PLEASE
don't whine when they try to balance things out...it trivializes the real
issues around game balance.
Thanks for your time.
