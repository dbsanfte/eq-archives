from you if they were online when you got it.

No Drop has it's place. I heard that it was implemented to prevent newbies from banking their newbie
food and duping it by dying a few times when the food respawns during the first 3 lvls of a
character. I also think that making some quest items No Drop is perfectly acceptable. (Like the

like that was a "quick fix" to reduce camping. Which obviously didn't work very well, just increased
the camping. So now it's been moved to a quest item. (Which I give Verant the benefit of the doubt
and assume that was the long term goal. Instead of finding that their first fix didn't work so now
they are gonna try another.)

But making any and every cool item No Drop is pointless. Especially when Verant is making such a big
deal about a "Player based Economy". If they want people to buy/sell/trade with each other then just
about EVERYTHING should be something you can sell to another player. At the very least let mechant's
buy No Drop items (Like those stupid burnt out lightstones from wisps, what the hell are they doing
as No DropP? a quest?) and sell them to other players.

Just my No Drop rant :)
