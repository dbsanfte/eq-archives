I would doubt it, it would be a *LOT* of extra coding... Shadowstep, gate,
falling, SoW with the bard song (Now stacks) couple of other things. Also,
I accidentally did a preliminary test on this last night. I was *DEEP* in
Sol B, deeper then I'd want to go without SoW on, and I pinged a Greater
Kobold Shaman with an arrow. *JUST* as he turned around but before he hit
me for the first time, there was a net burp. Not noticing this I ran all
the way to
the spot where we pull to, to see no one there (During net burps people
dissappear as I'm sure you've seen.) a moment later, the connection came
back. I was where I saw myself, full life, and about 20 seconds later the
Shaman came into view. Basically this means that:

1) While I wasn't there he didn't engage in combat.
2) He didn't move to follow until after the server knew where I was.
3) I saved about 400 hitpoints of damage that I usually take during that
run.

If it's not too much of a hassle I'm going to rewire my Hubs this afternoon
to put the one connection I play EQ from on the desktop to see if I can
repeativly do this. I'd give it a 50/50 chance, the connection burp was
near the server side and *MAY* have been server side lag. (Everyone in the
zone had the same burp at the same time.) Either way though it was a good
if accidental proof of concept.
