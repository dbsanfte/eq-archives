I'm pretty sure that all classes will have new armor in the game. While not all
of it will come from quests in the Temple of Solusek Ro, most of the armor will.
There will be SK armor, it has a black plate apperance (nice), ranger is green
chain, cleric gold plate, etc.

The possible problem that may arise, if the do these quests like they "fixed"
several of the other quests, is that if ANY of these quests rely on one static
spawn that you have to kill. Often times these spawns are 6-8 hours apart, are
heavily camped, etc. Anyone seen how badly Befallen and Kerra Isle are camped
(almost 24/7) now that the Paw of Opella information has become known? The
Testiment is even worse.

If these quests require one static spawn that has the necessary item rare or a
long spawn time, it's another useless quest IMO. I would much rather continue
to get experience and fight in places that are challenging, instead of sitting
on a static spawn for hours to get an item. I'd love to have the best equipment
in the game, but not at the expense of my enjoyment. I have a hard enough time
to put 3-4 hours together on a week night, let alone some of the 16 hours people
have camped for the rings from Mitty (Paw quest). My worst horror story is 12
hours for JBoot, but at least I was getting experience at the time.

If camping is what is required (even though it's now a "quest"), I really don't
see this as any improvement to the game.

Hopefully this will not be the case with these new quests.
