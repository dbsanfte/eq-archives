Well many have posted on this but sadly a few comments are wrong. Unless
they changed this quest, a giant fire beetle leg comes from the Queen
Klackniks. These can be found near Qeynos or Rivervale newbie areas. It is a
rare spawn. It is quite tough for newbies though, i suggest at least level
10-12 before doing it if not higher. been a long time since I killed one.

The beetles in karana are called Giant Beetles and DO NOT drop the items you
are looking for.
