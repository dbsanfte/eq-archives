I read an article which sounds similar to the one you say you read and I
believe it was Brad himself that was quoted as saying "no anti-camping
zones". But that article was released at least a month ago, (maybe longer)
and camping has become a major issue since then - hence all the changes
(lore items implemented; no drops more common; unbelievably slow/long spawn
rates for rares; changing respawn rates).

As for my pure speculation, I spent over 2 weeks (hours upon hours)
timing/camping various spawns throughout Befallen to test my theory. I even
had other players helping me out. According to what I found, in certain
areas, not all areas mind you, there's an anti-camping code. If you wish to
not believe, then so be it. But in my experience, they exist. I only wish I
had screenshots of the GM's and Guides I've talked to who said there was to
back up what I'm saying. I'd gladly send the screenshots to all who doubt.
