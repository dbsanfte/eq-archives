Well, my understanding is that you don't need to give them to a caster
to have them identified. Simply pick up the item in question with you
cursor then have the caster target you and cast the spell. Whatever
the targeted player is "holding" (ie Floating on the cursor) will be
indentified.

It took a little bit of trial and error, but I finally figured out
that that is how the spell works. I am a wizzie and when I cast the
spell, it would tell me that I didn't have a target. So I targeted
myself, and then it told me that I wasn't "holding" anything. That
took a while to realize I could have an object on my cursor and still
fire off a spell. :)

I have never tried it on other people, but I don't see why it wouldn't
work on them either.
