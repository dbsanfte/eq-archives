No, you don't lose exp from dueling or PvP. I belive you lose exp in a duel
if you die from a DOT or a pet, but this may have been fixed. I love to
duel -- my guild often holds sparring matches, not only to test drive your
char in PvP, but we compare characters and do groups duels to find the best
group PvP combos. (We are a dark race guild on Rallos Zek, so we role-play
evil). I think that when we do group sparring, it is the best way to
practice PvP. Esp when you go hunting humans, like we do. Strategies
against a good necro and his pet do not always apply to a SK/Cleric combo.

As I said before, I am a Cleric and I LOVE grouping with my Enchanter
friend. After we buff each other, we have about 300 extra AC and HP's if
not more... after all, with the two of us in a group we have some of the
best buffing spells in the game. When I am NOT fighting with a warriorr
class, it is hard because as soon as I smite/expulse, I am targeted and it
it too hard to heal or cast because my concentration is broken. The only
reason I like to fight with a SK is they taunt. But with my enchanter
friend, you get it all -- I buff, he de-buffs, I smite, his pet taunts, and
his pets also double-attacks.

Azboklin, you're right about pets in general. I think it's just a matter of
personal preference. imho though, as a Cleric, and I tihnk being a DE has
something to do with it -- Enchanters are the BEST to group with and PvP
with. But to answer your questions, no you don't lose exp in PvP yes you
lose all the money on your person and the victor has his choice of one item
from your inventory (heh which is why when I am about to die I shove my
screaming mace in my bag)

--Venkman, Rallos Zek
