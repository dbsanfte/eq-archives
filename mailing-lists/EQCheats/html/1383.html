David,

I have completed the Crafted Greaves quest twice now. You need the Dark
Circlet and two Star Rubies. You take these items to Ulan a Centaur in the
Centaur Village of South Karana. The /loc on the Centaur Village is 200,
-2400. I always Hail him and ask him about the item I want him to make
before I turn over the quest items to him. I keep my number one inventory
slot clear and do not stack the gems. This may be a bit anal but I want to
make sure not to lose anything, and never have doing it this way.

Good Luck!
