I'm not sure about a merchant that sells raw hide. I have never seen one,
but I know where you can get great amounts at if your willing to kill some
zombies. Go to eruds island (The one with the wisps) and go to the sunken
ship on the island. By the ship (underwater) 3 zombies spawn. They drop
Zombie Skins and Raw hide common, and rarely drop Bronze weapons. But the
good part is they spawn fast and usually by the time you clean up the 3'rd
zombie the 1'st one has spawned again. Probably with about 30mins to an
hour of camping you may be able to have a complete suit of raw hide.

Heres a tip for pulling the zombies. If your a caster of anysort with a
lull type of spell you can easily pull them with no worries. You can wade
into the water just enough where you can see CLEARLY all the way to the ship
without actually being considered swimming. It's not hard to do. just keep
taking small steps forward until the ship comes into plain view. You know
you've done this wrong if you see your breath meter pop up. Once you waded
in just enough to see all 3 zombies clearly you can lull 2 and pull one
without having to worry about outswimming them since you'll basically be on
land anyway.

If your a druid and want the best leather armor I recommend just buying some
reinforced leather from a tailor.
