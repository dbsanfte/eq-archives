You have to purchase a ruby from an NPC jewel merchant. So far, every
attempt to use any other ruby (there is one off a fish in Rivervale) has
failed. With the exception of killing Lynguna herself, since she usually
drops a ruby. (Seems kinda wierd to kill a unique NPC, get their loot, then
take it back to them in their next life). In case it's not obvious, Lynguna
will KOS any non-evil race.

The midnight mallet and Brutechopper have similar stats. Atk 14 & 15
respectively, and dly 47 & 48 respectively. The weight on both is around 11
I think. The midnight mallet has 5 charges of waking sleep. Whether or not
it can be recharged I do not know. Some say it's 5 per day, but I'm almost
certain that is incorrect... since I have one, and it's been sitting at 4
charges for a long time (many in-game days) I do not know if the
brutechopper has a magical effect.

I don't know about the Brutechopper, but the MM can only be used by shamans
or dwarven clerics.

It looks veyr large when holding it. My troll was standing next to a
halfling last night and the head of the mallet was almost as large as the
halfling was. The sucky thing tho is that it uses the attack animation for
a 2H staff. The little half-swipe-poke move. Which looks stupid with this
giant hammer. I was really looking forward to a big over-head smash
animation once I got the mallet. I put in a feedback for it, but I know
it'll never happen.

I've hit for 26 damage with it. It's a little slower than the rts. Not
twice as slow, or even half again as slow. So if you've got all your dex
and strength buffs going, you'll hit hard a lot. Ratio-wise it's identical
to the glowing wooden crook.

I'm on the innoruk server, and I'll sell mine cheap to anybody who wants to
buy it. The price is 100pp. Why am I selling it? Mostly because my
playstyle doesn't involve melee that much. (despite what people say,
shamans are only slightly above _suck_ in melee because their skillcap is
lower) I've also got a round of spells coming up and I seriously need to
upgrade my armor.
