Here's the repost =)

1. See below, otherwise hail the named cents and follow the prompts.
2. No, anyone can do the quest.
3. The Star Rubies, Fire Emeralds, Rubies, and Sapphires can be bought in a
number of places that sell the jewelling metals (Qeynos and Freeport
verified). The other components are dropped items.
4. WAR only items, all races though. Check sites like EQ'Lizer for the
actual stats on them.

Gauntlets: Aviak Charm (Aviak Avocets, South Karana), and 2 Star Rubies
Boots: Frost Totem (Ice Goblin High Shamans, Permafrost), 2 Star Rubies
Helm: Fire Totem (Fire Goblin High Clerics, Solusek), 2 Rubies
Vambrace: Eye of a Griffon (East Commonlands, North Karana, etc.), 2 Fire
Emeralds
Bracer: Evil Eye Optic Nerve (rare drop item like the Polished Bone Bracer),
a Sapphire
Greaves: Silver Circlet (Necromancer in Najena, Drelzna), 2 Star Rubies
Pauldrones: the Three Parts of the Rune of Dwarves (Evil Eyes, Runnyeye),
Fire Emerald
Breastplate: Werewolf Talons (Mistmoore, Mayong Tribe), 3 Rubies
