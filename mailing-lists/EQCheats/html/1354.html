Well, speaking from experience (last night), I was camping Trazdon. He's not
listed on any maps I've seen, so I'll tell ya his location first. He's down
around the corner from Bonecraker. His room should be just across the wall
from Bonecracker actually. Has some blood in the room with a misty type
waterfall. Two elementals, a mage plus pet, Trazdon's spawn point (can be
him or a mage from my experience), plus an ogre (officer when I was there,
he never cam edown to assist or anything so I never killed him). At level
32, the mages were still agressive, although two 149 damage spells kills
them. Pets are three of the same I think. Get em as they spawn and yer fine.
Trazdon isn't a whole lot harder, but again, I'm 32 so I resisted most of
his spells. He was low low blue to me, the mages were greens (agressive
green).

I held the room with little problem as a 32 Shaman (although a nice cleric
traded buffs with me for a sow, so I had like 804 hp...heh...that was nice).
I think it's a 20 minute spawn cycle.

Never camped the others, so I don't know for sure, but they should be about
the same.
