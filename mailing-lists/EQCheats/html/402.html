As far as I know they only come off of a fisherman in Qeynos hills, he
spawns exactly 6 hours apart, and only has the earing 1 in 10 times. The
fisherman is also 30+ level. A friend of mine has camped the spot in The

Maybe some of the monsters in the sewers should have them occasionally.
