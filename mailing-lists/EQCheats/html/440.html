BIBS - location was already given in a prior email. The Knight supposedly
spawns 3 times a day (or every 6 hours I've heard). We got him twice within
15 minutes, got two swords. This is rare. More likely he will spawn
several hours apart. The room that spawns him also spawns about 2 - 3 other
dazed goblins, and there is a false wall behind the green gems which
contains an alcove with 2 dazed miners. My group was in the 25ish range and
we could take all that no prob. Make sure you have root, chains of music,
etc. to stop runner or else you will have a dazed train on your hands. An
enchanter or bard is good for crowd control is you are actually going to
camp and room and fight.

Blackened Iron Armor I believe is one AC worse than banded, heavier, but has
the +ACI-cool+ACI- look, and some people pay a fortune for the stuff. It comes off
the warlord and sometimes elite guards. The King drops the crown,
medallion, and grotesque mask (not all 3 at once, not all the time). The
King is guarded by 4 elites, plus 2 in a room beyond. The warlord room is
before the King room, containing the warlord and 2 elites. I've seen high
teens to low twenties camping the warlord room since there are only 3 mobs.

Runnyeye is (on Tunare) not crowded and has good treasure and exp. You just
have to know how to play the dungeon. Also, if you camp the King's room be
prepared to fight Borxx (or to zone, depending on your party's level). My
party killed Borxx each and every time (he's lvl 30 - 33) and he always
drops the lens for the Staff of the Observers Quest.. The lens sells very
well by itself, but the staff sells for even more. He also occasionally
drops a bracelet. Polished Bone I think.

Anyhow..hope this helps.
