Hmm...

Lets look at the comparisons, given the possible duals (with no
Lay-on-Hands)
Paladin with Fiery Avenger Vs. Monk? Monk will win.
Paladin with Fiery Avenger Vs. Warrior? Warrior will probably win.
Paladin with Fiery Avenger Vs. Ranger? Probably a toss up.

Of course I'm not certain about these, but I think you'd have to prove me
wrong for me to believe it.

Think about it... Double attack / Dual wield makes a big difference. That's
4 attacks per round when you get up there. Against a monk there is no
contest... you have a delay _50_ weapon that does what? in the 100pt range?
ok, maybe 150? if that! a dmg 15 yields 24-27 hps, so it would stand to
reason that a dmg 30 weapon dishes out 50-60hps (if it's linear) so a dmg 37
would be like 70ish per hit using that reasoning, but I think it's more than
that.
But that monk... the monk will dish out... hmm.. 45+45+45+45 (4 punches)
+120 (flying kick) = 300pts TWICE in the time it will take the paladin to
swing his weapon once.

(On the innoruk server, there is a lvl 50 monk and lvl 50 warrior that duel
occasionally, the warrior is in full rubicite and the monk can kill him
before he even gets to half life.)

A warrior will be swinging 3-4 times per round with fast high damage weapons
for probably a total of 90-130 per round, and still getting two rounds worth
of hits in the time it takes the paladin to get in one. (it's not exactly
double, but it's close, 2 speed 28-32 weapons compared with one speed 50
weapon)

The the stats that have been rumored about are true, then the Fiery avenger
seems like a lot of hype (albeit a very cool weapon) about something that
will give the paladins a fighting chance. But the reasoning on that doesn't
hold much water either, because if it wasn't a totally bad-ass weapon, then
why would it be so hard to aquire?

So maybe it's dmg 50, delay 37? That would be a different story (he'd still
get schooled by a monk, but that's true for every class)

Kzak, Shaman who'd get schooled by everybody
