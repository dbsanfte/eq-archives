Befallen is wild.... I haven't yet figured out the Troll SK for the BBC (and
I've gotten about 5 BBC's between me and parties I've been in). SK will
spawn anywhere form 30 minutes to 6 hours.... I seen him spawn 30 minutes
after we killed him once, 2 BBCs in 30 minutes is nice... And, he hadden't
spawned more than once in 10 hours that entire day. The line is supposed to
be the spikes. Don't pass the spikes if you can help it.

The Thurg robes necro will spawn in a pattern. On the spot that she spawns,
spawns ghouls, necros, and the priest (take some firepower in for the
priest). She will NOT spawn (and the others will) if your past the last
ledge and actually in the room. You need to be atleast on the last
step/ledge going into the room. My guess, is that robes are possible every
30 or 40 minutes if done right.
