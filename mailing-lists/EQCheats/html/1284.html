The only halflings that raise faction with Neriak guards are the Najena
injured halfling and Leatherfoot Raider halfling in Nek Forest and give caps
over to Neriak warrior guild person. Yes it does lower faction with
Halflings. The regular halflings in Nek forest gave me hits against all of
Rivervale but no good ones for Neriak.

You can also go kill elf prisoners in Crushbone.

You can also kill elf nobles in In. Swamp.

Keep in mind that raising faction with Indigo Brotherhood does NOT raise
faction with the merchants and other groups in Neriak. I have found no way
to do so and believe me I have looked...
