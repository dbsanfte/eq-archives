Yup, that's what I meant. Sorry, if I wasn't clear about that. I am one of those
people that get used to hotkeys and rarely ever want them to change. As a result
common button maps for common clases are really good for me. I get really aggravated
when I keep hitting the spell book hotkey when I mean to be hitting taunt or
something like that. Even better example is the necro /pet commands. You don't wanna
be hitting the wrong buttons in a dungeon. =) Another thing to note, If you do the
same name over several servers then monkeying around with the hot buttons will have
the same effect for ALL characters for that name. I don't know if it's a bug or what,
but I was messing around with an alternate character on the Fennin Ro server (E marr
was down again.. go figure) and now that character's hot buttons ALWAYS disappear no
matter what server I login to. I guess I just have to delete the NAME.opt file and
then recreate the buttons to get them back, but it isn't that big a deal for me as
it's a the enchanter I started, but never play. If it was my main character, I would
have devoted a little more time to fixing it! :)

Anyway, it's just a little trick (and pitfall) I figured out and thought you might
enjoy knowing.
