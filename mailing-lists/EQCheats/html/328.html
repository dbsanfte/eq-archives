The Hag on very RARE occasion has some gossamer, I myself looted Gossamer
arm from the Hag once, I think this is very rare however because our party
killed probably 100 Hag's during our hunts in Unrest and that's the only
time I have personally ever seen anything on her at all except for the
occasional Runes. I hunt in Unrest allot. There are some other VERY nice
items in Unrest though like the Bloodstained Mantle and Bloodstained Tunic
(both taken off the Undead Knights of Unrest - tough skele's they are!) I
have sold the mantle many times easily bringing 40 to 50 plat it ye can't
wear it, the tunic sells for 200+pp. Also if ye feel really adventuresome or
just plain Lucky try the Dwarven Spirit in the basement he has the famous
Dwarven Work Boots and some nice jewelry as well.
The knights are usually about lvl 28 or 29 but they are hard to pull without
getting some of his friends. The Dwarven Spirit's lvl I am not sure of but I
think low to mid 30's perhaps someone on the list can confirm this. Unrest
is second only to Guk for Loot and I think other than the occasional trains
it is much safer than Guk.
Hope this info helps send me an email if you have any other questions and if
ye play on tarrew marr look me up, I'm lvl 29 Wizard name of Quindinar
Olo'rion

Good Luck!
Quin
