I've gotten about 9 HQ bear pelts in my career (as loot, not bought). ALL
except 2 of them i got from the little blackbears and young Kodiaks that
roam the Misty Thicket.
One other i got from a black bear in West Commons and the other I got from a
grizzly bear (i think it was a grizzly...coulda been a smaller one) in West
karana.

As i haven't exactly spent a HUGE amount of times going after bear pelts (I
mostly have tailored small sizes for newbies..i find the wee folk irrestibly
cute), i would think someone with the sole intent of getting HQ bear should
be able to do quite well going after them in Misty Thicket. Figure about 1
or 2 per game day in Misty thicket if you go after all the little bears that
spawn there. There's a time frame on Bear Spawns in Misty, so you will
have a good deal of downtime waiting for a game day to pass and a new spawn
come, but you are guaranteed to get the HQ pelts (unless I and a friend of
mine are just terribly luck).
