The thing the Shamans are miffed about is that our disease line of
spells used to do OBSCENE damage. The level 19 spell did 500 damage,
level 34 did 1600, and our level 49 did over 6000. Only thing was it
took 11+ minutes for the spell to wear off, so they were only good for
kiting. Our disease line had the duration cut by a factor of 10 and had
the damage doubled.

On the plus side though, our poison spells got a big power up.
Envenomed breath now does 25 points per tic for 21 tics. You can still
kite yellows fairly easily with just this spell, but the days of kiting
deep reds with ease are over for shamans.
