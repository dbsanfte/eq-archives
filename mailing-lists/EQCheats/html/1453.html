It states, somewhere, in the manual that part of the game is "protecting your
home" and city against other races. I wasn't involved directly, but happend to
be nearby auctioning when this happend. I never bothered to look it up, but the
GMs sure did.

The manual language was pointed out after several instances of guard training
where several pleayers were warned that this was a bannable offense. The GM had
already "marked" their account. After the peeps pointed this paragraph out, and
about a half hour, the lead GM said "We (the GMs) were incorrect. This is not a
bannable offense. I have removed the warning marks from your account."

I was there watching the whole thing. If they have come to some other
conclusion since this time, or if this was server specific, then so be it, but
this was the head GM, after contact by several guides and another GM. I assume
that the 30 minute delay was because they were contacting Verant associates
other than the GMs or had a quick GM meeting to discuss this.
