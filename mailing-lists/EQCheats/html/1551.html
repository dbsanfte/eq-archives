A somewhat tedious but still lucrative method involves Polar Bear Cloaks.
You can make these for 5 plat each in Everfrost, and they will sell for
10-15 on Faydwer... possibly more these days.

Involves travel and some work, but quite a lot of money can be made by doing
this.

Alternatively, you are close to Wisp time.... Greater Lightstones will get
you almost ten plat each, and they aren't terribly hard to find. :)
