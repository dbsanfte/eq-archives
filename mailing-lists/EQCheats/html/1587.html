Do the Shining Star of Light quest (involves getting honeycombs/jumjum juice
in Rivervale) then going to Qeynos and West Karanas in quick succession.
The SSoL is a 1HBlunt magical weapon and goes for anywhere between 40 and 50
plat (basically, it's a 6/28 magic mace with a Cleanse effect - some people
call it a poor man's Screaming Mace). On any given night, I can do this
quest in 45 minutes with SOW.

The ONLY caveat is getting your faction up with Priests of Life. Easiest
way to do this - kill rabid grizzlies and rabid wolves in Qeynos Hills and
give them to one of the Priestesses in the UFO saucer in the Cleric's guild
area of Qeynos (north qeynos, east side). If you do this, be warned - there
are two NPCs wandering the Qeynos Hills zone. Cros Treewind and Holly
Windstalker - they HATE when people kill the bears and wolves and will KOS
you if the see you and you are within their AGGRO range. They are level
30ish and tough to survive, even at level 22. My suggestion is to get
yourself SOWed and do you rabid animal hunting. Anyway, when you hand each
diseased pelt in, you get XPs. And bear pelts earn you up to 2plat per pelt
from the PRiestess.

Besides getting your faction up to amiable with the PriestsOfLife, there is
no killing involved and some decent XPs for each part of the quest. There
are several sites which detail the quest. Here's one:

http://www.gameznet.com/eq/quest/shiningstarlight.html

I've created and sold six (6!) SSoLs over the last 10 days. Three I've sold
for 40-ish.. a couple for 50+.. and one I "gave" to a low level cleric who
needed a break - for 12 plat. (Yes, I'm a nice guy).

The cost to you for the quest is 30 gold. And 45 minutes. It's a KILLER
way to make money. And if you REALLY want to have some fun, go sell the
mace in Greater Faydark - the High Elves there have access to NO low level
cleric weapons other than the Screaming Mace. And to tell you the truth,
when fighting Ghouls, I'd rather have the SSoL than the Screaming Mace. (the
SSoL's Cleanse effect removes GhoulRoot, diseases, poisons, etc during
combat).

It's a great low to medium level weapon!!!
