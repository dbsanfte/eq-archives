Here is the entire Soulfire quest, the way I understand it:

1. Get the note from Kalatrina in the Hall of Truth, top level.
2. Go to the spy, tell him 'Truth is good', and then give him the
letter.
3. After the Guard kills him, take his note and you are supposed to
give it to her, but she doesn't hand it back to you, so skip this part
of the quest. She noramlly tells you to go to
the Office of the People and speak with the woman there, but I'm
telling you right now.
4. At the Office of the People, you are again supposed to give her the
note, but she also doesn't give it back. Simply tell her "Who is
Zimel?', and she will tell you about Groflah, and
that Zimel's Blades in condemned.
5. First, go to the Hall of Truth, and tell Kalatrina that 'Zimels
Baldes is condemned.'. I believe this is where you first hear about
Soulfire (or is it the time you give it to her the
first time, I haven't done this part in a while).
6. Now, go to Groflah's Forge, and ask him 'Who is Zimel?'. He will
give you a negative response, and then give him the Zimel's Blades
note. He will tell you to meet him in the
Seafarer's Roost.
7. Go to the Seafarer's Roost (at the docks in East Freeport), and
wait for Groflah to show up. He is usually there at 3 am, but it's
fairly ramdom. When he shows, again ask him 'Who is
Zimel?', and he'll tell you about he's buddies with old Zimel. Now
give him the note, and he'll decode it for you. It says 'H E L P C A
P T I V E', on it, implying that the prisoner
under the arena is Zimel (as we suspected for some time).
8. While waiting at the Seafarer's Roost, do NOT go AFK, as you will
miss a crucial part of the quest. Randomly a drunk named Tykar Renlin
will show up, and will cuss out Captain Lucan
when a bard sings a song about him. He also says that Lucan imprisoned
him and his buddy, old Zimel. Ask him 'Who is Zimel?' and he will ask
you for a drink. Give him 4 Drom's
Champagnes, and he will give you a blanket called 'Bunker Cell #2' (I
think, but it's not important what it's called).
9. Go to the prisoner under the Arena. Hail him, and ask him 'Who is
Zimel?'. He will 'Stare deeply into your eyes', and say nothing. Ask
him 'Where is Soulfire?', and he'll ramble
something along the lines of 'Bog n Goo!! Freezing!! Hungry!!
Thirsty too!!'. This implies that he needs two items.
10. Go kill some snakes and get a few Snake Scales off them. Put them
in a brew barrel with a bottle, and a flask of water. This will give
you 'Bog Juice', one of the items that Zimel
asks for (Bog n Goo; this is the Bog, now for the Goo...)
11. Get some Fire Beetle Eyes (if you don't know where to get these,
you don't deserve Soulfire), and Rat ears, and put them into an oven.
You get 'Edible Goo', which Zimel also asks for
(This is the Goo part...)
12. Give the 'Bunker Cell #2', the 'Bog Juice', and the 'Edible Goo'
to Zimel. He gives you a key called H.K. 102, and he mutters something
about Tunaria's Corridor. If you've done the
Princess Quest in High Keep, you know what to do...
13. Take the Key to High Keep (located in Highpass Hold; this is the
narrow pass between Qeynos and Freeport, and the coninent used to be
called Tunaria, hence, Tunaria's Corridor). Give
the key to the Clerk in the bank, that's it. You get a note from Hayle
Mool, addressed to Zimel. It tells you to come get him in Qeynos, but
if you've been there before, you know he's
being held in Splitpaw...
14. In Splitpaw, find him (don't ask...) and give him the note. He
will tell you something I forgot already, but then you ask him 'What is
the Proof of Nobility?'. He will tell you that
he requires the note you gave him, The Brilliant Sword of Faith, the
Glowing Sword Hilt, and the Testimony of Truth (some of which are
sub-quests, explained below).
15. At this point, I HAVE NOT given him the items, at least in the
correct manner (long story, and rather embarassing; I'd perfer if you
didn't ask...). If you give the items one at a
time, I have heard (though this is not confirmed) he will hand you over
the Soulfire! Congrats, you should have the blade if you're smarter
than I am!

THE TEST OF TRUTH

This is also started in the Hall of Truth, and may not be started after
level 8. This is a very simple quest, so I will not explain it here...

THE BRILLIANT SWORD OF FAITH

1. Speak with the Paladin Guild Master at the top of the Hall of
Truth, and ask him everything pertaining to killing Lucan D'lere.
2. Once you have gotten the quest, assemble a team of TONS of level 45
plus people, because Captain Lucan is the badass leader of the Warrior
Guild in Freeport, and the head of the
Freeport Militia. He's level 48, so make sure you've got PLENTY of
time to spend killing him, since he's TOUGH. Tonight on my server
(Rodcet Nife) a group of about 20 level 40+ people
LOST to this bugger, and only killed him after dying three or four
times each.
3. If you get his Testimony from him (which rarely spawns, God knows
why...), return it to the man who gave you the quest, and he forks over
the Brilliant Sword of Faith.

GLOWING SWORD HILT

This is gained off a Shadow Knight in Mistmoore named Xicotl. I am not
sure, but I think it spawns every time, and he's in either the Guest
House or the Throne Room, if I recall
correctly. He's surrounded by other Shadow Knights and Necros, so be
prepared for another tough battle (though not as tough as the bloodbath
when you fight Lucan, that's just BRUTAL). I
should point out that whenever this guy gets pulled, the whole zone
clears, so be warned...


That's my take on Soulfire, correct me if I'm wrong... But I'm not. :
