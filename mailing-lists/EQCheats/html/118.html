In north karana, there are 3 different varieties of giant beetles that have
the same markings as the fire beetles. And some even put off light just
like a fire bettle, and when you kill then, there are little red eyes in the
inventory that say "Fire beetle eyes". So call them giant fire bettles if
you want, but when you target them, they are called:

?? : ~lvl 10-12 (sorry, I'm drawing a blank on the name of this one)
Borer Beetle : ~lvl 14-16
Scythe Beetle : ~lvl 20

Head out there, avoid the giants and griffs (this is like the homeland of
both). And make sure you con each one before starting in, because you could
easily be in over your head if you've got the wrong one.
