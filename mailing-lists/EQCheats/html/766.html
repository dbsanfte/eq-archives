>There are three factors that I know of that play into pricing from
>vendors: charisma, faction, and vendor multiple. As a High Elf
>Enchanter, I have a reasonably high base charisma, many charisma items,
>and a charisma spell. After some experimentation, I have come to the
>conclusion that charisma's effect on pricing maxes at just over 100.
>Higher charisma than that makes no difference in pricing.
>
>I also have spells which can improve my faction with vendors, and have
>found that faction higher than "looks at you indifferently" makes no
>difference in pricing. If it's lower than that, your price will be
>worse, but better won't improve prices any more.
>
>The final factor is the vendor multiple. Some vendors just sell things
>for a higher base price. Charisma and faction still have an effect, but
>the range of possible prices is higher.
>
>That's what I know about vendor pricing. Good luck.
