Ok first off, I am only a Level 16 monk so I do not have very many rare
spoilers for you guys. However, I have a few odds and ends that I have
picked up in my travels. Some of this stuff is what I have heard, so
take it as you will.

There is a glowing torch found on a rare spawn in Lavastorm...It gives 4
AC, adds +5 charisma, and can be used as a lighting source when held in
your hand.

After receiving the Squad Ring from the quest in Riverdale, you can use
it to kill Nillipus fairly easily. All you have to do is lure Nillipus
into the cleric's guide, where the ring can be recharged, and then use
the ring when you are low on health. You can then recharge the ring at
one on the NPC's. I do not have the squad ring yet, so i can not verify
that this works. I would appreciate it if someone would tell me how to
recharge the ring?

When talking to NPC's, they give keywords in brackets. To activate
these keywords you have to ask a question using the keyword. Usually, a
simple question such as What [keyword]? will do the trick. However,
when the keyword is a person, then it requires a Who [keyword]? I know
this sounds pretty simple, but it took me forever to figure out. :)

There are a pair of Shiny Silk Gloves which are found on the Queen
Spider in Lesser Faydark, i forget stats but I believe they are
magical. It would take about a level 16 group to get them.
