Piffle.

our Dot's can stack. Whooptie doo. Here is level 19 kiting for a
Shaman....4 dots are availible(if you use our AE spell) 1)Sicken 8dmg,
2/tic 2)Affliction 30 dmg 6/tic 3)Infectious Cloud 20 dmg 5/tic 4)
Tainted Breath 10dmg, 7/tic

Ok we do 88 points of DD from casting and 20 dmg/tic for 21
tics(assuming 3 castings of tainted breath) That rounds out to a tasty
508 damage. Pretty good you think. The mana cost of this is the
problem It cost 303 mana and 2 minutes to get this. This has a 1.68
dmg/mana ratio

Lets look at a druid strategy at level 14....
In two minutes, a druid can cast Stinging Swarm 3 times for 360 damage
and 195 mana. This has a 1.85 dmg/mana ratio. Add in one more swarm
for close to equal damage. Not only do Druids have a better dmg/mana
ratio, but you only had to cast 4 spells to get this compared to the
Shaman's 6 spells. This means 2 fewer chances of resists as well.

You are also doing more damage at a lower level. This means you can
take on higher level crits than a Shaman trying to kite. Few level
14-17(viable kiting range for a lvl 14 druid IMHO) crits have that many
HP's.
