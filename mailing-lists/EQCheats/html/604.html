Sean, perhaps your correct in your mathmatics... It would be over 1000
hours... But I don';t think the 'average' player is going to spend 20 hours
a week and only get 1-2 levels either... In fact, I reached level 5 in about
4 hours as I recall... It's the other 17 (I am now 22) that took me the
better part of 400+ hours to reach... but that is by my CHOICE! Because I
wanted to spend time chatting with others, selling stuff, making stuff and
just plain exploring places... And guess what... I still do all that! I
probably don't actually play 20 hours a week, more like 15 I would guess...
and I probably gain 1.5 levels in that time... So, it would be more like 750
hours to make level 50... But that is not the case either I would bet, not
for me anyway...

Oh, and I know MANY others that play regularly (daily) for at least 4 hours
per session... that would give them at least 28 hours per week... which is
why they (my friends) are 3-7 levels above me even though we started out
about the same time...

Besides, who cares?!?! This is the way that *I* like to play!! If you
want to be some *power-leveler* then go for it!! Crank up those levels as
fast as you can!

I think Verant did the right thing in making it easy to level up in the
BEGINNING, and harder to make levels later on... Sure, it's frustrating, but
that is the way of the world of Norrath!
