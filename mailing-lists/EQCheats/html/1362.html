I've done the Crafted Greaves quest (Just have Helm and Breastplate left+ACE-) and
you have the correct item. If you were to speak with Ulan Meadowgreen and ask
him about the greaves, he will tell you about a Dark Elf Necromancer who
terrorized his old home town, blah blah blah. Anyway, you need to give the Dark
Circlet and I believe 2 Star Rubies to Ulan (or his brother) to get the
greaves.

The best way I have found to get the cheapest prices on gems for the crafted
armor quests, is to make a Female Human Enchanter and base her in Freeport.
Bring her over to the blue house just outside the market area that has the bank
in it. The blue building is the gem shop. My lvl 1 Female Human Enchanter gets
star rubies for 68p, Sapphires for 105p. Cheapest prices around. That would
mean a pair of gauntlets 136p and you can easily sell them for 200p.

+AD4AKgAqACoAKg-IMPORTANT NOTE+ACEAKgAqACoAKg-
For the Crafted Armor quests, if they ask for 2 or more of the same gem, when
you give them the gems and the component, DO NOT stack the gems. Make sure you
separate each of them but give them all at one time.

Example: Crafted gauntlets call for an Aviak Charm and 2 Star Rubies. Give the
centaur the aviak charm, then 1 ruby, then another. So out of the 4 slots
available in the GIVE window, 3 are occupied.
