Kirby,
I don't have a high level necro but at my relative level 15 necro (and knowing others with high
level necros), I have experienced a lot of their peculiarities. I disagree with some of your
statements.

1. No necros aren't the most powerful class, in fact I really don't think there is one. If you are
basing it on damage spells then show me the necro's version of ice comet and you'll have me there.
Or show me the necro pet that can cast spells, or the necro that can dual wield .... you get the
picture. Each class is very good at something and no class is great at everything.

2. KOS is horrible plain and simple. You know it, you've experienced it. Try not being able to sell
to any non-evil vendor. If you are a DE then sure .. walk around Neriak and have at it. But don't
try it in a foreign city. A necro's power isn't to make up for being KOS, it's because they are
masters of the dead. Lifesteal, disease, poison, summoning undead ... these are all necro weapons,
not gifts from Verant for those wishing to play evil.

3. Root? Only at high levels when it's usually being done by someone else MUCH sooner.
Invisibility? Self only. Buffs? Only one ... spirit armor, that doesn't HURT us. Heals? They hurt
us as we cast them. Only trading life, not healing it. Our spells are nicely tuned to what the
whole idea of a necromancer should be.

4. Far more damage than any other class at the same level? Hardly. Make a necro ... try the solo
thing. Without a pet you'll die in a heartbeat. With one you can do okay ... but ONLY if you know
how to play a necro well. The same goes for any class. Play them well and you will do well, play
them like crap and ... you die.

So in a sense I don't agree. Yes I have a nice class, and yes I like my Necro. But he is not the
only good class, and he is definitely not the most powerful in the game. The way you worded your
email it sounds like you may have had a druid and might be a little mad that they were gimped. I
agree with the fact that major changes should never be patched without notification but Necros
should not take the ridicule of anyone just because a previously powerful class feels shortchanged.

Most of the changes being made to the Necro change have actually been corrections to things that
didn't work. And they still aren't all done, try using a DOT in a party and cry as you receive NO
experience if the DOT kills the creature. In addition, some spells are being removed and replaced
with less powerful spells. Charm is an example.

All I'm saying is that it appears Verant wants things balanced. If THEY think necros are too
powerful, they too will be gimped. But I sincerely believe that as a class they are what they
should be. Well rounded, and masters of undead.

-Haytred, lvl 15 DE Necro, E. Marr
