Yes big difference. Check eqvault for an essay on it, here are the basics:

Earth: Hits hard, slow, most hps
Fire: Hits hardest, fastest, fewest hps
Water: Hits weakest, not fast, many hps and high regen rate
Air: Hits weak, fast, high agi, not many hps

That is a -rough- breakdown, I haven't read the essay for a while and
haven't been a Mage for awhile either, but that is pretty much how it goes.
I liked Waters the best for solo tanking. Good luck!
