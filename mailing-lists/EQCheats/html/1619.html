You'll need at MINIMUM two enchanters to mez the Swirlspines. Going in
without enchanters to mez them, you'll all die or evac if you try to pull
Phin.

Other than that, two FULL SOLID groups should be able to do it, but it'd be
rough I think. There are like 10 guardians aruodn him,l with like a 20
minute spawn time (this is last time I went on a raid, which was 1-2 months
ago). Level 47-49 warrior guardians, that summon.

We got a buggy pull and pulled 5 guardians at once. We had 4+ full groups,
all 45+. Half of us died, other half evac'd. We never even got close to
pulling Phin. I think you need to kill off a few guardians quick, then pull
Phin and friends, and mes all but Phin. I wouldn't try to charm a swirlspine
(don't think you can, too high level), but if you did, as soon as that charm
breaks, that enchanter is a dead person.

Hope this helps, and let us know how it goes, and how you did what you did
(fail or succeed).
