For those interested in Shaman DOT spells... check out this site:
http://gladstone.uoregon.edu/~jwindshe/usg/

Click on "DOT Calc" over on the left side. Then type in your level and drag
the mouse over the little broken graphic on the right and it will give you a
readout of the damage at a particular level.

Which brings up a good point. All the shaman DOT spells used to vary in
damage depending on what level you are. But they gimped them so hard that
now they almost ALL 21 ticks, and do the same damange per tick that the lvl
19 spell, affliction, does exactly 135 damage whether you are lvl 19 or 40.
The way it used to work, affliction did about 400 damage at lvl 19 (I think)
and by the time you got to lvl 22, it was doing 500-600.

They all had variable initial damage, depending on level, and the number of
ticks would increase based on level too. So each time you leveled, your
DOT spell would be slightly different. I personally found that very
interesting. Since the lvl 5 sicken spell was essentially useless until
about lvl 15, where it's ratios leveled out to something you would actually
want to cast.

Now there isn't a single DOT spell that last longer than 21 ticks. Almost
all of them are more expensive to cast, they do less total damage, and they
are exactly the same every time you cast them, regardless of level.

Meanwhile, the druids and necros get fancy new buffed up spells. Don't get
me wrong, I don't want to sound like a whining little twit. I'm just
curious if any serious thought went into "The Great Shaman Gimping", and the
designers really honestly believe that it's more balanced now. And if so,
there must be some part of the equation that I'm missing.

BTW, shamans are not "better kiters" than druids. We do get SOW at lvl 9
(as opposed to 16), but we don't get any decent DOT spells until... uh... I
don't know now. It used to be lvl 19 was the magic level where you could
start kiting, but that's not the case now. I actually don't think it ever
happens now. At lvl 19, we're only doing 135 damage, at lvl 24, we're only
doing 200, and at lvl 34 comes a 500 damage spell. But I'm sure by that
time, 500 damage will seem like a drop in the bucket.

-Kzak

The thing the Shamans are miffed about is that our disease line of
spells used to do OBSCENE damage. The level 19 spell did 500 damage,
level 34 did 1600, and our level 49 did over 6000. Only thing was it
took 11+ minutes for the spell to wear off, so they were only good for
kiting. Our disease line had the duration cut by a factor of 10 and had
the damage doubled.

On the plus side though, our poison spells got a big power up.
Envenomed breath now does 25 points per tic for 21 tics. You can still
kite yellows fairly easily with just this spell, but the days of kiting
deep reds with ease are over for shamans.
