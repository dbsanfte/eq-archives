it to kill Nillipus fairly easily. All

When talking to NPC's, they give keywords in brackets. To activate
these keywords you have to ask a
question using the keyword. Usually, a simple question such as What
[keyword]? will do the trick.
However, when the keyword is a person, then it requires a Who
[keyword]? I know this sounds pretty
simple, but it took me forever to figure out. :)

There are a pair of Shiny Silk Gloves which are found on the Queen
Spider in Lesser Faydark, i forget stats
but I believe they are magical. It would take about a level 16 group to
get them.
