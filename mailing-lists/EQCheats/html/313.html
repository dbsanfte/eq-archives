Well, I am a friend of Dameat, and we took the advice of the spikes on the
floor being the spawn line and that worked VERY well last night in our quest
for the BBC. Infact, Any rumors of there being a reset point in the room is
totally wrong. The key point to getting the BBC is to keep the room clear at
ALL times. I ran in the room to make sure no spawns were hiding and it never
effected the spawn. We were there to obtain 2 Bone Bladed Claymores. Within
4 to 4 and a half hours the SK spawned 3 times. Each time he carried the BBC
with him. We had to wait for the third becuase the second spawn was stolen
under our noses. We walked out frustrated and tired, the people there can be
jerks, but with satisfaction that we obtained through battle the sword of
power and not through money. Good luck to all who try to camp him.

Adol , 16th Level Paladin of Freeport
