>Two classes really shine here since it is an Undead dungeon: Clerics and Paladins. <

Three classes shine. Necros are always good to have.. especially
against undead.



> Levels 21-24 can safely camp a room on the third floor(Lots of skel types, Carrion ghouls, specials)<

There are two main rooms on the third floor. The bar room. and the
hand room. Both have specials. and of course the balcony which just
has some nice pops.. nothing special there though


> Levels 30+ can begin to camp in the basement(Ghost, dark terrors, mortuary fungus, lots of other nasties I don't want to mess with)<

Ehh.. the basement isn't THAT bad. I've seen as low as 21 there before.
I wouldn't recommend going there before 25 though. Hags, greater dark
boned, werebats, and barbed bones frequent the basement. Then.. there
is a secret hallway that leads to the other crap.. the really bad stuff.
(the ghost, fungus, dark terrors and crap)



> FS weapons - Carrion ghouls <

Dry bones.. barbed bones.. greater dark bone (also have FS)

> Small ringmail - Carrion ghouls, barbed bone skels<

Dark bones.. ghouls also have small ringmail

> Small bronze plate - Dry bone skels

the occasional greater dark bone and barbed bone will have small bronze
