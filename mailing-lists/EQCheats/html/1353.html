You could do well in the Bartender room with a good group or even solo maybe.
Bartender spawns fairly often and drops Drake Hide Leggings. I was only
23rd lvl when I camped the bar room with 2 full groups of guildmates. There
is also a Goblin Drunkard that drops a foreman tunic I think. I just
scratched the surface of SOL A and I know there is a lot of other good stuff
there. I saw no Undead there, but there are spellcasting gobs like in
Runnyeye who may be able to see invisi.
