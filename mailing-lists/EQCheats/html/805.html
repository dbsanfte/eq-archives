For you mid to low characters out there who want to gain equipment by dueling, here is a nice trick. Have a high-level cleric friend
cast Symbol of Piznam or Symbol of Naltron before you duel your opponent. As long as you two are equaly matched equipment wise, you will
easily win the duel.
