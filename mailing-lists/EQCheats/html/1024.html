Honey Combs are used to get Honey Jum
which is needed for the Glowing Stein Quest (+10int +20 poison)

Honey Jum is made by getting 3 honey combs and giving them along with 5 gp
to a NPC in a shop next to Fools Gold tavern.

Items needed for glowing stein are:
Kiola Nut from the island of errosi
Eruds Tonic from Erudin
Koalindl Fish from Qeynos
Honey Jum from Rivervale

Twisterkahn Thunderstrike
Tarew Marr
