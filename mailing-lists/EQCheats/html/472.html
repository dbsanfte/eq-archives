In addition to saying you can go to Unrest now, I'd agree with
Lemac...Unrest is way overcrowded, and the trains are impossible compared to
other dungeons. I've never seens trains that bad. When they happen, you had
best MOVE. My shaman actually died there only once, but my other party
members and people I watched died there alot from the trains.

There are lots of places you can go at level 12, especially if your not a
KOS race/class by the good races int he world. Being a HE Bard, you can go
almost anywhere. NRo, SRo, Befallen, WK (about the upper end for WK though),
EK (would be lower end for EK), Paw, SK (lower end for SK), EC and WC. Lots
of places on the main continent would be excellent for you at 12. Beholder
would be ok in a group, take out some muddites or lower end minos. Tons of
places...come explore the main continent!
