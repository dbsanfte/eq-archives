I don't think so.... but then again it's been a while since I played my DE necro and tried it.

I was told by someone that the only reason the halflings were in Nektouls forrest was to raise
faction. I think it was more for the visiting trolls and orges that needed a little help to get
certain vendors to sell to them and such. It may be more trouble than it's worth for someone who is
KoS in Neriak. (There are guards that wander and you could run into them.. unlikely, but possible.
Especially, if some DE wanted to mess with you and trained one or two onto you.)

The easiest way to find out if faction goes down with all halflings would be to go to Nek forrest
and give a shout.... Most likely, there is someone who just killed one and can tell you. Or try
conning one of the halflings themselves.... if they like you as much as the regular halflings do
then I would guess you would not want to go to the halfling town after getting your faction up with
the evil races. But if they scowl at you then I think it would be pretty safe to take them out.
