Just a note on camping the bar room, If you stand behind the counter, the other 2 or 3 gobs that spawn in that room wont attack you... Im not sure if this is a bug or not, but stand back where the bartender spawns and you are completely safe from the other gobs in the room, as long as you dont come out from behind there.. you can even kill anything that spawns behind the bar. Of course if you aggro the other ones in the room then go behind the bar, they will follow :)
