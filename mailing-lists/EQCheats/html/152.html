There are a few other good 1h blunt items to replace that screaming mace.

You can get the cold iron morning star from permafrost. dropped by the
preacher, a rare spawn in the stage room. 9 dmg 35 delay.

or you can get the black enameled mace, dropped off ghoulish savant in lower
guk, tougher to get but its 8 dmg, 28 delay, and actually hits a lot harder
than the cold iron. friend does 30+ pts dmg with it.

or you can get the staff of writhing. 9 dmg 33 delay, plus chaotic feedback.
dropped by the noble in solusek a.

as for that mallet, i too am looking for it.
