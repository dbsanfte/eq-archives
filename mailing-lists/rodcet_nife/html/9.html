Subject: Dual "in" Rivervale


Dis story bout da time Dameat da Cleric, Adol da Paladin and me (Maglan da Rogue) wer out der travellin from one end of de Norrath to de udder. Wes had been runnin da longest time metinks so our tempers wer short and dat Dameat he likes to inscite bad tings anyhows.

Well on da road from da Permafrost to da Unrest, betwixt Beholder an Rivervale me esplains to des two fellars dat me has never been in da duel before. Well Adol, da Paladin bein a gud friend says to me dat he would like to show me da ropes an duel me at da nex stop- Rivervale.

Dat ok wit me I says. So Adol da Paladin an Maglan the Rogue wer set fer da duel in Rivervale.

Upon arrivin to da land of da furryfeet we picks out a gud spot fer dis duel. We decides dat outsid Fool's Gold is gud enuff. Not knowin how to start, Adol instructs me on wut to do. No sooner had me accepted when Adol commences to hurt me delicate Dwarven skin.

Dat's when sumtin happens dat me attributes to da fact me in gud wit da mighty Bristlebane. Whilst dat dern Adol was beatin on me, dem furryfooted deputies dat run around dat town overhears da ruckus. Well dey come over an commence to beat on da "evil" Adol. He makes it no more dan a few feet befor he succumbs to de onslaught. Har, da Maglan wins his first duel. At least by Rogue rules anyhow.

Dat da story of da Duel "in" Rivervale. Der's annuder story dat develops after dat but me only vaguly remembers dat one.
