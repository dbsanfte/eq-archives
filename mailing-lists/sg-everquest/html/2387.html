Subject: Glowing Stein Revisited


As you all know by now, alot of quest artifacts are now NODROP,
and that includes the Koalindl Fish Corpse that is so crucial to the
Glowing Stein quest.

So the sequence goes like this:

Fish corpse NODROP (plus some other items) -----> Bartender's
Compendium NODROP in Freeport.

Bartender's Compendium NODROP ------> Glowing Stein in Oggok

As you can see, the chain of items is NODROP all the way. This
makes it alot trickier, since the person who eventually goes to
Oggok (most likely an enchanter or someone with good faction with
ogres) MUST be the one to loot the fish for it's corpse.

The only safe way around this is to again use the mule character
tactic, but this time, the person actually doing the quest must be
there, ready to pounce and loot the fish corpse. The corpse will be
lootable solely by the person who gets the kill/xp, for the first 2
minutes, after which it becomes free-looting. So all you have to do
is wait for 2 minutes... This method has been confirmed through
testing by me, Sereph and Trifrost. Just watch out for other players
waiting for such an oppurtunity as well.

An alternative method, which has not been tested, and could have
more potential risk, is to group with the mule character who kills
the fish, but stay far far away.

Theoretically, because you do not personally initiate combat with
the fish, the guards/clerics/paladins should not KOS you, and you
should not get faction hits. But you should still be able to loot the
corpse since you are in the same group as the killer, so no need to
wait for 2 minutes. This method not tested yet...

That's all for now I guess...
