U know the way from zone in from the swamp to the lguk dead side rite?
After u cross the spider bridge, u go straight on to get to the dead
side. However, if u take the first Left instead of straight, u carry on,
don't drop into the water. When u come out to a room with alot of spikes
on the floor, take the first rite. Then take Left. U will reach a castle
looking wall with 2 froggies guarding it. That's the main entrance to
the ShinLord. Now, if u go deeper in, there's a room with a curtain
covering a hidden wall. The spot that the shinlord spawn will always
either spawn a Shin Knite (very common) or a Shin Lord (rare).
