WCS Hunt

YY, FF, Les, Fesse, Zan and me. We started off at the 3 bird towers, then
to the tower isle - all no luck. Then I brought all to the madman camp,
where I used to see Webclaw myself. We reached there, see a shaman and
kill it. After an hr or less, Web spawn and we kill it in water.

Then after 3 hrs wait we dun see Web anymore. Fes, Zan and YY left.
Remaining 3 stayed and finally we got 2 more WCS ;) The last one is very
tough, plus "some" pple was fooling ard and join in the fight with 50% HP
;) Almost get me killed - gate out with 20HP (we have lvl 30 wiz, 30 magi
and 27 wiz)!

Anyway, some tips to share with those who wanna camp for the staff:

1. Tank is quite useless as Webbie disappear itself the moment u MELEE or
ROOT it. So only way to hurt it is to NUKE it.

2. The real spawn spot is a underwater cave behind 3 birds tower. I try to
explore there solo b4 but not enuff air to swim in. I heard inside
sometime got deepwater goblin or gob shaman. Anyway from there Web will
swim many places in lake and finally settle down playing in the water near
the madman camp dock (SW corner of the lake).

3. Web AoE hit pretty hard. Make sure u got all the sv magic buffs. It
range from lvl 24 to 26, but is overpowered like Dvinn.

4. 1 named Madman spawn every now and then, hit pretty hard. Les say con
red to him, dunno true or not ;)

The staff got enchanter DoT spell effect - shallow breadth. 1HB, 5 dam.
Most impt, it look quite cool ;)

Good luck.
