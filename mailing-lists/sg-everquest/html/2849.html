About MM faction, it depends on whether you have killed any MM guys before.
Killing about 50 in wolf form will make your faction threateningly. Usually
when you kill a bard you gain about 1 point of faction while killing a MM
guy may lose about 5 point of faction (ratio)..... So just wolfform and con
a guy.

You kill the dino for level 49 research words,pages,runes and he will
occasionally drop diamonds, jacinths and very nice gems.... and he spawns
very fast too about 4 min if ph doesnt spawn.

For solo kiting wiz from 40 can do EK guards at the EK villages, gnome
guards or dwarf guards up to you depending on whose faction you wanna ruin.
The single spectre in ferrott is not too bad too cause they run pretty slow
giving you time to nuke, however ferrott terrain sucks making you run up and
down hills and spectres can suck your life which is irritating and increases
downtime. While pulling the spectre make sure you pull away from the spectre
cave so another spectre doesnt join you.

Its root all the way coz its costs the least mana and its a level 4 spell
which means that no matter how bad u kenna whack , at level 50 99% you can
channel it off. I would believe that the resist rate of root is lower than
immobilize and enstill since its a lower level spell. However immobilize is
used cause its 1.8 sec compared to root 2.0 sec and immobilize can last
pretty long (if you are lucky and you dont whack it[for separating spawns])

I dont know about OOT stuff but i know therse a spectre island with 4
spectres together, pulling one will pull all i think. Theres also the
seafury cyclops island where level 40 cyclops spawn.... lots of hp and this
special level 37(?) named HG. I dont think they are good xp.. Maybe in a
group you can do seafury cyclops or the HG , dont think you can do that
solo.

The preacher is a shaman or cleric and shaman mobs are wussies hehe. As long
as the stuff around preacher are green you should have no prob soloing them
in my opinion. Guarding the preacher should be level 24 (?) mobs so at about
34-36 they should be green.

Hadden is about level 28. He spawns every 8 hours 20 mins + - 15 min due to
server lag. I dont have exact loc but in qeynos hills in the NW corner of
the map there is this enlongated lake and there will be this hut with a boat
beside it. He spawns outside the hut next to the boat, on land. Many people
believe that he drops the FB earring 50% but so far I have killed him 19
times and all have been FB earring. IMO simple camp and you can trade it for
VERY nice items even though its not that hard to get it. BUT since innoruuk
is a old server, you may/will face a problem of 5 level 50s all camping him
at the same time. Careful the FB earring is lore so if you intend to farm it
give it to your secondary char :)

Most undead (except Befallen and yard trash in Unrest garden) will kill you
even if they are green/ultragreen to you. Befallen, however, I believe the
stuff there will not attack you because they belong to the
BefallenInhabitants faction.

The method I mentioned is not an exploit. Its just a method of pulling the
mob. It will still come after you and hit you (not stuck). Its easier this
way because you dont have to chiong up the house staircase and risk getting
beaten up by green mobs (mobs outside dont hit you). He is pretty decent xp
but he will HT you... spawns very often and drops both the common drop (BS
mantle) and rare (BS tunic) pretty often. Hp maybe around 1200 and is good
till 36? Decent xp while camping for the mystic cloak mob.

Well there is a kobold section, bug section, imp section and FG section
(naggy). The kobold section is the first section you will meet which
includes level 33-42 kobolds and the next section is the bug section.
Running without sow from the bug section to the zoneline is about 10-15 secs
at most so its pretty close. Bats are the lowest level around 39-41, lava
beetles 41-43, and lava duct crawlers 42-45. Bats have a 5-10 sec stun as a
proc, beetles a 100 dmg (?) fire DD proc while the LDC procs a 400+ or 600
dmg dot (blood boil).
