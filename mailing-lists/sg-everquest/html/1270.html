1. No need the books if you can find the recipies on the internet. But I
think its more fun to get the books and mastering the languages. This IS a
roleplaying game after all =)
2. You need a lexicon. Its like a container where you dump your stuff into
it and click on research. I bought mine from wiz guild in Felwithe but I
think FP mage guild sells it too. It was at 10pp when i bought it so FP
might be a little more.
3. To specialize in evocation, you need to keep casting evocation spells.
This is the IMPORTANT part, if your abjuration or some other spell schools
get to 50, The game will not warn you that yer gonna specialize in it. Once
its 51, Its considered as your specialize skill and the rest can't go above
50 already, so better keep an eye out for it especially if you wanna
specialize in evoc. btw, the specialize skill and the normal spell skill is
different skills alttogether. They will say something like " You are better
at specialize:Evocation (78)!" instead of just evocation.
Hope that helps =)
