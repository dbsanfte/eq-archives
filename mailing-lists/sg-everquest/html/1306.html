In my opinion, Kelethin is the best city to train pottery. Even better
than FP... in fact, FP is the worst for that particular skill.

The vendors are all there in Kelethin, and they are "open air"
vendors, so they can't run around and take the day off.

The vendor that sells the sketches is on the orc lift platform. The
vendor that sells the clay, glass shards and firing sheets is outside
the Emerald warriors.

The pottery wheel is a bit far off though, and usually is camped by
quite a number of people. From the first lift platform (the one that
has the ramp leading up to the smithing forge), take the south
bridge to a platform with an armour/leather merchant. From there,
go up 2 ramps. The wheel/kiln is next to the merchant that makes
Banded Orc Vests from 2 legionaire shoulder pads, a crushbone
belt and 10 gold.

Good luck, would-be potters. I think I'll try cooking next. *heh*
