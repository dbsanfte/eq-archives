Anyone train in sense direction? Just a tip to share. I set hotkey 4, 5, 6
to sense direction and link it to my keypad for movement (ie front, left,
right). Like that everytime I move, I use the skill. Now the skill reach
my lvl ceiling. When I rise lvl, it's easier and faster for me to increase
it further till the new lvl ceiling.

For those spell casters, the magery skill ceiling is 5+5/lvl. If u are lvl
7, ya ceiling for all magery skills is 5+(7x5)=40. Once u rise a lvl, stay
in town and keep casting & mediating until you reach lvl ceiling b4 u
venture out. (Unlike UO casting in EQ doesnt require reagents, so keep
casting and mediating) This will help u, coz at higher lvl, magery skills
rise faster. It's very time consuming to max all magery kills to lvl
ceiling everytime u gain a lvl, but at least do it for mediation,
chanelling and ya main magery skills. For eg, I'm a wiz, so I always max
out my evoke (offense) and adjure (defence) skills, not forgetting
mediation and chanelling.

Lastly, dun forget abt ya melee skills. Thou ya melee sux, but u will sux
further if u dun train at all. Ask the tankers in ya group to start the
attack, when monsters fight them, u move close and hit with ya melee weap
and at the same time keep casting ;)
