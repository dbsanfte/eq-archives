> Here's what I did to complete the quest.
>
> First, I raised faction with one person in Rathe Mountains to start the
>quest going. In this case, I chose the highelf lady who would give the
>Breastplate quest. She belongs to Clerics of Tunare faction so to raise
>faction, go to West Freeport the second floor of an inn (the only inn i
>think, near monks guild), you will find a dwarf. Go buy a lot of muffins
>from the bakery in West Freeport and start feeding the dwarf. Put the
>muffins in 4s. So it would be 4, 4, 4, 4. Each 4 is 1 slot. Then click give
>and you will receive 16 faction increases. Keep doing this until you feed
>about 200-300 (Estimate). Then the highelf cleric in rathe mountains will
>give you a quest to return to her 2 dark cauldrons. This brings you to the
>second part of the quest.
>
> Head to Mistmoore, go invisible, best to do with a level 50 wizard, go
>past the graveyard, down the tunnel, up the hill, through the castle gates,
>past the gargs, turn left and walk to the end, turn right at the leftmost
>door, walk past some mobs to the next door, turn left, turn right, go
>through the first door. You can get a map of Mistmoore in this case...
>Inside, you will find the Mistmoore cooks (ancille cooks). 2 of them and 1
>random creature. All of them will fall under 1 Ice comet but they all can
>HT, my level 50 paladin friend had to use LOH in this case to break the
>spawns. After killing them, spend about 4-6 hrs camping to get 4 dark
>cauldrons. (2 for my paladin friend, 2 for my paladin). Return the 2 dark
>cauldrons and receive your breastplate mold.
>
> Now, go to Sro, bring about 150pp along, head to the Northwest corner
of
>Sro, you will find a camp with a orc and a erudite. Give 3pp to the erudite
>to receive a faction increase with temple of solusek ro. Keep giving money
>until the erudite cons amiable to you.
>
> After that, head to Freeport and start training smithing skill. I used
>my wizard equipment to boost my dwarf int to 127 and took 9 continuous
hours
>to train blacksmithing to 101. To learn how to smith, go to
>http://www.graffe.com . Theres a link there which links to some KC
>Blacksmithing guide.
>
> About 50% of the quest is completed.
>
> We have to make bar of ronium now.
>
> We need 5 things to make bar of ronium
>
> 1. 2 enchanted platinum bars. Get this from a 34+ enchanter friend,
>costs 105ppX2 = 210pp
> 2. Sand of Ro. Get a SG killing group. Kill about 5 SGs get 1 Sand of
>Ro.
> 3. Soil of Underfoot. Head to LesserFay western most fairy camp. Start
>chopping at faeries and get 4 fairy dust. Get 200 bone chips and start
>giving them to the dwarvern paladin in Kaladim 4 at a time to raise faction
>to kindly. Give 4 fairy dust to the Cleric Priestess in Kaladim to receive
>Soil of Underfoot
> 4. Melatite. Go to SolA and start chopping at CWG. Clockwork gnomes.
>Those that are modelled CWG XC, MC or MB. About 1 in 10 will posesss
>Melatite.
> 5. Mistmoore Granite. While I was at Mistmoore just now, we camped 2
hrs
>for 2 Mistmoore Granite. There are 2 Gargs spawned inside the castle. Just
>as you enter. Note this gargs must be level 30+ (flouting, etc.) not those
>outside (sneering, jeering, grinning).
>
> Get a druid to teleport you back to Lavastorm, walk to Temple of
Solusek
>Ro. Hail Lord Searfire, ask him what is ronium and follow his cues,
>eventually you will get a cauldron. Now boost your wisdom to however high
>you can. Mine was 162. Put the 5 things into the 6 slot cauldron. Note:
>Seperate the 2 enchanted platinum bars into 1,1. Pray, click combine and
get
>bar of Ronium. Get the druid to teleport you to butcherblock, run to
>kaladim, find the forge near the warriors guild, put the mold and the bar
of
>ronium inside. Pray, click combine and get Breastplate of Ro.
>
> Total Time Taken : 27 Hrs
>
> Stats : AC 18 +4 STR +10 SV Fire
>
