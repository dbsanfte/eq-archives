I'm a circle higher since I like to have a full spellbook ) I don't really
know about magicians since I never played one, but I think a DD spell and a
Water E summoning spell at each circle would be best if yer trying to
economise since u never know when a person is gonna sell a much needed armor
piece or weapon. Of course, maybe there are other spells a magician need,

hours since lotsa mobs there.. Of course trade skills like blacksmithy
surely gonna be damn profitable in long run since can make a few hundred pp
in an hour, but thats gonna be a long time before you can start attempting
on it (heard cost price very high).
