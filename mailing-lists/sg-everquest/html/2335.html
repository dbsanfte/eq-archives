The house in the graveyard is supposed to be empty.. or the last time i
checked it was. Theres a few traps inside the house so not so advisible to
walk in.

The graveyard is good for high 20s and very low 30s with more familiars
there.

Sometimes an imp familiar will spawn on the left side of the graveyard.
Level 35 and drops the sacrificial dagger (dmg 5 delay 23 +5 int) not sure
about delay. This dagger cannot go into range slot.

If you are mid to high 30s you should proceed down the tunnel and fight. If
you are good, you can reach maid issa who drops the nightshade wreathe. (ac2
[?] +5 wis +5 int). She is level 38.

Generally mistmoore is good for low 20s to high 20s. Then as you proceed
further, the more buggy the zone gets. Like spellcasters will cast through
the walls then you die etc..

Loot wise, nothing extraordinary.. just some cash and maybe fs weapons.

After all, I cant wait to see Mayong Mistmoore. He is supposed to look real
cool.
