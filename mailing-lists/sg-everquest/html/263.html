Freeport is in my opinion the best place to run xp for levels 1-10. Firstly,
its the human city, so being the most frequently used race(Look at the
amount of classes they can play), the hunting grounds is the most varied.
East commons have stuff for level 1-10 and being such a linear area, you can
run away from stuff quite easily and zone out. As for befallen, try not to
go there at lower levels. Even if you are cautious, someone might train up
ghouls and such and they can root ya. Being level 6, their 30dam hit would
be very fatal indeed ) As for loot, commonlands are also one of the best
place for loot ) Fighting lioness and lions, you can get the occasional high
quality cat pelt or the ruined lion pet which can fetch for a few gold
pieces. The asps and darkweed snakes have giant snake fangs which go for up
to 1pp to human players and younger kodiacs sometime gives grizzly pelt
which sells for 4-5 gp to NPCs. Once you get about level 11-12, you can hunt
wisps. They give lightstones and greater lightstones which go for 1pp and
5pp respectively to other players and a bit less for NPC. Well if you need
any other information, feel free to send me a tell.

Leshrec
