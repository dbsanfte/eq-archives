My specialty is going to ulu places, sure got lots of places to hunt (and die)

15+ can go try Permafrost. Very good loot, but got a lot of ice giant running
around and not to mention Vox.......

Another good place is Kerra Isle, but the loot there like shit. Full group of
around level 10 can hunt the high guards while 15+ can hunt necros

Another place for the whole gang to hunt is at the Ocean of Tears. The Aqua
Goblin island has mobs ranging from level 14-22

The 10-15 group can visit Dagnor's Cauldron while the 17++ one can go Unrest
there. This 2 are not so ulu and can be quite full.

Rathe Mountains also not bad for exp, can handle people from level 1 to 50. But
there's 2 fucked up sphinx there + hill giants, cyclops and griffons flying
around. Very risky.

Beholder maze has good loot, but the XP is not good enough IMO.

About Split Paw, the xp there quite good, and can get RTS from the NPC Necro in
there + Snakeskin armor and all sort of useful things. Train prone.

Najena, a tad dangerous. 15-20 can hunt around the first few rooms, going
deeper is hard unless you can find a cleric or necro due to the necros and
their pets past this point. Good loot, good XP, but very train prone.
