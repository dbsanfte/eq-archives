I'm still bind to PF, but teleport to Felwithe for supplies/unloading, and
teleport to NK when hunting birdman.
I can go PF anytime but we won't go too far unless we have 2 real tanks
(not semi-tanks) in the group. The place give very good xp for group of
lvl 20-24, up to the metal gate area (which is the begining area for PF
only). Loots can be quite good if we go beyond flag room, coz the bronze
legging I got is from flag room. Above lvl 25 we can go in further and the
inner part are good for lvl 30s. I'm not familiar with the region outside
PF. Throw me in the snowland I sure get lost. Luckily wiz got teleport
spells which save my traveling ;)

I'm interested to join the PF team (after National Day?). Now trying very
hard to get to lvl 24 at birdmen region. Comparing 1 night soloing in PF
and one night grouping with FF and a tank at birdmen region, birdmen give
me faster xp.

Btw, Lake's gnoll camp at zone entance give RTS and JKS, no need to go Paw
liao. The place is quite safe since it's near zone. On the other hand,
there is at least 7-8 gnoll guards, gnoll shamans and normal gnolls there.
If you attack one, all will rush to u. Only exception is when u r lvl 18
and above, where the other gnolls will not join in the fight. But gnoll
shamans will still heal their race even if they dun join in the fight.

Ziltus.

p/s: Last night I found a place FULL of fish! It's at a spot along the
coast in one of the islands in Lake. It's a deserted island with a empty
tower and there is a bow cannon in the tower. U can just stand at the
spot, turn on combat mode and start targeting at teh fish - one hit kill 1
fish which give u at least 1-2 scale to a fish. At the right side of the
island is a wooden plank where a birdman guard spawn one at a time. Very
good secret training spot for solo tank or semi-tank. Err loc? Sorry, I
lost my way and end up on that island due to heavy fog, I also dunno the
way back to tat island, hehe.
