Well, I have a level 5 Human Paladin called Liath in Prexus, now hanging around
Qeynos Hill. When you manage get to BB, send a /tell to see if I'm online, I'll
wait for you in Freeport.

The easiest way to get to Butcherblock Mountain is to find a level 4 Gnomish
Enchanter to cast invisible on you and then run like hell to Steamfont
Mountain, into Lesser Faydark and finally into Greater Faydark. At Greater
Faydark, find a High Elven Enchanter to cast invisibility on you (in case it
wears off, or to be a bit kiasee) and quickly dash towards Butcherblock
Mountain.

Invisible usually last a good 15 minutes unless the enchanter has a damn lousy
Divination rating.

I really missed playing an enchanter...... especially minor illusion and the
lull line of spells.
