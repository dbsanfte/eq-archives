***A NEW CONTINENT IS DISCOVERED!!!***

Intrepid explorers have returned from the edge of the EverQuest world
bearing tidings that will forever alter the history of Norrath. A new
continent, Kunark, has been found south of Faydwer beyond the Timorous
Deep. It is rumored that ships will soon set sail from Antonica and Faydwer
bound for this new world.

You will be able to explore this new land in March of 2000, when the The
Ruins of Kunark Expansion Pack becomes available. The Ruins of Kunark
Expansion Pack will add startling new features to EverQuest, including more
than 20 new zones - lakes, jungles, crypts, dungeons, ancient towers and
underground castles.

Kunark will contain breathtaking graphics and more object animations such
as earthquakes and other violent natural phenomena. There will be a new
playable race -- the Iksar (Lizardmen who can play as Monks). And you'll
meet visually stunning new creatures such as grey dragons, giant scorpions
and man-eating plants.

With the Expansion Pack, you'll even be able to go beyond level 50!

For detailed information and tantalizing screenshots, visit the new Ruins
of Kunark Web site @ http://www.everquest.com/kunark.
