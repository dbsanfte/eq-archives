1st floor not tat train-proned, but the warrior and radier room yes. If u
can go there early with a kakis (eg YY, DF or Ser/Fesse) and book the
outlook room, u shld be pretty safe (since other mobs wont go into the
outlook room unless u stick out ya head).

The xp is very good. I think with 3 person in the room, u shld be able to
get 1 bubb/1.5hr, 2 person 1 bubb/hr, and 1 person 1 bubb/30min. Basically
at below 29 (b4 u get ya lvl 29 pet), it's best to stick in the room with 1
more kakis and ya earth pet.

Below 29, NEVER ever try to take a clawpick guard SOLO, even if it con blue
to u. Stick in the outlook room and u shld be able to lvl fast. Once at lvl
29 with 1 or 2 bubb (provision for death), u can start venturing to the
war/rad room, with at least 2 tank and a healer off coz ;)

Just to let u know the danger of war/rad room train. Last night FF and I
was training 1st floor, we booked the guard corner. Later when go back
from bank, someone train from war/rad room - 2 war, 2 rad, 1 outlook? FF
die, I think, within 5 seconds coz he is the 1st to kena train.

One good thing abt HH keep, is tat the gem vendor, food vendor, bank all
located within same zone and very close to the dungeon. Very convenient.

If u lost, go print a map of HH pass and HH keep. Bind yaself the moment u
enter HH keep, so tat any train u can gate out fast and zone immediately.
No need to bind outside zone, coz in order for clawpick to chase u all the
way to zone, they need to go thru many guards. In fact, after u gate, u
can med near zone and if mob finally come to u, then zone lor.
