hehe dun call me senior wizard lah, my elf looks younger than yours =P. As
for binding, it is actually target based but can only be used in group. This
is to prevent abuse of it, like u walk anywhere, and u feel like it u just
bind someone. So no need to ungroup to bind yourself, just target yourself
and bind. To bind group members, u have to target individually and bind, no
shortcut. And yes u can bind yourself anywhere u want, but not group
members. As for the shiny brass shield, you gotta camp there for at least
2-3 days to get one unless u damn lucky. I camp there and killed 10 trainers
and i only got some raw hide armor. Of course wizard there no problem cos
its so easy to steal kills )
Woven armor is best Mid level armor for wiz. Later there is gossimer and
damask armor which looks real cool, but woven will do for now. Its same AC
as Patchwork but MUCH lighter. As for the area which u get, must be at least
level 16+. If u wanna hunt in the swamp, have to be careful of troll guards
and named troll NPCs. Of course u can hunt in guk, but beware of trains
there. I went there today and got killed twice before i got a mesh leggings
and some other netted and woven stuff.
btw, crook seldom loads on LJ, maybe as rare as trainer i dunno, but trainer
can be done at level 12+, maybe con blue i'm not sure. He hits for about 20
but usually theres someone tank so u just shoot at it.
Rabies is treated as a disease so a cure disease should do it, and it lasts
quite long. Can't remember how long 45 mins real time i think.
Btw, anyone interested to hunt in Najena, the dungeon in Lavestorm? We
should get together one day and go there. Xp quite good, and unlike the
other dungeons, its quite bright and airy ) Of course an expedition to guk
would be cool too.
