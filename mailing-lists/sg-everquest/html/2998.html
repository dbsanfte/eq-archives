Hi all,

After knowing there are over hundred of local players in both EQ and UO,
it's kinda sian to know that we are all split over different shards and
servers. Same thing happening in D2 USwest, USeast and Asia servers. Thus
I was thinking, if most, if not all, of the local players all playing in
the same server, even under different guilds/clans (like tDw, TAA and
Celestials in UO Baja shard), it'll be good to have so many of us playing
together. We can conduct large-scale events, organize RL gatherings, form
alliance, talk-cock-sing-song using Singlish and many other happenings.
Most important of all, our local players can take care of one another,
share tips and knowledge etc. I still missed the days when UO Baja server
down at 3-4am and our gang of UO kakis went Geylang to makan frog leg
porride, carrot cake etc and go back home at 5am and continue UOing when
the server is up ;)

Anyway, in order to promote SG online RPGing, I've setup a mailing list
called SG-Online-RPGers at eGroup. To subscribe, simply send a mail to:
SG-Online-RPGers-subscribe@egroups.com

I hope this mailing list will help to promote online RPG gaming in
Singapore. Eg, whenever a new player cannot decide which servers to join,
he can find the answer here; or if anyone is aware of any new online RPG,
pls update everyone on the list, even for beta testing.

That's all for the time being, thx folks ;P
