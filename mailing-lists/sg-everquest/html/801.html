Flowing black robe is ac8,+5 stamina, +3 intellegience. Classes are Wizard, necro, Magician and Enchanter. Found On Najena herself in Najena.
Robe of the oracle is ac9, +5 wisdom +5 intellegience, +30 mana and classes are Same as FBR but +monk too. Found in OOT and Lower Guk.
Staff of observers is Damage 12, Delay 35 weight 10, classes are cleric, druid, enchanter, magician, shaman and wizard. +5 int +5 wis, mana +30, 30(I think) charges of sentinel spell. Quest item from killing a Named Evil Eye. Best 2h staff IMHO
Staff Of writhing is damage 9, delay 33 and classes are necro, enchanter, wizards, magicians and bards. Has a spell of chaotic feedback which does 48 damage.Found in Solusek on a kobold.
