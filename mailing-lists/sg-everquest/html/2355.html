> 3. The deceiver mask (cant remember exact name, turn u to DE). Dropped by

Guise of the deceiver. Go to the dead side in lguk. Swim to bedroom, take
the leftmost path and hug the right wall until you see the 2 froglok ghoul
walk straight and turn left at the end. You will see a ladder up. Up the
ladder is the assassin/supplier room. There will be 3 spawn spots. Middle
spot is supplier spot. Assassin is right spot. All the while you will need
invs to undead to make it there.

*WARNING* Uniques can see through invis, just like the ghoul assassin, so
get ready to rush up the ladder and start beating the froglok ghouls.
Usually there will be 2 or 3 low lvl 30 froglok ghouls. The assassin is
about 35 and he can backstab for 80 X 2 and drops guise of the deceiver (brd
rog wearable +12 cha (?) +7 sv magic ac4) as common and serrated bone dirk,
rogues best weapon as rare. The supplier is also about 35 and drops some bag
(8 slots and reduces 75% (?) weight) as common and the thick banded belt (+1
5 str all/all weight 1.0(?)) as rare.

> 6. Fesse pls advise me the reasonable price for the following jewelry:
> - 7int/7str necklace
> - 4int earring (a pair)

Platinum sapphire necklace. Cost price is 105pp + 100pp = 205pp. Market
price can range anywhere from 600pp to 3000pp. 1200pp is a reasonable price
from the jeweler to ask from you.

Golden sapphire earring. Cost price is 10.5pp + 100pp = 110.5pp. Ranges from
300pp to 1400pp. 600pp is a good price. 1000pp for 2 earrings.

For the buyer, its always the cheaper the better. The best is to be a
jeweler yourself. Like me and just make yourself those items and forget
about the hassle of bargaining.

Dex + Skill in 1H blunt affects the chance of chaotic feedback from
appearing. I used a cracked staff since level 1 to level 29 and I use it
whenever im in a group and my skill in 1h blunt is now 100. Doesnt seem to
rise anymore. Anyway, for a wizard, its totally crap to use Staff of the
observers. Its a 2h blunt weapon (heavy like hell) that only gives +5 int.
You would be better off in a staff of the wheel (1h blunt and 1.0 weight) +
glowing stein combo = 20int and adds other stats too.

Save all your skill points from level 1. Put 1 into those that need to be
started off and dont waste it on any others and just let time improve them.
Because at higher levels, these points will come in very handy. Max out
research to 175 and Spec: evoc to 200 which will need quite a lot of skill
points. Then at the end of the day (level 50), you will have plenty of
points to mold your character as you like. (ie. maybe you would like to max
