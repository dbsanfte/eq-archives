I feel tat every servers will ultimately grow "mature", meaning more
twinked chars will show up as the time goes by.
The joy of playing online RPG like UO and EQ comes from the different
stages of char development in the game (newbie, intermediate, experienced,
veteran, god-like). No doubts it's easier to train a char with somebody
twinking you, but you will lost the fun, the good, the bad, the sad, the
fear, the frustration, the joy etc...all the experiences u are supposed to
encounter when playing this game.

For those twinked chars you come across, most of them has a high-lvl char
behind supporting. This means that those players also spend their time
going thru' the newbie stage. If everything comes so easily, u will soon
lost interest in the game.

Personally I think quite number of those exisitng kakis can easily spare
10-20pp for a newbie kakis buying a descent set of newbie
weap/armor/stuffs. I think with this 'twinking', it's already good enuff
to start off training ya char. On the other hand, sometime it's best for
new-comers to spend a week or so, playing a brand new char w/o any
twinking, to get the feel of the game and decide if it is the class/race
that you are going for. If every new comers come into server and suga suga
create new char, we help to twink abit, and delete char to re-create
another one and expect twinking again, then it's not tat fair to those
helping. So it's always a 2-sided coins ;)

Just like playing RTS game. If u hack a saved game and play the game by
building only the more powerful units to rush ya opponents, u will never
learn the usefulness of other units. For most of the solo campaign game,
normally there is 1 or 2 missions where the most powerful unit (which is
normally slowest) is no longer a useful element in completing the mission.
At this stage, only those players who really play from scatch will be able
to tackle the problems and complete the mission safely.

Some players reached lvl 50 first, some reach lvl 50 later. Ultimately we
will all be lvl 50s. Just ask yaself, what kinda lvl 50 u wanna be? ;)

Karan, si-bek jiat-lat, hahahah ;P
