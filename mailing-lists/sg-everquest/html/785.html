I think piercing is better for necros than 2hblunt. Several reasons for
this..
1. Certain necro spells like Vampiric embrace require you to hit your
opponent for it to take effect. Since piercing has lesser delay than blunt,
it should mean more chances of hiting.
2. The best 2hblunt, which i think is the staff of observers is NOT
wieldable by necros, only other caster types. I read this from a EQ
equipment guide, but it may be wrong, so please check it out first.
3. If possible, max out your piercing skills during the first few levels,
its much easier since from level 1-4, the pet is DAMN useless, a little more
powerful than a decaying skeleton. From level 4 onwards, the pet is a little
better, but once you get to level 6, either you must group with a warrior or
wizard, since your pet can only take 2-3 blows from the white and sometimes
blue stuff....
This is from my experience from playing a necro to level 8 so after that yer
on your own ) Good luck.
