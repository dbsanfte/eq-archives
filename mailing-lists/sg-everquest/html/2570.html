Get your merc right up next to the wall... then click and drag up...
all the way. Only flat roofs can be climbed up onto. It's just like
changing stance... for example, when changing stance from prone
to standing, you click on the merc and drag up, you will see 2 up
arrows next to him, right? Drag somemore, and you will see
another kind of "ladder" arrow if you are next to a roof.
