After the servers were patched 2 to 3 weeks ago, Najena spawned very rarely.
Used to be almost 2 elementals then 1 Najena (100% has FBR). Now I heard
from my friends Najena only spawns once a day. Just a warning. That is why
FBR prices go up.

Drelzna room is easy to hold. Just seperate the middle spawn from the other
2 spawns about a few minutes. I camped the room at level 29 with a level 27
necro and we did it nicely. Get the necro to summon a pet in the centre of
the room and Drelzna will HT the pet and nothing will happen to you. I was
pretty lucky with Jboots, reach there first spawn already got my pair then
another group took over coz the necro had to sleep then 24 hours later the
group decided to give up then me and necro friend camp for another hour and
got his pair.

And for research for wizards, I dont put a single practice point into
research since all our research spells are useless until level 39
(immobilize) so i just dump all my runes to train up skill regardless
whether i get my suicide spell (AoE) or not. And an interesting bug (?) to
note is that if you are level 39 and havent put the first point into
research yet, you can immediately research level 39 spells once you put a
point in, and dont have to go through level 16,20,24,29,34 spells...

And brownies happen to KOS everyone except unknown races (treeform,
wolfform, illusions etc...). Killing them will only lose fraction with
brownies which KOS everyone including wood elfs. Tough bunch of level 40
druids.

For those trying to get Crude Steins (+10 sta +15 cha effect: minor healing)
from Ogre Bouncers (lvl 37) , they are very tough. At level 37 Vs the level
37 ogre bouncer, the ogre bouncer resisted more than 20 roots and a lot of
spells which did the least partial dmg. Get druids, shamans, necros if you
want to fight them and dot them to death.

Also, I just met NoLife in Prexus, Singaporean too Level 35 Shaman. Anybody
knows if he is on this list?
