I got the following off the EQ Casters website... interesting thing
about DEX, INT and WIS with regards to fizzles and interrupts...

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
I spoke with GZ and got some intersting information:

There is currently no plans for a 49 Wizard Fire Based DD Spell.
There is no new information on if or when 44 and 49 Magican
Summon Item spells will be implemented.

Some clarification on some statistic issues though:

The chance of specialisation kicking in for Wisdom based casters
is effected by Wisdom.

The chance of specialisation kicking in for Intelligence based
casters is effected by intelligence.

Dexterity does not effect chances of recovering and successfully
casting. It does effect however how often a special procs if you
have one on your weapon.

The only skill which effects recovery for casting when interrupted is
Channelling. No other skill or statistic effects this.

If specialisation successfully rolls it will effect your chance of
fizzling and the amount of mana used.

Higher Intelligence will reduce fizzles for Intelligence based casters.

Higher Wisdom will reduce fizzles for Wisdom Based casters.

Good alternatives for pure mages then are Agility to avoid being hit
in the first place or Stamina for extra hitpoints.
