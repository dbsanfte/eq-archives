Attached is a pretty useful article for grouping. I got it from UOSS,
re-formatted it into MS word for you guys to print out for reading and
reference.

The main points are:
- Form an XP efficient group (dun get a full house hunting 1 single blue
mob)
- Move as a group, dun leave the slow ones behind (Bard pls take note of
this point, dun leave those with slow system and run by yaself, hehe. In
the end all get killed or lost all over the place).
- Get prepared and rdy for the hunt (For God sake dun come to hunt and
start begging for foods/drinks, there are many npc merchants that sell this
at 1 sp plus. I get at least 40 of each when time I buy my supplies. FYI
when teaming with FF so far, I never ask a single food and drink from him,
thou he can summon alot. Those players who have the habit of begging
food/drink every where they go, I will ask them to fly kite, hehe).
- PLAN, most impt, PLAN. Tanks dun act cowboy and charge b4 casters are
rdy. Magi keep ya pet at ya side or dismiss it when hunt at heavily
trained places. Casters dun scare of OOM and use ya mana at the right
time. Make up an escape plan b4 hunting. Set a sentry when casters
mediating. Stated a clear looting order b4 members start to fight for
loot. Etc etc.
- Always compromise and be patient.
