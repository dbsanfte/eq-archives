Creating Guilds
If you decide that none of the existing guilds are quite what you
are looking for, you may found your own guild. In order to establish your
guild you
must have an acceptable name, charter, and a membership of 10 players who
wish to form this guild.

The following process needs to be followed to gain approval for you
guild:The
leader of the guild must email eqguilds@.... The subject of this
email
must contain the NAME of the guild. In the body of this email the leader
must
include his station name, his chat handle, the name of the character that
will be the
leader of the guild, the name of the primary server of the guild, and a
list of the
other 9 players who have agreed to establish this guild.

In addition to the above information, the guild leader must include a
charter for
their guild. This charter should detail their guild's goals and role in
Norrath.The 9
founding members of the guild must email eqguilds@.... The subject
of
this email must contain the NAME of the guild. In the body of this email
the
founding members must include their station name, their chat handle, and
their
character name. Please do not have more than 9 people send in the
information.

Once all of the information has been received, it will be reviewed by the
staff at
Verant Interactive. If any reservations about either the name or the
charter arise,
the guild leader will be notified via email and be offered an opportunity
to modify
either as required.Once the name and charter have been approved, the guild
leader will be notified via email that the guild is ready to be
created.Once the guild
leader has received notification that the guild has been approved, it is
his
responsibility to meet with the designated guild creation GM on his server
during
the designated time.
