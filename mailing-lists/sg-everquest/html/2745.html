There are basically 3 sides to SolA : Gnome, Goblin Castle and Goblins

In the Gnome side, they will not KOS you unless you are evil race/class or have lost a lot of faction with them (solusek mining co.)
Gnomes will KOS blazing elementals and Singe and Kindle if they attack you within the range of a gnome

Captain Bipnupple (sp?)
common: brick of blue ore weight 22.5 (useless item)
rare : gnomish environmental suit (ac11[?] gives 9 to magic/fire/cold resists [?])

Gabbie Mardoddle
common: Mithril Quill 4/19 piercing
rare: molten cloak ac4 4int 4wis

Lava Elemental
common: lava potion (inferno shield)
rare: runed lava pendant (ac5 4str 5sv fire [?])

Clockwork EXG (huge female gnome clockwork) (you can see its drop whether its a shield or a bp)
common: charred guardian shield (ac10 9wis)
rare: charred guardian breastplate (ac16 3str 6sta)

Now the castle is where you zone from lavastorm where you have to pass through some metal grates

Inferno Goblin Bartender
common: drakescale leggings (ac5 5cha 5dex)
rare: drakescale sleeves (ac4 15hp 3sta [?])

Goblin Drunkard
common: frothy tunic (regen effect potion)
rare: fire crystal staff (5/28 shock of fire effect)

Goblin High Shaman
common: glowing stone band (ac2 infravision effect)
rare: platinum dragon totem (35 hp 50 mana melee slot item)

Solusek Goblin King
common: ring of the goblin lords (ac4 3str 3int)
rare: sceptre of flame (5/27 1hblunt 5sv fire 5sv magic 15 mana [?[)

Inferno Goblin Torturer
common: turquoise eyepatch (ac2 2int 2cha 2sta face item)
rare: momento box (6 slot box small items only 100% weight reduction)

Now the rest of the goblins in the zone carry:

Flame goblin foreman
common: foremans tunic (ac10 3str 3wis)
rare: obsidian scimitar (7/27 +6 agility 1h slash)

Inferno Goblin Captain
common: charred boots (ac6 5agi 5sv cold [?])
rare: charred gauntlets (ac7 3dex 5int)

Special mobs in the zone include:

Reckless Efreeti
common: platinum armband (3str 3wis)
rare: obsidian flameberge (20/50 2h slash effect: obsidian shatter 30dmg fire DD + AC debuff NOdrop)

Kindle
common: obsidian ring (ac3 3dex [?])
rare: impskin gloves (ac2 5int 10 mana)

Single
common: obsidian bead hoop (ac2 3agi 3int)
rare: drakescale belt (ac5 3dex 10sv fire)

Fire goblins have fire goblin skin needed for Robe of the elements quest (MAG)
Clockwork Models MC, MB, XC, XB have melatite which is needed for RNG, PAL, SK quests
Solusek Mage and Solusek Champions have firewalker boots and fiery vambraces respectively needed for lambent quest.

Marfen something is a gnome merchant that sells the tinkers bag 10 slot 100% weight reduction bag for 5000-7000pp . The bag is NOT lore.



I hope I didnt miss out anything as these are all written from mind =) therefore some stats may be slightly wrong.
