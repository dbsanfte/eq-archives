Not sure which UO shard you played but The Dark Wardens (tDw) is always the
#2-#4 biggest veteran guild in Baja (check OSI Baja guild list). Together
with TTW and DWN, we are always fighting for the #2 biggest. #1 in Baja is
Asia, which is made up of several HK/TW guilds. tDw is one pure guild that
started off as local guild (formed within 2 weeks after UO launched in
S'pore) and slowly expanded to the the biggest single international guild
(includes members from US, HK, Japan, S. Korea, Taiwan, Austrialia, UK,
etc). TWW, I think, is also one guild by itself while DWN is form by
several groups of players to make up the player-run town called Kingdom of
Dawn.

We are very well established in term of skills and resources (we have 20
over guild buildings, including keep, tower, 2-Ss, big brick etc, at one
time, but reduced after the house patch is in as we do not have so many
mules to hold the buildings). Other than GM mage/tank mages in the guild,
we also have several GM smiths, GM bowyers, GM alchemists, GM scribes, GM
bards (provokers), GM carpenters/tinkers etc. We even have GM poisoner
(assasin) and GM detective, hehe. As as today, I still have a castle deed,
a keep deed and a tower deed reserved for future guild village in T2A, not
to include other 2-S, big brick, big smithy house deeds ;)

We also own one of the most popular shops in Baja, The Asmandi (a big brick
house within west britain guard zone), which have more than 3000 visitors
hits, selling only GM suberb stuffs at very reasonable price. In the shop
we have standard price list which all vendors owners must comply (imagine
600gp for full AR30 plate set, all reagents at 1gp above npc shop etc) to
ensure the best quality stuffs sold as the most reasonable prices. The
shop is also well-known for it Charity Chest (a shop wreck metal chest
locked-down in the shop), where players can leave their unwanted suffs so
that newbies can find something useful from it. Pls do not think that the
chest is a trash bin, as newbies always find goodies inside, including
magical weapons ;) I myself can throw 20-30 magical stuffs inside in one
shot. The shop also provide free usage of training/thief dummies, forge,
anvil etc open to all players.

If you play around Baja server down time, often you will see a horde of
black horsemen in Cov entrance, Britain X-road or Britain cemetry, esp Cov,
where we rules the place most of the time. Our guild page is also the
hosted in Singularity Game site.

Just wanna introduce tDw to more locals players ;)
