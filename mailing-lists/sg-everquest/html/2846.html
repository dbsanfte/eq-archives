Heh theres no mob in Mistmoore called Mayong Mistmoore. Though mistmoore
stuff belong to Mayong Mistmoore faction, believed to be a level 53 vampire.
But in actual fact, Mistmoore is a very easy dungeon to solo at level 50...
about 95% of the mobs in the castle are level 35 or below. Only problem is
that you need to have wolf form and havent ruined mistmoore faction before
so you dont agro everything around you.

Alliwaezeur (sp?) is always level 50 but has much less hp than an average
level 50 mob... necro and maybe shamans are the only classes able to solo a
level 50 mob in 1 round without going oom

For gator armor in Uguk, i recommend at least 39.... new nuke and you should
be able to solo a level 30 ancient croc with root nuke

For runed circlet, 32+? is good enough when you get your fire nuke and able
to solo pull the mobs (ie. all green)

For fishbone earring, you almost must have jboots/sow or Rephas, an
enchanter beside Hadden is green to you so he wouldn't jump into the
fight... About high 30s for Rephas to be green to you i think...

Wk ogre shaman you need about level 48+ to solo. 1) The 4 ogre guards will
agro everyone within their HUGE agro radius (as huge as the furthest point
you can nuke them from) 2) If the ogre guards are green only 1 will come
after you and give you a nasty HT 3) Magic resist gear is very important
since you are taking a lot of HTs 4) If the shamans and chief goonda is
still blue to you they will jump in as well 5) Assuming you are level 48+
where everything is green to you, you pull 1 ogre guard, kill, 1 ogre guard,
then try to pull either goonda or the shaman directly beside him. 6) Ultra
tough camp if you are soloing.. no clarity and no heal and hard to keep up
with spawns 7) Very long spawn time...

OOT oracle and his body guard. oracle is 35 guard is 42. lose neriak
merchants faction i think when you kill them. I recommend only trying them
after getting ice comet as oracle is a nuker. Elemental shield and
diamondskin up. Root the guard. And kite the oracle using root nuke or run
nuke. After oracle is dead immediately gate out or if you feel confident you
can kill the guard who drops standard level 40+ mob jewelry (mithril amulet
92pp to merchants) If root breaks then you have to be skilful to try to root
the guard without taking too much damage from oracleagain.....

OOT dunnoe what ogre heh but theres a special named Hill Giant who spawns
very often.. Kite nuke at level 49+ with ice comet

No pet in unrest means serious downtime for you have to clear all the
greenies. But if you intend to camp the undead knight theres a trick... At
about level 40 where most mobs are green to you, cast levitate on yourself
and walk to the hill with a stream downwards in the garden (east of the
map), standing at the highest point of the small hill, you can target the
knight or his ph and nuke it, after a while the knight will go down the
stairs and come out the door and look for you, since all mobs should be
green nothing will agro you and you can kite nuke in the garden. Seems like
normal invis works for about 40% of the mobs (the weaker mobs like mummies
and dark bones) in there.

Befallen is pretty easy but you have to find a good place to med or else you
will get whacked. Level 30+ you can solo everything including bottom floor.

Sola .. some parts like foreman and predator only need about 30+ to solo
camp but if you wanna do lava elemental, gabbie and maybe some parts of the
castle, you will need to have ice comet...

SG is simply kite nuke kite nuke but bad xp and not efficient in terms of
time you have to med up (SG has much more hp than same level mob) / heal up
(if you get hit) / and the time you spend trying to gain speed so you can
nuke the SG

Maybe SolB you can do that since bugs section is pretty close to lavastorm
zone line. Maybe can pull bats, beetles and if you feel powerful you can try
lava duct crawlers...
