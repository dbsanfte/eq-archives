Most low lvl mobs can't see invisibility, so it's safe most of the time
travelling with it on. Since I haven't reach lvl 20, I can't comments on
monsters higher than that. So far I haven't meet any mobs that can see me
with invis.

Normally talking, ooc, etc wont reveal yaself. But if u try to loot,
inspect others, cast spells or attack, you will reveal yaself. It doesn't
give any AC bonus, only give u the ability to remain invisible to others.

As for CB, I mean soloing. If u can find a full group with tanks and
healers, u shld be able to survive quite well in the CB camp, but not in
the CB dungeon, due to the higher lvl mobs like dvinn, emperor etc. I ever
group with a team of 1 warrior, 1 ranger, 1 cleric, 1 wiz, 1 magi when I'm
lvl 5 and 6, we did quite well in CB camp.

Btw, maybe u wanna consider coming to FP when u reach lvl 8 and buy the new
scrolls, coz most of the kakis r in FP and we can help one another better.
I just met a high elf friend last night, who is 2 lvl above me when I
leave for FP, now she is 4 lvl below me. FP basically got more monsters to
hunt, better xp and loot too. Most imptly all our kakis are there, dun
have to worry abt been bullied, hehehe.
