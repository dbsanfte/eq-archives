Everytime you /consider something, there are three, independent
values given to you.

The first part of the wording (amiable, indifferent, apprehensively,
ready to attack), tells you the mobs FACTION in relation to yours.

The second half of the wording (safe opponent, upper hand, even
fight, gamble, tombstone), tells you the LEVEL of the mob relative
to yours.

The third piece of information is, you guessed it, the colour, which
tells you how much xp you will get.

I have ever /con 2 different mobs which say "risky, but you might
win" (the level of the mob is somewhere between "upper hand" and
"even fight"), but one is blue, the other is white.
