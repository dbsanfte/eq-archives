Gary, you were asking about these?

Check out http://everquest.gamestats.com/ for more info.

Here's a short excerpt...

Paladin Armor of Ro: This is an extremely involved quest. For
each piece of armor, you need to do three things.

1) You need to get the mold for the item. You do this by fetching
the quest items and turning them in to the NPC who gives the
item quest. You need to develop a high faction with the NPC's
Guild before he or she will give you the quest. You can get the
quests immediately from those who view you warmly. Those who
view you amiably will request that you go to their guild and talk
to someone there first before getting the quest. Those below
amiable will not talk to you. Below are the people who start the
quests and the methods of getting the molds for each item. All
molds are no drop lore items, so whoever turns in the items must
finish the quest.

Step 2 involves making a metal called ronium, and step 3 is to
combine the mould and the metal in a forge.
---------------------------

The steps seem very complicated, and you need high smithing
skill. Not sure if it's worth it or not....
