players to gain XP, while high lvl play gain stuffs (win-win situation for
both):

- high lvl player tank and hit a monster left 60% HP.
- lower lvl player start to nuke while high lvl player stop attacking and
just tank.
- once monser kena killed, lower lvl player will get all the xp coz he does
more dam than the high lvl player (both not in a group)
- this method is more effective than to team with high lvl player, whack
together and high lvl player quit from team when monsters abt to die
(anyway this method also need the lower lvl member to do more than 50% of
the dam).

This method is best for helping classes with high nuking ability, since the
lower lvl player have to do more than 50% of the dam. H/w, we can be
flexible in using this method as the team can be made up of 1 high lvl
player to tank and do 40% of the dam, while the remaining r made up of a
group of low lvl players (so that after the high lvl player inflict 40% dam
and the low lvl group finish the monster off with the remaining 60% dam).
This method required NO group between the high lvl player and the low lvl
players.

To be fair, I guess the low lvl players won't mind to let the high lvl
player to get all the loot for spending his time tanking and training them
;)

Alternatively, the high lvl player and the low lvl player group can take
turn to kill/loot, which to me it's fairer for both side.
