Highpass is mainly gnolls and orcs. Spawn there is good, and if yer the only
group there, xp moves quite fast too. But its mostly camped out and ya need
to be at least 15-16 levels since the orcs are around that level. Gnolls
tend to be lower level and lesser too so if yer can try there. As for loot,
the orcs give anywhere from silver to gold and sometimes rusty weapons and
ringmail armor. Named orc and gnoll will spawn occasionally who will
SOMETIMES load Shiny brass halberd and Polished granite tomahawk
respectively. They are about 20+ level i think but mostly, people will camp
there to steal kill them since the weapons fetch quite a good price on the
market.
