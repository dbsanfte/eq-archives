1. His aide is in a building you will meet later, but you need to catch 20
or more pokemons to get it. The aide is in a room where there are telescopes
(I think, can't remember)
2. You have to find HM like cut to be able to use it and you must teach the
HM to a pokemon. Cut is on the captain of the ship.
4.Carry 6 always, it doesn't hurt to carry those extra pokemons just in case
the pokemons faint or you need to counter another pokemon.
Water strong against Earth and Fire
Thunder strong against water and flying pokemons but weak against earth
Fire good against ice but weak against water
Earth good against physical attacks like scratch but weak against water
Still got some others but I can't remember, maybe try searching the web.
5.If you complete finish liao, DON'T use the megaball on anything less than
a mewtwo, since the megaball is 100% catch. Btw, the blue and red no
difference in terms of gameplay, except the different pokemons available and
the rarity of a certain pokemon in an area. The final dungeon also same,
except for the yellow version where the final dungeon is different.
