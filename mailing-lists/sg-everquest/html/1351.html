2. The autosplit doesn't split TOTAL cash, it just splits coins, so if lets
say got 6pp and there are 6 ppl in group, it will be 1pp each. 12 gold = 2
gold per person, But if there is 7 gold, then the person who loots it get 2
gold, and the remainder 5 gold goes to the party, with 1 gold to each
person.This applies to copper and silver too, therefore usually receiving 1
copper cause copper is the most common coin found on the mob.
