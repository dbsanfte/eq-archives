ok... as far as i know... najena herself (where u get tome or fbr) is level
28 mage.. she will havea elemental pet... sometimes when she doesnt spawn..
a elemental will spawn on her spot instead... her 2 guardians are 2
magicians bout level 20+ but i believe not more than level 25... they too
will have elemental pets...

as for drezlna (where u get bloodclaw or jboots) is level 25 necromancer...
she will have a pet skele... sometimes when she doesnt spawn... a
necromancer will spawn on her spot instead ... she spawns every 20 mins....
either her or one necro...
her 2 guardians are 2 necromancers bout level 20+ but i believe should not
be more than 22 since they all con blue to me when i was level 22 there...
they too will have pet skeletons...

if u managed to break the spawn at the beginning... then will be easy...
the last time...my group consist of 2 shadow knight level 20-25, plus me and
one magician level 25... we managed to hold that place for drezlna ... not
problem.... occasionally... there will be ogre guards, tentacles and moosh
that comes into the room and create trouble... they always choose to come at
the 'right' time... also.. somehow theres a bug in that room that sometimes
moosh can punch thru the wall... so be careful.... as for najena room...
there are ocasionally froglock ghouls that interrupts the room... others im
not sure... perhaps elementals... tanks are certainly good help... but 2 -3
high level spell casters can do the job liaoz... as fer monks... i think if
u camp Rathyl (or something like that)... theres a jewel piece YY might be
interested... hehe.. not sure if hes still interested... but i heard monks
needs em...Black Sapphire or something like that...

u need a golden key to get into najena's room and a purple one into drez's
room.... we can also camp the bone cracker for dark circlet... but thats
necro only item... hehe... or the ogre captian for some weapon... that tanks
might be interested.... thats all i know bout that stupid dungeon...
