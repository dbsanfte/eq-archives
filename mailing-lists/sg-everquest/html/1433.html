I read an article from EQ-lizer which talks abt the team comparison in TZ.
I wanna to copy & paste to the mailing list but it's already taken down
from the site. Anyway I will try to recap the main points. Let break the
4 teams into:
A. human, erudite, barbarian (the goons)
B. high elf, wood elf, half elf (the slims)
C. gnome, dwarf, halfling (the shorties)
D. dark elf, troll, ogre (the gansters)

The main points are as follows:
1. Amg the 4 teams, talk abt power-play, order from best to worst is D, C,
A, B (yeah, good races elves stats sux in EQ)
2. Talk abt the availabity and hunting 'territories" of different races
(taking into consideration of possible races conflicts as well), team D has
the best source of hunting ground.
3. Talk abt unity, team A is the worse since their races is separated while
team D is the best (all same continent).
4. Also take note that FP may be a big conflict land if there is not enuff
humans to enforce the region.
5. Talk abt lvling and lowest xp penalty, order from best to worst: A, B/C,
D
6. Comparing faction and npc relationship, from best to worst: C, A/B, D

IMHO:
- If we are going for fast lvling and willing to take the shit for night
blindness, we shld go for A. (Problem: Low availability of hunting
grounds).
- If we wanan look good and enjoy more roleplay element, we shld go for B
(Problem: Other than look, we sux big time).
- If we dun mind short sighted and wanna power play good races, we shld go
for C. (Problem: We look ugly)
- If we wanna rule and inflict fear to all hearts and power-play in own
server, we shld go for D. (Problem: we will be KOS in most area, not just
PvP but also PvNPC)

Entreri:
FYI real neutral race like gnome is not KOS in evil race region. So when a
gnome walk into DE city, they will charge him very expensive for buying
stuffs but they will not KOS him, unless his faction worse after killing
too many DE alliances.
Erudite and Barb are not nentral races I think. From the EQ Guide, only
gnome with another 1 or 2 races (dwarf and halfling?) are true neutral
races. H/w, due to the faction developed later, they can be KOS by evil
races npcs.

Leshrec:
Entreri is talking abt alignment, which mainly affect the relationship with
npc, ie good, neutral and evil.
In TZ, there are actually 4 teams (which involved PvP relationship):
human, erudite, barbarian (the goons)
high elf, wood elf, half elf (the slims)
gnome, dwarf, halfling (the shorties)
