Stat of glove is dunno ac 3 or 5, 5.5wt, heavy but give a black glove look
when u wear it. Here's the quest:

This item is obtainable by completing a quest in the Erudian Mage Guild
House. What you do is hail Vasile Jahnir and Say "The power of the
gatecallers is Summoning". If you have good faction with the Gatecallers he
will tell you to bring him 2 summoned food and 2 summoned Daggers. Give
these items to him making sure the food is not stacked when you give it to
him. Once you do this, you will receive the gloves. If your faction is not
up to par, kill Eliah Brooks (a Necromancer near the docks in Tox Forest,
loc +140, +1200).
