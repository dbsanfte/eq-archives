As a paladin, necro, or enchanter, I've never had luck with
soothe/calm/rest the dead. I tend to try them when I get a new one, but
haven't found one that is worth beans.

Harmony works to pull singles if you're outdoors and have a druid/ranger.
The Bard spell works to pull singles. The enchanter/cleric/necro versions
are pretty useless.

Mez or root work to keep one mob out when you pull the rest, and then to
keep mobs busy while you kill one at a time. It's not the same as single
pulls, but it's what we're good at.
