I have had a lot of fun and amazing experience 13-15
in Unrest, killing the yard beasts (I am 15 now). Solo
there can be tricky at 13, but doable at 14+. Get
couple more people and it is non-stop killing (and
non-stop experienc). As an enchantress, I find my
talents particularly useful there, since the beetles
and other undead aggro so easily. Mes them, charm
them, it is just a lot of fun. My philosophy is that
enchanters shine in a group in dungeons (speakning
from my wizard experience grouping with enchanters on
dungeon crawls). My goal is to try to stay in dungeons
from now on :) And contrary to some previous posters,
finding a group to join has not been that difficult.
Teaching them to fight "enchanter-style" is a
different matter though :)
