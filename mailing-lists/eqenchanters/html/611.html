Have to be very careful with pets in dungeons. I died yesterday in
blackburrow when a huge train (20 or so gnolls including elites and
several guardsmen) bubbled up from the depths after a partymembers pet
(a necro's skel) wandered off and attacked something. We eventually got
experience (seemed kind of funny getting exp out of the blue with us all
sitting/resting) which worried me but there were so many people in the
zone I thought the gnoll population was probably under control. HaHaHa,
don't think so anymore. They started trailing in and they just kept on
coming. I made the decision to run too late. I almost made it up but
an elite kept stunning me and then the gang would hit hit hit. So,
don't let your pets wander off and attack as you may see more of the bad
guys at once than you intended.
