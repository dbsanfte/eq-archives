At 24, I was soloing (no pet either) EE's without a problem. Under that,
your damage output may not be sufficient. Due to the charming the EE's do, I
would recommend not using a pet on them. Had a stein in one hand and a Skull
of Jhentra in the other (weapon, what's a weapon? really haven't used one
after level 22). Buff up with endure magic. Enthrall the pet, burn the EE
into the ground (WTYH is actully useful here), then mem blur the pet when
the eye is dead. Very important to tell anyone you are with not to touch the
pet (watching it slash itself for 32k always impresses people the first time
they see it). In the gorge, nearby critters will come and assist anything
you fight which is why the mino's jump in. The king room in the gorge (the
room with the big stone altar type thing and the 2 EE spawn spots) also has
some horrrible pathing issues. Putting up a pet there always attracted the
whole room on to me. It is one of those non pet friendly areas.
