200p for a stein is a good price. people will still pay around 250. The
trick is to happen to be in the right place at the right time. I haven't
had much luck at times in greater feydark GF trying to sell things and other
times you happen to meet the right person and trade a bunch of stuff.

If you have a stein and are trying to sell it, remember that you can get the
next stein prepared while you still have the first stein. You can hold a
stein, the compendium from Gregor, and items to make the compendium at the
same time. So, as soon as you sell the 1st stein you are ready to hit Ogguk
to make the next one because when you do sell there will probably be 2
people who want it at the same time of all things.
