I use this one occasionally depending on where I am and what is being pulled.
Recently we had a goblin get away from us in Solusek. (We had two not quite
full groups in the area of the Foreman/Predetor between levels of 24 and 31)
So as soon as the one got away I memorized Mesmerisation.

And boy was I glad that I did.

With the number of people there and the goblin finding well over a dozen
buddies to bring back to the Foreman room there would have been no way to
effectively enthrall each individual one knowing that it was kind of a free
for all in killing them all off. I was able to mesmerize most of them and I
did it a few times as they killed everything off. And I was standing in the
corner farthest from the one I had targeted so I only accidentally mesmerized
myself once.

Never seen anything like that and live to tell about it : )
