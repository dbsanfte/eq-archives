If you want to max conjuration, which I reccomend, walk through the newbie
area mesmerizing everything in sight. See, mesmerization uses conjuration
for some reason. I generally start a fight with mes, not that I need to
use
it, but just for the practice, as well as the free debuffs.

As far as pierce and 2hb, do both. You have the intelligence, and since it
only goes up a lousy 3 points a level, you can keep both at max fairly
easily.

Save those training points for specilization (when implemented), research,
and any trade skills.

In all, I think you have more than enough points to prime evocation or 2hb
some, it depends whether you want to take the time to max skills through
use, or use the points for a quick fix.
