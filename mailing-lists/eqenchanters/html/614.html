Our pets don't listen, but at level 12 we get the spell Charm which
brings new pets under our control. The charmed pets DO listen, so learn
your pet commands. Charming is great to do when you are in a group and
don't really need an animation. when fighting two monsters at once,
Charm one monster and tell it to attack the other. By the time charm
wears off you should have dispatched the first, or come close, so you
can then focus on the one you had charmed. Its also pretty good for a
laugh to see monsters fighting each other.

NOTE: if your pet gets killed while still charmed, you lose all the
loot it may have had. try not to get the pet killed unless you are
ding so strategically.
