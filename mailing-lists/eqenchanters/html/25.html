Subject: Enchanter Tip:


Once you cast minor illusion spell, you can take the spell off of you
by clicking the icon. This is great if you know when to use it, for
example:

you see a griffon coming towards you, but dont worry: cast minor
illusion and the griffon will ignore you, when it has passeed click the
spell to get back original form. This is much beter than gate...just
dont use illusion when the monster spots you =)
