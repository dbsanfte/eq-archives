my fave strategy when goin solo is this:

-make me my pet.
-take one monster at at time.
-when you see yer monster, cast root on him (i see no point in casting
whirl till you hurl, it takes for mana and isnt worth it on blue and white
monsters when root will suffice holding them in place)
-cast choke on it (18 plus persistant dmg)
-cast chaotic feedback on it (48dmg)
-run up to it, let it hit me once, this starts my pet attacking
-run back, cast chaotic feedback again
-run back to it before it kills your pet, and whack it twice, this usually
does the trick and you lose little or no health.
-meditate. this strategy does use a lot of mana, but mana is recovered
faster than health.

this makes soloing fun for me. cause i whoop ash. hehe.
