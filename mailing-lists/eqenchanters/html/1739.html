I used memory blur for the first time last night and I have some questions.

My party and I decided to try and tackle a hill giant. With all of us having
SoW and me having Memory Blur (MB) we didn't think we'd have a problem, even
though we are all low-mid 20's. Well, we couldn't touch that hill giant with
spells even after my tashani. The tank was hysterical and wouldn't run close
enough for me to get off a MB to get the giant off him. In the end, everyone
but me zoned, so the hill giant comes straight for me. I start pushing the
MB key... Target out of range!... Target out of range!... You begin to cast
a spell.... A hill giant blinks. The hill giant then changed direction and
went about his merry way. Cool!

Now that we know we can't take a hill giant, and bolstered by our experience
with the hill giant (noone died), we decided to try a griffon. This one
didn't go nearly as well. The griffon turned out to be just as magic
resistant as the hill giant, and a LOT faster. After unsuccessfully trying
our spells, the call went out for MB. The tank ran the griff near me and I
cast MB. A griffon blinks. Problem was, it kept chasing the tank! The tank
didn't like that news so he kept running for the zone. I chased him a bit to
try and get another cast of MB off. I guess at that point I was closer to
the griffon than the tank because it turned for me.
