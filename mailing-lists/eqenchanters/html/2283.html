On fighting in Lavastorm:

Be very very careful if you are fighting near the entrance to Najena. This has
happened to me and a friend of mine. After being ambushed by 3 fire elementals
while looting my corpse (I just got all my armor, but my bags were on my
corpse) I ran to the zone to Najena because there was no way I was going to
live otherwise. Instead of zoning into the hallway where you're supposed to end
up, I ended up in a room full of Magicians and pets and was immediately killed.
I had to petition to get my corpse (which took 2 hours). The GM that helped me
was very nice and pulled my corpse out to safety for me. When this happened to
a friend of mine the GM's weren't quite so nice. They didn't believe that he
just zoned in to avoid a monster they believed he was "exploring" in Najena (at
level 12!). It took him 2 hours of arguing with a GM before he got his corpse
back. Both GM's mentioned that this does happen occasionally at that zone edge
(the second GM said my friend's corpse was on the wrong side of the room :( ).
I have no idea if or when they plan on fixing it. :(

Has this happened to anyone else?
