Try crag spiders in East Karana. If you buff your pet with Quickness just
before the fight (along with your other buffs) and lead off with enthrall,
tashani, languid pace, choke they are pretty easy. Try to keep it on your
pet so you don't get poisoned and if you have the money use rune to help
your pet. Worked for me :)

Also, this technique works on the Ogre Raiders on the island in Timorous
Deep that you get to by taking the barrel barge from Oasis. There you don't
have to worry about poison, too. Just go between the one at the dock and
the one standing on the rocks. Be careful though, as this hurts faction
with The Overthere.
