Kaladim:
Kill goblins in butcher, aqua goblins, do the "Muffins for Pandros" quest
with about 2000 muffins (yes 2000), do "Pirate Earrings" quest and "Pirate
Bracers" quest, and finally do "Ogre Heads" quest in kaladim itself.

You have two strikes against you. 1) religion (for some odd reason all NPCs
instantly know your religion regardless of your illusion) 2) race (dwarves,
high elves, wood elves, gnomes, barbarians, and some humans are all KOS to
you). You will have to raise your faction as high as it can go with the
stormguard before your illusions will work (due to your religion).

Crushbone:
There is a very convoluted way to get the free faction by doing crushbone.
You kill stuff in crushbone. You take the hits with the Indigo Brotherhood.
You turn in orc picks to gain that faction back but lose it with
stormguard(dwarves) and emerald warriors(wood elves). You then go and kill
aqua goblins in oot to gain back the stormguard and emerald warriors. Whew.
Long complex way, but in the end you will get positives in everything.
