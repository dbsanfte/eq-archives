Solo hunting? Lavastorm is ok, you can hunt near Najena and zone if you have
a problem. Don't know how much gear you have, as you hit the harder things
you'll need a magic weapon. Yard trash in Unrest is also very good at this
level, there's about 6 spawn spots inside the maze just past the fountains
where you will be off the tracks. I think the regular aviaks (not the
guards) in front of the Arena in Lake Rathe should be ok, though they may be
too low. There's also a lizard man camp in Rathe Mountains (one outrunner
and 2 in camp). The Southern Ro dervish camps have an outrunner at each that
you can go back and forth between or join a group doing one of the camps
(the east one is preferable, there's a safe area near the grass where you
can pull to and no ghouls or dry bones will wander through). The solo dervs
may spawn a little tough on occasion, but you can kite those using fear.
Might be able to do the orc camp in misty thicket, not sure exactly how high
they are, but level 10-12 I think. If you can get 4 or 5 people together,
the orc camps in Rathe Mountains are great experience and loot. There's all
my good spots (good as in loot, experience, and rarely occupied).
