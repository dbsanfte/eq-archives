Jewelcraft training question:
I'm thinking you can't train in a trade skill above skill level 21
regardless of level. Here's some text from "Casters Realm":
http://eq.castersrealm.com/
<><><><>
If you start early without much money you will want to max your skill to 21
at the guild with practise points. After this you have to make items to
raise skill.
<><><><>
In other words you can't put any skill points into Jewelcraft after skill
level 21 even if you have points to spare and money.

Research training questions:
This is taken from a research info site, "Mythiran Tower":
http://www.mythiran.com/
<><><><>
Q: What's the deal with research at level 16? It only allowed me to put ONE
practice into the Research skill when I made level 16! How can I improve
this skill?

A:Sorry, folks, but at level 16 through level 18 you are only allowed that
ONE practice. Something to do with that spell circle. But never fear! Once
you break level 19 you can put 4 or 5 in there (depending upon your magic
class), then once at 20, the skies the limit...
<><><><>
Also, there is a way to practice your research w/o using real components:
http://mythiran.everquest.gamestats.com/practice.html

hope this helps,
