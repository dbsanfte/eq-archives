Almost forgot! Color Flux is your friend! Use it, but be careful of what
is around you because it is an Area Effect spell. Say, for example, you are
fighting a black bear in WC and you Color Flux it as all good Enchanters do.
But you didn't notice the asp that slithered near by after you started the
fight and got caught in the CF area. The CF may or may not have worked on
the asp, but either way you will have a pissed off snake in your face fairly
quickly. At level 4 your are done for.
