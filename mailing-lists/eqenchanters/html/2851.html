Your worry is not misplaced my friend. I fear very much you are setting
yourself up for big trouble later in your career. The "older" you become as
an enchanter the more you will be called upon to fullfill your primary
function in a group: crowd control. This is what we do. This is what we are.
I know it is entirely possible to level your wizard/enchanter combo to level
50 and beyond with never having learned to turn an overpull of 4+ mobs from
disaster into easy victory. Dont do it!! At level 12 you should already be
working on learning this art. Mezz , tashan, colorstuns, memblur, teamwork;
these are your tools. Its a challenging and difficult thing to learn but
very satisfying. There is NOTHING like the thrill seeing a dungeon floor
littered with the bodies of your enemies and knowing YOU made this possible.
And hearing your groupmates say "WOW!!" and "OOM hehe" and "you ROCK!" etc
