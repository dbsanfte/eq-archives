if it's a pet you need, I would suggest sticking with your summoned pet
since you know he won't turn on you in the middle of the fight. Charming I
use more for crowd control or in a situation where I have someone backing me
up if it breaks at the wrong time.

As for pets and weapons, it really doesn't do them any good (except make
them look cool sometimes :). It used to be that giving you pet a dagger
would increase it's speed without affecting damage, but that is not the case
anymore. Your pet will do the same damage at the same speed no matter what
it wields. Eventually, however, you pet will gain the ability to dual
wield, in which case giving it two weapons will help, but I forget what
level they get it at.
