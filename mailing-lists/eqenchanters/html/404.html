Subject: Trade Skills in general and Jewelry Craft in specific...


Have any of you noticed character attributes having a significant
effect on trade skills? I have this theory that, perhaps, your str and
dex ( maybe a few more ) have a very noticable effect on such things.

This came about after comparing experiences with an Erudin Wizard
friend of mine using Tailoring. At the time, I was playing a Monk, so
both my Str and Dex were significantly better than his, and I seemed to
be having much greater success than he was, with both Tailoring and Fishing,
of all things.

So... as an experiment, i have created a high elf enchanter with
the following statistics:
Str 55 Sta 65 Agl 85 Dex 95 Wis 95 Int 102 Cha 95

This character operates under the assumption that Str does not
play a vital role in jewelcraft. I've found the Int to still be within
reasonable levels, regarding spell use. (Although I seem to be getting
more resisted results than I remember from my other high elf enchanter.
Has anyone noted a Int to Spell resistance correlation?)

Anyway, I just thought I'd ask around. It might be worthwhile to
add, when posting success rates, your character's stats. Just a thought, and
I have seen some stats mentioned on some of the trade skill guides. Just not
all of them.
