On Sat, 27 May 2000, Randy Rustin wrote:

> 1> How do I get into Neriak without a guard playing squish the elf with me?

Wait until level 12 when you have Illusion:Dark Elf. If you want Enchant
Silver before that, hire an inkie to buy it for you.

> 2> Can you have an animation and a charmed pet at the same time?.

Nope.

> 3> Do charmed pets follow commands?

Yes. Animations are in permanent "guard me" mode, but a charmed pet will
follow all normal pet commands except "get lost".

> 4> Mez never seems to do anytime for me. What am I missing?

Not sure. Mez and the like are the strong points of the Enchanter class.
Even at L5, you should notice that it saves your life quite a bit.

It basically stuns a mob for 24 seconds OR until the mob gets attacked or
takes damage. You can only use it on something that doesn't have a DOT on
it and is not under attack by your pet or a party member.

Think of it as a spell that says "don't do anything until I'm ready to
deal with you". Use it:
- If you accidentally get more than one foe, to make one wait while you
kill the other.
- If your pet dies while soloing, to make your target wait while you mem
the animation spell and summon another pet.
- If you're about to run to the guards, to give yourself a 24 second
headstart.

> 5> Why does my pet always stand on what I'm trying to loot?

It's starved for attention.

> 6> Is being agnostic going to cause grief later?

No. Being agnostic is usually the best choice. Having a religion will
affect your faction even when you have an illusion up, which is a bad
thing.
