The Pearl Shard, Jade Shard, and Ice of Vellious are all items use for
Mage Research. Hmm, starting around the 20's if I remember right.
As an Enchanter/Jeweler they haven't much use to you other then as trade
items for Enchanter Research Pages. You could sell it at low cost to a
merchant, but it works out much better when you trade something you got for
FREE for something that you would have to pay someone else for. Trading
Research Materials is like trading baseball cards, (or Magic Cards for the
younger set); you both got what the other wants cheap, you trade and both
walk away happy with no loss of money out of their pocket.
So save those Words and Runes. Carry a couple with you in the field or in
town and every once in a while auction that you're looking to trade spell
research stuff with other classes. Never know when someone will kill a
critter that you didn't, but it had the page you need for that spell you've
been hunting after for 4 weeks.
