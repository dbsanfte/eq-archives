At 35, Lower Guk is a good spot for experience and loot, plus it's constantly
crowded. If you're looking for pick-up groups, it's prime. Sol B is also really
good, at least on E'Ci. Don't know how these dungeons are frequented on other

darn nice loot t'boot. Also, Kedge is very nice, if you don't mind a completely
different fighting environment.

want to be about 45 or so for Karnor's. Chardok is a really cool dungeon around 48+.
