Charisma has nothing to do with jeweling. Most people
claim that wisdom is the key, although recent articles
may have suggested dex is involved. I am a jeweler
with max skill and I practice at 191wis and 180int.

As far as costs go,1000p should get you up to 100
skill. The jump from electrum to gold (117-142 or so)
will cost you a couple thousand more. Maxing your
skill at 200 is likely to cost 50,000 or more. Lots
of fun. As long as you are charging reasonable rates
for your product, you will be able to pay for your
practice out of your profits.
