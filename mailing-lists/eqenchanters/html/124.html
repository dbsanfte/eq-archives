Mesmerize is two things: A "shit list" wipe and a 15 sec stun. If someone
attacks during that 15 secs, then they are now on top of the list. I usually
cast Tashan, CF and Choke, then Mes to get them pissed at the tank instead
of me. Lull is used to shrink their aggro radius so that you can
theoretically pull only one creature at a time. The problem is that it is
VERY unpredictable as to length, so I use charm instead (in a group) and
have one of them help our group beat the crap out of their buddies, then we
take out the pet when s/he gets pissed.
