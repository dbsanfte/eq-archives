My spell line up at level 37

1) Enthrall
2) Color shift (or whatever the highest in that line is)
3) Alacrity/Celerity soon (gota keep the tanks in the group speed up..since
we cant solo hehe)
4) Suffocate/or a lower DD spell.
5) Any debuff (usually slow, or insiped weakness)
6) Highest DD spell
7) Tashani line
8) Mezmerization/Clarity.....rotate when group needs clarity. i do clarity
on myself, then the group, so when mine is fading, i know theirs is too.

How i use it....using the number system above.
start of fight, single mob pull.....
7, 5, 4,.....wait before doing DD so the mod dont go too agrreso on
you...then 6. then sit and med...by this time the mob is almost dead from
the group and the spells.

Start of fight, Multiple Mob pull....
stand back so not to mezz self, then 8, if any resist, they now aggreso on
you, if so, 2, then 1 on them.
stand back again, and 8, now, 7 on all you can,7 will not break the mezz,
nor will any debuffs. then 8, 8, 8, 8, 8, etc....your job in multiple pull
is not to fight, the tanks can do it, you just need to keep them alive and
mezz the train. if you have time, do a debuff or so on the mod being fought,
but save mana to control the train. sit and med between mezzes :)
