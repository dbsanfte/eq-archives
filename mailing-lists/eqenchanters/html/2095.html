Hi Bahir,

I'm also new to the list, but I might be able to help a bit with the jewlrey
questions. More feedback is welcome :)

You can add more points into your Jewlrey Craft skill after the base 21 points,
but they'll cost you. The skills list will show how much it will cost to
increase the skill (given you have points to spend on them). I personally
prefer to increase my jewlrey skill by practicing making jewlrey instead of
spending the money on skill points. That would be the reason I don't add more
points to jewlrey anymore. My skill is still low (only at 35), but actually
making the jewlrey gives for getting the money you put into the skill back,
instead of spending the money for the points, and not seeing the money back..
Also, as an enchanter, money for making the jewlrey (or anything else for that
matter :) ) isn't too hard to come by if you're patient. We are the only
class that can complete the Stein of Mogguk quest easily, and the Stein is
worth quite a bit of money, (I just sold mine for 250pp). I've run this quest
twice now, and I'm planning on doing it a few more times.
