Just wanted to tell all you want to be Jewelers a bit.

I am a level 34 Enchanter on Bristlebane Server. Name is Aeramus Brainblaster

I recently got my skill to 200....yes, it took me over 4000pp to do it too.
now, at skill 200 you should be able to make everything...or so you would think.....i am letting you know that no Plat items are tivial, nor are they easy to make.
I was lucky enough to run a GM quest which rewarded me 25 Black Pearls. now, a plat BP ring usually sells for about 1000pp. from my 25 black pearls i have made 10 rings....and had 12 failures.....
i gave some to my Guild cause they helped with the GM quest. so i have 6 left in my INV.
i have tried to make 6 Platinum Ruby veils, which usually sell for 900pp, and from the 6, i succeeded in making 2! i kept one for me, and i also made myself 2 Jasper plat rings, wis plus6

for those who dont know, Wisdom helps in success rate of jewelcraft, so, i just upped my WIS 19 points for jewelcraft, and all the stats above are with that boost and my success rate is not good. it makes my WIS 126.

I just wanted to let you guys know a bit about jewelcrafting, if you have any questions, feel free to reply.

Its hard to sell to people, when they want a plat item, and you tell them there is a chance there will be a failure.......took me a while to figure out what to say to get them to trust me.
