mana.

to med up most of that by the end of the battle.

refreshed right after (I've spent the last couple months primarily
in Kedge Keep where Augment needs to be up all the time).

because of multiple Whirls (flux/shift in Kedge pulls stupid little

doesn't take too much mana either. With the barb warrior bashing and
the wizard stunning between nukes when Tishan's cycles plus me
