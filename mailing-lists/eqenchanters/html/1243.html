>Hello everyone,

>I have a few questions, some of which I think I know the answers to.

>1. In what ways does CHA effect our characters? A GM hinted around that it
effects some spells in some way or another, but she would not confirm
anything...

CHA lowers your buying price, and raises your selling price. It also
modifies your chance of Charm/Alliance type spells taking affect.

>2. What effects the Duration of a spell? anyone every notice that if a
level 14 druid SOW's you that it doesn't last no where near as long as if a
level 30 or so does it?

A lot of spells have a level based component. Spells like SoW and our Haze
series have a duration based on your level(at lvl 10 it lasts 10 rounds, at
30 it lasts 30 rounds). DD and DoT spells damage components are level
based(at level 10 it does 20 points at level 30 it does 40 points).

>3. Does anyone know what determines our "Hit-percentages" at higher levels
when transformed (using an illusion) say into a Fire Ele? (or any illusion
where we can become a Mob of some type) is it our 2H blunt that determines
hit or miss? Hand-to-hand? etc...

If you are still using a weapon, it would be whatever weapon skill is
relivent.

>4. Does anyone know of any illusion spells that we may aquire besides the
Race type ones (Dwarf, Human, High Elf, Troll, etc..) and these I have
list - (Tree, Earth Ele, Skeleton, Air Ele, Water Ele, Fire Ele, Dry Bone,
Spirit Wolf) So do you know of any that I have not listed?

Most if not all Illusion spells can be found in Erudin Palace in the
Enchanter Guild or in the Libarary. I haven't had the need/desire to go and
catalog all the spells there... yet.
