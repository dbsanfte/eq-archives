I would recommend asking a dark elf to get it for you. While it's true you
can wait for your dark elf illusion spell and invis yourself to the guards
you won't know the way through the maze that is neriak. The library is in a
zone called "The 3rd Gate" which is two zones in from the 1st neriak city
zone. You probably won't want to be wandering around in there any longer
than necessary so trying to find your way won't be much fun knowing your
spells could fail at any time. Many new dark elves ask for help navigating
through the city.

With the change a while back to allow multiple copies of spells to be
carried you may find someone to sell it to you. I'd make a button to
/auction Buying enchant silver spell and let a savvy businessperson help you
out.
