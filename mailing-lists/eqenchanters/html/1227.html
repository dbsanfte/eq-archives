Colorflux has a relatively short duration, mabye 2-5(tops!) seconds, but it
casts VERY fast, fastest casting spell I have. Dont expect to use it over
and over though, it takes a while to 'recharge'. Mana consumption is
relatively low. This is an escape spell, to get that extra 3 steps away
when you run. I have also used it successfully with a charging orc to
place him for a root, but you must be quick as it has a very short
duration. Its a short RAADIUS stun (everything close to you) basically.
