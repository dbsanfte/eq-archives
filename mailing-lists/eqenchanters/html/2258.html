Casting illusion Ogre will give you the _Base_ faction of an ogre. My
assumption is take that base faction, and subtract all the faction hits you
took from killing all of those bouncers (dont' forget your religion), and the
ending result will be the _Actual_ faction with the Ogre. If you kill enough
bouncers the illusion won't make a difference. If you are still KOS try
casting alliance/benevolence, to increase faction.
