The research items we use look like a page that has been tworn down the
middle, leaving the right or left half, you need both halves in order to
attempt to combine them. We also use tombs that have page numbers, they
look like a full page. They are combined in sequence, ie. tomb pages 8 and
9 are needed to attempt a combine of them to make a spell that can be scribed.
As for other classes, necro's use words, so do magicians and wizards use
runes.
