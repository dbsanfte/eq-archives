one thing to remember about purchasing spells is there are some which can't
be purchased. In this respect questing for spells is also known as
research. Bummer there's no exp for researching a spell I don't think
(haven't made one yet) but you can make your own spells.

Enchanters use pages (often there's a left and right half) and faded pages.
The faded pages have to be restored before you use them. Restoring is done
in one kind of container and combining pages is done in another (both found
in your guild probably on the tiny dagger merchant).

A very good research site is "The Mytherian Tower":
www.mythiran.com
