Hi there 21. I too stuggled at that lvl but here is what I finally
decided.

We enchanters love variety and dont like to actually level, but there
are times in our lives we must group in a xp farm type environment to
force us to do so.

For me that was high keep. I sat in guard room and other rooms off
and on for days. Literally. I found it was basically bub every 4
hours. I know that sound mind bending painful boring, but you need
to advance at times and being in group where you dont die is the
answer sometimes.

Dont kill ogres if you have done or plan to do the stien of mogguk
quest for self or others and definately dont kill treants, bad karma
friend.

I guess my advise is this, recognize why you dont level is disdain
for boredom, after all we have gazzillon spells and things we can do -
- how on earth do you expect us just to breeze and slow and tash
stuff.

BTW advice, dont use your evocation DDs, they are not useful for us
in groups and should not be used at all. Other classes are much more
effective at dealing damage, mana and otherwise. Keep in mind we are
not damage dealers but crowd control.

Color flux, stun etc are great.. mez here enthral there.. no no no
charms yet.... slow sit med..

Relax realize by doing less and controling the crowd you doing the
job you will till you are 60, good time to practice, dont
underestimate the value of the tasks you do do well, cuz nobody is
your match in them.
