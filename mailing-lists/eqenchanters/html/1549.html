The Damask Leggings are part of a Neriak Quest in which you have to have
good faction with the Necro Guild to perform.

S'Nez in the Lodge of the Dead, the reward is a damask armor item. I have
only done it once and i got the leggings i bet if you do it enough though
you will get the robe.
