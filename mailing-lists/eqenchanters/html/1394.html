This is true, but I would like to add that a 12th
level enchanter finds that wisps con blue to them, and
so do the things you fight while soloing incidentally.
Grizzly Bears in North Karana for instance. So when
you order the wisp to attack one of those, it will in
fact attack, and the grizzly bear won't be able to
damage it. Whether or not this is a good thing is
debatable though, since when charm wears off, you will
have an angry undamaged wisp attacking you.

One other thing though. When you charm a player on
Rallos Zek, and I would assume in duels, you CAN tell
the player to attack a deep red.
You tell (insertenemyname) "attack a hillgiant__00"
