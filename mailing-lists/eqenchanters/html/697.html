I have been reading and trying to catch up on this list from the beginning,

things.

My character's name is Gwynn and she is on Bristlebane. I have seen two
others on Bristlebane, is there anymore and what are your character's name?

I love the enchanter. I have to say I was having doubts when I bought the
8th level spells and most were pretty much useless, but the 12th level
spells renewed my faith in the class. I have to say that Whirl to you Hurl
is the best combat non damage spell there is. But I must say minor illusion
is the most useful of all, I think have overlooked it's greatness.

A full set of cloth armor can be purchased in two places in Erudin. The is
a female vendor near the supply shop and a below the balcony of the temple
of Quellious.

Pillage does exist. I was in Greater Faydark, I had just gated back after
purchasing the 8th level animation spell. The second message I recieved
after gating in was an auction of the spell Pillage. I inquired when told
it was an enchanter spell. The seller said he had completed a paladin quest
in Erudin "hundreds of times" and that is was a random quest reward. I
certainly didn't have the 3p and some odd gold that the vendors offered him
let alone whatever "finders fee" he would put on it. I didn't know how rare
it was. I wish I could remember the characters name.
