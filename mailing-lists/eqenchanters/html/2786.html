108 is a faded page, level 29 research items drop from level 29-34 critters.
Some may drop on big chargers and avocets in South Karana. Other spots to
check are Highkeep, Lost Temple of Cazic Thule, and the lower level areas of
Lower Guk. Just walk into the zone and /auction for em, will come to you,
clarity included (if you are level 29 already). Offer two or three plat
each, more if you really desperate. Also, check vendors EVERYWHERE. I've
bought more level 44 research items off of vendors already than I ever found
of 39 stuff hunting in Lower Guk and Sol B. I also advertise regularly in my
guild precisely what items I need which I have found is the best way to get
them. The main thing is to let people know what you need because if it
doesn't sell to a vendor for more than FS will, most people will just poof
it or let it rot.
