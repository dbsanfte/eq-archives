>I'll give you my personal strategies, but I've only won about 50/50, as
>there are some VERY skilled PK's on RZ (I.E. Alec of darkenbane for
>example). Really I believe that equally skilled enchanter vs. necromancer
>means quite a lot of pain for both parties, and ANY other class would fall
>more easily.
>
>I am level 35, and these are DUELLING spells, since these isnt a general
>purpose spell bank and you'll never catch me with these particular spells
>memorized while I"m fighting most MoBs
>
>1. spells I memorize
>tashani
>curse of the simple mind
>mana seive
>entrance
>root
>tepid deeds
>chaos flux
>anarchy
>
>This assumes that I've been able to call a pet, give it a dagger, and buff
>it with alcrity, obscure, rune3, and feedback.
>
>2. spells cast
>
>The first thing you HAVE to do is entrance the skelly pet. if you dont do
>this your own pet will simply fight the necros pet, and most likely lose.
>Then tasani the necro, followed by entrancing him. Cast curse of the simple
>mind on him, sit and forget curse of the simple mind and memorize strip
>enchantment. As soon as this spell is available stand up and cast it on him
>twice. Enthrall the pet again. If the necro is STILL entranced, hit
>him/her with a mana seive or two until they begin casting on you. (A wise
>necro will cast a small DoT on himself to prevent being entranced, so be
>careful).... entrance the necro again. sit and med until he wakes up. at
>this point it becomes a matter of mana remaining, and I usually choose to
>blast blast blast (no DoT for me to cast, I dont care for them in PvP) but
>you can continue to enthrall the pet. NOTE: if your OWN pet is attacking
>the necromancer you wont be able to entrance him, but really you wont need
>to... in THIS situation Tepid Deeds is one debuff you HAVE to cast on him.
>
>3. spells I've found to be useless when they'd be great in PvP if they
>worked
>
>a)Whirl spells - too short, slow casting, slow recast time... a damn shame
>this could be a great spell.
>b)charm and fear - damn verant, damn you to HELL. I was killed by a level
>50 wizard after being feared by a Necro NPC, now why can MoBs fear us but we
>cant fear us?
>c)DoT - Personally, too slow for me, although the damage to mana ratio is
>quite good
>d)weaken debuffs - its a casting fight. I definately use these when
>fighting a melee class, ESPECIALLY monks
>e)color flux line - handy for PvP but I'm afraid to hit my pet so I dont use
>it. If I have no pet this is a spell I use constantly
>
>I'd really love to hear other enchanter strategies for PvP, I know there are
>flaws in my strategy but this has worked for me so I dont alter it too much.
>Of course this guide is a perfect situation, and I know that if we're caught
>unawares we can be toast rather quick... of course I think we're the best
>class for getting away alive as well, too bad we dont have shadow step
>too!!!
