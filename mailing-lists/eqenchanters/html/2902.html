I"m told that the enchanters spell - soothe - is pretty useless, and the druids harmony will beat it anytime.  Depending on the camp, root can be helpful in pulling less mobs, also one I have used is Mesmerize (and series).  When you cast these though, any other nearby associated mobs will come running, so be careful.
 
Mesmerize is described as such (from eq.castersrealm.com)- lvl 8-
 

Mesmerise when successfully cast upon the NPC target holds the monster is a mesmerised state for around 30 seconds. During this time any non-damage debuff may be cast upon the target. These include Tashan, the Weaken Series, or Slow Series.

An mesmerised target has a chance of having its Hate List wiped, similiar to the effects of Memory Blur. Just make sure you are beyond the frenzy range when it wears off to increase your chance of success.

Example:  I use mes to pull when i kill guards in WC or the dwarves nearby.  Each camp having two mobs.

 

If the mob has high magic resistance, you might be able to tashan it first (both come running) and get one mez (or in my cast entrance) before they hit you.  Your taking a change thou :p
