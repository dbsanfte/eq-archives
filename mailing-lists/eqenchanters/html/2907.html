I would stick with LoIO. On the South zone wall near the Sarnak Fortress is
a tower with 4 goblin warriors or warlords in it. While facing the south
wall and looking at the tower from the hill (which is a roaming path for
Sarnak Recruits, Icebones, and Gobbies so do not fight there) to the left is
a small leveled area up a bit on the hill. This is a prefect pull-to spot. I
have seen people pull to the stones along the wall base, but when they run,
they run straight to the tower, and wandering Sarnak and Gobbies run
"through" that tower and agro onto you as well. Lots of PC corpses all over
down there once. =(

Anyway, my wife and I went from 26 to 29 down there at that one spot in a
week. Sarnak Recruits are 23 to 26 I believe, so you should do great there.
They wander across the valley floor and across the hill opposite you. No
need to wander very far even with the zone at 80+ people.

Stick it out, you will be much happier once you level. The xp cannot be
beat. I was bored to tears, but the rest of Kunark is not solo friendly for
the 25-33 range. Roughly. Frontier mountains was a fun zone but VERY
dangerous because of all the wandering mobs.

Warsilks Woods. I hate this zone.

Dalnir's Crypt. Great zone for a good group. Will get you to 35ish easy.
Only problem is getting there through Warsilks Woods. You will understand
why if you ever try to get there. Loot is good to.

I made 30th in Ak'Anon on the Cargo Clockwork. Pull it with a spiderling or
clockwork spider, and you will not get any interfeerence from the nearby
gnomes. The Cargo is 30th level every time and runs as if snared and is an
easy kite and will give you enought time to summon a pet. It hits for a max
of 77, and can hit pretty fast. MR isn't too bad, my druid at 29 would be
almost oom when it would die. Not sure if you can kite it as a Chanter, but
worth a try. When is dies, a driver pops out, but he does not agro on you.
Do not kill him as it is a big Gnome hit. You want to do the FA quest,
right? Heh.

Good hunting.
