Subject: Jewelcrafting - my results with skill level 18


I saw no change in prices from level 18 to level 22.
Here are my results so far with a charisma of around 110 (I'm at work
can't remember exactly):
Practiced to level 18.
17 cost a couple coppers
18 cost like 9 coppers
bought again at 20 which cost 1 silver
21 cost 1s 3c

so, BUY UP TO 21 it is soooooo much cheaper than failing. 21 is the max
I could buy by the way even though I have more points to spend.

At 18 Malachite was trivial for me. I have settled on using Jasper.
I've failed with it twice but been successful 8/10 times.

Here's the kicker, when I add up the cost of buying materials vs.
selling I am losing a couple of coppers. Can you believe that? High
charisma is awesome.
