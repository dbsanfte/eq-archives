Subject: You and your nerfs...


Face it, guys, EQ is a multiplayer game where the main fun comes from
grouping with human people to take down NPCs. Killing other people is a very
small part of EQ; the people on Non-pvp servers almost never have to atk
another person. Hmm...now you can't use some spells on other people... So
what? If its very important for you to be able to destroy other real people
in combat, there are plenty of other games (like Quake) that let you do
that, and you don't have to worry about your rocket launcher getting nerfed.
I am surprised at how much grief these new changes have caused. After all,
you can still charm NPCs, you can still color flux camps of monsters, you
can still cast fear on that orc. Sure, that big bad ogre warrior might kick
your butt in pvp. But when that warrior is in your group (which he most
likely will be, as opposed to dueling u), he will be praying for your next
spell as he watches the monster he is fighting rip him apart. Finally, you
can still pvp. You can still win. Some classes can't pvp at all. Thus, my
friends, I wish you good hunting and good luck.
