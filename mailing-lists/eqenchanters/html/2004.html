As you get to higher levels (i.e to level 7) and are still using lower level
pet (i.e. level 4 pet) the pets you conjure up will seem lower and lower to
you .. i.e. may get green ones just before you get to level 8 (and at any
level higher than 8 if you use a lower level pet). When you get a new pet
spell .. when acquiring the level at which that spell is available, you will
occasionally find you get a pet that is more 'powerful' .. in other words,
cons black / white to you.
