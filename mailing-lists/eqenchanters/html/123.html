We have 3 "books" that I have found, two are actually containers, and they
are as follows:

1> Book of Ancient Restoration - This has two slots for restoring damaged
pages.
2> Tome of Endless Enchantments - This is the thing to use for spell
research. You put in a page (or two for some spells), a parchment and a
quill and combine.
3> The End of an Age - this is our "recipe" book. Basically it states that
we need to assemble the tomes of several members of an ancient circle of
Keepers of the Craft. Two of which are Tasarian and Velishoul.
