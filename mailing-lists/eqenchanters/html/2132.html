Seriously, I'm currently level 13 with my Enchanter, and I solo frequently
(Every morning for about an hour). I try to group at night, when I have a
chance to play at night. Lag and disconnects also give me headaches (I've got
to lower my modem speed) Anyway, at level 13, I still use my level 4 DoT.
The level 12 version burns to much mana, although I'm slowly migrating to it,
and it will fully replace it when I hit 14. Since Level 4, I've kept to the
same strategy when soloing.

Pull with Tashen (If mob cons white or is high MR) otherwise I pull with
enfeeble
Enfeeble (if not already done)
Suff. Sphere.
Back off
Wait till pet starts getting whacked (takes about 20 seconds)
Engage mob
Auto attack
Stun
Suff Sphere
Repeat Stuns when spell cycles

When you hit the part of the battle when you are meleeing and stunning (and
tanking for your pet) you can throw in your DD spell for practice. This still
works for me, and it will turn Young Kodiaks into pussy cats. A high
blue/white will cost you about a bubble of mana at level 10. Truthfully, I am
nuking a bit more since I hit 12, but the mana cost is pretty trivial at this
point, and of course my Evo is maxed out. Another caveat, this is a very bad
tactic against Spell Casters, and Poisonous Critters, but thats a topic for
another E-Mail.

As for group combat, DD spells are the first to go. They are quickly replaced
with Soothe and/or/then mem-blur. I find mem-blur to be useless solo since
most of what I attack is KoS to me anyway.
