Subject: New Merchant - Spoiler


Some minor new info for everyone. After the patch there is now a Merchant
that sells Star Rubies, Fire Emeralds, and Sapphires as part of her normal
items.

It is M. Sparkle. She is located in West Commonlands in the small hut right
outside Befallen.

Prices are as follows:

Star Rubies - 156 plat
Fire Emeralds - 204 plat
Sapphires - 240 plat
