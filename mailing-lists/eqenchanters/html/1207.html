1. there is no Enchanter's recipe book for spells. This is good and bad,
good that you don't have to learn any other languages to read the recipe
books, bad because you have no idea what spell will be created unless you
find the information before hand. Also, on this note, i've read that the
higher the page number, the higher the level the spell will be created.

2. the mixing of the hate list people are talking about is 99% likely a
typo, I have seen 'nixing the hate list' to describe Mesmerize's action and
the letter m is right next to n on the keyboard.

3. crack rock is a lore item and will not combine with any metal to create
any type of item. nobody else has found any other weird item, pearl of odus,
light stones, etc to create any jewelry. apparently only generic stones
create jewelry. save the crack rocks to get mobs addicted to it so they
won't attack you because they want to buy your crack supply. a mob can never
have enuf crack and they know only Enchatners have the secret recipe to make
it. that's why mobs rage on us sooo much if we don't give them some crack
before the first draw of blood, at that point it is too late to offer the
crack since their passion is no longer focused on the smokin but on using
your head for their upcoming lawn bowling tournament. of course, the crack
recipe is only sold in Neriak, look for the merchant talking in elder
tier'del about the frankenstein remote control xray robot death machine that
lives in his liver. use the keyword 'skeezer' to get the conversation
rollin.

4. there has been an update to jewelry on the test server and this will soon
be patched to the rest of the servers. jade+silver now makes a +5 mana and
+5 ac and it can only get better with higher grades of metal!

5. if you haven't heard by now, Alliance is now actually useful. cast it on
others that you are buying/selling from when the items cost/sell more than a
plat to save yourself some money. if you already have wickedly high chr, you
might not notice any affect because you are at the best price level and
can't get any further increases in your buying/selling power. combining a
race illusion with Alliance may or may not improve your prices/keep you safe
from psycho guards, an email I got from GZ states that using Alliance and
race illusions together can and will get your better deals, more quests, or
keep you safe from certain psycho guards. this means that using both spells
at once doesn't always give enough of a boost in your overall faction to
notice the difference, which makes sense because if both spells 'had' to
raise your level at least by one that'd end up making that combo a tad too
powerful.

Gzatkin@... for helping Enchanters get their spells tweaked so that

head magic man at VI, in case you didn't know, and his main character is an
