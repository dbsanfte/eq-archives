it is a lost temple somewhere near the ogre city. i suggest you be at least
lvl 20 when you go there, and be with a group (or be someone who everyone
wants in a group). i go in there at lvl 22 and am usually the lowest level
in there, the levels range from 20 to 40. this is the place to get
rubicite, though it is extremely rare. no one will auction off the rubicite
they get, unless they are lucky enough to get 2 of the same type. even
then, it will sell for 1000 to 2000pp. either way, it's a good dungeon for
exp and loot (i made 2 bubbles of exp in 3 hours, killing mostly blue and
white stuff in a 3 person group). but, when it trins in there, watch out.
them lizards hit hard.
