As far as armor, there is better than woven, if you have the money or
ability to get it yourself. Gossimer and Damask are better than woven
in the cloth department. There are also mage-usable leather varieties
such as bear-hide and lupine. I don't know where to get it, but bone
armor is available from some mobs out in the Karanas.

For a one handed weapon, the new hot item is Dvinn's dagger, the
Dragoon Dirk. If you aren't capable of doing Dvinn yourself get a big
group that will dommit to staying through enough spawns to get one for
everyone. He is in the neighborhood of level 20 but hits much harder
than the average bear. Go to Crushbone and see what you can do.
