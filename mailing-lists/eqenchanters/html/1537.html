I am a member of the Wrath of the Dragonclaw Clan, but we are not solely
Veeshan worshippers. We are a two server guild (Bertoxxulous and Prexus)
that has different areas of study/concern associated with dragon types.
It's a great guild and we help each other out a great deal (with hunting,
trade skills, getting items we need, etc.) We have lots of fun and would
love to have both of you if you find it suits your needs. Have you checked
out the website? Anyhow, I find that agnostic is great for choosing to play
what you really want to play (role-playing, what a concept!) I chose
agnostic, but claim that it is only a term used by non-believers in my deity
"The Greater Unknown" , because they don't understand that there is a god
above even theirs.

Anyhow, I was disappointed with the lack of religious options as well
(although I suppose I do understand some reasoning for such on Verant's
part). I wish you both the best and I hope you find a guild that will make
you as happy as mine has made me. If you have any other questions, let me
know.
