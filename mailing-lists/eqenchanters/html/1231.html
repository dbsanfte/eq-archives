the best place you can go for soloing at this level is Lavastorm. It will
bring you at level 18 in no time. After 18, it's still good but not as good.
To get there, Take the boat to Freeport, go in East Commons, enter Nektulos
forest ( follow the right wall to get there )and turn into a dark elf then
follow the path straight all the way to lavastorm. as you enter lavastorm,
follow the right edge and you'll soon find the entrance of the Najena
dungeon. This is a great place to hunt. Xp is amazing on the lavas crawlers.
Loot is also pretty good ( although not extraordinary ). Hunt in Lavastorm
and zone in najena if there is any trouble.
Also, this place is full of darkies, ogres and trolls so if you like duels,
you'll have a good time.
Note: A magic weapon can help...try to buy or get a runed totem staff first.
