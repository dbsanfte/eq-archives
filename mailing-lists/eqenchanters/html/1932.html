Are these spells found on the new 'shadowed men' MoB in Lesser Fay? If so I
think I have destroyed my faction with them already (faction 415 and faction
416)

To anyone who hasnt encountered these beasts yet, go in a party... they are
level 25-30, are constantly invisible, and some are necromancers. They
carry shadow weapons (lore item, magic item) which disappear when you log
out. NPC will not buy these weapons, and they are all warrior only weapons.
Some of the MoBs also carry an 'ice cream cone' but none of them have an
item like that when you loot them (the cone is not available for looting).
When identified, these weapons are considered to be summoned.

These MoBs can run like hell, the only way to outrun them is with SoW or
faster buffs. They wont follow you indefinately. They DO assist each
other. You can find them at the big "quake" symbol straight north of the
Steamfront zone in lesser Faydark, and they spawn as a group of 6 Shadowed
men... get close and you can see their names.

If anyone finds out any information about a quest that these items are for,
please let me know here. I have placed some of these weapons in a bag in my
bank and I will let you know if they continue to exist when I log back in
later.

Cogg - Rallos Zek
