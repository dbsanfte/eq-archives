I set a hot button to say "Jaded Silver Ring, +5 MANA, 8PP or best
offer!" I hit the button each time I enter a zone. Sometimes I carry
1 extra with me, but I usually just sell the ones I'm wearing and go
back to the bank to get a new one (or make a new one) form myself. If
you are desperate for cash up front, sell the rings in your own guild's
vendors and you'll make a few GP profit over the price of the
components. I think Jade is the first item that is profitable to
produce for vendors, less failures of course.
