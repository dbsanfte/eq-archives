Okay, but she's a level 37 chanter- so unless she has some friends in
high places, her chanter is an alt., or she's one heck of a trader,
she's not going to have access to some of this stuff. In order
to contribute to the raid as an enchanter, you need to be:
-EBoots 44
-Sarnak Arcain Fetish 46-50
-Robe of the Foci 50-51 (if this is the HS robe- can't remember what
it's called)

The rest you should be able to get by fighting stuff your level or
below. If this is how you normally play the game, here are some
recommendations for alternate equipment:
1. Neck- Plat Saphire Necklace (7int 7str)
2. Head- Nightshade wreath (1ac 5int 5 wis)
3. Shoulders (embroidered black cape is a shoulder slot item, not
back)- Mantle of Mirth (2 int 2 wis shoulder item from droga, can't
remember the rest of the stats)
4. Back- Mystic Cloak (listed in shoulder slot) or Molten Cloak
5. Feet- Wolf Fur Slippers (just +mana, but no real upgrades until
EBoots)
6. Robe- Should be able to have at least a Oracle. Dalnir, SMR, FBCR,
Robe of the Foci are all upgrades.
