hunt giant wasps - 1 silver per wing. carry like 40 before selling.
that's 4 gold

hunt pawns - weapons and cloth armor sell for 1+ gold ea. I made 6 plat
in a long (8 hour day) of playing. Cracked staffs go for over 1 plat!

group and hunt bigger orcs - exp for yellow stuff and share the loot

As a level 9 necro on other char I help successfully take down wisps.
Our enchanter pets can hit wisps (pretty well) by the way. The trick is
having a tank that can take the damage while we chip away at the wisp.
