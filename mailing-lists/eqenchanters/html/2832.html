Subject: Charming the the Beast


I have been hunting in Sebelis some and have some ideas to share on
charms.

First let me say my tactics are for sure not perfect or to be used in
all situations but I think they are better than the ones I used when
I started.

On multi pulls of say 3 to 4 froggies in Sebelis. I use a Fasination
right off. I ask the tanks to attack any that are not mezd or it did
not stick to.

If more than one is not mezed, often they all are mezed, but
sometimes one or two resist. I recast fascination. Let them start
to work on the mob fist selected.

Then I try to pick out a non caster type from the remaing mobs (non
wiz or shammy) and tash him and cast charm on him. While I wait for
the fasinatioins to bust I root the mobs not charmed.

Once it is broken I immediately cast dazzle on it or them.

Then I tell my pet to attack the mob they are working on. If not too
late i slow it. But at minium i tash it. I tash any remaining mobs
and then med a tad. Maybe suck some mana from target if he is caster
type.

Charming the baddie being tanked or one of the ae mezed ones is a
tactic I recommend on higher lvl baddies.

I find charm is almost an essential tool now. I am very excited
about the new charm I will get at 53 and cannot wait)
