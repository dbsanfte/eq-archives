It's my understanding that quickness now lasts 5 minutes. Just cast it
on yourself and start a stop watch.

I use the fear strategy on things that hit too hard, like elementals.
For their level they pound harder than you would expect. I keep memory
blur memorized, too, so that I can get rid of any extra beasties that
frenzy as my feared target runs by. Obvioulsy this is only effective
in areas where you know the terrain and know that you can handle
anything that comes along, as an individual anyway. Mesmerizing a
frenzy candidate and then bluring it after you pass by will keep you
out of harm's way.

If all hell breaks loose you should have a few seconds before the
frezied beasts kill your pet. Use that time to Gate because invis will
do no good.
