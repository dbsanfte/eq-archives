I myself found this line very unreliable until pacify. It reduces agro
range, it does not eliminate it. You have to know the exact pathing to use
it well. If you do cast the spell on a critter, then pull one over it,
whatever you have calmed will most likely come also. You should start on the
farthest one in pathing and work toward you, pulling the closest. Harmony,
while much more reliable, is outdoor only. I've used pacify to split spider
camps in the Dreadlands and also in Lower Guk to pull safe hall spawns solo
(had to something while waiting for a group). When the spell is resisted and
the target doesn't agro, I will toss mem blur. It can be slow, it can be
unsuccessful, but when it does work, you will amaze your group. Especially
in a dungeon situation, I just tell the puller if it's less than 5, pull it,
we can handle it. And in anywhere near a good group, we do.
