i found it as an exit from Lesser Faydark.
when you enter Lesser faydark from the felwithe zone. follow the path, take
the first right. go all the way till the path ends. walk a little but
further out and you will see a Fairy Tower. stand in the doorway of the
fairy tower facing outwards. and go the direction (i believe it will be
SW....not sure) follow it all the way till you get to the mountain/wall of
this zone. turn right at the wall, and follow, the entrance to the castle
zone will be on your right along that wall.
