Decaying Skeletons are great. Bone chips stack, and sell for 1.2-1.4
silver each, and weich next to nothing. Also, a lot of times decaying
skels have weapons, which makes them easier to kill (they attack slower,
easier to get a spell off) and then you have MORE loot.

I always trash ruined pelts, they sell for 9 copper and take up a whole
slot, and weigh 3.5.

Cracked Giant Beetle Carapaces are good, they weigh exactly zero, and
sell for 1.8 gold.
