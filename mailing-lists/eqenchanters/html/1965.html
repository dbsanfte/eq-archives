Yes, you can, but since our animations follow critter rules, you can't
change the amount of damage it's capable of, only the speed of its attack.
Therefore, find the fastest weapon you can (and don't mind losing) and give
it to your animation. You can sharpen a few rusty daggers and carry em
around with you if you aren't to the point where you can spend 2p on the
faster store-bought daggers.
