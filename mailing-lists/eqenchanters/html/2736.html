agility really only affects your AC. For Erudites, Humans, and other lower

agility to raise your ac ONE point. 3 agility does not raise it one whit.
If you are Dark elf or other higher agility race ( i.e. more than 75 ) it
take 6 or 7 points of Agility to raise your AC ONE point.

Now keep in mind, agility affects your total AC, not the AC on the item, and
that agility HAS NO OTHER FUNCTION IN EQ OTHER THAN RAISING YOUR AC. (( For
anyone piping up that Agility affects your channeling, get a gun and shoot
