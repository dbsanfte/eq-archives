There are a number of places where being hit at the zone border plops you
somewhere else in the next zone. Here's some that I know of:

Unrest to Dagnar's Cauldron:
If you are hit on your way out of Unrest you may fall "below" the world.
You will find yourself in the water under the land which goes around the
lake. When this happened to my wife I suggested she swim off the edge.
Don't do that! She fell for 10,000 points of damage and I had to get my
necro to locate her corpse (which was beneath the land near 0,0). Without a
necro we would have never found it. Meanwhile we had petitioned a GM and he
was sad when he finally got to us because my wife had already looted her
corpse otherwise he would have res'ed her. When the same thing happened to
me I knew not to swim of the edge but what to do? I swam to the middle and
climbed up the island. Once you do this you can swim back to the land and
climb up.

Oasis to No Ro:
If you are hit hard enough (sand giant, etc.) on your way out of Unrest you
may find yourself near the inn but the kicker is you will fall for some
small amount of damage. It's not much but if you were almost dead you will
be dead after you fall. This is a change from a while back where you would
bounce back into Oasis at full health (which was kind of cool).

Beholder Maze to Runneyeye:
Had to run from a train that was led into the entrance of Runneyeye. Got
hit going through apparently and died. My corpse was not on either side of
the zone. I managed to find it in Runneye in the first alcove to the left
by the little stream.

Anyone know of others?
Shawn
