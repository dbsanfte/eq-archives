> I am a High Elf enchanter and i'm stuck at 1bub into 21st lvl.
> I need to know where I should be hunting.

Unrest is good and bad. You're the right level to be camping a room or
perhaps in a basement group, but the zone needs to be fairly populated to
work well. This is a good spot for pick-up groups if you play on peak or
near-peak hours. The mistmoore courtyard (up along the edge) is good too,
but finding a group there is spotty. If you think Unrest trains are bad,
wait until you fight in Mistmoore. Still, both zones have places that you
can fight without being in the train path.

Cazic is okay too - there's usually a group forming or looking for new
members, and your level is perfect for the maze. Don't fight in the
courtyard or you'll get trained often - go into the maze, pick a dead-end,
and pull to there.

SK is crowded and annoying, but there's always aviaks and centaurs to kill
and people to team up with.

Upper Guk is amazing experience, but you need to have a group already, as
nobody hangs out there looking for a group. Likewise Permafrost, and the
Goblin ruins or Iksar bandit cave in Warsliks Woods. You're at the high
end of the level range for Kurn's tower.

Gargoyles in OOT are profitable, and you can often find a partner to hunt
them.
