Dreadlands is better at 40-45 for Kunark zones. Find yourself a snarer
partner and snare and scare, 2 people can do 40-45 pretty fast at the
skellie camp in the NW corner. Or you should be able to find a group on the
wall of Karnor's anytime.

CoM : With one group pulling to zone, it's hard to insert past them to
another good location, but excellent exp.
Overthere: Sarnak knights top out at 39 or 40, not enough good exp there
past 40.
Kaesora : Great exp in a full group. Problem is finding a full group there.
Droga : Starts greening out at 42 or so.
Nurga : Believe it's lower level then Droga.
