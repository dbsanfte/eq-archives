Yes, i have a suggestion, dont solo....
Soloing is good for a while, but you need to realize that the objective of
this game is not to really solo but to find a well rounded group, and do
your part.
your part includes and does not include:
1) you are NOT a tank! there is no need for you to do any sort of hand to
hand combat!.... EVER!!! if your tank says ""RUN"" dont even think twice
about it. get your ass outta there!
2) you ARE crowd control (Mezmerization, Color Flux, Memory blur....these
are your specialties, learn how to use these, and people will love you.....i
have held off a train of about 12 mobs, while my group killed them off one
at a time.......you must communicate with your group, and tell them, if a
train comes, all assist <tank> this was all the mobs wont even move, cept
the one they are attacking. all you need to do, is keep casting Mezz about
once every 15 seconds)
3) you are a Buffer (buff your tanks, give them speed, str, and other stuff)
4) you are a debuffer (weaken, and slow down your enemies, this will help
your tanks live longer)
Everyone loves enchanters, we help people live during major combat
situations......
remember your role, and live it.
if you wanna do hand to hand combat, and solo, be a Tank, not an enchanter.
or if you wanna solo the wholel game, be a Necro hehe.
