hmmmm dying in pain skill, well, its not a pleasant thing, see, for every
time you die, your stupidity skill goes up 5 points. then when you reach
1000 in that skill, everytime you die, you get a dying in pain skill point.
what that means, is that for each death, you loose a random item, so you can
feel your characters pain. the item could be in the bank, or on your person,
or even money. its totally random, just dont let your stupidity skill get
that high, and you'll be fine :)
