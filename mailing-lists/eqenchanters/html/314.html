Ok, I have been keeping track of different combinations of jewels and this
is my list thus far in response to The Wanderer's post.
All of the below were made with Enchanted Bar's of silver.

1 Enchanted Bar of Silver + 1 Malachite = Silver Malachite Ring
1 Enchanted Bar of Silver + 1 Labis Lazuli = Silver Labis Lazuli
Ring
1 Enchanted Bar of Silver + 1 Turquoise = Silver Turquois
Bracelet
1 Enchanted Bar of Silver + 1 Hematite = Silver Hematite Ring
1 Enchanted Bar of Silver + 1 Cat's Eye Agate = Silver Cat's Eye Necklace
1 Enchanted Bar of Silver + 1 Bloodstone = Silver Bloodstone
Earring
1 Enchanted Bar of Silver + 1 Onyx = Silver Onyx
Bracelet
1 Enchanted Bar of Silver + 1 Jasper = Silver Jasper Ring
1 Enchanted Bar of Silver + 1 Carnelian = Silver Carnelian
Wedding Ring
1 Enchanted Bar of Silver + 1 Star Rose Quartz = Silver Rose Engagement Ring
1 Enchanted Bar of Silver + 1 Amber = Silver Amber Ring

Notice even though I used enchanted siver for all of the above no magical
jewelry was produced. Apparently a more expensive type of metal must be
used to get magic jewelry out of the above stones.
Below is a short list of the magic jewelry that I have created using
enchanted silver.

1 Enchanted Bar of Silver + 1 Jade = Jaded Silver Ring,
Effect: MANA +5
1 Enchanted Bar of Silver + 1 Pearl = Silvered Pearl Ring,
Effect: VS POISON +4
1 Enchanted Bar of Silver + 1 Topaz = Topaz Silver Necklace,
Effect: VS DISEASE +4
