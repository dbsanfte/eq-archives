As you are on Faydwer, I would recommend Butcherblock...you can kill skunks,
spiders, goblins...then dwarf skels, basilisks and bats.,..finally move onto
the chessboard for undead pawns around level 10 or slightly earlier grouped.
Alternatively, go to the Nybright camp in LFay around lvl 10...good xp and
they drop bronze weapons which sell for around 2pp each to merchants. You
could try Crushbone around level 8 but it tends to get very crowded and with
DVinn and Crush around, you are likely to get killed by a train or two.
