Subject: Enchanting Item Spells


The perfect enchanting item spell can be taken from a MUD I used to play
on. It is ALWAYS useful, has a limited duration, and is a constant
supply of income.

Featherweight:
High mana cost(150 or so)
20th level or so.

Reduces the weight of an item it is cast upon by 1/2. Lasts until the
player with the item logs off. Item has to be in the enchanter's hand
to have the spell cast upon it.

People would pay out the wazoo for this, and once you had a loyal
customer, they wouldn't go anywhere else.

"Can you featherweight my rubicite, total stranger?"

I've been wanting this one for a while.
