Yes, the enchanter is a group class, you can turn a party of 5 into a
nonstop killing machine. The power of an enchanter doesn't really shine
until later levels, tho they do quite nicely at earlier levels. What I mean
by that is people can party for a long time and take other classes or an
enchanter, it may not matter much to them.

But major runs at high levels, you end up hearing this "our enchanters are
not online, so we wont be able to go to the planes".

Yes, our class is just like clerics. You cannot do planes, dragons,
phinigel, well at all without your enchanters.

But I would suggest grouping, because understanding how to be most effective
in a group is important in these later levels when your group has 24 people.
Solo at higher levels... take on full rooms of blue mobs, take on live
tower, the ghoul sage or magi, etc solo when you are 50...because you will
be one of the few classes that actually can.
