Mesmerize is different than Lull. Mesmerize stops creatures from attacking
for a second, and is useful in groups when a creature "locks on" to you.
Back off after you mesmerize and it might start attacking someone else in
your group.

Lull makes a creature "want" to not attack you. Say there are 4 orcs in a
camp, and you can only take 1 at a time. Lull three and then lure the
fourth out of the camp. The others won't follow if lull was successful. If
it was... start running as they all will come running ;)
