At 21 I could solo the aviaks in lake rathe or a rook in South Karana. Buff
me and pet, med to full, quickness on pet. Enthrall target. If not resisted,
tashini, languid pace, disempower then med till enthrall breaks, right at
edge of spell casting range. Cast choke as it comes in, let it hit me so the
pet gets on it, cast whirl at down 2 bubbles of health and back off. Recast
whirl is it comes at me, toss in a DD as mana available. Shouldn't be too
difficult to find a group doing harriers at 21 either. Me and a necro could
solo a harrier at 22. No clue on Kunark zones under 35. I did treants till
29, only druids you get KOS with are the ones in Surefall Glade and at East
and North Karana druid rings, have had no problems at any of the other druid
rings. When I need to be ported to North K by a druid I just invis myself
for the ride. Did the end of 29, 30, and into 31 in HighKeep, spent the rest
of 31 and some of 32 in Unrest in the ghost room. Went to Cazic till I was
34 (look for archon base or top groups or grid (sewer) groups). Hit Lguk at
34 and been there since, almost 45 now and doing my magic in Lord room.
