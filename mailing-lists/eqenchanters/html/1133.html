Subject: Jewelcrafting and an idea


Well I've been making rings, earrings, bracelets since the beginning of the
public release, and I have to agree that there are some serious problems
with what we can make. I'm fortunate enough that I can enchant my own
metals, but I find that very few are interested in my wares. Strangely, I
have been getting requests from higher level characters to make them as
many jade rings as I can, perhaps they know something I dont. I sure have a
hard time selling them on my own though.

Anyways, what I wanted to put out there was an idea. Wouldnt it be
wonderful if we could not only enchant the metal, but the stone as well? I
think a +5 mana ring with 5 charges of shocking grasp would sell quite
well, or a +2vsFire ring with 1 charge of laying hands, or some other
combination. This is just an idea, but one I would love to see
implemented. I suspected that perhaps there was a way to scribe a spell
onto paper, and put this into the extra slots but I havent figured that one
out yet.

Another note... does anyone know what good the other 2 slots in our
jewelers kit are for? I have tried several combinations of things in these
slots, and other than losing a whole lot of money I did not learn anything
at all, except that it is a good way to waste money.
