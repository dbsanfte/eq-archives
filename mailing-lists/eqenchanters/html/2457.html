I had great luck in North Karanna with Pincer beetles. There are alot of
griffawns, griffeenes, and a few griffons not to mention Hill Giants.
However, if you fight near the guard tower near the gypsies the guards can
help you out. I died twice in a few levels, once to a second wisp that
snuck up on me when killing the first wisp and once by a hill giant that I
was watching fight two guards. I was drawn to attack it because it only had
the teeniest sliver of life left after workin the guards but of course I was
stupid and took 2 hits and died without landing a single point of damage.
Too bad I didn't remember my damage shield heh. Anyway, if you don't mind
killing bears and wolves there are LOTS of them in NK too. Not too many
people, high level people who can occassionly help you, and places to sell
nearby for non evil people I believe. Wolves and bears would probably be
better around 12 and pincer beetles as you hit 13 or 14. Be careful of
borer and scythe beetle which will help the pincers if you get to close.
