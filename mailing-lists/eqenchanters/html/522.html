Hail!

Let me say first off, your training points are VERY VERY valuable!! Use the
as wisely as you possible can. If you are not going to be serious about
jewel craft, dont use any there at all. I would highly suggest saving them
for Research and Speicalize and Dodge. As for Speicalize, once again, save
em if your about to get level 20 or already have, for at this time it is
broken. The way I understand it, specialize will allow you to raise your
schools of magic higher than currently available. As for Dodge, only put 1
training point to it, it will improve on it's own just fine if you keep
your melee skills up. As for the levels past 22, you will have very little
use for them excluding the luxuries such as languages, fishing (HA!) and
whatever. To comment about Research, it is the easiest skill to raise in
the game, yet hardest to find the pages we need to raise the skill. Unless
you want to be the last person to have the spells obtained by Research, I'd
highly suggest pouring all the points you can into this and buying the tomb
of Endless Enchantments (the red book). As for keeping your different
schools of magic max ed, that is simple enough with proper attention to
your current skill and making sure they are max ed. I personally feel it is
a total waste of training points to put them toward anything that will max
out on its own with just a little effort.
