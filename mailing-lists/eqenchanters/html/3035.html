Oasis is so crowded, and not the best learning place to learn how to be a
good enchanter for your later lvls (20 through 60, depending where you hunt
from 55 to 60). If I were you I would suggest going to Unrest behind the
house. Never hunt in front of the house too many trains. The back side of
the house is good place for your lvl and you can solo there too until you
can get a group. Learn your spells and what they actually do. You will be
one step ahead of everyone else anyways because, at least on my server,
most enchanters are power leveled up these days and that's one class if you
dont know what you are doing you cant fake it.
