The EASY way to get a runed totem staff is goto Lake Rathe Tear. It's
connected to the Mountians of Rathe or South Karana zones. There is a spawn
near the South Karana zone entrance of 11 gnolls. High shaman gnolls spawn
there that very often drop runed totem staffs. The camp there spawns undead
by night, gnolls by day. It supports lvl's 7-22. Enjoy!!
