Yes and no. Skellies will help each other, much like spiders will. It is easy
to avoid this since they are not static spawns. They will drop and wander.
The shores are safe and I have never been jumped there. Its kind of eerie
fighting within visual range of Spectres though. Fortunately, their aggro
range is small, and they do not wander.. I spent all of last night there (on
Brell) with a 20ish monk, and a 20ish Mage. They were soloing the gargoyles
while I took out the Skellies. It was truelly one of the most pleasurable EQ
experiences I had. 2 hours, a boat load of cash and exp, and not once did I
even come close to meeting my maker. Heck, I didn't even lose my pet.
GOODBYE OASIS!

To be fair, the loot still didn't compare to Karana bandits though.
