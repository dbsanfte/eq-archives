From: cdhenness@... (Chris Hennessy)

See Gregor in the inn near the dock in east Freeport who wants some
items in exchange for a brewers book.

He will tell you to collect four items.

Some nuts from the Ocean of Tears. They
are for sale on the first dock you come to for about 2gp.

Another item is Honey Jum from Rivervale. Get three
honeycombs from Bixies, auctions, or merchants and give them with
30gp (3pp usually works) to the alchemist opposite the armoror
(the one with Mangler the dog) in RV. She will give you the Honey
Jum.

Off to Sunny Qeynos. In the pool under the temple (flying saucer)
there is koalindl fish that you need to kill and get a corpse. This
is tricky as it ruins your faction. I created a lvl 1 warrior (mule)
and had no trouble killing the fish. Just check that the fish has a
corpse as loot then change to your enchanter. Loot the corpse and
grab the fish.

Erud's Tonic is in Erudin, not surprisingly. Buy an Innoruk's Kiss of
Death from the Blue Hawk Inn and go to the library. Give IKD to
Sinekin Highbrow on the top level and you get Erud's Tonic.

Back to Gregor in Freeport. I start by Alliancing him but that may
not be necessary. Give him all four items. After he gets them all he will
give you the brewer's book. Now the fun part.

Travel to Oggok. Yes you are KOS there (except for some DEs). NO
problem, you ARE an enchanter after all. Illus Dark Elf and invis
yourself from South Ro until you get to Oggok. Go and see Clurg the
bar Tender. He is in the Meet and Drink Tewby bar. Alliance him and
give him the book ... you should get the
Stein of Moggok in return. Gate your butt out of there )

Great quest, great item, great fun. EVERY enchanter should do this
quest )
