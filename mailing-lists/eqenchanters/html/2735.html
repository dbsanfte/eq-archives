Assuming you have Kunark:
I agree that Lake of Ill omen is the place to go around 22. My enchanter
has leveled from 20 to 26 in that zone and can easily keep going there until
30.

Some good spots at 22 in the lake are:
first binding: probly best to bind at the lake / mountain zone since this
is close to area where you will find groups
solo - dunno very dangerous solo and the zone is pretty big
small group - healer and tank partners - on west lake shore toward south or
south lake shore toward west (hint: clouds go west)
full group - outside sarnak fortress near bridge or in front of fortress or
pulling to lake / mtn zone

once you hit the upper 20s head to frontier mountains for brutes and
mountain giants in full group. At 26 they con from blue to red.

Finally on Kunark, don't be fooled by thinking Firona Vie is the place for
you since it's the first zone you hit in the new world assuming you come on
the boat. That zone is a nightmare and you'll easily die to a host of
things till you are around 30. I'm sure some people have made it work but
honestly the lake of ill omen is so much less stressful and rewarding.

No Kunark:
full group in upper guk or cazic thule. enchanters are sought in CT for
train stopping abilities and there are lots of lizards.
I recommend these place only if you don't have Kunark. If you have the
expansion go to the new lands!

hope that helps a bit.
