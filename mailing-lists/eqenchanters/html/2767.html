So alteration seems to be the suggestion. I accidently specialized in
alteration and was upset till I read this thread. Now I see it makes sense.
One thing I haven't seen mentioned though is that from my experience (and
that of a Shaman friend) you must put 1 point into each specialization
discipline before any of them will go up. My friend put a point only in the
one he wanted and never saw an increase until he took my advice and put
point into all the others.

If you think about it though this makes sense. You should specialize in
whatever you do the most which is determined by your style. So, as you cast
your most common spell for your style you will get increases in that
specialization. I cast Tashani ro pull and during battles when I'm mezing
so that was my most common spell.
