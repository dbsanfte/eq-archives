Ah Robequest! Such is the life of the enchanter, as it is with most magic
classes I suppose (except those nasty clerics... its not fair!)

Level 5 you should still be wearing a Jr. or Sr. apprentice robe... these
are NO-DROP items, so when that lvl 8 PK sees you he wont be interested in
your wares(I play on Zek). I have seen no end to the amout of level 10 and
less characters wearing the Flowing Black Robe, or Thamurgists robe, when
its impossible that they got them themselves. Now sure some get them as
gifts, and there's nothing wrong with that I suppose, but wouldnt you
treasure that robe more if you'd actually EARNED it?

The Thamurgists robes can be found in Befallen, down deep in its bowels, but
dont go looking for it until you're at least 15 and in a nice sized group.
The Flowing Black Robe is well beyond the reach of a level 5 character, so I
wouldnt worry about it just yet.... Also, if you get a chance head to Guk
for armours... you'll find many woven pieces inside if you are persistent,
and these are perfect for a magic user. You'll find this task fairly easy
on any server except Rallos Zek, where the ogres and trolls will eat you
alive if you're not a dark elf. I went in as a gnome disguised as a dark
elf, and everything was fine until my illusion wore out mid fight, as trolls
apparently dont like illusions very much. It was all I could do to
pacify/entertain them with minor illusion and charm to keep myself out of
their stew pot!

Cogg, lvl 18 gnomish enchanter
