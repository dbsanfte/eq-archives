Subject: Re: Decking out my Enchanter

On Fri, 9 Jun 2000, Melinda wrote:

> Im lvl 21, hi elf ench.. Currently Im wearing the gold robe from
> erudin and a nice lvl 50 mage gave me a staff of writhing.

Staff of writhing is ONLY good if you're killing very light greens (like
skeletons too dumb to leave you alone). It's got good dmg/delay, and the
proc (at L29) is nice, but -9 CHA is very painful.

If you have some L25+ friends to group with, you can probably get a rod of
insidious glamor (+12 CHA) or incandescent wand (procs color shift) from
Sultin in Solusek Ro.

Of course, for many enhanter situations, the 2-stein plan (crude and
moggok) is better than an actual weapon.

I agree. I use stein of mogok and crude stein for selling or when my charm
based attacks are important. At other times I use the insidious rod with a
crude stein. I was able to get the rod myself with the hardest part coming
from upper guk (priest I think). I patiently waited for a chance to loot
the golden rod.

you'll want to go for +cha and +int items with +cha being more important I
think; but it really depends on the situation. I have a savants cap, green
silken drape, and incandescent gloves (which are a very easy quest). Next
I'd like to quest for the incandescent mask but the rune in mistmoore will
be a bit of a pain I think.

I would buy gold or plat int or cha jewelry when you can collect the cash.
