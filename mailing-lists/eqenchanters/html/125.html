This is something of great debate. I personally do not experience any more
resists, etc than any other magi class, and I have a Charisma of 95,
however, many others talk all the time about being resisted 50% of the time
on whites and lower. For me the number is only around 20-25% of the time, so
I believe it does matter. You can also save a LOT of money on buying and
sell stuff and a much higher profit with a high Charisma. Everything is a
trade off in this game, so you have to decide what is important to you.
