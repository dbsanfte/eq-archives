I also did really well fighting Scarabs and Tarantulas in North Ro at that
level. With a pet and root you should be able to solo the yellows there.

Also, if you're up for the hike (and while you're at it questing) Try
blackburrow. At level 9 I did the Stein of Mogguk quest for the first time.
To increase my faction in Qeynos (there were a few that would kill me without
illusions) I fought in Blackburrow until level 10. With a group you can handle
the Elite Knoll Guards. They drop excellent loot and are great exp (they will
con red to you). You can also sell in Surefall with illusion wood elf and
alliance, and get excellent prices. Sure beats the run back to Qeynos :)

Also, if you can find a mule to do the Rivervale part of the quest (if you're
planning on doing the stein quest that is) Try the halfling camps in Nektulos.
Great exp, not too much loot though. You should be able to solo the lowest
level camp with Soothe and a pet. If you can get a group try one of the higher
level halfling camps. With soothe you will be able to handle them one at a
time. (side note: I killed a LOT of halflings, and when I got my illusion
halfling at level 12 I wasn't KOS in rivervale after casting alliance on the
one NPC that was still threatening to me. I stopped killing halflings at the
camps around level 12 though..)

Hope this helps :)
