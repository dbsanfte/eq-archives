Ok I've read a lot of things about protesting for the Enchanter's
cause...lots of ideas on how to protest but shouldn't we come up with a list
of balance issues that we can send to Verant.
The main issue seems to be the resistibility of our spells. Here is what I
think. All our spells are tested against Magic Resistance where other
classes spells are tested against Fire or Cold Resistance. If you are in
Lavastorm Mountain, all your fire based spell wont even harm a green
creature. So the trade off is that our spells can affect creatures of any
kind. The problem is that most of the magic-users get an extremely good MR
due to spells. Another problem is the fact that our damage spells dont do
any damage when resisted. Right now, our spells are almost never resisted by
greens, rarely by blues, rarely by whites ( although I could not cast a
single spell on Dorn when he was white to me ), sometimes resisted by
yellows, resisted 20-30% of the time by the reds that are one level above
yellows and resisted 100% by all other reds.
Here is my list of suggestions:
1) Damage spells should at least do some damage when resisted following
similar rules as the wizard spells with a lesser ratio maybe to keep a
balance with those easy-to-kill wizards,
2) Our spells should not jump from a 20-30% resistance to a full 100%
resistance, there should be a nicer ramp so that an enchanter is not
completely useless in a group of 6 people attacking one strong red target.

I dont have other suggestions because I personnaly think that enchanter is a
very strong class. I have an enchanter level 16 now and I had a necro level
16 before and my enchanter is almost as good in solo ( nothing can beat the
triple dots+feign death+let pet finish the critter combo of the necro ), and
much much better ( much more useful would be the word ) in groups. I am just
annoyed that sometimes I have to watch my friends kill reds for me while I
cant even cast a spell to help except buffs.

my 2 centimes,
Skara
