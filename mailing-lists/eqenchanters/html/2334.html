>>3) How does Alliance work? Does it always raise one faction level (e.g.
>>dubious to apprehensive)? Does it decrease faction with negative faction
>>groups (e.g. if I cast it on a deathfist orc, will it make me less liked
>>by the commons residents)?

One thing to note here in addition to what others have said. You can have
alliance (not sure about benevolence) cast on only one target "group" at a
time. In other words if you alliance a guard you are good with all guards
in the target guard's group. However, if you then decide to alliance a
merchant you lose the alliance on the guard group so keep track of who you
target. Finally, a counter between you and your target counts as a wall and
will block your spell.
