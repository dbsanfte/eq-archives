I would just like to add that the new mes series change rocks!
Goodbye mesmerize forever! See, I used to always use enthrall, but I would
add mes to my list as a back up in case enthrall was resisted, so i didn't
have to wait 5 seconds for a recast. I also used both when fighting tough
battles such as the Ghoul Lord or King. AE mesmerization is reserved for
trains, and weaker mobs. I learned my lesson when I tried to AE mesmerize 5
Imp Protectors. With everyone resisting and knocking me dead in a little
over 1 round (they were all red to me at the time). Instead, when fighting
really tough battle I pick them off 1 at a time, that way if it is resisted
I only have to deal with 1 mob attacking me at a time. And with 4 other Imps
beating on my party I need to act quickly.

So now I have Entrance as my main spell, and Enthrall as a back up, and in 1
level I will be using Dazzle/Entrance. I can't wait to use dazzle, because
entrance has kept most things entranced for 60+ seconds.
