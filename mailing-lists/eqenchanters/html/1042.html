well I heard that wisdom helps a lot with failures both in jewelry and
spells. For my character, I maxed out intelligence to 142 and I've put the
remaining 5 in charisma. If I need more charisma later, there are spells for
it. I can also make rings for only when I sell or buy. If I was to redo my
character, I would probably put some points in wisdom instead of charisma.
Stamina is kind of useless. You get more hp which can help a bit at low
level, but you really dont care about it at high levels. If everything goes
well, you wont get hit in a fight or just a bit. If it turns wrong, your
extra points from charisma wont save you, but extra mana given by
intelligence will. In the long run, high charisma is not an issue. You get
spells at level 20 that give you a high enough charisma ( at least I would
guess, I'm just at 18 ).
Now choosing Quellious instead of Agnostic was a bad idea. Although I didnt
know about it, so I guess I'm lucky I didnt pick it up. I had a high level
dark-elf necro before and I was tired to get KOS all the time, so when I had
the opportunity to pick agnostic, I jumped on it.
restarting your char? level 9 is still pretty low. You can make it back in a
week or a week and a half. Higher levels take time. If you want to restart
it, restart it now! Level 12 is the level at which enchanters really start
to shine ( just a bit ). As long as you're under that, it's ok. Level 11 was
a nightmare for me, but since I passed 12, I've been flying through levels.
my 2 cents,
Skara
