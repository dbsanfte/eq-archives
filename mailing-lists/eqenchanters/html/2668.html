Ulgarth,
I like the easy questions. Here are some answers.

1. At level 8(?) you will get Illusion Dark Elf. This will make them like
you enough not to squish you. If you can't wait until then, you can invis
yourself and run quickly.

2. You can only have one pet at a time. Animated or charmed.

3. Charmed pets do follow commands.

4. Not sure what you are asking here. The spell duration is short, maybe
15 secs or less.

5. Your pet stands on what you are looting because it is so dumb that it
can not understand simple commands like "guard here". It will always
happen. On a reality note, your pet will follow invisible "pathing".
Experiment with where you stand until you are able to loot with out your pet
being in the way.

6. Agnostic is the BEST choice you can make as an enchanter. Choosing any
religion will be a hindrance later on.

7. Practice "timing" your activities. Use a second hand to know how long
your Mez line of spells lasts. Be aware that every 6 seconds, the checks to
see if you are meditating or not and adjusts your mana bar appropriately.
This means that you can be medding during a battle, get up cast a spell, sit
down and med again, and if you were fast enough, not loose any mana
regeneration.
