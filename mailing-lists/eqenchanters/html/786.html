Depends. If I'm soloing I keep my latest pet spell memorized at all
times, recasting it and soon as my last one died. If I'm in a group I
don't usually even use my pet, unless it's a small group.
I usually have
1. Suffocating sphere (good dot spell before choke small mana use)
2. Chaotic Feedback (terrible mana use, but great when you need to
finish something off quick)
3. Tashan (always, resist sucks)
4. Mesmerize (The best spell before level 12. Period)
5. Color Flux (emergency spell, very useful, sucks when you realize you
need it and don't have it mem'd)

The last three depend on the makeup of my group, if I have a lot of
casters with me, I use Root, Fear and Mercyl's animation.
A lot of tanks will mean I use Enfeeble, Strengthen, and Fear.
If soloing I'll use Root, Mercyl's Animation, and Gate.
