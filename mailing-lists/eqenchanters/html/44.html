Hi there,

I started a high elf enchanter this weekend and enjoyed playing in
Greater Feydark. The loot is better I think which is important for
saving money for your upper level spells. Neriak, the dark elf city is
the other place I've started a character and know well. It is a long
walk in (three zones - forest to foriegn to commons to 3rd gate) to get
to the inner part of the city which is sometimes too long for me.
You'll die at the hands of NPCs alot as a dark elf but it's sure nice
seeing in the dark! I'm used as a scout alot.

Near Felwithe:
The giant wasp drones are a great source of income; selling their wings
brings me 1 silver apiece (with 110 charisma). Also, the orcs in this
area are wimpy. Orcs are a great source of weapons and cloth armor
(which you can wear). Weapons will sell for half a gold to more than a
gold all the way up to almost 1 plat for a cracked staff. Cloth armor
sells for 1.5 to 2 gold a piece I think.
