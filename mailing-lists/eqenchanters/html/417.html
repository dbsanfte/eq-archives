ok, i can help ya here, sorta. i came from qeynos to freeport, going
through highpass hold.

you go up to east karana, i usually followed the coast in the plains
because i went that way to SplitPaw. when you get to the first bridge, the
one that takes you to the zone with splitpaw, dont take that bridge.

follow the river to the second bridge. be careful of raiders that gather on
the coasts, they may con blue to you but they travel in large packs. when
you reach the second bridge(you willknow it's the right bridge, it is
fairly impressively built), cross it- you will zone. this is where if you
arent bad ass, you should cast invis on yourself. do NOT stop to fight
anything if you are below lvl 12. i suggest if you have bind, bind yourself
to this bridge, in case you die.

over the bridge, past a farm on your right (keep it on your right), there
is a path. follow that path till you hit a valley. watch out for griffs and
other baddies.

take the higher road (to the right) of the valley and follow it AAAALLLLL
the way to highpass hold. once you get into highpass hold, i beleive i
tried to stay to the left as much as possible trying to get around the
highpass maze. eventually you will get to highkeep, and inside there in one
of the rooms is someone selling all the animation spells.

hope this helps.
