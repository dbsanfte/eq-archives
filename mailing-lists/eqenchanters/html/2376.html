Subject: Soloing at 16


Orcs in the Rathe Mountain are great to solo at 16. Go in from the ferrott,
take the first right just inside the zone. There's a plateau in the middle
of the open area. One end of the plateau has an orc camp on it with 3 orcs,
the other hand has a paladin camp. Alliance the paladins, illusion human or
high elf if you are a dark elf, enthrall one of the orcs (vital to enthrall
the cleric if there is one, because he will cast on your pet and pull the
pet), run the other two to the paladins who will kill them. Repeat if need
be. At 16, I could then keep the camp beat down, plus there's a gypsy vendor
by the paladins for when you get loaded down. The orcs drop bronze weapons
and rawhide armor. Good cash and experience till 17 or 18.
