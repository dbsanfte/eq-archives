Indeed, steamfont is a great place to spend L9-12 or so. Watch for the
minotaur hero who comes out to that area and will kill you quickly. Also,
if incompetent people are hunting the mino caves, you can get pretty bad
slaver trains or a L30 necro (whose name I currenty forget) pulled from
the cave.

I'd also recommend Kurn's tower in Field of Bone starting around level 10.
The scalebone skellies in there are L8 or so, the greater skellies are
12-14, and the greater scalebones 11-13. You'll find challenges here up
to level 22 or so. Some of the other Kunark newbie areas (Warsliks, Lake
of Ill Omen) are also pretty sweet. The only problem is you can't use
Cabilis facilities until L20, so take a lot of supplies to last you
between trips to the evil outpost or FV.
