I have heard that as long as you are in good standing with the Knights of
Truth, you can take the quest, or at least complete the quest if you are too
high level to start it. The inforamtion below should help a little.

I do not know for sure if other Paladin Guilds have similar quests, but I for
one think they should.

For those Paladins who are above level 8 and want to start the Test of Truth
you can ask a lower level Paladin to get you the starting note, or (and I
don't really support this) I am told you can jump in on the quest by giving
Altunic Jartin a Medium Cloth Tunic. Altunic is located in East Commonlands,
and he is the one you normally give the note to. Doing this will skip a few
steps, and get you the first token, but I don't consider it very Honorable.
However, if this is your only option because you found out about the quest
late, and can't find a lower level Paladin in Freeport to help, than I still
believe it is every Paladin's right to TRY the Test of Truth.

Just make sure when you give Altunic the note, or if you just give him the
Tunic that he is in his house. It makes the quest much easier (you don't run
the risk of someone else killing the squire first). Altunic returns to his
house near the zone to West Freeport about every 15 minutes, and stays there
for around 5 minutes.
