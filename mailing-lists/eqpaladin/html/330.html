Grouping:
One of the best resources on grouping in EverQuest I have found:
http://eq.stratics.com/group.shtml

I do not 'hail' my opponents in order to have the group directed at a specific
targed, instead I use /g to communicate with my group on my intended target.
Some of my group members are higher level than I am, so it makes sense to use
the more powerful characters to triangulate towards the most powerful opponent.
If there is more than one target, and since my group tends to cluster up, it's
usually as simple as saying 'target the (orc/goblin/bandit/etc) on the left.'
Once the group knows definitevely which opponent to target (this usually only
takes a minute) and us tank-types are focused on the same target, we charge in
and begin smacking the beastie about. My order of preferred targets is:
1. whichever opponent does the most damage
2. any spell-casting opponents
3. any poisonous or diseased opponents
4. anything else

This method has worked pretty good overall so far. My next refinement is to
work on who gets targetted after the initial assault. I understand there is a
command to assist a party member, so I imagine using assist to have all
tank-types flogging the appropriate beastie.
