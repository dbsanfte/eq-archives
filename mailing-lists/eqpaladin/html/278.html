Subject: The fourth holy sword... (some spoiler information on the first 3 , but well known)


Well, thanks to one of our brothers, we've found out the stats on Ghoulbane - the first item on the agenda for every young paladin.

You should start thinking about putting a party together to retrieve Ghoulbane at about L30, or a little earlier if you have high level friends.

The Fiery Avenger is, of course, the L50 pipe dream. I have heard that you retrieve this off a fierce red dragon. Presumably this dragon is located in Solusek.

A third holy sword is Soulfire. This requires you to defeat Pietro Zarn in Freeport, as part of the Zimel's blades quest. Again, another L50 pipe dream.

Now... I have heard that there is another holy sword - the fourth in the collection.

Does anyone have any information on this fourth sword?

I can speculate that it's in the depths of Unrest - the underground level is fierce indeed, at least to mine eyes - or Mistmoore. Each would surely prove worthy locations for a paladin to claim the ultimate prize.
