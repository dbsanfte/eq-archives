There are also good magical 2h weapons like bone bladed claymore (17/45,
costs around 250pp, but i heard you can get it in Befallen for as low as
150pp) or blackened iron bastard sword (20/47, costs around 500pp, maybe
less in Runnyeye). But till 20 lvl your damage is limited to ~35, as
soon as you will raise to 20 you will start make 40+ damage with BBC or
even more with BIB.


> > 5) What types of armor should I be looking for?
> Full bronze plus magic.

It is better to buy bronze armor in zones where monsters drop it. Just
make sure these monsters drop bronze of your size (for example, in
Unrest monsters drop small sized bronze). But for your level, it is
better to loot bronze, not buy it :) With good group you can easely kill
dry bones, and get bronze and exp simultaneously. You can even solo them
at ~20 level.
