The easier minotaurs are in the minotaur caves in the Steamfont zone, which
is on the other continent, Faydwer. But, they aren't "easy" by any means at
6th level; I wouldn't try them solo.

There used to be lesser minotaurs in Beholder, a.k.a. the Gorge of Grobb,
over by Runnyeye and Misty Thicket, by Rivervale, but now I hear there are
only those very tough minotaurs :(

Easiest route is to buy one, around 5 platinum, from another player.
