The hammer spell is the same one that clerics get at level 9. It summons a
hammer, with about the same stats as a rusty one, maybe better, but it can
hit things that can only be hit by magic and magic items likes wisps. Its a
pretty good spell, especially for backup incase you die, and need a quick
weapon to help you get your body back.

I would suggest, though, that you put some skill points in the magic needed
to summon it, one point just won't do it. I forget which one it is off
hand, but none of our other spells from the 1st or 2nd circle use that magic
skill. There is also another magic skill that is never used until the spell
Ward Dead, evocation maybe, that you may want to put point into.
