Subject: Archery + Fletching


All right! This is gonna be great! Do you realize you can knock off
about 90 HP before the monster even gets to you thanks to archery? Well, I
talked to my ranger friend and I got a few tips for you paladins out there
(including me) who are going to start using bows for combat.

1) Find Pine Needles or a Tiny Dagger. Both use archery skill but they are a
melee weapon. Hang out in a place where there are blue mobs that other
people fight, such as trainer hill or somesuch area, and (after getting their
permission) attack the monster with the pine needle/tiny dagger. They won't
mind because you can only do one dmg with it.

2) Spend points on fletching and try to use it as much as possible, making
regular arrows with no skill in fletching is still a 50% (?) chance of
success. Also making your own arrows costs 4 cp while buying them is ~7-10.
Oh and if you hunt in blackburrow the gnolls occasionally drop arrows and
worn hunting bows.

3) Speaking of dropped bows, don't use them! Even the worst of the player
made bows are better than the mightiest NPC bow (except for magical, but I
mean regulars). From what he told me, most sell for 6-12 plat. I think he was
referring to the easiest ones to make.

4) Once you get high enough (22 for us, 9 for rangers) to root your opponent,
you will know the true power of archery. You can easily bring him down to
half health with only a few arrows. This will also make up for our lack of DD
spells vs. living creatures, both as a lure and finisher and even combat
support. Though melee is our forte' having some ranged skill will be
undoubtedly helpful.

5) When buying a playermade bow, look for the best dmg. If you have a choice
between a 9 dmg 40 delay and an 11 dmg 50 delay (theoretically speaking. I
don't know the actual stats) you should choose the higher damage one because
when you have no root spell and they jump on you, you want to hit them for as
much as possible in the time allotted.

6) Read this guide, it is one of the best I have ever found:
http://everquest.gamestats.com/Skills/Archery.htm

And for fletching:
http://everquest.gamestats.com/Skills/Fletching.htm

It is a shame that I can't find the URL for the Crossroads of
Norrath, they had an amazing guide about fletching. Showed you the costs
required and compared the dmg caused by a bow to a great staff.

Oh, and a little trick that my friend taught me. If you consider
this an exploit or something, tough. It is not. Put all of your containers
in the bank except for your fletching kit. Take your food and put one food
in each of your slots on your main inventory, so you'll have the kit and 7
food/water on your inventory. Now goto the fletching guy and buy one supply
of each needed for the arrow, click combine and Vwala (or however you say it)
you have an arrow without having to click 500 times. Put the arrow on your
ammo slot and do it again. See? That wasn't an exploit!

Goodluck everyone, I really do suggest you try archery as one of your
combat skills. It will pay off in the end, I assure you.
