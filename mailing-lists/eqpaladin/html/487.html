A lot of the higher level undead can only be hit by magic weapons. Don't go
to Befallen or Unrest without one. The ice boned and dry boned skeletons
can only be hit by magic and are nasty enough to be a tough fight even with
a magic weapon. I wouldn't want to go against one of those guys without
anything but my hardest hitting weapon. I think I started seeing a lot of
them around level 16 or so.

By the way, I never even bought the weapon summoning spells because I had my
combine by then, could never keep my 1-H blunt trained up, and would do more
damage with my magic sword anyway. I have not missed those spells. I
always have backup weapons in the bank for when I die and lately (level 20)
often have a magic backup weapon tucked away.
