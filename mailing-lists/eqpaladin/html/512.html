The only bronze that I personally have obtained have been from undead in
Unrest. Specifically from Dry-Boned Skeletons and Barbed Skeletons.
Unfortunately, at 12th level, those beasts would probably make short work of
you. Dry boned frequently /con black or yellow to me and barbed skeletons
almost always /con red. I can solo some of the dry boned with full mana and
LOH available, but I would stand no chance against a barbed with less than a
party of 3. I started adventuring in Unrest at 15th level, fighting
beetles, tormented dead and greater skeletons. Just stick to the right side
of the house and be careful of trains when you decide to go there. I would
wait until you get your repulse undead spell though, because that's when a
Paladin's ability against undead really begins to shine.
