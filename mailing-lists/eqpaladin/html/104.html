There are two ways to Riverval from Quynos. The one I reccomend is through
Karana. Follow the road straight up, or take the left side (safer but
longer) along the mountains, and run till you hit the coast. Then hook a
left. If you took the road, the road leads to it. What IT is, is a giant
Chainlink Bridge. Cross the bridge into the next Karana zone. Unless your
over level 17 don't go there with out invis or camoflauge because the
griffons, gnolls, and lion matriarchs will make short work of you. Follow
the road from the bridge to the entrance to Highpass gorge. Take the right
side of the pass, it goes up, and follow that to Higpass hold. No enemies
once you hit the gorge and take the highroad. Go through higpass and hook a
right up a narrow path way and just keep following the narrow turns. The
last leg of the journey is here. You must book through Higpass, the section
the High level orcs own. Invis is the best and SoW helps. From here you get
into a the woods, a low level woods. Follow the road to the right and you
are at Rivervale. These are a little vauge, but they work.

-Dycedarg
