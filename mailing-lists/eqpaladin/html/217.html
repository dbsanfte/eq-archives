Personally, I think that you want to keep both 1hs and 2hs maxed. Then, use the best sword that you have, irrespective of 1h or 2h.

I use a 2hs shiny brass halberd with pretty impressive stats and I hit for 34 34 often when my double attack clicks in. Compare that to 1hs combine long and bash... which does around 15 15 and 8 - All of those attacks... and it adds up to just 1 of my double hits. Also, bash is slowish to reset... meaning that you don't get to use it as quickly as your weapon.... making the comparison worse.

However, when I get Ghoulbane, I'll go back to 1hs - especially when fighting undead.

Then, after getting the Great Zweihander or Soulfire, I'll be back to 2hs.

Just depends on what is the best weapon that you have.

I think bash is pretty ordinary. Some people argue that it gives you time to get a heal off on yourself - once you hit L22 or higher... you're much better off rooting your opponent, backing back, getting in half a dozen heals, then rejoining the fray. Also, I don't bother using ward in combat with undead anymore either... unless to initiate an attack or kill a runner... so I don't need it to time my ward casting (and, if I do use ward, I normally dodge and parry myself out of trouble anyway).
