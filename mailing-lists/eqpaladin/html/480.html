I got my shiny brass shield at 16th, and my combine
longsword at 18th (I'm 19th in Unrest now); for two

was tired of having to run from ghouls that were blue

Newly armed with my enchanted arsenal, I took my
wizard and myself to Unrest and have since comenced to
group with others to beat back the hordes of undead

fun to fight with every shot going off while around me
dual wielding warriors and rangers, and other
paladins, are hitting with their offhands, or kicking
or bashing, for INVULNERABLE results.
