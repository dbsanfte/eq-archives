Most Mino Axes run about 4pp in the Kelethin/Steamfont area, as stated above
they are from the Mino Caves in Steamfont, if you want to get one the cheap
way i.e. go kill a Mino yourself I must stress the need for a group, as well
a Cleric would be important mainly for their cure disease. In Steamfont
there are diseased rats that LOVE to attack you when your in combat with
something else, this rat Disease lasts for about 30 mins (REAL TIME) and
during that time you will be realy hurting for HP's as I understand it you
won't regen HP during combat while diseased, and your max HP is choped, so
even when you heal your still in bad shape... The Rats are passive until
your fighting something else then they will join in. Another thing to worry
about is the Mino Hero who will just come out every once and a while to kill
anyone in sight :) he will KOS and I have watched him take out the Gnome
guards so at level 6, RUN if you hear of him being out in your area, or if
you see him.

I just ran through Beholder Maze last night on my way to quenos, I am a
level 11 High Elf Paladin, there were many Cavern Mino's walking around
they coned QUITE RED to me... so you would best avoid them unless your in a
Major group.
