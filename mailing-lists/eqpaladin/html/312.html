The premier shield is the Darkwood Shield obtainable from a named Gnoll in Highpass Hold, towards the Karana end. The area is continuously camped so bring a wizard friend, preferably of an obscene level. Between the rare spawn and the campers, do not be surprised if it takes you all day

The shield offers +9 to STR and AC12.

It's usefulness peaks at around L30 for a paladin when one obtains Ghoulbane. You are far better off using 2HS at lower levels and capitalise on double attack.
