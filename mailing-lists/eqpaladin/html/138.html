The back entrance to north freeport can be found very easily, if you are
standing at the gate entering the town/west freeport, looking INTO the town.
Follow the wall to your left. When the wall hits the corner you can walk
through a wall in that area that leads to the sewers, I walked through them
once and got lucky that i chose all the right paths, there are many to
choose from. You end up coming out at the fishing hut near the paladin
guild. There is also another sewer near the palaidin guild that leads to
east freeport. I guess this is so you can avoid guards all together. They
are really cool to walk through, I'm just shocked no thoughts of putting
monsters in there have happened yet.
