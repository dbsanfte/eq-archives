Comments from Llorfindal, L28 High Elf Paladin of Tunare:

To be frank, I find my low strength of 85 a real pain.

Consider a full suit of bronze plate, a shiny brass halberd, a lightstone (no weight) and 20 food and 20 water. The weight of that is 83.

That gives me a whopping 2 stones of carrying capacity. That will last about 1 minute in Cazic Thule.

The real test of strength is its impact on max damage at higher levels - is it worth it? Is AGI or DEX better?

My healing spells are useful if no cleric is on - I can get a small party together and we can cling on by our bootstraps for after battle healing, but the high mana doesn't help much since it's all post-battle and the high intelligence is overkill for skill gain - I max everything out by a third of the way into my first bubble each level. And that's with skills in the 140s.

But, as you say, the role playing potential is great.
