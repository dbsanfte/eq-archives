Hail fellow knights,

I'll try to answer you questions as best as I can. For the Ghoulsbane
stats, I've heard that its 1 handed, 15 dmg, 35 or 38 delay, and casts
dismiss undead for 140-150 pts of damage. As for a good area to fight,
try East Karana. There's a hill where gnoll reavers, war wolves, and
lions spawn during the day, and undead reavers and spirit stalkers at
night. Be warned though.......bring a group with you. There have been
times when my full party had 3 or 4 creatures on each person. Plus, the
guards at the farms will not kill spirit stalkers. The hill is
especially dangerous at night, but the exp is great. Good luck!
