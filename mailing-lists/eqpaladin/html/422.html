Subject: Archery is GOOD


It's not a cowardly weapon at all. In fact, I saved someone's life
yesterday while using a bow. He had a Kodiac chasing him in the commons and
I came to his rescue and hit the bear with a bow shot since there was no way
that I could have caught up to him. This distracted the bear long enough
for the rescuee to escape to safety. (of course I had a tough battle on my
hands and without my trusty Cleric sidekick, the bear would have probably
finished me as well) I would have to agree that it's not very paladin like
to stand back and pound shots into something while you let someone else
tank, but saving that person's life changed my attitude. Another nice thing
about the bow is that you can shoot while running. This you cannot do while
casting spells and while everyone likes root, I found that most of the time
the target would either resist it or run past my spell range (when I was
playing a class that had it)

-Bamphir of the 10th level on Tarrew Marr
