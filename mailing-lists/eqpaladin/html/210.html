I choose to play a paladin for power, plain and simple. I thought about
playing a ranger at first, but then decided on the paladin. Why? Because
the paladin has better buffs, better heals, and is better at physical combat.
As for the physical combat, the paladin has holy swords (I'm gonna try and
get Ghoulbane soon, pray for me) and is able to wear plate. Some might say
that rangers are able to dodge more and I agree with them, but if you put
more points into Agility when you make your character, you can have as much
if not more AC than a ranger. Remember that the armor they can wear is
limited to chain (well banded too) while a paladin can wear plate. Also at
15th we get lesser heal while they have to wait till 22nd to get it. We are
also the only hybrids able to cast Greater Heal for 400 HP.

What I want to know is why so many paladins are High Elves, the dwarves stats
are much better. Just put 10 in agility and 10 in intelligence and you'll
have one great warrior.

gotta admit that the charisma sucks though, but you can always have your
enchanter friend buy stuff for you =)

-Adaron, 17th level Paladin.
