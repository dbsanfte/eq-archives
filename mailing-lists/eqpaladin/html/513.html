Re: Unrest

At 12th you'll be fighting the 'yard trash'. Zoning
into Unrest, you follow the tunnel (it's a sewer like
tunnel, cool brickwork) and it opens out into a
grassed area with a front path that runs between a
pair of fountains/pools, then hedgemazes, then to the
front porch/door of the house. If you cut left or
right, you can go between the hedge mazes and the wall
to reach either side of the yard. Heading right
you'll find the gazebo; left you find the porch
entrance to the house, and both meet in the back at
the (oddly enough) back door.

On the yard surrounding the house is the 'yard trash',
Death Beetles, Jack-o-Lanterns, the occasional ghoul,
Greater Skeletons and Tormented Undead; all in the
10-13 -ish- range. Death Beetles will train onto you
if you're fighting undead, as will the other undead
near your fight if not otherwise occupied. A full low
level group (your level) can do pretty well cleaning
up the yard trash if you play smart. You'll also be
the love of the zone, as the higher levels get really
tired of pulling higher level stuff (20+ Lvl mobs
oftentimes) from the house and getting bogged down
with a handful of yard trash.

At about 15-18 -ish-, in a full group, you can start
pulling from the first floor. 19-23 -ish-, in a full
group, you can pull from the 1st or 2nd floors (but
make better loot and xp from the 2nd)

I haven't fought the 3rd floor or basement yet, but I
understand about 24-28 -ish--, in a full group, can
fight the third floor stuff; and that 29-35 -ish-, in
a full group, can fight in the basement. Beware the
Hag, uber nasty undead necromatic caster (with charm
and some really mean direct damage spells).

Drybone Skeletons and above (Skeletal Monks, I think,
and Barbed Bone Skeletons) drop small bronze. A lot
of things in the zone drop small rawhide and small
ringmail; and Carrion Ghouls and some other things
drop runes/words/pages, as well as Fine Steel weapons.

Unrest isn't a terribly hard zone to find a group in;
though sometimes you may have to be patient for a
while. DON'T solo here, even 20th Levels can have
problems trying to solo yard trash because more keeps
jumping in the fights. Fight at least in a pair, but
often you'll be in full groups, which is the only way
to fight Unrest.

You can walk into the zone in full leather and be in
full metal (ring and/or bronze) after a few days;
longer for full bronze. Most of the Unrest loot sells
pretty well in Butcherblock and Greater Faydark; how
else do you think low levels (sub 11 or so) get their
hands on rawhide and ringmail, and fine steel weapons?
Also, if you're a sub level looking for rawhide, ring
or fine steel, take cash and head to Unrest; a lot of
players will be happy to unload the encumberance for
case right there. You can get some good deals.

The best part of Unrest is no annoying doors and keys
to deal with. Befallen is seriously handicapped as a
fun zone because of the key thing.
