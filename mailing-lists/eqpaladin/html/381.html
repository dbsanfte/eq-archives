So it looks like there are 2 gypsy camps mentioned below:
Oasis - along coast till you hit dock and pillar. Turn inland till lake
on your left, gypsies on your right. Bad things - mainly Sand Giants

N Karana - Head inland from river at the point with the "spires" and the
bridge. Follow the road. Bad things - Griffins and Hill Giants

I know of two others not sure if they sell combine stuff or not:
Lavastorm - don't know their exact location or directions.

Rathe Mountains - from Lake Rathe entrance, follow left wall a long
ways. From Ferrott entrance follow right wall a little ways. Skip the
first right which leads back to a bad flying griffin looking thing with
a long egyptian sounding name. Ankhe....somethin.

3. The gypsies that sell combines (great investment) are located in the
Oasis of Marr. You have to zone south into the Oasis from Nro, walk
along the coast til you pass a small dock and see a largish pillar just
off the coast. When you see the pillar turn to the west and run down
the valley. At the end of the valley there will be a large lake to your
left and the gypsy camp to your right. The seller to the northwest has
the combines. A few words of warning. Sand Giants proliferate the area
so watch yer butts and take a friend with a high charisma to make the
actual purchase. I beleive with a 95 the cost for the longsword is
53-55 plat and a 2handed (my preference) is right around 112 plat. You
can also get them in North Karana but that's a long run from Fawdyer and
the prices are the same. In case your wondering where anyways, just
head north from the "white" bridge along the road, once again beware of
Griffins and Hill Giants. I've since moved on and am using a Shiny
Brass halberd (can hit for a max of 74 points a round with double attack
at my current level) but the combines are great for early teens.
