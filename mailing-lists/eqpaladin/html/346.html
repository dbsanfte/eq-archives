Aye, when you come out of Felwithe, the high elf city, make a left on the
path and take it past the first turn off (that leads to the Lesser Faydark),
keep going, thru the big white spires (those are actually points to which
higher level wizards gate), stick to the left when the path forks, and that
should lead to a rocky tunnel-like entrance to the Butcherblok mountains.
Go thru that and you will zone into Butcherblok, which is the land outside
the Dwarven city of Kaladim. There are 2 NPC Ogres who run about the path
in Butcherblok at times, and they are dangerous at your level; avoid them or
just run back thru the zone where you came if you see them coming. There
are also usually large snakes, skunks, and large spiders you can fight at
5th level to gain experience near to where you zoned in, just run along
ahead a bit and hunt them out.
