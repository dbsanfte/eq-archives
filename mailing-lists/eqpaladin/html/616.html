Subject: weapon choices at 17th level (was: taking the ghoulbane plunge)


This has been an interesting and informative thread, I'm glad I started it!
Consensus seems to be that:
A) Wait until you get past level 25 before even trying to get the sword,
B) Ghoulbane is extremely hard to get even at lvl 30 and above,
C) There are more appropriate weapons that I should be using until I hit
over level 25.

Seems like for us honest 17th thru 25th level Paladins our weapon choices
are pretty clear:
1. combine longsword or scimitar / shiny brass shield
2. shiny brass halberd
3. hammer of wrath / shiny brass shield
...with the nod going to the shiny brass halberd at level 20.

I *like* the way bash interrupts spells, and would regret not being able to
use it for 5 or more levels. If I switch to a shiny brass halberd then I am
walking away from 1-handed slashing skill which will need to be made up
after I do get ghoulbane.

Are there any other weapon/shield combos I am overlooking that actually
worked for some of you more accomplished Paladins?

Again, thanks everybody for the input.
Kaladin, 17th level Paladin (who is not well known for hiding in safe
corners of Norrath)
