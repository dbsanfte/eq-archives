>I have set up hot buttons for lots of different commands, but do not know

Go under socials and right click on any button you don't think you'll need,
such as consider (which is more easily done by hitting C anyway). You'll
be able to configure your macro simply by typing in the box on the center
of the screen. Start with /say and then your messsage. Don't forget to
rename your button, which you can do by clicking in the box at the top of
the screen with the current button name on it. Click done when you've put
in the text you want and renamed the button. I suggest dragging it to a
hot button spot so that it's easy to get to after that.
