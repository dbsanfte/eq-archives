I like the Dwarven Axe better, since I am able to attack much faster than the
mino axe. unfortunately I can only hit for a max of 13. In the end it adds
up to more damage than the mino thanks to the lower delay.

Look at it this way, DA is 26 delay, MA is 37.

After 8 seconds the DA can hit 3 times (26+26+26 = 72 / 10 = 7.2 seconds) and
do a max of 39 dmg (13 max per hit)

After 8 seconds the MA can hit twice (37+37 = 74 / 10 = 7.4 seconds) and do a
max of 34 dmg (17 max per hit)

But it really depends on personal choice. I really want that tomahawk axe,
same stats as the dwarven axe but it also casts that Berserker strength spell.
