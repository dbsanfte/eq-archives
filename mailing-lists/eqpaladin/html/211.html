Well, at least it encouraged some posting. =)

Well, for some reason I cannot play an evil class... I never could. I suck
at it. I can't be mean, and I hate when people are. However, it is these
types of people which make good people seem all the better. =)

I chose to be a Paladin because I wanted to help people. I wanted people to
have a hero... I loved the idea of LOH, and because I have such a skill,
I've acutally saved many lives. Even though most of the time people tend to
take it as something to pay me back on, as long as they are alive, I'm cool.
=)

I'm not a saint however. =) I do it partially to get positive feedback from
others. If they don't say thank you or anything, I'll actually get bummed.
However, it still makes me feel good that I helped someone out.

Also, I wanted to be on the front lines. I like a character that can duke
it out in battle long enough for another to escape.

I wanted to make sure I got the Holy Swords, because I hear they're the
neat-o-est in the game.

I chose a half-elf to give it a challenge. Most people are humans when
making Paladin's (from what I've seen) and being half-Filipino/half-white in
real life, I thought I could play such a race in the game as well.

I chose the goddess Erollisi cuz of my love of romance. However, I am
finding that even I don't think such stuff happens often in the game. For
some reason, as much as I roleplay, I don't want to hit on a guy playing a
girl. =) So my goddess only played the part of Tiresias loving all, even
dark elves, rather than focusing on any relationships.

Well, that's my half-cent thrown in. =)
