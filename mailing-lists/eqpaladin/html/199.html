I have been reading a lot lately about the bone bladed claymore, and I have
actually been hunting for it. However, I have noticed that the paladin
class is very often mentioned along with the bone bladed. I am not aware of
it being a paladin only sword, and the various web sites do not mention it
as such. Can someone who has actually wielded the sword give clarity to the
issue?

Also, I see a lot of paladins wielding 1h swords and a shield. Seriously
consider the benefits of 2handed weapons. I was fortunate enough to get the
2H Dwarven Axe (14 damage, 43 delay). And at level 12, I do 29 points of
damage a round. Delay is a factor, but consider it very closely. Exactly
how much faster is a weapon of 32 delay compared to 43 delay. What do those
11 points mean in terms of combat? Paladins have very good defense with no
armor restrictions, and healing (which can actually be done in combat if
timed right). Consider that Verant has balanced paladins with paladin only
offensive items, I have not heard of a paladin only armor yet. I think that
paladins are natural tanks, because of the number of hit points that we can
generate, but great offense compliments great defense. Consider upping your
offense with a good 2 hander.
