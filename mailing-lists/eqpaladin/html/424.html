Unfortunately, we [paladins] can't equip the tiny dagger or the pine needle
which is what is normally used to advance your archery. I simply sunk 25
skill points into it. This is a decent level to start your skill at and
it's actually raised up 4 points with about 75 arrows (7.5 gold worth). Not
too bad a trade off, but keep in mind that you have to hit
blue/white/yellow/red things in order to get results (I was hitting a lot of
green things at first and that 75 arrows is counting that so your results
may vary)
