You are on the list, however, most of the Paladin talk occurs on another
"onelist" at EQ_Holyswords@onelist.com <mailto:EQ_Holyswords@onelist.com> .
I would highly recommend signing up for that list in addition to this one.

As for advice, I can only speak for myself (as a high elf Paladin of Tunare)


Weapon Progression: (rusty longsword (until 6th), minotaur axe/kite shield
(until 13th), combine longsword/shiny brass shield (until 20th, which was
foolish on my part. I should have switched to 2H slashing long before
then), shiny brass halberd (until 25th), blackened iron bastard sword
(current)

Armor: All I can say is get banded as soon as you can and make due with
leather/patchwork until then. Since you are human, bronze plate will be
relatively expensive, but the weight shouldn't be too much of a problem.
The barbed arm plates and barbed leggings found in Befallen are quite nice.
As is the thick leather apron in Unrest.

Hunting Grounds: Greater Faydark, near Felwithe (until 6th), Orc Camps near
Kelethin (until 10th or so), Crushbone (until 14th), Befallen and W/E
Commonlands (until 16th), Unrest (17th through 27th, with breaks to go
traveling to various places in Antonica to explore)

I hope this helps somewhat. Peace favor you friend.
