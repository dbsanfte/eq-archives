Greetings, I also have a char of this type.

When EQ first started I found that chars of this type were more common, but as the game and its population matured people seemed to migrate to other mostly non-hybrid classes.

I personally chose this class largely for role-play type reasons, and the fact that the class suited me to a larger extent that the others...

I will say though, that it is a tough combination to play as we have a double penalty on gaining experience, 1 for being a hybrid class, and 2 for being a dwarf(low int)... This also affects skill progression especially in the craft type skills (smithing etc...) though I found it less of a problem with melee and spell casting as it took long enough to level that I had no trouble maxing out my melee skills...

I found it difficult to keep pace with some of my friends whom played elves largely and often a non hybrid class...
However it is a race/class combination I intend to stay with so good luck on your choice

One bit of advice I will recommend however is to max out as many melee skills as you can as you level, i.e. 1+2 hand blunt, 1+2 hand swords etc.... You will not likely have trouble maxing them all out as you level, but it can be an inconvenience to try and raise then later in life...
