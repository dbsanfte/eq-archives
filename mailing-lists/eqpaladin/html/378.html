Just to answer some questions I've seen arise.

To kill Mucktail, you need a nice party in the upper teens as a
minimum. This is mainly because you also have to handle regen around
him, which range from plain warriors to some nasty shamans and
champions.

1. To improve faction with the Freeport Militia, Don't kill anymore of
them ;) Turn in Orc scalps and Gnoll scalps to the respective captains
over the gates in Highhold Pass. This is challenging for anyone under a
high-teen level. The orc scalps are turned in right near there regen
(kith side of pass, and the gnolls are turned in on the karanas side of
the pass)

2. The Sword of Ak'allabakker (or whatever) is a myth. Some big
players were having fun at your expense.

3. The gypsies that sell combines (great investment) are located in the
Oasis of Marr. You have to zone south into the Oasis from Nro, walk
along the coast til you pass a small dock and see a largish pillar just
off the coast. When you see the pillar turn to the west and run down
the valley. At the end of the valley there will be a large lake to your
left and the gypsy camp to your right. The seller to the northwest has
the combines. A few words of warning. Sand Giants proliferate the area
so watch yer butts and take a friend with a high charisma to make the
actual purchase. I beleive with a 95 the cost for the longsword is
53-55 plat and a 2handed (my preference) is right around 112 plat. You
can also get them in North Karana but that's a long run from Fawdyer and
the prices are the same. In case your wondering where anyways, just
head north from the "white" bridge along the road, once again beware of
Griffins and Hill Giants. I've since moved on and am using a Shiny
Brass halberd (can hit for a max of 74 points a round with double attack
at my current level) but the combines are great for early teens.

4. Flash of light is very useful in conjunction with Taunt when trying
to pull mobs offa your healers and mages. I use sense the dead to raise
my divination skill. Unrest is the best place to work your Evocation,
Befallen is ok as well but that dungeon is a train derailment waiting to
happen and the regen on the blues and reds you need to Ward is almost
non-existent compared to Unrest. Just a note, I feel loot is better at
Unrest as well, and you'll have the opportunity to purchase bronze armor
and fine steel weapons (or eventually kill things that carry them).
