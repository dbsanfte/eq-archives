I use a Dwarven Axe right now (6 dmg 26 delay), so I am able to hit
them almost twice in the time that they are stunned. Plus the bash stun give
me time to run away if that is needed. Many people compare kick to bash, and
I must say that I take the side of bash. The actual damage done is less than
kick. Bash starts out at ~4 and goes up by 1 point every 15 or 20 points,
that's my experience anyway, while Kick does 9 max at first and starts to go
up once you hit 75 skill. What people forget is that the occasional stun (25
- 33% of the time?) lets you hit it with your weapon and not get hit back. I
don't know if the stun leaves him open to your attacks, meaning that his AC
goes down, but I've found that if the monster is stunned I am usually able to
hit him.

I know I sound like a powergamer, and that's because I am. I love to
learn all the inner nuances of a game and use them to my advantage. Don't
worry I always do bug reports on anything illegal that I find. What I'm
talking about is the things that you SHOULD know, for instance; what route
and NPC takes when it tries to run, which monster carries the best loot and
gives you the best XP.
