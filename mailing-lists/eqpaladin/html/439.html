Aye like for every class and every (non trade) skills. Our cap for combat

>If not, than is it true that we could fight as good as those
>warriors? If there are skill cap for our combat skills, well, the combat
>advantage of us would be to obtain a holy sword, am I right?

In pure melee fight, you won't be as good as a pure warrior. They will deal
more damages and tank more easily (with their kick, double attack, double
weapon, bash and all the other attacks they have). But they don't have any
spell, and those spells are of a huge help when you're resting between
fights. (and even during fights at a higher level).
As far as the holy sword is concerned, well I'm lev 22 and still have to see
one :) the weakest one is I think on a lev 30 monster, and I can't wait to
get it...
Anyway one thing you have to realize is that in a fight, and at higher
levels, you won't really be here to do the damage - same for the warriors.
Fight with some wizards who will in two spells take half of this monster's
hps while you can hardly do a scratch... But teach them that if they run
away when the monster turns on them, then you can't take the monster back on
you ;)
