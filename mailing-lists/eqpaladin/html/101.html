Yes, there's definitely a cheaper way. You should always check your NPCs
to see what they sell. Make sure you check their whole inventory as milk
often doesn't show up at the top of the list. Muffins tend to go for 6 cp
and milk tends to go for 5/6 cp. Your best bet is usually a baker or a
general merchant. For example, in Freeport, I buy muffins from Rashinda in
the Land Holder's Office and milk in the Merchant's Square from the guy in
the big blue building. My wife tells me that both of these items are also
readily available in Kelethin and Felwithe, if you happen to be on Fadwyar.
I've also seen several people selling muffins in High Hold, as well as
other places. As water flasks typically sell for 1 sp, 1 cp and rations
for multiple silver, you can see how buying milk and muffins can easily
save you money in the long run.
