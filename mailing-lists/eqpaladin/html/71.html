I am not sure how many want to read this but this is a spoiler so stop
reading if you want to figure it out on your own.


This quest is given by Lord Jekle (I am at work and don't remember the name
exactly) and he asks if you wish to assist the defenders. If you so agree he
tells you to bring him an orc oracle scroll. These scrolls are only found on
oracles that spawn in and near the orc castle in crushbone (I have never
witnessed nor heard of one spawning in the village).

Problems:
1) These oracles in crushbone are incredibly difficult to kill and should
only be attempted at lvl 10 plus
2) Most often you will have to engage the oracle as well as several
legionnaires and many of the other castle inhabitants (Royal Guards,
Prophet, Emissary, Emperor Crush and don't forget Dvinn)...All of which are
incredibly difficult to defeat and I have only seen players defeat Dvinn
once and heard of another time but never solo.
3) The reward from this quest was roughly 12 silver and some exp (not a
noticeable gain at lvl 11)

Notes:
After returning the scroll and completing the quest he did say that it was
too bad I wasn't a defender. Not sure what was meant by that. All in all I
would suggest that if you get a scroll while out adventuring you made 12
silver and a little exp but I would not go out of your way to complete this
quest.
