The titled used depends on the situation.

In military situations (on duty, at a military
function, etc...) the Military title (Captain,
Commander, etc...) would be used.

In purely civilian situations (having dinner,
socializing with nobility, etc...) the Nobility title
(Lord, Lady, Duke, Duchess, etc...) would be used.

The Knight title would be the least used, but would
often be combined with the nobility title.
