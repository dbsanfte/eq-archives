One of the best investments I could suggest would be the purchase of some
new rings. Around 20th level I purchased a pair of gold carnelian rings (+5
agility) for 65pp and firmly believe that they have saved my life on many
occasions. This is especially true when being run over by a train in Unrest
where that one extra miss/dodge/parry can mean the difference between life
and a gruesome death.

I would also look into the possibility of upgrading your two-handed weapon.
Although the 1H vs. 2H debate rages to this very day, I personally believe
that 2H is the way to go after 20th until/if you get Ghoulbane. A shiny
brass halberd and bone bladed claymore are both well within your price range
(~100pp on Povar). I personally preferred the halberd because that -8
agility hit with the claymore is brutal, but I know many of our brethren
that have been happy with both. In the mid-20's it's then time to start
looking for a blackened iron bastard sword. I have one and it's my pride
and joy...

Peace favor your sword,
