If everyone did some roleplaying, and used their native languages it would
be a much cooler world. Like going into a city where evryone spoke High Elf,
and no one really spoke common. Those who knew High Elf would be at an
advantage. Im wondering, If I learned troll as a NON-troll race, then got
some good faction not to be KOS and approached an NPC and spoke Troll, maybe
I could get a good quest, or at least not be turned away. Or maybe if I was
a Shadow Knight and learned Gnoll, then spoke with one. Who knows the
possiblities. Im sure if Verant thought about it there could really be some
good thing to com e out of it.
Also, on the topics of improvement, I would like to see monsters and
creatures actually bad together army style and lay seige to a city. Like
seeing a Great Shadow Knight and some Buddys lead an army of Gnolls againt
the city of Quynos. Admittedly alot of newbies would die, but some people
who were of the same class and such may be spared and could actually attack
a guard and survive. There would be lag due to the high numbers of
characters on the screen, but who wouldnt like to see that? Or even if a
Hill Giant could get to quynos and rampage the city, that would be an
awesome sight. Its better than spawning little craps about while people
camp. Verant has to mix it up a bit and through a little chaos in the mix.
Waiting for Spawn only can yeild a limited amount of enjoyment.
I would also like to Organize something on Veeshan Server. I sw the
clearing of Kerra Isle on Soulsek Ro went amazing (in fact some creative
players used their cash to arm the Kerrans before the raid, cool) . I think
If I can find enough people on Veeshan that would be interested, we could

of Quynos? This wouldn't be for EXP or items (though people will get that
