The ward undead spell is a difficult one to master, but your best bet is to
use it on newbie skels for a while to raise the skill. I spent about an
hour killing decaying skels and raised it around 35 points.

Since this is rather monotonous, I then teamed with a cleric and spent a few
hours roaming South Ro killing undead (ghouls, dry bones and the such). I
now cast Ward Undead 8 out of 10 times with a skill in evocation of about
60.

Good luck, but understand the only way to get better is to cast it and
regainng your mana takes a while.
