The dervish cutthroats make for some good cash. If you are over level 10 or
so, and have good group, you can tear into the Derv's near Freeport. I have
made as much as 25 platinum in one night there. They have dervish cutthroat
rings and sometimes have jewels and bronze weapons. That is the most
profitable place I've found so far. Does anyone else have more input?
