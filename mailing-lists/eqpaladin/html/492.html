I had the same problem with fizzles when I hit 15th
and got my 2nd rank spells; Evocation was low and I
fizzled continously with Ward (record stands at 16 in
a row).

I back burnered Ward for a few levels, then at 18th (I
was fighting Dervs in Ro at the time, so I could
afford to let it rest for awhile), devoted two hours
of practice to Ward.

Waiting to 18th gave me more mana, and also spells
that you're higher level than seem to be somewhat
easier to cast. That aside, I practiced it by finding
any undead I could kill (first decaying skeletons in
the newbie area, then zombies, mummies, and even a
ghoul); Warding to start the fight, then killing it.
Then I targeted the corpse and sat next to it Warding
and meditating as needed, until the corpse would
finally go away.

I brought Ward up to 52 in two hours of this, and
reduced the fizzles considerably. I went to Unrest
shortly after and now often use Ward to finish fights
when the mob starts running. My skill's currently at
69 (dude!), and should continue to increase. I
suppose I could level cap it to 95 in less than an
hour if I repeated the same procedure, but I haven't
bothered yet; it's moving up faster than I'm leveling.
Fizzle rate is less than 3 in 10 (most I've ever had
is 2 fizzles before getting it off).
