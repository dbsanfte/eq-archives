Subject: Current Holyswords list as of 09/14/99


As requested, I am posting the Current Holyswords List once again. There
really isn't that much new. There were rumors of two new Holy Swords that I
added. I know there have been a few items considered "Holy Weapons" that were
Paladin AND Cleric. I am sure I don't have all of these listed, so if someone
wants to add them to the list or e-mail them to me and i will do it.

Note: If any of the information below is inaccurate in any way post an
update, or send it directly to me and I will repost this message.

Man-o-War (Confirmed!)
Skill: 2H Blunt
Atk Delay: 41
DMG: 19
Effect: Chaotic Feedback
WT: 9.0
Class PAL
Race: ALL


Brilliant Sword of Faith (Confirmed!)
Quest Item
Damage: 20
Delay: 44
Type: 2H Slashing
Effects: +50 mana
Location: You must kill Lucan and return his testimony to the head of the
Paladin Guild in Freeport. He does not drop the testimony every time.
Note: This sword is required to get SoulFire.

"Brilliant Sword of Truth" (NEW)
This was one of two rumored newly found holy swords. It is in fact just the
BSoF, but someone got the name wrong. So unfortunately nothing new here.

Pearl Bladed Claymore (May also be called Shiny Bladed Claymore)
Damage: 17
Delay: 40
Type: 2H Slashing
Weight: 10.0
Note: Not sure if this is actually a holy sword or not, it was marked
as being a Paladin only sword, on the Everlore site (http://www.everlore.com).
Possibly retrieved from mistmoore. A
person, reported from beta, wielding it was doing damage in the 60's and
70's to undead. The name could have been shiny bladed claymore.
Update! No one has seen any evidence of this sword since Beta. It may
have been removed or renamed and made non-class specific.

"Shiny Brass Claymore" (New)
Once again this name appears in the rumor mill. When this list originally
formed we had rumors of this sword, but no proof. It is very likely that this
sword was, or is the same as the Pearl Bladed Claymore, which is also just a
rumor now. A while ago someone claimed seeing a weapon with a similar name in
Beta. Most likely if it ever did exist it was changed, name an all.

Ghoulbane (Confirmed!)
Pending Lore Item
Damage: 15
Delay: 34
Type: 1H Slashing
Effect: Dismiss Undead, Acts as a light source comparable to a
lightstone.
Weight: 9.5
Located: It is located in Upper Guk, on the Shin Lord.
Note: Both the Shin Lords respawn rate, and the respawn rate of the
weapon are very low, so it may take quite a bit of time to get the
weapon. Level needed to obtain: Full group as low as 28th. Full group
in early 30's to be safe. If you use the weapon at lower than 20th
level you will get the message "You do not have the will to command this
weapon" when it tries to activate dismiss undead. You have to kill a
Froglok Paladin to get this sword. Possibly a quest sword and the quest
is broken.

Fiery Avenger
Quest item
Damage: 35
Delay: 48
Type: 2H Slashing
Effects: Inferno Shock
Note: There is very little information on this sword, other than it was
2H, and at one point during the Beta they made it available to all
classes to test it (and show it off). At the moment it is believed to
be 35 Damage and 45 Speed, which would make learning that 2H Slash well
worth it. It is also understood that his is a quest item, but for what
guild, or from what NPC I don't know.
Update! It has been said that the great Fiery Avenger is not currently
available to players, and that it is only a GM toy. Will this change in the
update? Who knows. Of course, this is not official, and pretty much never
will be until we actually see the sword in a player's hand since Varrant isn't
likely to give too much info on this topic.
Also, it is very likely that this weapon will NOT be a holy sword. If we are
lucky, it will be available to all fighter classes.

SoulFire (Confirmed!)
Magic Item, Lore Item, No Drop
Charges: 5
Damage: 23
Delay: 45
STR: +7, WIS: +7
Effect: Complete Healing
Type: 2H Slashing
Weight: 5.0
Located: You are given SoulFire by Brother Hayle is Splitpaw after you
give him the note that he gives you back, your Testimony of Truth, the
Brilliant Sword of Faith, and SoulFires Hilt (Glowing Sword Hilt). A
complete walk-through of the SoulFire quest is available separately.
