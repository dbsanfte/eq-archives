I have found that the best way to improve your 1H blunt is to get a party
together of at least 2 tanks (yourself being one) and a couple of magic
users. By going to the mino caves (Steamfont) and drawing out trains, I have
gotten my skill up to 72. it took forever to get those first 20, but after
that it went pretty fast. it usually helps if the other tank is a higher
level than you and pretty good at taunting. be sure to always carry your
better weapon with you, though, as emergencies can arise pretty fast with a
train of 3 blue conning minos.
