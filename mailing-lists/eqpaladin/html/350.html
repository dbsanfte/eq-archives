Hail! If your in Rivervale, I suggest that ye try the Misty Thicket. At your
level there aren't many critters that you can't solo. And if you wish to
group you can take on multiple critters of young kodiaks, giant wasps, and
giant scarabs, as well as the orc camp that spawns 2 orcs usually
centurions. There's also 3 goblin camps, and at one of the camps there is a
goblin named Mooto. He is a magic user but at your level you should be able
to handle him solo without much of a problem. I tell ye this because I did
this at around your level. Good luck and plentiful hunting friend.
