either didn't have the hammer in your melee hand, or you were bashing with a
standard shield and it will say that the skeleton is invulnerable when you
are bashing. But otherwise, you should be able to hit the skeleton with the
hammer, although I would recommend haveing a party with you if that is your
only magic weapon.
