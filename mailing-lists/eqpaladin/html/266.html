I guess I have 2 thoughts on this one:
* as the servers mature, magical weapons will be easily obtained from high level players for modest prices (20p is enough, I would think, which is one third the cost of a combine long). I'm only L22, and I already have more tentacle whips than I need;
* when I was L15, I didn't have a magical weapon, and didn't have much of a need for one either. At L15 though, with Hammer of Wrath, you do get a magical weapon and I used that to fight undead in Unrest from 15 - 20.

As an aside, I think they have the game balance pretty good - by the time that you have the capability to fight ghouls, you've probably got enough money for a magic weapon.

On the holy swords being unique, I agree that they should be rare BUT I'd prefer the MOB that it is on to be the rare spawn, with the sword a more regular carry on that MOB - I don't want to "camp" at the spot where the MOB spawns for two months killing it 3000 times and still find I missed out.
