Gypsy camps usually have a vendor selling Combine weapons, which are
magical, lighter than their steel counterparts, and faster. However, they
usually cost in the range of 30-90 pp, depending on what you're looking
for. Personally, I saved up 43 pp for a Combine Scimitar and I like it
much better than my mino axe, plus it can hit all the nasties like wisps,
ghouls, and iceboned skeletons.

As far as what zone to make money in, I don't really have any suggestions
for you. Going after any MOB with a weapon nets you that weapon, which is
usually good for at least a couple of gold. Are you spending your money on
armor and such as you go along? I usually save up for things that I want,
like the Combine weapon, rather than spend money as I get it.
