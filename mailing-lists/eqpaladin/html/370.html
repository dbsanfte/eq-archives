For those that can get to lavastorm I've seen gypsies listed on maps of
that zone as well. Lavastorm is accessible only through Nektolus
forest, the newbie area for the Dark Elves. It is very dark in there
and is heavily patrolled by dark elf guards and heavy hitting creatures,
Stone Guardians and Iron Guardians.

Couple interesting points: I saw a person with a last name (20+ level
character) who presumably has spirit of the wolf (SoW) on them
outrunning a train of about 10 guardians so there are LOTs of them
roaming around the forest. Other interesting thing, did anyone else
notice the language of the giants is "Combine"...? I was wondering if
the giants originally made the combine weapons?
