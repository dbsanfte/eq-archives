I just hit 12th level yesterday, so I won't be going to Befallen or Unrest
anytime soon! However, I have been spending a lot of time at levels 10 and 11
in the Everfrost Peaks, soloing the snow leopards, orc troopers, and giant wooly
spiders, and grouping for orc mountaineers and mammoth calves. Ice bone
skeletons do wander the area, sometimes in groups of 2 or 3, and it can be
frustrating to know that I am dead meat unless somebody saves you by casting
spirit of the Wolf, or by taking on the ice bone (which cons white to me). The
last time this happened, i tried casting Spook the Dead at it, but it just
resisted the spell and kept on beating me to death, 14hp at a time. That was a
long, painful, ugly death. Needless to say, this was before I started carrying
the magician-summoned staff. I have gone up a level as well since then, so
hopefully my spooky spell will work at least once, and until it does I can at
least be beating it back a little.

Incidentally, 2Hblunt is 26, 1Hblunt is 44, 1Hslash is 64, bash is 64. I can
solo blue-con giant spiders with the 2H staff and have 2 bubbles of health left,
so I really hope that, should I absolutely need it, I can use LoH combined with
2H staff and the occasional heal spell to survive a single ice bone skeleton
onslaught. I don't want to test this theory, but it does give me some hope!

Drive back the legions of undead for me until I am strong enough to join you!

Your magic deficient Dwarven Paladin friend,
Kaladin
