Medium bronze armor can be found on Drez (very, very rare drop) in
Nejena or Solusek B.

Solusek B is lvl 30 and up. This is a very, very tough dungeon...
recommend a solid group of upper 30s preferably 40s or higher. The
Shaman can charm and the fighters hit like giants. Expect to die often,
but great experience... you'll basically make head way as far as
levelling and get some bronze in the process.
