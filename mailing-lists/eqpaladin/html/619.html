Another sword that has to be considered is the bone bladed claymore. If you
can put up with your agility being knocked down, it is lighter than the
shiny brass halberd which for some of us is of more importance.

Note, that the Obsidian Flamberge is usable by Paladins. This would make a
good low to high 20's two handed sword. As to letting one skill drop in
favor of the other.

I carry both serrated bone blade 1h slash with characteristics similar to
the combine long sword, and shield. Plus a bone bladed claymore. You
cannot beat the damage the 2 handed sword does, especially if you can find a
caster that can quicken you.

The one handed slash and shield is a great alternative if fighting casters.
You need the speed and hits to interrupt their spells.
