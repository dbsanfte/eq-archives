In response to the magic shield question. I currently have a Shiny Brass
Shield which is AC 10 and gives you +10 to magic resistance and puts off
light like a Lightstone. Additionally, it is magic, so you can bash wisps
and ice boned skeleton's with it.

In another post, I saw a message about another shield which I hope to obtain
someday.

The premier shield is the Darkwood Shield obtainable from a named Gnoll in
Highpass Hold, towards the Karana end. The area is continuously camped so
bring a wizard friend, preferably of an obscene level. Between the rare
spawn and the campers, do not be surprised if it takes you all day

The shield offers +9 to STR and AC12.

It's usefulness peaks at around L30 for a paladin when one obtains
Ghoulbane. You are far better off using 2HS at lower levels and capitalise
on double attack.
