These is what I noticed.
If you have an ability (not only on spells) from level 1, it can increase by
itself. When you have a new ability, you must spend at least one practice
point to learn it. And your guildmaster says "You have learnt the basics
of...". It seems that you then have a base that equals to your level. THEN
it can increase by itself. But it sometimes depends of the ability. For
example, some people think that Safe Fall for the monk never increases
except with practive points.
