> 1) I have both a mino axe and a FS longsword which
> should I be using?

Mino axe is 8/37 (0.216), FS Longsword is 6/28
(0.214). They're virtually identical in efficiency,
but the FS is 1/3rd faster. Use the FS because you'll
get more swings (skill raises faster, misses hurt
less, more caster interrupts). Of course, if you just
like to see *big* numbers (shrug), go with the mino
axe. But it's *slow*.

> 2) Considering the above weapons what is the next
> step up in weapons?

Those are at nearly the top of the 1-15 level 1hS
weapon scale (very top is Dwarven 1h Axe). The next
steps up are Barbed Leather Whip and Polished Granite
Tomahawk. BLW goes for about 150pp, PGT about 250pp.

> 4) Taking into account that I am currently level
> 16(bubble from 17th) and I
> currently have:
> 2 Onyx Earrings
> Spiked Leather Collar
> Leather Mask
> Small Ringmail Helm
> 2 Silver Amber Rings (+2 str)
> Banded Arms
> Leather Gloves
> 2 Banded Bracers
> Leather Shoulderpads
> Bronze Breastplate
> Bronze Cloak
> Bronze Leggings
> Leather Belt
> Banded Boots
> Shiny Brass Shield
> Am I at where most paladins (untwinked) at this
> level?

You're ahead of the curve compared to most preceding
16th level characters. Get into full bronze
everywhere you're not carrying magic armor (like neck
for Spiked Collar and shoulders for Rusty Spiked pads
or BS Mantle). I'd recommend magic bracers; Bracer of
Battle and Bracer of Erollsi are good suggestions.

> 5) What types of armor should I be looking for?

Full bronze plus magic.

> 6) How much does a BS mantle sell for ?

40pp - 60pp. If you want the low end go to Unrest and
auction. DO NOT expect to get it that low anywhere on
Antonica, and certainly not anywhere beyond Ferrott or
Highhold.
