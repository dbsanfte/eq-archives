I was close approaching level 14 when I made a trip over to Antonica. with a
heavily map-obsessed wizard in tote, we went all the way over to Qeynos. by
myself, hunting gnolls in Blackburrow for an hour or three, I made 10 pp.
after a few trips there and to the Bandit camps nearby, I got the 55 pp to
buy my combine long sword (and hunting in the catacombs got me up to level
15 when I was with my wizard friend). the trip is a pain and I highly
recommend camo if you are going the Beholder route. also, if you go through
HighHold, do *not* investigate the inn. an evil dark elf necro killed me
*twice* in there and finally my wizard friend had to be consented (he was
bound there, while I was at Rivervale). I found that Faydwer was lacking in
exp and money at my level, but now that I have a magical weapon, I may try
Dagnor's Cauldron when I get back home. I hope this helps.
