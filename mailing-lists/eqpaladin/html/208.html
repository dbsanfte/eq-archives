Why? Why not?

First there are the game advantages to Paladins:
Paladins are great fighters. Paladins get healing spells. Awesome special
ability (Lay Hands). Finally, Verant claims we will get the best magical
weapons.

Now the role-playing advantages:
Paladins are trusted me more people. You get to play the hero, saving people
and dying for a good cause.

The only thing I don't like it that it takes me longer to gain a level. BUT
since people like and trust me as a paladin I find it easier to group and
thus I have actually gained levels faster.
