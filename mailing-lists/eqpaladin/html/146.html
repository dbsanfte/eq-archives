Just wanted to add that once you zone to South Karena, you will be on a
bridge pointed south. To get to The Paw, just keep heading south for a few
minutes until you see a series of hills with two giant fanglike pillars
sticking up out of the ground. There will be a slightly conceled tunnel
there thats leads into The Paw. There should be some guards outside also,
that will help you identify it. If you use the /loc command, I beleive the
coordinates are like -3000, 780.
