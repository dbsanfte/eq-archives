This happens to me quite often. Blind is a very useful spell, but not in the
way it should be. If you blind a creature you are fighting, and then run it
will follow as if the spell did nothing. That part should be fixed. If you
blind a creature that is NOT fighting you, and it doesn't resist, it will often
run right by you, and eventually come back when it is unblinded to fight you.
Remember, the flash of light spell doesn't last very long. I have had this
happen to me many times.

When the blind spell becomes REALLY useful is when you use it as a taunt. All
spells in the game have an Anger (or Hate) rating. This is how monsters rate a
spell vrs direct damage, and why they will often attack a caster who has never
done any actual damage to them. Blind spells are very near the top of the Anger
rating. If you are ever fighting in a group and the monster decides to attack
your wizard (because the wizard just hit him for three 100pt fireballs) and your
taunt is doing nothing, Flash of Light the monster. He will VERY QUICKLY turn
to attack you. But be warned, it may be very difficult to get the monster
taunted off of you.

Also be careful using Flash of Light as a pull spell. It will often pull the
entire camp onto you, and no one will be able to change the little critters'
minds.
