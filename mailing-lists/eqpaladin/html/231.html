I started my paladin after playing a dark elf necromancer for a little
while. I guess it seemed logical to me to balance my evil character
with a good one. Just to let everyone know I do nice things with my
necromancer sometimes too. Last night I was in the Qeynos catacombs
invisible and came across a naked guy lost in the catacombs. I assumed
he had died and asked if this was so. He just didn't have any stuff yet
so I gave him some cloth armor and 5gp to get him started. Random acts
of kindness have helped me at various times.

Anyhow, I have always liked the idea of playing a holy knight and
paladins get some great combat skills/strengths. Something to remember
though is the game is balanced pretty well, so try out some other
combinations. I have:

a level 12 barbarian warrior - tank 360hps
a level 10 dark elf necromancer - fun for soloing
a level 6 high elf enchanter - created for jewelcraft
a level 5 dwarven cleric - fun to help people and dwarves are tough
fighters too
a level 5 wood elf ranger - tracking is really really neat
and of course my level 4 human paladin - the good guy fighter, fun to
help with lay hands

have fun!
shawn
