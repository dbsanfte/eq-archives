You are right, I petitioned the other day and was told that you have to
"seed" skills that you don't start with. These include the Taunt skill and
the magic skills (except Channeling, that one you should already know at
9th) Dodge, Bash, and others. You only need to put 1 point in to seed a
skill. After that, it will go up as you practice. In fact, at lvl 10 I
seeded my spell skills and discovered that they went up very quickly and
now, lvl 11 my Alteration skill is just as high as my 2 Hand Slash! (my
primary weapon skill)
