You might try fighting mobs that drop low weight items. For example, the
stone and iron guards in the North West corner of Nektulos forest drop gems,
rings, words, runes, and pages. All of these are light weight, and most
stack. I realize this area is a bit high for lvl 16, but the idea is still
sound. The common tactic is to loot everything, but those rusty weapons
just weight too much for all but the strongest races.

You could also try grouping with a high strength race to hold all the heavy
stuff, then split the proceeds. Of course, you have to find a trustworthy
PC to group with.
