Another place to get netted is from the low level lizards in feerrott, near
the Innothule swamp. This, however, is not suggested, as the ogre bouncers
(guards) tend to roam all over, and it's very easy to get lost. Also the
swamp is very dangerous, because there are a few higher level mobs in the
area, namely Jojongua the kobold. That, and it's pretty easy to gather
quite a crowd of frogs while you're fighting one. Another idea, though, is
to simply go to the swamp and auction to buy it. There should be plenty of
low level trolls with some to sell. Don't pay too much for it tho, as the
protection is equivalent to cloth.

Getting to Qeynos isn't that difficult, and is definitely possible at your
level. Check out this website for maps -

members.door.net/enigma/eqmap.htm

It would be best to travel with a shaman, druid, or bard for speed.

Good luck...
