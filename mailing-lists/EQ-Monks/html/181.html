That's the thing. In EQ, there's not supposed to be a BEST class. Each and
every class will have its ups and downs, and, in the end, we all need each
other. Sure, there might be imbalances at times, but I don't think you're going
to find a lot of groups refusing you just because you're a monk. If they do,
you probably didn't want to group with them anyway.

Personally, I love playing a monk. Not because we're the BEST class in the game
or do more melee damage than anyone else, but because the monk class is one of
the most unique classes in the game. Who else dishes out as much damage as we
do with their bare hands? Who else has as much AC as we do while wearing plain
looking clothes? (Yes, I _like_ looking ordinary.) Who else has a cool flying
kick? Who else (besides rogues) can jump from Kelethin and survive? For me,
playing a monk is enjoyable not because I'm the best figher in the game, but
because monks are unique and fun to roleplay.

But then, that's just my opinion. Go forth, and form your own.
