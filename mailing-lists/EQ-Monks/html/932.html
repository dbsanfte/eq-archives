Subject: How to unsubscribe


Hi,

I've noticed a few attempts to unsubscribe from the monk lists lately... all
in forms that will not work. For those wishing to do so, there are two ways
to unsubscribe:

1. Go to onelist.com, log in, and then unsubscribe from the list.
2. Send email to EQ_Monk-unsubscribe@onelist.com or
EQ-Monks-unsubscribe@onelist.com. The listserver will send you a
confirmation request, follow the directions in that email (you just have to
reply to that message), and the listserver will remove you from the list.
