West Commonlands is indeed much more populated with wisps. Try to get SOW
from someone - offer a druid or shaman a few gold and say that you will
come to them, or try and find one and ask while you are there... If you
stay between the trail and the inn (directly west of where the trail is),
Giants will rarely bother you. Kizdean Gix is mean, but never bothered my
monk... I usually roam around the hills surrounding the inn, and stay
close enough to the zone to make a run... Other than than, bind yourself
to Qeynos and take the boat to Wisp (Kerra) Isle... Find out where the
wisps spawn and camp there with a few other people... Good exp through
level 15 and excellent money...
