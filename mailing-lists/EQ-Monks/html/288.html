Don't know if this will help but here goes. In the Qeynos area you can
travel to BlackBurrow ("BB"), the entrance to which is on the Eastern side
of the Qeynos Hills Zone. Gnolls there are good hunting from levels 7 to 15,
more or less. The area is famous, and justly so, for "Trains" so stay near
the zone border at first. Also, strongly suggest you NOT go in hollow tree
to the left of cave exit as you go into BB, hehe.
