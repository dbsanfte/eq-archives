Subject: Issues with the new damage.


Now I am by no means Crazy enough to complain about the impending enhancements.
But I just wanted to share with some of you issues that we monks wil face once
the patch goes in. Every monk above level 20 will be doing a good deal more
damage - 15% more by level 50. What this means is that like rogues we will be
doing "hella taunts" with our whirling fists. I recommend you all dump some of
your dex and agi gear (keep agi above 120, but drop dex like a rock - detailed
log studies show it is useless to us!) and switch it out for AC stuff. I know
I am going to go in all AC gear from now on, concentrating on str as secondary.
I may also start using easgle strike a bit more (it taunts alot less than kick
and does almost as much damage).

Just be prepared for a change in tactics and gear once the patch goes live.
