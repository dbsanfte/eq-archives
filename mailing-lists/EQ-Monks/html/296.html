Adventurer,

As several of our number have recommended, Butcher Block is indeed a ripe
hunting ground. The beast therein are enemies to our kind, and swift
execution
of such foul creatures will only serve to improve the esteem with which all of
the monks of Qeynos regard you. Take all due precautions, however. Never turn
your back to a gnoll as they are prone to rally their dispicable brethren in a
vulgar call to arms against our kind. Carry a full supply of straps for
binding; you will suffer injuries both great and mild in the burrow that even
your thoughtful meditations may be unable to heal. And always head the
call of
others warning of approaching gnoll platoons; make haste for a safe spot but,
if you are able, assist those in need on your way.

There are other advantages to battling the gnolls of Blackburrow as well. I
would advise you to discuss those very beast with Master Phin at the house of
the Silent Fist. He may have a vital mission requiring your attention.

May Quellious guide you,

Manjushri
Monk of the 10th Order, disciple of Quellious the Tranquil
