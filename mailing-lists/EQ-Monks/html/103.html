I leveled from 10 to 13 quickly by killing Wisps and YKs in West
Commonlands. The trick to keep from dying is to get SOW cast on you by a
friendly Shaman (at least 9th level) or Druid (at least 14th level). This
will let you outrun the monsters if you get in trouble. Make sure you have
magic (netted/woven/mesh) gloves. The key to getting SOW is to offer a
donation every time you get it. 3-5 gold is probably good and they
shouldn't mind too much. You should be able to make good money with the
lightstones from Wisps as well. Also, kill any orc pawns and skeles you
see for loot and sell any weapons at the inn by the guards so they don't
slow you down.
