I thought I would provide a litttle summary on all the stats that are important
to monks and why they are important:

In order of importance:

1) STR thisaffects damage on both fists and Flying Kick

2) AGI this affects our ac and Mitigates (reduces) damage when we are hit

3) STA this allows us to keep fighting without getting fatigued (important at
high levels when fights last long and DW/DA go off more often) and adds
hitpoints.

4) Int/Wisdom these reduce our suceptability to CHARMING and Messmerizing
spells that some high level critters cast, and increase our learning rate. Good
to have for trade skills.

5) CHA prices you get at vendor.

NOTE: DEX this is UNIMPORTANT and does nothing except increase our LEARNING rate
of the melee skills.
