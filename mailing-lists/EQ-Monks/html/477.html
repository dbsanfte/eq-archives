Hey scot, I just wanted to point out to you as a higher level monk looking
back on his youthfull days that now is the perfect time for you and your
group to go down to guk - hug the right zone wall down through South Ro,
and then hug the left zone wall in innothule till you hit guk. YOU will
need a group.

1) Dungeons have better xp than outdoors - I think it is about 25% more
for killing the same level mob

2)Dungeons have great loot - cashwise and item wise. at 15 you are not
ready to camp for any good items yet but you can find mesh
easily in guk. also hangin out there you will see mesh and other cool
things auctioned at low prices often.

Go there with your group - you will need a Good group to get the most out
of guk - healer and someone who can root are important.

just some advice.
