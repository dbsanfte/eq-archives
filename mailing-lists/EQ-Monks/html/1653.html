All shackles are Iksar only, I have had all of them. I currently sport a
Shackle of Steel and Shackle of Bronze.
Shackle of Bronze: 6 ac 5 vs magic 0.2 weight.
Shackle of Steel: 9 ac 5 str 5 sta 5 vs magic 0.1 weight.
The Shackle of Bronze quest can be done in your high 20's low 30's with a
group I'd say. Mostly for safety, and the pieces in Permafrost and Frontier
Mountains, the rest is foraging and an easy quest.
The Shackle of Steel in your low to mid 40's with a group. That Droga
section can be tough.
