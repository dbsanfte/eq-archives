I've been posting this on the message board at:
http://server3.ezboard.com/beverquestmonks

And I thought I'd also post it here, as this is turning
out to be a lot of fun and so far I've had a lot of
support.

Event: Monk Madness

Server: Vallon Zek

Time: Starting this Friday at 9PM eastern US time. That
is GMT -5:00 for international monks. :)This will be an
ongoing event until Sunday Night. Play as little or as
much of that as you want to.

Story: An evil gnome wizard experimenting with cloning
captured a lone human monk in Freeport, and tried to
clone him. He inadvertently triggered a chain reaction
and suddenly there spawns many many many monks. All
identical. Being of one mind they are also all of one
guild, "The Armadillos of Norrath".

Naming: This is an event in which monks from all
servers who may know each other from messageboards will
participate. Therefore I think it is a great idea that
we use the naming convention '"your real monk
name"dillo'. Or a variation thereof. Such that I am
going to be Ipnodillo on vallon zek for the weekend. ;)

Goal: Have fun. Group up with as many monks as you can
and run around the zone punching everything in site. Go
on suicide missions. We'll be like ants.

To participate, create a new monk on Vallon Zek Friday
Night. The monk should be the male version with the
Priest of Discord face. We're gonna try to create a
guild for this as well, so look for a guild officer to
invite you. Please try to keep your guild tunic on at
all times so we have the monk "look" about us. Invite
all your friends to attend. This is an event to have
fun, not so heavy on the roleplaying, but lets just
have a LOT of fun.

To clarify, your monk should look like this:

http://www.shada.com/pod.jpg

The guild has been submitted. Lets all hope verant
approves it!

Invite your non monk friends who have always admired
your monkness as well! Lets try and make this event a
legend of it's own kind! :)

Just think. Hundreds of Identical Newbie Monks...
