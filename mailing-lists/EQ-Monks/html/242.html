Young Brother,

Do not fret!

True, advancing does take some time. I too have gone through bouts of shortened play time and RL commitments. I think we all run into levels that seem to take FOREVER to pass.

I found my personal hell ranges to be 14-17 and 22-24. I think it took me almost a month to do 3 levels. Now that I got through 24, I've noticed a marked inprovement in my skills and exp gain. I just hit 25 and got Dragon Punch; the satisfaction I get from hitting for higher rounds makes the hard work and persiverance well worth it.

Hell levels tend to appear just as you max out your special attacks and have to wait for the 'Next Big Monk Skill'.

Finding a good group does take time also. I was fortunate to find a great group of friends back when I was around level 11 or so. We have all be companions ever since, now our crowd runs around 20 people or so (18 - 31 range), all changing groups as the need arises or play time coincides.

Do shouts often, if you find a group that looks out for each other, latch onto them and build the friendship. If someone doesn't play their part in the group (cleric fights like a wizard and not a healer) it prolly means that they USED to play that class and haven't learned to play their new strengths. This is your indication to move on.

Hang in there Brother, all will come in time.
