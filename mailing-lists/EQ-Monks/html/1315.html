I know it's been said before, but it bears repeating.

Save every lightstone and greater lightstone you get. Go to North Karana,
and turn them in to the Gypsy - you get experience for both, and for the
greater lightstones you get a research container worth about 8-10 pp
depending on your faction.

A monk in my guild did this and went from 11-14 and made about 300 pp for a
weeks work, which is not bad for a 11-14 character. It's sometimes worth
even buying Greaters for 6 or 7 pp from others.
