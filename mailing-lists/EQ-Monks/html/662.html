Yes, as you suggest, there are two types of sewing kits. One is a small sewing
kit. The other is a large sewing kit; it contains space for the combination of
up to eight total items. I believe, if you are in the vicinity of Northern Ro
after exiting East Freeport, you can find such a kit by visiting the merchant
tailor in the leftmost room of the Inn located in the grassy area there. May
Quellious guide you.
