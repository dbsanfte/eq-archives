If you are pursuing making studded armor because of the satisfaction of
having made your armor yourself I wish you good fortune. If you are making
it because you want either to upgrade your armor or plan on selling it I am
afraid you will most likely be disappointed. Studded armor unfortunately is
far to difficult to make for the amount of protection it provides. For the
studs you must find a talented blacksmith willing to make them for you. If
you can find one they will most likely be priced at 1PP each. If you can
find someone to make them for you I recommend spending all you can to get as
many studs as possible. Because of the difficulty of studded armor you
will go though many, many studs on your way a suit of armor. Also, the
tailoring skill seems to improve very slowly after you have reached an
ability of 26. The other problem is that studded armor does not provide as
good protection as other types of armor you could find on the corpses of
certain creatures in Norrath. But as I stated at the beginning, if you are
able to make and wear a full suit of studded the self satisfaction could be
well worth all other obstacles.
