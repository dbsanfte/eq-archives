I don't know if this will help anyone looking for the Instill Doubt skill,
but as many of you are looking for this I thought I'd post. Let me know if
any of you get a chance to test this one!









"At Level 26 Bards can learn a skill called "Instill Doubt"
It causes your target to walk away from you.
Only one NPC in the game is known to be able to train a bard in this:
Caskin Marsheart, who may be found on the 2nd story of the bard's guild in
Freeport.
It should be just above Common in the list of skills.
Thanks and credit goes to the Concert Hall, a great site for bards. {
http://www.attcanada.net/~reaper/ }"
