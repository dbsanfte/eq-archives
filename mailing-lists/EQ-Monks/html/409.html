cloth is good and that sash quest is fine to run around 2-3. if i were you i
would not waste my money on leather or patchwork. its heavy and cost a lot
(not patchwork but its still heavy) and you will just get rid of it later...
at lvl 4 or 5 i would go to East Commons and kill beatles and snakes and a
black bear here and there. Just hang around the first inn and run to the
gaurds if you need to... also you can kill those little spiders as well.
The first big money item you want to buy is a set of netted or better gloves
(the order goes netted, woven, mesh) plan on spending at least 2 plat maybe
as much as 5 or 6 plat depending on the day. Also, i would not spend any
money on netted armor except for the gloves. Save you plat for at least
woven but buying netted gloves is fine. these gloves will allow you to hit
magic critters likes wisps and stuff which you really dont need to worry
about till at least lvl 8 and most likly 10. You really need a pair of
magic gloves so buy em even if your not ready to use em yet. Then as you
lvl up just start moving west through the commons and do a little grouping.
I went to befallen around lvl 7 (which is in West commons). If you stay on
the first floor and steer clear of trains it will provide you with good exp
and good loot. Its kinda risky gettin there and staying alive in there so
enter at your own risk. I fought in East and West Commons untill i hit lvl
15 after that you kinda have to move on or really start grouping. But the
commons can support you well from lvl 5 to 15
