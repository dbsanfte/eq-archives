Brother monk,

The Nybright sisters will serve as no obstacle to one of your training. Even
through fourteen orders of training, I have considered them less than equal
to
the task of one on one combat. Your abilities are best served in the undead
depths of Unrest or the outlying Cauldron. It would be my hope that no monk
among us would go so far as to take the life of a fellow human in order to
gain
some modicum of advancement. Our talents are best reserved for defending
those
less fortunate from the hordes of undead and staving off attacks from those
beast that are less civilized than we and which aggressively pursue us
unprovoked. I wish you good fortune in your travels.
