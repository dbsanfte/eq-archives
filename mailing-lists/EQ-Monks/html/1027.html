Good base for a high level are the following:

Fishbone Earring
Electrun Sapphire Earring
BP Necklace
Spider Silk Mask (think a named spider in Karana's drops)
Executioer's Hood
BP Ring
BP Ring
Serpentine Bracers
Azure Sleeves
Lupine Claw Guantlets
Foreman's Tunic
Cloak of Shadows
Flowing Black Silk Sash
Drake-hide leggings
Wolf-fur Slippers

Even better, next step up from the above are the following (some of this
comes from the planes):

replace the serpentine bracers with Shiverback-hide Wristbands
replace the azure sleves with Shiverback-hide Armbands
replace the lupine claw guantlets with Guantlets of Fiery Might
replace the Foresman tunic with Shiverback-hide Jerkin
replace the Cloak of Shadows with the Cloak of Flames
replace the Flowing Black Sash with a Thick Banded Belt
replace the Drake-hide leggins with Shiverback-hide leggins
replace the wolf-fur slippers with Shiverback-hide boots
