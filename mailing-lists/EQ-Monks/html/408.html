Ok I assume you started in the city of freeport

First you need to get your white training sash. Level
2 and 3 is the ideal time to get it. If you haven't
gotten it yet I won't ruin your first quest so you
will have to figure it out for yourself

By the time I was level five I pretty much had a full
set of cloth armor. Cloth is good for your level
because it is light and fairly cheap, you can buy it
in the Freeport Market, the shops near the bank.

You don't need to worry much about items until level
4or5, then I would start heading out. I would go to
through east freeport to the desert of ro and fight
near the inn. You won't have to worry about being
killed by madmen there and there is a good spawn of
beetle's and armadillos. Once you get to 7 you can
start killing orcs and snakes and stuff
