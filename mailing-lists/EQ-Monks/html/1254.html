Here is a pretty good list to start ya out.

Kizmet Bayashi


Fire Gear
------------------
Platinum Emerald Rings
Golden Emerald Bracers
White Gold Necklace (Froglok Noble, Lower Guk, Common Drop)
Earrings of Fire Reflection (Buy from the Lavastorm Gypsies)
Runed Bone Fork (Froglok Squire, Upper Guk, Rare Drop)
Crown of Froglok Kings (Froglok King, Lower Guk, Common Drop)
Dwarven Ringmail Tunic

Cold Gear
------------------
Platinum Peridot Rings
Golden Peridot Bracers
Crown of Froglok Kings (Froglok King, Lower Guk, Common Drop)
White Gold Necklace (Froglok Noble, Lower Guk, Common Drop)
Runed Bone Fork (Froglok Squire, Upper Guk, Rare Drop)
Dwarven Ringmail Tunic
Cloak of the Icebear
Mammoth Hide Legs (Goblin Alchemist, Permafrost, Common Drop)
Icy Greaves (Elite Goblin Guards, Permafrost, Uncommon Drop)

NOTE: There is plenty of other resist gear available. Do a little research
on a spoiler site and find out what else you can get.
