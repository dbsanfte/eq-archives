There really isn't anything that close anywhere...you can go to NK and
sell there or you can go to the centaurs and sell there. My advice is
that if you get someone that can sell for you if you have a friend you
trust enough to do this. Some times also there is someone in the zone
that is doing ATM stuff and will buy things from you (bronze,etc.) The
aviaks will sell to wood elves fine. The centaurs will sell to anyone
but they aren't too close. I don't remember but I think that the
aviaks sell bandages...or there is usually lots of mages running
around. If worse comes to worst you can get a mage to summon you
some. I have seen them sold in zone often. Anyway..just my 2 cents
worth.
