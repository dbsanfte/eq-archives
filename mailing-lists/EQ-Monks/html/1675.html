Mend is a skill you have to practice (unlike a pallys Lay on Hands), just
like fighting skills such as one handed blunt. Keep using it and the skill
will rise, once your skill goes over about 100 you won't see anymore
critical failures, they actually reduce your hit points instead of healing
them.

depending on your play style it can be very easy or quite hard to raise, if
you solo and use it as your main way of healing at lower levels it should
raise quite fast. if you group it can be harder to raise as its easy to fall
into the cleric heals me I'll save mend for an emergency.

i would use it whenever you can until it hits 100, a death at lower levels
because of a failed mend is nothing compared to a death at higher level
because you never trained the skill.
