Good point. A good place with few aggro mobs for probably 20 - 25 is East
Karana, near either the entrance to the gorge, or the road to hph. You can
kill gorge hounds, and if you get one or 2 others, crag spiders which are
great exp. My 22 ranger gets a bubble from less than 10 crags (soloing with
snare/archery, takes a LOT of arrows). They are usually about lvl 20 or 21.

Just stay away from the griffawns, even the ones that con green. They'll
tear you up quick with double attack that hits for 40's.
