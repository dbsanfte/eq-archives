Subject: Iksar Stat Test


I ran some tests last night on Iksar monks to see how the stats effected hp,
ac and attk. I know some of the people on the list could have told me this
information, so I'm passing the results along to the rest that haven't done
this experiment before as an FYI.

Every 5 points of Ag added increased AC by 1, for a max of 4 added AC if all
20 went into it.

About 80% of the points put into Str went into attk. All 20 pts into Str
improved attk by 16 points. The base was 10, then adding all 20 pts into
str almost tripled attk! The difference in battles was very noticeable.
Plus for some reason, my battle skills went up really fast compared to the
other tests with this configuration. Anyone know why?

Nothing for hp, ac or attk for dex. (I guess you all knew that.)

I started this exp to see what would happen with stamina. The results were
disappointing. It took 9 extra points of sta to increase hp by 1. 20 pts
into stamina still only increased hp by 1.


I know some of you have done this for human monks before. Were the results
similar, with the exception of better ac results for ag?
