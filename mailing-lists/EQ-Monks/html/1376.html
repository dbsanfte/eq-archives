Mino hero is level 40ish. you will need a full group of 35s to take him down (
you and 5 level 35-40 friends). A level 39 necro could take him down solo with
some difficulty. Depending on the server you play on he could be camped and so
you might want to bring a good NUKE along to ensure you earn the kill.

The first thing to do is raise qeynos warrior guild faction. Wiat let me take
that back... the first thing to do is go to www.gameznet.com and print out the
walk through for the quest. You have to do a silly quest to kerra isle before
you can get the hero bracer quest, and you have to con amiable before you can
get any quests at all.

Well rumor has it that if you kill minos and skeleton by the skelie camp he will
spawn in a matter of minutes (people have gotten 2 spawns an hour with this
method).

Take the shakle off the mino + faction with the warrior guild + complete the
first "runaway gladiator" quest, and you will get one hero bracers 9ac, 10 str,
5 poison, 5 disease.
