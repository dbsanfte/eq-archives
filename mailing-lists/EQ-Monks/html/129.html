Since my gear weighs in at around 11lbs, I can't carry a whole lot of loot.
So what I've started doing is more or less selling the stuff to other
people in my group for very little, like selling a Fine Steel Morning Star
to whoever in the group wants it for 1 plat. It's a deal for them 'cause
they can sell it to a vendor for 3 - 5 plat, and it's a deal for me because
I at least get some cash out of it.

The reason that I loot at all as opposed to taking just the spit is pretty
simple, you'll make a lot more if you loot it. If you haven't noticed, the
split isn't always equal between the looter and the rest of the group. For
a split to happen there must be at least as many coins of a given type as
there are group members. Example: Your group kills a critter that has 5
gold on it. If there are 4 people in the group, the looter gets 2 gold,
everyone else gets 1. If there are 5 people, 1 coin each. If there are
six, the looter gets all 5 coins and everyone else gets the shaft.

Brothers and Sisters, next time you think that you don't need much cash
send a tell to a master jeweler and ask them how much the high end black
pearl (agi/dex) jewelry costs. I have seen the plain jewels themselves
sell for over 50 platinum in auction on Tarew Marr, and they aren't in as
much demand as fire emeralds (str/dex).

Take care,
Dorath Quickfist
