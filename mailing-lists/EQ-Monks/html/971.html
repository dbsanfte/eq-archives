>1) I managed to buy some Cured Silk Armor for a
>relativily low price. So my choice is between Cured
>Silk Gi or Foreman's tunic. Any input?

I used to hate the look of mesh and my Lockjaw vest because they appeared
just like leather. When cured silk came around, I gave up the LJ vest to
wear the gi. After a while, though, I decided that combining the LJ vest
with cured silk armbands, pants, and sandals still gave me a distinctive
monk look while offering superior protection.

I recently acquired a Foreman's tunic from a friend and love it. It looks
like leather, too, but with the rest of my monk look I don't mind at all.
It will hold me until I can get a Syythrak hide vest and then eventually
shiverback.

The Foreman's tunic comes off the fire goblin foreman in Solusek's Eye
(SolA). It sells for 250-350pp, mostly because it's also desired by rangers
and druids. In my opinion it isn't worth the money compared to the LJ vest,
as +3 STR isn't going to change the way you play your monk.

>2) Where is a good place for a level 20 to hunt.
>>From 14-17 I hunted oasis and from 17-20 I hunted East
>Karana. Any recomendations?

Levels 17-20 should hunt in Highpass Hold. The orc side is phenomenal
experience and loot, and you can level very quickly. At level 20, move into
High Keep basement. Start out in a small (3-4 player) group in the lookout
room (any veteran will be able to show you where that is). After a level or
two, move into a full group in the guard room. With a well-rounded group,
you'll be able to power level here and make some great cash as well. This
room has taken me to 24th level.

It gets a little boring in terms of scenery, but the High Keep basement can
level you all the way into the low 30's by moving up to the warrior and
raider rooms.

I've travelled to Cazic Thule for a change of pace, and while it offers
some variety the trains are deadly. After I hit 25 I'll move to Unrest for
a look, and possibly even Mistmoore. Solusek's Eye and Najena are also
possibilities, if you have the means to sneak past the dark elf guards.
