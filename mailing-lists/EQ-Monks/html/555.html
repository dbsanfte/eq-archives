Brother Kaladiel,

The acquisition of Sashes and Headbands boils down to, you guessed it, Religion. EQ, as in RL, is divided by not only Races and Classes, but also Religious affiliation. The Ashen Order is a religious group who heeds to the calling of Quellious. The Silent Fist on the other hand (no pun intended) is Agnostic, following no deity. This determines the alignment of factions for us Monks Brothers and Sisters.

Repeated completion of the Silent Fist quests will build up your faction with the Ashen Order. If anyone knows of other ways to build faction, please post so that our Young Brothers and Sisters can achieve dual-quest status.
