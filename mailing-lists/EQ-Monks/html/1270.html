Subject: Here is my solution:


As a level 34 monk (all be it with slightly above average equipment for my
level) Here is what I did after verants "dissapointing" news:

I took my two plat bp rings off and placed em in my inventory. Grabbed a pair
of jagged bands. I switched from drake hide leggings to gator scale: 7 AC! best
monk legs in the game. (I camped the ancient croc for about 7 hours one day and
got em myself - made 2kpp selling the gator amrs in the process!)

Serp bracers were replaced first with an erolisi and a vox and then with a Hero
bracer and a vox bracer. I HIGHLY recommend the hero bracer despite its weight
it has Awsome AC and looks super cool with the black treant fists equiped.

I took off that exec hood and switched to a Crown of forglock kings - 12 just
rocks.

Caveat:
With all these changes I did notice mobs were hitting me for a lot less.
However duel wiel in particular was rising slowly (dex increase melee skill
learning rates) SO what I do now is after each level play for an hour our two
in "dex gear" to max out my skills quickly then switch back to the AC/STR gear
when I am done. I maxed out equipment I can get my dex to 145 and agi to 139.
With stats like those I learn SUPER fast ( like I say I am usually maxed all my
melee skills within an hour of levelling) then its back to the old AC/STR gear
for the real tough fights.
