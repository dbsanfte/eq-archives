I've noticed this as well. Black Burrows once had range of 60 to 80
players, and a high well over 100. Now it rarely has more than 40. Low
twenties is common.

With a high number of players on a server, no new players will start there
(maybe some). Even with the slowest leveling players, the average character
level will rise. This is also having a tremendous impact on the economies.
Items like shiny brass halberds, although great below level 20, are almost
mediocre at higher levels, so the prices are dropping.
