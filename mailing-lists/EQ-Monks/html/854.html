The only class that CAN'T Solo all the way to 50 is a Wizard.
However, with that said, only Shamans, Druids (less so with Kiting
curtailed), Monks, Necromancers, and Shadow Knights can do it effectively.
Enchanters, Magicians, Bards, Paladins, and Rangers can do it to some
extent. Clerics and Warriors could possibly do it, but that would be
nightmarish. Wizards simply can't due to the lack of a Pet.

Shadow Knights are really the class that was designed with Soloing
in mind. Especially the infamous Troll Shadow Knight. Until Kiting was
restricted though, they were not the best at it. Druids USED to be the best
Solo class in the game. Now, it is a toss-up between Shamans, Druids,
Monks, Necros, and SKs.

Personally, I would hate to try and Solo my way to 50 as an
Enchanter. The Pets we get are weak and Charm is "unstable". Don't get me
wrong, I love Charm but I like having a nice BIG Warrior right beside me to
Taunt the Mob off when Charm breaks.
