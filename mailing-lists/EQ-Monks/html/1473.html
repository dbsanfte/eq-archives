Nono... his spawn POINT spawns every 72-80 minutes. The alchemist, a
veteran, sentry, or a lookout will spawn. The alchemist is the rare spawn
at that spawn point.

In Misty, go to the single hut goblin camp. He shares the spawn point with
Mooto. It's the one just inside the hut. This is generally a 2-4 hour
camp. The goblin captain and gelatinous cube I think are on 20min spawns,
but I don't think they have placeholders.. i.e.: they always spawn.
Locations, I'm not too sure of, as they were pulled to the zone for me. :)
Alchemist in Misty is about the same level as the one in Runny. The goblin
captain is around level 22 or 23 I think, and so is the goblin captain.

They always have the quest items.
