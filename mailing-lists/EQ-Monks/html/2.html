Net gloves are the cheapest about 2 to 5 gold and least ac and most weight,
Woven are mid ac and weight for about 1 to 3 plat, and Mesh are the best ac
and weight for about 5 to 6 plat.
Net, Woven and Mesh are lighter than cloth and equal to or better than
leather products and all sorts of items are made from it

The best armor overall for a monk is Azure very hard to find and very
expensive, it comes from a level 35 - 40 dungeon.

All in all, if you have the money get the best available, you'll only spend
more upgrading later.
