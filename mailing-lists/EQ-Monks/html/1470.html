There are 3 spawn points, not 4 =)

The one on the left by the rock and the one in the middle are both 6 minute
spawns. The Alchemist spawn is very close to the middle spawn. This spawn
time ranges from 72-80 minutes. I have heard reports that the Beholder
Alchemist is level 19 consistently, however I cannot verify it as it was
green to me (lvl 37 at the time I did the quest).

Be prepared for the worst of camps. Personally, I spent 64+ hours waiting in
Beholder, and it finally arrived at the end of a 10 hour stretch. The key to
make him spawn seemed to be me saying "ok... one more placeholder then I
MUST sleep" *wink*

If you can manage to get the 2 6 minute spawns in a cycle, it is very easy
to tell when the ph spawn hits, as it will be in the middle of the canyon
and out of synch with the cycle. In all my hours camping, there was always a
ph spawn, it was never skipped.
