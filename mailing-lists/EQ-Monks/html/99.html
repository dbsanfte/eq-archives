For quite a while I was upset about the warrior petition, but after some
thinking I got over it. Sure, warriors and paladins can do a lot of
damage, if they have a 2 hander. But with a two hander they only get two
swings. Sure, they can dual wield, but most one handed weapons don't have
a very high damage. Sure, there are some but they aren't all that common.
Yeah, they'll be able to swing 4 times at level 29 like we do, but not for
as much damage as we can punch, and only if they have a good weapon will
they come close. At 29 I was helping a level 30 warrior friend to try and
get the great zweihander, and even with her damage and taunt combined I was
doing so much more damage she couldn't pull Karg off of me. Monks take
patience, but man o man is it worth it!

Think about this, Monks are the only class that can master hand to hand.
That means that up until level 20 other classes could (if they wanted
too), fight hand to hand just like a monk. They would have the same skill
in H2H as a monk, double attack and dual wield, and on top of that be able
to wear as much metal armor as they want. Hand to Hand can only start to
give damage bonuses at 101 skill (Master), or everybody would be able to
fight like a monk all the time. As far as I'm concerned, monks begin to
mature at 20th level, this is why I say monks take patience.

On the subject of feign death, I think it is one of the greatest skills in
the game! It is so convenient to have, we can go AFK at any time, in any
place, for any amount of time. Currently I'm level 30, and since I picked
up feign death I have only died 4 times.

Stick with it, it's well worth the time. :)

Dorath Quickfist
