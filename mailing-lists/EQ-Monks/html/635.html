Subject: Another take on twinking


I tend to run many different characters; monk, warrior, shaman, bard,
cleric, paladin, magician, and wizard. And yes, I twink between them, but
not outrageously so, and not so it unbalances anything. For example, my
monk (lvl 10) was generating cash at a healthy clip. So he gave all his
money to my warrior blacksmith (lvl 19). The blacksmith use the pp to make
a partial suite of large banded armor for my ogre shaman (lvl 8), who in
turn is looking for mesh armor in Upper Guk for my monk. I try to make it
more of a micro economy. If these three characters were owned by different
people, it wouldn't be a twink at all.

I have no doubt that there is outrageous twinking going on (saw a lvl 8 ogre
warrior with full bronze plate), but I think you can twink within reason and
not upset the game balance.
