Obsidian Bead Hoops are definately a good choince as everyone is saying.
they are the common drop off Singe in Solusek A The rare drop is another
good monk item being the Drakescale belt (Dex +3 Fire +10 AC 5). The only
other earrings I personally would recommend (that I know about anyways hehe)
are Gold Amber Earrings (Str +3), Electrum Sapphire Earrings (Str +4 Int
+2), or of course a Fishbone Earring (Dex +3 Enduring Breath).

As for Rings, the Jagged Bands are good, but there are a few rings to choose
from. Take a look <a
href="http://eq.castersrealm.com/misc/jewellery/jewel_ow.asp">here</a> for
some ideas. Personally, I am currently experimenting with increasing
strentgh, so I am saving for Platinum Fire Emerald rings, but there are a
few really good choices, depending on how you want to go.
