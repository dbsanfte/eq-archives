The Granite Bracer is dropped by Petrifin (sp?), a named basilisk, in the
Rathe
Mountains. The bracer is not lore like the Bracer of Erolissi and gives you
another plate armor color variant for the wrist. You could probably find this
item for sale, but the MOB is a rare around level 40s critter, so it may be
costly. For the curious, the stats on the bracer are 5 AC, 4 STR, 7 STA, and
0.8 weight. Wearable by ALL ALL.
