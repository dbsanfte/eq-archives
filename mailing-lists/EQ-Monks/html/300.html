Brother Zarvitz,

Your mind and your speed are your armor. Do not allow the frivolties of other
classes of adventures to cloud your mind and burden your body. Keep that one
item that affords you the greatest defense; a strong leather tunic will guard
your vital organs well. You will soon find a way to exchange it for a
sturdier
yet lighter mesh garment. Your bracers of mesh also provide you with strong
defense and add little to encumber your flow, so it may be to your
advantage to
retain those on your person. Soon, you may find it in your interest to locate
a jewler capable of crafting enchanted pieces to accelerate your innate
abilities; smaller such items can be had for only a few of your platinum
pieces, and may be well worth the expense. Do keep an eye out for traveling
merchants, and be not afraid to ask them how their jewels might help you.

May Quellious guide you,

Manjushri
Monk of the 10th Order, Disciple of Quellious, the Tranquil
