I can solo blues fairly easily. However, I need to find a place that
has a good spawn rate of them, close to a general merchant, and some
place that isn't overly camped. I could stay in the oasis and solo
blues easily. But it is way overcamped to be good.

Personally, I think trying to solo in a dungeon is not the right way to
go about it. You should instead find a place with non aggs or aggs spread
out enough that you can pick one off safely and safely kill it without
worrying about all the other creatures roaming about.
