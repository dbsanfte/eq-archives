Well my advice, is conditional on your starting stats, and the tyoe of group you
usually play with.

If you are a "tank" for your group (as you get higher and higher in level this
will become less and less viable) then a combo of errolisi and Vox might be
preferred to a serp/errolisi combo. The Errolisi will boost your damage output,
which will help you do more damge on ALL of your attacks (especially flying
kick) while the Vox provides just plain great AC at low wieght.


However if your base AGI is lower than 110, I would personally look after this
stat first. AGI below 110 is (for my personal taste) unacceptable for a monk.

I am lucky enough to have a weightless bag whereby I walk around with a second
set of equipment I switch into when I want to turn myslef into a "Human DOT".
For specific group situations and specialized circumstances various equipment
has advantages over others.

Generally speaking though, never let your AGI stay below 110, AC beats str - Str
is easily buffed by the druid, shaman or bard in your group.

So if your agi is below 110, consider using one of those serpentine bracers.
Otherwise I recomend a combo errolisi - Vox bracer on each arm.

Depending on your characters stats, and your own playing style there are valid
arguments to adjust this. Its up to you. This is just my recomendation. If you
are wondering what I use: hero bracer - serp combo.. and I am looking for a Vox
to replace my serp.
