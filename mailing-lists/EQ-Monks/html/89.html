mesh exists on a variety of froglogs in Guk. but here i'll try and give list
of where i've seen the most...

Tuk Warriors - no mesh, rare woven
Tuk Shaman - no mesh, rare woven
Tuk Knight - no mesh, sometimes woven
Gaz Warriors - rare mesh, no woven
Gaz Shaman - rare mesh, no woven
Gaz Knights - rare mesh, no woven
Ton Warriors - rare mesh, no woven
Ton Shaman - sometimes mesh, no woven
Ton Knights - sometimes mesh, no woven
Shin/Vis/Etc... - sometimes mesh, no woven

hope that saves some of you some time :)
