This entire discussion is silly and pointless. EQ is based on
a text MUD system that have been around for over 10 years
now. In the world of MUDs, there are MUDs that allow twinking
and ones that don't. Verant made the CHOICE when they
designed EQ to allow twinking, as they chose not to implement
any of the well-known ways of stopping twinking.

This was a conscious, design-level decision. This will not be
reversed any time in the future. So any arguments you are
making as to why twinking should not be allowed are fruitless
in terms of changing the game.

Then your arguments must be "moral" ones- "why you should
not twink your characters even though you can". And, well,
moral arguments are not easily solved.
