Im in Najena with a group of 20's now. My exp has been that if the green
pets dont jump you then youll want to make it deep in where there are many
safe places to setup and pull to. We like to setup by the ogre guard camp
fire near where the undead frogloks. We then camp that room and pull the
goblin wizards from around the way. If you go the other way towards
bonecrusher etc. instead of going lft into his room go rihgt and down the
hall and then take the T left. There are 2 magicians in there and 1 ogre
officer which is good to gang up on etc. Then you can stay there and camp
from there. Hope this helps.
