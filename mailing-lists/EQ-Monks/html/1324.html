HI everyone,

Well I was looking around for decent monk equipment that
would be (relatively) cheap to get. I found some jewellery that
is tough to beat

Gold Topaz Earring AC3 +8vsDisease wt0.1
Gold Pearl Necklace AC5 +9vsPoison wt0.1

The stones cost around 5pp each. The bars would cost 10pp.
If you can assemble the materials and find a nice enchanter,
they might be willing to make these for you for very little.
They should end up costing you 30pp max.

Short of the Diamondine Earrings (ac2 3agi 15hp), Obsidian
Hoops (ac2 3agi 3int) and Fishbone (waterbreathing), these
earrings are probably the best there is. I'm switching out
of the amber ones I have now as soon as I can find a shop
that sells topaz. Anyone know a shop??

The necklace is pretty good if you can't find or afford a
Collar of Undead Protection.
