OK here goes:

1) Start at the Zone from LAke rathe. YOu will see some cyclops and
Giant skells mulling around in an area infront of you.

2) Turn LEFT, HUGGING the zone wall unless you want to get your level 8-20
butt WHoooped!

3)You will pass a tent, and right after the tent you will see a
mountain ridge. It does quite reach all the way to teh Zone wall
along which you are sneaking creating something of a "pass" between the
two 'walls'. YOu want to go through that pass and then cross it to hugg
the mountain ridge on your right side. (Translation Turn Right after
crossing the pass and Hugg the wall again - This time it wont be the zone
wall but the wall of the ridge that youw ill be clinging to)

4) You will know you are on the right path if you come across an inn. Buy
sells stuff and stock up on food.

5) Continue on straight past the inn. YOu should be coming up on a second
mountain pass. GO through this one and bear slightly left. you will find
yourself in a narrow valley or gully (sp?)

6) You should see a Pillar with some bettles/scarabs around them. THis
will let you konw youare on the right path. Continue on running,
following the narrow "gully" that you are in.

7) Eventually you will see a change in the grennery of to your right - the
grass willl go from green to a mossy grean-brown color. This is the
mrking for the path to Ferrot.

8) NOTE: when you zone across to ferrot there is alot of danger - tehre is
a undead camp along the zone wall to teh right - You WILL NEED invis vs
undead (and SOW) to get past that alive), Following the path infront of
you leads to a bridge with paths leading to both OGGoK or the zone to
innothule. BE WARY OF BOUNCERS! THey are very mean to us goodly races
and 2 of them roam the entire ZONE - *sigh I have died many times to that
damned BOUNCER FLERB! *I spit on his grave!*

Anyway consider yourself warned.
Good Journeys to you.
