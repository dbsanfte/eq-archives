> It only makes sense to use your Mend the second you are around two
> bubbles down for 2 reasons.
>
> First off it really doens't matter when you mend, as long as you know
> you are going to need it, in this case, any time you lose more than
> about 1 1/2 bubbles you are going to need it, so I make a point to
> Always use mine around 2 bubbles down. Reason one is so your cleric
> (if you are in a party) doesn't waste mana on you. I always tell my
> clerics, only pop a heal my way if I drop below 3 bubbles. That way
> I can use my mend without the fear of the cleric casting a heal on me
> and one of us wasting either.
>
> Second main reason, specially when soloing, is that if you use it at the
> beginning of a big combat (ie when you first reach about 2 bubbles
> down) then you will have that mend button pop up a little bit more
> quicker for the next combat, rather than probably having to go the
> next combat without it.
