Oh my God... Feign Death along with Mend are the two biggest
advantages of being a monk in the first place. First off, take whatever
time you need and do nothing but pound that Feign Death button until you
max the skill out for your level (90 at level 17). I can GUARANTEE that
this skill WILL save your life repeatedly. There are a couple of things
which can increase your likelyhood of success, though. First, stop
attacking your opponent before you try to FD. Others claim that having low
HP as well as having your back turned will help, but my experience does not
bear this out.
I could go on and on about Feign Death (as a matter of fact, I
think I did in a post just last week), but please dont give up on it yet.
