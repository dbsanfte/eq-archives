Steel Warrior Initiation: This quest can be done around level 6 or so
(lower with a group). Over the course of it, you will receive several
faction changes raising Steel Warriors, Guards of Qeynos and Knights of
Truth and Lowering Corrupt Qeynos Guards and Freeport Militia. Go to
Brin Stolunger in Warriors Guild S. Qeynos, tell him you are a [young
steel warrior]. He will give you a ten slot arena sack and tell you to
collect 5 bat wings and 5 snake scales and combine them inside to create
a full sack. When you give it back to him, you get a few copper, xp,
faction and a note that you are to take to Axe Broadsmith in Qeynos
Hills. (He's in the hut by the pond) When you give him the note, he will
tell you to kill the [strange skeleton] across the pond. When you kill
it, bring it's skull to him as proof, and you'll get more coppers, xp,
faction and a letter of recommendation to take back to Brin Stolunger.
When you return to Qeynos and give the letter to Brin, you get MORE
coins, xp, faction, and another note. This one you take downstairs to
the animal pits below the arena, and give it to Grahan Rothkar who tells
you it's time for your Final Test. He will give you a key to open one of
the animal pens so you can enter and fight a young lion. When you kill
the young lion, bring it's pelt and meat to Grahan and you'll get MORE
copper, xp, faction and TADA... Strongbear Bracers. And he welcomes you
to the Steel Warriors! (Submitted by Mistabus).
