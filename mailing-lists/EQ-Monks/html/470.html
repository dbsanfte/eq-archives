The caiman/croc side of Oasis is ok, but it's usually so crowded. Try
hunting the other side (orc side) of the zone with a good group. I am
level 15, and hunting there is going great! All of the "an" orc warriors
are blue or white, orc clerics are blue and white. AND since my group
contains a level 18 mage, we can even take down the "a" orc warriors (Level
20 mob). There are more orcs than you can shake a stick at, plus the loot
is way better than fighting crocs. Also, on Brell at least, there is VERY
low competition for kills. I think at best I see one other group hunting
the ENTIRE side of the zone.

Normally we set up camp on a little ledge somewhere near the SW corner of
the zone. We more or less constant kill blue mobs, with the occasional
white or red thrown in.

Dornn, Level 15 monk, Brell Serilis
