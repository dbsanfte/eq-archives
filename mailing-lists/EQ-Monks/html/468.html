14 in Misty Thicket? I think that's a little high for there. And if I recall
correctly, 14 is the very upper edge for wisps and most will not give exp. I
would recommend either the Dervs in West Commons, or a trip with a party to
East Karanas for Reaver hunting. East Karanas, for those of you that don't
know, has a GREAT spread of critters. I started hunting there about 16,
found things to solo and things to group up with. At lvl 27, there are still
a few things there that will offer me exp. It is also right next door to
Highpass, should your friend want to take you there. Personally, I hate
Highpass... On Fennin Ro, it has become a total free for all, with solo
wizzies blasting everything in sight. Same thing with the Pickclaws in
Highkeep.

If you want to stay in Eastern Antonica, that's about it really. You could
probably group and go into Oasis, but it would be a little risky. Don't
limit yourself to just a few zones tho. Crushbone, Steamfront Minos, Aviaks,
Lake Rathe, Rathe Mts, Dagnors Cauldron, S. Ro... they all have some
critters that would suit you well.
